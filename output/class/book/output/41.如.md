## as; like
### sub-word
#### if
---
- **traditional:** 如
- **simplified:** 如
- **pronunciation:** rú
- **definition:** as; like
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 如果 (rú guǒ, "if")
  - 如此 (rú cǐ, "like this")
  - 比如 (bǐ rú, "for example")
  - 突如其来(來) (tū rú qí lái, "come suddenly")
- **etymology (如):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 女 (nǚ, "woman") + semantic: 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 女 as the phonetic with 口 as the semantic determinative indicating speech or utterance, yielding the idea of conforming to what is said and thus “as; like; according to.” An alternative interpretation sees 女 ideographically as a woman obeying an order, reinforcing the sense of compliance.
  - **component characters:**
    - 女 (nǚ, "woman")
    - 口 (kǒu, "mouth")
