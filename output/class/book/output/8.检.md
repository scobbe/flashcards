## to inspect
### sub-word
#### to examine; to check
---
- **traditional:** 檢
- **simplified:** 检
- **pronunciation:** jiǎn
- **definition:** to inspect
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 检(檢)查 (jiǎn chá, "to inspect")
  - 检(檢)测(測) (jiǎn cè, "to test")
  - 检(檢)验(驗) (jiǎn yàn, "to verify")
  - 检(檢)索 (jiǎn suǒ, "to search")
- **etymology (檢):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic 木 (mù, "wood") + phonetic 佥(僉) (qiān, "all").
  - **interpretation:** 木 provides the semantic domain of wooden objects or implements, while 佥(僉) supplies the sound. Some propose it originally indicated a blade atop a wooden spear, with later extension to standards and checking (e.g., wooden labels), yielding the modern sense of inspecting. An alternative note relates it to 剑(劍) (jiàn, "sword") via a blade reference.
  - **component characters:**
    - 木 (mù, "wood")
    - 佥(僉) (qiān, "all")
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%AA%A2
  - **simplification rule (检):** Replaced the phonetic 僉 with the simplified 佥 while retaining the 木 radical, reducing stroke count in the phonetic component.
