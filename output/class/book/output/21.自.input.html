Pictogram (象形): a nose — original form of 鼻 (OC *blids, “nose”). Later phonetically borrowed as an instance of jiajie (假借) for 自 (OC *ɦljids, “oneself; from”).

Folk etymology considers the meaning to arise from a habit of pointing at one’s nose to indicate oneself.

      Definitions

      [edit]

      自

      1. self, oneself; one's own
        自顧自／自顾自  ―  zìgùzì  ―  each minding his own business; each for himself
      2. certainly, of course
      3. personally
      4. since; from
        借自  ―  jiè zì  ―  (please add an English translation of this usage example)
        自左而右  ―  zì zuǒ ér yòu  ―  from left to right
      5. (swimming) short for 自由泳 (zìyóuyǒng, “freestyle”)
