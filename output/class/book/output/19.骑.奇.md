## strange
### sub-component
#### to ride
---
- **traditional:** 奇
- **simplified:** 奇
- **pronunciation:** qí
- **definition:** strange
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 奇怪 ( qíguài, "strange")
  - 奇迹(蹟) ( qíjì, "miracle")
  - 奇特 ( qítè, "peculiar")
  - 奇幻 ( qíhuàn, "fantasy")
- **etymology (奇):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 大 (dà, "big") + phonetic: 可 (kě, "may") → 奇 (qí, "strange").
  - **interpretation:** 大 serves as the semantic determinative, conveying greatness or extraordinariness that underlies senses like remarkable and, by extension, strange. 可 functions as the phonetic element in Old Chinese, guiding the pronunciation that evolved to qí.
  - **component characters:**
    - 大
    - 可
    - 奇
