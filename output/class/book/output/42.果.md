## fruit; result
### sub-word
#### if
---
- **traditional:** 果
- **simplified:** 果
- **pronunciation:** guǒ
- **definition:** fruit; result
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 水果 (shuǐ guǒ, "fresh fruit")
  - 结(結)果 (jié guǒ, "end result")
  - 后(後)果 (hòu guǒ, "adverse consequence")
  - 果汁 (guǒ zhī, "fruit juice")
- **etymology (果):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of fruits on a tree.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts fruits hanging on a tree, originally meaning literal fruit. By metaphor, it extended to mean the outcome or result—the 'fruit' of actions—which matches its modern senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
