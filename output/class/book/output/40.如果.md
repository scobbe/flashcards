## if
---
- **traditional:** 如果
- **simplified:** 如果
- **pronunciation:** rúguǒ
- **definition:** if
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 如果说(說) (rúguǒ shuō, "if we say")
  - 如果需要 (rúguǒ xūyào, "if needed")
  - 如果没(沒)有 (rúguǒ méiyǒu, "if there is no")
  - 如果可能 (rúguǒ kěnéng, "if possible")
- **etymology (如果):**
  - **component characters:**
    - None, compound word
