## immediately; right away
---
- **traditional:** 馬上
- **simplified:** 马上
- **pronunciation:** mǎshàng
- **definition:** immediately; right away
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 马(馬)上就来(來) (mǎ shàng jiù lái, "coming right away")
  - 马(馬)上见(見) (mǎ shàng jiàn, "see you soon")
  - 马(馬)上开(開)始 (mǎ shàng kāi shǐ, "start immediately")
  - 马(馬)上出发(發) (mǎ shàng chū fā, "set off immediately")
- **etymology (馬上):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 马(馬) (mǎ, "horse") + 上 (shàng, "on; upon") → literally 马背上(馬背上) (mǎ bèi shàng, "on the back of a horse") → adverbial sense "at once; immediately".
  - **interpretation:** Originally denotes being on a horse, i.e., on horseback. Because horses were the fastest transport in antiquity, the phrase extended to mean acting right away, which is the modern adverbial sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - 马
    - 馬
    - 上
    - 背
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A6%AC%E4%B8%8A
  - **simplification rule (马上):** Simplified by replacing 馬 with 马 to reduce strokes, while 上 remains unchanged.
