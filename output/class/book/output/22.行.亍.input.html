Definitions

[edit]

亍

  1. to stop; to stand still
  2. to step with the right foot
  3. to take small steps
  4. a surname
Compounds
[edit]
  • 彳亍 (chìchù)

Etymology 2

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 亍 – see 街 (“street; market; fair; etc.”).
(This character is the second-round simplified form of 街).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
