## upper arm
### sub-word
#### arm
---
- **traditional:** 膊
- **simplified:** 膊
- **pronunciation:** bó
- **definition:** upper arm
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 赤膊 (chìbó, "strip to the waist")
  - 打赤膊 (dǎ chìbó, "go shirtless")
  - 胳膊 (gēbo, "the arm")
  - 脱(脫)膊 (tuōbó, "bare one's arms")
- **etymology (膊):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 肉 (ròu, "flesh") + phonetic: 尃 (fū, "spread") → 膊 (bó, "upper arm").
  - **interpretation:** 肉 marks this as a body-part character, aligning with meanings like upper arm and shoulder. 尃 supplies the pronunciation, with historical sound correspondence leading to the modern reading bó.
  - **component characters:**
    - 肉
    - 尃
    - 膊
