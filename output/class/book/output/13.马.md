## horse
### sub-word
#### immediately; right away
---
- **traditional:** 馬
- **simplified:** 马
- **pronunciation:** mǎ
- **definition:** horse
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 骑(騎)马(馬) (qí mǎ, "ride a horse")
  - 马(馬)勺 (mǎ sháo, "big ladle")
  - 马(馬)赛(賽) (mǎ sài, "Marseille")
  - 马(馬)尔(爾)代夫 (mǎ ěr dài fū, "Maldives")
- **etymology (馬):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 马(馬) (mǎ, "horse") → head simplified to 目 (mù, "eye"); legs stylized as 灬 (huǒ, "four dots") → simplified modern form follows cursive of 马(馬) (mǎ, "horse").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a horse with a left-facing head, flowing mane, and four legs that later stylize as four dots. The simplified shape adopts a cursive-derived outline while preserving the original animal meaning, which naturally extends to senses like the chess piece and related compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 马
    - 馬
    - 目
    - 灬
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A6%AC
  - **simplification rule (马):** Adopted a cursive-derived outline of 馬 to reduce strokes, regularizing the complex interior into a simple frame as 马.
