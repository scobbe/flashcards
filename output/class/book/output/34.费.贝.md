## 
### sub-component
#### fee; expense
---
- **traditional:** 貝
- **simplified:** 贝
- **pronunciation:** bèi
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 宝(寶)贝(貝) (bǎobèi, "precious baby")
  - 贝(貝)壳(殼) (bèiké, "sea shell")
  - 贝(貝)类(類) (bèilèi, "shellfish category")
  - 贝(貝)雕 (bèidiāo, "shell carving")
- **etymology (貝):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a cowry shell used as money → simplified from a cursive-script form.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a cowry shell used as money in ancient China, hence the senses ‘shellfish/cowry’ and ‘money/currency’. In Simplified Chinese the graph follows a cursive-derived form with streamlined strokes to yield 贝.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%B2%9D
  - **simplification rule (贝):** Adopted the running-script form of 貝, merging interior details and reducing strokes for ease and speed.
