## 
### sub-component
#### continuous aspect particle
---
- **traditional:** 著
- **simplified:** 著
- **pronunciation:** zhuó / zháo / zhāo
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 着(著)火 (zháohuǒ, "catch fire")
  - 着(著)急 (zháojí, "be anxious")
  - 着(著)重 (zhuózhòng, "place emphasis")
  - 着(著)陆(陸) (zhuólù, "to land")
- **etymology (著):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: 艹(艸) (cǎo, "grass") + 者 (zhě, "person").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic determinative 艹(艸) indicating a plant-related sense with the phonetic 者 providing the sound. It originally referred to plant phenomena like blooming or bearing fruit, then broadened to senses of contacting, attaching, or resulting, which underlie modern uses such as wear, touch, ignite, and land.
  - **component characters:**
    - 艸
    - 者
