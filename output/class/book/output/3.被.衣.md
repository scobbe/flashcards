## clothing
### sub-component
#### by; passive marker
---
- **traditional:** 衣
- **simplified:** 衣
- **pronunciation:** yī
- **definition:** clothing
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 衣服 (yī fú, "clothes")
  - 内(內)衣 (nèi yī, "underwear")
  - 洗衣机(機) (xǐ yī jī, "washing machine")
  - 衣柜(櫃) (yī guì, "wardrobe")
- **etymology (衣):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of chest and upper clothing.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the chest with an upper garment draped over it. The original sense is a physical garment, which naturally extended to general clothing and, by metaphor, coverings or coatings. The graphic form later served as a container shape in derived clothing-related characters.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
