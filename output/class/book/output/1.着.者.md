## person
### sub-component
#### continuous aspect particle
---
- **traditional:** 者
- **simplified:** 者
- **pronunciation:** zhě
- **definition:** person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 记(記)者 (jì zhě, "journalist")
  - 工作者 (gōng zuò zhě, "worker")
  - 前者 (qián zhě, "the former")
  - 后(後)者 (hòu zhě, "the latter")
- **etymology (者):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane") → phonetic: 者 (zhě, "nominalizer/person").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a sugarcane stalk with nodes and a mouth below. The graph was later borrowed for its sound to write the nominalizer/agentive suffix 者 (zhě, "person; one who"), which matches its modern use. Some sources instead take the original pictograph as 煮 (zhǔ, "to boil") rather than 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane").
  - **component characters:**
    - 蔗
    - 者
