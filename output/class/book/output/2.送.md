## to see off; to take (someone)
---
- **traditional:** 送
- **simplified:** 送
- **pronunciation:** sòng
- **definition:** to see off; to take (someone)
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 送我回家 (sòng wǒ huí jiā, "take me home")
  - 送给(給)你 (sòng gěi nǐ, "give to you")
  - 送货(貨) (sòng huò, "deliver goods")
  - 送别(別) (sòng bié, "see off")
- **etymology (送):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 辶 (chuò, "walk") + phonetic: 灷 (zhuàn, "scorch") → phono-semantic compound.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 辶 as the semantic determinative for movement with 灷 as the phonetic element indicating the sound. The walking component suits meanings like sending, escorting, and delivering, while the phonetic guides the reading that developed into sòng.
  - **component characters:**
    - 辶
    - 灷
