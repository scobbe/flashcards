## by; passive marker
---
- **traditional:** 被
- **simplified:** 被
- **pronunciation:** bèi
- **definition:** by; passive marker
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 被问(問) (bèi wèn, "be questioned")
  - 被动(動) (bèi dòng, "passive")
  - 被告 (bèi gào, "defendant")
  - 被迫 (bèi pò, "be forced")
- **etymology (被):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: 衣 (yī, "clothing") + phonetic: 皮 (pí, "skin").
  - **interpretation:** The character is a phono-semantic compound with 衣 as the semantic determinative indicating clothing/covering and 皮 as the phonetic element approximating the historical sound. It originally meant to wear or be covered and later extended to mark the passive, indicating being affected or covered by an action.
  - **component characters:**
    - 衣 (yī, "clothing")
    - 皮 (pí, "skin")
