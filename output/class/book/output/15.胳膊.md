## arm
---
- **traditional:** 胳膊
- **simplified:** 胳膊
- **pronunciation:** gēbo
- **definition:** arm
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 胳膊被撞伤(傷) (gē bo bèi zhuàng shāng, "arm was injured")
  - 胳膊腕子 (gē bo wàn zi, "wrist")
  - 胳膊疼 (gē bo téng, "arm hurts")
  - 胳膊骨折 (gē bo gǔ zhé, "broken arm")
- **etymology (胳膊):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 胳 (gē, "arm") + 膊 (bo, "upper arm").
  - **interpretation:** The word combines two body-part morphemes, with 胳 as a bound form and 膊 denoting the upper arm, to express the whole arm. This semantic combination directly yields the modern sense “arm.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 胳
    - 膊
