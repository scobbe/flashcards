## to examine; to check
---
- **traditional:** 檢查
- **simplified:** 检查
- **pronunciation:** jiǎnchá
- **definition:** to examine; to check
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 检(檢)查身体(體) (jiǎn chá shēn tǐ, "medical checkup")
  - 检(檢)查员(員) (jiǎn chá yuán, "inspector")
  - 检(檢)查站 (jiǎn chá zhàn, "checkpoint")
  - 检(檢)查点(點) (jiǎn chá diǎn, "inspection point")
- **etymology (檢查):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 检(檢) (jiǎn, "inspect") + 查 (chá, "examine").
  - **interpretation:** A coordinate compound of 检 and 查 meaning to inspect and to examine. Combining the near-synonyms intensifies the notion of looking into something, yielding the modern sense of checking or inspecting across administrative, technical, and medical contexts.
  - **component characters:**
    - 检
    - 檢
    - 查
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%AA%A2%E6%9F%A5
  - **simplification rule (检查):** Simplified by replacing the 僉 phonetic in 檢 with its simplified form 佥, reducing strokes; 查 remained unchanged.
