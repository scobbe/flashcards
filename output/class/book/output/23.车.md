## vehicle; car
### sub-word
#### bicycle
---
- **traditional:** 車
- **simplified:** 车
- **pronunciation:** chē
- **definition:** vehicle; car
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 汽车(車) (qì chē, "car")
  - 火车(車) (huǒ chē, "train")
  - 车(車)站 (chē zhàn, "station")
  - 开(開)车(車) (kāi chē, "drive a car")
- **etymology (車):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: carriage seen from above with two large wheels and a canopy → load area indicated by 田 (tián, "field").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a carriage seen from above, showing wheels and canopy. As writing became vertical, the drawing was regularized, with the central compartment indicated by 田, which fits its modern use for vehicles and wheeled devices. The simplified 车 derives from the cursive form of 車.
  - **component characters:**
    - 田
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%BB%8A
  - **simplification rule (车):** Adopted the cursive form of 車 to create 车, reducing strokes and regularizing lines for faster writing.
