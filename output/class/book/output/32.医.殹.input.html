Definitions

[edit]

殹

  1. The sound of groaning.
  2. Used in the name of one of the Stone Drum Poems (石鼓文 (shígǔwén)), in the word 汧殹.
  3. A particle used in the middle or at the end of sentences. Similar to 也 (yě) and 兮 (xī).
