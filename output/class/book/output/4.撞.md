## to hit; collide
---
- **traditional:** 撞
- **simplified:** 撞
- **pronunciation:** zhuàng
- **definition:** to hit; collide
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 被车(車)撞 (bèi chē zhuàng, "be hit by a car")
  - 撞车(車) (zhuàng chē, "car crash")
  - 撞墙(牆) (zhuàng qiáng, "hit a wall")
  - 撞见(見) (zhuàng jiàn, "bump into someone")
- **etymology (撞):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 扌 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic: 童 (tóng, "child").
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is the semantic determinative indicating a hand-related action, fitting the sense of striking or colliding. 童 serves as the phonetic, reflecting the historical doŋ-series that yielded the modern pronunciation. Some sources note a possible etymological link with 冲(衝) (chōng, "dash, clash").
  - **component characters:**
    - 手
    - 童
