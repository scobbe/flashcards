#### medicine or drug; to medicate

- **contemporary usage:**

- **etymology (艸):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 草 (cǎo, "grass") → duplication of 屮 (chè, "sprout").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts tufts of grass, formed by repeating the sprout element 屮 to evoke blades of vegetation. This ancient graph later yielded the standard character 草 and survives as the grass radical form. Some sources misidentify 䒑 as a variant, but it actually depicts 止 over a boat and is unrelated.
