## to check; to search
### sub-word
#### to examine; to check
---
- **traditional:** 查
- **simplified:** 查
- **pronunciation:** chá
- **definition:** to check; to search
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 查票 (chá piào, "check tickets")
  - 查电(電)表 (chá diànbiǎo, "read electric meter")
  - 查词(詞)典 (chá cídiǎn, "look up in dictionary")
  - 查档(檔)案 (chá dàng'àn, "consult reference materials")
- **etymology (查):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 木 (mù, "wood") + phonetic: 且 (qiě, "and") → bottom stylized as 旦 (dàn, "dawn").
  - **interpretation:** 木 serves as the semantic determinative for wood/trees, matching the original senses ‘hawthorn’ and ‘wooden raft’, while 且 provides the approximate sound chá. Over time the graph was used for the verb ‘to check; to search’, with the plant sense preserved in 楂; the lower part was later regularized as 旦 in modern forms.
  - **component characters:**
    - 木 (mù, "wood")
    - 且 (qiě, "and")
    - 旦 (dàn, "dawn")
