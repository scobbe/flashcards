Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 医药 – see 醫藥 (“medicine; pharmaceuticals”).
(This term is the simplified form of 醫藥).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
medical; medicine; doctor
medical; medicine; doctor; to cure; to treat
 
 medicine; drug; cure
trad. (醫藥) 醫 藥
simp. (医药) 医 药

Noun

[edit]

醫藥

  1. medicine; pharmaceuticals

[edit]

  • 醫藥費 / 医药费 (yīyàofèi)
