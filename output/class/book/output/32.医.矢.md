## arrow
### sub-component
#### medical; doctor
---
- **traditional:** 矢
- **simplified:** 矢
- **pronunciation:** shǐ
- **definition:** arrow
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 矢口否认(認) (shǐ kǒu fǒu rèn, "flatly deny")
  - 矢志不渝 (shǐ zhì bù yú, "unswerving resolve")
  - 矢量 (shǐ liàng, "vector")
  - 矢量图(圖) (shǐ liàng tú, "vector graphic")
- **etymology (矢):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of an arrow.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts an arrow with a straight shaft and pointed head. The concrete meaning ‘arrow’ is primary; by later semantic extension in Classical Chinese, it also came to mean ‘to vow’.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
