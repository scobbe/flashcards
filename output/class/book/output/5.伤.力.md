## strength
### sub-component
#### to hurt; to wound
---
- **traditional:** 力
- **simplified:** 力
- **pronunciation:** lì
- **definition:** strength
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 力量 (lì liàng, "power")
  - 力争(爭) (lì zhēng, "strive for")
  - 力图(圖) (lì tú, "try to")
  - 力求 (lì qiú, "make every effort")
- **etymology (力):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 臂 (bì, "arm") → semantic: 力 (lì, "strength").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bent arm, associating the limb with physical power, which yields the sense of strength. Some sources alternatively see a wooden plough, 犁 (lí, "plough"), as the depicted object; either concrete referent motivates the meaning of physical force that extends to modern senses like power and capability.
  - **component characters:**
    - 臂
    - 力
