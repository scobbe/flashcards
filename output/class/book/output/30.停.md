## to stop; to park
---
- **traditional:** 停
- **simplified:** 停
- **pronunciation:** tíng
- **definition:** to stop; to park
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 停在路旁边(邊) (tíng zài lù páng biān, "park by roadside")
  - 停车(車) (tíng chē, "to park")
  - 停止 (tíng zhǐ, "to stop")
  - 停电(電) (tíng diàn, "power outage")
- **etymology (停):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 亻 (rén, "person") + phonetic: 亭 (tíng, "pavilion") → 停 (tíng, "to stop").
  - **interpretation:** 亻 serves as the semantic determinative, indicating a human action. 亭 provides the sound value tíng, while the combined form denotes a person halting or staying, matching modern senses like stopping and parking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 亻
    - 亭
    - 停
