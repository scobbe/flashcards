## sun
### sub-component
#### dawn
---
- **traditional:** 日
- **simplified:** 日
- **pronunciation:** rì
- **definition:** sun
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 日班 (rì bān, "day shift")
  - 日本 (rìběn, "Japan")
  - 日式 (rìshì, "Japanese-style")
  - 中日词(詞)典 (zhōng rì cídiǎn, "Chinese-Japanese dictionary")
- **etymology (日):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 日 (rì, "sun") → central dot or line added to distinguish it from similar enclosures.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the sun; a central dot or line was later added to avoid confusion with enclosure-like graphs. The original sense is the solar disk, which extended to mean a day in timekeeping and remains the core modern meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 日
