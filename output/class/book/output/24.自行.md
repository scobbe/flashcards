## by oneself; of one’s own accord
### sub-word
#### bicycle
---
- **traditional:** 自行
- **simplified:** 自行
- **pronunciation:** zìxíng
- **definition:** by oneself; of one’s own accord
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 自行其是 (zìxíngqíshì, "do as one pleases")
  - 自行火炮(砲) (zìxínghuǒpào, "self-propelled artillery")
  - 自行车(車) (zìxíngchē, "a bicycle")
  - 自行决(決)定 (zìxíngjuédìng, "decide on one's own")
- **etymology (自行):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 自 (zì, "self") + 行 (xíng, "go").
  - **interpretation:** 自 denotes the self, while 行 means to go or act; together they indicate acting or moving by oneself. The sense extends to doing something of one’s own accord; in astronomy it denotes a body’s own proper motion.
  - **component characters:**
    - 自
    - 行
