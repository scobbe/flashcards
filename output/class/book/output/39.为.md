## to be; to act as; for
### sub-word
#### to think; to assume
---
- **traditional:** 為
- **simplified:** 为
- **pronunciation:** wéi
- **definition:** to be; to act as; for
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 为(為)主 (wéi zhǔ, "be primary")
  - 为(為)止 (wéi zhǐ, "until")
  - 为(為)难(難) (wéi nán, "make things difficult")
  - 为(為)首 (wéi shǒu, "take the lead")
- **etymology (為):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 又 (yòu, "hand") + 象 (xiàng, "elephant") → 爪 (zhǎo, "hand; claw") + 象 (xiàng, "elephant").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a hand holding an elephant, conveying the act of handling or doing, which broadened to meanings like do, act, be, and the prepositional sense for. Some sources, such as Shuowen, alternatively interpret the figure as a female macaque.
  - **component characters:**
    - 又
    - 象
    - 爪
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%82%BA
  - **simplification rule (为):** Standardized the cursive-abbreviated form of 爲 into 为, reducing strokes and collapsing complex components for ease of writing.
