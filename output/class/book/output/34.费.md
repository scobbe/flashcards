## fee; expense
### sub-word
#### medical expenses
---
- **traditional:** 費
- **simplified:** 费
- **pronunciation:** fèi
- **definition:** fee; expense
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 费(費)用 (fèi yòng, "expenses")
  - 费(費)率 (fèi lǜ, "fee rate")
  - 费(費)劲(勁) (fèi jìn, "very strenuous")
  - 费(費)电(電) (fèi diàn, "consumes electricity")
- **etymology (費):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 弗 (fú, "not") + semantic: 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry").
  - **interpretation:** 贝(貝) is the semantic element indicating money or valuables, fitting modern senses like fees and expenses. 弗 serves as the phonetic component, guiding the pronunciation toward fèi; the monetary determinative explains why the character pertains to costs.
  - **component characters:**
    - 弗 (fú, "not")
    - 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry")
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%B2%BB
  - **simplification rule (费):** Simplified by replacing 貝 with 贝 and compressing the remaining strokes of 費 into the compact form 费, reducing strokes and regularizing components.
