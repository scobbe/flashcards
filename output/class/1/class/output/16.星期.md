## week
### sub-word
#### Sunday
---
- **traditional:** 星期
- **simplified:** 星期
- **pronunciation:** xīngqī
- **definition:** week
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 星期一 (xīngqīyī, "first day of week")
  - 星期五 (xīngqīwǔ, "fifth day of week")
  - 星期天 (xīngqītiān, "Sunday, weekend day")
  - 星期几(幾) (xīngqījǐ, "which day of week")
- **etymology (星期):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 星 (xīng, "star") + 期 (qī, "period") → 星期 (xīngqī, "week").
  - **interpretation:** 星 denotes celestial bodies, and 期 means a period or phase. Combined, they refer to a time period reckoned by the stars, which in modern usage became the seven-day week and the prefix used to form weekday names.
  - **component characters:**
    - 星
    - 期
