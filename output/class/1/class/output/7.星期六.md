## Saturday
---
- **traditional:** 星期六
- **simplified:** 星期六
- **pronunciation:** xīng qī liù
- **definition:** Saturday
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 星期六、星期天 (xīngqī liù, xīngqī tiān, "Saturday and Sunday")
  - 星期六晚上 (xīngqī liù wǎnshang, "Saturday night")
  - 星期六早上 (xīngqī liù zǎoshang, "Saturday morning")
  - 星期六购(購)物 (xīngqī liù gòuwù, "Saturday shopping")
- **etymology (星期六):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 星期 (xīngqī, "week") + 六 (liù, "six").
  - **interpretation:** Literally "week six": 星期 ‘week’ combined with 六 ‘six’ denotes the sixth day of the week. In modern usage this refers to Saturday.
  - **component characters:**
    - 星
    - 期
    - 六
