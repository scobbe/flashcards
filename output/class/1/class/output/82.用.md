## to use
### sub-word
#### need not or don't have to
---
- **traditional:** 用
- **simplified:** 用
- **pronunciation:** yòng
- **definition:** to use
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 用户(戶) (yònghù, "end user")
  - 用语(語) (yòngyǔ, "wording and terms")
  - 用电(電) (yòngdiàn, "electricity usage")
  - 用途 (yòngtú, "intended use")
- **etymology (用):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 桶 (tǒng, "bucket").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a water bucket. As a common implement for carrying and doing tasks, the graph generalized to the verbal sense of employing or using something.
  - **component characters:**
    - 桶
