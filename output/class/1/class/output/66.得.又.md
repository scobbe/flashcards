## hand
### sub-component
#### structural particle; must or have to
---
- **traditional:** 又
- **simplified:** 又
- **pronunciation:** yòu
- **definition:** hand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 又名 (yòu míng, "also known as")
  - 又红(紅)又专(專) (yòu hóng yòu zhuān, "red and expert")
  - 又惊(驚)又喜 (yòu jīng yòu xǐ, "surprised and delighted")
  - 少之又少 (shǎo zhī yòu shǎo, "extremely few")
- **etymology (又):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of right hand.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a right hand. In later usage, the graph came to write the adverb meaning "again; also", explained as an adverbial derivation of 有 (yǒu, "to have; there is"), while the sense "right" continued separately in 右 (yòu, "right").
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
