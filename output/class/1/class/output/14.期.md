## period or term; to expect
### sub-word
#### Sunday
---
- **traditional:** 期
- **simplified:** 期
- **pronunciation:** qī
- **definition:** period or term; to expect
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 期间(間) (qījiān, "time period")
  - 期待 (qīdài, "look forward to")
  - 期限 (qīxiàn, "deadline")
  - 学(學)期 (xuéqī, "semester")
- **etymology (期):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 其 (qí, "that") + semantic: 日 (rì, "sun") → phonetic: 其 (qí, "that") + semantic: 月 (yuè, "moon").
  - **interpretation:** 其 serves as the phonetic element, while 月 supplies the semantic field of time via the changing phases of the moon, yielding the sense "period". Earlier forms used 日 "sun", later replaced by 月 to emphasize lunar cycles and the idea of months.
  - **component characters:**
    - 其
    - 日
    - 月
