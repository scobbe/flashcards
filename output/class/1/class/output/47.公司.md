## company; firm
### sub-word
#### startup company
---
- **traditional:** 公司
- **simplified:** 公司
- **pronunciation:** gōngsī
- **definition:** company; firm
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 公司文化 (gōngsī wénhuà, "company culture")
  - 公司荣(榮)誉(譽) (gōngsī róngyù, "company prestige")
  - 公司简(簡)介 (gōngsī jiǎnjiè, "company profile")
  - 公司治理 (gōngsī zhìlǐ, "corporate governance")
- **etymology (公司):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 公 (gōng, "public") + 司 (sī, "department") → 公司 (gōngsī, "company").
  - **interpretation:** 公 means "public" and 司 means a department or to be in charge, so together they denote an organized office or agency. This broadened to designated commercial entities, giving the modern sense of a company or corporation.
  - **component characters:**
    - 公
    - 司
