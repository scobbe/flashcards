## occupy
### sub-component
#### shop or store
---
- **traditional:** 占
- **simplified:** 占
- **pronunciation:** zhàn
- **definition:** occupy
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 占(佔)据(據) (zhàn jù, "to occupy")
  - 占(佔)领(領) (zhàn lǐng, "to capture")
  - 占(佔)有 (zhàn yǒu, "to possess")
  - 占(佔)用 (zhàn yòng, "to take up")
- **etymology (占):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 卜 (bǔ, "divination") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 卜 “divination” with 口 “mouth”, indicating uttering or announcing a divination result. Originally it meant to divine or foretell. The modern sense “to occupy” is written 佔 in Traditional and is merged into 占 in Simplified orthography.
  - **component characters:**
    - 卜
    - 口
