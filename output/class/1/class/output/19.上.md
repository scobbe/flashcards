## up; to go; previous
### sub-word
#### morning
---
- **traditional:** 上
- **simplified:** 上
- **pronunciation:** shàng
- **definition:** up; to go; previous
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 上世纪(紀) (shàng shìjì, "last century")
  - 上集 (shàng jí, "previous episode")
  - 上班 (shàng bān, "go to work")
  - 上学(學) (shàng xué, "go to school")
- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: short stroke above 一 (yī, "one") indicating the upper position → 上 (shàng, "up").
  - **interpretation:** The character shows a short stroke placed above a horizontal line to depict the idea of something being above. This spatial relation extended to locative uses like "on/above" and further to temporal or ordinal senses such as "previous" and "first" in sequences.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 上
