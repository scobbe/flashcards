## one
### sub-word
#### same or identical
---
- **traditional:** 一
- **simplified:** 一
- **pronunciation:** yī
- **definition:** one
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一个(個) (yí gè, "a single")
  - 一些 (yì xiē, "a few")
  - 一起 (yì qǐ, "together with")
  - 一定 (yí dìng, "for sure")
- **etymology (一):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: horizontal stroke → 一 (yī, "one").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a single horizontal stroke used to tally one. The form directly denotes the numeral and has remained essentially unchanged, later serving as the radical for 'one' in compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
