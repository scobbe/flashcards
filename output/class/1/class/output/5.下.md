## down; to finish
### sub-word
#### get off work
---
- **traditional:** 下
- **simplified:** 下
- **pronunciation:** xià
- **definition:** down; to finish
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 天下 (tiān xià, "the world")
  - 地下 (dì xià, "under the ground")
  - 下层(層) (xià céng, "lower level")
  - 以下 (yǐ xià, "as follows")
- **etymology (下):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: short stroke placed below 一 (yī, "one") indicating "below" → standardized as 下 (xià, "down").
  - **interpretation:** It shows a short stroke beneath a horizontal line, marking the position "below". This spatial sense developed into modern meanings like "down/below" and extended to notions such as "lower, next" and verbs like "descend" or "put down".
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 下
