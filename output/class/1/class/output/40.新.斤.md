- **traditional:** 斤
- **simplified:** 斤
- **pronunciation:** jīn
- **definition:** axe
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 公斤 (gōng jīn, "kilogram")
  - 市斤 (shì jīn, "market catty")
  - 千斤顶(頂) (qiān jīn dǐng, "car jack")
  - 斤斤计(計)较(較) (jīn jīn jì jiào, "nitpick over trifles")
- **etymology (斤):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 斧 (fǔ, "axe").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts an axe, showing a head and handle. The graph was later borrowed to denote the traditional unit of weight “catty,” while the original ‘axe’ sense became archaic.
