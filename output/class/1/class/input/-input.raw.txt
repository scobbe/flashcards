1. 上班 shàngbān – to go to work, 我早上九点上班。Wǒ zǎoshang jiǔ diǎn shàngbān. I go to work at nine in the morning.
2. 下班 xiàbān – to get off work, 我下午五点下班。Wǒ xiàwǔ wǔ diǎn xiàbān. I get off work at five in the afternoon.
3. 星期六 xīngqīliù – Saturday, 星期六我不上班。Xīngqīliù wǒ bú shàngbān. I don’t work on Saturday.
4. 星期天 xīngqītiān – Sunday, 星期天我们去吃饭。Xīngqītiān wǒmen qù chīfàn. We go out to eat on Sunday.
5. 晚上 wǎnshang – evening, 晚上七点我在家。Wǎnshang qī diǎn wǒ zài jiā. I’m home at seven in the evening.
6. 早上 zǎoshang – morning, 早上好！Zǎoshang hǎo! Good morning!
7. 下午 xiàwǔ – afternoon, 下午两点开会。Xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn kāihuì. Meeting at two in the afternoon.
8. 中午 zhōngwǔ – noon, 中午十二点吃饭。Zhōngwǔ shí’èr diǎn chīfàn. I eat lunch at noon.
9. 小时 xiǎoshí – hour, 我工作八个小时。Wǒ gōngzuò bā gè xiǎoshí. I work eight hours.
10. 软件 ruǎnjiàn – software, 我们做新的软件。Wǒmen zuò xīn de ruǎnjiàn. We’re making new software.
11. 工程师 gōngchéngshī – engineer, 他是软件工程师。Tā shì ruǎnjiàn gōngchéngshī. He is a software engineer.
12. 新 xīn – new, 我买了新电脑。Wǒ mǎile xīn diànnǎo. I bought a new computer.
13. 我们做新的软件 Wǒmen zuò xīn de ruǎnjiàn – we make new software.
14. 创业公司 chuàngyè gōngsī – startup, 我在创业公司工作。Wǒ zài chuàngyè gōngsī gōngzuò. I work at a startup.
15. 时间 shíjiān – time, 你有时间吗？Nǐ yǒu shíjiān ma? Do you have time?
16. 你在CLI工作了几年？Nǐ zài CLI gōngzuò le jǐ nián? How many years have you worked at CLI?
17. 医生 yīshēng – doctor, 我没去医院。Wǒ méi qù yīyuàn. I didn’t go to the hospital.
18. 医院 yīyuàn – hospital, 我去医院看医生。Wǒ qù yīyuàn kàn yīshēng. I go to the hospital to see a doctor.
19. 需要 xūyào – to need, 我需要水。Wǒ xūyào shuǐ. I need water.
20. 等 děng – to wait, 请等一下。Qǐng děng yīxià. Please wait a moment.
21. 针灸 zhēnjiǔ – acupuncture, 我学过针灸。Wǒ xué guò zhēnjiǔ. I have studied acupuncture.
22. 中医 zhōngyī – Chinese medicine, 中医很好。Zhōngyī hěn hǎo. Chinese medicine is very good.
23. 页 yè – page, 第二页。Dì èr yè. Page two.
24. 的 de – possessive particle, 我的老师。Wǒ de lǎoshī. My teacher.
25. 得 děi – must, 我得工作。Wǒ děi gōngzuò. I must work.
26. 得 de (complement marker), 你说得很快。Nǐ shuō de hěn kuài. You speak very fast.
27. 告诉 gàosu – to tell, 请告诉我。Qǐng gàosu wǒ. Please tell me.
28. 睡 shuì – to sleep, 我睡得很好。Wǒ shuì de hěn hǎo. I sleep well.
29. 说 shuō – to speak, 她说中文说得很好。Tā shuō Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo. She speaks Chinese very well.
30. 穿 chuān – to wear, 她穿红衣服。Tā chuān hóng yīfu. She wears red clothes.
31. 一样 yīyàng – same, 我们的衣服一样。Wǒmen de yīfu yīyàng. Our clothes are the same.
32. 多 duō – many, 人很多。Rén hěn duō. There are many people.
33. 少 shǎo – few, 人很少。Rén hěn shǎo. There are few people.
34. 多少 duōshao – how many, 你的公司有多少人？Nǐ de gōngsī yǒu duōshao rén? How many people are in your company?
35. 汉堡 hànbǎo – hamburger, 我喜欢吃汉堡。Wǒ xǐhuān chī hànbǎo. I like to eat hamburgers.
36. 店 diàn – shop, 这家店很好。Zhè jiā diàn hěn hǎo. This shop is good.
37. 得 děi / 不用 bú yòng – must / not need, 你得吃饭。Nǐ děi chīfàn. You must eat. / 你不用去。Nǐ bú yòng qù. You don’t need to go.
38. 美元 měiyuán – US dollar, 我有一百美元。Wǒ yǒu yì bǎi měiyuán. I have one hundred US dollars.
39. 买的衣服太小。Mǎi de yīfu tài xiǎo. The clothes I bought are too small.
40. 我要换大的。Wǒ yào huàn dà de. I want to exchange for a bigger one.
41. 刚 gāng – just, 我刚吃早饭。Wǒ gāng chī zǎofàn. I just ate breakfast.
42. 刚刚 gānggāng – just now, 他刚刚走。Tā gānggāng zǒu. He just left.
43. 从…到… cóng … dào … – from … to …, 从北京到上海。Cóng Běijīng dào Shànghǎi. From Beijing to Shanghai.
44. 房子 fángzi – house, 旧金山的房子非常贵。Jiùjīnshān de fángzi fēicháng guì. Houses in San Francisco are very expensive.
45. 第一次 dì yī cì – first time, 这是我第一次来中国。Zhè shì wǒ dì yī cì lái Zhōngguó. This is my first time in China.
46. 过 guò – experiential aspect marker, 我去过中国。Wǒ qù guò Zhōngguó. I have been to China.
47. 学校 xuéxiào – school, 我在学校工作。Wǒ zài xuéxiào gōngzuò. I work at a school.
48. 员工 yuángōng – employee, 我是公司的员工。Wǒ shì gōngsī de yuángōng. I’m an employee of the company.
49. 服务员 fúwùyuán – waiter/waitress, 服务员很好。Fúwùyuán hěn hǎo. The waiter is nice.
50. 饭馆 fànguǎn – restaurant, 我们去饭馆吃饭。Wǒmen qù fànguǎn chīfàn. Let’s go eat at a restaurant.
51. 多音字 duōyīnzì – polyphonic character, “数”是一个多音字。“Shù” shì yí gè duōyīnzì. “Shu” is a polyphonic character.
52. 数 shǔ / shù – to count / number, 我会数一数。Wǒ huì shǔ yī shǔ. I’ll count. / 这是一个数。Zhè shì yí gè shù. This is a number.
53. 看一看 kàn yī kàn – take a look, 你看一看。Nǐ kàn yī kàn. Have a look.
54. 看一下 kàn yīxià – take a look, 看一下那个。Kàn yīxià nà ge. Look at that.
55. 看看 kànkan – look, 你看看书。Nǐ kànkan shū. Take a look at the book.
56. 数一下 shǔ yīxià – count it, 数一下钱。Shǔ yīxià qián. Count the money.
57. 数一数 shǔ yī shǔ – count, 数一数苹果。Shǔ yī shǔ píngguǒ. Count the apples.
58. 数数 shǔ shù – count, 小孩学数数。Xiǎohái xué shǔ shù. The child is learning to count.
59. 林 Lín – a surname, 林老师。Lín lǎoshī. Teacher Lin.
60. 娜 Nà – a name, 娜老师。Nà lǎoshī. Teacher Na.
61. 换 huàn – to exchange, 我想换钱。Wǒ xiǎng huàn qián. I want to exchange money.
62. 块 kuài – buck; piece, 三块钱。Sān kuài qián. Three bucks.
63. 毛 máo – dime (0.1 yuan), 一毛钱。Yì máo qián. Ten cents.
64. 旅行 lǚxíng – to travel, 我喜欢旅行。Wǒ xǐhuān lǚxíng. I like to travel.
65. 玩 wán – to have fun; to play, 我们去玩吧。Wǒmen qù wán ba. Let’s go have fun.
66. 住 zhù – to live, 我住在北京。Wǒ zhù zài Běijīng. I live in Beijing.
67. 介绍 jièshào – to introduce, 我介绍一下。Wǒ jièshào yīxià. Let me introduce (someone).
