## 以为(以為)
---
- **pinyin:** yǐwéi
- **definition:** to think (with more subjectivity)
- **examples:**
  - 我以为你已经走了(我以為你已經走了) (wǒ yǐwéi nǐ yǐjīng zǒu le, "I thought you had already left")
  - 我以为(我以為) (wǒ yǐwéi, "I thought; I assumed")
  - 别以为(別以為) (bié yǐwéi, "Don't assume")
  - 都以为(都以為) (dōu yǐwéi, "Everyone thought")
