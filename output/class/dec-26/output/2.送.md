## 送
---
- **pinyin:** sòng
- **definition:** to take someone somewhere; to see someone off
- **examples:**
  - 我开车送你回家吧(我開車送你回家吧) (wǒ kāichē sòng nǐ huíjiā ba, "Let me drive you home")
  - 我们去机场送行吧(我們去機場送行吧) (wǒmen qù jīchǎng sòngxíng ba, "Let's go to the airport to see them off")
  - 送我回家 (sòng wǒ huíjiā, "take me home")
  - 开着车送(開著車送) (kāi zhe chē sòng, "to take someone by car")
  - 我们到医院(我們到醫院) (wǒmen dào yīyuàn, "we go to the hospital")
  - 送我们门外(边)(送我們門外(邊)) (sòng wǒmen ménwài(biān), "see us to the door/outside")
  - 送到火车站(送到火車站) (sòng dào huǒchēzhàn, "take to the train station")
