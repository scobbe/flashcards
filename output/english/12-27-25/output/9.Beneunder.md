## Beneunder
---
- **definition:**
  - a Chinese lifestyle and outdoor brand known for sun-protection gear such as UV umbrellas, hats, and UPF clothing
- **etymology:**
  - coined from the Chinese brand name “蕉下” (Mandarin: jiāo xià), literally “under the banana (plant),” evoking cool shade
  - the English form blends “bene-” (from Latin bene, “well, good”) with English “under,” suggesting being well under cover or shade
- **history:**
  - launched in China in the mid-2010s; the English branding Beneunder appears by the late 2010s as the company targeted wider markets
  - grew rapidly in the early 2020s amid heightened UV awareness and e-commerce, popularizing UV umbrellas and UPF apparel
  - expanded internationally in the early 2020s via cross-border online platforms and pop-ups as sun-care fashion drew global attention
- **pronunciation:**
  - ben-ee-UN-der
