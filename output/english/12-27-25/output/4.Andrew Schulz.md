## Andrew Schulz
---
- **definition:**
  - an American stand-up comedian, actor, and podcaster known for sharp crowd work and provocative social commentary
  - host or cohost of The Brilliant Idiots and Flagrant; creator of the Netflix limited series Schulz Saves America
- **etymology:**
  - andrew from Greek Andreas “man; manly,” passing through Latin Andreas and Old French André into English
  - schulz is a German occupational surname from Middle High German Schultheize/Schultheiß “village headman or magistrate,” from Schuld “debt, dues” + heißen “to command”
- **history:**
  - born 1983 in New York City; gained early visibility in the early 2010s via MTV2’s Guy Code and viral stand-up clips
  - pivoted to independent, direct-to-YouTube releases (4:4:1 in 2017; 5:5:1 in 2018), helping popularize online-first stand-up distribution
  - co-founded The Brilliant Idiots (2013) and launched Flagrant (2017, rebranded 2021); released Netflix’s Schulz Saves America (2020) and the pay-per-view special Infamous (2022)
- **pronunciation:**
  - AN-droo SHUHLZ
