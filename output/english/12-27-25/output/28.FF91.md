## FF91
---
- **definition:**
  - a luxury, all-electric crossover/SUV model from Faraday Future, serving as the brand’s flagship
  - often used as shorthand for the company’s first production car and its high-end variants
- **etymology:**
  - formed from the initials "FF" (Faraday Future) plus the numerals "91"
  - in the company’s naming scheme, "9" marks the top tier and "1" the first generation; read as "ninety-one"
- **history:**
  - first unveiled in January 2017 at the Consumer Electronics Show, amid a surge of new EV startups
  - after financial turbulence, the company went public via a SPAC in 2021 and emphasized the "FF 91 Futurist" branding
  - limited production and initial customer deliveries began in 2023 from its Hanford, California facility
- **pronunciation:**
  - EFF nine-tee-ONE
