## Movado
---
- **definition:** Movado
  - a Swiss luxury watch brand known for minimalist designs, especially the Museum dial with a single dot at 12
  - a watch made by this company
- **origin:**
  - from Esperanto "movado" meaning "movement" or "motion"; built from "movi" (to move) + suffix "-ado" (ongoing action)
  - adopted as the company name in 1905 by a Swiss watchmaker founded in 1881, to evoke the motion of time and watch mechanisms
  - reflects early 20th-century interest in Esperanto as a modern, international branding language
- **pronunciation:**
  - moh-VAH-doh
