## Byredo
---
- **definition:** Byredo
  - a Swedish luxury fragrance and beauty brand known for minimalist design and niche perfumes
  - used informally to refer to one of the brand’s products (e.g., “a Byredo candle”)
- **origin:**
  - coined by founder Ben Gorham in Stockholm (2006) from the phrase “by redolence,” meaning “by way of scent”
  - redolence comes from Latin redolere, “to give off a smell; be fragrant”
  - the name underscores the brand’s focus on scent-driven memories and storytelling
- **pronunciation:**
  - bye-RAY-doh
