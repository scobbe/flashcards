## Byredo
---
- **definition:**
  - a Swedish luxury brand known for niche perfumes, candles, and beauty goods
  - by extension, a product from this brand, especially a perfume or candle
- **etymology:**
  - formed as a clipped blend of the English phrase “by redolence,” meaning “by way of fragrance/scent”
  - redolence comes from Latin redolere (“to give off a smell”), from re- (“again, back”) + olere (“to smell”)
- **history:**
  - founded in Stockholm in 2006 by Ben Gorham, entering English usage as the brand name that year
  - rose with the niche-perfumery wave in the late 2000s–2010s; notable launches include Gypsy Water (2008) and Bal d’Afrique (2009)
  - acquired by Spanish beauty group Puig in 2022, marking its shift into a larger global luxury portfolio
- **pronunciation:**
  - bye-RAY-doh
