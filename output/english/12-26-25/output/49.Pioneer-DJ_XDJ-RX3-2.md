## Pioneer-DJ/XDJ-RX3-2
---
- **definition:**
  - a branded model name for an all‑in‑one, 2‑channel DJ system by Pioneer DJ (XDJ‑RX3), used to mix music from USB drives and rekordbox
  - in listings, the extra “‑2” can appear as a slug or variant tag; the official model name is XDJ‑RX3
- **origin:**
  - pioneer dj is the DJ‑equipment brand from Japan’s Pioneer; “DJ” is short for “disc jockey”
  - “xdj” is a Pioneer DJ series focused on USB/rekordbox playback (no CD drives); “rx” labels the 2‑channel all‑in‑one layout; “3” marks the third generation
  - the line progressed from xdj‑rx (2015) to rx2 (2017) to rx3 (2021), becoming a staple for club and mobile DJs
- **pronunciation:**
  - pie-oh-NEER DEE-jay slash eks-DEE-jay AR-ex THREE dash TOO
