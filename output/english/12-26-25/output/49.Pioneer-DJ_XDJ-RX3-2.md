## Pioneer-DJ/XDJ-RX3-2
---
- **definition:**
  - a product model name used for a Pioneer DJ all-in-one digital DJ system, commonly referring to the XDJ-RX3 (third-generation RX series)
  - in some catalogs, the trailing "-2" marks a package or minor variant; the core hardware is the XDJ-RX3
- **etymology:**
  - pioneer from Middle French pionnier "foot soldier, pioneer," from Old French peon, from Latin pes "foot"; later broadened to mean pathfinder
  - dj is an initialism of "disc jockey," literally a person who operates records
  - xdj-rx3-2 is an alphanumeric brand-model string: xdj blends X + DJ; rx3 labels the RX line's third generation; "-2" is a variant suffix
- **history:**
  - the xdj-rx line debuted in 2015 as a standalone/usb all-in-one; the rx2 arrived in 2017, and the rx3 launched in 2021
  - the rx3 added a 10.1-inch touchscreen and cdj-3000-style workflow; serato dj pro support followed in 2022 amid a shift toward usb, laptop-optional setups
