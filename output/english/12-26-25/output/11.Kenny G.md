## Kenny G
---
- **definition:**
  - american smooth-jazz saxophonist known for melodic, easy-listening hits
  - used in pop culture as shorthand for ultra-smooth, mellow background jazz
- **origin:**
  - stage name of Kenneth Bruce Gorelick; 'Kenny' is a diminutive of 'Kenneth' and 'G' comes from his surname
  - the given name Kenneth traces to Scottish Gaelic Coinneach ('handsome, fair one') and/or Cináed ('born of fire')
  - the name became widely recognized in the 1980s–90s as his hits popularized smooth jazz, turning 'Kenny G' into a cultural reference
- **pronunciation:**
  - KEN-ee JEE
