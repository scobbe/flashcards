## Sool
---
- **definition:**
  - to incite or egg on, especially to set a dog on someone (dialect)
  - a Korean loanword used in English for alcoholic drinks, especially traditional brews like soju and makgeolli
- **origin:**
  - dialectal british and irish english; origin uncertain; commonly used in phrases like 'sool a dog on someone'
  - from Korean '술' (romanized 'sul'), meaning 'alcohol'; the spelling 'sool' is used in English to reflect the long “oo” sound
  - entered English via Korean cuisine and pop culture, broadening to refer to Korean liquors in general
- **pronunciation:**
  - SOOL
