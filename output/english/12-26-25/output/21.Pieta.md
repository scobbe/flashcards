## Pieta
---
- **definition:**
  - a sculpture or painting of the Virgin Mary mourning and holding the dead Jesus
- **origin:**
  - from Italian pietà meaning “pity, compassion,” ultimately from Latin pietas “devotion, dutifulness”
  - in medieval and Renaissance art it became the name for this mourning scene; Michelangelo’s 1498–99 marble made it famous worldwide
  - the Italian accent marks final stress: pietà
- **pronunciation:**
  - pee-eh-TAH
