## Crepuscular
---
- **definition:** Crepuscular
  - relating to twilight; dim or shadowy like dusk
  - of animals, active mainly at dawn and dusk
- **origin:**
  - from Latin crepusculum meaning twilight, via Late Latin crepuscularis meaning of twilight
  - tied to Latin creper/creperus meaning dark or uncertain, evoking the in-between light of dusk
  - entered English in the 1600s; later common in zoology and meteorology (crepuscular animals, crepuscular rays)
- **pronunciation:**
  - kri-PUHS-kyuh-ler
