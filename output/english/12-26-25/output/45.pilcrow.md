## Pilcrow
---
- **definition:**
  - the paragraph mark (¶), used in writing and typesetting to indicate a new paragraph or a paragraph break
  - in software, the icon that shows or toggles hidden formatting marks (often displayed as ¶)
- **origin:**
  - from middle english pilcrow(e), an altered form of “paragraph,” ultimately from latin paragraphus and greek paragraphos (‘beside’ + ‘write’)
  - the ¶ symbol evolved from a scribal C for latin capitulum (“little head”), used to flag new sections
  - medieval scribes often drew pilcrows in red ink (rubrication) to guide readers between sections
- **pronunciation:**
  - PIL-kroh
