## Philip Johnson
---
- **definition:**
  - american architect known for the Glass House and the AT&T (Sony) Building; a leading voice in modern and postmodern design
  - influential curator and critic who helped popularize the International Style through early exhibitions at MoMA
- **origin:**
  - proper name of Philip Johnson (1906-2005), an American architect and the first Pritzker Prize laureate (1979)
  - philip comes from Greek Philippos, meaning 'lover of horses' (philos 'friend' + hippos 'horse')
  - johnson means 'son of John'; John traces to Hebrew Yohanan, 'God is gracious'; his legacy is notable yet debated for 1930s fascist ties later renounced
- **pronunciation:**
  - FIL-ip JOHN-sun
