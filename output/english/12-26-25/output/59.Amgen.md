## Amgen
---
- **definition:** Amgen
  - a U.S.-based biotechnology company that develops, manufactures, and sells biologic medicines
- **origin:**
  - from “Applied Molecular Genetics,” the company’s original 1980 name; shortened to Amgen as the brand
  - built from applied + molecular genetics, reflecting early gene-focused research
  - founded in California during the early biotech boom; later headquartered in Thousand Oaks
- **pronunciation:**
  - AM-jen
