## Succotash
---
- **definition:**
  - a simple American dish of cooked corn and lima beans, often with butter and seasonings
  - more broadly, a mixed-vegetable side dish featuring corn as the base
- **origin:**
  - from Narragansett (an Algonquian language) sohquttahhash, meaning “broken/boiled corn kernels,” borrowed into English in colonial New England
  - originally a Native American preparation combining corn and beans; settlers adopted the name and dish, which became a regional staple
- **pronunciation:**
  - SUK-uh-tash
