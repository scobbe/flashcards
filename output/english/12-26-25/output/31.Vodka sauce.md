## Vodka sauce
---
- **definition:**
  - a rich, pink pasta sauce made from tomatoes and cream with a splash of vodka
  - often mildly spicy with garlic and chili, commonly served with penne or used as a pizza topping
- **origin:**
  - from Italian-American cuisine of the late 1970s–1980s, popularized as penne alla vodka; origin claims vary between New York and Rome, sometimes linked to promotions by vodka brands
  - vodka comes from Russian “vodka,” a diminutive of “voda” meaning “water”
  - sauce comes from Latin “salsa” (“salted, seasoned”) via Italian/French; the alcohol in vodka helps blend tomato and cream and carry aromatic flavors
- **pronunciation:**
  - VOD-kuh SAWSS
