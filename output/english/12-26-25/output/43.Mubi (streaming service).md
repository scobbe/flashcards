## Mubi (streaming service)
---
- **definition:**
  - a curated streaming service for arthouse, classic, and international films, known for a handpicked, rotating selection
  - a film company that also produces and distributes movies and runs an online film magazine
- **origin:**
  - founded in 2007 by Efe Çakarel as The Auteurs; rebranded as Mubi in 2010 to adopt a shorter, globally friendly name
  - mubi is a coined brand name (not an acronym), chosen for brevity and ease of pronunciation across languages
  - the earlier name invoked French auteur (“author”), reflecting a focus on director-led cinema before the broader Mubi brand
- **pronunciation:**
  - MOO-bee
