## Molding
---
- **definition:** Molding
  - the process of shaping a material in a mold by casting, pressing, or similar methods
  - a decorative strip or shaped profile used to trim walls, doors, ceilings, or furniture
  - the shaped outline or profile given to an edge or surface
- **origin:**
  - from mold (shape, form) + -ing; american spelling is “molding,” british is “moulding”
  - mold (form) comes via old french modle “model, pattern” from latin modulus, diminutive of modus “measure, manner”
  - not related to mold the fungus, which goes back to old english molde “soil, earth”
- **pronunciation:**
  - MOHL-ding
