## Divine madness
---
- **definition:** Divine madness
  - a state of inspired ecstasy believed to come from a god, leading to prophecy, vision, or poetry
  - in Plato, the idea that certain forms of madness are a divine gift that reveals truth
  - informally, overpowering creative inspiration that feels beyond rational control
- **origin:**
  - from ancient Greek theia mania meaning divine madness: theia = divine; mania = madness, frenzy
  - in Plato's Phaedrus and Ion, it names poetic, prophetic, erotic, and ritual inspiration
  - later echoed in Latin as furor divinus and revived by Renaissance and Romantic writers
- **pronunciation:**
  - dih-VINE MAD-ness
