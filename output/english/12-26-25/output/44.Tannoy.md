## Tannoy
---
- **definition:**
  - a public-address system or an announcement made over it (chiefly British; from the brand name)
  - the trademark name of a British company known for loudspeakers and PA equipment
- **origin:**
  - coined in the late 1920s from "tantalum alloy," the material used in the company's early rectifiers; adopted as the firm's brand name
  - founded by engineer Guy R. Fountain in London; the name later became closely tied to loudspeakers and public-address systems
  - in British English it genericized—people say "over the Tannoy"—though it remains a registered trademark and is usually capitalized
- **pronunciation:**
  - TAN-oy
