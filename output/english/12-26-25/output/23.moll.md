## Moll
---
- **definition:**
  - a gangster’s girlfriend or female companion
  - slang (esp. Australia/NZ): an insulting term for a woman; sometimes a dated term for a prostitute
- **origin:**
  - from Moll, a nickname for Mary, which came to mean “woman” in general
  - in 17th–18th‑century British thieves’ slang, moll referred to a prostitute or woman of the underworld
  - early 20th‑century American slang produced “gun moll,” meaning a criminal’s female partner
- **pronunciation:**
  - MAHL (rhymes with doll)
