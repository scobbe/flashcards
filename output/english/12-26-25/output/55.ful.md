## Ful
---
- **definition:** Ful
  - suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of” or “characterized by” (beautiful, helpful, forgetful)
  - suffix forming nouns meaning “the amount that fills a container or measure” (cupful, spoonful)
- **origin:**
  - from old english -ful, from the word full (“filled, complete”); ultimately from proto-germanic *fullaz
  - used in early english to build adjectives; later extended to quantity nouns like spoonful = “as much as a spoon holds”
  - related to german -voll and dutch -vol; spelled with one l in the suffix (-ful), and plurals are usually -fuls (spoonfuls)
- **pronunciation:**
  - FULL
