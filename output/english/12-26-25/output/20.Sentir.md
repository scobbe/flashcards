## Sentir
---
- **definition:** Sentir
  - a common verb in Spanish meaning to feel; also to be sorry for or regret something
  - a French verb meaning to feel or sense; also to smell or to have an odor
- **origin:**
  - from Latin sentire, meaning to feel or perceive, which also gave English words like sense, sentiment, consent, and sentient
  - entered Old French as sentir and Spanish as sentir with similar meanings; in French it also broadened to include the idea of smelling
  - not standard in English, but its Latin root underlies a large family of English words about feeling and perception
- **pronunciation:**
  - sen-TEER (Spanish), sahn-TEER (French)
