## Karahi
---
- **definition:** Karahi
  - a deep, round, thick-walled pan from the Indian subcontinent, like a wok with steeper sides, used for frying and simmering
  - a dish cooked in such a pan, as in "chicken karahi"
- **origin:**
  - from Urdu karahi and Hindi kadai/kadhai, meaning a deep frying pan
  - ultimately from Sanskrit kataha (kaṭāha), meaning cauldron or cooking pot
  - entered English via South Asian restaurants and diaspora in the 20th century; also spelled karai, kadai, or kadhai
- **pronunciation:**
  - kuh-RAH-hee
