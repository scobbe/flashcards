## Vuori
---
- **definition:**
  - a Finnish word meaning “mountain”
  - a U.S. activewear brand (founded 2015) named after the Finnish word
- **etymology:**
  - from Finnish vuori, literally “mountain”
  - entered English chiefly as an unchanged proper name (brand and surname) derived from the Finnish common noun
- **history:**
  - 2015: Vuori Clothing was founded by Joe Kudla in Encinitas, California, amid the rise of athleisure
  - 2021: received a $400 million investment from SoftBank Vision Fund 2, valuing the company around $4 billion and fueling rapid retail expansion
  - by the early 2020s, the name was widely recognized in English as a premium performance-apparel label emphasizing casual style and recycled materials
- **pronunciation:**
  - VUO-ree
