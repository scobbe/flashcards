## Vuori
---
- **definition:**
  - a U.S. activewear brand known for performance and casual apparel
  - the Finnish word for 'mountain'; also used for 'lining' in clothing contexts
- **origin:**
  - from Finnish vuori, meaning 'mountain' (it can also mean a garment's 'lining')
  - entered English mainly as the brand name of a California activewear company (founded 2015), chosen to evoke outdoor landscapes and endurance
- **pronunciation:**
  - VOO-ree
