## mountain
### shān
---
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: three mountain peaks; in oracle-bone script drawn as triangles rather than vertical strokes; compare 丘 (qiū, mound).
  - **interpretation:** Three peaks rising side by side depict a mountain range, giving a direct visual of rugged terrain. Early triangular forms were later regularized into the three-stroke shape used today. Common in compounds like 山脉 (shānmài, mountain range) and related to terms such as 岳 (yuè, high mountain).
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - Let’s go climb a mountain.
  - 那座山很高(那座山很高)。
    - Nà zuò shān hěn gāo.
    - That mountain is very tall.
  - 这条山路很危险(這條山路很危險)，小心点(小心點)。
    - Zhè tiáo shānlù hěn wēixiǎn, xiǎoxīn diǎn.
    - This mountain path is dangerous; be careful.
