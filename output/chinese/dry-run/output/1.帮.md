## to help
### bāng
---
- **traditional:** 幫
- **definition:** to help
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth
  - 邦
    - bāng
    - country
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 巾 (jīn, "cloth") + phonetic: 邦 (bāng, "country") =
    - assisting with cloth or materials ->
    - to help
  - **interpretation:** The character 幫 combines the meaning of 巾 (jīn, "cloth") with the phonetic element 邦 (bāng, "country"), symbolizing assistance or help with physical materials like cloth. This reflects the role of providing tangible support.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified form 帮, 巾 (jīn, "cloth") is retained while 邦 (bāng, "country") is kept intact from the traditional representation, aligning with historical simplification practices from 1956.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )来(來)帮(幫)你( )。
    - Wǒ lái bāng nǐ.
    - I will help you.
  - 他( )帮(幫)老( )师(師)，辅(輔)导(導)学(學)生( )。
    - Tā bāng lǎoshī, fǔdǎo xuésheng.
