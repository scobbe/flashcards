## 你没事吧(你沒事吧)
---
- **pinyin:** nǐ méi shì ba
- **definition:** are you okay?
- **characters:**
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you (singular); your (colloquial, esp. before kinship terms)
  - 没(沒)
    - méi
    - not, no (negator); do not have, without; to sink, submerge
  - 事
    - shì
    - matter, affair; thing (abstract), event; problem, trouble; job, task
  - 吧
    - ba
    - sentence-final particle for suggestion or mild command; sentence-final particle indicating uncertainty, probability, or seeking confirmation
- **examples:**
  - 你摔倒了(你摔倒了)，你没事吧(你沒事吧)？
    - nǐ shuāi dǎo le, nǐ méi shì ba?
    - You fell—are you okay?
