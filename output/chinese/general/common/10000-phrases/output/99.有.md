## 有
---
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **definition:** to have; there is/are
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: phonetic 又 (yòu, "again") + semantic 肉 (ròu, "meat").
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个问题(我有一個問題)。
    - wǒ yǒu yí gè wèntí
    - I have a question.
  - 桌子上有三本书(桌子上有三本書)。
    - zhuōzi shàng yǒu sān běn shū
    - There are three books on the table.
