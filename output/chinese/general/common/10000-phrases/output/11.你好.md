## 你好
---
- **pinyin:** nǐ hǎo
- **definition:** hello
- **characters:**
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you (informal singular)
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, well; OK, all right; very; easy to
- **etymology:** Combines 你 (nǐ, "you (informal singular)") with 好 (hǎo, "good") to express hello.
- **examples:**
  - 你好(你好)！
    - nǐ hǎo
    - Hello!
