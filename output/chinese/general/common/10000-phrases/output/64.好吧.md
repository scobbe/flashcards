## 好吧
---
- **pinyin:** hǎo ba
- **definition:** fine; okay then
- **characters:**
  - 好
    - hǎo, hào
    - good, fine, nice; OK, all right; very, quite; to like, be fond of
  - 吧
    - ba
    - sentence-final particle indicating suggestion or mild command; sentence-final particle seeking confirmation; particle indicating uncertainty, probability; particle indicating concession or reluctant agreement
- **etymology:** 好 (hǎo, "good; OK") states willingness or approval, while 吧 (ba, "sentence-final particle for suggestion/concession") downtones it into a hedged, face-saving acceptance—"good, then"—signaling compliance rather than enthusiasm in everyday Chinese pragmatics.
- **examples:**
  - 好吧(好吧)，我们就这么做(我們就這麼做)。
    - hǎo ba, wǒmen jiù zhème zuò
    - Fine, we'll do it this way.
  - 好吧(好吧)，那我先走了(那我先走了)。
    - hǎo ba, nà wǒ xiān zǒu le
    - Okay then, I'll get going.
