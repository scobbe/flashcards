## 好啊
---
- **pinyin:** hǎo a
- **definition:** sure!; okay!
- **characters:**
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine; well; okay; very
  - 啊
    - a
    - ah, oh; huh, eh; agreement, acknowledgment; sentence-final particle softening a statement or command
- **etymology:** As a standalone predicate, 好 (hǎo, "good") functions performatively to ratify a proposal—“good, proceed”—and attaching the sentence-final particle 啊 (a, "ah; agreement") adds warmth and softens the bluntness, turning the evaluation into friendly, immediate acceptance. In conversation this maps a judgment ("good") to a stance ("I agree"), so 好啊 nuances simple assent much like English “okay, sure!”.
- **examples:**
  - 好啊(好啊)，今晚一起去看电影(今晚一起去看電影)！
    - hǎo a, jīn wǎn yìqǐ qù kàn diànyǐng
    - Sure! Let's go watch a movie tonight!
  - 好啊(好啊)，我马上发给你(我馬上發給你)。
    - hǎo a, wǒ mǎshàng fā gěi nǐ
    - Okay, I’ll send it to you right away.
