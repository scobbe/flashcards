## 妈的(媽的)
---
- **pinyin:** mā de
- **definition:** damn it; fuck
- **characters:**
  - 妈(媽): mā, mom; mother; mum; ma
  - 的: de, possessive particle; of; attributive marker; structural particle
- **examples:**
  - 妈的，又下雨了！(媽的，又下雨了！) (mā de, yòu xià yǔ le, "Damn it, it's raining again!")
  - 妈的，把文件全删了！(媽的，把文件全刪了！) (mā de, bǎ wénjiàn quán shān le, "Fuck, I deleted all the files!")
