## 妈的(媽的)
---
- **pinyin:** mā de
- **definition:** damn; f*ck (expletive)
- **characters:**
  - 妈(媽)
    - mā
    - mother, mom
  - 的
    - de, dí, dì
    - attributive, possessive particle; nominalizer (thing which..., one who...); target, bull's-eye; real, true
- **etymology:** 妈(媽) (mā, "mother, mom") followed by 的 (de, "possessive/nominalizer") creates an unfinished genitive “mother’s…”, deliberately leaving the obscene noun unsaid so the fragment itself functions as a standalone curse. Rooted in the long-standing taboo of insulting one’s mother in Chinese vernacular, 的 provides the grammatical frame that signals an omitted invective and intensifies the outburst.
- **examples:**
  - 妈的(媽的)，又下雨了(又下雨了)！
    - mā de, yòu xià yǔ le
    - Damn, it's raining again!
  - 妈的(媽的)，你把文件删了(你把文件刪了)？
    - mā de, nǐ bǎ wénjiàn shān le
    - Fuck, you deleted the file?
