## 那么(那麼)
---
- **pinyin:** nàme
- **definition:** then; in that case
- **characters:**
  - 那
    - nà, nèi
    - that, those; then, in that case
  - 么(麼)
    - me
    - interrogative suffix; suffix forming adverbs of degree
- **etymology:** Combines 那 (nà, nèi, "that") with 么(麼) (me, "interrogative suffix") to express then; in that case.
- **examples:**
  - 如果你很忙(如果你很忙)，那么我们明天再谈吧(那麼我們明天再談吧)。
    - rúguǒ nǐ hěn máng, nàme wǒmen míngtiān zài tán ba
    - If you're busy, then we can talk tomorrow.
  - 路太堵了(路太堵了)，那么我们坐地铁吧(那麼我們坐地鐵吧)。
    - lù tài dǔ le, nàme wǒmen zuò dìtiě ba
    - The traffic is too heavy; in that case, let's take the subway.
