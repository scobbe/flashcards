## 你知道吗(你知道嗎)
---
- **pinyin:** nǐ zhīdào ma
- **definition:** do you know?; you know?
- **characters:**
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you, second person singular
  - 知
    - zhī
    - to know, be aware; to inform, notify; knowledge
  - 道
    - dào
    - way, path, road; method, principle; to say, speak; Tao, the Way
  - 吗(嗎)
    - ma
    - interrogative particle for yes-no questions
- **examples:**
  - 你知道吗(你知道嗎)，地铁几点停运(地鐵幾點停運)？
    - nǐ zhīdào ma, dìtiě jǐ diǎn tíngyùn?
    - Do you know when the subway stops running?
  - 这件事很复杂(這件事很複雜)，你知道吗(你知道嗎)？
    - zhè jiàn shì hěn fùzá, nǐ zhīdào ma?
    - This matter is complicated, you know?
