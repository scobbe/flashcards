## 晚安
---
- **pinyin:** wǎn'ān
- **definition:** good night
- **characters:**
  - 晚: wǎn, late; evening; night; later
  - 安: ān, safe; peaceful; quiet; install
- **examples:**
  - 晚安，明天见(晚安，明天見) (wǎn'ān, míngtiān jiàn, "Good night, see you tomorrow")
  - 妈妈，晚安(媽媽，晚安) (māma, wǎn'ān, "Good night, Mom")
