## 早上好
---
- **pinyin:** zǎoshang hǎo
- **definition:** good morning
- **characters:**
  - 早
    - zǎo
    - early; morning; soon; long ago
  - 上
    - shàng
    - up, above; previous, last; go up, ascend, board; attend, go to
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine; well; OK, all right; very
- **etymology:** Combines 早 (zǎo, "early"), 上 (shàng, "up"), and 好 (hǎo, "good") to convey good morning.
- **examples:**
  - 早上好(早上好)，老师(老師)！
    - zǎoshang hǎo, lǎoshī
    - Good morning, teacher!
