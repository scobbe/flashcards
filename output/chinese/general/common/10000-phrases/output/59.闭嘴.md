## 闭嘴(閉嘴)
---
- **pinyin:** bìzuǐ
- **definition:** shut up
- **characters:**
  - 闭(閉)
    - bì
    - close, shut; seal, block; turn off; end, conclude
  - 嘴
    - zuǐ
    - mouth; beak, bill; snout, muzzle; spout, nozzle
- **etymology:** An imperative verb–object: 闭(閉) (bì, "close; shut") evokes slamming a gate to cut passage, mapped onto the ‘gate of speech’—嘴 (zuǐ, "mouth")—so closing it metonymically commands silence. Choosing 嘴 rather than 口 adds a blunt, colloquial edge (cf. 住嘴 vs. 閉口/默), matching the rudeness of “shut up.”.
- **examples:**
  - 闭嘴(閉嘴)！
    - bì zuǐ
    - Shut up!
