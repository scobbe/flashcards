## 闭嘴(閉嘴)
---
- **pinyin:** bìzuǐ
- **definition:** shut up
- **characters:**
  - 闭(閉)
    - bì
    - close, shut; seal, block; turn off; end, conclude
  - 嘴
    - zuǐ
    - mouth; beak, bill; snout, muzzle; spout, nozzle
- **etymology:** Combines 闭(閉) (bì, "close") with 嘴 (zuǐ, "mouth") to express shut up.
- **examples:**
  - 闭嘴(閉嘴)！
    - bì zuǐ
    - Shut up!
