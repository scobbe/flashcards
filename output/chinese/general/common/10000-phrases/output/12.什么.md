## 什么(什麼)
---
- **pinyin:** shénme
- **definition:** what
- **characters:**
  - 什
    - shén, shí
    - what (in 什麼/什么); assorted goods, sundries; ten (in set phrases)
  - 么(麼)
    - me, yāo, mó
    - interrogative suffix (what, how); degree suffix meaning so, to such an extent; one (when reading numbers); insignificant, petty
- **etymology:** Combines 什 (shén, shí, "what (in 什麼/什么)") with 么(麼) (me, yāo, mó, "interrogative suffix (what") to express what.
- **examples:**
  - 你要什么(你要什麼)？
    - nǐ yào shénme
    - What do you want?
