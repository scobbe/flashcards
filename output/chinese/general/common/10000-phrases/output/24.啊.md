## 啊
---
- **pinyin:** a
- **definition:** ah; particle
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 阿 (ā, "prefix").
- **examples:**
  - 啊(啊)，我忘了带钥匙(我忘了帶鑰匙)！
    - a, wǒ wàng le dài yàoshi!
    - Ah, I forgot to bring my keys!
  - 慢点啊(慢點啊)，路很滑(路很滑)。
    - màn diǎn a, lù hěn huá.
    - Slow down, the road is very slippery.
