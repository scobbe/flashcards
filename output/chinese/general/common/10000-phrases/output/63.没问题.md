## 没问题(沒問題)
---
- **pinyin:** méi wèntí
- **definition:** no problem
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒): méi, not; without; have not; sink
  - 问(問): wèn, ask; inquire; question; consult
  - 题(題): tí, question; problem; topic; title
- **examples:**
  - 没问题，我明天给你送过去(沒問題，我明天給你送過去) (méi wèntí, wǒ míngtiān gěi nǐ sòng guòqù, "No problem, I'll deliver it to you tomorrow")
