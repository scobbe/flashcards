## 没错(沒錯)
---
- **pinyin:** méi cuò
- **definition:** that's right; correct
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒)
    - méi
    - not, no (negation); have not, do not have, be without; not yet
  - 错(錯)
    - cuò
    - wrong, incorrect; mistake, error; to miss (an opportunity or timing); to interleave, crisscross, stagger
- **etymology:** Literally “there isn’t any error”: 没(沒) (méi, "not; without") negates the existence of 错(錯) (cuò, "mistake"), so in conversation it asserts a prior statement contains zero error—hence “exactly/right.” Using 没 rather than 不 (bù, "not") shifts it from litotes to absolute: 不錯 evolved to mean “pretty good,” while 没錯 declares the factual absence of error, making it the emphatic confirmation.
- **examples:**
  - 没错(沒錯)，这就是我们需要的解决方案(這就是我們需要的解決方案)。
    - méi cuò, zhè jiù shì wǒmen xūyào de jiějué fāng'àn
    - That's right, this is the solution we need.
  - 你说得没错(你說得沒錯)，我们应该换个角度思考(我們應該換個角度思考)。
    - nǐ shuō de méi cuò, wǒmen yīnggāi huàn gè jiǎodù sīkǎo
    - What you said is correct; we should think from a different angle.
