## 我也是
---
- **pinyin:** wǒ yě shì
- **definition:** me too; so am I
- **characters:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I, me; my
  - 也
    - yě
    - also, too; final particle (classical), indicating assertion or emphasis
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be, is, am, are; yes; correct, right
- **etymology:** 我 (wǒ, "I/me") states the speaker, 也 (yě, "also") marks additive alignment with a previously asserted predicate, and 是 (shì, "to be/yes") serves as a pro-verb/copula that licenses ellipsis of that predicate—an economical reply meaning “I also am (so).” The modern pre-verbal 也 and affirmative 是 let the clause stand alone as agreement, hence the natural reading “me too/so am I” without repeating what came before.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢咖啡(她喜歡咖啡)，我也是(我也是)。
    - tā xǐhuan kāfēi wǒ yě shì
    - She likes coffee, me too.
  - 他是老师(他是老師)，我也是(我也是)。
    - tā shì lǎoshī wǒ yě shì
    - He's a teacher; so am I.
