## 我也是
---
- **pinyin:** wǒ yě shì
- **definition:** me too; same here
- **characters:**
  - 我: wǒ, I; me; my; our
  - 也: yě, also; too; as well; likewise
  - 是: shì, be; yes; correct; right
- **examples:**
  - 她说她喜欢猫，我也是(她說她喜歡貓，我也是) (tā shuō tā xǐhuan māo, wǒ yě shì, "She said she likes cats; me too")
  - 如果你要休息，我也是 (rúguǒ nǐ yào xiūxi, wǒ yě shì, "If you want to take a break, same here")
