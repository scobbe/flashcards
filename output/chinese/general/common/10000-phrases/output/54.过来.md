## 过来(過來)
---
- **pinyin:** guò lái
- **definition:** come here; come over
- **characters:**
  - 过(過): guò, pass; cross; spend time; celebrate
  - 来(來): lái, come; arrive; next; future
- **examples:**
  - 你过来一下(你過來一下) (nǐ guò lái yīxià, "Come here for a second")
  - 今晚要不要过来吃饭？(今晚要不要過來吃飯？) (jīn wǎn yào bù yào guò lái chīfàn?, "Do you want to come over for dinner tonight?")
