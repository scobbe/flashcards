## 过来(過來)
---
- **pinyin:** guòlái
- **definition:** come over; come here
- **characters:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - to pass, go across; to spend, celebrate (a holiday); exceed, be too much; indicating past experience (aspect marker)
  - 来(來)
    - lái
    - to come, arrive; coming, next, future; after a verb, indicates motion toward the speaker; come on, let's (interjection)
- **examples:**
  - 你有空的话(你有空的話)，晚上过来坐坐(晚上過來坐坐)。
    - nǐ yǒu kòng de huà, wǎn shàng guò lái zuò zuo
    - If you're free, come over in the evening for a bit.
  - 你过来一下(你過來一下)。
    - nǐ guò lái yí xià
    - Come here for a moment.
