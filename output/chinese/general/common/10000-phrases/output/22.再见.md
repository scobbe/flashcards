## 再见(再見)
---
- **pinyin:** zàijiàn
- **definition:** goodbye
- **characters:**
  - 再
    - zài
    - again, once more, re-; more, further; then, later
  - 见(見)
    - jiàn, xiàn
    - see, perceive; meet; be seen, appear; opinion, view
- **etymology:** Combines 再 (zài, "again") with 见(見) (jiàn, xiàn, "see") to express goodbye.
- **examples:**
  - 再见(再見)！
    - zàijiàn
    - Goodbye!
