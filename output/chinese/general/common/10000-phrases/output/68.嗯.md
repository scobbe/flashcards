## 嗯
---
- **pinyin:** èn
- **definition:** huh?; hmm?
- **etymology:** Contains 口 (kǒu, "mouth") indicating an affirmative hum.
- **examples:**
  - 嗯(嗯)，你在说什么(你在說什麼)？
    - èn nǐ zài shuō shénme
    - Huh? What are you saying?
  - 嗯(嗯)，我觉得这样做不太合适(我覺得這樣做不太合適)。
    - èn wǒ juéde zhèyàng zuò bú tài héshì
    - Hmm, I think doing it this way isn't quite appropriate.
