## 我爱你(我愛你)
---
- **pinyin:** wǒ ài nǐ
- **definition:** I love you
- **characters:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I, me; my; self, ego
  - 爱(愛)
    - ài
    - love; like, be fond of; be prone to, apt to; cherish, treasure
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you, singular
- **etymology:** In straightforward S–V–O order, 我 (wǒ, "I") is the agent, 愛 (ài, "love/cherish")—whose traditional graph includes 心 (xīn, "heart") to depict heart-driven attachment—takes 你 (nǐ, "you") as its object, literally “I, with my heart, cherish you.” The use of 你 (nǐ, "you") reflects modern vernacular (replacing classical 汝 (rǔ, "you")/爾 (ěr, "you")), and the phrase’s directness became common with 20th‑century vernacularization and Western romantic expression.
- **examples:**
  - 无论发生什么(無論發生什麼)，我爱你(我愛你)。
    - wúlùn fāshēng shénme, wǒ ài nǐ
    - No matter what happens, I love you.
