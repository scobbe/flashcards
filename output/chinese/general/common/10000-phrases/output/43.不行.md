## 不行
---
- **pinyin:** bù xíng
- **definition:** no way; not allowed; not OK
- **characters:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not; no; won't, refuse to; un-, non-, in- prefix in compounds
  - 行
    - xíng
    - to go, walk; to do, carry out; OK, all right, permitted; capable, competent
- **etymology:** Combines 不 (bù, "not") with 行 (xíng, "to go") to express no way; not allowed; not ok.
- **examples:**
  - 你想让我替你撒谎(你想讓我替你撒謊)，不行(不行)。
    - nǐ xiǎng ràng wǒ tì nǐ sāhuǎng, bù xíng
    - You want me to lie for you? No way.
  - 在图书馆大声说话不行(在圖書館大聲說話不行)。
    - zài túshūguǎn dàshēng shuōhuà bù xíng
    - Speaking loudly in the library is not allowed.
  - 这个方案不行(這個方案不行)，我们得换一个(我們得換一個)。
    - zhège fāng'àn bù xíng, wǒmen děi huàn yí gè
    - This plan isn't OK; we need to change it.
