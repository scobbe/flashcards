## 你说什么(你說什麼)
---
- **pinyin:** nǐ shuō shénme
- **definition:** what did you say?
- **characters:**
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you
  - 说(說)
    - shuō
    - to speak, to say; to explain; to persuade; theory, opinion
  - 什
    - shén
    - what; assorted, various; squad, file
  - 么(麼)
    - me
    - interrogative suffix, in what or how; tiny, small
- **etymology:** Combines 你 (nǐ, "you"), 说(說) (shuō, "to speak"), 什 (shén, "what"), and 么(麼) (me, "interrogative suffix") to express what did you say?.
- **examples:**
  - 对不起(對不起)，你说什么(你說什麼)？
    - duìbùqǐ, nǐ shuō shénme?
    - Sorry, what did you say?
