## 我知道
---
- **pinyin:** wǒ zhīdào
- **definition:** I know
- **characters:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I, me; my
  - 知
    - zhī
    - know, be aware; inform, notify; knowledge
  - 道
    - dào
    - road, path; method, means; the Dao, the Way; say, speak
- **examples:**
  - 我知道(我知道)。
    - wǒ zhīdào
    - I know.
