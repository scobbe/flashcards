## 为什么(為什麼)
---
- **pinyin:** wèishénme
- **definition:** why?
- **characters:**
  - 为(為)
    - wéi, wèi
    - for, for the sake of; because of, due to; to be, to act as, to serve as; to do, to make
  - 什
    - shén, shí
    - what (in 什么); assorted, various; things, goods, utensils; ten (archaic)
  - 么(麼)
    - me, mó, ma
    - interrogative particle (in 什么, 怎么, 多么); tiny, petty; modal particle, exclamatory (dialectal)
- **etymology:** Literally “for what,” with 为(為) (wèi, "for; on account of") framing cause/purpose and 什 (shén, "what") + 么(麼) (me, "interrogative suffix") forming “what,” so the question targets the motivating reason. The modern form reflects vernacular 為甚麼, where 什 is a graphic loan for 甚 (shén/shèn, "how much; what"), showing a shift from “to what extent” to “for what reason.”.
- **examples:**
  - 你为什么没来(你為什麼沒來)？
    - nǐ wèishénme méi lái
    - Why didn’t you come?
