## 我们走(我們走)
---
- **pinyin:** wǒmen zǒu
- **definition:** let's go
- **characters:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I, me
  - 们(們)
    - men
    - plural marker for pronouns and nouns referring to people
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - walk; leave, go away; go, move; die (euphemism)
- **etymology:** 我 (wǒ, 'I, me') plus 们(們) (men, 'plural marker') yields an inclusive 'we', and in Chinese a bare first-person plural statement with an action verb functions hortatively—stating 'we go' to initiate group action. 走 (zǒu, 'leave/go') is the conventional verb for setting off, so 我们走 pragmatically announces and invites immediate departure (often with an optional 吧 (ba, 'modal particle')), hence 'let's go'.
- **examples:**
  - 电影要开始了(電影要開始了)，我们走(我們走)！
    - diànyǐng yào kāishǐ le, wǒmen zǒu!
    - The movie is about to start, let's go!
