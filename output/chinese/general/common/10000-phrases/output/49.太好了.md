## 太好了
---
- **pinyin:** tài hǎo le
- **definition:** great; fantastic
- **characters:**
  - 太
    - tài
    - too, excessively; very; grand, supreme
  - 好
    - hǎo, hào
    - good, well; to like, be fond of; easy to
  - 了
    - le, liǎo
    - aspect particle marking completed action; modal particle indicating change of state; to finish, end; can, be possible
- **examples:**
  - 会议提前结束(會議提前結束)，太好了(太好了)！
    - huìyì tíqián jiéshù, tài hǎo le
    - The meeting ended early—great!
  - 你通过面试了(你通過面試了)，太好了(太好了)！
    - nǐ tōngguò miànshì le, tài hǎo le
    - You passed the interview—fantastic!
