## 等一下
---
- **pinyin:** děng yíxià
- **definition:** wait a moment
- **characters:**
  - 等
    - děng
    - to wait, await; grade, level, rank, class; equal; etcetera, and so on
  - 一
    - yī
    - one, single; a, an; once, one time; whole, entire
  - 下
    - xià
    - down, below, under; to go down, descend, get off; next, following; to fall (of rain, snow)
- **etymology:** 一 (yī, "one") quantifies a single, brief instance and 下 (xià, "down") is the directional complement that metaphorically marks a quick, light completion; together as 一下 they mean “for a moment/once,” so 等 (děng, "wait") + 一下 asks for just a short, politely softened pause. In vernacular Mandarin this resultative‑quantifying pattern became a standard way to make requests mild, hence the sense of “wait a moment.”.
- **examples:**
  - 等一下(等一下)，我马上回来(我馬上回來)。
    - děng yíxià, wǒ mǎshàng huílái
    - Wait a moment, I'll be right back.
