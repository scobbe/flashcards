## 谢谢(謝謝)
---
- **pinyin:** xièxie
- **definition:** thanks; thank you
- **characters:**
  - 谢(謝)
    - xiè
    - to thank, thanks; to decline, refuse; to wither, fade; Xie, a surname
  - 谢(謝)
    - xiè
    - to thank, thanks; to decline, refuse; to wither, fade; Xie, a surname
- **etymology:** Combines 谢(謝) (xiè, "to thank") with 谢(謝) (xiè, "to thank") to express thanks; thank you.
- **examples:**
  - 谢谢你的提醒(謝謝你的提醒)。
    - xièxie nǐ de tíxǐng
    - Thanks for the reminder.
  - 谢谢你(謝謝你)，张老师(張老師)。
    - xièxie nǐ, Zhāng lǎoshī
    - Thank you, Teacher Zhang.
