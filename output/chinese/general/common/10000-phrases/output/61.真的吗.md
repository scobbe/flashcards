## 真的吗(真的嗎)
---
- **pinyin:** zhēn de ma
- **definition:** really?; is that true?
- **characters:**
  - 真
    - zhēn
    - true, real, genuine; really, truly; truth, reality
  - 的
    - de
    - possessive marker, 's; attributive particle linking modifiers to nouns; nominalizer turning clauses, adjectives into nouns
  - 吗(嗎)
    - ma
    - question particle for yes/no questions, sentence-final
- **etymology:** Combines 真 (zhēn, "true"), 的 (de, "possessive marker"), and 吗(嗎) (ma, "question particle for yes/no questions") to convey really?; is that true?.
- **examples:**
  - 你今天不用上班(你今天不用上班)，真的吗(真的嗎)？
    - nǐ jīntiān bú yòng shàngbān, zhēn de ma
    - You don't have to work today, really?
  - 医生说手术很成功(醫生說手術很成功)，真的吗(真的嗎)？
    - yīshēng shuō shǒushù hěn chénggōng, zhēn de ma
    - The doctor said the surgery was very successful—is that true?
