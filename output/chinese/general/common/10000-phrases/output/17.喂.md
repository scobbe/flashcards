## 喂
---
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **definition:** hello (on the phone)
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 畏 (wèi, "fear").
- **examples:**
  - 喂(喂)，你好(你好)，请问你是小李吗(請問你是小李嗎)？
    - wèi nǐ hǎo qǐng wèn nǐ shì xiǎo lǐ ma
    - Hello? Hi, may I ask if you're Xiao Li?
