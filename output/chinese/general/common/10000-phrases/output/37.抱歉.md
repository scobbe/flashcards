## 抱歉
---
- **pinyin:** bàoqiàn
- **definition:** sorry; my apologies
- **characters:**
  - 抱
    - bào
    - to hold in the arms, carry; to hug, embrace; to harbor, cherish; to adopt (a child)
  - 歉
    - qiàn
    - to apologize, express regret; sorry, remorseful; deficient, lacking; poor harvest, crop failure
- **etymology:** 抱 (bào, "to hold; to harbor") evokes taking something to one's chest, and in emotion compounds it means inwardly bearing a feeling; 歉 (qiàn, "deficient; remorse") historically marks a shortfall (poor harvest) and carries the sense of owing from 欠 (qiàn, "to owe; lack"). Together they picture holding a sense of falling short—an internalized admission of indebtedness and regret, hence an apology.
- **examples:**
  - 抱歉(抱歉)，我来晚了(我來晚了)。
    - bàoqiàn, wǒ lái wǎn le
    - Sorry, I’m late.
  - 给您添麻烦了(給您添麻煩了)，抱歉(抱歉)。
    - gěi nín tiān máfan le, bàoqiàn
    - My apologies for the trouble.
