## 等等
---
- **pinyin:** děngděng
- **definition:** etc.; and so on
- **characters:**
  - 等
    - děng
    - to wait, await; class, grade, rank; equal; and so on, etc
  - 等
    - děng
    - to wait, await; class, grade, rank; equal; and so on, etc
- **etymology:** 等 (děng, "equal; class") in Classical Chinese was a postposed collective meaning "and those of the same kind," rooted in the notion of items of equal rank (the bamboo radical hints at tallying/classifying on slips); reduplication to 等等 iconically prolongs that open set, signaling an unfinished list—"and so on" (not the "to wait" sense).
- **examples:**
  - 请准备身份证(請準備身份證)、学生证(學生證)、银行卡等等(銀行卡等等)。
    - qǐng zhǔnbèi shēnfènzhèng xuéshēngzhèng yínhángkǎ děngděng
    - Please prepare your ID card, student card, bank card, etc.
  - 他们聊了音乐(他們聊了音樂)、电影(電影)、旅行等等话题(旅行等等話題)。
    - tāmen liáo le yīnyuè diànyǐng lǚxíng děngděng huàtí
    - They chatted about music, movies, travel, and so on.
