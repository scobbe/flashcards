## 很好
---
- **pinyin:** hěn hǎo
- **definition:** very good
- **characters:**
  - 很
    - hěn
    - very, quite, really
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine, nice; well, healthy; OK, all right; easy to
- **examples:**
  - 天气很好(天氣很好)，我们去散步吧(我們去散步吧)。
    - tiānqì hěn hǎo, wǒmen qù sànbù ba
    - The weather is very good; let's go for a walk.
