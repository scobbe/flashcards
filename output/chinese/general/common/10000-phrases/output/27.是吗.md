## 是吗(是嗎)
---
- **pinyin:** shì ma
- **definition:** really?; is that so?
- **characters:**
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be, is, are, am; yes, correct, right; indeed, really
  - 吗(嗎)
    - ma
    - question particle for yes–no questions
- **etymology:** Combines 是 (shì, "to be") with 吗(嗎) (ma, "question particle for yes–no questions") to express really?; is that so?.
- **examples:**
  - 是吗(是嗎)？
    - shì ma
    - Really?
  - 你明天要走(你明天要走)，是吗(是嗎)？
    - nǐ míngtiān yào zǒu, shì ma
    - You're leaving tomorrow, is that so?
