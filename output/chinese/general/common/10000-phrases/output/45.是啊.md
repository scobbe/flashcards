## 是啊
---
- **pinyin:** shì a
- **definition:** yeah; that's right
- **characters:**
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be; yes; right, correct; indeed, really
  - 啊
    - a
    - ah, oh; particle indicating affirmation, agreement; sentence-final particle softening a request or suggestion, moderating tone
- **etymology:** 是 (shì, "to be; correct") is the plain affirmative verdict; adding the sentence‑final particle 啊 (a, "ah; particle of affirmation/softening") shifts it from a stark “yes” to an affiliative, conversational assent—like English “yeah/indeed.” In Mandarin interaction, 啊 marks stance and rapport, so 是啊 reads as agreeing while aligning with the speaker’s feeling (“that’s right, so true”).
- **examples:**
  - 是啊(是啊)，很久不见(很久不見)。
    - shì a, hěn jiǔ bú jiàn
    - Yeah, long time no see.
  - 是啊(是啊)，你说得没错(你說得沒錯)。
    - shì a, nǐ shuō de méi cuò
    - That's right, you're right.
