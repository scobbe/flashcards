## 是啊
---
- **pinyin:** shì a
- **definition:** yeah; that's right
- **characters:**
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be; yes; right, correct; indeed, really
  - 啊
    - a
    - ah, oh; particle indicating affirmation, agreement; sentence-final particle softening a request or suggestion, moderating tone
- **examples:**
  - 是啊(是啊)，很久不见(很久不見)。
    - shì a, hěn jiǔ bú jiàn
    - Yeah, long time no see.
  - 是啊(是啊)，你说得没错(你說得沒錯)。
    - shì a, nǐ shuō de méi cuò
    - That's right, you're right.
