## 什么(什麼)
---
- **pinyin:** shénme
- **definition:** what?
- **characters:**
  - 什
    - shén
    - what; assorted, various; household goods, utensils; ten
  - 么(麼)
    - me
    - interrogative suffix; how, to what extent; small, insignificant
- **etymology:** Combines 什 (shén, "what") with 么(麼) (me, "interrogative suffix") to express what?.
- **examples:**
  - 你在做什么(你在做什麼)？
    - nǐ zài zuò shénme
    - What are you doing?
