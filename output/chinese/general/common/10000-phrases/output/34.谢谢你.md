## 谢谢你(謝謝你)
---
- **pinyin:** xièxie nǐ
- **definition:** thank you
- **characters:**
  - 谢(謝)
    - xiè
    - thank; decline, refuse; wither, fade; Xie, a surname
  - 谢(謝)
    - xiè
    - thank; decline, refuse; wither, fade; Xie, a surname
  - 你
    - nǐ
    - you, singular informal
- **etymology:** Rooted in ritual etiquette where 谢(謝) (xiè, "thank; decline; apologize") first denoted a courteous refusal/apology to a favor or gift—polite words that evolved into gratitude—its reduplication 谢谢 mirrors Mandarin’s verb-reduplication that softens and warms a brief polite act, while 你 (nǐ, "you") explicitly marks the addressee being thanked.
- **examples:**
  - 谢谢你(謝謝你)！
    - xièxie nǐ
    - Thank you!
