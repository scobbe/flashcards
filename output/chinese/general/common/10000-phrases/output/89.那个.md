## 那个(那個)
---
- **pinyin:** nàge
- **definition:** that...; um...
- **characters:**
  - 那
    - nà
    - that, that one; then, in that case
  - 个(個)
    - gè
    - general measure word, classifier for people and many things; individual, single
- **etymology:** 那 (nà, "that") sets up distal pointing, and 个(個) (gè, "individual; general classifier") fills the classifier slot that licenses a noun phrase; with the noun omitted it literally means “that one.” In speech this half-built pointing phrase is prolonged while the speaker searches for or avoids the noun, so 那个 functions as a hesitation filler—shifting from physical pointing to cognitive groping.
- **examples:**
  - 那个问题很复杂(那個問題很複雜)。
    - nàge wèntí hěn fùzá
    - That issue is very complex.
  - 那个(那個)，我还没想好(我還沒想好)。
    - nàge, wǒ hái méi xiǎng hǎo
    - Um, I haven’t decided yet.
