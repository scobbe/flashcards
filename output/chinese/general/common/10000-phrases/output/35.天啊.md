## 天啊
---
- **pinyin:** tiān a
- **definition:** oh my god
- **characters:**
  - 天
    - tiān
    - sky, heavens; day; weather; nature, innate quality
  - 啊
    - a
    - ah, oh; eh, huh; particle indicating affirmation, agreement; particle softening a statement or a request
- **examples:**
  - 天啊(天啊)，这也太贵了(這也太貴了)！
    - tiān a, zhè yě tài guì le
    - Oh my god, this is way too expensive!
