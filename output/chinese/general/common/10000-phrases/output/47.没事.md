## 没事(沒事)
---
- **pinyin:** méi shì
- **definition:** it's nothing; no problem
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒)
    - méi, mò
    - not, no; without, lacking; to sink, to submerge; to confiscate, to seize
  - 事
    - shì
    - matter, affair, thing; business, work; problem, trouble; task, duty
- **etymology:** 没(沒) (méi, "not; to sink/vanish") with 事 (shì, "affair; trouble") literally states that no matter exists, and the older sense of 没(沒) (méi, "to submerge/vanish") adds the nuance that any trouble has gone under—hence a reassuring sense that nothing's the matter. It echoes classical 無 (wú, "without") 事 (shì, "affair") and is a colloquial clipping of 没(沒)有事 used to downplay issues and put others at ease.
- **examples:**
  - 对不起打扰你了(對不起打擾你了)，没事(沒事)。
    - duìbuqǐ dǎrǎo nǐ le, méi shì.
    - Sorry for bothering you; it's nothing.
  - 如果你需要我帮忙搬家(如果你需要我幫忙搬家)，没事(沒事)，我七点就到(我七點就到)。
    - rúguǒ nǐ xūyào wǒ bāngmáng bānjiā, méi shì, wǒ qī diǎn jiù dào.
    - If you need me to help you move, no problem; I'll be there at seven.
