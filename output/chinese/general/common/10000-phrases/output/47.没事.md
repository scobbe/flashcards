## 没事(沒事)
---
- **pinyin:** méi shì
- **definition:** it's nothing; no problem
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒)
    - méi, mò
    - not, no; without, lacking; to sink, to submerge; to confiscate, to seize
  - 事
    - shì
    - matter, affair, thing; business, work; problem, trouble; task, duty
- **examples:**
  - 对不起打扰你了(對不起打擾你了)，没事(沒事)。
    - duìbuqǐ dǎrǎo nǐ le, méi shì.
    - Sorry for bothering you; it's nothing.
  - 如果你需要我帮忙搬家(如果你需要我幫忙搬家)，没事(沒事)，我七点就到(我七點就到)。
    - rúguǒ nǐ xūyào wǒ bāngmáng bānjiā, méi shì, wǒ qī diǎn jiù dào.
    - If you need me to help you move, no problem; I'll be there at seven.
