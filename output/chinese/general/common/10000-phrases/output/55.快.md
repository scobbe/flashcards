## 快
---
- **pinyin:** kuài
- **definition:** fast; hurry
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 忄 + phonetic 夬.
- **examples:**
  - 这辆车很快(這輛車很快)。
    - zhè liàng chē hěn kuài
    - This car is very fast.
  - 快点(快點)，我们要迟到了(我們要遲到了)！
    - kuài diǎn, wǒmen yào chídào le
    - Hurry up, we're going to be late!
