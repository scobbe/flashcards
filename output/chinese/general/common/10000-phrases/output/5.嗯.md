## 嗯
---
- **pinyin:** èn
- **definition:** mm-hmm; uh-huh
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 恩.
- **examples:**
  - 他问我听见没有(他問我聽見沒有)，我只回了声“嗯”(我只回了聲「嗯」)。
    - tā wèn wǒ tīngjiàn méiyǒu, wǒ zhǐ huí le shēng èn
    - He asked if I could hear him; I just replied, “mm-hmm.”
  - 嗯(嗯)，我同意(我同意)。
    - èn, wǒ tóngyì
    - Uh-huh, I agree.
