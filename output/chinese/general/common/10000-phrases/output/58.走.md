## 走
---
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **definition:** go; leave; walk
- **examples:**
  - 我们走吧(我們走吧) (wǒmen zǒu ba, "Let's go")
  - 我得先走了，回头再聊(我得先走了，回頭再聊) (wǒ děi xiān zǒu le, huítóu zài liáo, "I have to leave now; talk later")
  - 他每天走五公里 (tā měitiān zǒu wǔ gōnglǐ, "He walks five kilometers every day")
