## 真的
---
- **pinyin:** zhēn de
- **definition:** really; truly
- **characters:**
  - 真
    - zhēn
    - true, real, genuine; really, truly; truth
  - 的
    - de, dí, dì
    - possessive, attributive particle; nominalizing particle; target, bull's-eye; true, real (literary)
- **etymology:** Nominalizing 真 (zhēn, "true") with 的 (de, "attributive/nominalizer; originally 'target/bull's-eye'") yields "the true (one/thing)," which in vernacular became a sentential adverb "really" that asserts the remark hits the mark. The older reading 的 (dí, "true, real") subtly reinforces this truth-emphasizing use.
- **examples:**
  - 我真的没想到会这么难(我真的沒想到會這麼難)。
    - wǒ zhēn de méi xiǎng dào huì zhème nán
    - I really didn’t expect it to be this difficult.
