## 快走
---
- **pinyin:** kuài zǒu
- **definition:** hurry up and go; move it
- **characters:**
  - 快
    - kuài
    - fast, quick; soon; happy, pleased; sharp, keen
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - to walk; to go; to leave, depart; to move, travel
- **examples:**
  - 快走(快走)，这里不安全(這裡不安全)！
    - kuài zǒu, zhè lǐ bù ānquán!
    - Hurry up and go, it's not safe here!
  - 快走(快走)，我们只剩五分钟了(我們只剩五分鐘了)！
    - kuài zǒu, wǒmen zhǐ shèng wǔ fēnzhōng le!
    - Move it, we only have five minutes left!
