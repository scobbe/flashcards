## 爸爸
---
- **pinyin:** bàba
- **definition:** dad; father
- **characters:**
  - 爸
    - bà
    - dad, daddy, papa
  - 爸
    - bà
    - dad, daddy, papa
- **etymology:** Reduplicating 爸 (bà, "dad") follows the Chinese A–A kinship pattern that turns a kin title into an intimate, child-directed address (second syllable often neutralized), paralleling 妈妈 and 爷爷. The graph 爸 (bà, "dad") itself is a colloquial phonosemantic form—父 (fù, "father") + 巴 (bā, "phonetic")—and the universally easy infant syllable ba makes the doubled form naturally mark familiar, in-home ‘my dad’ versus formal 父親.
- **examples:**
  - 爸爸(爸爸)，你回来了吗(你回來了嗎)？
    - bàba, nǐ huí lái le ma?
    - Dad, are you back?
  - 爸爸是我最坚强的后盾(爸爸是我最堅強的後盾)。
    - bàba shì wǒ zuì jiānqiáng de hòudùn.
    - My father is my strongest support.
