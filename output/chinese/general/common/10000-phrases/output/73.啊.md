## 啊
---
- **pinyin:** a
- **definition:** ah!; oh! (particle)
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 阿 (ā, "prefix").
- **examples:**
  - 啊(啊)，好疼(好疼)！
    - a, hǎo téng
    - Ah, that hurts!
  - 原来是你啊(原來是你啊)！
    - yuánlái shì nǐ a
    - Oh, so it's you!
