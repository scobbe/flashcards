## 是的
---
- **pinyin:** shì de
- **definition:** yes
- **characters:**
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be; yes; right, correct
  - 的
    - de
    - possessive marker 's, of; attributive marker linking modifier to noun; nominalizer forming a noun phrase
- **etymology:** 是 (shì, "to be; correct") states truth/identity, and sentence-final 的 (de, "attributive/nominalizing particle") packages the prior proposition as a referent, yielding the elliptical reply "(what you said) is (so)"—a softened, polite confirmation. In vernacular usage since late imperial/modern Chinese, X+的 answers (e.g., 對的, 好的, 真的) serve as stand-alone affirmations, so 是的 naturally became a standard 'yes.'.
- **examples:**
  - 是的(是的)，我同意(我同意)。
    - shì de, wǒ tóngyì
    - Yes, I agree.
