## 是的
---
- **pinyin:** shì de
- **definition:** yes
- **characters:**
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be; yes; right, correct
  - 的
    - de
    - possessive marker 's, of; attributive marker linking modifier to noun; nominalizer forming a noun phrase
- **examples:**
  - 是的(是的)，我同意(我同意)。
    - shì de, wǒ tóngyì
    - Yes, I agree.
