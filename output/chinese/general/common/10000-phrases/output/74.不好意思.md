## 不好意思
---
- **pinyin:** bù hǎoyìsi
- **definition:** sorry; excuse me
- **characters:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not, no; un-, in-, non-
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine, well; okay, all right; very; easy to, convenient to
  - 意
    - yì
    - meaning, sense; idea, thought; intention, intent
  - 思
    - sī
    - to think, to consider; to miss, to long for; thought, thinking
- **etymology:** Literally “not a good sense/feeling,” 不 (bù, "not") + 好 (hǎo, "good") qualify 意思 (yìsi, "sense, intention, hint" from 意 (yì, "intention") + 思 (sī, "thought")), signaling the speaker’s inner unease at imposing or causing trouble—i.e., “I feel embarrassed.” In Northern Mandarin politeness, stating one’s embarrassment is a face-saving way to soften requests or apologies, so 不好意思 naturally serves as “excuse me/sorry.”.
- **examples:**
  - 不好意思(不好意思)，我来晚了(我來晚了)。
    - bù hǎoyìsi wǒ lái wǎn le
    - Sorry, I'm late.
  - 不好意思(不好意思)，请问洗手间在哪里(請問洗手間在哪裡)？
    - bù hǎoyìsi qǐngwèn xǐshǒujiān zài nǎlǐ
    - Excuse me, where is the restroom?
