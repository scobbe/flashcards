## 所以
---
- **pinyin:** suǒyǐ
- **definition:** so; therefore
- **characters:**
  - 所
    - suǒ
    - place, location; office, bureau, institute; classifier for houses, buildings, institutions; grammatical particle before a verb to nominalize, that which, what
  - 以
    - yǐ
    - to use, take; by means of, with; in order to, so as to; according to, based on; because of, due to
- **etymology:** Combines 所 (suǒ, "place") with 以 (yǐ, "to use") to express so; therefore.
- **examples:**
  - 天气太冷(天氣太冷)，所以我们决定在家待着(所以我們決定在家待著)。
    - tiānqì tài lěng suǒyǐ wǒmen juédìng zài jiā dāi zhe
    - It’s too cold, so we decided to stay home.
  - 证据确凿(證據確鑿)，所以他被判有罪(所以他被判有罪)。
    - zhèngjù quèzáo suǒyǐ tā bèi pàn yǒu zuì
    - The evidence is conclusive; therefore, he was found guilty.
