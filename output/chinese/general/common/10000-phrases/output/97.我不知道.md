## 我不知道
---
- **pinyin:** wǒ bù zhīdào
- **definition:** I don't know
- **characters:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I, me; my; self, ego
  - 不
    - bù
    - not; no; un-, non-
  - 知
    - zhī
    - to know, be aware of; to inform, notify
  - 道
    - dào
    - road, path; way, method; to say, speak; Dao, the Way
- **etymology:** Combines 我 (wǒ, "I"), 不 (bù, "not"), 知 (zhī, "to know"), and 道 (dào, "road") to express i don't know.
- **examples:**
  - 对不起(對不起)，我不知道(我不知道)。
    - duìbuqǐ wǒ bù zhīdào
    - Sorry, I don't know.
