## 没关系(沒關係)
---
- **pinyin:** méi guānxi
- **definition:** it's okay; no problem
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒)
    - méi
    - not, didn’t, haven’t; without, lacking
  - 关(關)
    - guān
    - to close, shut, turn off; to concern, relate to; pass, checkpoint; to imprison, lock up
  - 系(係)
    - xì
    - department (in a university); system, series; relation, connection; lineage, family line
- **examples:**
  - 没关系(沒關係)，你不是故意的(你不是故意的)。
    - méi guānxi, nǐ bú shì gùyì de
    - It's okay, you didn't do it on purpose.
  - 没关系(沒關係)，这是我应该做的(這是我應該做的)。
    - méi guānxi, zhè shì wǒ yīnggāi zuò de
    - No problem, it's what I should do.
