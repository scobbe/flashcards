## 不是
---
- **pinyin:** bú shì
- **definition:** not; isn't
- **characters:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not, no; un-, non-
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be, is, am, are; yes; correct, right; indeed, really
- **etymology:** Pre-verbal negator 不 (bù, "not") scopes over the copula 是 (shì, "to be; right"), denying identity/affirmation—literally 'not-be/not-right' and thus 'isn't'. In Classical Chinese, A 非 (fēi, "not; wrong") B was the standard 'A is not B'; as 是 (shì, "this; right") later grammaticalized into the copula, 不是 became the natural modern negation and a concise way to reject an assertion ('not so').
- **examples:**
  - 不是所有英雄都披着斗篷(不是所有英雄都披著斗篷)。
    - bú shì suǒyǒu yīngxióng dōu pī zhe dǒupéng
    - Not all heroes wear capes.
  - 这不是你的错(這不是你的錯)。
    - zhè bú shì nǐ de cuò
    - This isn't your fault.
