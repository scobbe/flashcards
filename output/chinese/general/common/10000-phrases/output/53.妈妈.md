## 妈妈(媽媽)
---
- **pinyin:** māma
- **definition:** mom; mother
- **characters:**
  - 妈(媽)
    - mā
    - mother, mom, mum, ma
  - 妈(媽)
    - mā
    - mother, mom, mum, ma
- **etymology:** Reduplicating 妈(媽) (mā, "mom") mirrors infant babble "ma-ma," a near‑universal labial syllable produced while nursing; in Chinese, such reduplication signals affectionate, intimate kin terms. Graphically, 妈(媽) places 女 (nǚ, "female") beside the phonetic 馬 (mǎ, "horse") to cue the ma sound, and doubling it highlights colloquial warmth versus more formal 母(母) (mǔ, "mother").
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈(媽媽)，我回来了(我回來了)。
    - māma wǒ huí lái le
    - Mom, I'm back.
  - 她是三个孩子的妈妈(她是三個孩子的媽媽)。
    - tā shì sān gè háizi de māma
    - She is the mother of three children.
