## 妈妈(媽媽)
---
- **pinyin:** māma
- **definition:** mom; mother
- **characters:**
  - 妈(媽): mā, mom; mother; ma; mum
  - 妈(媽): mā, mom; mother; ma; mum
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈，你回来了(媽媽，你回來了) (māma, nǐ huílái le, "Mom, you're back")
  - 我的妈妈是一名医生(我的媽媽是一名醫生) (wǒ de māma shì yī míng yīshēng, "My mother is a doctor")
