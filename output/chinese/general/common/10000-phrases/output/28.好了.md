## 好了
---
- **pinyin:** hǎo le
- **definition:** alright then; okay
- **characters:**
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine; to like, be fond of; easy to; very, quite
  - 了
    - le, liǎo
    - perfective aspect marker, completed action; change-of-state particle, new situation; to finish, to end; to understand, be clear
- **etymology:** Combines 好 (hǎo, "good") with 了 (le, liǎo, "perfective aspect marker") to express alright then; okay.
- **examples:**
  - 好了(好了)，我们走吧(我們走吧)。
    - hǎo le, wǒmen zǒu ba
    - Alright then, let's go.
  - 好了(好了)，我知道了(我知道了)。
    - hǎo le, wǒ zhīdào le
    - Okay, I got it.
