## 当然(當然)
---
- **pinyin:** dāngrán
- **definition:** of course; certainly
- **characters:**
  - 当(當)
    - dāng
    - to be, act as, serve as; should, ought to; when, just at (time or place); in the presence of, facing
  - 然
    - rán
    - so, thus; right, correct; suffix indicating manner or nature, -like, -ly
- **etymology:** Anchored in Classical Chinese usage, 当(當) (dāng, "ought to; fitting; to match") asserts normative appropriateness, while 然 (rán, "so; thus; right") is the classical 'thus/so'; together they say it ought to be thus, shifting from a prescriptive judgment to a naturalized certainty—hence of course. The scale-weight sense behind 当(當) reinforces the idea that the situation balances correctly, making the affirmation feel self-evidently proper.
- **examples:**
  - 当然可以(當然可以)，我很乐意帮你(我很樂意幫你)。
    - dāngrán kěyǐ, wǒ hěn lèyì bāng nǐ
    - Of course you can; I'm happy to help you.
  - 我们当然会支持你的决定(我們當然會支持你的決定)。
    - wǒmen dāngrán huì zhīchí nǐ de juédìng
    - We will certainly support your decision.
