## 小心
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎoxīn
- **definition:** be careful
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor; petty
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; mind; intention, intent; center, core
- **etymology:** Literally 'make the 心 (xīn, "heart; mind") small': 小 (xiǎo, "small") functions as 'to reduce/curb', so shrinking the inner seat of attention implies restraint and vigilance; this classical metaphor of a constrained mind contrasts with 大 (dà, "big") + 意 (yì, "intent") in 大意 'careless', where an expanded mind grows lax.
- **examples:**
  - 小心点儿(小心點兒)，地上很滑(地上很滑)。
    - xiǎoxīn diǎnr dì shang hěn huá
    - Be careful; the floor is very slippery.
