## Yao Bin (name)
### Yáo Bīn
---
- **traditional:** 姚濱
- **definition:** Yao Bin (name)
- **pinyin:** Yáo Bīn
- **components:**
  - 姚
    - yáo
    - handsome; elegant
  - 滨(濱)
    - bīn
    - shore; coast
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 姚 (yáo, "handsome; elegant") refers to the surname Yao. 濱(滨) (bīn, "shore; coast") is often used as a given name meaning "shore" or "coast", signifying a connection to nature or elegance.
  - **interpretation:** 姚濱(滨) is a common Chinese name, where 姚 (yáo, "handsome; elegant") is a family name, and 濱(滨) (bīn, "shore; coast") is typically a given name that may suggest elegance or a serene, natural beauty.
  - **simplification:** 滨 is the simplified form of 濱.
- **examples:**
  - 姚滨(濱)是一位杰(傑)出的科学(學)家。
    - Yáo Bīn shì yī wèi jié chū de kē xué jiā.
    - Yao Bin is an outstanding scientist.
  - 姚滨(濱)今年出国访问(國訪問)，带(帶)回了很多珍贵(貴)的学术资(學術資)源。
    - Yáo Bīn jīn nián chū guó fǎng wèn, dài huí le hěn duō zhēn guì de xué shù zī yuán.
    - This year, Yao Bin went abroad for an academic visit and brought back many valuable scholarly resources.

### 姚( )滨(濱) → 滨(濱)
- **traditional:** 濱
- **definition:** shore; coast
- **pinyin:** bīn
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 賓
    - bīn
    - guest
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 賓 (bīn, "guest") =
    - water near guest ->
    - shore
  - **interpretation:** The character 滨(濱) (bīn, "shore") combines 水(shuǐ, "water"), representing the water characteristic of a shore, with 賓(bīn, "guest"), which provides the pronunciation. This formation suggests a place where guests might arrive by water, reflecting coastal regions.
  - **simplification:** 濱 was simplified by removing parts of the 賓(bīn, "guest") component that were deemed less essential for maintaining pronunciation, while keeping the overall structure recognizable.
- **examples:**
  - 我沿着河滨(濱)散步。

    - I took a walk along the river shore.
  - 太阳(陽)落在滨(濱)海的尽头(盡頭)。
    - Tàiyáng luò zài bīn hǎi de jìntóu.
    - The sun set at the edge of the coast.
  - 沿滨(濱)公路风(風)景宜人。
    - Yán bīn gōnglù fēngjǐng yírén.
    - The scenic coastal road is pleasant.
