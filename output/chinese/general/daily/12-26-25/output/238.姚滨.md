## Yao Bin (name)
### Yáo Bīn

- **definition:** Yao Bin (name)
- **pinyin:** Yáo Bīn
- **components:**
  - 姚
    - yáo
    - surname; elegant; beautiful; handsome
  - 滨(濱)
    - bīn
    - shore; waterfront; riverside; coast
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** proper name (surname + given name)
  - **description:** 姚 (surname) + 滨/濱 (shore; waterfront) = personal name.
  - **interpretation:** A full Chinese name: 姚 is the family name, while 滨/濱 as a given name evokes the image of a shore or waterfront. Often chosen for its elegant sound and auspicious water connotations.
  - **simplification:** 濱 → 滨 by replacing the phonetic 賓 with its simplified form 宾 while retaining the water radical 氵; reduces strokes and regularizes the phonetic series.
- **examples:**
  - 姚滨是著名的花样滑冰教练(姚濱是著名的花樣滑冰教練)。

    - Yao Bin is a famous figure skating coach.
  - 今天的发布会上(今天的發布會上)，姚滨分享了训练经验(姚濱分享了訓練經驗)。
    - Jīntiān de fābùhuì shàng, Yáo Bīn fēnxiǎng le xùnliàn jīngyàn.
    - At today's press conference, Yao Bin shared training experience.
