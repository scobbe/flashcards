## oyster sauce
### háoyóu
---
- **definition:** oyster sauce
- **pinyin:** háoyóu
- **components:**
  - 蚝(蠔)
    - háo
    - oyster
  - 油
    - yóu
    - oil; sauce
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 蚝(蠔) (háo, "oyster") + 油(油) (yóu, "oil; sauce") =
    - A sauce made from oysters.
  - **interpretation:** 蚝油(蠔油) (háoyóu) combines the notion of 'oyster' with 'sauce', indicating a sauce primarily made from oysters. It is commonly used in Chinese cooking to add a rich, savory flavor to dishes.
  - **simplification:** 蚝(蠔) simplified to 蚝.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈炒菜时常用蚝油(媽媽炒菜時常用蠔油)，使菜肴更加美味(使菜肴更加美味)。

    - Mom often uses oyster sauce when cooking, making the dishes even more delicious.
  - 蚝油是制作中餐的必备调料(蠔油是製作中餐的必備調料)。
    - Háoyóu shì zhìzuò Zhōngcān de bìbèi tiáoliào.
    - Oyster sauce is an essential seasoning for making Chinese food.
