### oyster sauce
---
- **pinyin:** oyster sauce
- **components:**
  - 蚝(蠔)
    - háo
    - oyster; shellfish; food
  - 油
    - yóu
    - oil; liquid; greasy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 蚝(蠔) (háo, "oyster") + 油(油) (yóu, "oil") — oyster oil
  - **interpretation:** Oyster sauce is made from oysters and has a rich flavor. The combination of 蚝(蠔) (háo, "oyster") and 油(油) (yóu, "oil") makes sense, as it describes a savory, oily product derived from oysters.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用蚝油做菜(我喜歡用蠔油做菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng háo yóu zuò cài.
    - I like to use oyster sauce for cooking.
  - 这道菜的味道很鲜美，加了蚝油(這道菜的味道很鮮美，加了蠔油)。
    - Zhè dào cài de wèidào hěn xiānměi, jiāle háo yóu.
    - This dish tastes very delicious; it has added oyster sauce.
### 蚝油(蚝油) → 蚝(蚝)
- **definition:** oyster; shellfish; food
- **pinyin:** háo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 蚝 is derived from the component for shellfish and the phonetic element 合 which indicates the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts a type of shellfish, highlighted by the inclusion of its phonetic component, conveying the idea of an oyster in a rich context.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃蚝。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī háo.
    - I like to eat oysters.
  - 餐厅有新鲜的蚝。
    - Cāntīng yǒu xīnxiān de háo.
    - The restaurant has fresh oysters.
### 蚝油(蚝油) → 油(油)
- **definition:** oil; liquid; greasy
- **pinyin:** yóu
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 由
    - yóu
    - to follow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the water radical 氵 (shuǐ, 'water') with 由 (yóu, 'to follow') indicating a liquid substance.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests that oil is a liquid that follows or behaves similarly to water, emphasizing its fluid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了油(我買了油)，准备做菜(準備做菜)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yóu, zhǔnbèi zuò cài.
    - I bought oil, ready to cook.
  - 这油很好(這油很好)，适合煎炸(適合煎炸)。
    - Zhè yóu hěn hǎo, shìhé jiānzhà.
    - This oil is great, suitable for frying.
### 蚝油(蚝油) → 油(油) → 氵(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents water in its flowing form, emphasizing its liquid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
### 蚝油(蚝油) → 油(油) → 由(由)
- **definition:** to follow
- **pinyin:** yóu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Derived from the image of a person (the upper part) following a path or way (the lower part), indicating a following or route.
  - **interpretation:** This character conveys the idea of following a path or leading someone along a way, making it intuitive for the meaning of 'to follow'.
- **examples:**
  - 我通过这条路(我通過這條路)，由这里到达目的地(由這裡到達目的地)。
    - Wǒ tōngguò zhè tiáo lù, yóu zhèlǐ dào dá mùdì dì.
    - I follow this road, to reach the destination from here.
  - 小孩由父母带(小孩由父母帶)，享受快乐的时光(享受快樂的時光)。
    - Xiǎohái yóu fùmǔ dài, xiǎngshòu kuàilè de shíguāng.
    - The child is taken by the parents, enjoying happy times.
