## hometown
### jiāxiāng
---
- **traditional:** 家鄉
- **definition:** hometown
- **pinyin:** jiāxiāng
- **components:**
  - 家
    - jiā
    - home; family; house
  - 乡(鄉)
    - xiāng
    - village; countryside; hometown
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 家 (jiā, "home") + 乡(鄉) (xiāng, "village") refers to one's place of origin or hometown. Together they form 家( )乡(鄉) (jiāxiāng, "hometown"), emphasizing the idea of a home village or the place where one feels a deep connection due to personal roots or heritage.
  - **interpretation:** 家( )乡(鄉) (jiāxiāng, "hometown") signifies the place that is culturally and sentimentally significant to an individual, often where they were born and raised.
  - **simplification:** 家乡 is the simplified form of 家鄉.
- **examples:**
  - 我热爱家( )乡(鄉)。
    - Wǒ rè'ài jiāxiāng.
    - I love my hometown.
  - 每( )当(當)春( )节(節)到( )来(來)时(時)，我都会想念家( )乡(鄉)。
    - Měi dāng chūnjié dào lái shí, wǒ dōu huì xiǎngniàn jiāxiāng.
    - Whenever the Spring Festival arrives, I always miss my hometown.
  - 经(經)历(歷)长(長)途( )跋( )涉( )后(後)，我终于回到了家( )乡(鄉)。
    - Jīnglì chángtú báshè hòu, wǒ zhōngyú huí dào le jiāxiāng.
    - After a long journey, I finally returned to my hometown.
