## hometown
### jiāxiāng
---
- **definition:** hometown
- **pinyin:** jiāxiāng
- **components:**
  - 家
    - jiā
    - home; family
  - 乡(鄉)
    - xiāng
    - countryside; hometown; village
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 家(家) (jiā, "home") + 乡(鄉) (xiāng, "countryside; hometown") =
    - 家乡(家鄉) (jiāxiāng, "hometown")
  - **interpretation:** A 家(家) (jiā, "home") combined with 乡(鄉) (xiāng, "countryside; hometown") embodies the idea of one's original place of residence or where one's family comes from, thus representing the concept of 'hometown.'
  - **simplification:** 家鄉 -> 家乡
- **examples:**
  - 我想回家乡(我想回家鄉)，重温儿时记忆(重溫兒時記憶)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng huí jiāxiāng, chóngwēn érshí jìyì.
    - I want to return to my hometown and relive my childhood memories.
  - 家乡有许多美丽的风景(家鄉有許多美麗的風景)。
    - Jiāxiāng yǒu xǔduō měilì de fēngjǐng.
    - My hometown has many beautiful sceneries.
  - 离家乡越远(離家鄉越遠)，越感到心灵空虚(越感到心靈空虛)。
    - Lí jiāxiāng yuè yuǎn, yuè gǎndào xīnlíng kōngxū.
    - The farther you go from your hometown, the emptier you feel inside.

### 家乡(家鄉) → 乡(鄉)
- **definition:** countryside; hometown; village
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 𠨍
    - two people facing each other
  - 皀
    - round-mouthed food vessel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𠨍(𠨍) (two people facing each other) + 皀(皀) (round-mouthed food vessel) =
    - to feast; later phonetic borrowing/semantic shift ->
    - village ->
    - elimination of 郎(郎)
  - **interpretation:** The original character represented communal feasting, an important aspect of traditional village life. Over time, it came to represent the village itself. The simplified form removed 郎(郎) to streamline the character.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character 乡(鄉) was created by removing 郎(郎) and retaining the basic element related to gathering, thus simplifying the character for modern usage.
- **examples:**
  - 我回乡探亲(我回鄉探親)。
    - Wǒ huí xiāng tànqīn.
    - I returned to my hometown to visit relatives.
  - 我出生在乡村(我出生在鄉村)，那里充满了美好回忆(那裡充滿了美好回憶)。
    - Wǒ chūshēng zài xiāngcūn, nàlǐ chōngmǎnle měihǎo huíyì.
    - I was born in the countryside, where beautiful memories abound.
  - 老乡热情好客(老鄉熱情好客)。
    - Lǎoxiāng rèqíng hǎokè.
    - Villagers are hospitable.
