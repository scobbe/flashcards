### fresh milk
---
- **pinyin:** fresh milk
- **components:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh; delicious; bright; rare
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; (colloquially) mother
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鲜(鮮) (xiān, "fresh") + 奶 (nǎi, "milk") — fresh milk
  - **interpretation:** The term combines freshness with milk to specify a high-quality, recently produced dairy product. This combination emphasizes the desirability of consuming milk that is fresh.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都喝鲜奶(我每天都喝鮮奶)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dōu hē xiān nǎi.
    - I drink fresh milk every day.
  - 这个早餐要配鲜奶(這個早餐要配鮮奶)。
    - Zhège zǎocān yào pèi xiān nǎi.
    - This breakfast should be paired with fresh milk.

  - **description:** Semantic 魚 ( 'fish' ) + abbreviated phonetic 羴 ( 'to smell good' ) links to the idea of freshness and deliciousness.

  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing 魚 with 鱼 to reduce strokes, following modern simplification rules.
