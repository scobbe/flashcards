## 鲜(鮮)奶( )
---
## fresh milk
### xiānnǎi
---
- **traditional:** 鮮奶
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **components:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鮮(鲜) (xiān, "fresh") + 奶 (nǎi, "milk")
  - **interpretation:** The word 鮮(鲜)奶 (xiānnǎi, "fresh milk") combines 鮮(鲜) (xiān, "fresh") and 奶 (nǎi, "milk") to convey the idea of milk that is in its natural, unspoiled state. This term is commonly used to differentiate fresh milk from processed or powdered milk products.
  - **simplification:** 鲜奶 is the simplified form of 鮮奶.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早餐喝鲜(鮮)奶。
    - Wǒ měitiān zǎocān hē xiānnǎi.
    - I drink fresh milk for breakfast every day.
  - 早晨喝鲜(鮮)奶，有助健康。
    - Zǎochén hē xiānnǎi, yǒuzhù jiànkāng.
    - Drinking fresh milk in the morning is beneficial for health.

### 鲜(鮮)奶( ) → 奶
- **definition:** milk
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
  - 乃
    - nǎi
    - so
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 女 (nǚ, 'woman') + phonetic 乃 (nǎi, 'so') =
    - related to women, sound similar to 乃 ->
    - 'milk'
  - **interpretation:** 奶(nǎi) combines the semantic component 女 (nǚ, 'woman') to indicate a connection with women, specifically relating to breastfeeding, and 乃 (nǎi, 'so') as a phonetic component. This structure makes intuitive sense as it suggests a term linked to women, utilizing a sound similar to the word for nurturing.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈给宝宝(媽媽給寶寶)喝奶。
    - Māmā gěi bǎobǎo hē nǎi.
    - Mom gives the baby milk.
  - 牛奶可以帮(幫)助人体(體)吸收营养(營養)，尤其是儿(兒)童。
    - Niúnǎi kěyǐ bāngzhù réntǐ xīshōu yíngyǎng, yóuqí shì értóng.
    - Milk can help the body absorb nutrients, especially for children.
  - 他在超市买(買)了一瓶奶，打算明天做甜点(點)。
    - Tā zài chāoshì mǎile yī píng nǎi, dǎsuàn míngtiān zuò tiándiǎn.
    - He bought a bottle of milk at the supermarket and plans to make dessert tomorrow.
