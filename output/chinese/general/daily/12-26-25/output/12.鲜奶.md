## 鲜奶(鮮奶)
---
## fresh milk
### xiānnǎi
---
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **components:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh; bright; delicious
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; nurse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 鲜(鮮) (xiān, "fresh") + 奶(奶) (nǎi, "milk") =
    - freshly obtained milk
  - **interpretation:** The combination refers to milk that is freshly sourced or obtained, emphasizing its freshness and quality for consumption.
  - **simplification:** 鲜(鮮) simplified to 鲜.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝鲜奶(我喜歡喝鮮奶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē xiānnǎi.
    - I like drinking fresh milk.
  - 早餐时我喝鲜奶(早餐時我喝鮮奶)，感觉很营养(感覺很營養)。
    - Zǎocān shí wǒ hē xiānnǎi, gǎnjué hěn yíngyǎng.
    - At breakfast, I drink fresh milk and feel very nourished.
  - 小猫喝了鲜奶(小貓喝了鮮奶)，整个上午都活力充沛(整個上午都活力充沛)。
    - Xiǎomāo hē le xiānnǎi, zhěnggè shàngwǔ dōu huólì chōngpèi.
    - The kitten drank fresh milk, and was full of energy all morning.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 鲜(鮮)
- **definition:** fresh; bright; delicious
- **pinyin:** xiān
- **components:**
  - 鱼(魚)
    - yú
    - fish
  - 羴(羶)
    - shān
    - smell of mutton
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 魚(鱼) (yú, "fish") + 羴(羶) (shān, an abbreviation of 羴, "smell of mutton") as phonetic ->
    - delicious (fresh)
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic component 魚(鱼) (yú, "fish") suggesting freshness or taste, with the phonetic 羴(羶) (shān, "smell of mutton"), reinforcing the concept of delectable flavor.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by replacing 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") with 羴(羶) (shān) and contracting 魚 to 鱼.
- **examples:**
  - 这家餐厅的海鲜很鲜(這家餐廳的海鮮很鮮)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng de hǎixiān hěn xiān.
    - The seafood at this restaurant is very fresh.
  - 这个菜品不仅看起来鲜亮(這個菜品不僅看起來鮮亮)，而且尝起来鲜美(而且嚐起來鮮美)。
    - Zhège càipǐn bùjǐn kàn qǐlái xiān liàng, érqiě cháng qǐlái xiān měi.
    - Not only does this dish look bright, but it also taste delicious.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 鲜(鮮) → 鱼(魚)
- **definition:** fish
- **pinyin:** yú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a fish
  - **interpretation:** The character 魚(fish) in traditional form visually represents the shape of a fish, complete with head, body, and tail. The design clearly conveys the meaning of 'fish' through its pictorial elements.
  - **simplification:** 魚(fish) was simplified to 鱼 by reducing strokes, retaining enough of the original fish shape for recognition.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃鱼(我喜歡吃魚)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī yú.
    - I like eating fish.
  - 鱼在水中游(魚在水中游)，闪着银光(閃著銀光)。
    - Yú zài shuǐ zhōng yóu, shǎn zhe yín guāng.
    - The fish swims in water, shining with silver light.
  - 大海深不可测(大海深不可測)，鱼群在水中穿梭(魚群在水中穿梭)。
    - Dà hǎi shēn bù kě cè, yú qún zài shuǐ zhōng chuān suō.
    - The great sea is immeasurably deep, and schools of fish are darting through the water.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 鲜(鮮) → 羴(羴)
- **definition:** smell of mutton
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Triplication of 羊
  - **description:**
    - 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") repeated three times ->
    - strong smell of sheep ->
    - 羴(羴) (shān, "smell of mutton")
  - **interpretation:** The character 羴(羴) (shān) emphasizes the strong odor associated with sheep or mutton by repeating the character 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") three times.
- **examples:**
  - 烤全羊散发出浓郁的羴气味(烤全羊散發出濃郁的羶氣味)。
    - Kǎo quán yáng sàn fā chū nóng yù de shān qì wèi
    - The roasted mutton exudes a strong smell of mutton.
  - 田野里传来羊群的叫声(田野裡傳來羊群的叫聲)，夹杂着羴的味道(夾雜著羶的味道)。
    - Tián yě lǐ chuán lái yáng qún de jiào shēng, jiá zá zhe shān wèi dào
    - In the fields, the bleating of sheep could be heard, mixed with the smell of mutton.
  - 山村中空气悄悄弥漫着羴的气息(山村中空氣悄悄彌漫著羶的氣息)，令人陶醉(令人陶醉)。
    - Shān cūn zhōng kōng qì qiāo qiāo mí màn zhe shān de qì xī, lìng rén táo zuì
    - In the mountain village, the air quietly fills with the aroma of mutton, enchanting everyone.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 奶(奶)
- **definition:** milk; breast; nurse
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
  - 乃
    - nǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") + 乃(乃) (nǎi) =
    - relates to women ->
    - milk or nurturing function ->
    - final form as breast or nurse
  - **interpretation:** The character 奶(nǎi, "milk") combines 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") for its semantic association with nurturing or maternal aspects, and 乃(乃) (nǎi), which provides the phonetic hint. This makes the character effective in conveying ideas related to milk or nursing.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天喝牛奶(我每天喝牛奶)。
    - Wǒ měitiān hē niú nǎi.
    - I drink milk every day.
  - 妈妈给婴儿喂奶(媽媽給嬰兒喂奶)，婴儿健康成长(嬰兒健康成長)。
    - Māmā gěi yīng'ér wèi nǎi, yīng'ér jiànkāng chéngzhǎng.
    - Mother feeds milk to the baby, and the baby grows healthy.
  - 农场的奶牛产出新鲜奶(農場的奶牛產出新鮮奶)，商店里有很多奶制品(商店裡有很多奶製品)。
    - Nóngchǎng de nǎi niú chǎnchū xīnxiān nǎi, shāngdiàn lǐ yǒu hěnduō nǎi zhìpǐn.
    - The dairy cows on the farm produce fresh milk, and the store has many dairy products.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 奶(奶) → 女(女)
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing ->
    - modern form reversed facing right
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") originally depicted a woman with pronounced breasts, illustrating the concept of 'woman.' Over time, the pictogram evolved to face right in its modern form, losing details like the left breast to simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有魅力的女(她是一位有魅力的女)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi yǒu mèi lì de nǚ.
    - She is an attractive woman.
  - 那位女战士英勇无畏(那位女戰士英勇無畏)，带领大家前行(帶領大家前行)。
    - Nà wèi nǚ zhàn shì yīng yǒng wú wèi, dài lǐng dà jiā qián xíng.
    - That female warrior is brave and fearless, leading everyone forward.
### 鲜奶(鮮奶) → 奶(奶) → 乃(乃)
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person with protruding breasts (alternative theories depict a rope or dragging action) ->
    - conclusion marker
  - **interpretation:** The character 乃(乃) (nǎi, 'a conclusion marker; hence') is rooted in various interpretations, notably as a pictogram depicting either a person or a rope-related action. These images lend themselves to ideas of continuity or culmination, fitting its meaning as indicating a conclusion.
- **examples:**
  - 他努力学习(他努力學習)，成绩乃显著(成績乃顯著)。
    - tā nǔlì xuéxí, chéngjì nǎi xiǎnzhù.
    - He studies hard, and his results are remarkably evident.
  - 事实真相(事實真相)，乃水落石出(乃水落石出)。
    - shìshí zhēnxiàng, nǎi shuǐ luò shí chū.
    - The truth of the matter is, as water recedes, the rocks are revealed.
  - 风起云涌乃变化之始(風起雲湧乃變化之始)。
    - fēng qǐ yún yǒng nǎi biànhuà zhī shǐ.
    - The surging winds and billowing clouds mark the beginning of change.
