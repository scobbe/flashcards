## cucumber
### huánggua
---
- **definition:** cucumber
- **pinyin:** huánggua
- **components:**
  - 黄(黃)
    - huáng
    - yellow
  - 瓜
    - guā
    - melon; squash; gourd
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 黄(黃) (huáng, "yellow") + 瓜(瓜) (guā, "melon") =
    - cucumber
  - **interpretation:** The term 黄瓜(黃瓜) (huángguā) translates literally to 'yellow melon.' It refers to the cucumber, which is botanically a type of gourd and can have a yellowish tinge when ripe. By naming the cucumber this way, the focus is on its classification among the gourd family rather than its usual green color.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一根黄瓜(我買了一根黃瓜)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī gēn huángguā.
    - I bought a cucumber.
  - 我洗了黄瓜(我洗了黃瓜)，切成了薄片(切成了薄片)。
    - Wǒ xǐ le huángguā, qiē chéng le bó piàn.
    - I washed the cucumber and sliced it into thin pieces.
  - 夏天吃黄瓜(夏天吃黃瓜)，很解暑(很解暑)。
    - Xiàtiān chī huángguā, hěn jiěshǔ.
    - Eating cucumber in summer is very refreshing.
### 黄瓜(黃瓜) → 黄(黃)
