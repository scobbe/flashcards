## cucumber
### huánggua
---
- **definition:** cucumber
- **pinyin:** huánggua
- **components:**
  - 黄(黃)
    - huáng
    - yellow
  - 瓜
    - guā
    - melon; gourd; squash; cucumber
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 黄(黃) (huáng, "yellow") + 瓜(瓜) (guā, "melon") =
    - a type of melon perceived as having a yellowish tint ->
    - cucumber
  - **interpretation:** The word 黄瓜(黃瓜) (huánggua, "cucumber") combines 黄(黃) (huáng, "yellow") and 瓜(瓜) (guā, "melon") to refer to a type of melon with a yellowish hue. Although cucumbers are green, this naming highlights their classification within melons and their original color stages.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃黄瓜(我喜歡吃黃瓜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huángguā.
    - I like eating cucumbers.
  - 黄瓜既清脆(黃瓜既清脆)，又解渴(又解渴)。
    - Huángguā jì qīngcuì, yòu jiěkě.
    - Cucumbers are both crunchy and thirst-quenching.
  - 夏天时(夏天時)，我常买黄瓜(我常買黃瓜)。
    - Xiàtiān shí, wǒ cháng mǎi huángguā.
    - In summer, I often buy cucumbers.
### 黄瓜(黃瓜) → 黄(黃)
- **definition:** yellow
- **pinyin:** huáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram (象形)
  - **description:**
    - Originally a pictogram 黃 (huáng) ->
    - phonetically borrowed for 'yellow' ->
    - 廿 (口 as corrupted) + 田 as possible components ->
    - depicting ritual or jade, incorrect phono-semantic interpretation
  - **interpretation:** 黃(黄) (huáng, 'yellow') originated as a pictogram, later borrowed for 'yellow.' Confusion arose from misinterpreted components 廿 and 田, originally depicting a ritual aspect or jade-related imagery.
  - **simplification:** None, 黄(黃) was already a variant historically used alongside 黃(黃).
- **examples:**
  - 这只小鸡是黄的(這隻小雞是黃的)。
    - Zhè zhī xiǎo jī shì huáng de.
    - This chick is yellow.
  - 黄叶飘落时(黃葉飄落時)，秋意渐浓(秋意漸濃)。
    - Huáng yè piāo luò shí, qiū yì jiàn nóng.
    - When the yellow leaves fall, the autumn mood deepens.
### 黄瓜(黃瓜) → 瓜(瓜)
- **definition:** melon; gourd; squash; cucumber
- **pinyin:** guā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hanging melon with a short stem and tendrils
  - **interpretation:** The character 瓜(瓜) (guā, "melon") visually represents a melon on a vine, reflecting its meaning related to gourd-like fruits.
- **examples:**
  - 夏天来了(夏天來了)，瓜长得很快(瓜長得很快)。
    - Xiàtiān lái le, guā zhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - Summer has arrived, and the melons are growing quickly.
  - 农夫种瓜(農夫種瓜)，收获满仓(收穫滿倉)。
    - Nóngfū zhòng guā, shōuhuò mǎn cāng.
    - The farmer plants melons, and the harvest is bountiful.
  - 市场上有各种不同品种的瓜(市場上有各種不同品種的瓜)，味道鲜美(味道鮮美)。
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu gèzhǒng bùtóng pǐnzhǒng de guā, wèidào xiānměi.
    - There are various types of melons in the market, and they taste delicious.
