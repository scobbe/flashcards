### picturesque scenery
---
- **pinyin:** picturesque scenery
- **components:**
  - 江山
    - jiāngshān
    - rivers and mountains; land; territory
  - 如画(如畫)
    - rúhuà
    - like a painting; picturesque; beautiful
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江山(江山) (jiāngshān, 'rivers and mountains') + 如画(如畫) (rúhuà, 'like a painting') — rivers and mountains like a painting
  - **interpretation:** This phrase evokes a vivid image of natural beauty in landscapes. The combination expresses how the scenery is so beautiful it resembles a painting.
- **examples:**
  - 这幅画描绘了江山如画的美景(這幅畫描繪了江山如畫的美景)。
    - Zhè fú huà miáohuìle jiāngshān rúhuà de měijǐng.
    - This painting depicts the picturesque scenery of rivers and mountains.
  - 在这里，江山如画，令人陶醉(在這裡，江山如畫，令人陶醉)。
    - Zài zhèlǐ, jiāngshān rúhuà, lìng rén táozuì.
    - Here, the scenery is like a painting, enchanting everyone.
