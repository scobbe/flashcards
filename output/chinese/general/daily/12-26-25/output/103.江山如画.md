## picturesque scenery
### jiāngshān rúhuà
---
- **definition:** picturesque scenery
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān rúhuà
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
  - 如
    - rú
    - like
  - 画(畫)
    - huà
    - painting
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") + 山(山) (shān, "mountain") + 如(如) (rú, "like") + 画(畫) (huà, "painting") form the expression 'river/mountains' 'like' 'painting.' It conveys the idea of natural landscapes that resemble beautiful paintings.
  - **interpretation:** This phrase uses the imagery of rivers and mountains to evoke the idea of a landscape so beautiful it looks crafted like art. It's a poetic way to describe scenery.
- **examples:**
  - 这幅画描绘了江山如画(這幅畫描繪了江山如畫)。
    - Zhè fú huà miáohuì le jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - This painting depicts picturesque scenery.
  - 登高远眺时(登高遠眺時)，江山如画令人心旷神怡(江山如畫令人心曠神怡)。
    - Dēng gāo yuǎntiào shí, jiāngshān rúhuà lìngrén xīnkuàng shényí.
    - When standing high and gazing into the distance, the picturesque scenery brings peace of mind.
  - 夜色降临(夜色降臨)，江山如画显得更加壮美(江山如畫顯得更加壯美)。
    - Yèsè jiànglín, jiāngshān rúhuà xiǎn de gèng jiā zhuàngměi.
    - As night falls, the picturesque scenery appears even more magnificent.

    - concept of flowing water ->

  - **interpretation:** The character 江(江) (jiāng, "river") combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") for its association with water and liquid, with 工(工) (gōng, "work") to suggest the sound. Together, they evoke the sense of a flowing water body, which fits the meaning 'river.'
