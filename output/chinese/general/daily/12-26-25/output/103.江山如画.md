## 江山如画(江山如畫)
---
## picturesque scenery
### jiāngshān rúhuà
---
- **definition:** picturesque scenery
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān rúhuà
- **components:**
  - 江山
    - jiāngshān
    - rivers and mountains; landscape; country; realm
  - 如画(如畫)
    - rúhuà
    - like a painting; picturesque
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** idiom
  - **description:** 江山 + 如画 = rivers and mountains that look like a painting → picturesque landscape.
  - **interpretation:** Praises grand natural scenery, often extending to mean the land of one’s country looks beautifully composed. Emphasizes harmonious, painterly vistas.
  - **simplification:** 畫→画 in 如畫→如画 to reduce strokes via character simplification; 江山 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我们沿着湖边行走(我們沿著湖邊行走)，所见江山如画(所見江山如畫)。
    - Wǒmen yánzhe húbiān xíngzǒu, suǒ jiàn jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - We walked along the lakeside, and the scenery we saw was as picturesque as a painting.
  - 春日登高远望(春日登高遠望)，江山如画(江山如畫)。
    - Chūnrì dēnggāo yuǎnwàng, jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - In spring, climbing high and gazing into the distance, the landscape looks like a painting.
  - 艺术家在作品中描绘出江山如画的祖国(藝術家在作品中描繪出江山如畫的祖國)。
    - Yìshùjiā zài zuòpǐn zhōng miáohuì chū jiāngshān rúhuà de zǔguó.
    - The artist depicts a motherland of picturesque rivers and mountains in the work.
### 江山如画(江山如畫) → 江山
- **definition:** rivers and mountains; landscape; country; realm
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river; Yangtze; large river; waterway
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain; hill; peak; ridge
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(river) + 山(mountain) = rivers and mountains → landscape; realm/state.
  - **interpretation:** Literally “rivers and mountains,” a classical pairing that refers to the land’s natural scenery and, by metonymy, the territory or regime. Common in literary and historical contexts to praise scenery or speak of political power.
- **examples:**
  - 祖国的江山壮丽(祖國的江山壯麗)。
    - Zǔguó de jiāngshān zhuànglì.
    - The country’s landscape is magnificent.
  - 他励精图治(他勵精圖治)，巩固江山(鞏固江山)。
    - Tā lìjīng túzhì, gǒnggù jiāngshān.
    - He worked hard to govern and consolidate his realm.
  - 江山如画(江山如畫)，风景无限(風景無限)。
    - Jiāngshān rúhuà, fēngjǐng wúxiàn.
    - The rivers and mountains are picturesque, and the scenery is boundless.
### 江山如画(江山如畫) → 江山 → 江
- **definition:** river; Yangtze; large river; waterway
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work; craft
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 氵 “water” + phonetic 工 (OC *koːŋ).
  - **interpretation:** Water radical 氵 signals a water-related meaning, while 工 provided the pronunciation cue in Old Chinese. Over time the sound shifted to jiāng, but the semantic association with waterways remained clear.
- **examples:**
  - 我们沿着长江航行(我們沿著長江航行)。
    - Wǒmen yánzhe Cháng Jiāng hángxíng.
    - We sailed along the Yangtze River.
  - 雨季时(雨季時)，这条江水位上涨(這條江水位上漲)。
    - Yǔjì shí, zhè tiáo jiāng shuǐwèi shàngzhǎng.
    - During the rainy season, the water level of this river rises.
  - 渡江后(渡江後)，我们在江边露营(我們在江邊露營)。
    - Dù jiāng hòu, wǒmen zài jiāngbiān lùyíng.
    - After crossing the river, we camped by the riverbank.
### 江山如画(江山如畫) → 江山 → 江 → 工
- **definition:** work; craft
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a bladed tool to plow and mark a border; alternatively a carpenter’s square (cf. original meaning of 巨).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tool used to prepare the ground and set boundaries, naturally extending to the idea of labor and work. Some traditions instead see a carpenter’s square, connecting it with making and constructing, and with early senses linked to 巨.
- **examples:**
  - 我在工厂工作(我在工廠工作)。
    - Wǒ zài gōngchǎng gōngzuò.
    - I work in a factory.
  - 这些工人正在劳动(這些工人正在勞動)。
    - Zhèxiē gōngrén zhèngzài láodòng.
    - These workers are laboring now.
  - 他的工钱很高(他的工錢很高)。
    - Tā de gōngqián hěn gāo.
    - His wages are high.
### 江山如画(江山如畫) → 江山 → 山
- **definition:** mountain; hill; peak; ridge
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) — three mountain peaks; oracle forms show triangles rather than vertical strokes. Unrelated to 出, the top of 岁/豐, 屮, and 巾.
  - **interpretation:** A visual sketch of a jagged ridge: one tall central peak flanked by two smaller ones. Earlier inscriptions rendered the peaks as simple triangles that later regularized into three vertical lines. Similar-looking forms like 出 or elements in 岁/豐 are lookalikes with no historical connection.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We’re going to hike/climb in the mountains.
  - 那座山很高(那座山很高)。
    - Nà zuò shān hěn gāo.
    - That mountain is very tall.
  - 山上有很多树(山上有很多樹)。
    - Shān shàng yǒu hěn duō shù.
    - There are many trees on the mountain.
### 江山如画(江山如畫) → 如画(如畫)
- **definition:** like a painting; picturesque
- **pinyin:** rúhuà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 如(as/like) + 画(painting) = like a painting; picturesque.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the comparative 如 with 画 to evoke scenery so beautiful it seems painted. Commonly used to praise landscapes, towns, or seasonal scenes.
  - **simplification:** 畫 → 画 was simplified by reducing strokes for easier writing; 如 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 江南水乡风景如画(江南水鄉風景如畫)。
    - Jiāngnán shuǐxiāng fēngjǐng rúhuà.
    - The scenery of the Jiangnan water towns is picturesque.
  - 山川如画(山川如畫)，让人流连忘返(讓人流連忘返)。
    - Shānchuān rúhuà, ràng rén liúlián wàngfǎn.
    - The mountains and rivers look like a painting, making people reluctant to leave.
  - 春色如画(春色如畫)，到处生机勃勃(到處生機勃勃)。
    - Chūnsè rúhuà, dàochù shēngjī bóbó.
    - The spring scenery is like a painting, vibrant everywhere.
