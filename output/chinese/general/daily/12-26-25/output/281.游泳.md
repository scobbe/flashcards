## swim; swimming
### yóuyǒng
---
- **definition:** swim; swimming
- **pinyin:** yóuyǒng
- **components:**
  - 游
    - yóu
    - travel; wander; swim
  - 泳
    - yǒng
    - swim
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 游(游) (yóu, "travel; wander; swim") and 泳(泳) (yǒng, "swim") together denote the activity of swimming. 游(游) (yóu) by itself can imply motion or traveling through water, and 泳(泳) (yǒng) specifically means swimming. Together, they create the concept of swimming.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 游(游) (yóu) and 泳(泳) (yǒng) effectively conveys the idea of swimming as a motion through water.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 他每天游泳(他每天游泳)，保持身体健康(保持身體健康)。
    - Tā měitiān yóuyǒng, bǎochí shēntǐ jiànkāng.
    - He swims every day to keep healthy.
  - 我们去游泳(我們去游泳)，享受夏日乐趣(享受夏日樂趣)。
    - Wǒmen qù yóuyǒng, xiǎngshòu xiàrì lèqù.
    - We go swimming to enjoy the summer fun.
