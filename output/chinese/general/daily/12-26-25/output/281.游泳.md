### swim; swimming
---
- **pinyin:** swim; swimming
- **components:**
  - 游(遊)
    - yóu
    - swim; roam; travel; to be active
  - 泳
    - yǒng
    - swim; to swim
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 游(遊) (yóu, "swim; roam") + 泳(泳) (yǒng, "swim; to swim") — swim swimming
  - **interpretation:** This term combines the ideas of movement through water and the action of swimming. The first part emphasizes motion while the second part specifies the activity itself.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 她每天去游泳(她每天去游泳)。
    - Tā měitiān qù yóuyǒng.
    - She goes swimming every day.
