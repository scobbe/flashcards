## swim; swimming
### yóuyǒng
---
- **definition:** swim; swimming
- **pinyin:** yóuyǒng
- **components:**
  - 游(遊)
    - yóu
    - to swim; to travel; to wander
  - 泳
    - yǒng
    - to swim
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 游(遊) (yóu, "to swim; to travel") + 泳(泳) (yǒng, "to swim") =
    - swim; swimming
  - **interpretation:** The word 游泳 (yóuyǒng) combines 游(遊) (yóu, "to swim; to travel") and 泳(泳) (yǒng, "to swim") to depict the action of swimming. It emphasizes the physical act as well as the potential enjoyment or leisure activity associated with it.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 夏天来了(夏天來了)，我们去游泳(我們去游泳)。
    - Xiàtiān láile, wǒmen qù yóuyǒng.
    - When summer comes, we go swimming.
  - 他每天练习游泳(他每天練習游泳)，以备参加比赛(以備參加比賽)。
    - Tā měitiān liànxí yóuyǒng, yǐ bèi cānjiā bǐsài.
    - He practices swimming every day in preparation for competitions.
