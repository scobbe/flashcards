## refined products
### jīngzhìpǐn
---
- **traditional:** 精製品
- **definition:** refined products
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **components:**
  - 精
    - jīng
    - refined; essence; spirit; extract
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - to make; to produce; system; to manufacture
  - 品
    - pǐn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精 (jīng, "refined") + 制(製) (zhì, "to make") + 品 (pǐn, "product") refers to products that have undergone a refining or manufacturing process to enhance their quality or value.
  - **interpretation:** The term 精( )制(製)品( ) (jīngzhìpǐn, "refined products") signifies products that have been carefully processed or manufactured to achieve a high level of refinement or quality, emphasizing meticulous craftsmanship or production methods.
  - **simplification:** Simplified characters used in mainland China.
- **examples:**
