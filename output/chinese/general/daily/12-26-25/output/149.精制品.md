---
## refined products
### jīngzhìpǐn
---
- **definition:** refined products
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **components:**
  - 精
    - jīng
    - refined; essence; spirit; extract
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - to make; to produce; system; to manufacture
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - product; item; quality; article
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精(精) (jīng, "refined") + 制(製) (zhì, "to make") + 品(品) (pǐn, "product") refers to products that have undergone a refining or manufacturing process to enhance their quality or value.
  - **interpretation:** The term 精制品(精製品) (jīngzhìpǐn, "refined products") signifies products that have been carefully processed or manufactured to achieve a high level of refinement or quality, emphasizing meticulous craftsmanship or production methods.
  - **simplification:** Simplified characters used in mainland China.
- **examples:**
  - 他的店铺出售各种精制品(他的店鋪出售各種精製品)。
    - tā de diànpù chūshòu gè zhǒng jīngzhìpǐn.
    - His shop sells various refined products.
  - 工厂生产精制品(工廠生產精製品)，出口到世界各地(出口到世界各地)。
    - gōngchǎng shēngchǎn jīngzhìpǐn, chūkǒu dào shìjiè gèdì.
    - The factory manufactures refined products, and exports them to various parts of the world.
  - 我们购买只选用最好的精制品(我們購買只選用最好的精製品)。
    - wǒmen gòumǎi zhǐ xuǎnyòng zuì hǎo de jīngzhìpǐn.
    - We only purchase the best refined products.

- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ

### 精制品(精製品) → 制(製)
- **definition:** to make; to produce; system; to manufacture
- **pinyin:** zhì
- **components:**
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 制(制) (zhì, 'to cut out; to make') + Semantic: 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes') =
    - making clothes ->
    - manufacture/control
  - **interpretation:** The character 制(製) combines the idea of 制(制) (zhì, 'to cut out; to make'), which indicates an action, with 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes'), representing the product of that action. This suggests an act of crafting or creating clothing, which extends metaphorically to making or controlling systems and processes.
