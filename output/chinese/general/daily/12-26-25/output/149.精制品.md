## refined products
### jīngzhìpǐn
---
- **definition:** refined products
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **components:**
  - 精
    - jīng
    - refined; essence; spirit; skill
  - 制品(製品)
    - zhìpǐn
    - products; manufactured goods
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精(精) (jīng, "refined") + 制品(製品) (zhìpǐn, "products") combines to mean "refined products." The first part 精(精) (jīng, "refined") suggests something that has been processed or chosen for superior quality. The second part 制品(製品) (zhìpǐn, "products") refers to items or objects that have been manufactured or created, often through a deliberate and controlled process.
  - **interpretation:** 精(精) (jīng, "refined") and 制品(製品) (zhìpǐn, "products") together convey the idea of products that are carefully crafted or processed to achieve high quality.
- **examples:**
  - 这家工厂生产精制品(這家工廠生產精製品)，远销海外(遠銷海外)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngchǎng shēngchǎn jīngzhìpǐn, yuǎnxiāo hǎiwài.
    - This factory produces refined products, which are sold overseas.
  - 经过严格筛选(經過嚴格篩選)，这些精制品更显高端(這些精製品更顯高端)。
    - Jīngguò yángé shāixuǎn, zhèxiē jīngzhìpǐn gèng xiǎn gāoduān.
    - After rigorous selection, these refined products appear more high-end.
  - 精制品质量优良(精製品質量優良)。
    - Jīngzhìpǐn zhìliàng yōuliáng.
    - The quality of the refined products is excellent.
