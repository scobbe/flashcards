## refined products
### jīngzhìpǐn
---
- **definition:** refined products
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **components:**
  - 精制(精製)
    - jīngzhì
    - refine; finely process; elaborate; purify
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - product; goods; item; article
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精制 + 品 = finely processed/refined + product(s).
  - **interpretation:** Refers to goods that have undergone fine processing to improve purity, quality, or performance. Common in contexts such as food, chemicals, and pharmaceuticals.
  - **simplification:** 製 was simplified to 制 by dropping the 衣 component and reusing the existing character 制 to reduce strokes; 品 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 这家工厂专门生产粮油精制品(這家工廠專門生產糧油精製品)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngchǎng zhuānmén shēngchǎn liángyóu jīngzhìpǐn.
    - This factory specializes in producing refined grain and oil products.
  - 进口精制品需要严格检验(進口精製品需要嚴格檢驗)。
    - Jìnkǒu jīngzhìpǐn xūyào yángé jiǎnyàn.
    - Imported refined products require strict inspection.
  - 超市里有许多海盐精制品(超市裡有許多海鹽精製品)。
    - Chāoshì lǐ yǒu xǔduō hǎiyán jīngzhìpǐn.
    - There are many refined sea-salt products in the supermarket.
### 精制品(精製品) → 精制(精製)
- **definition:** refine; finely process; elaborate; purify
- **pinyin:** jīngzhì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精 (“refined; fine”) + 制/製 (“to make; manufacture”) = refine; fine processing.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of fineness/purity with making/processing to express producing something with high precision or further purification. Used both as a verb (to refine/process) and as an adjective (refined; highly processed).
  - **simplification:** 製 was simplified to 制 to reduce strokes and unify forms in the PRC, so 精製 → 精制; intuitively, the 衣 component in 製 was dropped and merged with 制.
- **examples:**
  - 这家工厂专门精制高纯度的盐(這家工廠專門精製高純度的鹽)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngchǎng zhuānmén jīngzhì gāo chúndù de yán.
    - This factory specializes in refining high-purity salt.
  - 这种精制糖口感很好(這種精製糖口感很好)，但营养价值较低(但營養價值較低)。
    - Zhè zhǒng jīngzhì táng kǒugǎn hěn hǎo, dàn yíngyǎng jiàzhí jiào dī.
    - This refined sugar tastes good, but its nutritional value is relatively low.
  - 通过精制工艺(透過精製工藝)，产品表面更加光滑(產品表面更加光滑)。
    - Tōngguò jīngzhì gōngyì, chǎnpǐn biǎomiàn gèngjiā guānghuá.
    - Through fine refining techniques, the product surface becomes smoother.

### 精制品(精製品) → 精制(精製) → 精(精) → 米(米)

- **etymology:**

### 精制品(精製品) → 精制(精製) → 制(製)
- **definition:** 
- **pinyin:** 
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree; wood
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
  - 未
    - wèi
    - not yet
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes; clothing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** 制: Ideogrammic compound (会意); 製: Ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound (形声).
  - **description:** 制: 木 “tree” + 刀 “knife” — to cut a tree; 木 also written as 未. 製: phonetic 制 + semantic 衣 “clothes”.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicts cutting wood with a knife, which extends to shaping, making, and exerting control. The traditional 製 adds the clothing component to indicate making garments, with 制 providing sound and related sense, and the word broadened to general manufacturing.
  - **simplification:** Simplified Chinese merged 製 into 制 by dropping the semantic 衣 and retaining the phonetic 制 for a simpler, already common form; meanings are disambiguated by context.
- **examples:**
  - 这家工厂制造高质量的手机(這家工廠製造高質量的手機)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngchǎng zhìzào gāo zhìliàng de shǒujī.
    - This factory manufactures high-quality phones.
  - 他们制定了新的评估制度(他們制定了新的評估制度)。
    - Tāmen zhìdìng le xīn de pínggū zhìdù.
    - They formulated a new evaluation system.
  - 他终于控制住了局面(他終於控制住了局面)。
    - Tā zhōngyú kòngzhì zhù le júmiàn.
    - He finally brought the situation under control.

- **pinyin:** wèi
