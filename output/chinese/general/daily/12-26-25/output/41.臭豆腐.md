## stinky tofu
### chòudòufu
---
- **definition:** stinky tofu
- **pinyin:** chòudòufu
- **components:**
  - 臭
    - chòu
    - stinky
  - 豆腐

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 臭(臭) (chòu, "stinky"), 豆腐(豆腐) (dòufu, "tofu")
