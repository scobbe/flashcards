## stinky tofu
### chòudòufu
---
- **definition:** stinky tofu
- **pinyin:** chòudòufu
- **components:**
  - 臭
    - chòu
    - stinky
  - 豆腐
    - dòufu
    - tofu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 臭(臭) (chòu, "stinky") + 豆腐(豆腐) (dòufu, "tofu"). 臭(臭) (chòu, "stinky") refers to the strong smell, and 豆腐(豆腐) (dòufu, "tofu") denotes the main ingredient.
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as 臭(臭) (chòu, "stinky") describes the distinct odor that characterizes this kind of tofu, which is appreciated for its unique flavor.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱吃臭豆腐(我愛吃臭豆腐)。
    - Wǒ ài chī chòudòufu.
    - I love eating stinky tofu.

    - Yèshì shàng rénshēng dǐngfèi, dàjiā zhēngxiāng pǐncháng chòudòufu.
    - The night market is bustling, and everyone competes to taste stinky tofu.
  - 虽然臭豆腐闻起来很臭(雖然臭豆腐聞起來很臭)，但味道却十分美味(但味道卻十分美味)。
    - Suīrán chòudòufu wénqǐlái hěn chòu, dàn wèidào què shífēn měiwèi.
    - Although stinky tofu smells very foul, its taste is extremely delicious.
