### stinky tofu
---
- **pinyin:** stinky tofu
- **components:**
  - 臭
    - chòu
    - stinky; smelly; foul; extreme
  - 豆腐
    - dòufǔ
    - tofu; bean curd
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 臭(臭) (chòu, "stinky") + 豆腐(豆腐) (dòufǔ, "tofu") — stinky tofu
  - **interpretation:** Stinky tofu is a well-known fermented snack in Chinese cuisine, characterized by its strong smell and distinctive flavor. The combination of 'stinky' and 'tofu' captures the essence of this beloved street food.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃臭豆腐(我喜歡吃臭豆腐)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī chòu dòufǔ!
    - I like to eat stinky tofu!
  - 在夜市上，臭豆腐很受欢迎(在夜市上，臭豆腐很受歡迎)。
    - Zài yèshì shàng, chòu dòufǔ hěn shòu huānyíng.
    - At the night market, stinky tofu is very popular.
  - 臭豆腐的味道独特(臭豆腐的味道獨特)。
    - Chòu dòufǔ de wèidào dútè.
    - The taste of stinky tofu is unique.
