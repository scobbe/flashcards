## stinky tofu
### chòudòufu
---
- **definition:** stinky tofu
- **pinyin:** chòudòufu
- **components:**
  - 臭
    - chòu
    - stinky
  - 豆腐
    - dòufu
    - tofu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 臭 (chòu, "stinky"), 豆腐 (dòufu, "tofu")
  - **interpretation:** 臭豆腐 (chòudòufu, "stinky tofu") refers to a popular East Asian snack made from fermented tofu, known for its strong odor. The fermentation process gives the tofu its characteristic smell, which is considered an acquired taste but enjoyed widely for its unique flavor.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)臭豆腐。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chòudòufu.
    - I like stinky tofu.
  - 她尝(嘗)了一口臭豆腐，觉(覺)得味道真特别(別)。
    - Tā cháng le yīkǒu chòudòufu, juéde wèidào zhēn tèbié.
    - She took a bite of stinky tofu, and thought its taste was really unique.
  - 小吃摊(攤)上卖(賣)臭豆腐，吸引了很多游(遊)客。
    - Xiǎochī tān shàng mài chòudòufu, xīyǐnle hěn duō yóukè.
    - The food stall selling stinky tofu attracted many tourists.
