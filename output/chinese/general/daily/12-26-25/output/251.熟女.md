## stylish mature woman
### shúnǚ
---
- **definition:** stylish mature woman
- **pinyin:** shúnǚ
- **components:**
  - 熟
    - shú
    - mature; ripe
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 熟(熟) (shú, "mature; ripe") and 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") combine to form 熟女(熟女) (shúnǚ, "stylish mature woman").
  - **interpretation:** The term 熟女(熟女) (shúnǚ) refers to a woman who is mature, both in age and demeanor, suggesting she may be sophisticated and stylish. This reflects a social recognition of the grace and confidence often attributed to women who have embraced their maturity.
- **examples:**
  - 那位熟女穿着时尚(那位熟女穿著時尚)。
    - Nà wèi shúnǚ chuānzhuó shíshàng.
    - That stylish mature woman is dressed fashionably.
  - 熟女走在街上(熟女走在街上)，吸引了许多目光(吸引了許多目光)。
    - Shúnǚ zǒu zài jiē shàng, xīyǐnle xǔduō mùguāng.
    - The stylish mature woman walked on the street, attracting many glances.
  - 熟女不仅具有成熟魅力(熟女不僅具有成熟魅力)，而且精通生活艺术(而且精通生活藝術)。
    - Shúnǚ bùjǐn jùyǒu chéngshú mèilì, érqiě jīngtōng shēnghuó yìshù.
    - A stylish mature woman not only possesses a mature charm, but also masters the art of living.
### 熟女(熟女) → 熟(熟)
- **definition:** mature; ripe
- **pinyin:** shú
- **components:**
  - 孰
    - shú
    - cooked; ripe
  - 灬
    - biāo
    - fire
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 孰(孰) (shú, "cooked; ripe") + Semantic: 灬(灬) ('fire') =

  - **interpretation:** The combination of 孰(孰) (shú, "cooked; ripe") as the phonetic element and 灬(灬) ("fire") as the semantic element illustrates the concept of something becoming mature or ripe through the process of cooking. This reflects the idea that heat (fire) transforms or completes the maturation process.

### 熟女(熟女) → 女(女)

  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity.
