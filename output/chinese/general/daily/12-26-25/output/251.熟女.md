---
## 
### stylish mature woman
---
- **pinyin:** stylish mature woman
- **components:**
  - 熟
    - shú
    - ripe; mature; familiar; cooked
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman; female
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 熟(熟) (shú, 'ripe; mature') + 女(女) (nǚ, 'woman') — mature woman
  - **interpretation:** This word refers to women who are both mature and stylish in appearance or demeanor. It emphasizes a sophisticated and confident image associated with maturity.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位时尚熟女(她是一位時尚熟女)。
    - tā shì yī wèi shíshàng shénǚ.
    - She is a stylish mature woman.
  - 熟女的魅力让人折服(熟女的魅力讓人折服)。
    - shénǚ de mèilì ràng rén zhéfú.
    - The charm of mature women is captivating.
### 熟女(熟女) → 熟(熟)
- **definition:** ripe; mature; familiar; cooked
- **pinyin:** shú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of something being fully developed or ready for consumption.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests the idea of ripeness or maturity, signifying that something has reached its optimal state, whether it be food or knowledge.
- **examples:**
  - 水果熟了(水果熟了)，可以吃了(可以吃了)！
    - shuǐguǒ shúle, kěyǐ chīle!
    - The fruit is ripe, we can eat it!
  - 我对这个问题很熟悉(我對這個問題很熟悉)，希望能帮你(希望能幫你)。
    - wǒ duì zhège wèntí hěn shúxī, xīwàng néng bāng nǐ.
    - I am very familiar with this issue, I hope I can help you.
### 熟女(熟女) → 女(女)
- **definition:** woman; female
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing, facing the right side.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a woman, emphasizing the female form with the inclusion of breasts and body posture. This makes it intuitive for signifying femininity.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位聪明的女学生。
    - tā shì yī wèi cōngmíng de nǚ xuéshēng。
    - She is a smart female student.
  - 这个女孩非常善良。
    - zhège nǚhái fēicháng shànliáng。
    - This girl is very kind.
