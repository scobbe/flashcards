## stylish mature woman
### shúnǚ
---
- **definition:** stylish mature woman
- **pinyin:** shúnǚ
- **components:**
  - 熟
    - shú
    - mature; ripe
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 熟 (shú, "mature; ripe") and 女 (nǚ, "woman") combine to form 熟女 (shúnǚ, "stylish mature woman").
  - **interpretation:** The term 熟女 (shúnǚ) refers to a woman who is mature, both in age and demeanor, suggesting she may be sophisticated and stylish. This reflects a social recognition of the grace and confidence often attributed to women who have embraced their maturity.
- **examples:**
  - 那位熟女穿着时(著時)尚。
    - Nà wèi shúnǚ chuānzhuó shíshàng.
    - That stylish mature woman is dressed fashionably.
  - 熟女走在街上，吸引了许(許)多目光。
    - Shúnǚ zǒu zài jiē shàng, xīyǐnle xǔduō mùguāng.
    - The stylish mature woman walked on the street, attracting many glances.
  - 熟女不仅(僅)具有成熟魅力，而且精通生活艺术(藝術)。
    - Shúnǚ bùjǐn jùyǒu chéngshú mèilì, érqiě jīngtōng shēnghuó yìshù.
    - A stylish mature woman not only possesses a mature charm, but also masters the art of living.
### 熟女 → 熟
- **definition:** mature; ripe
- **pinyin:** shú
- **components:**
  - 孰
    - shú
    - cooked; ripe
  - 灬
    - biāo
    - fire
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 孰 (shú, "cooked; ripe") + Semantic: 灬 ('fire') =
    - mature or ripe through cooking with fire ->
    - mature; ripe
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 孰 (shú, "cooked; ripe") as the phonetic element and 灬 ("fire") as the semantic element illustrates the concept of something becoming mature or ripe through the process of cooking. This reflects the idea that heat (fire) transforms or completes the maturation process.
- **examples:**
  - 苹(蘋)果熟了。
    - Píngguǒ shú le。
    - The apple is ripe.
  - 果园里(園裡)的梨子熟了，农(農)夫正忙着采(著採)摘。
    - Guǒyuán lǐ de lízi shú le，nóngfū zhèng mángzhe cǎizhāi。
    - The pears in the orchard are ripe, and the farmers are busy harvesting.
  - 夏天来临时(來臨時)，桃子开(開)始变(變)得熟软(軟)多汁。
    - Xiàtiān láilín shí，táozi kāishǐ biàn de shú ruǎn duō zhī。
    - When summer arrives, the peaches begin to turn ripe, soft, and juicy.
### 熟女 → 熟 → 孰
- **definition:** cooked; ripe
- **pinyin:** shú
- **components:**
  - 享
    - xiǎng
    - enjoy
  - 丸
    - wán
    - pill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𦎫 (phonetic) + 丮 (semantic) ->
    - corrupted into 享 (phonetic) + 丸 (semantic) ->
    - 孰
  - **interpretation:** The character 孰 originally combined the phonetic component 𦎫 with the semantic component 丮. Over time, these components transformed into 享 and 丸 respectively, leading to the current character form. This composition represents the character's meaning related to something processed or ripe.
### 熟女 → 熟 → 孰 → 享
- **definition:** enjoy
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an ancestral shrine
  - **interpretation:** The character 享 (xiǎng, "phonetic surrogate") originated as a pictogram representing an ancestral shrine, which is common in historical contexts where rituals and offerings were made to ancestors. This character continues to convey meanings related to offerings and enjoyment.
- **examples:**
  - 父母辛勤劳(勞)作，给(給)予子女享福。
    - Fùmǔ xīnqín láozuò ， gěiyǔ zǐnǚ xiǎng fú 。
    - Parents work hard and give their children a blissful life.
  - 我们(們)在宴会(會)中享美食。
    - Wǒmen zài yànhuì zhōng xiǎng měishí 。
    - We enjoy delicious food at the banquet.
  - 当(當)音乐响(樂響)起时(時)，大家开(開)始享受节(節)日气(氣)氛。
    - Dāng yīnyuè xiǎngqǐ shí ， dàjiā kāishǐ xiǎngshòu jiérì qìfēn 。
    - When the music starts, everyone begins to enjoy the festive atmosphere.
### 熟女 → 熟 → 孰 → 丸
- **definition:** pill
- **pinyin:** wán
- **components:**
  - 夗
    - yuàn
    - tilt
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally the same as 夗 ->
    - differentiated as 丸 ->
    - something round or that can roll
  - **interpretation:** The character 丸 originally shared its form with 夗, representing an idea of tilting or something round. This suggests an early association with roundness or rolling, reflecting its use for balls or pills today.
- **examples:**
  - 医(醫)生给(給)我一丸药(藥)。
    - Yīshēng gěi wǒ yī wán yào.
    - The doctor gave me one pill.
  - 这(這)丸药(藥)效果很好，我一天吃两(兩)次。
    - Zhè wán yào xiàoguǒ hěn hǎo, wǒ yītiān chī liǎng cì.
    - This pill is very effective; I take it twice a day.
  - 老中医(醫)常以丸治百病。
    - Lǎo zhōngyī cháng yǐ wán zhì bǎibìng.
    - Old Chinese medicine practitioners often use pills to cure various ailments.

- **definition:** tilt
- **pinyin:** yuàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an animal lying down about to die
  - **interpretation:** 夗 (yuàn, 'tilt') is a pictogram representing an animal lying down, conveying a sense of tilting or impending death.
### 熟女 → 熟 → 灬
- **definition:** fire
- **pinyin:** biāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flames or fire at the bottom of a character
  - **interpretation:** The character 灬(biāo, 'fire') visually resembles flames and is used to represent fire-related concepts, especially as a radical component in many Chinese characters.
- **examples:**
  - 灬是燃烧(燒)的象征(徵)。
    - biāo shì ránshāo de xiàngzhēng.
    - Fire is a symbol of burning.
  - 夜幕降临时(臨時)，灬映出温(溫)暖的光芒。
    - yè mù jiàng lín shí, biāo yìng chū wēnnuǎn de guāngmáng.
    - When night falls, fire radiates a warm glow.
  - 狂风(風)暴雨后(後)，灬象征(徵)重生。
    - kuáng fēng bào yǔ hòu, biāo xiàngzhēng chóngshēng.
    - After the storm, fire symbolizes rebirth.
### 熟女 → 女
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
- **examples:**
  - 那位才华横(華橫)溢的女，赢(贏)得了全场(場)注目。
    - Nà wèi cáihuá héngyì de nǚ, yíng dé le quánchǎng zhùmù.
    - That talented woman won everyone's attention.
  - 古时候‘女’常被用来象征温柔(古時候‘女’常被用來象徵溫柔)，而如今它代表独(獨)立自信。
    - Gǔshíhòu ‘nǚ’ cháng bèi yòng lái xiàngzhēng wēnróu, ér rújīn tā dàibiǎo dúlì zìxìn.
    - In ancient times, the character '女' was often used to symbolize gentleness, whereas today it represents independence and confidence.
