## character
### zì
---
- **definition:** character
- **pinyin:** zì
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 宀(宀) (mián, "roof") + 子(子) (zǐ, "child") =
    - child under roof ->
    - character/word
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 宀(宀) (mián, "roof") and 子(子) (zǐ, "child") suggests the practice of learning or knowing characters within a household, where a child is taught under a roof. This reflects the idea of education and nurturing knowledge at home.
- **examples:**
  - 我在纸上写字(我在紙上寫字)。
    - wǒ zài zhǐ shàng xiě zì.
    - I write characters on the paper.
  - 老师教我们写字(老師教我們寫字)，同学们仔细模仿(同學們仔細模仿)。
    - lǎoshī jiāo wǒmen xiě zì, tóngxuémen zǐxì mófǎng.
    - The teacher teaches us to write characters, and the students imitate carefully.
  - 古人重视字的美感(古人重視字的美感)，把字当作艺术(把字當作藝術)。
    - gǔrén zhòngshì zì de měigǎn, bǎ zì dāngzuò yìshù.
    - Ancient people valued the beauty of characters, treating them as art.
### 字(字) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(宀) (mián, 'roof') is a pictogram representing the shape of a roof, illustrating the concept of shelter or covering typically associated with roofs.
- **examples:**
  - 这个汉字包含宀(這個漢字包含宀)，表示房屋结构(表示房屋結構)。
    - Zhè ge hànzì bāohán mián, biǎoshì fángwū jiégòu.
    - This Chinese character contains the radical mián, indicating the structure of a house.
  - 汉字中有宀部(漢字中有宀部)，代表与家有关的概念(代表與家有關的概念)。
    - Hànzì zhōng yǒu mián bù, dàibiǎo yǔ jiā yǒuguān de gàiniàn.
    - Chinese characters with the mián radical represent concepts associated with home.
### 字(字) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an infant swaddled in cloth ->
    - symbolizes child
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(子) (zǐ, "child") originally depicted an infant wrapped in a cloth, illustrating the concept of a young child. This symbolic representation has expanded over time to include meanings such as "seed" or "particle," aligning with the concept of beginnings.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小子成绩优异(他的小子成績優異)。
    - Tā xiǎo zǐ chéngjì yōuyì.
    - His child has excellent grades.
  - 这颗子将发芽生长(這顆子將發芽生長)。
    - Zhè kē zǐ jiāng fāyá shēngzhǎng.
    - This seed will sprout and grow.
  - 当太阳升起时(當太陽升起時)，小子在院子里欢笑(小子在院子裡歡笑)。
    - Dāng tàiyáng shēngqǐ shí, xiǎo zǐ zài yuànzi lǐ huānxiào.
    - When the sun rises, the child laughs in the courtyard.
