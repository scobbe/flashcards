### character
---
- **pinyin:** character
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
  - 自
    - zì
    - self
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 'zi' (子) (child) suggesting something small or simple, with 'zi' (自) (self) indicating the notion of a written symbol or character.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a written form representing something fundamental, like a child's initial grasp of language.
- **examples:**
  - 这个字很简单(這個字很簡單)。
    - zhège zì hěn jiǎndān.
    - This character is very simple.
  - 请写下你的名字和字(請寫下你的名字和字)。
    - qǐng xiě xià nǐ de míngzì hé zì.
    - Please write down your name and character.
### 字(字) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a child or infant.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a small person or child, intuitively symbolizing youth and innocence.
- **examples:**
  - 这是我的孩子(這是我的孩子)。
    - Zhè shì wǒ de háizi.
    - This is my child.
  - 子女(子女)都很聪明(都很聰明)。
    - Zǐnǚ dōu hěn cōngmíng.
    - The children are all very smart.
### 字(字) → 自(自)
- **definition:** self
- **pinyin:** zì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person or character with a prominent nose.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually suggests a person looking at their own nose, representing self or oneself.
- **examples:**
  - 我觉得这是很重要的事情(我覺得這是很重要的事情)。
    - Wǒ juédé zhè shì hěn zhòngyào de shìqíng.
    - I think this is an important matter.
  - 自信能帮助你成功(自信能幫助你成功)。
    - Zìxìn néng bāngzhù nǐ chénggōng.
    - Self-confidence can help you succeed.
