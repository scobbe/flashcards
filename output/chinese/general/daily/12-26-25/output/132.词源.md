## etymology
### cíyuán
---
- **definition:** etymology
- **pinyin:** cíyuán
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; phrase
  - 源
    - yuán
    - source; origin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 词(詞) (cí, "word; phrase") and 源(源) (yuán, "source; origin") together refer to the study of the origin and historical development of words.
  - **interpretation:** A 词(詞) (cí, "word; phrase") must have a 源(源) (yuán, "source; origin"). By studying this source, one learns about the history and development of the word.
  - **simplification:** 词源 is the simplified form of 詞源.
- **examples:**
  - 学者探讨词源(學者探討詞源)，揭示语言演变(揭示語言演變)。
    - xué zhě tàn tǎo cí yuán, jiē shì yǔ yán yǎn biàn.
    - Scholars study the etymology, revealing the evolution of language.
  - 词源揭示了历史变迁(詞源揭示了歷史變遷)。
    - cí yuán jiē shì le lì shǐ biàn qiān.
    - Etymology reveals historical changes.
### 词源(詞源) → 词(詞)
- **definition:** word; phrase
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 言(言) (yán, "speech") + 司(司) (sī, phonetic) =
    - related to speech ->
    - word
  - **interpretation:** The character uses 言(言) (yán, "speech") to indicate its connection with spoken language. 司(司) (sī) is used phonetically to approximate the pronunciation of the character, which aligns with its meaning related to words and phrases.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 詞(詞) by replacing 言(言) (yán, "speech") with 讠(讠) (yán), a cursive form of the speech radical.
- **examples:**
  - 这个词很美(這個詞很美)。
    - Zhège cí hěn měi.

  - 古文中的每个词背后都有故事(古文中的每個詞背後都有故事)。
    - Gǔwén zhōng de měi gè cí bèihòu dōu yǒu gùshì.

### 词源(詞源) → 词(詞) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 舌(舌) (shé, "tongue") + horizontal stroke =
    - movement of tongue ->
    - speech
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") originates from the depiction of a tongue 舌(舌) with an added stroke to represent its movement, signifying the action of speaking. This visual representation highlights the tongue's role in speech, which is central to communication.
- **examples:**
  - 言简意赅可以表达深刻思想(言簡意賅可以表達深刻思想)。
    - yán jiǎn yì gāi kěyǐ biǎo dá shēn kè sī xiǎng。
    - Concise speech can express profound thoughts.
  - 他的言论总能激发思考(他的言論總能激發思考)。
    - tā de yán lùn zǒng néng jī fā sī kǎo。
    - His speech always inspires reflection.
  - 老师常说(老師常說)，言出必行(言出必行)。
    - lǎo shī cháng shuō, yán chū bì xíng。
    - The teacher often says that one should act on one's words.
### 词源(詞源) → 词(詞) → 司(司)

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic component 䇂(䇂) (sī) =
    - indicates sound and meaning related to governing or overseeing ->
    - "to manage"
  - **interpretation:** 司(司) (sī, "to manage") combines 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth"), indicating a verbal aspect, with 䇂(䇂) (sī), suggesting the action of overseeing or managing. The mouth suggests issuing commands or making reports as part of management duties.

- **examples:**

### 词源(詞源) → 源(源)
- **definition:** source; origin
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 原
    - yuán
    - original
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 原(原) (yuán, "original") =
    - water original ->
    - source
  - **interpretation:** 源(源) (yuán, "source") combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water"), indicating the element of water, with 原(原) (yuán, "original") as the phonetic component. Together, it refers to the origin or source of a body of water, symbolizing where it begins.
- **examples:**

    - Héliú de yuán zài gāoshān zhī diān.

    - Zhìhuì de zēngzhǎng yǒulài yú qínfèn, yuányú chízhī yǐ héng.
    - The growth of wisdom depends on diligence, originating from perseverance.
  - 生机盎然的创意源于自然(生機盎然的創意源於自然)。
