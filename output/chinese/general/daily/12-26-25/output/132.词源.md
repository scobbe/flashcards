## etymology
### cíyuán
---
- **traditional:** 詞源
- **definition:** etymology
- **pinyin:** cíyuán
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; phrase; expression
  - 源
    - yuán
    - origin; source; fountainhead
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 詞(词) (cí, "word") refers to words or expressions, while 源 (yuán, "origin") indicates the source or origin of something.
  - **interpretation:** 詞(词)源 (cíyuán, "etymology") combines 詞(词) (cí, "word") with 源 (yuán, "origin") to signify the study or origin of words. It reflects an investigation into how words have developed and where they come from, aligning with the Western concept of etymology.
  - **simplification:** Converted to simplified characters: 詞 to 词
- **examples:**
  - 词(詞)源研究十分重要。
    - Cíyuán yánjiū shífēn zhòngyào.
    - The study of etymology is very important.
  - 我从词(從詞)源中了解单词(單詞)的历(歷)史。
    - Wǒ cóng cíyuán zhōng liǎojiě dāncí de lìshǐ.
    - I learn about the history of words from their etymology.
  - 他对词(對詞)源感兴(興)趣，希望找到更多例子。
    - Tā duì cíyuán gǎn xìngqù, xīwàng zhǎodào gèng duō lìzi.
    - He is interested in etymology and hopes to find more examples.
### 词(詞)源( ) → 词(詞)
- **traditional:** 詞
- **definition:** word; phrase; expression
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 讠(言)
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司
    - sī
    - manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 司 (sī, "manage") =
    - word or term relating to speech ->
    - simplified by replacing 言 (yán, "speech") with 讠 (yán, "speech") ->
    - final form 词(詞)
  - **interpretation:** The character 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") combines the speech radical 言 (yán, "speech") with 司 (sī, "manage") to indicate a managed or structured form of speech, i.e., a word or term. The simplified form retains this concept, making writing faster with 讠 (yán, "speech") being a cursive form of the speech radical.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by changing the full radical 言 (yán, "speech") to its cursive form 讠 (yán, "speech"), facilitating quicker writing while retaining the original meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这个词(這個詞)意义丰(義豐)富。
    - Zhè ge cí yìyì fēngfù.
    - This word has a rich meaning.
  - 诗(詩)歌中的每个词(個詞)都富有韵(韻)律，展现(現)了作者的才华(華)。
    - Shīgē zhōng de měi ge cí dōu fùyǒu yùnlǜ, zhǎnxiànle zuòzhě de cáihuá.
    - Every word in the poem is full of rhythm, revealing the author's talent.

- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a curved line with a dot, symbolizing speech or words
  - **interpretation:** 讠(訁) (yán, "speech") is the simplified radical for words or speech. As a pictogram, it represents the essential concept of communication.
- **examples:**

    - Hànzì zhōng de yán gòujiàn, cháng chūxiàn zài yǒuguān huàyǔ de zì zhōng.
    - The 讠 component in Chinese characters often appears in characters related to speech.
  - 古人重视讠(視言)之力量，认为(認為)言传(傳)身教胜过万(勝過萬)言。
    - Gǔrén zhòngshì yán zhī lìliàng, rènwéi yán chuán shēnjiào shèngguò wànyán.
    - Ancient people valued the power of speech, believing that teaching by example was superior to a thousand words.
  - 含讠(言)字形的汉(漢)字，通常与(與)言语(語)表达(達)密切相关(關)。
    - Hán yán zìxíng de hànzì, tōngcháng yǔ yányǔ biǎodá mìqiè xiāngguān.
    - Chinese characters with the 讠 radical are usually closely associated with speech expression.

### 词(詞)源( ) → 词(詞) → 司 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开(張開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。

    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.
### 词(詞)源( ) → 源
- **definition:** origin; source; fountainhead
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 原
    - yuán
    - origin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + Phonetic: 原 (yuán, "origin") =
    - water source or origin ->
    - origin; source; fountainhead
  - **interpretation:** The character 源(yuán) uses 氵 to indicate its association with water, symbolizing the beginning or source of a river. The phonetic component 原 also means 'origin,' reinforcing the meaning of a point where something begins.
- **examples:**
  - 知识(識)的积(積)累是灵(靈)感的源，推动(動)了创(創)新的发(發)展。

    - The accumulation of knowledge is the source of inspiration, which propels the development of innovation.
  - 他的成功源于(於)不懈努力。
    - Tā de chénggōng yuányú bùxiè nǔlì.
    - His success originates from relentless effort.

- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**

  - 厂

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 泉 (quán, "spring") + 厂 (chǎng, "cliff") =

  - **interpretation:** The character 原(yuán) illustrates the concept of an origin by depicting a source of water, a spring 泉(quán), emerging from a cliff 厂(chǎng). ### 词(詞)源( ) → 源 → 原 → 泉 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**

    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.

  - 夏天很热(熱)，喝水能解暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺，沿岸绿树(綠樹)成荫(蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.
### 词(詞)源( ) → 源 → 原 → 厂(廠)
- **traditional:** 廠
- **definition:** cliff
- **pinyin:** chǎng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cliff or overhang
  - **interpretation:** The character 厂(hǎn, 'cliff') originally depicts a cliff or overhang, representing the natural landscape feature. This simple depiction efficiently conveys its meaning as a pictograph, illustrating the sloping edge and steepness of a cliff.
  - **simplification:** 厂(hǎn, 'factory') is the simplified form of 廠, where the character was reduced to its more basic pictogram form for modern usage, representing both 'cliff' and 'factory'.
- **examples:**
  - 他站在高厂边(邊)，凝视远处(視遠處)海浪。
    - Tā zhàn zài gāo hǎn biān, níng shì yuǎn chù hǎi làng.
    - He stands at the edge of the high cliff, gazing at the distant waves.
  - 我们(們)攀登陡厂壁，体验(體驗)大自然壮观(壯觀)。
    - Wǒmen pān dēng dǒu hǎn bì, tǐ yàn dà zì rán zhuàng guān.
    - We climbed the steep cliff face, experiencing the grandeur of nature.
