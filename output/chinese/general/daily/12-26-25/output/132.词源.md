### etymology
---
- **pinyin:** etymology
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; phrase; vocabulary
  - 源
    - yuán
    - source; origin; spring
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 词(詞) (cí, "word") + 源(yuán, "source") — word source
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to the study of the origin and historical development of words. The combination makes sense as it literally indicates the source or origin of words.
- **examples:**
  - 词源是语言学的重要部分(詞源是語言學的重要部分)。
    - Cíyuán shì yǔyánxué de zhòngyào bùfèn.
    - Etymology is an important part of linguistics.
  - 我对汉字的词源很感兴趣(我對漢字的詞源很感興趣)。
    - Wǒ duì hànzì de cíyuán hěn gǎn xìngqù.
    - I am very interested in the etymology of Chinese characters.
