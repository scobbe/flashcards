### still; yet
---
- **pinyin:** still; yet
- **components:**
  - 还(還)
    - hái
    - still; yet; also; rather
  - 是
    - shì
    - is; to be
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 还(還) (hái, "still; yet") + 是 (shì, "is") — still is
  - **interpretation:** This phrase suggests a continuation or acknowledgment of a situation. Combining both characters indicates that something remains true or unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 现在还是很早(現在還是很早)。
    - xiànzài háishì hěn zǎo.
    - It is still very early now.
  - 你还是喜欢这个(你還是喜歡這個)。
    - nǐ háishì xǐhuān zhège.
    - You still like this.
