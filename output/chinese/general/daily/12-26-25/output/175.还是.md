## still; yet
### háishi
---
- **definition:** still; yet
- **pinyin:** háishi
- **components:**
  - 还(還)
    - hái
    - still; also
  - 是
    - shì
    - is; to be
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 还(還) (hái, "still; also") combined with 是(是) (shì, "is; to be") results in the understanding of "still; yet."
  - **interpretation:** In this context, 还(還) (hái, "still") implies continuation or persistence, whereas 是(是) (shì, "to be") provides the state of being. Together, they convey the sense of something being ongoing or unchanged despite circumstances.
  - **simplification:** 还(還) retains the simplified form of the originally traditional character.
- **examples:**
  - 他还是没有改变主意(他還是沒有改變主意)。
    - Tā háishi méiyǒu gǎibiàn zhǔyì.
    - He still hasn't changed his mind.
  - 虽然遇到困难还是要努力(雖然遇到困難還是要努力)，追逐梦想(追逐夢想)。
    - Suīrán yùdào kùnnán háishi yào nǔlì, zhuīzhú mèngxiǎng.
    - Even when facing difficulties, you still must work hard to pursue your dreams.
  - 无论时光如何流逝(無論時光如何流逝)，他还是守住初心(他還是守住初心)。
    - Wúlùn shíguāng rúhé liúshì, tā háishi shǒuzhù chūxīn.
    - No matter how time passes, he still remains true to his original aspirations.
