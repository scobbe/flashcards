## education
### jiàoyù
---
- **definition:** education
- **pinyin:** jiàoyù
- **components:**
  - 教
    - jiào
    - to teach; religion
  - 育
    - yù
    - to nurture; to raise; education
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 教(教) (jiào, "to teach") + 育(育) (yù, "to nurture; to raise") combine to form 教育(教育) (jiàoyù, "education"), capturing the essence of teaching and nurturing as integral parts of the educational process.
  - **interpretation:** The word emphasizes both teaching and nurturing as complementary aspects of education, highlighting the idea of learning as a holistic development process.
- **examples:**
  - 良好的教育奠定成功基石(良好的教育奠定成功基石)。
    - Liánghǎo de jiàoyù diàndìng chénggōng jīshí.
    - Good education lays the foundation for success.
  - 现代教育促进创新(現代教育促進創新)，课堂讨论激发思考(課堂討論激發思考)。
    - Xiàndài jiàoyù cùjìn chuàngxīn, kètáng tǎolùn jīfā sīkǎo.
    - Modern education fosters innovation, and classroom discussions stimulate thinking.
### 教育(教育) → 教(教)
- **definition:** to teach; religion
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **components:**
  - 爻
    - yáo
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
  - 攵
    - pū
    - teaching cane
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 爻(爻) (yáo) + semantic 子(子) (zǐ, "child") + semantic 攵(攵) (pū, "teaching cane") ->
    - teaching a child
  - **interpretation:** The character 教(教) (jiào, "teaching") combines 爻(爻) (yáo) as a phonetic component, indicating the pronunciation, with 子(子) (zǐ, "child") and 攵(攵) (pū, "teaching cane") to depict the act of instructing or imparting knowledge to a child, emphasized by the authoritative stick.
- **examples:**
  - 老师教学生数学(老師教學生數學)。
    - Lǎoshī jiào xuéshēng shùxué.
    - The teacher teaches students mathematics.
  - 他研究佛教经典(他研究佛教經典)，追寻心灵智慧(追尋心靈智慧)。
    - Tā yánjiū fójiào jīngdiǎn, zhuīxún xīnlíng zhìhuì.
    - He studies Buddhist scriptures and pursues spiritual wisdom.
### 教育(教育) → 教(教) → 爻(爻)
- **pinyin:** yáo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 乂 (crossing marks) + 乂 (crossing marks) =
    - two stacked ‘X’ marks ->
    - representations for divination or calculation
  - **interpretation:** The character 爻(yáo) represents two intersecting marks, symbolizing crossing or interaction, which is associated with divinatory practices or calculations. This visual representation aligns with symbolic uses in traditional practices such as yarrow stalk divinations.
- **examples:**
  - 卦象中的爻代表着变化(卦象中的爻代表著變化)。
    - Guàxiàng zhōng de yáo dàibiǎo zhe biànhuà.
    - The yáo in the hexagram symbolizes change.
  - 《易经》中六爻构成完整的卦(《易經》中六爻構成完整的卦)。
    - Yìjīng zhōng liù yáo gòuchéng wánzhěng de guà.
    - The six yáo in the Yijing form a complete hexagram.
  - 古人用爻解释天地变化(古人用爻解釋天地變化)，显示阴阳原理(顯示陰陽原理)。
    - Gǔrén yòng yáo jiěshì tiāndì biànhuà, xiǎnshì yīnyáng yuánlǐ.
    - Ancient people used the yáo to interpret the changes in heaven and earth, revealing the principles of yin and yang.
### 教育(教育) → 教(教) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an infant swaddled in cloth ->
    - symbolizes child
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(子) (zǐ, "child") originally depicted an infant wrapped in a cloth, illustrating the concept of a young child. This symbolic representation has expanded over time to include meanings such as "seed" or "particle," aligning with the concept of beginnings.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小子成绩优异(他的小子成績優異)。
    - Tā xiǎo zǐ chéngjì yōuyì.
    - His child has excellent grades.
  - 这颗子将发芽生长(這顆子將發芽生長)。
    - Zhè kē zǐ jiāng fāyá shēngzhǎng.
    - This seed will sprout and grow.
  - 当太阳升起时(當太陽升起時)，小子在院子里欢笑(小子在院子裡歡笑)。
    - Dāng tàiyáng shēngqǐ shí, xiǎo zǐ zài yuànzi lǐ huānxiào.
    - When the sun rises, the child laughs in the courtyard.
### 教育(教育) → 教(教) → 攵(攵)
- **definition:** teaching cane
- **pinyin:** pū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand holding a stick or cane, used for teaching or correction
  - **interpretation:** The character 攵(攵) (pū, "teaching cane") visually represents a hand holding a stick, symbolizing the act of teaching, disciplining, or guiding. This aligns with its association as a component in other characters related to discipline or action driven by instruction.
- **examples:**
  - 古时候老师拿着攵(古時候老師拿著攵)，训诫学生(訓誡學生)。
    - Gǔ shíhòu lǎoshī ná zhe pū, xùn jiè xuéshēng.
    - In ancient times, teachers used the teaching cane to admonish students.
  - 他用攵管束顽皮学生(他用攵管束頑皮學生)。
    - Tā yòng pū guǎnshù wánpí xuéshēng.
    - He disciplines naughty students with the teaching cane.
### 教育(教育) → 育(育)
- **definition:** to nurture; to raise; education
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally 毓(毓) (yù, "give birth") ->
    - depicting 母(母) (mǔ, "woman") + 子(子) (zǐ, "child") ->
    - evolved into more stylized and geometric form with 毓(毓) elements forming 每(每) + 㐬(㐬) ->
    - final form 育(育)
  - **interpretation:** The character 育(育) evolved from a pictogram illustrating the concept of birth and nurturing, symbolizing a woman and child. It shows how characters representing nurturing have evolved from their literal depictions to more abstract forms over time.
  - **simplification:** None, as 育(育) is the same in both traditional and simplified Chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 学校致力于全面育人(學校致力於全面育人)。
    - Xuéxiào zhìlì yú quánmiàn yùrén.
    - The school is committed to holistic education.
  - 家长要用心育儿(家長要用心育兒)。
    - Jiāzhǎng yào yòngxīn yù ér.
    - Parents should nurture their children wholeheartedly.
  - 他热心传道(他熱心傳道)，用爱心育人(用愛心育人)。
    - Tā rèxīn chuándào, yòng àixīn yùrén.
    - He passionately preaches and nurtures people with love.
