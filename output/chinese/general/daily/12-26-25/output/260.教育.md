## 教育(教育)
---
## 
### education
---
- **pinyin:** education
- **components:**
  - 教
    - jiāo
    - teach; instruct; educate
  - 育
    - yù
    - cultivate; raise; nurture
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 教(教) (jiāo, 'teach') + 育(育) (yù, 'cultivate') — teaching and cultivating
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as 教 (jiāo, 'teach') emphasizes the act of imparting knowledge, while 育 (yù, 'cultivate') focuses on the growth and development of individuals, embodying the overall process of education.
- **examples:**
  - 教育是非常重要的。(教育是非常重要的。)
    - jiàoyù shì fēicháng zhòngyào de.
    - Education is very important.
  - 好的教育能够改变一个人的未来。(好的教育能夠改變一個人的未來。)
    - hǎo de jiàoyù nénggòu gǎibiàn yīgè rén de wèilái.
    - A good education can change a person's future.
### 教育(教育) → 教(教)
- **definition:** teach; instruct; educate
- **pinyin:** jiāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is a combination of the semantic element '教' that relates to teaching or doctrine and a phonetic element that suggests pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of teaching is clearly represented, as it's visually linked to both the act of imparting knowledge and a methodical structure around it.
- **examples:**
  - 他在教授英语(他在教授英語)。
    - tā zài jiàoshòu yīngyǔ.
    - He is teaching English.
  - 这本书教我很多(這本書教我很多)。
    - zhè běn shū jiāo wǒ hěnduō.
    - This book teaches me a lot.
### 教育(教育) → 育(育)
- **definition:** cultivate; raise; nurture
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat
  - 上
    - shàng
    - above
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component '肉' (ròu, 'meat') indicating nurturing or nourishment with the phonetic component '上' (shàng, 'above') — this suggests raising something up by providing nourishment.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the process of nurturing or cultivating, as one raises and supports growth through provision. This makes intuitive sense since nurturing often involves caring for something's development.
- **examples:**
  - 我在家里育儿(我在家裡育兒)。
    - Wǒ zài jiālǐ yù'ér.
    - I raise children at home.
  - 老师育人(老師育人)。
    - Lǎoshī yùrén.
    - Teachers nurture students.
  - 希望这个项目能育出更多人才(希望這個項目能育出更多人才)。
    - Xīwàng zhège xiàngmù néng yù chū gèng duō réncái.
    - I hope this project can cultivate more talents.
### 教育(教育) → 育(育) → 肉(肉)
- **definition:** meat
- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the ribs of an animal's torso or a slice of meat.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents meat, showing either the ribs of an animal or a singular piece of meat, making it intuitively associated with its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃肉(我喜歡吃肉)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī ròu.
    - I like to eat meat.
  - 这个菜里面有很多肉(這個菜裡面有很多肉)。
    - Zhège cài lǐmiàn yǒu hěnduō ròu.
    - This dish has a lot of meat in it.
  - 他每天运动，身体更健康(他每天運動，身體更健康)。
    - Tā měitiān yùndòng, shēntǐ gèng jiànkāng.
    - He exercises every day, and his body is healthier.
### 教育(教育) → 育(育) → 上(上)
- **definition:** above
- **pinyin:** shàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of 'up' or 'above'.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of elevation or position above something else.
- **examples:**
  - 我想上去(我想上去)，看风景(看風景)！
    - Wǒ xiǎng shàngqù, kàn fēngjǐng！
    - I want to go up and see the scenery!
  - 桌子上有书(桌子上有書)。
    - Zhuōzi shàng yǒu shū.
    - There are books on the table.
  - 他跑上山(他跑上山)。
    - Tā pǎo shàng shān.
    - He runs up the mountain.
