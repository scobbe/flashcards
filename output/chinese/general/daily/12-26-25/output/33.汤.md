## soup
### tāng
---
- **definition:** soup
- **pinyin:** tāng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - spread
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 昜(昜) (yáng, "spread") =
    - liquid related to heat ->
    - soup
  - **interpretation:** The character 湯(湯) (tāng, "soup") combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") to represent liquid and 昜(昜) (yáng, "spread") to evoke the idea of heat and dispersion, connecting to the steaming nature of soup.
  - **simplification:** 湯(湯) was simplified to 汤(湯) by replacing 昜(昜) with simplified form 𠃓 to streamline the writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝汤(我喜歡喝湯)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē tāng.
    - I like to drink soup.
  - 妈妈做了鸡汤(媽媽做了雞湯)，我喝得很开心(我喝得很開心)。
    - Māmā zuòle jītāng, wǒ hē de hěn kāixīn.
    - Mom made chicken soup, and I drank it happily.
  - 冬天里喝热汤(冬天裡喝熱湯)，感觉全身暖和(感覺全身暖和)。
    - Dōngtiān lǐ hē rè tāng, gǎnjué quánshēn nuǎnhuo.
    - In winter, drinking hot soup makes you feel warm all over.
### 汤(湯) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** radical
  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the simplified radical form of 水, often used to signify water-related meanings when combined with other components in phono-semantic compounds.
- **examples:**
  - 请喝水(請喝水)。
    - Qǐng hē shuǐ.
    - Please drink water.
  - 我们需要水(我們需要水)，保持身体的平衡(保持身體的平衡)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào shuǐ, bǎochí shēntǐ de pínghéng.
    - We need water to maintain the balance of our bodies.
  - 水润万物(水潤萬物)，孕育生命(孕育生命)。
    - Shuǐ rùn wànwù, yùnyù shēngmìng.
    - Water nourishes all things and nurtures life.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜)
- **definition:** spread
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - branch
  - 彡
    - shān
    - rays
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日(日) (rì, "sun") + 丂(丂) + 彡(彡) =
    - rays of the sun over a tree branch ->
    - spread
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts the sun's rays spreading over a branch, symbolizing the idea of spreading or disseminating like sunlight.
- **examples:**
  - 她的笑容在脸上昜开(她的笑容在臉上昜開)。
    - Tā de xiàoróng zài liǎn shàng yáng kāi.
    - Her smile spread across her face.
  - 早晨的雾气昜开(早晨的霧氣昜開)，整个村庄变得明朗(整個村莊變得明朗)。
    - Zǎochén de wùqì yáng kāi, zhěnggè cūnzhuāng biàn dé mínglǎng.
    - The morning fog spread, and the whole village became clear.
  - 花香随风昜散(花香隨風昜散)，心情也随之舒畅(心情也隨之舒暢)。
    - Huāxiāng suí fēng yáng sàn, xīnqíng yě suí zhī shūchàng.
    - The scent of flowers spread with the wind, and the mood became relaxed.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 日(日)
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the sun ->
    - dot/line added for distinction from similar characters
  - **interpretation:** The character 日(日) (rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun, with a central line added to differentiate it from other similar-looking characters. This design closely resembles ancient symbols for the sun in various cultures.
- **examples:**
  - 日出东方(日出東方)。
    - Rì chū dōngfāng.
    - The sun rises in the east.
  - 日光灿烂(日光燦爛)，心情愉快(心情愉快)。
    - Rìguāng cànlàn, xīnqíng yúkuài.
    - The sunlight is brilliant, and the mood is cheerful.
  - 每个日子都有新希望(每個日子都有新希望)。
    - Měi gè rìzi dōu yǒu xīn xīwàng.
    - Every day holds new hope.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 丂(丂)
- **definition:** branch
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Conflation of several characters
  - **description:**
    - 丂(丂) (kǎo, "branch") may combine elements from wind instrument 竽 and exhaled air 吁 ->
    - depiction of air flow or sigh
  - **interpretation:** The character 丂(kǎo, "branch") is likely a fusion of earlier forms related to sound or air, capturing notions linked to exhaling or air movement. Its components suggest both physical breath and sound-related elements.
- **examples:**
  - 大树上的丂很细(大樹上的丂很細)。
    - Dà shù shàng de kǎo hěn xì.
    - The branch on the big tree was very thin.
  - 园中老树分出许多丂(園中老樹分出許多丂)，相互交错(相互交錯)。
    - Yuán zhōng lǎo shù fēn chū xǔ duō kǎo, xiāng hù jiāo cuò.
    - In the garden, the old trees branched out many limbs that interwove with each other.
  - 宋代诗中用丂寓意连绵不断(宋代詩中用丂寓意連綿不斷)。
    - Sòng dài shī zhōng yòng kǎo yù yì lián mián bù duàn.
    - In Song Dynasty poetry, the branch was used as a metaphor for unbroken continuity.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 彡(彡)
- **definition:** rays
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts light or rays; like shimmering lines
  - **interpretation:** The character 彡(彡) (shān, "rays") visually represents the appearance of rays of light or the shimmering effect of something radiant.
- **examples:**
  - 阳光洒满大地(陽光灑滿大地)，彡般的光辉映出温暖(彡般的光輝映出溫暖)。
    - Yángguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì, shān bān de guānghuī yìngchū wēnnuǎn.
    - The sunlight fills the earth, and rays-like brilliance reveals its warmth.
  - 海边的波光闪烁(海邊的波光閃爍)，如彡释出的光芒般迷人(如彡釋出的光芒般迷人)。
    - Hǎibiān de bōguāng shǎnshuò, rú shān shìchū de guāngmáng bān mírén.
    - The sparkling waves on the seaside resemble rays emanating radiant light, enchanting to behold.
  - 清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光(清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光)。
    - Qīngchén de lùzhū sì shān shèchū de xīguāng.
    - In the early morning, dewdrops seem to emit rays of dawn light.
