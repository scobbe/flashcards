---
## 
### soup
---
- **pinyin:** soup
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - to shine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 氵 (water) + phonetic 昜, reflecting the idea of a liquid dish made from boiling.
  - **interpretation:** The character connects water with the process of cooking, intuitively conveying the concept of soup as a liquid food.
  - **simplification:** 湯 simplifies to 汤 by reducing strokes (the component 昜 changes to 𠃓).
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝汤(我喜歡喝湯)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān hē tāng.
    - I like to drink soup.
  - 这汤很好喝(這湯很好喝)。
    - zhè tāng hěn hǎo hē.
    - This soup is very good to drink.
### 汤(湯) → 氵(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents water in its flowing form, emphasizing its liquid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜)
- **definition:** to shine
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - a component or mark
  - 彡
    - shān
    - bristles or rays
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Comprised of 日 (rì, 'sun') + 丂 + 彡, it represents the rays of the sun over a tree branch.
  - **interpretation:** The image of sunlight shining through branches intuitively conveys the meaning of shining or brightness.
- **examples:**
  - 昜在树枝上照耀。(昜在樹枝上照耀。)
    - yáng zài shùzhī shàng zhàoyào.
    - The rays of the sun shine on the branches.
  - 这幅画中的昜色很美。(這幅畫中的昜色很美。)
    - zhè fú huà zhōng de yángsè hěn měi.
    - The brightness in this painting is beautiful.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 日(日)
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a circular shape representing the sun.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the sun with its round shape, intuitively connecting it to light and warmth.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的天气不错，太阳很大(今天的天氣不錯，太陽很大)。
    - Jīntiān de tiānqì bùcuò, tàiyang hěn dà.
    - The weather today is nice, the sun is bright.
  - 我喜欢在太阳下散步(我喜歡在太陽下散步)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài tàiyang xià sànbù.
    - I like to walk in the sun.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 丂(丂)
- **definition:** a component or mark
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of a small object or a mark.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a simple mark, making it intuitive as a symbol for something minimal and basic.
- **examples:**
  - 这个符号(這個符號)是用来表示某个标记(某個標記)。
    - zhè ge fúhào shì yòng lái biǎoshì mǒu ge biāojì.
    - This symbol is used to indicate a certain mark.
  - 在字典里(在字典裡)，丂不是常用的字符(丂不是常用的字符)。
    - zài zìdiǎn lǐ, kǎo bù shì cháng yòng de zìfú.
    - In the dictionary, 丂 is not a commonly used character.
### 汤(湯) → 昜(昜) → 彡(彡)
- **definition:** bristles or rays
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a series of bristles or rays radiating outward.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents bristles or rays, suggesting a visual of things extending or radiating from a central point.
- **examples:**
