## Stephen
### Sīdìfēn
---
- **definition:** Stephen
- **pinyin:** Sīdìfēn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phonetic transliteration
  - **description:** Phonetic rendering: sī + dì + fēn ≈ “Stephen.”
  - **interpretation:** Used as the standard Mandarin transliteration of the English given name Stephen. Chosen for sound rather than semantic content.
- **examples:**
  - 斯蒂芬今天来了(斯蒂芬今天來了)。
    - Sīdìfēn jīntiān lái le.
    - Stephen came today.
  - 我认识一位叫斯蒂芬的老师(我認識一位叫斯蒂芬的老師)。
    - Wǒ rènshi yí wèi jiào Sīdìfēn de lǎoshī.
    - I know a teacher named Stephen.
  - 斯蒂芬·霍金是著名的物理学家(斯蒂芬·霍金是著名的物理學家)。
    - Sīdìfēn Huòjīn shì zhùmíng de wùlǐxuéjiā.
    - Stephen Hawking is a famous physicist.
