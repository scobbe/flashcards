## leaves
### shùyè
---
- **definition:** leaves
- **pinyin:** shùyè
- **components:**
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
  - 叶(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 树(樹) (shù, "tree") + 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf")
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 树(樹) (shù, "tree") and 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") to form 树叶(樹葉) (shùyè, "leaves") makes sense as leaves are directly associated with trees. Trees produce leaves, so this word describes the plant part that grows on branches.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹) is the simplified form of 樹, and 叶(葉) is the simplified form of 葉.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天到来时(秋天到來時)，树叶变得斑斕(樹葉變得斑斕)。
    - Qiūtiān dàolái shí, shùyè biàn de bānlán.
    - When autumn arrives, the leaves turn colorful.
  - 微风吹过(微風吹過)，树叶轻轻落下(樹葉輕輕落下)。
    - Wēifēng chuīguò, shùyè qīngqīng luòxià.
    - When the gentle wind blows, the leaves fall softly.
  - 风起时(風起時)，树叶在空中跳舞(樹葉在空中跳舞)。
    - Fēngqǐ shí, shùyè zài kōngzhōng tiàowǔ.
    - When the wind rises, the leaves dance in the air.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 尌
    - shù
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 木(木) (mù, "tree") + phonetic: 尌(尌) (shù) =
    - tree-like object described by 尌 ->
    - tree
  - **interpretation:** The character 树(樹) (shù, "tree") combines 木(木) (mù, "tree") for its meaning, and 尌(尌) (shù) provides the pronunciation. Together, these components create a character that is pronounced like 尌(尌) and relates to trees.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹): The simplified form retains 木(木) ("tree") while modifying 尌 to simplify strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 那棵树非常茂盛(那棵樹非常茂盛)。
    - Nà kē shù fēicháng màoshèng.
    - That tree is very lush.
  - 微风拂过树梢(微風拂過樹梢)，阳光洒满枝头(陽光灑滿枝頭)。
    - Wēifēng fúguò shùshāo, yángguāng sǎ mǎn zhītóu.
    - A gentle breeze brushes the treetops, while sunlight fills the branches.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这把椅子的主要材料是木(這把椅子的主要材料是木)。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木(工匠精心雕刻木)，制成精美家具(制成精美家具)。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 尌(尌)
- **definition:** phonetic component
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 壴
    - shù
    - drum
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "tree") + 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") =
    - to plant a tree; 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") was added later ->
    - 壴(壴) (shù, "drum") + 寸(寸) as phonetic
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 木(木) (mù, "tree") combined with 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") represented the action of planting a tree. Over time, 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") was incorporated, evolving into 壴(壴) (shù, "drum"), fusing the representations and leading to some misinterpretation as 壴 plus 寸.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 尌(尌) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布每一寸都是精心制作(這塊布每一寸都是精心製作)。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请用卷尺量一寸(請用卷尺量一寸)，每一寸数据都准确(每一寸數據都準確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.

### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉)
- **definition:** leaf
- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 枼(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 枼 (yè, "leaf") =
    - vegetation-like and phonetic element ->
    - leaf
  - **interpretation:** In 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf"), the 艹 (cǎo, "grass") component suggests vegetation, while 枼 (yè, "leaf") provides the sound. This combination aptly conveys the meaning of a leaf, aligning both meaning and pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified form 叶(葉), the component 艹 (cǎo, "grass") is removed, and 枼 (yè, "leaf") alone suggests both the phonetic and semantic aspects, reducing the complexity by focusing solely on the phonetic element.
- **examples:**
  - 秋风起时(秋風起時)，落叶纷纷飘落(落葉紛紛飄落)。
    - Qiū fēng qǐ shí, luò yè fēn fēn piāo luò.
    - When the autumn wind rises, the leaves fall one after another.
  - 树上的叶很美(樹上的葉很美)。
    - Shù shàng de yè hěn měi.
    - The leaves on the tree are beautiful.
  - 他轻轻地拿起一片叶(他輕輕地拿起一片葉)，仔细端详(仔細端詳)。
    - Tā qīng qīng de ná qǐ yī piàn yè, zǐ xì duān xiáng.
    - He gently picked up a leaf and examined it carefully.
