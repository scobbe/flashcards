## leaves
### shùyè
---
- **definition:** leaves
- **pinyin:** shùyè
- **components:**
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
  - 叶(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 树(樹) (shù, "tree") + 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf")
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 树(樹) (shù, "tree") and 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") to form 树叶(樹葉) (shùyè, "leaves") makes sense as leaves are directly associated with trees. Trees produce leaves, so this word describes the plant part that grows on branches.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹) is the simplified form of 樹, and 叶(葉) is the simplified form of 葉.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天到来时(秋天到來時)，树叶变得斑斕(樹葉變得斑斕)。
    - Qiūtiān dàolái shí, shùyè biàn de bānlán.
    - When autumn arrives, the leaves turn colorful.
  - 微风吹过(微風吹過)，树叶轻轻落下(樹葉輕輕落下)。
    - Wēifēng chuīguò, shùyè qīngqīng luòxià.
    - When the gentle wind blows, the leaves fall softly.
  - 风起时(風起時)，树叶在空中跳舞(樹葉在空中跳舞)。
    - Fēngqǐ shí, shùyè zài kōngzhōng tiàowǔ.
    - When the wind rises, the leaves dance in the air.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 尌
    - shù
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 木(木) (mù, "tree") + phonetic: 尌(尌) (shù) =
    - tree-like object described by 尌 ->
    - tree
  - **interpretation:** The character 树(樹) (shù, "tree") combines 木(木) (mù, "tree") for its meaning, and 尌(尌) (shù) provides the pronunciation. Together, these components create a character that is pronounced like 尌(尌) and relates to trees.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹): The simplified form retains 木(木) ("tree") while modifying 尌 to simplify strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 那棵树非常茂盛(那棵樹非常茂盛)。
    - Nà kē shù fēicháng màoshèng.
    - That tree is very lush.
  - 微风拂过树梢(微風拂過樹梢)，阳光洒满枝头(陽光灑滿枝頭)。
    - Wēifēng fúguò shùshāo, yángguāng sǎ mǎn zhītóu.
    - A gentle breeze brushes the treetops, while sunlight fills the branches.

### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉)

- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 枼(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 枼 (yè, "leaf") =
    - vegetation-like and phonetic element ->
    - leaf
  - **interpretation:** In 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf"), the 艹 (cǎo, "grass") component suggests vegetation, while 枼 (yè, "leaf") provides the sound. This combination aptly conveys the meaning of a leaf, aligning both meaning and pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified form 叶(葉), the component 艹 (cǎo, "grass") is removed, and 枼 (yè, "leaf") alone suggests both the phonetic and semantic aspects, reducing the complexity by focusing solely on the phonetic element.
- **examples:**

    - Shù shàng de yè hěn měi.
    - The leaves on the tree are beautiful.
  - 他轻轻地拿起一片叶(他輕輕地拿起一片葉)，仔细端详(仔細端詳)。
    - Tā qīng qīng de ná qǐ yī piàn yè, zǐ xì duān xiáng.

- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 葉

  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts leaves growing from a tree ->
    - original form of 葉(叶) (yè, 'leaf') ->

  - **interpretation:** The character 枼(yè, 'leaf') visually resembles leaves sprouting from a tree, preserving its pictographic origin in 葉(叶) (yè, 'leaf'). Over time, it was also used phonetically to represent the concept of 'flat.'
- **examples:**

    - Qiūtiān láilín shí, yè fēnfēn piāoluò.

  - 春雨滋润着枼(春雨滋潤著葉)，让花儿更显娇媚(讓花兒更顯嬌媚)。
