## leaves
### shùyè
---
- **definition:** leaves
- **pinyin:** shùyè
- **components:**
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
  - 叶(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 树(樹) (shù, "tree"), 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") - referring to the foliage of a tree.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 树(樹) (shù, "tree") and 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") logically signifies the leaves that grow on trees.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹 simplified to 树), 叶(葉 simplified to 叶)
- **examples:**
  - 秋风吹过时(秋風吹過時)，树叶在空中飘落(樹葉在空中飄落)。
    - Qiū fēng chuī guò shí, shù yè zài kōng zhōng piāo luò.
    - When the autumn wind blows, the leaves float in the air.
  - 微风轻拂时(微風輕拂時)，树叶轻轻摇摆(樹葉輕輕搖擺)。
    - Wēi fēng qīng fú shí, shù yè qīng qīng yáo bǎi.
    - When the gentle breeze blows, the leaves sway softly.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 尌
    - shù
    - to plant a tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "tree") + 尌(尌) (shù, "to plant a tree") =
    - tree-planting action ->
    - extra 木(木) added to differentiate ->
    - 樹
  - **interpretation:** The character 樹(樹) (shù, "tree") combines the semantic 木(木) (mù, "tree") with the phonetic 尌(尌) (shù, "to plant a tree") to convey the concept of planting or cultivating trees. Adding another 木(木) emphasizes the tree-related meaning.
  - **simplification:** 树(樹): 壴(壴) component replaced with 又(又) (yòu, "again") as a cursive shorthand in reference to 樹.
- **examples:**
  - 这是一棵高大的树(這是一棵高大的樹)。
    - Zhè shì yī kē gāodà de shù.
    - This is a tall tree.
  - 老农在树下休息(老農在樹下休息)，体会大自然的气息(體會大自然的氣息)。
    - Lǎo nóng zài shù xià xiūxí, tǐhuì dàzìrán de qìxī.
    - The old farmer rests under the tree, experiencing the breath of nature.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom
  - **interpretation:** 木(木) (mù, "tree") visually represents a tree with branches at the top and roots below. This pictorial depiction is straightforward, resembling the structure of a tree and its natural growth.
- **examples:**
  - 庭院里有一棵参天古木(庭院裡有一棵參天古木)。
    - Tíngyuàn lǐ yǒu yī kē cāntiān gǔ mù.
    - There is a towering ancient tree in the courtyard.
  - 他走到森林深处(他走到森林深處)，看见一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁(看見一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁)。
    - Tā zǒu dào sēnlín shēnchù, kànjiàn yī kē gū línglíng de mù chùlì zài lùpáng.
    - He walked into the deep forest and saw a solitary tree standing by the roadside.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 尌(尌)
- **definition:** to plant a tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "tree") + 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") =
    - to plant a tree
  - **interpretation:** The character 尌 represents the action of planting, combining 木(木) (mù, "tree") for the concept of a tree and 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") to indicate a manual action. Originating with elements bright under symbol 权(权) showing authority or balance, the modern form reflects a merger of 木 and 豆. This transformation into 壴 resembles a concept involving balance or cultivation, hence conveying the idea of tree planting more complexly.
  - **simplification:** When the character transitioned to 樹(树), an extra 木(木) (mù, "tree") was introduced for clarity, while the simplified form adopts 又(又) (yòu, "again") for 壴, aligning phonetic and visual ease.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天尌了一棵橡树(我今天尌了一棵橡樹)。
    - wǒ jīntiān shù le yī kē xiàngshù
    - I planted an oak tree today.
  - 农夫辛勤劳动(農夫辛勤勞動)，尌了一片树林(尌了一片樹林)。
    - nóngfū xīnqín láodòng, shù le yī piàn shùlín
    - The farmer, after hard work, planted a grove.
  - 他在河边尌了一棵柳树(他在河邊尌了一棵柳樹)，期盼春风再临(期盼春風再臨)。
    - tā zài hébiān shù le yī kē liǔshù, qīpàn chūnfēng zài lín
    - He planted a willow tree by the river, hoping for the return of the spring breeze.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 树(樹) → 尌(尌) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram (指事)
  - **description:**
    - Line on hand indicating pulse point on forearm ->
    - original form of 肘 (zhǒu, 'elbow') ->
    - unit of measurement derived from this distance
  - **interpretation:** 寸(cùn, 'inch') represents a specific point on the forearm used for pulse measurement, historically linked to its measurement definition. The form depicts a line that combines with the hand to signify this location, showing a logical evolution from anatomy to distance measurement.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布的宽度只有五寸(這塊布的寬度只有五寸)。
    - Zhè kuài bù de kuāndù zhǐyǒu wǔ cùn.
    - The width of this cloth is only five inches.
  - 木匠在制作家具时(木匠在製作家具時)，要精确到一寸(要精確到一寸)。
    - Mùjiàng zài zhìzuò jiājù shí, yào jīngquè dào yī cùn.
    - Carpenters must be precise to the inch when making furniture.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉)
- **definition:** leaf
- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 枼
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 枼(枼) (yè, "leaf") =
    - phonetic support with mouth suggesting collection ->
    - leaf
  - **interpretation:** The character 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") combines the phonetic component 枼(枼) (yè, "leaf") with 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth"), using the latter to suggest the action of gathering or collecting leaves, as if by mouth.
  - **simplification:** 葉(葉) → 叶(葉): 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") retains phonetic support while representing multiple pictured leaves more simply.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天的树上飘落一片叶(葉)。
    - qiū tiān de shù shàng piāo luò yī piàn yè(葉)。
    - A leaf drifts down from the tree in autumn.
  - 风轻轻吹动叶(葉)，摇曳出诗意(詩意)。
    - fēng qīng qīng chuī dòng yè(葉)，yáo yè chū shī yì(詩意)。
    - The wind gently blows the leaf, swaying with poetic charm.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth

- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") visually represents an open mouth, making it straightforward to connect to its meaning related to speaking and openings.
- **examples:**

    - Please open your mouth.
  - 他家有三口人(他家有三口人)，个个活泼可爱(個個活潑可愛)。
    - Tā jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, gègè huópō kě'ài.
    - His family consists of three people, and each one is lively and adorable.
  - 医生检查了他的口(醫生檢查了他的口)，确认健康无虞(確認健康無虞)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchále tā de kǒu, quèrèn jiànkāng wú yú.

### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉) → 枼(葉)
- **definition:** leaf
- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 艸
    - cǎo
    - grass
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艸(草) (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 枼(枼) (yè, "leaf") ->
    - leaf
  - **interpretation:** The character 枼(葉) (yè, "leaf") combines 艸(艸) (cǎo, "grass"), indicative of plants, with a phonetic component 枼(枼) (yè, "leaf") to derive the meaning of 'leaf.'
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 葉 (yè, "leaf") remains unchanged in simplified Chinese as it is commonly used in its traditional form.
- **examples:**
  - 新鲜的枼在晨曦中闪耀(新鮮的枼在晨曦中閃耀)。
    - Xīnxiān de yè zài chénxī zhōng shǎnyào.
    - Fresh leaves shine in the morning light.
  - 春风拂面时(春風拂面時)，枼随风摇曳(枼隨風搖曳)。
    - Chūnfēng fú miàn shí, yè suí fēng yáoyè.
    - When the spring breeze brushes the face, the leaves sway in the wind.
### 树叶(樹葉) → 叶(葉) → 枼(葉) → 艸(艸)
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts grass blades by doubling 屮(屮) (chè, 'grass')
  - **interpretation:** The character 艸(艸) (cǎo, 'grass; plant') originates as a pictogram representing the blades of grass, depicted by duplicating the character for grass 屮(屮) (chè, 'grass'). This duplication visually conveys the idea of a patch or stand of grass.
- **examples:**
  - 山坡上长满了艸(山坡上長滿了艸)。
    - Shān pō shàng zhǎng mǎn le cǎo.
    - The hillside is covered with grass.
  - 风吹过田野(風吹過田野)，艸随风摇曳(艸隨風搖曳)。
    - Fēng chuī guò tián yě, cǎo suí fēng yáo yè.
    - The wind blows across the fields, and the grass sways in the breeze.
  - 早晨的露珠滋润了艸(早晨的露珠滋潤了艸)。
    - Zǎochén de lù zhū zī rùn le cǎo.
    - The morning dew nourishes the grass.
