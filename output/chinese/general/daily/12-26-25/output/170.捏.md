## pinch
### niē
---
- **definition:** pinch
- **pinyin:** niē
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 聂(聶)
    - niè
    - whisper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(扌) (shǒu, 'hand') + phonetic 聂(聶) (niè, 'whisper') =
    - action involving the hands ->
    - pinch
  - **interpretation:** The character 捏(捏) (niē, 'pinch') combines 扌(扌) (shǒu, 'hand') to indicate a manual action, with 聂(聶) (niè, 'whisper') serving as a phonetic component. The combination suggests a light touch, akin to pinching.
- **examples:**
  - 不要捏我的脸(不要捏我的臉)。
    - Bú yào niē wǒ de liǎn.
    - Don't pinch my face.
  - 小猫咪被人捏了一下(小貓咪被人捏了一下)，显得又好笑又可爱(顯得又好笑又可愛)。
    - Xiǎo māomī bèi rén niē le yīxià, xiǎn de yòu hǎoxiào yòu kě ài.
    - The kitten, after being pinched a little by someone, looked both funny and cute.
### 捏(捏) → 扌(扌)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Derived from its seal script form
  - **description:**
    - Seal script 扌 (shǒu, "hand") ->
    - simplified grouping for hand-related terms
  - **interpretation:** 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") functions as a shorthand or radical form used mainly in compounded characters, retaining the meaning of 'hand.' This facilitates the creation of characters that involve manual action or activities related to the hands.
  - **simplification:** None, as 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") is already a simplified component derived for ease in writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我的手很温暖(我的手很溫暖)。
    - Wǒ de shǒu hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - My hand is very warm.
  - 她伸出手(她伸出手)，邀请大家(邀請大家)。
    - Tā shēn chū shǒu, yāo qǐng dàjiā.
    - She stretched out her hand, inviting everyone.
  - 握手表示友好(握手表示友好)，促进合作(促進合作)。
    - Wò shǒu biǎoshì yǒuhǎo, cùjìn hézuò.
    - A handshake signifies friendship, promoting cooperation.
### 捏(捏) → 聂(聶)
- **definition:** whisper
- **pinyin:** niè
- **components:**
  - 耳
    - ěr
    - ear
  - 龍
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 耳(耳) (ěr, "ear") + 龍(龍) (lóng, "dragon") as a phonetic component (plough shape) + reduced elements for phonetic hints ->
    - representing whispering
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 耳(耳) (ěr, "ear"), indicating something related to hearing, with a phonetic component resembling a variation of 龍(龍) (lóng, "dragon") to imply whispering, an action closely related to ears.
  - **simplification:** The traditional 龍(龍) (lóng, "dragon") was simplified into three repeating sections resembling 屮(屮) (chè, "sprout") placed above the ear radical to imitate a light, whispered sound.
- **examples:**
  - 他轻声聂(他輕聲聶)，传递秘密(傳遞秘密)。
    - Tā qīng shēng niè, chuándì mìmì.
    - He whispers softly, conveying secrets.
  - 月光洒下(月光灑下)，他聂温柔的承诺(他聶溫柔的承諾)，消散于夜风(消散於夜風)。
    - Yuè guāng sǎ xià, tā niè wēn róu de chéng nuò, xiāo sàn yú yè fēng.
    - Under the moonlight, he whispers a gentle promise that fades into the night breeze.
  - 夜深人静时(夜深人靜時)，他聂心事(他聶心事)，悄然传情(悄然傳情)。
    - Yè shēn rén jìng shí, tā niè xīn shì, qiǎo rán chuán qíng.
    - In the stillness of the night, he whispers his innermost thoughts, subtly conveying his feelings.
