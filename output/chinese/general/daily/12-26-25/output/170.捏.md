## pinch
### niē
---
- **definition:** pinch
- **pinyin:** niē
- **components:**
  - 扌(手)
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 涅
    - niè
    - mud
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") + 涅 (niè, "mud") =
    - hand action related to mud ->
    - pinch
  - **interpretation:** The character 捏 (niē, "pinch") combines 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") to suggest a manual action, with 涅 (niè, "mud") to indicate the sound component. Together, they imply the action of using one's hand to pinch or mold something, similar to handling mud.

### 捏 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )伸( )出( )扌(手)接( )住( )了( )飞(飛)来(來)的( )球( )。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.

### 捏 → 涅 → 氵

- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**

### 捏 → 涅 → 黒(黑)
- **traditional:** 黑
- **definition:** black
- **pinyin:** hēi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Variant of 黑
  - **description:**
    - 黑 (hēi, "black") simplified ->
    - two dots replaced by one stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 黒 is a simplified variant of 黑 (hēi, "black"), created by merging the two original dots into a single stroke, akin to other simplifications in Japanese script reform.
  - **simplification:** This is a reform-based simplification where two dots in 黑 (hēi, "black") were combined into one stroke, similar to how 毋 (wú, "do not") simplifies 母 (mǔ, "mother").
- **examples:**
  - 这本书的封面是黑的。
    - Zhè běn shū de fēngmiàn shì hēi(黑) de.
    - The cover of this book is black.
  - 夜( )幕( )降( )临(臨)时(時)，公( )园(園)里(裡)变(變)得( )黑( )漆( )漆( )。

    - When night falls, the park turns pitch black.
