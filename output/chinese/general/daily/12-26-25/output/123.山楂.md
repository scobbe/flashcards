## hawthorn
### shānzhā
---
- **definition:** hawthorn
- **pinyin:** shānzhā
- **components:**
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
  - 楂
    - zhā
    - hawthorn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 山 (shān, "mountain") and 楂 (zhā, "hawthorn"). The Chinese hawthorn is a small tree or shrub commonly found in mountainous regions, influencing its name.
  - **interpretation:** The term 山楂 (shānzhā, "hawthorn") effectively captures the habitat where hawthorns naturally grow, often in mountainous areas. Therefore, the name reflects both the plant and its common environment.
- **examples:**
  - 我很喜欢(歡)吃山楂。
    - Wǒ hěn xǐhuān chī shānzhā.
    - I really enjoy eating hawthorn.
  - 夏日炎炎，一碗冰镇(鎮)山楂汤(湯)使人清凉(涼)。
    - Xiàrì yányán, yī wǎn bīngzhèn shānzhā tāng shǐ rén qīngliáng.
    - On a scorching summer day, a bowl of chilled hawthorn soup refreshes you.
  - 山楂不仅(僅)好吃，还对(還對)消化有益。
    - Shānzhā bùjǐn hǎochī, hái duì xiāohuà yǒuyì.
    - Not only is hawthorn tasty, but it also aids digestion.
### 山楂 → 山
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three mountain peaks
  - **interpretation:** The character 山(shān, "mountain") visually represents the concept of a mountain using three vertical lines that suggest peaks. This depiction is consistent with early forms found in Oracle Bone Script, emphasizing the notion of natural elevation associated with mountains.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)去爬山。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We go climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山让(讓)人惊叹(驚嘆)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān ràng rén jīngtàn.
    - That mountain astonishes people.
  - 远处(遠處)的山隐约(隱約)在云(雲)中，令人心旷(曠)神怡。
    - Yuǎnchù de shān yǐnyuē zài yún zhōng, lìng rén xīnkuàng shényí.
    - The distant mountain fades into the clouds, leaving one in a state of peace.
### 山楂 → 楂
- **definition:** hawthorn
- **pinyin:** zhā
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 𣬉
    - zhā
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木 (mù, "wood") + phonetic: 𣬉 (zhā) =
    - tree with phonetic association ->
    - "hawthorn"
  - **interpretation:** The character 楂 (zhā, "hawthorn") combines 木 (mù, 'wood'), indicating it's a type of tree, with the component 𣬉 (zhā) to give the sound. This effectively conveys the phonetic and semantic qualities associated with a hawthorn tree.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱(愛)吃楂，这种(這種)果实(實)很甜。
    - Wǒ ài chī zhā, zhè zhǒng guǒshí hěn tián.
    - I love eating hawthorn; this fruit is very sweet.
  - 厨师(廚師)用楂制(製)作酱(醬)料，佐餐效果特别(別)好。
    - Chúshī yòng zhā zhìzuò jiàngliào, zuǒcān xiàoguǒ tèbié hǎo.
    - The chef uses hawthorn to make a sauce, and it pairs especially well with meals.
### 山楂 → 楂 → 木
- **definition:** wood
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把椅子的主要材料是木。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
### 山楂 → 楂 → 𣬉
- **definition:** phonetic component
- **pinyin:** zhā
- **components:**
  - 髟
    - biāo
    - hair
  - 加
    - jiā
    - add
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 髟 (biāo, 'hair') + 加 (jiā, 'add') =
    - combines with 加 as phonetic element
  - **interpretation:** The character 𣬉 is composed of the component 髟 (biāo, 'hair') which suggests a relation to hair or a similar idea, and 加 (jiā, 'add') functioning as the phonetic component. This indicates 𣬉 is used for its sound, likely in a word related to hair.
### 山楂 → 楂 → 𣬉 → 髟
- **definition:** hair
- **pinyin:** biāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person with long hair ->
    - represents long hair
  - **interpretation:** The character 髟(biāo, "hair") is a pictogram, symbolizing a person adorned with long hair, emphasizing its characteristic texture and appearance.
- **examples:**
  - 她的髟柔顺(順)如丝(絲)。
    - Tā de biāo róushùn rú sī.
    - Her hair is as smooth as silk.
  - 他每天早上花时间(時間)整理髟，保持整洁(潔)。
    - Tā měitiān zǎoshang huā shíjiān zhěnglǐ biāo, bǎochí zhěngjié.
    - He spends time every morning tidying his hair, keeping it neat.
  - 这(這)本字典详细(詳細)介绍(紹)了髟的历(歷)史。
    - Zhè běn zìdiǎn xiángxì jièshào le biāo de lìshǐ.
    - This dictionary details the history of the hair radical.
### 山楂 → 楂 → 𣬉 → 加
- **definition:** add
- **pinyin:** jiā
- **components:**
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 力 (lì, "strength") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - applying strength vocally ->
    - add
  - **interpretation:** The character 加 (jiā, "add") shows the idea of using strength in a vocal or pronounced way, suggesting an emphasis or increase, which aligns with the meaning of 'to add.'
- **examples:**

    - Qǐng jiā táng.

  - 厨师(廚師)在汤里(湯裡)加盐(鹽)，让(讓)味道更浓(濃)。

  - 他总(總)是加一点(點)努力，来(來)克服困难(難)。
    - Tā zǒngshì jiā yīdiǎn nǔlì, lái kèfú kùnnán.
    - He always adds a bit more effort to overcome difficulties.

- **examples:**

### 山楂 → 楂 → 𣬉 → 加 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开(張開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, dàshēng hǎnjiào.
    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.
