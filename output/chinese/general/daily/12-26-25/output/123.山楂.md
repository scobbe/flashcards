## hawthorn
### shānzhā
---
- **definition:** hawthorn
- **pinyin:** shānzhā
- **components:**
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
  - 楂
    - zhā
    - hawthorn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 山(山) (shān, "mountain") and 楂(楂) (zhā, "hawthorn"). The Chinese hawthorn is a small tree or shrub commonly found in mountainous regions, influencing its name.
  - **interpretation:** The term 山楂(山楂) (shānzhā, "hawthorn") effectively captures the habitat where hawthorns naturally grow, often in mountainous areas. Therefore, the name reflects both the plant and its common environment.
- **examples:**
  - 我很喜欢吃山楂(我很喜歡吃山楂)。
    - Wǒ hěn xǐhuān chī shānzhā.
    - I really enjoy eating hawthorn.
  - 夏日炎炎(夏日炎炎)，一碗冰镇山楂汤使人清凉(一碗冰鎮山楂湯使人清涼)。
    - Xiàrì yányán, yī wǎn bīngzhèn shānzhā tāng shǐ rén qīngliáng.
    - On a scorching summer day, a bowl of chilled hawthorn soup refreshes you.
  - 山楂不仅好吃(山楂不僅好吃)，还对消化有益(還對消化有益)。
    - Shānzhā bùjǐn hǎochī, hái duì xiāohuà yǒuyì.
    - Not only is hawthorn tasty, but it also aids digestion.
### 山楂(山楂) → 山(山)
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three mountain peaks
  - **interpretation:** The character 山(shān, "mountain") visually represents the concept of a mountain using three vertical lines that suggest peaks. This depiction is consistent with early forms found in Oracle Bone Script, emphasizing the notion of natural elevation associated with mountains.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We go climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山让人惊叹(那座大山讓人驚嘆)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān ràng rén jīngtàn.
    - That mountain astonishes people.
  - 远处的山隐约在云中(遠處的山隱約在雲中)，令人心旷神怡(令人心曠神怡)。
    - Yuǎnchù de shān yǐnyuē zài yún zhōng, lìng rén xīnkuàng shényí.
    - The distant mountain fades into the clouds, leaving one in a state of peace.
### 山楂(山楂) → 楂(楂)
- **definition:** hawthorn
- **pinyin:** zhā
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 𣬉
    - zhā
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + phonetic: 𣬉 (zhā) =
    - tree with phonetic association ->
    - "hawthorn"
  - **interpretation:** The character 楂(楂) (zhā, "hawthorn") combines 木(木) (mù, 'wood'), indicating it's a type of tree, with the component 𣬉 (zhā) to give the sound. This effectively conveys the phonetic and semantic qualities associated with a hawthorn tree.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱吃楂(我愛吃楂)，这种果实很甜(這種果實很甜)。
    - Wǒ ài chī zhā, zhè zhǒng guǒshí hěn tián.
    - I love eating hawthorn; this fruit is very sweet.
  - 厨师用楂制作酱料(廚師用楂製作醬料)，佐餐效果特别好(佐餐效果特別好)。
    - Chúshī yòng zhā zhìzuò jiàngliào, zuǒcān xiàoguǒ tèbié hǎo.
    - The chef uses hawthorn to make a sauce, and it pairs especially well with meals.
### 山楂(山楂) → 楂(楂) → 木(木)
- **definition:** wood
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这把椅子的主要材料是木(這把椅子的主要材料是木)。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木(工匠精心雕刻木)，制成精美家具(制成精美家具)。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
### 山楂(山楂) → 楂(楂) → 𣬉(𣬉)
- **definition:** phonetic component
- **pinyin:** zhā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** The character 𣬉 is a pictogram showing a plaited hair braid, which is indicative of its usage primarily as a phonetic component in various other Chinese characters.
  - **interpretation:** 𣬉 depicts braided hair and is used as a phonetic element in other characters, but it doesn't carry a specific meaning independently.
- **examples:**
  - 这个汉字利用了𣬉作声旁(這個漢字利用了𣬉作聲旁)。
    - Zhège hànzì lìyòng le zhā zuò shēngpáng.
    - This Chinese character uses 𣬉 as its phonetic component.
  - 传统字学认为𣬉是声旁的重要代表(傳統字學認為𣬉是聲旁的重要代表)。
    - Chuántǒng zì xué rènwéi zhā shì shēngpáng de zhòngyào dàibiǎo.
    - Traditional Chinese philology considers 𣬉 an important phonetic component.
