## Zhongzheng District, Taipei
### Zhōngzhèng Qū
---
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **components:**
  - 中正
    - zhōngzhèng
    - upright; central; name honoring Chiang Kai-shek
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; region; area; administrative division
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle"; "central") and 正(正) (zhèng, "upright"; "correct") form 中正(中正) (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central"), a name symbolizing balance and virtue in governance, often associated with nationalist leader Chiang Kai-shek. 區(區) (qū, "district") refers to an administrative division or region.
  - **interpretation:** 中正(中正) (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central") reflects an ideal of balanced and righteous governance, which aligns with themes in Chinese political philosophy. The district is named in honor of Chiang Kai-shek, underscoring the historical significance of these ideals. 中正區 is a district that embodies these values in contemporary Taiwan.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在中正区(我住在中正區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngzhèng Qū.
    - I live in Zhongzheng District.
  - 中正区拥有丰富的历史(中正區擁有豐富的歷史)，并承载许多文化传承(並承載許多文化傳承)。
    - Zhōngzhèng Qū yǒngyǒu fēngfù de lìshǐ, bìng chéngzài xǔduō wénhuà chuánchéng.
    - Zhongzheng District boasts a rich history, and it carries many cultural heritages.
  - 每逢假日，中正区的街道热闹非凡(每逢假日，中正區的街道熱鬧非凡)。
    - Měi féng jiàrì, Zhōngzhèng Qū de jiēdào rènào fēifán.
    - On holidays, the streets of Zhongzheng District are bustling with life.
