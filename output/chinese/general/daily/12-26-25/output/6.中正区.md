- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **components:**

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle"; "central") and 正(正) (zhèng, "upright"; "correct") form 中正(中正) (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central"), a name symbolizing balance and virtue in governance, often associated with nationalist leader Chiang Kai-shek. 區(區) (qū, "district") refers to an administrative division or region.
  - **interpretation:** 中正(中正) (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central") reflects an ideal of balanced and righteous governance, which aligns with themes in Chinese political philosophy.
