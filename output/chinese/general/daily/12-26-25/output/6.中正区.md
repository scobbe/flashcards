## Zhongzheng District, Taipei
### Zhōngzhèng Qū
---
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **components:**
  - 中正
    - zhōngzhèng
    - middle; upright; proper; neutral
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle") and 正(正) (zhèng, "upright; correct") together form 'Zhongzheng', a reference often associated with neutrality and righteousness. 區(區) (qū, "district") indicates a specific administrative area.
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to a district that embodies centrality and correctness, named 'Zhongzheng' for its association with integrity. It's a place within the administrative division of Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在中正区(中正區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngzhèng Qū.
    - I live in Zhongzheng District.
  - 中正区(中正區)是台北市的政治中心(是臺北市的政治中心)，拥有丰富的历史文化(擁有豐富的歷史文化)。
    - Zhōngzhèng Qū shì Táiběi shì de zhèngzhì zhōngxīn, yǒngyǒu fēngfù de lìshǐ wénhuà.
    - Zhongzheng District is the political center of Taipei City, possessing a rich history and culture.
  - 游客走访中正区(中正區)，体验当地历史故事(體驗當地歷史故事)。
    - Yóukè zǒufǎng Zhōngzhèng Qū, tǐyàn dāngdì lìshǐ gùshì.
    - Visitors explore Zhongzheng District to experience local historical tales.
