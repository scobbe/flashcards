### Zhōngzhèng Qū

- **traditional:** 中正區
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **components:**

  - 区(區)

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中 (zhōng, "middle"; "central") and 正 (zhèng, "upright"; "correct") form 中正 (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central"), a name symbolizing balance and virtue in governance, often associated with nationalist leader Chiang Kai-shek. 區 (qū, "district") refers to an administrative division or region.
  - **interpretation:** 中正 (zhōngzhèng, "upright and central") reflects an ideal of balanced and righteous governance, which aligns with themes in Chinese political philosophy. The district is named in honor of Chiang Kai-shek, underscoring the historical significance of these ideals. 中正區 is a district that embodies these values in contemporary Taiwan.
