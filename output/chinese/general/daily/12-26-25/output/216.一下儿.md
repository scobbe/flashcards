## a bit; for a moment
### yíxiàr
---
- **traditional:** 一下兒
- **definition:** a bit; for a moment
- **pinyin:** yíxiàr
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
  - 下
    - xià
    - down
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - child; son; diminutive suffix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 一(yī, "one") + 下(xià, "down") + 儿(兒) (ér, "child or diminutive suffix")
  - **interpretation:** 一( )下( )儿(兒) (yíxiàr) is commonly used in Mandarin to soften the tone of a request or action, indicating a short or quick action. The character 一(yī, "one") suggests singularity, and 下(xià, "down") often indicates a brief duration or a minor action, while 儿(兒) (ér, "child or diminutive suffix") adds an informal tone.
  - **simplification:** 儿 simplified to 儿
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)坐( )一( )下( )儿(兒)。
    - Qǐng zuò yíxiàr.
    - Please have a seat for a moment.
  - 我( )休( )息( )一( )下( )儿(兒)，再( )继(繼)续(續)工( )作( )。
    - Wǒ xiūxí yíxiàr, zài jìxù gōngzuò.
    - I'll rest for a moment and then continue working.
  - 你( )试(試)一( )下( )儿(兒)，看看效果如何。
    - Nǐ shì yíxiàr, kànkan xiàoguǒ rúhé.
    - Try it for a bit and see how it works.
