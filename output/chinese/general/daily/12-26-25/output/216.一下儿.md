### a bit; for a moment
---
- **pinyin:** a bit; for a moment
- **components:**
  - 一下
    - yīxià
    - a bit; for a moment
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - suffix used in spoken Chinese to convey familiarity
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 一(一) (yī, 'one') + 下(下) (xià, 'down') + 儿(兒) (ér, 'suffix used in spoken Chinese to convey familiarity') — one down auxiliary
  - **interpretation:** The term 一下儿 suggests a brief action or moment, commonly used in casual conversation. The addition of 儿 conveys a sense of familiarity and lightness to the action.
- **examples:**
  - 我去一下儿(我去一下兒)，马上回来(馬上回來)！
    - Wǒ qù yīxiàér, mǎshàng huílái！
    - I’ll be right back for a moment!
  - 等一下儿(等一下兒)，我有点事(我有點事)要处理(要處理)。
    - Děng yīxiàér, wǒ yǒudiǎn shì yào chǔlǐ.
    - Wait a moment, I have something to deal with.

- **definition:** suffix used in spoken Chinese to convey familiarity
- **pinyin:** ér

- **examples:**
