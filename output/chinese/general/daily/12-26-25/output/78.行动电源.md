## power bank
### xíngdòngdiànyuán
---
- **definition:** power bank
- **pinyin:** xíngdòngdiànyuán
- **components:**
  - 行动(行動)
    - xíngdòng
    - to act; action; mobile; movement
  - 电源(電源)
    - diànyuán
    - power supply; power source; electrical source; power
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 行动 + 电源 = mobile/on-the-move + power source → portable power supply (power bank).
  - **interpretation:** Names a portable power source used to charge devices while on the move. Contrasts with fixed 电源 found in walls or equipment.
  - **simplification:** 動→动 and 電→电 were simplified in the PRC to reduce stroke count; 源 unchanged, so 行動電源 → 行动电源.
- **examples:**
  - 我忘带行动电源(我忘帶行動電源)。
    - Wǒ wàng dài xíngdòng diànyuán.
    - I forgot to bring my power bank.
  - 我的手机快没电了(我的手機快沒電了)，借我一下你的行动电源(借我一下你的行動電源)。
    - Wǒ de shǒujī kuài méi diàn le, jiè wǒ yíxià nǐ de xíngdòng diànyuán.
    - My phone is almost out of battery; lend me your power bank for a moment.
  - 这个行动电源容量一万毫安时(這個行動電源容量一萬毫安時)，能充两次手机(能充兩次手機)。
    - Zhège xíngdòng diànyuán róngliàng yí wàn háo'ān shí, néng chōng liǎng cì shǒujī.
    - This power bank has a 10,000 mAh capacity and can charge a phone twice.
### 行动电源(行動電源) → 行动(行動)
- **definition:** to act; action; mobile; movement
- **pinyin:** xíngdòng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 行 ‘carry out; do’ + 动 ‘move’ = take action; be in motion/operation.
  - **interpretation:** Combines carrying out (行) with movement (动) to express acting or putting plans into motion. Also used attributively, especially in Taiwan, to mean mobile/portable.
  - **simplification:** 動 → 动 by replacing 重 with 云 and retaining 力, reducing strokes; 行 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要立即采取行动(我們需要立即採取行動)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào lìjí cǎiqǔ xíngdòng.
    - We need to take action immediately.
  - 他的行动非常迅速(他的行動非常迅速)。
    - Tā de xíngdòng fēicháng xùnsù.
    - His actions are very swift.
  - 在台湾，手机常叫行动电话(在臺灣，手機常叫行動電話)。
    - Zài Táiwān, shǒujī cháng jiào xíngdòng diànhuà.
    - In Taiwan, mobile phones are often called xíngdòng diànhuà.
### 行动电源(行動電源) → 电源(電源)
- **definition:** power supply; power source; electrical source; power
- **pinyin:** diànyuán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 电 + 源 = source/origin of electricity (power supply).
  - **interpretation:** Names the origin or supply of electric power—anything that provides electrical energy, from batteries to wall outlets (mains). Common in technical and everyday contexts for power inputs and switches.
  - **simplification:** 電 → 电: simplified by reducing strokes and removing the 'rain' component; 源 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 把电脑接到电源(把電腦接到電源)。
    - Bǎ diànnǎo jiē dào diànyuán.
    - Connect the computer to a power source.
  - 请关闭电源以免触电(請關閉電源以免觸電)。
    - Qǐng guānbì diànyuán yǐmiǎn chùdiàn.
    - Please turn off the power to avoid electric shock.
  - 这个充电器支持多种电源电压(這個充電器支持多種電源電壓)。
    - Zhège chōngdiànqì zhīchí duō zhǒng diànyuán diànyā.
    - This charger supports multiple power supply voltages.
