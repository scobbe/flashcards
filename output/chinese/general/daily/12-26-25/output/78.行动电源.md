- **pinyin:** power bank
- **components:**

    - action; conduct; behavior; movement
  - 电源(電源)
    - diànyuán

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 行动(行動) (xíngdòng, "action") + 电源(電源) (diànyuán, "power source") — action power source
  - **interpretation:** A 行动(行動) (xíngdòng, "action") for 电源(電源) (diànyuán, "power source") refers to a device providing mobile energy. This combination makes sense because a power bank is a portable energy source that allows users to take action without the constraint of being plugged into an outlet.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一个行动电源(我需要一個行動電源)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yī gè xíngdòng diànyuán.
    - I need a power bank.
  - 他的行动电源(他的行動電源)没电了！
    - Tā de xíngdòng diànyuán méi diàn le!
    - His power bank is out of battery!
