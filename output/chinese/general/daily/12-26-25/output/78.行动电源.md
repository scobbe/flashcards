## power bank
### xíngdòngdiànyuán
---
- **traditional:** 行動電源
- **definition:** power bank
- **pinyin:** xíngdòngdiànyuán
- **components:**
  - 行動
    - xíngdòng
    - action; movement
  - 電源
    - diànyuán
    - power source
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 行動 (xíngdòng, "action; movement") + 電源 (diànyuán, "power source") =
    - power source for movement ->
    - power bank
  - **interpretation:** The term 行動電源 (xíngdòngdiànyuán, "power bank") makes sense as it literally describes a portable power source that facilitates movement or action, which is essential for charging devices on the go. This combination reflects the practical use of a power bank in ensuring devices remain powered while being mobile.
- **examples:**
  - 我买(買)了一个(個)行动电(動電)源。
    - Wǒ mǎile yīgè xíngdòngdiànyuán.
    - I bought a power bank.
  - 在旅行时(時)，行动电(動電)源很方便。
    - Zài lǚxíng shí, xíngdòngdiànyuán hěn fāngbiàn.
    - When traveling, the power bank is very convenient.
  - 他推荐购买(薦購買)行动电(動電)源。
    - Tā tuījiàn gòumǎi xíngdòngdiànyuán.
    - He recommended buying a power bank.
