## power bank
### xíngdòngdiànyuán
---
- **definition:** power bank
- **pinyin:** xíngdòngdiànyuán
- **components:**
  - 行动(行動)
    - xíngdòng
    - to act; action; mobile; movement
  - 电源(電源)
    - diànyuán
    - power supply; power source; electrical source; power
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 行动 + 电源 = mobile/on-the-move + power source → portable power supply (power bank).
  - **interpretation:** Names a portable power source used to charge devices while on the move. Contrasts with fixed 电源 found in walls or equipment.
  - **simplification:** 動→动 and 電→电 were simplified in the PRC to reduce stroke count; 源 unchanged, so 行動電源 → 行动电源.
- **examples:**
  - 我忘带行动电源(我忘帶行動電源)。

    - Wǒ de shǒujī kuài méi diàn le, jiè wǒ yíxià nǐ de xíngdòng diànyuán.
    - My phone is almost out of battery; lend me your power bank for a moment.
  - 这个行动电源容量一万毫安时(這個行動電源容量一萬毫安時)，能充两次手机(能充兩次手機)。
    - Zhège xíngdòng diànyuán róngliàng yí wàn háo'ān shí, néng chōng liǎng cì shǒujī.
    - This power bank has a 10,000 mAh capacity and can charge a phone twice.
