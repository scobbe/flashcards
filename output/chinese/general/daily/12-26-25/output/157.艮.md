## Gen trigram
### gèn
---
- **definition:** Gen trigram
- **pinyin:** gèn
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, "eye") + 人(人) (rén, "person") =
    - a person looking back ->
    - disobedience/limit
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 目(目) (mù, "eye") and 人(人) (rén, "person") to depict someone looking back, symbolizing hesitation or the idea of a limit, suggesting the act of reconsideration or disobedience.
- **examples:**
  - 古文字中(古文字中)，艮作为表音符号较为常见(艮作為表音符號較為常見)。
    - Gǔ wénzì zhōng, gèn zuòwéi biǎoyīn fúhào jiào wéi chángjiàn.
    - In ancient scripts, 'gèn' is commonly used as a phonetic component.
  - 他欣赏艮的独特音调(他欣賞艮的獨特音調)。
    - Tā xīnshǎng gèn de dútè yīndiào.
    - He appreciates the unique tone of 'gèn'.
  - 诗人用艮作为音韵的象征(詩人用艮作為音韻的象徵)，表达内心的顿悟(表達內心的頓悟)。
    - Shīrén yòng gèn zuòwéi yīnyùn de xiàngzhēng, biǎodá nèixīn de dùnwù.
    - The poet uses 'gèn' as a symbol of phonetics to express inner enlightenment.
### 艮(艮) → 目(目)
- **definition:** eye
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(目) (mù, "eye") represents a human eye but is now commonly depicted rotated by 90 degrees. It serves as the basis for several other characters related to vision or perception.
- **examples:**
  - 医生检查我的目(醫生檢查我的目)，确认没有异常(確認沒有異常)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchá wǒ de mù, quèrèn méiyǒu yìcháng.
    - The doctor examined my eye and confirmed there was nothing abnormal.
  - 他左目受伤(他左目受傷)，依旧炯炯有神(依舊炯炯有神)。
    - Tā zuǒ mù shòushāng, yījiù jiǒngjiǒng yǒu shén.
    - His left eye was injured, yet it still shone with a determined spark.
  - 那只猫的目似乎闪烁智慧(那隻貓的目似乎閃爍智慧)。
    - Nà zhī māo de mù sìhū shǎnshuò zhìhuì.
    - The cat's eye seemed to shimmer with wisdom.
### 艮(艮) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts side view of a standing man
  - **interpretation:** The character 人(人) (rén, "person") visually represents a human figure standing upright, highlighting key anatomical features like an arm and a leg. This pictogram captures the essence of a person in a simple, efficient form.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个好人(他是一個好人)。
    - Tā shì yīgè hǎorén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 很多人喜欢运动(很多人喜歡運動)，也喜欢音乐(也喜歡音樂)。
    - Hěnduō rén xǐhuān yùndòng, yě xǐhuān yīnyuè.
    - Many people like sports, and they also like music.
  - 人多力量大(人多力量大)。
    - Rénduō lìliàng dà.
    - There is strength in numbers.
