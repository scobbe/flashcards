## portrait
### xiàoxiàng
---
- **definition:** portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiào
    - resemble; similar
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; statue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 肖(肖) (xiào, "resemble") + 像(像) (xiàng, "image") =
    - a resembling image ->
    - portrait
  - **interpretation:** A 肖像(肖像) (xiàoxiàng, "portrait") is an image that resembles the appearance of a person. The character 肖(肖) (xiào, "resemble") signifies likeness, while 像(像) (xiàng, "image") denotes a representation or picture, aptly capturing the essence of a portrait.
- **examples:**
  - 他画了一幅精致的肖像(他畫了一幅精緻的肖像)。
    - Tā huà le yī fú jīngzhì de xiàoxiàng.
    - He painted a delicate portrait.
  - 宫廷里悬挂着国王的肖像(宮廷裡懸掛著國王的肖像)，彰显王朝的荣耀(彰顯王朝的榮耀)。
    - Gōngtíng lǐ xuánguàzhe guówáng de xiàoxiàng, zhāngxiǎn wángcháo de róngyào.
    - In the palace, the king's portrait hangs, showcasing the dynasty's glory.

- **definition:** image; likeness; statue
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - elephant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 亻(亻) (rén, "person") + phonetic: 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant") =
    - person + likeness of an elephant
  - **interpretation:** In 像(像) (xiàng, "image"), the component 亻(亻) (rén, "person") indicates that the character is related to human figures or likenesses. 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant") serves as a phonetic component, hinting at the word's pronunciation, as well as symbolizing something large or prominent. Together, they suggest an image or likeness that conveys significant form.
- **examples:**
  - 这尊雕像像活着的英雄(這尊雕像像活著的英雄)。
    - Zhè zūn diāoxiàng xiàng huózhe de yīngxióng.

  - 他的面庞像雕刻般精致(他的面龐像雕刻般精緻)，给人留下深刻的印象(給人留下深刻的印象)。
    - Tā de miànpáng xiàng diāokè bān jīngzhì, gěi rén liúchū shēnkè de yìnxiàng.
    - His features are as exquisitely carved as a sculpture, leaving a deep impression on people.
  - 孩子的笑容像一幅生动的画像(孩子的笑容像一幅生動的畫像)。
    - Háizi de xiàoróng xiàng yī fú shēngdòng de huàxiàng.
