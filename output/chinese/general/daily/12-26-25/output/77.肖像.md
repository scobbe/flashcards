## 
### portrait
---
- **pinyin:** portrait
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - to resemble; similar; likeness; small
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; statue; portrait
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 肖(肖) (xiāo, 'to resemble') + 像(像) (xiàng, 'image') — resemblance image
  - **interpretation:** This term makes sense as a '肖像' refers to an image that resembles a person, essentially capturing their likeness in portrait form. The components together reflect the concept of an image that bears a resemblance, which is fitting for a portrait.
- **examples:**
  - 他画了一幅肖像(他畫了一幅肖像)。
    - Tā huàle yī fú xiāoxiàng.
    - He painted a portrait.
  - 肖像能展现出人的个性(肖像能展現出人的個性)。
    - Xiāoxiàng néng zhǎnxiàn chū rén de gèxìng.
    - A portrait can showcase a person's personality.
### 肖像(肖像) → 肖(肖)
- **definition:** to resemble; similar; likeness; small
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 交
    - jiāo
    - to intersect
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the phonetic component 交 (jiāo, 'to intersect') and the semantic component 肉 (ròu, 'meat') suggesting a resemblance or similarity in form.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines elements that suggest interaction or intersection, leading to the concept of resemblance or similarity, particularly in appearance.
- **examples:**
  - 他长得很肖似她(他長得很肖似她)，他们真是一对！
    - Tā zhǎng de hěn xiāo sì tā, tāmen zhēn shì yī duì！
    - He looks very similar to her, they really are a pair!
  - 这幅画的肖像(這幅畫的肖像)让人很感动。
    - Zhè fú huà de xiàoxiàng ràng rén hěn gǎndòng.
    - The portrait in this painting is very touching.
  - 他们的肖像(他們的肖像)在博物馆展出。
    - Tāmen de xiàoxiàng zài bówùguǎn zhǎnchū.
    - Their likenesses are exhibited in the museum.
### 肖像(肖像) → 肖(肖) → 交(交)
- **definition:** to intersect
- **pinyin:** jiāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human figure with crossed legs, suggesting the idea of intersection.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a person with crossed legs intuitively conveys the concept of intersecting or connecting paths.
- **examples:**
  - 他们在街上交叉(他們在街上交叉)。
    - Tāmen zài jiē shàng jiāochā.
    - They intersect on the street.
  - 这条路和那条路交(這條路和那條路交)。
    - Zhè tiáo lù hé nà tiáo lù jiāo.
    - This road intersects with that road.
  - 两条线交汇(兩條線交匯)。
    - Liǎng tiáo xiàn jiāohuì.
    - The two lines intersect.
### 肖像(肖像) → 肖(肖) → 肉(肉)
- **definition:** meat
- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts ribs of an animal’s torso or a slice of meat.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents meat, making it intuitive as a symbol for the substance.
- **examples:**
  - 我想吃肉。(我想吃肉。)
    - Wǒ xiǎng chī ròu.
    - I want to eat meat.
  - 他是素食者，不吃肉。(他是素食者，不吃肉。)
    - Tā shì sùshí zhě, bù chī ròu.
    - He is a vegetarian and does not eat meat.
### 肖像(肖像) → 像(像)
- **definition:** image; likeness; statue; portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - elephant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 亻 (meaning 'person') and the phonetic component 象 (meaning 'elephant').
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a resemblance to a person, akin to how an elephant's portrayal might evoke the shape or presence of one. This connection makes sense as images or statues often depict human forms.
- **examples:**
  - 这个雕像很像他。(這個雕像很像他。)
    - Zhège diāoxiàng hěn xiàng tā.
    - This statue looks a lot like him.
  - 我想画一幅像。(我想畫一幅像。)
    - Wǒ xiǎng huà yī fú xiàng.
    - I want to paint a portrait.
### 肖像(肖像) → 像(像) → 亻(亻)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** A stylization of 人, which represents a person or human figure.
  - **interpretation:** This character represents a simplified form of a person, emphasizing humanity and individual identity.
- **examples:**
  - 我是一个人(我是一个人)。
    - Wǒ shì yī gè rén.
    - I am a person.
  - 人的权利很重要(人的權利很重要)。
    - Rén de quánlì hěn zhòngyào.
    - The rights of a person are very important.
### 肖像(肖像) → 像(像) → 象(象)
- **definition:** elephant
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an elephant with parts representing the trunk, head, and body.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents an elephant, with distinct features like the trunk and body clearly illustrated.
- **examples:**
  - 大象在森林里(大象在森林裡)。
    - dà xiàng zài sēn lín lǐ.
    - The elephant is in the forest.
  - 我喜欢看大象(我喜歡看大象)。
    - wǒ xǐ huān kàn dà xiàng.
    - I like to see elephants.
