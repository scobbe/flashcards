## portrait
### xiàoxiàng
---
- **definition:** portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiào
    - resemble
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - likeness; statue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 肖(肖) (xiào, "resemble") + 像(像) (xiàng, "likeness; statue") =
    - resemble a likeness ->
    - portrait
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 肖(肖) (xiào, "resemble") and 像(像) (xiàng, "likeness") refers to something that closely resembles a person's appearance, which is what a portrait aims to capture.
- **examples:**
  - 他画了一幅肖像(他畫了一幅肖像)。
    - Tā huà le yī fú xiàoxiàng.
    - He painted a portrait.
  - 博物馆展出了名家拍摄的肖像(博物館展出了名家拍攝的肖像)，吸引了众多观众(吸引了眾多觀眾)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchūle míngjiā pāishè de xiàoxiàng, xīyǐnle zhòngduō guānzhòng.
    - The museum exhibited portraits taken by famous artists, attracting many visitors.
  - 肖像不仅记录了相貌(肖像不僅記錄了相貌)，更多传递了情感(更多傳遞了情感)。
    - Xiàoxiàng bù jǐn jìlùle xiāngmào, gèngduō chuándìle qínggǎn.
    - Portraits not only record appearances, but also convey deeper emotions.
### 肖像(肖像) → 肖(肖)
- **definition:** resemble
- **pinyin:** xiào
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') + Semantic: 肉(肉) (ròu, 'meat') ->
    - resembles
  - **interpretation:** The character 肖(xiào, 'resemble') combines the phonetic component 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') with the semantic component 肉(肉) (ròu, 'meat'). This suggests that the word relates to a likeness or resemblance, as if something small represents the larger whole, akin to how a portion of meat can resemble the whole animal.
- **examples:**
  - 他的面容肖出父亲(他的面容肖出父親)。
    - Tā de miànróng xiào chū fùqīn.
    - His facial features resemble his father.
  - 他那纤瘦的身影肖古代英雄(他那纖瘦的身影肖古代英雄)，令人惊叹(令人驚嘆)。
    - Tā nà xiānshòu de shēnyǐng xiào gǔdài yīngxióng, lìng rén jīngtàn.
    - His slender figure resembles an ancient hero, evoking admiration.
  - 他的眼神肖出忧郁(他的眼神肖出憂鬱)。
    - Tā de yǎnshén xiào chū yōuyù.
    - His eyes resemble melancholy.

### 肖像(肖像) → 肖(肖) → 肉(肉)
- **definition:** meat
- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the ribs of an animal’s torso ->
    - physical representation of a slice of meat
  - **interpretation:** The character 肉(ròu, "meat") is intended to visually represent the ribs or a cut of meat, emphasizing its function and meaning as 'meat'. Its depiction has stylized into 月(yuè, "moon") when included alongside phonetic components in other characters, showing visual simplification over time.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃肉(我喜歡吃肉)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī ròu.
    - I like to eat meat.
  - 厨师烤肉(廚師烤肉)，香味四溢(香味四溢)。
    - Chúshī kǎo ròu, xiāngwèi sì yì.
    - The chef grills meat, and the aroma fills the room.
  - 餐厅供应烤肉(餐廳供應烤肉)，顾客排队等候(顧客排隊等候)。
    - Cāntīng gōngyìng kǎo ròu, gùkè páiduì děnghòu.
    - The restaurant serves grilled meat, and customers wait in line.
### 肖像(肖像) → 像(像)

- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻(人)
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻(人) (rén, "person") + 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant") =
    - person + likeness ->
    - likeness/statue
  - **interpretation:** The character 像 combines the semantic component 亻(亻) (rén, "person") with 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant"), which serves as the phonetic component. This implies a representation or likeness of a person or object, akin to creating a statue or an image.
- **examples:**

  - 这尊雕像看起来像一位古代英雄(這尊雕像看起來像一位古代英雄)。
