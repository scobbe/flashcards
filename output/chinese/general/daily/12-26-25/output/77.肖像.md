## portrait
### xiàoxiàng
---
- **definition:** portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiào
    - resemble; similar
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; statue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 肖 (xiào, "resemble") + 像 (xiàng, "image") =
    - a resembling image ->
    - portrait
  - **interpretation:** A 肖像 (xiàoxiàng, "portrait") is an image that resembles the appearance of a person. The character 肖 (xiào, "resemble") signifies likeness, while 像 (xiàng, "image") denotes a representation or picture, aptly capturing the essence of a portrait.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )画(畫)了( )一( )幅( )精( )致(緻)的( )肖( )像( )。
    - Tā huà le yī fú jīngzhì de xiàoxiàng.

    - In the palace, the king's portrait hangs, showcasing the dynasty's glory.
