### lotus flower
---
- **pinyin:** lotus flower
- **components:**
  - 荷
    - hé
    - lotus; carry; load; dike
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; spend; flowery; fancy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 荷(荷) (hé, "lotus") + 花(花) (huā, "flower") — lotus flower
  - **interpretation:** The term 荷花 refers specifically to the flower of the lotus plant, a symbol of purity and beauty in Chinese culture. It combines the idea of the lotus, which grows in muddy waters but produces beautiful flowers, with the notion of a flower itself.
- **examples:**
  - 荷花在夏天盛开(荷花在夏天盛開)。
    - Héhuā zài xiàtiān shèngkāi.
    - Lotus flowers bloom in summer.
  - 我喜欢看荷花(我喜歡看荷花)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān kàn héhuā.
    - I enjoy looking at lotus flowers.
