## lotus flower
### héhuā

- **definition:** lotus flower
- **pinyin:** héhuā
- **components:**
  - 荷
    - hé
    - lotus; load
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 荷(荷) (hé, "lotus") + 花(花) (huā, "flower") =
    - lotus flower
  - **interpretation:** The term 荷花 (héhuā) meaning 'lotus flower' combines 荷(荷) (hé, "lotus") with 花(花) (huā, "flower"). This clearly denotes the flower of the lotus plant.
- **examples:**
  - 池塘里盛开着荷花(池塘裡盛開著荷花)。
    - Chítáng lǐ shèngkāi zhe héhuā.
    - Lotus flowers are blooming in the pond.
  - 夏天来临时(夏天來臨時)，荷花显得格外妖娆(荷花顯得格外妖嬈)。
    - Xiàtiān láilín shí, héhuā xiǎnde géwài yāoráo.
    - When summer arrives, lotus flowers appear exceptionally charming.
