## what
### shénme
---
- **definition:** what
- **pinyin:** shénme
- **components:**
  - 什
    - shén
    - what
  - 么(麼)
    - me
    - interrogative particle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 什(什) (shén, "what") + 么(麼) (me, "interrogative particle") indicates uncertainty or enquiry about something unspecified or unknown.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 什(什) (shén, "what") with 么(麼) (me, "interrogative particle") forms a phrase used to inquire about the identity, nature, or quality of something.
  - **simplification:** Modern characters have simplified forms, but the meanings remain the same.
- **examples:**
  - 你想知道什么(你想知道什麼)？
    - Nǐ xiǎng zhīdào shénme?
    - What do you want to know?
  - 你看到什么(你看到什麼)，感觉惊讶(感覺驚訝)。
    - Nǐ kàn dào shénme, gǎnjué jīngyà.
    - You saw something and felt surprised.
  - 她问我什么(她問我什麼)，我不知道(我不知道)。
    - Tā wèn wǒ shénme, wǒ bù zhīdào.
    - She asked me what, but I didn't know.
### 什么(什麼) → 什(什)
- **definition:** what
- **pinyin:** shén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 十
    - shí
    - ten
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人(人) (rén, "person") + 十(十) (shí, "ten") =
    - grouping of ten people ->
    - what
  - **interpretation:** 什(什) (shén, "what") combines the image of a person and the number ten, suggesting the idea of a group or collection, which ties to asking 'what' in the context of assembly or identifying members of a group.
- **examples:**
  - 你想吃什么(你想吃什麼)？
    - Nǐ xiǎng chī shénme?
    - What do you want to eat?
  - 他问我什么(他問我什麼)，我没说出来(我沒說出來)。
    - Tā wèn wǒ shénme, wǒ méi shuō chūlái.
    - He asked me what, but I didn't reveal anything.
  - 你觉得什么(你覺得什麼)，是生活中最重要的(是生活中最重要的)。
    - Nǐ juéde shénme, shì shēnghuó zhōng zuì zhòngyào de.
    - What do you think is the most important in life?
### 什么(什麼) → 什(什) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts side view of a standing man
  - **interpretation:** The character 人(人) (rén, "person") visually represents a human figure standing upright, highlighting key anatomical features like an arm and a leg. This pictogram captures the essence of a person in a simple, efficient form.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个好人(他是一個好人)。
    - Tā shì yīgè hǎorén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 很多人喜欢运动(很多人喜歡運動)，也喜欢音乐(也喜歡音樂)。
    - Hěnduō rén xǐhuān yùndòng, yě xǐhuān yīnyuè.
    - Many people like sports, and they also like music.
  - 人多力量大(人多力量大)。
    - Rénduō lìliàng dà.
    - There is strength in numbers.
### 什么(什麼) → 什(什) → 十(十)
- **definition:** ten
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a vertical stroke ->
    - later 一 (horizontal stroke) added =
    - cross ->
    - represents the number ten
  - **interpretation:** While 十(十) (shí, "ten") initially depicted a single stroke indicative of its meaning, the addition of 一(一) (yī, "one") to form a cross visualizes the concept of completeness or a full set of ten.
- **examples:**
  - 十个人来参加(十個人來參加)，宴会十分热闹(宴會十分熱鬧)。
    - Shí gè rén lái cānjiā, yànhuì shífēn rè nào.
    - Ten people came to join, and the party was very lively.
  - 数字十代表完美(數字十代表完美)。
    - Shùzì shí dàibiǎo wánměi.
    - The number ten represents perfection.
  - 十点钟快到了(十點鐘快到了)，大家纷纷赶路(大家紛紛趕路)。
    - Shí diǎn zhōng kuài dàoliǎo, dàjiā fēnfēn gǎn lù.
    - It will be ten o'clock soon, and everyone hurries along the road.
### 什么(什麼) → 么(麼)
- **definition:** interrogative particle
- **pinyin:** me
- **components:**
  - 麻
    - má
    - hemp
  - 幺
    - yāo
    - small, tiny
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 麻(麻) (má, 'hemp') + semantic: 幺(幺) (yāo, 'small, tiny') ->
    - interrogative marker
  - **interpretation:** The character 么(麼) (me, 'interrogative particle') combines the phonetic 麻(麻) (má, 'hemp') with the semantic component 幺(幺) (yāo, 'small, tiny') to form a marker used in questions, emphasizing its role as a diminutive or minimal inquiry.
  - **simplification:** 么(麼) simplified by using 幺(幺) (yāo, 'small, tiny') alone, eliminating 麻(麻) (má, 'hemp').
- **examples:**
  - 你想吃什么(你想吃什麼)？
    - Nǐ xiǎng chī shénme?
    - What do you want to eat?
  - 这是什么(這是什麼)？
    - Zhè shì shénme?
    - What is this?
  - 他到底想说什么(他到底想說什麼)？
    - Tā dàodǐ xiǎng shuō shénme?
    - What exactly does he want to say?
### 什么(什麼) → 么(麼) → 麻(麻)
- **definition:** hemp
- **pinyin:** má
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts hemp plant
  - **interpretation:** The character 麻(má, "hemp") visually represents a hemp plant with leaves and stalks, embodying its inherent meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他买了一捆麻(他買了一捆麻)，准备制作绳子(準備製作繩子)。
    - Tā mǎile yī kǔn má, zhǔnbèi zhìzuò shéngzi.
    - He bought a bundle of hemp and prepared to make rope.
  - 他们用麻(他們用麻)织出了一条漂亮的围巾(織出了一條漂亮的圍巾)。
    - Tāmen yòng má zhīchūle yītiáo piàoliang de wéijīn.
    - They wove a beautiful scarf using hemp.
### 什么(什麼) → 么(麼) → 幺(幺)
- **definition:** small, tiny
- **pinyin:** yāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a thin silk thread
  - **interpretation:** 幺(yāo, "small, tiny") is a pictogram representing a slender silk thread, emphasizing the delicate and fine nature associated with something small or tiny.
- **examples:**
  - 那幺小的鸟儿飞过来(那幺小的鳥兒飛過來)。
    - Nà yāo xiǎo de niǎo ér fēi guò lái.
    - That tiny bird flew over.
  - 她捧起那颗珠子(她捧起那顆珠子)，细看那幺小的工艺(細看那幺小的工藝)。
    - Tā pěng qǐ nà kē zhūzi, xì kàn nà yāo xiǎo de gōngyì.
    - She picked up the bead and examined its tiny craftsmanship.
