## call; shout
### jiào
---
- **definition:** call; shout
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 丩
    - jiū
    - (phonetic)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + 丩(丩) (jiū) =
    - sound production involving mouth ->
    - call/shout
  - **interpretation:** The character 叫 (jiào) uses 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") to indicate sounds made by the mouth, with 丩(丩) (jiū) providing the phonetic component. This combination effectively conveys the meaning of 'to call' or 'to shout'.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈叫我吃饭(媽媽叫我吃飯)。
    - Māmā jiào wǒ chīfàn.
    - Mother calls me to eat.
  - 老师叫学生安静(老師叫學生安靜)，课堂恢复秩序(課堂恢復秩序)。
    - Lǎoshī jiào xuéshēng ānjìng, kètáng huīfù zhìxù.
    - The teacher calls for the students to be quiet, and the class regains order.
  - 他在比赛时大声叫加油(他在比賽時大聲叫加油)。
    - Tā zài bǐsài shí dàshēng jiào jiāyóu.
    - He shouted 'come on' loudly during the game.
### 叫(叫) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") visually represents an open mouth, making it straightforward to connect to its meaning related to speaking and openings.
- **examples:**
  - 请张开你的口(請張開你的口)。
    - Qǐng zhāngkāi nǐ de kǒu.
    - Please open your mouth.
  - 他家有三口人(他家有三口人)，个个活泼可爱(個個活潑可愛)。
    - Tā jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, gègè huópō kě'ài.
    - His family consists of three people, and each one is lively and adorable.
  - 医生检查了他的口(醫生檢查了他的口)，确认健康无虞(確認健康無虞)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchále tā de kǒu, quèrèn jiànkāng wú yú.
    - The doctor examined his mouth and confirmed there were no health concerns.
