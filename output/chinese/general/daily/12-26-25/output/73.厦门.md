## Xiamen
### Xiàmén
---
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - mansion
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate; door; entrance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 厦(廈) (xià, "mansion") and 门(門) (mén, "gate") together form a name for the city of Xiamen.
  - **interpretation:** 厦门(廈門) (Xiàmén, "Xiamen") originates from the area's historical significance as a gateway to the sea. "厦" indicates the grand structures or mansions often found in the city, while "门" signifies its strategic importance as a gate or entrance point.
- **examples:**
  - 我从北京坐飞机去厦门(我從北京坐飛機去廈門)。
    - Wǒ cóng Běijīng zuò fēijī qù Xiàmén.
    - I take a plane from Beijing to Xiamen.
  - 厦门有美丽的海岸线(廈門有美麗的海岸線)，吸引了许多游客(吸引了許多遊客)。
    - Xiàmén yǒu měilì de hǎi'ànxiàn, xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Xiamen has a beautiful coastline, attracting many tourists.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈)
- **definition:** mansion
- **pinyin:** xià
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified and variant traditional form
  - **description:** simplified and variant traditional form of 廈(廈) (xià, "mansion")
  - **interpretation:** 厦(廈) (xià, "mansion") is both a simplified and variant traditional form. The character retains the meaning and essence of grandeur associated with a mansion.
- **examples:**
  - 那座老厦矗立在小镇中心(那座老廈矗立在小鎮中心)。
    - Nà zuò lǎo xià chùlì zài xiǎo zhèn zhōngxīn.
    - That old mansion stands in the center of the small town.
  - 他梦想拥有一座宽敞的厦(他夢想擁有一座寬敞的廈)，举办盛大的晚宴(舉辦盛大的晚宴)。
    - Tā mèngxiǎng yōngyǒu yī zuò kuānchǎng de xià, jǔbàn shèngdà de wǎnyàn.
    - He dreams of owning a spacious mansion, where he can host a grand banquet.
### 厦门(廈門) → 门(門)
- **definition:** gate; door; entrance
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 門(門) (mén, "gate") depicts a gate ->
    - simplified to 门(门) (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 门(門) (mén, "gate") represents the image of a double-sided gate. It simplifies to 门(门) but retains the depiction of an entrance or gateway.
  - **simplification:** The character 门(门) is a simplified form of 門(門), where the complex structure is replaced by a more straightforward representation while preserving the overall image of a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 学校门前站着一位老师(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - Xuéxiào mén qián zhànzhe yī wèi lǎoshī(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - A teacher stands at the school gate.
  - 请你从这扇门(請你從這扇門)进来(進來)，把鞋子(把鞋子)脱掉(脫掉)。
    - Qǐng nǐ cóng zhè shàn mén(請你從這扇門) jìnlái(進來)，bǎ xiézi(把鞋子) tuōdiào(脫掉)。
    - Please come in through this door and take off your shoes.
  - 夜深了(夜深了)，只有微弱的光从门缝里泄出(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - Yè shēn le(夜深了)，zhǐ yǒu wēiruò de guāng cóng mén fèng lǐ xiè chū(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - It's late at night, and only a faint light seeps through the gap in the door.
