## 厦门(廈門)
---
## 
### Xiamen
---
- **pinyin:** Xiamen
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - shà
    - building; tall structure; mansion; house
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate; door; entrance; family
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 厦(廈) (shà, "building;") + 门(門) (mén, "gate;") — building gate
  - **interpretation:** The name '厦门' can be interpreted as 'the gate of the building', which suggests a significant entrance or landmark in this coastal city.
- **examples:**
  - 我计划去厦门旅行(我計畫去廈門旅行)。
    - Wǒ jìhuà qù Xiàmén lǚxíng.
    - I plan to travel to Xiamen.
  - 厦门是一个美丽的城市(廈門是一個美麗的城市)。
    - Xiàmén shì yī gè měilì de chéngshì.
    - Xiamen is a beautiful city.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈)
- **definition:** building; tall structure; mansion; house
- **pinyin:** shà
- **components:**
  - 屋
    - wū
    - house
  - 夏
    - xià
    - summer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic element '屋' (roof) indicating a structure and the phonetic element '夏' (xià) which provides the sound.
  - **interpretation:** The connection is clear; a '屋' (wū, 'house') indicates a building, and the sound is derived from '夏' (xià). This evokes imagery of tall structures.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在一座高大豪华的廈(我住在一座高大豪華的廈)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài yī zuò gāodà háohuá de shà.
    - I live in a tall and luxurious mansion.
  - 这座廈的设计很美丽(這座廈的設計很美麗)。
    - Zhè zuò shà de shèjì hěn měilì.
    - The design of this building is very beautiful.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 屋(屋)
- **definition:** house
- **pinyin:** wū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** 屋(屋) (wū, "house") originally depicted a shelter with a roof over a space to live in.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of a protective shelter, signifying a place where people live and find security.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在一栋屋子(我住在一棟屋子)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài yī dòng wūzi.
    - I live in a building.
  - 这屋子很温暖(這屋子很溫暖)。
    - Zhè wūzi hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - This house is very warm.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 夏(夏)
- **definition:** summer
- **pinyin:** xià
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the season of summer, originally depicting the heat of the sun.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively evokes the warmth and brightness associated with summer, as it features elements suggesting the sun and heat.
- **examples:**
  - 夏天很热。(夏天很熱。)
    - xiàtiān hěn rè.
    - Summer is very hot.
  - 我喜欢夏季的阳光。(我喜歡夏季的陽光。)
    - wǒ xǐhuān xiàjì de yángguāng.
    - I love the sunlight of summer.
### 厦门(廈門) → 门(門)
- **definition:** gate; door; entrance; family
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a gate with two doors that can be opened or closed.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents an entrance or barrier, symbolizing access or separation.
- **examples:**
  - 这扇门很漂亮(這扇門很漂亮)。
    - Zhè shàn mén hěn piàoliang.
    - This door is very beautiful.
  - 门口有很多人(門口有很多人)。
    - Ménkǒu yǒu hěn duō rén.
    - There are many people at the entrance.
