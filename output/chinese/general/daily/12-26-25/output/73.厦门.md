## Xiamen
### Xiàmén
---
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - large building
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door; gate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 厦(廈) (xià, "large building") and 门(門) (mén, "door or gate") combine to denote a figurative 'gate' or 'entrance' associated with significant structures. Together, they form a proper noun for the city of Xiamen, a coastal city in China.
  - **interpretation:** 厦(廈) (xià, "large building") and 门(門) (mén, "door") imply an entrance or gateway, signifying Xiamen's historical role as a major port.
- **examples:**
  - 我想去厦门(廈門)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù Xiàmén.
    - I want to go to Xiamen.
  - 我听说厦门(廈門)，海滩很美(海灘很美)。
    - Wǒ tīng shuō Xiàmén, hǎi tān hěn měi.
    - I heard that Xiamen has a very beautiful beach.
  - 厦门(廈門)有许多美食(有許多美食)，吸引了大量游客(吸引了大量遊客)。
    - Xiàmén yǒu xǔduō měishí, xīyǐn le dàliàng yóukè.
    - Xiamen has many delicacies, which attract a large number of tourists.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈)
- **definition:** large building
- **pinyin:** xià
- **components:**
  - 广
    - guǎng
    - house on cliff
  - 夏
    - xià
    - great
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(广) (guǎng, "house on cliff") + 夏(夏) (xià, "great") =
    - great house on a cliff ->
    - large building
  - **interpretation:** The character 厦(廈) (xià, "large building") combines 广(广) (guǎng, "house on cliff") as the semantic component, suggesting shelter or a structure, with 夏(夏) (xià, "great") as both the phonetic and semantic element, reinforcing the idea of a grand or substantial building.
  - **simplification:** Traditional 廈 was simplified by reducing the structure under the house radical 广 to simplify the complex structure of 夏.
- **examples:**
  - 这座厦很宏伟(這座廈很宏偉)。
    - Zhè zuò xià hěn hóngwěi.
    - This large building is magnificent.
  - 他站在厦前(他站在廈前)，眺望远方景色(眺望遠方景色)。
    - Tā zhàn zài xià qián, tiàowàng yuǎnfāng jǐngsè.
    - He stands in front of the large building, gazing at the distant scenery.
  - 那间古老的厦吸引了众多游客(那間古老的廈吸引了眾多遊客)。
    - Nà jiān gǔlǎo de xià xīyǐn le zhòngduō yóukè.
    - That ancient large building attracts many tourists.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 广(廣)
- **definition:** house on cliff
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 黄(黃)
    - huáng
    - yellow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, 'broad') originally depicted a sloped, expansive building. It combines the semantic: 广 (guǎng, 'shelter') + phonetic: 黄 (huáng, 'yellow, huáng', providing the 'guǎng' sound) =
    - broad, expansive indicating a wide space under a roof.
  - **interpretation:** The character 广(廣) (guǎng, 'broad') effectively conveys the notion of a wide and expansive space, highlighting its original architectural context.
  - **simplification:** 广 (guǎng, 'broad') is derived from the cursive shorthand of the traditional character 廣 (guǎng, 'broad'). It omitted the complex phonetic 黄 (huáng, 'yellow'), simplifying to the component 广 (guǎng, 'shelter').
- **examples:**
  - 这个公园很广(這個公園很廣)。
    - Zhège gōngyuán hěn guǎng.
    - This park is very broad.
  - 村庄周围的田野广阔(村莊周圍的田野廣闊)，气候宜人(氣候宜人)。
    - Cūnzhuāng zhōuwéi de tiányě guǎngkuò, qìhòu yírén.
    - The fields around the village are vast, and the climate is pleasant.
  - 他的视野很广(他的視野很廣)。
    - Tā de shìyě hěn guǎng.
    - His perspective is broad.

### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 夏(夏)
- **definition:** great
- **pinyin:** xià
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 頁
    - yè
    - head; man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日(日) (rì, "sun") + 頁(頁) (yè, "head; man") ->
    - man under scorching sun ->
    - 夏 (xià, "summer")
  - **interpretation:** The character 夏 (xià, "summer") illustrates the idea of a person enduring the intense heat of the sun, aligning with the experience of summer. The evolution through different scripts reflects changes and adaptations such as replacing 頁(頁) (yè, "head; man") with other elements like 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot").
  - **simplification:** The current form of 夏 (xià) is a simplified version of the seal script, primarily by removing 𦥑 ("two hands") and the legs from the top part 頁(頁) (yè, "head").
- **examples:**
  - 他的才华夏(他的才華夏)。
    - Tā de cáihuá xià.
    - His talent is great.
  - 这家餐厅的菜品夏(這家餐廳的菜品夏)，服务态度也夏(服務態度也夏)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng de càipǐn xià, fúwù tàidù yě xià.
    - The restaurant’s dishes are great, and the service attitude is also great.

### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 夏(夏) → 頁(頁)
- **definition:** head; man
- **pinyin:** yè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 首(首) (shǒu, "head") + 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person") =
    - head of kneeling person, emphasizing head

It evolved to mean 'page' or 'leaf' as an alternative form of 葉 and retains its original meaning in the radical form.
  - **simplification:** 頁 was simplified to 页 by adopting a cursive script shorthand of the traditional form 頁.
- **examples:**

### 厦门(廈門) → 门(門)
- **definition:** door; gate
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a pair of doors on a hinge ->
    - entrance, gate
  - **interpretation:** The character 门(門) (mén, "gate") visually represents a pair of double doors, suggesting the concept of an entrance or gateway.
  - **simplification:** The simplified form 门(門) reduced the two discrete doors and central hinge to a more streamlined single component, capturing the essence of 'gate' in a simpler form.
- **examples:**
  - 那扇门很坚固(那扇門很堅固)。
    - Nà shàn mén hěn jiāngù.
    - That door is very sturdy.
  - 你推开门(你推開門)，走进房间(走進房間)。
    - Nǐ tuī kāi mén, zǒu jìn fángjiān.
    - You push the door open and walk into the room.
