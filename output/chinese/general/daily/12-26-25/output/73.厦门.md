## Xiamen
### Xiàmén
---
- **traditional:** 廈門
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - mansion
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate; door; entrance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 厦(廈) (xià, "mansion") and 门(門) (mén, "gate") together form a name for the city of Xiamen.
  - **interpretation:** 厦门(廈門) (Xiàmén, "Xiamen") originates from the area's historical significance as a gateway to the sea. "厦" indicates the grand structures or mansions often found in the city, while "门" signifies its strategic importance as a gate or entrance point.
- **examples:**
  - 我从(從)北京坐飞机(飛機)去厦门(廈門)。
    - Wǒ cóng Běijīng zuò fēijī qù Xiàmén.
    - I take a plane from Beijing to Xiamen.
  - 厦门(廈門)有美丽(麗)的海岸线(線)，吸引了许(許)多游(遊)客。
    - Xiàmén yǒu měilì de hǎi'ànxiàn, xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Xiamen has a beautiful coastline, attracting many tourists.

- **definition:** cliff

  - **description:** Depicts a cliff or overhang

  - **simplification:** 厂(hǎn, 'factory') is the simplified form of 廠, where the character was reduced to its more basic pictogram form for modern usage, representing both 'cliff' and 'factory'.
