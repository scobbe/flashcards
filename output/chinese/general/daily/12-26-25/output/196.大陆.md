## mainland
### dàlù
---
- **traditional:** 大陸
- **definition:** mainland
- **pinyin:** dàlù
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big; large") and 陆(陸) (lù, "land") combine to mean "mainland." This refers to a large expanse of land or continent, distinct from islands.
  - **interpretation:** The term 大陆(陸) (dàlù, "mainland") is often used in contrast to islands or smaller landforms, emphasizing the vastness and primary status of the landmass.
- **examples:**
  - 我来(來)自大陆(陸)。
    - Wǒ láizì dàlù.
    - I come from the mainland.
  - 大陆经济发(陸經濟發)展迅速。
    - Dàlù jīngjì fāzhǎn xùnsù.
    - The mainland's economy is developing rapidly.
  - 我们计划访问(們計劃訪問)大陆(陸)，探索这里(這裡)的文化。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà fǎngwèn dàlù, tànsuǒ zhèlǐ de wénhuà.
    - We plan to visit the mainland and explore the local culture.
### 大( )陆(陸) → 大
- **definition:** big; large
- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫 (fū, 'man')
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(dà) depicts a person facing forward, representing 'big' by suggesting the idea of someone standing prominently. Although often thought of as a person with outstretched arms, early scripts do not support this interpretation. It was originally the form of 夫 (fū, 'man') and was later phonetically borrowed for the meaning 'big.'
- **examples:**
  - 这个(這個)房子很大。
    - Zhè ge fángzi hěn dà.
    - This house is very big.
  - 大海波涛汹涌(濤洶湧)，展现(現)出大自然的力量。
    - Dàhǎi bōtāo xiōngyǒng, zhǎnxiàn chū dàzìrán de lìliàng.
    - The vast ocean waves surge, showcasing the power of nature.
  - 大人物登场(場)，留下传(傳)奇。
    - Dà rénwù dēngchǎng, liúxià chuánqí.
    - Great figures make an appearance, leaving behind legends.
### 大( )陆(陸) → 大 → 夫
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a variant form of 大 (dà, "big") representing an adult man with a hairpin ->
    - became associated with 夫 (fū, "man").
  - **interpretation:** 夫 (fū, "man") was visually similar to 大 (dà, "big") and was originally interchangeable. The addition of the horizontal stroke was interpreted as a hairpin, symbolizing adulthood for men.
- **examples:**
  - 此夫心怀(懷)大志。
    - Cǐ fū xīn huái dà zhì.
    - This man has lofty aspirations.
  - 他勤劳(勞)努力，堪称(稱)良夫。
    - Tā qín láo nǔ lì, kān chēng liáng fū.
    - He works diligently and is regarded as an exemplary man.
### 大( )陆(陸) → 陆(陸)
- **traditional:** 陸
- **definition:** land
- **pinyin:** lù
- **components:**
  - 阝(阜)
    - fù
    - mound
  - 坴
    - lù
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound') + Phonetic: 坴 (lù), used for phonetic value ->
    - land, elevated ground
  - **interpretation:** The character 陸(陆) (lù, 'land') combines the semantic component 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound'), indicating something related to earth or elevation, with 坴 (lù) for its phonetic representation. This effectively communicates the idea of elevated or significant land.
  - **simplification:** 陸 was simplified to 陆(陸) by retaining the 阝(阜) component for semantic value and using a simpler version of 坴 for phonetic association.
- **examples:**
  - 那艘船终将驶向陆(陸)。
    - Nà sōu chuán zhōng jiāng shǐ xiàng lù.
    - That boat will eventually sail towards land.
  - 他们(們)跨过(過)波涛(濤)，抵达陆(陸)。
    - Tāmen kuà guò bōtāo, dìdá lù.
    - They crossed the waves and reached the land.
### 大( )陆(陸) → 陆(陸) → 阝
- **definition:** mound
- **pinyin:** fù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a small mound or hillock.
  - **interpretation:** The character 阝 (fù, 'mound') visually represents a small elevation of land, resembling a hillside or embankment.
- **examples:**
  - 小山顶(頂)上耸(聳)立着阝(著阜)。

    - A small mound stands atop the hill.
  - 山坡上长满(長滿)了绿(綠)色的草丛(叢)，山丘边(邊)坐落着(著)一座古老的阝(阜)。
    - Shānpō shàng cháng mǎn le lǜsè de cǎocóng, shānqiū biān zuòluò zhe yī zuò gǔlǎo de fù.
    - The hillside is covered with green grass, and an ancient mound sits by the hill.
  - 远处(遠處)沙丘边隐约(邊隱約)可见(見)一堆阝(阜)，仿佛历(彷彿歷)史的见证(見證)。
    - Yuǎnchù shāqiū biān yǐnyuē kějiàn yī duī fù, fǎngfú lìshǐ de jiànzhèng.
    - In the distance, a pile of mounds is barely visible by the sand dunes, as if witnessing history.

### 大( )陆(陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴 → 圥 → 𡴆 → 屮

### 大( )陆(陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴 → 土

- **examples:**
