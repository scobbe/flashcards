## 大陆(大陸)
---
## 
### mainland
---
- **pinyin:** mainland
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large; huge; great
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land; shore; continent; mainland
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, 'big') + 陸(陸) (lù, 'land') — big land
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to a large landmass, emphasizing its size and significance. The combination of 'big' and 'land' accurately describes what a continent or mainland represents.
- **examples:**
  - 大陆是一个广阔的地方(大陸是一個廣闊的地方)。
    - Dàlù shì yī gè guǎngkuò de dìfāng.
    - The mainland is a vast place.
  - 我们要去大陆旅行(我們要去大陸旅行)。
    - Wǒmen yào qù Dàlù lǚxíng.
    - We are going to travel to the mainland.
### 大陆(大陸) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; large; huge; great
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person facing forward, representing the original form of 夫 (夫) (fū, 'man') with a phonetic borrowing for 'big'.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a broad presence, emphasizing size and importance, akin to a person with a commanding stance.
- **examples:**
  - 这个房子很大(這個房子很大)。
    - Zhège fángzi hěn dà.
    - This house is very big.
  - 我有一个大计划(我有一個大計畫)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè dà jìhuà.
    - I have a big plan.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸)
- **definition:** land; shore; continent; mainland
- **pinyin:** lù
- **components:**
  - 阝
    - fú
    - mound
  - 坴
    - lù
    - land (phonetic component)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Contains the semantic component 阝 (a radical associated with land or hills) and the phonetic component 坴, which contributes to the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a land-related radical and a phonetic element suggests the concept of land or shore, linking sound to meaning effectively.
  - **simplification:** The character simplifies by replacing the original 坴 with the component 击, which maintains a similar sound while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去陆地(我們去陸地)？
    - wǒmen qù lùdì?
    - Are we going to the mainland?
  - 这个岛上有很多陆生动物(這個島上有很多陸生動物)。
    - zhège dǎo shàng yǒu hěn duō lù shēng dòngwù.
    - There are many terrestrial animals on this island.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 阝(阜)
- **definition:** mound
- **pinyin:** fú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hill or mound.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually suggests the outline of a hill, representing elevation in the landscape.
- **examples:**
  - 阜山(阜山)，很壮观(很壯觀)。
    - fù shān, hěn zhuànguān.
    - Fushan is very spectacular.
  - 我们爬上阜(我們爬上阜)，俯瞰风景(俯瞰風景)。
    - wǒmen pá shàng fù, fǔkàn fēngjǐng.
    - We climbed the hill and overlooked the scenery.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴(墏)

- **components:**

  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component '土' (tǔ, 'earth') indicating land or ground, and the phonetic component '麦' (mài, 'wheat'), which contributes to the pronunciation.
- **examples:**

### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴(墏) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel.
  - **interpretation:** This visual representation of soil or clay conveys the material's fundamental nature in the earth, highlighting its role in forming and supporting life.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要好的土来种花(我們需要好的土來種花)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào hǎo de tǔ lái zhòng huā.
    - We need good soil to plant flowers.
  - 土可以用来建房子(土可以用來建房子)。
    - Tǔ kěyǐ yòng lái jiàn fángzi.
    - Soil can be used to build houses.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴(墏) → 麦(麥)
- **definition:** wheat
- **pinyin:** mài
- **components:**
  - 來
    - lái
    - to come
  - 夊
    - sū
    - footprint pointing down; to walk slowly
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 來 (lái, 'to come') + semantic 夊 (sū, 'footprint pointing down; to walk slowly') — represents the grain's association with wheat and its growth.
  - **interpretation:** This character evokes the concept of wheat coming from the ground as it grows, represented by 夊. The phonetic part suggests a connection to its historical meaning.
  - **simplification:** 麦(麥) simplified by stroke reduction, eliminating the complex components of 夊.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃麦片(我喜歡吃麥片)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī mài piàn.
    - I like to eat oats.
  - 小麦是主食之一(小麥是主食之一)。
    - Xiǎo mài shì zhǔshí zhī yī.
    - Wheat is one of the staple foods.
  - 这麦子收成很好(這麥子收成很好)。
    - Zhè mài zi shōuchéng hěn hǎo.
    - This wheat harvest is very good.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴(墏) → 麦(麥) → 來(來)
- **definition:** to come
- **pinyin:** lái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts wheat with the middle line representing the ear, upward lines for leaves, and downward lines for stem and roots.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents wheat, which historically symbolizes nourishment and growth. This connection is reflected in its use for 'to come', suggesting a bringing forth or arrival of sustenance.
- **examples:**
  - 他要來了(他要來了)，我们应该准备好(我們應該準備好)。
    - tā yào lái le, wǒmen yīnggāi zhǔnbèi hǎo.
    - He is coming, we should be ready.
  - 请她來(請她來)！
    - qǐng tā lái!
    - Please have her come!
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸) → 坴(墏) → 麦(麥) → 夊(夊)
- **definition:** footprint pointing down; to walk slowly
- **pinyin:** sū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a footprint pointing downward, representing slow walking.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a downward-facing footprint intuitively signifies a slow, deliberate motion, as if someone is carefully walking or trailing their feet.
- **examples:**
  - 我慢慢走(我慢慢走)，不着急。
    - Wǒ màn màn zǒu, bù zhuó jí.
    - I walk slowly, without rushing.
  - 他的脚步声很轻(他的腳步聲很輕)。
    - Tā de jiǎobù shēng hěn qīng.
    - His footsteps are very light.
