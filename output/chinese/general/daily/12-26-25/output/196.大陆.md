## mainland
### dàlù
---
- **definition:** mainland
- **pinyin:** dàlù
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big; large") and 陆(陸) (lù, "land") combine to mean "mainland." This refers to a large expanse of land or continent, distinct from islands.
  - **interpretation:** The term 大陆(大陸) (dàlù, "mainland") is often used in contrast to islands or smaller landforms, emphasizing the vastness and primary status of the landmass.
- **examples:**
  - 我来自大陆(我來自大陸)。
    - Wǒ láizì dàlù.
    - I come from the mainland.
  - 大陆经济发展迅速(大陸經濟發展迅速)。
    - Dàlù jīngjì fāzhǎn xùnsù.
    - The mainland's economy is developing rapidly.
  - 我们计划访问大陆(我們計劃訪問大陸)，探索这里的文化(探索這裡的文化)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà fǎngwèn dàlù, tànsuǒ zhèlǐ de wénhuà.
    - We plan to visit the mainland and explore the local culture.
### 大陆(大陸) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; large
- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫(夫) (fū, 'man')
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(dà) depicts a person facing forward, representing 'big' by suggesting the idea of someone standing prominently. Although often thought of as a person with outstretched arms, early scripts do not support this interpretation. It was originally the form of 夫(夫) (fū, 'man') and was later phonetically borrowed for the meaning 'big.'
- **examples:**

    - Zhè ge fángzi hěn dà.

  - 大海波涛汹涌(大海波濤洶湧)，展现出大自然的力量(展現出大自然的力量)。
    - Dàhǎi bōtāo xiōngyǒng, zhǎnxiàn chū dàzìrán de lìliàng.
    - The vast ocean waves surge, showcasing the power of nature.
  - 大人物登场(大人物登場)，留下传奇(留下傳奇)。
    - Dà rénwù dēngchǎng, liúxià chuánqí.
    - Great figures make an appearance, leaving behind legends.
### 大陆(大陸) → 大(大) → 夫(夫)
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a variant form of 大(大) (dà, "big") representing an adult man with a hairpin ->
    - became associated with 夫(夫) (fū, "man").
  - **interpretation:** 夫(夫) (fū, "man") was visually similar to 大(大) (dà, "big") and was originally interchangeable. The addition of the horizontal stroke was interpreted as a hairpin, symbolizing adulthood for men.
- **examples:**

    - He works diligently and is regarded as an exemplary man.
### 大陆(大陸) → 陆(陸)
- **definition:** land
- **pinyin:** lù
- **components:**
  - 阝(阜)
    - fù
    - mound
  - 坴
    - lù
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound') + Phonetic: 坴(坴) (lù), used for phonetic value ->
    - land, elevated ground
  - **interpretation:** The character 陸(陆) (lù, 'land') combines the semantic component 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound'), indicating something related to earth or elevation, with 坴(坴) (lù) for its phonetic representation. This effectively communicates the idea of elevated or significant land.
  - **simplification:** 陸(陸) was simplified to 陆(陸) by retaining the 阝(阜) component for semantic value and using a simpler version of 坴(坴) for phonetic association.
- **examples:**
  - 那艘船终将驶向陆(陸)。
    - Nà sōu chuán zhōng jiāng shǐ xiàng lù.
    - That boat will eventually sail towards land.
  - 他们跨过波涛(他們跨過波濤)，抵达陆(陸)。
    - Tāmen kuà guò bōtāo, dìdá lù.
    - They crossed the waves and reached the land.
