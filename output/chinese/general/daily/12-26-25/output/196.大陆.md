## mainland
### dàlù
---
- **definition:** mainland
- **pinyin:** dàlù
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big; large") and 陆(陸) (lù, "land") combine to mean "mainland." This refers to a large expanse of land or continent, distinct from islands.
  - **interpretation:** The term 大陆(大陸) (dàlù, "mainland") is often used in contrast to islands or smaller landforms, emphasizing the vastness and primary status of the landmass.
- **examples:**
  - 我来自大陆(我來自大陸)。
    - Wǒ láizì dàlù.
    - I come from the mainland.
  - 大陆经济发展迅速(大陸經濟發展迅速)。
    - Dàlù jīngjì fāzhǎn xùnsù.
    - The mainland's economy is developing rapidly.
  - 我们计划访问大陆(我們計劃訪問大陸)，探索这里的文化(探索這裡的文化)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà fǎngwèn dàlù, tànsuǒ zhèlǐ de wénhuà.
    - We plan to visit the mainland and explore the local culture.
