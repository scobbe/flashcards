## 崾岘(崾岘)
---
## 
### Yaoxian (place name)
---
- **pinyin:** Yaoxian (place name)
- **components:**
  - 崾
    - yá
    - rocky; rugged
  - 岘
    - xiàn
    - mountain; hill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 崾(崾) (yá, "rocky"); 岘(岘) (xiàn, "mountain") — rocky mountain
  - **interpretation:** 崾岘 refers to a place characterized by its rocky terrain. The combination of a description of the landscape with a term for mountain makes the name significant for its geographical features.
- **examples:**
  - 崾岘在西南方。(崾岘在西南方。)
    - Yáoxiàn zài xīnán fāng.
    - Yaoxian is located in the southwest.
  - 我们去过崾岘。(我們去過崾岘。)
    - Wǒmen qù guò Yáoxiàn.
    - We have been to Yaoxian.
### 崾岘(崾岘) → 崾(崾)
- **definition:** rocky; rugged
- **pinyin:** yá
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a rocky landscape with abrupt peaks and slopes.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents rugged terrain, implying a landscape dominated by uneven rock formations.
- **examples:**
  - 崾崾而立(崾崾而立)，令人敬畏。
    - yáyá ér lì，lìng rén jìngwèi。
    - The rugged cliffs stand tall, instilling awe.
  - 这座山崾(這座山崾)，攀登不易。
    - zhè zuò shān yá，pāndēng bù yì。
    - This rocky mountain is difficult to climb.
### 崾岘(崾岘) → 岘(岘)
- **definition:** mountain; hill
- **pinyin:** xiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a mountain peak.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a mountain peak, evoking the notion of a hill or high landform.
- **examples:**
  - 岘山(岘山)
    - xiàn shān
    - Mount Xian
  - 岘港(岘港)
    - xiàn gǎng
    - Da Nang, Vietnam
  - 岘俱(岘俱)
    - xiàn jù
    - A literary reference to mountains.
