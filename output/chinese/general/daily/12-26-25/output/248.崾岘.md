## Yaoxian (place name)
### yǎoxiàn
---
- **definition:** Yaoxian (place name)
- **pinyin:** yǎoxiàn
- **components:**
  - 崾
    - yǎo
    - ridge
  - 岘(縣)
    - xiàn
    - mountain pass; county
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 崾(崾) (yǎo, "ridge") and 岘(縣) (xiàn, "mountain pass") form the place name, referring to a specific location known for its geographical features.
  - **interpretation:** The name 崾岘(yǎoxiàn) describes a place characterized by its ridges and mountain passes, likely indicating the terrain or landscape prominent in this area.
  - **simplification:** 岘 is used for both simplified and traditional versions of this character.
- **examples:**
  - 我今年去崾岘(我今年去崾峴)旅行。
    - Wǒ jīnnián qù yǎoxiàn lǚxíng.
    - I will travel to Yaoxian this year.
  - 我听说崾岘(我聽說崾峴)风景秀丽(風景秀麗)，吸引众多游客(吸引眾多遊客)。
    - Wǒ tīngshuō yǎoxiàn fēngjǐng xiùlì, xīyǐn zhòngduō yóukè.
    - I heard that Yaoxian has beautiful scenery, attracting many tourists.
  - 古人赞美崾岘(古人讚美崾峴)美景(美景)，今人留恋旧游(今人留戀舊遊)。
    - Gǔrén zànměi yǎoxiàn měijǐng, jīnrén liúliàn jiùyóu.
    - Ancient people praised the beautiful scenery of Yaoxian, and modern people fondly remember their past visits.
### 崾岘(崾縣) → 崾(崾)
- **definition:** ridge
- **pinyin:** yǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 山(山) (shān, 'mountain') + phonetic: 妖(妖) (yāo, 'sprite') =
    - combination of mountain and phonetic element suggesting sound
  - **interpretation:** 崾(崾) (yǎo, 'ridge') combines 山(山) (shān, 'mountain'), indicating the terrain-related meaning, with 妖(妖) (yāo, 'sprite') to provide the pronunciation. This reflects a place feature often associated with rugged or mystical terrain.
- **examples:**
  - 远处的崾隐约可见(遠處的崾隱約可見)。
    - Yuǎnchù de yǎo yǐnyuē kějiàn.
    - The distant ridge can be faintly seen.
  - 游客沿着山路向崾攀登(遊客沿著山路向崾攀登)，欣赏壮丽风光(欣賞壯麗風光)。
    - Yóukè yánzhe shānlù xiàng yǎo pāndēng, xīnshǎng zhuànglì fēngguāng.
    - Tourists climb along the mountain road towards the ridge, admiring the magnificent scenery.
  - 崾矗立在山巅上(崾矗立在山巔上)，映衬着落日余晖(映襯著落日餘暉)。
    - Yǎo chùlì zài shāndiān shàng, yìngchèn zhe luòrì yúhuī.
    - The ridge stands tall on the mountain peak, highlighted by the afterglow of the setting sun.
### 崾岘(崾縣) → 岘(縣)
- **definition:** mountain pass; county
- **pinyin:** xiàn
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 糸
    - sī
    - silk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, "eye") + 木(木) (mù, "tree") + 糸(糸) (sī, "silk") =
    - to hang something with a string using a tree and eye ->
    - administrative division
  - **interpretation:** The components of 目(目) (mù, "eye"), 木(木) (mù, "tree"), and 糸(糸) (sī, "silk") creatively symbolize something hanging, which through phonetic borrowing, came to represent an administrative area. This illustrates the concept of suspension metaphorically.
  - **simplification:** 岘(岘) as a character related to 縣(縣) due to its usage in referring to a mountain pass and not in the context of hanging or administrative roles, shows no specific simplification from the original compound.
- **examples:**
  - 边境的岘风光旖旎(邊境的縣風光旖旎)。
    - Biānjìng de xiàn fēngguāng yǐnǐ.
    - The scenic vista of the border county is enchanting.
  - 我住在古老的岘(我住在古老的縣)，那里有许多传统习俗(那裡有許多傳統習俗)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài gǔlǎo de xiàn, nàlǐ yǒu xǔduō chuántǒng xísú.
    - I live in an ancient county, where many traditional customs thrive.
  - 登山客在崎岖的岘(登山客在崎嶇的縣)，停下来欣赏远处的美景(停下來欣賞遠處的美景)。
    - Dēngshānkè zài qíqū de xiàn, tíng xiàlái xīnshǎng yuǎnchù de měijǐng.
    - The climbers, on the rugged mountain pass, paused to admire the distant beautiful scenery.

### 崾岘(崾縣) → 岘(縣) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom

- **examples:**

    - He walked into the deep forest and saw a solitary tree standing by the roadside.
### 崾岘(崾縣) → 岘(縣) → 糸(糸)
- **definition:** silk
- **pinyin:** sī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a skein of silk
  - **interpretation:** The character 糸(糸) (sī, "silk") visually represents twisted threads of silk, capturing the appearance and texture of a silk skein. This depiction makes intuitive sense for representing the concept of silk due to the familiar form of such threads in historical textile production.
- **examples:**
  - 工匠用糸织布(工匠用糸織布)，制作精美服饰(製作精美服飾)。

  - 博物馆展出古代糸织品(博物館展出古代糸織品)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū gǔdài sī zhī pǐn.
    - The museum exhibits ancient silk-woven artifacts.
  - 在传统纺织技艺中(在傳統紡織技藝中)，糸扮演了重要的角色(糸扮演了重要的角色)。
    - Zài chuántǒng fǎngzhī jìyì zhōng, sī bànyǎn le zhòngyào de juésè.
