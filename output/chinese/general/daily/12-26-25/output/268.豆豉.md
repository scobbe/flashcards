## 
### fermented black beans
---
- **pinyin:** fermented black beans
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean; pulse; legume; seed
  - 豉
    - chǐ
    - fermented; sauce; condiment; paste
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") + 豉(豉) (chǐ, "fermented") — bean fermented
  - **interpretation:** The term 豆豉 refers to fermented soybeans, commonly used in Chinese cuisine. This combination makes sense as it describes the key ingredients and their preparation method.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用豆豉做菜。(我喜歡用豆豉做菜。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng dòuchǐ zuò cài.
    - I like to cook with fermented black beans.
  - 豆豉可以增加菜肴的风味。(豆豉可以增加菜餚的風味。)
    - Dòuchǐ kěyǐ zēngjiā cài yáo de fēngwèi.
    - Fermented black beans can enhance the flavor of dishes.
### 豆豉(豆豉) → 豆(豆)
- **definition:** bean; pulse; legume; seed
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a container, originally representing a type of seed.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a container that holds seeds, evoking the idea of beans that grow inside it.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃豆腐(我喜歡吃豆腐)，它很美味(它很好吃)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dòufu, tā hěn měiwèi.
    - I like to eat tofu; it is very delicious.
  - 豆子(豆子)很营养(很營養)。
    - Dòuzi hěn yíngyǎng.
    - Beans are very nutritious.
### 豆豉(豆豉) → 豉(豉)
- **definition:** fermented; sauce; condiment; paste
- **pinyin:** chǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 豉 represents fermented soybeans, with its structure suggesting the association with food and fermentation.
  - **interpretation:** Fermented soybeans are a significant ingredient in various Asian cuisines, especially in sauces and dishes, which relates directly to the character's meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃豆豉(我喜歡吃豆豉)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dòuchǐ！
    - I love eating fermented soybeans!
  - 豆豉可以用来做调料(豆豉可以用來做調料)。
    - Dòuchǐ kěyǐ yòng lái zuò tiáoliào。
    - Fermented soybeans can be used as a condiment.
