## fermented black beans
### dòuchǐ
---
- **definition:** fermented black beans
- **pinyin:** dòuchǐ
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 豉
    - chǐ
    - fermented; ferment soybeans
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") refers to the general category of beans, while 豉(豉) (chǐ, "fermented") refers to the process of fermentation, which is essential to making this food product. Together, 豆豉(豆豉) (dòuchǐ, "fermented black beans") describes the fermented form of beans used as a seasoning.
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because it describes a type of bean, 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean"), that has undergone the fermentation process, 豉(豉) (chǐ, "fermented"), resulting in fermented black beans that are commonly used in Chinese cuisine.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈炒豆豉鱼(媽媽炒豆豉魚)。
    - Māmā chǎo dòuchǐ yú.
    - Mother stir-fries fish with fermented black beans.
  - 这道菜有豆豉提味(這道菜有豆豉提味)，非常美味(非常美味)。
    - Zhè dào cài yǒu dòuchǐ tíwèi, fēicháng měiwèi.
    - This dish uses fermented black beans to enhance the flavor, making it very delicious.
