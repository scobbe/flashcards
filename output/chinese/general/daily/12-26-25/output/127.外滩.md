### the Bund
---
- **pinyin:** the Bund
- **components:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside; foreign; external; outer
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach; sandbank; shoal; flatland
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 外(外) (wài, "outside") + 滩(灘) (tān, "beach") — outside beach
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a place that is situated along the waterfront or riverside, emphasizing its position as a notable external area. The combination highlights the Bund's location by the river and its geographical significance.
- **examples:**
  - 我明天去外滩散步(我明天去外灘散步)。
    - wǒ míngtiān qù wàitān sànbù.
    - I will go for a walk at the Bund tomorrow.
  - 外滩的夜景非常美(外灘的夜景非常美)。
    - wàitān de yèjǐng fēicháng měi.
    - The night view of the Bund is very beautiful.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外)
- **definition:** outside; foreign; external; outer
- **pinyin:** wài
- **components:**
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - divine
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 卜 (bǔ, 'divine') + phonetic 月 (yuè, 'moon') — associated with the idea of 'outside' as the visible part of the moon.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines a divination element with the moon, suggesting what is observable from the outside perspective.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢在外面玩(我喜歡在外面玩)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài wàimiàn wán.
    - I like to play outside.
  - 外面天气很好(外面天氣很好)。
    - Wàimiàn tiānqì hěn hǎo.
    - The weather outside is nice.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外) → 卜(卜)
- **definition:** divine
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the act of divination, often represented as lines or marks indicating foretelling of the future.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively connects to the concept of seeking knowledge about the future through marks or signs, symbolizing the ancient practice of casting lots or using oracle bones.
- **examples:**
  - 古人通过卜卦进行预测(古人透過卜卦進行預測)。
    - Gǔ rén tōngguò bǔgà jìnxíng yùcè.
    - Ancient people predicted through divination.
  - 卜辞是最早的汉字之一(卜辭是最早的漢字之一)。
    - Bǔcí shì zuì zǎo de hànzì zhī yī.
    - The divination inscriptions are among the earliest Chinese characters.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外) → 月(月)
- **definition:** moon
- **pinyin:** yuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the shape of the moon.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles the crescent shape of the moon, which is fitting since its meaning refers to the moon itself.
- **examples:**
  - 今晚有月亮(今晚有月亮)。
    - jīn wǎn yǒu yuè liàng，
    - There is a moon tonight.
  - 这个月要去旅行(這個月要去旅行)。
    - zhè ge yuè yào qù lǚ xíng，
    - I will travel this month.
  - 我们在月光下散步(我們在月光下散步)。
    - wǒ men zài yuè guāng xià sàn bù。
    - We walked under the moonlight.
### 外滩(外灘) → 滩(灘)
- **definition:** beach; sandbank; shoal; flatland
- **pinyin:** tān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified
  - **description:** Simplified from 灘 ( tán, "beach; sandbank; shoal; flatland ").
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as it streamlines the original character while retaining its meaning associated with flat, sandy areas found near water.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved replacing the complex character 難 (nán, "difficult") with its simpler variant 难 (nàn, "difficult"). This choice was made to create a more accessible character for common usage.
- **examples:**
  - 这个滩适合游泳。(這個灘適合游泳。)
    - zhè ge tān shì hé yóu yǒng.
    - This beach is suitable for swimming.
  - 我们在滩上野餐。(我們在灘上野餐。)
    - wǒmen zài tān shàng yě cān.
    - We had a picnic on the sandbank.
