## the Bund
### wàitān
---
- **definition:** the Bund
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **components:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside; external; foreign; in addition
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach; shoal; sandbank; river shoal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 外(outside) + 滩/灘(sandbank; shoal) = outer riverbank → the Bund.
  - **interpretation:** Literally “outer sandbank,” originally the riverbank outside Shanghai’s old city wall. Now a proper noun for the famous waterfront along the Huangpu River.
  - **simplification:** 灘 → 滩 by simplifying the phonetic 難 to 难 under the water radical, reducing strokes while keeping the water semantic.
- **examples:**
  - 我们晚上去外滩看夜景(我們晚上去外灘看夜景)。
    - Wǒmen wǎnshang qù Wàitān kàn yèjǐng.
    - We’re going to the Bund at night to see the city lights.
  - 外滩沿着黄浦江(外灘沿著黃浦江)，有很多历史建筑(有很多歷史建築)。
    - Wàitān yánzhe Huángpǔ Jiāng, yǒu hěn duō lìshǐ jiànzhù.
    - The Bund runs along the Huangpu River and has many historic buildings.
  - 在外滩可以乘坐游船(在外灘可以乘坐遊船)，俯瞰浦东的天际线(俯瞰浦東的天際線)。
    - Zài Wàitān kěyǐ chéngzuò yóuchuán, fǔkàn Pǔdōng de tiānjìxiàn.
    - At the Bund you can take a cruise and look over the Pudong skyline.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外)
- **definition:** outside; external; foreign; in addition
- **pinyin:** wài
- **components:**
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - to divine; divination
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon; month
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 卜 + phonetic: 月 (OC *ŋod).
  - **interpretation:** 卜 contributes the semantic idea (originally associated with divination), while 月 provides the sound, aligning with the Old Chinese reconstruction. The semantic link to “outside” is not transparent in the phono-semantic analysis; some scholars propose an alternative ideogrammic reading involving the moon’s partial phases, but the mainstream view treats 月 here as phonetic.
- **examples:**
  - 我们对外贸易增长很快(我們對外貿易增長很快)。
    - Wǒmen duìwài màoyì zēngzhǎng hěn kuài.
    - Our foreign trade is growing very quickly.
  - 他住在城外的一个小村子(他住在城外的一個小村子)。
    - Tā zhù zài chéngwài de yí gè xiǎo cūnzi.
    - He lives in a small village outside the city.
  - 这家餐厅接待很多外国游客(這家餐廳接待很多外國遊客)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng jiēdài hěn duō wàiguó yóukè.
    - This restaurant serves many foreign tourists.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外) → 卜(蔔)
- **definition:** to divine; divination
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram (象形): crack in an oracle bone used for divination.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the branching crack made on turtle shell or bone during pyromancy, whose patterns were read as omens. The simple vertical line with a side notch mirrors the appearance of such fissures.
- **examples:**
  - 我们请他卜一卦(我們請他卜一卦)。
    - Wǒmen qǐng tā bǔ yī guà.
    - We asked him to divine a hexagram.
  - 他会卜吉凶(他會卜吉凶)。
    - Tā huì bǔ jíxiōng.
    - He can divine whether things will be auspicious or inauspicious.
  - 用龟甲卜问前途(用龜甲卜問前途)。
    - Yòng guījiǎ bǔwèn qiántú.
    - They use turtle shells to divine the future.
### 外滩(外灘) → 外(外) → 月(月)
- **definition:** moon; month
- **pinyin:** yuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): pictographic representation of a crescent moon; historically related to 夕 and once interchangeable; unrelated to 多, where it depicts two pieces of meat stylized as 夕.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a crescent, so the meanings ‘moon’ and by extension ‘month’ arise directly from the image. 夕 was once used in similar contexts but later diverged in meaning, while the lookalike element in 多 is a stylized ‘meat’ component and not this graph.
- **examples:**
  - 今天是这个月的第一天(今天是這個月的第一天)。
    - Jīntiān shì zhège yuè de dì yī tiān.
    - Today is the first day of this month.
  - 下个月我想去旅行(下個月我想去旅行)。
    - Xià gè yuè wǒ xiǎng qù lǚxíng.
    - I want to travel next month.
  - 今晚是满月(今晚是滿月)，月光照亮了山谷(月光照亮了山谷)。
    - Jīn wǎn shì mǎn yuè, yuè guāng zhàoliàng le shān gǔ.
    - Tonight is a full moon, and the moonlight illuminates the valley.
### 外滩(外灘) → 滩(灘)
- **definition:** beach; shoal; sandbank; river shoal
- **pinyin:** tān
- **components:**
  - 难(難)
    - nán
    - difficult; hardship
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 氵 (water) + phonetic: 難 (simplified 难)
  - **interpretation:** The water radical signals a meaning connected to bodies of water and shorelines. 難/难 serves as the phonetic element (historically related in sound), while the watery radical carries the sense of shoals, sandbanks, and beaches.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by replacing the phonetic 難 with its simplified form 难 to reduce strokes; the water radical was retained, so the meaning stays the same.
- **examples:**
  - 河水退去后露出沙滩(河水退去後露出沙灘)。
    - Héshuǐ tuìqù hòu lùchū shātān.
    - After the river recedes, a sandbank emerges.
  - 我们在海滩上散步(我們在海灘上散步)。
    - Wǒmen zài hǎitān shàng sànbù.
    - We take a walk on the beach.
  - 船靠近河滩时要小心浅滩(船靠近河灘時要小心淺灘)。
    - Chuán kàojìn hétān shí yào xiǎoxīn qiǎntān.
    - Be careful of shallow shoals when the boat nears the river shoal.
### 外滩(外灘) → 滩(灘) → 难(難)
- **definition:** difficult; hardship
- **pinyin:** nán
- **components:**
  - 又
    - yòu
    - again; right hand
  - 隹
    - zhuī
    - short-tailed bird
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 隹 (‘bird’) + phonetic: 暵 (abbreviated); later phonetically borrowed for other senses.
  - **interpretation:** 隹 contributes the idea of a bird, reflecting that 難 originally named a kind of bird, while the element from 暵 provided the sound nán. The word was later borrowed to express the sense ‘difficult’, and modern 难 is the simplified graphic form of that earlier character.
  - **simplification:** Stroke reduction by replacing the complex left component of 難 with 又, an unorthodox variant recorded since the Ming; proposed in ROC’s 1935 list (retracted 1936) and officially adopted by the PRC in 1956.
- **examples:**
  - 这个问题很难(這個問題很難)。
    - Zhège wèntí hěn nán.
    - This problem is difficult.
  - 学起来不难(學起來不難)。
    - Xué qǐlái bù nán.
    - It isn’t hard to learn.
  - 别为难他(別為難他)。
    - Bié wéinán tā.
    - Don’t make things difficult for him.
### 外滩(外灘) → 滩(灘) → 难(難) → 又(又)
- **definition:** again; right hand
- **pinyin:** yòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram – a right hand; original form of 右; unrelated to 𠬤 (> 译). Often written as 彐 in many modern characters (e.g., 事, 聿, 雪, 急, 爭, 尹, 建).
  - **interpretation:** Early graphs depict a right hand, which later gave rise to 右 ‘right (side)’. The adverbial sense ‘again’ developed later by semantic extension. In many compounds a similar shape is written as 彐, but that is a graphic convention rather than a direct historical component here.
- **examples:**
  - 他又来了(他又來了)。
    - Tā yòu lái le.
    - He came again.
  - 请向右转(請向右轉)。
    - Qǐng xiàng yòu zhuǎn.
    - Please turn right.
  - 她又买了一本书(她又買了一本書)。
    - Tā yòu mǎi le yī běn shū.
    - She bought another book.
### 外滩(外灘) → 滩(灘) → 难(難) → 隹(隹)
- **definition:** short-tailed bird
- **pinyin:** zhuī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a short-tailed bird (e.g., a sparrow). Compare 鳥.

    - Yǔ “niǎo” xiāngbǐ, “zhuī” qiángdiào wěiba duǎn, xiǎo ér mǐnjié.
