## the Bund
### wàitān
---
- **definition:** the Bund
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **components:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach; bank
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 外(外) (wài, "outside") + 滩(灘) (tān, "beach; bank") =
    - an area outside the main land that serves as a bank or beach, the Bund in Shanghai is historically an area along the riverbank.
  - **interpretation:** The word 外滩(外灘) (wàitān, "the Bund") refers to a riverbank area beyond the city's core. Historically, the Bund in Shanghai is located along the river and is renowned for its financial and commercial significance.
  - **simplification:** 滩(灘) with simpler component
- **examples:**
  - 游客在外滩(外灘)，感受历史的变迁(感受歷史的變遷)。
    - Yóukè zài wàitān, gǎnshòu lìshǐ de biànqiān.
    - Tourists at the Bund feel the changes of history.
  - 夜幕降临时(夜幕降臨時)，外滩展现迷人景致(外灘展現迷人景致)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín shí, wàitān zhǎnxiàn mírén jǐngzhì.
    - When night falls, the Bund displays its enchanting scenery.
