- **pinyin:** Starbucks
- **components:**
  - 星
    - xīng
    - star; celestial body; fame; celestial
  - 巴克
    - bākè
    - buck; slang for dollar; hold fast; to cling to
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 星(星) (xīng, "star") + 巴克(巴克) (bākè, "buck") — star buck
  - **interpretation:** This term combines 'star' and 'buck' to represent a global brand known for its coffee. The 'star' implies excellence and recognition, while 'buck' relates to currency, reinforcing its commercial aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝星巴克的咖啡(我喜歡喝星巴克的咖啡)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē xīng bākè de kāfēi.
    - I like to drink Starbucks coffee.
  - 星巴克的店在街角(星巴克的店在街角)。
    - Xīng bākè de diàn zài jiē jiǎo.
    - The Starbucks store is on the corner.
