## scrambled eggs
### chǎodàn
---
- **definition:** scrambled eggs
- **pinyin:** chǎodàn
- **components:**
  - 炒
    - chǎo
    - stir-fry
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 炒(炒) (chǎo, "stir-fry") + 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") =
    - scrambled eggs ->
    - scrambled eggs
  - **interpretation:** 炒(炒) (chǎo, "stir-fry") refers to the cooking method, while 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") indicates the main ingredient. Together, they describe a dish of eggs cooked using this technique.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃炒蛋(我喜歡吃炒蛋)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī chǎodàn.
    - I like to eat scrambled eggs.
  - 早上起床后(早上起床後)，我会做炒蛋(我會做炒蛋)。
    - Zǎoshang qǐchuáng hòu, wǒ huì zuò chǎodàn.
    - After waking up in the morning, I make scrambled eggs.
  - 他在厨房快速炒蛋(他在廚房快速炒蛋)，味道非常好(味道非常好)。
    - Tā zài chúfáng kuàisù chǎodàn, wèidào fēicháng hǎo.
    - He quickly makes scrambled eggs in the kitchen, and they taste very good.
### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 炒(炒)
- **definition:** stir-fry
- **pinyin:** chǎo
- **components:**
  - 火
    - huǒ
    - fire
  - 少
    - shǎo
    - few
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 火(火) (huǒ, "fire") + phonetic: 少(少) (shǎo, "few") =
    - fire and cooking with a few ingredients ->
    - stir-fry
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic component 火(火) (huǒ, "fire") indicating cooking with flames, and the phonetic component 少(少) (shǎo, "few"), suggesting the sound and the idea of cooking quickly with minimal ingredients.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈喜欢炒蛋(媽媽喜歡炒蛋)。
    - Māmā xǐhuān chǎo dàn。
    - Mom likes stir-fried eggs.
  - 先把材料准备好(先把材料準備好)，再开始炒菜(再開始炒菜)。
    - Xiān bǎ cáiliào zhǔnbèi hǎo, zài kāishǐ chǎo cài。
    - First prepare the ingredients, then start stir-frying.

### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 炒(炒) → 少(少)
- **definition:** few
- **pinyin:** shǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts four dots, perhaps three grains of sand, to indicate 'a few, a little' ->
    - phonetically borrowed from 小 (小) (xiǎo, 'small').
  - **interpretation:** 少(少) (shǎo, 'few') originally depicted a few grains of sand, showing quantity, with a later phonetic borrowing from 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') due to its semantic similarity.
  - **simplification:** None, traditional is the same as simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 这个城市的人少(這個城市的人少)。
    - Zhège chéngshì de rén shǎo.
    - There are few people in this city.
  - 他的时间少(他的時間少)，所以他工作效率低(所以他工作效率低)。
    - Tā de shíjiān shǎo, suǒyǐ tā gōngzuò xiàolǜ dī.
    - He has little time, so his work efficiency is low.
  - 他的经验少(他的經驗少)，因此他成长缓慢(因此他成長緩慢)。
    - Tā de jīngyàn shǎo, yīncǐ tā chéngzhǎng huǎnmàn.
    - He has little experience, therefore he grows slowly.
### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 蛋(蛋)
- **definition:** egg
- **pinyin:** dàn
- **components:**
  - 虫
    - chóng
    - insect
  - 旦
    - dàn
    - dawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 虫(虫) (chóng, "insect") + phonetic: 旦(旦) (dàn, "dawn") =
    - insect-related thing resembling dawn ->
    - egg
  - **interpretation:** The character 蛋 (dàn, "egg") combines the semantic component 虫(虫) (chóng, "insect"), hinting at life and biology, with the phonetic component 旦(旦) (dàn, "dawn"), providing the pronunciation. The combination suggests something related to life originating like dawn, hence an egg.
- **examples:**

    - dàn shì yī zhǒng chángjiàn de shícái.

  - 妈妈做的蛋(媽媽做的蛋)，尤为美味(尤為美味)。
    - māma zuò de dàn, yóuwéi měiwèi.

### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 蛋(蛋) → 虫(蟲)
- **definition:** insect
- **pinyin:** chóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted as a snake ->
    - Later borrowed to represent general worms/insects ->
    - 虺 (huǐ, "a type of snake") created to retain snake meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 虫 originally illustrated a snake, which was extended metaphorically to mean insects and related creatures due to their similar wriggling motion. This borrowing led to the creation of 虺 to preserve the specific meaning of a venomous snake.
- **examples:**
  - 小虫在草丛中爬行(小虫在草叢中爬行)。
    - Xiǎo huǐ zài cǎo cóng zhōng pá xíng.
    - A small insect is crawling in the grass.
  - 夜晚降临时(夜晚降臨時)，虫声四起(虫聲四起)。
    - Yè wǎn jiàng lín shí, huǐ shēng sì qǐ.
    - When night falls, the sounds of insects rise all around.
### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 蛋(蛋) → 旦(旦)

- **pinyin:** dàn
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 丁
    - dīng
    - fourth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日(日) (rì, "sun") over 丁(丁) (dīng) ->
    - depiction of sun appearing at dawn ->
    - final form 旦
  - **interpretation:** The character juxtaposes 日(日) (rì, "sun") over the horizontal 丁(丁) (dīng), resembling the sun rising above the horizon at dawn, offering a visual representation of daybreak.
  - **simplification:** None, for 旦(旦). Both traditional and simplified characters are identical.
- **examples:**

### 炒蛋(炒蛋) → 蛋(蛋) → 旦(旦) → 丁(丁)
