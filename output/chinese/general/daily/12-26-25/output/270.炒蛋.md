---
- **definition:** scrambled eggs
- **pinyin:** chǎodàn
- **components:**
  - 炒
    - chǎo

  - 蛋

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 炒(炒) (chǎo, "stir-fry") and 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") come together to describe a dish where eggs are stir-fried, resulting in scrambled eggs.
  - **interpretation:** 炒(炒) (chǎo, "stir-fry") is a common cooking method in Chinese cuisine, which involves stirring and cooking ingredients in hot oil, while 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") refers to the primary ingredient of this dish.
