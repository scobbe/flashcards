### scrambled eggs
---
- **pinyin:** scrambled eggs
- **components:**
  - 炒
    - chǎo
    - stir-fry; fry; quick-cook
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg; egg-shaped
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 炒(炒) (chǎo, "stir-fry") + 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") — stir-fried egg
  - **interpretation:** Stir-fried method of cooking combined with eggs creates a popular dish. This combination emphasizes the cooking technique applied to the basic ingredient of eggs.
- **examples:**
  - 我早餐吃炒蛋。(我早餐吃炒蛋。)
    - Wǒ zǎocān chī chǎodàn.
    - I eat scrambled eggs for breakfast.
  - 你知道怎么做炒蛋吗？(你知道怎麼做炒蛋嗎？)
    - Nǐ zhīdào zěnme zuò chǎodàn ma?
    - Do you know how to make scrambled eggs?

  - **interpretation:** The presence of 火 (huǒ, 'fire') highlights the cooking process, while the phonetic component suggests how the word sounds. This makes it easy to associate the action with cooking methods that involve heat.
- **examples:**
