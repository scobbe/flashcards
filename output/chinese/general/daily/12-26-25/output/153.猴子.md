### monkey
---
- **pinyin:** monkey
- **components:**
  - 猴
    - hóu
    - monkey; ape; marmoset; to amuse
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; small thing; seed; earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 猴(猴) (hóu, 'monkey') + 子(子) (zǐ, 'child; small thing') — monkey child
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 猴(猴) (hóu, 'monkey') and 子(子) (zǐ, 'child; small thing') indicates a small or young version of a monkey. This makes sense as the term 'monkey child' colloquially refers to the playful nature of monkeys.
- **examples:**
  - 猴子很可爱(猴子很可愛)。
    - hóuzi hěn kě’ài.
    - The monkey is very cute.
  - 我看到一只猴子(我看到一隻猴子)。
    - wǒ kàn dào yī zhī hóuzi.
    - I saw a monkey.
### 猴子(猴子) → 猴(猴)
- **definition:** monkey; ape; marmoset; to amuse
- **pinyin:** hóu
- **components:**
  - 犭
    - quǎn
    - dog
  - 侯
    - hóu
    - marquis; wait
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 犭 (quǎn, 'dog') indicating a type of animal with the phonetic component 侯 (hóu).
  - **interpretation:** The character's makeup suggests a lively animal, incorporating the meaning of a 'dog' for its animal nature and the sound component to indicate its pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 猴子很聪明(猴子很聰明)，总是惹人笑(總是惹人笑)。

  - 我家有只猴(我家有隻猴)，非常活泼(非常活潑)。
    - wǒ jiā yǒu zhī hóu, fēicháng huópō.
    - My family has a monkey, it's very lively.
  - 公园里的猴子(公園裡的猴子)很有趣(很有趣)。
    - gōngyuán lǐ de hóuzi hěn yǒuqù.

### 猴子(猴子) → 猴(猴) → 侯(侯)
- **definition:** marquis; wait
- **pinyin:** hóu
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** This character contains the phonetic element 侯 (hóu) and the semantic element 人 (rén), suggesting a person of noble status or rank.
  - **interpretation:** The combination hints at a noble person, as marquises were often associated with leadership or high social standing.
- **examples:**
