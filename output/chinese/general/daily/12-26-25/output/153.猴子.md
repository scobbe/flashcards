## monkey
### hóuzi
---
- **definition:** monkey
- **pinyin:** hóuzi
- **components:**
  - 猴
    - hóu
    - monkey
  - 子
    - zi
    - child; offspring
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 猴(猴) (hóu, "monkey") refers to the animal itself, while 子(子) (zi, "child; offspring") is often used as a suffix in nouns to denote a smaller or diminutive form. Together, 猴子 (猴子) (hóuzi, "monkey") refers to the monkey both in the sense of the animal and as a common noun.
  - **interpretation:** The word 猴子 (猴子) (hóuzi, "monkey") combines the animal's name with a suffix to create the common and familiar term for a monkey in Chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 猴子喜欢吃香蕉(猴子喜歡吃香蕉)。
    - hóuzi xǐhuān chī xiāngjiāo.
    - Monkeys like to eat bananas.
  - 在森林里看到一只猴子(在森林裡看到一隻猴子)，它机灵地在树上跳跃(它機靈地在樹上跳躍)。
    - zài sēnlín lǐ kàn dào yī zhī hóuzi, tā jīlíng de zài shù shàng tiàoyuè.
    - In the forest, I saw a monkey, and it nimbly jumped on a tree.
  - 那只小猴子又调皮又可爱(那只小猴子又調皮又可愛)。
    - nà zhī xiǎo hóuzi yòu tiáopí yòu kě'ài.
    - That little monkey is both mischievous and cute.
### 猴子(猴子) → 猴(猴)
- **definition:** monkey
- **pinyin:** hóu
- **components:**
  - 犭
    - quǎn
    - dog
  - 侯
    - hóu
    - marquis
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 犭(犭) (quǎn, "dog") + phonetic 侯(侯) (hóu, "marquis") =
    - animal phoneticized by 侯 ->
    - monkey
  - **interpretation:** 猴(猴) (hóu, "monkey") combines 犭(犭) (quǎn, "dog") as a radical indicating an animal, with 侯(侯) (hóu, "marquis") as a phonetic element that provides the sound. This forms the character for monkey by linking the concept of an animal with the sound similar to '侯'.
- **examples:**
  - 一只猴爬上高高的树(一隻猴爬上高高的樹)。
    - Yī zhī hóu pá shàng gāogāo de shù.
    - A monkey climbs up the tall tree.
  - 猴在森林中跳跃(猴在森林中跳躍)，寻找美味的果子(尋找美味的果子)。
    - Hóu zài sēnlín zhōng tiàoyuè, xúnzhǎo měiwèi de guǒzi.
    - The monkey leaps in the forest, searching for delicious fruits.
### 猴子(猴子) → 猴(猴) → 犭(犭)
- **definition:** dog
- **pinyin:** quǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the side view of a dog
  - **interpretation:** The character 犭(quǎn, "dog") is a simplified form depicting a dog in profile, used as a component in other characters to signify relation to animals or beasts.
- **examples:**
  - 看这个偏旁是犭(看這個偏旁是犭)，说明许多字与狗有关(說明許多字與狗有關)。
    - Kàn zhège piānpáng shì quǎn, shuōmíng xǔduō zì yǔ gǒu yǒuguān.
    - Observing that this radical is 犭 indicates that many characters are related to dogs.
  - 犭作为偏旁常用于涉及动物的字(犭作為偏旁常用於涉及動物的字)，传达出狗的形象(傳達出狗的形象)。
    - Quǎn zuòwéi piānpáng cháng yòng yú shèjí dòngwù de zì, chuándá chū gǒu de xíngxiàng.
    - The radical 犭, used in characters related to animals, conveys the image of a dog.
  - 学习汉字时(學習漢字時)，了解犭的起源有助于记忆字形(了解犭的起源有助於記憶字形)。
    - Xuéxí hànzì shí, liǎojiě quǎn de qǐyuán yǒuzhù yú jìyì zìxíng.

### 猴子(猴子) → 猴(猴) → 侯(侯)
- **definition:** marquis
- **pinyin:** hóu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts target for arrows ->
    - representation of marquis as distant goal
  - **interpretation:** Originally a pictogram showing a target for arrows, this character captures the idea of a marquis as a distant, lofty position to aim for. The visual metaphor aligns the role of a marquis, often considered a noble target or goal within a hierarchy, with the target imagery.
- **examples:**
  - 他被封为侯(侯)。
    - Tā bèi fēng wéi hóu.
    - He was conferred the title of marquis.
  - 他出身名门(他出身名門)，后来晋升为侯(後來晉升為侯)。
    - Tā chūshēn míngmén, hòulái jìnshēng wéi hóu.
    - He was born into a famous family, and later promoted to marquis.
### 猴子(猴子) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child; offspring
- **pinyin:** zi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a baby with a large head and spread arms, with legs wrapped in a blanket
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(zǐ, "child") is a pictorial representation of a baby, emphasizing characteristics like a large head and outstretched arms typical of infants, which conveys the essence of a child. This visual element helps infer its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这小子很调皮(這小子很調皮)。
    - Zhè xiǎo zǐ hěn tiáopí.
    - This kid is very naughty.
  - 他的小子爱打篮球(他的小子愛打籃球)，每天都锻炼身体(每天都鍛煉身體)。
    - Tā de xiǎo zǐ ài dǎ lánqiú, měitiān dōu duànliàn shēntǐ.
    - His child loves playing basketball, and practices every day.
  - 孤独的子需要关爱(孤獨的子需要關愛)。
    - Gūdú de zǐ xūyào guān'ài.
    - A lonely child needs care.
