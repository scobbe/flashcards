## bouquet
### huāshù
---
- **definition:** bouquet
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle; bunch; to bind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花(花) (huā, "flower") + 束(束) (shù, "bundle") =
    - bouquet
  - **interpretation:** A 花(花) (huā, "flower") 束(束) (shù, "bundle") is a collection of flowers arranged together as a bouquet. The term directly embodies the idea of a 'bundle of flowers', capturing the visual and physical arrangement typically associated with bouquets.
- **examples:**
  - 我给母亲买了一束花束(我給母親買了一束花束)。
    - Wǒ gěi mǔqīn mǎile yī shù huāshù.
    - I bought a bouquet for my mother.
  - 他在纪念日晚宴上送她花束(他在紀念日晚宴上送她花束)，表达了深情(表達了深情)。
    - Tā zài jìniànrì wǎnyàn shàng sòng tā huāshù, biǎodále shēnqíng.
    - He gave her a bouquet during the anniversary dinner, expressing his deep affection.
  - 花店为婚礼准备了一束花束(花店為婚禮準備了一束花束)。
    - Huādiàn wèi hūnlǐ zhǔnbèile yī shù huāshù.
    - The florist prepared a bouquet for the wedding.

### 花束(花束) → 束(束)
- **definition:** bundle; bunch; to bind
- **pinyin:** shù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bundle of sticks tied together
  - **interpretation:** The character 束(束) (shù, 'bundle') visually represents a group of items, such as sticks or reeds, that have been gathered and tied tightly together. This depiction is direct and intuitive for conveying the idea of a bundle.
- **examples:**
  - 他拎着一束鲜花(他拎著一束鮮花)。
    - Tā līn zhe yī shù xiānhuā.
    - He is carrying a bundle of fresh flowers.
  - 园丁修剪完花朵后(園丁修剪完花朵後)，将它们整理成一束(將它們整理成一束)。
    - Yuándīng xiūjiǎn wán huāduǒ hòu, jiāng tāmen zhěnglǐ chéng yī shù.
    - After trimming the flowers, the gardener arranged them into a bundle.
  - 小贩把蔬菜捆成一束(小販把蔬菜捆成一束)，然后去市场出售(然後去市場出售)。
    - Xiǎofàn bǎ shūcài kǔn chéng yī shù, ránhòu qù shìchǎng chūshòu.
    - The vendor bundled the vegetables and then went to the market to sell them.
