## bouquet
### huāshù
---
- **definition:** bouquet
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle; bunch; to bind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花 (huā, "flower") + 束 (shù, "bundle") =
    - bouquet
  - **interpretation:** A 花 (huā, "flower") 束 (shù, "bundle") is a collection of flowers arranged together as a bouquet. The term directly embodies the idea of a 'bundle of flowers', capturing the visual and physical arrangement typically associated with bouquets.
- **examples:**
  - 我给(給)母亲买(親買)了一束花束。
    - Wǒ gěi mǔqīn mǎile yī shù huāshù.
    - I bought a bouquet for my mother.
  - 他在纪(紀)念日晚宴上送她花束，表达(達)了深情。
    - Tā zài jìniànrì wǎnyàn shàng sòng tā huāshù, biǎodále shēnqíng.
    - He gave her a bouquet during the anniversary dinner, expressing his deep affection.
  - 花店为(為)婚礼准备(禮準備)了一束花束。
    - Huādiàn wèi hūnlǐ zhǔnbèile yī shù huāshù.
    - The florist prepared a bouquet for the wedding.
### 花束 → 花
- **definition:** flower
- **pinyin:** huā
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic 化 (huà, "transform") =
    - plant + phonetic indicator ->
    - flower
  - **interpretation:** 花 (huā, "flower") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") indicating its association with vegetation, and 化 (huà, "transform") for its phonetic value. This illustrates both the natural aspect and the phonetic pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 花香四溢。
    - huā xiāng sì yì。
    - The fragrance of flowers fills the air.
  - 阳(陽)光明媚，花儿争艳(兒爭艷)。
    - yáng guāng míng mèi，huā ér zhēng yàn。
    - The bright sunshine shines as the flowers compete in beauty.
  - 她用花装点(裝點)房间(間)。
    - tā yòng huā zhuāng diǎn fáng jiān。
    - She decorates the room with flowers.
### 花束 → 花 → 艹
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(來時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 花束 → 花 → 化
- **definition:** transform
- **pinyin:** huà
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 𠤎
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person") upside down =
    - two people, one upright, one reversed ->
    - transformation or change
  - **interpretation:** The character 化 (huà, "transform") visually represents change or transformation, signified by two 人 (rén, "person") characters in opposite orientations, suggesting reversal or alteration.
- **examples:**
  - 科学进(學進)步，推动(動)社会(會)化转(轉)型。
    - Kēxué jìnbù, tuīdòng shèhuì huà zhuǎnxíng.
    - Scientific progress transforms social structures.
  - 时间(時間)能让伤(讓傷)痛化作记忆(記憶)。
    - Shíjiān néng ràng shāngtòng huà zuò jìyì.
    - Time transforms pain into memories.
  - 困境中他不断(斷)努力，生命终将(終將)化为辉(為輝)煌。
    - Kùnjìng zhōng tā bùduàn nǔlì, shēngmìng zhōng jiāng huà wéi huīhuáng.
    - Amid difficulties he perseveres, and his life will eventually transform into brilliance.
### 花束 → 花 → 化 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person") is a stylization of 人 (rén, "person") ->
    - simplified for use as a component in other characters
  - **interpretation:** 亻 (rén, "person") is used as a component form of 人 (rén, "person") in compound characters, representing the human or person-related aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 亻是构(構)成许(許)多汉(漢)字的关键(關鍵)部首。
    - Rén shì gòuchéng xǔduō hànzì de guānjiàn bùshǒu.
    - The radical 亻 is a key component in many Chinese characters.
  - 学习汉(學習漢)字时(時)，了解亻的意义(義)很重要。
    - Xuéxí hànzì shí, liǎojiě rén de yìyì hěn zhòngyào.
    - When learning Chinese characters, understanding the meaning of 亻 is important.
  - 古人造字时(時)常取亻以表达(達)人物情感，因此亻贯(貫)穿于(於)文明史中。
    - Gǔrén zào zì shí cháng qǔ rén yǐ biǎodá rénwù qínggǎn, yīncǐ rén guànchuān yú wénmíng shǐ zhōng.
    - Ancient people often used the radical 亻 to express human emotions in character formation, and thus 亻 runs through the history of civilization.
### 花束 → 花 → 化 → 𠤎
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - Two 人 (rén, "person"), one upright (亻) and one upside down (𠤎) ->
    - reversal or change
  - **interpretation:** 924
- **examples:**
  - 他是勇敢的𠤎。
    - Tā shì yǒnggǎn de rén.
    - He is a brave person.
  - 这个(這個)𠤎勤奋(奮)好学(學)，为(為)家人争(爭)光。
    - Zhège rén qínfèn hàoxué, wèi jiārén zhēngguāng.
    - This person is diligent and studious, bringing honor to his family.
  - 老𠤎智慧丰(豐)富。
    - Lǎo rén zhìhuì fēngfù.
    - The elderly person is wise and knowledgeable.
### 花束 → 束
- **definition:** bundle; bunch; to bind
- **pinyin:** shù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bundle of sticks tied together
  - **interpretation:** The character 束 (shù, 'bundle') visually represents a group of items, such as sticks or reeds, that have been gathered and tied tightly together. This depiction is direct and intuitive for conveying the idea of a bundle.
- **examples:**
  - 他拎着(著)一束鲜(鮮)花。
    - Tā līn zhe yī shù xiānhuā.
    - He is carrying a bundle of fresh flowers.
  - 园(園)丁修剪完花朵后(後)，将(將)它们(們)整理成一束。
    - Yuándīng xiūjiǎn wán huāduǒ hòu, jiāng tāmen zhěnglǐ chéng yī shù.
    - After trimming the flowers, the gardener arranged them into a bundle.
  - 小贩(販)把蔬菜捆成一束，然后(後)去市场(場)出售。
    - Xiǎofàn bǎ shūcài kǔn chéng yī shù, ránhòu qù shìchǎng chūshòu.
    - The vendor bundled the vegetables and then went to the market to sell them.
