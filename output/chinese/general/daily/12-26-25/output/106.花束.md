## 花束(花束)
---
## 
### bouquet
---
- **pinyin:** bouquet
- **components:**
  - 花(華)
    - huā
    - flower; blossom; fame; spend
  - 束
    - shù
    - bunch; tie; bind; limit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 花(華) (huā, "flower") + 束(束) (shù, "bunch") — flower bunch
  - **interpretation:** A 花束(華束) (huāshù, "bouquet") is a collection of flowers arranged together, emphasizing the beauty of flowers grouped in a tied bunch. This combination effectively describes the arrangement of flowers as a tied bunch.
- **examples:**
  - 我想送你一个花束(我想送你一個花束)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng sòng nǐ yīgè huāshù.
    - I want to give you a bouquet.
  - 这个花束很美(這個花束很美)。
    - Zhège huāshù hěn měi.
    - This bouquet is beautiful.
  - 她收到了一个花束(她收到了一个花束)。
    - Tā shōudàole yīgè huāshù.
    - She received a bouquet.
