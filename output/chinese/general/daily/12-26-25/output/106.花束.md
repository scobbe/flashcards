## bouquet
### huāshù
---
- **definition:** bouquet
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle; bunch; to bind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花(花) (huā, "flower") + 束(束) (shù, "bundle") =
    - a bouquet; a bundle of flowers
  - **interpretation:** This word makes sense as it directly combines 花(花) (huā, "flower") with 束(束) (shù, "bundle") to refer to a bouquet, which is essentially a bundle or cluster of flowers.
- **examples:**
  - 她送给我一束花束(她送給我一束花束)。
    - Tā sòng gěi wǒ yī shù huāshù.
    - She gave me a bouquet.
  - 在婚礼上(在婚禮上)，他递上了一束花束(他遞上了一束花束)。
    - Zài hūnlǐ shàng, tā dì shàng le yī shù huāshù.
    - At the wedding, he handed over a bouquet.
  - 他的生日(他的生日)，收到了一束红色的花束(收到了一束紅色的花束)。
    - Tā de shēngrì, shōu dào le yī shù hóngsè de huāshù.
    - On his birthday, he received a red bouquet.
