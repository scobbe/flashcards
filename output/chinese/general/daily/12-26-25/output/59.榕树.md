### banyan tree
---
- **pinyin:** banyan tree
- **components:**
  - 榕
    - róng
    - banyan tree; shade; entertainment; strong
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; wooden; Thai; control
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 榕(榕) (róng, "banyan tree") + 树(樹) (shù, "tree") — banyan tree
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 榕(榕) (róng, "banyan tree") with 树(樹) (shù, "tree"), directly indicating the type of tree it represents. Banyan trees are known for their impressive size and shade, making them noteworthy in various landscapes.
- **examples:**
  - 榕树下面很凉快(榕樹下面很涼快)。
    - Róng shù xiàmiàn hěn liángkuai.
    - It is very cool under the banyan tree.
  - 这棵榕树非常大(這棵榕樹非常大)。
    - Zhè kē róng shù fēicháng dà.
    - This banyan tree is very large.

### 榕树(榕樹) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree; wooden; Thai; control
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 又(壴)
    - yòu
    - again; also (used as phonetic)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component 木 (mù, 'tree') and the phonetic component 壴 (which became 又 in simplification).
  - **interpretation:** The character includes the component for 'tree,' clearly relating it to its meaning. The phonetic component provides guidance on pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing the component 壴 with 又, which is visually simpler and retains the phonetic clue.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树很高(這棵樹很高)。
    - Zhè kē shù hěn gāo.
    - This tree is very tall.
  - 我喜欢在树下读书(我喜歡在樹下讀書)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài shùxià dúshū.
    - I enjoy reading under the tree.
  - 这树开花了(這樹開花了)。
    - Zhè shù kāihuāle.
    - This tree has bloomed.
