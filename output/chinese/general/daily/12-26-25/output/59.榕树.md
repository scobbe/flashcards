## banyan tree
### róngshù
---
- **definition:** banyan tree
- **pinyin:** róngshù
- **components:**
  - 榕
    - róng
    - banyan
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 榕(榕) (róng, "banyan"), 树(樹) (shù, "tree")
  - **interpretation:** 榕树(榕樹) (róngshù, "banyan tree") refers specifically to a type of tree known for its expansive roots and ability to grow in various shapes and forms. Banyan trees are significant in many cultures and landscapes, known for providing ample shade and often growing to impressive sizes.

  - 大榕树下人们聚集(大榕樹下人們聚集)，谈天说地(談天說地)。
    - Dà róngshù xià rénmen jùjí, tán tiān shuō dì.
