## 
### picture; map; plan
---
- **pinyin:** picture; map; plan
- **components:**
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth, ground
  - 吾
    - wú
    - I, me
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 图(圖) (tú, "picture; map; plan") combines the radical for 'drawing' or 'copying', represented by the component 土 (tǔ, 'earth, ground'), suggesting the base or ground of illustrations, with the phonetic element 吾 (wú), indicating its pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The association of earth with drawings implies that maps and pictures are grounded in the reality of the landscape.
  - **simplification:** The simplification was done by replacing the traditional component '圗' with '土', which retains a similar meaning related to the earth while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我在看地图(我在看地圖)。
    - Wǒ zài kàn dìtú.
    - I am looking at the map.
  - 他画了一幅图(他畫了一幅圖)。
    - Tā huàle yī fú tú.
    - He painted a picture.
### 图(圖) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth, ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel.
  - **interpretation:** This visual representation of soil or clay conveys the material's fundamental nature in the earth, highlighting its role in forming and supporting life.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要好的土来种花(我們需要好的土來種花)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào hǎo de tǔ lái zhòng huā.
    - We need good soil to plant flowers.
  - 土可以用来建房子(土可以用來建房子)。
    - Tǔ kěyǐ yòng lái jiàn fángzi.
    - Soil can be used to build houses.
### 图(圖) → 吾(吾)
- **definition:** I, me
- **pinyin:** wú
- **components:**
  - 五
    - wǔ
    - five
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 五 (五, wǔ, 'five') + semantic 口 (口, kǒu, 'mouth') — a representation of 'I, me' as voiced through the mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively combines the idea of the mouth with the phonetic element representing 'five', suggesting self-identity as expressed verbally.
- **examples:**
  - 我是学生(我是學生)，我喜欢学习(我喜歡學習)。
    - Wǒ shì xuéshēng, wǒ xǐhuān xuéxí.
    - I am a student, I like studying.
  - 吾以谦逊而知足(吾以謙遜而知足)。
    - Wú yǐ qiānxùn ér zhīzú.
    - I am content with humility.
### 图(圖) → 吾(吾) → 五(五)
- **definition:** five
- **pinyin:** wǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Originally written as 𠄡, it depicts the palm lines from extending fingers.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the five fingers of a hand, making it intuitive to remember the number five.
- **examples:**
  - 我有五个苹果。(我有五個蘋果。)
    - wǒ yǒu wǔ gè píngguǒ.
    - I have five apples.
  - 他有五只鸟。(他有五隻鳥。)
    - tā yǒu wǔ zhī niǎo.
    - He has five birds.
  - 这本书有五篇文章。(這本書有五篇文章。)
    - zhè běn shū yǒu wǔ piān wénzhāng.
    - This book has five articles.
### 图(圖) → 吾(吾) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character is a pictograph depicting the shape of a mouth, which intuitively represents the concept of 'mouth' itself.
- **examples:**
  - 我吃东西(我吃東西)，需要用口(需要用口)。
    - Wǒ chī dōngxi, xūyào yòng kǒu.
    - I eat food, and I need to use my mouth.
  - 口腔(口腔)健康很重要(健康很重要)。
    - Kǒuqiāng jiànkāng hěn zhòngyào.
    - Oral health is very important.
