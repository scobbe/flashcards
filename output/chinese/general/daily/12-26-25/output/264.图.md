## picture; map; plan
### tú
---
- **definition:** picture; map; plan
- **pinyin:** tú
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 㝛
    - dīng
    - male adult
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 囗(囗) (wéi, 'enclosure') + phonetic 㝛(丁) (dīng, 'male adult') ->
    - 㝛(丁) (shān, 'mountain') appears as a sound component
  - **interpretation:** The character 囗(囗) (wéi, 'enclosure') suggests a bounded area, which complements the concept of a map or plan which is a delineation of space. The phonetic 㝛(丁) gives the sound to the character.
  - **simplification:** During simplification, the traditional component 㝛 was replaced by a simplified inner structure to create 图(圖).
- **examples:**
  - 这张图讲述了历史故事(這張圖講述了歷史故事)。
    - Zhè zhāng tú jiǎngshù le lìshǐ gùshì.
    - This picture tells a historical story.
  - 他画了一张图(他畫了一張圖)，展示了未来规划(展示了未來規劃)。
    - Tā huà le yī zhāng tú, zhǎnshì le wèilái guīhuà.
    - He drew a picture, showcasing his future plan.
### 图(圖) → 囗(囗)
- **definition:** enclosure
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an enclosure or boundary
  - **interpretation:** 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") is a pictogram representing a square or rectangular boundary, signifying the concept of enclosing.
- **examples:**
  - 动物园内的狮舍设有宽敞的囗(動物園內的獅舍設有寬敞的囗)。
    - Dòngwùyuán nèi de shīshè shè yǒu kuānchǎng de wéi。
    - The zoo's lion enclosure is spacious.
  - 工程师设计了新型的囗(工程師設計了新型的囗)，保护了实验设备(保護了實驗設備)。
    - Gōngchéngshī shèjìle xīnxíng de wéi, bǎohùle shíyàn shèbèi。
    - The engineer designed a new type of enclosure, which protected the experimental equipment.
  - 运动场周围设置了牢固的囗(運動場周圍設定了牢固的囗)。
    - Yùndòngchǎng zhōuwéi shèzhìle láogù de wéi。
    - A sturdy enclosure has been installed around the sports field.
### 图(圖) → 㝛(㝛)
- **definition:** male adult
- **pinyin:** dīng
- **components:**
  - 毋
    - wú
    - do not
  - 卯
    - mǎo
    - branch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 毋(母) (wú, 'do not') + phonetic: 卯(卯) (mǎo, 'branch') =
    - male adult
  - **interpretation:** The character 㝛 combines the semantic element 毋(母) (wú, 'do not'), which may imply negation or opposition, with the phonetic element 卯(卯) (mǎo, 'branch'). Together, they are used to form a character representing a male adult.
  - **simplification:** Simplification is not applicable.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个成熟稳重的㝛(他是一個成熟穩重的㝛)。
    - Tā shì yīgè chéngshú wěnzhòng de dīng.
    - He is a mature and dependable male adult.
  - 工作中他总是展现出领导才能(工作中他總是展現出領導才能)，在生活里他展现出深厚情义(在生活裡他展現出深厚情義)。
    - Gōngzuò zhōng tā zǒng shì zhǎnxiàn chū lǐngdǎo cáinéng, zài shēnghuó lǐ tā zhǎnxiàn chū shēnhòu qíngyì.
    - At work, he always demonstrates his leadership potential, and in life he shows deep affection.
### 图(圖) → 㝛(㝛) → 毋(毋)
- **definition:** do not
- **pinyin:** wú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 母(母) (mǔ, "mother") borrowed ->
    - specialized form with one stroke replacing two dots ->
    - 毋(毋) (wú, "do not")
  - **interpretation:** The character 毋 initially borrowed the pictogram for 母(母) (mǔ, "mother"). To differentiate the meanings while maintaining the original form, an adjustment was made by reducing the two dots to a single stroke, resulting in 毋(毋) (wú, "do not").
- **examples:**
  - 毋贪小利(毋貪小利)。
    - Wú tān xiǎo lì.
    - Do not be greedy for trivial gains.
  - 做事要稳重(做事要穩重)，毋急躁行事(毋急躁行事)。
    - Zuò shì yào wěn zhòng, wú jí zào xíng shì.
    - Be steady in your actions, and do not act hastily.
  - 毋忘国耻(毋忘國恥)，勇创佳绩(勇創佳績)。
    - Wú wàng guó chǐ, yǒng chuàng jiā jì.
    - Never forget the national humiliation, and strive to achieve excellence.
### 图(圖) → 㝛(㝛) → 卯(卯)
- **definition:** branch
- **pinyin:** mǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a pair of knives for butchering ->
    - sacrificial practice ->
    - obscured meaning ->
    - open door/mortise re-interpretation
  - **interpretation:** 卯(mǎo) originally depicted knives used in sacrifices, a practice that fell from use, obscuring the original meaning. Over time, the character was reinterpreted as a reversed door or woodworking mortise.
- **examples:**
  - 那棵大树的卯长得十分粗壮(那棵大樹的卯長得十分粗壯)。
    - Nà kē dà shù de mǎo zhǎng dé shí fēn cū zhuàng。
    - The branch of that big tree grows very stoutly.
  - 春天来临时(春天來臨時)，树上的卯开始抽芽(樹上的卯開始抽芽)。
    - Chūn tiān lái lín shí, shù shàng de mǎo kāi shǐ chōu yá。
    - When spring arrives, the branches on the tree begin to bud.
  - 他指着那根粗大的卯(他指著那根粗大的卯)，赞叹不已(讚嘆不已)。
    - Tā zhǐ zhe nà gēn cū dà de mǎo, zàn tàn bù yǐ。
    - He pointed at that thick branch and was full of admiration.
