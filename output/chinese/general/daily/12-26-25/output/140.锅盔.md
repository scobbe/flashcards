## round baked flatbread
### guōkuī
---
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **components:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot") + 盔(盔) (kuī, "helmet") =
    - round baked flatbread
  - **interpretation:** The term 锅盔(鍋盔) (guōkuī) describes a type of flatbread that traditionally resembled the shape of a helmet or pot lid, with a firm and round structure. This combination suggests the shape and cooking method of the flatbread.
- **examples:**
  - 我早餐吃锅盔(鍋盔)。
    - Wǒ zǎocān chī guōkuī.
    - I eat guokui for breakfast.
  - 市场里有很多种锅盔(鍋盔)，风味各异(風味各異)。
    - Shìchǎng lǐ yǒu hěnduō zhǒng guōkuī, fēngwèi gè yì.
    - There are many kinds of guokui in the market, each with a different flavor.
  - 老店里传统制作的锅盔(鍋盔)，外酥里嫩(外酥裏嫩)。
    - Lǎo diàn lǐ chuántǒng zhìzuò de guōkuī, wài sū lǐ nèn.
    - The old shop makes traditional guokui, crispy outside and tender inside.
### 锅盔(鍋盔) → 锅(鍋)
- **definition:** pot
- **pinyin:** guō
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 金(金) (jīn, "metal") + 咼(咼) (guō, phonetic) ->
    - pot made of metal
  - **interpretation:** The character 鍋(鍋) (guō, "pot") combines 金(金) (jīn, "metal"), indicating that it is an object made of metal, with 咼(咼) (guō), which provides the pronunciation. This combination effectively describes a metal pot or vessel.
  - **simplification:** 鍋(鍋) was simplified to 锅(鍋) by changing 釒(釒) (jīn, "metal") to 钅(钅) (jīn, "metal") and 咼(咼) was simplified to 呙(呙) (guō) to maintain phonetic consistency, reflecting a common simplification strategy where the metal radical is simplified for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 这口锅很结实(這口鍋很結實)。
    - Zhè kǒu guō hěn jiéshí.
    - This pot is very sturdy.
  - 老爷子用旧锅炖汤(老爺子用舊鍋燉湯)，回忆起往昔岁月(回憶起往昔歲月)。
    - Lǎo yézi yòng jiù guō dùn tāng, huíyì qǐ wǎngxī suìyuè.
    - The old man stews soup with an old pot, recalling the bygone days.
  - 厨房里摆满了各式各样的锅(廚房裡擺滿了各式各樣的鍋)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ bǎi mǎn le gè shì gè yàng de guō.
    - The kitchen is filled with pots of various kinds.
### 锅盔(鍋盔) → 盔(盔)
- **definition:** helmet
- **pinyin:** kuī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the shape of a helmet with exaggerated sides for protection
  - **interpretation:** This character visually represents a helmet, with its flared sides mimicking the protective structure of a helmet.
- **examples:**
  - 士兵戴上盔(士兵戴上盔)，准备出征(準備出征)。
    - Shìbīng dàishàng kuī, zhǔnbèi chūzhēng.
    - The soldier dons a helmet, and prepares for battle.
  - 他用盔保护头部(他用盔保護頭部)。
    - Tā yòng kuī bǎohù tóubù.
    - He uses a helmet to protect his head.
