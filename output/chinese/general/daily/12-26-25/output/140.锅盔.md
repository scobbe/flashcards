## round baked flatbread
### guōkuī
---
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **components:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot") and 盔(盔) (kuī, "helmet") combine to describe a type of baked flatbread that resembles the shape of a round helmet or pot. The word reflects the visual similarity to these objects, emphasizing the bread's rounded and typically somewhat thick form.
  - **interpretation:** The term '锅盔(鍋盔) (guōkuī)' describes a flatbread whose shape is reminiscent of a pot or helmet. This alludes to its round and thick appearance, which visually mimics these objects.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃锅盔(我喜歡吃鍋盔)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī guōkuī.
    - I like eating guokui (round baked flatbread).
  - 在路边摊可以买到锅盔(在路邊攤可以買到鍋盔)，外皮酥脆味道独特(外皮酥脆味道獨特)。
    - Zài lùbiān tān shàng kěyǐ mǎidào guōkuī, wàipí sūcuì wèidào dútè.
    - You can buy guokui at a street stall, and its crust is crisp with a unique flavor.
### 锅盔(鍋盔) → 锅(鍋)
- **definition:** pot
- **pinyin:** guō
- **components:**
  - 钅(金)
    - jīn
    - metal
  - 呙
    - guō
    - pot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 钅(金) (jīn, 'metal') + phonetic: 呙(呙) (guō, 'pot') =
    - metal pot
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 钅(金) (jīn, 'metal') to indicate the material of the object and 呙(呙) (guō, 'pot') as the phonetic element, reflecting the character's association with metal containers.
  - **simplification:** 鍋(鍋)→锅(鍋): 钅(金) (jīn, 'metal') replaced 金(金) for a simpler form while retaining the semantic element.
- **examples:**

    - Māma yòng guō, zhǔle yī guō tāng.
    - Mom used a pot to cook some soup.
  - 在厨房角落里(在廚房角落裡)，那口锅闪着光(那口鍋閃著光)。
