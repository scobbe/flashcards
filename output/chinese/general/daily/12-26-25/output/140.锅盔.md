### round baked flatbread
---
- **pinyin:** round baked flatbread
- **components:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot; wok; stove; vessel
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet; headgear; cap; hood
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot") + 盔(盔) (kuī, "helmet") — pot helmet
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a type of flatbread that is typically cooked in a pot. '锅(鍋) (guō, "pot")' refers to the cooking method, while '盔(盔) (kuī, "helmet")' suggests the shape or appearance of the bread.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃锅盔(我喜歡吃鍋盔)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī guōkuī.
    - I like to eat round baked flatbread.
  - 这种锅盔在市场上很受欢迎(這種鍋盔在市場上很受歡迎)。
    - Zhè zhǒng guōkuī zài shìchǎng shàng hěn shòu huānyíng.
    - This kind of round baked flatbread is very popular at the market.
### 锅盔(鍋盔) → 锅(鍋)
- **definition:** pot; wok; stove; vessel
- **pinyin:** guō
- **components:**
  - 釒
    - jīn
    - metal
  - 咼
    - guō
    - to gurgle; pot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 鍋 consists of the metal radical 釒 and the phonetic component 咼.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts a metal vessel associated with cooking, as the metal radical emphasizes the material it is made of and the phonetic component suggests its function as a cooking pot or wok.
  - **simplification:** 鍋 is simplified to 锅 by replacing the traditional radical 釒 with its simplified form 钅; this reduction makes the character easier to write while retaining its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我想买一个锅(我想買一個鍋)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī gè guō.
    - I want to buy a pot.
  - 她在锅里煮汤(她在鍋裡煮湯)。
    - Tā zài guō lǐ zhǔ tāng.
    - She is cooking soup in the pot.
  - 这个锅很好用(這個鍋很好用)。
    - Zhège guō hěn hǎo yòng.
    - This pot is very useful.
