## round baked flatbread
### guōkuī
---
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **components:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot") and 盔(盔) (kuī, "helmet") combine to describe a type of baked flatbread that resembles the shape of a round helmet or pot. The word reflects the visual similarity to these objects, emphasizing the bread's rounded and typically somewhat thick form.
