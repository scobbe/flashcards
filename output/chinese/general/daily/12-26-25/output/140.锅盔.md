## round baked flatbread
### guōkuī
---
- **traditional:** 鍋盔
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **components:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot") and 盔 (kuī, "helmet") combine to describe a type of baked flatbread that resembles the shape of a round helmet or pot. The word reflects the visual similarity to these objects, emphasizing the bread's rounded and typically somewhat thick form.
  - **interpretation:** The term '锅(鍋)盔 (guōkuī)' describes a flatbread whose shape is reminiscent of a pot or helmet. This alludes to its round and thick appearance, which visually mimics these objects.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃锅(鍋)盔。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī guōkuī.
    - I like eating guokui (round baked flatbread).
  - 在路边摊(邊攤)可以买(買)到锅(鍋)盔，外皮酥脆味道独(獨)特。
    - Zài lùbiān tān shàng kěyǐ mǎidào guōkuī, wàipí sūcuì wèidào dútè.
    - You can buy guokui at a street stall, and its crust is crisp with a unique flavor.
