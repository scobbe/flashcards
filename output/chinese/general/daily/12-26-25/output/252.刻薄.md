## unkind; harsh; mean
### kèbó
---
- **definition:** unkind; harsh; mean
- **pinyin:** kèbó
- **components:**
  - 刻
    - kè
    - to cut; carve; moment; severe
  - 薄
    - bó
    - thin; meagre; light; slight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 刻(刻) (kè, "to cut") refers to carving or engraving, often suggesting precision or severity. 薄(薄) (bó, "thin; meagre") implies lack of substance or depth.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 刻(刻) (kè, "to cut") and 薄(薄) (bó, "thin") suggests a demeanor that is cutting or sharp in a way that lacks warmth or empathy, hence the meaning 'unkind; harsh; mean.'
- **examples:**
  - 她的刻薄令人胆寒(她的刻薄令人膽寒)。
    - Tā de kèbó lìng rén dǎn hán.
    - Her meanness is intimidating.
  - 他说话直率(他說話直率)，但有时显得刻薄(但有時顯得刻薄)。
    - Tā shuōhuà zhíshuài, dàn yǒu shí xiǎn de kèbó.
    - He speaks frankly, but sometimes he appears mean.
  - 他的刻薄使朋友远离(他的刻薄使朋友遠離)。
    - Tā de kèbó shǐ péngyǒu yuǎn lí.
    - His meanness drives friends away.
