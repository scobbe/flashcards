## unkind; harsh; mean
### kèbó
---
- **definition:** unkind; harsh; mean
- **pinyin:** kèbó
- **components:**
  - 刻
    - kè
    - to cut; carve; moment; severe
  - 薄
    - bó
    - thin; meagre; light; slight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 刻 (kè, "to cut") refers to carving or engraving, often suggesting precision or severity. 薄 (bó, "thin; meagre") implies lack of substance or depth.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 刻 (kè, "to cut") and 薄 (bó, "thin") suggests a demeanor that is cutting or sharp in a way that lacks warmth or empathy, hence the meaning 'unkind; harsh; mean.'
- **examples:**
  - 她的刻薄令人胆(膽)寒。
    - Tā de kèbó lìng rén dǎn hán.
    - Her meanness is intimidating.
  - 他说话(說話)直率，但有时显(時顯)得刻薄。
    - Tā shuōhuà zhíshuài, dàn yǒu shí xiǎn de kèbó.
    - He speaks frankly, but sometimes he appears mean.
  - 他的刻薄使朋友远离(遠離)。
    - Tā de kèbó shǐ péngyǒu yuǎn lí.
    - His meanness drives friends away.
### 刻薄 → 刻
- **definition:** to cut; carve; moment; severe
- **pinyin:** kè
- **components:**
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
  - 亥
    - hài
    - 亥 (hài)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 刀 (dāo, "knife") + phonetic: 亥 (hài) =
    - cutting or carving with a knife ->
    - to cut, carve
  - **interpretation:** 刻 (kè, "to cut; carve") combines the knife 刀 (dāo, "knife") as the semantic component, indicating the action of cutting or carving, with 亥 (hài) providing the phonetic aspect. This combination effectively communicates the action of cutting or carving.
- **examples:**
  - 他用刻刀刻花，展示艺术(藝術)。
    - Tā yòng kè dāo kè huā, zhǎnshì yìshù.
    - He carved flowers with a carving knife, showcasing his art.
  - 此刻心情难(難)耐。
    - Cǐ kè xīnqíng nán nài.
    - At this moment, his emotions were overwhelming.

- **pinyin:** dāo

  - **interpretation:** 刀 (dāo, "knife") is a pictographic representation of a knife or blade, with a sharp edge and a handle, resembling the form of a basic cutting tool.
- **examples:**
  - 他用锋(鋒)利的刀，切开(開)蔬菜。

  - 厨师(廚師)小心地持刀，为(為)客人切肉。
    - chúshī xiǎoxīn de chí dāo, wèi kèrén qiē ròu.
    - The chef holds the knife carefully and slices meat for the guest.
### 刻薄 → 刻 → 亥
- **definition:** 亥 (hài)
- **pinyin:** hài
- **components:**
  - 荄
    - hài
    - roots
  - 豕
    - shǐ
    - pig
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts plant's roots underground or an animal (original form of 荄 (hài, "roots")) ->
    - associated with 豕 (shǐ, "pig") in spirit
  - **interpretation:** The character 亥 (hài) is a pictogram with debated depiction origins. It either illustrates plant roots or an animal resembling pigs, linking it to the Chinese zodiac and calendar.
- **examples:**
  - 现(現)在是亥时(時)。
    - Xiànzài shì hài shí.
    - It is the hai hour.
  - 夜深了，亥时(時)悄然降临(臨)。
    - Yè shēn le, hài shí qiǎo rán jiàng lín.
    - The night has deepened, and the hai hour descends quietly.
  - 钟声(鐘聲)敲响(響)，预(預)示着(著)亥时来临(時來臨)。
    - Zhōng shēng qiāo xiǎng, yù shì zhe hài shí lái lín.
    - The chimes ring out, indicating that the hai hour has arrived.

### 刻薄 → 薄
- **definition:** thin; meagre; light; slight
- **pinyin:** bó
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 溥
    - pǔ
    - widespread
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 艹 (cǎo, 'grass') + phonetic: 溥 (pǔ, 'widespread') → thin, meagre
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 艹 (cǎo, 'grass') as the semantic component, indicating associations with plants or fields, with 溥 (pǔ, 'widespread') as the phonetic component, suggesting sound. Together, they convey ideas of thinness or lightness, often applicable to plant materials.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)件衣服很薄。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfú hěn bó.
    - This piece of clothing is very thin.
  - 报(報)告内(內)容太薄，需要补(補)充。
    - Bàogào nèiróng tài bó, xūyào bǔchōng.
    - The report is too meagre; it needs to be supplemented.
  - 他戴的眼镜(鏡)框很薄，透出一丝(絲)精致(緻)。
    - Tā dài de yǎnjìng kuàng hěn bó, tòuchū yī sī jīngzhì.
    - The thin frame of his glasses exudes a touch of delicacy.

### 刻薄 → 薄 → 溥 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**

    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.

### 刻薄 → 薄 → 溥 → 尃
- **definition:** phonetic
- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 甫
    - fǔ
    - garden
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 甫 (fǔ, 'garden') + 寸 (cùn, 'hand') =
    - reference to a sprout growing in a field with the action of a hand ->
    - to distribute crops
  - **interpretation:** 尃(fū) is a phono-semantic compound where 甫 (fǔ, 'garden') suggests the sound and possibly a visual reference to sprouting crops, while 寸 (cùn, 'hand') provides a semantic aspect of distributing or managing these plants. The combination emphasizes activity related to horticulture, especially the handling and distribution of plants or crops.
### 刻薄 → 薄 → 溥 → 尃 → 甫
- **definition:** garden
- **pinyin:** fǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a broad field, originally representing a seedling sprouting from the earth, later associated with 'man' or 'male name'.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a pictogram representing soil and a growing plant, 甫 (fǔ, 'man') evolved to also symbolize a person, often used in names as a prefix denoting masculinity or adulthood.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位正直的甫。
    - Tā shì yī wèi zhèngzhí de fǔ.
    - He is an upright man.
  - 古时(時)英豪皆尊甫为(為)表率。

    - Wénshǐ míngjiā zànyáng zhè wèi fǔ.
    - Prominent writers and historians praised this man.
### 刻薄 → 薄 → 溥 → 尃 → 寸
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸 (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块(這塊)布每一寸都是精心制(製)作。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请(請)用卷尺量一寸，每一寸数据(數據)都准确(準確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.
