## unkind; harsh; mean
### kèbó
---
- **definition:** unkind; harsh; mean
- **pinyin:** kèbó
- **components:**
  - 刻
    - kè
    - severe; carve
  - 薄
    - bó
    - slight; thin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 刻(刻) (kè, "severe; carve") + 薄(薄) (bó, "slight; thin") =
    - suggests an unkind, harsh attitude by implying a severity paired with a lack of empathy or depth of feeling.
  - **interpretation:** The combination implies a harsh and unfeeling attitude by associating severity with superficiality.
- **examples:**
  - 他对朋友很刻薄(他對朋友很刻薄)。
    - Tā duì péngyǒu hěn kèbó.
    - He is very unkind to his friends.
  - 他讲话时很刻薄(他講話時很刻薄)，伤了别人的心(傷了別人的心)。
    - Tā jiǎnghuà shí hěn kèbó, shāngle biérén de xīn.
    - He speaks harshly, injuring others' feelings.
  - 他的笑容背后隐藏着刻薄(他的笑容背後隱藏著刻薄)。
    - Tā de xiàoróng bèihòu yǐncángzhe kèbó.
    - Behind his smile, a mean nature is concealed.
