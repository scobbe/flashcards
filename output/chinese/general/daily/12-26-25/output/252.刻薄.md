### unkind; harsh; mean
---
- **pinyin:** unkind; harsh; mean
- **components:**
  - 刻
    - kè
    - cut; engrave; severe; deep
  - 薄
    - bó
    - thin; weak; light; unkind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 刻(刻) (kè, "cut") + 脆(脆) (cuì, "brittle; weak") — cut weak
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 刻(刻) (kè, "cut") and 薄(薄) (bó, "weak") suggests an action that causes deep emotional pain or vulnerability. This reflects the harshness associated with being unkind or mean.
- **examples:**
  - 他说话很刻薄(他說話很刻薄)，让人感觉不舒服(讓人感覺不舒服)！
    - tā shuōhuà hěn kèbó, ràng rén gǎnjué bù shūfú！
    - He speaks very unkindly, making people feel uncomfortable!
  - 你不应该对人刻薄(你不應該對人刻薄)，我们要相互尊重(我們要相互尊重)。
    - nǐ bù yīnggāi duì rén kèbó, wǒmen yào xiānghù zūnzhòng。
    - You shouldn't be harsh to others; we should respect each other.
