### loser (internet slang)
---
- **pinyin:** loser (internet slang)
- **components:**
  - 屌
    - diǎo
    - penis; to scold; to fuck
  - 丝(絲)
    - sī
    - silk; thread; to be slender
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 屌(屌) (diǎo, "penis") + 丝(絲) (sī, "silk") — penis silk
  - **interpretation:** The term 屌丝 (diǎosī) combines 屌 (diǎo, "penis") and 丝 (sī, "silk") to create a humorous take on someone perceived as a loser or someone of lower status in society. It suggests both a sexual connotation and a sense of fragility or lack of strength, fitting the slang usage in modern contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 他真是个屌丝(他真是個屌絲)，总是抱怨生活。
    - tā zhēn shì gè diǎosī, zǒng shì bào yuàn shēng huó。
    - He is really a loser, always complaining about life.
  - 我们这群屌丝(我們這群屌絲)，总是聚在一起玩游戏。
    - wǒ men zhè qún diǎosī, zǒng shì jù zài yī qǐ wán yóu xì。
    - We, this group of losers, always gather to play games.
