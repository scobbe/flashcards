## loser (internet slang)
### diǎosī
---
- **definition:** loser (internet slang)
- **pinyin:** diǎosī
- **components:**
  - 屌
    - diǎo
    - penis
  - 丝(絲)
    - sī
    - silk; thread
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 屌(屌) (diǎo, "penis") + 丝(絲) (sī, "silk; thread") combine to form 屌丝(屌絲), originally internet slang used to describe someone perceived as a loser or someone unsuccessful and lacking wealth, typically used in a humorous or self-deprecating manner.
  - **interpretation:** 屌(屌) (diǎo, "penis") and 丝(絲) (sī, "silk; thread") together form a slang term reflecting a self-deprecating or humorous portrayal of someone viewed as underachieving or unsuccessful in a material sense.
  - **simplification:** 屌絲 is the traditional form of 屌丝.
- **examples:**
  - 他觉得自己是屌丝(他覺得自己是屌絲)。
    - Tā juéde zìjǐ shì diǎosī.
    - He thinks he is a loser.
  - 在网上(在網上)，他自称屌丝(他自稱屌絲)。
    - Zài wǎngshàng, tā zìchēng diǎosī.
    - Online, he calls himself a loser.

### 屌丝(屌絲) → 丝(絲)
- **definition:** silk; thread
- **pinyin:** sī
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 糸(糸) (mì, 'silk') + 糸(糸) (mì, 'silk') =
    - two silkworm threads ->
    - silk
  - **interpretation:** The character combines two 糸(糸) (mì, 'silk') to represent the idea of silk threads. This reflects the original process of producing silk by intertwining multiple threads.
  - **simplification:** 丝(絲) is the simplified form where both 糸 components are represented by a single version, created to streamline the character while maintaining the concept of thread.
- **examples:**
