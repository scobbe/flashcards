### sit; ride
---
- **pinyin:** sit; ride
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Combines 留 (liú, 'to stay') and 土 (tǔ, 'ground').
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of remaining seated on the ground, suggesting stability and rest.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢坐在这里(我喜歡坐在這裡)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān zuò zài zhèlǐ.
    - I like to sit here.
  - 请你坐下(請你坐下)。
    - qǐng nǐ zuòxià.
    - Please sit down.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留)
- **definition:** to stay
- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 丣
    - liú
    - phonetic component
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 丣 (liú) combined with semantic 田 (tián, 'field').
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts the phonetic element indicating sound and a field which suggests a connection to agricultural context, as staying often relates to remaining in one place, like in a field.
- **examples:**
  - 我想留在这里(我想留在這裡)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng liú zài zhèlǐ.
    - I want to stay here.
  - 留下来吧(留下來吧)。
    - Liú xiàlái ba.
    - Stay!

### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 田(田)
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictographic representation of a field divided into four sections.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a farmland, showcasing how it is often divided for cultivation, reflecting its agricultural significance.
- **examples:**
  - 我在田里工作。
    - Wǒ zài tián lǐ gōngzuò.
    - I work in the field.
  - 春天，田野非常美丽。
    - Chūntiān, tiányě fēicháng měilì.
    - In spring, the fields are very beautiful.
### 坐(坐) → 土(土)
- **definition:** ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the material of earth or clay, symbolizing the ground we walk on. This intuitive connection is seen in its stylized shape that resembles a lump of earth.
- **examples:**
  - 地上有土(地上有土)，可以种植物(可以種植物)。
    - Dì shàng yǒu tǔ, kěyǐ zhǒng zhíwù.
    - There is soil on the ground, suitable for planting.
  - 这个地方土质很好(這個地方土質很好)。
    - Zhège dìfāng tǔzhì hěn hǎo.
    - The soil quality in this area is very good.
