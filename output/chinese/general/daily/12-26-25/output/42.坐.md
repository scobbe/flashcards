## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 留 (liú, "to stay") + 土 (tǔ, "ground") =
    - staying on the ground ->
    - sit
  - **interpretation:** The character 坐 (zuò, "sit") combines 留 (liú, "to stay") to convey the idea of remaining or staying, with 土 (tǔ, "ground") to indicate being positioned on the ground. This conveys the action of sitting down, highlighting being in a stable position on the ground.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)坐！
    - Qǐng zuò!
    - Please sit down!
  - 我坐在公园(園)的长(長)椅上。
    - Wǒ zuò zài gōngyuán de chángyǐ shàng.
    - I sit on the park bench.
  - 去旅行时(時)，他喜欢(歡)坐火车(車)享受美景。
    - Qù lǚxíng shí, tā xǐhuān zuò huǒchē xiǎngshòu měijǐng.
    - When traveling, he likes to ride the train and enjoy the beautiful scenery.
### 坐 → 留
- **definition:** to stay
- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 畱
    - liú
    - "stay"
  - 丣
    - liú
    - "phonetic component"
  - 田
    - tián
    - "field"
  - 卯
    - mǎo
    - "phonetic component"
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 畱 (liú) originally: phonetic 丣 (liú) + semantic 田 (tián, "field") ->
    - 留 evolved: phonetic 卯 (mǎo) + semantic 田(田, "field") =
    - field-related action ->
    - to stay
  - **interpretation:** The character 留 (liú) combines 田 (tián, "field"), indicating a place of settlement or work, with phonetic elements 卯 (mǎo) and 丣 (liú) related to staying or remaining. This suggests staying or being present in a field or place.
- **examples:**
  - 我决(決)定留在学(學)校。
    - Wǒ juédìng liú zài xuéxiào.
    - I decided to stay at school.
  - 老师(師)建议(議)留在班级(級)，继续(繼續)提高成绩(績)。
    - Lǎoshī jiànyì liú zài bānjí, jìxù tígāo chéngjī.
    - The teacher suggested staying in the class to continue improving the grades.

### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 丣 → 酉
- **definition:** wine vessel
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **components:**
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a wine vessel with a pointed bottom ->
    - original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol")
  - **interpretation:** 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") originally depicted a wine vessel, indicating the integral role of alcohol in traditional cycles, such as moon phases or ceremonial events. It is the original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol"), highlighting its use in fermentation processes.

    - Rìluò huánghūn hòu, dàdì jīnglìle yīgè yǒu de xúnhuán.

### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 丣 → 酉 → 酒

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 田
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a field divided into four sections
  - **interpretation:** The character 田(tián, 'field') visually represents a field with sections, indicating its agricultural use and importance in ancient times.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)民在田里(裡)耕作。
    - Nóngmín zài tián lǐ gēngzuò.
    - The farmer works in the field.
  - 那边(邊)的田长满(長滿)了庄(莊)稼，农(農)民收获(穫)忙碌。
    - Nàbiān de tián cháng mǎn le zhuāngjià, nóngmín shōuhuò mánglù.
    - The field over there is full of crops, and the farmer is busy harvesting.
  - 秋收时节(時節)，人们(們)忙于(於)收割田里(裡)的稻谷。
    - Qiūshōu shíjié, rénmen máng yú shōugē tián lǐ de dàogǔ.
    - During the harvest season, people are busy reaping the rice in the fields.
### 坐 → 留 → 卯
- **definition:** "phonetic component"
- **pinyin:** mǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Originally depicts a pair of knives used in a ritual sacrifice, showing an animal or human cut in half for Shang dynasty sacrifices.
  - **interpretation:** 卯(mǎo) was originally a term associated with sacrificial rituals involving cutting, reflecting cultural practices of the Shang dynasty. Over time, its meaning has shifted away from these origins.
- **examples:**
  - 这个(這個)字的卯部分揭示了它的读(讀)音，构(構)成了汉(漢)字的声(聲)音系统(統)。
    - Zhège zì de mǎo bùfen jiēshì le tā de dúyīn, gòuchéng le hànzì de shēngyīn xìtǒng.
    - The phonetic component of this character reveals its pronunciation, forming the sound system of Chinese characters.
  - 研究卯的作用有助于(於)理解汉(漢)字结构(結構)。
    - Yánjiū mǎo de zuòyòng yǒuzhù yú lǐjiě hànzì jiégòu.
    - Studying the role of the phonetic component helps to understand the structure of Chinese characters.
### 坐 → 土
- **definition:** ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a mound of earth
  - **interpretation:** The character 土(tǔ, "earth") visually represents a mound or piece of land, with a horizontal line indicating the surface and a vertical line the height of the mound. It's a straightforward depiction of earth or soil.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)民耕土，种(種)出丰(豐)收。
    - Nóngmín gēng tǔ, zhòng chū fēngshōu.
    - Farmers cultivate the earth and harvest abundantly.
  - 院子里(裡)的土，适(適)合种(種)花。
    - Yuànzi lǐ de tǔ, shìhé zhòng huā.
    - The soil in the yard is suitable for planting flowers.
