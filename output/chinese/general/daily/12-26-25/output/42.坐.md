## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - stay; remain; keep
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth; soil; ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 会意: 留 (‘to stay’) + 土 (‘ground’).
  - **interpretation:** Combines 留 ‘to stay’ with 土 ‘ground’ to convey remaining on the ground, i.e., being seated. From this sense comes the extended usage ‘to take’ a vehicle, as one sits in it to ride.
- **examples:**
  - 我们坐在地上休息(我們坐在地上休息)。
    - Wǒmen zuò zài dìshàng xiūxi.
    - We sit on the ground to rest.
  - 请坐这辆公交车去市中心(請坐這輛公交車去市中心)。
    - Qǐng zuò zhè liàng gōngjiāochē qù shì zhōngxīn.
    - Please take this bus to the city center.
  - 我每天坐地铁上班(我每天坐地鐵上班)。
    - Wǒ měitiān zuò dìtiě shàngbān.
    - I take the subway to work every day.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留)
- **definition:** stay; remain; keep
- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
  - 卯
    - mǎo
    - 4th Earthly Branch
  - 丣
    - yǒu
    - 10th Earthly Branch (archaic)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 田 ‘field’ + phonetic: 卯 (bronze); later phonetic: 丣 (small seal).
  - **interpretation:** Component 田 cues the semantic idea of a place/field, while the phonetic element supplies the pronunciation. Over time the script replaced the original phonetic 卯 with 丣 in small seal, but the semantic base and overall reading remained in the same sound family.
- **examples:**
  - 我们留在这里休息(我們留在這裡休息)。
    - Wǒmen liú zài zhèlǐ xiūxi.
    - We will stay here to rest.
  - 请把我的电话号码留着(請把我的電話號碼留著)。
    - Qǐng bǎ wǒ de diànhuà hàomǎ liú zhe.
    - Please keep my phone number.
  - 雨停了(雨停了)，但地上还留着水(但地上還留著水)。
    - Yǔ tíng le, dàn dìshang hái liú zhe shuǐ.
    - The rain has stopped, but water still remains on the ground.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 田(田)
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: pictographic representation of a field divided into four sections.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a top-down view of a plot divided by paths or furrows, which naturally came to denote farmland. The square-with-cross motif also lent itself in some ancient contexts to a mask-like image, influencing related graphs.
- **examples:**
  - 这片田很肥沃(這片田很肥沃)。
    - Zhè piàn tián hěn féiwò.
    - This field is very fertile.
  - 他们在田里劳动(他們在田裡勞動)。
    - Tāmen zài tián lǐ láodòng.
    - They are working in the fields.
  - 春天农民在田里插秧(春天農民在田裡插秧)。
    - Chūntiān nóngmín zài tián lǐ chāyāng.
    - In spring, farmers transplant rice seedlings in the fields.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 卯(卯)
- **definition:** 4th Earthly Branch
- **pinyin:** mǎo
- **components:**
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a sacrifice/body split in two with a pair of knives; old variant 戼.
  - **interpretation:** An early graph shows two knives cutting a carcass, reflecting Shang sacrificial practice. That concrete sense later faded, and the graph was borrowed to write the fourth Earthly Branch. The ‘open door/mortise’ idea comes from later folk etymology, not the oldest forms.
- **examples:**
  - 在十二地支中(在十二地支中)，卯对应东方和春天(卯對應東方和春天)。
    - Zài shí'èr dìzhī zhōng, mǎo duìyìng dōngfāng hé chūntiān.
    - In the twelve Earthly Branches, Mao corresponds to the east and to spring.
  - 我们在卯时出发(我們在卯時出發)。
    - Wǒmen zài mǎo shí chūfā.
    - We set out at the Mao hour.
  - 生肖属兔对应地支卯(生肖屬兔對應地支卯)。
    - Shēngxiào shǔ tù duìyìng dìzhī mǎo.
    - The Rabbit zodiac corresponds to the Earthly Branch Mao.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 卯(卯) → 刀(刀)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram — a knife; original form 𠚣, seen in a variant of 利 (𥝢) and atop 黎.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a knife with a distinct blade and handle. The graph was streamlined into the current two-stroke form and also serves as a component in characters like 利 and 黎.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿着一把刀(他拿著一把刀)。
    - Tā názhe yì bǎ dāo.
    - He is holding a knife.
  - 厨师用刀切蔬菜(廚師用刀切蔬菜)。
    - Chúshī yòng dāo qiē shūcài.
    - The chef uses a knife to cut vegetables.
  - 这把刀很锋利(這把刀很鋒利)。
    - Zhè bǎ dāo hěn fēnglì.
    - This knife is very sharp.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 丣(丣)
- **definition:** 10th Earthly Branch (archaic)
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: wine jar (ancient/variant form of 酉).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a wine vessel with a stopper, the same object later standardized as 酉. By extension it served as the 10th Earthly Branch, associated with the dusk period and the west.
- **examples:**
  - 古籍里(古籍裏)，丣是地支的第十位(丣是地支的第十位)，对应傍晚的酉时(對應傍晚的酉時)。
    - Gǔjí lǐ, yǒu shì dìzhī de dì shí wèi, duìyìng bàngwǎn de yǒu shí.
    - In old texts,丣 is the tenth Earthly Branch(丣 is the tenth Earthly Branch), corresponding to the evening You hour.
  - 在甲骨文研究中(在甲骨文研究中)，丣常被解释为盛酒的器形(丣常被解釋為盛酒的器形)。
    - Zài jiǎgǔwén yánjiū zhōng, yǒu cháng bèi jiěshì wéi shèng jiǔ de qìxíng.
    - In oracle-bone studies, 丣 is often interpreted as the shape of a wine vessel.
  - 这个古字偶尔出现在历法术语和古文献中(這個古字偶爾出現在曆法術語和古文獻中)。
    - Zhège gǔzì ǒu'ěr chūxiàn zài lìfǎ shùyǔ hé gǔ wénxiàn zhōng.
    - This archaic character occasionally appears in calendrical terminology and classical literature.
### 坐(坐) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth; soil; ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a lump of clay on the ground or on a potter’s wheel; the central dot was later stylized into a short horizontal stroke.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a clod of earth resting on the ground, later streamlined into a vertical line with top and bottom bars. The upper bar developed from an earlier dot to distinguish it from similar graphs.
- **examples:**
  - 这里的土很干(這裡的土很乾)。
    - Zhèlǐ de tǔ hěn gān.
    - The soil here is very dry.
  - 他把一把土撒在花盆里(他把一把土撒在花盆裡)。
    - Tā bǎ yì bǎ tǔ sǎ zài huāpén lǐ.
    - He sprinkled a handful of soil into the flowerpot.
  - 建房前要先检测土质(建房前要先檢測土質)。
    - Jiàn fáng qián yào xiān jiǎncè tǔzhì.
    - Before building a house, the soil quality must be tested.
