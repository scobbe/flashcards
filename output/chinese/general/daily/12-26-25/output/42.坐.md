## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 留(留) (liú, "to stay") + 土(土) (tǔ, "ground") =
    - staying on the ground ->
    - sitting
  - **interpretation:** The character 坐(坐) (zuò, "sit") combines 留(留) (liú, "to stay") with 土(土) (tǔ, "ground") to suggest the idea of remaining grounded or seated. This perfectly captures the essence of sitting, where one remains in place on the ground.
- **examples:**
  - 请坐(請坐)。
    - Qǐng zuò.
    - Please sit down.
  - 我坐公交车去学校(我坐公交車去學校)，朋友站在一旁(朋友站在一旁)。
    - Wǒ zuò gōngjiāo chē qù xuéxiào, péngyǒu zhàn zài yīpáng.
    - I take the bus to school, and my friend stands aside.
  - 夕阳下的人坐在椅子上(夕陽下的人坐在椅子上)，享受宁静时刻(享受寧靜時刻)。
    - Xīyáng xià de rén zuò zài yǐzi shàng, xiǎngshòu níngjìng shíkè.
    - The person sits on a chair under the sunset, enjoying a peaceful moment.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留)
- **definition:** to stay
- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 丣
    - yǒu
    - n/a
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - In bronze inscriptions: phonetic 卯 (mǎo) + semantic 田 (tián, "field") ->
    - Later forms: phonetic 丣 (yǒu) + semantic 田 (tián, "field") ->
    - 留 (liú, "to stay")
  - **interpretation:** The character 留 (liú) evolved from combining a field, 田 (tián), indicating a place, with phonetic elements 卯 (mǎo) and later 丣 (yǒu). This suggests remaining in a place, hence the meaning 'to stay.'
  - **simplification:** None, 留 is used as both traditional and simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 请你留步(請你留步)。
    - Qǐng nǐ liú bù.
    - Please wait.
  - 他决定留在学校(他決定留在學校)，专心学习(專心學習)。
    - Tā juédìng liú zài xuéxiào, zhuānxīn xuéxí.
    - He decided to stay at school and study diligently.
  - 母亲吩咐他留在家中(母親吩咐他留在家中)，不要四处乱跑(不要四處亂跑)。
    - Mǔqīn fēnfù tā liú zài jiā zhōng, bùyào sìchù luànpǎo.
    - Mother instructed him to stay at home and not run around.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 丣(丣)
- **definition:** n/a
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a crescent moon above a horizontal line
  - **interpretation:** The character 丣(yǒu) depicts a simple image of a crescent moon, which may originally represent a specific time of the lunar month. This is consistent with its use in ancient calendar systems.
- **examples:**
  - 我丣很多书(我丣很多書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu hěn duō shū.
    - I have many books.
  - 他丣新的衣服(他丣新的衣服)，喜欢穿着时髦(喜歡穿著時髦)。
    - Tā yǒu xīn de yīfú, xǐhuān chuānzhe shímáo.
    - He has new clothes and likes to dress fashionably.
  - 春日暖阳下(春日暖陽下)，大家丣欢愉心情(大家丣歡愉心情)。
    - Chūnrì nuǎnyáng xià, dàjiā yǒu huānyú xīnqíng.
    - Under the warm spring sunshine, everyone has a joyful mood.
### 坐(坐) → 留(留) → 田(田)
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a field
  - **interpretation:** The character 田(tián, 'field') is a pictogram that represents the layout of a field, showing a square with lines inside to indicate plots of land.
- **examples:**
  - 那块田肥沃(那塊田肥沃)。
    - Nà kuài tián féiwò.
    - That field is fertile.
  - 我们在田边休息(我們在田邊休息)，享受大自然的美景(享受大自然的美景)。
    - Wǒmen zài tián biān xiūxí, xiǎngshòu dà zìrán de měijǐng.
    - We rest by the field and enjoy the beauty of nature.
### 坐(坐) → 土(土)
- **definition:** ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel ->
    - stylized as a vertical stroke with a horizontal stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 土(tǔ, 'earth') visually represents a mound or pile of earth, capturing the essence of soil or clay. The simplification to strokes reflects the transition to a more abstract and recognizable symbol.
- **examples:**
  - 农夫耕种土(農夫耕種土)。
    - Nóngfū gēngzhòng tǔ.
    - The farmer plows the earth.
  - 雨后湿润的土(雨後濕潤的土)，孕育着生命(孕育著生命)。
    - Yǔ hòu shīrùn de tǔ, yùnyù zhe shēngmìng.
    - The moist soil after the rain nurtures life.
  - 儿童在土上玩耍(兒童在土上玩耍)。
    - Értóng zài tǔ shàng wánshuǎ.
    - Children play on the ground.
