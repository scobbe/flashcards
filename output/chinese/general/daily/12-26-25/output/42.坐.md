## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 留 (liú, "to stay") + 土 (tǔ, "ground") =
    - staying on the ground ->
    - sit
  - **interpretation:** The character 坐 (zuò, "sit") combines 留 (liú, "to stay") to convey the idea of remaining or staying, with 土 (tǔ, "ground") to indicate being positioned on the ground. This conveys the action of sitting down, highlighting being in a stable position on the ground.
- **examples:**
