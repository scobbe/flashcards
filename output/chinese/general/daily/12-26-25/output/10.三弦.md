## three-stringed Chinese lute used in Pingtan
### sānxián
---
- **definition:** three-stringed Chinese lute used in Pingtan
- **pinyin:** sānxián
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 弦
    - xián
    - string; chord
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 三(三) (sān, "three") + 弦(弦) (xián, "string") =
    - a musical instrument with three strings
  - **interpretation:** 三弦 (sānxián) refers to a traditional Chinese musical instrument known for having three strings. This combination of characters directly describes the instrument, highlighting its principal feature—the number of strings.
- **examples:**
  - 评弹表演中常以三弦传情(評彈表演中常以三弦傳情)。
    - Píngtán biǎoyǎn zhōng cháng yǐ sānxián chuánqíng.
    - In Pingtan performances, the three-stringed instrument is often used to convey emotions.
  - 老艺人熟练弹奏三弦(老藝人熟練彈奏三弦)，诉说古老评弹传奇(訴說古老評彈傳奇)。
    - Lǎo yìrén shúliàn dànzòu sānxián, sùshuō gǔlǎo píngtán chuánqí.
    - The veteran artist skillfully plays the three-stringed instrument, recounting the ancient legends of Pingtan.
### 三弦(三弦) → 三(三)
- **definition:** three
- **pinyin:** sān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 一(一) (yī, "one") x 3 =
    - 三(strokes in parallel) ->
    - "three"
  - **interpretation:** The character 三(sān, "three") visually represents the number three as three parallel horizontal lines, each line symbolizing a single unit. Its simplicity and direct logic make it an effective way to depict the number.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了三本书(我買了三本書)。
    - Wǒ mǎile sān běn shū.
    - I bought three books.
  - 他有三支笔(他有三支筆)，参加了三次比赛(參加了三次比賽)。
    - Tā yǒu sān zhī bǐ, cānjiāle sān cì bǐsài.
    - He has three pens, and participated in three competitions.
  - 三个人(三個人)，一起旅行(一起旅行)。
    - Sān gèrén, yìqǐ lǚxíng.
    - Three people traveled together.
### 三弦(三弦) → 弦(弦)
- **definition:** string; chord
- **pinyin:** xián
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - sī
    - silk
  - 玄
    - xuán
    - dark; mysterious
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 糸(纟) (sī, "silk") + 玄(玄) (xuán, phonetic) =
    - silk-related word with the sound of xuán ->
    - string (of an instrument) or chord
  - **interpretation:** 弦(弦) (xián, "string") combines the semantic element 糸(纟) (sī, "silk") with the phonetic 玄(玄) (xuán) to convey the idea of a string made of silk, akin to those used in musical instruments.
- **examples:**
  - 琴上弦紧绷(琴上弦緊繃)，奏出悠扬旋律(奏出悠揚旋律)。
    - qín shàng xián jǐn bēng, zòu chū yōu yáng xuán lǜ.

  - 小提琴的弦有着细腻的音色(小提琴的弦有著細膩的音色)。
    - xiǎo tí qín de xián yǒu zhe xì nì de yīn sè.
    - The violin's string has a delicate tone.
  - 古琴的弦悠长绵远(古琴的弦悠長綿遠)，传承古韵(傳承古韻)。
