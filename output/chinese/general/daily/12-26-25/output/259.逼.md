## force; compel
### bī
---
- **definition:** force; compel
- **pinyin:** bī
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 畐
    - fú
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 辶(辶) (chuò, 'walk') + phonetic: 畐(畐) (fú) =
    - movement towards a point ->
    - force or compel
  - **interpretation:** The character 逼(逼) (bī, 'force') combines the walking radical 辶(辶) (chuò, 'walk'), suggesting movement, with the phonetic 畐(畐) (fú), to indicate moving towards or pressuring something. This aligns with the meaning of forcing or compelling.
- **examples:**
  - 他逼我回答问题(他逼我回答問題)。
    - Tā bī wǒ huídá wèntí.
    - He forced me to answer the question.
  - 老板逼员工加班(老闆逼員工加班)，工期紧迫(工期緊迫)。
    - Lǎobǎn bī yuángōng jiābān, gōngqī jǐnpò.
    - The boss compelled the employees to work overtime, and the project deadline was tight.
### 逼(逼) → 辶(辵)

- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 辵
    - chuò
    - walk

    - used as a radical in conjunction with other components
  - **interpretation:** 辶(辵) (chuò, "walk") is derived by simplifying 能(mp) (néng, "self-reliant") structure from the character 辵. This simplified form emphasizes the act of motion or walking and is widely used as a radical in various motion-related characters.
- **examples:**

    - Xuéxí hànzì shí(學習漢字時)，chuò zuòwéi bùshǒu chūxiàn(辶作為部首出現)。
    - When studying Chinese characters, '辶' appears as a radical.
  - 许多表示走路的字(許多表示走路的字)，都含有辶这一部分(都含有辶這一部分)。
    - Xǔduō biǎoshì zǒulù de zì(許多表示走路的字)，dōu hányǒu chuò zhè yī bùfen(都含有辶這一部分)。
    - Many characters that denote walking contain the radical '辶'.
  - 古人写字时(古人寫字時)，常利用辶表达行走之意(常利用辶表達行走之意)。
