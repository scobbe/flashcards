## force; compel
### bī
---
- **definition:** force; compel
- **pinyin:** bī
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 畐
    - fú
    - abundant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk") + 畐(畐) (fú, "abundant") =
    - forceful movement ->
    - compel
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk"), suggesting movement or action, with 畐(畐) (fú, "abundant"), indicating the idea of applying force or pressure, thus conveying the sense of compel.
- **examples:**
  - 他逼我做出选择(他逼我做出選擇)。
    - Tā bī wǒ zuò chū xuǎnzé.
    - He forced me to make a choice.
  - 老板逼员工加班(老闆逼員工加班)，以完成项目(以完成項目)。
    - Lǎobǎn bī yuángōng jiābān, yǐ wánchéng xiàngmù.
    - The boss compelled the employees to work overtime to complete the project.
### 逼(逼) → 辶(辶)
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - walk
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 彳(彳) (chì, "walk") + 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop") ->
    - combined to suggest movement ->
    - stylized as 龰(龰)
  - **interpretation:** This character integrates 彳(彳) (chì, "walk") and 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop") to depict the action of moving with a pause, symbolically representing the concept of movement and travel. Over time, its form was stylized into 龰(龰).
- **examples:**
  - 古代书法中常见辶部(古代書法中常見辶部)，表现出行走之意(表現出行走之意)。
    - Gǔdài shūfǎ zhōng chángjiàn chuò bù, biǎoxiàn chū xíngzǒu zhī yì.
    - In ancient calligraphy, the radical '辶' is commonly seen, expressing the idea of movement.
  - 他行走时借助辶之意(他行走時借助辶之意)，步伐灵动自然(步伐靈動自然)。
    - Tā xíngzǒu shí jièzhù chuò zhī yì, bùfá língdòng zìrán.
