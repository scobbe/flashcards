### force; compel
---
- **pinyin:** force; compel
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - movement
  - 畐
    - fú
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 辶 which relates to movement, and the phonetic component 畐.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of force or compulsion through movement, suggesting a pushing or driving action.
- **examples:**
  - 他逼我去做决定(他逼我去做決定)。
    - Tā bī wǒ qù zuò juédìng.
    - He forces me to make a decision.
  - 她感到生活的压力(她感到生活的壓力)。
    - Tā gǎndào shēnghuó de yālì.
    - She feels the pressure of life.
  - 他们逼迫我们履行合同(他們逼迫我們履行合同)。
    - Tāmen bīpò wǒmen lǚxíng hétong.
    - They compel us to fulfill the contract.
### 逼(逼) → 辶(辶)
- **definition:** movement
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - xiè
    - walk
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot or to stop
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Combines 彳 (xiè, 'walk') + 止 (zhǐ, 'foot' or 'to stop') to convey the concept of movement.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively conveys movement by illustrating the act of walking with the foot and the action of stopping.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢走路(我喜歡走路)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zǒulù.
    - I like to walk.
  - 外面很冷，走快点(外面很冷，走快點)。
    - Wàimiàn hěn lěng, zǒu kuài diǎn.
    - It's cold outside, walk faster.
### 逼(逼) → 辶(辶) → 彳(彳)
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** xiè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a street intersection as seen from above from the left side of the character 行.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation of a street intersection intuitively connects to the meaning of walking or movement in various directions.
- **examples:**
  - 我在街上走(我在街上走)。
    - Wǒ zài jiē shàng zǒu.
    - I am walking on the street.
  - 他每天都散步(他每天都散步)。
    - Tā měitiān dōu sànbù.
    - He takes a walk every day.
### 逼(逼) → 辶(辶) → 止(止)
- **definition:** foot or to stop
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a foot that is stopping.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a foot in a position of halting movement, conveying the sense of stopping.
- **examples:**
  - 我想停下来(我想停下來)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng tíng xiàlái.
    - I want to stop.
  - 请在这里止步(請在這裡止步)。
    - Qǐng zài zhèlǐ zhǐ bù.
    - Please stop here.
### 逼(逼) → 畐(畐)
- **definition:** phonetic component
- **pinyin:** fú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an overflowing measure of grain or crops.
  - **interpretation:** The image of an overflowing measure suggests abundance and fertility, emphasizing the idea of fertility in agricultural contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 在古代，人们希望田地能畐满好收成(在古代，人們希望田地能畐滿好收成)。
    - zài gǔdài, rénmen xīwàng tiándì néng fúmǎn hǎo shōuchéng.
    - In ancient times, people hoped for their fields to be filled with good harvests.
  - 这字常用于古书中(這字常用於古書中)。
    - zhè zì cháng yòng yú gǔ shū zhōng.
    - This character is often used in ancient texts.
