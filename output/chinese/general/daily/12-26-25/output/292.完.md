## 
### finished; intact
---
- **pinyin:** finished; intact
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 完 is composed of the phonetic component 宀 (mián, 'roof') suggesting a dwelling, and the semantic component 完 (wán, 'complete, finished'), which reinforces the idea of completion or wholeness.
  - **interpretation:** The inclusion of a roof signifies protection and wholeness, implying that something is intact and completed within a secure shelter.
- **examples:**
  - 这个计划已完工(這個計劃已完工)。
    - Zhège jìhuà yǐ wángōng。
    - This project is finished.
  - 一切都完好无损(一切都完好無損)。
    - Yīqiè dōu wánhǎo wúsǔn。
    - Everything is intact.
### 完(完) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of a roof
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles a roof structure, evoking the idea of shelter or home.
- **examples:**
  - 房子有个宀(房子有個宀)，很温暖(很溫暖)。
    - fángzi yǒu gè mián, hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - The house has a roof, which is very warm.
  - 小屋的宀很古老(小屋的宀很古老)。
    - xiǎowū de mián hěn gǔlǎo.
    - The roof of the cottage is very old.
