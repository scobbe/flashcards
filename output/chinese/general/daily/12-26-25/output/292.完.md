## finished; intact
### wán
---
- **definition:** finished; intact
- **pinyin:** wán
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 元
    - yuán
    - beginning
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 宀(宀) (mián, 'roof') + 元(元) (yuán, 'beginning') =
    - a house with everything needed for completion ->
    - finished
  - **interpretation:** The character 完(wán, 'finished') combines 宀(宀, 'roof'), suggesting protection or an enclosed space, with 元(元, 'beginning'), indicating the idea of a whole or complete state. Together, they signify something that is 'finished' or 'intact'.
- **examples:**
  - 作业做完了(作業做完了)。
    - Zuòyè zuòwán le.
    - The homework is finished.
  - 我吃完饭了(我吃完飯了)。
    - Wǒ chī wán fàn le.
    - I finished eating.
  - 我写完报告了(我寫完報告了)，准备提交(準備提交)。
    - Wǒ xiě wán bàogào le, zhǔnbèi tíjiāo.
    - I finished writing the report and am ready to submit it.
### 完(完) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(宀) (mián, 'roof') is a pictogram representing the shape of a roof, illustrating the concept of shelter or covering typically associated with roofs.
- **examples:**
  - 这个汉字包含宀(這個漢字包含宀)，表示房屋结构(表示房屋結構)。
    - Zhè ge hànzì bāohán mián, biǎoshì fángwū jiégòu.
    - This Chinese character contains the radical mián, indicating the structure of a house.
  - 汉字中有宀部(漢字中有宀部)，代表与家有关的概念(代表與家有關的概念)。
    - Hànzì zhōng yǒu mián bù, dàibiǎo yǔ jiā yǒuguān de gàiniàn.
    - Chinese characters with the mián radical represent concepts associated with home.
### 完(完) → 元(元)
- **definition:** beginning
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a figure with two lines 二 (èr, "two") for a head (one connected to body, one above it), emphasizing the head.
  - **interpretation:** The character 元(yuán, "beginning") visually emphasizes the head, suggesting the start or origin of something, akin to the concept of a 'head' of a river or 'beginning' of an event.
- **examples:**
  - 元往往承载着希望(元往往承載著希望)。
    - Yuán wǎng wǎng chéngzài zhe xīwàng.
    - The beginning often carries hope.
  - 新生活从元开始(新生活從元開始)，每一天充满机遇(每一天充滿機遇)。
    - Xīn shēnghuó cóng yuán kāishǐ, měi yītiān chōngmǎn jīyù.
    - A new life begins at the start, and every day is full of opportunities.
  - 元之初往往蕴含无限可能(元之初往往蘊含無限可能)。
    - Yuán zhī chū wǎngwǎng yùnhán wúxiàn kěnéng.
    - The very beginning often contains infinite possibilities.
