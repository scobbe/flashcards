## morel mushroom
### yángdùjùn
---
- **definition:** morel mushroom
- **pinyin:** yángdùjùn
- **components:**
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
  - 肚
    - dù
    - stomach; belly
  - 菌
    - jùn
    - fungus; bacterium; mushroom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") + 肚(肚) (dù, "stomach") + 菌(菌) (jùn, "fungus; mushroom")
  - **interpretation:** The word 羊肚菌 (yángdùjùn) literally translates to 'sheep-stomach fungus'. This name is due to the appearance of the morel mushroom, which has a texture and pattern reminiscent of a sheep's stomach lining. This similarity in appearance is a typical way in Chinese to describe objects through familiar imagery.
- **examples:**
  - 今晚的汤里有羊肚菌(今晚的湯裡有羊肚菌)。
    - Jīn wǎn de tāng lǐ yǒu yángdùjùn.
    - There are morel mushrooms in tonight's soup.
  - 农家小院里发现了鲜嫩的羊肚菌(農家小院裡發現了鮮嫩的羊肚菌)，山路边隐约飘来诱人的香气(山路邊隱約飄來誘人的香氣)。
    - Nóngjiā xiǎoyuàn lǐ fāxiàn le xiānnèn de yángdùjùn, shānlù biān yǐnyuē piāolái yòurén de xiāngqì.
    - In a rural courtyard, tender morel mushrooms were discovered, and along the mountain path, an enticing aroma floated.
  - 在森林深处(在森林深處)，偶遇一群采摘羊肚菌的农人(偶遇一群採摘羊肚菌的農人)。
    - Zài sēnlín shēnchù, ǒuyù yī qún cǎizhāi yángdùjùn de nóngrén.
    - Deep in the forest, I accidentally met a group of farmers picking morel mushrooms.
