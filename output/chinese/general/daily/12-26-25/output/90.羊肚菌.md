### morel mushroom
---
- **pinyin:** morel mushroom
- **components:**
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep; goat; ram
  - 肚
    - dù
    - belly; abdomen; stomach
  - 菌
    - jùn
    - mushroom; fungus; bacterium
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 羊(羊) (yáng, 'sheep') + 肚(肚) (dù, 'belly') + 菌(菌) (jùn, 'mushroom') — sheep belly mushroom
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a type of edible mushroom known for its shape, which is reminiscent of the folds in a sheep's belly. This straightforward naming convention connects the mushroom's appearance to an easily recognizable form in nature.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天要去市场买羊肚菌。
    - Wǒ jīntiān yào qù shìchǎng mǎi yángdǔjùn.
    - I am going to the market today to buy morel mushrooms.
  - 羊肚菌在烹饪中非常受欢迎。
    - Yángdǔjùn zài pēngrèn zhōng fēicháng shòu huānyíng.
    - Morel mushrooms are very popular in cooking.
