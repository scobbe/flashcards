## celebrate
### qìng
---
- **definition:** celebrate
- **pinyin:** qìng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified character
  - **description:** 慶(慶) (qìng, "celebrate") simplified: eliminate bottom right 心(心) (xīn, "heart") component to leave top 10 大(大) (dà, "big")
  - **interpretation:** The simplification of 慶(慶) (qìng, "celebrate") removed the 心(心) (xīn, "heart") part, focusing visually on the 'big' joyous aspect without the emotional heart component.
  - **simplification:** In the simplification, 心(心) (xīn, "heart") was eliminated, leaving 大(大) (dà, "big") to simplify the character while retaining its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我们庆祝胜利(我們慶祝勝利)。
    - Wǒmen qìngzhù shènglì.
    - We celebrate victory.
  - 家庭聚会时(家庭聚會時)，我们庆祝团圆(我們慶祝團圓)。
    - Jiātíng jùhuì shí, wǒmen qìngzhù tuányuán.
    - During family gatherings, we celebrate reunion.
