## Zhanjiang City
### Zhànjiāng Shì
---
- **definition:** Zhanjiang City
- **pinyin:** Zhànjiāng Shì
- **components:**
  - 湛江
    - Zhànjiāng
    - Zhanjiang (toponym); (lit.) deep/limpid river
  - 市
    - shì
    - city; municipality; market
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 湛江 + 市 = city named Zhanjiang.
  - **interpretation:** Proper noun for the prefecture-level city in Guangdong; 湛江 is the toponym and 市 marks its administrative status as a city. Literally, 湛 means deep/limpid and 江 means river.
- **examples:**
  - 湛江市位于广东省西南部(湛江市位於廣東省西南部)。
    - Zhànjiāng Shì wèiyú Guǎngdōng Shěng xīnán bù.
    - Zhanjiang City is located in the southwest of Guangdong Province.
  - 我们计划去湛江市旅游(我們計劃去湛江市旅遊)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà qù Zhànjiāng Shì lǚyóu.
    - We plan to travel to Zhanjiang City.
  - 湛江市有湛江湾和雷州半岛等景点(湛江市有湛江灣和雷州半島等景點)。
    - Zhànjiāng Shì yǒu Zhànjiāng Wān hé Léizhōu Bàndǎo děng jǐngdiǎn.
    - Zhanjiang City has attractions such as Zhanjiang Bay and the Leizhou Peninsula.
### 湛江市 → 湛江
- **definition:** Zhanjiang (toponym); (lit.) deep/limpid river
- **pinyin:** Zhànjiāng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 湛 (deep; clear) + 江 (river) = Zhanjiang; literally “deep/clear river.”
  - **interpretation:** A toponym built from morphemes meaning “deep/clear” and “river,” evoking a clear, deep waterway. As a city name, it highlights the locale’s close connection to rivers and the sea in southwestern Guangdong.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算去湛江旅行(我打算去湛江旅行)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn qù Zhànjiāng lǚxíng.
    - I plan to travel to Zhanjiang.
  - 湛江的海鲜很有名(湛江的海鮮很有名)。
    - Zhànjiāng de hǎixiān hěn yǒumíng.
    - The seafood in Zhanjiang is very famous.
  - 名字里“湛”有深而清的意思，“江”指河流(名字裡「湛」有深而清的意思，「江」指河流)。
    - Míngzi lǐ “zhàn” yǒu shēn ér qīng de yìsi, “jiāng” zhǐ héliú.
    - In the name, “湛” conveys the sense of deep and clear, and “江” means river.
### 湛江市 → 市
- **definition:** city; municipality; market
- **pinyin:** shì
- **components:**
  - 兮
    - xī
    - exclamatory particle
  - 之
    - zhī
    - of (classical); it
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 兮 (“bustling”) + phonetic 之 (OC *tjɯ).
  - **interpretation:** Combines 兮 as a sense clue for a bustling, lively place with 之 providing the Old Chinese sound, matching the reconstructed reading. The association with liveliness fits a market, which then broadened to the urban ‘city’ sense.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在市中心等你(我們在市中心等你)。
    - Wǒmen zài shì zhōngxīn děng nǐ.
    - We are waiting for you in the city center.
  - 早市很热闹(早市很熱鬧)。
    - Zǎoshì hěn rènào.
    - The morning market is lively.
  - 市政府发布了新的交通规定(市政府發布了新的交通規定)。
    - Shì zhèngfǔ fābùle xīn de jiāotōng guīdìng.
    - The municipal government issued new traffic rules.
### 湛江市 → 市 → 兮
- **definition:** exclamatory particle
- **pinyin:** xī
- **components:**
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - fork in a tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound (會意)
  - **description:** Ideogrammic: 丂 (“fork in a tree”) + two parallel vertical strokes above (finer branches; sound of wind); originally the same as 乎.
  - **interpretation:** Imagery of a branching trunk with twigs evokes wind passing through branches, yielding an exclamatory sound. It later became a sentence-final particle expressing emotion or emphasis in Classical Chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 美人兮(美人兮)，见之不忘(見之不忘)。
    - měi rén xī, jiàn zhī bù wàng.
    - Fair beauty—upon seeing her, one does not forget.
  - 山高兮(山高兮)，水长(水長)。
    - shān gāo xī, shuǐ cháng.
    - High are the mountains; the waters run long.
  - 吾心伤兮(吾心傷兮)，泪下如雨(淚下如雨)。
    - wú xīn shāng xī, lèi xià rú yǔ.
    - My heart aches; tears fall like rain.
### 湛江市 → 市 → 兮 → 丂
- **definition:** fork in a tree
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** conflation
  - **description:** Conflation of several characters.
  - **interpretation:** Not a regular pictophonetic or ideogrammic formation; scribes merged features of multiple similar-looking graphs into a single standardized form. Its attested sense relates to troubled breathing, aligning its usage with notions of breath or obstruction rather than a clear semantic-phonetic decomposition.
- **examples:**
  - 奔跑过后(奔跑過後)，他丂得厉害(他丂得厲害)。
    - Bēnpǎo guòhòu, tā kǎo de lìhai.
    - After running, he was panting badly.
  - 山路陡峭(山路陡峭)，走几步就觉得丂(走幾步就覺得丂)。
    - Shānlù dǒuqiào, zǒu jǐ bù jiù juéde kǎo.
    - The mountain path is steep; after a few steps one feels out of breath.
  - 医生问他是否有丂和胸闷的症状(醫生問他是否有丂和胸悶的症狀)。
    - Yīshēng wèn tā shìfǒu yǒu kǎo hé xiōngmèn de zhèngzhuàng.
    - The doctor asked whether he had symptoms of panting and chest tightness.
### 湛江市 → 市 → 之
- **definition:** of (classical); it
- **pinyin:** zhī
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - stop; foot
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound (會意/会意)
  - **description:** 止 ‘foot’ + 一 ‘the beginning place’; originally written as 㞢, meaning ‘to go’.
  - **interpretation:** Combines a foot with a starting mark to picture setting off or moving forward. The footprint element later reduced to a dot, and the streamlined graph came to serve broad grammatical functions in Classical Chinese such as a genitive/objet pronoun.
- **examples:**
  - 此事之因，尚难尽述(此事之因，尚難盡述)。
    - Cǐ shì zhī yīn, shàng nán jìn shù.
    - The cause of this matter is hard to fully explain.
  - 我之前没见过他(我之前沒見過他)。
    - Wǒ zhīqián méi jiàn guò tā.
    - I hadn’t seen him before.
  - 取之不尽，用之不竭(取之不盡，用之不竭)。
    - Qǔ zhī bù jìn, yòng zhī bù jié.
    - Take from it without end; use it without exhausting it.
### 湛江市 → 市 → 之 → 止
- **definition:** stop; foot
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a footprint pointing up (originally “foot”), later stylized as 龰; oracle forms show three toes and a sole.
  - **interpretation:** A footprint with toes pointing upward represents the foot. The sense broadened from the physical foot to the act of halting one’s step, hence “to stop”; the specific ‘toe’ meaning was preserved in 趾.
- **examples:**
  - 请立即停止前进(請立即停止前進)。
    - Qǐng lìjí tíngzhǐ qiánjìn.
    - Please stop advancing immediately.
  - 此处止步，非请勿入(此處止步，非請勿入)。
    - Cǐ chù zhǐbù, fēi qǐng wù rù.
    - Stop here; do not enter without invitation.
  - 药到病除，疼痛已止(藥到病除，疼痛已止)。
    - Yào dào bìng chú, téngtòng yǐ zhǐ.
    - With the right medicine, the illness is cured and the pain has stopped.
### 湛江市 → 市 → 之 → 一
- **definition:** one
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** a horizontal stroke, indicating the number 'one'.
  - **interpretation:** A single horizontal line visually represents the concept of oneness. As the simplest numeral, it set the pattern for 二 and 三 by repeating the stroke to show increasing quantity.
- **examples:**
  - 我只有一本书(我只有一本書)。
    - Wǒ zhǐ yǒu yì běn shū.
    - I only have one book.
  - 他一到就给我打电话(他一到就給我打電話)。
    - Tā yí dào jiù gěi wǒ dǎ diànhuà.
    - As soon as he arrives, he calls me.
  - 一个问题都没有(一個問題都沒有)。
    - Yí gè wèntí dōu méiyǒu.
    - There isn’t a single problem.
