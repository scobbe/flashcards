## attempt; try; intend
### qǐtú
---
- **definition:** attempt; try; intend
- **pinyin:** qǐtú
- **components:**
  - 企
    - qǐ
    - to plan; to stand on tiptoe
  - 图(圖)
    - tú
    - to map; to plan
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 企(企) (qǐ, "to plan; to stand on tiptoe") + 图(圖) (tú, "to map; to plan") =
    - to attempt or try to achieve something through planning.
  - **interpretation:** The word combines the idea of planning and standing on tiptoe to reach something with the concept of mapping out or planning. It conveys the sense of making an effort or attempt to achieve a goal.
- **examples:**
  - 他企图骗取公司资金(他企圖騙取公司資金)。
    - Tā qǐtú piànqǔ gōngsī zījīn.
    - He attempted to defraud the company funds.
  - 他们企图逃离现场(他們企圖逃離現場)，以躲避追捕(以躲避追捕)。
    - Tāmen qǐtú táolí xiànchǎng, yǐ duǒbì zhuībǔ.
    - They attempted to flee the scene to evade capture.
  - 她企图用谎言掩盖真相(她企圖用謊言掩蓋真相)。
    - Tā qǐtú yòng huǎngyán yǎngài zhēnxiàng.
    - She attempted to cover up the truth with lies.

### 企图(企圖) → 图(圖)
- **definition:** to map; to plan
- **pinyin:** tú
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 㝛
    - dīng
    - male adult
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 囗(囗) (wéi, 'enclosure') + phonetic 㝛(丁) (dīng, 'male adult') ->
    - 㝛(丁) (shān, 'mountain') appears as a sound component
  - **interpretation:** The character 囗(囗) (wéi, 'enclosure') suggests a bounded area, which complements the concept of a map or plan which is a delineation of space. The phonetic 㝛(丁) gives the sound to the character.
  - **simplification:** During simplification, the traditional component 㝛 was replaced by a simplified inner structure to create 图(圖).
- **examples:**
  - 这张图讲述了历史故事(這張圖講述了歷史故事)。
    - Zhè zhāng tú jiǎngshù le lìshǐ gùshì.

  - 他画了一张图(他畫了一張圖)，展示了未来规划(展示了未來規劃)。
    - Tā huà le yī zhāng tú, zhǎnshì le wèilái guīhuà.
    - He drew a picture, showcasing his future plan.

### 企图(企圖) → 图(圖) → 㝛(㝛) → 毋(毋)
- **definition:** do not
- **pinyin:** wú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 母(母) (mǔ, "mother") borrowed ->
    - specialized form with one stroke replacing two dots ->
    - 毋(毋) (wú, "do not")
  - **interpretation:** The character 毋 initially borrowed the pictogram for 母(母) (mǔ, "mother"). To differentiate the meanings while maintaining the original form, an adjustment was made by reducing the two dots to a single stroke, resulting in 毋(毋) (wú, "do not").
- **examples:**

    - Wú tān xiǎo lì.
    - Do not be greedy for trivial gains.
  - 做事要稳重(做事要穩重)，毋急躁行事(毋急躁行事)。
    - Zuò shì yào wěn zhòng, wú jí zào xíng shì.
    - Be steady in your actions, and do not act hastily.
  - 毋忘国耻(毋忘國恥)，勇创佳绩(勇創佳績)。
    - Wú wàng guó chǐ, yǒng chuàng jiā jì.
    - Never forget the national humiliation, and strive to achieve excellence.
### 企图(企圖) → 图(圖) → 㝛(㝛) → 卯(卯)
- **definition:** branch
- **pinyin:** mǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

    - sacrificial practice ->
    - obscured meaning ->
    - open door/mortise re-interpretation
