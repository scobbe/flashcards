## attempt; try; intend
### qǐtú
---
- **definition:** attempt; try; intend
- **pinyin:** qǐtú
- **components:**
  - 企
    - qǐ
    - to plan; stand on tiptoe; look forward to
  - 图(圖)
    - tú
    - to plan; to scheme; intention; chart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 企(企) (qǐ, "to plan or stand on tiptoe") and 图(圖) (tú, "to plan or intend")
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 企(企) (qǐ, "to plan or stand on tiptoe") and 图(圖) (tú, "to plan or intend") expresses the notion of actively planning or intending to achieve a goal. Both components involve aspects of making a deliberate effort or intention.
  - **simplification:** simplified from 企圖
- **examples:**
  - 他企图逃跑(他企圖逃跑)。
    - Tā qǐtú táopǎo.
    - He attempted to escape.
  - 公司企图拓展市场(公司企圖拓展市場)，忽视了内部管理(忽視了內部管理)。
    - Gōngsī qǐtú tuòzhǎn shìchǎng, hūshìle nèibù guǎnlǐ.
    - The company attempted to expand into the market while neglecting internal management.
  - 他企图说服老师(他企圖說服老師)，改变态度(改變態度)。
    - Tā qǐtú shuōfú lǎoshī, gǎibiàn tàidù.
    - He attempted to persuade his teacher to change his attitude.

- **pinyin:** tú
- **components:**
  - 囧
    - jiǒng
    - window
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 囧(囧) (jiǒng, "window") + semantic: 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - a picture drawn within a boundary ->
    - picture/map
  - **interpretation:** The character 图(图) (tú) combines 囧(囧) (jiǒng, "window") as the phonetic element, suggesting a rectangular shape like a window or a frame, with 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") as a semantic component, indicating an enclosed area, apt for depicting enclosed spaces like a map or diagram.
