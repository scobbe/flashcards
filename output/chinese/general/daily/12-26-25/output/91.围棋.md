## Go game
### wéiqí
---
- **definition:** Go game
- **pinyin:** wéiqí
- **components:**
  - 围(圍)
    - wéi
    - surround
  - 棋
    - qí
    - board game; chess
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") + 棋(棋) (qí, "board game") refers to the board game Go, which involves strategic placement of pieces to surround more territory than the opponent.
  - **interpretation:** 围棋(圍棋) (wéiqí, "Go game") literally translates to a board game (棋) where players aim to surround (围) their opponent's pieces to capture territory. It is a traditional and highly strategic Chinese game, known for its rich history and deep complexity.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢围棋(我喜歡圍棋)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān wéiqí.
    - I like to play Go.
  - 昨天我和朋友下围棋(昨天我和朋友下圍棋)，双方激烈对抗(雙方激烈對抗)。
    - Zuótiān wǒ hé péngyǒu xià wéiqí, shuāngfāng jīliè duìkàng.
    - Yesterday, my friend and I played Go, and both sides competed fiercely.
  - 围棋不仅锻炼思维(圍棋不僅鍛鍊思維)，还培养耐心(還培養耐心)。
    - Wéiqí bùjǐn duànliàn sīwéi, hái péiyǎng nàixīn.
    - Go not only sharpens the mind, but also develops patience.
### 围棋(圍棋) → 围(圍)
- **definition:** surround
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - soft leather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") + 韋(韋) (wéi, "soft leather") =
    - something surrounded by an enclosure ->
    - surround
  - **interpretation:** The character 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") combines the concept of an enclosure with the phonetic element 韋(韋) (wéi, "soft leather"). This combination intuitively suggests the idea of surrounding something, much like enclosing soft leather around an object.
  - **simplification:** 圍(圍) → 围(圍): 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") was simplified by removing the internal component 韋(韋) (wéi, "soft leather").
- **examples:**
  - 士兵围困敌军(士兵圍困敵軍)。
    - Shìbīng wéikùn díjūn.
    - Soldiers surrounded the enemy army.
  - 村民围绕湖边举行庆典(村民圍繞湖邊舉行慶典)。
    - Cūnmín wéirào húbiān jǔxíng qìngdiǎn.
    - Villagers held a celebration around the lake.
  - 老师围着学生讲解科学(老師圍著學生講解科學)。
    - Lǎoshī wéizhe xuéshēng jiǎngjiě kēxué.
    - The teacher explained science while gathering the students around.

### 围棋(圍棋) → 棋(棋) → 其(其)
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket pictogram) ->
    - 丌 (stand) added under basket as phonetic and to enhance meaning ->
    - borrowed for pronoun 'his' ->
    - 箕(箕) (jī, 'basket') derived with additional 竹(竹) (zhú, 'bamboo')
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a basket, symbolized by 𠀠, emphasizing its use. The addition of 丌 beneath the basket strengthened the meaning of a structural object or container, eventually leading to its phonetic borrowing to represent the pronoun 'his'. To reclaim the original meaning of a 'basket', 箕(箕) (jī, 'basket') was formed with an added bamboo radical.
- **examples:**
  - 将军因其英勇闻名(將軍因其英勇聞名)。
    - Jiāngjūn yīn qí yīngyǒng wénmíng。
    - The general is famous for his valor.
  - 同袍尊其忠诚(同袍尊其忠誠)，士卒佩服其英明(士卒佩服其英明)。
    - Tóngpáo zūn qí zhōngchéng, shìzú pèifú qí yīngmíng。
    - His comrades revere his loyalty, and the soldiers admire his wisdom.
