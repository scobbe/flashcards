## Go game
### wéiqí
---
- **definition:** Go game
- **pinyin:** wéiqí
- **components:**
  - 围(圍)
    - wéi
    - surround
  - 棋
    - qí
    - board game; chess
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") refers to the act of surrounding, and 棋(棋) (qí, "board game") represents the game played on a board.
  - **interpretation:** In the game of 围棋(圍棋) (wéiqí, "Go"), players surround and capture each other's stone pieces on a board. The term highlights the strategic nature of surrounding in the game.
  - **simplification:** 围(圍) is the simplified form of 圍.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢围棋(我喜歡圍棋)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan wéiqí.
    - I like the game of Go.
  - 他每天练习围棋(他每天練習圍棋)，梦想成为围棋大师(夢想成為圍棋大師)。
    - Tā měitiān liànxí wéiqí, mèngxiǎng chéngwéi wéiqí dàshī.
    - He practices Go every day and dreams of becoming a Go master.
  - 昔日士子以围棋论交(昔日士子以圍棋論交)，今人依然爱好围棋(今人依然愛好圍棋)。
    - Xīrì shìzi yǐ wéiqí lùn jiāo, jīnrén yīrán àihào wéiqí.
    - In ancient times, scholars socialized through Go, and today people still enjoy the game.
