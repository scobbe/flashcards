## Go game
### wéiqí
---
- **traditional:** 圍棋
- **definition:** Go game
- **pinyin:** wéiqí
- **components:**
  - 围(圍)
    - wéi
    - surround
  - 棋
    - qí
    - board game; chess
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") + 棋 (qí, "board game") refers to the board game Go, which involves strategic placement of pieces to surround more territory than the opponent.
  - **interpretation:** 围(圍)棋 (wéiqí, "Go game") literally translates to a board game (棋) where players aim to surround (围) their opponent's pieces to capture territory. It is a traditional and highly strategic Chinese game, known for its rich history and deep complexity.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢围(歡圍)棋。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān wéiqí.
    - I like to play Go.
  - 昨天我和朋友下围(圍)棋，双(雙)方激烈对(對)抗。
    - Zuótiān wǒ hé péngyǒu xià wéiqí, shuāngfāng jīliè duìkàng.
    - Yesterday, my friend and I played Go, and both sides competed fiercely.
  - 围(圍)棋不仅锻炼(僅鍛鍊)思维(維)，还(還)培养(養)耐心。
    - Wéiqí bùjǐn duànliàn sīwéi, hái péiyǎng nàixīn.
    - Go not only sharpens the mind, but also develops patience.
### 围(圍)棋( ) → 围(圍)
- **traditional:** 圍
- **definition:** surround
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - soft leather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 囗 (wéi, "enclosure") + 韋 (wéi, "soft leather") =
    - something surrounded by an enclosure ->
    - surround
  - **interpretation:** The character 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") combines the concept of an enclosure with the phonetic element 韋 (wéi, "soft leather"). This combination intuitively suggests the idea of surrounding something, much like enclosing soft leather around an object.
  - **simplification:** 圍 → 围(圍): 囗 (wéi, "enclosure") was simplified by removing the internal component 韋 (wéi, "soft leather").
- **examples:**
  - 士兵围(圍)困敌军(敵軍)。
    - Shìbīng wéikùn díjūn.
    - Soldiers surrounded the enemy army.
  - 村民围绕(圍繞)湖边举(邊舉)行庆(慶)典。
    - Cūnmín wéirào húbiān jǔxíng qìngdiǎn.
    - Villagers held a celebration around the lake.
  - 老师围着学(師圍著學)生讲(講)解科学(學)。
    - Lǎoshī wéizhe xuéshēng jiǎngjiě kēxué.
    - The teacher explained science while gathering the students around.
### 围(圍)棋( ) → 围(圍) → 囗
- **definition:** enclosure
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an enclosure
  - **interpretation:** The character 囗 (wéi, 'enclosure') is a pictogram representing a square or rectangular enclosure, emblematic of walls or boundaries. It visually reflects a basic enclosed area delineated by straight lines.
- **examples:**
  - 博物馆(館)外有坚(堅)固的囗，保护着历(護著歷)史文物。
    - Bówùguǎn wài yǒu jiāngù de wéi, bǎohùzhe lìshǐ wénwù.

  - 古城外围(圍)的囗，确(確)保了居民的安全。
    - Gǔchéng wàiwéi de wéi, quèbǎole jūmín de ānquán.
    - The ancient city's enclosure ensures the residents' safety.

- **definition:** soft leather
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts leather being twisted or tanned
  - **interpretation:** The character 韋 (wéi, 'soft leather') visually represents a process in leatherworking, such as twisting or tanning, which softens the tough material.
- **examples:**
  - 这种(這種)韋皮手感细腻(細膩)，更适(適)合制(製)作高档(檔)家具。
    - Zhè zhǒng wěi pí shǒu gǎn xì nì, gèng shì hé zhì zuò gāo dàng jiā jù.
    - This kind of soft leather has a delicate texture and is more suitable for making high-end furniture.
  - 博物馆(館)展出了许(許)多用韋制成的文物，吸引了众(眾)多游(遊)客。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchūle xǔduō yòng wěi zhìchéng de wénwù, xīyǐnle zhòngduō yóukè.
    - The museum exhibited numerous artifacts made of soft leather, attracting many visitors.
### 围(圍)棋( ) → 棋
- **definition:** board game; chess
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wooden
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木 (mù, "wooden") + 其 (qí, "his") =
    - object made of wood, phonetically related to 'his' ->
    - board game
  - **interpretation:** The character 棋(qí) combines 木 (mù, "wood") to indicate that traditional board games are made of wood, with 其 (qí) providing the pronunciation link. This aligns with the nature of board games, typically played on wooden boards.
- **examples:**
  - 他经(經)常下棋。
    - Tā jīngcháng xià qí.
    - He often plays chess.
  - 他们(們)在公园(園)下棋，享受美好时(時)光。
    - Tāmen zài gōngyuán xià qí, xiǎngshòu měihǎo shíguāng.
    - They play chess in the park and enjoy a wonderful time.
  - 她学习(學習)棋艺(藝)，以提高智力。
    - Tā xuéxí qí yì, yǐ tígāo zhìlì.
    - She studies chess techniques to improve her intellect.

### 围(圍)棋( ) → 棋 → 其
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket pictogram) ->
    - 丌 (stand) added under basket as phonetic and to enhance meaning ->
    - borrowed for pronoun 'his' ->
    - 箕 (jī, 'basket') derived with additional 竹 (zhú, 'bamboo')
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a basket, symbolized by 𠀠, emphasizing its use. The addition of 丌 beneath the basket strengthened the meaning of a structural object or container, eventually leading to its phonetic borrowing to represent the pronoun 'his'. To reclaim the original meaning of a 'basket', 箕 (jī, 'basket') was formed with an added bamboo radical.
- **examples:**
  - 将军(將軍)因其英勇闻(聞)名。
    - Jiāngjūn yīn qí yīngyǒng wénmíng。
    - The general is famous for his valor.
  - 同袍尊其忠诚(誠)，士卒佩服其英明。
    - Tóngpáo zūn qí zhōngchéng, shìzú pèifú qí yīngmíng。
    - His comrades revere his loyalty, and the soldiers admire his wisdom.
### 围(圍)棋( ) → 棋 → 其 → 箕
- **definition:** basket
- **pinyin:** jī
- **components:**
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + 其 (qí, "his") =
    - combines bamboo element with phonetic component 其 to convey 'basket' ->
    - specialization of 其
  - **interpretation:** The character 箕 (jī, "basket") was created to specify the meaning of 'basket' by adding the bamboo radical 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") to the character 其 (qí, "his"), which provides the phonetic cue. This reflects its use as a container often made from bamboo.
- **examples:**
  - 老农(農)用箕收割稻谷(穀)。
    - Lǎo nóng yòng jī shōugē dào gǔ。
    - The old farmer uses a basket to harvest rice.
  - 在村口，小贩将粮(販將糧)食倒进(進)箕中。
    - Zài cūnkǒu, xiǎofàn jiāng liángshí dào jìn jī zhōng。
    - At the village entrance, the vendor pours grain into the basket.
