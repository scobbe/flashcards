### Go game
---
- **pinyin:** Go game
- **components:**
  - 围(圍)
    - wéi
    - surround; encircle; enclose; blockade
  - 棋
    - qí
    - board game; chess; strategy; play
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 围(圍) (wéi, "surround") + 棋(棋) (qí, "board game") — surround board game
- **examples:**
  - 我在学习围棋(我在學習圍棋)。
    - Wǒ zài xuéxí wéiqí.
    - I am learning Go.
  - 围棋比赛(圍棋比賽)非常有趣。
    - Wéiqí bǐsài fēicháng yǒuqù.
    - The Go competition is very interesting.
### 围棋(圍棋) → 围(圍)
- **definition:** surround; encircle; enclose; blockade
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - to surround
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 囗 ("enclosure") + phonetic 韋 ("to surround")
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts the concept of an enclosure being surrounded, conveying the meaning of encirclement clearly. This makes intuitive sense as it visually represents the idea of something being enclosed or surrounded by boundaries.
- **examples:**
  - 他们围住了这个地方(他們圍住了這個地方)。
    - Tāmen wéi zhùle zhège dìfāng.

  - 我家周围有很多树(我家周圍有很多樹)。
    - Wǒ jiā zhōuwéi yǒu hěn duō shù.
    - There are many trees surrounding my house.

### 围棋(圍棋) → 围(圍) → 韋(韋)
- **definition:** to surround
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character is historically associated with leather or hide, indicating a form of protection, which connects to the idea of surrounding or enclosing.
  - **interpretation:** The protective quality of leather evokes the notion of surrounding or enclosing, similar to how an animal hide might envelop and protect what is inside.
- **examples:**
  - 韋編三絕(韋編三絕)，書法藝術無人能敵(書法藝術無人能敵)。
