### Bā Jīn
---
- **definition:** Ba Jin
- **pinyin:** Bā Jīn
- **components:**
  - 巴
    - bā
    - to cling; a surname
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold; metal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 巴 (bā, "to cling; a surname") is often used as a proper noun or surname, and 金 (jīn, "gold; metal") is also commonly used as a surname or to represent wealth or value.
  - **interpretation:** 巴金(Bā Jīn) is a well-known pen name of the famed 20th-century Chinese writer. The pen name reflects both personal identity as a surname and the value or richness associated with his literary contributions.
- **examples:**
  - 我经(經)常阅读(閱讀)巴金的小说(說)。
    - Wǒ jīngcháng yuèdú Bā Jīn de xiǎoshuō.

  - 巴金的作品深刻，影响(響)了一代人。
    - Bā Jīn de zuòpǐn shēnkè, yǐngxiǎngle yī dài rén.
    - Ba Jin's works are profound and have influenced a generation.
  - 我在讲(講)座中听(聽)到巴金的演讲(講)。
    - Wǒ zài jiǎngzuò zhōng tīngdào Bā Jīn de yǎnjiǎng.
    - I heard Ba Jin's speech at the lecture.

- **pinyin:** jīn
- **components:**

  - 呂
    - lǚ
    - two blocks of metal
  - 亼
    - jī
    - open mouth flipped upside down
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

### 巴金 → 金 → 呂

- **pinyin:** lǚ

  - 鋁
    - lǚ
    - ingot
  - 膂
    - lǚ

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts two blocks of metal ->
    - original form of 鋁 (lǚ, 'ingot') ->
    - borrowed phonetically for 膂 (lǚ, 'spine') and 呂 (lǚ, 'music notes')
