## Zhongnanhai
### Zhōngnánhǎi
---
- **definition:** Zhongnanhai
- **pinyin:** Zhōngnánhǎi
- **components:**
  - 中海
    - Zhōnghǎi
    - Zhonghai (Middle Lake in Beijing); central sea; middle lake
  - 南海
    - Nánhǎi
    - Nanhai (South Lake in Beijing); south sea; South China Sea
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中海 (Middle Lake) + 南海 (South Lake) = the combined lakes/compound in Beijing.
  - **interpretation:** Names the area encompassing the Zhonghai and Nanhai lakes west of the Forbidden City; here 海 refers to lakes. By extension it serves as a metonym for China’s central leadership compound.
- **examples:**
  - 中南海位于紫禁城西侧(中南海位於紫禁城西側)。
    - Zhōngnánhǎi wèi yú Zǐjìnchéng xīcè.
    - Zhongnanhai is located to the west of the Forbidden City.
  - 媒体常用“中南海”代指中国中央领导层(媒體常用「中南海」代指中國中央領導層)。
    - Méitǐ cháng yòng “Zhōngnánhǎi” dàizhǐ Zhōngguó zhōngyāng lǐngdǎo céng.
    - The media often uses “Zhongnanhai” as a metonym for China’s top leadership.
  - 游客不能随意进入中南海(遊客不能隨意進入中南海)。
    - Yóukè bùnéng suíyì jìnrù Zhōngnánhǎi.
    - Visitors cannot freely enter Zhongnanhai.
### 中南海 → 中海
- **definition:** Zhonghai (Middle Lake in Beijing); central sea; middle lake
- **pinyin:** Zhōnghǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中 (middle/central) + 海 (sea/large lake) = the central lake among Beijing’s “Three Seas.”
  - **interpretation:** Specifically names the middle of the three connected lakes (Beihai, Zhonghai, Nanhai) in central Beijing. Today it lies within the Zhongnanhai leadership compound and is not generally open to the public.
- **examples:**
  - 中海位于中南海景区内(中海位於中南海景區內)，是三海之一(是三海之一)。
    - Zhōnghǎi wèi yú Zhōngnánhǎi jǐngqū nèi, shì Sānhǎi zhī yī.
    - Zhonghai is located within the Zhongnanhai area and is one of the Three Seas.
  - 游客不能随意进入中海附近的区域(遊客不能隨意進入中海附近的區域)，因为这里是重要的党政机关所在地(因為這裡是重要的黨政機關所在地)。
    - Yóukè bù néng suíyì jìnrù Zhōnghǎi fùjìn de qūyù, yīnwèi zhèlǐ shì zhòngyào de dǎngzhèng jīguān suǒzàidì.
    - Visitors cannot freely enter the area near Zhonghai because it houses important party and government offices.
  - 北京的三海——北海、中海、南海——历史上属于皇家园林(北京的三海——北海、中海、南海——歷史上屬於皇家園林)。
    - Běijīng de Sānhǎi——Běihǎi, Zhōnghǎi, Nánhǎi——lìshǐ shàng shǔyú huángjiā yuánlín.
    - Beijing’s Three Seas—Beihai, Zhonghai, and Nanhai—historically belonged to the imperial gardens.
### 中南海 → 南海
- **definition:** Nanhai (South Lake in Beijing); south sea; South China Sea
- **pinyin:** Nánhǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 南 (south) + 海 (sea; lake) = southern waters; also a named 'South Lake'.
  - **interpretation:** Combines a direction with a water-body noun to denote waters to the south or a toponym. Most often refers to the South China Sea; in Beijing it names the South Lake within Zhongnanhai, and it appears in place names like Nanhai District.
- **examples:**
  - 台风正向南海移动，渔船已回港避风(颱風正向南海移動，漁船已回港避風)。
    - Táifēng zhèng xiàng Nánhǎi yídòng, yúchuán yǐ huígǎng bìfēng.
    - The typhoon is moving toward the South China Sea, and fishing boats have returned to harbor to take shelter.
  - 这幅画描绘了北京中南海的南海景色(這幅畫描繪了北京中南海的南海景色)。
    - Zhè fú huà miáohuì le Běijīng Zhōngnánhǎi de Nánhǎi jǐngsè.
    - This painting depicts the scenery of the South Lake in Beijing’s Zhongnanhai.
  - 佛山的南海区产业发达，交通便利(佛山的南海區產業發達，交通便利)。
    - Fóshān de Nánhǎi Qū chǎnyè fādá, jiāotōng biànlì.
    - Nanhai District in Foshan has a developed industry and convenient transportation.
