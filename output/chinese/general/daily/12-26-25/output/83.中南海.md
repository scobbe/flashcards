### Zhongnanhai
---
- **pinyin:** Zhongnanhai
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle; center; within; among
  - 南
    - nán
    - south; southern; southward; towards the south
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea; ocean; large body of water; maritime
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle") + 南(南) (nán, "south") + 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") — middle south sea
  - **interpretation:** Zhongnanhai refers to a specific area in Beijing that serves as a central government compound. It is a notable place for its political importance and historical significance.
- **examples:**
  - 中南海是中国的政治中心。(中南海是中國的政治中心。)
    - Zhōngnán hǎi shì Zhōngguó de zhèngzhì zhōngxīn.
    - Zhongnanhai is the political center of China.
  - 很多重要决策在中南海作出。(很多重要決策在中南海作出。)
    - Hěnduō zhòngyào juécè zài Zhōngnán hǎi zuòcù.
    - Many important decisions are made in Zhongnanhai.
### 中南海(中南海) → 中(中)
- **definition:** middle; center; within; among
- **pinyin:** zhōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a flagpole with a drum, placed in the center to gather people and detect the wind direction.
  - **interpretation:** The iconography of a flagpole suggests a point of gathering or focus, which aligns with the meaning of 'middle' or 'center'.
- **examples:**
  - 我在中国(我在中國)。
    - Wǒ zài Zhōngguó.
    - I am in China.
  - 他在中间(他在中間)等我。
    - Tā zài zhōngjiān děng wǒ.
    - He waits for me in the middle.
  - 请站在中间(請站在中間)。
    - Qǐng zhàn zài zhōngjiān.
    - Please stand in the center.
### 中南海(中南海) → 南(南)
- **definition:** south; southern; southward; towards the south
- **pinyin:** nán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 南 (nán) originally depicted a bird flying south, indicating direction, based on its association with seasonal migrations.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively represents direction, as it signifies a place where the sun rises, depicting a common geographical orientation in relation to the cardinal directions.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在南方(我住在南方)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài nánfāng.
    - I live in the south.
  - 南风起(南風起)，天气变暖(天氣變暖)。
    - Nánfēng qǐ, tiānqì biàn nuǎn.
    - The south wind rises, the weather warms up.
  - 我们往南走(我們往南走)。
    - Wǒmen wǎng nán zǒu.
    - We are heading south.
### 中南海(中南海) → 海(海)
- **definition:** sea; ocean; large body of water; maritime
- **pinyin:** hǎi
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 每
    - měi
    - each; every
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic component 氵 (water) + phonetic component 每 (pronounced 'měi').
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of water (氵) associated with the phonetic element (每), indicating a connection to vast bodies of water.
- **examples:**
  - 海洋很广阔(海洋很廣闊)，我们可以去游泳(我們可以去游泳)。
  - 我喜欢看海(我喜歡看海)。
  - 海鸥在海上飞翔(海鷗在海上飛翔)。

### 中南海(中南海) → 海(海) → 每(每)
- **definition:** each; every
- **pinyin:** měi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 每 represents the concept of 'every' or 'each', often depicted as a person (representing individual) and a line below indicating a repetitive action or inclusion.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as it conveys the idea of every individual represented by the person being included in a group.
- **examples:**
  - 每个人都有权利(每個人都有權利)。
    - měi gè rén dōu yǒu quánlì.
    - Everyone has rights.
  - 每次会议都很重要(每次會議都很重要)。
    - měi cì huìyì dōu hěn zhòngyào.
    - Every meeting is important.
