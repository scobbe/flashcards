### children of officials
---
- **pinyin:** children of officials
- **components:**
  - 官
    - guān
    - official; government official; to manage; to officiate
  - 二代
    - èr dài
    - second generation; second generation of a family; descendant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 官(官) (guān, "official") + 二代(二代) (èr dài, "second generation") — second generation of officials
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to the children of government officials who may benefit from their parents' status. The combination highlights a lineage associated with political power.
- **examples:**
  - 他们被称为官二代(官二代)。
    - Tāmen bèi chēng wèi guān èr dài.
    - They are referred to as the children of officials.
  - 许多官二代(官二代)在事业上取得成功。
    - Xǔduō guān èr dài zài shìyè shàng qǔdé chénggōng.
    - Many children of officials achieve success in their careers.

### 官二代(官二代) → 二代(二代)
- **definition:** second generation; second generation of a family; descendant
- **pinyin:** èr dài
- **components:**
  - 二
    - èr
    - two; second; secondarily
  - 代
    - dài
    - generation; era; substitute; instead
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 二(二) (èr, "two") + 代(代) (dài, "generation") — second generation
  - **interpretation:** This word refers specifically to the second generation of a lineage or family. Using '二' (èr, "two") emphasizes that it is the subsequent generation, while '代' (dài, "generation") identifies the group of descendants.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个二代(他是一個二代)。
    - Tā shì yī gè èr dài.
    - He is a second-generation descendant.
  - 二代人在社会中有不同的挑战。(二代人在社會中有不同的挑戰。)
    - Èr dài rén zài shèhuì zhōng yǒu bùtóng de tiǎozhàn.
    - Second generation individuals face different challenges in society.
### 官二代(官二代) → 二代(二代) → 二(二)
- **definition:** two; second; secondarily
- **pinyin:** èr
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Represents the number two as two horizontal lines.
  - **interpretation:** The two parallel lines clearly depict the concept of 'two' visually.
- **examples:**
  - 我有二个苹果。(我有二個蘋果。)
    - Wǒ yǒu èr gè píngguǒ.
    - I have two apples.
  - 二月份很冷。(二月份很冷。)
    - Èr yuè fèn hěn lěng.
    - February is very cold.
### 官二代(官二代) → 二代(二代) → 代(代)
- **definition:** generation; era; substitute; instead
- **pinyin:** dài
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents an era or generation, signifying substitution or replacement.
  - **interpretation:** This character indicates a sequence of generations or eras, reflecting the concept of one thing taking the place of another.
- **examples:**
  - 这代人(這代人)真聪明(真聰明)！
    - zhè dài rén zhēn cōngmíng！
    - This generation is really smart!
  - 我们需要代替(代替)旧的方式(舊的方式)。
    - wǒmen xūyào dàitì jiù de fāngshì。
    - We need to replace the old methods.
  - 他是我的代(代)友。
    - tā shì wǒ de dài yǒu。
    - He is my substitute.
