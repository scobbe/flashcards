## children of officials
### guān’èrdài
---
- **definition:** children of officials
- **pinyin:** guān’èrdài
- **components:**
  - 官
    - guān
    - official
  - 二代
    - èrdài
    - second generation; junior
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 官(官) (guān, "official") + 二(二) (èr, "two; second") + 代(代) (dài, "generation")
  - **interpretation:** 官(官) refers to 'officials,' and 二代(二代), meaning 'second generation,' indicates the children of those officials. This term describes the offspring of government officials who may benefit from their parents' status and connections.
- **examples:**
  - 他是官二代(官二代)，家境优渥(家境優渥)。
    - Tā shì guān’èrdài, jiājìng yōuwò.
    - He is the child of an official, with a wealthy family background.
  - 官二代常常引起争议(官二代常常引起爭議)，公众认为他们获得了不公平的优势(公眾認為他們獲得了不公平的優勢)。
    - Guān’èrdài chángcháng yǐnqǐ zhēngyì, gōngzhòng rènwéi tāmen huòdéle bù gōngpíng de yōushì.
    - Official second-generation individuals often spark controversy, as the public believes they have received unfair advantages.
