## 可不(可不)
---
## 
### exactly; right
---
- **pinyin:** exactly; right
- **components:**
  - 可
    - kě
    - can; may; able; it is acceptable
  - 不
    - bù
    - not; no
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 可(可) (kě, 'can; may') + 不(不) (bù, 'not') — can not
  - **interpretation:** This phrase expresses a sense of agreement or confirmation, implying that something is indeed acceptable or possible. It combines the idea of 'can' with 'not' to form a colloquial expression often used for emphasis.
- **examples:**
  - 这个方案可不可以？(這個方案可不可以？)
    - zhège fāng'àn kě bù kěyǐ？
    - Is this plan acceptable?
  - 你可不可以帮我？(你可不可以幫我？)
    - nǐ kě bù kěyǐ bāng wǒ？
    - Can you help me?

### 可不(可不) → 不(不)
- **definition:** not; no
- **pinyin:** bù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the calyx of a flower, later combined with the character for mouth (口).
  - **interpretation:** The imagery of the calyx implies a barrier or obstruction, which resonates with the concept of negation or refusal.
- **examples:**
  - 我不喜欢这个。(我不喜歡這個。)
    - Wǒ bù xǐhuān zhège.
    - I do not like this.
  - 这不是真的。(這不是真的。)
    - Zhè bù shì zhēn de.
    - This is not true.
