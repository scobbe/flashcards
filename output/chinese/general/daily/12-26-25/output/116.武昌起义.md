## Wuchang Uprising
### Wǔchāng Qǐyì
---
- **definition:** Wuchang Uprising
- **pinyin:** Wǔchāng Qǐyì
- **components:**
  - 武昌
    - Wǔchāng
    - Wuchang (city)
  - 起义(起義)
    - qǐyì
    - uprising; rise; righteous
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 武昌(武昌) (Wǔchāng, "Wuchang") refers to a city in China, known for its historical significance. 起义(起義) (qǐyì, "uprising") where 起(起) (qǐ, "rise") and 义(義) (yì, "righteousness") together refer to an uprising or rebellion.
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to the uprising that began in the city of 武昌 (Wǔchāng), marking the start of significant political change in China.
  - **simplification:** 起義 is simplified as 起义.
- **examples:**
  - 武昌起义改变了历史(武昌起義改變了歷史)。
    - Wǔchāng Qǐyì gǎibiàn le lìshǐ.
    - The Wuchang Uprising changed history.
  - 辛亥革命开始后(辛亥革命開始後)，武昌起义成为转折点(武昌起義成為轉折點)。
    - Xīnhài gémìng kāishǐ hòu, Wǔchāng Qǐyì chéngwéi zhuǎnzhédiǎn.
    - After the Xinhai Revolution began, the Wuchang Uprising became a turning point.
