## Wuchang Uprising
### Wǔchāng Qǐyì
---
- **definition:** Wuchang Uprising
- **pinyin:** Wǔchāng Qǐyì
- **components:**
  - 武昌
    - Wǔchāng
    - Wuchang (place name); former city in Hubei; district of Wuhan
  - 起义(起義)
    - Qǐyì
    - uprising; insurrection; revolt; rebellion
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** proper noun; historical event
  - **description:** 武昌 + 起义 = uprising that occurred in Wuchang.
  - **interpretation:** Names the 1911 uprising that began in Wuchang and ignited the Xinhai Revolution. It became the catalyst for the fall of the Qing dynasty.
  - **simplification:** 起義 → 起义: 義 was simplified to 义 during script reform to reduce strokes; 武昌 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 武昌起义爆发于1911年10月10日(武昌起義爆發於1911年10月10日)。
    - Wǔchāng Qǐyì bàofā yú yījiǔyīyī nián shí yuè shí rì.
    - The Wuchang Uprising broke out on October 10, 1911.
  - 它引发了辛亥革命并推翻清朝(它引發了辛亥革命並推翻清朝)。
    - Tā yǐnfā le Xīnhài Gémìng bìng tuīfān Qīngcháo.
    - It triggered the Xinhai Revolution and overthrew the Qing dynasty.
  - 名称来自湖北武汉的武昌区(名稱來自湖北武漢的武昌區)。
    - Míngchēng láizì Húběi Wǔhàn de Wǔchāng Qū.
    - The name comes from Wuchang District in Wuhan, Hubei.
### 武昌起义(武昌起義) → 武昌
- **definition:** Wuchang (place name); former city in Hubei; district of Wuhan
- **pinyin:** Wǔchāng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 武 (martial) + 昌 (prosperous; flourishing) = place name implying “martial prosperity.”
  - **interpretation:** Historic toponym chosen to signal a flourishing military power during the Three Kingdoms era (renamed by Sun Quan). Now denotes the Wuchang district of Wuhan in Hubei.
- **examples:**
  - 武昌起义发生在1911年(武昌起義發生在1911年)，是辛亥革命的开端(是辛亥革命的開端)。
    - Wǔchāng Qǐyì fāshēng zài yī jiǔ yī yī nián, shì Xīnhài Gémìng de kāiduān.
    - The Wuchang Uprising occurred in 1911 and marked the beginning of the Xinhai Revolution.
  - 我住在武汉的武昌区(我住在武漢的武昌區)，离东湖很近(離東湖很近)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Wǔhàn de Wǔchāng Qū, lí Dōnghú hěn jìn.
    - I live in Wuchang District of Wuhan, very close to East Lake.
  - 历史上(歷史上)，武昌曾是重要的军事和政治中心(武昌曾是重要的軍事和政治中心)。
    - Lìshǐ shàng, Wǔchāng céng shì zhòngyào de jūnshì hé zhèngzhì zhōngxīn.
    - Historically, Wuchang was an important military and political center.
### 武昌起义(武昌起義) → 起义(起義)
- **definition:** uprising; insurrection; revolt; rebellion
- **pinyin:** Qǐyì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 起 ‘rise’ + 义/義 ‘righteous cause’ = to rise for a righteous cause → an uprising.
  - **interpretation:** Originally emphasized a morally justified revolt; in modern usage it broadly refers to any uprising or insurrection, especially organized resistance against authority.
  - **simplification:** 義 → 义: stroke reduction by adopting a cursive/abbreviated form used in handwriting; high-frequency moral head ‘righteousness/justice’ was retained in a simpler graph. 起 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 农民在压迫下(農民在壓迫下)，发动起义(發動起義)。
    - Nóngmín zài yāpò xià, fādòng qǐyì.
    - The peasants launched an uprising under oppression.
  - 这次起义(這次起義)失败了(失敗了)。
    - Zhè cì qǐyì shībàile.
    - This uprising failed.
  - 他们起义(他們起義)是为了争取自由(是為了爭取自由)。
    - Tāmen qǐyì shì wèile zhēngqǔ zìyóu.
    - They rose up to fight for freedom.
