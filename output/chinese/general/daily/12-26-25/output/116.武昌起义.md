### Wuchang Uprising
---
- **pinyin:** Wuchang Uprising
- **components:**
  - 武
    - wǔ
    - military; martial; force; strength
  - 昌
    - chāng
    - prosperous; flourishing; thriving; bright
  - 起義
    - qǐyì
    - uprising; revolt; insurrection; rebellion
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 武(武) (wǔ, 'military') + 昌(昌) (chāng, 'prosperous') + 起義(起义) (qǐyì, 'uprising') — prosperous military uprising
  - **interpretation:** The 武昌起义 represents a significant historical event where the military (武) sought to establish a prosperous (昌) change through revolt (起义). This combination reflects the martial nature and ambition for prosperity driving the uprising.
- **examples:**
  - 武昌起义是辛亥革命的开端。
    - Wǔchāng qǐyì shì Xīnhài gémìng de kāiduān。
    - The Wuchang Uprising was the beginning of the Xinhai Revolution.
  - 很多人纪念武昌起义的英雄。
    - Hěnduō rén jìniàn Wǔchāng qǐyì de yīngxióng。
    - Many people commemorate the heroes of the Wuchang Uprising.
