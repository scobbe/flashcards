### Qin Shi Huang (first emperor of Qin)
---
- **pinyin:** Qin Shi Huang (first emperor of Qin)
- **components:**
  - 秦
    - qín
    - Qin; a state in ancient China
  - 始
    - shǐ
    - begin; start; commence
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - emperor; royal; sovereign
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 秦(秦) (qín, "Qin", a state in ancient China) + 始皇(始皇) (shǐhuáng, "first emperor") — first emperor of Qin
  - **interpretation:** This term designates the first emperor of the Qin dynasty, who accomplished the unification of China. It represents the beginning of imperial rule in China.
- **examples:**
  - 秦始皇统一了中国(秦始皇統一了中國)。
    - Qín Shǐ Huáng tǒngyī le zhōngguó.
    - Qin Shi Huang unified China.
  - 秦始皇是中国历史上第一个皇帝(秦始皇是中國歷史上第一個皇帝)。
    - Qín Shǐ Huáng shì zhōngguó lìshǐ shàng dì yī gè huángdì.
    - Qin Shi Huang was the first emperor in Chinese history.
