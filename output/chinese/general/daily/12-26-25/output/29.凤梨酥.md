- **definition:** resuscitate
- **pinyin:** sū
- **components:**
  - 魚
    - yú
    - fish
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 魚(魚) (yú, "fish") as phonetic + 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") as semantic ->
    - sound and meaning combined ->
    - final form 穌
  - **interpretation:** The character 穌(sū, "resuscitate") combines 魚 (yú, "fish") to indicate sound with 禾 (hé, "grain"), which contributes to the overall meaning of revitalization, akin to agricultural growth.
