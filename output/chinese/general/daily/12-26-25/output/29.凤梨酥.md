## pineapple cake
### fènglísū
---
- **traditional:** 鳳梨酥
- **definition:** pineapple cake
- **pinyin:** fènglísū
- **components:**
  - 鳳梨
    - fènglí
    - pineapple
  - 酥
    - sū
    - crisp; flaky
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鳳 (fèng, "phoenix") + 梨 (lí, "pear") + 酥 (sū, "crisp")
  - **interpretation:** 鳳梨 (fènglí, "pineapple") refers to the fruit itself, using a metaphorical reference to a phoenix to denote exoticness and nobility. 酥 (sū, "crisp") describes the texture of the pastry, which is flaky and delicate, giving the overall meaning of a sweet, crisp pastry made with pineapple.
  - **simplification:** 鳳 to 凤
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天买(買)了凤(鳳)梨酥。
    - Wǒ zuótiān mǎile fènglísū.
    - I bought a pineapple cake yesterday.
  - 周(週)末时(時)，我跟朋友一起品尝凤(嚐鳳)梨酥。
    - Zhōumò shí, wǒ gēn péngyǒu yīqǐ pǐncháng fènglísū.
    - On the weekend, I tasted pineapple cake with a friend.
  - 这种凤(這種鳳)梨酥，外皮松(鬆)脆。
    - Zhè zhǒng fènglísū, wàipí sōngcuì.
    - This type of pineapple cake has a crispy crust.
