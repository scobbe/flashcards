## temple
### sì
---
- **definition:** temple
- **pinyin:** sì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 之(之) (zhī, "go") + 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") =
    - measure out space ->
    - temple
  - **interpretation:** The character 寺(sì, "temple") combines 之(之) (zhī, "go") as phonetic and 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") as semantic, suggesting measuring or demarcating a sacred space, thus relating to a temple.
  - **simplification:** None, as 寺(sì, "temple") is the same in both traditional and simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 寺是一处承载信仰的场所(寺是一處承載信仰的場所)。
    - sì shì yī chù chéngzài xìnyǎng de chǎngsuǒ。
    - The temple is a place that carries people's faith.
  - 信徒来到寺(信徒來到寺)，寻求心灵慰藉(尋求心靈慰藉)。
    - xìntú lái dào sì, xúnqiú xīnlíng wèijiè。
    - Believers come to the temple, seeking solace for their spirits.
  - 僧侣在寺旁诵经(僧侶在寺旁誦經)，净化心灵(淨化心靈)。
    - sēng lǚ zài sì páng sòng jīng, jìnghuà xīn líng。
    - Monks recite scriptures by the temple, purifying their spirits.
