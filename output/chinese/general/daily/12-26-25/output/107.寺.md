### temple
---
- **pinyin:** temple
- **components:**
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character represents an establishment for religious worship and is formed with the radical 土 (tǔ, 'earth') and the phonetic 寺 (sì, 'temple') attached.
  - **interpretation:** The earth component suggests a physical location where a temple is built, emphasizing its foundation in the land. This makes intuitive sense as temples are structures that connect the spiritual world with the physical environment.
- **examples:**
  - 这个寺非常古老(這個寺非常古老)，有很多历史(有很多歷史)。
    - Zhège sì fēicháng gǔlǎo, yǒu hěnduō lìshǐ.
    - This temple is very old, with a lot of history.
  - 我想参观寺庙(我想參觀寺廟)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng cānguān sìmiào.
    - I want to visit the temple.
### 寺(寺) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel.
  - **interpretation:** This visual representation of soil or clay conveys the material's fundamental nature in the earth, highlighting its role in forming and supporting life.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要好的土来种花(我們需要好的土來種花)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào hǎo de tǔ lái zhòng huā.
    - We need good soil to plant flowers.
  - 土可以用来建房子(土可以用來建房子)。
    - Tǔ kěyǐ yòng lái jiàn fángzi.
    - Soil can be used to build houses.
