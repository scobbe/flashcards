- **definition:** Da’an District, Taipei

    - qū

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big") + 安(安) (ān, "peace") form the name 大安(大安) (Dà’ān, "Da’an") to refer to 'Da’an District,' a significant district in Taipei, Taiwan. It is named with the aspiration for 'great peace,' and these character meanings are common in place names throughout Chinese culture. 區(区) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** The combination 大安(大安) (Dà’ān, "Da’an") implies a place of 'great peace' or tranquility. The name reflects aspirations or historical ideals for harmony within the region. Located in Taipei, 大安區(大安区) (Dà’ān Qū, "Da’an District") is known for its dynamic neighborhoods and vibrant culture.

- **examples:**

    - Da’an District integrates tradition with modernity, with convenient transportation and complete facilities.
  - 大安区是台北的文化中心(大安區是台北的文化中心)，吸引了众多游客(吸引了眾多遊客)。
