## Da’an District, Taipei
### Dà’ān Qū
---
- **definition:** Da’an District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dà’ān Qū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large
  - 安
    - ān
    - peace; safe
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big; large") + 安(安) (ān, "peace; safe") + 区(區) (qū, "district; area")
  - **interpretation:** The name 大安區 (Dà’ān Qū) can be interpreted as a 'large peaceful district', reflecting either an aspirational or historical characteristic of the Taipei district it refers to.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在大安区(我住在大安區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dà’ān Qū.
    - I live in Da’an District, Taipei.
  - 大安区风景优美(大安區風景優美)，吸引许多游客(吸引許多遊客)。
    - Dà’ān Qū fēngjǐng yōuměi, xīyǐn xǔduō yóukè.
    - Da’an District has beautiful scenery, attracting many tourists.
### 大安区(大安區) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; large
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**

    - borrowed for 大(大) (dà, "big")
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(大) (dà, "big") originally depicted a person standing, which later evolved to express the meaning 'big.' The resemblance to a person was used phonetically to convey largeness, not necessarily to portray outstretched arms as sometimes explained.

- **etymology:**

  - **interpretation:** The character combines 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") with 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") to convey the concept of an area where multiple people or goods are contained, illustrating the concept of a district or area.
