## Da’an District, Taipei
### Dà’ān Qū
---
- **traditional:** 大安區
- **definition:** Da’an District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dà’ān Qū
- **components:**
  - 大安
    - Dà’ān
    - Da’an; great peace
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big") + 安 (ān, "peace") form the name 大安 (Dà’ān, "Da’an") to refer to 'Da’an District,' a significant district in Taipei, Taiwan. It is named with the aspiration for 'great peace,' and these character meanings are common in place names throughout Chinese culture. 區(区) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** The combination 大安 (Dà’ān, "Da’an") implies a place of 'great peace' or tranquility. The name reflects aspirations or historical ideals for harmony within the region. Located in Taipei, 大安區(区) (Dà’ān Qū, "Da’an District") is known for its dynamic neighborhoods and vibrant culture.
  - **simplification:** 區
- **examples:**
  - 我住在大安区(區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dà’ān Qū.
    - I live in Da’an District.
  - 大安区(區)融合传统与现(傳統與現)代，交通便利且设(設)施完善。
    - Dà’ān Qū rónghé chuántǒng yǔ xiàndài, jiāotōng biànlì qiě shèshī wánshàn.
    - Da’an District integrates tradition with modernity, with convenient transportation and complete facilities.
  - 大安区(區)是台北的文化中心，吸引了众(眾)多游(遊)客。
    - Dà’ān Qū shì Táiběi de wénhuà zhōngxīn, xīyǐn le zhòngduō yóukè.
    - Da’an District is the cultural center of Taipei, attracting many tourists.

### 大( )安( )区(區) → 区(區)

- **definition:** district; area; region
- **pinyin:** qū

    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
  - 乂

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (enclosed space) + 品 (many people) =

  - **simplification:** 區 was simplified to 区(區) by reducing 品 to 乂 (yì, 'to manage'), keeping the enclosure meaning while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在繁华(華)的区(區)。

    - Ancient literati often sought inspiration in this region.
  - 他参观(參觀)了多个区(個區)，体验(體驗)了不同的文化。

    - He visited several districts and experienced diverse cultures.
### 大( )安( )区(區) → 区(區) → 匸

- **pinyin:** xì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.

### 大( )安( )区(區) → 区(區) → 乂
- **definition:** to manage
- **pinyin:** yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a tool (similar to a sickle or a pair of scissors) for mowing or weeding ->
    - used for governing ->
    - final form 乂
  - **interpretation:** The character 乂 originally depicted a tool used for cutting, and by extension, it was associated with governing due to the notion of cutting or managing weeds as a metaphor for ruling effectively. This sense of order is crucial in governance.
- **examples:**
