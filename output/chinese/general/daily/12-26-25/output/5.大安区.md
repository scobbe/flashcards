### Da’an District, Taipei
---
- **pinyin:** Da’an District, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 大安
    - dà'ān
    - big peace
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big") + 安(安) (ān, "peace") — big peace area
  - **interpretation:** 大安区 refers to a district in Taipei, signifying a place known for its tranquility and spaciousness. The name reflects the aspiration for a peaceful and expansive area.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在大安区(我住在大安區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dà'ān qū.
    - I live in Da'an District.
  - 大安区有很多公园(大安區有很多公園)。
    - Dà'ān qū yǒu hěn duō gōngyuán.
    - Da'an District has many parks.
