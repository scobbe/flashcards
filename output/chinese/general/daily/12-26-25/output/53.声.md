### sound; voice
---
- **pinyin:** sound; voice
- **components:**
  - 殸
    - xié
    - a variant phonetic component
  - 耳
    - ěr
    - ear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic 殸 (殸) (xié, a variant with a similar pronunciation) + semantic 耳 (耳) (ěr, 'ear') — evokes sound perceived by the ear.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts how sound is associated with the ear, indicating the auditory experience. The combination of phonetic and semantic parts provides a clear link to its meaning of 'sound' or 'voice'.
- **examples:**
  - 他的声音很好听(他的聲音很好聽)。
    - tā de shēngyīn hěn hǎotīng.
    - His voice is very pleasant to hear.
  - 我喜欢听音乐(我喜歡聽音樂)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuè.
    - I like listening to music.
### 声(聲) → 殸(殸)
- **definition:** a variant phonetic component
- **pinyin:** xié
- **components:**
  - 声(聲)
    - shēng
    - stone chime
  - 殳
    - shū
    - hand holding a hammer or a mallet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 声(聲) (shēng, 'stone chime') + 殳 (shū, 'hand holding a hammer or a mallet') — a stone chime being struck by a mallet.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the action of striking a stone chime with a mallet, depicting both the instrument and its use. It intuitively conveys the sound-producing action associated with musical instruments made from stone.
- **examples:**
  - 我们应该去听磬的声音(我們應該去聽磬的聲音)。
    - Wǒmen yīnggāi qù tīng qìng de shēngyīn.
    - We should go listen to the sound of the stone chime.
  - 这块石磬很美(這塊石磬很美)。
    - Zhè kuài shíqìng hěn měi.
    - This stone chime is beautiful.
### 声(聲) → 殸(殸) → 殳(殳)
- **definition:** hand holding a hammer or a mallet
- **pinyin:** shū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand holding a hammer or mallet.
  - **interpretation:** The character illustrates a hand gripping a hammer, suggesting the action of striking or hitting. This evokes the imagery of using a tool for various tasks.
- **examples:**
  - 古代使用殳进行战斗(古代使用殳進行戰鬥)。
    - Gǔdài shǐyòng shū jìnxíng zhàndòu.
    - In ancient times, the 殳 was used in battles.
  - 殳是一种兵器(殳是一種兵器)。
    - Shū shì yī zhǒng bīngqì.
    - 殳 is a type of weapon.
  - 书法中有写殳(書法中有寫殳)。
    - Shūfǎ zhōng yǒu xiě shū.
    - In calligraphy, there is the character 殳.
### 声(聲) → 耳(耳)
- **definition:** ear
- **pinyin:** ěr
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an ear, illustrating its shape and structure.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the shape of an ear, making it intuitive for conveying the meaning 'ear'.
- **examples:**
  - 他有一只大耳朵(他有一隻大耳朵)。
    - Tā yǒu yī zhī dà ěrduǒ.
    - He has a big ear.
  - 她的耳环很漂亮(她的耳環很漂亮)。
    - Tā de ěrhuán hěn piàoliang.
    - Her earrings are beautiful.
