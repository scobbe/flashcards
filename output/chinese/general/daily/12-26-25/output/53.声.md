## sound; voice
### shēng
---
- **definition:** sound; voice
- **pinyin:** shēng
- **components:**
  - 殸
    - qian
    - bronze bell
  - 耳
    - ěr
    - ear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 殸(殸) (qian, "bronze bell") + semantic: 耳(耳) (ěr, "ear") =
    - sound of bell heard with ears ->
    - sound
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 殸(殸) (qian, "bronze bell") representing the type of sound made and 耳(耳) (ěr, "ear") emphasizing the act of hearing. This reflects how sound is perceived.
  - **simplification:** 声(聲) simplifies by removing 耳(耳) (ěr, "ear"), focusing on the basic component 殸(殸) (qian, "bronze bell") to represent sound.
- **examples:**
  - 小鸟歌声悦耳(小鳥歌聲悅耳)。
    - xiǎo niǎo gē shēng yuè ěr。
    - The little bird's song is melodious.
  - 他的声音像山谷回响(他的聲音像山谷回響)，让人心醉(讓人心醉)。
    - tā de shēng yīn xiàng shān gǔ huí xiǎng，ràng rén xīn zuì。
    - His voice echoes like a valley, captivating everyone.
  - 教室里传来阵阵声(教室裡傳來陣陣聲)。
    - jiào shì lǐ chuán lái zhèn zhèn shēng。
    - Bursts of sound come from the classroom.
