### herbal tea
---
- **pinyin:** herbal tea
- **components:**
  - 凉(涼)
    - liáng
    - cool; chilly; refreshing; alleviate
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea; beverage; drink; infusion
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 凉(涼) (liáng, 'cool') + 茶(茶) (chá, 'tea') — cool tea
  - **interpretation:** 凉茶 is a traditional drink known for its cooling properties, often consumed to alleviate heat and promote health. The combination of 'cool' and 'tea' directly relates to its refreshing quality.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝凉茶(我喜歡喝涼茶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē liángchá.
    - I like to drink herbal tea.
  - 夏天喝凉茶很解暑(夏天喝涼茶很解暑)。
    - Xiàtiān hē liángchá hěn jiě shǔ.
    - Drinking herbal tea in summer is very refreshing.
### 凉茶(涼茶) → 凉(涼)
- **definition:** cool; chilly; refreshing; alleviate
- **pinyin:** liáng
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 水 (water) and the phonetic component 凉 (liáng). The water component suggests something associated with coolness or refreshment.
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys a sense of coolness associated with water, creating an intuitive link to refreshing qualities.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing the radical '氵' (water) with '凉' to maintain phonetic sound while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 天气很凉快(天氣很涼快)，我想去海边(我想去海邊)。
    - Tiānqì hěn liángkuai, wǒ xiǎng qù hǎibiān.
    - The weather is very cool, and I want to go to the beach.
  - 喝水是为了保持凉爽(喝水是為了保持涼爽)。
    - Hē shuǐ shì wèile bǎochí liángshuǎng.
    - Drinking water is to stay cool.
