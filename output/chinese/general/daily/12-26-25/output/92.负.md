### carry on the back
---
- **pinyin:** carry on the back
- **components:**
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
  - 符
    - fú
    - sign; rule
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character consists of the semantic component 车 (chē, 'vehicle') indicating transport, and the phonetic component 符 (fú), which gives a clue to pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively conveys the idea of carrying something on your back while using a vehicle, emphasizing the act of transport.
  - **simplification:** This character has the same structure in both forms: it was simplified by omitting the traditional variant stroke methods, making it easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 他负担很重(他負擔很重)，需要休息(需要休息)。
    - Tā fùdān hěn zhòng, xūyào xiūxí.
    - He has a heavy burden and needs to rest.
  - 负责任(負責任)是成人的义务(是成人的義務)。
    - Fù zérèn shì chéngrén de yìwù.
    - Taking responsibility is an adult's duty.
