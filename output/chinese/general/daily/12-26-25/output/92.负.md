## carry on the back
### fù
---
- **definition:** carry on the back
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - ⺈
    - none
    - person
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - cowry
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ⺈(⺈) ('person') + 貝(貝) (bèi, 'cowry') =
    - person carrying a cowry ->
    - 'carry on the back'
  - **interpretation:** The character 負(負) (fù, 'carry on the back') combines a representation of a person ⺈(⺈) and a cowry 貝(貝), suggesting the act of carrying a valuable object (cowry shells were used as money) on one's back.
- **examples:**
  - 小明负上书包去上学(小明負上書包去上學)。
    - Xiǎo Míng fù shàng shūbāo qù shàngxué.
    - Xiaoming carries his schoolbag on his back to school.
  - 登山者负重攀岩(登山者負重攀巖)，不惧陡峭山路(不懼陡峭山路)。
    - Dēngshān zhě fù zhòng pānyán, bù jù dǒuqiào shānlù.
    - The mountaineer carries heavy loads to climb, and does not fear the steep mountain paths.
  - 旅行者负着沉重行囊(旅行者負著沉重行囊)，穿越崎岖道路(穿越崎嶇道路)。
    - Lǚxíng zhě fù zhe chénzhòng xíngnáng, chuānyuè qíqū dàolù.
    - The traveler carries a heavy backpack and crosses rugged roads.
### 负(負) → ⺈(⺈)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** none
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the head or a person
  - **interpretation:** The character ⺈ is a pictogram symbolizing a simplified image of a person, often used as a component in other characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我看到一个⺈(我看到一個⺈)，他在街上走路(他在街上走路)。
    - Wǒ kàn dào yīgè ⺈, tā zài jiē shàng zǒulù.
    - I saw a person, and he was walking on the street.
  - 小⺈喜欢笑(小⺈喜歡笑)，温暖了大家的心(溫暖了大家的心)。
    - Xiǎo ⺈ xǐhuān xiào, wēnnuǎn le dàjiā de xīn.
    - The little person loves to laugh, warming everyone's hearts.
  - 有⺈追逐梦想(有⺈追逐夢想)。
    - Yǒu ⺈ zhuīzhú mèngxiǎng.
    - There is a person chasing dreams.
### 负(負) → 貝(貝)
- **definition:** cowry
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cowrie shell ->
    - used as currency in ancient China
  - **interpretation:** 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") depicts the distinctive shape of a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China due to its unique appearance and rarity.
- **examples:**
  - 他收藏一枚贝(他收藏一枚貝)，用作古代贸易的信物(用作古代貿易的信物)。
    - Tā shōucáng yī méi bèi, yòng zuò gǔdài màoyì de xìnwù.
    - He collects a cowry shell, using it as a token in ancient trade.
  - 清朝时贝(清朝時貝)，常被镶嵌于玉器(常被鑲嵌於玉器)。
    - Qīngcháo shí bèi, cháng bèi xiāngqiàn yú yùqì.
    - During the Qing dynasty, cowries were often inlaid in jade pieces.
