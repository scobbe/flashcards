## Doctrine of the Mean; middle way
### zhōngyōng zhī dào
---
- **definition:** Doctrine of the Mean; middle way
- **pinyin:** zhōngyōng zhī dào
- **components:**
  - 中庸
    - zhōngyōng
    - moderation; the mean; due measure; mediocrity
  - 之道
    - zhī dào
    - the way of; doctrine of; method of; principle of
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中庸 + 之道 = the way/principle of moderation (the Mean).
  - **interpretation:** Confucian principle of avoiding extremes and acting with balanced appropriateness according to context. Emphasizes harmony and proportion rather than bland compromise.
- **examples:**
  - 在处理冲突时，要遵循中庸之道(在處理衝突時，要遵循中庸之道)。
    - Zài chǔlǐ chōngtū shí, yào zūnxún zhōngyōng zhī dào.
    - When handling conflicts, one should follow the Doctrine of the Mean.
  - 他主张在经济政策上走中庸之道(他主張在經濟政策上走中庸之道)。
    - Tā zhǔzhāng zài jīngjì zhèngcè shàng zǒu zhōngyōng zhī dào.
    - He advocates taking a middle way in economic policy.
  - 中庸之道并非妥协，而是把握分寸与时机(中庸之道並非妥協，而是把握分寸與時機)。
    - Zhōngyōng zhī dào bìngfēi tuǒxié, ér shì bǎwò fēncùn yǔ shíjī.
    - The Doctrine of the Mean is not compromise but grasping proportion and timing.
### 中庸之道 → 中庸
- **definition:** moderation; the mean; due measure; mediocrity
- **pinyin:** zhōngyōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中 (center/mean) + 庸 (constant/ordinary) = the constant mean; moderation; title of a Confucian classic.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of centrality with constancy to express balance maintained as a steady norm. In Confucianism it names the Doctrine of the Mean and the ideal of moderation and impartiality.
- **examples:**
  - 在处理争端时(在處理爭端時)，他坚持中庸之道(他堅持中庸之道)。
    - Zài chǔlǐ zhēngduān shí, tā jiānchí zhōngyōng zhī dào.
    - When handling disputes, he adheres to the Doctrine of the Mean.
  - 我最近在研读《中庸》(我最近在研讀《中庸》)。
    - Wǒ zuìjìn zài yándú «Zhōngyōng».
    - I have been studying the Doctrine of the Mean recently.
  - 领导风格要避免极端(領導風格要避免極端)，追求中庸而不平庸(追求中庸而不平庸)。
    - Lǐngdǎo fēnggé yào bìmiǎn jíduān, zhuīqiú zhōngyōng ér bù píngyōng.
    - A leadership style should avoid extremes, seeking moderation without mediocrity.
### 中庸之道 → 之道
- **definition:** the way of; doctrine of; method of; principle of
- **pinyin:** zhī dào
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** classical construction
  - **description:** 之 (genitive ‘of’) + 道 (way; doctrine) = ‘the way/principles of …’.
  - **interpretation:** Classical genitive pattern meaning “the way of X,” often used to state principles, methods, or doctrines in an elevated style. A topic or domain precedes 之道 (e.g., 学习之道) to express its guiding method or principles.
- **examples:**
  - 学习之道在于坚持(學習之道在於堅持)。
    - xuéxí zhī dào zàiyú jiānchí.
    - The way of learning lies in perseverance.
  - 处世之道贵在谦和(處世之道貴在謙和)。
    - chǔshì zhī dào guì zài qiānhé.
    - The way to conduct oneself in society values humility and harmony.
  - 治国之道应以民为本(治國之道應以民為本)。
    - zhìguó zhī dào yīng yǐ mín wéi běn.
    - The principles of governing should put the people first.
