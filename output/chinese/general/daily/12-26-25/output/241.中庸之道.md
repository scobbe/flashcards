### Doctrine of the Mean; middle way
---
- **pinyin:** Doctrine of the Mean; middle way
- **components:**
  - 中庸
    - zhōngyōng
    - the Doctrine of the Mean; moderation; harmony; balance
  - 之
    - zhī
    - of; 's; that
  - 道
    - dào
    - way; path; doctrine; principle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中庸(中庸) (zhōngyōng, 'the Doctrine of the Mean') + 之(之) (zhī, 'of') + 道(道) (dào, 'way') — the way of the Doctrine of the Mean
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to a central philosophical concept in Confucianism that emphasizes moderation and balance in all aspects of life. It suggests a pathway that avoids extremes, seeking harmony and equilibrium.
- **examples:**
  - 中庸之道非常重要。
    - zhōngyōng zhī dào fēicháng zhòngyào.
    - The Doctrine of the Mean is very important.
  - 我们应该遵循中庸之道。
    - wǒmen yīnggāi zūnxún zhōngyōng zhī dào.
    - We should follow the middle way.
  - 中庸之道教导我们平衡。
    - zhōngyōng zhī dào jiàodǎo wǒmen pínghéng.
    - The Doctrine of the Mean teaches us balance.
