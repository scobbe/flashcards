### fine jade; surname Qi
---
- **pinyin:** fine jade; surname Qi
- **components:**
  - 其
    - qí
    - to be
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 琪 is composed of the phonetic component 其 (qí, 'to be') and the semantic component 玉 (yù, 'jade'). Together, they suggest something like 'jade-like' or 'fine jade'.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the beauty and preciousness of jade through its components, implying something exquisite or valuable.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个琪的项链(我有一個琪的項鍊)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī gè qí de xiàngliàn.
    - I have a necklace made of fine jade.
  - 她的名字叫琪(她的名字叫琪)。
    - Tā de míngzì jiào Qí.
    - Her name is Qi.

### 琪(琪) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem.
  - **interpretation:** The character symbolizes jade, reflecting its importance and usage in ritualistic and decorative contexts, much like the cong itself.
- **examples:**
  - 这件玉器很美丽(這件玉器很美麗)。
    - Zhè jiàn yùqì hěn měilì.
    - This piece of jade is beautiful.
  - 我想买玉(我想買玉)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yù.
    - I want to buy jade.
