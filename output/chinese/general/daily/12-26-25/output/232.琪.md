## fine jade; surname Qi
### Qí
---
- **definition:** fine jade; surname Qi
- **pinyin:** Qí
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 其
    - qí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") + 其(其) (qí) =
    - representation of fine jade ->
    - fine jade
  - **interpretation:** The character 琪 combines 玉(玉) (yù, "jade"), which suggests the substance or value, with 其(其) (qí) acting as a phonetic component to guide pronunciation. This makes sense as it conveys the idea of a valuable and beautiful type of jade.
- **examples:**
  - 她家传的琪非常珍贵(她家傳的琪非常珍貴)。
    - Tā jiā chuán de Qí fēicháng zhēnguì.
    - The fine jade passed down in her family is very precious.
  - 他遇见了一位姓琪的老师(他遇見了一位姓琪的老師)，那个人才华横溢(那個人才能橫溢)。
    - Tā yùjiàn le yī wèi xìng Qí de lǎoshī, nàgè rén cáihuá héngyì.
    - He met a teacher with the surname Qi, who was extremely talented.
### 琪(琪) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Picture of a cong (ritual jade totem) ->
    - three pieces of jade strung together ->
    - current form
  - **interpretation:** The character 玉(yù, "jade") visually resembles a picture of a ritual jade object, emphasizing its cultural and material significance.
- **examples:**
  - 这块玉非常光滑(這塊玉非常光滑)。
    - Zhè kuài yù fēicháng guānghuá.

  - 博物馆展出了精美玉器(博物館展出了精美玉器)，吸引了众多观众(吸引了眾多觀眾)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū le jīngměi yùqì, xīyǐn le zhòngduō guānzhòng.
    - The museum exhibited exquisite jade artifacts, attracting numerous visitors.
### 琪(琪) → 其(其)
- **pinyin:** qí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally 𠀠 as a basket ->
    - 丌 added as a stand/phono-semantic component ->
    - 𠔝 ->
    - borrowed as pronoun ->
    - 其
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting a basket, the character 其 evolved by adding 丌, which might have functioned as a phonetic cue in ancient Chinese, resulting in a form that eventually became a pronoun. To reclaim the original meaning of 'basket', 箕 was created with the 竹 radical.
- **examples:**
  - 这字读作其(這字讀作其)。
    - Zhè zì dú zuò qí.
    - This character is pronounced as qí.
  - 作者选择字其(作者選擇字其)用于诗句末尾(用於詩句末尾)，显示其风韵(顯示其風韻)。
    - Zuòzhě xuǎnzé zì qí yòng yú shījù mòwěi, xiǎnshì qí fēngyùn.
    - The author chooses the character '其' for the end of verses, demonstrating its phonetic charm.
  - 这字既简单又具有独特的音韵(這字既簡單又具有獨特的音韻)，其发音清脆悦耳(其發音清脆悅耳)。
    - Zhè zì jì jiǎndān yòu jùyǒu dútè de yīnyùn, qí fāyīn qīngcuì yuè'ěr.
    - This character is not only simple but also has a unique musicality, and its pronunciation is crisp and pleasant.
