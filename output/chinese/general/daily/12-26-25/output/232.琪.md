## fine jade; surname Qi
### Qí
---
- **definition:** fine jade; surname Qi
- **pinyin:** Qí
- **components:**
  - 王
    - wáng
    - king
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 王(王) (wáng, "king"; indicative of jade or precious stones) + phonetic: 其(其) (qí) =
    - phonetic element reinforces pronunciation ->
    - fine jade
  - **interpretation:** The character 琪(琪) combines the semantic component 王(王) indicating valuable objects like jade, with the phonetic component 其(其) to suggest the sound 'qí'. This blend conveys the idea of 'fine jade', illustrating both preciousness and correct pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 这块精雕细琢的琪石(這塊精雕細琢的琪石)蕴含着大自然的灵气(蘊含著大自然的靈氣)。
    - Zhè kuài jīngdiāo xìzhuó de qí shí, yùnhánzhe dàzìrán de língqì.
    - This finely carved piece of jade contains the natural spirit.
  - 琪先生在国际会议上发表了重要演讲(琪先生在國際會議上發表了重要演講)。
    - Qí xiānshēng zài guójì huìyì shàng fābiǎo le zhòngyào yǎnjiǎng.
    - Mr. Qi delivered an important speech at an international conference.
### 琪(琪) → 王(王)
- **definition:** king
- **pinyin:** wáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a ritual axe representing the king's authority
  - **interpretation:** This character represents the form of a ceremonial axe, which was the symbol of a king's power in ancient China. The structure emphasizes the sacred and authoritative role of the king, separate from the nuanced folk interpretations linking Heaven, Man, and Earth.
- **examples:**
  - 他被称为王(他被稱為王)，领导人民(領導人民)。
    - Tā bèi chēng wéi wáng, lǐngdǎo rénmín.
    - He is called the king, leading his people.
  - 古代的君主常被尊称为王(古代的君主常被尊稱為王)。
    - Gǔdài de jūnzhǔ cháng bèi zūnchēng wéi wáng.
    - Ancient rulers were often respectfully called kings.
  - 在祭祀仪式上(在祭祀儀式上)，人们向王致敬(人們向王致敬)。
    - Zài jìsì yíshì shàng, rénmen xiàng wáng zhìjìng.
    - During the ceremonial sacrifices, people salute the king.
### 琪(琪) → 其(其)
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket pictogram) ->
    - 丌 (stand) added under basket as phonetic and to enhance meaning ->
    - borrowed for pronoun 'his' ->
    - 箕(箕) (jī, 'basket') derived with additional 竹(竹) (zhú, 'bamboo')
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a basket, symbolized by 𠀠, emphasizing its use. The addition of 丌 beneath the basket strengthened the meaning of a structural object or container, eventually leading to its phonetic borrowing to represent the pronoun 'his'. To reclaim the original meaning of a 'basket', 箕(箕) (jī, 'basket') was formed with an added bamboo radical.
- **examples:**
  - 将军因其英勇闻名(將軍因其英勇聞名)。
    - Jiāngjūn yīn qí yīngyǒng wénmíng。
    - The general is famous for his valor.
  - 同袍尊其忠诚(同袍尊其忠誠)，士卒佩服其英明(士卒佩服其英明)。
    - Tóngpáo zūn qí zhōngchéng, shìzú pèifú qí yīngmíng。
    - His comrades revere his loyalty, and the soldiers admire his wisdom.
### 琪(琪) → 其(其) → 箕(箕)
- **definition:** basket
- **pinyin:** jī
- **components:**
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 竹(竹) (zhú, "bamboo") + 其(其) (qí, "his") providing sound ->
    - specialized character for "basket"
  - **interpretation:** The character 箕(箕) (jī, "basket") combines 竹(竹) (zhú, "bamboo"), reflecting the material commonly used to make baskets, with 其(其) (qí, "his"), providing the sound component to signify its specialized meaning. This association illustrates how bamboo was integral to basket construction.
- **examples:**

    - After the farmhouse finished harvesting, they used a basket to scoop up the rice.
  - 节日市场上(節日市場上)，摊贩用彩色箕展示新鲜蔬果(攤販用彩色箕展示新鮮蔬果)。
    - Jiérì shìchǎng shàng, tānfàn yòng cǎisè jī zhǎnshì xīnxiān shūguǒ。
    - At the festive market, vendors used colorful baskets to display fresh fruits and vegetables.
  - 他买了一个木制箕(他買了一個木製箕)，用来扫院子(用來掃院子)。
    - Tā mǎile yīgè mùzhì jī, yòng lái sǎo yuànzi。
    - He bought a wooden basket used to sweep the yard.
### 琪(琪) → 其(其) → 箕(箕) → 竹(竹)
- **definition:** bamboo
- **pinyin:** zhú
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts bamboo stalks with leaves
  - **interpretation:** The character 竹(竹) (zhú, "bamboo") visually represents two stalks of bamboo with leaves at the top, illustrating the appearance of bamboo plants traditionally found in nature.
- **examples:**
