### bènshǒu bènjiǎo
---
- **traditional:** 笨手笨腳
- **definition:** clumsy; awkward
- **pinyin:** bènshǒu bènjiǎo
- **components:**

  - 笨
    - bèn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 笨 (bèn, "stupid; clumsy") refers to a lack of dexterity or intelligence. 手 (shǒu, "hand") and 腳(脚) (jiǎo, "foot") represent the physical extremities that are associated with actions and movements. The repetition of 笨 (bèn, "stupid; clumsy") with 手 (shǒu, "hand") and 腳(脚) (jiǎo, "foot") emphasizes an overall clumsiness or awkwardness in physical movements.
  - **interpretation:** By stating that both the hands and feet are clumsy, 笨手笨腳 (bènshǒu bènjiǎo) conveys a strong sense of overall awkwardness in physical coordination.
- **examples:**
