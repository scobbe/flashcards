## Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
### Shíkùmén
---
- **traditional:** 石庫門
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 库(庫)
    - kù
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door; gate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 石 (shí, "stone") + 库(庫) (kù, "warehouse; storehouse") + 门(門) (mén, "door; gate")
  - **interpretation:** The term 石( )库(庫)门(門) (shíkùmén) describes a traditional Shanghainese architectural style where stone is used prominently in the construction of door frames and arches. This style combines Western and Chinese architectural elements, often featuring stone portals with a courtyard behind, reminiscent of a 'stone warehouse gate'.

### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 石
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone beneath a cliff, where the upper part originally looked like a cliff (厂) but was distorted into 丆 over time.
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') visually represents a stone in a natural setting, highlighting the element of a cliff or mountain. Its visual design aims to reflect the appearance of rocks lodged or embedded in a mountainous or cliff-like setting.
- **examples:**

### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫)
- **traditional:** 庫
- **definition:** warehouse; storehouse
- **pinyin:** kù
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - shelter
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter") + 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") =
    - shelter for vehicles ->
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - **interpretation:** The character 库(庫) (kù) combines 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter"), depicting a spacious building, with 車(车) (chē, "vehicle"), indicating its use as a place to house vehicles or goods. This combination suggests a structure designed for storage purposes.
  - **simplification:** 庫→库(庫): 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") was replaced with a simpler form of the character, adopting the same phonetic properties.
- **examples:**

### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣) → 宀

### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 门(門)
