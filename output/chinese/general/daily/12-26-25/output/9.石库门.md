## Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
### Shíkùmén
---
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 庫
    - kù
    - warehouse
  - 門
    - mén
    - door; gate; entrance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 石(石) (shí, "stone") + 库(庫) (kù, "warehouse") + 门(門) (mén, "door") =
    - Stone warehouse gate, referring to a specific architectural style with stone-gated architecture commonly found in Shanghai.
  - **interpretation:** Shikumen is a unique architectural style that combines Western and Chinese elements, characterized by stone walls and a courtyard-like layout, symbolized by the stone warehouse gates.
- **examples:**
  - 上海的石库门建筑独具风格(上海的石庫門建築獨具風格)。
    - Shànghǎi de Shíkùmén jiànzhù dújù fēnggé.
    - Shanghai's Shikumen architecture has a unique style.
  - 石库门体现了老上海的历史文化(石庫門體現了老上海的歷史文化)，成为城市的地标(成為城市的地標)。
    - Shíkùmén tǐxiàn le lǎo Shànghǎi de lìshǐ wénhuà, chéngwéi chéngshì de dìbiāo.
    - The Shikumen reflects the historical culture of old Shanghai and has become a landmark of the city.
  - 走在石库门小巷中(走在石庫門小巷中)，仿佛穿越回了旧时光(仿佛穿越回了舊時光)。
    - Zǒu zài Shíkùmén xiǎoxiàng zhōng, fǎngfú chuānyuè huí le jiù shíguāng.
    - Walking through the alleys of Shikumen feels as if one is transported back to old times.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 石(石)
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a stone beneath a cliff (厂); cliff distorted into 丆 ->
    - 石
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, "stone") is a pictogram representing a stone located beneath a cliff. The image has evolved over time, with the original depiction of a cliff (厂) being simplified to 丆, helping to maintain its connection to a rocky or stony landscape.
- **examples:**
  - 这块石很坚硬(這塊石很堅硬)。
    - Zhè kuài shí hěn jiānyìng.
    - This stone is very hard.
  - 山脚下有石(山腳下有石)，溪水冲洗石面(溪水衝洗石面)。
    - Shān jiǎoxià yǒu shí, xīshuǐ chōngxǐ shímiàn.
    - There are stones at the foot of the mountain, and the stream washes the stone surfaces.
  - 青石台阶(青石臺階)，见证岁月(見證歲月)。
    - Qīng shí táijiē, jiànzhèng suìyuè.
    - The blue stone steps witness the passage of time.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 庫(庫)
- **definition:** warehouse
- **pinyin:** kù
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - broad
  - 車
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, "broad") + 車(車) (chē, "vehicle") =
    - space for vehicles ->
    - storage, warehouse
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 广(廣) (guǎng, "broad") and 車(車) (chē, "vehicle") suggests a large, open area for storing vehicles or goods, aligning with the meaning of a warehouse.
- **examples:**
  - 工厂的原料存放在庫(工廠的原料存放在庫)。
    - Gōngchǎng de yuánliào cúnfàng zài kù.
    - The factory stores its raw materials in the warehouse.
  - 公司采购的设备放入新庫(公司採購的設備放入新庫)，以确保安全储存(以確保安全儲存)。
    - Gōngsī cǎigòu de shèbèi fàng rù xīn kù, yǐ quèbǎo ānquán chǔcún.
    - The equipment purchased by the company is placed in the new warehouse, to ensure safe storage.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 庫(庫) → 广(廣)
- **definition:** broad
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 黄(黃)
    - huáng
    - yellow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, 'broad') originally depicted a sloped, expansive building. It combines the semantic: 广 (guǎng, 'shelter') + phonetic: 黄 (huáng, 'yellow, huáng', providing the 'guǎng' sound) =
    - broad, expansive indicating a wide space under a roof.
  - **interpretation:** The character 广(廣) (guǎng, 'broad') effectively conveys the notion of a wide and expansive space, highlighting its original architectural context.
  - **simplification:** 广 (guǎng, 'broad') is derived from the cursive shorthand of the traditional character 廣 (guǎng, 'broad'). It omitted the complex phonetic 黄 (huáng, 'yellow'), simplifying to the component 广 (guǎng, 'shelter').
- **examples:**
  - 这个公园很广(這個公園很廣)。
    - Zhège gōngyuán hěn guǎng.
    - This park is very broad.
  - 村庄周围的田野广阔(村莊周圍的田野廣闊)，气候宜人(氣候宜人)。
    - Cūnzhuāng zhōuwéi de tiányě guǎngkuò, qìhòu yírén.
    - The fields around the village are vast, and the climate is pleasant.
  - 他的视野很广(他的視野很廣)。
    - Tā de shìyě hěn guǎng.
    - His perspective is broad.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 庫(庫) → 广(廣) → 黄(黃)
- **definition:** yellow
- **pinyin:** huáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram (象形)
  - **description:**
    - Originally a pictogram 黃 (huáng) ->
    - phonetically borrowed for 'yellow' ->
    - 廿 (口 as corrupted) + 田 as possible components ->
    - depicting ritual or jade, incorrect phono-semantic interpretation
  - **interpretation:** 黃(黄) (huáng, 'yellow') originated as a pictogram, later borrowed for 'yellow.' Confusion arose from misinterpreted components 廿 and 田, originally depicting a ritual aspect or jade-related imagery.
  - **simplification:** None, 黄(黃) was already a variant historically used alongside 黃(黃).
- **examples:**
  - 这只小鸡是黄的(這隻小雞是黃的)。
    - Zhè zhī xiǎo jī shì huáng de.
    - This chick is yellow.
  - 黄叶飘落时(黃葉飄落時)，秋意渐浓(秋意漸濃)。
    - Huáng yè piāo luò shí, qiū yì jiàn nóng.
    - When the yellow leaves fall, the autumn mood deepens.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 庫(庫) → 車(車)
- **definition:** vehicle
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a carriage seen from above: large wheels on both sides, sun shade on top ->
    - strokes become wheels, 田 (tián, "field") marks loading area ->
    - modern vertical depiction of carriage
  - **interpretation:** The character 車(chē, "vehicle") visually represents a carriage as it would be seen from above, highlighting key features like wheels and a covered loading area.
- **examples:**
  - 车是一种方便的交通工具(車是一種方便的交通工具)。
    - Chē shì yī zhǒng fāngbiàn de jiāotōng gōngjù.
    - The vehicle is a convenient means of transportation.
  - 他开车(他開車)，去接朋友(去接朋友)。
    - Tā kāichē, qù jiē péngyǒu.
    - He drives to pick up his friend.
  - 老车虽然年久失修(老車雖然年久失修)，仍然可靠(仍然可靠)。
    - Lǎo chē suīrán niánjiǔ shī xiū, réngrán kěkào.
    - Although the old car is long in disrepair, it remains reliable.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 門(門)
- **definition:** door; gate; entrance
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a double-leaved gate ->
    - symbolizes entrance/exit
  - **interpretation:** The character 門(mén, "gate") vividly represents a gate with its door panels, emphasizing its function as an entrance or exit. This pictorial representation aligns with its meaning, making it intuitive for recognizing its literal interpretation as a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 他打开门(他打開門)，迎接访客(迎接訪客)。
    - Tā dǎkāi mén(門)， yíngjiē fǎngkè(訪客)。
    - He opens the gate to welcome the visitors.
  - 请轻轻推开门(請輕輕推開門)。
    - Qǐng qīngqīng tuī kāi mén(門)。
    - Please gently push open the gate.
  - 邻居敞开后门(鄰居敞開後門)，让狗儿进出(讓狗兒進出)。
    - Línjū chǎngkāi hòu mén(後門)， ràng gǒu'ér jìnchū(進出)。
    - The neighbor opens the back gate to let the dog in and out.
