## Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
### Shíkùmén
---
- **traditional:** 石庫門
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 库(庫)
    - kù
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door; gate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 石 (shí, "stone") + 库(庫) (kù, "warehouse; storehouse") + 门(門) (mén, "door; gate")
  - **interpretation:** The term 石库门(庫門) (shíkùmén) describes a traditional Shanghainese architectural style where stone is used prominently in the construction of door frames and arches. This style combines Western and Chinese architectural elements, often featuring stone portals with a courtyard behind, reminiscent of a 'stone warehouse gate'.
- **examples:**
  - 石库门(庫門)是上海的传统(傳統)建筑(築)。
    - Shíkùmén shì Shànghǎi de chuántǒng jiànzhú.
    - Shikumen is a traditional architectural style of Shanghai.
  - 游(遊)客在石库门(庫門)巷弄中徘徊，感受老上海的韵(韻)味。
    - Yóukè zài shíkùmén xiàngnòng zhōng páihuái, gǎnshòu lǎo Shànghǎi de yùnwèi.
    - Tourists wander in the Shikumen lanes, experiencing the charm of old Shanghai.
  - 老居民讲(講)述石库门(庫門)背后(後)的历(歷)史，给(給)人留下深刻印象。
    - Lǎo jūmín jiǎngshù shíkùmén bèihòu de lìshǐ, gěi rén liúxià shēnkè yìnxiàng.
    - Elderly residents recount the history behind Shikumen, leaving a lasting impression.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 石
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone beneath a cliff, where the upper part originally looked like a cliff (厂) but was distorted into 丆 over time.
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') visually represents a stone in a natural setting, highlighting the element of a cliff or mountain. Its visual design aims to reflect the appearance of rocks lodged or embedded in a mountainous or cliff-like setting.
- **examples:**
  - 他在河边发现(邊發現)一块(塊)石。
    - Tā zài hé biān fāxiàn yī kuài shí.
    - He discovered a piece of stone by the riverside.
  - 孩子们(們)喜欢(歡)堆石，玩得开(開)心。
    - Háizimen xǐhuān duī shí, wán de kāixīn.
    - Children like to stack stones, playing happily.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫)
- **traditional:** 庫
- **definition:** warehouse; storehouse
- **pinyin:** kù
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - shelter
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter") + 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") =
    - shelter for vehicles ->
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - **interpretation:** The character 库(庫) (kù) combines 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter"), depicting a spacious building, with 車(车) (chē, "vehicle"), indicating its use as a place to house vehicles or goods. This combination suggests a structure designed for storage purposes.
  - **simplification:** 庫→库(庫): 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") was replaced with a simpler form of the character, adopting the same phonetic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 货(貨)物被安全存储(儲)在库(庫)中。
    - Huòwù bèi ānquán cúnchǔ zài kù zhōng.
    - The goods are safely stored in the warehouse.
  - 公司收到新材料，立即将(將)其分类(類)存入库(庫)。
    - Gōngsī shōudào xīn cáiliào, lìjí jiāng qí fēnlèi cúnrù kù.
    - The company received new materials and immediately sorted them into the warehouse.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣)
- **traditional:** 廣
- **definition:** shelter
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with a roof, similar to 宀 (mián, "roof") ->
    - a building with three walls ->
    - shelter
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter") visually represents a three-walled structure under a roof, conveying the concept of shelter or covering. It is closely related to the character 宀 (mián, "roof"), which includes four walls, indicating the structural nuance captured by ancient scribes.
  - **simplification:** 广(廣) was simplified by using the pictographic element alone, omitting the phonetic component 黃.
- **examples:**
  - 暴风骤(風驟)雨时(時)，山洞广(廣)可供人躲避。
    - Bào fēng zhòu yǔ shí, shān dòng guǎng kě gòng rén duǒ bì.
    - When the storm rages, the cave provides shelter.
  - 旅途中疲惫时(憊時)，老树(樹)下广给(廣給)予行人庇护(護)。
    - Lǚ tú zhōng pí bèi shí, lǎo shù xià guǎng jǐ yǔ xíng rén bì hù.
    - During a tiring journey, the shelter under the old tree gives travelers protection.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣) → 宀
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉(漢)字代表家的温(溫)暖。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.
    - Chinese characters that include the radical '宀' represent the warmth of home.
  - 屋顶(頂)遮风挡(風擋)雨，家园(園)散发温(發溫)馨。
    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
    - The roof shields from wind and rain, and the home radiates warmth.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 车(車)
- **traditional:** 車
- **definition:** vehicle
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a carriage seen from above with large wheels and a sun shade ->
    - vertical strokes represent wheels and loading area ->
    - depicts a vehicle
  - **interpretation:** The character 車 (chē, "vehicle") visually represents a traditional carriage, with its design evolving into a more simplified vertical depiction over time.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)是一辆电动(輛電動)的车(車)。
    - Zhè shì yī liàng diàndòng de chē.
    - This is an electric vehicle.
  - 我在车(車)站等车(車)，准备(準備)乘车(車)。
    - Wǒ zài chēzhàn děng chē, zhǔnbèi chéng chē.
    - I wait at the station for the bus, and I am ready to board.
  - 他开车(開車)回家，沿途欣赏风(賞風)景。
    - Tā kāi chē huí jiā, yán tú xīnshǎng fēngjǐng.
    - He drives home, enjoying the scenery along the way.
### 石( )库(庫)门(門) → 门(門)
- **traditional:** 門
- **definition:** door; gate
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 門 (mén, "gate") depicts a gate ->
    - simplified to 门 (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 门(門) (mén, "gate") represents the image of a double-sided gate. It simplifies to 门 but retains the depiction of an entrance or gateway.
  - **simplification:** The character 门 is a simplified form of 門, where the complex structure is replaced by a more straightforward representation while preserving the overall image of a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 学(學)校门(門)前站着(著)一位老师(師)。
    - Xuéxiào mén qián zhànzhe yī wèi lǎoshī(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - A teacher stands at the school gate.
  - 请(請)你从这(從這)扇门(門)进来(進來)，把鞋子脱(脫)掉。
    - Qǐng nǐ cóng zhè shàn mén(請你從這扇門) jìnlái(進來)，bǎ xiézi(把鞋子) tuōdiào(脫掉)。
    - Please come in through this door and take off your shoes.
  - 夜深了，只有微弱的光从门缝里泄(從門縫裡洩)出。
    - Yè shēn le(夜深了)，zhǐ yǒu wēiruò de guāng cóng mén fèng lǐ xiè chū(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - It's late at night, and only a faint light seeps through the gap in the door.
