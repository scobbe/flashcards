### Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
---
- **pinyin:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 库(庫)
    - kù
    - storehouse
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 石(石) (shí, "stone") + 库(庫) (kù, "storehouse") + 门(門) (mén, "door") — stone storehouse door
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a style using stone as the primary material for homes with distinctive doorways. This architectural style reflects the blend of traditional Chinese courtyard homes and Western influences in urban Shanghai.
- **examples:**
  - 石库门是上海的独特建筑风格(石庫門是上海的獨特建築風格)。
    - Shíkùmén shì Shànghǎi de dútè jiànzhú fēnggé.
    - Shikumen is a unique architectural style in Shanghai.
  - 我在石库门附近散步(我在石庫門附近散步)。
    - Wǒ zài Shíkùmén fùjìn sànbù.
    - I am walking near Shikumen.
  - 石库门代表了历史与现代的结合(石庫門代表了歷史與現代的結合)。
    - Shíkùmén dàibiǎo le lìshǐ yǔ xiàndài de jiéhé.
    - Shikumen represents the blend of history and modernity.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 石(石)
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone beneath a cliff, which resembles a cave set into the side of a cliff or mountain.
  - **interpretation:** This image intuitively represents a stone's natural habitat at the base of a cliff, emphasizing its solidity and connection to the earth.
- **examples:**
  - 我找到了石头。
    - Wǒ zhǎodàole shítou.
    - I found a stone.
  - 这座山上有很多石块。
    - Zhè zuò shān shàng yǒu hěnduō shíkuài.
    - There are many stones on this mountain.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 库(庫)
- **definition:** storehouse
- **pinyin:** kù
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - building
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic part 广 (guǎng, "building") with the phonetic part 車 (chē, "vehicle") to convey the concept of a building associated with storage.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively suggests a structure or building (广) used for storing items, with the phonetic element indicating its pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 这个库里有很多书(這個庫裡有很多書)。
    - Zhège kù lǐ yǒu hěn duō shū.
    - There are many books in this storehouse.
  - 把货物存放在库里(把貨物存放在庫裡)。
    - Bǎ huòwù cúnfàng zài kù lǐ.
    - Store the goods in the storehouse.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣)
- **definition:** building
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a structure with a roof, suggesting a building or shelter.
  - **interpretation:** The character illustrates the concept of a broad, open space beneath a cover, evoking the idea of a building or structure.
- **examples:**
  - 这座建筑很宽敞(這座建築很寬敞)。
    - Zhè zuò jiànzhú hěn kuānchǎng.
    - This building is very spacious.
  - 他在广场上休息(他在廣場上休息)。
    - Tā zài guǎngchǎng shàng xiūxí.
    - He is resting in the square.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 库(庫) → 车(車)
- **definition:** vehicle
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a carriage viewed from above, originally symbolizing a vehicle. The oracle bone script featured large wheels on both sides and a sun shade atop the carriage.
  - **interpretation:** This visual representation makes sense as it shows the structure of the carriage, emphasizing its functionality as a vehicle. The wheels on either side highlight movement and transport.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一辆车。(我買了一輛車。)
    - Wǒ mǎile yī liàng chē.
    - I bought a car.
  - 这个车很快。(這個車很快。)
    - Zhège chē hěn kuài.
    - This vehicle is very fast.
### 石库门(石庫門) → 门(門)
- **definition:** door
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a gate with two doors that can be opened or closed.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents an entrance or barrier, symbolizing access or separation.
- **examples:**
  - 这扇门很漂亮(這扇門很漂亮)。
    - Zhè shàn mén hěn piàoliang.
    - This door is very beautiful.
  - 门口有很多人(門口有很多人)。
    - Ménkǒu yǒu hěn duō rén.
    - There are many people at the entrance.
