### strange
---
- **pinyin:** strange
- **components:**
  - 奇
    - qí
    - strange; unusual; wonderful; odd
  - 怪
    - guài
    - strange; odd; to blame; monster
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 奇(奇) (qí, 'strange; unusual') + 怪(怪) (guài, 'strange; odd') — strange strange
  - **interpretation:** The repetition of the meaning 'strange' in this compound emphasizes the peculiarity of something. This reflects the sensibility of expressing the concept of 'strangeness' more vividly.
- **examples:**
  - 这个地方真奇怪(這個地方真奇怪)，我从未见过(我從未見過)。
    - zhège dìfang zhēn qíguài, wǒ cóng wèi jiàn guò.
    - This place is really strange; I have never seen it before.
  - 她的行为很奇怪(她的行為很奇怪)，大家都很疑惑(大家都很疑惑)。
    - tā de xíngwéi hěn qíguài, dàjiā dōu hěn yíhuò.
    - Her behavior is very strange; everyone is puzzled.
### 奇怪(奇怪) → 奇(奇)
- **definition:** strange; unusual; wonderful; odd
- **pinyin:** qí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Composed of two main parts: the upper part is related to 'strange' or 'wonder', and the bottom part depicts 'machinery', implying unusual or extraordinary mechanisms.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of wonder about things that are not ordinary, blending the elements of strangeness and uniqueness.
- **examples:**
  - 这个故事很奇怪(這個故事很奇怪)，让我觉得很有趣(讓我覺得很有趣)。
    - Zhège gùshì hěn qíguài, ràng wǒ juédé hěn yǒuqù。
    - This story is strange, it makes me feel very interesting.
  - 她有一个奇妙的想象力(她有一個奇妙的想象力)。
    - Tā yǒu yīgè qímiào de xiǎngxiànglì。
    - She has a wonderful imagination.
### 奇怪(奇怪) → 怪(怪)
- **definition:** strange; odd; to blame; monster
- **pinyin:** guài
- **components:**
  - 忄
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 圣
    - shèng
    - holy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 忄 (xīn, 'heart') indicating feelings or emotions with the phonetic component 圣 (shèng, 'holy') to create the meaning of something that evokes unusual or odd feelings.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of heart as an emotional center and a phonetic element suggesting something significant evokes the sense of strangeness or oddity, as it often relates to feelings about unusual occurrences.
- **examples:**
  - 这件事很怪(這件事很怪)，让我感到不安(讓我感到不安)！
    - Zhè jiàn shì hěn guài, ràng wǒ gǎndào bù'ān!
    - This matter is strange, it makes me feel uneasy!
  - 他总是有怪异的想法(他總是有怪異的想法)。
    - Tā zǒng shì yǒu guài yì de xiǎngfǎ.
    - He always has strange ideas.
### 奇怪(奇怪) → 怪(怪) → 忄(心)
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a simple representation of a heart, symbolizing emotions and feelings.
  - **interpretation:** The heart shape intuitively represents emotions and affection, making it a fitting symbol for feelings.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个好心(我有一個好心)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī gè hǎo xīn.
    - I have a good heart.
  - 他心里很高兴(他心裡很高興)。
    - Tā xīn lǐ hěn gāo xìng.
    - He is very happy in his heart.
