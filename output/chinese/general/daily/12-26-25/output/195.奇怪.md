## strange
### qíguài
---
- **definition:** strange
- **pinyin:** qíguài
- **components:**
  - 奇
    - qí
    - strange; odd; unique; rare
  - 怪
    - guài
    - weird; strange; blame; monster
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 奇(奇) (qí, "strange; odd") and 怪(怪) (guài, "weird; strange") combine to form 奇怪(奇怪) (qíguài, "strange").
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 奇(奇) (qí, "strange; odd") and 怪(怪) (guài, "weird; strange") strengthens the idea of 'strange' by bringing together two characters that convey unusualness or abnormality.
- **examples:**
  - 这件事情真奇怪(這件事情真奇怪)。
    - Zhè jiàn shìqíng zhēn qíguài.
    - This matter is really strange.
  - 他看起来的表情很奇怪(他看起來的表情很奇怪)，让大家感到困惑(讓大家感到困惑)。
    - Tā kàn qǐlái de biǎoqíng hěn qíguài, ràng dàjiā gǎndào kùnhuò.
    - His expression looked strange, making everyone feel puzzled.

### 奇怪(奇怪) → 奇(奇) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big
- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫(夫) (fū, 'man')

  - 大人物登场(大人物登場)，留下传奇(留下傳奇)。
    - Dà rénwù dēngchǎng, liúxià chuánqí.
