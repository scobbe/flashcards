### qíguài
---
- **definition:** strange
- **pinyin:** qíguài
- **components:**
  - 奇
    - qí
    - strange; odd; unique; rare
  - 怪

  - **description:** 奇 (qí, "strange; odd") and 怪 (guài, "weird; strange") combine to form 奇怪 (qíguài, "strange").
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 奇 (qí, "strange; odd") and 怪 (guài, "weird; strange") strengthens the idea of 'strange' by bringing together two characters that convey unusualness or abnormality.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)件( )事( )情( )真( )奇( )怪( )。
