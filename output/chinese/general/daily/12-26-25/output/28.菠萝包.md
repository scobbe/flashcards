## pineapple bun
### bōluóbāo
---
- **traditional:** 菠蘿包
- **definition:** pineapple bun
- **pinyin:** bōluóbāo
- **components:**
  - 菠
    - bō
    - spinach; leafy vegetable
  - 萝(蘿)
    - luó
    - vines; fraxinus; radish
  - 包
    - bāo
    - package; wrap; bun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 菠萝(蘿)包 (bōluóbāo, "pineapple bun") is a type of sweet pastry with a crunchy, sugary topping resembling the texture of a pineapple.
  - **interpretation:** The name 菠萝(蘿)包 (bōluóbāo) describes a bun (包) with a topping that visually resembles a pineapple (菠萝/菠蘿) due to its pattern and color, even though it contains no pineapple. It is popular in Hong Kong and reflects the creativity of Cantonese bakery culture.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢(歡)吃菠萝(蘿)包。
    - Tā xǐhuān chī bōluóbāo.
    - He likes to eat pineapple buns.
  - 早上经过面(經過麵)包店，我买(買)了一个(個)菠萝(蘿)包。
    - Zǎoshang jīngguò miànbāodiàn, wǒ mǎile yīgè bōluóbāo.
    - I passed by a bakery in the morning and bought a pineapple bun.
