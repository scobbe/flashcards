## 菠萝包(菠蘿包)
---
## pineapple bun
### bōluóbāo
---
- **definition:** pineapple bun
- **pinyin:** bōluóbāo
- **components:**
  - 菠萝(菠蘿)
    - bōluó
    - pineapple
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bun; bread roll; bag; to wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 菠萝 + 包 = pineapple + bun → a sweet bun with a pineapple-like crust.
  - **interpretation:** Named for its pineapple-like crackled crust; classic versions contain no actual pineapple.
  - **simplification:** Only 蘿 → 萝 was simplified by replacing 羅 with 罗 under the grass radical, reducing strokes; 菠 and 包 are unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢早餐吃菠萝包(我最喜歡早餐吃菠蘿包)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan zǎocān chī bōluóbāo.
    - I love eating pineapple buns for breakfast.
  - 香港的面包店常卖热腾腾的菠萝包(香港的麵包店常賣熱騰騰的菠蘿包)。
    - Xiānggǎng de miànbāo diàn cháng mài rè téngténg de bōluóbāo.
    - Bakeries in Hong Kong often sell steaming-hot pineapple buns.
  - 它的表皮像菠萝但通常不含菠萝馅(它的表皮像菠蘿但通常不含菠蘿餡)。
    - Tā de biǎopí xiàng bōluó dàn tōngcháng bù hán bōluó xiàn.
    - Its crust looks like a pineapple, but it usually has no pineapple filling.
### 菠萝包(菠蘿包) → 菠萝(菠蘿)
- **definition:** pineapple
- **pinyin:** bōluó
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phonetic loan (transliteration)
  - **description:** 菠(bō, with grass radical) + 萝(luó, with grass radical) ≈ bōluó = pineapple.
  - **interpretation:** Transliterated plant name: characters were chosen mainly for sound, with the grass radical signaling a botanical term. In many regions (esp. Taiwan), 凤梨 is the more common synonym.
  - **simplification:** 蘿 was simplified to 萝 by adopting 罗 for 羅 and using the simplified grass radical; 菠 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我最爱菠萝(我最愛菠蘿)。
    - Wǒ zuì ài bōluó.
    - I love pineapple.
  - 超市有新鲜的菠萝(超市有新鮮的菠蘿)。
    - Chāoshì yǒu xīnxiān de bōluó.
    - The supermarket has fresh pineapples.
  - 在台湾，人们常说凤梨而不是菠萝(在臺灣，人們常說鳳梨而不是菠蘿)。
    - Zài Táiwān, rénmen cháng shuō fènglí ér bù shì bōluó.
    - In Taiwan, people usually say fènglí instead of bōluó.
### 菠萝包(菠蘿包) → 包
- **definition:** bun; bread roll; bag; to wrap
- **pinyin:** bāo
- **components:**
  - 巳
    - sì
    - 6th Earthly Branch; zodiac sign Snake
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): a fetus (巳 sì) in the womb (勹), or a swaddled baby.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a swaddled infant or a curled fetus enclosed by a wrapper, conveying enclosure and bundling. The image naturally extends to actions and objects for wrapping, hence meanings like “wrap,” “package,” and “bag.”
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一个背包(我買了一個背包)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī gè bèibāo.
    - I bought a backpack.
  - 请把礼物包起来(請把禮物包起來)。
    - Qǐng bǎ lǐwù bāo qǐlái.
    - Please wrap the gift.
  - 快递已经把包裹送到了(快遞已經把包裹送到了)。
    - Kuàidì yǐjīng bǎ bāoguǒ sòng dào le.
    - The courier has already delivered the package.
### 菠萝包(菠蘿包) → 包 → 巳
- **definition:** 6th Earthly Branch; zodiac sign Snake
- **pinyin:** sì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram (象形): coiled snake; an alternate theory analyzes it as a foetus; graph similar to 已.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a coiled serpent, which aligns with its use as the sixth Earthly Branch associated with the Snake. Its visual similarity to 已/己 has led to historical confusion and variant explanations of the graph.
- **examples:**
  - 现在是巳时(現在是巳時)。
    - Xiànzài shì sì shí.
    - It is the Si hour (9–11 a.m.).
  - 他属蛇，对应的地支是巳(他屬蛇，對應的地支是巳)。
    - Tā shǔ shé, duìyìng de dìzhī shì sì.
    - He is of the Snake sign; the corresponding Earthly Branch is Si.
  - 农历常用十二地支记时，其中之一是巳(農曆常用十二地支記時，其中之一是巳)。
    - Nónglì cháng yòng shí’èr dìzhī jì shí, qízhōng zhī yī shì sì.
    - The lunar calendar often uses the twelve Earthly Branches to mark hours; one of them is Si.
