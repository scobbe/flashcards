### pineapple bun
---
- **pinyin:** pineapple bun
- **components:**
  - 菠萝(菠蘿)
    - bōluó
    - pineapple; fruit; tropical; sweet
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bun; wrap; package; contain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 菠萝(菠蘿) (bōluó, "pineapple") + 包(包) (bāo, "bun") — pineapple bun
  - **interpretation:** This word literally describes a bun (包) filled or topped with pineapple (菠萝). The combination highlights the sweet and tropical flavor profile of this type of bread roll.
- **examples:**
  - 我想吃一个菠萝包。
    - Wǒ xiǎng chī yīgè bōluó bāo.
    - I want to eat a pineapple bun.
  - 菠萝包在香港非常受欢迎。
    - Bōluó bāo zài Xiānggǎng fēicháng shòu huānyíng.
    - The pineapple bun is very popular in Hong Kong.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 艹(艸) (cǎo, 'grass', 'plants') and the phonetic component 娄(婁) (lóu), suggesting a type of plant associated with roots.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines a plant-related component with one indicating sound, emphasizing that it refers to a root vegetable. This makes intuitive sense as radishes grow underground and are part of the plant family.
