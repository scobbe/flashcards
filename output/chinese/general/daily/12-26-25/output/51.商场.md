## shopping mall
### shāngchǎng
---
- **traditional:** 商場
- **definition:** shopping mall
- **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
- **components:**
  - 商
    - shāng
    - commerce; business
  - 场(場)
    - chǎng
    - open space; place; field; stage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 商 (shāng, "commerce") + 场(場) (chǎng, "open space" or "place") =
    - a place for commerce
  - **interpretation:** A 商( )场(場) (shāngchǎng, "shopping mall") is a large open space dedicated to commerce where various shops and businesses are situated. The combination of 商 (shāng, "commerce") with 场(場) (chǎng, "open space" or "place") conveys the idea of a designated area for trade and services, aligning with modern commercial practices.
  - **simplification:** Customary simplification by converting 場 to 场
