### shāngchǎng
---
- **definition:** shopping mall
- **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
- **components:**
  - 商
    - shāng

### 商场(商場) → 场(場) → 昜(昜) → 彡(彡)
- **definition:** rays
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts light or rays; like shimmering lines
  - **interpretation:** The character 彡(彡) (shān, "rays") visually represents the appearance of rays of light or the shimmering effect of something radiant.
- **examples:**
  - 阳光洒满大地(陽光灑滿大地)，彡般的光辉映出温暖(彡般的光輝映出溫暖)。
    - Yángguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì, shān bān de guānghuī yìngchū wēnnuǎn.
    - The sunlight fills the earth, and rays-like brilliance reveals its warmth.
  - 海边的波光闪烁(海邊的波光閃爍)，如彡释出的光芒般迷人(如彡釋出的光芒般迷人)。
    - Hǎibiān de bōguāng shǎnshuò, rú shān shìchū de guāngmáng bān mírén.
    - The sparkling waves on the seaside resemble rays emanating radiant light, enchanting to behold.
  - 清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光(清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光)。
    - Qīngchén de lùzhū sì shān shèchū de xīguāng.
    - In the early morning, dewdrops seem to emit rays of dawn light.
