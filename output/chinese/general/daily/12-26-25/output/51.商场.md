### shopping mall
---
- **pinyin:** shopping mall
- **components:**
  - 商
    - shāng
    - trade; commerce; business; merchant
  - 场(場)
    - chǎng
    - place; field; site; arena
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 商(商) (shāng, 'trade') + 场(場) (chǎng, 'place') — trade place
  - **interpretation:** The term 商场 combines 商(商) (shāng, 'trade') with 场(場) (chǎng, 'place'), indicating a location designated for trade activities. It is a common term for places where commercial transactions occur.
- **examples:**
  - 我去商场购物。(我去商場購物。)
    - Wǒ qù shāngchǎng gòuwù.
    - I go shopping at the mall.
  - 商场里有很多品牌。(商場裡有很多品牌。)
    - Shāngchǎng lǐ yǒu hěn duō pǐnpái.
    - There are many brands in the shopping mall.
