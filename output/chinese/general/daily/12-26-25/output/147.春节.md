## Spring Festival
### Chūn Jié
---
- **definition:** Spring Festival
- **pinyin:** Chūn Jié
- **components:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring
  - 节(節)
    - jié
    - festival; holiday; joint; segment
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 春(春) (chūn, "spring") + 節(節) (jié, "festival") =
    - Spring Festival
  - **interpretation:** The Spring Festival is a major holiday marking the celebration of spring and the beginning of the lunar new year in Chinese culture.
- **examples:**
  - 春节是中国最重要的节日(春節是中國最重要的節日)。
    - Chūn Jié shì Zhōngguó zuì zhòngyào de jiérì.
    - Spring Festival is the most important festival in China.
  - 我们在春节期间游览名胜(我們在春節期間遊覽名勝)，感受传统文化魅力(感受傳統文化魅力)。
    - Wǒmen zài Chūn Jié qíjiān yóulǎn míngshèng, gǎnshòu chuántǒng wénhuà mèilì.
    - During the Spring Festival, we visit scenic spots and experience the charm of traditional culture.
  - 春节期间人们互赠红包(春節期間人們互贈紅包)，祈盼一年平安幸福(祈盼一年平安幸福)。
    - Chūn Jié qíjiān rénmen hùzèng hóngbāo, qí pàn yī nián píng'ān xìngfú.
    - During the Spring Festival, people exchange red envelopes and wish for a year of peace and happiness.
