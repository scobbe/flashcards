### Spring Festival
---
- **pinyin:** Spring Festival
- **components:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring; youth; warm; to tremble
  - 节(節)
    - jié
    - festival; section; node; to tie together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 春(春) (chūn, 'spring') + 节(節) (jié, 'festival') — spring festival
  - **interpretation:** 春(春) (chūn, 'spring') signifies the season of renewal, while 节(節) (jié, 'festival') indicates a celebration. Together, 春节(春節) (chūnjié) represents a festive time welcoming the arrival of spring.
- **examples:**
  - 春节是中国最重要的节日。(春節是中國最重要的節日。)
    - Chūnjié shì Zhōngguó zuì zhòngyào de jiérì.
    - The Spring Festival is the most important holiday in China.
  - 我们在春节期间会团聚。(我們在春節期間會團聚。)
    - Wǒmen zài Chūnjié qījiān huì tuánjù.
    - We will reunite during the Spring Festival.
