### overseas Chinese
---
- **pinyin:** overseas Chinese
- **components:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - China; magnificent; splendid
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - overseas Chinese; to reside abroad
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 华(華) (huá, "China; magnificent; splendid") + 侨(僑) (qiáo, "overseas Chinese; to reside abroad") — Chinese residing abroad
  - **interpretation:** The term describes individuals of Chinese descent living outside of China. This combination makes sense as it reflects both their cultural heritage and their current residency status.
- **examples:**
  - 很多华侨都回国探亲(很多華僑都回國探親)。
    - hěnduō huáqiáo dōu huíguó tànqīn。
    - Many overseas Chinese return home to visit relatives.
  - 华侨为国家的发展贡献了很多(華僑為國家的發展貢獻了很多)。
    - huáqiáo wèi guójiā de fāzhǎn gòngxiànle hěnduō。
    - Overseas Chinese have contributed a lot to the country's development.
### 华侨(華僑) → 华(華)
- **definition:** China; magnificent; splendid
- **pinyin:** huá
- **components:**
  - 虍
    - hū
    - tiger
  - 華
    - huá
    - magnificent
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 华(華) (huá, 'magnificent; splendid') combines the semantic component 虍 (hū, 'tiger') with the phonetic component 華 (huá), originally implying something extravagant like the beautiful fur of a tiger.
  - **interpretation:** The presence of the 虍 (hū, 'tiger') evokes a sense of luxury and grandeur, while 華 (huá) carries the meaning associated with beauty and brilliance.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved reducing the number of strokes by replacing 華 with 华, which is a more cursive form yet maintains phonetic similarity.
- **examples:**
  - 中国(中國)是一个美丽的国家(國家)，有着丰富的文化和历史(歷史)。
    - Zhōngguó shì yīgè měilì de guójiā, yǒuzhe fēngfù de wénhuà hé lìshǐ。
    - China is a beautiful country, rich in culture and history.
  - 这件衣服真华丽(華麗)，非常适合你。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfú zhēn huálì, fēicháng shìhé nǐ。
    - This dress is truly magnificent and suits you perfectly.
