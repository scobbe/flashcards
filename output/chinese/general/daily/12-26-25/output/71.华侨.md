## overseas Chinese
### huáqiáo
---
- **traditional:** 華僑
- **definition:** overseas Chinese
- **pinyin:** huáqiáo
- **components:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - China; magnificent
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - to live abroad
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 华(華) (huá, "China; magnificent") + 侨(僑) (qiáo, "to live abroad")
  - **interpretation:** 华(華)侨(僑) (huáqiáo) means 'overseas Chinese.' The word combines 华(華) (huá), representing 'China' and symbolizing magnificence, and 侨(僑) (qiáo), which means 'to live abroad.' This combination reflects Chinese people who establish themselves outside of China while maintaining cultural ties to their homeland.
