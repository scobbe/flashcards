## overseas Chinese
### huáqiáo
---
- **definition:** overseas Chinese
- **pinyin:** huáqiáo
- **components:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - Chinese; flowery; illustrious
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - to sojourn; reside abroad
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 华(華) (huá, "Chinese") refers to anything related to China or Chinese culture, while 侨(僑) (qiáo, "to sojourn") indicates someone living temporarily in a foreign land. Together, they mean Chinese people living abroad.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of these characters creates a term for Chinese nationals residing outside of China. It reflects both their cultural identity as Chinese (华(華)), and their status as expatriates or temporary residents (侨(僑)) in another country.
  - **simplification:** The simplified form 华侨 is derived by simplifying the characters 華 to 华 and 僑 to 侨.
- **examples:**
  - 很多华侨在国外努力工作(很多華僑在國外努力工作)。
    - Hěn duō huáqiáo zài guówài nǔlì gōngzuò.
    - Many overseas Chinese work hard abroad.
  - 政府推出新政策(政府推出新政策)，支持华侨创业(支持華僑創業)。
    - Zhèngfǔ tuīchū xīn zhèngcè, zhīchí huáqiáo chuàngyè.
    - The government has introduced new policies to support the entrepreneurship of overseas Chinese.
