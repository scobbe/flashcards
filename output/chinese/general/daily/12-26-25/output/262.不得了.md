## 不得了(不得了)
---
## 
### terrible; extremely; amazing
---
- **pinyin:** terrible; extremely; amazing
- **components:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 得
    - dé
    - obtain; able to
  - 了
    - le
    - completed action marker
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 不(不) (bù, 'not') + 得(得) (dé, 'obtain, able to') + 了(了) (le, 'completed action marker') — not able to finish
  - **interpretation:** The term suggests a state where something is beyond tolerance or control. It indicates something is so extreme that one cannot manage or cope with it.
- **examples:**
  - 那场比赛真是不得了！
    - Nà chǎng bǐsài zhēn shì bùdéliǎo！
    - That game was truly amazing!
  - 他的表演不得了，太精彩了！
    - Tā de biǎoyǎn bùdéliǎo, tài jīngcǎi le！
    - His performance was incredible, so wonderful!
  - 这件事让大家不得了！
    - Zhè jiàn shì ràng dàjiā bùdéliǎo！
    - This matter left everyone in shock!
### 不得了(不得了) → 不(不)
- **definition:** not
- **pinyin:** bù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the calyx of a flower, later combined with the character for mouth (口).
  - **interpretation:** The imagery of the calyx implies a barrier or obstruction, which resonates with the concept of negation or refusal.
- **examples:**
  - 我不喜欢这个。(我不喜歡這個。)
    - Wǒ bù xǐhuān zhège.
    - I do not like this.
  - 这不是真的。(這不是真的。)
    - Zhè bù shì zhēn de.
    - This is not true.
### 不得了(不得了) → 得(得)
- **definition:** obtain; able to
- **pinyin:** dé
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - běi
    - cowry
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** The character 得 represents an ideogrammic compound formed from 貝 (běi, "cowry") and 又 (yòu, "hand") — symbolizing the action of picking up a cowry, which is associated with obtaining valuables.
  - **interpretation:** The imagery of a hand picking up cowries effectively conveys the concept of obtaining or acquiring something valuable.
- **examples:**
  - 我得到了很多帮助。(我得到了很多幫助。)
    - Wǒ dédàole hěnduō bāngzhù.
    - I received a lot of help.
  - 你得去那里。(你得去那裡。)
    - Nǐ děi qù nàlǐ.
    - You must go there.
### 不得了(不得了) → 得(得) → 貝(貝)
- **definition:** cowry
- **pinyin:** běi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cowrie shell which was used as currency in ancient China.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a cowrie shell, linking it directly to its historical use as money. This makes it intuitive as currency symbolizing wealth and trade.
- **examples:**
  - 我用貝(我用贝)买东西。
    - Wǒ yòng bèi mǎi dōngxi.
    - I use cowries to buy things.
  - 古代人用貝(古代人用贝)交易。
    - Gǔdài rén yòng bèi jiāoyì.
    - Ancient people used cowries for trade.
### 不得了(不得了) → 得(得) → 又(又)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** yòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a right hand.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a hand, suggesting the action or agency associated with it. This is intuitive as hands are often involved in actions and decisions.
- **examples:**
  - 他又来了(他又來了)！
    - tā yòu lái le！
    - He has come again!
  - 我又忘记了(我又忘記了)。
    - wǒ yòu wàng jì le。
    - I have forgotten again.
  - 她又在笑(她又在笑)。
    - tā yòu zài xiào。
    - She is laughing again.
### 不得了(不得了) → 了(了)
- **definition:** completed action marker
- **pinyin:** le
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Used as a completion marker in Chinese, indicating that an action has been accomplished.
  - **interpretation:** The character '了' signifies that an action has reached its conclusion. It intuitively represents finality or completion in action.
- **examples:**
  - 我吃了(我吃了)。
    - wǒ chī le.
    - I have eaten.
  - 他走了(他走了)。
    - tā zǒu le.
    - He has left.
