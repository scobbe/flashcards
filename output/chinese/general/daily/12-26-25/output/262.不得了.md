## terrible; extremely; amazing
### bùdéliǎo
---
- **definition:** terrible; extremely; amazing
- **pinyin:** bùdéliǎo
- **components:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 得
    - dé
    - obtain; can
  - 了
    - liǎo
    - finish; complete
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 不(不) (bù, "not") + 得(得) (dé, "obtain; can") + 了(了) (liǎo, "finish; complete")
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 不(不) (bù, "not"), 得(得) (dé, "obtain; can"), and 了(了) (liǎo, "finish; complete") expresses an extreme condition or state that cannot be easily handled or concluded, leading to meanings such as 'terrible', 'extremely', or 'amazing'.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的天气不得了(今天的天氣不得了)。
    - Jīntiān de tiānqì bùdéliǎo.
    - Today's weather is extremely bad.
  - 那部电影精彩得不得了(那部電影精彩得不得了)，观众拍手称快(觀眾拍手稱快)。
    - Nà bù diànyǐng jīngcǎi dé bùdéliǎo, guānzhòng pāishǒu chēngkuài.
    - That movie is incredibly brilliant, and the audience applauds enthusiastically.

### 不得了(不得了) → 得(得)
- **definition:** obtain; can
- **pinyin:** dé
- **components:**
  - 貝

    - cowry

    - yòu
    - hand
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - inch
  - 彳
    - chì
    - step
  - 旦
    - dàn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") + 又(又) (yòu, "hand") ->
    - obtaining valuables ->
    - 彳(彳) (chì, "step") added, 又 changed to 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch"), 貝 corrupted to 旦(旦) (dàn, "dawn") ->

Over time, the hand component evolved into 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") due to script changes, and 貝(貝) transformed into 旦(旦) (dàn, "dawn"), illustrating the character's phonetic and graphic evolution.
  - **simplification:** None. Traditional and simplified forms are the same for 得.
- **examples:**
  - 我得完成工作(我得完成工作)。
    - Wǒ děi wánchéng gōngzuò.
    - I have to finish the work.
  - 他跑得很快(他跑得很快)。

    - He runs very fast.
  - 我得了一张票(我得了一張票)。
