### water spinach
---
- **pinyin:** water spinach
- **components:**
  - 通心
    - tōngxīn
    - communicate; heart-to-heart; open; understand
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish; cuisine; food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 通心(通心) (tōngxīn, 'communicate') + 菜(菜) (cài, 'vegetable') — communicating vegetable
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 通心(通心) (tōngxīn, 'communicate') suggests a connection between people, while 菜(菜) (cài, 'vegetable') refers to the edible part. Water spinach is often known for its tender texture and is a popular vegetable that is refreshing and easy to prepare.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃通心菜(我喜歡吃通心菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī tōngxīn cài.
    - I like to eat water spinach.
  - 她的汤里加了通心菜(她的湯裡加了通心菜)。
    - Tā de tāng lǐ jiāle tōngxīn cài.
    - She added water spinach to her soup.
