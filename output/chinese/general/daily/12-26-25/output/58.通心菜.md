## water spinach
### tōngxīncài
---
- **definition:** water spinach
- **pinyin:** tōngxīncài
- **components:**
  - 通
    - tōng
    - connect; pass through
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; core
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 通(通) (tōng, "connect; pass through") + 心(心) (xīn, "heart; core") + 菜(菜) (cài, "vegetable") =
    - a vegetable with a hollow or open stem
  - **interpretation:** The name 通心菜 (tōngxīncài) refers to water spinach, a vegetable characterized by its hollow stems that allow water to flow through, hence the idea of 'passing through the heart' or 'open core'.
