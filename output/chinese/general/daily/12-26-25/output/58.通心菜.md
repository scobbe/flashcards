## water spinach
### tōngxīncài
---
- **definition:** water spinach
- **pinyin:** tōngxīncài
- **components:**
  - 通
    - tōng
    - through; open; connect; communicate
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; center; core
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish; cuisine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 通 (tōng, "through"), 心 (xīn, "heart"), 菜 (cài, "vegetable"). The term 通心菜 (tōngxīncài) refers to water spinach, a popular leafy vegetable in Asia characterized by its hollow stems. The name literally translates to 'through heart vegetable', highlighting the hollow stem that runs through the plant.
  - **interpretation:** The name reflects water spinach's hollow stem, which allows water to flow through, much like the heart circulates blood. This characteristic is distinct and practical for its growth in water environments.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈(媽媽)做了通心菜炒蛋。
    - Māmā zuò le tōngxīncài chǎo dàn.
    - Mom made water spinach stir-fried with eggs.
  - 超市有新鲜(鮮)的通心菜，味道非常好。
    - Chāoshì yǒu xīnxiān de tōngxīncài, wèidào fēicháng hǎo.
    - The supermarket has fresh water spinach, and it tastes very good.
  - 农(農)夫种(種)植通心菜，收获时节(穫時節)到了。
    - Nóngfū zhòngzhí tōngxīncài, shōuhuò shíjié dàole.
    - The farmer cultivates water spinach, and the harvest season has arrived.
### 通心菜 → 通
- **definition:** through; open; connect; communicate
- **pinyin:** tōng
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 甬
    - yǒng
    - wine cup
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 辶 (chuò, 'walk') + phonetic 甬 (yǒng, 'wine cup') =
    - movement with fluidity ->
    - through; open; connect; communicate
  - **interpretation:** The addition of 辶 (chuò, 'walk') implies movement or progression, while 甬 (yǒng, 'wine cup') serves as the phonetic element. The combination suggests moving through an area or connecting points, reinforcing the character's meanings related to passage and communication.
- **examples:**

    - Zhè tiáo lù tōng xiàng shān dǐng.
    - This road leads to the mountain top.

    - Tā xīwàng néng tōng yóujiàn, yǔ yuǎnfāng qīnyǒu bǎochí liánxì.
    - He hopes to communicate through email, maintaining contact with distant relatives and friends.
  - 现(現)代科技使世界相通。
    - Xiàndài kējì shǐ shìjiè xiāng tōng.
    - Modern technology connects the world.

- **pinyin:** chuò

  - 辵

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** combining form
  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** 辶(辵) (chuò, "walk") is derived by simplifying 能(mp) (néng, "self-reliant") structure from the character 辵. This simplified form emphasizes the act of motion or walking and is widely used as a radical in various motion-related characters.

    - Xǔduō biǎoshì zǒulù de zì(許多表示走路的字)，dōu hányǒu chuò zhè yī bùfen(都含有辶這一部分)。
    - Many characters that denote walking contain the radical '辶'.
  - 古人写(寫)字时(時)，常利用辶表达(達)行走之意。

    - When ancient people wrote characters, they often used '辶' to express the idea of walking.
### 通心菜 → 通 → 辶(辵) → 辵
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - walk
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 彳 (chì, "walk") + 止 (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop") ->
    - 𣥆 ->
    - stylized as 龰 ->
    - 辵
  - **interpretation:** The character 辵 combines 彳 (chì, "walk"), which represents movement, with 止 (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop"), suggesting the concept of stepping or traveling forward. This character represents the idea of movement, particularly the action of walking or traveling, as originally depicted by the more conservative form 𣥆. Over time, the bottom component has been stylized as 龰, forming the current representation.
- **examples:**
  - 他辵在乡间(鄉間)的小路上。
    - Tā chuò zài xiāngjiān de xiǎolù shàng.
    - He walks on the rural path.
  - 晨光洒满(灑滿)大地，他辵出门开(門開)始旅程。
    - Chén guāng sǎ mǎn dàdì, tā chuò chūmén kāishǐ lǚchéng.
    - As the morning light fills the earth, he sets out to begin his journey.
