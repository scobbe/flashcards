## doubanjiang; chili bean paste
### dòubànjiàng
---
- **definition:** doubanjiang; chili bean paste
- **pinyin:** dòubànjiàng
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 瓣
    - bàn
    - petal; section
  - 酱(醬)
    - jiàng
    - sauce
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") + 瓣(瓣) (bàn, "petal; section") + 酱(醬) (jiàng, "sauce")
  - **interpretation:** 豆瓣酱(豆瓣醬) (dòubànjiàng) is a paste made from beans that are fermented and combined with other ingredients to create a flavorful sauce. The name reflects its main ingredients: 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") represents the primary ingredient, while 瓣(瓣) (bàn, "section") suggests the breaking down or sectional preparation involved, and 酱(醬) (jiàng, "sauce") indicates its use as a condiment.
- **examples:**
  - 我在厨房调味(我在廚房調味)，喜欢用豆瓣酱做菜(喜歡用豆瓣醬做菜)。
    - Wǒ zài chúfáng tiáowèi, xǐhuān yòng dòubànjiàng zuòcài.
    - I season food in the kitchen, and I like to cook using doubanjiang.
  - 豆瓣酱是调味的秘密(豆瓣醬是調味的祕密)。
    - Dòubànjiàng shì tiáowèi de mìmì.
    - Doubanjiang is the secret of seasoning.
  - 这道菜味道浓郁(這道菜味道濃郁)，离不开豆瓣酱的调料(離不開豆瓣醬的調料)。
    - Zhè dào cài wèidào nóngyù, lí bù kāi dòubànjiàng de tiáoliào.
    - This dish has a rich flavor, and it would not be complete without the seasoning of doubanjiang.
