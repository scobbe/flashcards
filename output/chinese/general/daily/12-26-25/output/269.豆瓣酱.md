### doubanjiang; chili bean paste
---
- **pinyin:** doubanjiang; chili bean paste
- **components:**
  - 豆瓣
    - dòubàn
    - bean; broad bean
  - 酱(醬)
    - jiàng
    - sauce; paste; condiment
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") + 蘭(蘭) (lán, "broad beans") + 醬(醬) (jiàng, "sauce") — bean sauce
  - **interpretation:** 豆瓣(豆瓣) (dòubàn, "bean") refers to the broad beans used in the paste, while 醬(醬) (jiàng, "sauce") indicates the product type. This combination makes sense as a flavorful condiment widely used in cooking.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用豆瓣酱做菜(我喜歡用豆瓣醬做菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng dòubànjiàng zuò cài.
    - I love using chili bean paste to cook.
