## plain; simple
### pǔ
---
- **traditional:** 樸
- **definition:** plain; simple
- **pinyin:** pǔ
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - divination
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 木 (mù, "tree") + phonetic 卜 (bǔ, "divination") =
    - plain, simple wood
  - **interpretation:** The character 朴(pǔ) combines 木 (mù, "tree") as the semantic component since it relates to wood, with 卜 (bǔ, "divination") providing the phonetic aspect, hinting at the pronunciation. The combination conveys the idea of plainness or simplicity akin to unrefined wood.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 朴(pǔ) from 樸(pǔ) as part of the official Chinese Character Simplification Scheme in 1956, replacing the more complex traditional character with a simpler version that retained the original meaning and phonetic cues.
- **examples:**
  - 他过着朴(過著樸)的生活。
    - Tā guòzhe pǔ de shēnghuó.
    - He lives a plain life.
  - 他偏爱朴(愛樸)的设计(設計)，追求简单(簡單)的美。
    - Tā piān'ài pǔ de shèjì, zhuīqiú jiǎndān de měi.
    - He prefers plain designs, pursuing simple beauty.
  - 他的书(書)法风(風)格朴(樸)，透露出深厚的功底。
    - Tā de shūfǎ fēnggé pǔ, tòulù chū shēnhòu de gōngdǐ.
    - His calligraphy has a plain style, revealing profound mastery.
### 朴(樸) → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把椅子的主要材料是木。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
### 朴(樸) → 卜
- **definition:** divination
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a divination crack in a tortoise shell or bone
  - **interpretation:** The character 卜(bǔ, "divination") represents the practice of divination used in ancient China, often involving the interpretation of cracks in tortoise shells or bones.
- **examples:**
  - 占卜是一种(種)神秘的艺术(藝術)。
    - Zhānbǔ shì yī zhǒng shénmì de yìshù.
    - Divination is a mysterious art.
  - 他通过(過)卜算未来(來)，探寻隐(尋隱)秘的命运(運)。
    - Tā tōngguò bǔsuàn wèilái, tànxún yǐnmì de mìngyùn.
    - He uses divination to predict the future, seeking out his hidden destiny.
  - 古代人常以卜问(問)吉凶，以求平安和顺(順)。
    - Gǔdài rén cháng yǐ bǔ wèn jíxiōng, yǐ qiú píng'ān hé shùn.
    - Ancient people often practiced divination to determine fortune, in order to pursue peace and order.
