## plain; simple
### pǔ
---
- **definition:** plain; simple
- **pinyin:** pǔ
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 䑕(鼠)
    - shǔ
    - rat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + 䑕(鼠) (shǔ) as phonetic =
    - denotes plain, simple
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(木) (mù, "wood") is used as the semantic element to suggest something natural and unrefined, while 朴 was originally composed with 䑕(鼠) (shǔ) as the phonetic component to imply sound and meaning, leading to the sense of being plain or simple.
  - **simplification:** 樸(樸) was simplified to 朴(朴) by removing the 䑕(鼠) (shǔ) component, making it a more straightforward representation without a change in pronunciation or meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他的房子设计很朴(他的房子設計很樸)，装饰也很简单(裝飾也很簡單)。
    - Tā de fángzi shèjì hěn pǔ, zhuāngshì yě hěn jiǎndān.
    - The design of his house is plain, and the decoration is also simple.
  - 她喜爱那幅朴(她喜愛那幅樸)画。
    - Tā xǐ'ài nà fú pǔ huà.
    - She loves that plain painting.
### 朴(樸) → 木(木)
- **definition:** wood
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom
  - **interpretation:** 木(木) (mù, "tree") visually represents a tree with branches at the top and roots below. This pictorial depiction is straightforward, resembling the structure of a tree and its natural growth.
- **examples:**
  - 庭院里有一棵参天古木(庭院裡有一棵參天古木)。
    - Tíngyuàn lǐ yǒu yī kē cāntiān gǔ mù.
    - There is a towering ancient tree in the courtyard.
  - 他走到森林深处(他走到森林深處)，看见一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁(看見一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁)。
    - Tā zǒu dào sēnlín shēnchù, kànjiàn yī kē gū línglíng de mù chùlì zài lùpáng.
    - He walked into the deep forest and saw a solitary tree standing by the roadside.
### 朴(樸) → 䑕(鼠)
- **definition:** rat
- **pinyin:** shǔ
- **components:**
  - 鼠
    - shǔ
    - rat
  - 區
    - qū
    - area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 鼠(鼠) (shǔ, "rat") consists of the semantic 疋(疋) (yī, "foot") suggesting a mammal, and the phonetic component 區(區) (qū, "area"). Together they form the notion of "rat."
  - **interpretation:** 鼠(鼠) (shǔ, "rat") combines a component related to animals with another phonetic component, signifying the creature commonly associated with both those sounds and a description of its classification as a small mammal.
  - **simplification:** None, as 䑕 is an alternative or ancient form of 鼠, not traditionally simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里出现了一只䑕(鼠)。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ chūxiàn le yī zhī shǔ.
    - A rat appeared in the park.
  - 夜晚来临时(夜晚來臨時)，䑕在田野间奔跑(鼠在田野間奔跑)。
    - Yèwǎn láilín shí, shǔ zài tiányě jiān bēnpǎo.
    - When night fell, the rat was running across the fields.
### 朴(樸) → 䑕(鼠) → 鼠(鼠)
- **definition:** rat
- **pinyin:** shǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a mouse or rat ->
    - 臼(臼) (jiù, "mortar") for the open mouth and teeth + elements below for feet and tail ->
    - 鼠 (shǔ, "rat")
  - **interpretation:** The character 鼠(shǔ, "rat") visually represents a rat with an open mouth depicted by 臼(臼) (jiù, "mortar") on the top and lower elements suggesting its body and tail, highlighting the distinctive features of a rat.
- **examples:**
  - 科学家用鼠做实验(科學家用鼠做實驗)。
    - Kēxuéjiā yòng shǔ zuò shíyàn.
    - Scientists conduct experiments using rats.
  - 夜幕降临时(夜幕降臨時)，鼠开始出没(鼠開始出沒)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín shí, shǔ kāishǐ chūmò.
    - When night falls, rats begin to roam.
  - 老鼠偷吃粮食(老鼠偷吃糧食)。
    - Lǎoshǔ tōu chī liángshí.
    - Rats steal food.
### 朴(樸) → 䑕(鼠) → 區(區)
- **definition:** area
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - hiding
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - many people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸(匸) (xì, "hiding") + 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") =
    - many people enclosed ->
    - district; area
  - **interpretation:** The character 區(區) (qū, "district; area") combines the idea of many people 品(品) within an enclosed space 匸(匸), representing a designated area or district.
  - **simplification:** 區(區) (qū) was simplified to 区(区) by replacing 品(品, "many people") with 乂(乂, "manage"), reducing stroke count.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在富裕的区(我住在富裕的區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài fùyù de qū.
    - I live in a prosperous district.
  - 这个区不大(這個區不大)，但有悠久历史(但有悠久歷史)。
    - Zhège qū bù dà, dàn yǒu yōujiǔ lìshǐ.
    - This district is small, but it has a long history.
  - 我们将在城郊新区(我們將在城郊新區)，举办文化节(舉辦文化節)。
    - Wǒmen jiāng zài chéngjiāo xīn qū, jǔbàn wénhuà jié.
    - We will hold a cultural festival in the suburban new district.
