## 朴(樸)
---
## 
### plain; simple
---
- **pinyin:** plain; simple
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - divination
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 木 (mù, 'wood') + phonetic 菐 (bó) — represents simplicity or plainness, associated with the natural quality of wood.
  - **interpretation:** This character evokes the idea of being grounded or down-to-earth, just like wood's natural state without embellishments.
  - **simplification:** 朴 is a simplified form of 樸, where the complex component 菐 is replaced by a simpler character 卜. This choice likely emphasizes readability and ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 他的风格很朴素(他的風格很樸素)，让我感到舒服(讓我感到舒服)！
    - tā de fēnggé hěn pǔsù, ràng wǒ gǎndào shūfú！
    - His style is very plain, making me feel comfortable!
  - 这棵树的木材很樸实(這棵樹的木材很樸實)。
    - zhè kē shù de mùcái hěn pǔshí.
    - The wood of this tree is very plain.
  - 她的设计风格一直是朴实无华(她的設計風格一直是樸實無華)。
    - tā de shèjì fēnggé yīzhí shì pǔshí wúhuá.
    - Her design style has always been simple and unadorned.
### 朴(樸) → 木(木)
- **definition:** wood
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
### 朴(樸) → 卜(卜)
- **definition:** divination
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the act of divination, often represented as lines or marks indicating foretelling of the future.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively connects to the concept of seeking knowledge about the future through marks or signs, symbolizing the ancient practice of casting lots or using oracle bones.
- **examples:**
  - 古人通过卜卦进行预测(古人透過卜卦進行預測)。
    - Gǔ rén tōngguò bǔgà jìnxíng yùcè.
    - Ancient people predicted through divination.
  - 卜辞是最早的汉字之一(卜辭是最早的漢字之一)。
    - Bǔcí shì zuì zǎo de hànzì zhī yī.
    - The divination inscriptions are among the earliest Chinese characters.
