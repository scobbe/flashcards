### emit; give off
---
- **pinyin:** emit; give off
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 毛
    - máo
    - fur, hair
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 亻 (rén, "person") and the phonetic component 毛 (máo, "fur, hair").
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the image of a person emitting something, as if hair or fur is being let out. This combines the idea of a person and the action of emitting.
- **examples:**
  - 他冒出一阵烟(他冒出一陣煙)！
    - tā mào chū yī zhèn yān！
    - He emitted a puff of smoke!
  - 这植物能冒出鲜花(這植物能冒出鮮花)。
    - zhè zhíwù néng mào chū xiān huā。
    - This plant can give off fresh flowers.
  - 冒险并不是总是坏事(冒險並不是總是壞事)。
    - màoxiǎn bìng bù shì zǒng shì huài shì。
    - Adventuring is not always a bad thing.
### 冒(冒) → 亻(亻)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** A stylization of 人, which represents a person or human figure.
  - **interpretation:** This character represents a simplified form of a person, emphasizing humanity and individual identity.
- **examples:**
  - 我是一个人(我是一个人)。
    - Wǒ shì yī gè rén.
    - I am a person.
  - 人的权利很重要(人的權利很重要)。
    - Rén de quánlì hěn zhòngyào.
    - The rights of a person are very important.
### 冒(冒) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** fur, hair
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts hair or fur, representing the fine, thin, and elongated structure of hair strands.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation directly relates to hair, capturing its essence in a simple form.
- **examples:**
  - 他的头发很长(他的頭髮很長)。
    - tā de tóufà hěn cháng.
    - His hair is very long.
  - 这件衣服的毛料很好(這件衣服的毛料很好)。
    - zhè jiàn yīfú de máoliào hěn hǎo.
    - The fabric of this clothing is very good.
