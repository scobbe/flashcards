## emit; give off
### mào
---
- **definition:** emit; give off
- **pinyin:** mào
- **components:**
  - 冃
    - mào
    - head-covering
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 冃(冃) (mào, "head-covering") + 目(目) (mù, "eye") =
    - emit something from head or face ->
    - give off
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 冃(冃) (mào, "head-covering") and 目(目) (mù, "eye") to suggest the action of something coming out or being emitted, aligning with the idea of emitting or giving off.
- **examples:**
  - 工厂冒出浓浓的黑烟(工廠冒出濃濃的黑煙)。
    - Gōngchǎng màochū nóngnóng de hēiyān.
    - The factory emits thick black smoke.
  - 火山喷发时(火山噴發時)，热气冒出浓烈的气味(熱氣冒出濃烈的氣味)。
    - Huǒshān pēnfā shí, rèqì màochū nóngliè de qìwèi.
    - When the volcano erupts, hot air gives off a strong odor.
  - 厨房里(廚房裡)，锅中的油冒出滋滋的烟雾(鍋中的油冒出滋滋的煙霧)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ, guōzhōng de yóu màochū zīzī de yānwù.
    - In the kitchen, the oil in the pot emits sizzling smoke.
### 冒(冒) → 冃(冃)
- **definition:** head-covering
- **pinyin:** mào
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a head-covering
  - **interpretation:** 冃(冃) (mào, "head-covering") is a pictogram representing a simple head-covering or cap.
- **examples:**
  - 这个冃很漂亮(這個冃很漂亮)。
    - Zhège mào hěn piàoliang.
    - This head-covering is very beautiful.
  - 冬天来临时(冬天來臨時)，他戴上冃防寒(他戴上冃防寒)。
    - Dōngtiān láilín shí, tā dàishàng mào fánghán.
    - When winter arrives, he puts on a head-covering to guard against the cold.
  - 在宴会中(在宴會中)，她选择了精致的冃(她選擇了精緻的冃)。
    - Zài yànhuì zhōng, tā xuǎnzé le jīngzhì de mào.
    - At the banquet, she chose an exquisite head-covering.
### 冒(冒) → 目(目)
- **definition:** eye
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(目) (mù, "eye") represents a human eye but is now commonly depicted rotated by 90 degrees. It serves as the basis for several other characters related to vision or perception.
- **examples:**
  - 医生检查我的目(醫生檢查我的目)，确认没有异常(確認沒有異常)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchá wǒ de mù, quèrèn méiyǒu yìcháng.
    - The doctor examined my eye and confirmed there was nothing abnormal.
  - 他左目受伤(他左目受傷)，依旧炯炯有神(依舊炯炯有神)。
    - Tā zuǒ mù shòushāng, yījiù jiǒngjiǒng yǒu shén.
    - His left eye was injured, yet it still shone with a determined spark.
  - 那只猫的目似乎闪烁智慧(那隻貓的目似乎閃爍智慧)。
    - Nà zhī māo de mù sìhū shǎnshuò zhìhuì.
    - The cat's eye seemed to shimmer with wisdom.
