## 草莓(草莓)
---
## 
### strawberry
---
- **pinyin:** strawberry
- **components:**
  - 草
    - cǎo
    - grass; herbal; wild; plant
  - 莓
    - méi
    - berry; shrub; small fruit; bush
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 草(草) (cǎo, "grass") + 莓(莓) (méi, "berry") — grass berry
  - **interpretation:** The term combines 草(草) (cǎo, "grass") for its growth habit and 莓(莓) (méi, "berry") to describe its fruit type, making it a fitting name for this berry.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃草莓(我喜歡吃草莓)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī cǎoméi.
    - I like to eat strawberries.
  - 草莓蛋糕非常好吃(草莓蛋糕非常好吃)。
    - Cǎoméi dàngāo fēicháng hǎochī.
    - Strawberry cake is very delicious.
### 草莓(草莓) → 草(草)
- **definition:** grass; herbal; wild; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cluster of grass blades.
  - **interpretation:** This character visually represents the appearance of grass, with the upward strokes suggesting blades growing from the ground.
- **examples:**
  - 草地上有很多花(草地上有很多花)。
    - Cǎodì shàng yǒu hěnduō huā.
    - There are many flowers on the grass.
  - 他喜欢在草原上跑步(他喜歡在草原上跑步)。
    - Tā xǐhuān zài cǎoyuán shàng pǎobù.
    - He likes to run on the grassland.
### 草莓(草莓) → 莓(莓)
- **definition:** berry; shrub; small fruit; bush
- **pinyin:** méi
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 梅
    - méi
    - plum
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 由艹(草) (cǎo, 'grass')表示植物，加上梅(梅) (méi, 'plum'), 用於表示莓果類植物。
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a type of berry plant, indicating both its botanical nature and the specific fruit. The grass radical suggests that it belongs to the plant family.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃草莓。(我喜歡吃草莓。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī cǎoméi.
    - I like to eat strawberries.
  - 莓果非常美味。(莓果非常美味。)
    - Méiguǒ fēicháng měiwèi.
    - Berries are very delicious.
### 草莓(草莓) → 莓(莓) → 艹(艸)
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts grass or plants, typically represented above ground.
  - **interpretation:** The character shows tufts of grass, symbolizing vegetation that grows close to the earth.
  - **simplification:** 艹 is the simplified form of 艸; the top radicals are reduced for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 艹是草字头(艸是草字頭)。
    - cǎo shì cǎo zì tóu.
    - 艹 is the grass radical.
  - 许多汉字都有艹(許多漢字都有艸)。
    - xǔduō hànzì dōu yǒu cǎo.
    - Many Chinese characters have 艹.
### 草莓(草莓) → 莓(莓) → 梅(梅)
- **definition:** plum
- **pinyin:** méi
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 每
    - měi
    - every
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines semantic 木 (mù, 'tree') to indicate a tree and phonetic 每 (měi) to provide the sound.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually connects to the tree component, suggesting it is a type of tree, while the phonetic element contributes to its pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 梅子很好吃(梅子很好吃)。
    - Méizi hěn hǎo chī.
    - Plums are very delicious.
  - 我爱梅花(我愛梅花)。
    - Wǒ ài méihuā.
    - I love plum blossoms.
### 草莓(草莓) → 莓(莓) → 梅(梅) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
### 草莓(草莓) → 莓(莓) → 梅(梅) → 每(每)
- **definition:** every
- **pinyin:** měi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 每 represents the concept of 'every' or 'each', often depicted as a person (representing individual) and a line below indicating a repetitive action or inclusion.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as it conveys the idea of every individual represented by the person being included in a group.
- **examples:**
  - 每个人都有权利(每個人都有權利)。
    - měi gè rén dōu yǒu quánlì.
    - Everyone has rights.
  - 每次会议都很重要(每次會議都很重要)。
    - měi cì huìyì dōu hěn zhòngyào.
    - Every meeting is important.
