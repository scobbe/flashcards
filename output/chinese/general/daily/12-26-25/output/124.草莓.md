- **definition:** strawberry
- **pinyin:** cǎoméi
- **components:**
  - 草
    - cǎo
    - grass; herb
  - 莓
    - méi
    - berry
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 草(草) (cǎo, "grass; herb") + 莓(莓) (méi, "berry") =
    - 草莓(草莓) (cǎoméi, "strawberry")
  - **interpretation:** The word 草莓 (cǎoméi) combines 草(草) (cǎo, "grass; herb") and 莓(莓) (méi, "berry") to describe a fruit that grows close to the ground like grass or herbs, thus the name 'strawberry'.
- **examples:**

    - Wǒ xǐhuān cǎoméi.

    - Wǒ mǎile cǎoméi, zuòchéngle měiwèi de guǒjiàng.
    - I bought strawberries and made delicious jam.
  - 这个草莓园很大(這個草莓園很大)，种满了各种各样的草莓(種滿了各種各樣的草莓)。
    - Zhège cǎoméi yuán hěn dà, zhòng mǎnle gè zhǒng gè yàng de cǎoméi.
    - This strawberry field is huge, filled with all kinds of strawberries.

    - 网(网) (wǎng, 'net') + 能(能) (néng, 'bear') =
