## measles
### shāzi
---
- **definition:** measles
- **pinyin:** shāzi
- **components:**
  - 痧
    - shā
    - rash; measles
  - 子
    - zi
    - child; small thing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 痧(痧) (shā, "rash") + 子(子) (zi, "child; small thing"), referring to measles
  - **interpretation:** The word 痧子 (shāzi) refers to measles by combining 痧(痧) (shā, "rash"), which represents the characteristic rash associated with the disease, and 子(子) (zi, "child; small thing"), indicating something small or relating to children, as measles commonly affects young children.
- **examples:**
  - 他得了痧子(他得了痧子)。
    - Tā déle shāzi.
    - He contracted measles.
  - 当孩子暴露在阳光下时(當孩子暴露在陽光下時)，痧子容易传播(痧子容易傳播)。
    - Dāng háizi bàolù zài yángguāng xià shí, shāzi róngyì chuánbò.
    - When children are exposed to sunlight, measles spreads easily.
  - 医生建议及时治疗痧子(醫生建議及時治療痧子)。
    - Yīshēng jiànyì jíshí zhìliáo shāzi.
    - The doctor advised prompt treatment for measles.
