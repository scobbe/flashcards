### measles
---
- **pinyin:** measles
- **components:**
  - 痧
    - shā
    - rash; spots; blotch
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; offspring; seed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 痧(沙) (shā, "rash") + 子(子) (zǐ, "child; offspring") — rash child
  - **interpretation:** This term describes a rash that is commonly associated with children, indicating that measles often affects younger individuals. The combination logically reflects the idea of a rash occurring in a child.
- **examples:**
  - 孩子得了痧子(孩子得了痧子)，需要及时看医生(需要及時看醫生)。
    - Hái zǐ dé le shā zǐ, xū yào jí shí kàn yī shēng.
    - The child has measles and needs to see a doctor promptly.
  - 痧子很容易传染(痧子很容易傳染)，要注意卫生(要注意衛生)。
    - Shā zǐ hěn róng yì chuán rǎn, yào zhù yì wèi shēng.
    - Measles is highly contagious, so hygiene must be observed.
### 痧子(痧子) → 痧(瘙)
- **definition:** rash; spots; blotch
- **pinyin:** shā
- **components:**
  - 病
    - bìng
    - disease
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Derived from the components representing a skin condition and discomfort.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the discomfort associated with skin conditions, conveying the idea of illness in relation to the skin.
  - **simplification:** 瘙(瘙) is simplified to 痧(瘧) through stroke reduction and component substitution, focusing on clarity.
- **examples:**
  - 我得了瘙痒的皮疹(我得了瘙癢的皮疹)。
    - Wǒ déle shāyǎng de pízhěn.
    - I have a rash that itches.
  - 这种药可以缓解皮肤的痧(這種藥可以緩解皮膚的痧)。
    - Zhè zhǒng yào kěyǐ huǎnjiě pífū de shā.
    - This medicine can relieve skin rash.
