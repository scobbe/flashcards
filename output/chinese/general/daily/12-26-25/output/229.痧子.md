## measles
### shāzi
---
- **definition:** measles
- **pinyin:** shāzi
- **components:**
  - 痧
    - shā
    - an acute rash
  - 子
    - zi
    - child; noun suffix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 痧(痧) (shā, "an acute rash") refers to a category of skin eruptions or acute conditions in traditional Chinese medicine, often associated with various illnesses. 子(子) (zi, "child; noun suffix") is a suffix that can denote a diminutive or serve as a noun marker.
  - **interpretation:** The word 痧子 (shāzi) combines 痧(痧) (shā, "an acute rash") with 子(子) (zi, "child; noun suffix") to describe a specific type of rash or skin eruption, specifically referring to diseases like measles. It indicates a condition primarily affecting children.
- **examples:**
  - 他生了痧子(他生了痧子)。
    - Tā shēng le shāzi.
    - He got measles.
  - 痧子传染得很快(痧子傳染得很快)，全村都受到了影响(全村都受到了影響)。
    - Shāzi chuánrǎn dé hěn kuài, quán cūn dōu shòudàole yǐngxiǎng.
    - The measles spread quickly, affecting the whole village.
### 痧子(痧子) → 痧(痧)
- **definition:** an acute rash
- **pinyin:** shā
- **components:**
  - 疒
    - nè
    - sickness
  - 沙
    - shā
    - sand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 疒(疒) (nè, "sickness") + 沙(沙) (shā, "sand") =
    - sickness related to rash ->
    - acute rash
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 疒(疒) (nè, "sickness") and 沙(沙) (shā, "sand") suggests a sickness that appears like sand on the skin, hence the imagery of an acute rash.
- **examples:**
  - 皮肤上出现了痧(皮膚上出現了痧)。
    - Pífū shàng chūxiàn le shā.
    - An acute rash appeared on the skin.
  - 他发现脸上长了痧(他發現臉上長了痧)，于是赶紧就医(於是趕緊就醫)。
    - Tā fāxiàn liǎn shàng zhǎng le shā, yúshì gǎnjǐn jiùyī.
    - He discovered an acute rash on his face and immediately sought medical treatment.

### 痧子(痧子) → 痧(痧) → 沙(沙)
- **definition:** sand
- **pinyin:** shā
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 少
    - shǎo
    - to lack
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") + 少(少) (shǎo, "to lack") =
    - water that lacks solids ->
    - sand
  - **interpretation:** The character 沙(shā, "sand") combines 水(shuǐ, "water") to suggest a fluid environment with 少(shǎo, "to lack") to denote scarcity or absence, representing sand's granular texture as compared to solid rock.
- **examples:**
  - 沙静静地躺在海滩上(沙靜靜地躺在海灘上)。
    - Shā jìngjìng de tǎng zài hǎitān shàng.
    - The sand lies quietly on the beach.
  - 孩子们在沙中玩耍(孩子們在沙中玩耍)，堆起一座小城堡(堆起一座小城堡)。
    - Háizimen zài shā zhōng wánshuǎ, duī qǐ yī zuò xiǎo chéngbǎo.

### 痧子(痧子) → 痧(痧) → 沙(沙) → 少(少)
- **definition:** to lack
- **pinyin:** shǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts four dots, perhaps three grains of sand, to indicate 'a few, a little' ->
    - phonetically borrowed from 小 (小) (xiǎo, 'small').
  - **interpretation:** 少(少) (shǎo, 'few') originally depicted a few grains of sand, showing quantity, with a later phonetic borrowing from 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') due to its semantic similarity.
  - **simplification:** None, traditional is the same as simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 这个城市的人少(這個城市的人少)。
    - Zhège chéngshì de rén shǎo.
    - There are few people in this city.
  - 他的时间少(他的時間少)，所以他工作效率低(所以他工作效率低)。
    - Tā de shíjiān shǎo, suǒyǐ tā gōngzuò xiàolǜ dī.
    - He has little time, so his work efficiency is low.
  - 他的经验少(他的經驗少)，因此他成长缓慢(因此他成長緩慢)。
    - Tā de jīngyàn shǎo, yīncǐ tā chéngzhǎng huǎnmàn.
    - He has little experience, therefore he grows slowly.
### 痧子(痧子) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child; noun suffix
- **pinyin:** zi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an infant swaddled in cloth ->
    - symbolizes child
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(子) (zǐ, "child") originally depicted an infant wrapped in a cloth, illustrating the concept of a young child. This symbolic representation has expanded over time to include meanings such as "seed" or "particle," aligning with the concept of beginnings.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小子成绩优异(他的小子成績優異)。
    - Tā xiǎo zǐ chéngjì yōuyì.
    - His child has excellent grades.
  - 这颗子将发芽生长(這顆子將發芽生長)。
    - Zhè kē zǐ jiāng fāyá shēngzhǎng.
    - This seed will sprout and grow.
  - 当太阳升起时(當太陽升起時)，小子在院子里欢笑(小子在院子裡歡笑)。
    - Dāng tàiyáng shēngqǐ shí, xiǎo zǐ zài yuànzi lǐ huānxiào.
    - When the sun rises, the child laughs in the courtyard.
