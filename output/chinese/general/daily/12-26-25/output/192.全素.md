## vegan
### quánsù
---
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** quánsù
- **components:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - whole; entire
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain; vegetarian; simple
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 全(全) (quán, "whole") refers to something complete or entire, and 素(素) (sù, "plain; vegetarian") originally means simple, plain, or referring to vegetarian food. In this context, 全素 describes a diet that is entirely vegetarian, better known as vegan.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 全(全) (quán, "whole") and 素(素) (sù, "plain; vegetarian") indicates a diet that is entirely plant-based, without any animal products. This reflects the concept of veganism, emphasizing a complete adherence to vegetarian principles.
- **examples:**
  - 我选择全素(我選擇全素)。
    - Wǒ xuǎnzé quánsù.
    - I choose a vegan lifestyle.
  - 他坚持全素(他堅持全素)，注重环保(注重環保)。
    - Tā jiānchí quánsù, zhùzhòng huánbǎo.
    - He adheres to a vegan lifestyle, emphasizing environmental protection.
  - 全素食物营养丰富(全素食物營養豐富)。
    - Quánsù shíwù yíngyǎng fēngfù.
    - Vegan food is nutritionally rich.

### 全素(全素) → 素(素)
- **definition:** plain; vegetarian; simple
- **pinyin:** sù
- **components:**
  - 糸(絲)
    - sī
    - silk
  - 粟
    - sù

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 糸(絲) (sī, "silk") + phonetic: 粟(粟) (sù, "millet") =
    - combining silk and millet ->
    - suggests plainness or simplicity
  - **interpretation:** The character 素 originally depicted plain, undyed silk, suggesting simplicity or purity. The phonetic element 粟(粟) (sù, "millet") supports the sound and also relates to a basic, unadorned life.
- **examples:**

    - Tā xiàngwǎng yì zhǒng sùyǎ de fēnggé.

### 全素(全素) → 素(素) → 粟(粟)
- **definition:** millet
- **pinyin:** sù
- **components:**
  - 角
    - jiǎo
    - horn
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 西
    - xī
    - west
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Oracle bone script shows seeds → 角(角) (jiǎo, "horn") for phonetic + 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") for semantic → 角 evolved to 𠧪 → further changed to 西(西) (xī, "west")
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting seeds, 粟(sù, "millet") combined phonetic and semantic elements. 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") indicates the grain aspect, while 角(角) (jiǎo) initially added sound. Over time, the original depiction evolved through several script forms to match pronunciation changes.
- **examples:**

  - 农夫把粟从田里收集(農夫把粟從田裡收集)，准备储存过冬(準備儲存過冬)。

    - The farmer collects the millet from the fields, preparing to store it for the winter.
  - 古代诗人常以粟象征丰收(古代詩人常以粟象徵豐收)，歌颂农事的喜悦(歌頌農事的喜悅)。
    - Gǔdài shīrén cháng yǐ sù xiàngzhēng fēngshōu, gēsòng nóngshì de xǐyuè.

### 全素(全素) → 素(素) → 粟(粟) → 西(西)
- **definition:** west
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a bag or basket ->
    - borrowed for phonetic value to mean 'west'
  - **interpretation:** The character 西(xī, 'west') originally depicted a bag or basket and was phonetically borrowed to represent 'west'.
