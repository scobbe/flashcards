### vegan
---
- **pinyin:** vegan
- **components:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - whole; complete; entire
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain; vegetarian; element; color
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 全(全) (quán, 'whole; complete') + 素(素) (sù, 'plain; vegetarian') — whole vegetarian
  - **interpretation:** The term reflects a diet that is entirely plant-based. It emphasizes a complete avoidance of animal products, promoting a holistic approach to vegetarianism.
- **examples:**
  - 我吃全素饮食(我吃全素飲食)，非常健康(非常健康)。
    - Wǒ chī quánsù yǐnshí, fēicháng jiànkāng.
    - I follow a vegan diet, which is very healthy.
  - 越来越多的人选择全素生活(越來越多的人選擇全素生活)。
    - Yuè lái yuè duō de rén xuǎnzé quánsù shēnghuó.
    - More and more people choose a vegan lifestyle.

### 全素(全素) → 素(素)
- **definition:** plain; vegetarian; element; color
- **pinyin:** sù
- **components:**
  - 须(須)
    - xū
    - sound, must
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk, thread
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 素 is composed of the phonetic element '须' (xū), which suggests sound, and the semantic element '糸' (mì), which relates to threads or silk. This reflects the idea of plainness or simplicity in textiles and materials.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a sound element and a visual element related to silk creates a sense of simplicity and purity, as silk is often associated with fine fabrics that can also have a plain or basic style.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢素食(我喜歡素食)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān sùshí.
    - I like vegetarian food.
  - 他的素描很美(他的素描很美)。
    - Tā de sùmiáo hěn měi.
    - His sketch is beautiful.
  - 这件素衣很舒服(這件素衣很舒服)。
    - Zhè jiàn sù yī hěn shūfú.
    - This plain dress is very comfortable.
