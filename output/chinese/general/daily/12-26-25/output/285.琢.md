### carve; polish (jade)
---
- **pinyin:** carve; polish (jade)
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 卓
    - zhuó
    - outstanding
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of 玉 (jade) indicating its relation to jade and the sound component 卓 (zhuó), which provides pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The character denotes the act of shaping or refining jade into something exquisite, using high-quality materials.
- **examples:**
  - 他正在琢磨这块玉石(他正在琢磨這塊玉石)，希望能制作出精美的作品(希望能製作出精美的作品)！
  - 老师教我们如何琢玉(老師教我們如何琢玉)。
    - Lǎoshī jiào wǒmen rúhé zhuó yù.
    - The teacher teaches us how to carve jade.
### 琢(琢) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem.
  - **interpretation:** The character symbolizes jade, reflecting its importance and usage in ritualistic and decorative contexts, much like the cong itself.
- **examples:**
  - 这件玉器很美丽(這件玉器很美麗)。
    - Zhè jiàn yùqì hěn měilì.
    - This piece of jade is beautiful.
  - 我想买玉(我想買玉)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yù.
    - I want to buy jade.
### 琢(琢) → 卓(卓)
- **definition:** outstanding
- **pinyin:** zhuó
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 卓 represents a concept of being elevated or outstanding. Its composition suggests an idea of being above the ordinary.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of standing out above others, making it intuitive for conveying the meaning of 'outstanding'.
- **examples:**
  - 他非常卓越，值得学习。(他非常卓越，值得學習。)
    - tā fēicháng zhuóyuè, zhídé xuéxí.
    - He is outstanding and worth learning from.
  - 她的表现非常卓越。(她的表現非常卓越。)
    - tā de biǎoxiàn fēicháng zhuóyuè.
    - Her performance is extremely outstanding.
