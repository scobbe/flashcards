## carve; polish (jade)
### zhuó
---
- **definition:** carve; polish (jade)
- **pinyin:** zhuó
- **components:**
  - 𤣩
    - yù
    - jade
  - 豖
    - zhù
    - castrated pig
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𤣩(𤣩) (yù, "jade") + 豖(豖) (zhù, "castrated pig") =
    - semantic element jade + phonetic element pig ->
    - polish or carve jade
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic element of jade with a phonetic component that depicts a castrated pig. The pig component, while primarily phonetic, could symbolically refer to cutting, aligning with the meaning of polishing jade.
- **examples:**
  - 工匠琢玉(工匠琢玉)，使之更加璀璨(使之更加璀璨)。
    - gōngjiàng zhuó yù, shǐ zhī gèngjiā cuǐcàn.
    - The craftsman polishes the jade, making it even more dazzling.
  - 他亲手琢玉(他親手琢玉)。
    - tā qīnshǒu zhuó yù.
    - He personally polishes the jade.
  - 在月色中琢玉(在月色中琢玉)，闪耀出古典韵味(閃耀出古典韻味)。
    - zài yuèsè zhōng zhuó yù, shǎnyào chū gǔdiǎn yùnwèi.
    - Polishing jade under the moonlight, it exudes a classic charm.
### 琢(琢) → 𤣩(𤣩)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a jade artifact
  - **interpretation:** The character 𤣩 is a pictographic representation of a jade artifact, capturing the essence of jade's importance and beauty in early Chinese culture.
- **examples:**
  - 他手中的𤣩闪烁着温润的光芒(他手中的𤣩閃爍著溫潤的光芒)。
    - Tā shǒuzhōng de yù shǎnshuò zhe wēn rùn de guāngmáng.
    - The jade in his hand glints with a gentle luster.
  - 这块𤣩色彩斑斓(這塊𤣩色彩斑斕)，传承了千年的工艺(傳承了千年的工藝)。
    - Zhè kuài yù sècǎi bānlán, chuánchéng le qiān nián de gōngyì.
    - This jade with vibrant colors has preserved a thousand years of craftsmanship.
  - 他的收藏中有一块罕见的𤣩(他的收藏中有一塊罕見的𤣩)。
    - Tā de shōucáng zhōng yǒu yī kuài hǎnjiàn de yù.
    - In his collection, there is a rare piece of jade.
### 琢(琢) → 豖(豖)
- **definition:** castrated pig
- **pinyin:** zhù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 豕(豕) (shǐ, "pig") ->
    - depicts a male pig with detached penis ->
    - indicating a castrated male pig
  - **interpretation:** The character 豖(豖) (zhù, "castrated pig") visually represents a male pig missing its reproductive organ, symbolizing castration. This representation is grounded in its oracle bone script origins and not in later folk or derivative interpretations.
- **examples:**
  - 农户特意养豖(農戶特意養豖)，以供应市场低脂肉(以供應市場低脂肉)。
    - Nónghù tèyì yǎng zhù, yǐ gōngyìng shìchǎng dīzhī ròu。
    - The farmer purposely raises castrated pigs to supply low-fat meat to the market.
  - 厨师用豖(廚師用豖)，炖汤调味(燉湯調味)。
    - Chúshī yòng zhù, dùn tāng tiáowèi。
    - The chef uses the castrated pig to stew a broth for seasoning.
