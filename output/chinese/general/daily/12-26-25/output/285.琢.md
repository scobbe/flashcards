---
- **definition:** carve; polish (jade)
- **pinyin:** zhuó
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") + 石(石) (shí, "stone") =
    - related to working with jade ->
    - phonetic: 豆(豆) (dòu) suggests pronunciation
  - **interpretation:** The character 琢(zhuó, "carve; polish") suggests the process of shaping and refining jade, a task that involves skillful use of stone tools. The presence of 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") highlights the context of working with valuable stones, while 豆(豆) (dòu) provides a phonetic clue to the character's pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 工匠琢玉雕龙(工匠琢玉雕龍)。

  - 他不断琢玉(他不斷琢玉)，雕琢出美丽纹路(雕琢出美麗紋路)。
    - Tā bùduàn zhuó yù, diāozhuó chū měilì wénlù.
    - He constantly polishes jade, carving out beautiful patterns.
