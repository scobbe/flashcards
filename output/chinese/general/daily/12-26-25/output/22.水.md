## 水(水)
---
## 
### water
---
- **pinyin:** water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts water flowing.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the movement of water, evoking a sense of fluidity and freshness. It intuitively connects to the element it signifies, capturing the essence of water's flowing nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水很重要。
    - shuǐ hěn zhòngyào。
    - Water is very important.
  - 我想喝水。
    - wǒ xiǎng hē shuǐ。
    - I want to drink water.
  - 水是生命的源泉。
    - shuǐ shì shēngmìng de yuánquán。
    - Water is the source of life.
