## 平遥(平遙)
---
## 
### Pingyao
---
- **pinyin:** Pingyao
- **components:**
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat; level; peaceful
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo
    - distant; far away; remote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 平(平) (píng, "flat") + 遙(遙) (yáo, "distant") — flat and distant
  - **interpretation:** The name 平遥 (Píngyáo) suggests a place that is both flat and distant. This makes sense as it refers to a city known for its historical significance and geographical features.
- **examples:**
  - 我去过平遥(我去過平遙)，那里很美。
    - Wǒ qùguò Píngyáo, nàlǐ hěn měi.
    - I have been to Pingyao; it is very beautiful.
  - 平遥的古城吸引了很多游客(平遙的古城吸引了很多遊客)。
    - Píngyáo de gǔ chéng xīyǐnle hěnduō yóukè.
    - The ancient city of Pingyao attracts many tourists.
### 平遥(平遙) → 平(平)
- **definition:** flat; level; peaceful
- **pinyin:** píng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character represents a concept of being flat or level, derived from its visual depiction of evenness.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of stability and tranquility as it visually symbolizes a flat surface. This makes intuitive sense as flatness is often associated with calmness and balance.
- **examples:**
  - 这块土地很平(這塊土地很平)，适合农作(適合農作)。
    - Zhè kuài tǔdì hěn píng, shìhé nóngzuò.
    - This piece of land is flat, suitable for farming.
  - 生活要平稳(生活要平穩)，才能快乐(才能快樂)。
    - Shēnghuó yào píngwěn, cáinéng kuàilè.
    - Life needs to be peaceful to be happy.
### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙)
- **definition:** distant; far away; remote
- **pinyin:** yáo
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 尭(遙)
    - yáo
    - to stand out, high
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 辶 (walking) indicating movement or distance and the phonetic component 尭 (yáo, a surname) giving it the sound.
  - **interpretation:** This character evokes the idea of traveling a long distance since it combines the idea of movement with the concept of distance represented by the semantic component.
- **examples:**
  - 这个地方很遥远(這個地方很遙遠)。
    - Zhège dìfāng hěn yáoyuǎn.
    - This place is very far away.
  - 我们每年去海边，那里遥望大海(我們每年去海邊，那裡遙望大海)。
    - Wǒmen měinián qù hǎibiān, nàlǐ yáowàng dàhǎi.
    - We go to the seaside every year to gaze far out at the sea.
  - 他是一个遥不可及的人(他是一個遙不可及的人)。
    - Tā shì yīgè yáo bù kě jí de rén.
    - He is someone who is far from reach.
### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙) → 辶(辶)
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - xiè
    - walk
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot or to stop
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Combines 彳 (xiè, 'walk') + 止 (zhǐ, 'foot' or 'to stop') to convey the concept of movement.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively conveys movement by illustrating the act of walking with the foot and the action of stopping.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢走路(我喜歡走路)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zǒulù.
    - I like to walk.
  - 外面很冷，走快点(外面很冷，走快點)。
    - Wàimiàn hěn lěng, zǒu kuài diǎn.
    - It's cold outside, walk faster.
### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙) → 辶(辶) → 彳(彳)
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** xiè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a street intersection as seen from above from the left side of the character 行.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation of a street intersection intuitively connects to the meaning of walking or movement in various directions.
- **examples:**
  - 我在街上走(我在街上走)。
    - Wǒ zài jiē shàng zǒu.
    - I am walking on the street.
  - 他每天都散步(他每天都散步)。
    - Tā měitiān dōu sànbù.
    - He takes a walk every day.
### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙) → 辶(辶) → 止(止)

### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙) → 尭(尭)
- **definition:** to stand out, high
- **pinyin:** yáo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Simplified from 堯 (垚 → 卉) in Japanese shinjitai, retaining the meaning of standing out or being high.
  - **interpretation:** The character 尭 suggests an elevated status, reflecting its roots that connote height and prominence.
- **examples:**
  - 尭只是一种表达而已(尭只是種表達而已)。
    - yáo zhǐ shì yī zhǒng biǎodá éryǐ.
    - Yáo is just a form of expression.
  - 这个词出现在古书中(這個詞出現在古書中)。
    - zhège cí chūxiàn zài gǔshū zhōng.
    - This term appears in ancient texts.
