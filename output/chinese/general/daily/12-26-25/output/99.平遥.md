## Pingyao
### Píngyáo
---
- **definition:** Pingyao
- **pinyin:** Píngyáo
- **components:**
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat; peaceful
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo
    - distant; remote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 平(平) (píng, 'flat; peaceful') refers to flatness or tranquility, while 遥(遙) (yáo, 'distant; remote') suggests distance or remoteness. Together, 平遥(平遙) can describe a place known for its landscape or historical significance.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 平(平) (píng, 'flat; peaceful') and 遥(遙) (yáo, 'distant; remote') suggests a place that is tranquil and somewhat removed from bustling urban centers, reflecting the geography and historical heritage of Pingyao, a well-preserved ancient city in China.
- **examples:**
  - 我去平遥旅游(我去平遙旅遊)。
    - Wǒ qù Píngyáo lǚyóu.
    - I am going to visit Pingyao.
  - 平遥古城风景优美(平遙古城風景優美)，吸引了许多游客(吸引了許多遊客)。
    - Píngyáo gǔchéng fēngjǐng yōuměi, xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Pingyao Ancient City has beautiful scenery, attracting many visitors.
