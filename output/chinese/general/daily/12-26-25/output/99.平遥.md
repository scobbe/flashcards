## Pingyao
### Píngyáo
---
- **traditional:** 平遙
- **definition:** Pingyao
- **pinyin:** Píngyáo
- **components:**
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat; peaceful
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo
    - distant; remote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 平 (píng, 'flat; peaceful') refers to flatness or tranquility, while 遥(遙) (yáo, 'distant; remote') suggests distance or remoteness. Together, 平遥(遙) can describe a place known for its landscape or historical significance.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 平 (píng, 'flat; peaceful') and 遥(遙) (yáo, 'distant; remote') suggests a place that is tranquil and somewhat removed from bustling urban centers, reflecting the geography and historical heritage of Pingyao, a well-preserved ancient city in China.
- **examples:**
  - 我去平遥(遙)旅游(遊)。
    - Wǒ qù Píngyáo lǚyóu.
    - I am going to visit Pingyao.
  - 平遥(遙)古城风(風)景优(優)美，吸引了许(許)多游(遊)客。
    - Píngyáo gǔchéng fēngjǐng yōuměi, xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Pingyao Ancient City has beautiful scenery, attracting many visitors.
### 平( )遥(遙) → 平
- **definition:** flat; peaceful
- **pinyin:** píng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted a flat, level object ->
    - evolved to signify both 'flat' and 'peaceful' due to the association of flatness with stability
  - **interpretation:** The character 平 symbolizes the concept of flatness, which naturally extends to ideas of equality and peace because a flat surface is stable and calm. This character captures the intuition that levelness in both physical and metaphorical senses promotes harmony.
- **examples:**
  - 湖面平静(靜)。
    - Hú miàn píng jìng.
    - The surface of the lake is calm.
  - 这(這)片土地平坦，适(適)合耕作。
    - Zhè piàn tǔ dì píng tǎn, shì hé gēng zuò.
    - This piece of land is flat and suitable for farming.
  - 人们(們)向往平和。
    - Rén men xiàng wǎng píng hé.
    - People yearn for a peaceful state.
### 平( )遥(遙) → 遥(遙)
- **traditional:** 遙
- **definition:** distant; remote
- **pinyin:** yáo
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - movement
  - 杳
    - yǎo
    - hidden
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 辶 (chuò, 'movement') + phonetic: 杳 (yǎo, 'hidden') =
    - signifies moving away into obscurity or distance ->
    - 'distant; remote'
  - **interpretation:** The character 遥(遙) (yáo, 'distant; remote') combines the idea of movement with the element 杳 (yǎo, 'hidden'), suggesting movement that leads to a place so far it's hidden from view.
  - **simplification:** 遥(遙) retains 杳 while the traditional 辶 ('movement') remains unchanged, maintaining both meaning and phonetic elements for clarity.
- **examples:**
  - 那遥远(遙遠)的山川藏着(著)秘密。
    - Nà yáoyuǎn de shānchuān cángzhe mìmì。
    - Those distant mountains conceal secrets.
  - 我们(們)登上高峰，远(遠)望遥远(遙遠)村庄(莊)。
    - Wǒmen dēng shàng gāofēng, yuǎn wàng yáoyuǎn cūnzhuāng。
    - We climbed the high peak and gazed at the distant village.
### 平( )遥(遙) → 遥(遙) → 辶(辵)

- **definition:** movement
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 辵
    - chuò
    - walk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** combining form
  - **description:**
    - Simplified form of 辵 (chuò, "walk") ->
    - used as a radical in conjunction with other components
  - **interpretation:** 辶(辵) (chuò, "walk") is derived by simplifying 能(mp) (néng, "self-reliant") structure from the character 辵. This simplified form emphasizes the act of motion or walking and is widely used as a radical in various motion-related characters.
- **examples:**
  - 学习汉(學習漢)字时(時)，辶作为(為)部首出现(現)。
    - Xuéxí hànzì shí(學習漢字時)，chuò zuòwéi bùshǒu chūxiàn(辶作為部首出現)。
    - When studying Chinese characters, '辶' appears as a radical.
  - 许(許)多表示走路的字，都含有辶这(這)一部分。
    - Xǔduō biǎoshì zǒulù de zì(許多表示走路的字)，dōu hányǒu chuò zhè yī bùfen(都含有辶這一部分)。
    - Many characters that denote walking contain the radical '辶'.
  - 古人写(寫)字时(時)，常利用辶表达(達)行走之意。
    - Gǔrén xiězì shí(古人寫字時)，cháng lìyòng chuò biǎodá xíngzǒu zhī yì(常利用辶表達行走之意)。
    - When ancient people wrote characters, they often used '辶' to express the idea of walking.
### 平( )遥(遙) → 遥(遙) → 辶(辵) → 辵

- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì

    - zhǐ

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 彳 (chì, "walk") + 止 (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop") ->
    - 𣥆 ->
    - stylized as 龰 ->

- **examples:**

### 平( )遥(遙) → 遥(遙) → 杳 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来临时(來臨時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒满(灑滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.
