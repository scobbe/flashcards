- **definition:** Pingyao
- **pinyin:** Píngyáo
- **components:**

    - píng
    - flat; level; smooth
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 平(平) (píng, "flat") indicates a sense of level or smoothness, while 遥(遙) (yáo, "far") suggests a sense of distance or expanse. Together, they form the name of a place, Pingyao.
  - **interpretation:** The name 平遥(平遙) (Píngyáo) suggests a place that is level and expansive, reflecting the topographical or cultural character of the location. It is the name of an ancient city in Shanxi, China, known for its well-preserved city walls.

    - Wǒ jìhuà míngnián qù Píngyáo lǚxíng, pǐncháng dāngdì měishí.
