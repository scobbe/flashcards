### Wánglǎojí
---
- **definition:** Wong Lo Kat (beverage brand)
- **pinyin:** Wánglǎojí
- **components:**
  - 王

  - **type:** eponymic compound

    - This beverage is very popular in Guangdong.
  - 由于商标纠纷(由於商標糾紛)，内地产品改名为加多宝(內地産品改名為加多寶)。
    - Yóuyú shāngbiāo jiūfēn, nèidì chǎnpǐn gǎimíng wéi Jiāduōbǎo.

### 王老吉(王老吉) → 王(王)
- **definition:** Wang (surname); king
- **pinyin:** Wáng
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Pictogram of a ritual (jade) axe kept near the throne for ceremonies; symbol of royal power.
