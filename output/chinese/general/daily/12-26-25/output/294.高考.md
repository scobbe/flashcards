### national college entrance exam
---
- **pinyin:** national college entrance exam
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall; elevated
  - 考
    - kǎo
    - test; exam; to consider
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高(高) (gāo, 'high') + 考(考) (kǎo, 'test') — high test
  - **interpretation:** The term reflects the significance of this exam as a rigorous and pivotal assessment for high school students seeking university admission in China. Thus, it is fittingly named as a 'high test'.
- **examples:**
  - 我每年都为高考努力学习(我每年都為高考努力學習)。
    - Wǒ měi nián dōu wèi gāo kǎo nǔlì xuéxí.
    - I work hard every year for the national college entrance exam.
  - 高考是中国学生的重要考试(高考是中國學生的重要考試)。
    - Gāo kǎo shì Zhōngguó xuéshēng de zhòngyào kǎoshì.
    - The college entrance exam is an important test for Chinese students.
### 高考(高考) → 高(高)
- **definition:** high; tall; elevated
- **pinyin:** gāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tall building.
  - **interpretation:** The character gives the impression of vertical height, representing a tall structure. This evokes the concept of being high or elevated.
- **examples:**
  - 这座建筑真高(這座建築真高)！
    - Zhè zuò jiànzhù zhēn gāo！
    - This building is really tall!
  - 他比我高(他比我高)。
    - Tā bǐ wǒ gāo.
    - He is taller than me.
  - 云彩在高空中飘(雲彩在高空中飄)。
    - Yúncǎi zài gāokōng zhōng piāo.
    - The clouds float in the high sky.
### 高考(高考) → 考(考)
- **definition:** test; exam; to consider
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **components:**
  - 耳
    - ěr
    - ear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 考 is comprised of the radical 耳 (ěr, 'ear') and the component 考, which depicts ideas of consideration and examination.
  - **interpretation:** Examining and testing require careful consideration, metaphorically represented by the ear for listening and taking in information. This connection emphasizes the thoughtful process involved in assessments.
- **examples:**
  - 我考试(我考試)，要好好准备(要好好準備)。
    - Wǒ kǎoshì, yào hǎohǎo zhǔnbèi.
    - I have an exam, and I need to prepare well.
  - 他在考虑(他在考慮)这项提案(這項提案)。
    - Tā zài kǎolǜ zhè xiàng tí'àn.
    - He is considering this proposal.
