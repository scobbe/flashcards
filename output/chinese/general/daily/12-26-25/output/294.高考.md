## national college entrance exam
### gāokǎo
---
- **definition:** national college entrance exam
- **pinyin:** gāokǎo
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall; above average; loud
  - 考
    - kǎo
    - exam; test; to verify; to check
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高(高) (gāo, "high") + 考(考) (kǎo, "exam") combines to form 高考(高考) (gāokǎo, "national college entrance exam").
  - **interpretation:** 高考(高考) (gāokǎo, "national college entrance exam") is a significant assessment in China that determines university entry for students. 高(高) (gāo, "high") indicates the level or importance of the exam, while 考(考) (kǎo, "exam") explicitly states its nature as a test.
- **examples:**
  - 高考决定命运(高考決定命運)。
    - Gāokǎo juédìng mìngyùn.
    - The national college entrance exam determines one's destiny.
  - 学生努力复习(學生努力複習)，希望高考取得好成绩(希望高考取得好成績)。
    - Xuéshēng nǔlì fùxí, xīwàng gāokǎo qǔdé hǎo chéngjì.
    - Students study hard, hoping to achieve good results in the national college entrance exam.
### 高考(高考) → 高(高)
- **definition:** high; tall; above average; loud
- **pinyin:** gāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tall building
  - **interpretation:** The character 高(gāo, "high; tall") visually represents a tall structure or building, symbolizing height or elevation. Unlike 京(jīng, "capital"), it is straightforwardly a representation of height.
- **examples:**
  - 这座山高(這座山高)。
    - Zhè zuò shān gāo.
    - This mountain is high.
  - 那棵树长得高(那棵樹長得高)，比其他树高很多(比其他樹高很多)。
    - Nà kē shù zhǎng de gāo, bǐ qítā shù gāo hěn duō.
    - That tree grows tall, much taller than the other trees.
  - 飞机在高空飞行(飛機在高空飛行)。
    - Fēijī zài gāokōng fēixíng.
    - The airplane flies in high altitude.

- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **components:**
  - 老
    - lǎo
    - old
  - 丂
    - kǎo

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 老(老) (lǎo, "old") + 丂(丂) (kǎo, phonetic component) =
    - old person giving an exam ->
    - exam, test
  - **interpretation:** The character 考(考) (kǎo, "exam") combines the semantic element 老(老) (lǎo, "old"), hinting at wisdom or authority, with the phonetic component 丂(丂) (kǎo), signifying the act of verifying or testing knowledge.
