## national college entrance exam
### gāokǎo
---
- **definition:** national college entrance exam
- **pinyin:** gāokǎo
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall; above average
  - 考
    - kǎo
    - test; exam; inspect
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高考 (gāokǎo) is a combination of 高(高) (gāo, "high") and 考(考) (kǎo, "test"). This refers to the "high-level test" that is the national college entrance examination in China.
  - **interpretation:** 高(高) (gāo, "high") refers to the advanced or upper level, while 考(考) (kǎo, "test") refers to examination. Together, 高考 (gāokǎo) specifically signifies the important and challenging test students must take to enter higher education institutions.
- **examples:**
  - 高中毕业后(高中畢業後)，他开始准备高考(他開始準備高考)。
    - Gāozhōng bìyè hòu, tā kāishǐ zhǔnbèi gāokǎo.
    - After graduating high school, he started preparing for the national college entrance exam.
  - 父母反复讨论高考政策(父母反覆討論高考政策)，学生则认真复习考试内容(學生則認真複習考試內容)。
    - Fùmǔ fǎnfù tǎolùn gāokǎo zhèngcè, xuéshēng zé rènzhēn fùxí kǎoshì nèiróng.
    - Parents repeatedly discuss college entrance exam policies, while students diligently review exam content.
  - 很多学生为了高考放弃了假期(很多學生為了高考放棄了假期)。
    - Hěn duō xuéshēng wèile gāokǎo fàngqìle jiàqī.
    - Many students give up their holidays for the national college entrance exam.
### 高考(高考) → 高(高)
- **definition:** high; tall; above average
- **pinyin:** gāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tall building, representing the concept of height.
  - **interpretation:** The character 高(gāo, "high") visually resembles a tall building, which intuitively conveys the meaning of height or tallness.
- **examples:**
  - 他很高(他很高)。
    - Tā hěn gāo.
    - He is very tall.
  - 树很高(樹很高)，房子也高(房子也高)。
    - Shù hěn gāo, fángzi yě gāo.
    - The tree is tall, and the house is high as well.
  - 山很高(山很高)，云彩在山顶飘过(雲彩在山頂飄過)。
    - Shān hěn gāo, yúncǎi zài shāndǐng piāoguò.
    - The mountain is high, and the clouds drift over its peak.
### 高考(高考) → 考(考)
- **definition:** test; exam; inspect
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **components:**
  - 老
    - lǎo
    - old
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - breath
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 老(老) (lǎo, "old") + 丂(丂) (kǎo, "breath") =
    - elder's breath guiding ->
    - inspect, test
  - **interpretation:** The character 考(kǎo) combines 老(老) (lǎo, "old") as a semantic element suggesting experience or wisdom with 丂(丂) (kǎo, "breath") as a phonetic component. This reflects the notion of examining or testing, akin to an elder using their experience to inspect or test.
- **examples:**
  - 他要考高等数学(他要考高等數學)。
    - Tā yào kǎo gāoděng shùxué.
    - He is going to take the advanced mathematics exam.
  - 老师考查作业(老師考查作業)，以了解学生的学习情况(以了解學生的學習情況)。
    - Lǎoshī kǎo chá zuòyè, yǐ liǎojiě xuéshēng de xuéxí qíngkuàng.
    - The teacher inspects the homework to understand the students' learning progress.
  - 他们忙着准备明天的考试(他們忙著準備明天的考試)。
    - Tāmen mángzhe zhǔnbèi míngtiān de kǎoshì.
    - They are busy preparing for tomorrow's exam.

### 高考(高考) → 考(考) → 丂(丂)
- **definition:** breath
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Conflation of several characters
  - **description:**
    - 丂(丂) (kǎo, "branch") may combine elements from wind instrument 竽 and exhaled air 吁 ->
    - depiction of air flow or sigh
  - **interpretation:** The character 丂(kǎo, "branch") is likely a fusion of earlier forms related to sound or air, capturing notions linked to exhaling or air movement. Its components suggest both physical breath and sound-related elements.
- **examples:**
  - 大树上的丂很细(大樹上的丂很細)。
    - Dà shù shàng de kǎo hěn xì.
    - The branch on the big tree was very thin.
  - 园中老树分出许多丂(園中老樹分出許多丂)，相互交错(相互交錯)。
    - Yuán zhōng lǎo shù fēn chū xǔ duō kǎo, xiāng hù jiāo cuò.
    - In the garden, the old trees branched out many limbs that interwove with each other.
  - 宋代诗中用丂寓意连绵不断(宋代詩中用丂寓意連綿不斷)。
    - Sòng dài shī zhōng yòng kǎo yù yì lián mián bù duàn.
    - In Song Dynasty poetry, the branch was used as a metaphor for unbroken continuity.
