- **pinyin:** vegan
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure; clear; unmixed; unadulterated
  - 素食
    - sùshí
    - vegetarian food; plant-based diet; vegetarian cuisine; dietary restriction
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person; -er (suffix for people); one who; agent
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 纯(純) (chún, 'pure') + 素食(素食) (sùshí, 'vegetarian food') + 者(者) (zhě, 'person') — pure vegetarian person
  - **interpretation:** A 纯素食者(純素食者) (chún sùshí zhě) is a person who strictly eats only plant-based food without any animal products. This term makes sense as it highlights the purity of the diet and the commitment to vegetarianism.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一个纯素食者(她是一個純素食者)，从未吃过任何动物产品。
    - tā shì yī gè chún sùshí zhě, cóng wèi chī guò rènhé dòngwù chǎnpǐn.
    - She is a vegan and has never eaten any animal products.
  - 越来越多的人转向纯素食者(越來越多的人轉向純素食者)，以提高健康和环保意识。
    - yuè lái yuè duō de rén zhuǎn xiàng chún sùshí zhě, yǐ tígāo jiànkāng hé huánbǎo yìshí.
    - More and more people are turning to veganism to improve health and environmental awareness.
