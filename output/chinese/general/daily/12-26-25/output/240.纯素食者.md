## vegan
### chúnsùshízhě
---
- **traditional:** 純素食者
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** chúnsùshízhě
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure; simple
  - 素
    - sù
    - vegetarian; simple; plain
  - 食
    - shí
    - food; eat
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person who does something; -er
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 纯(純) (chún, "pure") + 素(sù, "vegetarian; simple; plain") + 食(shí, "food") + 者 (zhě, "person who does something") =
    - a person who adheres to a pure form of a vegetarian diet, namely veganism.
  - **interpretation:** The term 纯(純)素食者 (chúnsùshízhě) describes a person who chooses to follow a diet that excludes all animal products, including meat, dairy, and eggs, thus 'purely' consuming plant-based foods. This reflects a dedication to a lifestyle that emphasizes the consumption of plants in their simplest form, associated with ethical or health considerations.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位纯(純)素食者。
    - Tā shì yī wèi chún sù shí zhě.
    - He is a vegan.
  - 纯(純)素食者多追求健康，蔬菜水果丰(豐)富美味。
    - Chún sù shí zhě duō zhuīqiú jiànkāng, shūcài shuǐguǒ fēngfù měiwèi.
    - Vegans often pursue health, and their vegetables and fruits are rich and tasty.
  - 选择纯(選擇純)素食者生活方式，他追求自然与宁静(與寧靜)。
    - Xuǎnzé chún sù shí zhě shēnghuó fāngshì, tā zhuīqiú zìrán yǔ níngjìng.
    - By choosing a vegan lifestyle, he seeks nature and tranquility.
