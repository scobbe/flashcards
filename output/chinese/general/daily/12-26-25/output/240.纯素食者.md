## vegan
### chúnsùshízhě
---
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** chúnsùshízhě
- **components:**
  - 纯素(純素)
    - chún sù
    - pure vegetarian
  - 食
    - shí
    - food; meal
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person; one who
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 纯(純) (chún, "pure") + 素(sù, "vegetarian") + 食(shí, "food") + 者(zhě, "person") =
    - a person who eats vegan food.
  - **interpretation:** The word 纯素食者 is a logical composition. It describes a person 者(者) (zhě, "person") whose diet 食(食) (shí, "food") is 素(素) (sù, "vegetarian") in a 纯(純) (chún, "pure") form, meaning strictly plant-based or vegan.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位纯素食者(他是一位純素食者)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi chúnsùshízhě.
    - He is a vegan.
  - 许多纯素食者(許多純素食者)，相信健康饮食(相信健康飲食)。
    - Xǔduō chúnsùshízhě, xiāngxìn jiànkāng yǐnshí.
    - Many vegans believe in a healthy diet.
  - 作为纯素食者(作為純素食者)，她喜欢探索植物性食品(她喜歡探索植物性食品)。
    - Zuòwéi chúnsùshízhě, tā xǐhuān tànsuǒ zhíwùxìng shípǐn.
    - As a vegan, she enjoys exploring plant-based foods.
### 纯素食者(純素食者) → 纯素(純素)
- **definition:** pure vegetarian
- **pinyin:** chún sù
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure; clean; unmixed; solely
  - 素
    - sù
    - element; plain; white; vegetable-based food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 纯(純) (chún, "pure") + 素(素) (sù, "element; plain; vegetable-based food") together emphasize the idea of purely vegetable-based or plant-originating, hence 'vegan.'
  - **interpretation:** The word combines the idea of 'pure' with 'plain' or 'vegetable-based,' underscoring the concept of a diet consisting solely of plant-based elements, which aligns perfectly with the meaning of 'vegan.'
  - **simplification:** 纯→純
- **examples:**
  - 我享受纯素饮食(我享受純素飲食)。
    - Wǒ xiǎngshòu chúnsù yǐnshí.
    - I enjoy a vegan diet.
  - 她选择了纯素生活(她選擇了純素生活)，坚持健康理念(堅持健康理念)。
    - Tā xuǎnzé le chúnsù shēnghuó, jiānchí jiànkāng lǐniàn.
    - She has chosen a vegan lifestyle and adheres to healthy principles.
  - 这家餐厅提供纯素餐点(這家餐廳提供純素餐點)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng chúnsù cāndiǎn.
    - This restaurant offers vegan dishes.
### 纯素食者(純素食者) → 纯素(純素) → 纯(純)
- **definition:** pure; clean; unmixed; solely
- **pinyin:** chún
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 屯
    - tún
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") + phonetic: 屯(屯) (tún) =
    - pure silk ->
    - pure
  - **interpretation:** 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") gives the semantic idea of purity or cleanness, relating to the texture or quality of silk, while 屯(屯) (tún) serves as a phonetic component indicating the character's pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** 純(純) was simplified to 纯(純), with 屯(屯) (tún) being retained, while 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") was simplified to 纟(纟), a three-stroke cursive form that retains semantic meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 牛奶纯(牛奶純)。
    - Niúnǎi chún.
    - The milk is pure.
  - 这药是纯(這藥是純)，没有添加剂(沒有添加劑)。
    - Zhè yào shì chún, méiyǒu tiānjiājì.
    - This medicine is pure and contains no additives.

### 纯素食者(純素食者) → 者(者)
- **definition:** person; one who
- **pinyin:** zhě
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a sugarcane with full stems, with some points around (either leaves or drops of sweet liquid) and an open and smiling mouth at the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a sugarcane plant, capturing its essence through the image of a stalk with leaves or droplets and a mouth, possibly representing sweetness. It was later phonetically borrowed for more abstract meanings like 'one who' or '-er'.
- **examples:**
  - 勇敢者无惧(勇敢者無懼)。
    - yǒnggǎn zhě wú jù.
    - The brave one is fearless.
  - 善良者受人尊敬(善良者受人尊敬)。
    - shànliáng zhě shòu rén zūnjìng.
    - The kind-hearted one is respected by others.
  - 明智者总能发现机会(明智者總能發現機會)，勤劳者必有所获(勤勞者必有所獲)。
    - míngzhì zhě zǒng néng fāxiàn jīhuì, qínláo zhě bì yǒu suǒ huò.
    - The wise one always finds opportunities, and the diligent one always reaps rewards.
