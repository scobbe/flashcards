## 
### ocean; foreign
---
- **pinyin:** ocean; foreign
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 洋 is a compound of the water radical and the character 羊 (yáng, "sheep"). The water radical suggests a connection to the ocean, while the sheep component relates to the idea of something foreign or exotic, as sheep are often associated with pastoral lands.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the image of vast waters associated with foreign concepts, linking the sea's openness to new experiences.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去海洋公园(我們去海洋公園)。
    - Wǒmen qù hǎiyáng gōngyuán.
    - We are going to the ocean park.
  - 这是一个洋人(這是一個洋人)。
    - Zhè shì yīgè yángrén.
    - This is a foreigner.
  - 洋葱(洋蔥)很好吃。
    - Yángcōng hěn hǎochī.
    - Onions are very delicious.
### 洋(洋) → 氵(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents water in its flowing form, emphasizing its liquid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
### 洋(洋) → 羊(羊)
- **definition:** sheep
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a sheep, which is easily recognizable with its distinct shape.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles a sheep, evoking its form and features.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢羊。(我喜歡羊。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yáng.
    - I like sheep.
  - 羊肉很好吃。(羊肉很好吃。)
    - Yángròu hěn hǎo chī.
    - Mutton is very delicious.
