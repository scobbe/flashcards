## 江山(江山)
---
## 
### landscape; country
---
- **pinyin:** landscape; country
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river; stream; watercourse; river system
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain; hill; peak; range
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") + 山(山) (shān, "mountain") — river mountain
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 江(江) (jiāng, "river") and 山(山) (shān, "mountain") evokes a picturesque natural landscape, embodying both natural beauty and the essence of the country itself. It reflects the geographical features that characterize a nation's scenery.
- **examples:**
  - 江山如画(江山如畫)，我喜欢这里(我喜歡這裡)。
    - jiāngshān rúhuà, wǒ xǐhuān zhèlǐ.
    - The landscape is like a painting, I like this place.
  - 在这片江山(在這片江山)里生活很幸福(裡生活很幸福)。
    - zài zhè piàn jiāngshān lǐ shēnghuó hěn xìngfú.
    - Living in this country is very happy.
### 江山(江山) → 江(江)
- **definition:** river; stream; watercourse; river system
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water with waves symbolizing a river.
  - **interpretation:** The image of flowing water evokes the constant movement and life associated with rivers.
- **examples:**
  - 长江(長江)
    - Chángjiāng
    - Yangtze River.
  - 这条江很美(這條江很美)
    - Zhè tiáo jiāng hěn měi
    - This river is very beautiful.
  - 他的家在江边(他的家在江邊)
    - Tā de jiā zài jiāng biān
    - His home is by the river.
### 江山(江山) → 山(山)
- **definition:** mountain; hill; peak; range
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three mountain peaks.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation of three peaks captures the essence of a mountain, symbolizing height and majesty.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We are going to climb the mountain.
  - 山上有很多树。
    - Shān shàng yǒu hěn duō shù.
    - There are many trees on the mountain.
