## landscape; country
### jiāngshān
---
- **definition:** landscape; country
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") and 山(山) (shān, "mountain") together create the meaning of the country's natural landscape or the country as a whole.
  - **interpretation:** 江山(江山) (jiāngshān, "landscape; country") metaphorically represents the country or nation by highlighting its natural features of rivers and mountains, which have historically been pivotal in defining and defending territories in China.
- **examples:**
  - 江山如此多娇(江山如此多嬌)。
    - Jiāngshān rúcǐ duō jiāo.
    - The landscape is so enchanting.
  - 他保护江山(他保護江山)，像守护自己的命根子(像守護自己的命根子)。
    - Tā bǎohù jiāngshān, xiàng shǒuhù zìjǐ de mìnggēnzǐ.
    - He protects his country as if guarding his own lifeline.
### 江山(江山) → 江(江)
- **definition:** river
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 工(工) (gōng, from its Old Chinese pronunciation) =
    - water-related meaning reinforced by phonetic component ->
    - river
  - **interpretation:** The character 江(江) (jiāng, "river") combines the water radical 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") with the phonetic component 工(工) (gōng). This combination signifies a large body of water, fitting the meaning "river."
- **examples:**
  - 这条江水流湍急(這條江水流湍急)。
    - Zhè tiáo jiāng shuǐ liú tuān jí.
    - This river flows rapidly.
  - 江面上漂浮着渔船(江面上飄浮著漁船)。
    - Jiāng miàn shàng piāo fú zhe yú chuán.
    - Fishing boats float on the river surface.
  - 夜晚月光映在江上(夜晚月光映在江上)。
    - Yè wǎn yuè guāng yìng zài jiāng shàng.
    - At night, the moonlight reflects on the river.
### 江山(江山) → 江(江) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源(水是生命之源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉字中偏旁氵(漢字中偏旁氵)，代表水(代表水)。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明(河水清澈透明)，鱼儿欢快戏水(魚兒歡快戲水)。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.

### 江山(江山) → 江(江) → 工(工)
- **definition:** work
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bladed tool to plow the ground and to mark a border, or a carpenter's square
  - **interpretation:** 工(工) (gōng, "work") represents either a tool used in agriculture for marking or a carpenter's square, reflecting its connection to traditional manual labor and craftsmanship.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天下午去做工(他每天下午去做工)。
    - Tā měitiān xiàwǔ qù zuò gōng.

### 江山(江山) → 山(山)
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three mountain peaks
  - **interpretation:** The character 山(shān, "mountain") visually represents the concept of a mountain using three vertical lines that suggest peaks. This depiction is consistent with early forms found in Oracle Bone Script, emphasizing the notion of natural elevation associated with mountains.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We go climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山让人惊叹(那座大山讓人驚嘆)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān ràng rén jīngtàn.
    - That mountain astonishes people.
  - 远处的山隐约在云中(遠處的山隱約在雲中)，令人心旷神怡(令人心曠神怡)。
    - Yuǎnchù de shān yǐnyuē zài yún zhōng, lìng rén xīnkuàng shényí.
    - The distant mountain fades into the clouds, leaving one in a state of peace.
