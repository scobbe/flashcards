## landscape; country
### jiāngshān
---
- **definition:** landscape; country
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") and 山(山) (shān, "mountain") combine to convey the concepts of landscape or country. Rivers and mountains are essential elements of a nation's geographical makeup and often symbolize the natural beauty and expanse of a country.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 江(江) (jiāng, "river") and 山(山) (shān, "mountain") reflects the fundamental elements that shape the landscape and identity of a country.
- **examples:**
  - 江山如此多娇(江山如此多嬌)。
    - Jiāngshān rúcǐ duōjiāo.
    - The country is so beautiful.
  - 守护江山(守護江山)，传承文化(傳承文化)。
    - Shǒuhù jiāngshān, chuánchéng wénhuà.
    - Protecting the country and preserving its culture.
### 江山(江山) → 江(江)
- **definition:** river
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 工(工) (gōng, "work") =
    - concept of flowing water ->
    - river
  - **interpretation:** The character 江(江) (jiāng, "river") combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") for its association with water and liquid, with 工(工) (gōng, "work") to suggest the sound. Together, they evoke the sense of a flowing water body, which fits the meaning 'river.'
- **examples:**
  - 江水浩荡(江水浩蕩)。
    - Jiāng shuǐ hàodàng.
    - The river's water is vast and majestic.
  - 秋风起时(秋風起時)，江面雾气蒙蒙(江面霧氣濛濛)。
    - Qiū fēng qǐ shí, jiāng miàn wù qì méng méng.
    - When the autumn wind rises, mist shrouds the river's surface.
  - 旅人徜徉于江边(旅人徜徉於江邊)，思绪随流水飘远(思緒隨流水飄遠)。
    - Lǚrén chángyáng yú jiāng biān, sīxù suí liúshuǐ piāoyuǎn.
    - The traveler strolls by the riverside, his thoughts drifting away with the flowing water.

### 江山(江山) → 江(江) → 工(工)
- **definition:** work
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bladed tool to plow ground or a carpenter’s square
  - **interpretation:** The character 工(gōng, "work") originates from the depiction of a tool used either for agriculture, like a plow, or for carpentry, such as a square, relating to the concept of 'work' or 'labor'.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天去工厂(他每天去工廠)，认真做工(認真做工)。

  - 她喜欢与工友讨论技术(她喜歡與工友討論技術)。
    - Tā xǐhuān yǔ gōngyǒu tǎolùn jìshù.
    - She enjoys discussing technology with her coworkers.
  - 老板鼓励工人多做工(老闆鼓勵工人多做工)，以提高效率(以提高效率)。
    - Lǎobǎn gǔlì gōngrén duō zuò gōng, yǐ tígāo xiàolǜ.

### 江山(江山) → 山(山)
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Mountain peaks depicted by three strokes → represents a mountain
  - **interpretation:** 山(山) (shān, 'mountain') visually depicts the silhouette of a mountain range. The character gives an intuitive sense of elevation and jagged terrain through the three peaks.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We are going to climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山实在壮观(那座大山實在壯觀)，让人心生敬畏(讓人心生敬畏)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān shí zài zhuàngguān, ràng rén xīnshēng jìngwèi.
    - That mountain is truly magnificent, evoking awe.
  - 古人登山不仅是为了观景(古人登山不僅是為了觀景)，更是在寻找心灵的寄托(更是在尋找心靈的寄托)。
    - Gǔrén dēng shān bù jǐn shì wèi le guānjǐng, gèng shì zài xúnzhǎo xīnlíng de jìtuō.
    - In ancient times, climbing mountains was not only for enjoying the view but also for seeking spiritual solace.
