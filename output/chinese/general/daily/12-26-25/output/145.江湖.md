## 江湖(江湖)
---
## 
### martial world
---
- **pinyin:** martial world
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river; river system; river basin; stream
  - 湖
    - hú
    - lake; pond; lagoon; water body
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") + 湖(湖) (hú, "lake") — river lake
  - **interpretation:** The term 江湖 represents a metaphorical place that encompasses the world of martial artists and wandering heroes. The combination of 'river' and 'lake' suggests a vast and dynamic landscape where these characters interact.
- **examples:**
  - 江湖上有很多侠客(江湖上有很多俠客)。
    - jiānghú shàng yǒu hěnduō xiá kè.
    - There are many heroes in the martial world.
  - 他在江湖中非常有名(他在江湖中非常有名)。
    - tā zài jiānghú zhōng fēicháng yǒumíng.
    - He is very famous in the martial world.
