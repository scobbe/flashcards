## martial world
### jiānghú
---
- **definition:** martial world
- **pinyin:** jiānghú
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 湖
    - hú
    - lake
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江(江) (jiāng, "river") and 湖(湖) (hú, "lake") together create a metaphorical and historical term referring to the community or world of martial artists and their activities, often outside of the regular societal rules.
  - **interpretation:** The term 江湖(江湖) (jiānghú, "martial world") evokes the imagery of vast, connected bodies of water symbolizing an expansive, unregulated realm where martial artists roam and interact.
- **examples:**
  - 他闯荡江湖(他闖蕩江湖)，经历无数风波(經歷無數風波)。
    - Tā chuǎngdàng jiānghú, jīnglì wúshù fēngbō.
    - He ventures into the martial world, experiencing countless challenges.
  - 江湖充满了恩怨情仇(江湖充滿了恩怨情仇)。
    - Jiānghú chōngmǎn le ēnyuàn qíngchóu.
    - The martial world is full of grudges and vendettas.
  - 为了生计(為了生計)，他不得不涉足江湖(他不得不涉足江湖)。
    - Wèi le shēngjì, tā bùdé bù shèzú jiānghú.
    - For the sake of livelihood, he had no choice but to step into the martial world.
