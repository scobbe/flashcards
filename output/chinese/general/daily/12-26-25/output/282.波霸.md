### bōbà
---
- **definition:** big boobs (slang)
- **pinyin:** bōbà
- **components:**
  - 波
    - bō
    - wave
  - 霸
    - bà
    - tyrant; dominant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 波(波) (bō, "wave") + 霸(霸) (bà, "tyrant; dominant") combine to create a term that suggests something large and dominant like big waves, playfully referring to large breasts.
  - **interpretation:** The word 波霸 (bōbà) uses the imagery of 'waves' (波) to evoke the notion of curves and motion, while 'tyrant' (霸) suggests something that is dominating or impressive in size.
- **examples:**
  - 她的波霸非常引人注目(她的波霸非常引人注目)。

    - Her big boobs are extremely eye-catching.

    - Tā de bōbà yǐn qǐ le dàjiā tánlùn, wǒ bùdébù chéngrèn zhè xīyǐn le hěn duō mùguāng.
    - Her big boobs sparked everyone's conversation, and I had to admit they attracted many glances.
