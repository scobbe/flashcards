## big boobs (slang)
### bōbà
---
- **definition:** big boobs (slang)
- **pinyin:** bōbà
- **components:**
  - 波
    - bō
    - wave; ripple; surge
  - 霸
    - bà
    - overlord; tyrant; large; dominant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 波 (bō, "wave") refers to a rounded or undulating shape, while 霸 (bà, "tyrant; overlord") conveys dominance or largeness in its colloquial use.
  - **interpretation:** The term 波 (bō, "wave") suggests a rounded form, and 霸 (bà, "overlord") emphasizes the large size or prominence. Together, 波霸 (bōbà) is a slang expression referring to prominent or sizable breasts.
- **examples:**
  - 电(電)影中的女主角拥(擁)有波霸美誉(譽)。
    - Diànyǐng zhōng de nǚzhǔjiǎo yǒngyǒu bōbà měiyù.
    - The movie's leading actress is celebrated for her big boobs (slang).
  - 有人调(調)侃她的波霸，表示赞赏(讚賞)其魅力。
    - Yǒurén tiáokǎn tā de bōbà, biǎoshì zànshǎng qí mèilì.
    - Some tease her for her big boobs (slang), while also admiring her charm.
  - 这(這)家时装(時裝)店常被称为(稱為)波霸聚集地。
    - Zhè jiā shízhuāngdiàn cháng bèi chēngwéi bōbà jùjí dì.
    - This boutique is often dubbed a hotspot for big boobs (slang).
### 波霸 → 波
- **definition:** wave; ripple; surge
- **pinyin:** bō
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 皮
    - pí
    - surface
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 皮 (pí, "surface") =
    - water surface ->
    - wave
  - **interpretation:** The character 波(bō, "wave") combines the water radical 氵 to indicate relation to water, with 皮 serving as the phonetic component, hinting at fluidity or a surface feature like a wave.
- **examples:**
  - 海风轻(風輕)拂时(時)，水面泛起微波。
    - Hǎi fēng qīng fú shí, shuǐ miàn fàn qǐ wēi bō.
    - When the sea breeze caresses, ripples appear on the water's surface.
  - 小船在湖中荡(蕩)漾着(著)波。
    - Xiǎo chuán zài hú zhōng dàng yàng zhe bō.
    - The small boat drifts on the lake with gentle waves.
### 波霸 → 波 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 波霸 → 波 → 皮
- **definition:** surface
- **pinyin:** pí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a surface or the skin of an animal
  - **interpretation:** The character 皮 (pí, 'surface') visually represents an animal hide or skin stretched out, emphasizing the idea of an outer covering or surface. It captures the concept of a boundary between the interior and exterior, as seen in animal skins used as material.
- **examples:**
  - 这张(這張)桌子的皮很光滑。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi de pí hěn guānghuá.
    - The surface of this table is very smooth.
  - 新鞋的皮非常柔软(軟)，适(適)合长时间(長時間)行走。
    - Xīn xié de pí fēicháng róuruǎn, shìhé cháng shíjiān xíngzǒu.
    - The surface of the new shoes is very soft and suitable for long walks.
### 波霸 → 霸
- **definition:** overlord; tyrant; large; dominant
- **pinyin:** bà
- **components:**
  - 革
    - gé
    - change
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 革 (gé, "change") + 月 (yuè, "moon") =
    - changes in moonlight ->
    - 雨 (yǔ, "rain") added ->
    - final form 霸
  - **interpretation:** The character 霸 originally depicted the concept of changes occurring with the moon, which connoted authority and dominance. The addition of 雨 (yǔ, "rain") as a phonetic component refined its association with natural influence and dominance.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢称(歡稱)霸武林。
    - Tā xǐhuān chēng bà wǔlín.
    - He enjoys dominating the martial arts world.
  - 这(這)座建筑设计(築設計)得十分霸气(氣)，引来众(來眾)人赞叹(讚歎)。
    - Zhè zuò jiànzhù shèjì dé shífēn bàqì, yǐn lái zhòng rén zàntàn.
    - The building is designed in a strikingly dominant style, attracting widespread admiration.
  - 那位霸主独(獨)自统(統)治着边(著邊)疆。
    - Nà wèi bàzhǔ dúzì tǒngzhì zhe biānjiāng.
    - That overlord rules the border alone.
### 波霸 → 霸 → 革
- **definition:** change
- **pinyin:** gé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stretched animal hide: head and tail/legs visible with hands briefly added
  - **interpretation:** The character 革 (gé, "change") originally depicted a stretched animal hide, emphasizing the transformation process involved in treatment of the hide. Over time, this visual representation came to symbolize broader transformations or changes.
- **examples:**
  - 公司需要革新，才能适应(適應)市场变(場變)化。
    - Gōngsī xūyào géxīn，cái néng shìyìng shìchǎng biànhuà。
    - The company needs to innovate in order to adapt to market changes.
  - 我们应当(們應當)革除旧习(舊習)，采纳(採納)新理念。
    - Wǒmen yīngdāng géchú jiùxí，cǎinà xīn lǐniàn。
    - We should abolish old practices and adopt new ideas.
  - 时(時)代在革，记录着(記錄著)巨变(變)。
    - Shídài zài gé，jìlù zhe jùbiàn。
    - The era is changing, recording tremendous transformations.
### 波霸 → 霸 → 月
- **definition:** moon
- **pinyin:** yuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a crescent moon
  - **interpretation:** The character 月(yuè, "moon") is a pictographic representation of a crescent moon, straightforwardly capturing the curved shape often associated with the moon's appearance. Though interchangeable with 夕 (xī, "evening") in the past, it distinctly represents the moon in this usage.
- **examples:**
  - 夜空挂着(掛著)明月。
    - Yè kōng guà zhe míng yuè.
    - The bright moon hangs in the night sky.
  - 人们赏(們賞)月，寄托(託)相思。
    - Rén men shǎng yuè, jì tuō xiāng sī.
    - People enjoy the moon, expressing their longing.
### 波霸 → 霸 → 雨
- **definition:** rain
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it ->
    - horizontal stroke added later ->
    - rain
  - **interpretation:** 雨 (yǔ, "rain") visually represents rain falling from a cloud, capturing its essential form.
- **examples:**
  - 今天雨下得很大。
    - Jīntiān yǔ xià de hěn dà.
    - It rained hard today.
  - 他忘记带伞(記帶傘)，雨却(卻)毫不留情。
    - Tā wàngjì dài sǎn, yǔ què háobù liúqíng.
    - He forgot to bring an umbrella, and the rain showed no mercy.
  - 细(細)雨如丝(絲)，轻(輕)拂着(著)花瓣。
    - Xì yǔ rú sī, qīng fú zhe huā bàn.
    - The fine rain falls like silk, gently brushing the petals.
