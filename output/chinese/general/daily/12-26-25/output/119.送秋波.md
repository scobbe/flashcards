## cast amorous glances
### sòng qiūbō
---
- **definition:** cast amorous glances
- **pinyin:** sòng qiūbō
- **components:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - send; deliver; give as a gift; see off
  - 秋波
    - qiūbō
    - autumn wave; flirtatious glance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 送(送) (sòng, "send") + 秋波(秋波) (qiūbō, "autumn wave")
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 送秋波 (sòng qiūbō) metaphorically means to cast amorous glances. 秋波(秋波) (qiūbō, "autumn wave") refers to the soft and gentle look in one's eyes, evoking the idea of a calm autumn scene or a gentle wave, often used in poetry to describe a flirtatious or enchanting gaze. By combining it with 送(送) (sòng, "send"), it creates the meaning of sending or directing such gazes toward someone.
- **examples:**
  - 她在晚会中送秋波(她在晚會中送秋波)，让他心动(讓他心動)。
    - Tā zài wǎnhuì zhōng sòng qiūbō, ràng tā xīndòng.
    - She cast amorous glances during the party, making his heart flutter.
  - 帅哥见到她便送秋波(帥哥見到她便送秋波)，悄悄表达心意(悄悄表達心意)。
    - Shuài gē jiàn dào tā biàn sòng qiūbō, qiāoqiāo biǎodá xīnyì.
    - The handsome guy, upon seeing her, cast amorous glances, subtly expressing his feelings.
  - 在音乐会间隙，他悄悄送秋波(在音樂會間隙，他悄悄送秋波)，引起了她的注意(引起了她的注意)。
    - Zài yīnyuèhuì jiànxì, tā qiāoqiāo sòng qiūbō, yǐnqǐ le tā de zhùyì.
    - During the intermission of the concert, he quietly cast amorous glances, catching her attention.
