### cast amorous glances
---
- **pinyin:** cast amorous glances
- **components:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - send; deliver; convey; escort
  - 秋波
    - qiūbō
    - autumn wave; loving glance; romantic look
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 送(送) (sòng, 'send') + 秋波(秋波) (qiūbō, 'autumn wave') — send autumn waves
  - **interpretation:** The phrase refers to the act of sending amorous glances, likening them to gentle and flowing autumn waves. This imagery conveys romance and flirtation, often used in literary contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 她对我送秋波(她對我送秋波)，我非常害羞(我非常害羞)。
  - 在那个夜晚(在那個夜晚)，我们互相送秋波(我們互相送秋波)。
  - 这些诗句中充满了送秋波(這些詩句中充滿了送秋波)。
### 送秋波(送秋波) → 送(送)
- **definition:** send; deliver; convey; escort
- **pinyin:** sòng
- **components:**
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - walk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 走 (zǒu, 'walk') and the phonetic component 送 (sòng), relating to movement and the idea of sending something or someone.
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests movement (走) towards delivering something (送), highlighting the act of sending or escorting.
- **examples:**
  - 我送你回家(我送你回家)。
    - Wǒ sòng nǐ huí jiā.
    - I will escort you home.
  - 她送了一份礼物(她送了一份禮物)。
    - Tā sòngle yī fèn lǐwù.
    - She sent a gift.
  - 我们一起送行(我們一起送行)。
    - Wǒmen yīqǐ sòngxíng.
    - We will send him off together.
### 送秋波(送秋波) → 送(送) → 走(走)
- **definition:** walk
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a running man with additional elements showing the foot.
  - **interpretation:** The character showing a running figure conveys the concept of movement in its basic form. The addition of elements depicts action and physical activity associated with running.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by changing the top component from 夭 to 土 and stylizing the bottom as 龰. The stroke count was reduced in a way that made the character easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 他正在走路(他正在走路)。
    - tā zhèngzài zǒulù
    - He is walking.
  - 我喜欢快走(我喜歡快走)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān kuài zǒu
    - I like to walk quickly.
  - 他们在公园里走(他們在公園裡走)。
    - tāmen zài gōngyuán lǐ zǒu
    - They are walking in the park.
### 送秋波(送秋波) → 秋波(秋波)
- **definition:** autumn wave; loving glance; romantic look
- **pinyin:** qiūbō
- **components:**
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn; fall; season; harvest
  - 波
    - bō
    - wave; ripples; billow; fluctuations
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 秋(秋) (qiū, "autumn") + 波(波) (bō, "wave") — autumn wave
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a romantic or affectionate glance, likening it to the gentle undulation of waves in the autumn season. The use of 'autumn' adds a sense of warmth and beauty to the idea of a glance.
- **examples:**
  - 她向我投来秋波(她向我投來秋波)，我心里雀跃不已(我心裏雀躍不已)。
    - Tā xiàng wǒ tóu lái qiūbō, wǒ xīn lǐ quèyuè bù yǐ.
    - She cast a loving glance at me, and I felt immense joy in my heart.
  - 秋波荡漾(秋波蕩漾)，我情不自禁(我情不自禁)。
    - Qiūbō dàngyàng, wǒ qíng bù zì jīn.
    - The waves of autumn glistened, and I couldn't help myself.

- **pinyin:** qiū
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character is composed of the elements for 'grain' (禾) and 'to come' (到), suggesting the season when grain is harvested.
