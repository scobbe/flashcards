### Yang Bin (name)
---
- **pinyin:** Yang Bin (name)
- **components:**
  - 杨(楊)
    - yáng
    - poplar; surname; west; sun-side
  - 斌
    - bīn
    - refined; cultured; talented; elegant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 杨(楊) (yáng, "poplar") + 斌(斌) (bīn, "refined") — refined poplar
  - **interpretation:** The name combines 杨(楊) (yáng, "poplar"), symbolizing strength and resilience, and 斌(斌) (bīn, "refined"), suggesting elegance and intelligence. This combination reflects positive attributes in a person's character.
- **examples:**
  - 杨斌在学校学习。
    - Yáng Bīn zài xuéxiào xuéxí.
    - Yang Bin is studying at school.
  - 我认识一个叫杨斌的人。
    - Wǒ rènshí yīgè jiào Yáng Bīn de rén.
    - I know a person named Yang Bin.

- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - to project; to shine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** This character is simplified from 楊 ( 昜 → 𠃓 ), where 昜 serves as the phonetic component suggesting sound, and the木 part (木) conveys the meaning related to trees.
