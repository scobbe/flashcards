## Yang Bin (name)
### Yáng Bīn
---
- **traditional:** 楊斌
- **definition:** Yang Bin (name)
- **pinyin:** Yáng Bīn
- **components:**
  - 杨(楊)
    - yáng
    - a surname
  - 斌
    - bīn
    - refined; literary
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 楊(杨) (yáng, "a surname") is a common Chinese surname. 斌 (bīn, "refined; literary") refers to a person who is cultivated or possesses literary talent. Together, 杨斌/楊斌 (Yáng Bīn) is used as a personal name.
  - **interpretation:** 杨斌/楊斌 (Yáng Bīn) is a Chinese name combining a common surname with a given name suggesting refinement and cultural sophistication.
  - **simplification:** Simplified characters used
- **examples:**
  - 杨(楊)斌热爱(熱愛)音乐(樂)。
    - Yáng Bīn rè'ài yīnyuè.
    - Yang Bin loves music.
  - 杨(楊)斌去了图书馆(圖書館)，寻(尋)找珍贵(貴)的手稿。
    - Yáng Bīn qùle túshūguǎn, xúnzhǎo zhēnguì de shǒugǎo.
    - Yang Bin went to the library, searching for valuable manuscripts.
  - 昨晚杨(楊)斌与(與)朋友共进(進)晚餐，交流了彼此的梦(夢)想。
    - Zuówǎn Yáng Bīn yǔ péngyǒu gòng jìn wǎncān, jiāoliú le bǐcǐ de mèngxiǎng.
    - Last night, Yang Bin dined with friends and exchanged their dreams.
### 杨(楊)斌( ) → 杨(楊)
- **traditional:** 楊
- **definition:** a surname
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - part of the phonetic element
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木 (mù, "tree") + phonetic: 昜 (yáng) =
    - tree ->
    - poplar tree, surname
  - **interpretation:** 杨(楊) (yáng) combines 木 (mù, "tree") as the semantic component indicating something related to wood or trees, with 昜 (yáng), which provides the phonetic component suggesting the pronunciation. This character refers to the poplar tree and is also a common Chinese surname.
  - **simplification:** 杨(楊): The character component 鱼(魚) (yú, "fish") was removed in the simplified form, keeping 木(木, "tree") and 昜(昜, "yáng") for phonetic representation.
- **examples:**
  - 王与杨为(與楊為)好朋友。
    - Wáng yǔ Yáng wéi hǎo péngyǒu.
    - Wang and Yang are good friends.
  - 杨(楊)先生来访(來訪)，带来礼(帶來禮)物。
    - Yáng xiānshēng láifǎng, dài lái lǐwù.
    - Mr. Yang visited and brought gifts.

- **pinyin:** yáng

    - sun
  - 丂
    - kǎo

  - 彡
    - xiǎn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日 (rì, "sun") + 丂 (kǎo, "breath") + 彡 (xiǎn, "rays of light") =

  - **interpretation:** The character 昜(yáng) portrays the concept of the sun's rays shining down, symbolized by 日 (rì, "sun") with 丂 representing a tree branch and 彡 depicting the sun's rays. This compound visually encapsulates the light given off by the sun.
- **examples:**

    - Fēng ér chuīguò liǔ shāo shí, xīn zhōng de mèngxiǎng yě suí yáng qǐ.
    - As the wind passes the tips of the willows, the dreams in the heart rise as well.
### 杨(楊)斌( ) → 杨(楊) → 昜 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来临时(來臨時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒满(灑滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.

- **definition:** breath
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of breath flowing up

### 杨(楊)斌( ) → 杨(楊) → 昜 → 彡
- **definition:** rays of light
- **pinyin:** xiǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts three strands of hair ->
    - used to indicate three light rays in certain contexts
  - **interpretation:** 彡(shān, "rays") originally depicted strands of hair but also came to represent light rays, reflecting a change in symbolic meaning over time. Its use as a representation of 'ornament' and in characters like 昜 and 參 highlights its versatility in indicating rays or decorative elements.
- **examples:**
  - 天空洒(灑)下金色的彡，大地迎来温(來溫)暖。
    - Tiānkōng sǎ xià jīnsè de shān, dàdì yíng lái wēnnuǎn.
    - The sky showers golden rays, and the earth welcomes warmth.
  - 晨曦中的彡闪(閃)耀着(著)希望。
    - Chénxī zhōng de shān shǎnyào zhe xīwàng.
    - The rays in the morning light shine with hope.
