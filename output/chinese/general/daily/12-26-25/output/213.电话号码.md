### telephone number
---
- **pinyin:** telephone number
- **components:**
  - 电话(電話)
    - diànhuà
    - telephone; electric speech
  - 号码(號碼)
    - hàomǎ
    - number; code
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 电(電) (diàn, 'electric') + 话(話) (huà, 'speech') — electric speech + 号(號) (hào, 'number') + 码(碼) (mǎ, 'code') — number code
  - **interpretation:** The word combines the concept of electric speech and a number code. This makes sense as a telephone number is a specific code used to facilitate electric speech communication.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一个电话号码(我需要一個電話號碼)，能拨打给你。
    - Wǒ xūyào yī gè diànhuà hàomǎ, néng bōdǎ gěi nǐ.
    - I need a telephone number that can call you.
  - 请告诉我你的电话号码(請告訴我你的電話號碼)。
    - Qǐng gàosù wǒ nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ.
    - Please tell me your telephone number.
