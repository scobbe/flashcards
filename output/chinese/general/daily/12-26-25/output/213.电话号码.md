## telephone number
### diànhuà hàomǎ
---
- **traditional:** 電話號碼
- **definition:** telephone number
- **pinyin:** diànhuà hàomǎ
- **components:**
  - 电话(電話)
    - diànhuà
    - telephone; phone call
  - 号码(號碼)
    - hàomǎ
    - number; code
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 電 (diàn, "electricity") + 話 (huà, "speech") forms 電話 (diànhuà, "telephone"), which refers to the device for transmitting spoken communication through electric signals. 號 (hào, "number") + 碼 (mǎ, "code") combine as 號碼 (hàomǎ, "number"), meaning a numerical identifier. Together, 電話 (diànhuà, "telephone") + 號碼 (hàomǎ, "number") =
    - 'telephone number'.
  - **interpretation:** A 電話 (diànhuà, "telephone") is used for communication, and its 號碼 (hàomǎ, "number") is the unique sequence that identifies a particular phone line for dialing.
  - **simplification:** 電話號碼 is the same in simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 请给(請給)我你的电话号码(電話號碼)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ.
    - Please give me your telephone number.
  - 我忘记(記)了电话号码(電話號碼)，需要再确认(確認)。
    - Wǒ wàngjìle diànhuà hàomǎ, xūyào zài quèrèn.
    - I forgot the telephone number, and I need to confirm it again.
