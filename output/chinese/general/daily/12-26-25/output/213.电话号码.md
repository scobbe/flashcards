## telephone number
### diànhuà hàomǎ
---
- **definition:** telephone number
- **pinyin:** diànhuà hàomǎ
- **components:**
  - 电话(電話)
    - diànhuà
    - telephone
  - 号码(號碼)
    - hàomǎ
    - number
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 电(電) (diàn, "electric") + 话(話) (huà, "speech; language") + 号码(號碼) (hàomǎ, "number") =
    - telephone number
  - **interpretation:** The word 电话(電話) (diànhuà, "telephone") refers to an electric device used for communication, combining 电(電) (diàn, "electric") and 话(話) (huà, "speech; language"). Together with 号码(號碼) (hàomǎ, "number"), it denotes a specific set of digits used to identify and connect to a telephone line.
- **examples:**
  - 请把你的电话号码(請把你的電話號碼)，告诉我(告訴我)。
    - Qǐng bǎ nǐ de diànhuà hàomǎ, gàosù wǒ.
    - Please give me your telephone number.
  - 我忘记了(我忘記了)，电话号码(電話號碼)在哪儿(在哪兒)？
    - Wǒ wàngjì le, diànhuà hàomǎ zài nǎr?
    - I forgot where the telephone number is?
