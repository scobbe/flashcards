### remember; recall
---
- **pinyin:** remember; recall
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; miss; want
  - 起來
    - qǐlái
    - rise; get up; start to
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想(想) (xiǎng, 'think; miss; want') + 起來(起來) (qǐlái, 'rise; get up; start to') — to think of or recall
  - **interpretation:** This phrase signifies the action of recalling or remembering something. It combines the idea of thinking (想) with the concept of starting to remember (起來).
- **examples:**
  - 我想起來了(我想起來了)，那本書是誰寫的(那本書是誰寫的)。
    - wǒ xiǎng qǐlái le, nà běn shū shì shéi xiě de。
    - I remembered, who wrote that book?
  - 她想起來(她想起來)，約會的時間是幾點(約會的時間是幾點)。
    - tā xiǎng qǐlái, yuēhuì de shíjiān shì jǐ diǎn。
    - She recalled that the appointment time was at what hour.

### 想起来(想起來) → 起來(起來)
- **definition:** rise; get up; start to
- **pinyin:** qǐlái
- **components:**
  - 起
    - qǐ
    - rise; get up; start; initiate
  - 來
    - lái
    - come; arrive; approach; return
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 起(起) (qǐ, "rise") + 來(來) (lái, "come") — rise and come
  - **interpretation:** 起來(起來) denotes the action of rising and coming into a state of activity. This combination emphasizes the idea of getting up and taking initiative.
- **examples:**
  - 我早上七點起來(我早上七點起來)，準備吃早餐(準備吃早餐)。
    - wǒ zǎoshang qī diǎn qǐlái, zhǔnbèi chī zǎocān。
    - I get up at seven in the morning, preparing to eat breakfast.
  - 他要求我起來(他要求我起來)幫忙搬家(幫忙搬家)。
    - tā yāoqiú wǒ qǐlái bāngmáng bān jiā。
    - He asked me to get up to help move.

### 想起来(想起來) → 起來(起來) → 來(來)
- **definition:** come; arrive; approach; return
- **pinyin:** lái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts wheat with the middle line representing the ear, upward lines for leaves, and downward lines for stem and roots.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents wheat, which historically symbolizes nourishment and growth. This connection is reflected in its use for 'to come', suggesting a bringing forth or arrival of sustenance.
- **examples:**
  - 他要來了(他要來了)，我们应该准备好(我們應該準備好)。
    - tā yào lái le, wǒmen yīnggāi zhǔnbèi hǎo.
    - He is coming, we should be ready.
  - 请她來(請她來)！
    - qǐng tā lái!
    - Please have her come!
