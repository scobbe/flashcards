## remember; recall
### xiǎngqǐlái
---
- **definition:** remember; recall
- **pinyin:** xiǎngqǐlái
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think
  - 起來
    - qǐlái
    - rise; come up
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想(想) (xiǎng, "think") + 起(起) (qǐ, "rise") + 来(來) (lái, "come") forms the idea of bringing something to mind or recalling it.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 想(想) (xiǎng, "think"), 起(起) (qǐ, "rise"), and 来(來) (lái, "come") suggests the action of bringing a thought to the forefront, which implies remembering or recalling something.
- **examples:**
  - 我突然想起来(我突然想起來)。
    - Wǒ túrán xiǎngqǐlái.
    - I suddenly remembered.
  - 听到那首歌(聽到那首歌)，我情不自禁想起来往事(我情不自禁想起來往事)。
    - Tīngdào nà shǒu gē, wǒ qíngbùzìjīn xiǎngqǐlái wǎngshì.
    - Hearing that song, I couldn't help but recall the past.
  - 他一说出名字(他一說出名字)，我就想起来旧事(我就想起來舊事)。
    - Tā yī shuō chū míngzi, wǒ jiù xiǎngqǐlái jiùshì.
    - When he mentioned the name, I immediately remembered old memories.

### 想起来(想起來) → 起來(起來) → 來(來)
- **definition:** come; arrive; return; arrive
- **pinyin:** lái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a wheat plant with an ear (middle line), leaves (upward lines), stem and roots (downward lines) ->
    - character for 麥(麥) (mài, 'wheat') ->
    - borrowed phonetic for 來(來) (lái, 'to come')
  - **interpretation:** The character 來(來) (lái, 'to come') originated as a pictograph of wheat, illustrating the plant's various parts. While it was borrowed to mean 'to come,' its original depiction is still visible in the structure, emphasizing temporal and phonetic parallels between harvesting and arrival.
- **examples:**
