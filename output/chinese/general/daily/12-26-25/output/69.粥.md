## congee
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 䰜
    - lì
    - cauldron
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") + 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") =
    - cooking rice in a cauldron with steam ->
    - 鬻(鬻) (yù, "congee") ->
    - reduced form 粥(粥) (zhōu, "congee")
  - **interpretation:** The character 粥(粥) (zhōu, "congee") originates from the combination of a cauldron, 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron"), and 米(米) (mǐ, "rice"), depicting the process of making congee by boiling rice in a cauldron. The original complex form 鬻(鬻) (yù, "congee") was simplified to 粥(粥).
  - **simplification:** 粥(粥) is a reduced form of the original 鬻(鬻), simplifying by removing the representation of steam and leaving the core concept of cooking rice.
- **examples:**
  - 这碗粥很好喝(這碗粥很好喝)。
    - Zhè wǎn zhōu hěn hǎohē.
    - This bowl of porridge is very tasty.
  - 清晨煮粥做早餐(清晨煮粥做早餐)，让人精神饱满(讓人精神飽滿)。
    - Qīngchén zhǔ zhōu zuò zǎocān, ràng rén jīngshén bǎomǎn.
    - Having congee for breakfast in the early morning makes one feel energetic.
### 粥(粥) → 䰜(䰜)
- **definition:** cauldron
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound / ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - Ancient form of 鬲(鬲) (lì, "cauldron") ->
    - depiction of clay pot ->
    - 虍(虍) (hū, "tiger stripes") added to form 魏(魏, "state in China"), then 鬻(鬻, "sell") simplified semantic/phono compound: 鬳(鬳) (Yǎn, "two-part vessel") + 瓦(瓦) (wǎ, "earthenware") =
    - earthen cooking vessel
  - **interpretation:** 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") is an ancient form related to 鬲(鬲) (lì, "cauldron"), uniquely capturing the appearance and material of traditional pottery vessels used for cooking. The components reflect its dual nature as both a depiction of the pot and a combination of cooking and material elements.
  - **simplification:** None, as 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") is used to trace the historical form related to 鬲(鬲) (lì, "cauldron").
- **examples:**
  - 古代祭祀时必备䰜(古代祭祀時必備䰜)，象征权力(象徵權力)。
    - Gǔdài jìsì shí bì bèi lì, xiàngzhēng quánlì.
    - In ancient sacrificial rites, the cauldron was indispensable and symbolized power.
  - 博物馆展出一尊精美的䰜(博物館展出一尊精美的䰜)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū yī zūn jīngměi de lì.
    - The museum exhibits an exquisitely crafted cauldron.
### 粥(粥) → 米(米)
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts rice kernels without husk
  - **interpretation:** The character 米(mǐ, "rice") visually represents rice grains without their husks, highlighting its use as a staple food. Its horizontal stroke can be seen as a bamboo sieve, symbolizing the process of removing husks from the rice.
- **examples:**
  - 他买了一袋米(他買了一袋米)。
    - Tā mǎi le yī dài mǐ.
    - He bought a bag of rice.
  - 超市里有好质量的米(超市裡有好質量的米)，适合做各种美食(適合做各種美食)。
    - Chāoshì lǐ yǒu hǎo zhìliàng de mǐ, shìhé zuò gè zhǒng měishí.
    - The supermarket has high-quality rice, which is suitable for making various delicacies.
  - 农民在田里种米(農民在田裡種米)。
    - Nóngmín zài tián lǐ zhòng mǐ.
    - Farmers grow rice in the field.
