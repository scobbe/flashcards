## congee
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 鬻
    - yù
    - congee
  - 䰜
    - lì
    - cauldron
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 鬻 (yù, "congee") =
    - 䰜 (lì, "cauldron") + 米 (mǐ, "rice") ->
    - congee; 粥 (zhōu, "porridge") is a reduced form of 鬻
  - **interpretation:** The character 粥 (zhōu, "porridge") was originally the character 鬻 (yù, "congee"), which depicted rice being cooked in a cauldron, symbolizing its usage as a food item. Over time, 粥 became a simplified form for convenience.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天喝粥。
    - Tā měitiān hē zhōu.
    - He drinks porridge every day.
  - 我喜欢(歡)粥，因为(爲)它很养(養)胃。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhōu, yīnwèi tā hěn yǎngwèi.
    - I like porridge because it is good for the stomach.
  - 冬天里(裡)煮粥，让(讓)人感到温(溫)暖。
    - Dōngtiān lǐ zhǔ zhōu, ràng rén gǎndào wēnnuǎn.
    - Cooking porridge in winter makes people feel warm.
### 粥 → 鬻
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 鬲
    - lì
    - cauldron
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 弓
    - gōng
    - bow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 鬲 (lì, "cauldron") + 米 (mǐ, "rice") =
    - depiction of rice being cooked in a cauldron, with 弓 (gōng, "bow") representing steam ->
    - original meaning "congee"
  - **interpretation:** The character 鬻 initially depicted the act of cooking rice in a cauldron, which gave it the meaning "congee." It later got borrowed for its phonetic value to represent "to sell," but originally it directly related to the preparation of rice in a pot.
### 粥 → 鬻 → 鬲
- **definition:** cauldron
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram of a pottery vessel ->
    - stylized as a cauldron
  - **interpretation:** 鬲(lì, "cauldron") is a pictogram representing a pottery vessel or cauldron used in ancient cooking practices. It visually depicts the shape of an ancient pot, emphasizing its use in cooking and ceremonies.
- **examples:**
  - 考古学(學)家在遗(遺)址中发现(發現)了一个(個)古老的鬲。
    - Kǎogǔ xuéjiā zài yízhǐ zhōng fāxiàn le yīgè gǔlǎo de lì。
    - Archaeologists discovered an ancient cauldron at the ruins.
  - 博物馆(館)展出了古代的鬲，说(說)明远(遠)古人的生活。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū le gǔdài de lì, shuōmíng yuǎngǔ rén de shēnghuó。
    - The museum displayed an ancient cauldron, which illustrates the life of ancient people.
  - 在古代，鬲不仅是烹饪器具(在古代，鬲不僅是烹飪器具)，更是宗教祭祀的重要象征(徵)。
    - Zài gǔdài, lì bùjǐn shì pēngrèn qìjù, gèng shì zōngjiào jìsì de zhòngyào xiàngzhēng。
    - In ancient times, the cauldron was not only a cooking utensil, but also an important symbol in religious ceremonies.
### 粥 → 鬻 → 米
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts rice kernels ->
    - 米(mǐ, 'rice')
  - **interpretation:** The character 米(mǐ, 'rice') visually represents dehusked rice kernels to differentiate it from 粟. The horizontal stroke may symbolize a bamboo sieve, emphasizing the process of separating grains from their husks.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃米饭(飯)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī mǐfàn.
    - I like to eat rice.
  - 她从农(從農)村带(帶)回一袋米，用来(來)煮饭(飯)。
    - Tā cóng nóngcūn dài huí yī dài mǐ, yòng lái zhǔ fàn.
    - She brought a bag of rice from the countryside to cook.
  - 市场(場)上有新米出售。
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu xīn mǐ chūshòu.
    - New rice is available in the market.
### 粥 → 鬻 → 弓
- **definition:** bow
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bow, showing the shape of an arched weapon
  - **interpretation:** The character 弓 (gōng, "bow") visually represents the curved shape of a bow, a straightforward pictogram emphasizing its physical form.
- **examples:**
  - 弓是古老的武器。
    - Gōng shì gǔlǎo de wǔqì.
    - The bow is an ancient weapon.
  - 他拉起弓，瞄准远处(準遠處)的目标(標)。
    - Tā lā qǐ gōng, miáo zhǔn yuǎn chù de mùbiāo.
    - He draws the bow and aims at a distant target.
  - 女战(戰)士操纵(縱)弓，迅速释(釋)放箭矢。
    - Nǚ zhànshì cāozòng gōng, xùnsù shìfàng jiànshǐ.
    - The female warrior manipulates the bow and swiftly releases the arrows.
### 粥 → 䰜
- **definition:** cauldron
- **pinyin:** lì
- **components:**
  - 鬲
    - lì
    - cauldron
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** 䰜 is an ancient form of 鬲 (lì, "cauldron") depicting a cauldron or similar cooking vessel
  - **interpretation:** The character 䰜 depicts a cooking vessel, specifically a cauldron, in a manner similar to 鬲 (lì, "cauldron"), emphasizing its function as a container for cooking.
