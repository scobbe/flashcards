## congee
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 鬻
    - yù
    - congee
  - 䰜
    - lì
    - cauldron
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 鬻(鬻) (yù, "congee") =
    - 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") + 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") ->
    - congee; 粥(粥) (zhōu, "porridge") is a reduced form of 鬻(鬻)
  - **interpretation:** The character 粥(粥) (zhōu, "porridge") was originally the character 鬻(鬻) (yù, "congee"), which depicted rice being cooked in a cauldron, symbolizing its usage as a food item. Over time, 粥(粥) became a simplified form for convenience.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天喝粥(他每天喝粥)。
    - Tā měitiān hē zhōu.
    - He drinks porridge every day.
  - 我喜欢粥(我喜歡粥)，因为它很养胃(因爲它很養胃)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhōu, yīnwèi tā hěn yǎngwèi.
    - I like porridge because it is good for the stomach.
  - 冬天里煮粥(冬天裡煮粥)，让人感到温暖(讓人感到溫暖)。
    - Dōngtiān lǐ zhǔ zhōu, ràng rén gǎndào wēnnuǎn.
    - Cooking porridge in winter makes people feel warm.
### 粥(粥) → 鬻(鬻)
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 䰜
    - lì
    - cauldron
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 弓
    - gōng
    - bow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") + 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") =
    - cauldron cooking rice, with 弓(弓) (gōng, "bow") depicting steam ->
    - congee
  - **interpretation:** The character 鬻 combines a depiction of a cauldron 䰜(䰜) (lì, "cauldron") with 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") to represent the cooking of rice into a porridge-like dish. The presence of 弓(弓) (gōng, "bow") suggests steam rising from the cooking process, reinforcing the concept of cooking congee. This character was later used as a phonetic loan for the concept of 'selling.'
### 粥(粥) → 鬻(鬻) → 䰜(䰜)
- **definition:** cauldron
- **pinyin:** lì
- **components:**
  - 鬲
    - lì
    - cauldron
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** 䰜 depicts a cauldron, an ancient form of 鬲(鬲) (lì, "cauldron")
  - **interpretation:** The character 䰜 is an ancient representation of a cauldron, depicting its shape as used in pottery. Over time, this character evolved into 鬲(鬲) (lì, "cauldron"), which retains the fundamental depiction of a vessel used for cooking.
### 粥(粥) → 鬻(鬻) → 䰜(䰜) → 鬲(鬲)
- **definition:** cauldron
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram of a pottery vessel ->
    - stylized as a cauldron
  - **interpretation:** 鬲(lì, "cauldron") is a pictogram representing a pottery vessel or cauldron used in ancient cooking practices. It visually depicts the shape of an ancient pot, emphasizing its use in cooking and ceremonies.
- **examples:**
  - 考古学家在遗址中发现了一个古老的鬲(考古學家在遺址中發現了一個古老的鬲)。
    - Kǎogǔ xuéjiā zài yízhǐ zhōng fāxiàn le yīgè gǔlǎo de lì。
    - Archaeologists discovered an ancient cauldron at the ruins.
  - 博物馆展出了古代的鬲(博物館展出了古代的鬲)，说明远古人的生活(說明遠古人的生活)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū le gǔdài de lì, shuōmíng yuǎngǔ rén de shēnghuó。
    - The museum displayed an ancient cauldron, which illustrates the life of ancient people.
  - 在古代，鬲不仅是烹饪器具(在古代，鬲不僅是烹飪器具)，更是宗教祭祀的重要象征(更是宗教祭祀的重要象徵)。
    - Zài gǔdài, lì bùjǐn shì pēngrèn qìjù, gèng shì zōngjiào jìsì de zhòngyào xiàngzhēng。
    - In ancient times, the cauldron was not only a cooking utensil, but also an important symbol in religious ceremonies.
### 粥(粥) → 鬻(鬻) → 米(米)
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts rice kernels ->
    - 米(mǐ, 'rice')
  - **interpretation:** The character 米(mǐ, 'rice') visually represents dehusked rice kernels to differentiate it from 粟. The horizontal stroke may symbolize a bamboo sieve, emphasizing the process of separating grains from their husks.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃米饭(我喜歡吃米飯)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī mǐfàn.
    - I like to eat rice.
  - 她从农村带回一袋米(她從農村帶回一袋米)，用来煮饭(用來煮飯)。
    - Tā cóng nóngcūn dài huí yī dài mǐ, yòng lái zhǔ fàn.
    - She brought a bag of rice from the countryside to cook.
  - 市场上有新米出售(市場上有新米出售)。
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu xīn mǐ chūshòu.
    - New rice is available in the market.
