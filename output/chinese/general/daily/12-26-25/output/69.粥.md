## congee
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** congee
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 弓
    - gōng
    - bow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Reduced form of 鬻 ‘congee’ (ideogrammic compound).
  - **interpretation:** A streamlined graphic descended from 鬻, which depicted the idea of porridge through meaningful elements rather than sound. Its meaning comes from the image of rice-based gruel, not from a phonetic clue.
- **examples:**
  - 早上我喜欢喝一碗热粥(早上我喜歡喝一碗熱粥)。
    - Zǎoshang wǒ xǐhuān hē yì wǎn rè zhōu.
    - In the morning I like to drink a bowl of hot congee.
  - 生病时(生病時)，他只吃白粥(他只吃白粥)。
    - Shēngbìng shí, tā zhǐ chī bái zhōu.
    - When sick, he only eats plain congee.
  - 这家店的皮蛋瘦肉粥很有名(這家店的皮蛋瘦肉粥很有名)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de pídàn shòuròu zhōu hěn yǒumíng.
    - This shop’s century egg and lean pork congee is very famous.
### 粥(粥) → 米(米)
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: dehusked rice kernels; horizontal stroke as a bamboo sieve; cf. 粟 (with husk); unrelated to 木/禾/来.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts dehusked rice grains being sifted on a bamboo sieve, contrasting with 粟 which shows husked kernels. Despite visual resemblance, its form is not derived from 木, 禾, or 来.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两斤米(我買了兩斤米)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le liǎng jīn mǐ.
    - I bought two jin of rice.
  - 这家店的东北米很香(這家店的東北米很香)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de Dōngběi mǐ hěn xiāng.
    - The Northeastern rice at this shop is very fragrant.
  - 请把米洗一洗(請把米洗一洗)，再煮饭(再煮飯)。
    - Qǐng bǎ mǐ xǐ yì xǐ, zài zhǔ fàn.
    - Please rinse the rice, then cook it.
### 粥(粥) → 弓(弓)
- **definition:** bow
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a bow.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the curved bow and its string in a simple outline. The shape evokes the bend and draw of archery and later serves as a component in characters related to pulling or shooting.
- **examples:**
  - 他拉开弓(他拉開弓)。
    - Tā lā kāi gōng.
    - He draws the bow.
  - 弓箭手瞄准目标(弓箭手瞄準目標)。
    - Gōngjiànshǒu miáozhǔn mùbiāo.
    - The archer aims at the target.
  - 这把弓很结实(這把弓很結實)。
    - Zhè bǎ gōng hěn jiēshi.
    - This bow is very sturdy.
