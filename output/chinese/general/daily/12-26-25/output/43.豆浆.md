## soy milk
### dòujiāng
---
- **definition:** soy milk
- **pinyin:** dòujiāng
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 浆(漿)
    - jiāng
    - liquid; pulp; starch; syrup
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") refers to the soybeans used as the primary ingredient, while 浆(漿) (jiāng, "liquid") denotes the liquid form that these beans are processed into, creating the common beverage known as soy milk.
  - **interpretation:** The combination 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") and 浆(漿) (jiāng, "liquid") effectively describes a liquid made from beans, which is what soy milk is.
  - **simplification:** 豆(豆) and 浆(漿) both remained unchanged in their simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝豆浆(我喜歡喝豆漿)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē dòujiāng.
    - I like to drink soy milk.
  - 豆浆搭配油条(豆漿搭配油條)，是一种传统早餐(是一種傳統早餐)。
    - Dòujiāng dāpèi yóutiáo, shì yī zhǒng chuántǒng zǎocān.
    - Soy milk paired with fried dough sticks is a traditional breakfast.
  - 清晨读书时(清晨讀書時)，我配上一杯豆浆(我配上一杯豆漿)。
    - Qīngchén dúshū shí, wǒ pèi shàng yī bēi dòujiāng.
    - While reading in the early morning, I have a cup of soy milk.

### 豆浆(豆漿) → 浆(漿)
- **definition:** liquid; pulp; starch; syrup
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 将(將)
    - jiāng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 将(將) (jiāng)
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") as the semantic component to indicate a liquid or water-related substance, with 将(將) (jiāng) providing the phonetic component. The combination aptly describes various liquid forms such as pulp, starch, or syrup.
  - **simplification:** 漿(漿)→浆(漿): 将(將) (jiāng) is a much simplified version where the amount of strokes is reduced.
- **examples:**
  - 早餐时(早餐時)，我喝热豆浆(我喝熱豆漿)。
    - zǎo cān shí, wǒ hē rè dòu jiāng.
    - In the morning, I drink hot soybean milk.
  - 他用浆搅拌面糊(他用漿攪拌麵糊)，为煎饼做准备(為煎餅做準備)。
    - tā yòng jiāng jiǎo bàn miàn hú, wèi jiān bǐng zuò zhǔn bèi.
    - He mixes the slurry into the batter, preparing to make pancakes.
  - 工匠将浆刷在木板上(工匠將漿刷在木板上)，以增强粘合力(以增強黏合力)。
    - gōng jiàng jiāng jiāng shuā zài mù bǎn shàng, yǐ zēng qiáng zhān hé lì.
    - The craftsman brushed the liquid onto the wooden board to enhance its adhesive strength.

### 豆浆(豆漿) → 浆(漿) → 将(將)
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 爿
    - pán
    - small table
  - ⺼
    - ròu
    - meat
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 爿(爿) (pán, "small table") + semantic: ⺼(⺼) (ròu, "meat") + semantic: 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") =
    - meat offered on table with hand ->
    - lead
  - **interpretation:** The character 将(將) (jiāng, "lead") combines the meat ⺼(⺼) (ròu, "meat") and hand 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") components, indicating the action of offering meat as tribute, a leader's action. Phonetically, 爿(爿) (pán) suggests the idea of a table or platform where offerings are made, symbolizing leadership and guidance.
- **examples:**
  - 他将带领我们(他將帶領我們)，走向胜利(走向勝利)。
    - Tā jiāng dàilǐng wǒmen, zǒuxiàng shènglì.

    - Lǎoshī jiāng yǐndǎo xuéshēng, zǒuxiàng zhīshì de hǎiyáng.
    - The teacher will guide the students towards the ocean of knowledge.
  - 将军将率领军队(將軍將率領軍隊)，冲锋陷阵(衝鋒陷陣)。
    - Jiāngjūn jiāng shuàilǐng jūnduì, chōngfēng xiànzhèn.

### 豆浆(豆漿) → 浆(漿) → 将(將) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram (指事)
  - **description:**
    - Line on hand indicating pulse point on forearm ->
    - original form of 肘 (zhǒu, 'elbow') ->
    - unit of measurement derived from this distance
  - **interpretation:** 寸(cùn, 'inch') represents a specific point on the forearm used for pulse measurement, historically linked to its measurement definition. The form depicts a line that combines with the hand to signify this location, showing a logical evolution from anatomy to distance measurement.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布的宽度只有五寸(這塊布的寬度只有五寸)。
    - Zhè kuài bù de kuāndù zhǐyǒu wǔ cùn.
    - The width of this cloth is only five inches.
  - 木匠在制作家具时(木匠在製作家具時)，要精确到一寸(要精確到一寸)。
    - Mùjiàng zài zhìzuò jiājù shí, yào jīngquè dào yī cùn.
    - Carpenters must be precise to the inch when making furniture.
