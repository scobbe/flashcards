## branch; axe handle
### kē
---
- **definition:** branch; axe handle
- **pinyin:** kē
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 可
    - kě
    - can
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "tree") as semantic + 可(可) (kě, "can") as phonetic =
    - tool made of wood ->
    - branch; axe handle
  - **interpretation:** The character 柯(kē, "branch; axe handle") uses 木(木) (mù, "tree") to suggest a wood-related object, while the phonetic 可(可) (kě, "can") indicates the pronunciation. This character reflects tools crafted from wood, like handles or branches.
- **examples:**
  - 他的斧柯(他的斧柯)牢固耐用(牢固耐用)。
    - tā de fǔ kē láogù nàiyòng.
    - His axe handle is solid and durable.
  - 古树的柯(古樹的柯)向天空延伸(向天空延伸)，微风中轻轻摇曳(微風中輕輕搖曳)。
    - gǔ shù de kē xiàng tiānkōng yánshēn, wēifēng zhōng qīngqīng yáoyè.
    - The ancient tree branch extends toward the sky, swaying gently in the breeze.
### 柯(柯) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom
  - **interpretation:** 木(木) (mù, "tree") visually represents a tree with branches at the top and roots below. This pictorial depiction is straightforward, resembling the structure of a tree and its natural growth.
- **examples:**
  - 庭院里有一棵参天古木(庭院裡有一棵參天古木)。
    - Tíngyuàn lǐ yǒu yī kē cāntiān gǔ mù.
    - There is a towering ancient tree in the courtyard.
  - 他走到森林深处(他走到森林深處)，看见一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁(看見一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁)。
    - Tā zǒu dào sēnlín shēnchù, kànjiàn yī kē gū línglíng de mù chùlì zài lùpáng.
    - He walked into the deep forest and saw a solitary tree standing by the roadside.
### 柯(柯) → 可(可)
- **definition:** can
- **pinyin:** kě
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - exhaled air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + 丂(丂) (kǎo, "exhaled air") =
    - sound from mouth ->
    - express agreement or permission ->
    - can
  - **interpretation:** The character 可(kě, "can") combines the semantic element 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") with a phonetic component 丂(丂) (kǎo, "exhaled air"), indicating an action performed with the mouth such as singing or exhaling. Over time, it has become associated with the sense of being allowed or able to do something, captured by the modern meaning 'can'.
- **examples:**
  - 只要你认真练习(只要你認真練習)，就可提高技能(就可提高技能)。
    - Zhǐyào nǐ rènzhēn liànxí, jiù kě tígāo jìnéng.
    - As long as you practice earnestly, you can improve your skills.
  - 你可相信自己的能力(你可相信自己的能力)。
    - Nǐ kě xiāngxìn zìjǐ de nénglì.

  - 困境面前(困境面前)，我们可一起奋进(我們可一起奮進)。

### 柯(柯) → 可(可) → 丂(丂)
