### branch; axe handle
---
- **pinyin:** branch; axe handle
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 可
    - kě
    - can, may
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** A combination of 木 (mù, 'tree') and 可 (kě, 'can, may'), suggesting a tree-related object that can be used or is useful.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of a branch or handle, relating to something functional taken from a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 我用柯来砍树(我用柯來砍樹)。
    - wǒ yòng kē lái kǎn shù.
    - I use the branch to chop the tree.
  - 这把斧头的柯很结实(這把斧頭的柯很結實)。
    - zhè bǎ fǔtóu de kē hěn jiéshí.
    - The handle of this axe is very sturdy.
### 柯(柯) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
