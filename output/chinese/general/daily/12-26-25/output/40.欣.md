## happy; joyful
### xīn
---
- **definition:** happy; joyful
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **components:**
  - 斤
    - jīn
    - axe
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 斤(斤) (jīn, 'axe') + semantic 欠(欠) (qiàn, 'yawn') ->
    - expressing joy
  - **interpretation:** The character 欣(xīn) combines 斤(斤) (jīn, 'axe') as a phonetic component and 欠(欠) (qiàn, 'yawn') as a semantic component to convey the sense of delight or satisfaction, as if opening up with a yawn of contentment.
- **examples:**
  - 听到好消息时(聽到好消息時)，他满怀欣喜(他滿懷欣喜)。
    - Tīngdào hǎo xiāoxī shí, tā mǎnhuái xīnxǐ.
    - When he heard the good news, he was filled with delight.
  - 邀请到来时(邀請到來時)，她欣然应允(她欣然應允)。
    - Yāoqǐng dàolái shí, tā xīnrán yìngyǔn.
    - When the invitation arrived, she accepted it with joy.
### 欣(欣) → 斤(斤)
- **definition:** axe
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an axe
  - **interpretation:** The character 斤(斤) (jīn, "axe") is a pictogram that visually represents an axe. Its shape has evolved over time, but its fundamental design still mirrors the silhouette of an axe, making it easy to recognize and remember.
- **examples:**
  - 他用斤砍树(他用斤砍樹)。
    - Tā yòng jīn kǎn shù.
    - He uses the axe to chop down a tree.
  - 工匠挥舞着斤(工匠揮舞著斤)，雕刻出精美的木雕(雕刻出精美的木雕)。
    - Gōngjiàng huīwǔ zhe jīn, diāokè chū jīngměi de mùdiāo.
    - The craftsman swung the axe, carving exquisite wooden sculptures.
### 欣(欣) → 欠(欠)
- **definition:** yawn
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - A kneeling person with mouth open ->
    - yawn
  - **interpretation:** The pictogram 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") depicts a person kneeling with an open mouth, which captures the physical action of yawning.
- **examples:**
  - 他刚起床时(他剛起床時)，便打了个欠(便打了個欠)。
    - Tā gāng qǐchuáng shí, biàn dǎ le gè qiàn.
    - He yawned as soon as he woke up.
  - 看书时(看書時)，不时打个欠(不時打個欠)。
    - Kàn shū shí, bù shí dǎ gè qiàn.
    - While reading, he occasionally yawned.
