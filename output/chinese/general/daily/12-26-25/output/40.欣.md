## happy; joyful
### xīn
---
- **definition:** happy; joyful
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **components:**
  - 斤
    - jīn
    - axe
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 斤(斤) (jīn, "axe") + Semantic: 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") =
    - depicts an expression of joy or delight
  - **interpretation:** The character 欣(xīn, "happy") combines 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") representing an open mouth, often related to expressions, with 斤(斤) as the phonetic component to convey the sound. Together, they suggest an expression associated with joy.
- **examples:**
  - 他欣然接受邀请(他欣然接受邀請)。
    - Tā xīnrán jiēshòu yāoqǐng.
    - He gladly accepted the invitation.
  - 听闻喜讯后(聽聞喜訊後)，他欣然起舞(他欣然起舞)。
    - Tīngwén xǐxùn hòu, tā xīnrán qǐwǔ.
    - After hearing the good news, he danced joyfully.
### 欣(欣) → 斤(斤)
- **definition:** axe
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**

- **examples:**

### 欣(欣) → 欠(欠)
- **definition:** yawn
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person in a bent posture with an open mouth, suggesting the act of yawning or slackening
  - **interpretation:** The character 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") visually represents a person with their mouth open, capturing the physical gesture associated with yawning. It symbolizes a relaxed or tired state.
- **examples:**
  - 他打哈欠(他打哈欠)。
    - tā dǎ hāqiàn
    - He yawns.
  - 上课时他总是打哈欠(上課時他總是打哈欠)，让老师分心(讓老師分心)。
    - shàngkè shí tā zǒng shì dǎ hāqiàn, ràng lǎoshī fēnxīn
    - He keeps yawning during class, distracting the teacher.
  - 会议中(會議中)，他不自觉地打哈欠(他不自覺地打哈欠)。
    - huìyì zhōng, tā bù zìjué dì dǎ hāqiàn
    - During the meeting, he involuntarily yawns.
