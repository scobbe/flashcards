## movie star
### diànyǐng míngxīng
---
- **definition:** movie star
- **pinyin:** diànyǐng míngxīng
- **components:**
  - 电影(電影)
    - diànyǐng
    - film; movie; cinema; motion picture
  - 明星
    - míngxīng
    - star; celebrity; famous person; luminary
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 电影 (film; ‘electric shadow’) + 明星 (star; celebrity) = movie star
  - **interpretation:** Combines the medium (film) with “star/celebrity” to denote a person famous in cinema. 明星 retains its literal “bright star” metaphor for fame.
  - **simplification:** 电 is the simplified form of 電, reducing strokes for easier writing; 影、明、星 are unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 她是著名的电影明星(她是著名的電影明星)。
    - Tā shì zhùmíng de diànyǐng míngxīng.
    - She is a famous movie star.
  - 这位电影明星(這位電影明星)在红毯上(在紅毯上)引起了(引起了)媒体的关注(媒體的關注)。
    - Zhè wèi diànyǐng míngxīng zài hóngtǎn shang yǐnqǐ le méitǐ de guānzhù.
    - This movie star drew the media’s attention on the red carpet.
  - 他从电视演员(他從電視演員)转型为(轉型為)电影明星(電影明星)。
    - Tā cóng diànshì yǎnyuán zhuǎnxíng wéi diànyǐng míngxīng.
    - He transitioned from a TV actor to a movie star.
### 电影明星(電影明星) → 电影(電影)
- **definition:** film; movie; cinema; motion picture
- **pinyin:** diànyǐng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 电(electric) + 影(shadow/image) = motion picture (electrically projected images).
  - **interpretation:** Literally “electric shadows,” referring to images projected by electrical technology. Evokes early cinema’s moving shadows on a screen.
  - **simplification:** 電 was simplified to 电 to reduce strokes during character simplification; 影 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢看外国电影(我最喜歡看外國電影)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan kàn wàiguó diànyǐng.
    - I like watching foreign films the most.
  - 这部电影在电影院上映(這部電影在電影院上映)。
    - Zhè bù diànyǐng zài diànyǐngyuàn shàngyìng.
    - This movie is being released in theaters.
  - 他对电影历史很感兴趣(他對電影歷史很感興趣)。
    - Tā duì diànyǐng lìshǐ hěn gǎn xìngqù.
    - He is very interested in film history.
### 电影明星(電影明星) → 明星
- **definition:** star; celebrity; famous person; luminary
- **pinyin:** míngxīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 明 ‘bright; famous’ + 星 ‘star’ = celebrity; luminary (lit. bright star).
  - **interpretation:** Literally “bright star.” By extension, someone who stands out in the public eye, especially in entertainment or sports.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位国际知名的电影明星(她是一位國際知名的電影明星)。
    - Tā shì yí wèi guójì zhīmíng de diànyǐng míngxīng.
    - She is an internationally renowned movie star.
  - 他从普通演员成长为一线明星(他從普通演員成長為一線明星)。
    - Tā cóng pǔtōng yǎnyuán chéngzhǎng wéi yīxiàn míngxīng.
    - He rose from an ordinary actor to a top-tier celebrity.
  - 清晨东方的天空出现了一颗明亮的明星(清晨東方的天空出現了一顆明亮的明星)。
    - Qīngchén dōngfāng de tiānkōng chūxiàn le yì kē míngliàng de míngxīng.
    - In the early morning eastern sky, a bright star appeared.
