## movie star
### diànyǐng míngxīng
---
- **traditional:** 電影明星
- **definition:** movie star
- **pinyin:** diànyǐng míngxīng
- **components:**
  - 电(電)影
    - diànyǐng
    - movie; film
  - 明星
    - míngxīng
    - star; celebrity
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 电(電) (diàn, "electric") + 影 (yǐng, "shadow" or "film") =
    - 电(電)影 (diànyǐng, "movie"); 明 (míng, "bright" or "famous") + 星 (xīng, "star") =
    - 明星 (míngxīng, "star" or "celebrity")
  - **interpretation:** 电(電)影 (diànyǐng, "movie") literally refers to a film made with electrical technology, while 明星 (míngxīng, "star") denotes someone famous and shining. A 电(電)影明星 is a renowned and celebrated individual in the world of cinema.
  - **simplification:** 電; 影; 明; 星
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)位电(電)影明星热爱(熱愛)表演。
    - Zhè wèi diànyǐng míngxīng rè'ài biǎoyǎn.
    - This movie star loves acting.
  - 电(電)影明星走上红(紅)毯，接受观众热(觀眾熱)烈掌声(聲)。
    - Diànyǐng míngxīng zǒu shàng hóng tǎn, jiēshòu guānzhòng rèliè zhǎngshēng.
    - The movie star walked the red carpet and received enthusiastic applause from the audience.
  - 许(許)多年轻(輕)人梦(夢)想成为电(為電)影明星。
    - Xǔduō niánqīngrén mèngxiǎng chéngwéi diànyǐng míngxīng.
    - Many young people dream of becoming movie stars.
