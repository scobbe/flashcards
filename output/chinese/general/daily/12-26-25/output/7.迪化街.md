### Dihua Street, Taipei
---
- **pinyin:** Dihua Street, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 迪化
    - díhuà
    - to enlighten; to illuminate; to expand; to develop
  - 街
    - jiē
    - street; road; avenue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 迪化(迪化) (díhuà, "to enlighten or illuminate") + 街(街) (jiē, "street") — enlightening street
  - **interpretation:** The term '迪化街' refers to a street known for its historical and cultural significance in Taipei, symbolizing a place of enlightenment and development. It represents a vibrant area where tradition and modernity coexist.
- **examples:**
  - 我常去迪化街(我常去迪化街)，那里有很多小店。
    - Wǒ cháng qù Dihua jiē, nàlǐ yǒu hěn duō xiǎo diàn.
    - I often go to Dihua Street, where there are many small shops.
  - 迪化街(迪化街)是台北一个热门的旅游地点。
    - Dihua jiē shì Táiběi yīgè rèmén de lǚyóu dìdiǎn.
    - Dihua Street is a popular tourist spot in Taipei.
