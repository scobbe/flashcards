## Dihua Street, Taipei
### Díhuà Jiē
---
- **definition:** Dihua Street, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Díhuà Jiē
- **components:**
  - 迪化
    - Díhuà
    - Dihua (ROC-era name of Ürümqi); to enlighten; to transform (archaic)
  - 街
    - jiē
    - street; thoroughfare; commercial street
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** proper noun; toponymic compound
  - **description:** 迪化 (old ROC name of Ürümqi) + 街 (street) = Dihua Street.
  - **interpretation:** Formed by a toponym plus the generic street suffix, a common naming pattern in Taipei. 迪化 reflects ROC-era practice of using mainland place names for streets after 1945.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在台北的迪化街买年货(我們在臺北的迪化街買年貨)。
    - Wǒmen zài Táiběi de Díhuà Jiē mǎi niánhuò.
    - We buy New Year goods on Dihua Street in Taipei.
  - 迪化街保留了许多古老的店屋(迪化街保留了許多古老的店屋)。
    - Díhuà Jiē bǎoliú le xǔduō gǔlǎo de diànwū.
    - Dihua Street preserves many old shophouses.
  - 每到过年前(每到過年前)，这里都非常热闹(這裏都非常熱鬧)。
    - Měi dào guònián qián, zhèlǐ dōu fēicháng rènào.
    - Before Lunar New Year, it gets very lively here.
