## pet
### chǒngwù
---
- **definition:** pet
- **pinyin:** chǒngwù
- **components:**
  - 宠(寵)
    - chǒng
    - to favor; to pamper; to dote on; favored
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing; object; creature; matter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 宠(寵, to favor; pamper) + 物(thing; creature) = pampered creature (pet).
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of indulgence with 'creature,' highlighting animals kept and doted on by humans. Emphasizes companionship rather than utility.
  - **simplification:** 寵 was simplified to 宠 by replacing 龍 with 龙 and dropping the 心 component, reducing strokes for ease of writing; 物 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我家有两只可爱的宠物(我家有兩隻可愛的寵物)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǒu liǎng zhī kě'ài de chǒngwù.
    - My family has two cute pets.
  - 他从小想养宠物(他從小想養寵物)，后来选择了一只猫(後來選擇了一隻貓)。
    - Tā cóng xiǎo xiǎng yǎng chǒngwù, hòulái xuǎnzé le yì zhī māo.
    - Since childhood he wanted to keep a pet, and later he chose a cat.
  - 宠物生病时(寵物生病時)，我们会带它去动物医院(我們會帶它去動物醫院)。
    - Chǒngwù shēngbìng shí, wǒmen huì dài tā qù dòngwù yīyuàn.
    - When a pet is sick, we take it to the animal hospital.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵)
- **definition:** to favor; to pamper; to dote on; favored
- **pinyin:** chǒng
- **components:**
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; mind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 宀 + phonetic 龍
  - **interpretation:** A roof element 宀 conveys a household or shelter context, while 龍 provides the sound and an association with high status or special favor. Together they convey the idea of someone specially favored or indulged within the household, hence “to dote on” or “to pamper.”
  - **simplification:** Replaced the phonetic 龍 with its simplified form 龙 and dropped the 心 component, yielding 宀 + 龙 while preserving the phonetic value.
- **examples:**
  - 她太宠孩子了(她太寵孩子了)。
    - Tā tài chǒng háizi le.
    - She spoils the child too much.
  - 他在宫里很得宠(他在宮裡很得寵)。
    - Tā zài gōng lǐ hěn dé chǒng.
    - He is much favored in the palace.
  - 这只狗是他最宠爱的伙伴(這隻狗是他最寵愛的夥伴)。
    - Zhè zhī gǒu shì tā zuì chǒng’ài de huǒbàn.
    - This dog is his most beloved companion.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵) → 龙(龍)
- **definition:** dragon
- **pinyin:** lóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: originally a serpent with a whiskered mouth and eyes; current standard form developed in large seal script. The simplified 龙 comes via variant 𢅛 of 龍, removing 帝 and reducing the 彡 strokes.
  - **interpretation:** Evokes a whiskered, serpentine creature with eyes and maw; later scripts abstracted the curves and details into a compact emblem. The PRC simplification streamlines earlier variants by dropping an upper element and trimming the beard strokes while keeping the dragon’s iconic silhouette.
  - **simplification:** Stroke-count reduction in script reform: removed the 帝 component from the 𢅛 variant and pared down 彡 to speed writing and improve legibility.
- **examples:**
  - 中国人把龙当作吉祥的象征(中國人把龍當作吉祥的象徵)。
    - Zhōngguó rén bǎ lóng dāngzuò jíxiáng de xiàngzhēng.
    - Chinese people regard the dragon as an auspicious symbol.
  - 今年是龙年(今年是龍年)。
    - Jīnnián shì lóngnián.
    - This year is the Year of the Dragon.
  - 那条龙在云中盘旋(那條龍在雲中盤旋)。
    - Nà tiáo lóng zài yún zhōng pánxuán.
    - That dragon is circling in the clouds.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵) → 心(心)
- **definition:** heart; mind
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – a heart, now highly stylized.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of the anatomical heart; its dots and curves abstracted into a compact, balanced form over time. Related side-forms like 忄 in compounds cue meanings tied to feelings, thought, or the mind.
- **examples:**
  - 我很关心你(我很關心你)。
    - Wǒ hěn guānxīn nǐ.
    - I care about you a lot.
  - 他一时分心(他一時分心)，犯了错误(犯了錯誤)。
    - Tā yīshí fēnxīn, fàn le cuòwù.
    - He got distracted for a moment and made a mistake.
  - 放下心里的担忧(放下心裏的擔憂)，安心休息吧(安心休息吧)。
    - Fàngxià xīnlǐ de dānyōu, ānxīn xiūxi ba.
    - Let go of the worries in your heart and rest.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物)
- **definition:** thing; object; creature; matter
- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow; ox
  - 勿
    - wù
    - do not; must not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 牛 + phonetic 勿 (OC *mɯd)
  - **interpretation:** Combines the semantic element 牛 ‘cow’ hinting at concrete beings or goods, with the phonetic 勿 to suggest the pronunciation. The sense broadened from livestock or tangible creatures to the general idea of things, objects, and matters.
- **examples:**
  - 我对未知的事物很好奇(我對未知的事物很好奇)。
    - Wǒ duì wèizhī de shìwù hěn hàoqí.
    - I am very curious about unknown things.
  - 这件物品很贵(這件物品很貴)。
    - Zhè jiàn wùpǐn hěn guì.
    - This item is expensive.
  - 动物需要食物和水(動物需要食物和水)。
    - Dòngwù xūyào shíwù hé shuǐ.
    - Animals need food and water.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** cow; ox
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: bull’s head with two horns and two ears; perhaps unrelated to 告; unrelated to 先.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bull’s head with prominent horns and ears, a direct visual rendering of the animal. Stylization over time simplified the lines, but the bovine shape remains evident.
- **examples:**
  - 这头牛很壮(這頭牛很壯)。
    - Zhè tóu niú hěn zhuàng.
    - This ox is strong.
  - 牛奶很新鲜(牛奶很新鮮)。
    - Niúnǎi hěn xīnxiān.
    - The milk is very fresh.
  - 牧民在草原上赶牛(牧民在草原上趕牛)。
    - Mùmín zài cǎoyuán shàng gǎn niú.
    - The herders drive cattle on the grassland.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物) → 勿(勿)
- **definition:** do not; must not
- **pinyin:** wù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram; rebus (phonetic loan)
  - **description:** Pictogram: blood on a knife; originally the graph for 刎 (“to cut”). Later jiajie (假借) for 勿 (“do not”) from oracle-bone times.
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicted a bloody blade referring to 刎 ‘to cut (the throat)’. Because the sound was similar, the graph was borrowed to write the negating word ‘do not’, which became its standard meaning. It is unrelated to the right part of a variant of 利 (𥝢 → 黎) and to 匆.
- **examples:**
  - 请勿吸烟(請勿吸煙)。
    - Qǐng wù xīyān.
    - Please do not smoke.
  - 勿忘初衷(勿忘初衷)。
    - Wù wàng chūzhōng.
    - Do not forget your original intention.
  - 非工作人员勿入(非工作人員勿入)。
    - Fēi gōngzuò rényuán wù rù.
    - Non-staff, do not enter.
