## pet
### chǒngwù
---
- **definition:** pet
- **pinyin:** chǒngwù
- **components:**
  - 宠(寵)
    - chǒng
    - favor; pamper
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing; object
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 宠(寵) (chǒng, "favor; pamper") + 物(物) (wù, "thing; object") =
    - favored thing ->
    - pet
  - **interpretation:** The word 宠物(寵物) (chǒngwù, "pet") combines 宠(寵) (chǒng, "favor; pamper") and 物(物) (wù, "thing; object"), reflecting the idea of an animal being a favored or pampered object in a household.
  - **simplification:** traditional 寵 simplified to 宠
- **examples:**
  - 我家养了一只宠物(我家養了一隻寵物)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǎng le yī zhī chǒngwù.
    - My family has a pet.
  - 宠物给我很多快乐(寵物給我很多快樂)，它们总是陪伴我(它們總是陪伴我)。
    - Chǒngwù gěi wǒ hěn duō kuàilè, tāmen zǒng shì péibàn wǒ.
    - Pets bring me a lot of joy, and they always accompany me.
  - 我喜欢照顾宠物(我喜歡照顧寵物)，因为它们让我感到温暖(因為它們讓我感到溫暖)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhàogù chǒngwù, yīnwèi tāmen ràng wǒ gǎn dào wēnnuǎn.
    - I like taking care of pets because they make me feel warm.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵)
- **definition:** favor; pamper
- **pinyin:** chǒng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 龍(龙)
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 宀(宀) (mián, "roof") + phonetic: 龍(龙) (lóng, "dragon") =
    - protection under a roof granted to a revered creature ->
    - favor; pamper
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the sheltering element 宀(宀) (mián, "roof") with the phonetic and majestic 龍(龙) (lóng, "dragon"), conveying the idea of special attention or privilege granted to someone cherished, like a dragon sheltered in a house.
  - **simplification:** 寵(寵) was simplified to 宠(宠) by reducing 龍(龙) to a more cursive and phonetic form that still resembles the association with protection or favor.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈宠孩子(媽媽寵孩子)。
    - māmā chǒng háizi.
    - Mother pampers her child.
  - 他宠女儿(他寵女兒)，带她去游乐园(帶她去遊樂園)。
    - tā chǒng nǚér, dài tā qù yóulèyuán.
    - He dotes on his daughter and takes her to the amusement park.
  - 李先生宠爱宠物(李先生寵愛寵物)。
    - lǐ xiānshēng chǒng'ài chǒngwù.
    - Mr. Li spoils his pet.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉字代表家的温暖(含有宀的漢字代表家的溫暖)。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.
    - Chinese characters that include the radical '宀' represent the warmth of home.
  - 屋顶遮风挡雨(屋頂遮風擋雨)，家园散发温馨(家園散發溫馨)。
    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
    - The roof shields from wind and rain, and the home radiates warmth.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵) → 龍(龍)
- **definition:** dragon
- **pinyin:** lóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized dragon with exaggerated features, including a body, claws, and tail.
  - **interpretation:** The character 龍(龍) (lóng, "dragon") represents a mythical creature in Chinese folklore, known for its power, majesty, and symbolic links to strength and good fortune. Its stylized depiction emphasizes these attributes.
- **examples:**
  - 古老的传说里有一条龍(古老的傳說裡有一條龙)，它能呼风唤雨(它能呼風喚雨)。
    - Gǔlǎo de chuánshuō lǐ yǒu yī tiáo lóng, tā néng hū fēng huàn yǔ。
    - In ancient legends, there is a dragon; it can summon wind and rain.
  - 节日里舞龍表演吸引众人(節日裡舞龙表演吸引眾人)。
    - Jiérì lǐ wǔ lóng biǎoyǎn xīyǐn zhòngrén。
    - During festivals, the dragon dance performance attracts everyone.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物)
- **definition:** thing; object
- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 牛
    - niú
    - ox
  - 勿
    - wù
    - do not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 牛(牛) (niú, 'ox') + phonetic: 勿(勿) (wù, 'do not') =
    - items related to oxen ->
    - generic 'thing' or 'object'
  - **interpretation:** The character 物 evolved from the combination of an ox, a familiar and essential domestic animal, and 勿(勿) (wù), which serves as the phonetic component. This suggests a broad category of things concerning daily life and utility.
- **examples:**
  - 这个物引起了人们的注意(這個物引起了人們的注意)。
    - Zhège wù yǐnqǐ le rénmen de zhùyì.
    - This object attracted people's attention.
  - 古代的学者重视物理现象(古代的學者重視物理現象)，探讨物质的本原(探討物質的本原)。
    - Gǔdài de xuézhě zhòngshì wùlǐ xiànxiàng, tàntǎo wùzhì de běnyuán.
    - Ancient scholars valued physical phenomena, exploring the essence of matter.
  - 生活中的每种物都承载着记忆(生活中的每種物都承載著記憶)。
    - Shēnghuó zhōng de měi zhǒng wù dōu chéngzài zhe jìyì.
    - Every object in life carries memories.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** ox
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the head and horns of an ox
  - **interpretation:** The character 牛(牛) (niú, "ox") is a pictogram representing the head and horns of an ox, reflecting its importance in agriculture and daily life in ancient China.
- **examples:**
  - 农民牵着牛(農民牽著牛)，去市场买东西(去市場買東西)。
    - Nóngmín qiānzhe niú，qù shìchǎng mǎi dōngxī。
    - The farmer leads the ox and goes to the market to buy things.
  - 农家养牛(農家養牛)，生活更加富足(生活更加富足)。
    - Nóngjiā yǎng niú，shēnghuó gèngjiā fùzú。
    - Rural families raise oxen, making their lives more prosperous.
  - 这个牛非常听话(這個牛非常聽話)。
    - Zhège niú fēicháng tīnghuà。
    - This ox is very obedient.
### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物) → 勿(勿)
- **definition:** do not
- **pinyin:** wù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts blood on a knife ->
    - original character of 刎 (wěn, "to cut") ->
    - jiajie for 勿 (wù, "do not")
  - **interpretation:** The character 勿(wù), originally illustrated blood on a knife, visually represented the action of cutting, connecting to its initial meaning. Over time, it was borrowed to express prohibition or negation, showing the evolution beyond its pictorial roots.
- **examples:**
  - 进入图书馆时(進入圖書館時)，请勿大声喧哗(請勿大聲喧嘩)。
    - Jìnrù túshūguǎn shí, qǐng wù dàshēng xuānhuá.
    - When entering the library, please do not speak loudly.
  - 办公区内勿吸烟(辦公區內勿吸煙)。
    - Bàngōng qū nèi wù xīyān.
    - Do not smoke in the office area.
  - 行路时(行路時)，勿追逐打闹(勿追逐打鬧)。
    - Xínglù shí, wù zhuīzhú dǎnào.
    - When walking, do not engage in chasing and rough play.
