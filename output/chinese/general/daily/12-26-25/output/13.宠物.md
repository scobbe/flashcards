## 宠物(寵物)
---
## 
### pet
---
- **pinyin:** pet
- **components:**
  - 宠(寵)
    - chǒng
    - to pamper; to love
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing; object
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 宠(寵) (chǒng, 'to pamper; to love') + 物(物) (wù, 'thing; object') — a thing that is pampered
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to animals that are kept for companionship and are treated with affection. A 宠物(寵物) (chǒngwù, 'pet') is an animal that one shows love and care for.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一只狗作为宠物(我有一隻狗作為寵物)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī zhī gǒu zuòwéi chǒngwù.
    - I have a dog as a pet.
  - 她非常爱护她的宠物(她非常愛護她的寵物)。
    - Tā fēicháng àihù tā de chǒngwù.
    - She takes great care of her pet.
  - 宠物需要定期检查(寵物需要定期檢查)。
    - Chǒngwù xūyào dìngqī jiǎnchá.
    - Pets need regular check-ups.
### 宠物(寵物) → 宠(寵)
- **definition:** to pamper; to love
- **pinyin:** chǒng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 龍
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Contains the semantic component 宀 (mián, 'roof') indicating a house or dwelling, and the phonetic component 龍 (lóng, 'dragon') that gives it its sound.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of pampering is represented by a caring environment, such as a home, combined with the dragon, which symbolizes power and affection, suggesting a strong love for those within the household.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢宠物(我喜歡寵物)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chǒngwù.
    - I like pets.
  - 他总是宠爱他的孩子(他總是寵愛他的孩子)。
    - Tā zǒng shì chǒng'ài tā de háizi.
    - He always dotes on his children.

- **pinyin:** lóng

- **examples:**

### 宠物(寵物) → 物(物)

- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 兀
    - wù
    - to stand or to exist
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is composed of the phonetic component 兀 (wù) and the semantic component 牛 (niú, 'cow'). The combination suggests a connection to physical forms or substances.
  - **interpretation:** The cow may symbolize a tangible item, which aligns with the meaning of a material object or substance.
- **examples:**
  - 这件物品很有价值(這件物品很有價值)。

  - 我在检查这些物品(我在檢查這些物品)。
