### 宠(寵)物( ) → 宠(寵)
- **traditional:** 寵
- **definition:** favor; pamper
- **pinyin:** chǒng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 龍(龙)
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 宀 (mián, "roof") + phonetic: 龍(龙) (lóng, "dragon") =
    - protection under a roof granted to a revered creature ->
    - favor; pamper
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the sheltering element 宀 (mián, "roof") with the phonetic and majestic 龍(龙) (lóng, "dragon"), conveying the idea of special attention or privilege granted to someone cherished, like a dragon sheltered in a house.
  - **simplification:** 寵 was simplified to 宠 by reducing 龍(龙) to a more cursive and phonetic form that still resembles the association with protection or favor.
- **examples:**
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)宠(寵)孩( )子( )。

### 宠(寵)物( ) → 宠(寵) → 宀
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
