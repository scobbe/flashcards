### idiot
---
- **pinyin:** idiot
- **components:**
  - 笨
    - bèn
    - stupid; foolish; clumsy; dull
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg; ovum; spawn; testicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 笨(笨) (bèn, 'stupid; foolish') + 蛋(蛋) (dàn, 'egg') — stupid egg
  - **interpretation:** The term ‘笨蛋’ translates literally to ‘stupid egg,’ which is a metaphorical way to describe someone as foolish or silly. This makes sense as it combines a description of unskillfulness (笨) with a playful or dismissive term (蛋) that suggests fragility or lack of seriousness.
- **examples:**
  - 他真是个笨蛋！
    - Tā zhēn shì gè bèn dàn！
    - He is truly an idiot!
  - 不要做笨蛋的事。
    - Bù yào zuò bèn dàn de shì.
    - Don't do idiotic things.
  - 这笨蛋总是忘记重要的事情。
    - Zhè bèn dàn zǒng shì wàng jì zhòng yào de shìqíng.
    - This idiot always forgets important things.
