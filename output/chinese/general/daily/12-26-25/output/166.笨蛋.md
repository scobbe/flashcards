## idiot
### bèndàn
---
- **definition:** idiot
- **pinyin:** bèndàn
- **components:**
  - 笨
    - bèn
    - stupid
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 笨(笨) (bèn, "stupid") + 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg") =
    - a foolish or stupid person ->
    - idiot
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 笨(笨) (bèn, "stupid") with 蛋(蛋) (dàn, "egg"), suggesting someone whose thinking or actions are perceived as foolish, akin to calling someone a "dummy" in English.
- **examples:**
  - 你这个笨蛋(你這個笨蛋)，竟然不会做这道题(竟然不會做這道題)。
    - Nǐ zhège bèndàn, jìngrán bù huì zuò zhè dào tí.
    - You idiot, you actually can't solve this problem.
  - 他叫张明(他叫張明)，常常被人叫做笨蛋(常常被人叫做笨蛋)。
    - Tā jiào Zhāng Míng, chángcháng bèi rén jiào zuò bèndàn.
    - His name is Zhang Ming, and he is often called an idiot by others.
  - 看你做事这么笨(看你做事這麼笨)，真是个笨蛋(真是個笨蛋)。
    - Kàn nǐ zuòshì zhème bèn, zhēn shì gè bèndàn.
    - Watching how clumsily you work, you're really an idiot.

- **pinyin:** chóng
- **etymology:**
