## beetroot
### hóngcàitóu
---
- **traditional:** 紅菜頭
- **definition:** beetroot
- **pinyin:** hóngcàitóu
- **components:**
  - 红(紅)
    - hóng
    - red
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head; top
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 紅 (hóng, "red"), 菜 (cài, "vegetable"), 頭 (tóu, "head")
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 紅 (hóng, "red"), 菜 (cài, "vegetable"), and 頭 (tóu, "head") to describe a red-colored vegetable that is bulbous, which accurately depicts the appearance of a beetroot. Beetroot is known for its red root, resembling a round or bulbous shape.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃红(紅)菜头(頭)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī hóngcàitóu.
    - I like eating beetroot.
  - 夏天来临时(來臨時)，红(紅)菜头变(頭變)得格外鲜艳(鮮豔)。
    - Xiàtiān láilín shí, hóngcàitóu biàn dé géwài xiānyàn.
    - When summer arrives, the beetroot becomes exceptionally vibrant.
  - 农(農)田里种满(裡種滿)了各种(種)蔬菜，红(紅)菜头(頭)也不例外。
    - Nóngtián lǐ zhòng mǎn le gèzhǒng shūcài, hóngcàitóu yě bù lìwài.
    - The fields are filled with all kinds of vegetables, and beetroot is no exception.
