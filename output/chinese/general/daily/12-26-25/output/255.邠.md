### Bin (ancient place name)
---
- **pinyin:** Bin (ancient place name)
- **components:**
  - 分
    - fēn
    - divide
  - 邑
    - yì
    - city
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the phonetic 分(分) (fēn, 'divide') with the semantic 邑(邑) (yì, 'city') — indicating a city defined or divided by the phonetic element.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a city that has a specific identity tied to its phonetic component, suggesting a place known for its distinctiveness.
- **examples:**
  - 这就是邠国(這就是邠國)。
    - Zhè jiù shì Bīn guó.
    - This is the State of Bin.
  - 在中国古代，邠是一个重要的地方(在中國古代，邠是一個重要的地方)。
    - Zài Zhōngguó gǔdài, Bīn shì yīgè zhòngyào de dìfāng.
    - In ancient China, Bin was an important place.
### 邠(邠) → 分(分)
- **definition:** divide
- **pinyin:** fēn
- **components:**
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
  - 𠂇
    - fēn
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is composed of the semantic component 刀 (dāo, 'knife') which suggests cutting, and the phonetic component 𠂇 (fēn), indicating the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The inclusion of the knife suggests the action of cutting, which relates directly to the meaning of dividing. This visual representation makes it intuitive, as division often entails cutting something into parts.
- **examples:**
  - 我想分开那些书(我想分開那些書)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng fēnkāi nàxiē shū.
    - I want to divide those books.
  - 分配工作(分配工作)。
    - Fēnpèi gōngzuò.
    - Distributing work.

### 邠(邠) → 邑(邑)
- **definition:** city
- **pinyin:** yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Originally depicted a small settlement with a wall around it.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the image of a fortified settlement, symbolizing a community or town.
- **examples:**
  - 这个邑的经济发展很快(這個邑的經濟發展很快)。
    - zhège yì de jīngjì fāzhǎn hěn kuài.
    - The economy of this town is developing rapidly.
  - 古邑的遗址吸引了许多游客(古邑的遺址吸引了許多遊客)。
    - gǔ yì de yízhǐ xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - The ruins of the ancient town attract many tourists.
  - 他们在这个邑里生活了很多年(他們在這個邑裡生活了很多年)。
    - tāmen zài zhège yì lǐ shēnghuóle hěn duō nián.
    - They have lived in this town for many years.
