## Bin (ancient place name)
### Bīn
---
- **definition:** Bin (ancient place name)
- **pinyin:** Bīn
- **components:**
  - 分
    - fēn
    - divide
  - 邑
    - yì
    - city
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic 分(分) (fēn) + semantic 邑(邑) (yì, "city")
  - **interpretation:** The character 邠 combines 分(分) (fēn) to provide a phonetic cue with 邑(邑) (yì, "city") to indicate a location, reflecting its historical use as a place name.
- **examples:**
  - 学者们研究邠的历史(學者們研究邠的歷史)。
    - Xuézhěmen yánjiū Bīn de lìshǐ.
    - Scholars study the history of Bin.
  - 我走访古城(我走訪古城)，探寻邠的遗迹(探尋邠的遺蹟)。
    - Wǒ zǒufǎng gǔchéng, tànxún Bīn de yíjì.
    - I visit ancient cities and explore the relics of Bin.
  - 考古学家在邠发现了珍贵文物(考古學家在邠發現了珍貴文物)。
    - Kǎogǔ xuéjiā zài Bīn fāxiàn le zhēnguì wénwù.
    - Archaeologists have discovered valuable artifacts in Bin.
### 邠(邠) → 分(分)
- **definition:** divide
- **pinyin:** fēn
- **components:**
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
  - 八
    - bā
    - eight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 刀(刀) (dāo, 'knife') + 八(八) (bā, 'eight') =
    - cutting (knife) in two (eight-like separation) ->
    - divide
  - **interpretation:** The character 分(分) (fēn, 'divide') combines 刀(刀) (dāo, 'knife'), representing the act of cutting, and 八(八) (bā, 'eight'), symbolizing separation or division, suggesting the concept of dividing or splitting something into parts.
- **examples:**
  - 他把蛋糕分成均等的几份(他把蛋糕分成均等的幾份)，分享给了朋友(分享給了朋友)。
    - Tā bǎ dàngāo fēnchéng jūnděng de jǐ fèn, fēnxiǎng gěi le péngyǒu.
    - He divided the cake into equal portions and shared it with friends.
  - 老师将班级分成若干组(老師將班級分成若干組)。
    - Lǎoshī jiāng bānjí fēnchéng ruògān zǔ.
    - The teacher divided the class into several groups.
  - 她把时间分得很细(她把時間分得很細)。
    - Tā bǎ shíjiān fēn de hěn xì.
    - She divided her time very precisely.
### 邠(邠) → 分(分) → 刀(刀)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a knife or blade
  - **interpretation:** 刀(刀) (dāo, 'knife') resembles the shape of a knife with a handle and blade. It is a straightforward representation of the tool.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿着锋利的刀(他拿著鋒利的刀)，准备切肉(準備切肉)。
    - Tā názhe fēnglì de dāo, zhǔnbèi qiē ròu.
    - He holds a sharp knife, ready to cut meat.
  - 厨房里放着一把古老的刀(廚房裡放著一把古老的刀)，看起来非常锋利(看起來非常鋒利)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ fàngzhe yī bǎ gǔlǎo de dāo, kàn qǐlái fēicháng fēnglì.
    - In the kitchen, an old knife is displayed, and it looks very sharp.
### 邠(邠) → 分(分) → 八(八)
- **definition:** eight
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts two bent lines representing division ->
    - borrowed to mean 'eight' due to homonymy
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting the action of dividing, 八(bā, 'eight') was borrowed to represent the number eight because of a phonetic similarity, rendering its original meaning obsolete. The original meaning is preserved in characters like 分(fēn, 'divide') and 別(bié, 'separate').
- **examples:**
  - 他深吸一口八(他深吸一口八)。
    - Tā shēn xī yī kǒu bā.
    - He took a deep breath.
  - 她的八充满温柔(她的八充滿溫柔)，散发着诗意(散發著詩意)。
    - Tā de bā chōngmǎn wēnróu, sànfā zhe shīyì.
    - Her speech is full of tenderness and exudes a poetic charm.
  - 他轻轻讲出一段八(他輕輕講出一段八)，令听者陶醉(令聽者陶醉)。
    - Tā qīngqīng jiǎng chū yī duàn bā, lìng tīngzhě táozuì.
    - He softly delivered a speech that enchanted his listeners.
### 邠(邠) → 邑(邑)
- **definition:** city
- **pinyin:** yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a city
  - **interpretation:** The character 邑(邑) (yì, "city") visually represents an ancient walled city, with an enclosed area suggesting a settlement.
- **examples:**
  - 那座邑依山傍水(那座邑依山傍水)。
    - Nà zuò yì yī shān bàng shuǐ.
    - That city is nestled between mountains and water.
  - 他踏入邑时(他踏入邑時)，仿佛穿越到古代(仿佛穿越到古代)。
    - Tā tà rù yì shí, fǎng fú chuān yuè dào gǔ dài.
    - When he stepped into the city, it felt like traveling back to ancient times.
