- **definition:** engineer
- **pinyin:** gōngchéngshī
- **components:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
  - 程
    - chéng
    - process; journey
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - expert; teacher; master
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 工(工) (gōng, "work") + 程(程) (chéng, "process") + 师(師) (shī, "expert") =
    - work process expert ->

    - He is an engineer who is passionate about solving technical problems.
