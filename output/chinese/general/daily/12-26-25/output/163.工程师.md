### engineer
---
- **pinyin:** engineer
- **components:**
  - 工程
    - gōngchéng
    - engineering; project; work; construction
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - master; teacher; expert; skilled worker
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 工程(工程) (gōngchéng, "engineering") + 师(師) (shī, "master; teacher") — engineering master
  - **interpretation:** The term describes a professional who specializes in engineering, highlighting both their expertise in the field and their role as a practitioner or teacher. An 工程师 (gōngchéngshī) is someone skilled in designing and managing engineering projects.
- **examples:**
  - 这个工程师设计了一座桥(這個工程師設計了一座橋)。
    - Zhège gōngchéngshī shèjìle yīzuò qiáo.
    - This engineer designed a bridge.
  - 她想成为一名优秀的工程师(她想成為一名優秀的工程師)。
    - Tā xiǎng chéngwéi yī míng yōuxiù de gōngchéngshī.
    - She wants to become an excellent engineer.
  - 工程师负责这个项目的各个方面(工程師負責這個項目的各個方面)。
    - Gōngchéngshī fùzé zhège xiàngmù de gège fāngmiàn.
    - The engineer is responsible for various aspects of the project.
