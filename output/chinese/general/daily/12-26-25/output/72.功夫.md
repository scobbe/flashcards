## kung fu
### gōngfu
---
- **definition:** kung fu
- **pinyin:** gōngfu
- **components:**
  - 功
    - gōng
    - achievement; merit; work
  - 夫
    - fu
    - man; husband; fellow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 功(gōng, "achievement") + 夫(fu, "man") =
    - martial arts skill
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 功(gōng, "achievement") refers to mastery or skill, while 夫(fu, "man") suggests a practitioner. Together, they form the term 功夫(gōngfu, "kung fu"), which signifies the skill or achievement attained through the practice of martial arts.
- **examples:**
  - 李小龙擅长功夫(李小龍擅長功夫)。
    - Lǐ Xiǎolóng shàncháng gōngfu.
    - Bruce Lee excels in kung fu.
  - 他们在武馆学习功夫(他們在武館學習功夫)，以传承古老文化(以傳承古老文化)。
    - Tāmen zài wǔguǎn xuéxí gōngfu, yǐ chuánchéng gǔlǎo wénhuà.
    - They learn kung fu in the martial arts school to pass on the ancient culture.
### 功夫(功夫) → 功(功)
- **definition:** achievement; merit; work
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **components:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 工(工) (gōng, "work") + 力(力) (lì, "strength") =
    - work that requires strength ->
    - effort, achievement
  - **interpretation:** The character 功(功) (gōng, "achievement") combines 工(工) (gōng, "work") with 力(力) (lì, "strength") to symbolize work that requires effort and results in merit or achievement.
- **examples:**
  - 他通过努力工作取得了功(功)。
    - Tā tōngguò nǔlì gōngzuò qǔdé le gōng.
    - He achieved merit through hard work.
  - 他在比赛中表现出色(他在比賽中表現出色)，赢得了宝贵的功(功)。
    - Tā zài bǐsài zhōng biǎoxiàn chūsè, yíngdé le bǎoguì de gōng.
    - He performed brilliantly in the competition, earning valuable achievement.
### 功夫(功夫) → 功(功) → 工(工)
- **definition:** work
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bladed tool to plow ground or a carpenter’s square
  - **interpretation:** The character 工(gōng, "work") originates from the depiction of a tool used either for agriculture, like a plow, or for carpentry, such as a square, relating to the concept of 'work' or 'labor'.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天去工厂(他每天去工廠)，认真做工(認真做工)。

    - Tā xǐhuān yǔ gōngyǒu tǎolùn jìshù.
    - She enjoys discussing technology with her coworkers.
  - 老板鼓励工人多做工(老闆鼓勵工人多做工)，以提高效率(以提高效率)。
    - Lǎobǎn gǔlì gōngrén duō zuò gōng, yǐ tígāo xiàolǜ.

### 功夫(功夫) → 夫(夫)
- **definition:** man; husband; fellow
- **pinyin:** fu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 大(大) (dà, "big") with an added stroke on top to indicate a head ->
    - powerful person ->
    - man/husband
  - **interpretation:** The character 夫(夫) (fū, "man") resembles the character 大(大) (dà, "big") with an additional stroke on top to represent the head. It suggests a powerful or authoritative figure, naturally extended to mean "man" or "husband."
- **examples:**
  - 他是个好夫(他是個好夫)。
    - Tā shì gè hǎo fū.
    - He is a good husband.
  - 妻子尊敬着贤夫(妻子尊敬著賢夫)，家庭生活充满和谐(家庭生活充滿和諧)。
    - Qīzi zūnjìng zhe xián fū, jiātíng shēnghuó chōngmǎn héxié.
    - The wife respects her virtuous husband, and the family life is full of harmony.
