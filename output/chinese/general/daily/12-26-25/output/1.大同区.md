## Datong District, Taipei
### Dàtóng Qū
---
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **components:**
  - 大同
    - dàtóng
    - Datong; name of the district
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 大(大) (dà, "big") + 同(同) (tóng, "same") =
    - 大同(大同) (dàtóng, "Datong, name of the district") + 区(區) (qū, "district") =
    - 大同區(大同區) (Dàtóng Qū, "Datong District")
  - **interpretation:** Datong District in Taipei gets its name from 大同(大同) (dàtóng, "Datong") combined with 区(區) (qū, "district"), identifying it as a geographical area.
  - **simplification:** 區 is simplified to 区
- **examples:**
  - 大同区是台北市的一个历史悠久的地区(大同區是台北市的一個歷史悠久的地區)。
    - Dàtóng Qū shì Táiběi Shì de yīgè lìshǐ yōujiǔ de dìqū.
    - Datong District is a historic area in Taipei City.
  - 游客来到大同区(遊客來到大同區)，欣赏老街的风情(欣賞老街的風情)。
    - Yóukè lái dào Dàtóng Qū, xīnshǎng lǎo jiē de fēngqíng.
    - Visitors come to Datong District to admire the charm of its old streets.

### 大同区(大同區) → 大同(大同) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; great
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 人(人) (rén, "person") ->
    - borrowed for 大(大) (dà, "big")
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(大) (dà, "big") originally depicted a person standing, which later evolved to express the meaning 'big.' The resemblance to a person was used phonetically to convey largeness, not necessarily to portray outstretched arms as sometimes explained.
- **examples:**

### 大同区(大同區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") + 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") =
    - many people enclosed ->
    - district/area
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") with 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") to convey the concept of an area where multiple people or goods are contained, illustrating the concept of a district or area.
  - **simplification:** 區(區)→区(區): Simplified by reducing 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") inside 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") to suit modern writing styles.
- **examples:**
  - 他住在商业区(商業區)。
    - Tā zhù zài shāngyè qū.
    - He lives in the commercial district.
  - 城市里有两个区(區)，一个是商业区(商業區)，另一个是居住区(居住區)。
    - Chéngshì lǐ yǒu liǎng gè qū, yī gè shì shāngyè qū, lìng yī gè shì jūzhù qū.
    - The city has two districts, one is the commercial district and the other is the residential district.
  - 这个区(區)环境优美(環境優美)，吸引了很多游客(吸引了很多遊客)。
    - Zhège qū(區) huánjìng yōuměi(環境優美), xīyǐn le hěn duō yóukè(吸引了很多遊客).
