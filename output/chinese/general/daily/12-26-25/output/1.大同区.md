## Datong District, Taipei
### Dàtóng Qū
---
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **components:**
  - 大同
    - dàtóng
    - Great Unity; Datong
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region; zone
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大同(大同) (dàtóng, "Great Unity") is a Confucian utopian ideal from the Book of Rites (禮記/礼记), and 區(區) (qū, "district") refers to an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** The term 大同區 (大同區) (dàtóng qū, "Datong District") is named after a philosophical concept emphasizing harmony and unity. It symbolizes the aspiration for a harmonious community within the administrative area.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在大同区(大同區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dàtóng Qū.
    - I live in Datong District.
  - 大同区(大同區)历史悠久(歷史悠久)，街道繁华(街道繁華)。
    - Dàtóng Qū lìshǐ yōujiǔ, jiēdào fánhuá.
    - Datong District has a long history, and its streets are bustling.
  - 大同区(大同區)保留传统(保留傳統)，吸引游客(吸引遊客)。
    - Dàtóng Qū bǎoliú chuántǒng, xīyǐn yóukè.
    - Datong District preserves traditions and attracts tourists.

### 大同区(大同區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district; area; region; zone
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (enclosed space) + 品 (many people) =
    - many people within an enclosure ->
    - area, district
  - **interpretation:** The character 區(區) (qū, 'district') visually represents an area where many people reside, symbolized by 品(品) (pǐn, 'goods/people') enclosed within 匸(匸) (xì, 'enclosure'). This layout effectively conveys the idea of a defined space containing a community or district.
  - **simplification:** 區(區) was simplified to 区(區) by reducing 品(品) to 乂(乂) (yì, 'to manage'), keeping the enclosure meaning while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在繁华的区(我住在繁華的區)。

    - Ancient literati often sought inspiration in this region.

### 大同区(大同區) → 区(區) → 匸(匸)

  - **type:** pictogram

    - depicts an enclosure hiding something or someone ->
    - evolved to represent a concealed space or item
  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.
