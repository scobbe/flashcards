### Datong District, Taipei
---
- **pinyin:** Datong District, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 大同
    - dàtóng
    - big; large; same; together
  - 區
    - qū
    - area; district; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big, large") + 同(同) (tóng, "same, together") — big together
  - **interpretation:** 大同区 refers to a specific district in Taipei known for its vibrant community and culture. The name suggests a sense of large unity or harmony within the area.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在大同區。(我住在大同區。)
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dàtóng qū.
    - I live in Datong District.
  - 大同區有很多美食。(大同區有很多美食。)
    - Dàtóng qū yǒu hěn duō měishí.
    - Datong District has a lot of delicious food.
