## strongman
### dàlìshì
---
- **definition:** strongman
- **pinyin:** dàlìshì
- **components:**
  - 大力
    - dàlì
    - great strength; with great force; vigorously; strongly
  - 士
    - shì
    - man; person; specialist; warrior
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大力 + 士 = person of great strength.
  - **interpretation:** Denotes someone possessing exceptional physical power, like a circus strongman or weightlifter. Can also be used figuratively for someone who can take on heavy responsibilities.
- **examples:**
  - 那位大力士在马戏团里举起了巨大的杠铃(那位大力士在馬戲團裡舉起了巨大的槓鈴)。
    - Nà wèi dàlìshì zài mǎxìtuán lǐ jǔqǐ le jùdà de gànglíng.
    - That strongman lifted a huge barbell in the circus.
  - 他从小就被称为大力士(他從小就被稱為大力士)。
    - Tā cóng xiǎo jiù bèi chēngwéi dàlìshì.
    - He has been called a strongman since childhood.
  - 公司需要一个推进项目的“大力士”(公司需要一個推進項目的「大力士」)。
    - Gōngsī xūyào yí gè tuījìn xiàngmù de “dàlìshì”.
    - The company needs a ‘strongman’ to push the project forward.
### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力)
- **definition:** great strength; with great force; vigorously; strongly
- **pinyin:** dàlì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (big; great) + 力 (strength; force) = great strength; vigorously.
  - **interpretation:** Literally “great strength,” used adverbially to mean doing something vigorously or with great effort, and in some contexts as a noun phrase for mighty force. Common in formal policy/announcement language (e.g., 大力推进).
- **examples:**
  - 我们要大力支持科技创新(我們要大力支持科技創新)。
    - Wǒmen yào dàlì zhīchí kējì chuàngxīn.
    - We must strongly support scientific and technological innovation.
  - 政府正在大力推进绿色能源建设(政府正在大力推進綠色能源建設)。
    - Zhèngfǔ zhèngzài dàlì tuījìn lǜsè néngyuán jiànshè.
    - The government is vigorously advancing the development of green energy.
  - 他用大力把门推开了(他用大力把門推開了)。
    - Tā yòng dàlì bǎ mén tuī kāi le.
    - He used great force to push the door open.
### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力) → 大(大)
- **definition:**

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a person facing forward; original form of 夫, later phonetically borrowed for “big”.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicts a human figure viewed from the front, not necessarily with exaggerated outstretched arms. ### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力) → 力(力)
- **definition:** 
- **pinyin:** 
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): interpreted as a bent arm extending from the body, or as a wooden plough; original form 𠠲.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an arm applying force, a concrete image that came to represent strength and effort. Some traditions also saw it as a plough, linking power with labor in the fields.
- **examples:**
  - 他力气很大(他力氣很大)。
    - Tā lìqi hěn dà.
    - He is very strong.
  - 我们要尽力完成任务(我們要盡力完成任務)。
    - Wǒmen yào jìn lì wánchéng rènwù.
    - We must do our best to finish the task.
  - 科技给人类带来巨大力量(科技給人類帶來巨大力量)。
    - Kējì gěi rénlèi dàilái jùdà lìliàng.
    - Technology brings great power to humanity.
### 大力士(大力士) → 士(士)
- **definition:** man; person; specialist; warrior
- **pinyin:** shì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: war/ritual axe; later ‘soldier’ → ‘officer/intellectual’; related to 王, 圭, 金.
  - **interpretation:** Earliest forms depicted an axe, a symbol of martial power and authority, which naturally extended to mean a soldier and then an officer or learned gentleman. Connections to 王, 圭, and 金 reflect the ritual or jade/metal axe lineage in related graphs.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一名士兵(他是一名士兵)。
    - Tā shì yì míng shìbīng.
    - He is a soldier.
  - 他是个绅士(他是個紳士)。
    - Tā shì ge shēnshì.
    - He is a gentleman.
  - 古代的士人重视礼仪(古代的士人重視禮儀)。
    - Gǔdài de shìrén zhòngshì lǐyí.
    - In ancient times, scholar-gentry valued ritual propriety.
