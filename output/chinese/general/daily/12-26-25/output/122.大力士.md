### strongman
---
- **pinyin:** strongman
- **components:**
  - 大力
    - dàlì
    - great strength; strong; forceful; powerful
  - 士
    - shì
    - man; warrior; scholar; gentleman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大力(大力) (dàlì, 'great strength') + 士(士) (shì, 'man; warrior') — strong man
  - **interpretation:** The term 大力士 refers to a person with exceptional physical strength. It makes sense as it combines the idea of great power with the notion of a strong individual.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个大力士(他是一個大力士)，可以轻松举起重物(可以輕鬆舉起重物)。
    - Tā shì yī gè dàlì shì, kěyǐ qīngsōng jǔqǐ zhòngwù.
    - He is a strongman, able to easily lift heavy objects.
  - 这场比赛有很多大力士(這場比賽有很多大力士)，他们都很强壮(他們都很強壯)。
    - Zhè chǎng bǐsài yǒu hěn duō dàlì shì, tāmen dōu hěn qiángzhuàng.
    - This competition has many strongmen, and they are all very muscular.
### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力)
- **definition:** great strength; strong; forceful; powerful
- **pinyin:** dàlì
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; great; large; important
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength; power; force; energy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, 'big; great') + 力(力) (lì, 'strength; power') — great strength
  - **interpretation:** 大(大) (dà, 'big; great') emphasizes the intensity of 力(力) (lì, 'strength; power'). Together, they express the concept of considerable physical or figurative strength.
- **examples:**
  - 他有大力(他有大力)，能举重物(能舉重物)。
    - tā yǒu dàlì, néng jǔ zhòngwù.
    - He has great strength and can lift heavy objects.
  - 这项工作需要大力(這項工作需要大力)，很具挑战性(很具挑戰性)。
    - zhè xiàng gōngzuò xūyào dàlì, hěn jù tiǎozhàn xìng.
    - This task requires great effort; it is very challenging.
### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; great; large; important
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person facing forward, representing the original form of 夫 (夫) (fū, 'man') with a phonetic borrowing for 'big'.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a broad presence, emphasizing size and importance, akin to a person with a commanding stance.
- **examples:**
  - 这个房子很大(這個房子很大)。
    - Zhège fángzi hěn dà.
    - This house is very big.
  - 我有一个大计划(我有一個大計畫)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè dà jìhuà.
    - I have a big plan.
### 大力士(大力士) → 大力(大力) → 力(力)
- **definition:** strength; power; force; energy
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arm bending out from the side of the body or a wooden plough.
  - **interpretation:** The representation of an arm suggests physical strength and effort, aligning with the character's meaning of strength.
- **examples:**
  - 我有力量(我有力量)。
    - wǒ yǒu lìliàng.
    - I have strength.
  - 通过努力(通過努力)，我变得更强壮(變得更強壯)。
    - tōngguò nǔlì, wǒ biàn dé gèng qiángzhuàng.
    - Through effort, I have become stronger.
### 大力士(大力士) → 士(士)
- **definition:** man; warrior; scholar; gentleman
- **pinyin:** shì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** A war axe, which later evolved to represent 'soldier' and 'officer, intellectual'.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts an upside-down ritual axe, reflecting the association with military and governance roles historically.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位士(士)，很有学问。
    - Tā shì yī wèi shì, hěn yǒu xuéwèn.
    - He is a scholar, very knowledgeable.
  - 士(士)兵正在执行任务。
    - Shìbīng zhèng zài zhíxíng rènwu.
    - The soldier is carrying out a mission.
  - 古代士(士)人受人尊敬。
    - Gǔdài shìrén shòu rén zūnjìng.
    - In ancient times, scholars were respected.
