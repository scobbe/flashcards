## 毛衣(毛衣)
---
## 
### sweater
---
- **pinyin:** sweater
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; fur; feather; down
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes; garment; dress; coat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 毛(毛) (máo, 'hair') + 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes') — hair clothes
  - **interpretation:** This word makes sense because a 毛衣 (máoyī, 'sweater') is typically made from materials that have a soft, hair-like texture, providing warmth and comfort like a garment.
- **examples:**
  - 今天天气很冷，我需要穿毛衣。(今天天氣很冷，我需要穿毛衣。)
    - jīntiān tiānqì hěn lěng, wǒ xūyào chuān máoyī.
    - The weather is very cold today, I need to wear a sweater.
  - 她买了一件新的毛衣。(她買了一件新的毛衣。)
    - tā mǎile yī jiàn xīn de máoyī.
    - She bought a new sweater.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** hair; fur; feather; down
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts hair or fur, representing the fine, thin, and elongated structure of hair strands.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation directly relates to hair, capturing its essence in a simple form.
- **examples:**
  - 他的头发很长(他的頭髮很長)。
    - tā de tóufà hěn cháng.
    - His hair is very long.
  - 这件衣服的毛料很好(這件衣服的毛料很好)。
    - zhè jiàn yīfú de máoliào hěn hǎo.
    - The fabric of this clothing is very good.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 衣(衣)
- **definition:** clothes; garment; dress; coat
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a garment or clothing worn by a person.
  - **interpretation:** The character looks like a person wearing a robe, which intuitively represents 'clothes'.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢这件衣服(我喜歡這件衣服)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhè jiàn yīfú.
    - I like this piece of clothing.
  - 他每天都穿不同的衣服(他每天都穿不同的衣服)。
    - Tā měitiān dōu chuān bùtóng de yīfú.
    - He wears different clothes every day.
  - 这些衣服很便宜(這些衣服很便宜)。
    - Zhèxiē yīfú hěn piányí.
    - These clothes are very cheap.
