## sweater
### máoyī
---
- **definition:** sweater
- **pinyin:** máoyī
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - fur; hair
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 毛(毛) (máo, "fur; hair") + 衣(衣) (yī, "clothing") =
    - 毛衣 (毛衣) (máoyī, "sweater").
  - **interpretation:** The word 毛衣 (毛衣) (máoyī, "sweater") combines 毛(毛) (máo, "fur; hair") which suggests warmth, with 衣(衣) (yī, "clothing"), indicating that this is a type of garment. A 毛衣 (毛衣) (máoyī, "sweater") is thus a piece of clothing that provides warmth, akin to how fur or hair protects against cold.
- **examples:**
  - 他穿着红色毛衣(他穿著紅色毛衣)，感觉很温暖(感覺很溫暖)。
    - Tā chuānzhe hóngsè máoyī, gǎnjué hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - He wears a red sweater and feels very warm.
  - 冬天冷时穿毛衣(冬天冷時穿毛衣)。
    - Dōngtiān lěng shí chuān máoyī.
    - Wear a sweater when it is cold in winter.
  - 妈妈给我织了一件毛衣(媽媽給我織了一件毛衣)，让我感到温馨(讓我感到溫馨)。
    - Māmā gěi wǒ zhī le yī jiàn máoyī, ràng wǒ gǎndào wēnxīn.
    - Mom knitted a sweater for me, making me feel cherished.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** fur; hair
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts fur or hair
  - **interpretation:** The character 毛(máo, "hair") originally depicted strands of hair or fur with a central vertical line representing a strand. This visualization makes it intuitive as it clearly resembles an abstract form of hair or fur.
- **examples:**
  - 猫毛(貓毛)柔软(柔軟)。
    - Māo máo róuruǎn.
    - Cat fur is soft.
  - 他摘下了帽子(他摘下了帽子)，露出了浓密的头毛(露出了濃密的頭毛)。
    - Tā zhāi xià le màozi, lù chū le nóngmì de tóu máo.
    - He took off his hat and revealed his thick hair.
  - 小狗的毛(小狗的毛)软绵绵(软綿綿)，让人爱不释手(讓人愛不釋手)。
    - Xiǎo gǒu de máo ruǎn miánmián, ràng rén ài bù shì shǒu.
    - The puppy's fur is soft and cuddly, making one love it dearly.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 衣(衣)
- **definition:** clothing
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the outline of the chest and upper clothing ->
    - clothes
  - **interpretation:** The character 衣(yī, 'clothes') visually represents the shape of upper body garments by outlining the chest area. Despite its simplicity, this form effectively conveys the concept of clothing.
- **examples:**
  - 天冷时(天冷時)，你应该多穿衣服(你應該多穿衣服)。
    - Tiān lěng shí, nǐ yīnggāi duō chuān yīfú.
    - When it's cold, you should wear more clothes.
  - 旧衣服需要修补(舊衣服需要修補)。
    - Jiù yīfú xūyào xiūbǔ.
    - Old clothes need mending.
