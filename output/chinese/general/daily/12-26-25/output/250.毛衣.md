### máoyī
---
- **definition:** sweater
- **pinyin:** máoyī
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; fur
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 毛 (máo, "hair; fur") + 衣 (yī, "clothing") =
    - 毛衣 (máoyī, "sweater"), clothing made from hair or fur material
  - **interpretation:** A 毛衣 (máoyī, "sweater") is a piece of clothing typically made from materials like wool, which is derived from the hair or fur of animals. This reflects the original nature of sweaters being woven from natural fibers.
- **examples:**
  - 天( )气(氣)转(轉)冷( )时(時)，我穿上毛衣。
    - Tiānqì zhuǎn lěng shí, wǒ chuān shàng máoyī.

  - 冬( )天( )来(來)了( )，毛( )衣( )温(溫)暖( )舒( )适(適)。
    - Dōngtiān lái le, máoyī wēnnuǎn shūshì.
    - Winter has come, and the sweater is warm and comfortable.
  - 我( )喜( )爱(愛)这(這)件( )毛( )衣( )。
    - Wǒ xǐ'ài zhè jiàn máoyī.
    - I really like this sweater.
