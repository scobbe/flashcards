## sweater
### máoyī
---
- **definition:** sweater
- **pinyin:** máoyī
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; fur; wool; tenth of a yuan
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothing; clothes; garment; coat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 毛 (fur/wool) + 衣 (clothing) = woolen clothing (sweater)
  - **interpretation:** Literally “wool/fur clothing,” highlighting a garment made from animal fibers like wool. In modern usage it refers to sweaters and knit pullovers.
- **examples:**
  - 我冬天常穿毛衣(我冬天常穿毛衣)。
    - Wǒ dōngtiān cháng chuān máoyī.
    - I often wear a sweater in winter.
  - 这件毛衣很保暖(這件毛衣很保暖)，颜色也很好看(顏色也很好看)。
    - Zhè jiàn máoyī hěn bǎonuǎn, yánsè yě hěn hǎokàn.
    - This sweater is very warm, and the color looks great.
  - 奶奶亲手织了毛衣(奶奶親手織了毛衣)。
    - Nǎinai qīnshǒu zhī le máoyī.
    - Grandma knitted a sweater by hand.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** hair; fur; wool; tenth of a yuan
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – feather or hair.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a sketch of a tuft of hair or a feather, with strokes suggesting individual strands. The modern form is a streamlined version that still conveys the idea of fine hair or down.
- **examples:**
  - 他头上有很多细毛(他頭上有很多細毛)。
    - Tā tóu shàng yǒu hěn duō xì máo.
    - He has a lot of fine hair on his head.
  - 猫的毛很软(貓的毛很軟)。
    - Māo de máo hěn ruǎn.
    - A cat’s fur is very soft.
  - 他连一根毛也没动(他連一根毛也沒動)。
    - Tā lián yì gēn máo yě méi dòng.
    - He didn’t move a single hair.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 衣(衣)
- **definition:** clothing; clothes; garment; coat
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: outline of the chest and upper clothing; compare the upper part of 文. Can split to enclose another component.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a torso draped in a garment, giving the meaning “clothes.” In later formations it often acts as an enclosing frame or as the clothing component in compounds, embedding other elements in characters like 裹 and 襄.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天穿新衣(我今天穿新衣)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān chuān xīn yī.
    - I am wearing new clothes today.
  - 请把外衣挂起来(請把外衣掛起來)。
    - Qǐng bǎ wàiyī guà qǐlái.
    - Please hang up the outer garment.
  - 她的衣袖被风吹起(她的衣袖被風吹起)。
    - Tā de yīxiù bèi fēng chuī qǐ.
    - Her sleeve was lifted by the wind.
