## sweater
### máoyī
---
- **definition:** sweater
- **pinyin:** máoyī
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; fur
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 毛(毛) (máo, "hair; fur") + 衣(衣) (yī, "clothing") =
    - 毛衣(毛衣) (máoyī, "sweater"), clothing made from hair or fur material
  - **interpretation:** A 毛衣(毛衣) (máoyī, "sweater") is a piece of clothing typically made from materials like wool, which is derived from the hair or fur of animals. This reflects the original nature of sweaters being woven from natural fibers.
- **examples:**
  - 天气转冷时(天氣轉冷時)，我穿上毛衣(我穿上毛衣)。
    - Tiānqì zhuǎn lěng shí, wǒ chuān shàng máoyī.
    - When the weather turns cold, I put on a sweater.
  - 冬天来了(冬天來了)，毛衣温暖舒适(毛衣溫暖舒適)。
    - Dōngtiān lái le, máoyī wēnnuǎn shūshì.
    - Winter has come, and the sweater is warm and comfortable.
  - 我喜爱这件毛衣(我喜愛這件毛衣)。
    - Wǒ xǐ'ài zhè jiàn máoyī.
    - I really like this sweater.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** hair; fur
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a feather or a strand of hair
  - **interpretation:** The character 毛(máo, 'hair') visually represents a feather or hair, conveying the concept of something that is thin and soft.
- **examples:**
  - 猫的毛很柔软(貓的毛很柔軟)。
    - Māo de máo hěn róuruǎn.
    - The cat's hair is very soft.
  - 他早上醒来时(他早上醒來時)，发现额前飘落一缕毛(發現額前飄落一縷毛)。
    - Tā zǎoshang xǐng lái shí, fāxiàn é qián piāoluò yī lǚ máo.
    - When he woke up in the morning, he found a strand of hair floating on his forehead.
  - 他家小狗的毛非常蓬松(他家小狗的毛非常蓬鬆)。
    - Tā jiā xiǎogǒu de máo fēicháng péngsōng.
    - His puppy's hair is very fluffy.
### 毛衣(毛衣) → 衣(衣)
- **definition:** clothing
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a basic outline of a garment with sleeves hanging down.
  - **interpretation:** The character 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes') clearly resembles a simple garment, with parts representing sleeves and the body of clothing. This visual representation aligns with its meaning of 'clothes' or 'clothing.'
- **examples:**
  - 这件衣很特别(這件衣很特別)。
    - Zhè jiàn yī hěn tè bié.
    - This piece of clothing is very special.
  - 冬天来了(冬天來了)，人们换上了暖和的衣(人們換上了暖和的衣)。
    - Dōngtiān lái le, rénmen huàn shàng le nuǎn huó de yī.
    - Winter has arrived, and people put on warm clothes.
