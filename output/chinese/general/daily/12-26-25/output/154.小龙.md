## snake (zodiac)
### xiǎolóng
---
- **definition:** snake (zodiac)
- **pinyin:** xiǎolóng
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") + 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon") refers to a small dragon, which can metaphorically refer to a snake, especially in the context of the Chinese zodiac.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") with 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon") suggests a creature similar to a dragon but smaller in size, which in the context of the zodiac, relates to a snake.
- **examples:**
  - 今年小龙格外活跃(今年小龍格外活躍)。
    - Jīnnián xiǎolóng géwài huóyuè.
    - This year, the zodiac snake is particularly active.
  - 在中国传统文化中，小龙寓意着智慧(在中國傳統文化中，小龍寓意著智慧)，被视为祥瑞的象征(被視為祥瑞的象徵)。
    - Zài Zhōngguó chuántǒng wénhuà zhōng, xiǎolóng yùyì zhe zhìhuì, bèi shìwéi xiángruì de xiàngzhēng.
    - In Chinese traditional culture, the zodiac snake symbolizes wisdom and is regarded as an auspicious sign.
  - 传说中，小龙神出鬼没(傳說中，小龍神出鬼沒)。
    - Chuánshuō zhōng, xiǎolóng shén chū guǐmò.
    - According to legend, the zodiac snake appears and disappears mysteriously.
### 小龙(小龍) → 小(小)
- **definition:** small
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Three small dots ->
    - small
  - **interpretation:** The character 小(xiǎo, 'small') is an ideogram that originally used three small dots to visually represent the concept of smallness. Over time, these dots became elongated, maintaining the meaning of 'small.'
- **examples:**
  - 我有小猫(我有小貓)，它喜欢睡觉(它喜歡睡覺)。
    - Wǒ yǒu xiǎo māo, tā xǐhuān shuìjiào.
    - I have a small cat, it loves to sleep.
  - 这本书很小(這本書很小)。
    - Zhè běn shū hěn xiǎo.
    - This book is very small.
  - 小花在院子里玩耍(小花在院子裡玩耍)，笑声传遍四方(笑聲傳遍四方)。
    - Xiǎo Huā zài yuànzi lǐ wánshuǎ, xiàoshēng chuánbiàn sìfāng.
    - Little Hua plays in the yard, and her laughter spreads in all directions.
### 小龙(小龍) → 龙(龍)
- **definition:** dragon
- **pinyin:** lóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified form
  - **description:**
    - 𢅛 (variant with 龍 and 帝 elements) ->
    - 帝 element removed =
    - 尨 form ->
    - 彡 element simplified further =
    - 龙
  - **interpretation:** The character 龙 is a simplified version of the traditional 龍, achieved by removing complex strokes: initially through the removal of 帝, and further simplifying the 尨 component.
  - **simplification:** Removal of the character 帝 (dì, 'emperor') and simplifying the 彡 (shān, 'hair/bristles') component produced a more streamlined shape for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 龙在云中盘旋(龍在雲中盤旋)。
    - Lóng zài yún zhōng pánxuán.
    - The dragon spirals among the clouds.
  - 小镇传说有神龙(小鎮傳說有神龍)，守护村庄免遭灾祸(守護村莊免遭災禍)。
    - Xiǎo zhèn chuánshuō yǒu shénlóng, shǒuhù cūnzhuāng miǎn zāihuò.
    - Local legends tell of a divine dragon, guarding the village from calamity.
  - 古代诗人常以龙比才子(古代詩人常以龍比才子)，赋予他们神秘力量(賦予他們神秘力量)。
    - Gǔdài shīrén cháng yǐ lóng bǐ cáizǐ, fùyǔ tāmen shénmì lìliàng.
    - Ancient poets often compared talented individuals to dragons, bestowing upon them mysterious powers.
