## 小龙(小龍)
---
## 
### snake (zodiac)
---
- **pinyin:** snake (zodiac)
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little; minor; few
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon; imperial; fierce; mythical creature
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') + 龙(龍) (lóng, 'dragon') — small dragon
  - **interpretation:** The term '小龙' describes a smaller, youthful form of the dragon, which is often associated with strength and auspiciousness in Chinese culture. In the zodiac, it signifies a particular personality type marked by energy and vitality.
- **examples:**
  - 小龙在十二生肖中代表着好运。(小龍在十二生肖中代表著好運。)
    - Xiǎo lóng zài shí'èr shēngxiào zhōng dàibiǎo zhe hǎoyùn.
    - The Small Dragon represents good luck in the twelve zodiac signs.
  - 我属小龙，我的性格很活泼。(我屬小龍，我的性格很活潑。)
    - Wǒ shǔ xiǎo lóng, wǒ de xìnggé hěn huópō.
    - I am born in the year of the Small Dragon, and my personality is very lively.
### 小龙(小龍) → 小(小)
- **definition:** small; little; minor; few
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a small or small-scale object.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents something that is small or compact in size, resembling a dot or a short stroke.
- **examples:**
  - 这个房间很小(這個房間很小)。
    - Zhège fángjiān hěn xiǎo。
    - This room is very small.
  - 我有一个小猫(我有一個小貓)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè xiǎo māo。
    - I have a small cat.
### 小龙(小龍) → 龙(龍)
- **definition:** dragon; imperial; fierce; mythical creature
- **pinyin:** lóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a dragon, a symbol of power and authority in Chinese culture.
  - **interpretation:** The dragon symbolizes strength and is often associated with imperial power, representing greatness and auspiciousness.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from traditional 龙(龍) by stroke reduction, focusing on making the character easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 龙是中国文化的重要象征(龍是中國文化的重要象徵)。
    - Lóng shì zhōngguó wénhuà de zhòngyào xiàngzhēng.
    - The dragon is an important symbol in Chinese culture.
  - 在中国传统里，龙代表着权力(在中國傳統裡，龍代表著權力)。
    - Zài zhōngguó chuántǒng lǐ, lóng dàibiǎozhe quánlì.
    - In Chinese tradition, the dragon represents power.
