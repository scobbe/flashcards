## snake (zodiac)
### xiǎolóng
---
- **traditional:** 小龍
- **definition:** snake (zodiac)
- **pinyin:** xiǎolóng
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") + 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon")
  - **interpretation:** In the context of the Chinese zodiac, 小( )龙(龍) (xiǎolóng, "small dragon") refers to the "snake," symbolically representing a smaller version of a dragon. In Chinese culture, the dragon is a mythical creature symbolizing power and strength, and the snake is often seen as its earthly counterpart. By referring to the snake as a small dragon, it acknowledges the snake's link to the similarly agile and revered dragon.
  - **simplification:** 小龙
