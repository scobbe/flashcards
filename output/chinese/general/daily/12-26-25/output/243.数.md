### number; count
---
- **pinyin:** number; count
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** This character is composed of the semantic component 數 (shù, 'to count, number') and the phonetic component 於 (yú).
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively combines the meaning of counting with a phonetic element that aids in its pronunciation. This makes it clear that the character relates to the concept of numbers or counting.
  - **simplification:** The character replaced the traditional form 數 with its simplified form 数 by reducing the number of strokes for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一些数字(我需要一些數字)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yīxiē shùzì.
    - I need some numbers.
  - 请给我一个数字(請給我一個數字)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yīgè shùzì.
    - Please give me a number.
