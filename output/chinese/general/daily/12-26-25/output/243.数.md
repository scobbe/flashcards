## number; count
### shù
---
- **definition:** number; count
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 婁
    - lóu
    - bundle
  - 攴
    - pū
    - tap, hit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 婁(婁) (lóu, "bundle") + semantic 攴(攴) (pū, "tap, hit") =
    - counting taps or strokes ->
    - number, count
  - **interpretation:** 數(數) (shù) combines 婁(婁) (lóu, "bundle"), a component providing the sound, and 攴(攴) (pū, "tap, hit"), suggesting the repetitive action of counting or tallying. This aligns with its meaning related to numbers and counting.
  - **simplification:** 數(數) was simplified to 数(數) by reducing 婁(婁) (lóu, "bundle") to 攵(攵) (pū, "tap"), retaining the counting nuance but in a more concise form.
- **examples:**
  - 老师让学生数书本(老師讓學生數書本)。
    - Lǎoshī ràng xuéshēng shù shūběn.
    - The teacher asked the students to count the books.
  - 他数了所有的数字(他數了所有的數字)，然后写下答案(然後寫下答案)。
    - Tā shù le suǒyǒu de shùzì, ránhòu xiě xià dá'àn.
    - He counted all the numbers and then wrote down the answers.
  - 村民数着夜空中的星星(村民數著夜空中的星星)，讨论每个数字(討論每個數字)。
    - Cūnmín shù zhe yèkōng zhōng de xīngxīng, tǎolùn měi gè shùzì.
    - The villagers were counting the stars in the night sky while discussing each number.
### 数(數) → 婁(婁)
- **definition:** bundle
- **pinyin:** lóu
- **components:**
  - 樓
    - lóu
    - to gather
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 女(女) (nǚ, 'woman') + 樓(楼) (lóu, 'to gather') =
    - combines feminine element with the concept of gathering ->
    - bundle
  - **interpretation:** 婁(婁) (lóu, 'bundle') combines 女(女) (nǚ, 'woman') indicating a semantic role and 樓(楼) (lóu, 'to gather') as the phonetic aspect, suggesting the idea of gathering together.
- **examples:**
  - 他把麦子整理成一婁(他把麥子整理成一婁)。
    - Tā bǎ màizǐ zhěnglǐ chéng yī lóu.
    - He bundled the wheat.
  - 农民辛勤劳动(農民辛勤勞動)，将稻草捆成一婁(將稻草捆成一婁)。
    - Nóngmín xīnqín láodòng, jiāng dàocǎo kǔn chéng yī lóu.
    - The farmer worked hard, bundling the straw.
  - 清秋时节(清秋時節)，农家把刚收的麦捆成一婁(農家把剛收的麥捆成一婁)。
    - Qīngqiū shíjié, nóngjiā bǎ gāng shōu de mài kǔn chéng yī lóu.
    - In the cool autumn season, the farmer bundled the freshly harvested wheat.
### 数(數) → 婁(婁) → 樓(樓)
- **definition:** to gather
- **pinyin:** lóu
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 婁
    - lóu
    - gather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, 'wood') + 婁(婁) (lóu, 'gather') as phonetic =
    - wooden structure ->
    - multi-story building
  - **interpretation:** The character 樓(lóu) suggests a multi-story wooden structure, using 木(木) (mù, 'wood') for the material and 婁(婁) (lóu, 'gather') as the phonetic component to give the pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** 楼(樓): 婁(婁) simplified to keep phonetic function, but with fewer strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 朋友楼在一起(朋友樓在一起)。
    - péngyǒu lóu zài yìqǐ.
    - Friends gather together.
  - 家人楼在客厅(家人樓在客廳)，朋友也楼在餐厅(朋友也樓在餐廳)。
    - jiārén lóu zài kètīng, péngyǒu yě lóu zài cāntīng.
    - Family gathers in the living room, and friends gather in the dining room.
  - 学者楼在图书馆(學者樓在圖書館)。
    - xuézhě lóu zài túshūguǎn.
    - Scholars gather in the library.
### 数(數) → 婁(婁) → 樓(樓) → 木(木)
- **definition:** wood
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom
  - **interpretation:** 木(木) (mù, "tree") visually represents a tree with branches at the top and roots below. This pictorial depiction is straightforward, resembling the structure of a tree and its natural growth.
- **examples:**
  - 庭院里有一棵参天古木(庭院裡有一棵參天古木)。
    - Tíngyuàn lǐ yǒu yī kē cāntiān gǔ mù.
    - There is a towering ancient tree in the courtyard.
  - 他走到森林深处(他走到森林深處)，看见一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁(看見一棵孤零零的木矗立在路旁)。
    - Tā zǒu dào sēnlín shēnchù, kànjiàn yī kē gū línglíng de mù chùlì zài lùpáng.
    - He walked into the deep forest and saw a solitary tree standing by the roadside.
### 数(數) → 婁(婁) → 女(女)
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing ->
    - modern form reversed facing right
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") originally depicted a woman with pronounced breasts, illustrating the concept of 'woman.' Over time, the pictogram evolved to face right in its modern form, losing details like the left breast to simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有魅力的女(她是一位有魅力的女)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi yǒu mèi lì de nǚ.
    - She is an attractive woman.
  - 那位女战士英勇无畏(那位女戰士英勇無畏)，带领大家前行(帶領大家前行)。
    - Nà wèi nǚ zhàn shì yīng yǒng wú wèi, dài lǐng dà jiā qián xíng.
    - That female warrior is brave and fearless, leading everyone forward.
### 数(數) → 攴(攴)
- **definition:** tap, hit
- **pinyin:** pū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts hand (又) holding a tool ->
    - stylized as 卜
  - **interpretation:** The character started as a pictogram of a hand holding a tool, signifying an action to hit. Over time, the tool element became stylized into the component 卜, which is now recognized as the phonetic part.
- **examples:**
  - 他的攴很灵巧(他的攴很靈巧)。
    - Tā de pū hěn língqiǎo.
    - His hand is very dexterous.
  - 受伤后(受傷後)，他的攴失去了灵活性(他的攴失去了靈活性)，医生建议他康复(醫生建議他康復)。
    - Shòushāng hòu, tā de pū shīqùle línghuóxìng, yīshēng jiànyì tā kāngfù.
    - After the injury, his hand lost its agility, and the doctor advised him to recover.
