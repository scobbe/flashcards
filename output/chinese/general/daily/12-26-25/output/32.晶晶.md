## sparkling; gleaming
### jīngjīng
---
- **definition:** sparkling; gleaming
- **pinyin:** jīngjīng
- **components:**
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晶(晶) (jīng, "crystal") + 晶(晶) (jīng, "crystal"). The repetition of 晶(晶) (jīng, "crystal") emphasizes the sparkling or gleaming quality, akin to how crystals shine or glisten.
  - **interpretation:** The word reinforces the dazzling brightness or sparkle by repeating the character for 'crystal,' suggesting something that glimmers vividly.
- **examples:**
  - 钻石在阳光下闪耀出晶晶的光辉(鑽石在陽光下閃耀出晶晶的光輝)。
    - Zuànshí zài yángguāng xià shǎnyào chū jīngjīng de guānghuī.
    - The diamond shines with a sparkling brilliance under the sunlight.
  - 她的眼睛像闪烁的星光(她的眼睛像閃爍的星光)，透出晶晶的神采(透出晶晶的神采)。
    - Tā de yǎnjīng xiàng shǎnsuò de xīngguāng, tòuchū jīngjīng de shéncǎi.
    - Her eyes are like twinkling starlight, exuding a sparkling charm.
### 晶晶(晶晶) → 晶(晶)
- **definition:** crystal
- **pinyin:** jīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日(日) (rì, 'sun') triplicated =
    - multiple suns ->
    - brightness ->
    - crystal
  - **interpretation:** The character 晶(jīng) represents the intense brightness associated with having three suns, metaphorically capturing the lustrous and clear qualities of a crystal.
- **examples:**
  - 这颗晶闪烁着迷人的光辉(這顆晶閃爍著迷人的光輝)。
    - Zhè kē jīng shǎnshuò zhe mírén de guānghuī.
    - This crystal shines with enchanting radiance.
  - 这枚戒指中镶嵌着一颗晶(這枚戒指中鑲嵌著一顆晶)，显得格外璀璨(顯得格外璀璨)。
    - Zhè méi jièzhǐ zhōng xiāngqiàn zhe yī kē jīng, xiǎnde géwài cuǐcàn.
    - A crystal is inlaid in this ring, making it exceptionally brilliant.
