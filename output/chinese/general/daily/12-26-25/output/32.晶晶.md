## sparkling; gleaming
### jīngjīng
---
- **definition:** sparkling; gleaming
- **pinyin:** jīngjīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晶 (jīng, "sparkle") repeated for emphasis, indicating something that is sparkling or gleaming.
  - **interpretation:** The repetition of 晶 (jīng, "sparkle") emphasizes the brightness or gleaming quality of an object or light. In Chinese, repeating a character can intensify the meaning, suggesting something that is shining or reflecting light vibrantly.
- **examples:**

### 晶晶 → 晶
- **definition:** sparkle; sparkle; crystal; bright

- **components:**

### 晶晶 → 晶 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
