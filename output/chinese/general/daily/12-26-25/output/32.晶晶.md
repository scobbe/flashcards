## 晶晶(晶晶)
---
## 
### sparkling; gleaming
---
- **pinyin:** sparkling; gleaming
- **components:**
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal; sparkling; bright; clear
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal; sparkling; bright; clear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晶(晶) (jīng, "crystal") + 晶(晶) (jīng, "crystal") — crystal crystal
  - **interpretation:** The repetition of 晶(晶) emphasizes a sense of brightness and sparkling clarity. This duplication conveys an enhanced feeling of vividness, often associated with light and brilliance.
- **examples:**
  - 水面上闪烁着晶晶的光芒(水面上閃爍著晶晶的光芒)。
    - Shuǐmiàn shàng shǎnshuò zhe jīngjīng de guāngmáng.
    - The water surface sparkles with shimmering light.
  - 这颗宝石真晶晶(這顆寶石真晶晶)。
    - Zhè kē bǎoshí zhēn jīngjīng.
    - This gemstone is truly sparkling.
### 晶晶(晶晶) → 晶(晶)
- **definition:** crystal; sparkling; bright; clear
- **pinyin:** jīng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Triplication of 日(日) (rì, 'sun'), conveying brightness and clarity.
  - **interpretation:** The repetition of the character for the sun symbolizes an enhanced brightness, reflecting the clarity and sparkle characteristic of crystals.
- **examples:**
  - 这颗水晶很漂亮(這顆水晶很漂亮)。
    - Zhè kē shuǐjīng hěn piàoliang.
    - This crystal is very beautiful.
  - 晶莹剔透的玻璃让人赞叹(晶瑩剔透的玻璃讓人讚嘆)。
    - Jīngyíng tītòu de bōlí ràng rén zàntàn.
    - The sparkling glass is astonishing.
### 晶晶(晶晶) → 晶(晶) → 日(日)
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the round shape of the sun with rays emanating from it.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the sun, suggesting its bright, circular form and its importance in providing light.
- **examples:**
  - 今天是晴天(日)，阳光明媚。
    - jīntiān shì qíngtiān (日)，yángguāng míngmèi。
    - Today is a sunny day (sun), and the sunlight is beautiful.
  - 我喜欢早晨的阳光(日)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān zǎochén de yángguāng (日)。
    - I love the morning sunlight (sun).
  - 日出而作，日落而息。
    - rì chū ér zuò，rì luò ér xī。
    - Work at sunrise and rest at sunset.
