## pillow
### zhěntou
---
- **traditional:** 枕頭
- **definition:** pillow
- **pinyin:** zhěntou
- **components:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - pillow
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") originally referred to an object to rest your head, and 頭(头) (tóu, "head") specifies the purpose of the pillow, indicating it is for the head.
  - **interpretation:** 枕頭(头) (zhěntou, "pillow") combines the concept of a supporting object for the head, making it clear the item is meant to hold or rest the head during sleep or rest.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)用柔软(軟)的枕头(頭)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng róuruǎn de zhěntou.
    - I like to use a soft pillow.
  - 睡觉时(覺時)，我总(總)是需要一个(個)舒服的枕头(頭)。
    - Shuìjiào shí, wǒ zǒng shì xūyào yī gè shūfu de zhěntou.
    - When sleeping, I always need a comfortable pillow.
  - 他送给(給)我一个(個)新枕头(頭)。
    - Tā sòng gěi wǒ yī gè xīn zhěntou.
    - He gave me a new pillow.

### 枕( )头(頭) → 枕 → 冘 → 儋 → 擔 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞来(飛來)的球。

    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺术(藝術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细节(細節)。
