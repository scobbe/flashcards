## pillow
### zhěntou
---
- **definition:** pillow
- **pinyin:** zhěntou
- **components:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - pillow
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕(枕) (zhěn, "pillow") is the object upon which one rests their head while sleeping; 頭(頭) (tóu, "head") refers to the anatomical part, highlighting the primary use of the pillow for supporting the head during sleep.
  - **interpretation:** A 枕頭(枕頭) (zhěntou, "pillow") is essentially a 'head pillow,' which emphasizes its purpose as a cushion for one's head while resting or sleeping.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一个新枕头(我買了一個新枕頭)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yīgè xīn zhěntou.
    - I bought a new pillow.
  - 睡觉前他整理枕头(睡覺前他整理枕頭)，确保睡眠质量(確保睡眠質量)。
    - Shuìjiào qián tā zhěnglǐ zhěntou, quèbǎo shuìmián zhìliàng.
    - Before sleeping, he tidies his pillow, ensuring the quality of his sleep.
### 枕头(枕頭) → 枕(枕)
- **definition:** pillow
- **pinyin:** zhěn
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 冘
    - yín
    - to rest one's head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + 冘(冘) (yín, "to rest one's head") =
    - wooden object for resting head ->
    - pillow
  - **interpretation:** The character 枕(枕) (zhěn, "pillow") combines 木(木) (mù, "wood"), indicating the material, and 冘(冘) (yín, "to rest one's head"), suggesting its purpose as a support for the head, resulting in the meaning of a pillow.
- **examples:**
  - 我睡觉时喜欢用枕(我睡覺時喜歡用枕)。
    - Wǒ shuìjiào shí xǐhuān yòng zhěn.
    - I like to use a pillow when I sleep.
  - 夜晚躺下时(夜晚躺下時)，我喜欢把头放在舒服的枕上(我喜歡把頭放在舒服的枕上)。
    - Yèwǎn tǎng xià shí, wǒ xǐhuān bǎ tóu fàng zài shūfú de zhěn shàng.
    - When lying down at night, I like to place my head on a comfortable pillow.
  - 老人常用旧枕(老人常用舊枕)，回忆往昔(回憶往昔)。
    - Lǎorén cháng yòng jiù zhěn, huíyì wǎngxī.

### 枕头(枕頭) → 头(頭)
- **definition:** head
- **pinyin:** tóu
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 頁(页)
    - yè
    - head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") + semantic 頁(页) (yè, "head") ->
    - head
  - **interpretation:** The character 頭(頭) combines 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") which serves as a phonetic component, with 頁(页) (yè, "head") representing the meaning of "head." The combination effectively conveys the concept of a head using sound and meaning.
  - **simplification:** 頭(頭) is simplified to 头(頭) by shortening 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") to ⺀ (a cursive form), and 頁(页) (yè, "head") to 大 (simplified form).
- **examples:**
  - 他把书放在头上(他把書放在頭上)。
    - Tā bǎ shū fàng zài tóu shàng.
    - He placed the book on his head.
  - 妈妈教我如何洗头(媽媽教我如何洗頭)，并提醒我注意水温(並提醒我注意水溫)。
    - Māma jiào wǒ rúhé xǐ tóu, bìng tíxǐng wǒ zhùyì shuǐwēn.
    - My mother taught me how to wash my hair and reminded me to check the water temperature.
  - 那只狗好奇地抬起头(那只狗好奇地抬起頭)。
    - Nà zhī gǒu hàoqí de tái qǐ tóu.
    - The dog lifted its head curiously.

### 枕头(枕頭) → 头(頭) → 頁(頁)
- **definition:** head
- **pinyin:** yè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 首(首) (shǒu, "head") + 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person") =
    - head of kneeling person, emphasizing head
  - **interpretation:** The character 頁 originally depicted the head of a kneeling person, combining 首(首) (shǒu, "head") with the body addition of 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person"), emphasizing the concept of 'head'. It evolved to mean 'page' or 'leaf' as an alternative form of 葉 and retains its original meaning in the radical form.
  - **simplification:** 頁 was simplified to 页 by adopting a cursive script shorthand of the traditional form 頁.
- **examples:**
  - 书的最后一页(書的最後一頁)，写着作者的名字(寫著作者的名字)。
    - Shū de zuìhòu yī yè, xiězhe zuòzhě de míngzì.
    - The last page of the book bears the author's name.
  - 请翻到第十页(請翻到第十頁)，开始阅读(開始閱讀)。
    - Qǐng fān dào dì shí yè, kāishǐ yuèdú.
    - Please turn to the tenth page to start reading.
  - 这张杂志的封面在第三页(這張雜誌的封面在第三頁)，呈现了美丽的图案(呈現了美麗的圖案)。
    - Zhè zhāng zázhì de fēngmiàn zài dì sān yè, chéngxiàn le měilì de tú'àn.
    - The cover of this magazine on the third page displays a beautiful pattern.
