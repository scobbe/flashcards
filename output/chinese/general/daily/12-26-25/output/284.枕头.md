### pillow
---
- **pinyin:** pillow
- **components:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - to rest one's head; pillow; cushion; support
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head; top; chief; leader
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕(枕) (zhěn, 'to rest one's head') + 头(頭) (tóu, 'head') — head resting
  - **interpretation:** 枕头 refers to an object that supports the head during sleep or rest. This combination makes sense because a pillow is used specifically for resting one's head.
- **examples:**
  - 我的枕头很舒服(我的枕頭很舒服)。
    - wǒ de zhěn tóu hěn shū fu.
    - My pillow is very comfortable.
  - 她需要换一个新的枕头(她需要換一個新的枕頭)。
    - tā xū yào huàn yī gè xīn de zhěn tóu.
    - She needs to change to a new pillow.
