## pillow
### zhěntou
---
- **definition:** pillow
- **pinyin:** zhěntou
- **components:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - pillow; to rest the head on; cushion; bolster
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head; top/end; first/beginning; noun suffix/thing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕 (pillow; to rest the head) + 头 (noun suffix ‘head/thing’) = pillow
  - **interpretation:** Combines the base noun 枕 with the high‑frequency suffix 头 to denote a concrete object for resting the head. In speech 头 is often neutral tone here (zhěntou), highlighting its suffix function.
  - **simplification:** 頭 was simplified to 头 to reduce strokes for a very common suffix; 枕 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢软一点的枕头(我喜歡軟一點的枕頭)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan ruǎn yìdiǎn de zhěntou.
    - I like pillows that are a bit softer.
  - 旅行时我会带上自己的枕头(旅行時我會帶上自己的枕頭)。
    - Lǚxíng shí wǒ huì dài shang zìjǐ de zhěntou.
    - When traveling, I bring my own pillow.
  - 枕头太高会让脖子不舒服(枕頭太高會讓脖子不舒服)。
    - Zhěntou tài gāo huì ràng bózi bù shūfu.
    - A pillow that’s too high can make your neck uncomfortable.

### 枕头(枕頭) → 头(頭)
- **definition:** head; top/end; first/beginning; noun suffix/thing
- **pinyin:** tóu
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; great
  - 页(頁)
    - yè
    - page; leaf
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified from cursive form of 頭
  - **description:** Simplified from 頭: 豆 (phonetic) → ⺀; 頁/页 (semantic “head”) → 大. Traditional 頭 is phono-semantic: semantic 頁 + phonetic 豆.
  - **interpretation:** A cursive abbreviation of 頭 where the original phonetic 豆 was reduced to a dot-like stroke and the semantic 頁/页 was streamlined to 大. The simplification preserves overall silhouette while dropping the original phonetic-semantic clarity.
  - **simplification:** Cursive-derived stroke reduction to ease writing: 豆 → ⺀ and 頁/页 → 大; merges and streamlines parts of 頭.
- **examples:**
  - 我头疼了(我頭疼了)。

  - 他站在队伍的最前头(他站在隊伍的最前頭)。

    - He is standing at the very front of the line.
  - 这条路的尽头是海边(這條路的盡頭是海邊)。
    - Zhè tiáo lù de jìntóu shì hǎibiān.
    - At the end of this road is the seaside.
