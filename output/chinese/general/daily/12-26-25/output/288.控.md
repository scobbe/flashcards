## control; accuse
### kòng
---
- **definition:** control; accuse
- **pinyin:** kòng
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 空
    - kōng
    - empty
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") + 空(空) (kōng, "empty") =
    - hand reaching into emptiness ->
    - control or accuse
  - **interpretation:** The character 控(控) (kòng, "control; accuse") depicts a hand 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") reaching into or manipulating 空(空) (kōng, "empty"), symbolizing the act of controlling or accusing, as if grasping or managing emptiness.
- **examples:**
  - 学校加强对学生上网时间的控制(學校加強對學生上網時間的控制)。
    - Xuéxiào jiāqiáng duì xuéshēng shàngwǎng shíjiān de kòngzhì.
    - The school has strengthened its control over students' internet usage time.
  - 证据确凿时(證據確鑿時)，检察官便敢控诉嫌疑人(檢察官便敢控訴嫌疑人)。
    - Zhèngjù quèzáo shí, jiǎncháguān biàn gǎn kòngsù xiányírén.
    - When the evidence is irrefutable, the prosecutor dares to accuse the suspect.

  - **interpretation:** 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") functions as a shorthand or radical form used mainly in compounded characters, retaining the meaning of 'hand.' This facilitates the creation of characters that involve manual action or activities related to the hands.

### 控(控) → 空(空)
- **definition:** empty
- **pinyin:** kōng
- **components:**
  - 穴
    - xué
    - cave, hole
  - 工
    - gōng
    - worker
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 穴(穴) (xué, "cave, hole") + phonetic 工(工) (gōng) =
    - cave or structure, void inside ->
    - empty
  - **interpretation:** The character 空(kōng, "empty") combines 穴(穴) (xué, "cave, hole") as the semantic component, suggesting an open space or void, with 工(工) (gōng) as the phonetic element. This combination intuitively represents the concept of emptiness associated with a space or cavity.
- **examples:**
  - 房间很空(房間很空)。
    - Fángjiān hěn kōng.
    - The room is empty.
  - 书架很空(書架很空)，缺少许多书籍(缺少許多書籍)。
    - Shūjià hěn kōng, què shǎo xǔ duō shūjí.
    - The bookshelf is empty and lacks many books.
  - 他的心感到空(他的心感到空)，没有了温暖(沒有了溫暖)。
    - Tā de xīn gǎndào kōng, méiyǒu le wēnnuǎn.
    - His heart feels empty and has lost its warmth.
