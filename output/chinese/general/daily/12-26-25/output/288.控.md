### control; accuse
---
- **pinyin:** control; accuse
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 空
    - kōng
    - empty
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is composed of the semantic component 扌 (shǒu, 'hand') indicating an action related to hands, and the phonetic component 空 (kōng, 'empty').
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests an action performed by the hand that is related to absence or lack, which aligns with the meanings of 'control' and 'accuse'. For instance, controlling something can imply overseeing or managing, while accusing can often signify laying a claim on something absent.
- **examples:**
  - 我可以控诉他(我可以控訴他)，因为他做错了事情(因為他做錯了事情)！
    - Wǒ kěyǐ kòngsù tā, yīnwèi tā zuò cuòle shìqíng!
    - I can accuse him because he made a mistake!
  - 我们可以控住局势(我們可以控住局勢)，避免更多的损失(避免更多的損失)。
    - Wǒmen kěyǐ kòng zhù júshì, bìmiǎn gèng duō de sǔnshī.
    - We can control the situation to avoid further losses.
  - 他对她的控诉很有力(他對她的控訴很有力)，让人难以反驳(讓人難以反駁)。
    - Tā duì tā de kòngsù hěn yǒulì, ràng rén nán yǐ fǎnbó.
    - His accusation against her was powerful and hard to refute.
### 控(控) → 扌(扌)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of a hand.
  - **interpretation:** This character visually captures the essence of a hand, focusing on its central role in many actions and components of life.
- **examples:**
  - 我用手写字(我用手寫字)。
    - Wǒ yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - I use my hand to write.
  - 手很重要(手很重要)。
    - Shǒu hěn zhòngyào.
    - The hand is very important.
### 控(控) → 空(空)
- **definition:** empty
- **pinyin:** kōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of emptiness or void.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of a space that is void or lacking substance.
- **examples:**
  - 这个箱子是空的(這個箱子是空的)。
    - zhège xiāngzi shì kōng de.
    - This box is empty.
  - 他的房间空荡荡的(他的房間空蕩蕩的)。
    - tā de fángjiān kōngdàngdàng de.
    - His room is empty.
