## air bubble
### qìpào
---
- **traditional:** 氣泡
- **definition:** air bubble
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas; spirit
  - 泡
    - pào
    - bubble; foam; blister
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 气(氣) (qì, "air; gas") refers to air or gas, often considered the vital force in traditional Chinese thought. 泡 (pào, "bubble") signifies a bubble. Combined, 气(氣)泡 (qìpào) refers to an "air bubble" as it is a bubble filled with air.
  - **interpretation:** The word 气(氣)泡 (qìpào) directly combines the concepts of air and a bubble, clearly describing a bubble filled with air.
- **examples:**
  - 气(氣)泡在水中闪烁(閃爍)。
    - qìpào zài shuǐ zhōng shǎnshuò.
    - Air bubbles shimmer in the water.
  - 他喝汽水时(時)，欣赏气(賞氣)泡上升。
    - tā hē qìshuǐ shí, xīnshǎng qìpào shàngshēng.
    - He admires the rising air bubbles while drinking soda.
### 气(氣)泡( ) → 气(氣)
- **traditional:** 氣
- **definition:** air; gas; spirit
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 乞
    - qǐ
    - beg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Clouds flowing through the sky ->
    - altered to avoid confusion with 三 (sān, "three") ->
    - 乞 (qǐ, "beg") as a variant for phonetic borrowing
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(qì, "air") started as a pictogram representing clouds in the sky, which evolved to differentiate from the character for "three." Over time, 乞 was used as a variant for phonetic reasons, specifically for the meaning "to beg." The meanings 'air, flow' were later borrowed from 气.
- **examples:**
  - 房间里(間裡)的气(氣)很清新。
    - Fángjiān lǐ de qì hěn qīngxīn。
    - The air in the room is very fresh.
  - 打开(開)窗户(戶)，让(讓)新鲜(鮮)的气进来(氣進來)。
    - Dǎkāi chuānghù, ràng xīnxiān de qì jìnlái。
    - Open the window to let fresh air in.
  - 他深吸一口气(氣)，感觉(覺)心情舒畅(暢)。
    - Tā shēn xī yīkǒu qì, gǎnjué xīnqíng shūchàng。
    - He took a deep breath and felt his mood lift.
### 气(氣)泡( ) → 气(氣) → 三

- **pinyin:** sān
- **etymology:**

  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** The character 三 (sān, "three") is an ideogram depicting the concept of the number three through the use of three parallel horizontal strokes. This straightforward representation makes it consistent with related numeric characters like 一 (yī, "one") and 二 (èr, "two").

### 气(氣)泡( ) → 泡
- **definition:** bubble; foam; blister
- **pinyin:** pào
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 包
    - bāo
    - wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 包 (bāo) =
    - water-like appearance of foam ->
    - bubble; foam; blister
  - **interpretation:** The character 泡(pào, "bubble; foam; blister") combines 氵 (shuǐ, "water") to indicate its watery nature with 包 (bāo) providing the sound component, effectively representing the concept of foam or bubbles forming on water.
- **examples:**
  - 汤(湯)沸腾时会产(騰時會產)生很多泡。
    - Tāng fèiténg shí huì chǎnshēng hěnduō pào.
    - When the soup boils, many bubbles form.
  - 他的手被烫(燙)出一个(個)大泡，十分疼痛。
    - Tā de shǒu bèi tàng chū yīgè dà pào, shífēn téngtòng.
    - His hand developed a large blister from the burn, and it hurts a lot.
### 气(氣)泡( ) → 泡 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
