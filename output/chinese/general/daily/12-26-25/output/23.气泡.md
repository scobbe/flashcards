## air bubble
### qìpào
---
- **definition:** air bubble
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas
  - 泡
    - pào
    - bubble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 气(氣) (qì, "air; gas") + 泡(泡) (pào, "bubble") =
    - air bubble
  - **interpretation:** The term combines 气(氣) (qì, "air; gas") with 泡(泡) (pào, "bubble") to indicate a bubble that is filled with air or gas.
- **examples:**
  - 泡沫中的气泡(泡沫中的氣泡)闪烁(閃爍)。
    - Pàomò zhōng de qìpào shǎnshuò.
    - The bubbles in the foam shimmer.
  - 水面上冒出许多气泡(水面上冒出許多氣泡)，预示着热水即将沸腾(預示著熱水即將沸騰)。
    - Shuǐmiàn shàng mào chū xǔduō qìpào, yùshìzhe rèshuǐ jíjiāng fèiténg.
    - Many air bubbles emerged on the water surface, indicating that the hot water is about to boil.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air; gas
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Three horizontal strokes representing clouds ->
    - adjusted with a vertical line to avoid confusion with 三(三) (sān, "three") ->
    - 气(氣) (qì, "air") as flowing air
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(氣) (qì, "air") originated from a pictorial representation of clouds flowing in the sky, providing an intuitive association with the concept of air or gas.
- **examples:**
  - 燃气灶要定期检查(燃氣灶要定期檢查)。
    - rán qì zào yào dìngqí jiǎnchá.
    - The gas stove should be checked regularly.
  - 风吹进房间(風吹進房間)，新鲜气息扑面而来(新鮮氣息撲面而來)。
    - fēng chuī jìn fángjiān, xīnxiān qìxí pūmiàn ér lái.
    - The wind blows into the room, and a fresh breeze hits the face.
  - 释放毒气有害健康(釋放毒氣有害健康)。
    - shìfàng dú qì yǒuhài jiànkāng.
    - Releasing toxic gas is harmful to health.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡)
- **definition:** bubble
- **pinyin:** pào
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 包
    - bāo
    - wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 包(包) (bāo, "wrap") =
    - bubbles formed in water ->
    - bubbles
  - **interpretation:** The character 泡(pào) combines the water radical 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") with 包(包) (bāo, "wrap"), indicating the formation of a bubble, which can be seen as water wrapping around air. It effectively captures the nature of bubbles appearing in water.
- **examples:**
  - 孩子们在池塘边玩泡(孩子們在池塘邊玩泡)，笑声不断(笑聲不斷)。
    - Háizimen zài chítáng biān wán pào, xiàoshēng bùduàn.
    - Children play with bubbles by the pond, and laughter continuously fills the air.
  - 透明的泡在阳光下闪烁(透明的泡在陽光下閃爍)。
    - Tòumíng de pào zài yángguāng xià shǎnshuò.
    - Transparent bubbles shimmer under the sunlight.
  - 他喝汽水时见起大量泡(他喝汽水時見起大量泡)，感觉非常新奇(感覺非常新奇)。
    - Tā hē qìshuǐ shí jiàn qǐ dàliàng de pào, gǎnjué fēicháng xīnqí.
    - When he drank soda, a multitude of bubbles emerged, and he felt very intrigued.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** radical
  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the simplified radical form of 水, often used to signify water-related meanings when combined with other components in phono-semantic compounds.
- **examples:**
  - 请喝水(請喝水)。
    - Qǐng hē shuǐ.
    - Please drink water.
  - 我们需要水(我們需要水)，保持身体的平衡(保持身體的平衡)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào shuǐ, bǎochí shēntǐ de pínghéng.
    - We need water to maintain the balance of our bodies.
  - 水润万物(水潤萬物)，孕育生命(孕育生命)。
    - Shuǐ rùn wànwù, yùnyù shēngmìng.
    - Water nourishes all things and nurtures life.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡) → 包(包)
- **definition:** wrap
- **pinyin:** bāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 巳(巳) (sì, 'fetus') in 勹(勹) (bāo, 'womb') ->
    - swaddled baby ->
    - wrap
  - **interpretation:** The character 包(bāo, 'wrap') visually represents a fetus or baby wrapped, alluding to the concept of enclosing or containing, which extends to the modern interpretation of wrapping.
- **examples:**
  - 她认真地包饺子(她認真地包餃子)。
    - Tā rènzhēn de bāo jiǎozi.
    - She carefully wraps dumplings.
  - 你帮我把礼物包好(你幫我把禮物包好)，然后送给朋友(然後送給朋友)。
    - Nǐ bāng wǒ bǎ lǐwù bāo hǎo, ránhòu sòng gěi péngyǒu.
    - Please help me wrap the gift, then deliver it to a friend.
  - 他正在包粽子(他正在包粽子)，为端午节准备礼物(為端午節準備禮物)。
    - Tā zhèngzài bāo zòngzi, wèi Duānwǔ Jié zhǔnbèi lǐwù.
    - He is wrapping zongzi and preparing gifts for the Dragon Boat Festival.
