## 
### air bubble
---
- **pinyin:** air bubble
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas; breath; vital principle
  - 泡
    - pào
    - bubble; foam; soak; wet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 气(氣) (qì, "air") + 泡(pào, "bubble") — air bubble
  - **interpretation:** 气泡 refers to a bubble formed in a liquid, typically containing air. The combination is logical as it describes a bubble that consists of air.
- **examples:**
  - 水中有很多气泡(水中有很多氣泡)。
    - Shuǐ zhōng yǒu hěn duō qìpào.
    - There are many air bubbles in the water.
  - 我喜欢喝带气泡的饮料(我喜歡喝帶氣泡的飲料)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē dài qìpào de yǐnliào.
    - I like to drink carbonated beverages.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air; gas; breath; vital principle
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts clouds flowing through the sky, originally represented by three horizontal strokes to express the feeling of the sky.
  - **interpretation:** The image of clouds gives a sense of air and breath, representing something vital and ever-present in nature.
  - **simplification:** 气 is the simplified form of 氣, achieved through stroke reduction.
- **examples:**
  - 我觉得空气很清新(我覺得空氣很清新)。
    - Wǒ juédé kōngqì hěn qīngxīn.
    - I feel the air is very fresh.
  - 他们讨论气候变化(他們討論氣候變化)。
    - Tāmen tǎolùn qìhòu biànhuà.
    - They are discussing climate change.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡)
- **definition:** bubble; foam; soak; wet
- **pinyin:** pào
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 包
    - bāo
    - to wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic element 氵 (water) and the phonetic element 包 (to wrap).
  - **interpretation:** The water element indicates a relationship to liquids, while the phonetic element contributes a sound effect that evokes a sense of wrapping or encompassing, akin to how bubbles contain air.
- **examples:**
  - 洗澡时会产生泡(洗澡時會產生泡)。
    - xǐ zǎo shí huì chǎn shēng pào。
    - Bubbles will form while taking a bath.
  - 他喜欢喝泡水(他喜歡喝泡水)。
    - tā xǐ huān hē pào shuǐ。
    - He likes to drink bubbly water.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡) → 氵(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents water in its flowing form, emphasizing its liquid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
### 气泡(氣泡) → 泡(泡) → 包(包)
- **definition:** to wrap
- **pinyin:** bāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** 代表着包裹物体的概念，里外之间形成一种保护性包围。
  - **interpretation:** The character visually conveys the idea of encompassing or wrapping something, forming a protective shell around it.
- **examples:**
  - 我包了一份礼物(我包了一份禮物)。
    - Wǒ bāole yī fèn lǐwù.
    - I wrapped a gift.
  - 请把这些文件包好(請把這些文件包好)。
    - Qǐng bǎ zhèxiē wénjiàn bāo hǎo.
    - Please wrap these documents nicely.
