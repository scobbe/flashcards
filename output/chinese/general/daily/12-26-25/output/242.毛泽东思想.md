## Mao Zedong Thought
### Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng
---
- **traditional:** 毛澤東思想
- **definition:** Mao Zedong Thought
- **pinyin:** Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; surname Mao
  - 泽东(澤東)
    - Zédōng
    - Zedong; eastern marsh
  - 思想
    - sīxiǎng
    - thought; thinking
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 毛 (máo, "hair"; "surname Mao"), 泽(澤) (zé, "marsh"; "beneficial influence"), 东(東) (dōng, "east"; "surname Dong"), 思 (sī, "think"; "ponder"), 想 (xiǎng, "consider"; "wish")
  - **interpretation:** 毛泽东(澤東)思想 (Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng) refers to the ideological and political principles of Mao Zedong, the founding father of the People's Republic of China. It combines 毛泽东(澤東) (Máo Zédōng, "Mao Zedong") as the person and 思想 (sīxiǎng, "thought"; "thinking") to signify Mao's body of ideas and their influence on Chinese society and governance.
- **examples:**
  - 毛泽东(澤東)思想深刻影响(響)了中国历(國歷)史。
    - Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng shēnkè yǐngxiǎng le Zhōngguó lìshǐ.
    - Mao Zedong Thought profoundly influenced China's history.
  - 学习(學習)毛泽东(澤東)思想，可以了解革命精神。
    - Xuéxí Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng, kěyǐ liǎojiě gémìng jīngshén.
    - Studying Mao Zedong Thought helps to understand the revolutionary spirit.
  - 在课(課)堂上讨论(討論)毛泽东(澤東)思想，激发(發)了学(學)生热(熱)情。
    - Zài kètáng shàng tǎolùn Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng, jīfā le xuéshēng rèqíng.
    - Discussing Mao Zedong Thought in class stirred the students' enthusiasm.

- **traditional:** 澤東
- **definition:** Zedong; eastern marsh
- **pinyin:** Zédōng
- **components:**
  - 泽(澤)
    - zé
    - marsh; wetland; benefit; grace
  - 东(東)
    - dōng
    - east; host; landlord
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 澤(泽) (zé, "marsh") refers to a wetland or swampy area which nourishes and supports life. 東(东) (dōng, "east") indicates the direction of the sunrise, associated with beginnings.
  - **interpretation:** The word 泽东(澤東) (Zédōng) combines the concept of a life-nurturing marshland with the direction east. Symbolically, it can represent the idea of a fertile and sustaining area to the east. The name is famously associated with Mao Zedong, the former leader of China.
  - **simplification:** 泽 is the simplified form of 澤, and 东 is the simplified form of 東.
- **examples:**
  - 毛泽东(澤東)领导(領導)革命，创(創)建新中国(國)。
    - Máo Zédōng lǐngdǎo gémìng, chuàngjiàn xīn Zhōngguó.
    - Mao Zedong led the revolution and founded the new China.
  - 泽东(澤東)原指东边(東邊)的沼泽(澤)，后来(後來)成为(為)名字。
    - Zédōng yuán zhǐ dōngbiān de zhǎozé, hòulái chéngwéi míngzì.
    - Zedong originally referred to an eastern marsh and later became a name.

### 毛( )泽(澤)东(東)思( )想( ) → 泽(澤)东(東) → 东(東)
- **traditional:** 東
- **definition:** east; host; landlord
- **pinyin:** dōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Originally a pictogram of a bundle; simplified to 东 (dōng, "east") in cursive, mirroring 車 (chē, "vehicle") to 车
  - **interpretation:** The character 東 originally depicted a bundle of objects tied together, visually symbolizing the concept of 'east' through its form. As writing evolved, the character was simplified to 东, following a similar reduction from 車 to 车. This simplification focused on retaining core visual elements while removing complexity.
  - **simplification:** The simplification from 東 to 东 involved a reduction to a cursive shorthand form, parallel to 車 becoming 车.
- **examples:**
  - 太阳从东边(陽從東邊)升起。
    - Tàiyáng cóng dōngbiān shēngqǐ.
    - The sun rises from the east.
  - 风从东(風從東)面吹来(來)。
    - Fēng cóng dōngmiàn chuī lái.
    - The wind blows from the east.
