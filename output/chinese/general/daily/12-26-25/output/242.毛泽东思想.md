## Mao Zedong Thought
### Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng
---
- **definition:** Mao Zedong Thought
- **pinyin:** Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng
- **components:**
  - 毛泽东(毛澤東)
    - Máo Zédōng
    - Mao Zedong
  - 思想
    - sīxiǎng
    - thought; ideology; philosophy; doctrine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 毛泽东 (Mao Zedong) + 思想 (thought; ideology) = Mao Zedong Thought.
  - **interpretation:** Names the ideological system attributed to Mao Zedong, foundational in PRC political discourse. Often appears in formulations such as “马克思列宁主义、毛泽东思想”.
  - **simplification:** Proper-name characters were simplified in PRC orthography: 澤→泽, 東→东; 思想 unchanged. The simplifications reduce stroke count and standardize writing; traditional form 毛澤東思想 remains in Taiwan/HK.
- **examples:**
  - 毛泽东思想对中国政治产生了深远影响(毛澤東思想對中國政治產生了深遠影響)。
    - Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng duì Zhōngguó zhèngzhì chǎnshēng le shēnyuǎn yǐngxiǎng.
    - Mao Zedong Thought has had a profound impact on Chinese politics.
  - 许多课程讨论毛泽东思想的历史地位(許多課程討論毛澤東思想的歷史地位)。
    - Xǔduō kèchéng tǎolùn Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng de lìshǐ dìwèi.
    - Many courses discuss the historical status of Mao Zedong Thought.
  - 这本书系统阐述了毛泽东思想(這本書系統闡述了毛澤東思想)。
    - Zhè běn shū xìtǒng chǎnshù le Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng.
    - This book systematically expounds Mao Zedong Thought.
### 毛泽东思想(毛澤東思想) → 毛泽东(毛澤東)
- **definition:** Mao Zedong
- **pinyin:** Máo Zédōng
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - Máo
    - Mao (surname); hair; fur; coarse
  - 泽东(澤東)
    - Zédōng
    - beneficence to the East; grace to the East; marsh-east (lit.); to favor the East (lit.)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** personal name
  - **description:** 毛 (surname) + 泽东 (given name ‘beneficence to the East’) = Mao Zedong.
  - **interpretation:** Common surname paired with a two-character given name literally meaning ‘grace/beneficence to the East’. Refers to the 20th‑century Chinese leader, founder of the PRC.
  - **simplification:** 泽东 simplifies 澤東: 泽 is the simplified form of 澤 (water radical retained with reduced phonetic), and 东 simplifies 東 by streamlining interior strokes; PRC character simplification applied uniformly, including personal names.
- **examples:**
  - 毛泽东(毛澤東)是中国的革命家和政治家。
    - Máo Zédōng shì Zhōngguó de gémìngjiā hé zhèngzhìjiā.
    - Mao Zedong was a Chinese revolutionary and statesman.
  - 很多地名和机构以毛泽东(毛澤東)命名。
    - Hěn duō dìmíng hé jīgòu yǐ Máo Zédōng mìngmíng.
    - Many place names and institutions are named after Mao Zedong.
  - 学者们研究毛泽东(毛澤東)思想的历史影响。
    - Xuézhěmen yánjiū Máo Zédōng sīxiǎng de lìshǐ yǐngxiǎng.
    - Scholars study the historical impact of Mao Zedong Thought.
### 毛泽东思想(毛澤東思想) → 毛泽东(毛澤東) → 毛
- **definition:** Mao (surname); hair; fur; coarse
- **pinyin:** Máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – feather or hair.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a sketch of a tuft of hair or a feather, with strokes suggesting individual strands. The modern form is a streamlined version that still conveys the idea of fine hair or down.
- **examples:**
  - 他头上有很多细毛(他頭上有很多細毛)。
    - Tā tóu shàng yǒu hěn duō xì máo.
    - He has a lot of fine hair on his head.
  - 猫的毛很软(貓的毛很軟)。
    - Māo de máo hěn ruǎn.
    - A cat’s fur is very soft.
  - 他连一根毛也没动(他連一根毛也沒動)。
    - Tā lián yì gēn máo yě méi dòng.
    - He didn’t move a single hair.
### 毛泽东思想(毛澤東思想) → 毛泽东(毛澤東) → 泽东(澤東)
- **definition:** beneficence to the East; grace to the East; marsh-east (lit.); to favor the East (lit.)
- **pinyin:** Zédōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 泽(beneficence; favor) + 东(east) = beneficence to the East.
  - **interpretation:** Common as a masculine given name expressing the wish that one’s beneficence or blessings reach the eastern lands. Also usable literally in toponyms as “marsh to the east.”
  - **simplification:** 澤→泽 simplifies the complex phonetic component; 東→东 reduces interior strokes—both to lower stroke count in the PRC character reform.
- **examples:**
  - 他叫泽东(他叫澤東)。
    - Tā jiào Zédōng.
    - His name is Zedong.
  - 父母给他取名泽东(父母給他取名澤東)，寓意恩泽惠及东方(寓意恩澤惠及東方)。
    - Fùmǔ gěi tā qǔmíng Zédōng, yùyì ēnzé huìjí dōngfāng.
    - His parents named him Zedong, implying blessings benefiting the East.
  - 泽东一词(澤東一詞)在地名里也可表示东边的沼泽(在地名裡也可表示東邊的沼澤)。
    - Zédōng yīcí zài dìmíng lǐ yě kě biǎoshì dōngbiān de zhǎozé.
    - The term Zedong in place names can also mean a marsh to the east.
### 毛泽东思想(毛澤東思想) → 思想
- **definition:** thought; ideology; philosophy; doctrine
- **pinyin:** sīxiǎng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 思 think + 想 think/intend = thought; ideology.
  - **interpretation:** Combines two near-synonyms to intensify the sense of mental activity, extending from personal thinking to a system of ideas or ideology. Common in academic, political, and philosophical contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 他的思想很开放(他的思想很開放)。
    - Tā de sīxiǎng hěn kāifàng.
    - His thinking is very open-minded.
  - 政府重视青少年的思想教育(政府重視青少年的思想教育)。
    - Zhèngfǔ zhòngshì qīngshàonián de sīxiǎng jiàoyù.
    - The government values ideological education for adolescents.
  - 这本书探讨了自由与秩序的思想(這本書探討了自由與秩序的思想)。
    - Zhè běn shū tàntǎo le zìyóu yǔ zhìxù de sīxiǎng.
    - This book explores ideas about freedom and order.
