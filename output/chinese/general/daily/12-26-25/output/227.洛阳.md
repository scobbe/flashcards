## Luoyang
### Luòyáng
---
- **traditional:** 洛陽
- **definition:** Luoyang
- **pinyin:** Luòyáng
- **components:**
  - 洛
    - luò
    - a river; water
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun; positive principle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 洛 (luò, "a river or water") and 阳(陽) (yáng, "sun; positive principle"). 洛阳(陽) (luòyáng) is the name of a historical city in China, where it refers to a place located by the Luo River with a sunny or favorable position—often south of a river due to the association of 阳(陽) (yáng, "sun") with the south side of a hill or north of a river.
  - **interpretation:** 洛阳(陽) (luòyáng) is a historically significant city, once the capital of several Chinese dynasties. Its name reflects its geographic and auspicious positioning by the Luo River, emphasizing prosperity and centrality.
- **examples:**
  - 洛阳(陽)是历(歷)史名城。
    - Luòyáng shì lìshǐ míngchéng.
    - Luoyang is a historic city.
  - 春天来临时(來臨時)，洛阳(陽)迎来(來)繁花似锦(錦)。
    - Chūntiān láilín shí, Luòyáng yínglái fánhuā sì jǐn.
    - When spring arrives, Luoyang welcomes a profusion of flowers.
### 洛( )阳(陽) → 洛
- **definition:** a river; water
- **pinyin:** luò
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 各
    - gè
    - each
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, 'water') + phonetic: 各 (gè, 'each') =
    - flowing water with sound of 'gè' ->
    - river, water
  - **interpretation:** The character 洛(luò) combines the water radical 氵 to represent its meaning associated with water, and 各 for its phonetic component to determine pronunciation. It relates to flowing water like a river.
- **examples:**
  - 洛静静(靜靜)流淌。
    - Luò jìngjìng liútǎng.
    - The Luo flows quietly.
  - 春日融融照洛岸，柳絮纷飞(紛飛)。
    - Chūnrì róngróng zhào Luò àn, liǔxù fēnfēi.
    - The gentle spring sun illuminates the banks of the Luo, while the willow catkins flutter.
### 洛( )阳(陽) → 洛 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.

### 洛( )阳(陽) → 洛 → 各 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开(張開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, dàshēng hǎnjiào.
    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.

  - **description:** Depicts a footprint pointing down

### 洛( )阳(陽) → 阳(陽)
- **traditional:** 陽
- **definition:** sun; positive principle
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - sunshine
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 昜 (yáng, "sunshine") + Semantic: 阜 (fù, "hill") ->
    - Brightness or influence from a hill exposed to the sun, extended to mean "male" or "positive."
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 昜 (yáng, "sunshine") depicted sunlight. The addition of 阜 (fù, "hill") emphasizes exposure to sunlight on a hill, leading to the association with brightness or positivity.
  - **simplification:** 阳(陽) simplifies the traditional 陽 by replacing 昜 with 日 (rì, "sun"), reflecting the focus on sunlight in the character's meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他是家中的阳(陽)。
    - Tā shì jiāzhōng de yáng.
    - He is the male figure in the family.
  - 在这场运动会(這場運動會)上，他的阳(陽)性能量感染了队(隊)友。
    - Zài zhè chǎng yùndònghuì shàng, tā de yángnéng liàng gǎnrǎn le duìyǒu.
    - At this sports meet, his positive energy inspired his teammates.

### 洛( )阳(陽) → 阳(陽) → 昜 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来临时(來臨時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒满(灑滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.

### 洛( )阳(陽) → 阳(陽) → 昜 → 彡
- **definition:** rays of light
- **pinyin:** xiǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts three strands of hair ->
    - used to indicate three light rays in certain contexts
  - **interpretation:** 彡(shān, "rays") originally depicted strands of hair but also came to represent light rays, reflecting a change in symbolic meaning over time. Its use as a representation of 'ornament' and in characters like 昜 and 參 highlights its versatility in indicating rays or decorative elements.
- **examples:**
  - 天空洒(灑)下金色的彡，大地迎来温(來溫)暖。
    - Tiānkōng sǎ xià jīnsè de shān, dàdì yíng lái wēnnuǎn.
    - The sky showers golden rays, and the earth welcomes warmth.
  - 晨曦中的彡闪(閃)耀着(著)希望。
    - Chénxī zhōng de shān shǎnyào zhe xīwàng.
    - The rays in the morning light shine with hope.
