## Luoyang
### Luòyáng
---
- **definition:** Luoyang
- **pinyin:** Luòyáng
- **components:**
  - 洛
    - luò
    - river name
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun; positive; masculine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 洛阳(洛陽) has 洛(洛) (luò, "river name") and 阳(陽) (yáng, "sun; positive; masculine"). 洛(洛) is named after a river running through the area, while 阳(陽) suggests the northern bank of a river, being the sunny side.
  - **interpretation:** 洛阳(洛陽) (Luòyáng) is the name of a historical city in China, named for its location on the northern bank of the Luo River, a spot traditionally associated with sun and positivity.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算参观洛阳(我打算參觀洛陽)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn cānguān Luòyáng.
    - I plan to visit Luoyang.
  - 古都洛阳(古都洛陽)，见证了千年的文明(見證了千年的文明)。
    - Gǔdū Luòyáng, jiànzhèngle qiānnián de wénmíng.
    - The ancient capital Luoyang has witnessed a millennium of civilization.
  - 秋天来了(秋天來了)，洛阳的景色更加迷人(洛陽的景色更加迷人)。
    - Qiūtiān láile, Luòyáng de jǐngsè gèngjiā mírén.
    - Autumn has arrived, and Luoyang's scenery is even more enchanting.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** The character 洛(luò) refers to a river due to the semantic component 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") indicating its connection with water, and 各(各) (gè, "each") providing the phonetic aspect to form the sound of the name.

### 洛阳(洛陽) → 洛(洛) → 各(各) → 夂(夂)
- **definition:** sole of foot
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Footprint pointing down
  - **interpretation:** The character 夂(夂) (zhǐ, 'sole of foot') is a visual representation of a downward-pointing footprint, indicating the sole of a foot. Although the Shuowen interpretation suggests its relation to legs and a rearward approach, this is not supported by historical evidence from ancient inscriptions. Therefore, the main accepted interpretation remains a depiction of a footprint.
  - **simplification:** None, 夂 is the same in both simplified and traditional forms.
- **examples:**

  - 中医认为(中醫認為)，调理夂部位可促进血液循环(調理夂部位可促進血液循環)。

### 洛阳(洛陽) → 阳(陽)
- **definition:** sun; positive; masculine
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - sunshine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunshine") + 阜(阜) (fù, "hill") =
    - sunshine over hill ->
    - sun/masculine quality ->
    - 阝(阝) simplified form
  - **interpretation:** The character 阳(陽) (yáng, "sun") originally emphasized the idea of sunlight, represented by 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunshine"). 阜(阜) (fù, "hill") was added to highlight the sun's presence over a hill, symbolizing openness and positivity. The traditional character was simplified to 阳 (陽) by replacing 阜(阜) with 阝(阝), capturing the essence more concisely.
  - **simplification:** 陽(陽) was simplified to 阳(陽) by reducing 阜(阜) to its component 阝(阝), reflecting a cursive shorthand for the semantic radical.
- **examples:**
  - 温暖的阳光(溫暖的陽光)，照耀大地(照耀大地)。
    - Wēnnuǎn de yángguāng，zhàoyào dàdì。
    - The warm sunlight illuminates the earth.
  - 他散发着阳刚的气质(他散發著陽剛的氣質)，如同正午的阳光(如同正午的陽光)。
    - Tā sànfāzhe yánggāng de qìzhì，rútóng zhèngwǔ de yángguāng。
    - He exudes a masculine aura, like the midday sun.
  - 午后的阳气(午後的陽氣)，使人感到温暖(使人感到溫暖)。
    - Wǔhòu de yángqì，shǐ rén gǎndào wēnnuǎn。
    - The afternoon sun's vitality makes people feel warm.
