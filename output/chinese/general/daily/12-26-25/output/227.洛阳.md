- **pinyin:** Luoyang
- **components:**
  - 洛
    - luò
    - river; brook; flow
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun; positive; male; brightness
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 洛(洛) (luò, 'river') + 阳(陽) (yáng, 'sunshine') — river sunshine
  - **interpretation:** The name Luoyang combines elements referring to a river and sunlight, suggesting a place that is bright and closely connected to flowing waters. This name reflects the geographical features of the area, which has numerous rivers and a rich cultural history.
- **examples:**
  - 洛阳的牡丹很有名(洛陽的牡丹很有名)。
    - Luòyáng de mǔdān hěn yǒumíng.
    - The peonies in Luoyang are very famous.
  - 我想去洛阳旅游(我想去洛陽旅遊)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù Luòyáng lǚyóu.
    - I want to travel to Luoyang.

### 洛阳(洛陽) → 阳(陽)
- **definition:** sun; positive; male; brightness
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - sunshine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 阜 (hill) + phonetic 昜 (sunshine). The character originally referred to sunshine, with the addition of 阜 emphasizing its connection to the sun's positive aspects.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of brightness and elevation, associating the sun with positivity and energy. It reflects the duality of the sun being both a celestial body and a symbol of masculinity.
  - **simplification:** 陽(阳) (yáng, 'sun; male; positive') was simplified by reducing the strokes and using 日 (sun) to represent brightness more straightforwardly.
- **examples:**
  - 阳光明媚(陽光明媚)，生活充满希望(生活充滿希望)。

  - 阳光(陽光)洒在大地上(灑在大地上)。
    - Yángguāng sǎ zài dàdì shàng.
    - The sun shines down upon the earth.
