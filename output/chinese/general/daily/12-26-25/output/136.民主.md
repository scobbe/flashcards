## democracy
### mínzhǔ
---
- **definition:** democracy
- **pinyin:** mínzhǔ
- **components:**
  - 民
    - mín
    - people
  - 主
    - zhǔ
    - master; lord
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 民 (mín, "people"), 主 (zhǔ, "master; lord") - The term 民主 (mínzhǔ) represents the concept where the power or rule belongs to the people, aligning with the fundamental idea of democracy where citizens have a role in decision-making and governance.
  - **interpretation:** This combination signifies the concept of 'democracy,' emphasizing that the people are the primary holders of authority in the state.
- **examples:**
  - 人民渴望民主，追求公平正义(義)。
    - Rénmín kěwàng mínzhǔ, zhuīqiú gōngpíng zhèngyì.
    - The people long for democracy, striving for fairness and justice.
  - 民主促使社会进(會進)步。
    - Mínzhǔ cùshǐ shèhuì jìnbù.
    - Democracy fosters social progress.
### 民主 → 民
- **definition:** people
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an eye stabbed by a needle, symbolizing oppression of the people ->
    - people
  - **interpretation:** The character 民 (mín, 'people') originally depicted an eye being pierced by a tool, a metaphor for the suffering and experiences of the common people. This imagery evolved to represent the broader concept of the populace.
- **examples:**
  - 勤劳(勞)的民，构(構)成国(國)家的支柱。
    - Qínláo de mín, gòuchéng guójiā de zhīzhù.
    - Hardworking people form the backbone of the nation.
  - 民是国(國)家不可忽视(視)的力量。
    - Mín shì guójiā bùkě hūshì de lìliàng.
    - The people are an indispensable force in the country.
  - 民聚沙成塔。
    - Mín jù shā chéng tǎ.
    - People can achieve great feats through unity.
### 民主 → 主
- **definition:** master; lord
- **pinyin:** zhǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a torch with a flame on top, symbolizing guidance or leadership
  - **interpretation:** The character 主 (zhǔ, "master") represents a torch with a flame, symbolizing a guiding or leading figure, akin to a master or lord providing direction.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我们(們)家的主。
    - Tā shì wǒmen jiā de zhǔ.
    - He is the master of our family.
  - 僧侣(侶)虔诚祷(誠禱)告主。
    - Sēnglǚ qiánchéng dǎogào zhǔ.
    - The monks devoutly prayed to the Lord.
  - 臣子忠心辅(輔)佐主。
    - Chénzi zhōngxīn fǔzuǒ zhǔ.
    - The ministers loyally assist their master.
