## democracy
### mínzhǔ
---
- **definition:** democracy
- **pinyin:** mínzhǔ
- **components:**
  - 民
    - mín
    - people
  - 主
    - zhǔ
    - master; lord
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 民(民) (mín, "people"), 主(主) (zhǔ, "master; lord") - The term 民主(民主) (mínzhǔ) represents the concept where the power or rule belongs to the people, aligning with the fundamental idea of democracy where citizens have a role in decision-making and governance.
  - **interpretation:** This combination signifies the concept of 'democracy,' emphasizing that the people are the primary holders of authority in the state.
- **examples:**
  - 人民渴望民主(人民渴望民主)，追求公平正义(追求公平正義)。
    - Rénmín kěwàng mínzhǔ, zhuīqiú gōngpíng zhèngyì.
    - The people long for democracy, striving for fairness and justice.
  - 民主促使社会进步(民主促使社會進步)。
    - Mínzhǔ cùshǐ shèhuì jìnbù.
    - Democracy fosters social progress.
### 民主(民主) → 民(民)
- **definition:** people
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an eye stabbed by a needle, symbolizing oppression of the people ->
    - people
  - **interpretation:** The character 民(民) (mín, 'people') originally depicted an eye being pierced by a tool, a metaphor for the suffering and experiences of the common people. This imagery evolved to represent the broader concept of the populace.
- **examples:**
  - 勤劳的民(勤勞的民)，构成国家的支柱(構成國家的支柱)。
    - Qínláo de mín, gòuchéng guójiā de zhīzhù.
    - Hardworking people form the backbone of the nation.
  - 民是国家不可忽视的力量(民是國家不可忽視的力量)。
    - Mín shì guójiā bùkě hūshì de lìliàng.
    - The people are an indispensable force in the country.
  - 民聚沙成塔(民聚沙成塔)。
    - Mín jù shā chéng tǎ.
    - People can achieve great feats through unity.
### 民主(民主) → 主(主)
- **definition:** master; lord
- **pinyin:** zhǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a torch with a flame on top, symbolizing guidance or leadership
  - **interpretation:** The character 主(主) (zhǔ, "master") represents a torch with a flame, symbolizing a guiding or leading figure, akin to a master or lord providing direction.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我们家的主(他是我們家的主)。
    - Tā shì wǒmen jiā de zhǔ.
    - He is the master of our family.
  - 僧侣虔诚祷告主(僧侶虔誠禱告主)。
    - Sēnglǚ qiánchéng dǎogào zhǔ.
    - The monks devoutly prayed to the Lord.
  - 臣子忠心辅佐主(臣子忠心輔佐主)。
    - Chénzi zhōngxīn fǔzuǒ zhǔ.
    - The ministers loyally assist their master.
