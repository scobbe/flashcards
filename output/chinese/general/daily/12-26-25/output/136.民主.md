## 
### democracy
---
- **pinyin:** democracy
- **components:**
  - 民
    - mín
    - people; citizen; nation; subjects
  - 主
    - zhǔ
    - master; lord; host; main; primary
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 民(民) (mín, "people") + 主(主) (zhǔ, "master; lord; host") — people master
  - **interpretation:** This term combines 民(民) (mín, "people") with 主(主) (zhǔ, "master"), indicating a system in which the people have sovereignty and power. Hence, it reflects the concept of governance by the people's will.
- **examples:**
  - 我们生活在一个民主国家(我們生活在一個民主國家)。
    - wǒmen shēnghuó zài yīgè mínzhǔ guójiā。
    - We live in a democratic country.
  - 民主制度能确保人民的权利(民主制度能確保人民的權利)。
    - mínzhǔ zhìdù néng quèbǎo rénmín de quánlì。
    - The democratic system can ensure the rights of the people.
### 民主(民主) → 民(民)
- **definition:** people; citizen; nation; subjects
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of 'the people'; claimed to depict an eye pierced by a dagger.
  - **interpretation:** The imagery evokes vulnerability, reflecting the plight and struggles of people.
- **examples:**
  - 这个地区的民众很团结(這個地區的民眾很團結)。
    - Zhège dìqū de mínzhòng hěn tuánjié.
    - The people in this area are very united.
  - 政府听取了民意(政府聽取了民意)。
    - Zhèngfǔ tīngqǔle mínyì.
    - The government listened to the people's opinions.
  - 许多民众表达了不满(許多民眾表達了不滿)。
    - Xǔduō mínzhòng biǎodále bùmǎn.
    - Many citizens expressed their dissatisfaction.
### 民主(民主) → 主(主)
- **definition:** master; lord; host; main; primary
- **pinyin:** zhǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the idea of a 'master' or 'lord,' signifying authority or primary status.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively conveys the concept of leadership or being central since it embodies the role of someone who governs or holds control over others.
- **examples:**
  - 这个家是我主(這個家是我主)，我负责一切(我負責一切)。
  - 他是我的主(他是我的主)，我尊重他的意见(我尊重他的意見)。
  - 在这个项目中，我担任主(在這個項目中，我擔任主)，希望能成功(希望能成功)。
