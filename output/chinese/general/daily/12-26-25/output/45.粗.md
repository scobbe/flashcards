## coarse; rough
### cū
---
- **definition:** coarse; rough
- **pinyin:** cū
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 且
    - qiě
    - further
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 米 (mǐ, "rice") + phonetic 且 (qiě, "further") =
    - rice in abundance or coarseness ->
    - coarse; rough
  - **interpretation:** The character 粗(cū) combines 米 (mǐ, "rice") suggesting a grainy texture with 且 (qiě, "further") as the phonetic element, together implying something that is not finely processed, hence the meaning 'coarse; rough'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块(這塊)石头(頭)表面粗。
    - Zhè kuài shítou biǎomiàn cū.
    - The surface of this stone is coarse.
  - 他的表达(達)方式粗，让(讓)人难(難)以接受。
    - Tā de biǎodá fāngshì cū, ràng rén nányǐ jiēshòu.
    - His way of expression is coarse, making it hard for people to accept.
  - 这(這)根绳(繩)子编织(編織)得很粗，但却(卻)十分结实(結實)。
    - Zhè gēn shéngzi biānzhī de hěn cū, dàn què shífēn jiéshí.
    - This rope is woven roughly, yet it is very sturdy.
### 粗 → 米
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts rice kernels ->
    - 米(mǐ, 'rice')
  - **interpretation:** The character 米(mǐ, 'rice') visually represents dehusked rice kernels to differentiate it from 粟. The horizontal stroke may symbolize a bamboo sieve, emphasizing the process of separating grains from their husks.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃米饭(飯)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī mǐfàn.
    - I like to eat rice.
  - 她从农(從農)村带(帶)回一袋米，用来(來)煮饭(飯)。
    - Tā cóng nóngcūn dài huí yī dài mǐ, yòng lái zhǔ fàn.
    - She brought a bag of rice from the countryside to cook.
  - 市场(場)上有新米出售。
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu xīn mǐ chūshòu.
    - New rice is available in the market.
### 粗 → 且
- **definition:** further
- **pinyin:** qiě
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone altar or a sacrificial vessel used for chopping and holding meat
  - **interpretation:** The character 且(qiě, 'further') likely represents a stone altar or a sacrificial vessel from a top-down view, emphasizing its association with rituals or offerings. Its early usage linked it to the concept of ancestor worship or ritual practices, which later evolved to incorporate additional meanings.
- **examples:**
  - 他努力工作，且不断学习(斷學習)。
    - Tā nǔlì gōngzuò, qiě bù duàn xuéxí.
    - He works hard, and further, he keeps learning continuously.
  - 你且等待一会儿(會兒)，机会(機會)即将来临(將來臨)。
    - Nǐ qiě děngdài yīhuǐr, jīhuì jíjiāng láilín.
    - Please wait a moment, as further opportunity is about to arrive.
