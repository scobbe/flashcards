### coarse; rough
---
- **pinyin:** coarse; rough
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 且
    - qiě
    - and, moreover
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines semantic 米 (rice) meaning 'grain' with phonetic 且 (qiě). This suggests the rough texture associated with unpolished rice grains.
  - **interpretation:** The image of rough or coarse relates to the idea of raw or unrefined materials, like unprocessed rice, which has a rugged texture.
- **examples:**
  - 这件衣服有点粗。(這件衣服有點粗。)
    - Zhè jiàn yīfú yǒudiǎn cū.
    - This piece of clothing is a bit coarse.
  - 粗糙的表面不适合。
    - Cūcāo de biǎomiàn bù shìhé.
    - The rough surface is not suitable.
### 粗(粗) → 米(米)
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts rice kernels without husk.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents the grains of rice, illustrating them as small, separate entities. The horizontal stroke suggests a bamboo sieve, emphasizing the removal of the husk.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢米(我喜歡米)，常常做米饭(常常煮米飯)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān mǐ, chángcháng zuò mǐfàn.
    - I like rice, often making rice dishes.
  - 米是中国的重要粮食(米是中國的重要糧食)。
    - mǐ shì zhōngguó de zhòngyào liángshí.
    - Rice is an important staple in China.
### 粗(粗) → 且(且)
- **definition:** and, moreover
- **pinyin:** qiě
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts multiple interpretations, such as a stone altar or a sacrificial vessel viewed from above.
  - **interpretation:** These interpretations make sense as they relate to concepts of offerings and ancestors, emphasizing the connection between the physical altar and the act of sacrifice.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃饭且喝水(我喜歡吃飯且喝水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chīfàn qiě hē shuǐ.
    - I like to eat and drink water.
  - 他学习中文且很努力(他學習中文且很努力)！
    - Tā xuéxí zhōngwén qiě hěn nǔlì!
    - He studies Chinese and works hard!
