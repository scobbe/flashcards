## coarse; rough
### cū
---
- **definition:** coarse; rough
- **pinyin:** cū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") + phonetic 且(且) (qiě) =
    - rice pronunciation queued ->
    - coarse
  - **interpretation:** The character 粗(cū) combines 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") for its semantic association with granular texture, and 且(且) (qiě) for phonetic guidance. This pairing effectively suggests the texture of poorly polished rice grains, relating to the meaning of 'coarse' or 'rough.'
- **examples:**
  - 这束麻绳粗(這束麻繩粗)。
    - Zhè shù máshéng cū.
    - This hemp rope is coarse.
  - 他觉得纸张质地太粗(他覺得紙張質地太粗)，不能用于精细的书法(不能用於精細的書法)。
    - Tā juédé zhǐzhāng zhìdì tài cū, bùnéng yòng yú jīngxì de shūfǎ.
    - He thinks the texture of the paper is too coarse, making it unsuitable for fine calligraphy.
  - 古代工匠用粗糙的石块雕刻宫殿(古代工匠用粗糙的石塊雕刻宮殿)。
    - Gǔdài gōngjiàng yòng cūcāo de shíkuài diāokè gōngdiàn.
    - Ancient craftsmen carved palaces with rough stones.
