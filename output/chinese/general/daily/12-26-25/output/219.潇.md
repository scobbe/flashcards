## deep and clear (literary); brisk
### xiāo
---
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 萧(蕭)
    - xiāo
    - rustling sound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") + 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph) =
    - clear water characteristics ->
    - deep and clear water
  - **interpretation:** The character 潇 (瀟) combines 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") for its semantic component, indicating a relationship with water, with the phonetic 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" to convey the sound of flowing water. This reflects the ideas of clarity and briskness associated with moving water.
  - **simplification:** The character 潇 is the simplified form of 瀟, retaining the semantic 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") component while simplifying the complex 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" component by replacing it with the simpler 萧(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)", retaining its phonetic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 秋风潇洒(秋風瀟灑)。
    - qiū fēng xiāo sǎ
    - The autumn wind is brisk and elegant.
  - 他的步伐潇洒(他的步伐瀟灑)，眼神深远(眼神深遠)。
    - tā de bùfá xiāo sǎ, yǎnshén shēnyuǎn
    - His stride is brisk and relaxed, and his gaze is profound.
  - 湖面波光潇洒(湖面波光瀟灑)，映出天际长空(映出天際長空)。
    - húmiàn bōguāng xiāo sǎ, yìng chū tiānjì chángkōng
    - The shimmering waves on the lake reflect the vast, clear sky.
### 潇(瀟) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水(水) (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水是一种生命之源(水是一種生命之源)。
    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 夏天很热(夏天很熱)，喝水能解暑(喝水能解暑)。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺(河水潺潺)，沿岸绿树成荫(沿岸綠樹成蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭)
- **definition:** rustling sound
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 肖(肖) (xiāo, "resemble") =
    - grass rustling sound ->
    - rustling sound
  - **interpretation:** 萧(蕭) (xiāo) combines the grass radical 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") with the phonetic 肖(肖) (xiāo, "resemble") to signify the sound of wind rustling through grass. This character effectively conveys the auditory imagery associated with wind passing through vegetation.
  - **simplification:** 蕭(蕭)→萧(蕭): The component 肖(肖) (xiāo, "resemble") directly replaced 簫(箫) (xiāo, "end-blown flute") for simplicity, focusing on the phonetic aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 夜风吹过竹林时(夜風吹過竹林時)，传来阵阵萧声(傳來陣陣蕭聲)。
    - Yè fēng chuī guò zhúlín shí, chuán lái zhèn zhèn xiāo shēng.
    - When the night breeze blows through the bamboo grove, a rustling sound is heard.
  - 落叶纷飞时(落葉紛飛時)，耳边回响着萧的低语(耳邊迴響著蕭的低語)。
    - Luò yè fēn fēi shí, ěr biān huí xiǎng zhe xiāo de dī yǔ.
    - As the leaves flutter down, a rustling whisper echoes by the ear.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 艹(艹)
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写字时用艹作草字的偏旁(他寫字時用艹作草字的偏旁)。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(春天到來時)，艹生长得很快(艹生長得很快)。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 肖(肖)
- **definition:** resemble
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon/flesh
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") + Phonetic: 月(月) (yuè, "moon/flesh") =
    - small-like or resemble ->
    - resemblance
  - **interpretation:** 肖(肖) (xiào, "resemble") combines 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") for resembling something in a smaller form, with 月(月) (yuè, "moon/flesh") acting as the phonetic component. This suggests a likeness or resemblance, much like something that resembles but is smaller or of similar form.
- **examples:**
  - 他长得很肖他的父亲(他長得很肖他的父親)。
    - Tā zhǎng de hěn xiào tā de fùqīn.
    - He greatly resembles his father.
  - 那幅画中的人物肖古代仕女(那幅畫中的人物肖古代仕女)，散发着艺术的韵味(散發著藝術的韻味)。
    - Nà fú huà zhōng de rénwù xiào gǔdài shìnǚ, sànfā zhe yìshù de yùnwèi.
    - The figure in that painting resembles an ancient court lady and exudes artistic charm.
  - 她的笑容总是与花开的季节肖似(她的笑容總是與花開的季節肖似)，让人心情愉快(讓人心情愉快)。
    - Tā de xiàoróng zǒng shì yǔ huākāi de jìjié xiào sì, ràng rén xīnqíng yúkuài.
    - Her smile always resembles the blossoming season, uplifting people's moods.

### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 肖(肖) → 月(月)
- **definition:** moon/flesh
- **pinyin:** yuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a crescent moon
  - **interpretation:** The character 月(yuè, "moon") is a pictographic representation of a crescent moon, straightforwardly capturing the curved shape often associated with the moon's appearance. Though interchangeable with 夕 (xī, "evening") in the past, it distinctly represents the moon in this usage.
- **examples:**
  - 夜空挂着明月(夜空掛著明月)。
    - Yè kōng guà zhe míng yuè.
    - The bright moon hangs in the night sky.
  - 人们赏月(人們賞月)，寄托相思(寄託相思)。
    - Rén men shǎng yuè, jì tuō xiāng sī.
    - People enjoy the moon, expressing their longing.
