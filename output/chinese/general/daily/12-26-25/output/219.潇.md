## deep and clear (literary); brisk
### xiāo
---
- **traditional:** 瀟
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 萧(蕭)
    - xiāo
    - rustling sound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 水 (shuǐ, "water") + 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph) =
    - clear water characteristics ->
    - deep and clear water
  - **interpretation:** The character 潇 (瀟) combines 水 (shuǐ, "water") for its semantic component, indicating a relationship with water, with the phonetic 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" to convey the sound of flowing water. This reflects the ideas of clarity and briskness associated with moving water.
  - **simplification:** The character 潇 is the simplified form of 瀟, retaining the semantic 水 (shuǐ, "water") component while simplifying the complex 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" component by replacing it with the simpler 萧 (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)", retaining its phonetic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 秋( )风(風)潇(瀟)洒(灑)。
    - qiū fēng xiāo sǎ
    - The autumn wind is brisk and elegant.
  - 他( )的( )步( )伐( )潇(瀟)洒(灑)，眼( )神( )深( )远(遠)。
    - tā de bùfá xiāo sǎ, yǎnshén shēnyuǎn
    - His stride is brisk and relaxed, and his gaze is profound.
  - 湖( )面( )波( )光( )潇(瀟)洒(灑)，映( )出( )天( )际(際)长(長)空( )。
    - húmiàn bōguāng xiāo sǎ, yìng chū tiānjì chángkōng
    - The shimmering waves on the lake reflect the vast, clear sky.

### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭)
- **traditional:** 蕭
- **definition:** rustling sound
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") =
    - grass rustling sound ->
    - rustling sound
  - **interpretation:** 萧(蕭) (xiāo) combines the grass radical 艹 (cǎo, "grass") with the phonetic 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") to signify the sound of wind rustling through grass. This character effectively conveys the auditory imagery associated with wind passing through vegetation.
  - **simplification:** 蕭→萧(蕭): The component 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") directly replaced 簫(箫) (xiāo, "end-blown flute") for simplicity, focusing on the phonetic aspect.
- **examples:**

    - When the night breeze blows through the bamboo grove, a rustling sound is heard.
  - 落( )叶(葉)纷(紛)飞(飛)时(時)，耳( )边(邊)回(迴)响(響)着(著)萧(蕭)的( )低( )语(語)。
