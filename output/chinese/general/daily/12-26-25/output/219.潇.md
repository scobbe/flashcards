## deep and clear (literary); brisk
### xiāo
---
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 蕭
    - xiāo
    - distant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 蕭(蕭) (xiāo) =
    - flowing water in a secluded or distant area ->
    - distant; secluded
  - **interpretation:** The character 潇(瀟) (xiāo, "distant; secluded") uses 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") to suggest the theme of water, while 蕭(蕭) (xiāo) provides the pronunciation and evokes the idea of remote or secluded locations, enhancing the meaning.
  - **simplification:** None, since simplified is identical to traditional.
- **examples:**
  - 他向往潇远的隐居生活(他向往瀟遠的隱居生活)。
    - Tā xiàngwǎng xiāoyuǎn de yǐnjū shēnghuó.
    - He longs for a secluded, distant life.
  - 离开城市的喧嚣(離開城市的喧囂)，他去了潇静的山林(他去了瀟靜的山林)。
    - Líkāi chéngshì de xuānxiāo, tā qù le xiāojìng de shānlín.
    - Leaving the noisy city, he went to a secluded mountain forest.
  - 山水之间常透露着一丝潇意(山水之間常透露著一絲瀟意)。
    - Shānshuǐ zhījiān cháng tòulù zhe yīsī xiāoyì.
    - A hint of seclusion often emerges amid the landscape.
