## deep and clear (literary); brisk
### xiāo
---
- **traditional:** 瀟
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 萧(蕭)
    - xiāo
    - rustling sound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 水 (shuǐ, "water") + 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph) =
    - clear water characteristics ->
    - deep and clear water
  - **interpretation:** The character 潇 (瀟) combines 水 (shuǐ, "water") for its semantic component, indicating a relationship with water, with the phonetic 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" to convey the sound of flowing water. This reflects the ideas of clarity and briskness associated with moving water.
  - **simplification:** The character 潇 is the simplified form of 瀟, retaining the semantic 水 (shuǐ, "water") component while simplifying the complex 蕭(萧) (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)" component by replacing it with the simpler 萧 (xiāo, "rustling sound/ph)", retaining its phonetic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 秋风潇洒(風瀟灑)。
    - qiū fēng xiāo sǎ
    - The autumn wind is brisk and elegant.
  - 他的步伐潇洒(瀟灑)，眼神深远(遠)。
    - tā de bùfá xiāo sǎ, yǎnshén shēnyuǎn
    - His stride is brisk and relaxed, and his gaze is profound.
  - 湖面波光潇洒(瀟灑)，映出天际长(際長)空。
    - húmiàn bōguāng xiāo sǎ, yìng chū tiānjì chángkōng
    - The shimmering waves on the lake reflect the vast, clear sky.
### 潇(瀟) → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水是一种(種)生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 夏天很热(熱)，喝水能解暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺，沿岸绿树(綠樹)成荫(蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭)
- **traditional:** 蕭
- **definition:** rustling sound
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") =
    - grass rustling sound ->
    - rustling sound
  - **interpretation:** 萧(蕭) (xiāo) combines the grass radical 艹 (cǎo, "grass") with the phonetic 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") to signify the sound of wind rustling through grass. This character effectively conveys the auditory imagery associated with wind passing through vegetation.
  - **simplification:** 蕭→萧(蕭): The component 肖 (xiāo, "resemble") directly replaced 簫(箫) (xiāo, "end-blown flute") for simplicity, focusing on the phonetic aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 夜风(風)吹过(過)竹林时(時)，传来阵阵萧声(傳來陣陣蕭聲)。
    - Yè fēng chuī guò zhúlín shí, chuán lái zhèn zhèn xiāo shēng.
    - When the night breeze blows through the bamboo grove, a rustling sound is heard.
  - 落叶纷飞时(葉紛飛時)，耳边回响着萧(邊迴響著蕭)的低语(語)。
    - Luò yè fēn fēi shí, ěr biān huí xiǎng zhe xiāo de dī yǔ.
    - As the leaves flutter down, a rustling whisper echoes by the ear.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 艹
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(來時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 肖
- **definition:** resemble
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon/flesh
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 小 (xiǎo, "small") + Phonetic: 月 (yuè, "moon/flesh") =
    - small-like or resemble ->
    - resemblance
  - **interpretation:** 肖 (xiào, "resemble") combines 小 (xiǎo, "small") for resembling something in a smaller form, with 月 (yuè, "moon/flesh") acting as the phonetic component. This suggests a likeness or resemblance, much like something that resembles but is smaller or of similar form.
- **examples:**
  - 他长(長)得很肖他的父亲(親)。
    - Tā zhǎng de hěn xiào tā de fùqīn.
    - He greatly resembles his father.
  - 那幅画(畫)中的人物肖古代仕女，散发着艺术(發著藝術)的韵(韻)味。
    - Nà fú huà zhōng de rénwù xiào gǔdài shìnǚ, sànfā zhe yìshù de yùnwèi.
    - The figure in that painting resembles an ancient court lady and exudes artistic charm.
  - 她的笑容总(總)是与(與)花开(開)的季节(節)肖似，让(讓)人心情愉快。
    - Tā de xiàoróng zǒng shì yǔ huākāi de jìjié xiào sì, ràng rén xīnqíng yúkuài.
    - Her smile always resembles the blossoming season, uplifting people's moods.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 肖 → 小
- **definition:** small
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three small dots arranged symmetrically to represent something small
  - **interpretation:** The character 小(xiǎo, 'small') uses three small strokes to symbolize size or quantity, visually capturing the essence of 'smallness'.
- **examples:**
  - 这个(這個)小猫(貓)很可爱(愛)。
    - Zhège xiǎo māo hěn kě'ài.
    - This small cat is very cute.
  - 这(這)座小桥横(橋橫)跨河流，连(連)接两(兩)岸。
    - Zhè zuò xiǎo qiáo héngkuà héliú, liánjiē liǎng àn.
    - This small bridge spans the river, connecting both banks.
  - 请给(請給)我一小杯水。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī xiǎo bēi shuǐ.
    - Please give me a small cup of water.
### 潇(瀟) → 萧(蕭) → 肖 → 月
- **definition:** moon/flesh
- **pinyin:** yuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a crescent moon
  - **interpretation:** The character 月(yuè, "moon") is a pictographic representation of a crescent moon, straightforwardly capturing the curved shape often associated with the moon's appearance. Though interchangeable with 夕 (xī, "evening") in the past, it distinctly represents the moon in this usage.
- **examples:**
  - 夜空挂着(掛著)明月。
    - Yè kōng guà zhe míng yuè.
    - The bright moon hangs in the night sky.
  - 人们赏(們賞)月，寄托(託)相思。
    - Rén men shǎng yuè, jì tuō xiāng sī.
    - People enjoy the moon, expressing their longing.
