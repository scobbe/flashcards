### deep and clear (literary); brisk
---
- **pinyin:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 蕭
    - xiāo
    - to flow quietly
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic part 水 (shuǐ, 'water') combined with phonetic 蕭 (xiāo, 'to flow quietly').
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the image of water flowing quietly, reflecting the meaning of light rain or drizzle.
- **examples:**
  - 今天天气凉爽，适合游玩(今天天氣涼爽，適合遊玩)！
    - Jīntiān tiānqì liángshuǎng, shìhé yóuwán！
    - The weather is cool today, perfect for outing!
  - 淅淅沥沥，雨点欢快地落下(淅淅瀝瀝，雨點歡快地落下)。
    - Xīxī lìlì, yǔdiǎn huānkuài de luòxià.
    - The rain falls joyfully, pitter-pattering on the ground.
### 潇(瀟) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the movement of water, evoking a sense of flow and fluidity. It is a direct representation of water as an element essential for life.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命的来源(水是生命的來源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng de láiyuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水(我喜歡喝水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
  - 海水很咸(海水很鹹)。
    - Hǎishuǐ hěn xián.
    - Sea water is very salty.
### 潇(瀟) → 蕭(蕭)
- **definition:** to flow quietly
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 萧(蕭)
    - xiāo
    - to flow quietly
  - 草
    - cǎo
    - grass
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 蕭 combines the phonetic part 萧 (xiāo) and the semantic component 草 (cǎo, 'grass') suggesting an association with flowing grasses or plants.
  - **interpretation:** The imagery of flowing grass evokes a sense of quiet movement in nature, aligning with the meaning of 'to flow quietly'.
- **examples:**
  - 风吹过草地(風吹過草地)，四周静悄悄(四周靜悄悄)。
    - Fēng chuī guò cǎodì, sìzhōu jìng qiāoqiāo.
    - The wind blows through the grass, and everything around is very quiet.
  - 湖水蕭蕭，令人放松(湖水蕭蕭，令人放鬆)。
    - Hú shuǐ xiāo xiāo, lìng rén fàngsōng.
    - The lake water flows quietly, making one feel relaxed.
### 潇(瀟) → 蕭(蕭) → 萧(蕭)
- **definition:** to flow quietly
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass radical
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Comprises of the semantic component related to plants (艹) and the phonetic element (肖) which suggests the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of gentle flowing or rustling reminiscent of quiet plant movements in the wind.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天的风吹得树叶萧萧而落(秋天的風吹得樹葉蕭蕭而落)。
    - Qiūtiān de fēng chuī de shùyè xiāoxiāo ér luò.
    - In autumn, the wind blows and the leaves fall quietly.
  - 水流萧萧，传来阵阵水声(水流蕭蕭，傳來陣陣水聲)。
    - Shuǐ liú xiāoxiāo, chuán lái zhèn zhèn shuǐ shēng.
    - The water flows quietly, bringing waves of sound.
  - 他的歌声如风萧萧入耳(他的歌聲如風蕭蕭入耳)。
    - Tā de gēshēng rú fēng xiāoxiāo rù ěr.
    - His singing is like the soft wind entering the ear.
### 潇(瀟) → 蕭(蕭) → 萧(蕭) → 艹(艸)
- **definition:** grass radical
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts grass or plants, typically represented above ground.
  - **interpretation:** The character shows tufts of grass, symbolizing vegetation that grows close to the earth.
  - **simplification:** 艹 is the simplified form of 艸; the top radicals are reduced for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 艹是草字头(艸是草字頭)。
    - cǎo shì cǎo zì tóu.
    - 艹 is the grass radical.
  - 许多汉字都有艹(許多漢字都有艸)。
    - xǔduō hànzì dōu yǒu cǎo.
    - Many Chinese characters have 艹.
