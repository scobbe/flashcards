## Kaili City
### Kǎilǐ
---
- **definition:** Kaili City
- **pinyin:** Kǎilǐ
- **components:**
  - 凯(凱)
    - kǎi
    - triumphant; victorious
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - village; neighborhood; inner; inside
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 凯(凱) (kǎi, "triumphant") + 里(里) (lǐ, "village; neighborhood") =
    - the name of Kaili City, suggesting a place of triumph.
  - **interpretation:** The name 凯里(凱里) (Kǎilǐ) combines the notion of triumph with the concept of community or village. It suggests a place of success or victory, possibly reflecting aspirations or historical significance attributed to Kaili City.
- **examples:**
  - 我计划明天去凯里(我計劃明天去凱里)。
    - Wǒ jìhuà míngtiān qù Kǎilǐ.
    - I plan to go to Kaili tomorrow.
  - 凯里风光迷人(凱里風光迷人)，吸引了众多游客(吸引了眾多遊客)。
    - Kǎilǐ fēngguāng mírén, xīyǐnle zhòngduō yóukè.
    - The scenery of Kaili is charming, attracting many tourists.
  - 在贵州的腹地有凯里(在貴州的腹地有凱里)。
    - Zài Guìzhōu de fùdì yǒu Kǎilǐ.
    - Kaili is located in the heartland of Guizhou.
