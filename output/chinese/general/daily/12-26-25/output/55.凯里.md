## Kaili City
### Kǎilǐ
---
- **traditional:** 凱里
- **definition:** Kaili City
- **pinyin:** Kǎilǐ
- **components:**
  - 凯(凱)
    - kǎi
    - triumphant; victorious
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - village; neighborhood; inner; inside
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 凯(凱) (kǎi, "triumphant") + 里 (lǐ, "village; neighborhood") =
    - the name of Kaili City, suggesting a place of triumph.
  - **interpretation:** The name 凯(凱)里 (Kǎilǐ) combines the notion of triumph with the concept of community or village. It suggests a place of success or victory, possibly reflecting aspirations or historical significance attributed to Kaili City.
- **examples:**
  - 我计划(計劃)明天去凯(凱)里。
    - Wǒ jìhuà míngtiān qù Kǎilǐ.
    - I plan to go to Kaili tomorrow.
  - 凯(凱)里风(風)光迷人，吸引了众(眾)多游(遊)客。
    - Kǎilǐ fēngguāng mírén, xīyǐnle zhòngduō yóukè.
    - The scenery of Kaili is charming, attracting many tourists.
  - 在贵(貴)州的腹地有凯(凱)里。
    - Zài Guìzhōu de fùdì yǒu Kǎilǐ.
    - Kaili is located in the heartland of Guizhou.

- **traditional:** 凱
- **definition:** triumphant; victorious
- **pinyin:** kǎi
- **components:**
  - 豈
    - qǐ
    - drum
  - 几
    - jǐ
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phono-semantic: 豈 (qǐ, "drum") + 几 (jǐ, phonetic) =
    - music of triumph ->
    - victorious
  - **interpretation:** '凱' combines 豈 (qǐ, "drum") representing the triumphant music and 几 (jǐ) as the phonetic component. Originally, this composition reflected music played during victorious celebrations.
  - **simplification:** Simplified as 凯 by replacing 豈 (qǐ, "drum") with 己 (jǐ, "self") for phonetic resemblance, reducing complexity.
- **examples:**
