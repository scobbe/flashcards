### Kaili City
---
- **pinyin:** Kaili City
- **components:**
  - 凯(凱)
    - kǎi
    - victory; triumph
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - inside; village; unit of measurement
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 凯(凱) (kǎi, "victory") + 里(里) (lǐ, "inside; village; unit of measurement") — victory village
  - **interpretation:** The name 'Kaili' suggests a place of victory, possibly indicating historical significance or a prosperous community. As a city, it serves as a hub for local culture and history.
- **examples:**
  - 凯里是贵州的一个城市。(凱里是貴州的一個城市。)
    - Kǎilǐ shì Guìzhōu de yīgè chéngshì.
    - Kaili is a city in Guizhou.
  - 我们要去凯里旅行。(我們要去凱里旅行。)
    - Wǒmen yào qù Kǎilǐ lǚxíng.
    - We are going to travel to Kaili.
