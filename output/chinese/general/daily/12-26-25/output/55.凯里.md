## Kaili City
### Kǎilǐ
---
- **definition:** Kaili City
- **pinyin:** Kǎilǐ
- **components:**
  - 凯(凱)
    - kǎi
    - triumph
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - village; distance unit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 凯(凱) (kǎi, "triumph") + 里(里) (lǐ, "village; distance unit")
  - **interpretation:** The name reflects a meaning of 'triumphant village' or 'triumph within', possibly symbolizing prosperity or success of the area. In contemporary usage, it specifically refers to Kaili City in Guizhou Province, China.
- **examples:**
  - 凯里是一座美丽的城市(凱里是一座美麗的城市)。
    - Kǎilǐ shì yī zuò měilì de chéngshì.
    - Kaili is a beautiful city.
  - 我计划明年访问凯里(凱里)，体验当地风情(當地風情)。
    - Wǒ jìhuà míngnián fǎngwèn Kǎilǐ, tǐyàn dāngdì fēngqíng.
    - I plan to visit Kaili next year to experience the local culture.
  - 近年来我关注了凯里(凱里)，因为那里充满活力(因為那裡充滿活力)。
    - Jìnnián lái wǒ guānzhùle Kǎilǐ, yīnwèi nàlǐ chōngmǎn huólì.
    - In recent years, I have been paying attention to Kaili because it is full of vitality.
### 凯里(凱里) → 凯(凱)
- **definition:** triumph
- **pinyin:** kǎi
- **components:**
  - 凱
    - kǎi
    - triumph
  - 豈
    - qǐ
    - drum
  - 几
    - jǐ
    - table
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 凱(凱) (kǎi, "triumph") =
    - phonetic 豈(豈) (qǐ, "drum") + semantic 几(几) (jǐ, "table") ->
    - originally music of triumph ->
    - general victory
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 豈(豈) (qǐ, "drum"), suggesting sound or music, with 几(几) (jǐ, "table"), to reflect music played in triumphant or victorious circumstances. Over time, it evolved to broadly represent 'triumph.'
  - **simplification:** 凱(凱)→凯(凱): 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") simplified to 己(己) (jǐ, "self") for visual simplicity while retaining sound, with 己 being easier to write.
- **examples:**

    - Tā jīngguò bùxiè nǔlì, kǎixuán ér guī.
    - He returned in triumph after relentless effort.
  - 凯代表着胜利(凱代表著勝利)。
