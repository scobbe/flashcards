## return; turn back
### huí
---
- **definition:** return; turn back
- **pinyin:** huí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts rotation (originally a spiral) ->
    - concept of returning or turning back
  - **interpretation:** The character 回(回) (huí, "return") visually represents a circular motion or spiral, symbolizing the action of returning or coming back.
- **examples:**
  - 我决定回家(我決定回家)。
    - Wǒ juédìng huí jiā.
    - I decided to return home.
  - 他回头看了看(他回頭看了看)，确认找到了路(確認找到了路)。
    - Tā huítóu kàn le kàn, quèrèn zhǎodào le lù.
    - He turned back to take a look, confirming that he found the way.
  - 钟声响起时(鐘聲響起時)，她便回故乡了(她便回故鄉了)。
    - Zhōngshēng xiǎngqǐ shí, tā biàn huí gùxiāng le.
    - When the bell rang, she immediately returned to her hometown.
