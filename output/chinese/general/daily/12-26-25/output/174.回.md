## return; turn back
### huí
---
- **definition:** return; turn back
- **pinyin:** huí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") around 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - area enclosed by an outer layer
  - **interpretation:** The character 回 (huí, "return; turn back") depicts an area enclosed within an outer boundary, symbolizing returning or circling back to a point of origin.
- **examples:**
  - 他回家了(他回家了)。
    - Tā huí jiā le.
    - He returned home.
  - 他们回故乡时(他們回故鄉時)，泪水溢满双眼(淚水溢滿雙眼)。
    - Tāmen huí gùxiāng shí, lèishuǐ yìmǎn shuāngyǎn.
    - When they returned to their hometown, tears filled their eyes.
