## 
### return; turn back
---
- **pinyin:** return; turn back
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts rotation (originally a spiral).
  - **interpretation:** The spiral shape suggests movement around a center point, evoking the concept of returning or turning back.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要回去(我們需要回去)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào huíqù.
    - We need to go back.
  - 车子转回去(車子轉回去)。
    - Chēzi zhuǎn huíqù.
    - The car turned back.
