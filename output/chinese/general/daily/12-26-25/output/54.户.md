## household; door
### hù
---
- **definition:** household; door
- **pinyin:** hù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts one-half of 門(門) (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 戶(戶) (hù, "household; door") resembles the side view of a gate, representing its function as an entrance or access point similar to a door or household entry.
- **examples:**
  - 每户人家都有自己的故事(每戶人家都有自己的故事)。
    - Měi hù rénjiā dōu yǒu zìjǐ de gùshì.
    - Every household has its own story.
  - 这个小区住着数百户(這個小區住著數百戶)，居民和睦相处(居民和睦相處)。
    - Zhège xiǎoqū zhùzhe shù bǎi hù, jūmín hémù xiāngchǔ.
    - Hundreds of households live in this community, and the residents live in harmony.
