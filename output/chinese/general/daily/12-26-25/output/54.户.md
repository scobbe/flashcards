## 
### household; door
---
- **pinyin:** household; door
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a structure with a door opening, representing a household or dwelling.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a door suggests entry and exit points for a home, indicating the idea of household or shelter.
- **examples:**
  - 我们家户口在这里(我們家戶口在這裡)，请登记(請登記)。
    - Wǒmen jiā hùkǒu zài zhèlǐ, qǐng dēngjì.
    - Our household registration is here, please register.
  - 每户都有自己的规定(每戶都有自己的規定)。
    - Měi hù dōu yǒu zìjǐ de guīdìng.
    - Every household has its own regulations.
