## household; door
### hù
---
- **definition:** household; door
- **pinyin:** hù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts one-half of 門(門) (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** 户(戶) (hù, "household; door") visually represents one side of a gate or door. This pictogram effectively conveys the concept of an opening or entrance.
  - **simplification:** None, as 户(戶) is the simplified form retaining the same structure.
- **examples:**
  - 每户都有自己的故事(每戶都有自己的故事)。
    - Měi hù dōu yǒu zìjǐ de gùshì.
    - Every household has its own story.
  - 这片村庄里住着五十户(這片村莊裡住著五十戶)，安静而和谐(安靜而和諧)。
    - Zhè piàn cūnzhuāng lǐ zhùzhe wǔshí hù, ānjìng ér héxié.
    - Fifty households live in this village, in peaceful harmony.
  - 入夜时分(入夜時分)，院中的户扉静静关闭(院中的戶扉靜靜關閉)。
    - Rù yè shí fēn, yuàn zhōng de hù fēi jìng jìng guān bì.
    - At nightfall, the door of the courtyard quietly closes.
