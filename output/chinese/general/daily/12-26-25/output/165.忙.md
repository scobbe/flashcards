## busy
### máng
---
- **definition:** busy
- **pinyin:** máng
- **components:**
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 亡
    - máng
    - gone
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 心(心) (xīn, "heart") + 亡(亡) (máng, "gone") =
    - heart in a state of absence ->
    - busy
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 心(心) (xīn, "heart") and 亡(亡) (máng, "gone") suggests a state where there is no peace or stillness at heart, reflecting the nature of being busy or preoccupied.
- **examples:**
  - 我工作很忙(我工作很忙)，下午还要开会(下午還要開會)。
    - Wǒ gōngzuò hěn máng, xiàwǔ hái yào kāihuì.
    - I am busy with work, and I have a meeting in the afternoon.
  - 今天太忙了(今天太忙了)。
    - Jīntiān tài máng le.
    - Today is extremely busy.
### 忙(忙) → 心(心)
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the shape of a heart organ with three lobes
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, 'heart') visually resembles a heart organ with its three internal sections, emphasizing its role as a core or central part of the human body.
- **examples:**
  - 我的心充满了爱(我的心充滿了愛)。
    - Wǒ de xīn chōngmǎnle ài.
    - My heart is filled with love.
  - 他把心放在艺术上(他把心放在藝術上)，追求完美(追求完美)。
    - Tā bǎ xīn fàng zài yìshù shàng, zhuīqiú wánměi.
    - He devotes his heart to art and pursues perfection.
### 忙(忙) → 亡(亡)
- **definition:** gone
- **pinyin:** máng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a line on 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") representing the cutting edge ->
    - evolved to 亡(亡) (máng, "gone")
  - **interpretation:** 亡(亡) (máng, "gone") originally depicted a cutting edge, emphasizing the concept of something being cut off or having vanished.
- **examples:**
  - 战场硝烟散尽(戰場硝煙散盡)，英雄已亡了(英雄已亡了)。
    - Zhànchǎng xiāoyān sànjìn, Yīngxióng yǐ mángle.
    - When the battlefield’s smoke cleared, the heroes were gone.
  - 回忆随风亡(回憶隨風亡)。
    - Huíyì suí fēng máng.
    - Memories are gone with the wind.
  - 他的名字虽历经岁月仍不曾亡(他的名字雖歷經歲月仍不曾亡)，被世人铭记(被世人銘記)。
    - Tā de míngzì suī lìjīng suìyuè réng bùcéng máng, bèi shìrén míngjì.
    - Though his name has withstood the passage of time and has not been lost, it is remembered by the world.
