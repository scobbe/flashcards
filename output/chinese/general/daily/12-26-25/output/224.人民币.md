## Renminbi (RMB)
### rénmínbì
---
- **definition:** Renminbi (RMB)
- **pinyin:** rénmínbì
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - the people; citizenry; populace; common people
  - 币(幣)
    - bì
    - currency; money; coin; banknote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人民 (people) + 币/幣 (currency) = people's currency (PRC currency).
  - **interpretation:** Names the national currency of the People's Republic of China; in daily use it often refers to 元 (yuán) as a unit. Conveys an official, state-backed currency of the people.
  - **simplification:** 幣 was simplified to 币 in the PRC to reduce strokes during mid-20th-century script reforms; 人民 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我用人民币付款(我用人民幣付款)。
    - Wǒ yòng Rénmínbì fùkuǎn.
    - I pay with renminbi.
  - 这家商店只收人民币(這家商店只收人民幣)。
    - Zhè jiā shāngdiàn zhǐ shōu Rénmínbì.
    - This shop only accepts RMB.
  - 人民币兑美元的汇率今天上升了(人民幣兌美元的匯率今天上升了)。
    - Rénmínbì duì Měiyuán de huìlǜ jīntiān shàngshēng le.
    - The RMB exchange rate against the U.S. dollar rose today.

- **definition:** person;human;people;someone
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**

### 人民币(人民幣) → 人民(人民) → 民(民)
- **definition:** people;commoners;citizenry;subjects
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram; later jiajie (phonetic loan) for “the people”.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a pictorial graph whose precise image is uncertain, it was later borrowed (jiajie) to write the word ‘people’. Claims that it depicts an eye pierced by a tool are likely folk etymology and are not supported by phonetic evidence linking it to 盲.
- **examples:**
  - 中国人民团结一致(中國人民團結一致)。
    - Zhōngguó rénmín tuánjié yīzhì.
    - The Chinese people are united.
  - 政府应倾听民意(政府應傾聽民意)。
    - Zhèngfǔ yīng qīngtīng mínyì.
    - The government should listen to public opinion.
  - 战乱时期(戰亂時期)，许多平民流离失所(許多平民流離失所)。
    - Zhànluàn shíqī, xǔduō píngmín liúlí shīsuǒ.
    - During wartime, many civilians are displaced.
### 人民币(人民幣) → 币(幣)
- **definition:** currency; money; coin; banknote
- **pinyin:** bì
- **components:**
  - 敝
    - bì
    - worn; tattered; to damage
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - towel; cloth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic 敝 + semantic 巾 (“towel/cloth”); simplified from 幣 (敝 → 丿)
  - **interpretation:** Originally indicated cloth used as offerings or gifts, which extended to the idea of currency. 敝 supplied the sound while also evoking cloth, and 巾 provided the semantic field; the simplification reduced 敝 to a single stroke, weakening the phonetic cue.
  - **simplification:** Reduced strokes by replacing the complex phonetic 敝 with the single stroke 丿, largely preserving only the cloth-derived semantic hint.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要稳定的货币来发展经济(我們需要穩定的貨幣來發展經濟)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào wěndìng de huòbì lái fāzhǎn jīngjì.
    - We need stable currency to develop the economy.
  - 他把外币换成人民币(他把外幣換成人民幣)。
    - Tā bǎ wàibì huàn chéng Rénmínbì.
    - He exchanged foreign currency for renminbi.
  - 旅行前准备一些小面额的纸币比较方便(旅行前準備一些小面額的紙幣比較方便)。
    - Lǚxíng qián zhǔnbèi yīxiē xiǎo miàn'é de zhǐbì bǐjiào fāngbiàn.
    - It is more convenient to prepare some small-denomination banknotes before traveling.
### 人民币(人民幣) → 币(幣) → 敝(敝)
- **definition:** worn; tattered; to damage
- **pinyin:** bì
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth; towel
  - 攴
    - pū
    - tap; strike (as a radical/component)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 巾 + semantic: 攴; left-side later evolved into 㡀 with dots indicating dust/dirt.
  - **interpretation:** Cloth (巾) supplies the sound and evokes fabric, while 攴 marks an action, suggesting beating or wearing down. The dotted cloth form (㡀) visually hints at dusty, grimy fabric, aligning with senses like worn, tattered, and damaged.
- **examples:**
  - 敝人有些话想说(敝人有些話想說)。
    - Bìrén yǒuxiē huà xiǎng shuō.
    - I, your humble self, have something to say.
  - 这件衣服已经很敝旧了(這件衣服已經很敝舊了)。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfu yǐjīng hěn bìjiù le.
    - This piece of clothing is already very worn-out.
  - 如有打扰(如有打擾)，还望见谅(還望見諒)，敝处致歉(敝處緻歉)。
    - Rú yǒu dǎrǎo, hái wàng jiànliàng, bì chù zhìqiàn.
    - If there was any disturbance, we at our humble place apologize.

### 人民币(人民幣) → 币(幣) → 敝(敝) → 攴(攴)
- **definition:** tap; strike (as a radical/component)
- **pinyin:** pū
- **components:**
  - 又
    - yòu
    - again; right hand (as component)
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - to divine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Hand 又 holding a tool (later stylized to resemble phonetic 卜), depicting “to hit”.
  - **interpretation:** Shows a right hand grasping an implement used to strike, capturing the action of tapping or hitting. Later stylization made the implement look like 卜, a graphic accident rather than a link to divination or to characters that only look similar.
- **examples:**
  - 古籍中偶见攴(古籍中偶見攴)，表示用棍轻敲(表示用棍輕敲)。
    - Gǔjí zhōng ǒu jiàn pū, biǎoshì yòng gùn qīng qiāo.
    - In old texts, one occasionally sees 攴, meaning to tap lightly with a stick.
  - 这个偏旁叫攴字旁(這個偏旁叫攴字旁)，多与打、教等义有关(多與打、教等義有關)。
    - Zhège piānpáng jiào pū zìpáng, duō yǔ dǎ, jiào děng yì yǒuguān.
    - This side component is called the ‘攴’ radical and often relates to meanings like hitting or teaching.
### 人民币(人民幣) → 币(幣) → 敝(敝) → 攴(攴) → 又(又)
- **definition:** again; right hand (as component)
- **pinyin:** yòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram – a right hand; original form of 右; unrelated to 𠬤 (> 译). Often written as 彐 in many modern characters (e.g., 事, 聿, 雪, 急, 爭, 尹, 建).
  - **interpretation:** Early graphs depict a right hand, which later gave rise to 右 ‘right (side)’. The adverbial sense ‘again’ developed later by semantic extension. In many compounds a similar shape is written as 彐, but that is a graphic convention rather than a direct historical component here.
- **examples:**
  - 他又来了(他又來了)。
    - Tā yòu lái le.
    - He came again.
  - 请向右转(請向右轉)。
    - Qǐng xiàng yòu zhuǎn.
    - Please turn right.
  - 她又买了一本书(她又買了一本書)。
    - Tā yòu mǎi le yī běn shū.
    - She bought another book.
### 人民币(人民幣) → 币(幣) → 敝(敝) → 攴(攴) → 卜(蔔)
- **definition:** to divine
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram (象形): crack in an oracle bone used for divination.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the branching crack made on turtle shell or bone during pyromancy, whose patterns were read as omens. The simple vertical line with a side notch mirrors the appearance of such fissures.
- **examples:**
  - 我们请他卜一卦(我們請他卜一卦)。
    - Wǒmen qǐng tā bǔ yī guà.
    - We asked him to divine a hexagram.
  - 他会卜吉凶(他會卜吉凶)。
    - Tā huì bǔ jíxiōng.
    - He can divine whether things will be auspicious or inauspicious.
  - 用龟甲卜问前途(用龜甲卜問前途)。
    - Yòng guījiǎ bǔwèn qiántú.
    - They use turtle shells to divine the future.
