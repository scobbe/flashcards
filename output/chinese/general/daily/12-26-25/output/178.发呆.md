## be in a daze
### fādāi
---
- **definition:** be in a daze
- **pinyin:** fādāi
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - emit; issue; distribute
  - 呆
    - dāi
    - dull; slow-witted; blank
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 发(發) (fā, "emit") + 呆(呆) (dāi, "dull") creates a sense of being mentally "emitting" or "vacant" in a dull state, leading to the idea of being in a daze.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of emitting or dispersing thought with dullness results in a mental state where one might seem distant or daydreaming.
- **examples:**
  - 他发呆时(他發呆時)，凝视远方(凝視遠方)。
    - Tā fādāi shí, níngshì yuǎnfāng.
    - He spaces out while gazing into the distance.
  - 学生上课时(學生上課時)，常常发呆(常常發呆)。
    - Xuéshēng shàngkè shí, chángcháng fādāi.
    - Students are often in a daze during class.
  - 他坐在公园里(他坐在公園裡)，发呆看着云朵(發呆看著雲朵)。
    - Tā zuò zài gōngyuán lǐ, fādāi kànzhe yúnduǒ.
    - He sits in the park, staring blankly at the clouds.
### 发呆(發呆) → 发(發)
- **definition:** emit; issue; distribute
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 癹(發)
    - bā
    - launch
  - 攴
    - pū
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram: 癹(發) (bā, "launch") depicting a bow firing an arrow + 攴(攴) (pū, "hand") added for action ->
    - evolved to 癹(癹) (bā, "launch") as phonetic component + hand as semantic for issuing ->
    - 发(發)
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a bow shooting an arrow, symbolizing issuance or distribution. Over time, the addition of a hand indicated an action related to issuing or spreading, which aligns with its meanings.
  - **simplification:** 发(發) was simplified by retaining the phonetic component 癹(癹) (bā, "launch") to represent both 發 and 髮, merging these separate characters into one simplified form.
- **examples:**
  - 老板发奖金(老闆發獎金)，员工们都很高兴(員工們都很高興)。
    - Lǎobǎn fā jiǎngjīn, yuángōng men dōu hěn gāoxìng.
    - The boss distributed bonuses, and the employees were very happy.
  - 学校发教材(學校發教材)，同学们仔细阅读(同學們仔細閱讀)。
    - Xuéxiào fā jiàocái, tóngxué men zǐxì yuèdú.
    - The school issued textbooks, and the students read them carefully.
