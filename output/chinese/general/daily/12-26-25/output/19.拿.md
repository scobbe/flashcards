## take; hold
### ná
---
- **definition:** take; hold
- **pinyin:** ná
- **components:**
  - 奴
    - nú
    - servant
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 合
    - hé
    - combine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 奴(奴) (nú, "servant") + 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") =
    - taking with the hand ->
    - later evolved to include 合(合) (hé, "combine") to form a new glyph for 'take, hold'
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the phonetic component 奴(奴) (nú, "servant") with 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") to portray the action of a hand taking or holding. The addition of 合(合) (hé, "combine") in 拿 evolved it as an accepted alternative glyph, streamlining meaning and form from its earlier versions.
- **examples:**
  - 请你把书拿给我(請你把書拿給我)。
    - Qǐng nǐ bǎ shū ná gěi wǒ.
    - Please take the book to me.
  - 他拿起电话(他拿起電話)，拨打紧急呼叫(撥打緊急呼叫)。
    - Tā ná qǐ diànhuà, bōdǎ jǐnjí hūjiào.
    - He picked up the phone, and dialed for emergency help.
  - 她先拿出钥匙(她先拿出鑰匙)，打开门(打開門)。
    - Tā xiān ná chū yàoshi, dǎkāi mén.
    - She first took out the key, then opened the door.
