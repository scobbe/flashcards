## help
### bāngzhù
---
- **definition:** help
- **pinyin:** bāngzhù
- **components:**
  - 帮(幫)
    - bāng
    - assist
  - 助
    - zhù
    - aid; assist; help; support
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 帮(幫) (bāng, "assist") + 助(助) (zhù, "aid") =
    - assist or aid
  - **interpretation:** This word combines the concepts of assistance and aid, emphasizing a collaborative effort in providing help.
  - **simplification:** The word 帮助 (帮) is simplified by omitting complex elements from 幫, such as replacing the phonetic element 邦 with simplified components.
- **examples:**
  - 我经常帮助别人(我經常幫助別人)。
    - Wǒ jīngcháng bāngzhù biérén.
    - I often help others.
  - 老师帮助学生(老師幫助學生)，复习功课(複習功課)。
    - Lǎoshī bāngzhù xuéshēng, fùxí gōngkè.
    - The teacher helps the students review their homework.
  - 在困难时(在困難時)，互相帮助显得尤为重要(互相幫助顯得尤為重要)。
    - Zài kùnnán shí, hùxiāng bāngzhù xiǎnde yóuwéi zhòngyào.
    - In difficult times, mutual help is especially important.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫)
- **definition:** assist
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth
  - 邦
    - bāng
    - country
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 幫: semantic 帛(帛) (bó, "silk") + phonetic 封(封) (fēng, "seal") =
    - upper part of shoe made of silk ->
    - assist; simplifying to 帮: 幚(幫) (phono-semantic compound) → 帛(帛) (bó, "silk") replaced with 巾(巾) (jīn, "cloth"), phonetic 邦(邦) (bāng) adds assisting meaning
  - **interpretation:** The original character 幫 combined 封(封) for sound with 帛(帛) for the semantic element of silk, representing the upper part of a shoe. In the simplified form 帮, the silk radical 帛 was replaced by the cloth radical 巾 for simplicity, and 邦(邦) added a similar sound.
  - **simplification:** 帮(幫): The character 幚 was simplified by replacing the silk radical 帛 with the more common cloth radical 巾, while switching the phonetic component 封 to 邦 to preserve the sound.
- **examples:**
  - 我帮你(我幫你)，完成任务(完成任務)。
    - Wǒ bāng nǐ, wánchéng rènwù.
    - I help you complete the task.
  - 他帮老人(他幫老人)，买药(買藥)。
    - Tā bāng lǎorén, mǎi yào.
    - He helps the elderly buy medicine.
  - 请帮我(請幫我)，一个忙(一個忙)。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ, yī gè máng.
    - Please do me a favor.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 巾(巾)
- **definition:** cloth
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hanging piece of cloth
  - **interpretation:** The character 巾(jīn) is a straightforward pictogram, visually representing a piece of cloth. Its simple design captures the essence of a cloth item hanging down, making the character intuitive to understand.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿出一块巾(他拿出一塊巾)，擦拭脸上的汗水(擦拭臉上的汗水)。
    - tā ná chū yī kuài jīn, cā shì liǎn shàng de hàn shuǐ.
    - He took out a cloth and wiped the sweat off his face.
  - 小店推荐一种新型的绣花巾(小店推薦一種新型的繡花巾)，既美观又实用(既美觀又實用)。
    - xiǎo diàn tuī jiàn yī zhǒng xīn xíng de xiù huā jīn, jì měi guān yòu shí yòng.
    - The small shop recommends a new embroidered cloth, which is both beautiful and practical.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦(邦)
- **definition:** country
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 丰(豐)
    - fēng
    - phonetic indicator
  - 阝
    - None
    - mound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 丰(豐) (fēng) + semantic: 阝(阝) ('left-ear' radical resembling a 'mound') =
    - represent a country
  - **interpretation:** The character 邦(bāng, "country") combines the phonetic component 丰(豐) (fēng, a phonetic indicator) with the semantic 阝(mound-shaped radical) to signify territories marked by enclosures, akin to the boundaries of a country.
- **examples:**
  - 这个邦有悠久历史(這個邦有悠久歷史)。
    - Zhège bāng yǒu yōujiǔ lìshǐ.
    - This country has a long history.
  - 我们热爱这个邦(我們熱愛這個邦)，致力于国家发展(致力於國家發展)。
    - Wǒmen rè'ài zhège bāng, zhìlì yú guójiā fāzhǎn.
    - We love this country and are committed to its development.
  - 邻邦之间(鄰邦之間)，应互相尊重(應互相尊重)。
    - Lín bāng zhījiān, yīng hùxiāng zūnzhòng.
    - Neighboring countries should respect one another.

### 帮助(幫助) → 助(助)

- **components:**
  - 且
    - qiě
    - furthermore
  - 力

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 且(且) (qiě, "furthermore") + semantic: 力(力) (lì, "strength") =
    - helping with one's strength ->
    - aid
  - **interpretation:** The character 助(助) (zhù, "aid") combines 且(且) (qiě, "furthermore") as a phonetic component with 力(力) (lì, "strength") to suggest giving strength or assistance to others.
- **examples:**
