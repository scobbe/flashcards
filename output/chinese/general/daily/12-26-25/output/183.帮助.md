### help
---
- **pinyin:** help
- **components:**
  - 帮(幫)
    - bāng
    - help; assist; support; aid
  - 助
    - zhù
    - help; assist; aid; promote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 帮(幫) (bāng, 'help') + 助(助) (zhù, 'assist') — help assist
  - **interpretation:** The word combines elements of 'help' and 'assist', indicating a collaborative action. This makes sense as both morphemes convey the idea of providing support to someone.
- **examples:**
  - 我想帮助你(我想幫助你)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng bāngzhù nǐ.
    - I want to help you.
  - 他在帮助我们(他在幫助我們)。
    - Tā zài bāngzhù wǒmen.
    - He is helping us.
