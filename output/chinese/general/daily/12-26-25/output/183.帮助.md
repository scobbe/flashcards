## help
### bāngzhù
---
- **definition:** help
- **pinyin:** bāngzhù
- **components:**
  - 帮(幫)
    - bāng
    - help; assist; group
  - 助
    - zhù
    - aid; assist; help; support
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 帮(幫) (bāng, "help; assist") and 助(助) (zhù, "aid; assist") together emphasize the action of providing assistance or support.
  - **interpretation:** 帮(幫) (bāng, "help; assist") and 助(助) (zhù, "aid; assist") both imply offering support or help to someone else. Their combination strengthens the notion of providing assistance or aiding others.
- **examples:**
  - 我常常帮助有需要的人(我常常幫助有需要的人)。
    - Wǒ chángcháng bāngzhù yǒu xūqiú de rén.
    - I often help those in need.
  - 他帮助邻居(他幫助鄰居)，也给予了鼓励(也給予了鼓勵)。
    - Tā bāngzhù línjū, yě jǐyǔle gǔlì.
    - He helps his neighbors and also gives them encouragement.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫)
- **definition:** help; assist; group
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth
  - 邦
    - bāng
    - nation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified
  - **description:**
    - 幚(幫) (bāng, "help") with 帛(帛) (bó, "silk") replaced by 巾(巾) (jīn, "cloth") ->
    - variant form 幚(幚) (bāng, "help") ->
    - phonetic 封(封) (fēng, "seal") replaced with 邦(邦) (bāng, "nation") ->
    - simplified to 帮
  - **interpretation:** The character 帮(bāng) was simplified from 幫 by replacing complex components with simpler ones, while retaining the phonetic and semantic elements related to 'help'. By using 邦(bāng, "nation") as the phonetic component, the character preserves its pronunciation and essential meaning. The simplification reflects a streamlined writing process by altering the cloth radical to巾(jīn, "cloth").
  - **simplification:** The complex 幫 was simplified to 帮 by replacing 帛(bó, "silk") with 巾(jīn, "cloth") and 封(fēng, "seal") with the more streamlined 邦(bāng, "nation") for phonetic similarity.
- **examples:**
  - 她常常帮邻居买菜(她常常幫鄰居買菜)。
    - tā chángcháng bāng línjū mǎi cài
    - She often helps her neighbors buy groceries.
  - 妈妈帮我做作业(媽媽幫我做作業)，让我学习更多(讓我學習更多)。
    - māma bāng wǒ zuò zuòyè, ràng wǒ xuéxí gèng duō
    - Mom helps me with my homework, letting me learn more.
  - 他加入了一个帮派(他加入了一個幫派)，获得了新的朋友(獲得了新的朋友)。
    - tā jiārùle yīgè bāngpài, huòdéle xīn de péngyǒu
    - He joined a group, making new friends.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 巾(巾)

- **pinyin:** jīn

- **examples:**
  - 爸爸用干净的巾擦手(爸爸用乾淨的巾擦手)。

  - 她拿着一条巾(她拿著一條巾)，轻轻擦拭眼角(輕輕擦拭眼角)。
    - Tā názhe yì tiáo jīn, qīngqīng cā shì yǎnjiǎo.
    - She holds a cloth and gently wipes the corners of her eyes.
  - 老人用巾给手上包扎伤口(老人用巾給手上包扎傷口)。
    - Lǎorén yòng jīn gěi shǒu shàng bāozā shāngkǒu.
    - The elderly person uses a cloth to bandage the wound on his hand.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦(邦)
- **definition:** nation
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 丰
    - fēng
    - abundant
  - 阝
    - fù
    - earthen mound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 丰(丰) (fēng, "abundant") + Semantic: 阝(阝) (fù, "earthen mound") =
    - representation of an earthen mound + abundant resources ->
    - nation
  - **interpretation:** The character 邦(bāng, "nation") combines the idea of wealth or resources suggested by 丰(丰) with the earthen boundary suggested by 阝(阝), symbolizing a defined area with resources, which is the concept of a nation.
- **examples:**
  - 历史塑造了这个邦(歷史塑造了這個邦)，使之繁荣昌盛(使之繁榮昌盛)。
    - Lìshǐ sùzào le zhège bāng, shǐ zhī fánróng chāngshèng。
    - History shaped this nation, making it prosperous.
  - 一个善良的邦能让人民安居乐业(一個善良的邦能讓人民安居樂業)。
    - Yīgè shànliáng de bāng néng ràng rénmín ānjū lèyè。
    - A good nation enables its people to live in peace and prosperity.

- **components:**
  - 力

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

    - aid or assist
  - **interpretation:** The character 助 combines 力(力) (lì, "strength") as the semantic component, conveying the idea of providing support or assistance, and 且(且) (qiě) as the phonetic component. This combination emphasizes offering strength or help to someone.
