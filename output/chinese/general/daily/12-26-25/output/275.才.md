## 
### talent; only then
---
- **pinyin:** talent; only then
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Originally depicted as a person with arms raised, symbolizing an individual with the ability to produce or create.
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys the sense of ability or talent that comes from a person who can produce something remarkable.
- **examples:**
  - 他有很多才华(他有很多才華)。
    - Tā yǒu hěn duō cáihuá.
    - He has a lot of talents.
  - 这是一种很特别的才能(這是一種很特別的才能)。
    - Zhè shì yī zhǒng hěn tèbié de cáinéng.
    - This is a very special ability.
