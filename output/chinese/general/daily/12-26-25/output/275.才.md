## talent; only then
### cái
---
- **definition:** talent; only then
- **pinyin:** cái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a sharp peg
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 才(cái) depicted a sharp peg, a straightforward pictogram. This form suggests a sense of precision and purpose, similar to the concept of 'talent' as skillful precision.
- **examples:**
  - 他十岁才会骑自行车(他十歲才會騎自行車)。
    - Tā shí suì cái huì qí zìxíngchē.
    - He can ride a bicycle only when he is ten years old.
  - 只有勤奋学习(只有勤奮學習)，才能展现才华(才能展現才華)。
    - Zhǐ yǒu qín fèn xuéxí, cái néng zhǎn xiàn cái huá.
    - Only by studying diligently can one show their talent.
  - 他有才(他有才)，但平时谦虚低调(但平時謙虛低調)。
    - Tā yǒu cái, dàn píng shí qiān xū dī diào.
    - He has talent, but he is usually modest and low-key.
