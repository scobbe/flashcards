## comb
### shūzi
---
- **definition:** comb
- **pinyin:** shūzi
- **components:**
  - 梳
    - shū
    - comb; to comb; to smooth; to untangle
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son; seed; noun suffix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 梳 + 子 = comb (tool/noun).
  - **interpretation:** Adds the nominal suffix 子 to the root 梳 ‘to comb’ to name the object. Emphasizes a small, tangible implement.
- **examples:**
  - 我用梳子梳头(我用梳子梳頭)。
    - Wǒ yòng shūzi shū tóu.
    - I use a comb to comb my hair.
  - 这把梳子很轻(這把梳子很輕)，带在包里很方便(帶在包裡很方便)。
    - Zhè bǎ shūzi hěn qīng, dài zài bāo lǐ hěn fāngbiàn.
    - This comb is light, and carrying it in the bag is convenient.
  - 请把梳子递给我(請把梳子遞給我)。
    - Qǐng bǎ shūzi dì gěi wǒ.
    - Please pass me the comb.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳)
- **definition:** comb; to comb; to smooth; to untangle
- **pinyin:** shū
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood; tree
  - 疏
    - shū
    - sparse; to clear; to dredge
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 木 + phonetic: 疏 (abbreviated; OC *sŋra, *sŋras).
  - **interpretation:** 木 points to a wooden implement such as a comb. 疏 supplies the sound shū and evokes senses like making things sparse or clear, aligning with combing and untangling.
- **examples:**
  - 我用梳子梳头发(我用梳子梳頭髮)。
    - Wǒ yòng shūzi shū tóufà.
    - I use a comb to comb my hair.
  - 理发师细心地把头发梳顺了(理髮師細心地把頭髮梳順了)。
    - Lǐfàshī xìxīn de bǎ tóufà shū shùn le.
    - The hairdresser carefully combed the hair smooth.
  - 先把思路梳理清楚(先把思路梳理清楚)。
    - Xiān bǎ sīlù shūlǐ qīngchu.
    - First sort out the train of thought clearly.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 木(木)
- **definition:** wood; tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram (象形)
  - **description:** a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom (more visible in earlier forms).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tree with spreading branches above and roots below, as seen clearly in early scripts. The vertical stroke is the trunk, while the short horizontal strokes became stylized limbs and roots in the regular form.
- **examples:**
  - 这张桌子是木头做的(這張桌子是木頭做的)。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi shì mùtou zuò de.
    - This table is made of wood.
  - 山上长满了树木(山上長滿了樹木)。
    - Shān shang zhǎng mǎn le shùmù.
    - The mountainside is covered with trees.
  - 木材被运到工厂加工(木材被運到工廠加工)。
    - Mùcái bèi yùn dào gōngchǎng jiāgōng.
    - The timber was transported to the factory for processing.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 疏(疏)
- **definition:** sparse; to clear; to dredge
- **pinyin:** shū
- **components:**
  - 疋
    - pǐ
    - bolt of cloth; foot
  - 㐬
    - shū
    - to open; disperse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound; phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic 疋 + semantic 㐬; also analyzed as ideogrammic.
  - **interpretation:** 㐬 suggests branching or dispersal, evoking ideas of separation, thinning out, and opening a passage. 疋 supplies the sound value, and together they point to senses like sparse spacing and clearing or dredging channels.
- **examples:**
  - 雨后山上的松林显得稀疏(雨後山上的松林顯得稀疏)。
    - Yǔ hòu shān shàng de sōnglín xiǎnde xīshū.
    - After the rain, the pine forest on the mountain looks sparse.
  - 市政部门正在疏通河道(市政部門正在疏通河道)，防止城市内涝(防止城市內澇)。
    - Shìzhèng bùmén zhèngzài shūtōng hédào, fángzhǐ chéngshì nèilào.
    - The municipal department is dredging the river channel to prevent urban flooding.
  - 长期不联系(長期不聯係)，朋友之间难免会渐渐疏远(朋友之間難免會漸漸疏遠)。
    - Chángqī bù liánxì, péngyǒu zhījiān nánmiǎn huì jiànjiàn shūyuǎn.
    - Without contact for a long time, friends inevitably grow distant.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 疏(疏) → 疋(疋)
- **definition:** bolt of cloth; foot
- **pinyin:** pǐ
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - to stop; pictograph of a foot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: foot 止 with a leg/thigh on top; similar to 足; unrelated to the bottom of 是 (which is 止 on oracle bones).
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicts a leg above a foot, producing an image close to 足. It was later extended and borrowed as a measure word for bolts of cloth, while its resemblance to the lower part of 是 is accidental since that part derives from 止.
- **examples:**
  - 他买了一疋布(他買了一疋布)。
    - tā mǎi le yì pǐ bù.
    - He bought one bolt of cloth.
  - 市场上这种布按疋计价(市場上這種布按疋計價)。
    - shìchǎng shàng zhè zhǒng bù àn pǐ jìjià.
    - In the market, this kind of cloth is priced by the bolt.
  - 裁缝说还差半疋(裁縫說還差半疋)。
    - cáiféng shuō hái chà bàn pǐ.
    - The tailor said we’re short by half a bolt.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 疏(疏) → 疋(疋) → 止(止)
- **definition:** to stop; pictograph of a foot
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a footprint pointing up (originally “foot”), later stylized as 龰; oracle forms show three toes and a sole.
  - **interpretation:** A footprint with toes pointing upward represents the foot. The sense broadened from the physical foot to the act of halting one’s step, hence “to stop”; the specific ‘toe’ meaning was preserved in 趾.
- **examples:**
  - 请立即停止前进(請立即停止前進)。
    - Qǐng lìjí tíngzhǐ qiánjìn.
    - Please stop advancing immediately.
  - 此处止步(此處止步)，非请勿入(非請勿入)。
    - Cǐ chù zhǐbù, fēi qǐng wù rù.
    - Stop here; do not enter without invitation.
  - 药到病除(藥到病除)，疼痛已止(疼痛已止)。
    - Yào dào bìng chú, téngtòng yǐ zhǐ.
    - With the right medicine, the illness is cured and the pain has stopped.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 疏(疏) → 㐬
- **definition:** to open; disperse
- **pinyin:** shū
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: inverted 子 (newborn) with three lower lines representing amniotic fluid or hair.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an upside-down newborn emerging, with trailing lines suggesting flowing fluid or hair. The imagery naturally extends to senses of unfolding, opening out, and dispersing.
- **examples:**
  - 风起时(風起時)，乌云㐬而散(烏雲㐬而散)。
    - Fēng qǐ shí, wūyún shū ér sàn.
    - When the wind rises, the dark clouds open up and disperse.
  - 清晨(清晨)，花穗在微风中渐次㐬(花穗在微風中漸次㐬)。
    - Qīngchén, huāsuì zài wēifēng zhōng jiàncì shū.
    - At dawn, the flower tassels gradually open in the breeze.
  - 将锁解下(將鎖解下)，门扉自㐬(門扉自㐬)。
    - Jiāng suǒ jiě xià, ménfēi zì shū.
    - Once the lock was undone, the door panels opened by themselves.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳) → 疏(疏) → 㐬 → 子(子)
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: image of a baby with a large head and spread arms; legs wrapped in a blanket.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a swaddled baby with a prominent head and outstretched arms. Over time the figure was stylized into the modern form while still evoking an infant.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我唯一的儿子(他是我唯一的兒子)。
    - Tā shì wǒ wéiyī de érzi.
    - He is my only son.
  - 她抱起孩子(她抱起孩子)。
    - Tā bào qǐ háizi.
    - She picked up the child.
  - 这个孩子三岁了(這個孩子三歲了)。
    - Zhège háizi sān suì le.
    - This child is three years old.
