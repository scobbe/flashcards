## comb
### shūzi
---
- **definition:** comb
- **pinyin:** shūzi
- **components:**
  - 梳
    - shū
    - comb
  - 子
    - zi
    - small thing; child; seed; suffix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 梳(梳) (shū, "comb") + 子(子) (zi, "small thing") =
    - comb
  - **interpretation:** A 梳(梳) (shū, "comb") is a tool used for grooming hair, and 子(子) (zi, "small thing") is a common suffix in Chinese that denotes a small object. The combination suggests a small hand-held tool for grooming.
- **examples:**
  - 她用梳子梳理长发(她用梳子梳理長髮)。
    - Tā yòng shūzi shūlǐ chángfǎ.
    - She uses a comb to comb her long hair.
  - 我买了一个漂亮的梳子(我買了一個漂亮的梳子)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yīgè piàoliàng de shūzi.
    - I bought a pretty comb.
  - 妈妈修剪头发时(媽媽修剪頭髮時)，总喜欢用这个梳子(總喜歡用這個梳子)。
    - Māmā xiūjiǎn tóufǎ shí, zǒng xǐhuān yòng zhège shūzi.
    - When mom trims hair, she always likes to use this comb.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
