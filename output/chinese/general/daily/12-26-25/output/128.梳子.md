## comb
### shūzi
---
- **definition:** comb
- **pinyin:** shūzi
- **components:**
  - 梳
    - shū
    - comb; brush; smooth out
  - 子
    - zi
    - small object; child; seed; particle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 梳(梳) (shū, "comb") + 子(子) (zi, "small object suffix")
  - **interpretation:** A 梳子 is a small object used for combing hair, where 梳(梳) denotes the act of combing, and 子(子) is a common suffix indicating a small item.
- **examples:**
  - 我用梳子梳头发(我用梳子梳頭髮)。
    - Wǒ yòng shūzi shū tóufǎ.
    - I comb my hair with a comb.
  - 妈妈买了梳子(媽媽買了梳子)，让我整理头发(讓我整理頭髮)。
    - Māmā mǎi le shūzi, ràng wǒ zhěnglǐ tóufǎ.
    - Mom bought a comb to help me style my hair.
  - 小狗看着梳子(小狗看著梳子)，觉得很新奇(覺得很新奇)。
    - Xiǎo gǒu kànzhe shūzi, juéde hěn xīnqí.
    - The little dog looked at the comb and found it fascinating.
### 梳子(梳子) → 梳(梳)
- **definition:** comb; brush; smooth out
- **pinyin:** shū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + 疏(疏) (shū, "to disperse") =
    - wood used for dispersing or smoothing ->
    - comb
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 木(木) (mù, "wood"), as combs were traditionally made of wood, with the phonetic component 疏(疏) (shū, "to disperse"), suggesting the function of a comb in smoothing and untangling hair.
- **examples:**
  - 请用梳梳头发(請用梳梳頭髮)。
    - Qǐng yòng shū shū tóufǎ.
    - Please comb your hair.
  - 妈妈帮我梳头(媽媽幫我梳頭)，让我看起来整洁(讓我看起來整潔)。
    - Māmā bāng wǒ shū tóu, ràng wǒ kàn qǐlái zhěngjié.
    - Mom combed my hair, making me look neat.
  - 在夏天(在夏天)，我喜欢用竹梳梳头(我喜歡用竹梳梳頭)。
    - Zài xiàtiān, wǒ xǐhuān yòng zhú shū shū tóu.
    - In summer, I like using a bamboo comb.
### 梳子(梳子) → 子(子)
- **definition:** small object; child; seed; particle
- **pinyin:** zi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an infant swaddled in cloth ->
    - symbolizes child
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(子) (zǐ, "child") originally depicted an infant wrapped in a cloth, illustrating the concept of a young child. This symbolic representation has expanded over time to include meanings such as "seed" or "particle," aligning with the concept of beginnings.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小子成绩优异(他的小子成績優異)。
    - Tā xiǎo zǐ chéngjì yōuyì.
    - His child has excellent grades.
  - 这颗子将发芽生长(這顆子將發芽生長)。
    - Zhè kē zǐ jiāng fāyá shēngzhǎng.
    - This seed will sprout and grow.
  - 当太阳升起时(當太陽升起時)，小子在院子里欢笑(小子在院子裡歡笑)。
    - Dāng tàiyáng shēngqǐ shí, xiǎo zǐ zài yuànzi lǐ huānxiào.
    - When the sun rises, the child laughs in the courtyard.
