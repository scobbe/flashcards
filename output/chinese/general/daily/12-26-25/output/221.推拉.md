## push and pull
### tuīlā
---
- **definition:** push and pull
- **pinyin:** tuīlā
- **components:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - to push; to shove; to promote; to infer
  - 拉
    - lā
    - to pull; to drag; to draw; to play (a string instrument)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 推 (push) + 拉 (pull) = paired opposite actions used together.
  - **interpretation:** Pairs two opposite motion verbs to express applying force forward or backward. Common on doors and for describing opposing forces or a push–pull dynamic.
- **examples:**
  - 门上写着“推”和“拉”(門上寫著“推”和“拉”)
    - Mén shàng xiězhe “tuī” hé “lā”.
    - The door has “push” and “pull” written on it.
  - 请先推一下(請先推一下)，再拉开门(再拉開門)。
    - Qǐng xiān tuī yíxià, zài lā kāi mén.
    - Please push first, then pull the door open.
  - 两人你推我拉地搬沙发(兩人你推我拉地搬沙發)。
    - Liǎng rén nǐ tuī wǒ lā de bān shāfā.
    - The two of them moved the sofa, one pushing and the other pulling.
### 推拉(推拉) → 推(推)
- **definition:** to push; to shove; to promote; to infer
- **pinyin:** tuī
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 隹
    - zhuī
    - short-tailed bird
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 扌 (“hand”) + phonetic 隹 (OC *tjul) – push with the hand.
  - **interpretation:** A hand component signals an action done manually, while 隹 contributes the sound tuī and a sense of motion. Together they express applying force with the hand to move something forward or advance an idea.
- **examples:**
  - 他用力推门(他用力推門)。
    - Tā yòng lì tuī mén.
    - He pushed the door hard.
  - 公司在网上大力推广新产品(公司在網上大力推廣新產品)。
    - Gōngsī zài wǎngshàng dàlì tuīguǎng xīn chǎnpǐn.
    - The company is vigorously promoting the new product online.
  - 根据现有证据(根據現有證據)，我们可以推断他的动机(我們可以推斷他的動機)。
    - Gēnjù xiànyǒu zhèngjù, wǒmen kěyǐ tuīduàn tā de dòngjī.
    - Based on the existing evidence, we can infer his motive.
### 推拉(推拉) → 推(推) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: hand and fingers; five digits, with top stroke as bent middle finger and horizontal strokes as fingers.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a human hand: the top stroke stands for a bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes stand for the remaining fingers. In contrast to related claw forms that show three digits, it consistently presents a five‑finger human hand.
- **examples:**
  - 我手上有伤口(我手上有傷口)。
    - Wǒ shǒu shàng yǒu shāngkǒu.
    - I have a wound on my hand.
  - 请举起右手(請舉起右手)。
    - Qǐng jǔ qǐ yòu shǒu.
    - Please raise your right hand.
  - 他动手做饭(他動手做飯)。
    - Tā dòngshǒu zuò fàn.
    - He started cooking by hand.

### 推拉(推拉) → 拉(拉)
- **definition:** to pull; to drag; to draw; to play (a string instrument)
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand; set up
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 手 “hand” + phonetic: 立
  - **interpretation:** Combines the hand component with 立 to indicate a hand action such as pulling or dragging. 立 contributed the historical sound value, while 手 anchors the meaning in manual motion, leading to verbs of drawing or tugging.
- **examples:**
  - 他拉开门进来(他拉開門進來)。
    - Tā lā kāi mén jìnlái.
    - He pulled the door open and came in.
  - 请你别拉我的衣服(請你別拉我的衣服)。
    - Qǐng nǐ bié lā wǒ de yīfu.
    - Please don't pull my clothes.
  - 他们一起把车拉出泥坑(他們一起把車拉出泥坑)。
    - Tāmen yìqǐ bǎ chē lā chū níkēng.
    - They pulled the car out of the mud pit together.
### 推拉(推拉) → 拉(拉) → 立(立)
- **definition:** stand; set up
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram — a person standing on the ground; compare 大 and 文. Also a contracted form of 辛 on top of 妾 and 童; unrelated to 音.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person standing on the ground, yielding meanings like “to stand” and “to set up/establish.” The 立-shaped top also appears as a contracted element in graphs such as 妾 and 童, but it bears no etymological relation to 音.
- **examples:**
  - 请大家起立(請大家起立)。
    - Qǐng dàjiā qǐlì.
    - Please stand up, everyone.
  - 我们立了一个新目标(我們立了一個新目標)。
    - Wǒmen lìle yí gè xīn mùbiāo.
    - We set a new goal.
  - 他在门口立着(他在門口立著)，不说话(不說話)。
    - Tā zài ménkǒu lìzhe, bù shuōhuà.
    - He stood at the doorway without speaking.
