### push and pull
---
- **pinyin:** push and pull
- **components:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - push; press; recommend; deduce
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; lead; reach
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 推(推) (tuī, "push") + 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") — push and pull
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 推(推) (tuī, "push") and 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") describes actions that are opposite yet complementary in moving objects. It reflects the process of applying force in both directions.
- **examples:**
  - 这个门需要推拉(這個門需要推拉)。
    - Zhège mén xūyào tuīlā.
    - This door needs to be pushed and pulled.
  - 我在推拉重物(我在推拉重物)。
    - Wǒ zài tuīlā zhòngwù.
    - I am pushing and pulling heavy objects.
### 推拉(推拉) → 推(推)
- **definition:** push; press; recommend; deduce
- **pinyin:** tuī
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 退
    - tuì
    - to retreat/grow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 推 combines the semantic element of 'hand' (扌) and the phonetic element of 'to grow' (退) to convey the idea of using one's hand to push or recommend.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as using a hand (扌) symbolizes the action of pushing, while the phonetic component offers a sound connection.
- **examples:**
  - 我推他的主意(我推他的主意)，希望你喜欢(希望你喜歡)。
    - wǒ tuī tā de zhǔyì, xīwàng nǐ xǐhuān.
    - I push his idea, hoping you like it.
  - 请推一下门(請推一下門)。
    - qǐng tuī yīxiàmén.
    - Please push the door.

### 推拉(推拉) → 拉(拉)
- **definition:** pull; drag; lead; reach
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 手 (手) (shǒu, 'hand') to signify action done by hand and the phonetic component 立 (立) (lì, 'stand') for pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a hand and the sound component suggests the action of pulling or dragging something with the hands, making the meaning intuitive.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手拉门(他用手拉門)。
    - Tā yòng shǒu lā mén.
    - He pulls the door with his hand.
  - 请帮我拉一下(請幫我拉一下)。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ lā yīxià.
    - Please help me pull it a bit.
### 推拉(推拉) → 拉(拉) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human hand with fingers, where the top stroke represents the bent over middle finger and horizontal strokes represent two fingers each.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents a hand by depicting its five distinct fingers, distinguishing it from other characters that represent claws or simplified forms of hands.
- **examples:**
  - 我用手(我用手)写字。
    - Wǒ yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - I write with my hand.
  - 请用手(請用手)打电话。
    - Qǐng yòng shǒu dǎ diànhuà.
    - Please use your hand to make a phone call.
