## push and pull
### tuīlā
---
- **definition:** push and pull
- **pinyin:** tuīlā
- **components:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - push
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 推(推) (tuī, "push") + 拉(拉) (lā, "pull"). This combination refers to the actions of pushing and pulling, often used in contexts like doors that can be opened by pushing them in one direction and pulling them in the opposite direction.
  - **interpretation:** The term describes the opposing actions of pushing and pulling, illustrating the mechanical actions commonly seen in doors or machinery operation.
- **examples:**
  - 请你推拉门(請你推拉門)。
    - Qǐng nǐ tuīlā mén.
    - Please push and pull the door.
  - 孩子们喜欢推拉玩具(孩子們喜歡推拉玩具)，增添了许多乐趣(增添了許多樂趣)。
    - Háizimen xǐhuān tuīlā wánjù, zēngtiānle xǔduō lèqù.
    - Children enjoy playing with push-pull toys, which adds a lot of fun.
  - 新大楼的自动门可以推拉(新大樓的自動門可以推拉)。
    - Xīn dàlóu de zìdòng mén kěyǐ tuīlā.
    - The automatic door of the new building can be pushed and pulled.

### 推拉(推拉) → 推(推) → 隹(隹)
- **definition:** short-tailed bird
- **pinyin:** zhuī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a short-tailed bird
  - **interpretation:** 隹(隹) (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") originated as a pictorial representation of a bird with a short tail, a feature common in sparrows and similar birds. It is the original character for depicting small birds and its form suggests this particular physical trait, distinguishing it from more generic bird characters like 鳥(鳥) (niǎo, "bird").
  - **simplification:** None, identical in traditional and simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 早晨时分(早晨時分)，我在公园中遇到一只隹(我在公園中遇到一只隹)。
    - Zǎochén shífēn, wǒ zài gōngyuán zhōng yùdào yì zhī zhuī.

  - 黄昏时刻(黃昏時刻)，隹在树枝间轻快地跳跃(隹在樹枝間輕快地跳躍)。
    - Huánghūn shíkè, zhuī zài shùzhī jiān qīngkuài de tiàoyuè.
    - At dusk, the short-tailed bird lightly hops between the branches.
