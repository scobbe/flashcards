### mixed blood
---
- **pinyin:** mixed blood
- **components:**
  - 混
    - hùn
    - mix; confuse; blend
  - 血
    - xuè
    - blood; family lineage; vitality
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 混(混) (hùn, "mix") + 血(血) (xuè, "blood") — mix blood
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to individuals of mixed racial or ethnic descent. It makes sense as it literally describes the blending of different bloodlines.
- **examples:**
  - 她是混血儿(她是混血兒)，有着不同的文化背景(有著不同的文化背景)。
    - tā shì hùn xuè ér, yǒuzhe bùtóng de wénhuà bèijǐng.
    - She is of mixed blood and has a different cultural background.
  - 越来越多的混血孩子(越來越多的混血孩子)在这里出生(在這裡出生)。
    - yuè lái yuè duō de hùn xuè háizi zài zhèlǐ chūshēng.
    - More and more mixed-blood children are being born here.
### 混血(混血) → 混(混)
- **definition:** mix; confuse; blend
- **pinyin:** hùn
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 昏
    - hūn
    - dusk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 混 is composed of the water component 水 (shuǐ, 'water') and the phonetic component 昏 (hūn, 'dusk'), suggesting a mixing of water as one can see in murky waters during dusk.
  - **interpretation:** Water (水) mixing with the concept of dusk (昏) evokes the idea of blending and obscurity, like how colors might mix in dim light.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要把这些材料混在一起(我們需要把這些材料混在一起)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào bǎ zhèxiē cáiliào hùn zài yīqǐ.
    - We need to mix these materials together.
  - 他把颜色混得很好(他把顏色混得很好)。
    - Tā bǎ yánsè hùn de hěn hǎo.
    - He mixed the colors very well.

### 混血(混血) → 血(血)
- **definition:** blood; family lineage; vitality
- **pinyin:** xuè
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - drop
  - 皿
    - mǐn
    - container
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 一 (yī, 'drop of blood') + 皿 (mǐn, 'container') — a drop of blood inside a chalice for sacrificial uses.
  - **interpretation:** This character visually represents the concept of a drop of blood collected in a vessel, highlighting its importance in rituals and sacrifices. Such a depiction emphasizes blood as a vital substance used in ancestral offerings.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要献血(我們需要獻血)，帮助病人(幫助病人)！
    - wǒmen xūyào xiàn xuè, bāngzhù bìngrén!
    - We need to donate blood to help patients!
  - 血液是生命的源泉(血液是生命的源泉)。
    - xuèyè shì shēngmìng de yuánquán.
    - Blood is the source of life.
  - 许多文化都有用血进行祭祀的传统(許多文化都有用血進行祭祀的傳統)。
    - xǔduō wénhuà dōu yǒu yòng xuè jìnxíng jìsì de chuántǒng.
    - Many cultures have traditions of using blood in sacrifices.
