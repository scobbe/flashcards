## mixed blood
### hùnxuè
---
- **definition:** mixed blood
- **pinyin:** hùnxuè
- **components:**
  - 混
    - hùn
    - mix; confuse; blend
  - 血
    - xuè
    - blood; bloodline; lineage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 混 (hùn, 'mix') + 血 (xuè, 'blood') =
    - mix blood ->
    - mixed blood
  - **interpretation:** The term 混血 (hùnxuè, 'mixed blood') describes a person of mixed racial or ethnic ancestry. It emphasizes the concept of blending different 'bloodlines' or heritages, highlighting the diversity and combination of different genetic backgrounds.
- **examples:**
  - 他拥(擁)有混血的魅力。
    - Tā yǒngyǒu hùnxuè de mèilì.
    - He has the charm of mixed blood.
  - 他的长(長)相别(別)具一格，因为(爲)他是混血。
    - Tā de zhǎngxiàng biéjù yīgé, yīnwèi tā shì hùnxuè.
    - His appearance is unique because he is of mixed blood.
  - 她在众(眾)人中脱颖(脫穎)而出，混血赋(賦)予了她异国风(異國風)情。
    - Tā zài zhòngrén zhōng tuōyǐng ér chū, hùnxuè fùyǔ le tā yìguó fēngqíng.
    - She stands out among the crowd; her mixed blood gives her an exotic charm.
### 混血 → 混
- **definition:** mix; confuse; blend
- **pinyin:** hùn
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 昆
    - kūn
    - elder brother
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(水) (shuǐ, 'water') + phonetic: 昆 (kūn, 'elder brother') =
    - water plus sound of 昆 ->
    - to mix or blend
  - **interpretation:** The character 混(hùn) combines the semantic element 氵(水) (shuǐ, 'water') suggesting fluidity or mixing, with the phonetic element 昆 (kūn, 'elder brother'), hinting at the pronunciation. This reflects the concept of blending or mixing, much like different substances would when water is involved.
- **examples:**
  - 他把面(麵)粉和水混在一起，制(製)成了面团(麵團)。
    - Tā bǎ miànfěn hé shuǐ hùn zài yīqǐ，zhìchéngle miàntuán。
    - He mixed flour and water together, and made dough.
  - 他经(經)常把事实(實)和虚构(虛構)混淆起来(來)，使人困惑。
    - Tā jīngcháng bǎ shìshí hé xūgòu hùnxiáo qǐlái，shǐ rén kùnhuò。
    - He often confuses facts with fiction, which puzzles people.
### 混血 → 混 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 混血 → 混 → 昆
- **definition:** elder brother
- **pinyin:** kūn
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 比
    - bǐ
    - compare
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日 (rì, 'sun') + phonetic: 比 (bǐ, 'compare') =
    - combined light of two suns ->
    - descendants
  - **interpretation:** The character 昆 (kūn, 'descendants') uses the sun 日 (rì, 'sun') to symbolize extension or brightness, while 比 (bǐ, 'compare'), as a phonetic component, suggests reproduction or a continuation of a lineage. The combined imagery of two lights metaphorically communicates to the continuation of the family line through descendants.
- **examples:**
  - 他把祖先的遗(遺)志传给(傳給)了他的昆。
    - Tā bǎ zǔxiān de yízhì chuán gěi le tā de kūn。
    - He passed his ancestors' will to his descendants.
  - 家族兴(興)旺靠传(傳)承美德，昆代绵(綿)延见证(見證)未来(來)。
    - Jiāzú xīngwàng kào chuánchéng měidé, kūn dài miányán jiànzhèng wèilái。
    - Family prosperity relies on passing on virtues, and the descendants sustain the legacy for the future.
  - 长辈(長輩)辛勤耕耘历(歷)史，昆继(繼)承文化薪火相传(傳)。
    - Zhǎngbèi xīnqín gēngyún lìshǐ, kūn jìchéng wénhuà xīnhuǒ xiāngchuán。
    - The elders diligently cultivate history, while the descendants inherit and pass on the cultural legacy.
### 混血 → 混 → 昆 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来临时(來臨時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒满(灑滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.
### 混血 → 混 → 昆 → 比
- **definition:** compare
- **pinyin:** bǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Two figures standing side by side ->
    - concept of comparison
  - **interpretation:** The character 比(bǐ, "compare") depicts two human figures standing side by side, illustrating the act of comparing one thing to another. It's a visual representation that captures the idea of juxtaposition and evaluation.
- **examples:**
  - 他比我跑得快。
    - Tā bǐ wǒ pǎo de kuài.
    - He runs faster than I do.
  - 这(這)件衣服比那件衣服便宜。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfu bǐ nà jiàn yīfu piányí.
    - This piece of clothing is cheaper than that one.
  - 他比我聪(聰)明，而且比她勤奋(奮)。
    - Tā bǐ wǒ cōngmíng, érqiě bǐ tā qínfèn.
    - He is smarter than I am, and also more hardworking than she is.
### 混血 → 血
- **definition:** blood; bloodline; lineage
- **pinyin:** xuè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the container for sacrificial blood ->
    - 'blood'
  - **interpretation:** The character 血(xuè, "blood") originally depicted a container with blood dripping from it. This imagery effectively conveys the concept of blood, crucial both biologically and culturally.
- **examples:**
  - 英雄誓言以血为(為)盟。
    - Yīngxióng shìyán yǐ xuè wéi méng.
    - The hero swore an oath sealed with blood.
  - 家族的血脉(脈)永远(遠)相连(連)，传(傳)承着荣(著榮)耀。
    - Jiāzú de xuèmài yǒngyuǎn xiānglián, chuánchéng zhe róngyào.
    - The family's bloodline is forever connected, inheriting glory.
  - 流淌的鲜(鮮)血见证(見證)了牺(犧)牲。
    - Liútǎng de xuèxiě jiànzhèng le xīshēng.
    - The flowing blood testified to the sacrifice.
