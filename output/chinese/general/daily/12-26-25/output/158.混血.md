## mixed blood
### hùnxuè
---
- **definition:** mixed blood
- **pinyin:** hùnxuè
- **components:**
  - 混
    - hùn
    - mixed; blend
  - 血
    - xuè
    - blood
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 混(混) (hùn, "mixed") and 血(血) (xuè, "blood") combine to describe a person of mixed ethnic or racial ancestry.
  - **interpretation:** This combination of 混(混) (hùn, "mixed") and 血(血) (xuè, "blood") is used to refer to someone with a heritage that includes two or more different ethnicities or races.
- **examples:**
  - 他是混血(混血)。
    - Tā shì hùnxuè.
    - He is of mixed blood.
  - 她是混血女性(混血女性)，继承多元文化(多元文化)。
    - Tā shì hùnxuè nǚxìng, jìchéng duōyuán wénhuà.
    - She is a mixed-blood woman and inherits a multicultural background.
  - 在国际舞台上(國際舞台上)，混血代表着融合与创新(融合與創新)。
    - Zài guójì wǔtái shàng, hùnxuè dàibiǎo zhe rónghé yǔ chuàngxīn.
    - On the international stage, mixed blood represents integration and innovation.
