### beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
---
- **pinyin:** beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
- **components:**
  - 沉鱼(沉魚)
    - chén yú
    - sink fish
  - 落雁
    - luò yàn
    - falling wild geese
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 沉鱼(沉魚) (chén yú, 'sink fish') + 落雁(落雁) (luò yàn, 'falling wild geese') — beautiful woman causing fish to sink and birds to fall
  - **interpretation:** This phrase symbolizes extreme beauty that captivates and stuns both nature and creatures, illustrating how extraordinary attractiveness can evoke awe. The imagery creates a vivid picture of beauty's overwhelming effect.
- **examples:**
  - 她的美丽如沉鱼落雁，让人惊叹。
    - tā de měilì rú chén yú luò yàn, ràng rén jīngtàn。
    - Her beauty is like that of sinking fish and falling wild geese, astonishing everyone.
  - 在古代，沉鱼落雁常用来形容绝世美女。
    - zài gǔdài, chén yú luò yàn cháng yòng lái xíngróng juéshì měinǚ。
    - In ancient times, 'sinking fish and falling wild geese' was often used to describe a peerless beauty.
