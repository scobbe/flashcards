## beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
### chén yú luò yàn
---
- **definition:** beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
- **pinyin:** chén yú luò yàn
- **components:**
  - 沉
    - chén
    - sink
  - 鱼(魚)
    - yú
    - fish
  - 落
    - luò
    - fall; drop
  - 雁
    - yàn
    - wild goose
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 沉(沉) (chén, "sink") + 鱼(魚) (yú, "fish") + 落(落) (luò, "fall") + 雁(雁) (yàn, "wild goose") =
    - beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall. This phrase refers to a legendary level of beauty so stunning that fish forget to swim and sink, while birds forget to fly and fall.
  - **interpretation:** The phrase captures an ideal of beauty that is so profound it disrupts the natural order, with fish captivated to the point of sinking and birds so distracted they fall from the sky.
- **examples:**
  - 她美丽的面容让人想到沉鱼落雁(她美麗的面容讓人想到沉魚落雁)，令世人惊叹不已(令世人驚嘆不已)。
    - Tā měilì de miànróng ràng rén xiǎngdào chén yú luò yàn, lìng shìrén jīngtàn bùyǐ.
    - Her beautiful features evoke the beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall, leaving the world in awe.
  - 她的美貌仿佛能使沉鱼落雁(她的美貌彷彿能使沉魚落雁)，令文人雅士心醉(令文人雅士心醉)。
    - Tā de měimào fǎngfú néng shǐ chén yú luò yàn, lìng wénrén yǎshì xīnzuì.
    - Her enchanting beauty seems to embody the kind that sinks fish and makes birds fall, captivating the refined literati.
