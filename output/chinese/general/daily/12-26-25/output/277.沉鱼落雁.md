## beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
### chén yú luò yàn
---
- **definition:** beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
- **pinyin:** chén yú luò yàn
- **components:**
  - 沉鱼(沉魚)
    - chén yú
    - sinking fish; to make fish dive (by stunning beauty)
  - 落雁
    - luò yàn
    - falling wild geese; to make geese fall (by stunning beauty)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** idiom (chengyu; compound)
  - **description:** 沉鱼 (sink fish) + 落雁 (falling geese) = beauty so stunning that fish dive and geese drop.
  - **interpretation:** Hyperbolic praise for extraordinary feminine beauty; often paired with 闭月羞花 and linked to legendary beauties Xi Shi (沉鱼) and Wang Zhaojun (落雁).
  - **simplification:** 魚 → 鱼 in PRC simplification to reduce strokes; 沉 and 落雁 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 她的美貌可谓沉鱼落雁(她的美貌可謂沉魚落雁)。
    - Tā de měimào kěwèi chén yú luò yàn.
    - Her beauty could be described as so stunning that fish sink and geese fall.
  - 古人用沉鱼落雁来称颂绝色女子(古人用沉魚落雁來稱頌絕色女子)。
    - Gǔrén yòng chén yú luò yàn lái chēngsòng juésè nǚzǐ.
    - The ancients used ‘chén yú luò yàn’ to praise women of peerless beauty.
  - 这幅画把女子画得有沉鱼落雁之姿(這幅畫把女子畫得有沉魚落雁之姿)。
    - Zhè fú huà bǎ nǚzǐ huà de yǒu chén yú luò yàn zhī zī.
    - This painting portrays the woman with beauty said to sink fish and fell geese.
### 沉鱼落雁(沉魚落雁) → 沉鱼(沉魚)
- **definition:** sinking fish; to make fish dive (by stunning beauty)
- **pinyin:** chén yú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word (verb–object)
  - **description:** 沉(sink) + 鱼(fish) = fish sink/dive; by extension, beauty so stunning that fish dive to hide.
  - **interpretation:** Literally describes fish diving under the water; metonymic abbreviation of the idiom 沉鱼落雁, praising breathtaking female beauty, traditionally linked with Xīshī.
  - **simplification:** 魚 → 鱼 to reduce strokes; 沉 unchanged. The word simplifies because of the 魚 component.
- **examples:**
  - 她有沉鱼之貌(她有沉魚之貌)。
    - Tā yǒu chényú zhī mào.
    - She has beauty that makes fish dive.
  - 西施常被称为“沉鱼”，源自成语“沉鱼落雁”(西施常被稱為「沉魚」，源自成語「沉魚落雁」。)
    - Xīshī cháng bèi chēng wèi “chényú”, yuánzì chéngyǔ “chényú luòyàn”.
    - Xishi is often labeled “chenyu,” from the idiom “fish sink, geese fall.”
  - 炮声一响，湖中沉鱼，水面立刻安静下来(炮聲一響，湖中沉魚，水面立刻安靜下來)。
    - Pàoshēng yī xiǎng, hú zhōng chényú, shuǐmiàn lìkè ānjìng xiàlái.
    - At the bang of the cannon, the fish in the lake dived and the surface quickly grew still.
