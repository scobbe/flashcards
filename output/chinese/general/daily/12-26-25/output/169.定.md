### decide; fix
---
- **pinyin:** decide; fix
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 正
    - zhèng
    - correct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 宀 (mián, 'roof') suggesting a fixed or stable place, with the phonetic element 正 (zhèng, 'correct') which contributes to the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of a roof over a correct or stable place evokes a sense of fixing or determining a location or situation.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要定一个时间(我們需要定一個時間)。
    - wǒmen xūyào dìng yīgè shíjiān.
    - We need to set a time.
  - 请你定这个计划(請你定這個計劃)。
    - qǐng nǐ dìng zhège jìhuà.
    - Please finalize this plan.
  - 他总是定下目标(他總是定下目標)。
    - tā zǒng shì dìng xià mùbiāo.
    - He always sets goals.
### 定(定) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of a roof
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles a roof structure, evoking the idea of shelter or home.
- **examples:**
  - 房子有个宀(房子有個宀)，很温暖(很溫暖)。
    - fángzi yǒu gè mián, hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - The house has a roof, which is very warm.
  - 小屋的宀很古老(小屋的宀很古老)。
    - xiǎowū de mián hěn gǔlǎo.
    - The roof of the cottage is very old.
### 定(定) → 正(正)
- **definition:** correct
- **pinyin:** zhèng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Originally depicted the idea of straightness and correctness.
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys a sense of uprightness or correctness, as it visually aligns with these concepts.
- **examples:**
  - 我们要正直(我們要正直)，做一个正义的人(做一個正義的人)。
  - 他的话很正(他的話很正)，非常有道理(非常有道理)。
  - 她的选择是正的(她的選擇是正的)，值得尊重(值得尊重)。
