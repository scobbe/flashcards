## beverage
### yǐnliào
---
- **definition:** beverage
- **pinyin:** yǐnliào
- **components:**
  - 饮(飲)
    - yǐn
    - drink; swallow
  - 料
    - liào
    - material; ingredient; estimate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") refers to the act of consuming liquids, and 料(料) (liào, "material; ingredient") refers to substances used in making something. Together, 饮料(飲料) (yǐnliào, "beverage") indicates a prepared drink made from various ingredients.
  - **interpretation:** A 饮料(飲料) (yǐnliào, "beverage") is a liquid concoction meant for consumption, often made with multiple ingredients to create a flavorful drink.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝饮料(我想喝飲料)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng hē yǐnliào.
    - I want to drink a beverage.
  - 商店里有各种各样的饮料(商店裡有各種各樣的飲料)。
    - Shāngdiàn lǐ yǒu gèzhǒng gèyàng de yǐnliào.
    - There are various kinds of beverages in the store.
  - 他吃完饭后(他吃完飯後)，买了一瓶饮料(買了一瓶飲料)。
    - Tā chī wán fàn hòu, mǎi le yì píng yǐnliào.
    - After he finished his meal, he bought a bottle of beverage.
### 饮料(飲料) → 饮(飲)
- **definition:** drink; swallow
- **pinyin:** yǐn
- **components:**
  - 食
    - shí
    - to eat
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 酉(酉) (yǒu, "bottle") + 人(人) (rén, "person") + 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") ->
    - person bending over a bottle to drink ->
    - bronze script: 口 separate, become 今(今) (jīn), rest of body becomes 欠(欠) (qiàn) ->
    - final: 食(食) (shí, "eat") + 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") ->
    - 飲(飲)
  - **interpretation:** The character illustrates the act of drinking as a person bent over a bottle. Over time, the depiction was stylized, repurposing 食(食) (shí, "eat") and 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") to retain the original meaning phonetically and semantically.
  - **simplification:** In simplified Chinese, 食(食) (shí, "eat") is retained and 欠(欠) (qiàn, "yawn") remains, forming 饮. The character's form was likely adjusted for ease while maintaining pronunciation and meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我想饮茶(我想飲茶)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yǐn chá.
    - I want to drink tea.
  - 炎热的夏天(炎熱的夏天)，我常常饮上一杯冰水(我常常飲上一杯冰水)。
    - Yánrè de xiàtiān, wǒ chángcháng yǐn shàng yì bēi bīngshuǐ.
    - In the hot summer, I often drink a glass of iced water.
  - 他饮完了酒(他飲完了酒)，便上路了(便上路了)。
    - Tā yǐn wán le jiǔ, biàn shànglù le.
    - After he finished his drink, he set off.
