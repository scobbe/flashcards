## beverage
### yǐnliào
---
- **traditional:** 飲料
- **definition:** beverage
- **pinyin:** yǐnliào
- **components:**
  - 饮(飲)
    - yǐn
    - drink; swallow
  - 料
    - liào
    - material; ingredient; estimate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") refers to the act of consuming liquids, and 料 (liào, "material; ingredient") refers to substances used in making something. Together, 饮(飲)料 (yǐnliào, "beverage") indicates a prepared drink made from various ingredients.
  - **interpretation:** A 饮(飲)料 (yǐnliào, "beverage") is a liquid concoction meant for consumption, often made with multiple ingredients to create a flavorful drink.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝饮(飲)料。
    - Wǒ xiǎng hē yǐnliào.
    - I want to drink a beverage.
  - 商店里(裡)有各种(種)各样(樣)的饮(飲)料。
    - Shāngdiàn lǐ yǒu gèzhǒng gèyàng de yǐnliào.
    - There are various kinds of beverages in the store.
  - 他吃完饭后(飯後)，买(買)了一瓶饮(飲)料。
    - Tā chī wán fàn hòu, mǎi le yì píng yǐnliào.
    - After he finished his meal, he bought a bottle of beverage.
### 饮(飲)料( ) → 饮(飲)
- **traditional:** 飲
- **definition:** drink; swallow
- **pinyin:** yǐn
- **components:**
  - 食
    - shí
    - to eat
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 酉 (yǒu, "bottle") + 人 (rén, "person") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth") ->
    - person bending over a bottle to drink ->
    - bronze script: 口 separate, become 今 (jīn), rest of body becomes 欠 (qiàn) ->
    - final: 食 (shí, "eat") + 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") ->
    - 飲
  - **interpretation:** The character illustrates the act of drinking as a person bent over a bottle. Over time, the depiction was stylized, repurposing 食 (shí, "eat") and 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") to retain the original meaning phonetically and semantically.
  - **simplification:** In simplified Chinese, 食 (shí, "eat") is retained and 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") remains, forming 饮. The character's form was likely adjusted for ease while maintaining pronunciation and meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我想饮(飲)茶。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yǐn chá.
    - I want to drink tea.
  - 炎热(熱)的夏天，我常常饮(飲)上一杯冰水。
    - Yánrè de xiàtiān, wǒ chángcháng yǐn shàng yì bēi bīngshuǐ.
