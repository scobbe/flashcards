## beverage
### yǐnliào
---
- **definition:** beverage
- **pinyin:** yǐnliào
- **components:**
  - 饮(飲)
    - yǐn
    - drink
  - 料
    - liào
    - material; expect; estimate; feed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") and 料(料) (liào, "material") together form 饮料(飲料) (yǐnliào, "beverage").
  - **interpretation:** A 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") made from various 料(料) (liào, "materials") refers to any prepared liquid for consumption, thus defining a beverage.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢喝冰饮料(他喜歡喝冰飲料)。
    - Tā xǐhuān hē bīng yǐnliào.
    - He likes to drink cold beverages.
  - 夏日炎炎(夏日炎炎)，人们渴望饮料(人們渴望飲料)。
    - Xiàrì yányán, rénmen kěwàng yǐnliào.
    - On hot summer days, people long for beverages.
### 饮料(飲料) → 饮(飲)
- **definition:** drink
- **pinyin:** yǐn
- **components:**
  - 饣(食)
    - shí
    - food
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 饣(食) (shí, "food") + phonetic: 欠(欠) (qiàn) =
    - consuming food/drink ->
    - drink
  - **interpretation:** The character 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") combines the food radical 饣(食) (shí, "food") with 欠(欠) (qiàn), which often implies a mouth-related action. Together, they naturally convey the act of drinking.
  - **simplification:** 饣(食) (shí, "food") is a simplified form of the full 食(食) to streamline writing. 欠(欠) remained the same.
- **examples:**
  - 我饮水(我飲水)。
    - Wǒ yǐn shuǐ.
    - I drink water.
  - 他喜欢饮咖啡(他喜歡飲咖啡)，早晨提神效果好(早晨提神效果好)。
    - Tā xǐhuān yǐn kāfēi, zǎochén tíshén xiàoguǒ hǎo.
    - He likes to drink coffee, and it has a refreshing effect in the morning.
  - 运动后应及时饮水(運動後應及時飲水)。
    - Yùndòng hòu yīng jíshí yǐn shuǐ.
    - After exercising, one should drink water promptly.
### 饮料(飲料) → 饮(飲) → 饣(食)
- **definition:** food
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram ( 象形 )
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a mouth 亼 (jié, 'assemble') over a bowl of rice on a stand, represented by 皀 (bó, 'pictogram'), resembling but unrelated to 良 (liáng, 'good') or 艮 (gěn, 'stopping') ->
    - food
  - **interpretation:** The character 食(食) (shí, 'food') visually represents a scene of eating, with a mouth positioned above a bowl or stand of food, encapsulating the concept of nourishment.
  - **simplification:** 饣 is a simplified radical form representing 食(食). It retains the core idea by using a cursive form that resembles the original components, primarily focusing on the 'food' aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 许多汉字中含有饣部(許多漢字中含有食部)，它们与食物有密切关联(它們與食物有密切關聯)。
    - Xǔduō hànzì zhōng hányǒu shí bù, tāmen yǔ shíwù yǒu mìqiè guānlián.
    - Many Chinese characters contain the '饣' radical, which is closely related to food.
  - 博物馆展览介绍了饣的演变(博物館展覽介紹了食的演變)，反映出人们对食的热爱(反映出人們對食的熱愛)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnlǎn jièshào le shí de yǎnbiàn, fǎnyìng chū rénmen duì shí de rè'ài.
    - The museum exhibition introduced the evolution of the '饣' radical, reflecting people's love for food.

### 饮料(飲料) → 料(料)

- **pinyin:** liào
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ

    - dǒu

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") + 斗(斗) (dǒu, "measuring container" or phonetic "estimate") =
    - measuring grain ->
    - material; expect; estimate; feed
  - **interpretation:** The character 料(liào) combines 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") with 斗(斗) (dǒu, "measuring container"), both of which contribute to the idea of measuring and estimating quantities of grain. This directly ties to the meanings of preparing or planning materials and expectations.

### 饮料(飲料) → 料(料) → 米(米)

- **pinyin:** mǐ
