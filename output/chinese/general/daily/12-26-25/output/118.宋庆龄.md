## Song Qingling
### Sòng Qìnglíng
---
- **definition:** Song Qingling
- **pinyin:** Sòng Qìnglíng
- **components:**
  - 宋
    - Sòng
    - Song (surname); Song dynasty; ancient state of Song
  - 庆龄(慶齡)
    - Qìnglíng
    - Qingling (given name); celebrate; felicitation; age/years
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** personal name
  - **description:** 宋 (surname) + 庆龄 (given name) = the personal name “Song Qingling”.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the family name 宋 with the given name 庆龄, literally “celebrate + years/age.” Refers to Soong Ching-ling, a major 20th‑century Chinese political figure and wife of Sun Yat-sen.
  - **simplification:** 慶→庆 simplifies complex components; 齡→龄 via the 齒→齿 series. Both reduce stroke count and follow standard PRC simplification patterns.
- **examples:**
  - 宋庆龄是中国近现代史上的重要人物(宋慶齡是中國近現代史上的重要人物)。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng shì Zhōngguó jìn xiàndài shǐ shàng de zhòngyào rénwù.
    - Song Qingling is an important figure in China’s modern and contemporary history.
  - 她是孙中山的妻子，也曾担任中华人民共和国名誉主席(她是孫中山的妻子，也曾擔任中華人民共和國名譽主席)。
    - Tā shì Sūn Zhōngshān de qīzi, yě céng dānrèn Zhōnghuá Rénmín Gònghéguó míngyù zhǔxí.
    - She was the wife of Sun Yat-sen and also served as the Honorary President of the PRC.
  - 人们常用宋庆龄的名字命名学校和道路(人們常用宋慶齡的名字命名學校和道路)。
    - Rénmen cháng yòng Sòng Qìnglíng de míngzi mìngmíng xuéxiào hé dàolù.
    - People often name schools and roads after Song Qingling.
### 宋庆龄(宋慶齡) → 宋
- **definition:** Song (surname); Song dynasty; ancient state of Song
- **pinyin:** Sòng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood; tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound (會意)
  - **description:** Ideogrammic compound (會意): 宀 (“roof”) + 木 (“tree; wood”).
  - **interpretation:** Combines the roof sign with a tree/wood to suggest a timbered building or hall, later associated with an ancestral hall or dwelling. The graph was adopted as the name of a state and subsequently a surname and the name of a dynasty, and it also labels the Song-style typeface.
- **examples:**
  - 他姓宋(他姓宋)。
    - Tā xìng Sòng.
    - His surname is Song.
  - 宋朝在科技和文化上很发达(宋朝在科技和文化上很發達)。
    - Sòng Cháo zài kējì hé wénhuà shàng hěn fādá.
    - The Song dynasty was advanced in science and culture.
  - 这份报告用宋体排版(這份報告用宋體排版)。
    - Zhè fèn bàogào yòng Sòngtǐ páibǎn.
    - This report is typeset in the Song typeface.
### 宋庆龄(宋慶齡) → 宋 → 木
- **definition:** wood; tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram (象形)
  - **description:** a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom (more visible in earlier forms).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tree with spreading branches above and roots below, as seen clearly in early scripts. The vertical stroke is the trunk, while the short horizontal strokes became stylized limbs and roots in the regular form.
- **examples:**
  - 这张桌子是木头做的(這張桌子是木頭做的)。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi shì mùtou zuò de.
    - This table is made of wood.
  - 山上长满了树木(山上長滿了樹木)。
    - Shān shang zhǎng mǎn le shùmù.
    - The mountainside is covered with trees.
  - 木材被运到工厂加工(木材被運到工廠加工)。
    - Mùcái bèi yùn dào gōngchǎng jiāgōng.
    - The timber was transported to the factory for processing.
### 宋庆龄(宋慶齡) → 庆龄(慶齡)
- **definition:** Qingling (given name); celebrate; felicitation; age/years
- **pinyin:** Qìnglíng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 庆(慶, celebrate; auspicious) + 龄(齡, age) = celebratory age; blessed longevity
  - **interpretation:** Literary/ceremonial term praising a person's age and longevity, often used in felicitations for elders. Conveys an age endowed with auspicious blessings and worthy of celebration.
  - **simplification:** 慶→庆 drops the 心 component and adopts a simpler historical form; 齡→龄 replaces the complex 齒 with simplified 齿 while keeping the phonetic 令, improving writeability.
- **examples:**
  - 祝您福寿绵长(祝您福壽綿長)，庆龄常在(慶齡常在)。
    - Zhù nín fúshòu miáncháng, qìnglíng cháng zài.
    - Wish you long-lasting blessings and longevity; may your celebrated years endure.
  - 长者九十寿辰(長者九十壽辰)，全家齐来祝贺其庆龄(全家齊來祝賀其慶齡)。
    - Zhǎngzhě jiǔshí shòuchén, quánjiā qí lái zhùhè qí qìnglíng.
    - At the elder’s 90th birthday, the whole family gathered to congratulate his blessed longevity.
