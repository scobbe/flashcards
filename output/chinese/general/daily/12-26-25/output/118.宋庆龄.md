## Song Qingling
### Sòng Qìnglíng
---
- **traditional:** 宋慶齡
- **definition:** Song Qingling
- **pinyin:** Sòng Qìnglíng
- **components:**
  - 宋
    - Sòng
    - Song; surname
  - 庆(慶)
    - Qìng
    - celebrate; congratulate; praise
  - 龄(齡)
    - líng
    - age; years; time
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 宋 (Sòng, "Song") is a surname that can be traced back to the Song Dynasty. 庆(慶) (Qìng, "celebrate") conveys a sense of joy and auspiciousness. 龄(齡) (líng, "age") refers to a person's age, but in names, it often suggests wisdom or longevity.
  - **interpretation:** 宋 (Sòng, "Song") is the surname, and 慶齡 (Qìnglíng) together can suggest 'joyful and wise'. Song Qingling (宋慶齡) was a prominent Chinese political figure and the wife of Sun Yat-sen, playing an integral role in the political landscape of China.
- **examples:**
  - 宋庆龄(慶齡)致力于国际(於國際)和平，获(獲)得了世界的认(認)可。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng zhìlì yú guójì hépíng, huòdéle shìjiè de rènkě.
    - Song Qingling dedicated her efforts to international peace and earned global recognition.
  - 宋庆龄(慶齡)的智慧与(與)勇气(氣)激励(勵)了无数(無數)人。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng de zhìhuì yǔ yǒngqì jīlìle wúshù rén.
    - Song Qingling's wisdom and courage inspired countless people.
  - 宋庆龄(慶齡)倡导(導)的人权(權)理念影响(響)深远(遠)。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng chàngdǎo de rénquán lǐniàn yǐngxiǎng shēnyuǎn.
    - Song Qingling's advocacy for human rights has had a profound impact.
