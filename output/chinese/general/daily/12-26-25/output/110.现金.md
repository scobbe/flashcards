## cash
### xiànjīn
---
- **definition:** cash
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **components:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - current; present; appear
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold; money; metal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 现(現) (xiàn, "current"; "present"; "appear") refers to the present time or situation. 金(金) (jīn, "gold"; "money"; "metal") represents wealth or monetary resources. Together, 现金(現金) (xiànjīn) describes money that is immediately available for transactions - cash.
  - **interpretation:** The term 现金(現金) (xiànjīn, "cash") signifies money available for use right now, emphasizing both its availability and standard function as a currency.
- **examples:**
  - 我用现金买了面包(我用現金買了麵包)。
    - Wǒ yòng xiànjīn mǎile miànbāo.
    - I bought bread with cash.
  - 商店只接受现金(商店只接受現金)，不接受信用卡(不接受信用卡)。
    - Shāngdiàn zhǐ jiēshòu xiànjīn, bù jiēshòu xìnyòngkǎ.
    - The store accepts only cash, not credit cards.
  - 出门前(出門前)，他检查是否携带了现金(他檢查是否攜帶了現金)。
    - Chūmén qián, tā jiǎnchá shìfǒu xiédài le xiànjīn.
    - Before leaving home, he checked whether he brought cash.
