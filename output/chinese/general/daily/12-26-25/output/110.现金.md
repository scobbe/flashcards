### cash
---
- **pinyin:** cash
- **components:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - current; present
  - 金(錢)
    - jīn
    - money; metal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 现(現) (xiàn, 'current; present') + 金(錢) (jīn, 'money; metal') — current money
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to money that is available immediately, as opposed to credit or other forms of payment. It makes sense as it literally denotes 'present money' or readily available funds.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一些现金(我需要一些現金)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yīxiē xiànjīn.
    - I need some cash.
  - 商店只接受现金(商店只接受現金)。
    - Shāngdiàn zhǐ jiēshòu xiànjīn.
    - The store only accepts cash.
