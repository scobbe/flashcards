## cash
### xiànjīn
---
- **definition:** cash
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **components:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - current; present; existing
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold; money; metals
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 现(現) (xiàn, 'current; present') combined with 金(金) (jīn, 'gold; money') refers to money that is currently available in a tangible, physical form.
  - **interpretation:** The term 现金(現金) (xiànjīn) indicates money that is immediately accessible and usable, like physical currency, which is 'gold' or 'money' you can physically handle right now.
- **examples:**
  - 我用现金购买礼物(我用現金購買禮物)。
    - Wǒ yòng xiànjīn gòumǎi lǐwù.
    - I buy gifts with cash.
  - 商店促销时(商店促銷時)，顾客倾向于使用现金(顧客傾向於使用現金)。
    - Shāngdiàn cùxiāo shí, gùkè qīngxiàng yú shǐyòng xiànjīn.
    - During store promotions, customers tend to use cash.
  - 他习惯于用信用卡支付(他習慣於用信用卡支付)，但在小摊上他更愿意用现金(但在小攤上他更願意用現金)。
    - Tā xíguàn yú yòng xìnyòngkǎ zhīfù, dàn zài xiǎotān shàng tā gèng yuànyì yòng xiànjīn.
    - He is used to paying with credit cards, but at small stalls he prefers to use cash.
