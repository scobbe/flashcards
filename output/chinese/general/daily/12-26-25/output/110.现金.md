## cash
### xiànjīn
---
- **traditional:** 現金
- **definition:** cash
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **components:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - current; present; appear
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold; money; metal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 现(現) (xiàn, "current"; "present"; "appear") refers to the present time or situation. 金 (jīn, "gold"; "money"; "metal") represents wealth or monetary resources. Together, 现(現)金 (xiànjīn) describes money that is immediately available for transactions - cash.
  - **interpretation:** The term 现(現)金 (xiànjīn, "cash") signifies money available for use right now, emphasizing both its availability and standard function as a currency.
- **examples:**
  - 我用现(現)金买(買)了面(麵)包。
    - Wǒ yòng xiànjīn mǎile miànbāo.
    - I bought bread with cash.
  - 商店只接受现(現)金，不接受信用卡。
    - Shāngdiàn zhǐ jiēshòu xiànjīn, bù jiēshòu xìnyòngkǎ.
    - The store accepts only cash, not credit cards.
  - 出门(門)前，他检(檢)查是否携带(攜帶)了现(現)金。
    - Chūmén qián, tā jiǎnchá shìfǒu xiédài le xiànjīn.
    - Before leaving home, he checked whether he brought cash.
