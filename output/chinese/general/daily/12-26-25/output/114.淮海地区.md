## Huaihai region
### Huáihǎi dìqū
---
- **definition:** Huaihai region
- **pinyin:** Huáihǎi dìqū
- **components:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea
  - 地区(地區)
    - dìqū
    - region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River") + 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") + 地区(地區) (dìqū, "region") =
    - Huaihai region
  - **interpretation:** The Huaihai region refers to the geographical area in China between the Huai River and the coast. It combines 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River"), representing the river's influence in the region, with 海(海) (hǎi, "sea"), indicating proximity to the sea. The term 地区(地區) (dìqū, "region") denotes the area or locality of interest.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海地区风景秀丽(淮海地區風景秀麗)。
    - Huáihǎi dìqū fēngjǐng xiùlì.
    - The Huaihai region has beautiful scenery.
  - 淮海地区历史悠久(淮海地區歷史悠久)，文化底蕴深厚(文化底蘊深厚)。
    - Huáihǎi dìqū lìshǐ yōujiǔ, wénhuà dǐyùn shēnhòu.
    - The Huaihai region has a long history and a profound cultural heritage.
  - 我计划去淮海地区旅行(我計劃去淮海地區旅行)，体验当地风土人情(體驗當地風土人情)。
    - Wǒ jìhuà qù Huáihǎi dìqū lǚxíng, tǐyàn dāngdì fēngtǔ rénqíng.
    - I plan to travel to the Huaihai region to experience the local customs and lifestyle.

### 淮海地区(淮海地區) → 淮(淮) → 隹(隹)
- **definition:** short-tailed bird
- **pinyin:** zhuī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a short-tailed bird
  - **interpretation:** 隹(隹) (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") originated as a pictorial representation of a bird with a short tail, a feature common in sparrows and similar birds. It is the original character for depicting small birds and its form suggests this particular physical trait, distinguishing it from more generic bird characters like 鳥(鳥) (niǎo, "bird").
  - **simplification:** None, identical in traditional and simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 早晨时分(早晨時分)，我在公园中遇到一只隹(我在公園中遇到一只隹)。
    - Zǎochén shífēn, wǒ zài gōngyuán zhōng yùdào yì zhī zhuī.
    - In the early morning, I encountered a short-tailed bird in the park.
  - 黄昏时刻(黃昏時刻)，隹在树枝间轻快地跳跃(隹在樹枝間輕快地跳躍)。
    - Huánghūn shíkè, zhuī zài shùzhī jiān qīngkuài de tiàoyuè.
    - At dusk, the short-tailed bird lightly hops between the branches.
