### Huaihai region
---
- **pinyin:** Huaihai region
- **components:**
  - 淮海
    - huái hǎi
    - Huai River; sea/ocean
  - 地区(地區)
    - dì qū
    - region; area; district
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River") + 海(海) (hǎi, "sea/ocean") + 地区(地區) (dìqū, "region/area") — Huai River sea region
  - **interpretation:** 淮海地区 refers to a region named after the Huai River and the sea, indicating its geographical significance. This combination highlights the area’s connection to both the river and its coastal aspects.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海地区是中国一个重要的经济区(淮海地區是中國一個重要的經濟區)。
    - Huái hǎi dì qū shì Zhōngguó yī gè zhòngyào de jīngjì qū.
    - The Huaihai region is an important economic area in China.
  - 我们计划在淮海地区开设分支机构(我們計畫在淮海地區開設分支機構)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà zài Huái hǎi dì qū kāishè fēnzhī jīgòu.
    - We plan to establish a branch office in the Huaihai region.
