### Chen Yi
---
- **pinyin:** Chen Yi
- **components:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - to display; to explain; ancient; surname
  - 毅
    - yì
    - resolute; brave; steadfast
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 陳(陈) (chén, "to display; to explain") + 毅 (yì, "resolute; brave") — display bravery
  - **interpretation:** This name combines the meanings of 'display' and 'resolute', reflecting a strong character and commitment. It is well-known as the name of a prominent Chinese military leader.
- **examples:**
  - 陈毅是中国的一位著名将领(陳毅是中國的一位著名將領)。
    - Chén Yì shì Zhōngguó de yī wèi zhùmíng jiànglǐng。
    - Chen Yi is a famous general in China.
  - 在众多的历史人物中(在眾多的歷史人物中)，陈毅的贡献值得铭记(陳毅的貢獻值得銘記)。
    - Zài zhòngduō de lìshǐ rénwù zhōng, Chén Yì de gòngxiàn zhídé míngjì。
    - Among many historical figures, Chen Yi's contributions are worth remembering.
### 陈毅(陳毅) → 陈(陳)
- **definition:** to display; to explain; ancient; surname
- **pinyin:** chén
- **components:**
  - 阝
    - fǔ
    - hill
  - 辰
    - chén
    - time
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Comprises the semantic element 阝 (a hill) and the phonetic element 辰 (chén, 'time').
  - **interpretation:** A hill represents a place or a way of showcasing something, and 'time' suggests it's a display of something from the past.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by replacing the component 陳 (chén) with 陈 (chén), which has fewer strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 他在展览会上展示了许多古董(他在展覽會上展示了許多古董)。
    - Tā zài zhǎnlǎn huì shàng zhǎnshìle xǔduō gǔdǒng。
    - He exhibited many antiques at the exhibition.
  - 这件作品表现出古代文化(這件作品表現出古代文化)。
    - Zhè jiàn zuòpǐn biǎoxiàn chū gǔdài wénhuà。
    - This piece of work displays ancient culture.
  - 他的姓是陈(他的姓是陳)。
    - Tā de xìng shì Chén。
    - His surname is Chen.

### 陈毅(陳毅) → 陈(陳) → 辰(辰)
- **definition:** time
- **pinyin:** chén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents a celestial body or time, originally referring to the Chinese zodiac sign (the Dragon) and thus associated with specific moments.
  - **interpretation:** The character implies time as it relates to celestial movements and astrology, evoking the cyclical nature of time in Chinese tradition.
- **examples:**
  - 我们计算时间(我們計算時間)。
    - Wǒmen jìsuàn shíjiān.
    - We calculate time.
  - 辰时是早上九点到十一点(辰時是早上九點到十一點)。
    - Chén shí shì zǎoshang jiǔ diǎn dào shíyī diǎn.
    - Chén time is from 9 AM to 11 AM.
