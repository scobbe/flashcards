## longevity
### shòu
---
- **definition:** longevity
- **pinyin:** shòu
- **components:**
  - 耂
    - lǎo
    - old
  - 𢏚
    - unknown
    - unknown pronunciation
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 耂(耂) (lǎo, "old") + 𢏚(𢏚) (unknown pronunciation) with decorative 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") and 又(又) (yòu, "hand") ->
    - 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") replacing 又 ->
    - 壽(壽)
  - **interpretation:** The character 壽(壽) (shòu, "longevity") combines elements representing age and experience with phonetic components to convey the concept of living a long life.
  - **simplification:** In simplified form, 寿(壽) drops the decorative elements and consolidates components, prioritizing phonetic elements for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 祝你长寿(祝你長壽)。
    - Zhù nǐ chángshòu.
    - Wishing you longevity.
  - 福如东海(福如東海)，寿比南山(壽比南山)。
    - Fú rú dōnghǎi, shòu bǐ nánshān.
    - May your blessings be as vast as the Eastern Sea and your longevity as enduring as the Southern Mountain.
