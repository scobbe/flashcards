## longevity
### shòu
---
- **definition:** longevity
- **pinyin:** shòu
- **components:**
  - 士
    - shì
    - scholar
  - 叀
    - zhuān
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 士(士) (shì, "scholar") + phonetic: 叀(叀) (zhuān) =
    - scholar sitting to listen to learn and live long ->
    - longevity
  - **interpretation:** The character 寿(壽) (shòu, "longevity") combines a depiction of scholarship and learning with a phonetic component to convey the idea of living a long life through knowledge and wisdom.
  - **simplification:** During simplification, 壽(壽) was streamlined to 寿(壽) by removing the complex elements and retaining a simpler form while preserving the phonetic and semantic essence.
- **examples:**
  - 他盼望长寿(他盼望長壽)。
    - Tā pànwàng chángshòu.
    - He hopes for longevity.
  - 家人团聚时(家人團聚時)，大家都祝愿长寿(大家都祝願長壽)。
    - Jiārén tuánjù shí, dàjiā dōu zhùyuàn chángshòu.
    - When the family gathers, everyone wishes for longevity.
  - 寿星收到贺卡祝福(壽星收到賀卡祝福)。
    - Shòuxīng shōudào hèkǎ zhùfú.
    - The birthday person receives wishes on a greeting card.
### 寿(壽) → 士(士)
- **definition:** scholar
- **pinyin:** shì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an upside-down ritual axe ->
    - later meaning soldier ->
    - eventually officer or intellectual
  - **interpretation:** The character 士(士) (shì, 'officer') originally depicted a ritual axe, symbolizing authority and power. Over time, it evolved to signify roles associated with leadership and scholarship.
- **examples:**

### 寿(壽) → 叀(叀)
- **definition:** phonetic
- **pinyin:** zhuān
- **components:**
  - 專
    - zhuān
    - specialized
  - 惠
    - huì
    - favor
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a spindle ->
    - Original form of 專(專) (zhuān, "specialized") and 惠(惠) (huì, "favor")
  - **interpretation:** The character 叀 originally depicted a spindle, which symbolizes the spinning process. This imagery was then borrowed and adapted into the characters 專(專) (zhuān, "specialized") and 惠(惠) (huì, "favor"), but 叀 itself remains as the pictographic representation.
### 寿(壽) → 叀(叀) → 專(專)
- **definition:** specialized
- **pinyin:** zhuān
- **components:**
  - 叀
    - zhuān
    - spindle
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - inch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 叀(叀) (zhuān, "spindle") + 又(又) (yòu, "hand") =
    - a hand turning a spindle ->
    - semantic 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") later replaced 又 ->

  - **interpretation:** 專(zhuān) ("focused") combines the concept of a spindle, which symbolizes spinning or bundling, with the notion of a hand, suggesting action or manipulation. The hand component was later stylized to an inch, indicating precision or concentration, aligning with the idea of focusing multiple elements into one.

### 寿(壽) → 叀(叀) → 專(專) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** inch
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布每一寸都是精心制作(這塊布每一寸都是精心製作)。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请用卷尺量一寸(請用卷尺量一寸)，每一寸数据都准确(每一寸數據都準確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.
