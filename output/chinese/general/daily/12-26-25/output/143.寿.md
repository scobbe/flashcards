### longevity
---
- **pinyin:** longevity
- **components:**
  - 壽
    - shòu
    - longevity
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 壽 (shòu, 'longevity') with the phonetic component, indicating a concept of long life.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the idea of a long life and health, a common wish in Chinese culture.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing 壽 with 寿, which retains the meaning but uses fewer strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 祝你长寿(祝你長壽)！
    - Zhù nǐ cháng shòu！
    - Wishing you longevity!
  - 寿桃是生日的象征(壽桃是生日的象徵)。
    - Shòutáo shì shēngrì de xiàngzhēng。
    - Longevity peaches are symbols of birthdays.
### 寿(壽) → 壽(壽)
- **definition:** longevity
- **pinyin:** shòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character symbolizes the concept of longevity and long life.
  - **interpretation:** The character portrays the idea of enduring life and well-being, resonating with cultural values surrounding longevity.
- **examples:**
  - 祝你长寿！
    - zhù nǐ cháng shòu！
    - Wishing you longevity!
  - 寿桃象征长寿。
    - shòu táo xiàngzhēng cháng shòu。
    - Peach of longevity symbolizes long life.
  - 我们庆祝他的百岁寿诞。
    - wǒmen qìngzhù tā de bǎi suì shòu dàn。
    - We celebrate his hundredth birthday.
