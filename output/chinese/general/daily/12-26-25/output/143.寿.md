## longevity
### shòu
---
- **definition:** longevity
- **pinyin:** shòu
- **components:**
  - 士
    - shì
    - scholar
  - 叀
    - zhuān
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 士(士) (shì, "scholar") + phonetic: 叀(叀) (zhuān) =
    - scholar sitting to listen to learn and live long ->
    - longevity
  - **interpretation:** The character 寿(壽) (shòu, "longevity") combines a depiction of scholarship and learning with a phonetic component to convey the idea of living a long life through knowledge and wisdom.
  - **simplification:** During simplification, 壽(壽) was streamlined to 寿(壽) by removing the complex elements and retaining a simpler form while preserving the phonetic and semantic essence.
- **examples:**
  - 他盼望长寿(他盼望長壽)。
    - Tā pànwàng chángshòu.
    - He hopes for longevity.
  - 家人团聚时(家人團聚時)，大家都祝愿长寿(大家都祝願長壽)。
    - Jiārén tuánjù shí, dàjiā dōu zhùyuàn chángshòu.
    - When the family gathers, everyone wishes for longevity.
  - 寿星收到贺卡祝福(壽星收到賀卡祝福)。
    - Shòuxīng shōudào hèkǎ zhùfú.
    - The birthday person receives wishes on a greeting card.
### 寿(壽) → 士(士)
- **definition:** scholar
- **pinyin:** shì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an upside-down ritual axe ->
    - later meaning soldier ->
    - eventually officer or intellectual
  - **interpretation:** The character 士(士) (shì, 'officer') originally depicted a ritual axe, symbolizing authority and power. Over time, it evolved to signify roles associated with leadership and scholarship.
- **examples:**
  - 博物馆今日展出了士(士)，展示古代祭祀器物(展示古代祭祀器物)。
    - Bówùguǎn jīnrì zhǎnchūle shì(士)，zhǎnshì gǔdài jìsì qìwù(展示古代祭祀器物)。
    - The museum exhibited the shì today, showcasing ancient sacrificial vessels.
  - 考古学家在遗址中发现了一把士(士)，被认为是祭祀用的珍贵器物(被認為是祭祀用的珍貴器物)。
    - Kǎogǔ xuéjiā zài yízhǐ zhōng fāxiànle yī bǎ shì(士)，bèi rènwéi shì jìsì yòng de zhēnguì qìwù(被認為是祭祀用的珍貴器物)。
    - Archaeologists discovered a shì at the site, which is considered a precious ritual artifact.
  - 工匠精心打造出的士(士)，在阳光下闪烁着金属光芒(在陽光下閃爍着金屬光芒)。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn dǎzàochū de shì(士)，zài yángguāng xià shǎnshuò zhe jīnshǔ guāngmáng(在陽光下閃爍着金屬光芒)。
    - The shì, crafted meticulously by artisans, shimmers under the sunlight with a metallic glow.
### 寿(壽) → 叀(叀)
- **definition:** phonetic
- **pinyin:** zhuān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a spindle
  - **interpretation:** The character 叀(叀) (zhuān, "phonetic") originally depicted a spindle, capturing the form of a tool used for spinning thread.
- **examples:**
  - 学者研究叀的音义(學者研究叀的音義)。
    - Xuézhě yánjiū zhuān de yīnyì.
    - Scholars study the phonetic meaning of 叀.
  - 书法中常用叀表达音韵(書法中常用叀表達音韻)，显示其独特魅力(顯示其獨特魅力)。
    - Shūfǎ zhōng chángyòng zhuān biǎodá yīnyùn, xiǎnshì qí dútè mèilì.
    - In calligraphy, 叀 is often used to express phonetics, showcasing its unique charm.
