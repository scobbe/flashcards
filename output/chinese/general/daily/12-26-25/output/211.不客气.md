## you’re welcome; don’t mention it
### bú kèqi
---
- **definition:** you’re welcome; don’t mention it
- **pinyin:** bú kèqi
- **components:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 客
    - kè
    - guest
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - manner; air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 不(不) (bù, "not") + 客(客) (kè, "guest") + 气(氣) (qì, "manner; air") =
    - "you're welcome" or "don't mention it"
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 不客气(不客氣) (bú kèqì, "you're welcome") is used as a polite response to 'thank you,' emphasizing that no formalities or special courtesy (客气) is necessary. Originally, the characters 客(客) (kè, "guest") and 气(氣) (qì, "manner") together suggest the idea of showing etiquette or hospitality as one would to a guest. By adding 不(不) (bù, "not"), it conveys the relaxed, friendly sense that there's no need for the person to feel like a guest engaging in formalities.
- **examples:**
  - 谢谢你的帮助(謝謝你的幫助)，不客气(不客氣)。
    - Xièxiè nǐ de bāngzhù，bú kèqi。
    - Thank you for your help, you're welcome.
  - 有困难时(有困難時)，你可以说不客气(你可以說不客氣)。
    - Yǒu kùnnán shí，nǐ kěyǐ shuō bú kèqi。
    - When in difficulty, you can say 'you're welcome'.

- **examples:**

    - Tā shì rèqíng de kè, wèi wǒmen dài lái kuàilè.

### 不客气(不客氣) → 客(客) → 宀(宀)

- **pinyin:** mián

    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.

### 不客气(不客氣) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** manner; air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 乞
    - qǐ
    - beg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Clouds flowing through the sky ->
    - altered to avoid confusion with 三(三) (sān, "three") ->
    - 乞(乞) (qǐ, "beg") as a variant for phonetic borrowing
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(qì, "air") started as a pictogram representing clouds in the sky, which evolved to differentiate from the character for "three." Over time, 乞 was used as a variant for phonetic reasons, specifically for the meaning "to beg." The meanings 'air, flow' were later borrowed from 气.
- **examples:**
  - 房间里的气很清新(房間裡的氣很清新)。
    - Fángjiān lǐ de qì hěn qīngxīn。
    - The air in the room is very fresh.
  - 打开窗户(打開窗戶)，让新鲜的气进来(讓新鮮的氣進來)。
    - Dǎkāi chuānghù, ràng xīnxiān de qì jìnlái。
    - Open the window to let fresh air in.
  - 他深吸一口气(他深吸一口氣)，感觉心情舒畅(感覺心情舒暢)。
    - Tā shēn xī yīkǒu qì, gǎnjué xīnqíng shūchàng。
    - He took a deep breath and felt his mood lift.
