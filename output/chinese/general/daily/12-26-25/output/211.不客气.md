## 不客气(不客氣)
---
## 
### you’re welcome; don’t mention it
---
- **pinyin:** you’re welcome; don’t mention it
- **components:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - no; not
  - 客气(客氣)
    - kèqì
    - courteous; polite; manners
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 不(不) (bù, 'no; not') + 客气(客氣) (kèqì, 'courteous; polite') — not polite
  - **interpretation:** The phrase indicates a lack of formality in a response to gratitude. It suggests that one should not feel the need to be excessively polite.
- **examples:**
  - 谢谢你！不客气！
    - Xièxiè nǐ！Bù kèqì！
    - Thank you! You’re welcome!
  - 我很感激你的帮助，不客气。
    - Wǒ hěn gǎnjī nǐ de bāngzhù, bù kèqì。
    - I really appreciate your help, you’re welcome.

- **examples:**

### 不客气(不客氣) → 客气(客氣)
- **definition:** courteous; polite; manners
- **pinyin:** kèqì
- **components:**
  - 客
    - kè
    - guest; visitor; customer; client
  - 气(氣)
    - qì

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 客(客) (kè, 'guest') + 气(氣) (qì, 'energy; air; manner') — guest manner
  - **interpretation:** The term describes the manner in which one interacts with guests or others, emphasizing politeness and courtesy. A courteous atmosphere is often created when one treats guests with respect.
- **examples:**
  - 他对我非常客气。(他對我非常客氣。)

    - qǐng bùyào kèqì.
    - Please don't be polite.
  - 这种客气让我觉得很温暖。(這種客氣讓我覺得很溫暖。)
    - zhè zhǒng kèqì ràng wǒ juédé hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - This kind of courtesy makes me feel warm.
### 不客气(不客氣) → 客气(客氣) → 客(客)

- **pinyin:** kè
- **components:**
  - 宀

  - 各
    - gè

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 宀 (roof) combined with phonetic 各, together representing the concept of a guest or visitor.

    - Tā shì wǒ de lǎo gùkè.

### 不客气(不客氣) → 客气(客氣) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air; gas; energy; manner
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts clouds flowing through the sky.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the ethereal nature of air, akin to clouds that move freely in the sky. This visualization evokes a sense of the intangible and fluid qualities of air.

- **examples:**
