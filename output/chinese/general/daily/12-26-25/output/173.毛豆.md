## edamame
### máodòu
---
- **definition:** edamame
- **pinyin:** máodòu
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair; fur; down; feather
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean; pea; pulse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 毛(毛) (máo, "hair; fur; down") + 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean") =
    - 毛豆(毛豆) (máodòu, "edamame")
  - **interpretation:** The term 毛豆(毛豆) (máodòu) refers to young soybeans, also known as edamame, which are picked while still green and tender. The word evokes the image of the soft, downy fuzz on the pods, combining 毛(毛) (máo, "hair; fur; down") with 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean").
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢毛豆(我喜歡毛豆)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān máodòu.
    - I like edamame.
  - 妈妈煮毛豆(媽媽煮毛豆)，做成美味小吃(做成美味小吃)。
    - Māmā zhǔ máodòu, zuò chéng měiwèi xiǎochī.
    - Mom boiled edamame and turned them into a delicious snack.
  - 在餐厅里，他们提供毛豆(在餐廳裡，他們提供毛豆)，搭配清爽饮料(搭配清爽飲料)。
    - Zài cāntīng lǐ, tāmen tígōng máodòu, dāpèi qīngshuǎng yǐnliào.
    - At the restaurant, they serve edamame paired with refreshing drinks.
### 毛豆(毛豆) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** hair; fur; down; feather
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts fur or hair
  - **interpretation:** The character 毛(máo, "hair") originally depicted strands of hair or fur with a central vertical line representing a strand. This visualization makes it intuitive as it clearly resembles an abstract form of hair or fur.
- **examples:**
  - 猫毛(貓毛)柔软(柔軟)。
    - Māo máo róuruǎn.
    - Cat fur is soft.
  - 他摘下了帽子(他摘下了帽子)，露出了浓密的头毛(露出了濃密的頭毛)。
    - Tā zhāi xià le màozi, lù chū le nóngmì de tóu máo.
    - He took off his hat and revealed his thick hair.
  - 小狗的毛(小狗的毛)软绵绵(软綿綿)，让人爱不释手(讓人愛不釋手)。
    - Xiǎo gǒu de máo ruǎn miánmián, ràng rén ài bù shì shǒu.
    - The puppy's fur is soft and cuddly, making one love it dearly.
### 毛豆(毛豆) → 豆(豆)
- **definition:** bean; pea; pulse
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a container ->
    - borrowed for 'bean'
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicting a container, 豆(豆) (dòu, 'bean') was repurposed phonetically to represent the plant name 'bean', displacing the older term 菽(shū).
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃豆(我喜歡吃豆)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dòu.
    - I like eating beans.
  - 豆浆能提供营养(豆漿能提供營養)，让你一整天都精力充沛(讓你一整天都精力充沛)。
    - Dòujiāng néng tígōng yíngyǎng, ràng nǐ yī zhěng tiān dōu jīnglì chōngpèi.
    - Bean milk provides nutrition, letting you be energetic all day.
