## 
### edamame
---
- **pinyin:** edamame
- **components:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - fur; hair; down
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean; pea
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 毛(毛) (máo, "fur; hair; down") + 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean; pea") — hairy bean
  - **interpretation:** The term 毛豆 describes edamame because the young soybeans are often covered with soft, fine hairs, making them appear 'hairy'. This descriptive combination highlights the physical attribute of the beans.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃毛豆(我喜歡吃毛豆)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān chī máodòu.
    - I like to eat edamame.
  - 毛豆是健康的小吃(毛豆是健康的小吃)。
    - máodòu shì jiànkāng de xiǎochī.
    - Edamame is a healthy snack.
### 毛豆(毛豆) → 毛(毛)
- **definition:** fur; hair; down
- **pinyin:** máo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts hair or fur, representing the fine, thin, and elongated structure of hair strands.
  - **interpretation:** The visual representation directly relates to hair, capturing its essence in a simple form.
- **examples:**
  - 他的头发很长(他的頭髮很長)。
    - tā de tóufà hěn cháng.
    - His hair is very long.
  - 这件衣服的毛料很好(這件衣服的毛料很好)。
    - zhè jiàn yīfú de máoliào hěn hǎo.
    - The fabric of this clothing is very good.
### 毛豆(毛豆) → 豆(豆)
- **definition:** bean; pea
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a container, originally representing a type of seed.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a container that holds seeds, evoking the idea of beans that grow inside it.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃豆腐(我喜歡吃豆腐)，它很美味(它很好吃)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dòufu, tā hěn měiwèi.
    - I like to eat tofu; it is very delicious.
  - 豆子(豆子)很营养(很營養)。
    - Dòuzi hěn yíngyǎng.
    - Beans are very nutritious.
