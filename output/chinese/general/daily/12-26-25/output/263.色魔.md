### sex fiend; pervert
---
- **pinyin:** sex fiend; pervert
- **components:**
  - 色
    - sè
    - color; appearance; hue; form
  - 魔
    - mó
    - demon; evil spirit; enchant; charm
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 色(色) (sè, 'color; appearance') + 魔(魔) (mó, 'demon; evil spirit') — color demon
  - **interpretation:** The combination '色' (sè) and '魔' (mó) suggests an entity that is obsessively fixated on appearance or desire, thus forming a term for a person with perverse sexual cravings. This makes sense in describing someone with an unhealthy obsession with the aesthetic aspects of sexuality.
- **examples:**
  - 他居然被称为色魔(他居然被稱為色魔)，真让人震惊(真讓人震驚)！
    - tā jūrán bèi chēng wéi sè mó, zhēn ràng rén zhènjīng!
    - He was actually called a sex fiend, which is really shocking!
