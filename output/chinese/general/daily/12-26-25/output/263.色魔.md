## sex fiend; pervert
### sèmó
---
- **definition:** sex fiend; pervert
- **pinyin:** sèmó
- **components:**
  - 色
    - sè
    - color; lust
  - 魔
    - mó
    - demon; evil spirit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 色(色) (sè, "color; lust") + 魔(魔) (mó, "demon; evil spirit") =
    - a demon lust ->
    - sex fiend
  - **interpretation:** The term 色魔 (sèmó) combines 色(色) (sè, "color; lust") with 魔(魔) (mó, "demon; evil spirit"), indicating a person whose behavior or mindset is dominated by lust, similar to a demon possessed by such desires.
- **examples:**
  - 他被人称作色魔(他被人稱作色魔)。
    - Tā bèi rén chēngzuò sèmó.
    - He is called a sex fiend.
  - 他举止古怪(他舉止古怪)，让人联想到色魔(讓人聯想到色魔)。
    - Tā jǔzhǐ gǔguài, ràng rén liánxiǎng dào sèmó.
    - His behavior is odd, making people think of a pervert.
  - 色魔的行为不可原谅(色魔的行為不可原諒)。
    - Sèmó de xíngwéi bùkě yuánliàng.
    - The pervert's behavior is unforgivable.
