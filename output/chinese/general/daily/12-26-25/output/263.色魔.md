## sex fiend; pervert
### sèmó
---
- **definition:** sex fiend; pervert
- **pinyin:** sèmó
- **components:**
  - 色
    - sè
    - color; sex
  - 魔
    - mó
    - demon; magic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 色 (sè, "color; sex") + 魔 (mó, "demon; magic") =
    - a demon or fiend associated with sex, indicating a person with perverted sexual behavior.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 色 (sè, "sex") and 魔 (mó, "demon") evokes the idea of a person who is overtaken by a demon-like obsession with sex, characterizing them as a pervert.
- **examples:**
  - 那个(個)人总(總)在阴(陰)暗角落出没(沒)，被大家称为(稱為)色魔。
    - Nàgè rén zǒng zài yīnyàn jiǎoluò chūmò, bèi dàjiā chēng wéi sèmó.
    - That person always lurks in dark corners, and is called a sex fiend by everyone.
  - 他是个彻头彻(個徹頭徹)尾的色魔。
    - Tā shì gè chè tóu chè wěi de sèmó.
    - He is a complete pervert.
  - 在酒吧里(裡)，那名色魔引起了轰动(轟動)。
    - Zài jiǔbā lǐ, nà míng sèmó yǐnqǐ le hōngdòng.
    - In the bar, that sex fiend caused a sensation.
### 色魔 → 色
- **definition:** color; sex
- **pinyin:** sè
- **components:**
  - 爪
    - zhǎo
    - claw; hand
  - 卩
    - jié
    - kneeling person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 爪 (zhǎo, "claw; hand") + 卩 (jié, "kneeling person") =
    - appearance or complexion ->
    - color, by extension sex
  - **interpretation:** The components depict a 'hand' and a 'kneeling person,' which traditionally came together in the character 色 (sè, "color") to convey the concept of complexion or facial appearance. This, through a symbolic extension, also referred to hues and later sexual attraction.
- **examples:**
  - 蓝(藍)天上闪烁着(閃爍著)柔和的色调(調)，大地呈现(現)出斑斕的色彩。
    - lán tiān shàng shǎn shuò zhe róu hé de sè diào，dà dì chéng xiàn chū bān lán de sè cǎi。
    - Under the blue sky, gentle hues shimmer, and the earth displays a variegated palette of colors.
  - 这(這)幅画传达(畫傳達)出丰(豐)富的色感。
    - zhè fú huà chuán dá chū fēng fù de sè gǎn。
    - This painting conveys a rich sense of color.
  - 他的一举(舉)一动(動)都流露着浓(著濃)厚的色欲(慾)。
    - tā de yì jǔ yì dòng dōu liú lòu zhe nóng hòu de sè yù。
    - Every action he takes reveals his strong sexual desire.
### 色魔 → 色 → 爪
- **definition:** claw; hand
- **pinyin:** zhǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hand grabbing from above, resembles a claw ->
    - stylized to represent claws
  - **interpretation:** The character 爪 (zhǎo, "claw") originally depicted a hand grabbing from above, stylized to resemble the form of claws.
- **examples:**
  - 猫(貓)的爪非常敏捷。
    - Māo de zhǎo fēicháng mǐnjié.
    - The cat's claws are very agile.
  - 狗的爪稳(穩)固，使它轻松(輕鬆)爬山。
    - Gǒu de zhǎo wěngù, shǐ tā qīngsōng páshān.
    - The dog's claws are sturdy, allowing it to climb mountains easily.
  - 鹰(鷹)的爪锋(鋒)利，能牢牢抓住猎(獵)物。
    - Yīng de zhǎo fēnglì, néng láoláo zhuā zhù lièwù.
    - The eagle's claws are sharp, enabling it to firmly grasp its prey.
### 色魔 → 色 → 卩
- **definition:** kneeling person
- **pinyin:** jié
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a kneeling person from sideview
  - **interpretation:** The character 卩 (jié, "kneeling person") is a simple pictogram showing the silhouette of a person kneeling. It is unrelated to other kneeling or similar figures in different contexts such as 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") where the mouth is open, or 女 (nǚ, "woman") where the figure represents a female. Its form is solely based on the posture of kneeling.
- **examples:**
  - 那位卩正虔诚(誠)祈祷(禱)。
    - Nà wèi jié zhèng qiánchéng qídǎo.
    - That kneeling person is praying devoutly.
  - 礼仪师(禮儀師)指出，卩代表谦(謙)卑与(與)敬意。
    - Lǐyí shī zhǐchū, jié dàibiǎo qiānbēi yǔ jìngyì.
    - The ritual master pointed out that the kneeling person represents humility and respect.
  - 在庄严仪(莊嚴儀)式中，卩象征着内(徵著內)心的虔敬。
    - Zài zhuāngyán yíshì zhōng, jié xiàngzhēngzhe nèixīn de qiánjìng.
    - In the solemn ceremony, the kneeling figure symbolizes inner devotion.
### 色魔 → 魔
- **definition:** demon; magic
- **pinyin:** mó
- **components:**
  - 麻
    - má
  - 鬼
    - guǐ
    - demon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 麻 (má) + Semantic: 鬼 (guǐ, "demon") =
    - represents a demonic or magical concept ->
    - demon; magic
  - **interpretation:** This character combines the phonetic 麻 (má), contributing the pronunciation, with the semantic 鬼 (guǐ, "demon"), emphasizing the supernatural and demonic meaning, creating a word associated with magic or demons.
- **examples:**
  - 黑夜降临时(臨時)，魔鬼出没于(沒於)街巷。
    - Hēiyè jiànglín shí, móguǐ chūmò yú jiēxiàng.
    - When night falls, demons appear in the alleys.
  - 古老传说(傳說)中，魔姬迷惑凡人。
    - Gǔlǎo chuánshuō zhōng, mó jī míhuò fánrén.
    - In ancient legends, a demon maiden bewilders mortals.
  - 人心易被魔诱(誘)惑。
    - Rénxīn yì bèi mó yòuhuò.
    - The human heart is easily tempted by demons.
### 色魔 → 魔 → 麻
- **pinyin:** má
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts hemp plants
  - **interpretation:** The character 麻(má, "hemp") visually represents hemp plants with fibers or threads. It shows the processing of hemp as an essential plant material.
- **examples:**
  - 这种(這種)衣服用麻纺织(紡織)。
    - Zhè zhǒng yīfú yòng má fǎngzhī.
    - This kind of clothing is woven with hemp.
  - 吃了太多辣椒，手就会变(會變)得麻。
    - Chī le tài duō làjiāo, shǒu jiù huì biàn dé má.
    - After eating too many chili peppers, the hand becomes numb.
  - 他跑步过后(過後)，双(雙)腿常常感到麻。
    - Tā pǎobù guòhòu, shuāngtuǐ chángcháng gǎndào má.
    - After running, his legs often feel numb.
### 色魔 → 魔 → 鬼
- **definition:** demon
- **pinyin:** guǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a kneeling man or figure with an ugly face (甶) + small 厶 (trace of tail) added later
  - **interpretation:** The character 鬼 (guǐ, "demon") visually represents a kneeling figure with a distinctive, mask-like face, evoking the image of a supernatural being or ghost. The addition of a small 厶 suggests a residual tail, enhancing its demon-like appearance. This depiction highlights the character's association with eerie, otherworldly entities.
- **examples:**
  - 深夜独(獨)行时(時)，忽遇鬼影飘(飄)忽。
    - Shēnyè dúxíng shí, hū yù guǐ yǐng piāohū.
    - Walking alone in the deep night, suddenly encountering a fleeting ghostly silhouette.
  - 村里(裡)流传(傳)故事说(說)鬼出没(沒)，人们(們)夜半小心提灯(燈)。
    - Cūn lǐ liúchuán gùshì shuō guǐ chūmò, rénmen yèbàn xiǎoxīn tí dēng.
    - Stories circulate in the village that demons haunt the area, so people cautiously carry lanterns at midnight.
