## ocean; seas
### hǎiyáng
---
- **definition:** ocean; seas
- **pinyin:** hǎiyáng
- **components:**
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea
  - 洋
    - yáng
    - ocean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") + 洋(洋) (yáng, "ocean") =
    - together represent large bodies of salt water on Earth.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") and 洋(洋) (yáng, "ocean") emphasizes a distinction and completeness of various large bodies of salt water.
- **examples:**
  - 海洋属于所有生物(海洋屬於所有生物)，是地球最珍贵的财富(是地球最珍貴的財富)。
    - hǎiyáng shǔyú suǒyǒu shēngwù, shì dìqiú zuì zhēnguì de cáifù.
    - The ocean belongs to all living beings; it is Earth's most precious treasure.
  - 海洋波涛汹涌(海洋波濤洶湧)，孕育着无数生命(孕育著無數生命)。
    - hǎiyáng bōtāo xiōngyǒng, yùnyù zhe wúshù shēngmìng.
    - The ocean's surging waves nurture countless lives.
  - 浩瀚海洋壮丽无比(浩瀚海洋壯麗無比)。
    - hào hàn hǎiyáng zhuànglì wúbǐ.
    - The vast ocean is magnificently sublime.
### 海洋(海洋) → 海(海)
- **definition:** sea
- **pinyin:** hǎi
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 每
    - měi
    - each
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 每(每) (měi, "each") =
    - water + phonetic component ->
    - sea
  - **interpretation:** 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") clearly represents the semantic element related to water, while 每(每) (měi) provides the phonetic component that hints at the pronunciation of 海(海) (hǎi). The combination effectively conveys the meaning related to large bodies of water like the sea.
- **examples:**
  - 海水温暖(海水溫暖)，鱼群活跃(魚群活躍)。
    - hǎi shuǐ wēnnuǎn, yú qún huóyuè.
    - The sea water is warm, and the schools of fish are lively.
  - 黄昏时分(黃昏時分)，我站在辽阔的海边(我站在遼闊的海邊)，眺望远方(眺望遠方)。
    - huánghūn shífèn, wǒ zhàn zài liáokuò de hǎibiān, tiàowàng yuǎnfāng.
    - At dusk, I stand by the vast sea and gaze into the distance.
  - 东海之滨(東海之濱)，潮起潮落(潮起潮落)。
    - dōng hǎi zhī bīn, cháoqǐ cháoluò.
    - On the shores of the East Sea, the tides rise and fall.
### 海洋(海洋) → 海(海) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** radical
  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the simplified radical form of 水, often used to signify water-related meanings when combined with other components in phono-semantic compounds.
- **examples:**
  - 请喝水(請喝水)。
    - Qǐng hē shuǐ.
    - Please drink water.
  - 我们需要水(我們需要水)，保持身体的平衡(保持身體的平衡)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào shuǐ, bǎochí shēntǐ de pínghéng.
    - We need water to maintain the balance of our bodies.
  - 水润万物(水潤萬物)，孕育生命(孕育生命)。
    - Shuǐ rùn wànwù, yùnyù shēngmìng.
    - Water nourishes all things and nurtures life.
### 海洋(海洋) → 海(海) → 每(每)
- **definition:** each
- **pinyin:** měi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person (bottom reminiscent of 母(mother)) and hair (top strokes) ->
    - each individual seen as separate ->
    - each
  - **interpretation:** The character 每(每) (měi, 'each') originated from a pictogram showing a person with hair, representing the idea of 'each' or 'every' individual as distinct.
- **examples:**
  - 每个孩子都有光明的未来(每個孩子都有光明的未來)。
    - Měi gè háizi dōu yǒu guāngmíng de wèilái。
    - Every child has a bright future.
  - 每次旅行都让我感受新奇(每次旅行都讓我感受新奇)，每个景点都独具魅力(每個景點都獨具魅力)。
    - Měi cì lǚxíng dōu ràng wǒ gǎnshòu xīnqí，Měi gè jǐngdiǎn dōu dú jù mèilì。
    - Every trip lets me experience novelty, and each tourist spot holds its own charm.
  - 每当太阳落山时(每當太陽落山時)，每只鸟儿便归巢(每隻鳥兒便歸巢)。
    - Měi dāng tàiyáng luòshān shí，Měi zhī niǎor biàn guī cháo。
    - Whenever the sun sets, each bird returns to its nest.
### 海洋(海洋) → 洋(洋)
- **definition:** ocean
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") =
    - large expanse of water ->
    - ocean
  - **interpretation:** The component 氵(氵) suggests the association with water, while 羊(羊) provides the pronunciation clue. Together, they convey the idea of the vastness or expansiveness of the ocean.
- **examples:**
  - 大洋是一片神秘的蓝色(大洋是一片神秘的藍色)。
    - Dà yáng shì yī piàn shénmì de lánsè.
    - The ocean is a mysterious blue expanse.
  - 她热爱洋装展现的独特风情(她熱愛洋裝展現的獨特風情)，追寻属于自己的梦想(追尋屬於自己的夢想)。
    - Tā rè'ài yángzhuāng zhǎnxiàn de dútè fēngqíng, zhuīxún shǔyú zìjǐ de mèngxiǎng.
    - She loves the unique charm showcased by foreign-style clothing and pursues her own dreams.
  - 他曾经乘船远渡大洋(他曾經乘船遠渡大洋)，探索未知的国度(探索未知的國度)。
    - Tā céngjīng chéng chuán yuǎndù dà yáng, tànsuǒ wèizhī de guódù.
    - He once sailed across the ocean to explore unknown lands.
