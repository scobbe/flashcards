## ocean; seas
### hǎiyáng
---
- **definition:** ocean; seas
- **pinyin:** hǎiyáng
- **components:**
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea
  - 洋
    - yáng
    - ocean; vast
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") refers to a body of saline water, often part of the ocean. 洋(洋) (yáng, "ocean; vast") signifies something expansive and broad, usually referencing the larger bodies of water that cover much of the Earth's surface. Together, 海洋(海洋) (hǎiyáng, "ocean; seas") denotes the vast, interconnected bodies of saltwater that cover the majority of the Earth's surface.
  - **interpretation:** 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") combined with 洋(洋) (yáng, "ocean; vast") creates a term that emphasizes the vastness and interconnectivity of the seas, covering the Earth's surface.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢海洋(我喜歡海洋)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hǎiyáng.
    - I like the ocean.
  - 科学家研究海洋(科學家研究海洋)，探索未知(探索未知)。
    - Kēxuéjiā yánjiū hǎiyáng, tànsuǒ wèizhī.
    - Scientists study the ocean and explore the unknown.
  - 深邃的海洋孕育生命(深邃的海洋孕育生命)。
    - Shēnsuì de hǎiyáng yùnyù shēngmìng.
    - The deep ocean nurtures life.

### 海洋(海洋) → 洋(洋)

- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") =
    - vastness of water ->
    - ocean; by extension ->
    - foreign
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") to denote a connection to water and 羊(羊) (yáng, "sheep") for its phonetic value. The vastness associated with oceans was conceptually extended to anything foreign.
- **examples:**
