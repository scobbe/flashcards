## ease; leisure; escape
### yì
---
- **definition:** ease; leisure; escape
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 兔
    - tù
    - rabbit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk") + 兔(兔) (tù, "rabbit") =
    - rabbit running ->
    - escape, ease
  - **interpretation:** The character 逸(yì) combines the 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk"), which signifies movement, with 兔(兔) (tù, "rabbit"), which adds the sound and also suggests the idea of a rabbit swiftly escaping. Together, they convey the notion of escaping or fleeing with ease, embodying the meanings of both leisure and escape.
- **examples:**
  - 他在安静的庭院中享受逸趣(他在安靜的庭院中享受逸趣)。
    - Tā zài ānjìng de tíngyuàn zhōng xiǎngshòu yìqù.
    - He enjoys leisure in the quiet courtyard.
  - 他喜欢逸居(他喜歡逸居)，远离城市喧嚣(遠離城市喧囂)。
    - Tā xǐhuān yìjū, yuǎnlí chéngshì xuānxiāo.
    - He likes to live in seclusion, away from the city's hustle and bustle.
  - 假日来临时(假日來臨時)，他计划逸出尘嚣(他計劃逸出塵囂)。
    - Jiàrì láilín shí, tā jìhuà yìchū chénxiāo.
    - When the holiday arrives, he plans to escape the hustle and bustle.
