---
## 
### ease; leisure; escape
---
- **pinyin:** ease; leisure; escape
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 逸 consists of the phonetic component 逸, suggesting ease or escape, and the semantic component, which relates to movement or traveling (行).
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the notion of ease with aspects of movement, evoking a sense of leisurely escape. This imagery of comfortable movement contributes to the overall meaning of ease and freedom from constraints.
- **examples:**
  - 生活很闲逸(生活很閒逸)。
    - Shēnghuó hěn xián yì.
    - Life is very leisurely.
  - 他向往逸生活(他嚮往逸生活)。
    - Tā xiàngwǎng yì shēnghuó.
    - He yearns for an easy life.
