## ease; leisure; escape
### yì
---
- **definition:** ease; leisure; escape
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - to stop; halt
  - 兔
    - tù
    - rabbit; hare
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Ideogrammic compound: 止 + 㚔 (oracle); later: 辵 + 兔.
  - **interpretation:** Early forms combined “foot/stop” with a component suggesting swift movement. Later the walking/motion element 辵 with a rabbit 兔 evoked rapid running, yielding senses like to flee or slip away, and by extension a carefree, unrestrained ease.
- **examples:**
  - 他趁夜逃逸(他趁夜逃逸)。
    - Tā chèn yè táoyì.
    - He escaped under cover of night.
  - 乡下生活安逸(鄉下生活安逸)。
    - Xiāngxià shēnghuó ānyì.
    - Life in the countryside is comfortable and leisurely.
  - 她的文风清新飘逸(她的文風清新飄逸)。
    - Tā de wénfēng qīngxīn piāoyì.
    - Her writing style is fresh and unrestrained.
### 逸 → 止
- **definition:** to stop; halt
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a footprint pointing up (originally “foot”), later stylized as 龰; oracle forms show three toes and a sole.
  - **interpretation:** A footprint with toes pointing upward represents the foot. The sense broadened from the physical foot to the act of halting one’s step, hence “to stop”; the specific ‘toe’ meaning was preserved in 趾.
- **examples:**
  - 请立即停止前进(請立即停止前進)。
    - Qǐng lìjí tíngzhǐ qiánjìn.
    - Please stop advancing immediately.
  - 此处止步，非请勿入(此處止步，非請勿入)。
    - Cǐ chù zhǐbù, fēi qǐng wù rù.
    - Stop here; do not enter without invitation.
  - 药到病除，疼痛已止(藥到病除，疼痛已止)。
    - Yào dào bìng chú, téngtòng yǐ zhǐ.
    - With the right medicine, the illness is cured and the pain has stopped.
### 逸 → 兔
- **definition:** rabbit; hare
- **pinyin:** tù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a rabbit.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of a rabbit with prominent ears and hind legs. The modern form has been stylized into regular strokes, but the silhouette still evokes the animal’s posture.
- **examples:**
  - 我家养了一只兔子(我家養了一隻兔子)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǎng le yì zhī tùzi.
    - My family has a pet rabbit.
  - 它最喜欢吃胡萝卜(它最喜歡吃胡蘿蔔)。
    - Tā zuì xǐhuan chī húluóbo.
    - It likes eating carrots the most.
  - 秋夜里(秋夜裡)，我们看见玉兔在月亮上奔跑(我們看見玉兔在月亮上奔跑)。
    - Qiū yè lǐ, wǒmen kànjiàn Yùtù zài yuèliàng shàng bēnpǎo.
    - On an autumn night, we saw the Jade Rabbit running on the moon.
