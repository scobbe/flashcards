## complete; whole
### quán
---
- **definition:** complete; whole
- **pinyin:** quán
- **components:**
  - 入
    - rù
    - enter
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 入(入) (rù, "enter") + 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") =
    - a whole piece of jade ->
    - complete
  - **interpretation:** The character 全(全) (quán, "complete") combines 入(入) (rù, "enter") with 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") to signify a complete or pure piece of jade, alluding to the idea of wholeness or integrity.
- **examples:**
  - 他的计划全实行了(他的計劃全實行了)。
    - Tā de jìhuà quán shíxíng le.
    - His plan was fully implemented.
  - 我们全家今晚聚餐(我們全家今晚聚餐)，享受温馨时光(享受溫馨時光)。
    - Wǒmen quánjiā jīnwǎn jùcān, xiǎngshòu wēnxīn shíguāng.
    - Our whole family is having dinner together tonight, enjoying a warm time.
  - 这件事全都是误会(這件事全都是誤會)。
    - Zhè jiàn shì quán dōu shì wùhuì.
    - This matter is completely a misunderstanding.
### 全(全) → 入(入)
- **definition:** enter
- **pinyin:** rù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Arrowhead ->
    - indicates 'to enter'
  - **interpretation:** The character 入(入) (rù, 'enter') visually represents an arrowhead pointing downwards, symbolizing the concept of entering a space or opening.
- **examples:**
  - 孩子走入教室(孩子走入教室)。
    - Háizi zǒurù jiàoshì.
    - The child enters the classroom.
  - 他入市(他入市)，开始新生活(開始新生活)。
    - Tā rù shì, kāishǐ xīn shēnghuó.
    - He enters the marketplace and begins a new life.
### 全(全) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Picture of a cong (ritual jade totem) ->
    - three pieces of jade strung together ->
    - current form
  - **interpretation:** The character 玉(yù, "jade") visually resembles a picture of a ritual jade object, emphasizing its cultural and material significance.
- **examples:**
  - 这块玉非常光滑(這塊玉非常光滑)。
    - Zhè kuài yù fēicháng guānghuá.
    - This piece of jade is very smooth.
  - 博物馆展出了精美玉器(博物館展出了精美玉器)，吸引了众多观众(吸引了眾多觀眾)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū le jīngměi yùqì, xīyǐn le zhòngduō guānzhòng.
    - The museum exhibited exquisite jade artifacts, attracting numerous visitors.
