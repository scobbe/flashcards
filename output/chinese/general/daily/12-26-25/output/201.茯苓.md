## poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
### fúlíng
---
- **definition:** poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
- **pinyin:** fúlíng
- **components:**
  - 茯
    - fú
    - poria root
  - 苓
    - líng
    - medicinal plant; poria
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 茯 (fú, "poria root") + 苓 (líng, "medicinal plant") come together to refer specifically to the medicinal fungus known as poria, used in traditional Chinese medicine for various health purposes.
  - **interpretation:** 茯苓(fúlíng, "poria") is a term from traditional Chinese medicine, where 茯 (fú, "poria root") refers to the part of the fungi used, and 苓 (líng, "medicinal plant") highlights its therapeutic applications.
- **examples:**

    - Traditional Chinese medicine believes that poria strengthens the spleen and offers health benefits.
