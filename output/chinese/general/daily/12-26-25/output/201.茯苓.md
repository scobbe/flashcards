## poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
### fúlíng
---
- **definition:** poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
- **pinyin:** fúlíng
- **components:**
  - 茯
    - fú
    - poria; tuckahoe
  - 苓
    - líng
    - a type of edible mushroom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 茯(茯) (fú, "poria; tuckahoe") and 苓(苓) (líng, "a type of edible mushroom") combine to refer to a specific medicinal fungus known for its health properties.
  - **interpretation:** 茯苓 refers to a medicinal fungus used in traditional Chinese medicine, combining the name and nature of the mushroom.
- **examples:**
  - 中药里常用茯苓(中藥裡常用茯苓)。
    - Zhōngyào lǐ cháng yòng fúlíng.
    - Fúlíng is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.
  - 医生推荐茯苓(醫生推薦茯苓)，配合枸杞调理身体(配合枸杞調理身體)。
    - Yīshēng tuījiàn fúlíng, pèihé gǒuqǐ tiáolǐ shēntǐ.
    - The doctor recommends using fúlíng, combined with goji berries to regulate the body.
