### sugar apple
---
- **pinyin:** sugar apple
- **components:**
  - 释(釋)
    - shì
    - to explain; release; set free; clarify
  - 迦
    - jiā
    - knowledge; enlightenment; a suffix in transliterated names; used in Buddhist terminology
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 释(釋) (shì, "to explain; release") + 迦(迦) (jiā, "knowledge; enlightenment") — releasing knowledge
  - **interpretation:** The term conveys a sense of spiritual enlightenment or release of knowledge, rooted in Buddhist tradition. It reflects the teachings associated with Buddhism, particularly those relating to understanding and liberation.
- **examples:**
  - 释迦摩尼是佛教的创始人(釋迦摩尼是佛教的創始人)。
    - Shìjiā Móní shì Fójiào de chuàngshǐrén.
    - Siddhartha Gautama is the founder of Buddhism.
  - 我在学习释迦的哲学(我在學習釋迦的哲學)。
    - Wǒ zài xuéxí Shìjiā de zhéxué.
    - I am studying the philosophy of the Buddha.
### 释迦(釋迦) → 释(釋)
- **definition:** to explain; release; set free; clarify
- **pinyin:** shì
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 和
    - hé
    - harmony, peace
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the components of the phonetic element 石 (shí, 'stone') combined with the semantic element 和 (hé, 'harmony, peace').
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests a release or clarification that brings harmony, as if setting something rigid like stone free into a peaceful state.
  - **simplification:** 釋 (釋) was simplified to 释 by removing a complex part and replacing it with a simpler character formed from cursive adoption.
- **examples:**
  - 他试图解释这一点(他試圖解釋這一點)。
    - Tā shìtú jiěshì zhè yīdiǎn.
    - He tried to explain this point.
  - 需要释放压力(需要釋放壓力)。
    - Xūyào shìfàng yālì.
    - Need to release stress.
