### shìjiā
---
- **traditional:** 釋迦
- **definition:** sugar apple
- **pinyin:** shìjiā
- **components:**
  - 释(釋)
    - shì

    - jiā

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word

  - **interpretation:** 释(釋)迦( ) (shìjiā, "sugar apple") is named after Shakyamuni Buddha because the fruit’s exterior resembles the curls of Buddha’s hair. The connection reflects cultural importance of Buddhism and symbolic representation in things like nature.
