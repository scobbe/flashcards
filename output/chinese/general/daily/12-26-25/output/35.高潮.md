### high tide; climax
---
- **pinyin:** high tide; climax
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall; elevated
  - 潮
    - cháo
    - tide; wave; current
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高(高) (gāo, 'high') + 潮(潮) (cháo, 'tide') — high tide
  - **interpretation:** The word refers to a peak or high point, whether in a physical or metaphorical sense. It captures the essence of rising intensity or prominence.
- **examples:**
  - 故事到了高潮，大家都很紧张。
    - gùshì dào le gāocháo, dàjiā dōu hěn jǐnzhāng。
    - The story reached its climax, and everyone was very tense.
  - 他的表演在晚会上达到了高潮。
    - tā de biǎoyǎn zài wǎnhuì shàng dádào le gāocháo。
    - His performance reached its climax at the evening party。
  - 这场比赛充满了高潮和低谷。
    - zhè chǎng bǐsài chōngmǎn le gāocháo hé dīgǔ。
    - This match was full of highs and lows.

  - 超
