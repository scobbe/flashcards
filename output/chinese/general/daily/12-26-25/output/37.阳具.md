### male genitals
---
- **pinyin:** male genitals
- **components:**
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun; male; positive
  - 具
    - jù
    - tool; implement; device
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 阳(陽) (yáng, "sun; male; positive") + 具(具) (jù, "tool; implement; device") — male implement
  - **interpretation:** This term literally translates to 'male tool' which directly refers to male genitals. The combination highlights the gender aspect and its biological function.
- **examples:**
  - 阳具的功能是繁殖和排尿(陽具的功能是繁殖和排尿)。
    - Yángjù de gōngnéng shì fánzhí hé páinià.
    - The function of male genitals is reproduction and urination.
  - 医生检查阳具(醫生檢查陽具)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchá yángjù.
    - The doctor examines the male genitals.
### 阳具(陽具) → 阳(陽)
- **definition:** sun; male; positive
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - sunshine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 阜 (hill) + phonetic 昜 (sunshine). The character originally referred to sunshine, with the addition of 阜 emphasizing its connection to the sun's positive aspects.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of brightness and elevation, associating the sun with positivity and energy. It reflects the duality of the sun being both a celestial body and a symbol of masculinity.
  - **simplification:** 陽(阳) (yáng, 'sun; male; positive') was simplified by reducing the strokes and using 日 (sun) to represent brightness more straightforwardly.
- **examples:**
  - 阳光明媚(陽光明媚)，生活充满希望(生活充滿希望)。
    - Yángguāng míngmèi, shēnghuó chōngmǎn xīwàng.
    - The sunshine is bright and beautiful; life is full of hope.
  - 阳光(陽光)洒在大地上(灑在大地上)。
    - Yángguāng sǎ zài dàdì shàng.
    - The sun shines down upon the earth.
