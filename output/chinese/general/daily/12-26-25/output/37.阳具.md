### 阳具(陽具) → 阳(陽)
- **definition:** sun; masculine
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
  - 昜

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunshine") + 阜(阜) (fù, "hill") =
    - sunshine over hill ->
    - sun/masculine quality ->
    - 阝(阝) simplified form
