### wonton
---
- **pinyin:** wonton
- **components:**
  - 馄(餛)
    - hún
    - a type of dumpling
  - 饨(飩)
    - tún
    - a type of food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 馄(餛) (hún, 'a type of dumpling') + 饨(飩) (tún, 'a type of food') — dumpling food
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as馄(餛) refers to a specific type of dumpling, while 饨(飩) indicates food, together representing a dish focused on this dumpling style.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃馄饨。(我喜歡吃餛飩。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī húntún.
    - I like to eat wontons.
  - 今天晚上我们去吃馄饨。(今天晚上我們去吃餛飩。)
    - Jīntiān wǎnshàng wǒmen qù chī húntún.
    - Tonight, we are going to eat wontons.
### 馄饨(餛飩) → 馄(餛)
- **definition:** a type of dumpling
- **pinyin:** hún
- **components:**
  - 餠
    - bǐng
    - cake
  - 鮑
    - bào
    - abalone
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 餠 (bǐng, 'cake') and the phonetic component 鮑 (bào, 'abalone') to convey the meaning of a type of dumpling.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the a sense of contained filling, referencing both food and the texture of dumplings.
  - **simplification:** 餛 is simplified to 馄 through stroke reduction, merging complex components to create a more streamlined character.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃饺子和馄饨(我喜歡吃餃子和餛飩)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī jiǎozi hé húntun！
    - I love to eat dumplings and wontons!
  - 今天的馄饨汤真好喝(今天的餛飩湯真好喝)！

    - Today's wonton soup is really delicious!
  - 馄饨是我的最爱(餛飩是我的最愛)！
    - Húntun shì wǒ de zuì'ài！
    - Wontons are my favorite!
