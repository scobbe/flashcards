## invoice
### fāpiào
---
- **traditional:** 發票
- **definition:** invoice
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - issue; emit
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; note
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 發 (fā, "issue; emit") and 票 (piào, "ticket; note") combine to form 發票, referring to an "invoice".
  - **interpretation:** The word 發票 (fāpiào, "invoice") makes sense as it combines 發 (fā, "issue") with 票 (piào, "ticket") to signify a document that records and issues the details of a transaction, much like a ticket or note.
  - **simplification:** 发票
- **examples:**
  - 请给(請給)我开(開)一张发(張發)票。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ kāi yī zhāng fāpiào.
    - Please issue me an invoice.
  - 商店给(給)我提供发(發)票，以便报销(報銷)。
    - Shāngdiàn gěi wǒ tígōng fāpiào, yǐbiàn bàoxiāo.
    - The store provided me with an invoice for reimbursement.
  - 他认(認)真保存发(發)票，以备(備)日后(後)核对(對)。
    - Tā rènzhēn bǎocún fāpiào, yǐbèi rìhòu héduì.
    - He kept the invoice carefully for future verification.
### 发(發)票( ) → 发(發)
- **traditional:** 發
- **definition:** issue; emit
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 攴
    - pū
    - tap
  - 癹
    - bá
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram of a bow firing an arrow; hand (攴 (pū, "tap")) added later ->
    - evolved into phonetic component 癹 (bá) ->
    - final form as phono-semantic
  - **interpretation:** The original depiction of a bow and arrow represents the act of emitting or issuing, like launching an arrow. The hand component implies control, leading to the contemporary concept of issuing or emitting something.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 发(發) by merging functions of 髮 (fà, "hair") in the 1956 Chinese Character Simplification Scheme, as both share the pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 电(電)台发(發)出柔和的音乐(樂)。
    - Diàntái fāchū róuhé de yīnyuè。
    - The radio emits soft music.
  - 厂(廠)房发(發)出刺鼻的气(氣)味。
    - Chǎngfáng fāchū cìbí de qìwèi。
    - The factory emits a pungent odor.
  - 汽车发(車發)出急促的鸣(鳴)笛，警告行人靠边(邊)行走。
    - Qìchē fāchū jí cù de míngdí, jǐnggào xíngrén kào biān xíngzǒu。
    - The car emits a rapid honk, warning pedestrians to move aside.
