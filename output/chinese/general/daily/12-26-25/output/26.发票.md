## invoice
### fāpiào
---
- **definition:** invoice
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - issue; distribute
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; note
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 发(發) (fā, "issue; distribute") + 票(票) (piào, "ticket; note") =
    - a document issued as a ticket or note for financial transactions, commonly known as an invoice.
  - **interpretation:** The word 发票(發票) combines the concept of issuing or distributing (发/發) with that of a ticket or note (票). This reflects the nature of invoices as documents that are formally issued by a seller to a buyer, detailing a transaction.
  - **simplification:** 发票(發票)
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一张发票(請給我一張發票)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī zhāng fāpiào.
    - Please give me an invoice.
  - 购物时(購物時)，请记得索取发票(請記得索取發票)。
    - Gòuwù shí, qǐng jìdé suǒqǔ fāpiào.
    - When shopping, please remember to ask for an invoice.
### 发票(發票) → 发(發)
- **definition:** issue; distribute
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 癹(發)
    - bā
    - launch
  - 攴
    - pū
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram: 癹(發) (bā, "launch") depicting a bow firing an arrow + 攴(攴) (pū, "hand") added for action ->
    - evolved to 癹(癹) (bā, "launch") as phonetic component + hand as semantic for issuing ->
    - 发(發)
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a bow shooting an arrow, symbolizing issuance or distribution. Over time, the addition of a hand indicated an action related to issuing or spreading, which aligns with its meanings.
  - **simplification:** 发(發) was simplified by retaining the phonetic component 癹(癹) (bā, "launch") to represent both 發 and 髮, merging these separate characters into one simplified form.
- **examples:**
  - 老板发奖金(老闆發獎金)，员工们都很高兴(員工們都很高興)。
    - Lǎobǎn fā jiǎngjīn, yuángōng men dōu hěn gāoxìng.
    - The boss distributed bonuses, and the employees were very happy.
  - 学校发教材(學校發教材)，同学们仔细阅读(同學們仔細閱讀)。
    - Xuéxiào fā jiàocái, tóngxué men zǐxì yuèdú.
    - The school issued textbooks, and the students read them carefully.
