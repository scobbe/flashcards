## invoice
### fāpiào
---
- **definition:** invoice
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - issue; emit
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; note
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 發(發) (fā, "issue; emit") and 票(票) (piào, "ticket; note") combine to form 發票(發票), referring to an "invoice".
  - **interpretation:** The word 發票(發票) (fāpiào, "invoice") makes sense as it combines 發(發) (fā, "issue") with 票(票) (piào, "ticket") to signify a document that records and issues the details of a transaction, much like a ticket or note.
  - **simplification:** 发票
- **examples:**
  - 请给我开一张发票(請給我開一張發票)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ kāi yī zhāng fāpiào.
    - Please issue me an invoice.
  - 商店给我提供发票(商店給我提供發票)，以便报销(以便報銷)。
    - Shāngdiàn gěi wǒ tígōng fāpiào, yǐbiàn bàoxiāo.
    - The store provided me with an invoice for reimbursement.
  - 他认真保存发票(他認真保存發票)，以备日后核对(以備日後核對)。
    - Tā rènzhēn bǎocún fāpiào, yǐbèi rìhòu héduì.
    - He kept the invoice carefully for future verification.
### 发票(發票) → 发(發)
- **definition:** issue; emit
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 攴
    - pū
    - tap
  - 癹
    - bá
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram of a bow firing an arrow; hand (攴(攴) (pū, "tap")) added later ->
    - evolved into phonetic component 癹(癹) (bá) ->
    - final form as phono-semantic
  - **interpretation:** The original depiction of a bow and arrow represents the act of emitting or issuing, like launching an arrow. The hand component implies control, leading to the contemporary concept of issuing or emitting something.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 发(發) by merging functions of 髮(髮) (fà, "hair") in the 1956 Chinese Character Simplification Scheme, as both share the pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 电台发出柔和的音乐(電台發出柔和的音樂)。
    - Diàntái fāchū róuhé de yīnyuè。
    - The radio emits soft music.
  - 厂房发出刺鼻的气味(廠房發出刺鼻的氣味)。
    - Chǎngfáng fāchū cìbí de qìwèi。
    - The factory emits a pungent odor.
  - 汽车发出急促的鸣笛(汽車發出急促的鳴笛)，警告行人靠边行走(警告行人靠邊行走)。
    - Qìchē fāchū jí cù de míngdí, jǐnggào xíngrén kào biān xíngzǒu。
    - The car emits a rapid honk, warning pedestrians to move aside.

### 发票(發票) → 票(票)
- **definition:** ticket; note
- **pinyin:** piào
- **components:**
  - 覀
    - yà
    - west
  - 示
    - shì
    - show
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 覀(覀) (yà, "west") + 示(示) (shì, "show") =
    - show identity or value ->
    - implying a ticket or note
  - **interpretation:** The character 票(票) (piào, "ticket") combines elements denoting direction and display, conceptualizing a document that demonstrates or verifies identity or value, like a ticket or banknote.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一张票(我買了一張票)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yī zhāng piào.
    - I bought a ticket.
  - 他订了电影票(他訂了電影票)，准备去看电影(準備去看電影)。
    - Tā dìngle diànyǐng piào, zhǔnbèi qù kàn diànyǐng.
    - He booked a movie ticket, preparing to watch a film.
  - 演唱会的票很快就卖完了(演唱會的票很快就賣完了)。
    - Yǎnchànghuì de piào hěn kuài jiù màiwánle.
    - The tickets for the concert sold out quickly.
### 发票(發票) → 票(票) → 覀(襾)
- **definition:** west
- **pinyin:** yà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cover or canopy, resembling a westerly direction based on early representations of enclosing or covering structures.
  - **interpretation:** The character 襾(襾) (yà, "west") is a pictogram representing a covering object, historically used as an orientation symbol for the west due to how it visually resembles a roof or lid, indicating enclosure or a boundary.
- **examples:**
  - 太阳落在覀(太陽落在覀)，繁星开始闪烁(繁星開始閃爍)。
    - Tàiyáng luò zài yà, fánxīng kāishǐ shǎnshuò.
    - The sun sets in the west, and the stars begin to twinkle.
  - 船只驶向覀(船隻駛向覀)，追逐着最后的阳光(追逐著最後的陽光)。
    - Chuánzhī shǐ xiàng yà, zhuīzhú zhe zuìhòu de yángguāng.
    - The ship sails towards the west, chasing the last sunlight.
