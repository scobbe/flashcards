---
## 
### invoice
---
- **pinyin:** invoice
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - issue; send out; distribute; emit
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; bill; receipt; vote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 发(發) (fā, 'issue') + 票(piào, 'bill; ticket') — issue bill
  - **interpretation:** This term describes a document that is issued to detail a transaction. An 发票(發票) (fāpiào, 'invoice') serves as a receipt confirming the sale and is used for accounting and tax purposes.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一张发票(我需要一張發票)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yī zhāng fāpiào.
    - I need an invoice.
  - 请把发票寄给我(請把發票寄給我)。
    - Qǐng bǎ fāpiào jì gěi wǒ.
    - Please send me the invoice.
### 发票(發票) → 发(發)
- **definition:** issue; send out; distribute; emit
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 弓
    - gōng
    - bow
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 發
    - fā
    - emit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Originally a pictogram representing a bow firing an arrow, later combined with a hand component which evolved into the phonetic element, leading to the current form.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the action of emitting force, akin to launching an arrow. This evokes concepts of sending out or distributing.
  - **simplification:** 髮(髮) was simplified to 發 by simplifying the components according to the 1956 Chinese Character Simplification Scheme.
- **examples:**
  - 我想發表我的想法(我想發表我的想法)！
    - Wǒ xiǎng fābiǎo wǒ de xiǎngfǎ！
    - I want to express my thoughts!
  - 他發了一條消息(他發了一條消息)。
    - Tā fāle yītiáo xiāoxī.
    - He sent out a message.
