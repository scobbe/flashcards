## 小妹(小妹)
---
## 
### little sister; young woman
---
- **pinyin:** little sister; young woman
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little; young; minor
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister; little sister
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") + 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") — small younger sister
  - **interpretation:** The term 小妹 refers to a younger sister or a young woman, often implying affection and endearment. The combination makes sense as it directly describes someone who is both younger and female.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个小妹！
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè xiǎomèi！
    - I have a little sister!
  - 小妹，你可以来我家玩。
    - Xiǎomèi, nǐ kěyǐ lái wǒ jiā wán。
    - Little sister, you can come to my house to play.
### 小妹(小妹) → 小(小)
- **definition:** small; little; young; minor
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a small or small-scale object.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents something that is small or compact in size, resembling a dot or a short stroke.
- **examples:**
  - 这个房间很小(這個房間很小)。
    - Zhège fángjiān hěn xiǎo。
    - This room is very small.
  - 我有一个小猫(我有一個小貓)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè xiǎo māo。
    - I have a small cat.
### 小妹(小妹) → 妹(妹)
- **definition:** younger sister; little sister
- **pinyin:** mèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a woman (女) who is younger or smaller, suggesting the idea of a younger sister.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the radical for 'woman' with concepts of youth, intuitively representing a younger female sibling.
- **examples:**
  - 她是我的妹妹(她是我的妹妹)。
    - Tā shì wǒ de mèimei.
    - She is my younger sister.
  - 我有两个妹妹(我有兩個妹妹)。
    - Wǒ yǒu liǎng gè mèimei.
    - I have two younger sisters.
