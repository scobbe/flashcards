## little sister; young woman
### xiǎomèi
---
- **definition:** little sister; young woman
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèi
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; young; little
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") + 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") =
    - a younger or little sister; can also refer to a young woman.
  - **interpretation:** The term combines 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") to denote younger or smaller in age and 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") to specify familial relation or age. It connotes a younger female either in a familial sense or more generally as a young woman.
- **examples:**
  - 我的小妹很可爱(我的小妹很可愛)。
    - Wǒ de xiǎomèi hěn kě'ài.
    - My little sister is very lovely.
  - 昨天见到小妹(昨天見到小妹)，她笑得很开心(她笑得很開心)。
    - Zuótiān jiàn dào xiǎomèi, tā xiào de hěn kāixīn.
    - Yesterday I met my little sister, and she laughed happily.
  - 小妹喜欢唱歌(小妹喜歡唱歌)。
    - Xiǎomèi xǐhuān chànggē.
    - My little sister likes singing.
### 小妹(小妹) → 小(小)
- **definition:** small; young; little
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Three small dots ->
    - small
  - **interpretation:** The character 小(xiǎo, 'small') is an ideogram that originally used three small dots to visually represent the concept of smallness. Over time, these dots became elongated, maintaining the meaning of 'small.'
- **examples:**
  - 我有小猫(我有小貓)，它喜欢睡觉(它喜歡睡覺)。
    - Wǒ yǒu xiǎo māo, tā xǐhuān shuìjiào.
    - I have a small cat, it loves to sleep.
  - 这本书很小(這本書很小)。
    - Zhè běn shū hěn xiǎo.
    - This book is very small.
  - 小花在院子里玩耍(小花在院子裡玩耍)，笑声传遍四方(笑聲傳遍四方)。
    - Xiǎo Huā zài yuànzi lǐ wánshuǎ, xiàoshēng chuánbiàn sìfāng.
    - Little Hua plays in the yard, and her laughter spreads in all directions.
### 小妹(小妹) → 妹(妹)
- **definition:** younger sister
- **pinyin:** mèi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - female, woman
  - 未
    - mèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 女(女) (nǚ, "female, woman") + phonetic: 未(未) (mèi) =
    - female with 未 sound ->
    - younger sister
  - **interpretation:** 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") combines the semantic element 女(女) (nǚ, "female, woman") with the phonetic 未(未) (mèi). This suggests a female role specifically tied to being a younger sibling, with 未 lending its pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 我有个妹妹(我有個妹妹)，她很可爱(她很可愛)。
    - Wǒ yǒu gè mèimei, tā hěn kě'ài.
    - I have a younger sister, she is very cute.
  - 放学后(放學後)，妹妹一起跑去公园(妹妹一起跑去公園)。
    - Fàngxué hòu, mèimei yīqǐ pǎo qù gōngyuán.
    - After school, my younger sister runs to the park together.
### 小妹(小妹) → 妹(妹) → 女(女)
- **definition:** female, woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing ->
    - modern form reversed facing right
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") originally depicted a woman with pronounced breasts, illustrating the concept of 'woman.' Over time, the pictogram evolved to face right in its modern form, losing details like the left breast to simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有魅力的女(她是一位有魅力的女)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi yǒu mèi lì de nǚ.
    - She is an attractive woman.
  - 那位女战士英勇无畏(那位女戰士英勇無畏)，带领大家前行(帶領大家前行)。
    - Nà wèi nǚ zhàn shì yīng yǒng wú wèi, dài lǐng dà jiā qián xíng.
    - That female warrior is brave and fearless, leading everyone forward.
### 小妹(小妹) → 妹(妹) → 未(未)
- **pinyin:** mèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts branches of tree before growth
  - **interpretation:** The character 未(未) (mèi, 'not yet') originally depicted a tree with incomplete branches, symbolizing something unfinished or not yet in existence. This visual metaphor accurately conveys the concept of 'not yet.'
- **examples:**
  - 他未出门(他未出門)。
    - Tā mèi chūmén.
    - He has not left.
  - 消息未传来(消息未傳來)，大家很担心(大家很擔心)。
    - Xiāoxī mèi chuán lái, dàjiā hěn dānxīn.
    - The news hasn't arrived, and everyone is worried.
  - 会议未开始(會議未開始)，请稍等(請稍等)。
    - Huìyì mèi kāishǐ, qǐng shāo děng.
    - The meeting hasn't started, please wait a moment.
