### coconut milk
---
- **pinyin:** coconut milk
- **components:**
  - 椰
    - yē
    - coconut
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 椰(椰) (yē, "coconut") + 奶(奶) (nǎi, "milk") — coconut milk
  - **interpretation:** 椰奶 (yēnǎi) directly translates to 'coconut milk', combining 椰 (yē, 'coconut') and 奶 (nǎi, 'milk'). This pairing makes sense as it describes the liquid extracted from coconuts used in cooking and beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 我正在喝椰奶。
    - Wǒ zhèngzài hē yēnǎi。
    - I am drinking coconut milk.
  - 椰奶在饮料中很受欢迎。
    - Yēnǎi zài yǐnliào zhōng hěn shòu huānyíng。
    - Coconut milk is very popular in beverages.
