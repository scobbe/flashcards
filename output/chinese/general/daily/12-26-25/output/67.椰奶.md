## coconut milk
### yēnǎi
---
- **definition:** coconut milk
- **pinyin:** yēnǎi
- **components:**
  - 椰
    - yē
    - coconut
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 椰(椰) (yē, "coconut") + 奶(奶) (nǎi, "milk")
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 椰(椰) (yē, "coconut") refers to the fruit from which the milk is extracted, and 奶(奶) (nǎi, "milk") denotes the liquid form, resulting in the term for 'coconut milk.'
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝椰奶(我喜歡喝椰奶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē yēnǎi.
    - I like to drink coconut milk.
  - 夏天喝椰奶(夏天喝椰奶)，感觉非常清爽(感覺非常清爽)。
    - Xiàtiān hē yēnǎi, gǎnjué fēicháng qīngshuǎng.
    - Drinking coconut milk in summer feels very refreshing.
  - 这道甜点用椰奶(這道甜點用椰奶)，制作而成(製作而成)。
    - Zhè dào tiándiǎn yòng yēnǎi, zhìzuò ér chéng.
    - This dessert is made with coconut milk.
