## unable
### wúfǎ
---
- **definition:** unable
- **pinyin:** wúfǎ
- **components:**
  - 无(無)
    - wú
    - without; none; not have; to lack
  - 法
    - fǎ
    - method; law; way; standard
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 无(無) (wú, "without") and 法(法) (fǎ, "method") together convey the sense of being without a method or way to do something, thus 'unable.'
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 无(無) (wú, "without") and 法(法) (fǎ, "method") expresses the idea of lacking a way or method to accomplish something, thereby meaning 'unable.'
  - **simplification:** No additional simplification beyond the usual simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 他遇到了难题(他遇到了難題)，无法继续前进(無法繼續前進)。
    - Tā yù dào le nán tí, wúfǎ jì xù qián jìn.
    - He encountered a tough problem, and he was unable to continue moving forward.
  - 由于信息不足(由於信息不足)，他无法做出正确判断(他無法做出正確判斷)。
    - Yóuyú xìnxī bùzú, tā wúfǎ zuòchū zhèngquè pànduàn.
    - Due to insufficient information, he was unable to make a correct judgment.
### 无法(無法) → 无(無)
- **definition:** without; none; not have; to lack
- **pinyin:** wú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person dancing with ox tails to pray for rain ->
    - borrowed to mean 'without' ->
    - original meaning transferred to 舞(舞) (wǔ, 'dance')
  - **interpretation:** The character 无(無) (wú, 'without') originally depicted a ritual dance and was later borrowed to represent the concept of lacking something, while the original dance meaning was transferred to 舞(舞) (wǔ, 'dance').
- **examples:**
  - 我无钱(我無錢)，不能买书(不能買書)。
    - Wǒ wú qián, bù néng mǎi shū.
    - I have no money, and I cannot buy books.
  - 他无烦恼(他無煩惱)。
    - Tā wú fánnǎo.
    - He is without worries.
  - 村庄无烟火(村莊無煙火)，只剩宁静(只剩寧靜)。
    - Cūn zhuāng wú yānhuǒ, zhǐ shèng níngjìng.
    - The village is without fireworks; only tranquility remains.
### 无法(無法) → 法(法)
- **definition:** method; law; way; standard
- **pinyin:** fǎ
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 去
    - qù
    - go
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + 去(去) (qù, "go") =
    - water flowing smoothly is like a law ->
    - rules, norms
  - **interpretation:** The character 法(fǎ, "law") combines 氵(shuǐ, "water") with 去(qù, "go") to convey the sense of rules or methods flowing smoothly like water, representing standardization and control.
  - **simplification:** The character 法 is the same in both traditional and simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 找到解决问题的法(找到解決問題的法)，十分重要(十分重要)。
    - Zhǎodào jiějué wèntí de fǎ, shífēn zhòngyào.
    - It is very important to find a method to solve problems.
  - 社会的稳定(社會的穩定)，依赖于公正的法(依賴於公正的法)。
    - Shèhuì de wěndìng, yīlài yú gōngzhèng de fǎ.
    - The stability of society depends on fair laws.

  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the simplified radical form of 水, often used to signify water-related meanings when combined with other components in phono-semantic compounds.
- **examples:**

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
