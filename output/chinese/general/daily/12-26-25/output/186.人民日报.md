## People’s Daily
### Rénmín Rìbào
---
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - the people; populace; citizens; masses
  - 日报(日報)
    - rìbào
    - daily newspaper; daily report; daily bulletin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人民 + 日报 = the people + daily (newspaper).
  - **interpretation:** Title means a daily newspaper for the people; in usage it names a major national newspaper in mainland China. Functions as a proper noun referring to that publication.
  - **simplification:** 報 → 报 per PRC character simplification to reduce strokes; other characters unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天读《人民日报》(我每天讀《人民日報》)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dú 《Rénmín Rìbào》。
    - I read the People’s Daily every day.
  - 这篇社论刊登在《人民日报》上(這篇社論刊登在《人民日報》上)。
    - Zhè piān shèlùn kāndēng zài 《Rénmín Rìbào》 shàng.
    - This editorial was published in the People’s Daily.
  - 《人民日报》发行量很大(《人民日報》發行量很大)。
    - 《Rénmín Rìbào》 fāxíngliàng hěn dà.
    - The People’s Daily has a large circulation.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 人民
- **definition:** the people; populace; citizens; masses
- **pinyin:** rénmín
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person;human;people;someone
  - 民
    - mín
    - people;commoners;citizenry;subjects
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人(person) + 民(populace; common people) = the people.
  - **interpretation:** Combines generic human beings with the populace to emphasize the collective body of ordinary citizens, often in political or legal contexts. Frequently appears in state and civic terms (e.g., courts, newspapers, governments).
- **examples:**
  - 我们要为人民服务(我們要為人民服務)。
    - Wǒmen yào wèi rénmín fúwù.
    - We must serve the people.
  - 人民有权监督政府(人民有權監督政府)。
    - Rénmín yǒuquán jiāndū zhèngfǔ.
    - The people have the right to supervise the government.
  - 人民的生活水平在不断提高(人民的生活水平在不斷提高)。
    - Rénmín de shēnghuó shuǐpíng zài bùduàn tígāo.
    - The people's standard of living is steadily improving.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 人民 → 人
- **definition:** person;human;people;someone
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg; left-side form 亻.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person in profile, with one stroke suggesting the torso/arm and the other the leg, hence the slightly splayed shape. In compounds it often appears as the left-hand form 亻 to mark human-related meaning; not to be confused with 彳.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个很好的人(他是一個很好的人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè hěn hǎo de rén.
    - He is a very good person.
  - 我认识很多人(我認識很多人)。
    - Wǒ rènshi hěn duō rén.
    - I know many people.
  - 这里有三个人(這裡有三個人)。
    - Zhèlǐ yǒu sān gè rén.
    - There are three people here.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 人民 → 民
- **definition:** people;commoners;citizenry;subjects
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram; later jiajie (phonetic loan) for “the people”.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a pictorial graph whose precise image is uncertain, it was later borrowed (jiajie) to write the word ‘people’. Claims that it depicts an eye pierced by a tool are likely folk etymology and are not supported by phonetic evidence linking it to 盲.
- **examples:**
  - 中国人民团结一致(中國人民團結一致)。
    - Zhōngguó rénmín tuánjié yīzhì.
    - The Chinese people are united.
  - 政府应倾听民意(政府應傾聽民意)。
    - Zhèngfǔ yīng qīngtīng mínyì.
    - The government should listen to public opinion.
  - 战乱时期，许多平民流离失所(戰亂時期，許多平民流離失所)。
    - Zhànluàn shíqī, xǔduō píngmín liúlí shīsuǒ.
    - During wartime, many civilians are displaced.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 日报(日報)
- **definition:** daily newspaper; daily report; daily bulletin
- **pinyin:** rìbào
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 日 (day) + 报/報 (report; newspaper) = a report or publication issued daily.
  - **interpretation:** Indicates something reported or published every day; in media it means a daily newspaper, in workplaces it can mean a daily report.
  - **simplification:** 報 was simplified to 报 to reduce strokes by streamlining components; 日 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天读日报(我每天讀日報)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dú rìbào.
    - I read the daily newspaper every day.
  - 公司要求大家每天提交日报(公司要求大家每天提交日報)。
    - Gōngsī yāoqiú dàjiā měitiān tíjiāo rìbào.
    - The company requires everyone to submit a daily report each day.
  - 这家日报创刊于1949年(這家日報創刊於1949年)。
    - Zhè jiā rìbào chuàngkān yú yī jiǔ sì jiǔ nián.
    - This daily newspaper was first published in 1949.
