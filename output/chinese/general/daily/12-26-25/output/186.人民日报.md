### People’s Daily
---
- **pinyin:** People’s Daily
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - people; populace; citizens
  - 日报(日報)
    - rìbào
    - daily newspaper; daily report; daily
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人民(人民) (rénmín, "people") + 日报(日報) (rìbào, "daily newspaper") — daily newspaper for the people
  - **interpretation:** 人民日报 is a newspaper that serves the citizens, focusing on news relevant to the people. The combination makes sense as it presents a daily report targeted at the populace.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天阅读人民日报(我每天閱讀人民日報)。
    - Wǒ měitiān yuèdú Rénmíng Rìbào.
    - I read the People's Daily every day.
  - 人民日报报道了国家的最新政策(人民日報報導了國家的最新政策)。
    - Rénmíng Rìbào bàodàole guójiā de zuìxīn zhèngcè.
    - The People's Daily reported on the latest national policies.

- **pinyin:** rìbào
- **components:**
  - 日报(日報)
    - rì
    - day; sun; date; daily
  - 报(報)
    - bào

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 日(日日) (rì, "day") + 报(報) (bào, "report/newspaper") — day report/newspaper
  - **interpretation:** The term combines 'day' and 'report/newspaper', indicating a publication released daily. This makes sense because a 日报 (rìbào) provides timely news and updates every day.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都读日报(我每天都讀日報)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dōu dú rìbào.

  - 日报上有很多新闻(日報上有很多新聞)。
    - Rìbào shàng yǒu hěnduō xīnwén.
    - There are many news articles in the daily report.

- **examples:**
