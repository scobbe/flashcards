## People’s Daily
### Rénmín Rìbào
---
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - people
  - 日报(日報)
    - rìbào
    - daily newspaper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 人(人) (rén, "person") + 民(民) (mín, "people") =
    - the people; 日(日) (rì, "day") + 報(报) (bào, "report") =
    - newspaper; all together, 人民日报(人民日報) (Rénmín Rìbào, "People’s Daily") means a daily newspaper of the people
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to a daily newspaper that serves as a publication for the general population, focusing on the interests and issues concerning the people.
  - **simplification:** 日報 -> 日报
- **examples:**
  - 人民日报发表了重要声明(人民日報發表了重要聲明)。
    - Rénmín Rìbào fābiǎo le zhòngyào shēngmíng.
    - People's Daily published an important statement.
  - 我每天阅读人民日报(我每天閱讀人民日報)，了解国家大事(了解國家大事)。
    - Wǒ měitiān yuèdú Rénmín Rìbào, liǎojiě guójiā dàshì.
    - I read the People's Daily every day to understand national affairs.

### 人民日报(人民日報) → 人民(人民) → 民(民)
- **definition:** people; citizens; nationality
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted an eye pierced by a dagger (claimed) ->
    - later borrowed for 民 (mín, 'the people')
  - **interpretation:** The character 民(mín) primarily serves as a loan character, having lost its original pictorial depiction possibly related to an eye and a dagger. Its current meaning as 'the people' is a result of its phonetic borrowing and widespread use.
- **examples:**
  - 民众拥有参与决策的权利(民眾擁有參與決策的權利)。
    - Mín zhòng yǒngyǒu cānyù juécè de quánlì.
    - The people have the right to participate in decision-making.
  - 国家依靠民意趋向稳定(國家依靠民意趨向穩定)，推动社会进步(推動社會進步)。
    - Guójiā yīkào mínyì qūxiàng wěndìng, tuīdòng shèhuì jìnbù.
    - The country relies on the people's opinions for stability, promoting social progress.
  - 选民积极行使权力(選民積極行使權力)，维护国家安全(維護國家安全)。
    - Xuǎn mín jījí xíngshǐ quánlì, wéihù guójiā ānquán.
    - Voters actively exercise their rights, safeguarding national security.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 日报(日報)
- **definition:** daily newspaper
- **pinyin:** rìbào
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - day; sun
  - 报(報)
    - bào
    - report; newspaper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 日(日) (rì, "day" or "sun") + 報(报) (bào, "report" or "newspaper") =
    - "daily newspaper"
  - **interpretation:** A 日報(日報) (rìbào, "daily newspaper") is a 報(報) (bào, "newspaper") that is published 日(日) (rì, "daily" or "every day"), providing daily news and updates.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved changing 報 to 报.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一份日报(我買了一份日報)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī fèn rìbào.
    - I bought a copy of the daily newspaper.
  - 早晨起床后(早晨起床後)，我习惯读日报(我習慣讀日報)。
    - Zǎochén qǐchuáng hòu, wǒ xíguàn dú rìbào.
    - After waking up in the morning, I usually read the daily newspaper.
  - 为了了解社会动态(為了了解社會動態)，他每天浏览日报(他每天瀏覽日報)。
    - Wèile liǎojiě shèhuì dòngtài, tā měitiān liúlǎn rìbào.
    - In order to keep up with social news, he browses the daily newspaper every day.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 日报(日報) → 日(日)
- **definition:** day; sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

    - dot/line added for distinction from similar characters
  - **interpretation:** The character 日(日) (rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun, with a central line added to differentiate it from other similar-looking characters. This design closely resembles ancient symbols for the sun in various cultures.
- **examples:**
  - 日出东方(日出東方)。
    - Rì chū dōngfāng.
    - The sun rises in the east.
  - 日光灿烂(日光燦爛)，心情愉快(心情愉快)。
    - Rìguāng cànlàn, xīnqíng yúkuài.
    - The sunlight is brilliant, and the mood is cheerful.
  - 每个日子都有新希望(每個日子都有新希望)。
    - Měi gè rìzi dōu yǒu xīn xīwàng.
    - Every day holds new hope.
### 人民日报(人民日報) → 日报(日報) → 报(報)
- **definition:** report; newspaper
- **pinyin:** bào
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") + 言(言) (yán, "speech") =
    - using hands to convey speech ->
    - reporting
  - **interpretation:** The character incorporates 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") to suggest the physical act of handling or delivering a message, combined with 言(言) (yán, "speech") to indicate conveying information verbally, illustrating the concept of reporting.
  - **simplification:** 報(報)→报(報): Originally complex 言蔽, revised to simplify visual complexity, hand radical retained for context.
- **examples:**
  - 今天他看了报(今天他看了報)。
    - Jīntiān tā kàn le bào.
    - He read the newspaper today.
  - 我从报上得知消息(我從報上得知消息)，心情十分激动(心情十分激動)。
    - Wǒ cóng bào shàng dézhī xiāoxī, xīnqíng shífēn jīdòng.
    - I learned the news from the newspaper, and my mood became very excited.
  - 午间他写了一篇报(午間他寫了一篇報)，供大家参考(供大家參考)。
    - Wǔjiān tā xiě le yī piān bào, gōng dàjiā cānkǎo.
    - At noon, he wrote a report for everyone to refer to.

### 人民日报(人民日報) → 日报(日報) → 报(報) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 舌(舌) (shé, "tongue") + horizontal stroke =
    - movement of tongue ->
    - speech
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") originates from the depiction of a tongue 舌(舌) with an added stroke to represent its movement, signifying the action of speaking. This visual representation highlights the tongue's role in speech, which is central to communication.
- **examples:**

    - lǎo shī cháng shuō, yán chū bì xíng。
