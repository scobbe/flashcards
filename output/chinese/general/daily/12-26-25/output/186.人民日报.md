## People’s Daily
### Rénmín Rìbào
---
- **traditional:** 人民日報
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - people
  - 日报(報)
    - rìbào
    - daily newspaper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人民(日報) (Rénmín Rìbào) consists of the characters 人 (rén, "person") and 民 (mín, "people") forming 人民 (rénmín, "people") combined with 日 (rì, "day") and 報 (bào, "newspaper"), forming 日報 (rìbào, "daily newspaper").
  - **interpretation:** 人民 (rénmín, "people") 日報 (rìbào, "daily newspaper") literally denotes a newspaper for the people that is published daily. It is the official newspaper of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, reflecting public policies and ideology.
- **examples:**
  - 《人民日报》报道了最新消息(《人民日報》報道了最新消息)。
    - 《Rénmín Rìbào》 bàodào le zuì xīn xiāoxī.
    - The People's Daily reported the latest news.
  - 我每天阅读(閱讀)人民日报(報)，以了(瞭)解国(國)家动态(動態)。
    - Wǒ měitiān yuèdú Rénmín Rìbào, yǐ liǎojiě guójiā dòngtài.
    - I read the People's Daily every day to understand the country's situation.
### 人( )民( )日( )报(報) → 人民

- **pinyin:** rénmín
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person; man; people; human

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 人 (rén, "person") + 民 (mín, "people") =
    - collective term for a population or the citizens of a country ->
    - the people
  - **interpretation:** 人民 (rénmín, "the people") combines 人 (rén, "person") and 民 (mín, "people") to convey the idea of a collective group of individuals that form the population of a nation or society. It emphasizes the concept of unity among individuals as constituents of a community or country.
- **examples:**
  - 人民是国(國)家的基石。
    - rénmín shì guójiā de jīshí
    - The people are the cornerstone of the nation.
  - 政府依靠人民，共同创(創)造繁荣(榮)。
    - zhèngfǔ yīkào rénmín, gòngtóng chuàngzào fánróng
    - The government relies on the people, together creating prosperity.
  - 革命属于(屬於)人民，人民实现(實現)了解放。
    - gémìng shǔyú rénmín, rénmín shíxiàn le jiěfàng
    - Revolution belongs to the people, and the people achieved liberation.

### 人( )民( )日( )报(報) → 日( )报(報)

### 人( )民( )日( )报(報) → 日( )报(報) → 日
- **definition:** day; sun; date; lifetime
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来临时(來臨時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒满(灑滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.
### 人( )民( )日( )报(報) → 日( )报(報) → 报(報)
- **traditional:** 報
- **definition:** report; newspaper; recompense; respond
- **pinyin:** bào
- **components:**
  - 扌(手)
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 𠬝(服)
    - fú
    - to serve
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") + 𠬝(服) (fú, "to serve") as a phonetic element =
    - hand delivering or serving a message ->
    - report, recompense
  - **interpretation:** The character 报(報) (bào) combines 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") to signify the action of handling or delivering something with 𠬝(服) (fú) to provide the phonetic hint. This fittingly represents the hand delivering a report or message, capturing the sense of conveying information or compensation.
  - **simplification:** 報 simplified to 报(報) by replacing 𠬝(服) with an even simpler element for phonetic clarity.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天看报(報)。
    - Wǒ měitiān kàn bào.
    - I read the newspaper daily.
  - 他收到好消息后(後)，立即报(報)喜。
    - Tā shōudào hǎo xiāoxī hòu, lìjí bào xǐ.
    - After receiving the good news, he immediately reported his joy.
### 人( )民( )日( )报(報) → 日( )报(報) → 报(報) → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞来(飛來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌制(手製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺术(藝術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细节(細節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
