## People’s Daily
### Rénmín Rìbào
---
- **traditional:** 人民日報
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **components:**
  - 人民
    - rénmín
    - people
  - 日( )报(報)
    - rìbào
    - daily newspaper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人(日)民(報) (Rénmín Rìbào) consists of the characters 人 (rén, "person") and 民 (mín, "people") forming 人民 (rénmín, "people") combined with 日 (rì, "day") and 報 (bào, "newspaper"), forming 日報 (rìbào, "daily newspaper").
  - **interpretation:** 人民 (rénmín, "people") 日報 (rìbào, "daily newspaper") literally denotes a newspaper for the people that is published daily. It is the official newspaper of the Central Committee of the Communist Party of China, reflecting public policies and ideology.
- **examples:**
  - 《人民日报》报道了最新消息(《人民日報》報道了最新消息)。
    - 《Rénmín Rìbào》 bàodào le zuì xīn xiāoxī.
    - The People's Daily reported the latest news.
  - 我( )每( )天( )阅(閱)读(讀)人( )民( )日( )报(報)，以( )了(瞭)解( )国(國)家( )动(動)态(態)。
    - Wǒ měitiān yuèdú Rénmín Rìbào, yǐ liǎojiě guójiā dòngtài.
    - I read the People's Daily every day to understand the country's situation.
