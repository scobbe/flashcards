## sparkling water
### qìshuǐ
---
- **definition:** sparkling water
- **pinyin:** qìshuǐ
- **components:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - steam; vapor
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 汽 (qì, "steam; vapor") + 水 (shuǐ, "water") =
    - water with vapor ->
    - sparkling water
  - **interpretation:** The word 汽水 (qìshuǐ) combines 汽 (qì, "steam; vapor") and 水 (shuǐ, "water"), suggesting water that contains bubbles of gas or vapor, typical of sparkling water. This aptly describes fizzy water beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)喝汽水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē qìshuǐ.
    - I like to drink sparkling water.
  - 夏天很热(熱)，一瓶汽水可以消暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, yī píng qìshuǐ kěyǐ xiāoshǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, and a bottle of sparkling water can cool you down.
### 汽水 → 汽
- **definition:** steam; vapor
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 气
    - qì
    - air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 气 (qì, "air") =
    - water vapor, steam
  - **interpretation:** 汽 (qì, "steam") cleverly combines the concepts of water and air to represent steam, highlighting its nature as vapor or steam formed from water and air.
- **examples:**
  - 热(熱)水烧开后产(燒開後產)生汽，蒸汽缓缓(氣緩緩)上升。
    - Rè shuǐ shāo kāi hòu chǎnshēng qì, zhēngqì huǎnhuǎn shàngshēng.
    - When hot water boils, it produces steam, and the vapor rises slowly.
  - 冷空气(氣)遇到热(熱)水时(時)形成汽，给(給)人带来(帶來)清凉(涼)感觉(覺)。
    - Lěng kōngqì yù dào rè shuǐ shí xíngchéng qì, gěi rén dài lái qīngliáng gǎnjué.
    - When cold air meets hot water, vapor is formed and it gives a refreshing feeling.
  - 锅炉(鍋爐)中常常产(產)生汽，驱动机(驅動機)器运转(運轉)。
    - Guōlú zhōng chángcháng chǎnshēng qì, qūdòng jīqì yùnzhuǎn.
    - Steam is often produced in boilers to drive machines.
### 汽水 → 汽 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 汽水 → 汽 → 气(氣)
- **traditional:** 氣
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 乞
    - qǐ
    - beg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Clouds flowing through the sky ->
    - altered to avoid confusion with 三 (sān, "three") ->
    - 乞 (qǐ, "beg") as a variant for phonetic borrowing
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(qì, "air") started as a pictogram representing clouds in the sky, which evolved to differentiate from the character for "three." Over time, 乞 was used as a variant for phonetic reasons, specifically for the meaning "to beg." The meanings 'air, flow' were later borrowed from 气.
- **examples:**
  - 房间里(間裡)的气(氣)很清新。
    - Fángjiān lǐ de qì hěn qīngxīn。
    - The air in the room is very fresh.
  - 打开(開)窗户(戶)，让(讓)新鲜(鮮)的气进来(氣進來)。
    - Dǎkāi chuānghù, ràng xīnxiān de qì jìnlái。
    - Open the window to let fresh air in.
  - 他深吸一口气(氣)，感觉(覺)心情舒畅(暢)。
    - Tā shēn xī yīkǒu qì, gǎnjué xīnqíng shūchàng。
    - He took a deep breath and felt his mood lift.
### 汽水 → 汽 → 气(氣) → 三
- **definition:** three
- **pinyin:** sān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 一 (yī, "one") triplicated ->
    - 三 (sān, "three")
  - **interpretation:** The character 三 (sān, "three") is an ideogram depicting the concept of the number three through the use of three parallel horizontal strokes. This straightforward representation makes it consistent with related numeric characters like 一 (yī, "one") and 二 (èr, "two").
- **examples:**
  - 今天我买(買)了三斤苹(蘋)果。
    - Jīntiān wǒ mǎi le sān jīn píngguǒ.
    - Today I bought three jin of apples.
  - 他有三支笔(筆)，我只想要其中一支。
    - Tā yǒu sān zhī bǐ, wǒ zhǐ xiǎng yào qízhōng yī zhī.
    - He has three pens, but I only want one of them.
  - 他们(們)吃了三碗面(麵)，觉(覺)得非常满(滿)意。
    - Tāmen chī le sān wǎn miàn, juéde fēicháng mǎnyì.
    - They ate three bowls of noodles and felt very satisfied.
### 汽水 → 汽 → 气(氣) → 乞
- **definition:** beg
- **pinyin:** qǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person bending over with a bowl, resembling the act of begging.
  - **interpretation:** The character 乞 (qǐ, "beg") visually suggests the posture of a person soliciting aid or alms. This imagery supports its meaning of asking for or requesting something, often in a context of necessity.
- **examples:**
  - 他在街头(頭)乞讨(討)。
    - Tā zài jiētóu qǐtǎo.
    - He begs on the street.
  - 街头(頭)的乞丐请(請)求施舍(捨)。
    - Jiētóu de qǐgài qǐngqiú shīshě.
    - The beggar on the street asks for alms.
  - 他乞求雨露，期盼滋润(潤)大地。
    - Tā qǐqiú yǔlù, qīpàn zīrùn dàdì.
    - He begs for rainwater, hoping to nourish the earth.
### 汽水 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水是一种(種)生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 夏天很热(熱)，喝水能解暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺，沿岸绿树(綠樹)成荫(蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.
