## sparkling water
### qìshuǐ
---
- **definition:** sparkling water
- **pinyin:** qìshuǐ
- **components:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - steam; vapor
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 汽(汽) (qì, "steam; vapor") + 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") =
    - water with vapor ->
    - sparkling water
  - **interpretation:** The word 汽水 (qìshuǐ) combines 汽(汽) (qì, "steam; vapor") and 水(水) (shuǐ, "water"), suggesting water that contains bubbles of gas or vapor, typical of sparkling water. This aptly describes fizzy water beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝汽水(我喜歡喝汽水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē qìshuǐ.
    - I like to drink sparkling water.
  - 夏天很热(夏天很熱)，一瓶汽水可以消暑(一瓶汽水可以消暑)。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, yī píng qìshuǐ kěyǐ xiāoshǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, and a bottle of sparkling water can cool you down.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽)
- **definition:** steam; vapor
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 气
    - qì
    - air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 气(气) (qì, "air") =
    - water vapor, steam
  - **interpretation:** 汽(汽) (qì, "steam") cleverly combines the concepts of water and air to represent steam, highlighting its nature as vapor or steam formed from water and air.
- **examples:**
  - 热水烧开后产生汽(熱水燒開後產生汽)，蒸汽缓缓上升(蒸氣緩緩上升)。
    - Rè shuǐ shāo kāi hòu chǎnshēng qì, zhēngqì huǎnhuǎn shàngshēng.
    - When hot water boils, it produces steam, and the vapor rises slowly.
  - 冷空气遇到热水时形成汽(冷空氣遇到熱水時形成汽)，给人带来清凉感觉(給人帶來清涼感覺)。
    - Lěng kōngqì yù dào rè shuǐ shí xíngchéng qì, gěi rén dài lái qīngliáng gǎnjué.
    - When cold air meets hot water, vapor is formed and it gives a refreshing feeling.
  - 锅炉中常常产生汽(鍋爐中常常產生汽)，驱动机器运转(驅動機器運轉)。
    - Guōlú zhōng chángcháng chǎnshēng qì, qūdòng jīqì yùnzhuǎn.
    - Steam is often produced in boilers to drive machines.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源(水是生命之源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉字中偏旁氵(漢字中偏旁氵)，代表水(代表水)。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明(河水清澈透明)，鱼儿欢快戏水(魚兒歡快戲水)。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 乞
    - qǐ
    - beg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Clouds flowing through the sky ->
    - altered to avoid confusion with 三(三) (sān, "three") ->
    - 乞(乞) (qǐ, "beg") as a variant for phonetic borrowing
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(qì, "air") started as a pictogram representing clouds in the sky, which evolved to differentiate from the character for "three." Over time, 乞 was used as a variant for phonetic reasons, specifically for the meaning "to beg." The meanings 'air, flow' were later borrowed from 气.
- **examples:**
  - 房间里的气很清新(房間裡的氣很清新)。
    - Fángjiān lǐ de qì hěn qīngxīn。
    - The air in the room is very fresh.
  - 打开窗户(打開窗戶)，让新鲜的气进来(讓新鮮的氣進來)。
    - Dǎkāi chuānghù, ràng xīnxiān de qì jìnlái。
    - Open the window to let fresh air in.
  - 他深吸一口气(他深吸一口氣)，感觉心情舒畅(感覺心情舒暢)。
    - Tā shēn xī yīkǒu qì, gǎnjué xīnqíng shūchàng。
    - He took a deep breath and felt his mood lift.

- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts flowing water

- **examples:**
