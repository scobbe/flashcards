### sparkling water
---
- **pinyin:** sparkling water
- **components:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - gas; steam
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 汽(汽) (qì, "gas; steam") + 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") — gas water
  - **interpretation:** This combination highlights the effervescence of the drink, as it consists of water infused with gas. The term naturally describes a refreshing beverage like sparkling water.
- **examples:**
  - 我昨晚喝了汽水(我昨晚喝了汽水)。
    - Wǒ zuówǎn hēle qìshuǐ.
    - I drank sparkling water last night.
  - 你想要点汽水吗(你想要點汽水嗎)？
    - Nǐ xiǎng yào diǎn qìshuǐ ma?
    - Do you want some sparkling water?
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽)
- **definition:** gas; steam
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 氵 (water) with the phonetic component 气 (air) to signify gas or steam.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively connects water and air, conveying the essence of steam or gas that arises from water.
- **examples:**
  - 汽油是交通工具的重要燃料。(汽油是交通工具的重要燃料。)
    - qìyóu shì jiāotōng gōngjù de zhòngyào ránliào.
    - Gasoline is an important fuel for vehicles.
  - 蒸汽机是工业革命的关键。(蒸汽機是工業革命的關鍵。)
    - Zhēngqì jī shì gōngyè gémìng de guānjiàn.
    - The steam engine was key to the industrial revolution.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 氵(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents water in its flowing form, emphasizing its liquid nature.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts clouds flowing through the sky.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the ethereal nature of air, akin to clouds that move freely in the sky. This visualization evokes a sense of the intangible and fluid qualities of air.
  - **simplification:** The simplification replaced the traditional character's more complex strokes with fewer, more streamlined lines for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 天上有气(天上有氣)，风吹得很大(風吹得很大)！
    - Tiānshàng yǒu qì, fēng chuī dé hěn dà!
    - There is air in the sky, and the wind is blowing strongly!
  - 我喜欢呼吸新鲜气(我喜歡呼吸新鮮氣)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hūxī xīnxiān qì.
    - I enjoy breathing fresh air.
  - 他的气(他的氣)很重！
    - Tā de qì hěn zhòng!
    - His aura is very strong!

- **examples:**
