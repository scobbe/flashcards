## sparkling water
### qìshuǐ
---
- **definition:** sparkling water
- **pinyin:** qìshuǐ
- **components:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - steam; gas
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 汽(qì, "steam"; "gas") + 水(shuǐ, "water") =
    - water that is infused with gas, or carbonated water.
  - **interpretation:** 汽水(qìshuǐ, "sparkling water") combines 汽(qì, "gas") and 水(shuǐ, "water") to describe water that contains bubbles, usually created by adding carbon dioxide, giving it a sparkling quality.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝汽水(我喜歡喝汽水)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān hē qìshuǐ.
    - I like to drink sparkling water.
  - 夏天炎热时(夏天炎熱時)，喝汽水很清爽(喝汽水很清爽)。
    - xiàtiān yánrè shí, hē qìshuǐ hěn qīngshuǎng.
    - In the hot summer, drinking sparkling water is very refreshing.
  - 玛丽点了汽水(瑪麗點了汽水)，祝她生日快乐(祝她生日快樂)。
    - mǎlì diǎn le qìshuǐ, zhù tā shēngrì kuàilè.
    - Mary ordered sparkling water, and she is wished a happy birthday.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽)
- **definition:** steam; gas
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 气
    - qì
    - air
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 气(气) (qì, "air") =
    - steam
  - **interpretation:** The character 汽(qì, "steam") combines 氵(shuǐ, "water") to represent the water aspect of steam, and 气(qì, "air") for the gaseous form, embodying the concept of steam as water in its gaseous state.
- **examples:**
  - 火车启动时(火車啟動時)，会冒出浓浓的汽(會冒出濃濃的汽)。
    - Huǒchē qǐdòng shí, huì mào chū nóngnóng de qì.
    - When the train starts, thick steam rises.
  - 热汽从杯子中升起(熱汽從杯子中升起)。
    - Rè qì cóng bēizi zhōng shēng qǐ.
    - Hot steam rises from the cup.
  - 锅底传来的汽让整个厨房充满温暖(鍋底傳來的汽讓整個廚房充滿溫暖)。
    - Guō dǐ chuán lái de qì ràng zhěng gè chúfáng chōngmǎn wēnnuǎn.
    - The steam rising from the pot fills the whole kitchen with warmth.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** radical
  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the simplified radical form of 水, often used to signify water-related meanings when combined with other components in phono-semantic compounds.
- **examples:**
  - 请喝水(請喝水)。
    - Qǐng hē shuǐ.
    - Please drink water.
  - 我们需要水(我們需要水)，保持身体的平衡(保持身體的平衡)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào shuǐ, bǎochí shēntǐ de pínghéng.
    - We need water to maintain the balance of our bodies.
  - 水润万物(水潤萬物)，孕育生命(孕育生命)。
    - Shuǐ rùn wànwù, yùnyù shēngmìng.
    - Water nourishes all things and nurtures life.
### 汽水(汽水) → 汽(汽) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Three horizontal strokes representing clouds ->
    - adjusted with a vertical line to avoid confusion with 三(三) (sān, "three") ->
    - 气(氣) (qì, "air") as flowing air
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(氣) (qì, "air") originated from a pictorial representation of clouds flowing in the sky, providing an intuitive association with the concept of air or gas.
- **examples:**
  - 燃气灶要定期检查(燃氣灶要定期檢查)。
    - rán qì zào yào dìngqí jiǎnchá.
    - The gas stove should be checked regularly.
  - 风吹进房间(風吹進房間)，新鲜气息扑面而来(新鮮氣息撲面而來)。
    - fēng chuī jìn fángjiān, xīnxiān qìxí pūmiàn ér lái.
    - The wind blows into the room, and a fresh breeze hits the face.
  - 释放毒气有害健康(釋放毒氣有害健康)。
    - shìfàng dú qì yǒuhài jiànkāng.
    - Releasing toxic gas is harmful to health.
### 汽水(汽水) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** The character 水(水) (shuǐ, 'water') visually represents the natural form of flowing water, which is intuitive given its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一杯水(請給我一杯水)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ.
    - Please give me a glass of water.
  - 夏天炎热时(夏天炎熱時)，池塘里的水清澈见底(池塘裡的水清澈見底)。
    - Xiàtiān yánrè shí, chítáng lǐ de shuǐ qīngchè jiàn dǐ.
    - When summer is scorching, the water in the pond is crystal clear.
