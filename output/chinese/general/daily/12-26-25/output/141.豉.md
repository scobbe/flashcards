### fermented soybeans
---
- **pinyin:** fermented soybeans
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 豉 represents fermented soybeans, with its structure suggesting the association with food and fermentation.
  - **interpretation:** Fermented soybeans are a significant ingredient in various Asian cuisines, especially in sauces and dishes, which relates directly to the character's meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃豆豉(我喜歡吃豆豉)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dòuchǐ！
    - I love eating fermented soybeans!
  - 豆豉可以用来做调料(豆豉可以用來做調料)。
    - Dòuchǐ kěyǐ yòng lái zuò tiáoliào。
    - Fermented soybeans can be used as a condiment.
