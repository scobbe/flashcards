## almond
### xìngrén
---
- **definition:** almond
- **pinyin:** xìngrén
- **components:**
  - 杏
    - xìng
    - apricot
  - 仁
    - rén
    - kernel; seed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 杏(杏) (xìng, "apricot") + 仁(仁) (rén, "kernel; seed") =
    - almond
  - **interpretation:** 杏(杏) (xìng, "apricot") refers to the apricot, and 仁(仁) (rén, "kernel; seed") signifies the edible seed inside a fruit. Together they form 杏仁(杏仁) (xìngrén), which means "almond," reflecting the almond's similarity to apricot kernels in shape and usage.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢杏仁(我喜歡杏仁)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān xìngrén.
    - I like almonds.
  - 早餐时(早餐時)，我喜欢吃杏仁(我喜歡吃杏仁)。
    - Zǎocān shí, wǒ xǐhuān chī xìngrén.
    - At breakfast, I like to eat almonds.
  - 制作杏仁饼干时(製作杏仁餅乾時)，她总是小心翼翼(她總是小心翼翼)。
    - Zhìzuò xìngrén bǐnggān shí, tā zǒng shì xiǎoxīn yìyì.
    - When making almond cookies, she is always very careful.
### 杏仁(杏仁) → 杏(杏)
- **definition:** apricot
- **pinyin:** xìng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - wood with a mouth ->
    - apricot tree
  - **interpretation:** The character 杏(xìng, "apricot") combines 木(mù, "wood") and 口(kǒu, "mouth") to evoke the wood of the apricot tree and its edible, tasty fruit, as if the tree provides food 'to the mouth'.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢杏(她喜歡杏)。
    - Tā xǐhuān xìng.
    - She likes apricots.
  - 春天到来时(春天到來時)，杏花盛开满园(杏花盛開滿園)。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, xìng huā shèngkāi mǎn yuán.
    - When spring arrives, apricot blossoms fill the garden.
  - 老农在树下沏茶(老農在樹下沏茶)，欣赏着成熟的杏(欣賞著成熟的杏)。
    - Lǎo nóng zài shù xià qì chá, xīnshǎng zhe chéngshú de xìng.
    - The old farmer makes tea under the tree, admiring the ripe apricots.

- **pinyin:** èr
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
