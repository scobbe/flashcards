### almond
---
- **pinyin:** almond
- **components:**
  - 杏
    - xìng
    - apricot; fruit
  - 仁
    - rén
    - nuts; benevolence; kindness
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 杏(杏) (xìng, 'apricot') + 脑(腦) (nǎo, 'nuts') — apricot nuts
  - **interpretation:** The term 杏仁 refers to the edible seeds of the apricot, highlighting the connection between apricots and nuts. This makes sense as almonds resemble the seeds of apricots, both botanically and in flavor.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃杏仁。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī xìngrén.
    - I like to eat almonds.
  - 杏仁可以用来做甜点。
    - Xìngrén kěyǐ yòng lái zuò tiándiǎn.
    - Almonds can be used to make desserts.
### 杏仁(杏仁) → 杏(杏)
- **definition:** apricot; fruit
- **pinyin:** xìng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 木 (mù, 'wood') + 口 (kǒu, 'mouth') – suggesting the fruit's pleasant taste.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of wood and mouth symbolizes the appeal of the apricot fruit, which is both a wood-producing plant and tasty to eat.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃杏子(我喜歡吃杏子)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī xìngzi.
    - I like to eat apricots.
  - 杏树长得很好(杏樹長得很好)。
    - Xìngshù zhǎng de hěn hǎo.
    - The apricot tree is growing very well.

### 杏仁(杏仁) → 仁(仁)
- **definition:** nuts; benevolence; kindness
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 二
    - èr
    - two
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 人 (rén, 'person') + semantic 二 (èr, 'two') — represents the concept of 'benevolence' based on the interaction between individuals.
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys the idea of kindness and benevolence, reflecting a relationship between two people and their interactions. This evokes a sense of community and empathy.
- **examples:**
  - 仁爱是最重要的美德(仁愛是最重要的美德)。
    - Rén ài shì zuì zhòngyào de měidé.

  - 我们应该对别人施以仁慈(我們應該對別人施以仁慈)。
    - Wǒmen yīnggāi duì biérén shī yǐ réncí.
    - We should show kindness to others.
### 杏仁(杏仁) → 仁(仁) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a person standing, emphasizing human form. This direct depiction makes it easy to associate the character with the meaning of a 'person' or 'human'.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个好人(他是一個好人)。
    - tā shì yī gè hǎo rén.

    - rén men dōu zhī dào zhè jiàn shì.
