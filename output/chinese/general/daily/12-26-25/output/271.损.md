### decrease; damage
---
- **pinyin:** decrease; damage
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 損
    - sǔn
    - to damage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic element 手 (shǒu, 'hand') indicating action and the phonetic element 損 (sǔn) which carries the overall meaning of decrease or damage.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of an action performed by the hand that results in damage or decrease. This makes intuitive sense as one often uses their hands to cause loss or harm.
- **examples:**
  - 损失很大(損失很大)！
    - sǔnshī hěn dà！
    - The loss is significant!
  - 他已经损坏了许多东西(他已經損壞了許多東西)。
    - tā yǐjīng sǔnhuài le xǔduō dōngxī.
    - He has already damaged many items.
  - 这次事故造成了严重的损失(這次事故造成了嚴重的損失)。
    - zhè cì shìgù zàochéng le yánzhòng de sǔnshī.
    - This accident caused serious damage.
### 损(損) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human hand with fingers, where the top stroke represents the bent over middle finger and horizontal strokes represent two fingers each.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents a hand by depicting its five distinct fingers, distinguishing it from other characters that represent claws or simplified forms of hands.
- **examples:**
  - 我用手(我用手)写字。
    - Wǒ yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - I write with my hand.
  - 请用手(請用手)打电话。
    - Qǐng yòng shǒu dǎ diànhuà.
    - Please use your hand to make a phone call.
### 损(損) → 損(損)
- **definition:** to damage
- **pinyin:** sǔn
- **components:**
  - 已
    - yǐ
    - already
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is formed from the semantic component 損 (sǔn, 'to damage') and the phonetic component 巳 (sì, 'already').
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 'to damage' and 'already' suggests that something is in a state of damage or loss. This makes intuitive sense as damage implies that something has been taken away or reduced.
- **examples:**
  - 这个物品损坏了(這個物品損壞了)，需要修理(需要修理)。
    - zhège wùpǐn sǔnhuài le, xūyào xiūlǐ.
    - This item is damaged and needs repair.
  - 损失太大了(損失太大了)，我很难接受(我很難接受)。
    - sǔnshī tài dà le, wǒ hěn nán jiēshòu.
    - The loss is too great, and I find it hard to accept.
### 损(損) → 損(損) → 已(已)
- **definition:** already
- **pinyin:** yǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** May have originally been the same character as 巳, which depicts either a snake or a fetus.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of completeness or something that has come to a conclusion, reminiscent of the life cycle represented by a fetus or snake.
- **examples:**
  - 我已经完成任务(我已經完成任務)。
    - Wǒ yǐjīng wánchéng rènwù.
    - I have already completed the task.
  - 他已经来了(他已經來了)。
    - Tā yǐjīng lái le.
    - He has already arrived.
