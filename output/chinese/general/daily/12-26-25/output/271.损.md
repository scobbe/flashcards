## decrease; damage
### sǔn
---
- **definition:** decrease; damage
- **pinyin:** sǔn
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 员(員)
    - yuán
    - person; member
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic: 員(员) (yuán) ->
    - action related to hands causing reduction ->
    - 損 (sǔn, "decrease; damage")
  - **interpretation:** The character 损 is formed by combining the hand radical 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") indicating a manual action, with 員(员) (yuán), providing the phonetic component. This suggests an action performed by hand that leads to a decrease or damage.
  - **simplification:** The traditional 損 (sǔn) was simplified to 损 by replacing 員(员) with its simplified form 员(员), retaining the phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 过度包装可能损(損)环境(環境)。
    - Guòdù bāozhuāng kěnéng sǔn(損) huánjìng(環境)。
    - Excessive packaging may damage the environment.
  - 高温可能损(損)电子产品(電子產品)，影响性能(影響性能)。
    - Gāowēn kěnéng sǔn(損) diànzǐ chǎnpǐn(電子產品)，yǐngxiǎng xìngnéng(影響性能)。
    - High temperatures may damage electronic products, affecting their performance.
  - 浪费粮食会损(損)国家利益(國家利益)。
    - Làngfèi liángshí huì sǔn(損) guójiā lìyì(國家利益)。
    - Wasting food will damage the national interest.
### 损(損) → 扌(扌)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Derived from its seal script form
  - **description:**
    - Seal script 扌 (shǒu, "hand") ->
    - simplified grouping for hand-related terms
  - **interpretation:** 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") functions as a shorthand or radical form used mainly in compounded characters, retaining the meaning of 'hand.' This facilitates the creation of characters that involve manual action or activities related to the hands.
  - **simplification:** None, as 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") is already a simplified component derived for ease in writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我的手很温暖(我的手很溫暖)。
    - Wǒ de shǒu hěn wēnnuǎn.
    - My hand is very warm.
  - 她伸出手(她伸出手)，邀请大家(邀請大家)。
    - Tā shēn chū shǒu, yāo qǐng dàjiā.
    - She stretched out her hand, inviting everyone.
  - 握手表示友好(握手表示友好)，促进合作(促進合作)。
    - Wò shǒu biǎoshì yǒuhǎo, cùjìn hézuò.
    - A handshake signifies friendship, promoting cooperation.
### 损(損) → 员(員)
- **definition:** person; member
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Simplified character
  - **description:**
    - 員(員) (yuán, "member") ->
    - simplified by replacing 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") with 贝(贝) (bèi, "cowry")
  - **interpretation:** The simplification of 員(員) into 员 replaced the traditional component 貝(貝) with its simpler form 贝(贝), maintaining the original meaning related to people or members.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved replacing the complex 貝(貝) with its single-stroke counterpart 贝(贝), reflecting a trend towards reducing stroke count.
- **examples:**
  - 他是团队中的重要成员(他是團隊中的重要成員)，备受大家尊敬(備受大家尊敬)。
    - Tā shì tuánduì zhōng de zhòngyào chéngyuán, bèishòu dàjiā zūnjìng.
    - He is an important member of the team, highly respected by everyone.
  - 每个部门都有自己的专职员工(每個部門都有自己的專職員工)。
    - Měi gè bùmén dōu yǒu zìjǐ de zhuānzhí yuángōng.
    - Every department has its own dedicated staff.
  - 他被选为班级的一员(他被選為班級的一員)，引以为豪(引以爲豪)。
    - Tā bèi xuǎn wéi bānjí de yīyuán, yǐnyǐ wéi háo.
    - He was chosen as a member of the class and takes pride in it.
