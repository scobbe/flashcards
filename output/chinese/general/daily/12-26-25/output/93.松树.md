---
## 
### pine tree
---
- **pinyin:** pine tree
- **components:**
  - 松
    - sōng
    - fir/pine/tree; relax; loose; surname
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; wood; block; plant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 松(松) (sōng, 'fir/pine/tree') + 树(樹) (shù, 'tree') — fir/pine tree
  - **interpretation:** This term directly describes a type of tree that is commonly found in nature. The parts represent the pine species along with its classification as a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 松树非常高大(松樹非常高大)。
    - Sōngshù fēicháng gāodà.
    - The pine tree is very tall.
  - 这片森林有很多松树(這片森林有很多松樹)。
    - Zhè piàn sēnlín yǒu hěnduō sōngshù.
    - This forest has many pine trees.
### 松树(松樹) → 松(松)
- **definition:** fir/pine/tree; relax; loose; surname
- **pinyin:** sōng
- **components:**
  - 林
    - lín
    - forest
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character combines the semantic part 林 (lín, 'forest') with the phonetic component 松 (sōng, 'fir/pine').
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a scene of a forest with fir trees, symbolizing both strength and a sense of relaxation when among nature.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢松树(我喜歡松樹)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān sōngshù.
    - I like pine trees.
  - 松了一口气(鬆了一口氣)，终于完成了工作(終於完成了工作)。
    - Sōngle yī kǒuqì, zhōngyú wánchéngle gōngzuò.
    - I relaxed and finally finished the work.
### 松树(松樹) → 松(松) → 林(林)
- **definition:** forest
- **pinyin:** lín
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** 林(林) (lín, 'forest') is formed by two 木(木) (mù, 'tree') characters, symbolizing a grouping of trees which effectively creates the concept of a forest.
  - **interpretation:** The visual repetition of trees conveys the idea of many trees together, creating a forest.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢在森林里散步。(我喜歡在森林裏散步。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài sēnlín lǐ sànbù.
    - I enjoy walking in the forest.
  - 这个公园有很多树木和林荫道。(這個公園有很多樹木和林蔭道。)
    - Zhège gōngyuán yǒu hěnduō shùmù hé lín yīn dào.
    - This park has many trees and shaded paths.
### 松树(松樹) → 松(松) → 林(林) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
### 松树(松樹) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree; wood; block; plant
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 又(壴)
    - yòu
    - again; also (used as phonetic)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component 木 (mù, 'tree') and the phonetic component 壴 (which became 又 in simplification).
  - **interpretation:** The character includes the component for 'tree,' clearly relating it to its meaning. The phonetic component provides guidance on pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing the component 壴 with 又, which is visually simpler and retains the phonetic clue.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树很高(這棵樹很高)。
    - Zhè kē shù hěn gāo.
    - This tree is very tall.
  - 我喜欢在树下读书(我喜歡在樹下讀書)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài shùxià dúshū.
    - I enjoy reading under the tree.
  - 这树开花了(這樹開花了)。
    - Zhè shù kāihuāle.
    - This tree has bloomed.

- **definition:** again; also (used as phonetic)
