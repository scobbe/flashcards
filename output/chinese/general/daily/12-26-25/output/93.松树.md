## pine tree
### sōngshù
---
- **definition:** pine tree
- **pinyin:** sōngshù
- **components:**
  - 松
    - sōng
    - pine
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 松(松) (sōng, "pine") + 树(樹) (shù, "tree")
  - **interpretation:** The word 松树(松樹) (sōngshù, "pine tree") combines 松(松) (sōng, "pine"), which refers to the type of tree known for its evergreen needles and cones, and 树(樹) (shù, "tree"), indicating a plant with a woody stem. Together, the term directly means 'pine tree,' highlighting the species of tree being described.
  - **simplification:** 松树 is the simplified form of 松樹.
- **examples:**
  - 这是一棵高大的松树(這是一棵高大的松樹)。
    - Zhè shì yī kē gāodà de sōngshù.
    - This is a tall pine tree.
  - 秋风扫过山坡(秋風掃過山坡)，带来松树的清香(帶來松樹的清香)。
    - Qiūfēng sǎoguò shānpō, dàilái sōngshù de qīngxiāng.
    - The autumn wind sweeps over the hill, carrying the fresh scent of pine trees.
