## showroom
### yàngbǎnjiān
---
- **traditional:** 樣板間
- **definition:** showroom
- **pinyin:** yàngbǎnjiān
- **components:**
  - 样(樣)
    - yàng
    - appearance; shape; manner
  - 板
    - bǎn
    - board; plank; model
  - 间(間)
    - jiān
    - room; between; space
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 样(樣) (yàng, "appearance") + 板 (bǎn, "board; model") + 间(間) (jiān, "room")
  - **interpretation:** The term 样(樣)板( )间(間) (yàngbǎnjiān, "showroom") combines 样(樣) (yàng, "appearance") to suggest an example or display, with 板 (bǎn, "model"), indicating a model or template, and 间(間) (jiān, "room"), referring to the physical space. Together, they describe a room that displays a model or example, often used in real estate to exhibit how a finished living space might look.
  - **simplification:** 样板间 is the simplified form of 樣板間.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )带(帶)我( )们(們)参(參)观(觀)样(樣)板( )间(間)。
    - Tā dài wǒmen cānguān yàngbǎnjiān.
    - He took us to visit the showroom.
  - 新( )的( )样(樣)板( )间(間)装(裝)修( )精( )美( )，吸( )引( )了( )很( )多( )顾(顧)客( )。
    - Xīn de yàngbǎnjiān zhuāngxiū jīngměi, xīyǐn le hěn duō gùkè.
    - The new showroom is exquisitely decorated and attracts many customers.
