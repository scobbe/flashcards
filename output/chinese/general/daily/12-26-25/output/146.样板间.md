## showroom
### yàngbǎnjiān
---
- **definition:** showroom
- **pinyin:** yàngbǎnjiān
- **components:**
  - 样(樣)
    - yàng
    - appearance; model
  - 板
    - bǎn
    - board; template
  - 间(間)
    - jiān
    - room
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 样(樣) (yàng, "appearance; model") + 板(板) (bǎn, "board; template") + 间(間) (jiān, "room") =
    - a room that serves as a model or template for others to emulate, generally referring to a showroom where designs or layouts are displayed.
  - **interpretation:** A 样板间(樣板間) (yàngbǎnjiān) is a room set up as a model to showcase design or furnishings, allowing potential buyers to visualize how a completed space might appear.
- **examples:**
  - 他们展示了现代样板间(他們展示了現代樣板間)。
    - Tāmen zhǎnshì le xiàndài yàngbǎnjiān.
    - They showcased a modern showroom.
  - 房地产公司建起了全新样板间(房地產公司建起了全新樣板間)，吸引了众多客户(吸引了眾多客戶)。
    - Fángdìchǎn gōngsī jiànqǐ le quán xīn yàngbǎnjiān, xīyǐn le zhòng duō kèhù.
    - The real estate company built a brand-new showroom, attracting many customers.
  - 样板间可以帮助购房者(樣板間可以幫助購房者)，更好地了解房屋布局(更好地了解房屋佈局)。
    - Yàngbǎnjiān kěyǐ bāngzhù gòufángzhě, gèng hǎo de liǎojiě fángwū bùjú.
    - The showroom can help homebuyers better understand the layout of the house.
### 样板间(樣板間) → 样(樣)
- **definition:** appearance; model
- **pinyin:** yàng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 羕
    - yàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "wood") + 羕(羕) (yàng) =
    - wood-related appearance ->
    - appearance; model
  - **interpretation:** The character 样(樣) (yàng) combines 木(木) (mù, "wood") as the semantic element, suggesting something shaped or made from wood, with 羕(羕) (yàng) providing the phonetic component, indicating pronunciation. This amalgamation emphasizes the idea of a standard or model, like one carved from wood.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character 样 shares the same components as the traditional 樣, retaining the semantic and phonetic elements while reducing the complexity for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 他的样子很迷人(他的樣子很迷人)。
    - Tā de yàngzi hěn mírén.
    - His appearance is very charming.
  - 他打扮得十分像样(他打扮得十分像樣)，看起来非常精神(看起來非常精神)。
    - Tā dǎbàn dé shífēn xiàngyàng, kàn qǐlái fēicháng jīngshén.
    - He dressed very decently, looking extremely spirited.
  - 设计师参考了古典样式(設計師參考了古典樣式)，创作出独特的作品(創作出獨特的作品)。
    - Shèjìshī cānkǎo le gǔdiǎn yàngshì, chuàngzuò chū dútè de zuòpǐn.

### 样板间(樣板間) → 间(間)
- **definition:** room
- **pinyin:** jiān
- **components:**
  - 門
    - mén
    - door
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 門(門) (mén, "door") + 日(日) (rì, "sun") =
    - a space where light enters through a door ->
    - room
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 門(門) (mén, "door") to indicate a bounded space or passage, with 日(日) (rì, "sun") suggesting light entering, together symbolizing the idea of a room or interval.
  - **simplification:** 间(間): The component 門(門) (mén, "door") was simplified to 门(门) (mén, "door"), retaining 日(日) (rì, "sun") for phonetic and semantic reasons.
