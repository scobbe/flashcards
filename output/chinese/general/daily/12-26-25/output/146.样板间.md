### showroom
---
- **pinyin:** showroom
- **components:**
  - 样板(樣板)
    - yàngbǎn
    - model; sample; specimen; example
  - 间(間)
    - jiān
    - room; space; interval; between
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 样板(樣板) (yàngbǎn, 'model; sample') + 间(間) (jiān, 'room; space') — model room
  - **interpretation:** A 样板间(樣板間) is a space displaying a model or example of a product, typically used in real estate or retail. This makes sense as it combines a model representation with a designated space for viewing.
- **examples:**
  - 我去看样板间了(我去看樣板間了)。
    - Wǒ qù kàn yàngbǎn jiān le.
    - I went to see the showroom.
  - 这家公司的样板间设计很独特(這家公司的樣板間設計很獨特)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngsī de yàngbǎn jiān shèjì hěn dútè.
    - The design of this company's showroom is very unique.

### 样板间(樣板間) → 间(間)
- **definition:** room; space; interval; between
- **pinyin:** jiān
- **components:**
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door
  - 𠂇
    - kān
    - space/interval
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the 'door' radical 門 (mén, 'door') and the phonetic component 'kan' from the character 𠂇, which denotes a space or interval.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a door suggests an opening or space, while the phonetic component emphasizes the idea of intervals or areas in between. This makes intuitive sense as it evokes the relationship of being inside or outside a space defined by a door.
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 間 was simplified to 间 by removing one horizontal stroke in the component of 'kan' (𠂇). This stroke reduction makes the character quicker and easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 我在这个房间(我在這個房間)。
    - Wǒ zài zhège fángjiān。
    - I am in this room.
  - 你能在这里找到间隙(你能在這裡找到間隙)。
    - Nǐ néng zài zhèlǐ zhǎodào jiànxì。
    - You can find a gap here.
