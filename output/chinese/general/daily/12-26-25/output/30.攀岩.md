### rock climbing
---
- **pinyin:** rock climbing
- **components:**
  - 攀
    - pān
    - climb; scale; clamber; ascend
  - 岩
    - yán
    - rock; cliff; boulder; stony
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 攀(趴) (pān, 'climb') + 岩(巖) (yán, 'rock') — climb rock
  - **interpretation:** This term describes the activity of climbing rocky surfaces. Climbing involves using skills to ascend steep terrain made of rock.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢攀岩(我喜歡攀岩)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān pān yán.
    - I enjoy rock climbing.
  - 攀岩运动越来越受欢迎(攀岩運動越來越受歡迎)。
    - Pān yán yùndòng yuè lái yuè shòu huānyíng.
    - Rock climbing is becoming increasingly popular.
  - 我们去攀岩吧(我們去攀岩吧)。
    - Wǒmen qù pān yán ba.
    - Let's go rock climbing.
