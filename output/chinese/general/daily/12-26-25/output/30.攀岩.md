## rock climbing
### pānyán
---
- **definition:** rock climbing
- **pinyin:** pānyán
- **components:**
  - 攀
    - pān
    - climb
  - 岩
    - yán
    - rock; cliff
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 攀(攀) (pān, "climb"), 岩(岩) (yán, "rock; cliff")
  - **interpretation:** The word 攀岩 (pānyán) translates to "rock climbing," referring specifically to the activity of climbing on natural rock formations or artificial rock walls. 攀(攀) (pān, "climb") represents the act of ascending, while 岩(岩) (yán, "rock; cliff") specifies the natural surface being climbed. This combination effectively conveys the idea of physically climbing rock surfaces, either as a sport or recreational activity.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢攀岩(他喜歡攀岩)。
    - Tā xǐhuān pānyán.
    - He likes rock climbing.
  - 周末他去海边攀岩(週末他去海邊攀岩)，体验自然乐趣(體驗自然樂趣)。
    - Zhōumò tā qù hǎibiān pānyán, tǐyàn zìrán lèqù.
    - On the weekend, he goes rock climbing by the seaside, experiencing the joy of nature.
  - 小明通过攀岩(小明通過攀岩)，不断挑战自己(不斷挑戰自己)。
    - Xiǎomíng tōngguò pānyán, bùduàn tiǎozhàn zìjǐ.
    - Xiaoming continuously challenges himself through rock climbing.

### 攀岩(攀岩) → 岩(巖)
- **definition:** rock; cliff
- **pinyin:** yán
- **components:**
  - 山
    - shān
    - hill
  - 石
    - shí
    - rock
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 山(山) (shān, "hill") + 石(石) (shí, "rock") =
    - combined elements representing a rocky hill or cliff ->
    - 岩(巖) (yán, "rock; cliff")
  - **interpretation:** The character 岩(巖) (yán, "rock; cliff") combines 山(山) (shān, "hill") and 石(石) (shí, "rock") to convey the image of a rocky hill or a cliff. The original form 巖 emphasized the phonetic 嚴(嚴) (yán), but later 岩 became the more common form.
  - **simplification:** The character 岩(巖) was originally 巖 in traditional script, simplified by dropping the 嚴 component and directly combining 山 and 石 to retain both the sound and meaning efficiently.
- **examples:**
  - 那块岩静静矗立在河边(那塊岩靜靜矗立在河邊)。
    - Nà kuài yán jìng jìng chùlì zài hé biān.
    - That rock stands quietly by the river bank.
  - 登上岩顶(登上岩頂)，看尽山川风光(看盡山川風光)。
    - Dēng shàng yán dǐng, kàn jìn shān chuān fēng guāng.
    - Climb to the top of the rock and behold the scenic mountains and rivers.
  - 海浪拍打着悬岩(海浪拍打著懸岩)，发出低沉的轰鸣(發出低沉的轟鳴)。
    - Hǎi làng pāidǎ zhe xuán yán, fāchū dīchén de hōngmíng.
    - The waves crash against the cliff, producing a deep, rumbling sound.

### 攀岩(攀岩) → 岩(巖) → 石(石)
- **definition:** rock
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone beneath a cliff, where the upper part originally looked like a cliff (厂) but was distorted into 丆 over time.
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') visually represents a stone in a natural setting, highlighting the element of a cliff or mountain. Its visual design aims to reflect the appearance of rocks lodged or embedded in a mountainous or cliff-like setting.
- **examples:**
  - 他在河边发现一块石(他在河邊發現一塊石)。
    - Tā zài hé biān fāxiàn yī kuài shí.
    - He discovered a piece of stone by the riverside.
  - 孩子们喜欢堆石(孩子們喜歡堆石)，玩得开心(玩得開心)。
    - Háizimen xǐhuān duī shí, wán de kāixīn.
    - Children like to stack stones, playing happily.
