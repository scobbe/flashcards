### Píng
---
- **definition:** surname Ping
- **pinyin:** Píng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted a flat, level object ->
    - evolved to signify both 'flat' and 'peaceful' due to the association of flatness with stability
  - **interpretation:** The character 平 symbolizes the concept of flatness, which naturally extends to ideas of equality and peace because a flat surface is stable and calm. This character captures the intuition that levelness in both physical and metaphorical senses promotes harmony.
