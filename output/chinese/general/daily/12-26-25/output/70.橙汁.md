## orange juice
### chéngzhī
---
- **definition:** orange juice
- **pinyin:** chéngzhī
- **components:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange
  - 汁
    - zhī
    - juice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 橙(橙) (chéng, "orange") + 汁(汁) (zhī, "juice") combines to mean 'orange juice', the liquid obtained from squeezing or pressing oranges.
  - **interpretation:** A liquid (汁) extracted from an orange (橙) is naturally understood to be orange juice, a beverage made from oranges.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝橙汁(我喜歡喝橙汁)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē chéngzhī.
    - I like to drink orange juice.
  - 他点了一杯橙汁(他點了一杯橙汁)，准备开始工作(準備開始工作)。
    - Tā diǎnle yī bēi chéngzhī, zhǔnbèi kāishǐ gōngzuò.
    - He ordered a glass of orange juice, and he was ready to start working.
