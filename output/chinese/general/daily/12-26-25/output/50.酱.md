## sauce
### jiàng
---
- **definition:** sauce
- **pinyin:** jiàng
- **components:**
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - drink
  - 將
    - jiāng
    - lead
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 酉(酉) (yǒu, "drink") + 將(將) (jiāng, "lead") =
    - liquid for cooking or drinking ->
    - sauce
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the idea of fermented liquids, like those used in drink, with a phonetic hint from 將(將) (jiāng, "lead"), suggesting a mixture or preparation, which fits the concept of a sauce.
  - **simplification:** 醬(醬) simplified to 酱(酱) by replacing 將(將) (jiāng, "lead") with its cursive form 丬(丬) (pán, "half tree trunk") and 夕(夕) (xī, "evening") for phonetic similarity.
- **examples:**
  - 这瓶酱很好吃(這瓶醬很好吃)。
    - Zhè píng jiàng hěn hǎo chī.
    - This bottle of sauce is delicious.
  - 妈妈做菜时(媽媽做菜時)，总喜欢用自制的酱(總喜歡用自製的醬)。
    - Māmā zuò cài shí, zǒng xǐhuān yòng zì zhì de jiàng.
    - When cooking, mom always likes using homemade sauce.
  - 厨房里的酱香浓郁(廚房裡的醬香濃郁)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ de jiàng xiāng nóng yù.
    - The aroma of the sauce in the kitchen is rich.

### 酱(醬) → 將(將) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** inch
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布每一寸都是精心制作(這塊布每一寸都是精心製作)。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请用卷尺量一寸(請用卷尺量一寸)，每一寸数据都准确(每一寸數據都準確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.
