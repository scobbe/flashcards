- **traditional:** 醬
- **definition:** sauce
- **pinyin:** jiàng
- **components:**
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - drink
  - 將
    - jiāng
    - lead
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 酉 (yǒu, "drink") + 將 (jiāng, "lead") =
    - liquid for cooking or drinking ->
    - sauce
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the idea of fermented liquids, like those used in drink, with a phonetic hint from 將 (jiāng, "lead"), suggesting a mixture or preparation, which fits the concept of a sauce.
  - **simplification:** 醬 simplified to 酱 by replacing 將 (jiāng, "lead") with its cursive form 丬 (pán, "half tree trunk") and 夕 (xī, "evening") for phonetic similarity.
