## sauce
### jiàng
---
- **definition:** sauce
- **pinyin:** jiàng
- **components:**
  - 将(將)
    - jiāng
    - lead
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - drink
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 將(将) (jiāng) 'lead' + 酉(酉) (yǒu, 'drink') =
    - vessel for liquid purposes ->
    - sauce
  - **interpretation:** The character 醬(醬) (jiàng, "sauce") combines 將(将) (jiāng, "lead") for its phonetic component with 酉(酉) (yǒu, "drink") for its semantic meaning, suggesting a liquid used in culinary applications.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 醬(醬) by replacing the complex 將(将) with 丬夕(夕), maintaining phonetic similarity without semantic loss.
- **examples:**
  - 这瓶酱很好吃(這瓶醬很好吃)。
    - Zhè píng jiàng hěn hǎochī.
    - This bottle of sauce is very delicious.
  - 妈妈用酱炒菜(媽媽用醬炒菜)，非常入味(非常入味)。
    - Māmā yòng jiàng chǎocài, fēicháng rùwèi.
    - Mom stir-fries vegetables with sauce, and it is very flavorful.
  - 晚上我蘸酱(晚上我蘸醬)，感觉特别鲜美(感覺特別鮮美)。
    - Wǎnshàng wǒ zhàn jiàng, gǎnjué tèbié xiānměi.
    - At night, I dip into the sauce and it tastes exceptionally fresh and delicious.
### 酱(醬) → 将(將)
- **definition:** lead
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 爿
    - pán
    - small table
  - ⺼
    - ròu
    - meat
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 爿(爿) (pán, "small table") + semantic: ⺼(⺼) (ròu, "meat") + semantic: 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") =
    - meat offered on table with hand ->
    - lead
  - **interpretation:** The character 将(將) (jiāng, "lead") combines the meat ⺼(⺼) (ròu, "meat") and hand 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") components, indicating the action of offering meat as tribute, a leader's action. Phonetically, 爿(爿) (pán) suggests the idea of a table or platform where offerings are made, symbolizing leadership and guidance.
- **examples:**
  - 他将带领我们(他將帶領我們)，走向胜利(走向勝利)。
    - Tā jiāng dàilǐng wǒmen, zǒuxiàng shènglì.
    - He will lead us towards victory.
  - 老师将引导学生(老師將引導學生)，走向知识的海洋(走向知識的海洋)。
    - Lǎoshī jiāng yǐndǎo xuéshēng, zǒuxiàng zhīshì de hǎiyáng.
    - The teacher will guide the students towards the ocean of knowledge.
  - 将军将率领军队(將軍將率領軍隊)，冲锋陷阵(衝鋒陷陣)。
    - Jiāngjūn jiāng shuàilǐng jūnduì, chōngfēng xiànzhèn.
    - The general will lead the troops into battle.
### 酱(醬) → 将(將) → 爿(爿)
- **definition:** small table
- **pinyin:** pán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts half of a tree trunk
  - **interpretation:** The character 爿(pán, 'small table') visually represents one side of a tree trunk, suggesting a flat surface akin to a narrow table.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一爿桌子(我買了一爿桌子)。
    - Wǒ mǎile pán zhuōzi.
    - I bought a small table.
  - 餐厅里有两爿桌子(餐廳裡有兩爿桌子)，客人们坐在旁边(客人們坐在旁邊)。
    - Cāntīng lǐ yǒu liǎng pán zhuōzi, kèrénmen zuò zài pángbiān.
    - There are two small tables in the dining room, and the guests are sitting beside them.
### 酱(醬) → 将(將) → ⺼(肉)
- **definition:** meat
- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a slab of meat
  - **interpretation:** The character ⺼(肉) (ròu, "meat") visually represents a piece of meat by depicting the muscle fibers within it. This pictographic representation makes it clear why the character stands for 'meat'.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃肉(我喜歡吃肉)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī ròu.
    - I like to eat meat.
  - 这块肉非常鲜美(這塊肉非常鮮美)，配上蔬菜更健康(配上蔬菜更健康)。
    - Zhè kuài ròu fēicháng xiānměi, pèishàng shūcài gèng jiànkāng.
    - This piece of meat is very delicious, and it is healthier with vegetables.
### 酱(醬) → 将(將) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram (指事)
  - **description:**
    - Line on hand indicating pulse point on forearm ->
    - original form of 肘 (zhǒu, 'elbow') ->
    - unit of measurement derived from this distance
  - **interpretation:** 寸(cùn, 'inch') represents a specific point on the forearm used for pulse measurement, historically linked to its measurement definition. The form depicts a line that combines with the hand to signify this location, showing a logical evolution from anatomy to distance measurement.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布的宽度只有五寸(這塊布的寬度只有五寸)。
    - Zhè kuài bù de kuāndù zhǐyǒu wǔ cùn.
    - The width of this cloth is only five inches.
  - 木匠在制作家具时(木匠在製作家具時)，要精确到一寸(要精確到一寸)。
    - Mùjiàng zài zhìzuò jiājù shí, yào jīngquè dào yī cùn.
    - Carpenters must be precise to the inch when making furniture.
### 酱(醬) → 酉(酉)
- **definition:** drink
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a wine vessel with a narrow neck and wide bottom
  - **interpretation:** 酉(酉) (yǒu, "drink") represents a wine vessel, indicating its association with alcohol and beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 朋友相聚时(朋友相聚時)，大家酉得尽兴(大家酉得盡興)。
    - Péngyǒu xiāngjù shí, Dàjiā yǒu dé jǐnxìng.
    - When friends gather, everyone drinks heartily.
  - 他爱在佳节时(他愛在佳節時)，独自酉美酒(獨自酉美酒)。
    - Tā ài zài jiājié shí, Dúzì yǒu měijiǔ.
    - He loves to drink fine wine on festive occasions by himself.
