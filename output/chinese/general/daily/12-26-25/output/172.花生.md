- **pinyin:** peanut
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom; spend; spend money
  - 生
    - shēng
    - grow; born; life; raw
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 花(花) (huā, "flower") + 生(生) (shēng, "grow; born") — flower grow
  - **interpretation:** The term 花生 refers to peanuts, literally meaning 'flower grow' since peanuts grow underground and produce flowers above ground. This combination captures the plant's unique growth pattern, where the flowers lead to the formation of the edible seeds.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃花生(我喜歡吃花生)，特别是炒的(特別是炒的)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huāshēng, tèbié shì chǎo de.
    - I like to eat peanuts, especially fried ones.
  - 花生的营养价值很高(花生的營養價值很高)。
    - Huāshēng de yíngyǎng jiàzhí hěn gāo.
    - Peanuts have a high nutritional value.
