### huāshēng
---
- **definition:** peanut
- **pinyin:** huāshēng
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 生
    - shēng
    - birth; grow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花(花) (huā, "flower") + 生(生) (shēng, "birth; grow") =
    - peanut
  - **interpretation:** The term 花生(花生) (huāshēng, "peanut") uses the idea of a 'flower' (花) growing or being born (生). This reflects the peanut's growth process, as it flowers above ground and the pods develop underground, which might resemble a 'flower giving birth,' connecting it metaphorically to the earth.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃花生(我喜歡吃花生)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huāshēng.
    - I like to eat peanuts.
  - 市集上常卖花生(市集上常賣花生)，价格公道(價格公道)。
    - Shìjí shàng cháng mài huāshēng, jiàgé gōngdào.
    - Peanuts are commonly sold at the market with fair prices.
  - 花生虽小(花生雖小)，营养丰富(營養豐富)。
    - Huāshēng suī xiǎo, yíngyǎng fēngfù.
    - Although peanuts are small, they are rich in nutrients.
