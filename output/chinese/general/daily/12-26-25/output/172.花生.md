### huāshēng
---
- **definition:** peanut
- **pinyin:** huāshēng
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 生
    - shēng
    - grow; life
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花(花) (huā, "flower") + 生(生) (shēng, "grow; life") =
    - flower life ->
    - peanut
  - **interpretation:** The term 花生 (huāshēng) humorously refers to peanuts as 'flower life,' which likely alludes to the unique growth pattern of peanuts where flowers appear on the plant above ground and then the nuts develop below ground.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃花生(我喜歡吃花生)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huāshēng.
    - I like to eat peanuts.
  - 他在市场上买花生(他在市場上買花生)，准备炒着吃(準備炒著吃)。
    - Tā zài shìchǎng shàng mǎi huāshēng, zhǔnbèi chǎo zhe chī.
    - He bought peanuts at the market, planning to fry them.
  - 花生是我最爱的小零食(花生是我最愛的小零食)。
    - Huāshēng shì wǒ zuì ài de xiǎo língshí.
    - Peanuts are my favorite snack.
