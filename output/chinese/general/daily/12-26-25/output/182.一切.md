## 一切(一切)
---
## 
### everything
---
- **pinyin:** everything
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one; single; a; unity
  - 切
    - qiē
    - cut; slice; all; entire
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 一(一) (yī, "one") + 切(切) (qiē, "cut; all") — one cut
  - **interpretation:** This term denotes the idea of encompassing all things or the entirety of a situation. The combination suggests a single unit that represents everything as a whole.
- **examples:**
  - 一切都会好起来的(一切都會好起來的)。
    - yīqiè dōu huì hǎo qǐlái de.
    - Everything will be fine.
  - 我们要接受一切(我們要接受一切)。
    - wǒmen yào jiēshòu yīqiè.
    - We must accept everything.
### 一切(一切) → 一(一)
- **definition:** one; single; a; unity
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** A horizontal stroke, indicating the number 'one'.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a single horizontal line, directly symbolizing the concept of one. This straightforward depiction makes it intuitive to understand its meaning at first glance.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个苹果(我有一個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī gè píngguǒ.
    - I have one apple.
  - 请给我一杯水(請給我一杯水)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ.
    - Please give me one cup of water.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切)
- **definition:** cut; slice; all; entire
- **pinyin:** qiē
- **components:**
  - 七
    - qī
    - seven
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 七 (qī, 'seven') + semantic 刀 (dāo, 'knife') — a character representing the action of cutting with a knife.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 七 (qī) and 刀 (dāo) illustrates both the sound and the meaning related to cutting, aligning the numeric sound with a tool used for that action.
- **examples:**
  - 我切了蔬菜(我切了蔬菜)，准备做沙拉(準備做沙拉)！
    - Wǒ qiē le shūcài, zhǔnbèi zuò shālā！
    - I sliced the vegetables, preparing to make a salad!
  - 请用刀切肉(請用刀切肉)。
    - Qǐng yòng dāo qiē ròu。
    - Please use a knife to cut the meat.
  - 他们切了一块蛋糕(他們切了一塊蛋糕)。
    - Tāmen qiē le yī kuài dàn gāo。
    - They sliced a piece of cake.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切) → 七(七)
- **definition:** seven
- **pinyin:** qī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** The character depicts a number with its distinct shape, resembling a stroke mark.
  - **interpretation:** It intuitively represents the numerical concept of seven due to its straightforward and unique form. The shape is simple and easy to recognize as a distinct symbol for a specific quantity.
- **examples:**
  - 我有七本书。(我有七本書。)
    - Wǒ yǒu qī běn shū.
    - I have seven books.
  - 七天后我们见。(七天後我們見。)
    - Qī tiān hòu wǒmen jiàn.
    - We will meet in seven days.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切) → 刀(刀)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** A pictogram representing a knife.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually depicts the shape of a knife, highlighting its sharp edge and blade. This makes it intuitive as it resembles the actual object.
- **examples:**
  - 我想买刀(我想買刀)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi dāo.
    - I want to buy a knife.
  - 她用刀切水果(她用刀切水果)。
    - Tā yòng dāo qiē shuǐguǒ.
    - She uses a knife to cut fruit.
