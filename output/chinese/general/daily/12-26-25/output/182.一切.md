## everything
### yīqiè
---
- **definition:** everything
- **pinyin:** yīqiè
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one; single
  - 切
    - qiè
    - all; entire
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 一(一) (yī, "one; single") combined with 切(切) (qiè, "all; entire") to denote "everything".
  - **interpretation:** This combination emphasizes inclusivity, as it merges the concept of 'one' with 'all' to encompass everything in its entirety.
- **examples:**
  - 一切皆有可能(一切皆有可能)。
    - yīqiè jiē yǒu kěnéng
    - Everything is possible.
  - 当夜幕降临时(當夜幕降臨時)，一切都变得神秘(一切都變得神秘)。
    - dāng yèmù jiànglín shí, yīqiè dōu biàn dé shénmì
    - When night falls, everything becomes mysterious.
  - 他失去了所有梦想(他失去了所有夢想)，一切从头开始(一切從頭開始)。
    - tā shīqù le suǒyǒu mèngxiǎng, yīqiè cóngtóu kāishǐ
    - He lost all his dreams, and everything starts anew.
### 一切(一切) → 一(一)
- **definition:** one; single
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Horizontal stroke ->
    - representing the number 'one'
  - **interpretation:** The character 一(yī, "one") is a simple horizontal line symbolizing singularity or unity, directly capturing the essence of oneness.
- **examples:**
  - 我只要一杯水(我只要一杯水)。
    - Wǒ zhǐyào yī bēi shuǐ.
    - I only need one glass of water.
  - 他一生追求自由(他一生追求自由)，一心为理想(一心為理想)。
    - Tā yīshēng zhuīqiú zìyóu, yīxīn wéi lǐxiǎng.
    - He dedicated his life to pursuing freedom, and wholeheartedly cherished his ideals.
  - 请给我一支笔(請給我一支筆)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī zhī bǐ.
    - Please give me one pen.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切)
- **definition:** all; entire
- **pinyin:** qiè
- **components:**
  - 七
    - qī
    - seven
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 七(七) (qī, "seven") + Semantic: 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") =
    - sound from 七, meaning from 刀 ->
    - cutting
  - **interpretation:** The character 切(切) (qiè, "cut") combines 七(七) (qī, "seven") for its phonetic element with 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") as the semantic component, indicating an action involving a knife, hence the meaning 'cut'.
- **examples:**
  - 一切都是那么美好(一切都是那麼美好)。
    - Yīqiè dōu shì nàme měihǎo.
    - Everything is so beautiful.
  - 面对困难时(面對困難時)，他付出了一切(他付出了一切)。
    - Miànduì kùnnán shí, tā fùchū le yīqiè.
    - When facing difficulties, he gave his all.
  - 在那一刻(在那一刻)，一切仿佛都静止了(一切彷彿都靜止了)。
    - Zài nà yī kè, yīqiè fǎngfú dōu jìngzhǐ le.
    - At that moment, everything seemed to stand still.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切) → 七(七)
- **definition:** seven
- **pinyin:** qī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts seven horizontal strokes cutting a vertical stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 七(qī, "seven") is a pictogram that visually represents the number seven. The horizontal strokes crossing over a vertical stroke resemble the idea of segmentation or division, perhaps reminiscent of tally marks.
- **examples:**
  - 我有七本书(我有七本書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu qī běn shū.
    - I have seven books.
  - 今天有七位客人(今天有七位客人)，需要热情接待(需要熱情接待)。
    - Jīntiān yǒu qī wèi kèrén, xūyào rèqíng jiēdài.
    - There are seven guests today, who need a warm welcome.
### 一切(一切) → 切(切) → 刀(刀)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a knife or blade
  - **interpretation:** 刀(刀) (dāo, 'knife') resembles the shape of a knife with a handle and blade. It is a straightforward representation of the tool.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿着锋利的刀(他拿著鋒利的刀)，准备切肉(準備切肉)。
    - Tā názhe fēnglì de dāo, zhǔnbèi qiē ròu.
    - He holds a sharp knife, ready to cut meat.
  - 厨房里放着一把古老的刀(廚房裡放著一把古老的刀)，看起来非常锋利(看起來非常鋒利)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ fàngzhe yī bǎ gǔlǎo de dāo, kàn qǐlái fēicháng fēnglì.
    - In the kitchen, an old knife is displayed, and it looks very sharp.
