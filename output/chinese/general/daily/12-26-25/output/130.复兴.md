## revive; rejuvenate
### fùxīng
---
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **components:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - return; repeat; again
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - prosper; rise; flourish; thrive
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 复(復) (fù, "return") and 兴(興) (xīng, "prosper; rise") express the concept of bringing something back to a state of prosperity or elevating it once again.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of returning to an original state and rising to prominence conveys the idea of restoring something to vitality and success.
- **examples:**
  - 他致力于复兴传统艺术(他致力於復興傳統藝術)。
    - Tā zhìlì yú fùxīng chuántǒng yìshù.
    - He is dedicated to reviving traditional arts.
  - 国家改革引领未来(國家改革引領未來)，带来经济复兴(帶來經濟復興)。
    - Guójiā gǎigé yǐnlǐng wèilái, dài lái jīngjì fùxīng.
    - National reforms lead the future and bring about economic rejuvenation.
  - 历史见证了复兴的奇迹(歷史見證了復興的奇蹟)。
    - Lìshǐ jiànzhèng le fùxīng de qíjì.
    - History bears witness to the miracle of revival.
### 复兴(復興) → 复(複)
- **definition:** return; repeat; again
- **pinyin:** fù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Room at the center of a corridor + footprint pointing down (夊) ->
    - originally meant 'going back' with a reference to meeting people, evolved to 'return, repeat'
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a corridor represented by the top component with a footprint suggests movement or returning, fitting the character's meaning of 'return' or 'repeat'.
- **examples:**
  - 每当我复习生词时(每當我複習生詞時)，记忆就加深了(記憶就加深了)。
    - Měi dāng wǒ fù xí shēng cí shí, jì yì jiù jiā shēn le.
    - Whenever I review new words, my memory deepens.
  - 音乐会将复演名曲(音樂會將復演名曲)。
    - Yīn yuè huì jiāng fù yǎn míng qǔ.
    - The concert will perform the famous piece again.
  - 受伤后他复出比赛(受傷後他復出比賽)。
    - Shòu shāng hòu tā fù chū bǐ sài.
    - After his injury, he returned to the competition.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興)
- **definition:** prosper; rise; flourish; thrive
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 同
    - tóng
    - "same"
  - 興
    - xīng
    - "lift"
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 同(同) (tóng) component borrowed as phonetic + 興(興) (xīng, 'lift') =
    - lift combined with 同 phonetic ->
    - concept of raising or flourishing
  - **interpretation:** The character incorporates the phonetic 同(同) (tóng, "same") with the action-oriented 興(興) (xīng, "lift"), jointly symbolizing the idea of uplifting or prospering.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by removing the top portion of 興(興), keeping only the phonetic resemblance of its components.
- **examples:**
  - 花园里花儿兴旺(花園裡花兒興旺)。
    - Huāyuán lǐ huār xīngwàng.
    - The flowers in the garden flourish.
  - 经济兴隆(經濟興隆)，人民安居乐业(人民安居樂業)。
    - Jīngjì xīnglóng, rénmín ānjū lèyè.
    - The economy prospers, and the people live in peace and work happily.
  - 古城再现昔日兴盛(古城再現昔日興盛)。
    - Gǔchéng zài xiàn xīrì xīngshèng.
    - The ancient city shows once again its former prosperity.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 同(同)
- **definition:** "same"
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a pipe ->
    - original form of 筒(筒) (tǒng, "pipe") ->
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") added as decoration
  - **interpretation:** The character 同(同) (tóng, "together; same") originally depicted a pipe, with 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") added later for decorative reasons, illustrating the idea of things coming together like a passageway.
- **examples:**
  - 我们同游公园(我們同遊公園)。
    - Wǒmen tóng yóu gōngyuán.
    - We tour the park together.
  - 他们穿着同样的衣服(他們穿著同樣的衣服)，看起来十分相似(看起來十分相似)。
    - Tāmen chuānzhe tóngyàng de yīfú, kàn qǐlái shífēn xiāngsì.
    - They wear the same clothes, and they look very similar.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 興(興)
- **definition:** "lift"
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 舁
    - yú
    - lift
  - 同
    - tóng
    - together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 舁(舁) (yú, "lift") + 同(同) (tóng, "together") =
    - raise together ->
    - "lift"
  - **interpretation:** This character uses 舁(舁) (yú, "lift") for the semantic idea of elevating or raising something, while 同(同) (tóng, "together") provides the phonetic cue. The idea of lifting or raising together contributes to the meaning of "lift."
- **examples:**
  - 他努力兴起那扇沉重的门(他努力興起那扇沉重的門)。
    - Tā xīng qǐ le nà shàn chénzhòng de mén.
    - He lifted that heavy door.
  - 他用手把箱子兴起(他用手把箱子興起)，然后走了(然後走了)。
    - Tā yòng shǒu bǎ xiāngzi xīng qǐ, ránhòu zǒu le.
    - He lifted the box with his hand, then left.
  - 队长激励大家兴起信心(隊長激勵大家興起信心)。
    - Duìzhǎng jīlì dàjiā xīng qǐ xìnxīn.
    - The captain inspired everyone to lift their spirits.
