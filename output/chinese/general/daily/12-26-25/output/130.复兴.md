## revive; rejuvenate
### fùxīng
---
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **components:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - return; repeat
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - rise; prosper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 復(復) (fù, "return; repeat") and 興(興) (xīng, "rise; prosper") together convey the meaning of reviving or rejuvenating something back to its prosperous state.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 復(復) (fù, "return; repeat") and 興(興) (xīng, "rise; prosper") reflects the idea of bringing something back to a state of prosperity or importance, echoing historical efforts to restore cultural or national greatness.
- **examples:**
  - 实现国家复兴需要改革(實現國家復興需要改革)，团结民心共创未来(團結民心共創未來)。
    - Shíxiàn guójiā fùxīng xūyào gǎigé, tuánjié mínxīn gòngchuàng wèilái.
    - Reviving the nation requires reform, uniting the people to create a bright future.
  - 文化复兴激发创新(文化復興激發創新)，吸引世界关注(吸引世界關注)。
    - Wénhuà fùxīng jīfā chuàngxīn, xīyǐn shìjiè guānzhù.
    - Cultural revival stimulates innovation and attracts global attention.
### 复兴(復興) → 复(復)
- **definition:** return; repeat
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - 夊
    - suī
    - footprint pointing down
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Top: A representation of a room in a corridor ->
    - Bottom: 夊 (suī, footprint pointing down) ->
    - Original meaning: going back to meet people in a room ->
    - Modern: return; repeat
  - **interpretation:** The character 复(復) (fù, 'return; repeat') combines a representation of a room in a corridor with 夊(夊) (suī, 'footprint pointing down'), highlighting the meaning of returning or going back, suggesting a movement back to a familiar or designated space.
- **examples:**
  - 请复述你的故事(請復述你的故事)。
    - qǐng fù shù nǐ de gù shì.
    - Please repeat your story.
  - 等你复来时(等你復來時)，我们一起庆祝(我們一起慶祝)。
    - děng nǐ fù lái shí, wǒ men yī qǐ qìng zhù.
    - When you return, we will celebrate together.
  - 复查后(復查後)，他发现问题依旧(他發現問題依舊)。
    - fù chá hòu, tā fā xiàn wèn tí yī jiù.
    - After rechecking, he found the problems unchanged.
### 复兴(復興) → 复(復) → 夊(夊)
- **definition:** footprint pointing down
- **pinyin:** suī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a footprint pointing down
  - **interpretation:** The character 夊(suī, "footprint pointing down") visually depicts the shape of a footprint, indicating an action of stepping or moving downward, contrasting with 止(zhǐ, "stop"), which illustrates a foot moving upwards.
- **examples:**
  - 书法家笔下的夊字传达出足迹向下的意境(書法家筆下的夊字傳達出足跡向下的意境)。
    - Shūfǎjiā bǐxià de suī zì chuándá chū zújì xiàng xià de yìjìng.
    - The calligraphic form of the character suī conveys the imagery of footprints pointing downward.
  - 古代雕刻中常用夊象征行走(古代雕刻中常用夊象徵行走)，展示追寻足迹的智慧(展示追尋足跡的智慧)。
    - Gǔdài diāokè zhōng cháng yòng suī xiàngzhēng xíngzǒu, zhǎnshì zhuīxún zújì de zhìhuì.
    - In ancient carvings, suī is often used to symbolize walking, displaying the wisdom of following one’s trail.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興)
- **definition:** rise; prosper
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 舁
    - yú
    - both hands of two people
  - 同
    - tóng
    - together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 舁(舁) (yú, 'both hands of two people') + 同(同) (tóng, 'together') =
    - two people lifting something together ->
    - rise, prosper
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 舁(舁) (yú, 'both hands') and 同(同) (tóng, 'together') to depict a joint effort, symbolizing the action of rising or prospering through cooperation.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to remove complexity while retaining the original structure: 兴(興) uses fewer strokes for simplicity.
- **examples:**
  - 公司业务日渐兴(公司業務日漸興)，市场环境逐步改善兴(市場環境逐步改善興)。
    - Gōngsī yèwù rìjiàn xīng, shìchǎng huánjìng zhúbù gǎishàn xīng.
    - The company's business is steadily prospering, and the market environment is gradually improving.
  - 改革开放使国家兴(改革開放使國家興)。
    - Gǎigé kāifàng shǐ guójiā xīng.

  - **description:**
