## 
### revive; rejuvenate
---
- **pinyin:** revive; rejuvenate
- **components:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - restore; repeat; return; revive
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - prosper; thrive; rise; flourish
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 复(復) (fù, "restore") + 兴(興) (xīng, "prosper") — restore prosperity
  - **interpretation:** 复兴 combines the ideas of restoring something to its former state and promoting its prosperity. This makes sense as reviving often leads to a renewed flourishing or growth.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要复兴文化(我們需要復興文化)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào fùxīng wénhuà.
    - We need to revive the culture.
  - 这个计划旨在复兴经济(這個計劃旨在復興經濟)。
    - Zhège jìhuà zhǐzài fùxīng jīngjì.
    - This plan aims to rejuvenate the economy.
### 复兴(復興) → 复(復)
- **definition:** restore; repeat; return; revive
- **pinyin:** fù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Depicts a room at the center of a corridor above a footprint pointing down, representing the concept of 'going back' to meet people.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a room suggests returning to a place for gatherings, while the footprint indicates a journey back. This evokes the idea of restoring or reviving connections.
- **examples:**
  - 我希望能复兴这个项目(我希望能復興這個項目)。
    - Wǒ xīwàng néng fùxīng zhège xiàngmù.
    - I hope to revive this project.
  - 每次复习都能帮助我记忆(每次複習都能幫助我記憶)。
    - Měicì fùxí dōu néng bāngzhù wǒ jìyì.
    - Each review helps me remember.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興)
- **definition:** prosper; thrive; rise; flourish
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 舁
    - yú
    - to lift
  - 同
    - tóng
    - together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 舁 (two hands of two people) + 同 (together) — depicts collaboration in lifting or raising something.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the idea of two people working together to lift or raise an object, symbolizing prosperity and growth through cooperation.
- **examples:**
  - 经济兴旺(經濟興旺)，人民富裕(人民富裕)！
    - jīngjì xīngwàng, rénmín fùyù!
    - The economy thrives, and the people are prosperous!
  - 文化兴盛(文化興盛)，艺术蓬勃发展(藝術蓬勃發展)。
    - wénhuà xīngshèng, yìshù péngbó fāzhǎn.
    - Culture flourishes, and art develops vigorously.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 舁(舁)
- **definition:** to lift
- **pinyin:** yú
- **components:**
  - 舟
    - zhōu
    - boat
  - 余
    - yú
    - surplus
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic element 舟 (zhōu, 'boat') suggesting a relationship to transportation, combined with the phonetic element 余 (yú) which suggests the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 舟 (zhōu, 'boat') implies lifting or carrying something on water, thus relating to the action of lifting.
- **examples:**
  - 古代人们舁着大米(古代人們舁著大米)，向市场走去(向市場走去)！
    - Gǔdàirénmen yúzhe dà mǐ, xiàng shìchǎng zǒuqù！
    - Ancient people lifted rice, heading towards the market!
  - 舁水的家伙真不简单(舁水的家伙真不簡單)。
    - Yú shuǐ de jiāhuo zhēn bù jiǎndān.
    - Lifting water is no easy task.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 舁(舁) → 舟(舟)
- **definition:** boat
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a boat seen from the top view, showing the hull shape.
  - **interpretation:** The simple representation of a boat gives an immediate sense of travel or transportation across water.
- **examples:**
  - 我们坐舟去旅行(我們坐舟去旅行)！
    - Wǒmen zuò zhōu qù lǚxíng！
    - We will take a boat for our trip!
  - 这里有很多舟(這裡有很多舟)。
    - Zhèlǐ yǒu hěn duō zhōu.
    - There are many boats here.
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 舁(舁) → 余(余)
- **definition:** surplus
- **pinyin:** yú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 余 represents the notion of surplus or excess, possibly picturing the idea of having more than what is needed.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively conveys the idea of having something left over or in excess, reflecting the abundance or surplus concept.
- **examples:**
  - 我的余钱不多(我的餘錢不多)，但足够用(但足夠用)。
  - 我们还有余粮(我們還有餘糧)，可以再待几天(可以再待幾天)。
  - 这是他的余生(這是他的餘生)，希望能过得快乐(希望能過得快樂)。
### 复兴(復興) → 兴(興) → 同(同)
- **definition:** together
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character represents the concept of sameness or being together. It visually combines elements suggesting unity and similarity.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively depicts the idea of unity or togetherness, symbolizing things that are similar or common.
- **examples:**
  - 我们是同学(我們是同學)。
    - Wǒmen shì tóngxué.
    - We are classmates.
  - 同样的情况(同樣的情況)。
    - Tóngyàng de qíngkuàng.
    - The same situation.
