## revive; rejuvenate
### fùxīng
---
- **traditional:** 復興
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **components:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - return; repeat
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - rise; prosper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 復 (fù, "return; repeat") and 興 (xīng, "rise; prosper") together convey the meaning of reviving or rejuvenating something back to its prosperous state.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 復 (fù, "return; repeat") and 興 (xīng, "rise; prosper") reflects the idea of bringing something back to a state of prosperity or importance, echoing historical efforts to restore cultural or national greatness.
- **examples:**
  - 实现国(實現國)家复兴(復興)需要改革，团结(團結)民心共创(創)未来(來)。
    - Shíxiàn guójiā fùxīng xūyào gǎigé, tuánjié mínxīn gòngchuàng wèilái.
    - Reviving the nation requires reform, uniting the people to create a bright future.
  - 文化复兴(復興)激发创(發創)新，吸引世界关(關)注。
    - Wénhuà fùxīng jīfā chuàngxīn, xīyǐn shìjiè guānzhù.
    - Cultural revival stimulates innovation and attracts global attention.
