## 小妹妹(小妹妹)
---
## 
### little sister; young girl
---
- **pinyin:** little sister; young girl
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little; young; minor
  - 妹妹
    - mèimei
    - younger sister; girl; younger female friend; little sister
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") + 妹妹(妹妹) (mèimei, "younger sister") — small younger sister
  - **interpretation:** This term refers affectionately to a young girl or little sister. The combination suggests a smaller or younger female sibling, indicating familiarity and warmth.
- **examples:**
  - 小妹妹很可爱(小妹妹很可愛)，总是喜欢笑(總是喜歡笑)。
    - xiǎo mèimei hěn kě'ài, zǒng shì xǐhuān xiào.
    - The little sister is very cute and always likes to smile.
  - 小妹妹的生日快到了(小妹妹的生日快到了)，我们要准备礼物(我們要準備禮物)。
    - xiǎo mèimei de shēngrì kuài dào le, wǒmen yào zhǔnbèi lǐwù.
    - The little sister's birthday is coming soon, and we need to prepare gifts.
### 小妹妹(小妹妹) → 小(小)
- **definition:** small; little; young; minor
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a small or small-scale object.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents something that is small or compact in size, resembling a dot or a short stroke.
- **examples:**
  - 这个房间很小(這個房間很小)。
    - Zhège fángjiān hěn xiǎo。
    - This room is very small.
  - 我有一个小猫(我有一個小貓)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè xiǎo māo。
    - I have a small cat.
### 小妹妹(小妹妹) → 妹妹(妹妹)
- **definition:** younger sister; girl; younger female friend; little sister
- **pinyin:** mèimei
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") + 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") — younger sister
  - **interpretation:** The word 妹妹 denotes a young female sibling or friend, doubling the character 妹 emphasizes the affectionate and familiar nature of the relationship.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个妹妹。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè mèimei.
    - I have a younger sister.
  - 妹妹很可爱。
    - Mèimei hěn kě'ài.
    - The younger sister is very cute.
  - 我们可以一起去玩，妹妹。
    - Wǒmen kěyǐ yīqǐ qù wán, mèimei.
    - We can go play together, little sister.
### 小妹妹(小妹妹) → 妹妹(妹妹) → 妹(妹)
- **definition:** younger sister; younger female friend; little sister
- **pinyin:** mèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a woman (女) who is younger or smaller, suggesting the idea of a younger sister.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the radical for 'woman' with concepts of youth, intuitively representing a younger female sibling.
- **examples:**
  - 她是我的妹妹(她是我的妹妹)。
    - Tā shì wǒ de mèimei.
    - She is my younger sister.
  - 我有两个妹妹(我有兩個妹妹)。
    - Wǒ yǒu liǎng gè mèimei.
    - I have two younger sisters.
