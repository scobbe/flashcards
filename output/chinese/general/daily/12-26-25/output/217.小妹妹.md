## little sister; young girl
### xiǎomèimei
---
- **definition:** little sister; young girl
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèimei
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little
  - 妹妹
    - mèimei
    - younger sister; young girl
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") denotes something small or little; 妹妹(妹妹) (mèimei, "younger sister") refers to a younger sister or a young girl. Together, 小妹妹 (xiǎomèimei) can mean 'little sister' or 'young girl', indicating youth or affection.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 小(小) (xiǎo, "small") and 妹妹(妹妹) (mèimei, "younger sister") creates a term of endearment or familiarity, often used to refer to a young girl or to address a younger sister.
- **examples:**
  - 我的小妹妹很可爱(我的小妹妹很可愛)。
    - Wǒ de xiǎomèimei hěn kě'ài.
    - My little sister is very cute.
  - 小妹妹正在学习(小妹妹正在學習)，期待表现出色(期待表現出色)。
    - Xiǎomèimei zhèngzài xuéxí, qīdài biǎoxiàn chūsè.
    - The little girl is studying and is eager to perform excellently.
  - 在游乐场遇见了小妹妹(在遊樂場遇見了小妹妹)，大家都很开心(大家都很開心)。
    - Zài yóulèchǎng yùjiànle xiǎomèimei, dàjiā dōu hěn kāixīn.
    - We met the little girl at the amusement park, and everyone was very happy.

### 小妹妹(小妹妹) → 妹妹(妹妹)
- **definition:** younger sister; young girl
- **pinyin:** mèimei
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") + 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") =
    - repetition for emphasis or endearment, referring collectively to younger sisters or a young girl
  - **interpretation:** 妹妹 (mèimei) combines 妹(妹) (mèi, "younger sister") twice to emphasize the idea of a younger female sibling, often used affectionately. The repetition not only reinforces the familial relationship but can also imply endearment or a familial role, similar to saying 'little sister' or affectionately addressing a young girl.
- **examples:**
  - 我的妹妹很可爱(我的妹妹很可愛)。
    - Wǒ de mèimei hěn kě'ài.
    - My younger sister is very cute.
  - 我陪妹妹玩耍(我陪妹妹玩耍)，她笑得很开心(她笑得很開心)。
    - Wǒ péi mèimei wánshuǎ, tā xiào de hěn kāixīn.
    - I play with my younger sister, and she laughs happily.
  - 妹妹在花园里采花(妹妹在花園裡採花)。
    - Mèimei zài huāyuán lǐ cǎihuā.
    - My younger sister picks flowers in the garden.
