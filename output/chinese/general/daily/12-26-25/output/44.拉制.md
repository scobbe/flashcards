## metal drawing process
### lāzhì
---
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **components:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; draw out
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make; manufacture; control; system
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") + 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") comes together to refer to the action of pulling or drawing a material, specifically in a manufacturing context.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") and 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") reflects a process in manufacturing where metal is drawn out through pulling, a common technique in the fabrication of metal products.
- **examples:**
  - 现代工厂运用拉制技术(現代工廠運用拉製技術)，制造精密金属线材(製造精密金屬線材)。
    - Xiàndài gōngchǎng yùnyòng lāzhì jìshù, zhìzào jīngmì jīnshǔ xiàncái。
    - Modern factories use the metal drawing process to manufacture precision metal wires.
  - 借助拉制工艺(借助拉製工藝)，金属板材变得更加坚韧(金屬板材變得更加堅韌)。
    - Jièzhù lāzhì gōngyì, jīnshǔ bǎncái biàn de gèngjiā jiānrèn。
    - With the help of the metal drawing process, metal plates become even tougher.
### 拉制(拉製) → 拉(拉)
- **definition:** pull; drag; draw out
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - hand 手(手) (shǒu, 'hand') + phonetic 立(立) (lì, 'stand') =
    - action performed with the hand, using the sound of 立(立) to signify 'pull'
  - **interpretation:** The character 拉(拉) (lā, 'pull') combines 手(手) (shǒu, 'hand') to indicate an action involving manipulation with a hand, while 立(立) (lì, 'stand') provides the phonetic pronunciation. The character effectively conveys the idea of using one's hand to 'pull' or 'drag' something.
- **examples:**
  - 他用力拉门(他用力拉門)。
    - Tā yònglì lā mén.
    - He pulls the door with force.
  - 她拉开窗帘(她拉開窗簾)，让阳光进来(讓陽光進來)。
    - Tā lā kāi chuānglián, ràng yángguāng jìn lái.
    - She pulls open the curtains to let the sunlight in.
  - 那只狗拉着绳子(那只狗拉著繩子)，追赶着主人(追趕著主人)。
    - Nà zhī gǒu lāzhe shéngzi, zhuīgǎn zhe zhǔrén.
    - The dog pulls its leash while chasing its owner.

  - **interpretation:** The character 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") is a direct pictorial representation of a human hand with all five fingers, which distinguishes it from similar characters representing claws or simplified fingers. This visually conveys its meaning as a hand clearly.
- **examples:**

    - Tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.

### 拉制(拉製) → 制(製)
- **definition:** make; manufacture; control; system
- **pinyin:** zhì
- **components:**
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 制(制) (zhì, 'to cut out; to make') + Semantic: 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes') =
    - making clothes ->
    - manufacture/control
  - **interpretation:** The character 制(製) combines the idea of 制(制) (zhì, 'to cut out; to make'), which indicates an action, with 衣(衣) (yī, 'clothes'), representing the product of that action. This suggests an act of crafting or creating clothing, which extends metaphorically to making or controlling systems and processes.
- **examples:**
  - 工厂制鞋(工廠製鞋)。
    - Gōngchǎng zhì xié.
    - The factory manufactures shoes.
  - 他们制订计划(他們製訂計劃)，确保生产顺利(確保生產順利)。
    - Tāmen zhìdìng jìhuà, quèbǎo shēngchǎn shùnlì.
    - They formulate plans to ensure smooth production.
  - 研究人员制备新材料(研究人員製備新材料)，推动科技进步(推動科技進步)。
    - Yánjiū rényuán zhìbèi xīn cáiliào, tuīdòng kējì jìnbù.
    - Researchers prepare new materials, driving technological progress.
