## metal drawing process
### lāzhì
---
- **traditional:** 拉製
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **components:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; draw out
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make; manufacture; control; system
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 拉 (lā, "pull") + 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") comes together to refer to the action of pulling or drawing a material, specifically in a manufacturing context.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 拉 (lā, "pull") and 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") reflects a process in manufacturing where metal is drawn out through pulling, a common technique in the fabrication of metal products.
- **examples:**
  - 现(現)代( )工( )厂(廠)运(運)用( )拉( )制(製)技( )术(術)，制(製)造( )精( )密( )金( )属(屬)线(線)材( )。
    - Xiàndài gōngchǎng yùnyòng lāzhì jìshù, zhìzào jīngmì jīnshǔ xiàncái。
    - Modern factories use the metal drawing process to manufacture precision metal wires.
  - 借( )助( )拉( )制(製)工( )艺(藝)，金( )属(屬)板( )材( )变(變)得( )更( )加( )坚(堅)韧(韌)。
    - Jièzhù lāzhì gōngyì, jīnshǔ bǎncái biàn de gèngjiā jiānrèn。
    - With the help of the metal drawing process, metal plates become even tougher.
