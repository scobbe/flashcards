## metal drawing process
### lāzhì
---
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **components:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make; system; manufacture
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") + 製(製) (zhì, "make; system; manufacture") =
    - pulling to form a product ->
    - metal drawing process
  - **interpretation:** The term 拉製(拉製) (lāzhì) combines the idea of 'pulling' or 'drawing' and 'manufacturing' to describe the process of drawing or pulling metal into a new shape or form.
- **examples:**
  - 现代工厂采用拉制(現代工廠採用拉製)，制造精密金属板(製造精密金屬板)。
    - xiàndài gōngchǎng cǎiyòng lāzhì, zhìzào jīngmì jīnshǔ bǎn.
    - Modern factories adopt the metal drawing process to manufacture precise metal sheets.
  - 这种金属通过拉制(這種金屬通過拉製)，获得高强度(獲得高強度)。
    - zhè zhǒng jīnshǔ tōngguò lāzhì, huòdé gāo qiángdù.
    - This metal attains high strength through the metal drawing process.
### 拉制(拉製) → 拉(拉)
- **definition:** pull
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 立(立) (lì, "stand") =
    - action involving hands ->
    - pull
  - **interpretation:** The character 拉(lā, "pull") combines 手(shǒu, "hand") indicating an action involving hands, with 立(lì, "stand"), which provides the pronunciation. Together, they represent the action of pulling.
- **examples:**
  - 孩子拉开窗帘(孩子拉開窗簾)。
    - Háizi lā kāi chuāng lián.
    - The child pulls open the curtain.
  - 他拉住绳子(他拉住繩子)，把风筝拉上高空(把風箏拉上高空)。
    - Tā lā zhù shéng zi, bǎ fēng zhēng lā shàng gāo kōng.
    - He holds the rope and pulls the kite up into the sky.

### 拉制(拉製) → 制(製)
- **definition:** make; system; manufacture
- **pinyin:** zhì
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, "tree") + 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") =
    - to cut a tree ->
    - 制(製)
  - **interpretation:** The character 制(製) (zhì, "make") visually combines 木(木) (mù, "tree") and 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife"), suggesting the action of cutting or shaping wood with a knife, which relates to crafting or creating something systematically.
  - **simplification:** None, as 制 is the simplified form used for both meanings. The character remains similar with slight smoothing for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 他制作了一幅名画(他製作了一幅名畫)。
    - Tā zhìzuò le yī fú mínghuà.
    - He made a famous painting.
  - 政府制定方案(政府製定方案)，工厂制造产品(工廠製造產品)。
    - Zhèngfǔ zhìdìng fāng'àn, gōngchǎng zhìzào chǎnpǐn.
    - The government formulated a plan, and the factory manufactured products.
  - 古代工匠精心制造兵器(古代工匠精心製造兵器)，传承独特技艺(傳承獨特技藝)。
    - Gǔdài gōngjiàng jīngxīn zhìzào bīngqì, chuánchéng dútè jìyì.
    - Ancient craftsmen meticulously made weapons, passing down unique skills.
