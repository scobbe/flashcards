### metal drawing process
---
- **pinyin:** metal drawing process
- **components:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; attract; stretch
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make; manufacture; system; control
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 拉(拉) (lā, "pull") + 制(製) (zhì, "make; manufacture") — pull making
  - **interpretation:** 拉制 combines the concepts of pulling and manufacturing, which accurately describes the process of drawing materials into desired shapes. In this context, it refers to the metal drawing process, crucial in various manufacturing industries.
- **examples:**
  - 金属拉制是一种重要的制造工艺。(金屬拉制是一種重要的製造工藝。)
    - jīnshǔ lāzhì shì yī zhǒng zhòngyào de zhìzào gōngyì.
    - Metal drawing is an important manufacturing process.
  - 在车间中应用拉制技术。(在車間中應用拉制技術。)
    - zài chējiān zhōng yìngyòng lāzhì jìshù.
    - The drawing technique is applied in the workshop.
### 拉制(拉制) → 拉(拉)
- **definition:** pull; drag; attract; stretch
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 手 (手) (shǒu, 'hand') to signify action done by hand and the phonetic component 立 (立) (lì, 'stand') for pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a hand and the sound component suggests the action of pulling or dragging something with the hands, making the meaning intuitive.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手拉门(他用手拉門)。
    - Tā yòng shǒu lā mén.
    - He pulls the door with his hand.
  - 请帮我拉一下(請幫我拉一下)。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ lā yīxià.
    - Please help me pull it a bit.
### 拉制(拉制) → 拉(拉) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human hand with fingers; the top stroke represents the bent middle finger, while the horizontal strokes signify two other fingers.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles a hand, emphasizing the detail of five fingers, which distinguishes it from other characters associated with claws.
- **examples:**
  - 我用手写字(我用手寫字)。
    - Wǒ yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - I use my hand to write.
  - 请把手洗干净(請把手洗乾淨)。
    - Qǐng bǎ shǒu xǐ gānjìng.
    - Please wash your hands clean.

### 拉制(拉制) → 制(製)
- **definition:** make; manufacture; system; control
- **pinyin:** zhì
- **components:**
  - 造
    - zào
    - to make
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component 造 (zào, 'to make') and the phonetic component 制 (zhì, 'to control').
  - **interpretation:** The idea of making (造) something is combined with the concept of control (制), which reflects the process of crafting and managing the conditions of production.
  - **simplification:** The character '製' was simplified by reducing the strokes in the component '衣' (clothing) to '衤', making it easier to write. The replacement keeps the original meaning while utilizing a more streamlined form.
- **examples:**
  - 我会制作手工艺品(我會製作手工藝品)。
    - Wǒ huì zhìzuò shǒugōngyìpǐn.
    - I can make handicrafts.
  - 这款家具是由当地工厂制造的(這款家具是由當地工廠製造的)。
    - Zhè kuǎn jiājù shì yóu dāngdì gōngchǎng zhìzàode.
    - This furniture is manufactured by a local factory.
