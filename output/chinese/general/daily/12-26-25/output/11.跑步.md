### run; jog
---
- **pinyin:** run; jog
- **components:**
  - 跑
    - pǎo
    - run; escape; run away; gallop
  - 步
    - bù
    - step; walk; pace; footstep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 跑(跑) (pǎo, 'run') + 步(步) (bù, 'step') — run step
  - **interpretation:** The term describes the action of running, emphasizing the action of taking steps. Running involves a rapid series of steps, making this combination logical.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都去跑步(我每天都去跑步)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dōu qù pǎobù.
    - I go running every day.
  - 他喜欢早晨跑步(他喜歡早晨跑步)。
    - Tā xǐhuān zǎochén pǎobù.
    - He enjoys jogging in the morning.
