## run; jog
### pǎobù
---
- **definition:** run; jog
- **pinyin:** pǎobù
- **components:**
  - 跑
    - pǎo
    - run; jog
  - 步
    - bù
    - step; pace; walk; march
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 跑步 (跑步) (pǎobù, 'run; jog') combines the act of moving quickly with steps.
  - **interpretation:** It's a combination of running and stepping, highlighting swift movement using footsteps.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天跑步(我每天跑步)。
    - Wǒ měitiān pǎobù.
    - I run every day.
  - 清晨微风中(清晨微風中)，他跑步锻炼身体(他跑步鍛鍊身體)。
    - Qīngchén wéifēng zhōng, tā pǎobù duànliàn shēntǐ.
    - In the early morning breeze, he runs to exercise his body.
  - 他喜欢在公园跑步(他喜歡在公園跑步)，享受自然(享受自然)。
    - Tā xǐhuān zài gōngyuán pǎobù, xiǎngshòu zìrán.
    - He likes to run in the park and enjoy nature.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 足(足) (zú, 'foot') + 包(包) (bāo, 'wrap') =

### 跑步(跑步) → 步(步)

- **pinyin:** bù
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
  - 少
    - shǎo
    - few
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") + 少(少) (shǎo, "few") =
    - pace or step ->
    - final meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 步(bù, "step") cleverly combines the images of a foot and a few, suggesting the action of taking steps. The phono-semantic structure highlights both the concept of walking and the sound.

- **examples:**

  - 队伍整齐地迈步(隊伍整齊地邁步)，向前推进(向前推進)。
    - Duìwǔ zhěngqí de mài bù, xiàng qián tuījìn.

### 跑步(跑步) → 步(步) → 少(少)
- **definition:** few
- **pinyin:** shǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts four dots, perhaps three grains of sand, to indicate 'a few, a little' ->
    - phonetically borrowed from 小 (小) (xiǎo, 'small').
  - **interpretation:** 少(少) (shǎo, 'few') originally depicted a few grains of sand, showing quantity, with a later phonetic borrowing from 小(小) (xiǎo, 'small') due to its semantic similarity.
  - **simplification:** None, traditional is the same as simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 这个城市的人少(這個城市的人少)。
    - Zhège chéngshì de rén shǎo.
    - There are few people in this city.
  - 他的时间少(他的時間少)，所以他工作效率低(所以他工作效率低)。
    - Tā de shíjiān shǎo, suǒyǐ tā gōngzuò xiàolǜ dī.
    - He has little time, so his work efficiency is low.
  - 他的经验少(他的經驗少)，因此他成长缓慢(因此他成長緩慢)。
    - Tā de jīngyàn shǎo, yīncǐ tā chéngzhǎng huǎnmàn.
    - He has little experience, therefore he grows slowly.
