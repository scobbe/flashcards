## run; jog
### pǎobù
---
- **definition:** run; jog
- **pinyin:** pǎobù
- **components:**
  - 跑
    - pǎo
    - run; escape
  - 步
    - bù
    - step; pace
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 跑(跑) (pǎo, "run") + 步(步) (bù, "step") is a combination that describes the action of taking running steps, thus forming the concept of running or jogging.
  - **interpretation:** By combining the action of running with steps, 跑步(pǎobù) captures the essence of moving rapidly on foot and is commonly used to describe the activity of jogging or running.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天跑步(我每天跑步)。
    - Wǒ měitiān pǎobù.
    - I run every day.
  - 他爱运动(他愛運動)，尤其喜欢跑步(尤其喜歡跑步)。
    - Tā ài yùndòng, yóuqí xǐhuān pǎobù.
    - He loves sports, especially running.
  - 春天到了(春天到了)，我去公园跑步(我去公園跑步)。
    - Chūntiān dàole, wǒ qù gōngyuán pǎobù.
    - Spring has arrived, and I go running in the park.
