## poetic name for Xiang River region
### Xiāoxiāng
---
- **traditional:** 瀟湘
- **definition:** poetic name for Xiang River region
- **pinyin:** Xiāoxiāng
- **components:**
  - 瀟
    - xiāo
    - clear
  - 湘
    - xiāng
    - Xiang River
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 瀟 (xiāo, "clear") refers to the clear water or surroundings; 湘 (xiāng, "Xiang River") is the name of a well-known river in Hunan province.
  - **interpretation:** The term 瀟湘 (xiāoxiāng) evokes the natural beauty and clear waters of the Xiang River region, a place historically revered in Chinese poetry and known for inspiring many literary works.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜爱潇(瀟)湘，那里(裡)的山水充满诗(滿詩)意。
    - Wǒ xǐ'ài Xiāoxiāng(瀟湘)，nàlǐ de shānshuǐ chōngmǎn shīyì(那裡的山水充滿詩意)。
    - I cherish Xiāoxiāng, where the landscapes brim with poetic charm.
  - 雨后潇(瀟)湘，雾气缭绕(霧氣繚繞)。
    - Yǔhòu Xiāoxiāng(瀟湘)，wùqì liǎorào(霧氣繚繞)。
    - After the rain, Xiāoxiāng is enveloped in swirling mist.
  - 潇(瀟)湘水畔寻梦(尋夢)。
    - Xiāoxiāng shuǐpàn xún mèng(瀟湘水畔尋夢)。
    - Dreams are sought along the riverside of Xiāoxiāng.
### 潇(瀟)湘( ) → 瀟
- **definition:** clear
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 蕭
    - xiāo
    - desolate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 水 (shuǐ, "water") + Phonetic: 蕭 (xiāo, "desolate") =
    - clear water ->
    - clear
  - **interpretation:** The character 瀟(xiāo, "clear") combines the water radical 水 (shuǐ, "water") indicating relation to fluidity or clarity, with the phonetic component 蕭 (xiāo, "desolate") for its sound. It suggests the idea of water, specifically highlighting its clarity or purity.
  - **simplification:** 潇(瀟): Simplified by modifying the component structure for ease of writing while retaining the phonetic element.
- **examples:**
  - 溪水在阳(陽)光下显(顯)得格外瀟。
    - Xīshuǐ zài yángguāng xià xiǎnde géwài xiāo.
    - The creek appears especially clear in the sunlight.
  - 晨风轻(風輕)拂，湖面显(顯)得瀟。
    - Chénfēng qīngfú, húmiàn xiǎnde xiāo.
    - The morning breeze gently brushes, and the lake appears clear.

    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.
