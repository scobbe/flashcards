### poetic name for Xiang River region
---
- **pinyin:** poetic name for Xiang River region
- **components:**
  - 潇(瀟)
    - xiāo
    - drizzle; to flow quietly
  - 湘
    - xiāng
    - Xiang River
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 潇(瀟) (xiāo, "drizzle; to flow quietly") + 湘(湘) (xiāng, "Xiang River") — drizzle Xiang River
  - **interpretation:** This word combines the images of soft rain with the famous Xiang River, evoking a sense of tranquility and beauty in the natural scenery. As a poetic name, it reflects the romanticized view of this region in literature.
- **examples:**
  - 潇湘的雨很有诗意(瀟湘的雨很有詩意)。
    - xiāo xiāng de yǔ hěn yǒu shī yì。
    - The rain in the Xiang River region is very poetic.
  - 潇湘夜雨让人思绪万千(瀟湘夜雨讓人思緒萬千)。
    - xiāo xiāng yè yǔ ràng rén sī xù wàn qiān。
    - The night rain in Xiang River brings countless thoughts.

    - Hú shuǐ xiāo xiāo, lìng rén fàngsōng.
    - The lake water flows quietly, making one feel relaxed.
