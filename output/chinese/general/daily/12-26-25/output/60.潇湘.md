## poetic name for Xiang River region
### Xiāoxiāng
---
- **definition:** poetic name for Xiang River region
- **pinyin:** Xiāoxiāng
- **components:**
  - 瀟
    - xiāo
    - clear
  - 湘
    - xiāng
    - Xiang River
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 瀟(瀟) (xiāo, "clear") refers to the clear water or surroundings; 湘(湘) (xiāng, "Xiang River") is the name of a well-known river in Hunan province.
  - **interpretation:** The term 瀟湘(瀟湘) (xiāoxiāng) evokes the natural beauty and clear waters of the Xiang River region, a place historically revered in Chinese poetry and known for inspiring many literary works.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜爱潇湘(瀟湘)，那里的山水充满诗意(那裡的山水充滿詩意)。
    - Wǒ xǐ'ài Xiāoxiāng(瀟湘)，nàlǐ de shānshuǐ chōngmǎn shīyì(那裡的山水充滿詩意)。
    - I cherish Xiāoxiāng, where the landscapes brim with poetic charm.
  - 雨后潇湘(瀟湘)，雾气缭绕(霧氣繚繞)。
    - Yǔhòu Xiāoxiāng(瀟湘)，wùqì liǎorào(霧氣繚繞)。
    - After the rain, Xiāoxiāng is enveloped in swirling mist.
  - 潇湘水畔寻梦(瀟湘水畔尋夢)。
    - Xiāoxiāng shuǐpàn xún mèng(瀟湘水畔尋夢)。
    - Dreams are sought along the riverside of Xiāoxiāng.
