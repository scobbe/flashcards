## decluttering; minimalism
### duànshělí
---
- **definition:** decluttering; minimalism
- **pinyin:** duànshělí
- **components:**
  - 断(斷)
    - duàn
    - sever
  - 舍(捨)
    - shě
    - give up
  - 离(離)
    - lí
    - separate; leave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 斷(斷) (duàn, "sever"), 捨(捨) (shě, "give up"), 離(離) (lí, "separate")
  - **interpretation:** The word 断舍离(斷捨離) (duànshělí) describes the philosophy and practice of decluttering and minimalism by severing one's attachment to unnecessary material possessions, giving them up, and separating from them. This approach is about creating a more simple and meaningful lifestyle.
- **examples:**
  - 我实践断舍离(我實踐斷捨離)。
    - Wǒ shíjiàn duàn shě lí.
    - I practice decluttering.
  - 生活需要断舍离(生活需要斷捨離)，心灵更清明(心靈更清明)。
    - Shēnghuó xūyào duàn shě lí, xīnlíng gèng qīngmíng.
    - Life requires decluttering, and the soul becomes clearer.
  - 断舍离帮助我找到了内心的平静(斷捨離幫助我找到了內心的平靜)。
    - Duàn shě lí bāngzhù wǒ zhǎodào le nèixīn de píngjìng.
    - Decluttering helps me find inner peace.

  - **description:**
