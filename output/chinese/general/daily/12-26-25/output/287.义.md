### surname Yi; righteousness
---
- **pinyin:** surname Yi; righteousness
- **components:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 我 (wǒ, 'I') combined with semantic 羊 (yáng, 'sheep') that signifies 'good; beautiful; auspicious'.
  - **interpretation:** The righteous qualities implied by 羊 suggest virtue and beauty, while 我 provides the phonetic sound, tying together a meaning of good character or justice.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved stroke reduction by removing one horizontal stroke from the character to form 义.
- **examples:**
  - 他很有义气(他很有義氣)，总是帮助朋友(總是幫助朋友)。
    - Tā hěn yǒu yìqì, zǒng shì bāngzhù péngyǒu.
    - He is very righteous, always helping his friends.
  - 义务教育是国家的基本制度(義務教育是國家的基本制度)。
    - Yìwù jiàoyù shì guójiā de jīběn zhìdù.
    - Compulsory education is the basic system of the country.
### 义(義) → 我(我)
- **definition:** I
- **pinyin:** wǒ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a trident-like weapon or tool, representing the idea of a protective instrument.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a personal connection, as it symbolizes a form of defense or assertiveness that relates to one's self-identity.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢这本书(我喜歡這本書)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhè běn shū.
    - I like this book.
  - 我有一个梦想(我有一個夢想)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yīgè mèngxiǎng.
    - I have a dream.
### 义(義) → 羊(羊)
- **definition:** sheep
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a sheep, which is easily recognizable with its distinct shape.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually resembles a sheep, evoking its form and features.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢羊。(我喜歡羊。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yáng.
    - I like sheep.
  - 羊肉很好吃。(羊肉很好吃。)
    - Yángròu hěn hǎo chī.
    - Mutton is very delicious.
