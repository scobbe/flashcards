## surname Yi; righteousness
### Yì
---
- **definition:** surname Yi; righteousness
- **pinyin:** Yì
- **components:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - good; beautiful; auspicious
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 我(我) (wǒ, "I") + Semantic: 羊(羊) (yáng, "good; beautiful; auspicious") =
    - symbolizing something admirable ->
    - righteous, justice
  - **interpretation:** The character 义(義) (yì, "righteous; justice") combines 我(我) (wǒ, "I") as the phonetic element, suggesting its pronunciation, with 羊(羊) (yáng) which signifies values like goodness and beauty, reinforcing its association with moral righteousness.
  - **simplification:** The character 义(義) was simplified by removing the complexity related to 羊(羊) (yáng) and reducing the strokes of 我(我) (wǒ), creating a simpler, more cursive form.
- **examples:**
  - 他追求正义(他追求正義)。
    - Tā zhuīqiú zhèngyì.
    - He pursues righteousness.
  - 面对邪恶他坚守正义(面對邪惡他堅守正義)，以义服人(以義服人)。
    - Miànduì xié'è tā jiānshǒu zhèngyì, yǐ yì fú rén.
    - In the face of evil, he upholds justice and wins people over with righteousness.
  - 正义必将战胜邪恶(正義必將戰勝邪惡)。
    - Zhèngyì bì jiāng zhànshèng xié'è.
    - Righteousness will eventually overcome evil.
### 义(義) → 我(我)
- **definition:** I
- **pinyin:** wǒ
- **components:**
  - 戈
    - gē
    - dagger-axe
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram 戈(戈) (gē, "dagger-axe") + phonetic 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") =
    - dagger-axe in hand ->
    - first person pronoun
  - **interpretation:** The character 我(水) (wǒ, "I") combines 戈(戈) (gē, "dagger-axe") and 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") picturing a person holding a weapon, symbolizing self-defense or self-autonomy, thus extended to mean "I".
- **examples:**
  - 我爱看电影(我愛看電影)。
    - Wǒ ài kàn diànyǐng.
    - I love watching movies.
  - 我今天早起锻炼(我今天早起鍛鍊)，然后去市场买菜(然後去市場買菜)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān zǎoqǐ duànliàn, ránhòu qù shìchǎng mǎicài.
    - I woke up early today to exercise, and then went to the market to buy groceries.
  - 我喜欢安静的环境(我喜歡安靜的環境)，也享受与朋友聊天(也享受與朋友聊天)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān ānjìng de huánjìng, yě xiǎngshòu yǔ péngyǒu liáotiān.
    - I like a quiet environment and also enjoy chatting with friends.
### 义(義) → 我(我) → 戈(戈)
- **definition:** dagger-axe
- **pinyin:** gē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a dagger-axe weapon
  - **interpretation:** The character 戈(gē, "dagger-axe") visually resembles a simple depiction of an ancient weapon used in Chinese warfare. It illustrates the basic structure of a pole with a blade attached, which was typical for the weapon.
- **examples:**
  - 戈是一种古老的兵器(戈是一種古老的兵器)。
    - gē shì yī zhǒng gǔlǎo de bīngqì.
    - The dagger-axe is an ancient weapon.
  - 古人使用戈战斗(古人使用戈戰鬥)，赢得了许多胜利(贏得了許多勝利)。
    - gǔrén shǐyòng gē zhàndòu, yíngdé le xǔduō shènglì.
    - The ancients used dagger-axes to fight, winning many victories.
### 义(義) → 我(我) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand
  - **interpretation:** 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") is a pictogram that visually represents a human hand, which aligns directly with its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他的手很灵巧(他的手很靈巧)。
    - tā de shǒu hěn língqiǎo。
    - His hand is very dexterous.
  - 他伸出手(他伸出手)，示意停下(示意停下)。
    - tā shēn chū shǒu, shì yì tíng xià。
    - He extended his hand, signaling to stop.
  - 小朋友握紧手(小朋友握緊手)，准备接住飞来的球(準備接住飛來的球)。
    - xiǎopéngyǒu wò jǐn shǒu, zhǔnbèi jiē zhù fēi lái de qiú。
    - The child clenched his hand, ready to catch the incoming ball.
### 义(義) → 羊(羊)
- **definition:** good; beautiful; auspicious
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a sheep
  - **interpretation:** The character 羊(羊) (yáng, 'sheep') visually represents the head and horns of a sheep, illustrating its distinct form. This makes it immediately recognizable as symbolizing a sheep.
- **examples:**
  - 羊是一种温顺的动物(羊是一種溫順的動物)。
    - yáng shì yī zhǒng wēnshùn de dòngwù。
    - Sheep are gentle animals.
  - 农夫养羊(農夫養羊)，生产羊毛(生產羊毛)。
    - nóngfū yǎng yáng，shēngchǎn yángmáo。
    - The farmer raises sheep, producing wool.
  - 羊群在草原上悠闲地游荡(羊群在草原上悠閒地遊蕩)。
    - yáng qún zài cǎoyuán shàng yōuxián de yóudàng。
    - The flock of sheep roams leisurely on the grassland.
