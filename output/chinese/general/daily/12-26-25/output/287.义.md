## surname Yi; righteousness
### Yì
---
- **definition:** surname Yi; righteousness
- **pinyin:** Yì
- **components:**
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to govern/cut
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern/cut") was originally used for its sound ->
    - 刈(刈) (yì, "to cut") displaced 乂(乂) ->
    - a stroke was added on top of 乂(乂) to distinguish it for the meaning of righteousness ->
    - final form 义(義)
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 乂(乂) was used for both its phonetic and semantic meanings but was mainly displaced by 刈(刈). To distinguish the idea of "righteousness," a stroke was added, creating a differentiation that allowed the character to develop its own unique meaning related to justice and propriety.
- **examples:**
  - 他行侠仗义(他行俠仗義)，扶危济困(扶危濟困)。
    - Tā xíng xiá zhàng yì, fú wēi jì kùn.
    - He upholds chivalry and helps those in distress.
  - 正义必胜(正義必勝)。
    - Zhèng yì bì shèng.
    - Justice will surely prevail.
  - 他始终坚守义(他始終堅守義)，维护公正(維護公正)。
    - Tā shǐ zhōng jiān shǒu yì, wèi hù gōng zhèng.
    - He always adheres to righteousness and upholds fairness.
### 义(義) → 乂(乂)
- **definition:** to govern/cut
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 刈
    - yì
    - mow/cut weed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tool similar to a knife or possibly a huge pair of scissors
  - **interpretation:** The character 乂 (yì, "to govern/cut") shows a tool, possibly a sickle or large scissors, reflecting its original purpose in cutting or mowing. This meaning influenced its derivative 刈 (yì, "to mow/cut weed").
### 义(義) → 乂(乂) → 刈(刈)
- **definition:** mow/cut weed
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 害
    - hài
    - harm
  - 刂
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 害(害) (hài, "harm") + 刂(刂) (dāo, "knife") =
    - action involving cutting with a knife ->
    - mow
  - **interpretation:** 刈(yì, "mow") combines 害(害) (hài, "harm") as the phonetic component, suggesting the sound, with 刂(刂) (dāo, "knife") which conveys the idea of an action involving cutting or mowing. Despite being a second-round simplified form of 割(割) (gē, "cut"), it retains the essence of cutting with a blade.
  - **simplification:** 刈(刈) is a second-round simplification of 割(割), where sound and component reduction was achieved by directly adopting the existing form, which aligns closely with 割's function and sound.
- **examples:**
  - 农民正在刈草(農民正在刈草)。
    - Nóngmín zhèngzài yì cǎo.
    - The farmer is mowing the grass.
  - 夏天来临时(夏天來臨時)，过长的杂草需要及时刈除(過長的雜草需要及時刈除)。
    - Xiàtiān láilín shí, guòcháng de zácǎo xūyào jíshí yì chú.
    - When summer arrives, overgrown weeds need to be mowed down in time.

### 义(義) → 乂(乂) → 刈(刈) → 害(害) → 宀(宀)
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉字代表家的温暖(含有宀的漢字代表家的溫暖)。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.
    - Chinese characters that include the radical '宀' represent the warmth of home.
  - 屋顶遮风挡雨(屋頂遮風擋雨)，家园散发温馨(家園散發溫馨)。
    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
    - The roof shields from wind and rain, and the home radiates warmth.

### 义(義) → 乂(乂) → 刈(刈) → 刂(刂)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Variant of 刀 ( dāo ).
  - **description:**
    - 刁(刁) (diāo, "clever") is a variant of 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") ->
    - simplification for clarity
  - **interpretation:** The character 刂(dāo) is a simplified variant of 刀(dāo), which is a pictogram depicting a knife. The two-stroke 刂(dāo) is often used as a radical in other characters related to cutting or knives. It maintains the core meaning and functionality of 刀(dāo) in a more streamlined form.
  - **simplification:** 刂(dāo) is a cursive shorthand of 刀(dāo), used as a radical for clarity and conciseness in compound characters.
- **examples:**
