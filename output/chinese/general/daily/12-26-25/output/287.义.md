## surname Yi; righteousness
### Yì
---
- **definition:** surname Yi; righteousness
- **pinyin:** Yì
- **components:**
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to govern/cut
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern/cut") was originally used for its sound ->
    - 刈(刈) (yì, "to cut") displaced 乂(乂) ->
    - a stroke was added on top of 乂(乂) to distinguish it for the meaning of righteousness ->
    - final form 义(義)
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 乂(乂) was used for both its phonetic and semantic meanings but was mainly displaced by 刈(刈). To distinguish the idea of "righteousness," a stroke was added, creating a differentiation that allowed the character to develop its own unique meaning related to justice and propriety.
- **examples:**
  - 他行侠仗义(他行俠仗義)，扶危济困(扶危濟困)。
    - Tā xíng xiá zhàng yì, fú wēi jì kùn.
    - He upholds chivalry and helps those in distress.
  - 正义必胜(正義必勝)。
    - Zhèng yì bì shèng.
    - Justice will surely prevail.
  - 他始终坚守义(他始終堅守義)，维护公正(維護公正)。
    - Tā shǐ zhōng jiān shǒu yì, wèi hù gōng zhèng.
    - He always adheres to righteousness and upholds fairness.
### 义(義) → 乂(乂)
- **definition:** to govern/cut
- **pinyin:** yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts crossed lines resembling scissors or a shearing motion ->
    - to cut or govern
  - **interpretation:** The character 乂(乂) (yì, 'to govern/cut') visually represents crossed lines, similar to scissors in action. This pictorial form evolved to also symbolize governance, suggesting control or regulation akin to the precision of cutting.
- **examples:**
  - 圣君乂天下(聖君乂天下)。
    - Shèng jūn yì tiānxià.
    - A sagacious ruler governs the world.
  - 皇帝以宽仁乂天下(皇帝以寬仁乂天下)，令百姓安居(令百姓安居)。
    - Huángdì yǐ kuān rén yì tiānxià, lìng bǎixìng ānjū.
    - The emperor governs the world with benevolence, enabling the people to live in peace.
  - 厨师用刀乂肉块(廚師用刀乂肉塊)，准备宴席(準備宴席)。
    - Chúshī yòng dāo yì ròukuài, zhǔnbèi yànxí.
    - The chef cuts the pieces of meat with a knife, preparing for the banquet.
