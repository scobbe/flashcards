### Great Wall
---
- **pinyin:** Great Wall
- **components:**
  - 长(長)
    - cháng
    - long; lasting; forever; senior
  - 城
    - chéng
    - city; wall; fortress; town
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 长(長) (cháng, 'long') + 城(城) (chéng, 'city; wall') — long wall
  - **interpretation:** The Great Wall is named so because it is an extensive and lengthy defensive structure. It served to protect cities and territories, emphasizing the practical aspect of its design as a long wall.
- **examples:**
  - 长城是中国的象征。(長城是中國的象徵。)
    - Chángchéng shì Zhōngguó de xiàngzhēng.
    - The Great Wall is a symbol of China.
  - 每年都有很多游客来长城。(每年都有很多遊客來長城。)
    - Měi nián dōu yǒu hěn duō yóukè lái Chángchéng.
    - Every year, many tourists come to the Great Wall.
