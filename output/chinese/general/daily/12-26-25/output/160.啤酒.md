### píjiǔ
---
- **definition:** beer
- **pinyin:** píjiǔ
- **components:**
  - 啤
    - pí
    - beer
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol; liquor; alcoholic beverage; wine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 啤(啤) (pí, "beer") is a phonetic transcription of the sound in "beer" and 酒(酒) (jiǔ, "alcohol") means "alcoholic beverage." Together, 啤酒(啤酒) refers simply to an "alcoholic beverage called beer."
  - **interpretation:** 啤(啤) (pí, "beer") combined with 酒(酒) (jiǔ, "alcohol") clearly expresses the concept of beer as an alcoholic beverage.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝啤酒(我想喝啤酒)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng hē píjiǔ.
    - I want to drink beer.
  - 他下班后去超市(他下班後去超市)，买了几瓶啤酒(買了幾瓶啤酒)。
    - Tā xiàbān hòu qù chāoshì, mǎile jǐ píng píjiǔ.
    - After work, he went to the supermarket and bought several bottles of beer.
  - 天气炎热时(天氣炎熱時)，啤酒能让人感到一丝凉爽(啤酒能讓人感到一絲涼爽)。
    - Tiānqì yánrè shí, píjiǔ néng ràng rén gǎndào yīsī liángshuǎng.
    - When the weather is scorching, beer can make people feel a bit cool.
