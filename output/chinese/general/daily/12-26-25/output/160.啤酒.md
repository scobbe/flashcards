### píjiǔ

- **definition:** beer
- **pinyin:** píjiǔ
- **components:**
  - 啤
    - pí
    - beer
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol; liquor; spirits; wine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 啤 (pí, "beer") and 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") combine to form the word 啤酒 (píjiǔ, "beer"). Here, 啤 doesn't have a standalone meaning in Chinese outside of this word, but 酒 is a general term for alcoholic beverages.
  - **interpretation:** 啤酒 (píjiǔ, "beer") combines the element referring to beer specifically with the character for alcohol, indicating beer as a type of alcoholic beverage.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )喜( )欢(歡)喝( )啤( )酒( )。

    - Wǎnshàng hé péngyǒu yīqǐ hē píjiǔ, liáo liáotiān.
    - In the evening, I drink beer with my friends and chat.
