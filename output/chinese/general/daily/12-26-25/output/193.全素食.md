## 全素食(全素食)
---
## 
### vegan diet
---
- **pinyin:** vegan diet
- **components:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - whole; all
  - 素食
    - sùshí
    - vegetarian food; plant-based diet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 全(全) (quán, 'whole; all') + 素食(素食) (sùshí, 'vegetarian food') — all vegetarian food
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because it emphasizes a diet consisting entirely of plant-based foods without any animal products. A 全素食 diet is totally free of meat, dairy, and eggs, aligning with ethical, health, or environmental choices.
- **examples:**
  - 越来越多人选择全素食(越來越多人選擇全素食)。
    - Yuè lái yuè duōrén xuǎnzé quán sùshí.
    - More and more people are choosing a vegan diet.
  - 他完全采用全素食(他完全採用全素食)。
    - Tā wánquán cǎiyòng quán sùshí.
    - He completely adopts a vegan diet.
