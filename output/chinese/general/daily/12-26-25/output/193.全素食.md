## vegan diet
### quánsùshí
---
- **definition:** vegan diet
- **pinyin:** quánsùshí
- **components:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - complete; whole; entire
  - 素食
    - sùshí
    - vegetarian food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 全(全) (quán, "complete"), 素(素) (sù, "vegetarian; plain; simple"), 食(食) (shí, "food")
  - **interpretation:** The term 全素食 (quánsùshí) describes a diet that is 'completely' (全(全) (quán, "complete")) based on 'vegetarian' (素(素) (sù, "vegetarian")) 'food' (食(食) (shí, "food")) products. This denotes a strict vegan diet, eliminating any animal-derived products.
- **examples:**
  - 我平时都吃全素食(我平時都吃全素食)。
    - Wǒ píngshí dōu chī quánsùshí.
    - I usually eat a vegan diet.
  - 为了健康(為了健康)，她选择全素食(她選擇全素食)。
    - Wèile jiànkāng, tā xuǎnzé quánsùshí.
    - For the sake of health, she chooses a vegan diet.
  - 全素食有助于保持身体健康(全素食有助於保持身體健康)。
    - Quánsùshí yǒu zhù yú bǎochí shēntǐ jiànkāng.
    - A vegan diet helps maintain good physical health.

### 全素食(全素食) → 素食(素食)
- **definition:** vegetarian food
- **pinyin:** sùshí
- **components:**
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain; simple; vegetarian
  - 食
    - shí
    - food; eat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 素(素) (sù, "plain; simple; vegetarian") + 食(食) (shí, "food; eat") =
    - simple/plain food - vegetarian food.
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to food prepared in a simple, non-elaborate way, focusing on plant-based ingredients, aligning with the idea of simplicity and purity found in vegetarian dishes.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天吃素食(我今天吃素食)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān chī sùshí.
    - I am eating vegetarian food today.
  - 他认为素食健康(他認為素食健康)，对身体有益(對身體有益)。
    - Tā rènwéi sùshí jiànkāng, duì shēntǐ yǒuyì.
    - He believes that vegetarian food is healthy and beneficial for the body.
  - 餐厅提供多种素食(餐廳提供多種素食)，让顾客有更多选择(讓顧客有更多選擇)。
    - Cāntīng tígōng duō zhǒng sùshí, ràng gùkè yǒu gèng duō xuǎnzé.
    - The restaurant offers various types of vegetarian food, giving customers more choices.
### 全素食(全素食) → 素食(素食) → 素(素)
- **definition:** plain; simple; vegetarian
- **pinyin:** sù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 廾(廾) (gǒng, "two hands") + 糸(糸) (mì, "silk thread") =
    - hands braiding thread ->
    - plain/simplistic ->
    - modern form resembles 生 without diagonal stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 素(sù, "plain") represents the action of hands braiding a silk thread, signifying simplicity and purity. It visually encapsulates the idea of creating something basic yet essential by hand.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢穿素色衣服(他喜歡穿素色衣服)。
    - Tā xǐhuān chuān sù sè yīfú.
    - He likes wearing plain-colored clothes.
  - 他不吃荤菜，只吃素食(他不吃葷菜，只吃素食)。
    - Tā bù chī hūncài, zhǐ chī sùshí.
    - He doesn't eat meat; he only eats vegetarian food.
  - 她坚持素食生活(她堅持素食生活)。
    - Tā jiānchí sùshí shēnghuó.
    - She adheres to a vegetarian lifestyle.
### 全素食(全素食) → 素食(素食) → 食(食)
- **definition:** food; eat
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 亼(亼) (jí, 'gather') above 艮(艮) (gèn, 'stopping') ->
    - mouth over bowl of rice ->
    - eating
  - **interpretation:** 食(食) (shí, 'eat') originally depicted a mouth over a bowl of rice, conveying the act of eating. This evolved into the current form, retaining the visual representation of consuming food.
- **examples:**
  - 我食快餐(我食快餐)。
    - Wǒ shí kuàicān.
    - I eat fast food.
  - 他食晚餐(他食晚餐)，然后出门(然後出門)。
    - Tā shí wǎncān, ránhòu chūmén.
    - He eats dinner, then goes out.
  - 古人云：食色性也(古人云：食色性也)。
    - Gǔrén yún: shí sè xìng yě.
    - The ancients said, 'Food and sex are human nature.'
