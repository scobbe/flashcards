## eat in
### tángshí
---
- **definition:** eat in
- **pinyin:** tángshí
- **components:**
  - 堂
    - táng
    - hall; chamber; central room; family
  - 食
    - shí
    - eat; food; meal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 堂(堂) (táng, "hall") + 食(食) (shí, "eat") =
    - to eat in a hall or establishment, referring to dining in a restaurant.
  - **interpretation:** The term 堂食(堂食) (tángshí, "eat in") refers to the act of dining in at a restaurant or food establishment, as opposed to taking food out. 堂(堂) (táng, "hall") suggests a formal space, like a dining hall, while 食(食) (shí, "eat") indicates the action taking place within that space. This combination identifies the setting and activity specifically as an indoor dining experience.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天选择堂食(我今天選擇堂食)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān xuǎnzé tángshí.
    - Today I choose to eat in.
  - 饭店推出堂食服务(飯店推出堂食服務)，吸引了许多客户(吸引了許多客戶)。
    - Fàndiàn tuīchū tángshí fúwù, xīyǐnle xǔduō kèhù.
    - The restaurant launched an eat-in service, attracting many customers.
  - 因为外卖不方便(因爲外賣不方便)，所以我们决定堂食(所以我們決定堂食)。
    - Yīnwèi wàimài bú fāngbiàn, suǒyǐ wǒmen juédìng tángshí.
    - Because takeout is inconvenient, we decided to eat in.
