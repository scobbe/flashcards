- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 堂 (táng, "hall") + 食 (shí, "eat") =
    - to eat in a hall or establishment, referring to dining in a restaurant.
  - **interpretation:** The term 堂食 (tángshí, "eat in") refers to the act of dining in at a restaurant or food establishment, as opposed to taking food out. 堂 (táng, "hall") suggests a formal space, like a dining hall, while 食 (shí, "eat") indicates the action taking place within that space. This combination identifies the setting and activity specifically as an indoor dining experience.
