## eat in
### tángshí
---
- **definition:** eat in
- **pinyin:** tángshí
- **components:**
  - 堂
    - táng
    - hall
  - 食
    - shí
    - eat; food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 堂(堂) (táng, "hall") + 食(食) (shí, "eat") - indicates dining within the premises, as if eating in a hall.
  - **interpretation:** In 堂食 (tángshí, "eat in"), 堂(堂) (táng, "hall") evokes the image of a formal setting or establishment where meals may be served, while 食(食) (shí, "eat") refers to the act of eating. Together, they convey the idea of consuming food on the premises of a dining establishment.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天选择堂食(堂食)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān xuǎnzé tángshí.
    - I chose to eat in today.
  - 这家餐厅提供堂食(堂食)，也供应外卖(外賣)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng tángshí, yě gōngyìng wàimài.
    - This restaurant offers eat-in service and also provides takeaway.
  - 因为疫情影响(因為疫情影響)，他们暂停堂食(堂食)。
    - Yīnwèi yìqíng yǐngxiǎng, tāmen zàntíng tángshí.
    - Due to the impact of the pandemic, they have suspended eat-in service.

### 堂食(堂食) → 食(食)
- **definition:** eat; food
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 亼(亼) (jí, 'gather') above 艮(艮) (gèn, 'stopping') ->
    - mouth over bowl of rice ->
    - eating
  - **interpretation:** 食(食) (shí, 'eat') originally depicted a mouth over a bowl of rice, conveying the act of eating. This evolved into the current form, retaining the visual representation of consuming food.
- **examples:**
  - 我食快餐(我食快餐)。
    - Wǒ shí kuàicān.
    - I eat fast food.
  - 他食晚餐(他食晚餐)，然后出门(然後出門)。
    - Tā shí wǎncān, ránhòu chūmén.
    - He eats dinner, then goes out.
  - 古人云：食色性也(古人云：食色性也)。
    - Gǔrén yún: shí sè xìng yě.
    - The ancients said, 'Food and sex are human nature.'
