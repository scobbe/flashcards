### Lingnan culture
---
- **pinyin:** Lingnan culture
- **components:**
  - 岭南(嶺南)
    - lǐngnán
    - Lingnan; southern ridge; southern
  - 文化
    - wénhuà
    - culture; civilization; education; literary
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 岭南(嶺南) (lǐngnán, 'Lingnan') + 文化(文化) (wénhuà, 'culture') — Lingnan culture
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to the cultural aspects of the Lingnan region, which includes Guangdong and Guangxi. The combination makes sense as it encapsulates both the geographical identity (岭南) and the cultural elements (文化) inherent to that area.
- **examples:**
  - 岭南文化深受欢迎(嶺南文化深受歡迎)。
    - Lǐngnán wénhuà shēn shòu huānyíng.
    - Lingnan culture is very popular.
  - 岭南文化融合了多种艺术形式(嶺南文化融合了多種藝術形式)。
    - Lǐngnán wénhuà rónghéle duōzhǒng yìshù xíngshì.
    - Lingnan culture integrates various art forms.
