### electric charge
---
- **pinyin:** electric charge
- **components:**
  - 电(電)
    - diàn
    - electricity; electric; electrical; voltage
  - 荷
    - hé
    - load; burden; weight; to bear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 电(電) (diàn, "electricity") + 荷 (hé, "load") — electric load
  - **interpretation:** A 电子(電子) (diànzǐ, "electron") has an electric charge, and it's essential in electricity and electronics. The term conveys the concept of electricity being a load or burden measured in various units.
- **examples:**
  - 电子(電子)在电荷的运动中非常重要。
    - Diànzǐ zài diànhè de yùndòng zhōng fēicháng zhòngyào。
    - Electrons are very important in the movement of electric charge.
  - 电荷的性质决定了它在电场中的行为。
    - Diànhè de xìngzhì juédìngle tā zài diànchǎng zhōng de xíngwéi。
    - The nature of electric charge determines its behavior in an electric field.
### 电荷(電荷) → 电(電)
- **definition:** electricity; electric; electrical; voltage
- **pinyin:** diàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 电(電) symbolizes electric energy, illustrated through the idea of lightning and electrical phenomena.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the notion of electric power as it relates to the visible force of lightning, emphasizing vitality and energy.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢电(我喜歡電)的产品。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān diàn de chǎnpǐn.
    - I like electric products.
  - 雷电(雷電)真令人惊叹！
    - Léi diàn zhēn lìng rén jīngtàn！
    - The lightning is truly amazing!
### 电荷(電荷) → 荷(荷)
- **definition:** load; burden; weight; to bear
- **pinyin:** hé
- **components:**
  - 艸
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 何
    - hé
    - what
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 艸(艸) (cǎo, 'grass; plant') + phonetic 何(何) (hé, 'what')
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts a plant element combined with the sound component 'he,' linking it to nature and questions regarding plants. This creates an association with growth and the natural world.
- **examples:**
  - 荷花盛开(荷花盛開)，美丽的景色(美麗的景色)。
    - Héhuā shèngkāi, měilì de jǐngsè.
    - Lotus flowers bloom beautifully, creating a stunning view.
  - 我喜欢吃荷叶饼(我喜歡吃荷葉餅)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī héyèbǐng.
    - I enjoy eating lotus leaf pancakes.
### 电荷(電荷) → 荷(荷) → 艸(艸)
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts grass with leaves and stems.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents clusters of grass, emphasizing its natural, organic form in a simplistic design.
- **examples:**
  - 我在草地上玩耍(我在草地上玩耍)。
    - Wǒ zài cǎo dì shàng wán shuǎ.
    - I play on the grass.
  - 鲜花和草一起生长(鮮花和草一起生長)。
    - Xiān huā hé cǎo yī qǐ shēng zhǎng.
    - Flowers and grass grow together.
  - 小羊在草地上吃草(小羊在草地上吃草)。
    - Xiǎo yáng zài cǎo dì shàng chī cǎo.
    - The lamb eats grass in the meadow.
### 电荷(電荷) → 荷(荷) → 何(何)
- **definition:** what
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 何 originally depicted a man with a pouch, reflecting the meaning of questioning or inquiry.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a man with a pouch symbolizes the act of searching for answers or 'what' one possesses.
- **examples:**
  - 这是什么？
    - Zhè shì shénme？
    - What is this?
  - 你要什么？
    - Nǐ yào shénme？
    - What do you want?
