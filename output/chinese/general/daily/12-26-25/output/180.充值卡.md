## recharge card
### chōngzhíkǎ
---
- **definition:** recharge card
- **pinyin:** chōngzhíkǎ
- **components:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - to charge; to fill; to act in place of; sufficient
  - 值
    - zhí
    - value; worth; to be worth; to deserve
  - 卡
    - kǎ
    - card; punch card; calorie
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 充 (chōng, "to charge"), 值 (zhí, "value"), 卡 (kǎ, "card")
  - **interpretation:** 充值卡 (chōngzhíkǎ, "recharge card") combines 充 (chōng, "to charge") and 值 (zhí, "value") to indicate the action of adding value, along with 卡 (kǎ, "card"), representing a card. Therefore, a 充值卡 is a card that is used to recharge or add value to an account, such as for mobile phones or prepaid services.
