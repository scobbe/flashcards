## recharge card
### chōngzhíkǎ
---
- **definition:** recharge card
- **pinyin:** chōngzhíkǎ
- **components:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - to charge; to fill; to act in place of; sufficient
  - 值
    - zhí
    - value; worth; to be worth; to deserve
  - 卡
    - kǎ
    - card; punch card; calorie
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 充(充) (chōng, "to charge"), 值(值) (zhí, "value"), 卡(卡) (kǎ, "card")
  - **interpretation:** 充值卡(充值卡) (chōngzhíkǎ, "recharge card") combines 充(充) (chōng, "to charge") and 值(值) (zhí, "value") to indicate the action of adding value, along with 卡(卡) (kǎ, "card"), representing a card. Therefore, a 充值卡 is a card that is used to recharge or add value to an account, such as for mobile phones or prepaid services.
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天买了一张充值卡(我昨天買了一張充值卡)。
    - Wǒ zuótiān mǎile yī zhāng chōngzhíkǎ.
    - I bought a recharge card yesterday.

    - Shǒujī liúliàng yòng wán shí, chōngzhíkǎ kěyǐ ràng wǒ jìxù shàngwǎng.
    - When my phone's data runs out, a recharge card lets me continue to go online.
  - 他不明白充值卡的使用方法(他不明白充值卡的使用方法)。
    - Tā bù míngbái chōngzhíkǎ de shǐyòng fāngfǎ.
    - He doesn't understand how to use the recharge card.
