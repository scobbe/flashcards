### recharge card
---
- **pinyin:** recharge card
- **components:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - to recharge; to fill; to pay for; to supply
  - 值卡
    - zhí kǎ
    - value card; worth card; price card; charge card
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 充(充) (chōng, 'to recharge') + 值卡(值卡) (zhí kǎ, 'value card') — recharge value card
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to a card used to add credits or value, especially in mobile or online services. The combination shows that this card serves the purpose of replenishing or increasing one's value for use.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要买一张充值卡。
    - Wǒ xūyào mǎi yī zhāng chōngzhí kǎ。

    - Please recharge the amount on this card to my account.
