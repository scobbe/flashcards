## recharge card
### chōngzhíkǎ
---
- **definition:** recharge card
- **pinyin:** chōngzhíkǎ
- **components:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - to charge; to fill; to act in place of; sufficient
  - 值
    - zhí
    - value; worth; to be worth; to deserve
  - 卡
    - kǎ
    - card; punch card; calorie
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 充 (chōng, "to charge"), 值 (zhí, "value"), 卡 (kǎ, "card")
  - **interpretation:** 充值卡 (chōngzhíkǎ, "recharge card") combines 充 (chōng, "to charge") and 值 (zhí, "value") to indicate the action of adding value, along with 卡 (kǎ, "card"), representing a card. Therefore, a 充值卡 is a card that is used to recharge or add value to an account, such as for mobile phones or prepaid services.
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天买(買)了一张(張)充值卡。
    - Wǒ zuótiān mǎile yī zhāng chōngzhíkǎ.
    - I bought a recharge card yesterday.
  - 手机(機)流量用完时(時)，充值卡可以让(讓)我继续(繼續)上网(網)。
    - Shǒujī liúliàng yòng wán shí, chōngzhíkǎ kěyǐ ràng wǒ jìxù shàngwǎng.
    - When my phone's data runs out, a recharge card lets me continue to go online.
  - 他不明白充值卡的使用方法。
    - Tā bù míngbái chōngzhíkǎ de shǐyòng fāngfǎ.
    - He doesn't understand how to use the recharge card.
### 充值卡 → 充
- **definition:** to charge; to fill; to act in place of; sufficient
- **pinyin:** chōng
- **components:**
  - 亠
    - tóu
    - lid
  - 儿
    - ér
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 서(充) (shān, "spear") + 儿(人, "person") =
    - person with a spear filling space ->
    - to fill
  - **interpretation:** The character 充 combines the idea of a person 儿 holding or wielding a spear or decorative object 氍, representing the act of filling or occupying a position. It effectively conveys the concept of 'filling up' or acting in place of someone, as it involves an agent (the person) and an object or action.
- **examples:**
  - 手机(機)需要充电(電)。
    - Shǒujī xūyào chōngdiàn.
    - The phone needs to be charged.
  - 他充当(當)，班长(長)的角色。
    - Tā chōngdāng, bānzhǎng de juésè.
    - He acted as the class monitor.
  - 水箱充满(滿)了雨水。
    - Shuǐxiāng chōngmǎn le yǔshuǐ.
    - The water tank is filled with rainwater.

### 充值卡 → 卡
- **definition:** card; punch card; calorie
- **pinyin:** kǎ
- **components:**
  - 扌(手)
    - shǒu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic: 卡 (qiǎ, "block") =
    - hand stops ->
    - derived sense of card, calorie
  - **interpretation:** The character 卡 (kǎ, "card") combines 扌(手) (shǒu, "hand") representing a holding or grasping action and 卡 (qiǎ, "block") to convey the idea of stopping or blocking. This hints at the function of a card as a tangible object one handles or processes for a specific purpose.
- **examples:**
  - 我用信用卡购(購)物。
    - Wǒ yòng xìnyòngkǎ gòuwù.
    - I shop using a credit card.
  - 请(請)刷卡，进(進)入大楼(樓)。
    - Qǐng shuākǎ, jìnrù dàlóu.
    - Please swipe your card, and then enter the building.
  - 这个运动(這個運動)消耗了很多卡路里。
    - Zhège yùndòng xiāohào le hěn duō kǎlùlǐ.
    - This exercise burned many calories.
### 充值卡 → 卡 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞来(飛來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌制(手製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺术(藝術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细节(細節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
    - The artist meticulously carved every detail of the hand.
