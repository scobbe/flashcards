## recharge card
### chōngzhíkǎ
---
- **definition:** recharge card
- **pinyin:** chōngzhíkǎ
- **components:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - recharge; fill
  - 值
    - zhí
    - value; worth
  - 卡
    - kǎ
    - card
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 充(充) (chōng, "recharge") + 值(值) (zhí, "value") + 卡(卡) (kǎ, "card") =
    - a card used to recharge value
  - **interpretation:** 充值卡(充值卡) (chōngzhíkǎ, "recharge card") refers to a card that is used to add value or credit to an account, such as a mobile phone balance or a prepaid service.
- **examples:**
  - 我用充值卡给手机充值(我用充值卡給手機充值)。
    - Wǒ yòng chōngzhíkǎ gěi shǒujī chōngzhí.
    - I use a recharge card to top up my phone.
  - 我买了一张充值卡(我買了一張充值卡)，打算给手机充值(打算給手機充值)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī zhāng chōngzhíkǎ, dǎsuàn gěi shǒujī chōngzhí.
    - I bought a recharge card, planning to add credit to my phone.
  - 他发现充值卡(他發現充值卡)很实用(很實用)。
    - Tā fāxiàn chōngzhíkǎ hěn shíyòng.
    - He found the recharge card very useful.
