### continent; river island
---
- **pinyin:** continent; river island
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - state, region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 水 (water) + phonetic 州 (zhōu, 'state, region')
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the concept of water (水) with a phonetic element that suggests areas or regions (州), evoking the idea of land related to waterways or islands.
- **examples:**
  - 我们住在澳洲(我們住在澳洲)，喜欢这里的天气(喜歡這裡的天氣)。
    - Wǒmen zhù zài Àozhōu, xǐhuān zhèlǐ de tiānqì.
    - We live in Australia, and we like the weather here.
  - 亚洲(亞洲)是六大洲之一(是六大洲之一)。
    - Yàzhōu shì liù dà zhōu zhī yī.
    - Asia is one of the six continents.
  - 他们计划去南美洲(他們計畫去南美洲)。
    - Tāmen jìhuà qù Nánměizhōu.
    - They plan to go to South America.
### 洲(洲) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the movement of water, evoking a sense of flow and fluidity. It is a direct representation of water as an element essential for life.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命的来源(水是生命的來源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng de láiyuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水(我喜歡喝水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
  - 海水很咸(海水很鹹)。
    - Hǎishuǐ hěn xián.
    - Sea water is very salty.
### 洲(洲) → 州(州)
- **definition:** state, region
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an islet in a river.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a landform surrounded by water, conveying the idea of a region or area, such as a state or territory.
- **examples:**
  - 他住在上海(他住在上海)。
    - tā zhù zài shànghǎi.
    - He lives in Shanghai.
  - 这个州的风景很美(這個州的風景很美)。
    - zhège zhōu de fēngjǐng hěn měi.
    - The scenery in this state is beautiful.
