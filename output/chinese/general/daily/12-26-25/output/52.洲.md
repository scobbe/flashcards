## continent; river island
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - ancient administrative division
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") + 州(州) (zhōu) =
    - water around a landmass ->
    - continent; river island
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") and 州(州) (zhōu), which shares the pronunciation, represents land surrounded by water, like a continent or river island.
- **examples:**
  - 亚洲是世界上最大的洲(亞洲是世界上最大的洲)。
    - Yàzhōu shì shìjiè shàng zuì dà de zhōu.
    - Asia is the largest continent in the world.
  - 江心的小洲静静地漂浮着(江心的小洲靜靜地漂浮著)。
    - Jiāngxīn de xiǎo zhōu jìngjìng de piāofú zhe.
    - The small river island in the middle of the river floats quietly.
### 洲(洲) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** The character 水(水) (shuǐ, 'water') visually represents the natural form of flowing water, which is intuitive given its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一杯水(請給我一杯水)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī bēi shuǐ.
    - Please give me a glass of water.
  - 夏天炎热时(夏天炎熱時)，池塘里的水清澈见底(池塘裡的水清澈見底)。
    - Xiàtiān yánrè shí, chítáng lǐ de shuǐ qīngchè jiàn dǐ.
    - When summer is scorching, the water in the pond is crystal clear.
### 洲(洲) → 州(州)
- **definition:** ancient administrative division
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an islet in a river
  - **interpretation:** The character 州(州) (zhōu, 'ancient administrative division') originally depicted an islet or a land area surrounded by rivers. The imagery of land surrounded by water relates to administrative divisions being distinct areas within a region.
- **examples:**
  - 唐朝时期(唐朝時期)，全国划分为若干州(全國劃分為若干州)。
    - Táng cháo shíqí, quán guó huà fēn wéi ruò gān zhōu.
    - During the Tang Dynasty, the country was divided into several states.
  - 古代行政区划中(古代行政區劃中)，州是重要单位(州是重要單位)。
    - Gǔ dài xíng zhèng qū huà zhōng, zhōu shì zhòng yào dān wèi.
    - In ancient administrative divisions, a state was an important unit.
  - 南方的州(南方的州)，曾是古时重要的行政区域(曾是古時重要的行政區域)。
    - Nán fāng de zhōu, céng shì gǔ shí zhòng yào de xíng zhèng qū yù.
    - The state in the southern region was once an important administrative area in ancient times.
