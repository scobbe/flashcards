## continent; river island
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - islet; state
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 水 ‘water’ + phonetic: 州
  - **interpretation:** Water element signals a relation to bodies of water. 州 provides the sound zhōu and originally referred to a river islet or sandbank, aligning with the meaning ‘river island’ and later extending to ‘continent.’
- **examples:**
  - 亚洲是世界上人口最多的洲(亞洲是世界上人口最多的洲)。
    - Yàzhōu shì shìjiè shàng rénkǒu zuì duō de zhōu.
    - Asia is one of the most populous continents in the world.
  - 这条大江中间有一座沙洲(這條大江中間有一座沙洲)。
    - Zhè tiáo dàjiāng zhōngjiān yǒu yī zuò shāzhōu.
    - There is a sandbar island in the middle of this great river.
  - 洲际航班需要飞很多小时(洲際航班需要飛很多小時)。
    - Zhōujì hángbān xūyào fēi hěn duō xiǎoshí.
    - Intercontinental flights take many hours.
### 洲 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram of water flowing; sometimes written as the variant 氺; next to a character it is compressed and stylized into 氵 (unrelated to 冫).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts flowing water with a main stream and side droplets. In compound characters it condenses to the left-side form 氵 to signal a water-related meaning, distinct from 冫 which indicates ice or cold.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝一杯水(我想喝一杯水)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng hē yì bēi shuǐ.
    - I want to drink a glass of water.
  - 城市的自来水很干净(城市的自來水很乾淨)。
    - Chéngshì de zìláishuǐ hěn gānjìng.
    - The tap water in the city is very clean.
  - 旱季来了(旱季來了)，我们要节约用水(我們要節約用水)。
    - Hànjì lái le, wǒmen yào jiéyuē yòngshuǐ.
    - The dry season has arrived; we should conserve water.
### 洲 → 州
- **definition:** islet; state
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** islet in a river
  - **interpretation:** Depicts small islets amid flowing water; early forms sketched sandbanks separated by channels. The meaning was later extended to administrative “states/prefectures,” while the water-marked form 洲 took over the literal “river islet” sense.
- **examples:**
  - 美国有五十个州(美國有五十個州)，每个州都有自己的法律(每個州都有自己的法律)。
    - Měiguó yǒu wǔshí gè zhōu, měi gè zhōu dōu yǒu zìjǐ de fǎlǜ.
    - The United States has fifty states, and each state has its own laws.
  - 我们开车穿过加利福尼亚州(我們開車穿過加利福尼亞州)。
    - Wǒmen kāichē chuānguò Jiālìfúníyà Zhōu.
    - We drove across the state of California.
  - 我们计划游历澳大利亚的几个州(我們計劃遊歷澳大利亞的幾個州)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà yóulì Àodàlìyà de jǐ gè zhōu.
    - We plan to travel through several states in Australia.
