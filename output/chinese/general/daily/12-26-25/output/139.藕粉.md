## lotus root starch
### ǒufěn
---
- **definition:** lotus root starch
- **pinyin:** ǒufěn
- **components:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder; starch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 藕(藕) (ǒu, "lotus root") + 粉(粉) (fěn, "powder; starch") =
    - starch made from lotus root ->
    - lotus root starch
  - **interpretation:** This word combines the term for 'lotus root' and 'powder' to refer to the starch obtained from lotus roots, commonly used in food preparation.
- **examples:**

    - Mom uses lotus root starch to make tangyuan.
  - 这道甜品用藕粉(這道甜品用藕粉)，口感特别细腻(口感特別細膩)。
    - Zhè dào tiánpǐn yòng ǒufěn, kǒugǎn tèbié xìnì.
