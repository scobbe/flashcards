### lotus root starch
---
- **pinyin:** lotus root starch
- **components:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root; water lily; root
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder; flour; starch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 藕(藕) (ǒu, "lotus root") + 粉(粉) (fěn, "starch/powder") — lotus root starch
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense as 藕(藕) (ǒu, "lotus root") is the source of the starch, and 粉(粉) (fěn, "starch/powder”) indicates the powdered form of this ingredient.
- **examples:**

    - ǒu fěn kěyǐ yòng lái zuò gè zhǒng měi shí。

  - 我喜欢吃藕粉汤(我喜歡吃藕粉湯)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān chī ǒu fěn tāng。
    - I like to eat lotus root starch soup.
