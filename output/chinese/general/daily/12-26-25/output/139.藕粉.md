## lotus root starch
### ǒufěn
---
- **definition:** lotus root starch
- **pinyin:** ǒufěn
- **components:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder; starch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") + 粉 (fěn, "powder") =
    - lotus root starch
  - **interpretation:** 藕粉 (ǒufěn, "lotus root starch") is a powder derived from the lotus root, a common ingredient used in Chinese cuisine for its nutritional benefits and unique texture. Mixing 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") with 粉 (fěn, "powder") aptly describes the product.
- **examples:**
  - 我买(買)了藕粉。
    - Wǒ mǎile ǒufěn.
    - I bought lotus root starch.
  - 妈妈(媽媽)用藕粉，做出了美味的汤圆(湯圓)。
    - Māmā yòng ǒufěn, zuò chūle měiwèi de tāngyuán.
    - Mom used lotus root starch to make delicious glutinous rice balls.
  - 藕粉可以用来(來)增稠，调(調)制传统(傳統)的甜品。
    - Ǒufěn kěyǐ yòng lái zēngchóu, tiáozhì chuántǒng de tiánpǐn.
    - Lotus root starch can be used as a thickener to prepare traditional desserts.

  - 分
    - fēn

- **etymology:**

  - **description:**
