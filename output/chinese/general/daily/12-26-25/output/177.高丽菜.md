- **pinyin:** gāolícài
- **components:**
  - 高麗
    - gāolí

  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高(高) (gāo, "high"), 麗(麗) (lì, "beautiful"), 菜(菜) (cài, "vegetable")
  - **interpretation:** 高麗(高麗) (gāolí, "Korea") refers historically to the Goryeo dynasty in Korea, known for its cultural influence in ancient times. 菜(菜) (cài, "vegetable") indicates this is a type of vegetable.     - Jīntiān shìchǎng shàng yǒu hěn duō xīnxiān de gāolícài, wǒmen dǎsuàn mǎi yīxiē huíjiā zuò cài.
    - There are many fresh cabbages at the market today, and we plan to buy some to cook at home.
  - 妈妈常常用高丽菜(媽媽常常用高麗菜)，做一锅清汤(做一鍋清湯)。
    - Māmā chángcháng yòng gāolícài, zuò yī guō qīngtāng.
    - Mom often uses cabbage to make a pot of clear soup.

    - Xuézhě yánjiū Gāolí lìshǐ, jiēshì qí dútè mèilì.
