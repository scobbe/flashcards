## cabbage
### gāolícài
---
- **traditional:** 高麗菜
- **definition:** cabbage
- **pinyin:** gāolícài
- **components:**
  - 高麗
    - gāolí
    - Korea; Goryeo dynasty
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高 (gāo, "high"), 麗 (lì, "beautiful"), 菜 (cài, "vegetable")
  - **interpretation:** 高麗 (gāolí, "Korea") refers historically to the Goryeo dynasty in Korea, known for its cultural influence in ancient times. 菜 (cài, "vegetable") indicates this is a type of vegetable. Thus, 高丽(麗)菜 (gāolícài) means 'Korean vegetable,' which is how cabbage is referred to in some regions, especially Taiwan.
- **examples:**
  - 我很喜欢(歡)吃高丽(麗)菜。
    - Wǒ hěn xǐhuān chī gāolícài.
    - I really like eating cabbage.
  - 今天市场(場)上有很多新鲜(鮮)的高丽(麗)菜，我们(們)打算买(買)一些回家做菜。
    - Jīntiān shìchǎng shàng yǒu hěn duō xīnxiān de gāolícài, wǒmen dǎsuàn mǎi yīxiē huíjiā zuò cài.
    - There are many fresh cabbages at the market today, and we plan to buy some to cook at home.
  - 妈妈(媽媽)常常用高丽(麗)菜，做一锅(鍋)清汤(湯)。
    - Māmā chángcháng yòng gāolícài, zuò yī guō qīngtāng.
    - Mom often uses cabbage to make a pot of clear soup.
### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 高麗
- **definition:** Korea; Goryeo dynasty
- **pinyin:** gāolí
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall
  - 麗
    - lí
    - beautiful; magnificent
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高 (gāo, "high") + 麗 (lí, "beautiful")
  - **interpretation:** 高麗 (gāolí) refers to the Goryeo dynasty, a historical Korean kingdom lasting from 918 to 1392. The name is derived from 고구려(Goguryeo), one of the Three Kingdoms of Korea, and was adopted as a mark of pride and distinctiveness. 高 (gāo, "high") and 麗 (lí, "beautiful") highlight the elevated and beautiful nature attributed to the kingdom.
- **examples:**
  - 高麗拥(擁)有丰(豐)富的文化遗产(遺產)。
    - Gāolí yǒngyǒu fēngfù de wénhuà yíchǎn.
    - Goryeo had a rich cultural heritage.
  - 学(學)者研究高麗历(歷)史，揭示其独(獨)特魅力。
    - Xuézhě yánjiū Gāolí lìshǐ, jiēshì qí dútè mèilì.
    - Scholars study Goryeo history, revealing its unique charm.
  - 高麗时(時)期曾是文化交流的中心。
    - Gāolí shíqī céng shì wénhuà jiāoliú de zhōngxīn.
    - During the Goryeo period, it was a center of cultural exchange.
### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 高麗 → 高
- **definition:** high; tall
- **pinyin:** gāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tall building
  - **interpretation:** The character 高(gāo, "high; tall") visually represents a tall structure or building, symbolizing height or elevation. Unlike 京(jīng, "capital"), it is straightforwardly a representation of height.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)座山高。
    - Zhè zuò shān gāo.
    - This mountain is high.
  - 那棵树长(樹長)得高，比其他树(樹)高很多。
    - Nà kē shù zhǎng de gāo, bǐ qítā shù gāo hěn duō.
    - That tree grows tall, much taller than the other trees.
  - 飞机(飛機)在高空飞(飛)行。
    - Fēijī zài gāokōng fēixíng.
    - The airplane flies in high altitude.

- **pinyin:** lí

  - **interpretation:** 麗 (lí, "beautiful") is a pictographic representation of a deer with antlers, symbolizing grace and beauty, reflective of the elegance often attributed to deer in various cultures.
- **examples:**

    - Zhè fú huà zhǎnxiànle lí de fēngcǎi.
    - This painting showcases elegant charm.
  - 她走进(進)大厅(廳)，看见墙(見牆)上挂着(掛著)麗的字画(畫)。
    - Tā zǒu jìn dàtīng, kànjiàn qiáng shàng guàzhe lí de zìhuà.
    - She walked into the hall, and saw elegant calligraphy hanging on the wall.
  - 那座山因云雾缭绕(雲霧繚繞)而显(顯)得格外麗。
    - Nà zuò shān yīn yúnwù liáorào ér xiǎndé géwài lí.
    - That mountain looks exceptionally elegant due to the swirling mist.
### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 菜
- **definition:** vegetable; dish
- **pinyin:** cài
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 采
    - cǎi
    - pluck
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic 采 (cǎi, "pluck") =
    - concept of plants gathered for consumption ->
    - vegetable; dish
  - **interpretation:** The character 菜(cài) conveys the idea of plants that are gathered, emphasizing the connection between agriculture and cuisine. The 艹 component implies vegetation, while 采 provides the phonetic cue and ties to the action of picking or collecting.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)道菜很美味。
    - Zhè dào cài hěn měiwèi.
    - This dish is very delicious.
  - 他擅长(長)烹饪(飪)，创(創)新菜式不断涌现(斷湧現)。
    - Tā shàncháng pēngrèn, chuàngxīn càishì bùduàn yǒngxiàn.
    - He excels at cooking, and innovative dishes keep emerging.
  - 街边(邊)小摊(攤)的菜色丰(豐)富。
    - Jiēbiān xiǎotān de càisè fēngfù.
    - The street vendor's variety of dishes is abundant.
### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 菜 → 艹
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**

    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(來時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 菜 → 采(採)
- **traditional:** 採
- **definition:** pluck
- **pinyin:** cǎi
- **components:**
  - 爪
    - zhǎo
    - claw
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 爪 (zhǎo, 'claw') + 木 (mù, 'tree') =
    - hand grasping tree branch ->
    - pluck
  - **interpretation:** The character 采(cǎi, 'pluck') visually represents a claw or hand reaching out to a tree, symbolizing the action of plucking or gathering. This illustrates the intuitive meaning through its pictorial components.
  - **simplification:** In contemporary Chinese, 采(cǎi) serves both phonetic and semantic functions. The simplified form replaces the traditional form 採(cǎi), which is composed of 扌(shǒu, 'hand') + 采(cǎi, 'pluck'), illustrating the action of plucking with a hand.
- **examples:**
  - 她在花园里采(園裡採)花。
    - Tā zài huāyuán lǐ cǎi huā.
    - She is plucking flowers in the garden.
  - 我和朋友一同去山上采(採)果，享受自然。
    - Wǒ hé péngyǒu yītóng qù shān shàng cǎi guǒ, xiǎngshòu zìrán.
    - My friend and I go to pluck fruits on the mountain, enjoying nature.

### 高( )丽(麗)菜( ) → 菜 → 采(採) → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
