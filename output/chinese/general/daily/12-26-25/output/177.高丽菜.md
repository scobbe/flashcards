### cabbage

- **pinyin:** cabbage
- **components:**
  - 高丽(高麗)
    - gāolì
    - Korea; high; tall
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish; cuisine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高丽(高麗) (gāolì, 'Korea') + 菜(cài, 'vegetable') — Korean vegetable
  - **interpretation:** This term makes sense as it refers to a type of vegetable known for its popularity in Korean cuisine. It's commonly recognized as a staple ingredient in many dishes.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天买了高丽菜(我今天買了高麗菜)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān mǎile gāolìcài.
    - I bought cabbage today.
  - 高丽菜在这个季节很新鲜(高麗菜在這個季節很新鮮)。
    - Gāolìcài zài zhège jìjié hěn xīnxiān.
    - Cabbage is very fresh this season.
