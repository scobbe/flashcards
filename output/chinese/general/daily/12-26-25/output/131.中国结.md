### Chinese knot
---
- **pinyin:** Chinese knot
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle; center; within; among
  - 国(國)
    - guó
    - country; nation; state
  - 结(結)
    - jié
    - knot; tie;結合; close
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, 'middle') + 国(國) (guó, 'country') + 结(結) (jié, 'knot') — country knot
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to a traditional decorative knot symbolizing auspiciousness and unity in Chinese culture. The combination makes sense as it represents a knot associated with the culture and heritage of China.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢中国结的设计(我喜歡中國結的設計)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhōngguó jié de shèjì.
    - I like the design of the Chinese knot.
  - 在节日里，人们常用中国结装饰房屋(在節日里，人們常用中國結裝飾房屋)。
    - Zài jiérì lǐ, rénmen cháng yòng zhōngguó jié zhuāngshì fángwū.
    - During festivals, people often use Chinese knots to decorate their homes.
### 中国结(中國結) → 中(中)
- **definition:** middle; center; within; among
- **pinyin:** zhōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a flagpole with a drum, placed in the center to gather people and detect the wind direction.
  - **interpretation:** The iconography of a flagpole suggests a point of gathering or focus, which aligns with the meaning of 'middle' or 'center'.
- **examples:**
  - 我在中国(我在中國)。
    - Wǒ zài Zhōngguó.
    - I am in China.
  - 他在中间(他在中間)等我。
    - Tā zài zhōngjiān děng wǒ.
    - He waits for me in the middle.
  - 请站在中间(請站在中間)。
    - Qǐng zhàn zài zhōngjiān.
    - Please stand in the center.
### 中国结(中國結) → 国(國)
- **definition:** country; nation; state
- **pinyin:** guó
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is a compound with the semantic component 囗 (encirclement) and the phonetic component 玉 (jade). The encirclement suggests a place or boundary while jade traditionally symbolizes value.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of a country is evoked by the boundary represented by 囗 (encirclement) enclosing something valuable like 玉 (jade), signifying the richness of the nation.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing 玉 (jade) with a simpler form, while maintaining the boundary component, thus retaining its core meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我来自中国(我來自中國)，这是我的国家(這是我的國家)。
    - Wǒ láizì Zhōngguó, zhè shì wǒ de guójiā.
    - I come from China, this is my country.
  - 日本(日本)是个美丽的国家(國家)。
    - Rìběn shì gè měilì de guójiā.
    - Japan is a beautiful country.

- **examples:**

### 中国结(中國結) → 国(國) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem.
  - **interpretation:** The character symbolizes jade, reflecting its importance and usage in ritualistic and decorative contexts, much like the cong itself.
- **examples:**
  - 这件玉器很美丽(這件玉器很美麗)。
    - Zhè jiàn yùqì hěn měilì.
    - This piece of jade is beautiful.
  - 我想买玉(我想買玉)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yù.
    - I want to buy jade.
### 中国结(中國結) → 结(結)
- **definition:** knot; tie;結合; close
- **pinyin:** jié
- **components:**
  - 糸(絲)
    - sī
    - silk
  - 夾
    - jiā
    - to insert, to interleave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component 糸 (sī, 'silk') indicating connection or tying, and the phonetic component 夾 (jiā, 'to insert, to interleave') suggesting the act of tying or knotting.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of silk ties and the action of interleaving resonates with the notion of making a knot, representing connections and closures.
- **examples:**
  - 他把绳子结(他把繩子結)成一个死结(成一個死結)。
    - tā bǎ shéngzi jié chéng yīgè sǐ jié。
    - He tied the rope into a dead knot.
  - 我们要结(我們要結)成一个团队(成一個團隊)。
    - wǒmen yào jié chéng yīgè tuánduì。
    - We want to form a team.
### 中国结(中國結) → 结(結) → 糸(糸)
- **definition:** silk
- **pinyin:** sī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a thread, originally used to represent silk.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the delicate threads of silk, emphasizing its significance in textiles.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢丝绸(我喜歡絲綢)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān sīchóu.
    - I like silk.
  - 他买了一条丝带(他買了一條絲帶)。
    - Tā mǎile yītiáo sīdài.
    - He bought a silk ribbon.
### 中国结(中國結) → 结(結) → 夾(夾)
- **definition:** to insert, to interleave
- **pinyin:** jiā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Comprised of the semantic component 夹 (jiā, "to clamp") and the phonetic component 加 (jiā, "to add") — suggesting the idea of clamping something in between while also evoking the sound.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the act of inserting or interleaving items, capturing the action of holding or clamping them together.
- **examples:**
  - 我夾了一些纸(我夾了一些紙)在书里(在書裡)。
    - Wǒ jiāle yīxiē zhǐ zài shū lǐ.
    - I inserted some paper into the book.
  - 他夾住了门(他夾住了門)。
    - Tā jiā zhùle mén.
    - He clamped the door.
  - 请把这些文件夾起来(請把這些文件夾起來)。
    - Qǐng bǎ zhèxiē wénjiàn jiā qǐlái.
    - Please interleave these documents.
