## Chinese knot
### Zhōngguó jié
---
- **traditional:** 中國結
- **definition:** Chinese knot
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó jié
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle; center; China
  - 国(國)
    - guó
    - country; nation
  - 结(結)
    - jié
    - knot; tie; bond
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") + 国(國) (guó, "country") + 结(結) (jié, "knot")
  - **interpretation:** 中国结(國結) (zhōngguó jié, "Chinese knot") refers to the traditional decorative knotting art from China, symbolizing unity, harmony, and eternity. These knots often serve as cultural symbols and are popular in art, fashion, and festivals.
- **examples:**
  - 这个(這個)中国结(國結)很精美。
    - Zhège Zhōngguó jié hěn jīngměi.
    - This Chinese knot is exquisite.
  - 她佩戴中国结(國結)，庆(慶)祝节(節)日。
    - Tā pèidài Zhōngguó jié, qìngzhù jiérì.
    - She wears a Chinese knot to celebrate the festival.
  - 老师讲(師講)述中国结(國結)的故事，引来学(來學)生的兴(興)趣。
    - Lǎoshī jiǎngshù Zhōngguó jié de gùshì, yǐnlái xuéshēng de xìngqù.
    - The teacher told the story of the Chinese knot, arousing the students' interest.
### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 中
- **definition:** middle; center; China
- **pinyin:** zhōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arrow piercing through the center of a target
  - **interpretation:** The character 中 (zhōng, "middle") visually represents an arrow passing through the central point of a target, illustrating the concept of 'center' or 'middle' effectively. This visual imagery is straightforward and memorable, corresponding closely with its meaning of being at the center or middle of something.
- **examples:**
  - 他站在道路中，看着远(著遠)方。
    - tā zhàn zài dàolù zhōng, kànzhe yuǎnfāng.

  - 房间(間)正中，挂着(掛著)一幅名画(畫)。
    - fángjiān zhèng zhōng, guàzhe yī fú mínghuà.
    - A famous painting hangs in the exact center of the room.
### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 国(國)
- **traditional:** 國
- **definition:** country; nation
- **pinyin:** guó
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 囗 (wéi, "enclosure") + 玉 (yù, "jade") as phonetic =
    - an enclosed area associated with valuables ->
    - country
  - **interpretation:** 国(國) (guó, "country") combines the meaning of enclosure from 囗 (wéi, "enclosure") with the pronunciation and cultural importance of 玉 (yù, "jade"), emphasizing a protected, valuable territory.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by replacing 玉 (yù, "jade") with 王 (wáng, "king") as a phonetic and visual simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 这个国(這個國)家拥(擁)有悠久的历(歷)史。
    - Zhège guójiā yǒngyǒu yōujiǔ de lìshǐ.
    - This country has a long history.
  - 我们应该团结国(們應該團結國)民，共创辉(創輝)煌。
    - Wǒmen yīnggāi tuánjié guómín, gòng chuàng huīhuáng.
    - We should unite the people of our nation and create a brilliant future.

### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 国(國) → 玉
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cong (琮, a ritual jade totem)
  - **interpretation:** 玉 (yù, "jade") visually represents the concept of jade, either as a cong or three pieces of jade strung together. It is distinct from 王 (wáng, "king") thanks to the added dot, ensuring clarity in their usage and meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他收藏了一块(塊)古玉。
    - Tā shōucáng le yī kuài gǔ yù.
    - He collected a piece of antique jade.
  - 晴空万(萬)里时(時)，玉石在阳(陽)光下闪烁(閃爍)。
    - Qíngkōng wànlǐ shí, yù shí zài yángguāng xià shǎnshuò.
    - Under a clear sky, the jade sparkled under the sunlight.
  - 在宫(宮)廷中，玉制(製)的饰(飾)物象征着(徵著)高贵(貴)。
    - Zài gōngtíng zhōng, yù zhì de shìwù xiàngzhēng zhe gāoguì.
    - In the palace, jade ornaments symbolize nobility.
### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 结(結)
- **traditional:** 結
- **definition:** knot; tie; bond
- **pinyin:** jié
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 吉
    - jí
    - lucky
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 糸 (mì, "silk") + phonetic: 吉 (jí, "lucky") =
    - tying silk threads together ->
    - knot; tie
  - **interpretation:** The character 结(結) (jié, "knot; tie") combines the semantic component 糸 (mì, "silk"), which relates to string or thread, with the phonetic 吉 (jí, "lucky") to convey the act of tying or binding, often symbolizing unity or connection.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character 结 replaces the traditional 結 by applying a cursive form for the 糸 (mì, "silk") radical, reducing strokes for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 他把绳(繩)子打了个结(個結)。
    - Tā bǎ shéngzǐ dǎ le gè jié.
    - He tied a knot in the rope.
  - 友情是一种(種)牢不可破的结(結)。
    - Yǒuqíng shì yī zhǒng láo bù kě pò de jié.
    - Friendship is a bond that cannot be broken.
  - 她和朋友结(結)下了深厚的情谊(誼)，共同走过风(過風)雨人生。
    - Tā hé péngyǒu jié xià le shēnhòu de qíngyì, gòngtóng zǒu guò fēngyǔ rénshēng.
    - She and her friend formed a deep bond as they faced life's challenges together.

    - Wǒmen cānguānle gǔdài de zhībù jìshù, duì sīchóu zhìzuò chǎnshēngle xìngqù.

### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 结(結) → 吉

- **pinyin:** jí
- **components:**
  - 士

    - scholar
  - 口

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 士 (shì, "scholar") (ritual axe) + 口 (kǒu, "mouth") ->
    - representation of rituals to acquire favor ->
    - auspiciousness
  - **interpretation:** The character 吉(jí) combines 士 (shì, "scholar") symbolizing a ritual axe and 口 (kǒu, "mouth"), suggesting ancient rituals for gaining ancestral or divine blessings. This association leads to the meaning of 'auspicious' or 'lucky'.

    - He drew a lucky card.
  - 这(這)次考试结(試結)果吉利，大家都感到欣慰。
    - Zhè cì kǎoshì jiéguǒ jílì, dàjiā dōu gǎndào xīnwèi.
    - The results of this exam were auspicious, and everyone felt relieved.
### 中( )国(國)结(結) → 结(結) → 吉 → 士

- **pinyin:** shì

    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
