## Chinese knot
### Zhōngguó jié
---
- **definition:** Chinese knot
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó jié
- **components:**
  - 中国(中國)
    - Zhōngguó
    - China
  - 结(結)
    - jié
    - knot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle") + 國(國) (guó, "country") + 結(結) (jié, "knot"). 中國(中國) refers to "China" and 結(結) means "knot".
  - **interpretation:** 中國(中國) (Zhōngguó, "China") represents the origin, and 結(結) (jié, "knot") signifies the artistic and symbolic knotting technique. Thus, a 中國結(中國結) is a knot that embodies traditional Chinese culture and aesthetics.
  - **simplification:** 中(中), 國(國), 結(結)
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢中国结(我喜歡中國結)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān Zhōngguó jié.
    - I like Chinese knots.
  - 中国结代表着友谊(中國結代表著友誼)，也是传统文化的象征(也是傳統文化的象徵)。
    - Zhōngguó jié dàibiǎozhe yǒuyì, yěshì chuántǒng wénhuà de xiàngzhēng.
    - The Chinese knot represents friendship and is also a symbol of traditional culture.

### 中国结(中國結) → 中国(中國) → 国(國)
- **definition:** country; nation
- **pinyin:** guó
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") + 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") =
    - enclosure signifying territory; phonetic element indicates pronunciation ->
    - country
  - **interpretation:** The character 国(國) (guó, "country") combines 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") representing a bounded area and 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") indicating the pronunciation. The enclosure suggests a defined territory, together forming the concept of a nation.
  - **simplification:** The traditional component 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") was replaced with 王(王) (wáng, "king") to simplify the character, using a visually similar element while maintaining the phonetic and semantic qualities.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱我的国家(我愛我的國家)。
    - Wǒ ài wǒ de guójiā.
    - I love my country.
  - 这个国家(這個國家)，位于东亚(位於東亞)。
    - Zhège guójiā, wèi yú Dōngyà.
    - This country is located in East Asia.
  - 国泰民安(國泰民安)。
    - Guótài mín'ān.
    - The country enjoys peace and prosperity.

### 中国结(中國結) → 结(結)
- **definition:** knot
- **pinyin:** jié
- **components:**
  - 纟(糸)
    - mì
    - silk
  - 吉
    - jí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 纟(糸) (mì, "silk") + phonetic: 吉(吉) (jí) =
    - tying silk threads ->
    - knot
  - **interpretation:** 结(結) (jié, "knot") represents the action of tying threads together, with 纟(糸) (mì, "silk") signifying the material and 吉(吉) (jí) providing the sound.
  - **simplification:** 纟(糸) (mì, "silk") in 结(結) (jié) is a simplified form of the silk radical, reducing complexity while maintaining the original meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他把绳子打了一个结(他把繩子打了一个結)。
    - Tā bǎ shéngzi dǎ le yīgè jié.
    - He tied a knot in the rope.
  - 他把蝴蝶结系在发梢(他把蝴蝶結系在發梢)，看上去非常漂亮(看上去非常漂亮)。
    - Tā bǎ húdié jié xì zài fàshāo, kàn shàngqù fēicháng piàoliang.
    - He tied a bow knot in his hair, and it looked very pretty.
  - 他轻轻一扯(他輕輕一扯)，绳子就结出了个圈(繩子就結出了個圈)。
    - Tā qīng qīng yī chě, shéngzi jiù jié chū le gè quān.
    - He gave a gentle pull, and the rope formed a loop.
