### surname Yang
---
- **pinyin:** surname Yang
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - to project; to shine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** This character is simplified from 楊 ( 昜 → 𠃓 ), where 昜 serves as the phonetic component suggesting sound, and the木 part (木) conveys the meaning related to trees.
  - **interpretation:** The character 杨 is associated with a type of tree (the willow) and encompasses both sound and meaning through its components. This reflects the character's connection to nature and environment.
  - **simplification:** 楊 was simplified by replacing 昜 with 𠃓 for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 杨树在公园里生长(楊樹在公園裡生長)，很高大(很高大)。
    - Yángshù zài gōngyuán lǐ shēngzhǎng, hěn gāodà.
    - The willow tree grows in the park, it is very tall.
  - 我家有一棵杨树(我家有一棵楊樹)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǒu yī kē Yángshù.
    - My home has a willow tree.
### 杨(楊) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
### 杨(楊) → 昜(昜)
- **definition:** to project; to shine
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - a component or mark
  - 彡
    - shān
    - bristles or rays
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Comprised of 日 (rì, 'sun') + 丂 + 彡, it represents the rays of the sun over a tree branch.
  - **interpretation:** The image of sunlight shining through branches intuitively conveys the meaning of shining or brightness.
- **examples:**
  - 昜在树枝上照耀。(昜在樹枝上照耀。)
    - yáng zài shùzhī shàng zhàoyào.
    - The rays of the sun shine on the branches.
  - 这幅画中的昜色很美。(這幅畫中的昜色很美。)
    - zhè fú huà zhōng de yángsè hěn měi.
    - The brightness in this painting is beautiful.
