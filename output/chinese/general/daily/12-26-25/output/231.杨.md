## surname Yang
### Yáng
---
- **definition:** surname Yang
- **pinyin:** Yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified character
  - **description:**
    - 楊(楊) (yáng, "willow") ->
    - 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunrise") simplified to 𠃓(𠃓) ("hook shape") ->
    - 杨
  - **interpretation:** 杨 is a simplified form of 楊, where 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunrise") was replaced by 𠃓(𠃓) ("hook shape"), preserving the pronunciation while simplifying the structure.
  - **simplification:** The component 昜(昜) (yáng, "sunrise") was replaced with 𠃓(𠃓) ("hook shape") to create a more streamlined character form.
- **examples:**
  - 杨老师很受学生喜爱(楊老師很受學生喜愛)。
    - Yáng lǎoshī hěn shòu xuéshēng xǐ'ài.
    - Teacher Yang is beloved by the students.
  - 杨家出名(楊家出名)，远近闻名(遠近聞名)。
    - Yáng jiā chūmíng, yuǎnjìn wénmíng.
    - The Yang family is famous far and wide.
  - 杨同学获得了奖学金(楊同學獲得了獎學金)。
    - Yáng tóngxué huòdéle jiǎngxuéjīn.
    - Student Yang received a scholarship.
