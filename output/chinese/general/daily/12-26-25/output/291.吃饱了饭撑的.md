## have nothing better to do
### chī bǎo le fàn chēng de
---
- **definition:** have nothing better to do
- **pinyin:** chī bǎo le fàn chēng de
- **components:**
  - 吃
    - chī
    - eat
  - 饱(飽)
    - bǎo
    - full
  - 了
    - le
    - particle indicating a completed action
  - 饭(飯)
    - fàn
    - meal
  - 撑(撐)
    - chēng
    - support; braced
  - 的
    - de
    - particle for possession or description
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 吃(吃) (chī, "eat") + 饱(飽) (bǎo, "full") + 了(了) (le, particle indicating a completed action) + 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal") + 撑(撐) (chēng, "support; braced") + 的(的) (de, particle for possession or description)
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 吃饱了饭撑的(吃飽了飯撐的) (chī bǎo le fàn chēng de) humorously implies that after eating a full meal, one has no pressing tasks or responsibilities, leading them to expend their energy on unnecessary or excessive actions. It conveys the idea of someone engaging in trivial activities because they have nothing better to do at the moment.
  - **simplification:** Simplified characters: 吃饱了饭撑的
- **examples:**
  - 他吃饱了饭撑的(他吃飽了飯撐的)，去公园散步(去公園散步)。
    - Tā chī bǎo le fàn chēng de, qù gōngyuán sànbù.
    - He has nothing better to do and goes for a walk in the park.
  - 别整天吃饱了饭撑的(別整天吃飽了飯撐的)，浪费时间追剧(浪費時間追劇)。
    - Bié zhěngtiān chī bǎo le fàn chēng de, làngfèi shíjiān zhuījù.
    - Don't just sit around idly and waste time binge-watching dramas.
