### bottle opener
---
- **pinyin:** bottle opener
- **components:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open; start; turn on; unwrap
  - 瓶
    - píng
    - bottle; jar; vase; jug
  - 器
    - qì
    - tool; device; instrument; gadget
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 开(開) (kāi, "open") + 瓶(瓶) (píng, "bottle") + 器(器) (qì, "tool") — open bottle tool
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because a 开瓶器 (kāi píng qì) is a tool specifically used to open bottles, reflecting its functional purpose in the name.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一个开瓶器。
    - Wǒ xūyào yí gè kāi píng qì.
    - I need a bottle opener.
  - 请帮我拿开瓶器。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ ná kāi píng qì.
    - Please help me get the bottle opener.
### 开瓶器(開瓶器) → 开(開)
- **definition:** open; start; turn on; unwrap
- **pinyin:** kāi
- **components:**
  - 門
    - mén
    - door
  - 一
    - yī
    - latch
  - 廾
    - gǒng
    - pair of hands
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** The character depicts a pair of hands (廾) opening a latched door (門) using a latch (一), symbolizing the action of opening.
  - **interpretation:** This representation makes intuitive sense as it visually conveys the action of two hands actively engaging with a door to open it.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by removing the structural component 門, resulting in 开, which retains the core meaning of opening.
- **examples:**
  - 我想开门。(我想開門。)
    - Wǒ xiǎng kāi mén.
    - I want to open the door.
  - 请打开窗户。(請打開窗戶。)
    - Qǐng dǎkāi chuānghù.
    - Please open the window.
