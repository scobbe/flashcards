## Zhongshan District, Taipei
### Zhōngshān Qū
---
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **components:**
  - 中山
    - zhōngshān
    - Zhongshan; Sun Yat-sen
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region; ward
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle") + 山(山) (shān, "mountain") form 中山(中山) (Zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") referring to Sun Yat-sen, a key figure in modern Chinese history. 區(區) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** 中山(中山) (Zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") commemorates Sun Yat-sen, known as the 'Father of the Nation' in Republic of China (Taiwan). 中山区(中山區) (Zhōngshān Qū, "Zhongshan District") in Taipei is named in his honor.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在中山区(我住在中山區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngshān Qū.
    - I live in Zhongshan District.
  - 中山区是台北市的热门商业区(中山區是臺北市的熱門商業區)，生活节奏很快(生活節奏很快)。
    - Zhōngshān Qū shì Táiběi shì de rè mén shāng yè qū，shēng huó jié zòu hěn kuài.
    - Zhongshan District is a popular commercial area in Taipei, where life moves at a fast pace.

### 中山区(中山區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district; area; region; ward
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - box
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - many people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified from 區
  - **description:**
    - 區(區) (qū, "district") originally consists of 匸(匸) (xì, "box") + 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") =
    - representation of multiple people within an enclosed area ->
    - simplification: 品(金) (pǐn, "many people") changed to 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern") for ease
  - **interpretation:** The character 區(區) (qū, "district") visually represented an area containing many people, signifying a district. The simplification to 区(區) (qū, "district") replaced 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") with 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern") which is visually less complex and more cursive-friendly.
  - **simplification:** The original character 區(區) (qū, "district") was simplified to 区(區) (qū, "district") by replacing 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") with the simpler 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern"), making it easier to write and remember while maintaining the sense of an enclosed area.
- **examples:**

    - Chéngshì àn gōngnéng fēnchéng duō gè qū, gè qū gè jù tèsè.
    - The city is divided into several districts based on function, and each district has its own distinct characteristics.
  - 古城设有历史文化区(古城設有歷史文化區)，保护传统韵味(保護傳統韻味)。
