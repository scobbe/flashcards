### Zhōngshān Qū
---
- **traditional:** 中山區
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **components:**

  - **interpretation:** 中山 (Zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") commemorates Sun Yat-sen, known as the 'Father of the Nation' in Republic of China (Taiwan). 中( )山( )区(區) (Zhōngshān Qū, "Zhongshan District") in Taipei is named in his honor.
