## Zhongshan District, Taipei
### Zhōngshān Qū
---
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle; center; China
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain; hill; peak
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle"), 山(山) (shān, "mountain"), 区(區) (qū, "district")
  - **interpretation:** The name 中山區 (Zhōngshān Qū, "Zhongshan District") refers to a district named after 中山(中山) (Zhōngshān), a common place and personal name in Chinese. It likely honors the famous leader Sun Yat-sen, whose name in Mandarin is Sūn Zhōngshān.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在中山区(中山區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngshān Qū.
    - I live in Zhongshan District, Taipei.
  - 中山区繁华热闹(中山區繁華熱鬧)，吸引了许多人(吸引了許多人)。
    - Zhōngshān Qū fánhuá rènào, xīyǐnle xǔduō rén.
    - Zhongshan District is bustling and vibrant, attracting many people.
  - 我在中山区散步(我在中山區散步)，了解了历史(了解了歷史)。
    - Wǒ zài Zhōngshān Qū sànbù, liǎojiěle lìshǐ.
    - I strolled in Zhongshan District and learned about history.
### 中山区(中山區) → 中(中)
- **definition:** middle; center; China
- **pinyin:** zhōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠁩 (flagpole with drum in center) ->
    - 中 (an arrow in the center of a target)
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a flagpole and drum, signifying a gathering point with the drum. Later interpretations see it as an arrow piercing the center of a target, aligning with its meaning of 'middle' or 'center'.
- **examples:**
  - 他站在教室中(他站在教室中)。
    - Tā zhàn zài jiàoshì zhōng.
    - He stands in the middle of the classroom.
  - 我喜欢中国菜(我喜歡中國菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān Zhōngguó cài.
    - I like Chinese food.
  - 我走在街道中(我走在街道中)，看见很多美丽的建筑(看見很多美麗的建築)。
    - Wǒ zǒu zài jiēdào zhōng, kànjiàn hěn duō měilì de jiànzhú.
    - I walk in the middle of the street and see many beautiful buildings.
### 中山区(中山區) → 山(山)
- **definition:** mountain; hill; peak
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Mountain peaks depicted by three strokes → represents a mountain
  - **interpretation:** 山(山) (shān, 'mountain') visually depicts the silhouette of a mountain range. The character gives an intuitive sense of elevation and jagged terrain through the three peaks.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We are going to climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山实在壮观(那座大山實在壯觀)，让人心生敬畏(讓人心生敬畏)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān shí zài zhuàngguān, ràng rén xīnshēng jìngwèi.
    - That mountain is truly magnificent, evoking awe.
  - 古人登山不仅是为了观景(古人登山不僅是為了觀景)，更是在寻找心灵的寄托(更是在尋找心靈的寄托)。
    - Gǔrén dēng shān bù jǐn shì wèi le guānjǐng, gèng shì zài xúnzhǎo xīnlíng de jìtuō.
    - In ancient times, climbing mountains was not only for enjoying the view but also for seeking spiritual solace.
### 中山区(中山區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district; area; region
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") + 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") =
    - many people enclosed ->
    - district/area
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") with 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") to convey the concept of an area where multiple people or goods are contained, illustrating the concept of a district or area.
  - **simplification:** 區(區)→区(區): Simplified by reducing 品(品) (pǐn, "goods/people") inside 匸(匸) (xì, "enclosure") to suit modern writing styles.
- **examples:**
  - 他住在商业区(商業區)。
    - Tā zhù zài shāngyè qū.
    - He lives in the commercial district.
  - 城市里有两个区(區)，一个是商业区(商業區)，另一个是居住区(居住區)。
    - Chéngshì lǐ yǒu liǎng gè qū, yī gè shì shāngyè qū, lìng yī gè shì jūzhù qū.
    - The city has two districts, one is the commercial district and the other is the residential district.
  - 这个区(區)环境优美(環境優美)，吸引了很多游客(吸引了很多遊客)。
    - Zhège qū(區) huánjìng yōuměi(環境優美), xīyǐn le hěn duō yóukè(吸引了很多遊客).
    - This district has a beautiful environment, attracting many tourists.
