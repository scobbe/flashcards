---
- **pinyin:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 中山
    - zhōngshān
    - Zhongshan
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中山(中山) (zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") + 区(區) (qū, "district") — Zhongshan District
  - **interpretation:** This name combines Zhongshan, which is a proper noun referring to a famous historical figure, Dr. Sun Yat-sen, with 区(區) (qū, "district"), indicating a designated administrative area. It makes sense as it designates a location named after a significant person.
- **examples:**

    - Zhōngshān qū gōngyuán hěn měi.
    - Zhongshan District Park is very beautiful.
  - 我住在中山区。(我住在中山區。)
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngshān qū.
    - I live in Zhongshan District.
