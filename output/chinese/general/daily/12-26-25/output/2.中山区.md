## Zhongshan District, Taipei
### Zhōngshān Qū
---
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **components:**
  - 中山
    - zhōngshān
    - Zhongshan; Sun Yat-sen
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; region; ward
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中(中) (zhōng, "middle") + 山(山) (shān, "mountain") form 中山(中山) (Zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") referring to Sun Yat-sen, a key figure in modern Chinese history. 區(區) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** 中山(中山) (Zhōngshān, "Zhongshan") commemorates Sun Yat-sen, known as the 'Father of the Nation' in Republic of China (Taiwan). 中山区(中山區) (Zhōngshān Qū, "Zhongshan District") in Taipei is named in his honor.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在中山区(我住在中山區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Zhōngshān Qū.
    - I live in Zhongshan District.
  - 中山区是台北市的热门商业区(中山區是臺北市的熱門商業區)，生活节奏很快(生活節奏很快)。
    - Zhōngshān Qū shì Táiběi shì de rè mén shāng yè qū，shēng huó jié zòu hěn kuài.
    - Zhongshan District is a popular commercial area in Taipei, where life moves at a fast pace.

### 中山区(中山區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district; area; region; ward
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (enclosed space) + 品 (many people) =
    - many people within an enclosure ->
    - area, district
  - **interpretation:** The character 區(區) (qū, 'district') visually represents an area where many people reside, symbolized by 品(品) (pǐn, 'goods/people') enclosed within 匸(匸) (xì, 'enclosure'). This layout effectively conveys the idea of a defined space containing a community or district.
  - **simplification:** 區(區) was simplified to 区(區) by reducing 品(品) to 乂(乂) (yì, 'to manage'), keeping the enclosure meaning while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在繁华的区(我住在繁華的區)。

    - I live in a bustling district.

    - Gǔ dài wén rén cháng zài zhè qū, xún mì líng gǎn.
    - Ancient literati often sought inspiration in this region.
  - 他参观了多个区(他參觀了多個區)，体验了不同的文化(體驗了不同的文化)。
