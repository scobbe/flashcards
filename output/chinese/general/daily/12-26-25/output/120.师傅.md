## master; skilled worker
### shīfu
---
- **definition:** master; skilled worker
- **pinyin:** shīfu
- **components:**
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - master
  - 傅
    - fù
    - mentor
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 師(師) (shī, "master") refers to a teacher, mentor, or expert in a field. 傅(傅) (fù, "mentor") originally meant a tutor or caretaker. Combined, 师傅(師傅) (shīfu) is used to address someone who is skilled or proficient in a particular craft or trade.
  - **interpretation:** Combining 师(師) (shī, "master") with 傅(傅) (fù, "mentor") creates a respectful term used to acknowledge someone's expertise or craftsmanship.
- **examples:**
  - 师傅(師傅)，我的车坏了(我的車壞了)。
    - Shīfu, wǒ de chē huài le.
    - Master, my car is broken.
  - 那位师傅修理汽车(那位師傅修理汽車)。
    - Nà wèi shīfu xiūlǐ qìchē.
    - That master repairs cars.
