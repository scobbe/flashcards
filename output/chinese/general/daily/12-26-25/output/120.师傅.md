## master; skilled worker
### shīfu
---
- **traditional:** 師傅
- **definition:** master; skilled worker
- **pinyin:** shīfu
- **components:**
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - master; teacher; expert
  - 傅
    - fù
    - instructor; tutor; teacher
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 师(師) (shī, "master") refers to a teacher, expert, or skilled person. 傅 (fù, "instructor") emphasizes the role of guiding or instructing others.
  - **interpretation:** The term 师(師)傅 (shīfu) conveys respect and acknowledgment of expertise or skill, often used to address someone who has acquired a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular field, whether in craftsmanship or teaching.
  - **simplification:** 师傅
- **examples:**
  - 他请师(請師)傅修理自行车(車)。
    - Tā qǐng shīfu xiūlǐ zìxíngchē.
    - He asked the master to repair the bicycle.
  - 师(師)傅手艺(藝)高超，顾(顧)客纷纷赞叹(紛紛讚嘆)。
    - Shīfu shǒuyì gāochāo, gùkè fēnfēn zàntàn.
    - The master's skill is exceptional, and the customers are full of praise.
