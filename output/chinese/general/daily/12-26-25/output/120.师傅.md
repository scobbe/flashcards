## master; skilled worker
### shīfu
---
- **definition:** master; skilled worker
- **pinyin:** shīfu
- **components:**
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - master; teacher; expert
  - 傅
    - fù
    - instructor; tutor; teacher
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 师(師) (shī, "master") refers to a teacher, expert, or skilled person. 傅(傅) (fù, "instructor") emphasizes the role of guiding or instructing others.
  - **interpretation:** The term 师傅(師傅) (shīfu) conveys respect and acknowledgment of expertise or skill, often used to address someone who has acquired a high level of knowledge or skill in a particular field, whether in craftsmanship or teaching.
  - **simplification:** 师傅
- **examples:**
  - 他请师傅修理自行车(他請師傅修理自行車)。
    - Tā qǐng shīfu xiūlǐ zìxíngchē.
    - He asked the master to repair the bicycle.
  - 师傅手艺高超(師傅手藝高超)，顾客纷纷赞叹(顧客紛紛讚嘆)。
    - Shīfu shǒuyì gāochāo, gùkè fēnfēn zàntàn.
    - The master's skill is exceptional, and the customers are full of praise.

### 师傅(師傅) → 师(師) → 𠂤(𠂤)

  - **description:** Depicts mounds or hills
  - **interpretation:** The character 𠂤 is a pictogram that visually resembles hills or mounds. This visual representation suggests its meaning as a natural elevation in the landscape. Alternatively, it can symbolize multiple rooms under a roof in complex characters like 官(官) (guān, "official").

### 师傅(師傅) → 师(師) → 帀(帀)
- **definition:** to surround
- **pinyin:** zā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts surrounding or enclosing
  - **interpretation:** The character 帀 originally depicted the act of surrounding or enclosing an area. Though it does not have additional components to represent phonetics or specific meanings, this depiction is straightforward in illustrating the concept of encirclement.
- **examples:**
  - 河流帀着岛屿(河流帀著島嶼)。
    - Héliú zā zhe dǎoyǔ.
    - The river surrounds the island.
  - 大海帀着孤岛(大海帀著孤島)，巍峨山峰帀着村庄(巍峨山峰帀著村莊)。
    - Dàhǎi zā zhe gūdǎo, wēi'é shānfēng zā zhe cūnzhuāng.
    - The ocean surrounds the solitary island, and the majestic peaks encircle the village.
### 师傅(師傅) → 傅(傅)
- **definition:** instructor; tutor; teacher
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - 亻(人)
    - rén
    - person
  - 尃
    - fū
    - disperse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 亻(人) (rén, "person") + Phonetic: 尃(尃) (fū, "disperse") =
    - person skilled in dispersing knowledge ->
    - instructor
  - **interpretation:** The character 傅(傅) (fù) represents someone who disperses or spreads knowledge, embodying the role of a teacher or tutor. The 人(亻) (rén, "person") component indicates that the term relates to a person, while 尃(尃) (fū, "disperse") suggests the action of communicating or spreading understanding.
- **examples:**
  - 我的傅很耐心(我的傅很耐心)。
    - Wǒ de fù hěn nàixīn.
    - My instructor is very patient.
  - 他是我最敬爱的傅(他是我最敬愛的傅)，总是仔细讲解(總是仔細講解)。
    - Tā shì wǒ zuì jìng'ài de fù, zǒng shì zǐxì jiǎngjiě.
    - He is my most respected tutor, always explaining carefully.
  - 课堂上傅的讲解清晰(課堂上傅的講解清晰)。
    - Kètáng shàng fù de jiǎngjiě qīngxī.
    - The tutor's explanations in class are clear.

### 师傅(師傅) → 傅(傅) → 尃(尃)

- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 甫
    - fǔ
    - garden
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound and ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 甫(甫) (fǔ, "garden") + Semantic: 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") ->
    - original reference to sprout in field ->
    - meaning "to distribute crops"
  - **interpretation:** The character 尃(fū, "disperse") combines the idea of a hand 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") involved in the act of distributing or spreading, alongside the notion of a sprout 甫(甫) (fǔ, "garden") growing in a field. ### 师傅(師傅) → 傅(傅) → 尃(尃) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸(寸) (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布每一寸都是精心制作(這塊布每一寸都是精心製作)。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请用卷尺量一寸(請用卷尺量一寸)，每一寸数据都准确(每一寸數據都準確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.
