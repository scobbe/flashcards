## Montreal
### Méngtèlì'ěr
---
- **definition:** Montreal
- **pinyin:** Méngtèlì'ěr
- **components:**
  - 蒙特
    - Méngtè
    - phonetic ‘Mont-’
  - 利尔(利爾)
    - Lì'ěr
    - phonetic ‘-real/-riel’
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phonetic transliteration
  - **description:** Phonetic transliteration: Méngtè + Lì'ěr ≈ “Montreal”.
  - **interpretation:** A Mandarin rendering of the English/French city name Montreal, chosen to approximate its pronunciation. Characters are selected for sound rather than meaning.
  - **simplification:** 尔 is the simplified form of 爾; Mainland usage standardizes 尔 for the -ěr sound in transliterations to reduce strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我去年去过蒙特利尔(我去年去過蒙特利爾)。
    - Wǒ qùnián qùguò Méngtèlì'ěr.
    - I visited Montreal last year.
  - 蒙特利尔的冬天很冷(蒙特利爾的冬天很冷)。
    - Méngtèlì'ěr de dōngtiān hěn lěng.
    - Winters in Montreal are very cold.
  - 她来自蒙特利尔(她來自蒙特利爾)。
    - Tā láizì Méngtèlì'ěr.
    - She is from Montreal.
### 蒙特利尔(蒙特利爾) → 蒙特
- **definition:** phonetic ‘Mont-’
- **pinyin:** Méngtè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word (phonetic transliteration)
  - **description:** 蒙(Méng) + 特(tè) = phonetic 'Mont-/Monte-'.
  - **interpretation:** Common transliteration chunk for 'Mont-/Monte-' in foreign proper nouns; carries sound value only, not meaning. Often appears at the start of names like 蒙特卡罗 and 蒙特利尔.
- **examples:**
  - 蒙特卡罗很有名(蒙特卡羅很有名)。
    - Méngtè Kǎluó hěn yǒumíng.
    - Monte Carlo is very famous.
  - 他住在蒙特利尔(他住在蒙特利爾)。
    - Tā zhù zài Méngtè Lì'ěr.
    - He lives in Montréal.
  - 我们飞往蒙特雷(我們飛往蒙特雷)。
    - Wǒmen fēiwǎng Méngtè Léi.
    - We are flying to Monterrey.
### 蒙特利尔(蒙特利爾) → 利尔(利爾)
- **definition:** phonetic ‘-real/-riel’
- **pinyin:** Lì'ěr
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** bound transliteration morpheme
  - **description:** 利(lì) + 尔(ěr) = approximates the foreign ending “-real/-riel”.
  - **interpretation:** Used as a phonetic chunk in transliterations to render the syllable sequence -real/-riel, especially in names, e.g., 蒙特利尔 for Montréal. It carries no independent meaning outside such proper names.
  - **simplification:** “爾” was simplified to “尔” by reducing strokes and components in the character; 利 remained unchanged.
