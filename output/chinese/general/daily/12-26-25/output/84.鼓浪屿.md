### Gulangyu Island
---
- **pinyin:** Gulangyu Island
- **components:**
  - 鼓浪
    - gǔlàng
    - drum wave
  - 屿(嶼)
    - yǔ
    - island
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鼓(鼓) (gǔ, 'drum') + 浪(浪) (làng, 'wave') + 屿(嶼) (yǔ, 'island') — drum wave island
  - **interpretation:** 鼓浪屿 evokes the image of waves that are rhythmically beating against the island, similar to drum sounds. This name captures both the natural beauty and the dynamic, lively spirit of the location.
- **examples:**
  - 我在鼓浪屿度假(我在鼓浪嶼度假)，享受美丽的风景(享受美麗的風景)！
    - Wǒ zài Gǔlàngyǔ dùjià, xiǎngshòu měilì de fēngjǐng!
    - I vacation on Gulangyu Island, enjoying the beautiful scenery!
  - 鼓浪屿吸引了许多游客(鼓浪嶼吸引了許多遊客)。
    - Gǔlàngyǔ xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Gulangyu Island attracts many tourists.
