- **definition:** Gulangyu Island
- **pinyin:** Gǔlàngyǔ
- **components:**

    - gǔ
    - drum
  - 浪
    - làng
    - wave
  - 屿(嶼)
    - yǔ
    - islet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鼓(鼓) (gǔ, "drum"); 浪(浪) (làng, "wave"); 屿(嶼) (yǔ, "islet")
  - **interpretation:** 鼓浪屿(鼓浪嶼) (Gǔlàngyǔ, "Gulangyu Island") combines 鼓(鼓) (gǔ, "drum"), 浪(浪) (làng, "wave"), and 屿(嶼) (yǔ, "islet") to describe an island known for its scenic beauty and the rhythmic sound of waves.   - **simplification:** 屿(嶼) (yǔ, "islet") simplified to 屿
- **examples:**

    - Many tourists enjoy visiting Gulangyu Island, admiring the classical architecture.

    - Gǔlàngyǔ yǐ qí dútè fēngqíng xīyǐn yóukè。
    - Gulangyu Island attracts tourists with its unique charm.
  - 我计划冬日游历鼓浪屿(我計劃冬日遊歷鼓浪嶼)，体验海岛风光(體驗海島風光)。
