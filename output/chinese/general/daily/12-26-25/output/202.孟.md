- **pinyin:** Mèng
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
  - 皿
    - mǐn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 子 (zǐ, "child; son") + phonetic 皿 (mǐn, "vessel") =
    - combines the concept of a child or son and the sound of 'mǐn' ->
    - surname Meng
