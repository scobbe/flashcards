## surname Meng
### Mèng
---
- **definition:** surname Meng
- **pinyin:** Mèng
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
  - 皿
    - mǐn
    - container
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 子(子) (zǐ, "child") + 皿(皿) (mǐn, "container") =
    - initial offering to ancestors ->
    - surname Meng
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 子(子) (zǐ, "child") and 皿(皿) (mǐn, "container") suggesting the notion of an "initial offering" or "eldest son," which historically was associated with leadership and respect, fitting the use as a surname.
- **examples:**
  - 孟家族历史悠久(孟家族歷史悠久)。
    - Mèng jiāzú lìshǐ yōujiǔ.
    - The Meng family has a long history.
  - 孟老师讲课生动(孟老師講課生動)，启发学生思考(啟發學生思考)。
    - Mèng lǎoshī jiǎngkè shēngdòng, qǐfā xuéshēng sīkǎo.
    - Teacher Meng teaches vividly and inspires students to think.
  - 孟氏家规代代相传(孟氏家規代代相傳)。
    - Mèng shì jiāguī dàidài xiāngchuán.
    - The Meng family's rules are passed down through generations.
### 孟(孟) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an infant swaddled in cloth ->
    - symbolizes child
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(子) (zǐ, "child") originally depicted an infant wrapped in a cloth, illustrating the concept of a young child. This symbolic representation has expanded over time to include meanings such as "seed" or "particle," aligning with the concept of beginnings.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小子成绩优异(他的小子成績優異)。
    - Tā xiǎo zǐ chéngjì yōuyì.
    - His child has excellent grades.
  - 这颗子将发芽生长(這顆子將發芽生長)。
    - Zhè kē zǐ jiāng fāyá shēngzhǎng.
    - This seed will sprout and grow.
  - 当太阳升起时(當太陽升起時)，小子在院子里欢笑(小子在院子裡歡笑)。
    - Dāng tàiyáng shēngqǐ shí, xiǎo zǐ zài yuànzi lǐ huānxiào.
    - When the sun rises, the child laughs in the courtyard.
### 孟(孟) → 皿(皿)
- **definition:** container
- **pinyin:** mǐn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a container with conjoined handles
  - **interpretation:** The character resembles a simplified outline of a container, typically a chalice or similar vessel, emphasizing its shape by showing handles that are now merged in the modern design.
- **examples:**
  - 这个皿很漂亮(這個皿很漂亮)。
    - Zhège mǐn hěn piàoliang.
    - This vessel is very beautiful.
  - 厨师把食物端到皿上(廚師把食物端到皿上)，准备开始上菜(準備開始上菜)。
    - Chúshī bǎ shíwù duān dào mǐn shàng, zhǔnbèi kāishǐ shàng cài.
    - The chef brought the food onto the vessel and prepared to serve.
  - 他用皿盛着新鲜的果汁(他用皿盛著新鮮的果汁)。
    - Tā yòng mǐn chéngzhe xīnxiān de guǒzhī.
    - He used the vessel to hold fresh juice.
