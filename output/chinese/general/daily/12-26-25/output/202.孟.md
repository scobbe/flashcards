## surname Meng
### Mèng
---
- **definition:** surname Meng
- **pinyin:** Mèng
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
  - 皿
    - mǐn
    - vessel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 子(子) (zǐ, "child; son") + phonetic 皿(皿) (mǐn, "vessel") =
    - combines the concept of a child or son and the sound of 'mǐn' ->
    - surname Meng
  - **interpretation:** The character 孟(mèng) combines the semantic element 子(子) (zǐ, "child; son") with 皿(皿) (mǐn, "vessel") to suggest a concept related to lineage or ancestral identity, as it often refers to the eldest brother or firstborn in a family, aligning with its usage as a surname.
- **examples:**
  - 孟老师很受学生欢迎(孟老師很受學生歡迎)。
    - Mèng lǎoshī hěn shòu xuéshēng huānyíng.
    - Teacher Meng is well-liked by the students.
  - 孟先生今天出席会议(孟先生今天出席會議)，分享了成功经验(分享了成功經驗)。
    - Mèng xiānshēng jīntiān chūxí huìyì, fēnxiǎngle chénggōng jīngyàn.
    - Mr. Meng attended the meeting today and shared his successful experience.
  - 《史记》记载有孟嘗君(《史記》記載有孟嘗君)，他心怀仁义(他心懷仁義)。
    - 《Shǐjì》 jìzǎi yǒu Mèngchángjūn, tā xīn huái rényì.
    - The Records of the Grand Historian mentions Lord Mengchang, who was known for his benevolence.
### 孟(孟) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a baby with a large head and spread arms, with legs wrapped in a blanket
  - **interpretation:** The character 子(zǐ, "child") is a pictorial representation of a baby, emphasizing characteristics like a large head and outstretched arms typical of infants, which conveys the essence of a child. This visual element helps infer its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这小子很调皮(這小子很調皮)。
    - Zhè xiǎo zǐ hěn tiáopí.
    - This kid is very naughty.
  - 他的小子爱打篮球(他的小子愛打籃球)，每天都锻炼身体(每天都鍛煉身體)。
    - Tā de xiǎo zǐ ài dǎ lánqiú, měitiān dōu duànliàn shēntǐ.
    - His child loves playing basketball, and practices every day.
  - 孤独的子需要关爱(孤獨的子需要關愛)。
    - Gūdú de zǐ xūyào guān'ài.
    - A lonely child needs care.
### 孟(孟) → 皿(皿)
- **definition:** vessel
- **pinyin:** mǐn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an empty container, such as a chalice ->
    - modern form conjoins handles ->
    - dish
  - **interpretation:** The character 皿(mǐn, 'dish') is a straightforward pictogram representing an empty container, highlighting its function to hold items, a role akin to modern dishes.
- **examples:**
  - 厨房里有一只精致的皿(廚房裡有一隻精緻的皿)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ yǒu yī zhī jīngzhì de mǐn。
    - There is a delicate dish in the kitchen.
  - 妈妈把菜盛在皿里(媽媽把菜盛在皿裡)，准备享用(準備享用)。
    - Māma bǎ cài shèng zài mǐn lǐ, zhǔnbèi xiǎngyòng。
    - Mom served the food in the dish, ready to be enjoyed.
