### surname Meng
---
- **pinyin:** surname Meng
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
  - 皿
    - mǐn
    - dish; container
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 子 (子) (zǐ, 'child; son') with the phonetic component 皿 (皿) (mǐn).
  - **interpretation:** The inclusion of 子 suggests a connection to the younger generation, while 皿 provides the phonetic sound, which helps in pronouncing the character.
- **examples:**
  - 我叫孟老师(我叫孟老師)，他教数学(他教數學)！
    - Wǒ jiào Mèng lǎoshī, tā jiāo shùxué!
    - I am called Teacher Meng, he teaches mathematics!
  - 孟子是伟大的思想家(孟子是偉大的思想家)。
    - Mèngzǐ shì wěidà de sīxiǎngjiā.
    - Mengzi is a great thinker.
### 孟(孟) → 子(子)
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a child or infant.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a small person or child, intuitively symbolizing youth and innocence.
- **examples:**
  - 这是我的孩子(這是我的孩子)。
    - Zhè shì wǒ de háizi.
    - This is my child.
  - 子女(子女)都很聪明(都很聰明)。
    - Zǐnǚ dōu hěn cōngmíng.
    - The children are all very smart.
### 孟(孟) → 皿(皿)
- **definition:** dish; container
- **pinyin:** mǐn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a shallow container or dish.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a dish or container, capturing its flat and hollow shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这个皿很漂亮(這個皿很漂亮)。
    - Zhège mǐn hěn piàoliang.
    - This dish is very beautiful.
  - 我需要一个新的皿(我需要一個新的皿)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yīgè xīn de mǐn.
    - I need a new container.
