- **definition:** be allergic
- **pinyin:** guòmǐn
- **components:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - exceed; pass through
  - 敏
    - mǐn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 过(過) (guò, "exceed") + 敏(敏) (mǐn, "sensitive")
  - **interpretation:** 过敏(過敏) (guòmǐn, "be allergic") consists of 过(過) (guò, "exceed") and 敏(敏) (mǐn, "sensitive"), implying a reaction where the sensitivity of the body exceeds a normal level. - **examples:**
  - 我很容易过敏(我很容易過敏)。
    - Wǒ hěn róngyì guòmǐn.

    - Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn, měi dào chūntiān jiù dǎ pēntì.

  - 出门前他总会检查食物(出門前他總會檢查食物)，以防过敏(以防過敏)。
    - Chūmén qián tā zǒng huì jiǎnchá shíwù, yǐ fáng guòmǐn.
    - Before going out, he always checks his food to prevent allergic reactions.
