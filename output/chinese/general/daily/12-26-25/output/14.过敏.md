### be allergic
---
- **pinyin:** be allergic
- **components:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - pass; exceed; go through; spend (time)
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - sensitive; quick; agile; clever
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 过(過) (guò, "pass; exceed") + 敏(敏) (mǐn, "sensitive; quick") — exceed sensitivity
  - **interpretation:** This term describes a condition where one's sensitivity to certain substances exceeds normal levels. The combination effectively conveys a concept where sensitivity is pronounced, which aligns with the experience of allergy sufferers.
- **examples:**
  - 我对花粉过敏(我對花粉過敏)，每年春天都很难受(每年春天都很難受)。
    - Wǒ duì huāfěn guò mǐn, měinián chūntiān dōu hěn nánshòu。
    - I'm allergic to pollen; I suffer every spring.
  - 如果你有过敏的症状(如果你有過敏的症狀)，应该去医院检查(應該去醫院檢查)。
    - Rúguǒ nǐ yǒu guò mǐn de zhèngzhuàng, yīnggāi qù yīyuàn jiǎnchá。
    - If you have allergic symptoms, you should go to the hospital for a check-up.
### 过敏(過敏) → 过(過)
- **definition:** pass; exceed; go through; spend (time)

  - 咼

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic part 辵 (denoting 'walk') with the phonetic part 咼.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of movement or passing through a space, aligning well with its meanings of going through or exceeding.
  - **simplification:** The simplification replaced the traditional 鼠 component with the more straightforward 过 to reduce the stroke count while maintaining the character's phonetic element.
- **examples:**
