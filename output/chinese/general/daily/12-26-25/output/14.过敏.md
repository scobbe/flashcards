## be allergic
### guòmǐn
---
- **traditional:** 過敏
- **definition:** be allergic
- **pinyin:** guòmǐn
- **components:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - exceed; pass through
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - sensitive; quick
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 过(過) (guò, "exceed") + 敏 (mǐn, "sensitive")
  - **interpretation:** 过(過)敏 (guòmǐn, "be allergic") consists of 过(過) (guò, "exceed") and 敏 (mǐn, "sensitive"), implying a reaction where the sensitivity of the body exceeds a normal level. Hence, it describes an allergic reaction where the body's response to certain stimuli is excessively sensitive.
  - **simplification:** simplified to remove extra strokes
- **examples:**
  - 我很容易过(過)敏。
    - Wǒ hěn róngyì guòmǐn.
    - I am easily allergic.
  - 我对(對)花粉过(過)敏，每到春天就打喷(噴)嚏。
    - Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn, měi dào chūntiān jiù dǎ pēntì.
    - I am allergic to pollen, and I sneeze every spring.
  - 出门(門)前他总会检(總會檢)查食物，以防过(過)敏。
    - Chūmén qián tā zǒng huì jiǎnchá shíwù, yǐ fáng guòmǐn.
    - Before going out, he always checks his food to prevent allergic reactions.
### 过(過)敏( ) → 过(過)
- **traditional:** 過
- **definition:** exceed; pass through
- **pinyin:** guò
- **components:**
  - 辵(辶)
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 咼
    - guō
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic 辵(辶) (chuò, "walk") + phonetic 咼 (guō) =
    - walking/passing ->
    - exceed/pass through
  - **interpretation:** 過 (guò, "exceed; pass through") combines the walking radical 辵(辶) (chuò, "walk") with the phonetic 咼 (guō), suggesting the concept of moving past or over something. This aligns with the meanings of exceeding limits or passing through a place.
  - **simplification:** The character 过 was simplified by removing the top part of 辵(辶) (chuò, "walk"), reducing the character to three strokes in 过.
- **examples:**
  - 他过马(過馬)路时(時)，一直保持警觉(覺)。
    - Tā guò mǎlù shí, yīzhí bǎochí jǐngjué.

    - Zhège shùzì guòle biāozhǔn.
