## be allergic
### guòmǐn
---
- **definition:** be allergic
- **pinyin:** guòmǐn
- **components:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - exceed; pass; through
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - sensitive; quick; clever
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 过(過) (guò, "exceed") + 敏(敏) (mǐn, "sensitive") =
    - an excessive sensitivity or response
  - **interpretation:** The word 过敏(過敏) (guòmǐn, "be allergic") describes a state wherein one's sensitivity is exceeded, as allergies involve an excessive reaction to substances that are generally harmless.
- **examples:**
  - 我对花粉过敏(我對花粉過敏)。
    - Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn.
    - I am allergic to pollen.
  - 由于灰尘过敏(由於灰塵過敏)，他经常打喷嚏(他經常打噴嚏)。
    - Yóuyú huīchén guòmǐn, tā jīngcháng dǎ pēntì.
    - Because he is allergic to dust, he often sneezes.
  - 每当接触宠物毛发(每當接觸寵物毛髮)，我都会过敏(我都會過敏)。
    - Měi dāng jiēchù chǒngwù máofǎ, wǒ dōu huì guòmǐn.
    - Whenever I come into contact with pet fur, I become allergic.
