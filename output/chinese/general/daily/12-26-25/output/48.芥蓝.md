## Chinese broccoli
### gàilán
---
- **definition:** Chinese broccoli
- **pinyin:** gàilán
- **components:**
  - 芥
    - gài
    - mustard; herb
  - 蓝(藍)
    - lán
    - blue; indigo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 芥(芥) (gài, "mustard; herb") + 蓝(藍) (lán, "blue; indigo") =
    - a type of vegetable resembling mustard, often with associated blue-green leaves, thus known as Chinese broccoli.
  - **interpretation:** 芥(芥) (gài, "mustard; herb") and 蓝(藍) (lán, "blue; indigo") combine to describe a leafy green vegetable, commonly known as Chinese broccoli. The term likely refers to the vegetable's mustard-like qualities and its typical blue-green leaf color.
  - **simplification:** 芥(芥) was simplified to 芥 and 藍 was simplified to 蓝.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃芥蓝(芥藍)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī gàilán.
    - I like eating Chinese broccoli.
  - 这道菜里有芥蓝(芥藍)，味道非常鲜美(味道非常鮮美)。
    - Zhè dào cài lǐ yǒu gàilán, wèidào fēicháng xiānměi.
    - This dish contains Chinese broccoli, and its flavor is very fresh and delicious.

  - **interpretation:** The character 芥(gài) is composed of 艸(艸), signifying its association with plants or herbs, combined with 介(介), which gives the sound and contributes to the idea of it being a specific kind of plant, namely mustard.

### 芥蓝(芥藍) → 蓝(藍)
- **definition:** blue; indigo
- **pinyin:** lán
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 监(監)
    - jiān
    - supervise
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") + 監(监) (jiān, "supervise") =
    - phonetic 监 with 草 radical for plants ->
    - indigo plant, blue color
  - **interpretation:** The character 蓝(藍) (lán, "blue") uses 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") to signify its connection to plants, combined with 監(监) (jiān, "supervise") for phonetic purposes. It historically referred to the indigo dye derived from plants, which corresponds to its usage in denoting the color blue.
  - **simplification:** 蓝(藍) was simplified by altering the phonetic component 監(监) with its simpler variant 监(监), keeping the meaning intact.
- **examples:**

  - 她穿着蓝裙子(她穿著藍裙子)，看起来十分迷人(看起來十分迷人)。

    - She wears a blue dress, looking very charming.
