## Chinese broccoli
### gàilán
---
- **definition:** Chinese broccoli
- **pinyin:** gàilán
- **components:**
  - 芥
    - jiè
    - mustard
  - 蓝(藍)
    - lán
    - blue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 芥(芥) (jiè, "mustard") + 藍(蓝) (lán, "blue") =
    - a type of vegetable, specifically Chinese broccoli
  - **interpretation:** 芥(芥) (jiè, "mustard") conveys a connection to the mustard family, while 藍(蓝) (lán, "blue") might relate to the common name or appearance. Together, they form the name for Chinese broccoli, known for its similarity to mustard greens.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃芥蓝(我喜歡吃芥藍)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī gàilán.
    - I like to eat Chinese broccoli.
  - 这家餐厅的芥蓝很新鲜(這家餐廳的芥藍很新鮮)，色泽诱人(色澤誘人)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng de gàilán hěn xīnxiān, sèzé yòurén.
    - The Chinese broccoli at this restaurant is very fresh, with an appealing color.
