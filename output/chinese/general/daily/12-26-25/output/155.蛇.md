## 蛇(蛇)
---
## 
### snake
---
- **pinyin:** snake
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a snake, showing its long, winding form.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a snake, a creature known for its elongated body and ability to glide smoothly on the ground. This depiction intuitively captures the essence of the animal's shape.
- **examples:**
  - 我看见一条蛇。
    - wǒ kànjiàn yī tiáo shé.
    - I saw a snake.
  - 蛇是冷血动物。
    - shé shì lěngxuè dòngwù.
    - Snakes are cold-blooded animals.
  - 他很怕蛇。
    - tā hěn pà shé.
    - He is very afraid of snakes.
