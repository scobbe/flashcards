## give as a present; send to
### sònggěi
---
- **definition:** give as a present; send to
- **pinyin:** sònggěi
- **components:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - deliver; send; carry out; see off
  - 给(給)
    - gěi
    - to give; for; provide; allow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 送(送) (sòng, "deliver; send") and 给(給) (gěi, "to give") combine to describe the action of giving or sending something to someone, often as a present or gift.
  - **interpretation:** 送给(送給) (sònggěi) makes sense as it combines the concepts of sending or delivering (送) with the notion of giving (给), highlighting the act of gifting or presenting something to someone.
- **examples:**
  - 我写了一张贺卡(我寫了一張賀卡)，送给远方的朋友(送給遠方的朋友)。
    - Wǒ xiěle yī zhāng hèkǎ, sònggěi yuǎnfāng de péngyǒu.
    - I wrote a greeting card and sent it to a friend far away.
  - 他把鲜花送给母亲(他把鮮花送給母親)。
    - Tā bǎ xiānhuā sònggěi mǔqīn.
    - He sent flowers to his mother.
