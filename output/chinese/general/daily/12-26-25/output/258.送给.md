## give as a present; send to
### sònggěi
---
- **definition:** give as a present; send to
- **pinyin:** sònggěi
- **components:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - send; deliver; see off
  - 给(給)
    - gěi
    - give; for; to
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 送(送) (sòng, "send; deliver") and 给(給) (gěi, "give") together refer to the action of giving something to someone, especially as a gift or present.
  - **interpretation:** 送(送) (sòng, "send; deliver") implies the act of delivering or sending something, while 给(給) (gěi, "give") indicates the act of giving. Together, they form the concept of presenting something to someone, highlighting both the act of delivery and the intention of giving.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈送给我一件礼物(媽媽送給我一件禮物)。
    - Māmā sònggěi wǒ yī jiàn lǐwù.
    - Mother gave me a gift.
  - 他送给朋友一本书(他送給朋友一本書)，表达了他深厚的友谊(表達了他深厚的友誼)。
    - Tā sònggěi péngyǒu yī běn shū, biǎodále tā shēnhòu de yǒuyì.
    - He gave his friend a book, expressing his deep friendship.
  - 老师送给学生一张作业表(老師送給學生一張作業表)。
    - Lǎoshī sònggěi xuéshēng yī zhāng zuòyè biǎo.
    - The teacher gave the student an assignment sheet.

### 送给(送給) → 给(給)
- **definition:** give; for; to
- **pinyin:** gěi
- **components:**
  - 糹
    - sī
    - silk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Simplified form
  - **description:**
    - 絜(絜) (gěi, "give") ->
    - 給(給) (gěi, "give"), where 絜's 糹(糹) (sī, "silk") is simplified to 纟(纟) (sī) ->
    - 给(給)
  - **interpretation:** The character 给(gěi, "give") simplifies 給(給) by replacing its silk radical 糹(糹) with a simpler form 纟(纟). This reflects a common method in character simplification to make writing faster and easier.
  - **simplification:** The silk radical 糹(糹) (sī) was simplified to 纟(纟) (sī) in the character 給(給), resulting in the simplified form 给(gěi, "give").
- **examples:**
