### chant; eulogize
---
- **pinyin:** chant; eulogize
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 永
    - yǒng
    - forever
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic element 言 (言) (yán, 'speech') and the phonetic element 永 (永) (yǒng, 'forever').
  - **interpretation:** The character reflects a long-lasting or eternal expression of speech, suggesting a poetic or chant-like quality. This connection to both speech and permanence conveys the idea of enduring praise or eulogy.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢诗歌和咏叹调(我喜歡詩歌和詠嘆調)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān shīgē hé yǒngtàn diào.
    - I enjoy poetry and chants.
  - 他的作品中有许多咏叹(他的作品中有許多詠嘆)。
    - Tā de zuòpǐn zhōng yǒu xǔduō yǒngtàn.
    - His works contain many eulogies.
### 咏(詠) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tongue (舌) moving, indicating speech.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively conveys the concept of speech through its representation of the tongue's movement as essential for verbal expression.
- **examples:**
  - 他说得很好(他說得很好)。
    - tā shuō de hěn hǎo.
    - He speaks very well.
  - 我喜欢听你讲话(我喜歡聽你講話)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān tīng nǐ jiǎnghuà.
    - I enjoy listening to you speak.
### 咏(詠) → 永(永)
- **definition:** forever
- **pinyin:** yǒng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a long river with tributaries.
  - **interpretation:** A river flowing continuously implies the concept of forever, as rivers don't stop flowing. The visual representation effectively conveys the idea of something lasting indefinitely.
- **examples:**
  - 永远支持你(永遠支持你)，我会陪伴你(我會陪伴你)。
    - yǒngyuǎn zhīchí nǐ, wǒ huì péibàn nǐ.
    - I will support you forever, and I will be by your side.
  - 爱是永恒的(愛是永恆的)。
    - Ài shì yǒnghéng de.
    - Love is eternal.
