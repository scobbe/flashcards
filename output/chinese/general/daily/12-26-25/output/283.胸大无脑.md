## bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
### xiōng dà wú nǎo
---
- **definition:** bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
- **pinyin:** xiōng dà wú nǎo
- **components:**
  - 胸大
    - xiōng dà
    - big-chested; large-breasted; busty; big chest
  - 无脑(無腦)
    - wú nǎo
    - brainless; mindless; thoughtless; dim-witted
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 胸大 (big chest) + 无脑 (brainless) = derogatory label implying attractiveness without intelligence.
  - **interpretation:** A pejorative internet slang phrase stereotyping women as busty but unintelligent; often considered sexist and insulting.
  - **simplification:** 无 and 脑 are simplified from 無 and 腦 to reduce stroke count; the body-part radical 月 is retained while complex components were streamlined in PRC orthographic reform.
- **examples:**
  - 她被贴上“胸大无脑”的标签(她被貼上「胸大無腦」的標籤)。
    - Tā bèi tiē shàng “xiōng dà wú nǎo” de biāoqiān.
    - She was labeled as a “bimbo.”
  - 这种说法带有性别歧视(這種說法帶有性別歧視)，不应随便使用(不應隨便使用)。
    - Zhè zhǒng shuōfǎ dàiyǒu xìngbié qíshì, bù yīng suíbiàn shǐyòng.
    - This expression is sexist and shouldn’t be used casually.
  - 有人反驳说能力与身材无关(有人反駁說能力與身材無關)。
    - Yǒu rén fǎnbó shuō nénglì yǔ shēncái wúguān.
    - Some argue that ability has nothing to do with body shape.

### 胸大无脑(胸大無腦) → 无脑(無腦)
- **definition:** brainless; mindless; thoughtless; dim-witted
- **pinyin:** wú nǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 无 ‘without’ + 脑 ‘brain’ = lacking brains → foolish; dim.
  - **interpretation:** Colloquial pejorative for acting without thinking or lacking judgment, common in internet slang. Often appears in set phrases like 无脑粉/无脑黑 for mindless fans/haters.
  - **simplification:** Both characters were simplified: 无 adopts an older cursive/ancient variant of 無; 腦 was streamlined to 脑 to reduce strokes in the phonetic component; pronunciation and meaning stayed the same.
- **examples:**
  - 他这种无脑的跟风太可笑了(他這種無腦的跟風太可笑了)。

  - 别做无脑投资(別做無腦投資)，要先研究风险(要先研究風險)。
    - Bié zuò wú nǎo tóuzī, yào xiān yánjiū fēngxiǎn.
    - Don't make brainless investments; study the risks first.
  - 网上有些无脑黑只会喷人(網上有些無腦黑只會噴人)。
