### bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
---
- **pinyin:** bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
- **components:**
  - 胸大
    - xiōng dà
    - big chest
  - 无脑(無腦)
    - wú nǎo
    - no brain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 胸(胸) (xiōng, 'chest') + 大(大) (dà, 'big') + 无(無) (wú, 'no') + 脑(腦) (nǎo, 'brain') — big chest no brain
  - **interpretation:** This phrase humorously describes someone who is attractive physically but perceived as lacking intelligence. Each component contributes to the overall meaning: 'big chest' refers to physical appeal while 'no brain' denotes a lack of smarts.
- **examples:**
  - 她被称作胸大无脑(她被稱作胸大無腦)，因为她只关注外表。
    - tā bèi chēng zuò xiōng dà wú nǎo, yīnwèi tā zhǐ guānzhù wàibiǎo.
    - She is called a bimbo because she only cares about appearance.
  - 很多人认为他是个胸大无脑(胸大無腦)的女孩。
    - hěn duō rén rènwéi tā shì gè xiōng dà wú nǎo de nǚhái.
    - Many people think she is a bimbo.
