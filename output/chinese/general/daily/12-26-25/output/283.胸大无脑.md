## bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
### xiōng dà wú nǎo
---
- **definition:** bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
- **pinyin:** xiōng dà wú nǎo
- **components:**
  - 胸
    - xiōng
    - chest
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
  - 无(無)
    - wú
    - no
  - 脑(腦)
    - nǎo
    - brain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 胸(胸) (xiōng, "chest"), 大(大) (dà, "big"), 无(無) (wú, "no"), 脑(腦) (nǎo, "brain")
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 胸大无脑 (胸大無腦) (xiōng dà wú nǎo) combines the concepts of having a 'big chest' and 'no brain' to describe someone perceived as physically attractive but lacking intelligence. It reflects a stereotype often associated with women, although the term is derogatory.
  - **simplification:** 无 to 無; 腦 to 脑
- **examples:**
  - 很多人误认为她是胸大无脑(很多人誤認為她是胸大無腦)，但是她实际上非常聪明(但是她實際上非常聰明)。
    - Hěn duō rén wù rènwéi tā shì xiōng dà wú nǎo, dànshì tā shíjì shàng fēicháng cōngmíng.
    - Many people mistakenly think she is a bimbo, but in fact she is very smart.
  - 有人甚至用胸大无脑(有人甚至用胸大無腦)来形容那些轻浮的人(來形容那些輕浮的人)。
    - Yǒurén shènzhì yòng xiōng dà wú nǎo lái xíngróng nàxiē qīngfú de rén.
    - Some even use 'big chest no brains' to describe those frivolous people.

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匕(匕) (bǐ, "spoon") + 巛(巛) (chuān, "hair") + 囟(囟) (xìn, "head; fontanel") ->
