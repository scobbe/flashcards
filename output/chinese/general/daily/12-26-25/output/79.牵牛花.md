## morning glory
### qiānniúhuā
---
- **traditional:** 牽牛花
- **definition:** morning glory
- **pinyin:** qiānniúhuā
- **components:**
  - 牵(牽)
    - qiān
    - to lead
  - 牛
    - niú
    - ox
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 牵(牽) (qiān, "to lead") + 牛 (niú, "ox") + 花 (huā, "flower") =
    - morning glory, a flower that twines around like it is leading an ox
  - **interpretation:** The name 牵(牽)牛花 (qiānniúhuā, "morning glory") evokes the image of the flower's tendrils twining around supports in a manner reminiscent of how a person might lead an ox by a rope. The plant's climbing habit is likened to the motion of guiding an ox.
- **examples:**
  - 清晨时(時)分，我在花园里发现(園裡發現)了美丽(麗)的牵(牽)牛花。
    - Qīngchén shífèn, wǒ zài huāyuán lǐ fāxiànle měilì de qiānniúhuā.
    - Early in the morning, I discovered beautiful morning glories in the garden.
  - 花架上爬满(滿)了牵(牽)牛花。
    - Huājià shàng pá mǎnle qiānniúhuā.
    - The trellis is covered with morning glories.
  - 黄(黃)昏时(時)刻，夕阳(陽)映照的牵(牽)牛花，显(顯)得格外动(動)人。
    - Huánghūn shíkè, xīyáng yìngzhào de qiānniúhuā, xiǎnde géwài dòngrén.
    - At dusk, the morning glories illuminated by the setting sun looked exceptionally enchanting.
### 牵(牽)牛( )花( ) → 牵(牽)
- **traditional:** 牽
- **definition:** to lead
- **pinyin:** qiān
- **components:**
  - 牜(牛)
    - niú
    - ox
  - 肙(玄)
    - xuán
    - mysterious
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 牜(牛) (niú, 'ox') + phonetic: 肙(玄) (xuán, 'mysterious/deep') =
    - leading an ox ->
    - lead/guide
  - **interpretation:** The character 牵(牽) (qiān, 'to lead') conveys the idea of leading an ox, a common agricultural activity in ancient China. The semantic component 牛 (niú, 'ox') represents the act of leading livestock, while the phonetic component 玄 (xuán, 'mysterious') contributes to the sound.
  - **simplification:** 牽 (qiān) was simplified by replacing 玄 with a simplified component structure to streamline writing.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈牵着(媽媽牽著)我，走在街上。
    - Māmā qiānzhe wǒ, zǒu zài jiēshàng.

  - 父亲牵着(親牽著)牛，穿过(過)田野。
    - Fùqīn qiānzhe niú, chuānguò tiányě.
    - Father leads the cow across the fields.
  - 京剧(劇)演员牵着(員牽著)木偶，演绎(繹)古典故事。
    - Jīngjù yǎnyuán qiānzhe mùǒu, yǎnyì gǔdiǎn gùshì.
    - The Peking opera actor leads the puppet to perform a classical story.

  - 化

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound

    - flower
  - **interpretation:** 花 (huā, "flower") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") indicating its association with vegetation, and 化 (huà, "transform") for its phonetic value. This illustrates both the natural aspect and the phonetic pronunciation.
