## morning glory
### qiānniúhuā
---
- **definition:** morning glory
- **pinyin:** qiānniúhuā
- **components:**
  - 牵(牽)
    - qiān
    - lead; pull; guide
  - 牛
    - niú
    - ox; cow
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 牛(牛) (niú, "ox") and 花(花) (huā, "flower") are combined to depict a plant called morning glory, often climbing in nature like leading or guiding an ox.
  - **interpretation:** 牵(牽) (qiān, "lead") and 牛(牛) (niú, "ox") together give the imagery of guiding or leading like leading an ox, while 花(花) (huā, "flower") refers to the flowering aspect of the morning glory. The term refers to the plant's vine-like growth that is reminiscent of leading an ox by pulling.
- **examples:**
  - 早晨来临时(早晨來臨時)，牵牛花绽放出迷人的色彩(牽牛花綻放出迷人的色彩)。
    - Zǎochén láilín shí, qiānniúhuā zhànfàng chū mírén de sècǎi.
    - When morning arrives, the morning glories burst into enchanting colors.
  - 校园小径旁(校園小徑旁)，牵牛花依附着古老的篱笆绽放(牽牛花依附著古老的籬笆綻放)。
    - Xiàoyuán xiǎojìng páng, qiānniúhuā yīfùzhe gǔlǎo de líbā zhànfàng.
    - Along the school path, the morning glories bloom as they cling to the ancient fence.
### 牵牛花(牽牛花) → 牵(牽)
- **definition:** lead; pull; guide
- **pinyin:** qiān
- **components:**
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
  - 亀(龜)
    - guī
    - turtle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") + phonetic: 亀(龜) (guī, "turtle") =
    - guiding a cow (牽) ->
    - lead, pull, guide
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") indicating the action involves cattle, with 亀(龜) (guī, "turtle") acting as a phonetic component. This imagery suggests the act of pulling or leading an animal.
  - **simplification:** 牽(牽)→牵(牽): The component 亀(龜) was simplified to resemble 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") to make the character easier to write while maintaining its association with leading or pulling.
- **examples:**
  - 母亲牵着孩子(母親牽著孩子)，走在乡间的小路上(走在鄉間的小路上)。
    - Mǔqīn qiānzhe háizi, zǒu zài xiāngjiān de xiǎolù shàng.
    - The mother leads her child, walking on a country lane.
  - 他牵着猫(他牽著貓)，慢慢地走回家(慢慢地走回家)。
    - Tā qiānzhe māo, màn màn de zǒu huí jiā.
    - He leads his cat, slowly walking back home.
  - 老师牵着学生(老師牽著學生)，参观博物馆(參觀博物館)。
    - Lǎoshī qiānzhe xuéshēng, cānguān bówùguǎn.
    - The teacher leads the students to visit the museum.
