### morning glory
---
- **pinyin:** morning glory
- **components:**
  - 牵(牽)
    - qiān
    - pull; lead; guide; drag
  - 牛花
    - niúhuā
    - cow flower; morning glory; flowering plant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 牵(牽) (qiān, "pull") + 牛花(牛花) (niúhuā, "cow flower") — pull cow flower
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense because the plant is often associated with climbing or winding around structures, thus the term implies a sense of 'pulling' as it grows. The 'cow' in 'cow flower' refers to its herbaceous nature, often found in fields.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢牵牛花(我喜歡牽牛花)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān qiānniúhuā.
    - I like morning glory.
  - 牵牛花在夏天开得很漂亮(牽牛花在夏天開得很漂亮)。
    - Qiānniúhuā zài xiàtiān kāi de hěn piàoliang.
    - Morning glories bloom beautifully in the summer.
