## exceed; surpass
### chāo
---
- **definition:** exceed; surpass
- **pinyin:** chāo
- **components:**
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - run
  - 召
    - zhào
    - [phonetic]
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 走(走) (zǒu, "run") + 召(召) (zhào) =
    - running beyond a target ->
    - exceed; surpass
  - **interpretation:** The character 超(chāo) combines 走(走) (zǒu, "run") for the concept of movement with 召(召) (zhào) as a phonetic, suggesting the idea of running past or exceeding a limit or target.
- **examples:**
  - 他的成绩超出预期(他的成績超出預期)。
    - Tā de chéngjì chāo chū yùqī.
    - His grades exceed expectations.
  - 这场比赛超越极限(這場比賽超越極限)。
    - Zhè chǎng bǐsài chāo yuè jíxiàn.
    - This match surpasses the limits.
### 超(超) → 走(走)
- **definition:** run
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - A running man ->
    - addition of 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") ->
    - 夭(夭) (yāo, "twist") simplified to 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") ->
    - stylized as 龰(龰) ("running vehicle")
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicting a person running, the character evolved by adding the component 止(止) to emphasize movement and using 土(土) for visual simplification. Its modern form retains the sense of motion through the stylized foot representation.
  - **simplification:** No simplification from traditional to simplified form as 走 and 赱 are identical in essence, although 赱 is a variant. In modern usage, 赱 is rarely used while 走 is standard.
- **examples:**
  - 危险出现时(危險出現時)，他立即走了(他立即走了)。
    - Wēixiǎn chūxiàn shí, tā lìjí zǒu le.
    - When danger appeared, he immediately ran away.
  - 为了赶上火车(為了趕上火車)，她拼命走出家门(她拼命走出家門)。
    - Wèile gǎn shàng huǒchē, tā pīnmìng zǒu chū jiāmén.
    - In order to catch the train, she ran out of the house desperately.
### 超(超) → 召(召)
- **definition:** [phonetic]
- **pinyin:** zhào
- **components:**
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic: 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") + Semantic: 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") → call out with mouth or serve wine → 召(召) (zhào, "summon")
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted hands over a wine vessel, representing inviting or serving. The modern form evolved by combining 刀(刀) (dāo, "knife") as a phonetic element with 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") to convey the act of calling or summoning.
- **examples:**
  - 国王召见大臣(國王召見大臣)。
    - Guówáng zhàojiàn dàchén.
    - The king summons his ministers.
  - 他受命于上(他受命於上)，召集同伴(召集同伴)。
    - Tā shòumìng yú shàng, zhàojí tóngbàn.
    - He, having received orders from above, summons his companions.
  - 老将军召唤部队(老將軍召喚部隊)。
    - Lǎo jiāngjūn zhàohuàn bùduì.
    - The veteran general calls his troops.
### 超(超) → 召(召) → 刀(刀)
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a knife or blade
  - **interpretation:** 刀(刀) (dāo, 'knife') resembles the shape of a knife with a handle and blade. It is a straightforward representation of the tool.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿着锋利的刀(他拿著鋒利的刀)，准备切肉(準備切肉)。
    - Tā názhe fēnglì de dāo, zhǔnbèi qiē ròu.
    - He holds a sharp knife, ready to cut meat.
  - 厨房里放着一把古老的刀(廚房裡放著一把古老的刀)，看起来非常锋利(看起來非常鋒利)。
    - Chúfáng lǐ fàngzhe yī bǎ gǔlǎo de dāo, kàn qǐlái fēicháng fēnglì.
    - In the kitchen, an old knife is displayed, and it looks very sharp.
### 超(超) → 召(召) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") visually represents an open mouth, making it straightforward to connect to its meaning related to speaking and openings.
- **examples:**
  - 请张开你的口(請張開你的口)。
    - Qǐng zhāngkāi nǐ de kǒu.
    - Please open your mouth.
  - 他家有三口人(他家有三口人)，个个活泼可爱(個個活潑可愛)。
    - Tā jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, gègè huópō kě'ài.
    - His family consists of three people, and each one is lively and adorable.
  - 医生检查了他的口(醫生檢查了他的口)，确认健康无虞(確認健康無虞)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchále tā de kǒu, quèrèn jiànkāng wú yú.
    - The doctor examined his mouth and confirmed there were no health concerns.
