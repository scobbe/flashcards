### exceed; surpass
---
- **pinyin:** exceed; surpass
- **components:**
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - run
  - 召
    - zhào
    - call
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 走 (zǒu, 'run') and the phonetic component 召 (zhào, 'call').
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests the action of running beyond or surpassing something, embodying the idea of exceeding limits.
- **examples:**
  - 他在比赛中超越了所有对手(他在比賽中超越了所有對手)，获得了冠军(獲得了冠軍)。
  - 你的成绩超出了我的预期(你的成績超出了我的預期)。
  - 科技的发展常常超乎我们的想象(科技的發展常常超乎我們的想像)。
### 超(超) → 走(走)
- **definition:** run
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a running man with additional elements showing the foot.
  - **interpretation:** The character showing a running figure conveys the concept of movement in its basic form. The addition of elements depicts action and physical activity associated with running.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by changing the top component from 夭 to 土 and stylizing the bottom as 龰. The stroke count was reduced in a way that made the character easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 他正在走路(他正在走路)。
    - tā zhèngzài zǒulù
    - He is walking.
  - 我喜欢快走(我喜歡快走)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān kuài zǒu
    - I like to walk quickly.
  - 他们在公园里走(他們在公園裡走)。
    - tāmen zài gōngyuán lǐ zǒu
    - They are walking in the park.
### 超(超) → 召(召)
- **definition:** call
- **pinyin:** zhào
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character consists of the semantic component口 (kǒu, 'mouth') representing the act of calling and the phonetic component召 (zhào) which provides the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The representation of a mouth indicates the action of calling or summoning someone vocally, clearly linking to the meaning of the character.
- **examples:**
  - 我在召你(我在召你)。
    - Wǒ zài zhào nǐ.
    - I am calling you.
  - 老师召集了所有的学生(老師召集了所有的學生)。
    - Lǎoshī zhàojíle suǒyǒu de xuéshēng.
    - The teacher called all the students together.
  - 他被召回去(他被召回去)。
    - Tā bèi zhàohuíqù.
    - He was called back.
### 超(超) → 召(召) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character is a pictograph depicting the shape of a mouth, which intuitively represents the concept of 'mouth' itself.
- **examples:**
  - 我吃东西(我吃東西)，需要用口(需要用口)。
    - Wǒ chī dōngxi, xūyào yòng kǒu.
    - I eat food, and I need to use my mouth.
  - 口腔(口腔)健康很重要(健康很重要)。
    - Kǒuqiāng jiànkāng hěn zhòngyào.
    - Oral health is very important.
