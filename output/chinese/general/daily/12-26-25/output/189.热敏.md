### thermo-sensitive
---
- **pinyin:** thermo-sensitive
- **components:**
  - 热(熱)
    - rè
    - hot; heat; warm
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - sensitive; quick; clever
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 热(熱) (rè, 'hot') + 敏(mǐn, 'sensitive') — hot sensitivity
  - **interpretation:** 热敏 describes a responsiveness to temperature changes. This term is used in contexts such as materials or devices that react to heat.
- **examples:**
  - 这款热敏打印机很受欢迎(這款熱敏打印機很受歡迎)。
    - Zhè kuǎn rèmǐn dǎyìnjī hěn shòu huānyíng.
    - This thermo-sensitive printer is very popular.
  - 热敏电阻用于温度测量(熱敏電阻用於溫度測量)。
    - Rèmǐn diànzǔ yòng yú wēndù cèliàng.
    - Thermo-sensitive resistors are used for temperature measurement.
