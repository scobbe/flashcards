- **traditional:** 熱敏
- **definition:** thermo-sensitive
- **pinyin:** rèmǐn
- **components:**
  - 热(熱)
    - rè
    - heat; hot; fever
  - 敏
    - mǐn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 热(熱) (rè, "heat") + 敏 (mǐn, "sensitive") =
    - being sensitive to changes in temperature.
  - **interpretation:** The term 热(熱)敏 (rèmǐn, "thermo-sensitive") describes a quality or a property of materials or devices that respond to heat. This is commonly applied in technology and materials science to refer to substances that alter their behavior or state in response to temperature changes.

  - 这种热(這種熱)敏材料在温(溫)度变(變)化时(時)保持稳(穩)定。
    - Zhè zhǒng rè mǐn cáiliào zài wēndù biànhuà shí bǎochí wěndìng.
    - This thermo-sensitive material remains stable when the temperature changes.
  - 工程师(師)利用热(熱)敏效应(應)校准设备(準設備)。
