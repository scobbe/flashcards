## thermo-sensitive
### rèmǐn
---
- **definition:** thermo-sensitive
- **pinyin:** rèmǐn
- **components:**
  - 热(熱)
    - rè
    - heat; hot; fever; fervent
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - sensitive; quick; agile; clever
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 热(熱) (rè, "heat") + 敏(敏) (mǐn, "sensitive") =
    - sensitive to heat ->
    - thermo-sensitive
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests an object or substance that reacts to or is affected by heat or temperature changes.
- **examples:**
  - 这台热敏打印机工作效率很高(這台熱敏打印機工作效率很高)。
    - Zhè tái rè mǐn dǎ yìn jī gōng zuò xiào lǜ hěn gāo.
    - This thermo-sensitive printer has high efficiency.
  - 这种热敏纸条(這種熱敏紙條)，遇热后会变黑(遇熱後會變黑)。
    - Zhè zhǒng rè mǐn zhǐ tiáo, yù rè hòu huì biàn hēi.
    - This thermo-sensitive paper turns black when heated.
  - 热敏元件(熱敏元件)，在温控系统中起着关键作用(在溫控系統中起着關鍵作用)。
    - Rè mǐn yuán jiàn, zài wēn kòng xì tǒng zhōng qǐ zhe guān jiàn zuò yòng.
    - The thermo-sensitive components play a key role in the temperature control system.
