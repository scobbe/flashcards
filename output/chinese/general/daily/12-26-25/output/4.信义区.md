### Xinyi District, Taipei
---
- **pinyin:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - trust; letter; faith; message
  - 义(義)
    - yì
    - righteousness; justice; meaning; duty
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area; zone; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 信(信) (xìn, "trust") + 义(義) (yì, "righteousness") + 区(區) (qū, "district") — trust righteousness district
  - **interpretation:** The name combines trust and righteousness, suggesting a community built on strong moral values, and it designates an urban area in Taipei known for its commercial and cultural significance.
- **examples:**
  - 信義區是台北的繁華地帶。(信義區是台北的繁華地帶。)
    - Xìnyìqū shì Táiběi de fánhuá dìdài.
    - Xinyi District is a bustling area in Taipei.
  - 我常去信義區逛街。(我常去信義區逛街。)
    - Wǒ cháng qù Xìnyìqū guàngjiē.
    - I often go shopping in Xinyi District.

### 信义区(信義區) → 区(區)
- **definition:** district; area; zone; region
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** The character combines the semantic component 区, which conveys a sense of division or area, with the phonetic component 口 (kǒu, 'mouth'), suggesting an area marked off or an opening.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as a district can be seen as an area that is delineated or set apart, often having defined boundaries. The imagery of a 'mouth' implies a place where things or people can enter or leave.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involves stroke reduction, where the complex character 環 (huán, 'to surround') is transformed into a simpler form, while preserving its essence.
- **examples:**
  - 我们住在一个安静的区(我們住在一個安靜的區)。
