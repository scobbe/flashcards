## Xinyi District, Taipei
### Xìnyì Qū
---
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **components:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - trust; believe
  - 義
    - yì
    - righteousness; justice
  - 區
    - qū
    - district; area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 信(信) (xìn, 'trust') + 義(義) (yì, 'righteousness') + 區(區) (qū, 'district') =
    - Xinyi District in Taipei, known for emphasizing trust and righteousness.
  - **interpretation:** The name 信義區 (Xìnyì Qū) reflects the values of trust and righteousness, suggesting a place characterized by these qualities. It is a well-known district in Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 信义区是台北市的时尚之都(信義區是台北市的時尚之都)。
    - Xìnyì Qū shì Táiběi shì de shíshàng zhī dū.
    - Xinyi District is the fashion capital of Taipei.
  - 我昨天去了信义区(我昨天去了信義區)，那里很热闹(那裡很熱鬧)。
    - Wǒ zuótiān qù le Xìnyì Qū, nàlǐ hěn rènao.
    - I went to Xinyi District yesterday, and it was lively.
