## Xinyi District, Taipei
### Xìnyì Qū
---
- **traditional:** 信義區
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **components:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - trust; letter; believe
  - 义(義)
    - yì
    - righteousness; justice; meaning
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; region; area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 信 (xìn, "trust") + 義 (yì, "righteousness") =
    - moral concepts suggesting a trustworthy and righteous area ->
    - 区(區) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** 信义(義) (xìnyì, "trust and righteousness") suggests a place governed by principles of trust and righteousness, reflecting positive attributes. Originally likely chosen for its auspicious qualities, this name now denotes a distinguished administrative region, Xinyi District, in Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在信义区(義區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Xìnyì Qū.
    - I live in Xinyi District.
  - 信义区(義區)经济(經濟)繁荣(榮)，吸引许(許)多国际(國際)企业(業)。
    - Xìnyì Qū jīngjì fánróng, xīyǐn xǔduō guójì qǐyè.
    - Xinyi District's economy is booming and attracts many international companies.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 信
- **definition:** trust; letter; believe
- **pinyin:** xìn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 言
    - yán
    - words
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 人 (rén, "person") + Semantic: 言 (yán, "words") =
    - person speaking words ->
    - trust, letter, believe
  - **interpretation:** 信(xìn, "trust; letter; believe") combines 人 (rén, "person") as the phonetic element with 言 (yán, "words") to signify the concept of words spoken by a person, which relates to trust and belief. The presence of 言 emphasizes the communicative aspect, hence extending the meaning to include letters.
- **examples:**
  - 他信我，因为(為)我总(總)是守时(時)。
    - Tā xìn wǒ, yīnwèi wǒ zǒngshì shǒushí.
    - He trusts me because I am always punctual.
  - 我写(寫)信给(給)朋友。
    - Wǒ xiě xìn gěi péngyǒu.
    - I write a letter to a friend.
  - 请(請)信自己的选择(選擇)。
    - Qǐng xìn zìjǐ de xuǎnzé.
    - Please trust your own choices.

### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 义(義)
- **traditional:** 義
- **definition:** righteousness; justice; meaning
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to govern/cut
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 乂 (yì, "to govern/cut") was originally used for its sound ->
    - 刈 (yì, "to cut") displaced 乂 ->
    - a stroke was added on top of 乂 to distinguish it for the meaning of righteousness ->
    - final form 义(義)
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 乂 was used for both its phonetic and semantic meanings but was mainly displaced by 刈. To distinguish the idea of "righteousness," a stroke was added, creating a differentiation that allowed the character to develop its own unique meaning related to justice and propriety.
- **examples:**
  - 他行侠(俠)仗义(義)，扶危济(濟)困。
    - Tā xíng xiá zhàng yì, fú wēi jì kùn.
    - He upholds chivalry and helps those in distress.
  - 正义(義)必胜(勝)。
    - Zhèng yì bì shèng.
    - Justice will surely prevail.
  - 他始终坚(終堅)守义(義)，维护(維護)公正。
    - Tā shǐ zhōng jiān shǒu yì, wèi hù gōng zhèng.
    - He always adheres to righteousness and upholds fairness.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 义(義) → 乂
- **definition:** to govern/cut
- **pinyin:** yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a tool (similar to a sickle or a pair of scissors) for mowing or weeding ->
    - used for governing ->
    - final form 乂
  - **interpretation:** The character 乂 originally depicted a tool used for cutting, and by extension, it was associated with governing due to the notion of cutting or managing weeds as a metaphor for ruling effectively. This sense of order is crucial in governance.
- **examples:**
  - 这些年，他致力于地方的治理(這些年，他致力於地方的治理)。
    - Zhèxiē nián, tā zhìlì yú dìfāng de zhìlǐ.
    - Over the years, he has dedicated himself to local governance.
  - 为民者，先自齐以治家(為民者，先自齊以治家)。
    - Wèi mín zhě, xiān zì qí yǐ zhì jiā.
    - Those who govern for the people, first manage their own households.
  - 长(長)者都希望后(後)代能好好治理天下。
    - Zhǎng zhě dōu xīwàng hòudài néng hǎohǎo zhìlǐ tiānxià.
    - The elders hope that future generations can govern the world well.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 区(區)
- **traditional:** 區
- **definition:** district; region; area
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods/people
  - 乂
    - yì
    - to manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (enclosed space) + 品 (many people) =
    - many people within an enclosure ->
    - area, district
  - **interpretation:** The character 區 (qū, 'district') visually represents an area where many people reside, symbolized by 品 (pǐn, 'goods/people') enclosed within 匸 (xì, 'enclosure'). This layout effectively conveys the idea of a defined space containing a community or district.
  - **simplification:** 區 was simplified to 区(區) by reducing 品 to 乂 (yì, 'to manage'), keeping the enclosure meaning while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在繁华(華)的区(區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài fán huá de qū.
    - I live in a bustling district.
  - 古代文人常在这区(這區)，寻觅灵(尋覓靈)感。
    - Gǔ dài wén rén cháng zài zhè qū, xún mì líng gǎn.
    - Ancient literati often sought inspiration in this region.
  - 他参观(參觀)了多个区(個區)，体验(體驗)了不同的文化。
    - Tā cān guān le duō gè qū, tǐ yàn le bù tóng de wén huà.
    - He visited several districts and experienced diverse cultures.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 区(區) → 匸
- **definition:** enclosure
- **pinyin:** xì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠃊 (symbolic graphic) ->
    - depicts an enclosure hiding something or someone ->
    - evolved to represent a concealed space or item
  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Its graphical form suggests something contained or hidden, which aligns with its meaning as a 'box' or an enclosure. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.
- **examples:**
  - 他把匸搬到了房间(間)角落。
    - Tā bǎ xì bān dào le fángjiān jiǎoluò.
    - He moved the box to the corner of the room.
  - 我们(們)在仓库(倉庫)找到了匸，里(裡)面装(裝)有秘密文件。
    - Wǒmen zài cāngkù zhǎodào le xì, lǐmiàn zhuāng yǒu mìmì wénjiàn.
    - We found the box in the warehouse, and it contained secret documents.
  - 工厂(廠)工人把旧(舊)匸清理了，然后(後)再利用。
    - Gōngchǎng gōngrén bǎ jiù xì qīnglǐ le, ránhòu zài lìyòng.
    - Factory workers cleaned the old box and then reused it.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 区(區) → 品
- **definition:** goods/people
- **pinyin:** pǐn
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - Three 口 (kǒu, 'mouth') arranged together =
    - multiple mouths speaking or consuming ->
    - representing many or various items
  - **interpretation:** The use of three mouths suggests plurality or variety. It's similar in structure to 晶 (jīng, 'sparkle'), which uses three 日 (rì, 'sun') to suggest brightness or vibrancy with multiple light sources.
- **examples:**

    - The competition attracted thousands of people, and everyone looked heroic.
  - 节(節)日庆(慶)典上品众(眾)多，场(場)面热(熱)烈非凡。
    - Jiérì qìngdiǎn shàng pǐn zhòngduō, chǎngmiàn rèliè fēifán.
    - The festive celebration saw a large crowd, and the scene was extraordinarily lively.
  - 广场(廣場)上聚集了众(眾)多品，大家热(熱)情互动(動)。
    - Guǎngchǎng shàng jùjíle zhòngduō pǐn, dàjiā rèqíng hùdòng.
    - The square gathered a multitude of people, and everyone interacted enthusiastically.
### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 区(區) → 品 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. - **examples:**

    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
