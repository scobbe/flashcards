## Xinyi District, Taipei
### Xìnyì Qū
---
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **components:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - trust; letter; believe
  - 义(義)
    - yì
    - righteousness; justice; meaning
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; region; area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 信(信) (xìn, "trust") + 義(義) (yì, "righteousness") =
    - moral concepts suggesting a trustworthy and righteous area ->
    - 区(區) (qū, "district") indicates an administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** 信义(信義) (xìnyì, "trust and righteousness") suggests a place governed by principles of trust and righteousness, reflecting positive attributes. Originally likely chosen for its auspicious qualities, this name now denotes a distinguished administrative region, Xinyi District, in Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在信义区(我住在信義區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Xìnyì Qū.
    - I live in Xinyi District.
  - 信义区(信義區)经济繁荣(經濟繁榮)，吸引许多国际企业(吸引許多國際企業)。
    - Xìnyì Qū jīngjì fánróng, xīyǐn xǔduō guójì qǐyè.
    - Xinyi District's economy is booming and attracts many international companies.

- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - box
  - 品
    - pǐn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified from 區
  - **description:**
    - 區(區) (qū, "district") originally consists of 匸(匸) (xì, "box") + 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") =
    - representation of multiple people within an enclosed area ->

  - **simplification:** The original character 區(區) (qū, "district") was simplified to 区(區) (qū, "district") by replacing 品(品) (pǐn, "many people") with the simpler 乂(乂) (yì, "to govern"), making it easier to write and remember while maintaining the sense of an enclosed area.
