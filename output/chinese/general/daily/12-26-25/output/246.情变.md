### breakup; change in relationship
---
- **pinyin:** breakup; change in relationship
- **components:**
  - 情
    - qíng
    - feeling; emotion; sentiment; affection
  - 變
    - biàn
    - change; transform; alter; shift
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 情(情) (qíng, "feeling; emotion") + 變(變) (biàn, "change; transform") — emotional change
  - **interpretation:** The combination describes a significant change in feelings or emotions, typically in the context of a relationship. Use of these words together effectively conveys the idea of a transformation or alteration in emotional states.
- **examples:**
  - 他们的情变得越来越复杂(他們的情變得越來越複雜)。
    - Tāmen de qíng biàn dé yuè lái yuè fùzá.
    - Their feelings have become increasingly complicated.
  - 情变让他们最终决定分开(情變讓他們最終決定分開)。
    - Qíng biàn ràng tāmen zuìzhōng juédìng fēnkāi.
    - The breakup in feelings led them to ultimately decide to separate.
