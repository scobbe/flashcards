## breakup; change in relationship
### qíngbiàn
---
- **definition:** breakup; change in relationship
- **pinyin:** qíngbiàn
- **components:**
  - 情
    - qíng
    - emotions; feelings
  - 变(變)
    - biàn
    - change; transform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 情(情) (qíng, "emotions; feelings") 變(變) (biàn, "change; transform")
  - **interpretation:** The word 情變 (qíngbiàn) refers to a change or transformation in emotions or relationships, particularly in the context of a romantic breakup. The combination of 情(情) (qíng, "emotions; feelings") and 變(變) (biàn, "change; transform") emphasizes an alteration in the emotional state or circumstances of a relationship.
- **examples:**
  - 他们之间情变(他們之間情變)。
    - Tāmen zhī jiān qíngbiàn.
    - Their relationship broke down.
  - 这段婚姻曾经幸福(這段婚姻曾經幸福)，如今却因情变而终止(如今日卻因情變而終止)。
    - Zhè duàn hūnyīn céngjīng xìngfú, rújīn què yīn qíngbiàn ér zhōngzhǐ.
    - This marriage was once happy, but now it has ended due to a breakup.
  - 他们因矛盾累积情变(他們因矛盾累積情變)，最终各奔东西(最終各奔東西)。
    - Tāmen yīn máodùn lěijī qíngbiàn, zuìzhōng gè bèn dōngxī.
    - They broke up due to accumulating conflicts and eventually went their separate ways.

### 情变(情變) → 变(變)
- **definition:** change; transform
- **pinyin:** biàn
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 又
    - yòu
    - again
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified and variant form
  - **description:** Simplified from 變 (biàn, "change"): 言(言) (yán, "speech") + 糸(糸) (mì, "silk"). The silk 糸 radical in the original has been omitted, and the 言(言) (yán) component has been merged with simplified 又(又) (yòu, "again") to create 变.
  - **interpretation:** The traditional character 變 (biàn, "change") combines the speech 言(言) (yán, "speech") and silk 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") elements, symbolizing change through communication and intricacy. The simplified form 变 streamlines this by removing silk 糸 and simplifying the rest for ease of writing.
  - **simplification:** 变 is the result of simplifying the traditional character 變 by removing the silk 糸 component and using 又(又) (yòu, "again") for a streamlined form.
- **examples:**
  - 时间在悄悄地变(時間在悄悄地變)。
    - Shíjiān zài qiāoqiāo de biàn.
    - Time is quietly changing.
  - 他的心态变得积极(他的心態變得積極)，看待问题更开放(看待問題更開放)。
    - Tā de xīntaì biàn dé jījí, kàndài wèntí gèng kāifàng.
    - His mindset became positive, and he viewed problems more openly.
  - 书籍的封面在风雨中变得斑驳(書籍的封面在風雨中變得斑駁)，承载着岁月的痕迹(承載著歲月的痕跡)。
    - Shūjí de fēngmiàn zài fēngyǔ zhōng biàn dé bānbó, chéngzài zhe suìyuè de hénjì.
    - The cover of the book became mottled in the wind and rain, bearing the marks of time.
