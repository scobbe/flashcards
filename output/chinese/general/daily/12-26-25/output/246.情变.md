## breakup; change in relationship
### qíngbiàn
---
- **definition:** breakup; change in relationship
- **pinyin:** qíngbiàn
- **components:**
  - 情
    - qíng
    - feeling; emotion; situation
  - 变(變)
    - biàn
    - change; transform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 情(情) (qíng, "feeling; emotion; situation") + 變(變) (biàn, "change; transform") together refer to a shift or transformation in the state of feelings or emotions, especially in the context of romantic relationships. This combination aptly captures the essence of a breakup or a significant change in a relationship.
  - **interpretation:** The word reflects the idea that the emotional state of a relationship can change, often specifically indicating a breakup or disruption.
- **examples:**
  - 他们因小误会而发生情变(他們因小誤會而發生情變)。
    - Tāmen yīn xiǎo wùhuì ér fāshēng qíngbiàn。
    - They broke up due to a minor misunderstanding.
  - 他的心里因为情变失去了温暖(他的心裡因為情變失去了溫暖)。
    - Tā de xīn lǐ yīnwèi qíngbiàn shīqùle wēnnuǎn。
    - His heart lost its warmth because of the breakup.
  - 他们曾经相知(他們曾經相知)，如今却因情变分道扬镳(如今卻因情變分道揚鑣)。
    - Tāmen céngjīng xiāng zhī, rújīn què yīn qíngbiàn fēndào yángbiāo。
    - They once understood each other, but now the breakup has led them to part ways.

### 情变(情變) → 变(變)
- **definition:** change; transform
- **pinyin:** biàn
- **components:**
  - 䖝(豸)
    - zhì
    - legendary monstrous cat
  - 苋(夘)
    - mào
    - plant variety
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 古文言 backgrounds: 䖝(豸) (zhì, 'legendary monstrous cat') + 苑(夗) (yuàn, 'park') =
    - a park containing strange beasts ->
    - a change or transformation
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a legendary monstrous cat within a park, symbolizing a transformation or change from the norm. Over time, the concept evolved to signify change more broadly.
  - **simplification:** 变(變): The traditional character 變 was simplified by removing the complex outer components and merging them into a simpler form of the character. 斿 was drastically simplified to a simple 又 (yòu, 'again'), which also acts as a portmanteau to retain phonetic and partial semantic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 春天来临时(春天來臨時)，花儿开始变红(花兒開始變紅)。
    - Chūntiān láilín shí, huā'ér kāishǐ biàn hóng.
    - When spring arrives, the flowers begin to turn red.
  - 面对困难时(面對困難時)，心态需要变得坚强(心態需要變得堅強)。
    - Miànduì kùnnán shí, xīntài xūyào biàn dé jiānqiáng.
    - In the face of difficulties, one's mindset needs to become strong.
