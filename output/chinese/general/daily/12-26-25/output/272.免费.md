## free of charge
### miǎnfèi
---
- **definition:** free of charge
- **pinyin:** miǎnfèi
- **components:**
  - 免
    - miǎn
    - exempt; avoid; remove; spare
  - 费(費)
    - fèi
    - cost; fee; expenses; spend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 免(免) (miǎn, "exempt") + 费(費) (fèi, "cost") combine to mean "free of charge." An item or service that is 免(免) (miǎn, "exempt") from 费(費) (fèi, "cost") does not require payment.
  - **interpretation:** In modern usage, 免费(免費) (miǎnfèi) signifies that something can be obtained or used without any monetary cost.
- **examples:**
  - 这家餐厅提供免费午餐(這家餐廳提供免費午餐)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng miǎnfèi wǔcān.
    - This restaurant offers a free lunch.
  - 学校图书馆免费借书(學校圖書館免費借書)，提高了学生阅读兴趣(提高了學生閱讀興趣)。
    - Xuéxiào túshūguǎn miǎnfèi jièshū, tígāo le xuéshēng yuèdú xìngqù.
    - The school library offers free book loans, which has increased students' interest in reading.
### 免费(免費) → 免(免)
- **definition:** exempt; avoid; remove; spare
- **pinyin:** miǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a man wearing a ceremonial hat ->
    - borrowed for abstract meanings
  - **interpretation:** The character 免(免) (miǎn, 'exempt; avoid') originally depicted a man with a ceremonial hat, emphasizing its use in significant ceremonies. Over time, it was borrowed for its abstract meanings related to exemption and avoidance, diverging from the literal depiction of ceremonial attire.
- **examples:**
  - 他免参加会议(他免參加會議)。
    - Tā miǎn cānjiā huìyì.
    - He is exempt from attending the meeting.
  - 为了免除额外费用(為了免除額外費用)，她选择提前付款(她選擇提前付款)。
    - Wèile miǎn chú éwài fèiyòng, tā xuǎnzé tíqián fùkuǎn.
    - In order to avoid extra charges, she chose to pay in advance.
  - 我们应免掉不必要的步骤(我們應免掉不必要的步驟)。
    - Wǒmen yīng miǎn diào bù bìyào de bùzhòu.
    - We should avoid unnecessary steps.
### 免费(免費) → 费(費)
- **definition:** cost; fee; expenses; spend
- **pinyin:** fèi
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - cowry
  - 弗
    - fú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") + phonetic 弗(弗) (fú) =
    - sound and idea of valuing, spending ->
    - expenditure
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry"), which often symbolizes money or valuables, with the phonetic 弗(弗) (fú), giving it the sound and further emphasizing the concept of spending or valuing. This reinforces the idea of an expense or cost.
  - **simplification:** 费(費): 贝(贝) (bèi, "cowry") retains the meaning and the sound is conveyed through simplified phonetic components.
- **examples:**
  - 他支付高额管理费(他支付高額管理費)，认为这是合理花费(認為這是合理花費)。
    - Tā zhīfù gāo'é guǎnlǐ fèi, rènwéi zhè shì hélǐ huāfèi。
    - He pays a high management fee and believes it is a reasonable expense.
  - 学校收取报名费(學校收取報名費)，用于购买设施(用於購買設施)。
    - Xuéxiào shōuqǔ bàomíng fèi, yòng yú gòumǎi shèshī。
    - The school charges an enrollment fee to purchase facilities.
