## free of charge
### miǎnfèi
---
- **definition:** free of charge
- **pinyin:** miǎnfèi
- **components:**
  - 免
    - miǎn
    - exempt; dismiss; avoid; release
  - 费(費)
    - fèi
    - fee; expense; cost; spend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 免(免) (miǎn, "exempt") and 费(費) (fèi, "fee") combined mean 'exempt from fee', indicating something is free of charge.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 免(免) (miǎn, "exempt") and 费(費) (fèi, "fee") clearly indicates the concept of not having to pay a fee. In contexts such as promotions or services, 免费(免費) (miǎnfèi) effectively communicates that there is no cost involved.
- **examples:**
  - 这家店提供免费饮料(這家店提供免費飲料)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn tígōng miǎnfèi yǐnliào.
    - This store offers free drinks.
  - 网上购物时(網上購物時)，运费是免费的(運費是免費的)。
    - Wǎng shàng gòuwù shí, yùn fèi shì miǎnfèi de.
    - When shopping online, shipping is free of charge.
  - 参加讲座能享受免费咨询(參加講座能享受免費諮詢)。
    - Cānjiā jiǎngzuò néng xiǎngshòu miǎnfèi zīxún.
    - Attending the lecture allows you to enjoy free consultation.

### 免费(免費) → 费(費)
- **definition:** fee; expense; cost; spend
- **pinyin:** fèi
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - shell/money
  - 弗
    - fú
    - not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 貝(貝) (bèi, 'shell/money') + 弗(弗) (fú, 'not') =
    - related to payments and expenses ->
    - fee
  - **interpretation:** 費(費) (fèi) represents matters related to expenses by combining 貝(貝) (bèi, 'shell/money'), which symbolizes wealth or money, with 弗(弗) (fú, 'not'), serving as the phonetic component. Historically, shells were used as currency, linking the character to financial transactions.
  - **simplification:** 費(費)→费(費): 貝(貝) (bèi, 'shell/money') was simplified by removing the inner strokes for a more streamlined form.
- **examples:**
  - 我花了很多钱(我花了很多錢)，支付学费(支付學費)。

    - Gōngsī wèi xīn xiàngmù zhīchū le gāo’é fèiyòng.
    - The company incurred high expenses for the new project.
