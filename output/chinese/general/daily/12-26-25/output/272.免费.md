## free of charge
### miǎnfèi
---
- **traditional:** 免費
- **definition:** free of charge
- **pinyin:** miǎnfèi
- **components:**
  - 免
    - miǎn
    - exempt; dismiss; avoid; release
  - 费(費)
    - fèi
    - fee; expense; cost; spend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 免 (miǎn, "exempt") and 费(費) (fèi, "fee") combined mean 'exempt from fee', indicating something is free of charge.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 免 (miǎn, "exempt") and 费(費) (fèi, "fee") clearly indicates the concept of not having to pay a fee. In contexts such as promotions or services, 免( )费(費) (miǎnfèi) effectively communicates that there is no cost involved.
- **examples:**

    - Zhè jiā diàn tígōng miǎnfèi yǐnliào.
    - This store offers free drinks.
  - 网(網)上( )购(購)物( )时(時)，运(運)费(費)是( )免( )费(費)的( )。
    - Wǎng shàng gòuwù shí, yùn fèi shì miǎnfèi de.
    - When shopping online, shipping is free of charge.
  - 参(參)加( )讲(講)座( )能( )享( )受( )免( )费(費)咨(諮)询(詢)。
