---
## scan a code
### sǎomǎ
---
- **definition:** scan a code
- **pinyin:** sǎomǎ
- **components:**
  - 扫(掃)
    - sǎo
    - sweep
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 扫(掃) (sǎo, "sweep") refers to the action of moving over a surface, like scanning, and 码(碼) (mǎ, "code") signifies the code being scanned. By combining these terms, 扫码(掃碼) (sǎomǎ, "scan a code") describes the action of using a device to sweep over a code for information capture.
  - **interpretation:** 扫(掃) (sǎo, "sweep") implies a scanning motion, while 码(碼) (mǎ, "code") refers to the digital or barcode being scanned.
  - **simplification:** 扫(掃), 码(碼)
- **examples:**
  - 请用扫码付款(請用掃碼付款)。
    - Qǐng yòng sǎomǎ fùkuǎn.
    - Please use scanning to pay.
  - 他扫码进门(他掃碼進門)，立刻进入大楼(立刻進入大樓)。
    - Tā sǎomǎ jìn mén, lìkè jìnrù dàlóu.
    - He scanned the code to enter, and immediately went into the building.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 扫(掃)
- **definition:** sweep
- **pinyin:** sǎo
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 帚
    - zhǒu
    - broomstick
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 手(手) (shǒu, "hand") + 帚(帚) (zhǒu, "broomstick") =
    - hand using a broomstick ->
    - sweep. Simplified by eliminating 冖(冖) (mì, "cover") and 巾(巾) (jīn, "cloth").
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the action of sweeping, with a hand holding a broomstick. The simplified form removed components that were less essential to the meaning, similar to the simplification process seen in 妇(婦) (fù, "woman").
  - **simplification:** 掃(掃) was simplified to 扫(掃) by removing 冖(冖) (mì, "cover") and 巾(巾) (jīn, "cloth"), streamlining the character's form while retaining the core elements of hand and broom.
- **examples:**
  - 我正在扫地(我正在掃地)。
    - Wǒ zhèngzài sǎo dì.
    - I am sweeping the floor.
  - 他每天扫院子(他每天掃院子)，保持环境整洁(保持環境整潔)。
    - Tā měitiān sǎo yuànzi, bǎochí huánjìng zhěngjié.
    - He sweeps the yard every day to keep the environment tidy.
  - 工人们扫街道(工人們掃街道)，为市民创造干净生活(為市民創造乾淨生活)。
    - Gōngrénmen sǎo jiēdào, wèi shìmín chuàngzào gānjìng shēnghuó.
    - Workers sweep the streets to create a clean life for the citizens.

### 扫码(掃碼) → 码(碼)
- **definition:** code
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 石(石) (shí, "stone") semantic + 馬(马) (mǎ, "horse") phonetic =
    - stone-like stability and sounds like 'mǎ' ->
    - code
  - **interpretation:** The character 码(mǎ) originally related to stones, using 石(石) (shí, "stone") for its stability. 馬(马) (mǎ, "horse") provides the phonetic component suggesting the pronunciation, which closely resembles its pronunciation in this context.
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 碼(码) was simplified by using 马(马) (mǎ, "horse") to streamline writing, while retaining the original pronunciation hint.
- **examples:**
  - 请扫描二维码(請掃描二維碼)。
    - Qǐng sǎomiáo èr wéi mǎ.
    - Please scan the QR code.
  - 你输入的验证码(你輸入的驗證碼)，有误(有誤)。
    - Nǐ shūrù de yànzhèng mǎ, yǒu wù.
    - The verification code you entered is incorrect.
  - 每个程序都有加密码(每個程序都有加密碼)，保护数据安全(保護數據安全)。
    - Měi gè chéngxù dōu yǒu jiāmìmǎ, bǎohù shùjù ānquán.
    - Every program has an encryption code to protect data security.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 码(碼) → 石(石)
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a stone beneath a cliff (厂); cliff distorted into 丆 ->
    - 石
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, "stone") is a pictogram representing a stone located beneath a cliff. The image has evolved over time, with the original depiction of a cliff (厂) being simplified to 丆, helping to maintain its connection to a rocky or stony landscape.
- **examples:**
  - 这块石很坚硬(這塊石很堅硬)。
    - Zhè kuài shí hěn jiānyìng.
    - This stone is very hard.
  - 山脚下有石(山腳下有石)，溪水冲洗石面(溪水衝洗石面)。
    - Shān jiǎoxià yǒu shí, xīshuǐ chōngxǐ shímiàn.
    - There are stones at the foot of the mountain, and the stream washes the stone surfaces.
  - 青石台阶(青石臺階)，见证岁月(見證歲月)。
    - Qīng shí táijiē, jiànzhèng suìyuè.
    - The blue stone steps witness the passage of time.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 码(碼) → 马(馬)
- **definition:** horse
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a horse with its head facing left, showing a flowing mane ->
    - 馬 (horse)
  - **interpretation:** The character 馬(馬) (mǎ, "horse") originated from a pictogram that visually represented a horse, with distinct features such as its head and mane captured in the ancient script. The simplification into 马(馬) retained the essence of the original pictogram by adopting a cursive shorthand form that maintained its recognizability.
  - **simplification:** 马 is simplified from 馬 by adopting a cursive script form, reducing the number of strokes while maintaining the recognizable elements of the horse's imagery.
- **examples:**
