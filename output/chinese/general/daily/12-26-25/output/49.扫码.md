---
## 
### scan a code
---
- **pinyin:** scan a code
- **components:**
  - 扫(掃)
    - sǎo
    - sweep; scan; brush; sweep away
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number; signal; symbol
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 扫(掃) (sǎo, 'sweep/scan') + 码(碼) (mǎ, 'code') — sweep (scan) code
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 扫(掃) (sǎo, 'sweep') conveys the action of scanning, while 码(碼) (mǎ, 'code') refers to the digital representation being scanned. Together, they describe the process of scanning a code, commonly seen in QR codes or barcodes.
- **examples:**
  - 我用手机扫码(我用手機掃碼)，付款很方便。
    - Wǒ yòng shǒujī sǎomǎ, fùkuǎn hěn fāngbiàn.
    - I use my phone to scan the code, paying is very convenient.
  - 商店里都可以扫码(商店裡都可以掃碼)买东西。
    - Shāngdiàn lǐ dōu kěyǐ sǎomǎ mǎi dōngxī.
    - You can scan codes to buy things in the store.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 扫(掃)
- **definition:** sweep; scan; brush; sweep away
- **pinyin:** sǎo
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 帚(掃)
    - zhǒu
    - broomstick
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** A combination of 手 (shǒu, "hand") and 帚 (zhǒu, "broomstick"), representing the action of using a hand to sweep.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a hand holding a broomstick vividly conveys the action of sweeping, making the meaning clear and intuitive.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by removing the components 冖 and 巾 to create a more concise form.
- **examples:**
  - 我在扫地(我在掃地)。
    - Wǒ zài sǎo dì.
    - I am sweeping the floor.
  - 请你扫一下(請你掃一下)。
    - Qǐng nǐ sǎo yīxià.
    - Please sweep a bit.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 扫(掃) → 手(手)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human hand with five fingers.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a hand and fingers, highlighting the five-finger structure typical of mammals. This makes it intuitive for denoting the concept of 'hand.'
- **examples:**
  - 我的手很冷(我的手很冷)。
    - wǒ de shǒu hěn lěng.
    - My hand is very cold.
  - 他用手写字(他用手寫字)。
    - tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - He writes with his hand.
  - 请伸出你的手(請伸出你的手)。
    - qǐng shēn chū nǐ de shǒu.
    - Please extend your hand.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 扫(掃) → 帚(掃)
- **definition:** broomstick
- **pinyin:** zhǒu
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 扫(掃)
    - sǎo
    - sweep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Combines 手 (shǒu, 'hand') with 帚 (zhǒu, 'broomstick') to represent the act of sweeping.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as it visually depicts a hand holding a broomstick, clearly illustrating the action of sweeping.
  - **simplification:** 掃(掃) was simplified to 帚(扫) by eliminating the 冖 and 巾 components, streamlining the character for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我用扫把(我用掃把)来打扫房间(來打掃房間)。
    - Wǒ yòng sàobǎ (wǒ yòng sàobǎ) lái dǎsǎo fángjiān (lái dǎsǎo fángjiān)。
    - I use a broom to clean the room.
  - 她正在用扫把(她正在用掃把)扫地(掃地)。
    - Tā zhèngzài yòng sàobǎ (tā zhèngzài yòng sàobǎ) sǎo dì (sǎo dì)。
    - She is currently sweeping the floor.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 码(碼)
- **definition:** code; number; signal; symbol
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **components:**
  - 犬(犭)
    - quǎn
    - dog
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 犭 (dog) suggesting 'to bark' and the phonetic component 马 (horse) which is pronounced 'mǎ'.
  - **interpretation:** The combination evokes an image of a dog barking, which may be associated with signaling or communication, thereby relating to the concept of a 'code' or 'symbol'. The horse phonetic implies speed or movement, further emphasizing communication.
  - **simplification:** It simplifies from the traditional form 碼 by using fewer strokes; the dog radical is modified for ease in modern writing.
- **examples:**
  - 这个代码(這個代碼)很复杂(很複雜)。
    - zhège dàimǎ hěn fùzá.
    - This code is very complex.
  - 请帮我解码(請幫我解碼)。
    - qǐng bāng wǒ jiěmǎ.
    - Please help me decode.

    - There is a dog at home, it walks with me every day.
### 扫码(掃碼) → 码(碼) → 马(馬)
- **definition:** horse
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a horse with its head facing left, showing a flowing mane.
  - **interpretation:** The horse's iconic shape and flowing mane are easily recognizable, making this character intuitive as a representation of a horse.
  - **simplification:** 马(馬) is simplified from 馬 following cursive script forms. The flowing mane is less detailed, and the legs become simplified dots.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢马(我喜歡馬)，尤其是赛马(賽馬)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān mǎ, yóuqí shì sàimǎ！
    - I like horses, especially horse racing!
  - 马儿在草地上吃草(馬兒在草地上吃草)。
    - Mǎ er zài cǎodì shàng chī cǎo。
    - The horse is grazing in the grass.
