## 好久不见(好久不見)
---
## long time no see
### hǎojiǔ bújiàn
---
- **definition:** long time no see
- **pinyin:** hǎojiǔ bújiàn
- **components:**
  - 好久
    - hǎojiǔ
    - a long time; quite a while; ages (colloq)
  - 不见(不見)
    - bújiàn
    - not see; be missing; disappear; not to be visible
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** set phrase (colloquial greeting)
  - **description:** 好久 (long time) + 不见 (not see) = haven't seen for a long time.
  - **interpretation:** Used as a friendly greeting when meeting someone after a long interval. Its structure also inspired the English calque “long time no see.”
  - **simplification:** 見 was simplified to 见 in the character reform to reduce strokes; the rest is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 好久不见，我们改天聚一聚。(好久不見，我們改天聚一聚)。
    - Hǎojiǔ bújiàn, wǒmen gǎitiān jù yí jù.
    - Long time no see; let’s get together another day.
  - 我在机场遇到老同学，第一句话就是“好久不见”。(我在機場遇到老同學，第一句話就是「好久不見」。)
    - Wǒ zài jīchǎng yùdào lǎo tóngxué, dì yī jù huà jiùshì “hǎojiǔ bújiàn”.
    - I ran into an old classmate at the airport, and the first thing I said was “long time no see.”
  - 好久不见的朋友终于回国了，大家都很开心。(好久不見的朋友終於回國了，大家都很開心)。
    - Hǎojiǔ bújiàn de péngyǒu zhōngyú huíguó le, dàjiā dōu hěn kāixīn.
    - A friend I hadn’t seen in ages finally returned to the country, and everyone was very happy.
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 好久
- **definition:** a long time; quite a while; ages (colloq)
- **pinyin:** hǎojiǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 好 (very) + 久 (long time) = quite a long time.
  - **interpretation:** Combines an intensifier with a duration noun to mean “a long/quite a while.” Often used in greetings (好久不见) and adverbially before verbs.
- **examples:**
  - 好久不见(好久不見)！
    - Hǎo jiǔ bú jiàn!
    - Long time no see!
  - 我们好久没联系了(我們好久沒聯繫了)。
    - Wǒmen hǎo jiǔ méi liánxì le.
    - We haven’t been in touch for a long time.
  - 我等了好久(我等了好久)，他才来(他才來)。
    - Wǒ děng le hǎo jiǔ, tā cái lái.
    - I waited for a long time before he finally came.
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 不见(不見)
- **definition:** not see; be missing; disappear; not to be visible
- **pinyin:** bújiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 不 (not) + 见 (see) = not see; be out of sight; disappear; gone
  - **interpretation:** Negates the act of seeing to indicate something is out of sight, unseen, or has disappeared. Often appears as 不见了 to mean something is missing/gone.
  - **simplification:** 见 is the simplified form of 見, reduced for fewer strokes in a high-frequency graph; 不 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我手机不见了(我手機不見了)。
    - Wǒ shǒujī bújiàn le.
    - My phone is gone.
  - 前面起雾(前面起霧)，他的人影很快不见了(他的人影很快不見了)。
    - Qiánmiàn qǐ wù, tā de rényǐng hěn kuài bújiàn le.
    - Fog rose ahead, and his figure quickly disappeared.
  - 灯太亮(燈太亮)，我看不见星星(我看不見星星)。
    - Dēng tài liàng, wǒ kàn bújiàn xīngxing.
    - The light is too bright; I can’t see the stars.
