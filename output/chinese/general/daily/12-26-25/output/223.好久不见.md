## 好久不见(好久不見)
---
## 
### long time no see
---
- **pinyin:** long time no see
- **components:**
  - 好久
    - hǎojiǔ
    - a long time; quite a while; long time; for a long time
  - 不见(不見)
    - bújiàn
    - not see; unseen; not be visible; to miss
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 好久(好久) (hǎojiǔ, 'a long time') + 不见(不見) (bújiàn, 'not see') — long time not see
  - **interpretation:** This phrase expresses the feeling of not having seen someone for a significant period. It conveys warmth and friendliness when reconnecting after a long time apart.
- **examples:**
  - 你还好吗，好久不见！
    - nǐ hái hǎo ma, hǎojiǔ bùjiàn！
    - How have you been? Long time no see!
  - 我刚到，感觉你很久不见。
    - wǒ gāng dào, gǎnjué nǐ hěn jiǔ bùjiàn。
    - I just arrived and feel like I haven't seen you in a long time.
  - 好久不见，你最近怎么样？
    - hǎojiǔ bùjiàn, nǐ zuìjìn zěnme yàng？
    - Long time no see, how have you been lately?
