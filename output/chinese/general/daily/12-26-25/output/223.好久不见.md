## long time no see
### hǎojiǔ bújiàn
---
- **definition:** long time no see
- **pinyin:** hǎojiǔ bújiàn
- **components:**
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good
  - 久
    - jiǔ
    - long time
  - 不
    - bù
    - no; not
  - 见(見)
    - jiàn
    - see
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 好(好) (hǎo, "good") + 久(久) (jiǔ, "long time") + 不(不) (bù, "no") + 见(見) (jiàn, "see") describes the situation where one has not seen another person for a good or long period of time.
  - **interpretation:** The phrase conveys the feeling of meeting someone after a long period of absence. It's a friendly greeting expressing that it has been a while since the two parties last met.
- **examples:**
  - 好久不见(好久不見)。
    - hǎojiǔ bújiàn.
    - Long time no see.
  - 我来到这里(我來到這裏)，好久不见(好久不見)。
    - wǒ láidào zhèlǐ, hǎojiǔ bújiàn.
    - I have come here, long time no see.
  - 多年未见的老友重逢时(多年未見的老友重逢時)，总会互道好久不见(總會互道好久不見)。
    - duōnián wèijiàn de lǎoyǒu chóngféng shí, zǒng huì hùdào hǎojiǔ bújiàn.
    - When old friends who haven't seen each other for years meet again, they always greet each other with 'long time no see.'
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 好(好)
- **definition:** good
- **pinyin:** hǎo
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") + 子(子) (zǐ, "child") =
    - woman with child ->
    - good
  - **interpretation:** The character 好(hǎo, "good") combines 女(nǚ, "woman") and 子(zǐ, "child") to symbolize the positive bond between a mother and her child. This pictorial association represents goodness and well-being, reflecting cultural values placed on family and offspring.
- **examples:**
  - 天气好(天氣好)。
    - Tiānqì hǎo.
    - The weather is good.
  - 你穿得好(你穿得好)，气质也好(氣質也好)。
    - Nǐ chuān de hǎo, qìzhì yě hǎo.
    - You dress well, and your demeanor is also good.
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 好(好) → 女(女)
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing ->
    - modern form reversed facing right
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") originally depicted a woman with pronounced breasts, illustrating the concept of 'woman.' Over time, the pictogram evolved to face right in its modern form, losing details like the left breast to simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有魅力的女(她是一位有魅力的女)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi yǒu mèi lì de nǚ.

  - 那位女战士英勇无畏(那位女戰士英勇無畏)，带领大家前行(帶領大家前行)。
    - Nà wèi nǚ zhàn shì yīng yǒng wú wèi, dài lǐng dà jiā qián xíng.
    - That female warrior is brave and fearless, leading everyone forward.

### 好久不见(好久不見) → 久(久)
- **definition:** long time
- **pinyin:** jiǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arrow tip
  - **interpretation:** 久(久) (jiǔ, 'long time') is a pictogram depicting an arrow tip, suggesting longevity or continuity. Throughout history, arrows were considered symbols that can endure over long distances and time.
- **examples:**
  - 我们认识很久(我們認識很久)。
    - Wǒmen rènshi hěnjiǔ.

    - He practiced the piano for a long time, and eventually achieved success.
  - 这条古路走了很久(這條古路走了很久)。
    - Zhè tiáo gǔlù zǒule hěnjiǔ.
    - This ancient road has been traveled for a long time.
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 不(不)
- **definition:** no; not
- **pinyin:** bù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the calyx of a flower
  - **interpretation:** The character 不 originally depicted a simple negation, with its shape resembling the calyx of a flower. Over time, it came to universally express negation, meaning 'not'.
- **examples:**
  - 我不喜欢喝茶(我不喜歡喝茶)。
    - Wǒ bù xǐhuān hē chá.
    - I do not like drinking tea.
  - 他不学习(他不學習)，也不锻炼(也不鍛煉)。
    - Tā bù xuéxí, yě bù duànliàn.
    - He does not study, nor does he exercise.
  - 这道菜不辣(這道菜不辣)。
    - Zhè dào cài bù là.
    - This dish is not spicy.
### 好久不见(好久不見) → 见(見)
- **definition:** see
- **pinyin:** jiàn
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 卩
    - jié
    - kneeling person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, "eye") + 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person") =
    - a kneeling human figure with a large eye for a head ->
    - "see"
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the eye 目(目) (mù, "eye") with a kneeling person 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person"), representing the act of perceiving or seeing, which aligns intuitively with the meaning "to see."
  - **simplification:** 见 is a simplified form derived from 見 by adopting a cursive script version, maintaining the essential elements in a more compact form.
- **examples:**
  - 我见到了新朋友(我見到了新朋友)。
    - Wǒ jiàn dào le xīn péngyǒu.
    - I met a new friend.
  - 他在路上见到她(他在路上見到她)，并且打了个招呼(並且打了個招呼)。
    - Tā zài lù shàng jiàn dào tā, bìngqiě dǎ le gè zhāohu.
    - He saw her on the road, and he greeted her warmly.
  - 你一见他(你一見他)，就露出笑容(就露出笑容)。
    - Nǐ yī jiàn tā, jiù lù chū xiàoróng.
    - The moment you see him, you break into a smile.
