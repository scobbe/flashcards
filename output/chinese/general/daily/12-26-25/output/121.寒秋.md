## late autumn
### hánqiū
---
- **definition:** late autumn
- **pinyin:** hánqiū
- **components:**
  - 寒
    - hán
    - cold; chilly
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn; fall
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 寒(寒) (hán, "cold") + 秋(秋) (qiū, "autumn") =
    - cold season of autumn ->
    - late autumn
  - **interpretation:** The term 寒秋 (hánqiū) conveys the notion of the cold period within the autumn season, characterizing the later, typically cooler phase of autumn.
- **examples:**
  - 寒秋悄然降临(寒秋悄然降臨)。
    - Hánqiū qiǎorán jiànglín.
    - Late autumn arrives silently.
  - 在寒秋的清晨(在寒秋的清晨)，露珠闪烁(露珠閃爍)。
    - Zài hánqiū de qīngchén, lùzhū shǎnshuò.
    - On a crisp late autumn morning, dewdrops sparkle.

### 寒秋(寒秋) → 秋(秋)
- **definition:** autumn; fall
- **pinyin:** qiū
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 火
    - huǒ
    - fire
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") + 火(火) (huǒ, "fire") =
    - burning of the grain stalks ->
    - autumn
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") and 火(火) (huǒ, "fire") to symbolize a time when crops have ripened and fields are cleared by burning, fitting the concept of autumn.

  - 枫叶在秋日余晖中飘落(楓葉在秋日餘暉中飄落)。
    - fēng yè zài qiū rì yú huī zhōng piāo luò
    - Maple leaves fall in the glow of autumn.
