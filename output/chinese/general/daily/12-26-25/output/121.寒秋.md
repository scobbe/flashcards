### late autumn
---
- **pinyin:** late autumn
- **components:**
  - 寒
    - hán
    - cold; chilly; frigid; frost
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn; fall; harvest; season
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 寒(寒) (hán, 'cold') + 秋(秋) (qiū, 'autumn') — cold autumn
  - **interpretation:** The combination refers to the coldness typically experienced during the autumn season. Connecting the word 'cold' with 'autumn' reflects the seasonal changes where temperatures drop.
- **examples:**
  - 寒秋的早晨很清冷(寒秋的早晨很清冷)。
    - hánqiū de zǎochén hěn qīnglěng.
    - The mornings in late autumn are very chilly.
  - 我喜欢寒秋的宁静(我喜歡寒秋的寧靜)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān hánqiū de níngjìng.
    - I like the tranquility of late autumn.
