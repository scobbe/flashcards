## morning glory
### lǎbahuā
---
- **definition:** morning glory
- **pinyin:** lǎbahuā
- **components:**
  - 喇叭
    - lǎbā
    - horn; trumpet
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇(喇) (lǎ, "horn") + 叭(叭) (bā, "trumpet sound") + 花(花) (huā, "flower")
  - **interpretation:** The term 喇叭花 (lǎbahuā, "morning glory") describes the morning glory flower, which blooms in a shape reminiscent of a trumpet or horn (喇叭). Thus, the name captures both the flower's appearance and its vibrant, funnel-shaped blooms.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里有盛开的喇叭花(公園裡有盛開的喇叭花)。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu shèngkāi de lǎbahuā.
    - There are blooming morning glories in the park.
  - 早晨看到喇叭花(早晨看到喇叭花)，心情就变好了(心情就變好了)。
    - Zǎochén kàn dào lǎbahuā, xīnqíng jiù biànhǎole.
    - Seeing morning glories in the early morning lifts the spirits.
