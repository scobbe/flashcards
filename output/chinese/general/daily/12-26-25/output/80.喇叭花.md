### morning glory
---
- **pinyin:** morning glory
- **components:**
  - 喇叭
    - lǎbā
    - trumpet; horn
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom; bloom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇叭(喇叭) (lǎbā, 'trumpet') + 花(花) (huā, 'flower') — trumpet flower
  - **interpretation:** This term combines 'trumpet' and 'flower' because of the shape of the morning glory blossom, which resembles a trumpet. The vivid colors and trumpet-like structure of the flower make it visually striking.
- **examples:**
  - 早上我看到喇叭花(早上我看到喇叭花)，真漂亮！
    - Zǎoshang wǒ kàn dào lǎbā huā, zhēn piàoliang!
    - I saw morning glories in the morning, they are so beautiful!
  - 喇叭花在阳光下开放(喇叭花在陽光下開放)，吸引了很多蜜蜂。
    - Lǎbā huā zài yángguāng xià kāifàng, xīyǐnle hěn duō mìfēng.
    - The morning glories bloom under the sunlight, attracting many bees.
