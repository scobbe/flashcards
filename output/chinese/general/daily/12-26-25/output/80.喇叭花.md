## morning glory
### lǎbahuā
---
- **definition:** morning glory
- **pinyin:** lǎbahuā
- **components:**
  - 喇叭
    - lǎbā
    - horn; trumpet
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn") + 叭 (bā, "trumpet sound") + 花 (huā, "flower")
  - **interpretation:** The term 喇叭花 (lǎbahuā, "morning glory") describes the morning glory flower, which blooms in a shape reminiscent of a trumpet or horn (喇叭). Thus, the name captures both the flower's appearance and its vibrant, funnel-shaped blooms.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里(園裡)有盛开(開)的喇叭花。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu shèngkāi de lǎbahuā.
    - There are blooming morning glories in the park.
  - 早晨看到喇叭花，心情就变(變)好了。
    - Zǎochén kàn dào lǎbahuā, xīnqíng jiù biànhǎole.
    - Seeing morning glories in the early morning lifts the spirits.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭
- **definition:** horn; trumpet
- **pinyin:** lǎbā
- **components:**
  - 喇
    - lǎ
    - horn; sound of a trumpet
  - 叭
    - bā
    - trumpet sound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn; sound of a trumpet") and 叭 (bā, "trumpet sound") combine to form "喇叭" (lǎbā, "horn; trumpet").
  - **interpretation:** The word 喇叭 (lǎbā, "horn; trumpet") consists of two components both associated with sound or musical noise, emphasizing the loud, attention-grabbing effect of a horn or trumpet.
- **examples:**
  - 司机(機)按喇叭叫停闯红灯(闖紅燈)的人。
    - Sījī àn lǎbā jiào tíng chuǎng hóngdēng de rén.
    - The driver honked the horn to stop the person running a red light.
  - 音乐会(樂會)上，喇叭和小号(號)演奏出激昂的旋律。
    - Yīnyuè huì shàng, lǎbā hé xiǎohào yǎnzòu chū jī'áng de xuánlǜ.
    - At the concert, the horn and the trumpet played an inspiring melody.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 喇
- **definition:** horn; sound of a trumpet
- **pinyin:** lǎ
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 拉
    - lā
    - to pull
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 拉 (lā, "to pull") =
    - mouth forming sound + phonetic 拉 ->
    - horn sound
  - **interpretation:** The character 喇 combines 口 (kǒu, "mouth"), representing the sound produced, with the phonetic 拉 (lā, "to pull"), suggesting the action required to produce a horn's sound. This effectively conveys the sound of a horn or trumpet.
- **examples:**
  - 当(當)喇响(響)起时(時)，士兵们(們)迅速集合。
    - Dāng lǎ xiǎngqǐ shí, shìbīng men xùnsù jíhé.
    - When the horn sounds, the soldiers quickly assemble.
  - 他们(們)在庆(慶)典上吹奏喇。
    - Tāmen zài qìngdiǎn shàng chuīzòu lǎ.
    - They play the horn at the celebration.
  - 传(傳)令员(員)吹响(響)喇，驱(驅)散敌(敵)人。
    - Chuánlìngyuán chuīxiǎng lǎ, qūsàn dírén.
    - The herald blows the horn, driving away the enemy.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 喇 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开(張開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, dàshēng hǎnjiào.
    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 喇 → 拉
- **definition:** to pull
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - hand 手 (shǒu, 'hand') + phonetic 立 (lì, 'stand') =
    - action performed with the hand, using the sound of 立 to signify 'pull'
  - **interpretation:** The character 拉 (lā, 'pull') combines 手 (shǒu, 'hand') to indicate an action involving manipulation with a hand, while 立 (lì, 'stand') provides the phonetic pronunciation. The character effectively conveys the idea of using one's hand to 'pull' or 'drag' something.
- **examples:**
  - 他用力拉门(門)。
    - Tā yònglì lā mén.
    - He pulls the door with force.
  - 她拉开(開)窗帘(簾)，让阳(讓陽)光进来(進來)。
    - Tā lā kāi chuānglián, ràng yángguāng jìn lái.
    - She pulls open the curtains to let the sunlight in.
  - 那只狗拉着绳(著繩)子，追赶着(趕著)主人。
    - Nà zhī gǒu lāzhe shéngzi, zhuīgǎn zhe zhǔrén.
    - The dog pulls its leash while chasing its owner.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 喇 → 拉 → 手
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand with fingers; top stroke is a bent over middle finger, horizontal strokes represent fingers
  - **interpretation:** The character 手 (shǒu, "hand") is a direct pictorial representation of a human hand with all five fingers, which distinguishes it from similar characters representing claws or simplified fingers. This visually conveys its meaning as a hand clearly.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手写(寫)字。
    - Tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - He writes with his hand.
  - 妈妈牵着(媽媽牽著)我的手走路。
    - Māmā qiān zhe wǒ de shǒu zǒu lù.
    - Mom holds my hand while walking.
  - 他的手很温(溫)暖，带给(帶給)我力量。
    - Tā de shǒu hěn wēn nuǎn, dài gěi wǒ lìliàng.
    - His hand is very warm, giving me strength.

### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 叭
- **definition:** trumpet sound
- **pinyin:** bā
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 巴
    - bā
    - sound
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 巴 (bā) =
    - sound made by mouth ->
    - trumpet sound
  - **interpretation:** The character 叭(bā) combines the semantic element 口 (kǒu, "mouth"), indicating that the sound originates from the mouth, with the phonetic component 巴 (bā) to suggest the pronunciation. This configuration emphasizes sounds that are articulated in a pronounced manner, such as a trumpet sound.
- **examples:**
  - 小号(號)演奏时(時)，会发出‘叭’声(會發出‘叭’聲)。
    - xiǎo hào yǎnzòu shí, huì fāchū 'bā' shēng.
    - When the trumpet is played, it produces a 'bā' sound.
  - 列车启动时(車啟動時)，传来(傳來)嘹亮的叭。
    - lièchē qǐdòng shí, chuán lái liáoliàng de bā.
    - When the train starts, a resounding 'bā' can be heard.
  - 在音乐会(樂會)上，那悠扬(揚)的叭成为节(為節)目的亮点(點)。
    - zài yīnyuèhuì shàng, nà yōuyáng de bā chéngwéi jiémù de liàngdiǎn.
    - At the concert, the melodious 'bā' became the highlight of the program.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭 → 叭 → 巴

  - **description:** Depicts a snake coiled around a branch

- **examples:**

  - 巴先生今天出席会议(會議)，分享成功经验(經驗)。
    - Bā xiānshēng jīntiān chūxí huìyì, fēnxiǎng chénggōng jīngyàn.

### 喇叭花 → 花
- **definition:** flower; blossom
- **pinyin:** huā
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic 化 (huà, "transform") =
    - plant + phonetic indicator ->
    - flower
  - **interpretation:** 花 (huā, "flower") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") indicating its association with vegetation, and 化 (huà, "transform") for its phonetic value. This illustrates both the natural aspect and the phonetic pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 花香四溢。
    - huā xiāng sì yì。
    - The fragrance of flowers fills the air.
  - 阳(陽)光明媚，花儿争艳(兒爭艷)。
    - yáng guāng míng mèi，huā ér zhēng yàn。
    - The bright sunshine shines as the flowers compete in beauty.
  - 她用花装点(裝點)房间(間)。
    - tā yòng huā zhuāng diǎn fáng jiān。
    - She decorates the room with flowers.
### 喇叭花 → 花 → 艹
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(來時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 喇叭花 → 花 → 化
- **definition:** transform
- **pinyin:** huà
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 𠤎
    - rén

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person") upside down =

  - **interpretation:** The character 化 (huà, "transform") visually represents change or transformation, signified by two 人 (rén, "person") characters in opposite orientations, suggesting reversal or alteration.
- **examples:**

  - 时间(時間)能让伤(讓傷)痛化作记忆(記憶)。
    - Shíjiān néng ràng shāngtòng huà zuò jìyì.

  - 困境中他不断(斷)努力，生命终将(終將)化为辉(為輝)煌。

### 喇叭花 → 花 → 化 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**

  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** 亻 (rén, "person") is used as a component form of 人 (rén, "person") in compound characters, representing the human or person-related aspect.

    - Xuéxí hànzì shí, liǎojiě rén de yìyì hěn zhòngyào.
    - When learning Chinese characters, understanding the meaning of 亻 is important.
  - 古人造字时(時)常取亻以表达(達)人物情感，因此亻贯(貫)穿于(於)文明史中。
    - Gǔrén zào zì shí cháng qǔ rén yǐ biǎodá rénwù qínggǎn, yīncǐ rén guànchuān yú wénmíng shǐ zhōng.
    - Ancient people often used the radical 亻 to express human emotions in character formation, and thus 亻 runs through the history of civilization.
