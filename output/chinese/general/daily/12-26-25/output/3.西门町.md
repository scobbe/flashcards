---

- **definition:** Ximending, Taipei

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 西 (xī, "west"), 門 (mén, "gate"), 町 (dīng, "pavilion; village; ward") is a term used in Taiwan to designate wards or urban districts.
  - **interpretation:** 西( )門(门)町( ) (Xīmén Dīng) refers to Ximending, a well-known shopping and entertainment district in Taipei, Taiwan. The name combines 西 (xī, "west") and 門 (mén, "gate"), indicating its historic location near the western gate of old Taipei, with 町 (dīng, "pavilion; village; ward") as a naming convention for urban districts, reflecting its vibrant community atmosphere.

### 西( )门(門)町( ) → 西
- **definition:** west
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a bag or basket ->
    - borrowed for phonetic value to mean 'west'
  - **interpretation:** The character 西(xī, 'west') originally depicted a bag or basket and was phonetically borrowed to represent 'west'. Although traditionally linked to the image of a bird in a nest symbolizing the setting sun, this interpretation is disputed. Instead, it reflects a simple phonetic borrowing.
