## 西门町(西門町)
---
## 
### Ximending, Taipei
---
- **pinyin:** Ximending, Taipei
- **components:**
  - 西
    - xī
    - west; western; westward; direction
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate; door; entrance; opening
  - 丁
    - dīng
    - block; section; nail; male
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 西(西) (xī, 'west') + 门(門) (mén, 'gate') + 丁(丁) (dīng, 'block'; 'section') — west gate block
  - **interpretation:** The name 'Ximending' translates literally to 'west gate block', referring to its location near a western gate within Taipei. This area is known for its shopping and vibrant atmosphere.
- **examples:**
  - 西门町是台北著名的购物区。(西門町是台北著名的購物區。)
    - Xīmén Dīng shì Táiběi zhùmíng de gòuwù qū.
    - Ximending is a famous shopping district in Taipei.
  - 我今天去西门町逛街。(我今天去西門町逛街。)
    - Wǒ jīntiān qù Xīmén Dīng guàngjiē.
    - I'm going to Ximending to shop today.
### 西门町(西門町) → 西(西)
- **definition:** west; western; westward; direction
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bag or basket, traditionally explained as a bird settling into its nest, symbolizing the setting of the sun, hence meaning 'west'.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a bird nesting suggests the end of the day as the sun sets in the west, creating a vivid mental link between the character and its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在西边(我們在西邊)，可以看到日落(可以看到日落)。
    - Wǒmen zài xībiān, kěyǐ kàn dào rìluò.
    - We are in the west, where we can see the sunset.
  - 他住在西方(他住在西方)，喜欢旅行(喜歡旅行)。
    - Tā zhù zài xīfāng, xǐhuān lǚxíng.
    - He lives in the west and enjoys traveling.
  - 西风(西風)叫人感到凉爽(叫人感到涼爽)。
    - Xī fēng jiào rén gǎndào liángshuǎng.
    - The west wind makes people feel cool.
### 西门町(西門町) → 门(門)
- **definition:** gate; door; entrance; opening
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a gate with two doors that can be opened or closed.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents an entrance or barrier, symbolizing access or separation.
- **examples:**
  - 这扇门很漂亮(這扇門很漂亮)。
    - Zhè shàn mén hěn piàoliang.
    - This door is very beautiful.
  - 门口有很多人(門口有很多人)。
    - Ménkǒu yǒu hěn duō rén.
    - There are many people at the entrance.
### 西门町(西門町) → 丁(丁)
- **definition:** block; section; nail; male
- **pinyin:** dīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the head of a nail.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents a nail's head, indicating its tight fit or attachment to materials, which resonates well with the meanings associated with stability and sectioning.
- **examples:**
  - 这个丁字钉很好用(這個丁字釘很好用)，可以用来固定材料(可以用來固定材料)。
    - Zhège dīng zì dīng hěn hǎo yòng, kěyǐ yòng lái gēndìng cáiliào.
    - This nail is very useful, it can be used to secure materials.
