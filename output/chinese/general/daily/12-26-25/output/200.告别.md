## say goodbye; farewell
### gàobié
---
- **definition:** say goodbye; farewell
- **pinyin:** gàobié
- **components:**
  - 告
    - gào
    - announce; report
  - 别(別)
    - bié
    - leave; part; farewell
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 告(告) (gào, "announce") + 别(別) (bié, "leave") =
    - gàobié (告別), "say goodbye; farewell"
  - **interpretation:** The word 告别(告別) (gàobié, "say goodbye; farewell") combines 告(告) (gào, "announce") and 别(別) (bié, "leave"). It conveys a sense of formally announcing one's departure, creating a feeling of saying goodbye or bidding farewell.
- **examples:**
  - 我们要告别昨天(我們要告別昨天)。
    - Wǒ yào gàobié zuótiān.
    - We must bid farewell to yesterday.
  - 他告别旧友(他告別舊友)，踏上新旅程(踏上新旅程)。
    - Tā gàobié jiùyǒu, tà shàng xīn lǚchéng.
    - He bid farewell to old friends and set off on a new journey.
### 告别(告別) → 告(告)
- **definition:** announce; report
- **pinyin:** gào
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Unclear
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram theory: tongue sticking out from mouth ->
    - speak; Ideogrammic compound theory: abbreviated 壴(壴) + 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - decorated drum + open mouth ->
    - announce
  - **interpretation:** The character 告(告) (gào, "announce") may derive from a depiction of speaking, either from a tongue or a drum plus mouth combination, reflecting its meaning of making an announcement.
- **examples:**
  - 校长告全校师生(校長告全校師生)。
    - xiào zhǎng gào quán xiào shī shēng。
    - The principal announced to all the school staff and students.
  - 记者告民众(記者告民眾)，灾情严重(災情嚴重)。
    - jì zhě gào mín zhòng, zāi qíng yán zhòng。
    - The reporter reported to the people that the disaster situation was severe.
  - 政府告最新安全规定(政府告最新安全規定)，民众认真遵守(民眾認真遵守)。
    - zhèng fǔ gào zuì xīn ān quán guī dìng, mín zhòng rèn zhēn zūn shǒu。
    - The government announced the latest safety regulations, and the citizens complied earnestly.
### 告别(告別) → 别(別)
- **definition:** leave; part; farewell
- **pinyin:** bié
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** variant
  - **description:** 別(別) (bié, "leave") with 另(另) (lìng, "another") replacing 𠮠(𠮠) as visually similar substitution
  - **interpretation:** The character 別(別) (bié, "leave") was simplified by replacing the complex component 𠮠(𠮠) with 另(另) (lìng, "another"), which resembles it visually, making the character easier to write.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by substituting 𠮠(𠮠) with visually similar 另(另) (lìng, "another") to form 别(别) (bié, "leave").
- **examples:**
  - 分别时请互道别(分別時請互道別)。
    - Fēnbié shí qǐng hù dào bié.
    - When parting, please say farewell to each other.
  - 在离别前他深深地叹了口气(在離別前他深深地嘆了口氣)，挥手道别(揮手道別)。
    - Zài líbié qián tā shēnshēn de tàn le kǒuqì, huī shǒu dào bié.
    - Before parting, he let out a deep sigh and waved goodbye.
  - 他们告别时眼含泪水(他們告別時眼含淚水)。
    - Tāmen gàobié shí yǎn hán lèishuǐ.
    - They parted with tears in their eyes.
