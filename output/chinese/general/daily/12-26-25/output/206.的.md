### possessive/attributive particle
---
- **pinyin:** possessive/attributive particle
- **components:**
  - 白
    - bái
    - white
  - 勺
    - sháo
    - ladle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Contains semantic 白 (白) (bái, 'white') suggesting clarity or purity and phonetic 勺 (勺) (sháo, 'ladle') contributing to its pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The inclusion of 白 conveys the idea of clarity associated with possession or attributes. The phonetic element 勺 supports the character's pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 这是我的书(這是我的書)。
    - zhè shì wǒ de shū.
    - This is my book.
  - 他的家很大(他的家很大)。
    - tā de jiā hěn dà.
    - His house is very big.
### 的(的) → 白(白)
- **definition:** white
- **pinyin:** bái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a simple representation of a bright light source.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents brightness or light, intuitively signifying purity and clarity, akin to the color white.
- **examples:**
  - 她穿着白裙子(她穿着白裙子)，看起来很美丽(看起來很美麗)！
  - 他的画非常白(他的畫非常白)，更加清晰(更加清晰)。
  - 天空很白(天空很白)，今天可能要下雨(今天可能要下雨)！
### 的(的) → 勺(勺)
- **definition:** ladle
- **pinyin:** sháo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents something (丶) within the ladle or spoon (勹).
  - **interpretation:** The character visually captures the essence of a ladle, showing a small dot indicating something contained within its curved shape, reflecting its function.
- **examples:**
  - 我用勺子吃汤(我用勺子吃湯)。
    - Wǒ yòng sháozi chī tāng.
    - I eat soup with a ladle.
  - 她给我递了一个勺(她給我遞了一個勺)。
    - Tā gěi wǒ dìle yīgè sháo.
    - She handed me a ladle.
  - 这勺很大(這勺很大)。
    - Zhè sháo hěn dà.
    - This ladle is very large.
