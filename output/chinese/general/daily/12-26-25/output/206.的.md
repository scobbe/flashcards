## possessive/attributive particle
### de
---
- **definition:** possessive/attributive particle
- **pinyin:** de
- **components:**
  - 白
    - bái
    - white
  - 勺
    - sháo
    - ladle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Original: 旳 (日 (rì, "sun") + 勺 (sháo, "ladle")) =
    - bright ->
    - semantic: 白 (bái, "white") + phonetic: 勺 (sháo, "ladle") =
    - "bright white" ->
    - possessive/attributive particle
  - **interpretation:** The development from 旳, using a sun radical to indicate brightness, to 的, with a white radical, aligns with the shift from a focus on brightness to a grammatical particle, while retaining the phonetic indication.
  - **simplification:** None, as the traditional and simplified forms are identical.
- **examples:**
  - 这是我的书(這是我的書)。
    - Zhè shì wǒ de shū.
    - This is my book.
  - 他买了一件漂亮的衣服(他買了一件漂亮的衣服)，送给他的朋友(送給他的朋友)。
    - Tā mǎi le yī jiàn piàoliàng de yīfú, sòng gěi tā de péngyǒu.
    - He bought a beautiful piece of clothing and gave it to his friend.
### 的(的) → 白(白)
- **definition:** white
- **pinyin:** bái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the sun or an object with radiance ->
    - white
  - **interpretation:** The character 白(bái, "white") originally depicted the radiance of the sun or a round object, conveying the concept of brightness associated with the color white.
- **examples:**
  - 那只猫的毛真白(那隻貓的毛真白)。
    - Nà zhī māo de máo zhēn bái.
    - The cat's fur is really white.
  - 他拿出一张白纸(他拿出一張白紙)，开始认真画画(開始認真畫畫)。
    - Tā ná chū yī zhāng bái zhǐ, kāi shǐ rèn zhēn huà huà.
    - He took out a white sheet of paper and began drawing carefully.
  - 海边的浪花很白(海邊的浪花很白)，在阳光下闪闪发光(在陽光下閃閃發光)。
    - Hǎi biān de làng huā hěn bái, zài yáng guāng xià shǎn shǎn fā guāng.
    - The sea foam at the beach is very white, sparkling under the sunlight.
### 的(的) → 勺(勺)
- **definition:** ladle
- **pinyin:** sháo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a ladle or spoon
  - **interpretation:** The character 勺(sháo, "ladle") represents the pictorial depiction of a ladle or spoon, capturing its simple form used for scooping.
- **examples:**
  - 他用勺舀汤(他用勺舀湯)。
    - tā yòng sháo yǎo tāng。
    - He uses a ladle to scoop up the soup.
  - 厨房里没有勺(廚房裡沒有勺)，我们只能用碗舀汤(我們只能用碗舀湯)。
    - chúfáng lǐ méiyǒu sháo, wǒmen zhǐ néng yòng wǎn yǎo tāng。
    - There is no ladle in the kitchen, so we have to use a bowl to scoop up the soup.
  - 大厨拿起一把勺(大廚拿起一把勺)，熟练地调配材料(熟練地調配材料)。
    - dà chú ná qǐ yī bǎ sháo, shúliàn de tiáopèi cáiliào。
    - The chef picks up a ladle skillfully, blending the ingredients.
