## Huai-Hai region
### Huái-Hǎi
---
- **definition:** Huai-Hai region
- **pinyin:** Huái-Hǎi
- **components:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea; ocean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River") is the name of a major river in China that historically served as a geographical boundary, and 海 (hǎi, "sea") refers to large bodies of saltwater. Together, 淮海 (Huái-Hǎi, "Huai-Hai") refers to the region encompassing the area around the Huai River and near the sea, typically indicating a significant and historical geographical area in China.
  - **interpretation:** The term 淮海 (Huái-Hǎi) identifies a specific geographical region in China, known for its location around the Huai River and proximity to bodies of water like the East China Sea. It highlights the importance of water bodies in defining historical and cultural regions.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海是中国(國)重要的农业区(農業區)。
    - Huái-Hǎi shì Zhōngguó zhòngyào de nóngyè qū.
    - Huai-Hai is an important agricultural region in China.
  - 淮海风(風)光旖旎，吸引了无数游(無數遊)客。
    - Huái-Hǎi fēngguāng yǐnǐ, xīyǐn le wúshù yóukè.
    - The picturesque scenery of Huai-Hai attracts countless tourists.
  - 历(歷)史上淮海曾经见证(經見證)了许(許)多重要事件。
    - Lìshǐ shàng Huái-Hǎi céngjīng jiànzhèng le xǔduō zhòngyào shìjiàn.
    - Historically, Huai-Hai has witnessed many important events.
### 淮海 → 淮
- **definition:** Huai River
- **pinyin:** huái
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 隹
    - zhuī
    - short-tailed bird
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") =
    - sound component ->
    - name of Huai River
  - **interpretation:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River") combines 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") to convey its association with a body of water, specifically a river, and 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") to provide the phonetic element. Together, they represent the Huai River, which is a major waterway in China.
- **examples:**
  - 沿着(著)淮河寻(尋)找诗(詩)意。
    - Yánzhe Huái hé xúnzhǎo shīyì.
    - Following the Huai River to seek poetic inspiration.
  - 春风(風)送暖时(時)，淮河水面闪烁着银(閃爍著銀)光。
    - Chūnfēng sòng nuǎn shí, Huái hé shuǐmiàn shǎnshuò zhe yínguāng.
    - When the spring breeze warms the air, the surface of the Huai River sparkles with silver light.
  - 夜幕降临后(臨後)，淮水流淌出古城的传说(傳說)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín hòu, Huái shuǐ liútǎng chū gǔchéng de chuánshuō.
    - After dusk falls, the Huai waters flow out legends of the ancient city.
### 淮海 → 淮 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**

    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼儿欢(魚兒歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 淮海 → 淮 → 隹
- **definition:** short-tailed bird
- **pinyin:** zhuī
- **components:**
  - 鷕
    - yǎo
    - cry of a bird
  - 唯
    - wéi
    - only
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a short‐tailed bird (e.g., a sparrow) ->
    - also original form of 唯 (wéi, "only") before evolution into 鷕 (yǎo, "cry of a bird")
  - **interpretation:** 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") is a pictogram representing a bird with a short tail. Its depiction served as the foundation for other characters related to birds, such as 鷕 (yǎo, "cry of a bird").
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)只短尾的隹在树(樹)林中飞(飛)翔。
    - Zhè zhī duǎnwěi de zhuī zài shùlín zhōng fēixiáng.
    - This short-tailed bird flies in the forest.
  - 博物馆里(館裡)展出了一件以隹为题(為題)材的古代绘画(繪畫)。
    - Bówùguǎn lǐ zhǎnchūle yī jiàn yǐ zhuī wèi tícái de gǔdài huìhuà.
    - The museum displays an ancient painting themed on the short-tailed bird.
  - 远(遠)古诗(詩)篇中常用隹来(來)象征(徵)自由，寓意生动(動)。
    - Yuǎngǔ shīpiān zhōng cháng yòng zhuī lái xiàngzhēng zìyóu, yùyì shēngdòng.
    - In ancient poems, the short-tailed bird is often used to symbolize freedom, conveying vivid meaning.

### 淮海 → 淮 → 隹 → 鷕 → 唯

  - 口

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") =
