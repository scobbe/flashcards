## Huai-Hai region
### Huái-Hǎi
---
- **definition:** Huai-Hai region
- **pinyin:** Huái-Hǎi
- **components:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea; ocean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River") is the name of a major river in China that historically served as a geographical boundary, and 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") refers to large bodies of saltwater. Together, 淮海(淮海) (Huái-Hǎi, "Huai-Hai") refers to the region encompassing the area around the Huai River and near the sea, typically indicating a significant and historical geographical area in China.
  - **interpretation:** The term 淮海(淮海) (Huái-Hǎi) identifies a specific geographical region in China, known for its location around the Huai River and proximity to bodies of water like the East China Sea. It highlights the importance of water bodies in defining historical and cultural regions.

    - Huai-Hai is an important agricultural region in China.
  - 淮海风光旖旎(淮海風光旖旎)，吸引了无数游客(吸引了無數遊客)。
    - Huái-Hǎi fēngguāng yǐnǐ, xīyǐn le wúshù yóukè.
    - The picturesque scenery of Huai-Hai attracts countless tourists.
  - 历史上淮海曾经见证了许多重要事件(歷史上淮海曾經見證了許多重要事件)。
    - Lìshǐ shàng Huái-Hǎi céngjīng jiànzhèng le xǔduō zhòngyào shìjiàn.
