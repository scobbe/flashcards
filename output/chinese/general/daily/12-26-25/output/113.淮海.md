## Huai-Hai region
### Huái-Hǎi
---
- **definition:** Huai-Hai region
- **pinyin:** Huái-Hǎi
- **components:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea; ocean; maritime
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River") + 海(海) (hǎi, "sea") refers to the Huai-Hai region, a geographical area associated with the Huai River and the sea.
  - **interpretation:** The term 淮海 refers to a region centered around the Huai River, stretching towards the sea. It is a geographic designation used to describe a specific area within China.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海地区风光旖旎(淮海地區風光旖旎)。
    - Huái-Hǎi dìqū fēngguāng yǐnǐ.
    - The Huai-Hai region has beautiful scenery.
  - 我计划去淮海(淮海)，体验当地文化(體驗當地文化)。
    - Wǒ jìhuà qù Huái-Hǎi, tǐyàn dāngdì wénhuà.
    - I plan to visit the Huai-Hai region and experience the local culture.
  - 淮海自古便是商贸重镇(淮海自古便是商貿重鎮)。
    - Huái-Hǎi zìgǔ biàn shì shāngmào zhòngzhèn.
    - The Huai-Hai region has long been a hub of commerce.
### 淮海(淮海) → 淮(淮)
- **definition:** Huai River
- **pinyin:** huái
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 隹
    - zhuī
    - short-tailed bird
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + Phonetic: 隹(隹) (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") =
    - name of a river
  - **interpretation:** The character 淮(淮) (huái, "Huai River") combines the water radical 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") indicating its association with a body of water, and the phonetic component 隹(隹) (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") which provides the pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** None, as the traditional and simplified forms are identical.
- **examples:**
  - 淮水滔滔(淮水滔滔)，载着历史沧桑(載著歷史滄桑)。
    - Huái shuǐ tāotāo, zài zhe lìshǐ cāngsāng.
    - The Huai water surges, carrying the vicissitudes of history.
  - 漫步淮岸(漫步淮岸)，感受江南风韵(感受江南風韻)。
    - Mànbù huái àn, gǎnshòu jiāngnán fēngyùn.
    - Strolling along the banks of the Huai, one feels the charm of Jiangnan.

  - **description:** Radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water")

### 淮海(淮海) → 淮(淮) → 隹(隹)
- **definition:** short-tailed bird
- **pinyin:** zhuī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a short-tailed bird
  - **interpretation:** 隹(隹) (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") originated as a pictorial representation of a bird with a short tail, a feature common in sparrows and similar birds. It is the original character for depicting small birds and its form suggests this particular physical trait, distinguishing it from more generic bird characters like 鳥(鳥) (niǎo, "bird").
  - **simplification:** None, identical in traditional and simplified forms.
- **examples:**
  - 早晨时分(早晨時分)，我在公园中遇到一只隹(我在公園中遇到一只隹)。
    - Zǎochén shífēn, wǒ zài gōngyuán zhōng yùdào yì zhī zhuī.
    - In the early morning, I encountered a short-tailed bird in the park.
  - 黄昏时刻(黃昏時刻)，隹在树枝间轻快地跳跃(隹在樹枝間輕快地跳躍)。
    - Huánghūn shíkè, zhuī zài shùzhī jiān qīngkuài de tiàoyuè.
    - At dusk, the short-tailed bird lightly hops between the branches.
### 淮海(淮海) → 海(海)
- **definition:** sea; ocean; maritime
- **pinyin:** hǎi
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 每
    - měi
    - each
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 每(每) (měi, "each") =
    - water + phonetic component ->
    - sea
  - **interpretation:** 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") clearly represents the semantic element related to water, while 每(每) (měi) provides the phonetic component that hints at the pronunciation of 海(海) (hǎi). The combination effectively conveys the meaning related to large bodies of water like the sea.
- **examples:**
  - 海水温暖(海水溫暖)，鱼群活跃(魚群活躍)。
    - hǎi shuǐ wēnnuǎn, yú qún huóyuè.
    - The sea water is warm, and the schools of fish are lively.
  - 黄昏时分(黃昏時分)，我站在辽阔的海边(我站在遼闊的海邊)，眺望远方(眺望遠方)。
    - huánghūn shífèn, wǒ zhàn zài liáokuò de hǎibiān, tiàowàng yuǎnfāng.
    - At dusk, I stand by the vast sea and gaze into the distance.
  - 东海之滨(東海之濱)，潮起潮落(潮起潮落)。
    - dōng hǎi zhī bīn, cháoqǐ cháoluò.
    - On the shores of the East Sea, the tides rise and fall.

### 淮海(淮海) → 海(海) → 每(每)
- **definition:** each
- **pinyin:** měi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person (bottom reminiscent of 母(mother)) and hair (top strokes) ->
    - each individual seen as separate ->
    - each
  - **interpretation:** The character 每(每) (měi, 'each') originated from a pictogram showing a person with hair, representing the idea of 'each' or 'every' individual as distinct.
- **examples:**
