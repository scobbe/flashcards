## garden
### yuán
---
- **definition:** garden
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 袁
    - yuán
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") + 袁(袁) (yuán) =
    - enclosed area ->
    - garden
  - **interpretation:** The character 园(園) (yuán, "garden") combines 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure"), indicating a bounded space, with the phonetic component 袁(袁) (yuán), suggesting the sound. This combination effectively conveys the idea of a 'garden' as an enclosed space for cultivation.
  - **simplification:** 园(園) uses a simplified 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") and omits the complex internal structure of 袁(袁), retained only as a phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 这座园十分宁静(這座園十分寧靜)。
    - Zhè zuò yuán shí fēn níng jìng.
    - This garden is very peaceful.
  - 孩子们在园玩耍(孩子們在園玩耍)，满园花香四溢(滿園花香四溢)。
    - Háizimen zài yuán wán shuǎ, mǎn yuán huā xiāng sì yì.
    - Children are playing in the garden, and the whole garden is filled with the fragrance of flowers.
  - 古人常在园内吟诗(古人常在園內吟詩)，借此寄托情怀(借此寄託情懷)。
    - Gǔ rén cháng zài yuán nèi yín shī, jiè cǐ jì tuō qíng huái.
    - Ancient scholars often recited poems in the garden, using it to express their sentiments.

  - 言
    - yán
    - word
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 甘
    - gān

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 言(言) (yán, "word") + something in 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") written like 甘(甘) =
    - sound coming from the mouth ->
    - vertical trait representing tongue disappeared ->

    - final form

- **examples:**

    - Zài héchéng hànzì zhōng, yīn juédìng dúyīn.

    - Zhège zì de yīn shǐ wǒmen liánxiǎng dào yuánshǐ fàyīn.
