### garden
---
- **pinyin:** garden
- **components:**
  - 圓
    - yuán
    - round
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Combines 園(圓) (yuán, 'round') and the earth radical 土(tǔ, 'earth') to imply a round plot of land or garden.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of a circular area of cultivated land, a space for gardens.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by removing the inner 柒 component while retaining the meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我在公园(我在公園)，享受美丽的花朵(享受美麗的花朵)！
    - Wǒ zài gōngyuán, xiǎngshòu měilì de huāduǒ!
    - I am in the park, enjoying the beautiful flowers!
  - 这个花园(這個花園)非常漂亮(非常漂亮)。
    - Zhège huāyuán fēicháng piàoliang.
    - This garden is very beautiful.
### 园(園) → 圓(圓)
- **definition:** round
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
  - 員
    - yuán
    - person/member
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic element 囗 (walled enclosure) which suggests something enclosed with the phonetic component 員 (yuán) indicating pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The concept of roundness can be visually represented by an enclosure that is complete and continuous, evoking a sense of wholeness.
- **examples:**
  - 这个圆的形状非常完美。(這個圓的形狀非常完美。)
    - zhège yuán de xíngzhuàng fēicháng wánměi.
    - The shape of this circle is very perfect.
  - 我们要画一个大圆。(我們要畫一個大圓。)
    - wǒmen yào huà yīgè dà yuán.
    - We need to draw a big circle.
### 园(園) → 圓(圓) → 囗(囗)
- **definition:** enclosure
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a square-shaped enclosure.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a boundary or enclosure, signifying protection or containment.
- **examples:**
  - 这个地方有一个很大的囗(這個地方有一個很大的囗)。
    - zhège dìfāng yǒu yī gè hěn dà de wéi.
    - This place has a very large enclosure.
  - 他的家在这个囗(他的家在這個囗)。
    - tā de jiā zài zhège wéi.
    - His home is in this enclosure.
### 园(園) → 圓(圓) → 員(員)
- **definition:** person/member
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 〇
    - wǔ
    - circle
  - 鼎
    - dīng
    - tripod
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** A compound character that incorporates a 〇 indicating the top edge of a 鼎 (dīng, 'tripod') which is round.
  - **interpretation:** The round shape (〇) at the top signifies the top of a tripod (鼎), which can represent the idea of a person or member being part of a larger structure.
- **examples:**
  - 他是公司的员工(他是公司的員工)。
    - tā shì gōngsī de yuángōng.
    - He is an employee of the company.
  - 我需要找一个团队成员(我需要找一個團隊成員)。
    - wǒ xūyào zhǎo yīgè tuánduì yuán.
    - I need to find a team member.
### 园(園) → 圓(圓) → 員(員) → 〇(〇)
- **definition:** circle
- **pinyin:** wǔ

  - **interpretation:** The character depicts a circular shape, which visually represents the concept of a 'circle'.

### 园(園) → 圓(圓) → 員(員) → 鼎(鼎)
- **definition:** tripod
- **pinyin:** dīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tripod, representing an ancient bronze vessel with three legs used for cooking or performing rituals.
  - **interpretation:** The three legs symbolize stability and support, integral for holding pots used in cooking or religious ceremonies.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用鼎炖汤。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng dǐng dùn tāng.
    - I like to use a cauldron to stew soup.
  - 古代人用鼎祭祀。
    - Gǔdài rén yòng dǐng jìsì.
    - Ancient people used cauldrons for sacrifices.
### 园(園) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel.
  - **interpretation:** This visual representation of soil or clay conveys the material's fundamental nature in the earth, highlighting its role in forming and supporting life.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要好的土来种花(我們需要好的土來種花)。
    - Wǒmen xūyào hǎo de tǔ lái zhòng huā.
    - We need good soil to plant flowers.
  - 土可以用来建房子(土可以用來建房子)。
    - Tǔ kěyǐ yòng lái jiàn fángzi.
    - Soil can be used to build houses.
