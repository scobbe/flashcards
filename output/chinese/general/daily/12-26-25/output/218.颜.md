## 
### surname Yan
---
- **pinyin:** surname Yan
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is composed of 颜色(顏色) (yánsè, 'color') which indicates a visual aspect, combined with the phonetic component 𠂇 (a variant of 颜) providing sound.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of a person's appearance, particularly their face and color, which are significant in identifying individuals.
- **examples:**
  - 颜老师是我的朋友。(顏老師是我的朋友。)
    - Yán lǎoshī shì wǒ de péngyǒu.
    - Teacher Yan is my friend.
  - 他有一个好看的颜。(他有一個好看的顏。)
    - Tā yǒu yīgè hǎokàn de yán.
    - He has a good-looking face.
