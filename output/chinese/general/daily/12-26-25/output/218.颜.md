## surname Yan
### Yán
---
- **definition:** surname Yan
- **pinyin:** Yán
- **components:**
  - 彦(彥)
    - yàn
    - elegant
  - 页(頁)
    - yè
    - page/head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified
  - **description:** 顏(顏) (yán, "face") → Simplified 彦(彥) (yàn, "elegant") and 页(頁) (yè, "page/head")
  - **interpretation:** The traditional character 顏(顏) (yán, "face") was simplified by using 彦(彥) (yàn, "elegant") instead of 彥(彥) and 页(頁) (yè, "page/head") instead of 頁(頁) while maintaining the original meanings.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by substituting components with their simplified counterparts: 彥 to 彦 and 頁 to 页.
- **examples:**
  - 我认识一个叫颜的学者(我認識一個叫顏的學者)。
    - Wǒ rènshi yīgè jiào Yán de xuézhě.
    - I know a scholar with the surname Yan.
  - 颜家每年举行家族聚会(顏家每年舉行家族聚會)。
    - Yán jiā měi nián jǔxíng jiāzú jùhuì.
    - The Yan family holds a family reunion every year.
  - 颜氏家族历史悠久(顏氏家族歷史悠久)。
    - Yán shì jiāzú lìshǐ yōujiǔ.
    - The Yan clan has a long history.
### 颜(顏) → 彦(彥)
- **definition:** elegant
- **pinyin:** yàn
- **components:**
  - 彡
    - shān
    - hair/bristle
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 彡(彡) (shān, "hair/bristle") + phonetic: 彥(言) (yán, "speech") =
    - refined speech ->
    - elegant
  - **interpretation:** The character 彦(彥) (yàn, "elegant") combines the semantic 彡(彡) (shān, "hair/bristle"), suggesting fine or delicate, with the phonetic 彥(言) (yán, "speech"), indicating articulation or refinement, thus conveying a sense of elegance.
  - **simplification:** None, 彦 is the simplified form of 彥.
- **examples:**
  - 她穿着古典旗袍(她穿著古典旗袍)，举止间透出一种彦的气质(舉止間透出一種彥的氣質)。
    - tā chuānzhuó gǔdiǎn qípáo, jǔzhǐ jiān tòuchū yī zhǒng yàn de qìzhí.
    - She wears a traditional qipao, and her demeanor exudes an elegant quality.
  - 他在书房认真研读经典(他在書房認真研讀經典)，神情中透露出一种典雅彦气(神情中透露出一種典雅彥氣)。
    - tā zài shūfáng rènzhēn yándú jīngdiǎn, shénqíng zhōng tòulù chū yī zhǒng diǎnyǎ yàn qì.
    - He studies the classics diligently in his study, and his expression reveals a refined elegance.
  - 那位彦士总是以谦和礼貌待人(那位彥士總是以謙和禮貌待人)。
    - nà wèi yàn shì zǒngshì yǐ qiānhé lǐmào dàirén.
    - That elegant gentleman always treats people with humility and courtesy.
### 颜(顏) → 彦(彥) → 彡(彡)
- **definition:** hair/bristle
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts light or rays; like shimmering lines
  - **interpretation:** The character 彡(彡) (shān, "rays") visually represents the appearance of rays of light or the shimmering effect of something radiant.
- **examples:**
  - 阳光洒满大地(陽光灑滿大地)，彡般的光辉映出温暖(彡般的光輝映出溫暖)。
    - Yángguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì, shān bān de guānghuī yìngchū wēnnuǎn.
    - The sunlight fills the earth, and rays-like brilliance reveals its warmth.
  - 海边的波光闪烁(海邊的波光閃爍)，如彡释出的光芒般迷人(如彡釋出的光芒般迷人)。
    - Hǎibiān de bōguāng shǎnshuò, rú shān shìchū de guāngmáng bān mírén.
    - The sparkling waves on the seaside resemble rays emanating radiant light, enchanting to behold.
  - 清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光(清晨的露珠似彡射出的曦光)。
    - Qīngchén de lùzhū sì shān shèchū de xīguāng.
    - In the early morning, dewdrops seem to emit rays of dawn light.
### 颜(顏) → 彦(彥) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 舌(舌) (shé, "tongue") + horizontal stroke =
    - movement of tongue ->
    - speech
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") originates from the depiction of a tongue 舌(舌) with an added stroke to represent its movement, signifying the action of speaking. This visual representation highlights the tongue's role in speech, which is central to communication.
- **examples:**
  - 言简意赅可以表达深刻思想(言簡意賅可以表達深刻思想)。
    - yán jiǎn yì gāi kěyǐ biǎo dá shēn kè sī xiǎng。
    - Concise speech can express profound thoughts.
  - 他的言论总能激发思考(他的言論總能激發思考)。
    - tā de yán lùn zǒng néng jī fā sī kǎo。
    - His speech always inspires reflection.
  - 老师常说(老師常說)，言出必行(言出必行)。
    - lǎo shī cháng shuō, yán chū bì xíng。
    - The teacher often says that one should act on one's words.
### 颜(顏) → 页(頁)
- **definition:** page/head
- **pinyin:** yè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 首(首) (shǒu, "head") + 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person") ->
    - head of a kneeling person ->
    - "head" ->
    - alternative usage for "page"
  - **interpretation:** Originally a pictogram depicting the head of a kneeling person, 頁(頁) (yè, "head") conveyed the idea of a head. Over time, it evolved to include the meaning of "page" as an alternative form for "sheet" or "leaf", though its origin is preserved as a radical in words related to the head.
  - **simplification:** None, as 页 is already a simplified form of the traditional character 頁.
- **examples:**
  - 请翻开这一页(請翻開這一頁)。
    - Qǐng fān kāi zhè yī yè.
    - Please turn to this page.
  - 书的一页(書的一頁)，记载着古老的故事(記載著古老的故事)。
    - Shū de yī yè, jì zǎi zhe gǔ lǎo de gù shì.
    - A page of the book records ancient stories.
  - 这一页(這一頁)，印有精美的插图(印有精美的插圖)。
    - Zhè yī yè, yìn yǒu jīng měi de chā tú.
    - This page is printed with exquisite illustrations.
