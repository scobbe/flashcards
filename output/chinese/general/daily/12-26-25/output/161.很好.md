## very good
### hěn hǎo
---
- **definition:** very good
- **pinyin:** hěn hǎo
- **components:**
  - 很
    - hěn
    - very
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good; fine; nice; ok
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 很(很) (hěn, "very") + 好(好) (hǎo, "good") =
    - "very good"
  - **interpretation:** The word 很好 combines 很(很) (hěn, "very"), which is used to express a high degree or intensity, with 好(好) (hǎo, "good"), indicating that something is very good or satisfactory.
- **examples:**
  - 这部电影很好(這部電影很好)。
    - Zhè bù diànyǐng hěn hǎo.
    - This movie is very good.
  - 今天的午餐很好(今天的午餐很好)，大家吃得很开心(大家吃得很開心)。
    - Jīntiān de wǔcān hěn hǎo, dàjiā chī de hěn kāixīn.
    - Today's lunch is very good, and everyone is eating happily.
