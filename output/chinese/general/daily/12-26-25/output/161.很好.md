### very good
---
- **pinyin:** very good
- **components:**
  - 很
    - hěn
    - very; quite; extremely; awfully
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good; well; fine; proper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 很(很) (hěn, "very") + 好(好) (hǎo, "good") — very good
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 很(很) (hěn, "very") and 好(好) (hǎo, "good") clearly expresses a strong affirmation of the quality being described. When people want to emphasize that something is good, they use this phrase.
- **examples:**
  - 这个东西很好(這個東西很好)。
    - Zhège dōngxī hěn hǎo.
    - This thing is very good.
  - 她的表演很好(她的表演很好)。
    - Tā de biǎoyǎn hěn hǎo.
    - Her performance is very good.
### 很好(很好) → 很(很)
- **definition:** very; quite; extremely; awfully
- **pinyin:** hěn
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì

  - 艮

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character 很 combines the phonetic element 艮 (gèn) and the semantic element 彳 (chì), which contributes to its meaning of 'very' or 'extremely'.
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests an intensity or depth of feeling, as 彳 reflects movement or step and 艮 conveys something that is steadfast.
- **examples:**
  - 我很喜欢这本书(我很喜歡這本書)！
    - Wǒ hěn xǐhuān zhè běn shū！

  - 今天的天气很热(今天的天氣很熱)。
    - Jīntiān de tiānqì hěn rè.
    - Today's weather is very hot.
  - 他很聪明(他很聰明)。
    - Tā hěn cōngmíng.
    - He is very smart.
