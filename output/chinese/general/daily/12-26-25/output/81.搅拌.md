## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") and 拌(拌) (bàn, "mix") both involve moving or combining substances together. 搅拌(攪拌) (jiǎobàn) effectively describes the act of stirring and mixing various elements to create a uniform combination.
  - **interpretation:** The word combines the concepts of stirring and mixing, implying an action that results in an even distribution of substances.
- **examples:**
  - 请搅拌蛋液(請攪拌蛋液)。
    - Qǐng jiǎobàn dàn yè.
    - Please stir the egg mixture.
  - 我在厨房搅拌材料(我在廚房攪拌材料)，做出美味的汤(做出美味的湯)。
    - Wǒ zài chúfáng jiǎobàn cáiliào, zuò chū měiwèi de tāng.
    - I stir the ingredients in the kitchen, making a delicious soup.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪)
- **definition:** stir
- **pinyin:** jiǎo
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 覺
    - jiào
    - feel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 覺(覺) (jiào, "feel") ->
    - hand action involving moving ->
    - stirring
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys the action of stirring using a hand, with 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") representing the manual aspect and 覺(覺) (jiào, "feel") providing the pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** 攪(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") was simplified by retaining its core elements in a more straightforward form, merging the traditional components.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈搅汤时(媽媽攪湯時)，加入调料(加入調料)。
    - māmā jiǎo tāng shí, jiārù tiáoliào.
    - Mom stirs the soup, adding seasonings.
  - 他搅饮料(他攪飲料)，直到味道均匀(直到味道均勻)。
    - tā jiǎo yǐnliào, zhídào wèidào jūnyún.
    - He stirs the drink until the taste is uniform.
  - 厨房里的机器不停地搅(廚房裡的機器不停地攪)，让食材更加入味(讓食材更加入味)。
    - chúfáng lǐ de jīqì bù tíng de jiǎo, ràng shícài gèng jiārù ròuwèi.

### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪) → 扌(扌)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Derived from its seal script form
  - **description:**
    - Seal script 扌 (shǒu, "hand") ->

  - **interpretation:** 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") functions as a shorthand or radical form used mainly in compounded characters, retaining the meaning of 'hand.' This facilitates the creation of characters that involve manual action or activities related to the hands.
  - **simplification:** None, as 扌(扌) (shǒu, "hand") is already a simplified component derived for ease in writing.
- **examples:**

    - Wò shǒu biǎoshì yǒuhǎo, cùjìn hézuò.
    - A handshake signifies friendship, promoting cooperation.
