## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") and 拌(拌) (bàn, "mix") together mean the action of stirring and mixing substances. This combination logically represents the process of actively blending materials, often for cooking or industrial purposes.
  - **interpretation:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") involves a motion of agitation, while 拌(拌) (bàn, "mix") refers to combining substances. Used together, they succinctly describe the common action in cooking or mixing substances by stirring.
- **examples:**
  - 他用木棒搅拌面糊(他用木棒攪拌麵糊)。
    - Tā yòng mùbàng jiǎobàn miànhú.
    - He stirs the batter with a wooden stick.
  - 妈妈在厨房搅拌汤汁(媽媽在廚房攪拌湯汁)，细心调配调料(細心調配調料)。
    - Māmā zài chúfáng jiǎobàn tāngzhī, xìxīn tiáopèi tiáoliào.
    - Mom stirs the soup in the kitchen, carefully mixing the seasonings.
  - 厨师搅拌酱汁时(廚師攪拌醬汁時)，火候掌握得恰到好处(火候掌握得恰到好處)。
    - Chúshī jiǎobàn jiàngzhī shí, huǒhòu zhǎngwò dé qià dào hǎochù.
    - The chef stirs the sauce, perfectly controlling the heat.

- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
