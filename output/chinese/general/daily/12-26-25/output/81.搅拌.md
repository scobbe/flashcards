## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **traditional:** 攪拌
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") and 拌 (bàn, "mix") together mean the action of stirring and mixing substances. This combination logically represents the process of actively blending materials, often for cooking or industrial purposes.
  - **interpretation:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") involves a motion of agitation, while 拌 (bàn, "mix") refers to combining substances. Used together, they succinctly describe the common action in cooking or mixing substances by stirring.
