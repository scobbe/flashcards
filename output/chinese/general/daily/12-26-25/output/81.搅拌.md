### stir; mix
---
- **pinyin:** stir; mix
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir; interfere; disturb
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix; blend; stir
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, 'stir') + 拌(攪拌) (bàn, 'mix') — stir mix
  - **interpretation:** The components suggest an action of combining ingredients through stirring. This process is essential in cooking and food preparation.
- **examples:**
  - 我在搅拌蛋糕的面糊(我在攪拌蛋糕的面糊)。
    - Wǒ zài jiǎobàn dàn gāo de miàn hú.
    - I am stirring the batter for the cake.
  - 厨师需要搅拌汤(廚師需要攪拌湯)。
    - Chúshī xūyào jiǎobàn tāng.
    - The chef needs to stir the soup.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪)
- **definition:** stir; interfere; disturb
- **pinyin:** jiǎo
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 韭
    - jiǔ
    - chives
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of 扌 (hand) as the semantic component indicating action and 韭 (chives) as the phonetic component.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the image of using one's hand to stir or disturb chives, emphasizing physical action in the process of mixing or interfering.
- **examples:**
  - 她在锅里搅拌材料(她在鍋裡攪拌材料)。
    - Tā zài guō lǐ jiǎobàn cáiliào.
    - She is stirring the ingredients in the pot.
  - 不要搅扰我(不要攪擾我)。
    - Bùyào jiǎorǎo wǒ.
    - Don't disturb me.

### 搅拌(攪拌) → 拌(拌)
- **definition:** mix; blend; stir
- **pinyin:** bàn
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic element 扌 (shǒu, 'hand') indicating action with the phonetic 平 (píng, 'flat') suggesting evenness or balance when mixing.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 'hand' and 'flat' evokes the action of stirring or mixing ingredients evenly with one's hands.
- **examples:**
  - 我在拌沙拉(我在拌沙拉)。
    - wǒ zài bàn shā lā.
    - I am mixing the salad.
  - 他喜欢拌米饭(他喜歡拌米飯)。
    - tā xǐ huān bàn mǐ fàn.
    - He likes to mix rice.
  - 她教我如何拌面团(她教我如何拌麵團)。
    - tā jiāo wǒ rú hé bàn miàn tuán.
    - She taught me how to mix the dough.
