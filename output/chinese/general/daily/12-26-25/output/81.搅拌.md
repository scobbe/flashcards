## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **traditional:** 攪拌
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") and 拌 (bàn, "mix") together mean the action of stirring and mixing substances. This combination logically represents the process of actively blending materials, often for cooking or industrial purposes.
  - **interpretation:** 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") involves a motion of agitation, while 拌 (bàn, "mix") refers to combining substances. Used together, they succinctly describe the common action in cooking or mixing substances by stirring.
- **examples:**
  - 他用木棒搅(攪)拌面(麵)糊。
    - Tā yòng mùbàng jiǎobàn miànhú.
    - He stirs the batter with a wooden stick.
  - 妈妈(媽媽)在厨(廚)房搅(攪)拌汤(湯)汁，细(細)心调(調)配调(調)料。
    - Māmā zài chúfáng jiǎobàn tāngzhī, xìxīn tiáopèi tiáoliào.
    - Mom stirs the soup in the kitchen, carefully mixing the seasonings.
  - 厨师搅(廚師攪)拌酱(醬)汁时(時)，火候掌握得恰到好处(處)。
    - Chúshī jiǎobàn jiàngzhī shí, huǒhòu zhǎngwò dé qià dào hǎochù.
    - The chef stirs the sauce, perfectly controlling the heat.
### 搅(攪)拌( ) → 搅(攪)
- **traditional:** 攪
- **definition:** stir
- **pinyin:** jiǎo
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 喬
    - qiáo
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌 (shǒu, 'hand') + 喬 (qiáo, phonetic) =
    - stirring motion with the hand ->
    - to stir
  - **interpretation:** The character 攪 (jiǎo, 'stir') combines the 扌 (shǒu, 'hand') radical, indicating an action performed by the hand, with 喬 (qiáo), which serves as the phonetic component, together representing the act of stirring.
  - **simplification:** The simplified form 搅 replaces the phonetic component 喬 (qiáo) with 丩(jiū), which has a similar sound, reducing the complexity of the character.
- **examples:**
  - 他搅动(攪動)了汤(湯)。
    - Tā jiǎo dòng le tāng.
    - He stirred the soup.
  - 她用勺子轻轻搅(輕輕攪)，糖浆变(漿變)得浓(濃)稠。
    - Tā yòng sháo zi qīng qīng jiǎo, táng jiāng biàn dé nóng chóu.
    - She gently stirred with a spoon, and the syrup became thick.
  - 古人常用木棍搅(攪)清泉。
    - Gǔ rén cháng yòng mù gùn jiǎo qīng quán.
    - Ancient people often stirred the clear spring water with a wooden stick.
### 搅(攪)拌( ) → 搅(攪) → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞来(飛來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.

    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.

    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
