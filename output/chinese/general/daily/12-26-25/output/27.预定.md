## schedule; book
### yùdìng
---
- **definition:** schedule; book
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance
  - 定
    - dìng
    - decide; fix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 预(預) (yù, "in advance") + 定(定) (dìng, "decide; fix") =
    - scheduling or booking decided in advance
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense because you are deciding on something, such as a time or place, ahead of time, typically a booking or a schedule.
  - **simplification:** 预(預) simplified to 预, no change for 定
- **examples:**
  - 我预定机票(我預定機票)。
    - wǒ yùdìng jīpiào
    - I booked a flight ticket.
  - 他们预定了会议室(他們預定了會議室)，打算开会(打算開會)。
    - tāmen yùdìng le huìyì shì, dǎsuàn kāihuì
    - They booked the meeting room, planning to have a meeting.
### 预定(預定) → 预(預)
- **definition:** in advance
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 予
    - yǔ
    - to give
  - 頁
    - yè
    - page
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 予(予) (yǔ, "to give") + semantic: 頁(頁) (yè, "page") ->
    - 預 ->
    - simplification 頁(頁) (yè, "page") to 页(页) (yè, "page") ->
    - 预
  - **interpretation:** The character 預 incorporates 頁(頁) (yè, "page") to convey the idea of preliminary thinking or planning, with 予(予) (yǔ, "to give") illustrating the sound. This combination suggests preparing or thinking ahead.
  - **simplification:** The component 頁(頁) (yè, "page") is simplified to 页(页) (yè, "page"), retaining phonetic and semantic aspects in a streamlined form.
- **examples:**
  - 我们预定了晚餐(我們預定了晚餐)。
    - Wǒmen yùdìngle wǎncān.
    - We reserved dinner in advance.
  - 他提前预习了课文(他提前預習了課文)。
    - Tā tíqián yùxíle kèwén.
    - He reviewed the lesson in advance.
  - 公司预安排了会议(公司預安排了會議)，员工们积极准备(員工們積極準備)。
    - Gōngsī yù ānpái le huìyì, yuángōngmen jījí zhǔnbèi.
    - The company scheduled the meeting in advance, and the employees prepared actively.
### 预定(預定) → 预(預) → 予(予)
- **definition:** to give
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a stylized hand holding a tool ->
    - by extension, giving
  - **interpretation:** The character 予(予) (yǔ, "to give") originally depicted a hand holding an object, symbolizing the action of giving. Over time, this simple pictogram was used to convey the concept of granting or bestowing.
- **examples:**
  - 老师予同学机会(老師予同學機會)。
    - Lǎoshī yǔ tóngxué jīhuì.

  - 父亲总是予孩子温暖(父親總是予孩子溫暖)，激励他们勇敢前行(激勵他們勇敢前行)。
    - Fùqīn zǒngshì yǔ háizi wēnnuǎn, jīlì tāmen yǒnggǎn qiánxíng.
    - The father always gives his children warmth, inspiring them to move forward bravely.
  - 我予朋友诚挚祝福(我予朋友誠摯祝福)。
    - Wǒ yǔ péngyǒu chéngzhì zhùfú.
    - I give my friends sincere blessings.
### 预定(預定) → 预(預) → 頁(頁)
- **definition:** page
- **pinyin:** yè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 首(首) (shǒu, "head") + 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person") =
    - head of kneeling person, emphasizing head
  - **interpretation:** The character 頁 originally depicted the head of a kneeling person, combining 首(首) (shǒu, "head") with the body addition of 卩(卩) (jié, "kneeling person"), emphasizing the concept of 'head'. It evolved to mean 'page' or 'leaf' as an alternative form of 葉 and retains its original meaning in the radical form.
  - **simplification:** 頁 was simplified to 页 by adopting a cursive script shorthand of the traditional form 頁.
- **examples:**
  - 书的最后一页(書的最後一頁)，写着作者的名字(寫著作者的名字)。
    - Shū de zuìhòu yī yè, xiězhe zuòzhě de míngzì.
    - The last page of the book bears the author's name.
  - 请翻到第十页(請翻到第十頁)，开始阅读(開始閱讀)。
    - Qǐng fān dào dì shí yè, kāishǐ yuèdú.
    - Please turn to the tenth page to start reading.
  - 这张杂志的封面在第三页(這張雜誌的封面在第三頁)，呈现了美丽的图案(呈現了美麗的圖案)。
    - Zhè zhāng zázhì de fēngmiàn zài dì sān yè, chéngxiàn le měilì de tú'àn.
    - The cover of this magazine on the third page displays a beautiful pattern.
### 预定(預定) → 定(定)
- **definition:** decide; fix
- **pinyin:** dìng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 正
    - zhèng
    - upright
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 宀(宀) (mián, "roof") + phonetic 正(正) (zhèng, "upright") =
    - roof sheltering the upright ->
    - decide; fix
  - **interpretation:** The character 定(dìng, "decide; fix") features 宀(宀) (mián, "roof"), suggesting shelter or stability, combined with the phonetic 正(正) (zhèng, "upright"), which hints at a consistent, correct, or firm stance. Together, they convey the idea of making a decision under stable conditions.
- **examples:**
  - 我们定了会议时间(我們定了會議時間)。
    - Wǒ dìng le huìyì shíjiān.
    - We fixed the meeting time.
  - 他定下目标(他定下目標)，全力追求梦想(全力追求夢想)。
    - Tā dìng xià mùbiāo, quánlì zhuīqiú mèngxiǎng.
    - He set his goal and pursued his dream with full effort.
  - 老师定了考试范围(老師定了考試範圍)，并解释了重点(並解釋了重點)。
    - Lǎoshī dìng le kǎoshì fànwéi, bìng jiěshì le zhòngdiǎn.
    - The teacher fixed the exam scope and explained the key points.

### 预定(預定) → 定(定) → 正(正)
- **definition:** upright
- **pinyin:** zhèng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a foot (止(止) (zhǐ, 'foot')) stepping forward in one direction ->
    - represents alignment and correctness
  - **interpretation:** The character 正(正) (zhèng, 'upright') originally depicted a foot, with the idea of steady movement in a straight line, symbolizing correctness and propriety.
- **examples:**
  - 他的姿势十分正(他的姿勢十分正)。
    - tā de zīshì shífēn zhèng.
    - His posture is very upright.
  - 这棵树长得正(這棵樹長得正)，没有歪斜(沒有歪斜)。
