## 
### schedule; book
---
- **pinyin:** schedule; book
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - to anticipate; to predict; to reserve; to prearrange
  - 定
    - dìng
    - to fix; to set; to determine; to decide
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 预(預) (yù, "to anticipate") + 定(dìng, "to fix") — to fix in advance
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense as it implies arranging something in advance. Booking or scheduling involves both the anticipation of a future event and the finalizing of arrangements.
- **examples:**
  - 我想预定一个房间(我想預定一個房間)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yùdìng yīgè fángjiān.
    - I want to book a room.
  - 请您提前预定座位(請您提前預定座位)。
    - Qǐng nín tíqián yùdìng zuòwèi.
    - Please reserve your seats in advance.
### 预定(預定) → 预(預)
- **definition:** to anticipate; to predict; to reserve; to prearrange
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 予
    - yǔ
    - to give
  - 頁
    - yè
    - page
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines phonetic 予 (yǔ, 'to give') with semantic 頁 (yè, 'page, leaf').
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests the idea of giving or assigning something ahead of time, as a page might be set aside for anticipation or reservation.
  - **simplification:** The character simplified by replacing 頁 (yè, 'page') with 页 (yè, 'leaf') to reduce strokes for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我预定了酒店(我預定了酒店)。
    - Wǒ yùdìngle jiǔdiàn.
    - I reserved a hotel.
  - 他预见了这个问题(他預見了這個問題)。
    - Tā yùjiànle zhège wèntí.
    - He predicted this issue.

### 预定(預定) → 定(定)
- **definition:** to fix; to set; to determine; to decide
- **pinyin:** dìng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 正
    - zhèng
    - correct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 宀 (mián, 'roof') suggesting a fixed or stable place, with the phonetic element 正 (zhèng, 'correct') which contributes to the pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of a roof over a correct or stable place evokes a sense of fixing or determining a location or situation.
- **examples:**
  - 我们需要定一个时间(我們需要定一個時間)。
    - wǒmen xūyào dìng yīgè shíjiān.
    - We need to set a time.
  - 请你定这个计划(請你定這個計劃)。
    - qǐng nǐ dìng zhège jìhuà.
    - Please finalize this plan.
  - 他总是定下目标(他總是定下目標)。
    - tā zǒng shì dìng xià mùbiāo.
    - He always sets goals.
