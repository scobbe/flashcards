## schedule; book
### yùdìng
---
- **definition:** schedule; book
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance; beforehand; before; prepare
  - 定
    - dìng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 預(預) (yù, "in advance") + 定(定) (dìng, "decide; settle") =
    - to arrange or schedule something in advance
  - **interpretation:** The word 預定(預定) (yùdìng, "schedule; book") is composed of 預(預) (yù, "in advance") and 定(定) (dìng, "decide; settle"). - **examples:**
  - 我预定了一张电影票(我預定了一張電影票)。

    - Tā yùdìngle huìyì shì, dǎsuàn ānpái tǎolùn.
