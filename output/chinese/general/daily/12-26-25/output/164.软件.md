## 软件(軟件)
---
## 
### software
---
- **pinyin:** software
- **components:**
  - 软(軟)
    - ruǎn
    - soft; flexible; mild; gentle
  - 件
    - jiàn
    - item; piece; component; matter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 软(軟) (ruǎn, "soft") + 件(件) (jiàn, "item") — soft item
  - **interpretation:** The term refers to items or programs that are not physical but operate within a computer system. It accurately describes software as a flexible and intangible component of technology.
- **examples:**
  - 我在安装软件(我在安裝軟件)。
    - Wǒ zài ānzhuāng ruǎnjiàn.
    - I am installing software.
  - 这个软件(這個軟件)很容易使用。
    - Zhège ruǎnjiàn hěn róngyì shǐyòng.
    - This software is very easy to use.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟)
- **definition:** soft; flexible; mild; gentle
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
  - 軟
    - ruǎn
    - soft
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the phonetic component 軟 (ruǎn, 'soft') and the meaning component 車 (chē, 'vehicle') to suggest flexibility and gentleness, similar to how a vehicle can be smoothly navigated.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by reducing the number of strokes from 軟 to 软. The component 車 was omitted, which aligns with the trend of simplifying characters by removing complex or redundant elements.
- **examples:**
  - 这个枕头真软(這個枕頭真軟)，我喜欢(我喜歡)。
    - Zhège zhěntou zhēn ruǎn, wǒ xǐhuān.
    - This pillow is really soft, I like it.
  - 他的语气很软(他的語氣很軟)，让人感觉舒服(讓人感覺舒服)。
    - Tā de yǔqì hěn ruǎn, ràng rén gǎnjué shūfú.
    - His tone is very mild, making people feel comfortable.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 车(車)
- **definition:** vehicle
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a carriage seen from above with large wheels on both sides and a sun shade.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a vehicle, evocative of a carriage with its prominent wheels, emphasizing transportation.
- **examples:**
  - 这辆车很新。(這輛車很新。)
    - Zhè liàng chē hěn xīn.
    - This vehicle is very new.
  - 我们要开车去旅行。(我們要開車去旅行。)
    - Wǒmen yào kāi chē qù lǚxíng.
    - We will drive to travel.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 軟(軟)
- **definition:** soft
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 車
    - chē
    - carriage
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - to lack / to owe
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Originally written 輭, a phono-semantic compound composed of the semantic 車 ('carriage') and the phonetic 耎 ('njonʔ'). Over time, 耎 transformed into 欠.
  - **interpretation:** The character's components depict a car (車) associated with the idea of weariness or weakness. This connection to tiredness links the concept of a heavy carriage to the softness associated with being exhausted or worn out.
- **examples:**
  - 这件衣服很软(這件衣服很軟)。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfú hěn ruǎn.
    - This piece of clothing is very soft.
  - 他的声音很软(他的聲音很軟)。
    - Tā de shēngyīn hěn ruǎn.
    - His voice is very soft.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 軟(軟) → 車(車)
- **definition:** carriage
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Originally a carriage seen from above with large wheels and a sun shade.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts a carriage as viewed from above, showcasing its structural elements like wheels and a loading area, which makes sense as it illustrates the function of the vehicle.
- **examples:**
  - 这辆车很快(這輛車很快)。
    - Zhè liàng chē hěn kuài.
    - This vehicle is fast.
  - 我需要修车(我需要修車)。
    - Wǒ xūyào xiū chē.
    - I need to repair the car.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 軟(軟) → 欠(欠)
- **definition:** to lack / to owe
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a kneeling person with their mouth open, which signifies a state of lacking or desiring something.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a kneeling person with an open mouth suggests a request or desire for something that is lacking. This visual evokes the idea of wanting or needing, aligning well with the character's meaning of 'to lack.'
- **examples:**
  - 我欠钱(我欠錢)，需要借一些。
    - Wǒ qiàn qián, xūyào jiè yīxiē.
    - I lack money and need to borrow some.
  - 他在欠缺方面努力(他在欠缺方面努力)。
    - Tā zài qiànquē fāngmiàn nǔlì.
    - He is working hard in areas where he lacks.
### 软件(軟件) → 件(件)
- **definition:** item; piece; component; matter
- **pinyin:** jiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character represents a piece or item in its form, denoting a unit or matter.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively conveys the idea of a single unit or matter, which can be understood as something that can be counted or specified.
- **examples:**
  - 这件事情很重要(這件事情很重要)。
    - Zhè jiàn shìqíng hěn zhòngyào.
    - This matter is very important.
  - 我有三件衣服(我有三件衣服)。
    - Wǒ yǒu sān jiàn yīfú.
    - I have three pieces of clothing.
