## 软件(軟件)
---
## software
### ruǎnjiàn
---
- **definition:** software
- **pinyin:** ruǎnjiàn
- **components:**
  - 软(軟)
    - ruǎn
    - soft; pliable; gentle; weak
  - 件
    - jiàn
    - item; piece; classifier (events/clothes); part/component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 软(soft) + 件(item; piece) = software (soft/immaterial components).
  - **interpretation:** Coined as a calque of English “software” to contrast with 硬件, grouping programs and data as the ‘soft’ items of a computer system. Standard in Mainland China and Hong Kong; Taiwan more often uses 軟體.
  - **simplification:** 軟 was simplified to 软 in the PRC reform (車-series to 车), while 件 is unchanged; reduces stroke count and aligns with the hardware/software contrast.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要安装这款软件(我需要安裝這款軟件)。
    - Wǒ xūyào ānzhuāng zhè kuǎn ruǎnjiàn.
    - I need to install this software.
  - 这个应用软件可以提高工作效率(這個應用軟件可以提高工作效率)。
    - Zhège yìngyòng ruǎnjiàn kěyǐ tígāo gōngzuò xiàolǜ.
    - This application software can improve work efficiency.
  - 与硬件相比(與硬件相比)，软件更新更频繁(軟件更新更頻繁)。
    - Yǔ yìngjiàn xiāngbǐ, ruǎnjiàn gēngxīn gèng pínfán.
    - Compared to hardware, software updates more frequently.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟)
- **definition:** soft; pliable; gentle; weak
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle; cart; car
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - owe; lack; yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 車 (“carriage”) + phonetic 耎 (→ 欠); originally written 輭.
  - **interpretation:** The left component 車 hints at the domain of wagons and hauling, evoking pliancy or weakness from wear and strain. The right side was the phonetic 耎, later replaced by 欠, which also suggests panting or fatigue, reinforcing the sense of softness/weakness.
  - **simplification:** Component simplification: 車 → 车 while 欠 was kept, yielding 软.
- **examples:**
  - 这个枕头很软(這個枕頭很軟)。
    - Zhège zhěntou hěn ruǎn.
    - This pillow is very soft.
  - 天气热(天氣熱)，巧克力变软了(巧克力變軟了)。
    - Tiānqì rè, qiǎokèlì biàn ruǎn le.
    - When it’s hot, the chocolate softens.
  - 她心肠很软(她心腸很軟)。
    - Tā xīncháng hěn ruǎn.
    - She is soft‑hearted.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 车(車)
- **definition:** vehicle; cart; car
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram — originally a carriage seen from above; 车 is from the cursive script of 車.
  - **interpretation:** A top-down depiction of a cart: two wheels and a central frame, later regularized into vertical strokes. The simplified 车 follows a cursive shorthand of 車, keeping the overall outline while dropping interior detail.
  - **simplification:** Derived from the cursive form of 車 and adopted as a simplified shape to reduce strokes and ease writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一辆新车(我買了一輛新車)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī liàng xīn chē.
    - I bought a new car.
  - 这条路上车很多(這條路上車很多)。
    - Zhè tiáo lù shàng chē hěn duō.
    - There are many vehicles on this road.
  - 火车八点到站(火車八點到站)。
    - Huǒchē bā diǎn dào zhàn.
    - The train arrives at eight.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 欠(欠)
- **definition:** owe; lack; yawn
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a kneeling person with mouth open for various actions (e.g., begging, speaking, yawning); reversed as 㒫 > 旡 (cf. 既), not to be confused with 无.
  - **interpretation:** A kneeling figure with an open mouth suggests exhaling or pleading, which extends to senses like owing or lacking and the physical act of yawning. The mirrored form later standardized as 旡 in compounds such as 既, distinct from 无 despite superficial similarity.
- **examples:**
  - 他欠我五块钱(他欠我五塊錢)。
    - Tā qiàn wǒ wǔ kuài qián.
    - He owes me five yuan.
  - 我一直打哈欠(我一直打哈欠)。
    - Wǒ yīzhí dǎ hāqian.
    - I keep yawning.
  - 我们欠经验(我們欠經驗)。
    - Wǒmen qiàn jīngyàn.
    - We lack experience.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 欠(欠) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg; left-side form 亻.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person in profile, with one stroke suggesting the torso/arm and the other the leg, hence the slightly splayed shape. In compounds it often appears as the left-hand form 亻 to mark human-related meaning; not to be confused with 彳.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个很好的人(他是一個很好的人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè hěn hǎo de rén.
    - He is a very good person.
  - 我认识很多人(我認識很多人)。
    - Wǒ rènshi hěn duō rén.
    - I know many people.
  - 这里有三个人(這裡有三個人)。
    - Zhèlǐ yǒu sān gè rén.
    - There are three people here.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 欠(欠) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 软件(軟件) → 件(件)
- **definition:** item; piece; classifier (events/clothes); part/component
- **pinyin:** jiàn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow; ox
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 人 "man" + 牛 "cow"
  - **interpretation:** Combines a person with a cow, evoking a person handling or counting livestock. By extension, it came to denote an individual item or case and is now widely used as a classifier, especially for clothing and matters.
- **examples:**
  - 这件衣服很合适(這件衣服很合適)。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfu hěn héshì.
    - This piece of clothing fits well.
  - 我今天有三件事要做(我今天有三件事要做)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān yǒu sān jiàn shì yào zuò.
    - I have three things to do today.
  - 这台机器少了一件零部件(這臺機器少了一件零部件)。
    - Zhè tái jīqì shǎo le yī jiàn língbùjiàn.
    - This machine is missing a component.
### 软件(軟件) → 件(件) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** cow; ox
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: bull’s head with two horns and two ears; perhaps unrelated to 告; unrelated to 先.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bull’s head with prominent horns and ears, a direct visual rendering of the animal. Stylization over time simplified the lines, but the bovine shape remains evident.
- **examples:**
  - 这头牛很壮(這頭牛很壯)。
    - Zhè tóu niú hěn zhuàng.
    - This ox is strong.
  - 牛奶很新鲜(牛奶很新鮮)。
    - Niúnǎi hěn xīnxiān.
    - The milk is very fresh.
  - 牧民在草原上赶牛(牧民在草原上趕牛)。
    - Mùmín zài cǎoyuán shàng gǎn niú.
    - The herders drive cattle on the grassland.
