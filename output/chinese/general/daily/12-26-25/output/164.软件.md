## software
### ruǎnjiàn
---
- **definition:** software
- **pinyin:** ruǎnjiàn
- **components:**
  - 软(軟)
    - ruǎn
    - soft
  - 件
    - jiàn
    - piece; item
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 軟件(软件) (ruǎnjiàn) combines 軟(软) (ruǎn, "soft") and 件(件) (jiàn, "piece; item") to form "software." This implies a flexible, non-physical program or application, contrasting with the tangibility of hardware.
  - **interpretation:** A 軟(软) (ruǎn, "soft") 件(件) (jiàn, "piece; item") suggests a program or application that is not a tangible item but exists digitally within a computer system.
  - **simplification:** 件 simplified; 軟 simplified as 软
- **examples:**
  - 这款软件很易使用(這款軟件很易使用)。
    - Zhè kuǎn ruǎnjiàn hěn yì shǐyòng.
    - This software is very easy to use.
  - 学生们安装软件(學生們安裝軟件)，老师讲解功能(老師講解功能)。
    - Xuéshēngmen ānzhuāng ruǎnjiàn, lǎoshī jiǎngjiě gōngnéng.
    - Students install the software, and the teacher explains its functions.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟)
- **definition:** soft
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - cart
  - 耎
    - ruǎn
    - soft
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 車(車) (chē, "cart") + phonetic: 耎(耎) (ruǎn, "soft") ->
    - softness related to movement
  - **interpretation:** The character 軟(软) (ruǎn, "soft") integrates 車(車) (chē, "cart"), linking to flexibility and movement, with 耎(耎) (ruǎn, "soft") as the phonetic, representing the sound aspect and refining the meaning of 'soft' to include portability or adaptability, like a cart that's easy to maneuver.
  - **simplification:** 軟→软: The 車(車) (chē, "cart") component was simplified to 车(车) (chē, "cart") for easier writing, while maintaining the phonetic component for sound and meaning consistency.
- **examples:**
  - 这枕头很软(這枕頭很軟)。
    - Zhè zhěntou hěn ruǎn.
    - This pillow is soft.
  - 面包烤得刚刚好(麵包烤得剛剛好)，非常软(非常軟)。
    - Miànbāo kǎo dé gānggāng hǎo, fēicháng ruǎn.
    - The bread is baked perfectly and is very soft.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 车(車)
- **definition:** cart
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a carriage seen from above, with large wheels on both sides and a sun shade on top ->
    - wheels simplified to strokes; loading area marked with 田(田) (tián, 'field') ->
    - final form 車(車) (chē, 'cart') as vertical depiction
  - **interpretation:** The character 車(車) (chē, 'cart') visually represents a cart from an overhead perspective, with the modern form simplifying the wheels and structure into fewer strokes. The use of 田(田) (tián, 'field') helps designate the loading area, making it easier to identify as a transport vehicle.
  - **simplification:** None, as 车(車) and 車(車) are distinct forms with 車(車) being traditional.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一辆车(我買了一輛車)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yī liàng chē.
    - I bought a cart.
  - 农夫用车(農夫用車)，拉着货物(拉著貨物)。
    - Nóngfū yòng chē, lāzhe huòwù.
    - The farmer uses the cart to pull goods.
  - 车坏了(車壞了)，修理工赶快修好(修理工趕快修好)。
    - Chē huàile, xiūlǐ gōng gǎnkuài xiūhǎo.
    - The cart broke, and the repairman quickly fixed it.
### 软件(軟件) → 软(軟) → 耎(耎)
- **definition:** soft
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Originally the same character as 需
  - **description:**
    - Original character 耎(耎) (ruǎn, "soft") was the same as 需(需) (xū, "need") ->
    - characters diverged during Han dynasty ->
    - 耎(耎) maintained semantic association with softness
  - **interpretation:** 耎(耎) (ruǎn, "soft") and 需(需) (xū, "need") were once the same character, but the meanings and characters split during the Han dynasty. 耎(耎) continued to convey 'soft' while 需(需) took on the meaning 'need'.
- **examples:**
  - 这件棉被耎(這件棉被耎)，令人爱不释手(令人愛不釋手)。
    - Zhè jiàn mián bèi ruǎn, lìng rén ài bù shì shǒu.
    - This quilt is soft, making it hard to put down.
  - 这个枕头耎(這個枕頭耎)，令我沉睡自如(令我沉睡自如)。
    - Zhège zhěn tóu ruǎn, lìng wǒ chén shuì zì rú.
    - This pillow is soft, allowing me to sleep soundly.
### 软件(軟件) → 件(件)
- **definition:** piece; item
- **pinyin:** jiàn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - man
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人(人) (rén, "man") + 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") =
    - man and cow, parts of a whole ->
    - piece; item
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 人(人) (rén, "man") and 牛(牛) (niú, "cow"), symbolizing that a man and cow together form a unit, thus representing an 'item' or 'piece' as any distinct piece of something.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一件衣服(我買了一件衣服)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yī jiàn yīfú.
    - I bought a piece of clothing.
  - 这件事很紧急(這件事很緊急)，需要马上解决(需要馬上解決)。
    - Zhè jiàn shì hěn jǐnjí, xūyào mǎshàng jiějué.
    - This matter is urgent and needs to be resolved immediately.
  - 她送给我一件礼物(她送給我一件禮物)。
    - Tā sòng gěi wǒ yī jiàn lǐwù.
    - She gave me a gift.

### 软件(軟件) → 件(件) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** cow
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bull's head with two horns and two ears
  - **interpretation:** The character 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") visually represents the distinct features of a bull, with the two upward curving horns and the two ears forming its shape. This direct pictographic representation makes it easily recognizable and intuitive as a depiction of a cow.
- **examples:**
  - 农夫养牛(農夫養牛)。
    - Nóngfū yǎng niú.
    - The farmer raises cows.
  - 田野开阔(田野開闊)，牛自由漫步(牛自由漫步)。
    - Tiányě kāikuò, niú zìyóu mànbù.
    - In the vast fields, the cows stroll freely.
