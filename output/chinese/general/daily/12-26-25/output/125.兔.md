## rabbit
### tù
---
- **definition:** rabbit
- **pinyin:** tù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a rabbit with long ears
  - **interpretation:** The character 兔(兔) (tù, "rabbit") visually represents the animal by depicting its prominent ears and body, effectively conveying the concept of a rabbit.
- **examples:**
  - 森林里跑着一只兔(森林裡跑著一隻兔)。
    - Sēnlín lǐ pǎozhe yī zhī tù.
    - A rabbit is running in the forest.
  - 她走进森林(她走進森林)，发现一只兔(發現一隻兔)。
    - Tā zǒu jìn sēnlín, fāxiàn yī zhī tù.
    - She walked into the forest and discovered a rabbit.
