## 
### rabbit
---
- **pinyin:** rabbit
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a rabbit.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents a rabbit, allowing for immediate recognition of the animal it signifies.
- **examples:**
  - 兔子很可爱(兔子很可愛)。
    - tùzi hěn kě'ài.
    - Rabbits are very cute.
  - 我养了一只兔(我養了一隻兔)。
    - wǒ yǎngle yī zhī tù.
    - I raised a rabbit.
