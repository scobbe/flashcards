## rabbit
### tù
---
- **definition:** rabbit
- **pinyin:** tù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a rabbit
  - **interpretation:** The character 兔(tù, "rabbit") visually represents the profile of a rabbit, with exaggerated features such as the ears and posture, to capture the essence of the animal.
- **examples:**
  - 野兔警觉(覺)地观(觀)察周围(圍)。
    - Yě tù jǐngjué de guānchá zhōuwéi.
    - The wild rabbit watches its surroundings alertly.
  - 夜晚降临时(臨時)，兔子开(開)始活跃(躍)。
    - Yèwǎn jiànglín shí, tùzi kāishǐ huóyuè.
    - At nightfall, the rabbit starts to become active.
