## Huaihai Campaign
### Huáihǎi zhànyì
---
- **traditional:** 淮海戰役
- **definition:** Huaihai Campaign
- **pinyin:** Huáihǎi zhànyì
- **components:**
  - 淮海
    - huáihǎi
    - Huai and sea; region between Huai River and the sea
  - 战(戰)役
    - zhànyì
    - battle; campaign
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River"), 海 (hǎi, "sea"), 战(戰) (zhàn, "war; battle"), 役 (yì, "campaign; battle")
  - **interpretation:** The 淮海 (Huáihǎi, "Huai and sea") refers to the geographical area between the Huai River and the sea. 战(戰)役 (zhànyì, "battle; campaign") signifies a military campaign. The word 淮海战(戰)役 (Huáihǎi zhànyì) refers specifically to the historical military campaign during the Chinese Civil War, fought in the region around the Huai River.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海战(戰)役震撼历(歷)史。
    - Huáihǎi zhànyì zhènhàn lìshǐ.
    - The Huaihai Campaign shook history.
  - 许(許)多人纪(紀)念淮海战(戰)役，铭记(銘記)那段历(歷)史。
    - Xǔduō rén jìniàn Huáihǎi zhànyì, míngjì nà duàn lìshǐ.
    - Many people commemorate the Huaihai Campaign, remembering that period of history.
