## 格(格)
---
## 
### grammatical unit
---
- **pinyin:** grammatical unit
- **components:**
  - 各
    - gè
    - each
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the phonetic component 各 (gè, 'each') and the semantic component 格 (gé, 'standard') to convey the idea of a standardized unit.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 'each' and 'standard' suggests a unit that defines or holds its own standard.
- **examples:**
  - 这个语法格很重要(這個語法格很重要)。
    - Zhège yǔfǎ gé hěn zhòngyào.
    - This grammatical unit is very important.
  - 他用这个格子来写作文(他用這個格子來寫作文)。
    - Tā yòng zhège gézi lái xiě zuòwén.
    - He uses this unit to write essays.
### 格(格) → 各(各)
- **definition:** each
- **pinyin:** gè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Indicates individual items or aspects, showing distinction among them.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests the idea of individuality, as it represents each person or thing separately.
- **examples:**
  - 各有千秋。
    - gè yǒu qiān qiū.
    - Each has its own strengths.
  - 每个人都有各自的梦想。
    - měi gè rén dōu yǒu gè zì de mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has their own dreams.
  - 各位请注意！
    - gè wèi qǐng zhùyì！
    - Everyone, please pay attention!
