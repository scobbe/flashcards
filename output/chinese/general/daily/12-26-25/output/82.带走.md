## 带走(帶走)
---
## take away
### dàizǒu
---
- **definition:** take away
- **pinyin:** dàizǒu
- **components:**
  - 带(帶)
    - dài
    - belt; band; zone; to bring
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - to walk; to go; to leave; to depart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 带(dài, bring/lead) + 走(zǒu, go/walk) = take away; carry off.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of bringing with the act of moving, indicating taking someone or something along as you leave. Common in everyday contexts (trash, takeout) and in official contexts (police escort).
  - **simplification:** 帶 was simplified to 带 by reducing stroke count and merging inner components for ease of writing; 走 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 请把垃圾带走(請把垃圾帶走)。
    - Qǐng bǎ lājī dàizǒu.
    - Please take the trash away.
  - 警察把嫌疑人带走了(警察把嫌疑人帶走了)。
    - Jǐngchá bǎ xiányírén dàizǒu le.
    - The police took the suspect away.
  - 这份外卖可以带走吗(這份外賣可以帶走嗎)？
    - Zhè fèn wàimài kěyǐ dàizǒu ma?
    - Can this order be taken to go?
### 带走(帶走) → 带(帶)
- **definition:** belt; band; zone; to bring
- **pinyin:** dài
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth; towel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a woven belt
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a belt woven from cloth, highlighting its textile nature. Over time the form was stylized, but it still evokes something that wraps around or is worn at the waist.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 帶 to 带 by streamlining the complex woven components and retaining the cloth element (巾), reducing strokes for easier writing while preserving the core idea.
- **examples:**
  - 他系上皮带出门(他繫上皮帶出門)。
    - Tā xì shàng pí dài chū mén.
    - He fastened his belt before going out.
  - 这条河两岸是湿地带(這條河兩岸是濕地帶)。
    - Zhè tiáo hé liǎng'àn shì shī dìdài.
    - The banks of this river are a wetland zone.
  - 出门别忘了带雨伞(出門別忘了帶雨傘)。
    - Chū mén bié wàng le dài yǔsǎn.
    - Don’t forget to bring an umbrella when going out.
### 带走(帶走) → 带(帶) → 巾
- **definition:** cloth; towel
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – hanging piece of cloth.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cloth draped and hanging, with the long stroke as the edge and the shorter strokes suggesting the folds. The image conveys a towel or piece of fabric suspended from a line or rod.
- **examples:**
  - 我用湿毛巾擦脸(我用濕毛巾擦臉)。
    - Wǒ yòng shī máojīn cā liǎn.
    - I use a wet towel to wipe my face.
  - 她戴着红色的头巾(她戴著紅色的頭巾)。
    - Tā dài zhe hóngsè de tóujīn.
    - She is wearing a red headscarf.
  - 冬天出门要围上围巾(冬天出門要圍上圍巾)。
    - Dōngtiān chūmén yào wéi shàng wéijīn.
    - In winter, put on a scarf before going out.
### 带走(帶走) → 走
- **definition:** to walk; to go; to leave; to depart
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **components:**
  - 夭
    - yāo
    - tender; die young
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth; soil
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - stop
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a running man; later addition 止 (“foot”); modern top 夭 simplified to 土 and bottom stylized as 龰; small-seal variant 𧺆.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a person in motion. A ‘foot’ element was later appended to emphasize movement; in regular script the top resembles 土 and the lower part is stylized, but the sense of going/walking remains.
- **examples:**
  - 时间不早了(時間不早了)，我先走了(我先走了)。
    - Shíjiān bù zǎo le, wǒ xiān zǒu le.
    - It’s getting late; I’ll head out first.
  - 他每天走十公里(他每天走十公里)。
    - Tā měitiān zǒu shí gōnglǐ.
    - He walks ten kilometers every day.
  - 这台机器不走了(這台機器不走了)。
    - Zhè tái jīqì bù zǒu le.
    - This machine has stopped running.
### 带走(帶走) → 走 → 夭
- **definition:** tender; die young
- **pinyin:** yāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Ideogram (指事): a figure leaning forward, presumably running, hence energetic and young; cf. the top of 走.
  - **interpretation:** A stylized person bent forward suggests motion and vigor, evoking the sense of youthful energy and tender growth. By extension, it also came to denote an early or premature end, as in dying young or something being cut short.
- **examples:**
  - 他年纪轻轻就夭折(他年紀輕輕就夭折)。
    - Tā niánjì qīngqīng jiù yāozhé.
    - He died young.
  - 计划因资金不足而夭折(計劃因資金不足而夭折)。
    - Jìhuà yīn zījīn bùzú ér yāozhé.
    - The plan was aborted due to insufficient funds.
  - 桃之夭夭(桃之夭夭)，灼灼其华(灼灼其華)。
    - Táo zhī yāoyāo, zhuózhuó qí huá.
    - The peach tree is luxuriant in blossom, its flowers blazing bright.
### 带走(帶走) → 走 → 土
- **definition:** earth; soil
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a lump of clay on the ground or on a potter’s wheel; the central dot was later stylized into a short horizontal stroke.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a clod of earth resting on the ground, later streamlined into a vertical line with top and bottom bars. The upper bar developed from an earlier dot to distinguish it from similar graphs.
- **examples:**
  - 这里的土很干(這裡的土很乾)。
    - Zhèlǐ de tǔ hěn gān.
    - The soil here is very dry.
  - 他把一把土撒在花盆里(他把一把土撒在花盆裡)。
    - Tā bǎ yì bǎ tǔ sǎ zài huāpén lǐ.
    - He sprinkled a handful of soil into the flowerpot.
  - 建房前要先检测土质(建房前要先檢測土質)。
    - Jiàn fáng qián yào xiān jiǎncè tǔzhì.
    - Before building a house, the soil quality must be tested.
### 带走(帶走) → 走 → 止
- **definition:** stop
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a footprint pointing up (originally “foot”), later stylized as 龰; oracle forms show three toes and a sole.
  - **interpretation:** A footprint with toes pointing upward represents the foot. The sense broadened from the physical foot to the act of halting one’s step, hence “to stop”; the specific ‘toe’ meaning was preserved in 趾.
- **examples:**
  - 请立即停止前进(請立即停止前進)。
    - Qǐng lìjí tíngzhǐ qiánjìn.
    - Please stop advancing immediately.
  - 此处止步，非请勿入(此處止步，非請勿入)。
    - Cǐ chù zhǐbù, fēi qǐng wù rù.
    - Stop here; do not enter without invitation.
  - 药到病除，疼痛已止(藥到病除，疼痛已止)。
    - Yào dào bìng chú, téngtòng yǐ zhǐ.
    - With the right medicine, the illness is cured and the pain has stopped.
