## take away
### dàizǒu
---
- **definition:** take away
- **pinyin:** dàizǒu
- **components:**
  - 带(帶)
    - dài
    - carry; bring; lead; belt
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - walk; go; move; leave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 带(帶) (dài, "carry") + 走(走) (zǒu, "walk") =
    - to take something or someone away by carrying it while walking.
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 带(帶) (dài, "carry") suggests having an item or person in one's possession, and 走(走) (zǒu, "walk") implies the action of moving from one place to another, typically on foot. Together, 带走(帶走) (dàizǒu, "take away") conveys the idea of removing or relocating something or someone by carrying them while walking.
- **examples:**
  - 他把玩具带走了(他把玩具帶走了)。
    - Tā bǎ wánjù dàizǒu le.
    - He took the toy away.
  - 警察到达现场(警察到達現場)，带走了所有证据(帶走了所有證據)。
    - Jǐngchá dàodá xiànchǎng, dàizǒu le suǒyǒu zhèngjù.
    - The police arrived at the scene and took away all the evidence.
  - 大风来了(大風來了)，把树叶带走了(把樹葉帶走了)。
    - Dà fēng lái le, bǎ shùyè dàizǒu le.
    - The strong wind came and took the leaves away.
### 带走(帶走) → 带(帶)
- **definition:** carry; bring; lead; belt
- **pinyin:** dài
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a woven belt
  - **interpretation:** The character 带(帶) (dài, 'belt') visually represents a woven belt, capturing the essence of its function as something that encircles or binds.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈带我去公园(媽媽帶我去公園)。
    - Māmā dài wǒ qù gōngyuán.
    - Mother takes me to the park.
  - 他带朋友去旅行(他帶朋友去旅行)，欣赏美丽风景(欣賞美麗風景)。
    - Tā dài péngyǒu qù lǚxíng, xīnshǎng měilì fēngjǐng.
    - He takes friends on a trip, enjoying the beautiful scenery.
  - 武术练习者穿上了蓝带(武術練習者穿上了藍帶)。
    - Wǔshù liànxí zhě chuān shàng le lán dài.
    - Martial arts practitioners put on blue belts.
### 带走(帶走) → 走(走)
- **definition:** walk; go; move; leave
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - A running man ->
    - addition of 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") ->
    - 夭(夭) (yāo, "twist") simplified to 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") ->
    - stylized as 龰(龰) ("running vehicle")
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicting a person running, the character evolved by adding the component 止(止) to emphasize movement and using 土(土) for visual simplification. Its modern form retains the sense of motion through the stylized foot representation.
  - **simplification:** No simplification from traditional to simplified form as 走 and 赱 are identical in essence, although 赱 is a variant. In modern usage, 赱 is rarely used while 走 is standard.
- **examples:**
  - 危险出现时(危險出現時)，他立即走了(他立即走了)。
    - Wēixiǎn chūxiàn shí, tā lìjí zǒu le.
    - When danger appeared, he immediately ran away.
  - 为了赶上火车(為了趕上火車)，她拼命走出家门(她拼命走出家門)。
    - Wèile gǎn shàng huǒchē, tā pīnmìng zǒu chū jiāmén.
    - In order to catch the train, she ran out of the house desperately.
