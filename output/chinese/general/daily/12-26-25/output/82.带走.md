## 带走(帶走)
---
## 
### take away
---
- **pinyin:** take away
- **components:**
  - 带(帶)
    - dài
    - carry; bring; take along
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - go; walk; leave; take away
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 带(帶) (dài,
- **examples:**
  - 我想带走这个。(我想帶走這個。)
    - Wǒ xiǎng dài zǒu zhège.
    - I want to take this away.
  - 请把垃圾带走。(請把垃圾帶走。)
    - Qǐng bǎ lājī dài zǒu.
    - Please take the trash away.
### 带走(帶走) → 带(帶)
- **definition:** carry; bring; take along
- **pinyin:** dài
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a woven belt.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a belt, which is often used to carry or hold items, aligning with the meaning of bringing or taking along.
- **examples:**
  - 我带了雨伞(我帶了雨傘)，以防下雨(以防下雨)！
  - 你能带我去那里吗(你能帶我去那裡嗎)？
  - 她总是带着微笑(她總是帶著微笑)！
### 带走(帶走) → 走(走)
- **definition:** go; walk; leave; take away
- **pinyin:** zǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a running man with additional elements showing the foot.
  - **interpretation:** The character showing a running figure conveys the concept of movement in its basic form. The addition of elements depicts action and physical activity associated with running.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by changing the top component from 夭 to 土 and stylizing the bottom as 龰. The stroke count was reduced in a way that made the character easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 他正在走路(他正在走路)。
    - tā zhèngzài zǒulù
    - He is walking.
  - 我喜欢快走(我喜歡快走)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān kuài zǒu
    - I like to walk quickly.
  - 他们在公园里走(他們在公園裡走)。
    - tāmen zài gōngyuán lǐ zǒu
    - They are walking in the park.
