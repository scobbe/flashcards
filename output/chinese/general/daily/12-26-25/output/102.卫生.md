## hygiene
### wèishēng
---
- **definition:** hygiene
- **pinyin:** wèishēng
- **components:**
  - 卫(衛)
    - wèi
    - guard; defend; protect
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; birth; grow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") + 生(生) (shēng, "life") =
    - protecting life ->
    - hygiene
  - **interpretation:** The concept of 卫生(衛生) (wèishēng, "hygiene") is about practices that protect life and health. By combining 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") and 生(生) (shēng, "life"), it conveys the idea of guarding life through cleanliness and sanitary measures.
- **examples:**
  - 定期打扫学校有助于保持卫生(定期打掃學校有助於保持衛生)。
    - Dìngqí dǎsǎo xuéxiào yǒuzhù yú bǎochí wèishēng.
    - Regularly cleaning the school helps maintain hygiene.
  - 厨房要讲卫生(廚房要講衛生)，做饭时要注意安全(做飯時要注意安全)。
    - Chúfáng yào jiǎng wèishēng, zuòfàn shí yào zhùyì ānquán.
    - The kitchen should adhere to hygiene, and one should pay attention to safety when cooking.
### 卫生(衛生) → 卫(衛)
- **definition:** guard; defend; protect
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **components:**
  - 行
    - xíng
    - walk
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - tanned leather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 行(行) (xíng, 'walk') + 韋(韋) (wéi, 'tanned leather') =
    - encircling movement ->
    - guard; defend
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 行(行) (xíng, 'walk') as the semantic component, suggesting movement or patrol, with 韋(韋) (wéi, 'tanned leather') as the phonetic component, to convey the concept of guarding or defense.
  - **simplification:** 衛(衛) was simplified to 卫(衛) by removing additional strokes and components, but retaining 韋(韋) (wéi) as the phonetic element for ease of writing and recognition.
- **examples:**
  - 她每天巡逻守卫社区(她每天巡邏守衛社區)。
    - Tā měitiān xúnluó shǒuwèi shèqū.
    - She patrols the community every day to guard it.
  - 国难当头时(國難當頭時)，人们决心卫国救民(人們決心衛國救民)。
    - Guó nàn dāng tóu shí, rénmen juéxīn wèi guó jiùmín.
    - In times of crisis, people are determined to defend the country and save the people.
  - 古代将士卫边疆(古代將士衛邊疆)。
    - Gǔdài jiàngshì wèi biānjiāng.
    - Ancient warriors defended the frontier.

### 卫生(衛生) → 卫(衛) → 韋(韋)
- **definition:** tanned leather
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **components:**
  - 舛
    - chuǎn
    - footprint
  - 囗
    - wéi
    - enclosure
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 舛(舛) (chuǎn, "footprint") + 囗(囗) (wéi, "enclosure") =
    - footsteps around an enclosure ->
    - became phonetically borrowed to indicate "tanned leather"
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted the concept of moving around an enclosed space, suggesting the act of surrounding. Over time, it was repurposed to represent the unrelated concept of "tanned leather" due to phonetic borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 这块韋皮(這塊韋皮)，质地柔韧(質地柔韌)。
    - Zhè kuài wéi pí, zhì dì róu rèn.

  - 工匠选用上等韋皮(工匠選用上等韋皮)，制成精美皮具(製成精美皮具)。
    - Gōngjiàng xuǎn yòng shàng děng wéi pí, zhì chéng jīng měi pí jù.
    - The craftsman selects top-quality tanned leather and makes exquisite leather goods.
  - 韋皮经过特殊处理后(韋皮經過特殊處理後)，更显耐用(更顯耐用)。
    - Wéi pí jīng guò tè shū chǔ lǐ hòu, gèng xiǎn nài yòng.
    - After special treatment, the tanned leather appears even more durable.
