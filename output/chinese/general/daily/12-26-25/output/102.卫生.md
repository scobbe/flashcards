## hygiene
### wèishēng
---
- **traditional:** 衛生
- **definition:** hygiene
- **pinyin:** wèishēng
- **components:**
  - 卫(衛)
    - wèi
    - guard; protect; defend
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; health; live; raw
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") + 生 (shēng, "life") =
    - guarding life ->
    - hygiene
  - **interpretation:** Hygiene involves practices that guard or protect one's health and life by promoting cleanliness and preventing disease. In this context, 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") implies protection, while 生 (shēng, "life") refers to health and life itself.
  - **simplification:** 卫生 is the simplified form of 衛生.
- **examples:**
  - 保持卫(衛)生很重要。
    - Bǎochí wèishēng hěn zhòngyào.
    - Maintaining hygiene is important.
  - 在餐厅(廳)用餐前，请检(請檢)查卫(衛)生。
    - Zài cāntīng yòngcān qián, qǐng jiǎnchá wèishēng.
    - Before dining in the restaurant, please check the hygiene.
  - 环(環)境卫(衛)生关系(關係)到健康。
    - Huánjìng wèishēng guānxì dào jiànkāng.
    - Environmental hygiene is related to health.
### 卫(衛)生( ) → 卫(衛)
- **traditional:** 衛
- **definition:** guard; protect; defend
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **components:**
  - 行
    - xíng
    - go
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - tanned leather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 行 (xíng, 'go') + 韋 (wéi, 'tanned leather') =
    - to guard or defend as if walking to protect ->
    - replacement by visually similar katakana ヱ
  - **interpretation:** The simplified form 卫 replaces the traditional character 衛 by using a visual analogy with the Japanese katakana ヱ, streamlining its components while preserving the original semantic layer of 'guarding' or 'protecting' implied by the notion of walking as protection.
  - **simplification:** 卫 simplifies 衛 through visual analogy with ヱ and cursive simplification, capturing key phonetic elements while omitting or altering strokes for brevity.
- **examples:**
  - 卫(衛)兵巡逻边(邏邊)境。
    - Wèi bīng xúnluó biānjìng.
    - The guards patrol the border.
  - 他肩负卫国责(負衛國責)任，誓言守护(護)家园(園)。
    - Tā jiānfù wèi guó zérèn, shìyán shǒuhù jiāyuán.
    - He carries the responsibility to defend the nation and vows to protect his homeland.
  - 警卫(衛)密切监(監)察入口。
    - Jǐngwèi mìqiè jiānchá rùkǒu.
    - The guards closely monitor the entrance.
### 卫(衛)生( ) → 卫(衛) → 行
- **definition:** go
- **pinyin:** xíng
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - step
  - 亍
    - chù

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a symmetric depiction of a street intersection; simplified asymmetrically ->

    - Tāmen xíng zài línjiān xiǎolù shàng.
    - They walk on the forest trail.
