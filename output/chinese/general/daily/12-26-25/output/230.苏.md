## surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
### Sū
---
- **definition:** surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
- **pinyin:** Sū
- **components:**
  - 艸
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 穌
    - sū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艸(艸) (cǎo, "grass; plant") + 穌(穌) (sū) =
    - grass and related phonetic ->
    - rebirth/resurrection connotation
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic element 艸(艸) (cǎo, "grass; plant"), suggesting something related to plants, with the phonetic 穌(穌) (sū), which provides the pronunciation. Together, they convey a sense of revival or rebirth commonly associated with growth or coming back to life, as seen in plants.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 蘇(蘇) through replacing 穌(穌) with 办(辦), using a cursive shorthand form to maintain phonetic elements while reducing stroke count.
- **examples:**
  - 苏先生参加了会议(蘇先生參加了會議)。
    - Sū xiānshēng cānjiāle huìyì.
    - Mr. Su participated in the meeting.
  - 许多人慕名而来苏(許多人慕名而來蘇)，旅游体验丰富(旅遊體驗豐富)。
    - Xǔ duō rén mùmíng ér lái Sū, lǚyóu tǐyàn fēngfù.
    - Many people come to Su (Jiangsu) in search of its reputation, and their travel experiences are enriching.
### 苏(蘇) → 艸(艸)
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts grass blades by doubling 屮(屮) (chè, 'grass')
  - **interpretation:** The character 艸(艸) (cǎo, 'grass; plant') originates as a pictogram representing the blades of grass, depicted by duplicating the character for grass 屮(屮) (chè, 'grass'). This duplication visually conveys the idea of a patch or stand of grass.
- **examples:**
  - 山坡上长满了艸(山坡上長滿了艸)。
    - Shān pō shàng zhǎng mǎn le cǎo.
    - The hillside is covered with grass.
  - 风吹过田野(風吹過田野)，艸随风摇曳(艸隨風搖曳)。
    - Fēng chuī guò tián yě, cǎo suí fēng yáo yè.
    - The wind blows across the fields, and the grass sways in the breeze.
  - 早晨的露珠滋润了艸(早晨的露珠滋潤了艸)。
    - Zǎochén de lù zhū zī rùn le cǎo.
    - The morning dew nourishes the grass.
### 苏(蘇) → 穌(穌)
- **pinyin:** sū
- **components:**
  - 魚
    - yú
    - fish
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 魚(魚) (yú, "fish") + Semantic: 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") =
    - combination of fish sound with grain semantic ->
    - final form 穌
  - **interpretation:** The character 穌 combines 魚(魚) (yú, "fish") for phonetic reasons with 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") to give a probable meaning related to agriculture, or the revival and renewal associated with harvests.
  - **simplification:** None, as the traditional character remains the same in simplified Chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 山间的溪流潺潺(山間的溪流潺潺)，气氛十分穌然(氣氛十分穌然)。
    - Shānjiān de xīliú chánchán, qìfēn shífèn sūrán.
    - The mountain stream murmurs, and the atmosphere is very serene.
  - 夜幕降临时(夜幕降臨時)，古城的街道显得异常穌穆(古城的街道顯得異常穌穆)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín shí, gǔchéng de jiēdào xiǎndé yìcháng sūmù.
    - When night falls, the streets of the ancient city appear unusually solemn.
