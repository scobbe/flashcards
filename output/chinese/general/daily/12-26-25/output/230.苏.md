## 苏(蘇)
---
## 
### surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
---
- **pinyin:** surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified
  - **description:** Simplified from 蘇 (sū, 'to revive') via the cursive script.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the concept of revival, intuitively reflecting the idea of bringing something back to life or restoring it.
  - **simplification:** The simplification involved stroke reduction through cursive adoption, making the character easier and quicker to write.
- **examples:**
  - 这种草药可以帮助我们苏醒(這種草藥可以幫助我們蘇醒)。
    - Zhè zhǒng cǎoyào kěyǐ bāngzhù wǒmen sūxǐng.
    - This herbal medicine can help us awake.
  - 他的成功让这座城市苏醒(他的成功讓這座城市蘇醒)。
    - Tā de chénggōng ràng zhè zuò chéngshì sūxǐng.
    - His success has awakened this city.
  - 这本书让我对生活有了新的苏思(這本書讓我對生活有了新的蘇思)。
    - Zhè běn shū ràng wǒ duì shēnghuó yǒule xīn de sūsī.
    - This book has given me new insights into life.
