## surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
### Sū
---
- **definition:** surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
- **pinyin:** Sū
- **components:**
  - 艹(艸)
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 穌
    - sū
    - revive
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艹(艸) (cǎo, "grass") + 穌(穌) (sū, "revive") =
    - grass reviving ->
    - used for the surname Su
  - **interpretation:** The character 苏(蘇) (sū) combines the semantic 艹(艸) (cǎo, "grass") with the phonetic 穌(穌) (sū, "revive"). The association of grass with revival is intuitive, as grass regenerates and grows back after unfavorable conditions. This makes it a fitting character for representing regeneration or a return to life, and by extension, the surname Su.
  - **simplification:** 艹(艸) (cǎo, "grass") retained to suggest plant-like revival, following simplification patterns common in Chinese script reform, while keeping the overall meaning recognizable.
- **examples:**
  - 苏先生今天到访江苏(蘇先生今天到訪江蘇)。
    - Sū xiānshēng jīntiān dàofǎng Jiāngsū.
    - Mr. Su visited Jiangsu today.
  - 我朋友姓苏(我朋友姓蘇)，他是个热心人(他是個熱心人)。
    - Wǒ péngyǒu xìng Sū, tā shì gè rèxīn rén.
    - My friend has the surname Su; he is a warm-hearted person.
### 苏(蘇) → 艹(艹)
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写字时用艹作草字的偏旁(他寫字時用艹作草字的偏旁)。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来时(春天到來時)，艹生长得很快(艹生長得很快)。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 苏(蘇) → 穌(穌)
- **definition:** revive
- **pinyin:** sū
- **components:**
  - 魚
    - yú
    - fish
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 魚(魚) (yú, "fish") as phonetic + 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") as semantic ->
    - sound and meaning combined ->
    - final form 穌
  - **interpretation:** The character 穌(sū, "resuscitate") combines 魚 (yú, "fish") to indicate sound with 禾 (hé, "grain"), which contributes to the overall meaning of revitalization, akin to agricultural growth.
  - **simplification:** The character 穌 was simplified to 稣 by removing the 魚(魚) (yú, "fish") component and retaining only parts that suggest clarity and modern sound.
- **examples:**
  - 他不慎溺水后(他不慎溺水後)，医生奋力穌他(醫生奮力穌他)。
    - tā bù shèn nì shuǐ hòu, yīshēng fèn lì sū tā.
    - He accidentally nearly drowned, and the doctor resuscitated him with full effort.
  - 他在实验室中(他在實驗室中)，穌起了细胞的活力(穌起了細胞的活力)。
    - tā zài shí yàn shì zhōng, sū qǐ le xì bāo de huó lì.
    - While in the laboratory, he revived the vitality of the cells.
  - 在抢救行动中(在搶救行動中)，医护人员全力穌病人(醫護人員全力穌病人)。
    - zài qiǎng jiù xíng dòng zhōng, yī hù rén yuán quán lì sū bìng rén.
    - During the rescue operation, the medical staff did their best to resuscitate the patient.
### 苏(蘇) → 穌(穌) → 魚(魚)
- **definition:** fish
- **pinyin:** yú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a fish
  - **interpretation:** The character 魚(yú, 'fish') visually resembles a fish with a head, body, and tail. This depiction makes it easy to relate the character to its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 河中有一尾魚(河中有一尾魚)，在陽光下閃著銀光(在陽光下閃著銀光)。
    - hé zhōng yǒu yī wěi yú, zài yángguāng xià shǎn zhe yín guāng。
    - There is a fish in the river, shimmering under the sunlight.
  - 海里有许多魚(海裡有許多魚)，在自由里游动(在自由裡游動)。
    - hǎi lǐ yǒu xǔ duō yú, zài zìyóu lǐ yóu dòng。
    - There are many fish in the sea, swimming freely.
### 苏(蘇) → 穌(穌) → 禾(禾)
- **definition:** grain
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stalk of grain or cereal plant
  - **interpretation:** The character 禾(禾) (hé, 'grain') visually represents a plant with roots and leaves, focusing on the appearance of staple crops like rice or millet. This clearly conveys its meaning as it resembles the actual shape of a plant grown for its grain.
- **examples:**
  - 收获的禾香弥漫在田野中(收穫的禾香彌漫在田野中)。
    - Shōuhuò de hé xiāng mímàn zài tiányě zhōng.
    - The fragrance of grain filled the fields.
  - 农民辛勤耕作(農民辛勤耕作)，收割禾穗(收割禾穗)。
    - Nóngmín xīnqín gēngzuò, shōugē hé suì.
    - The hardworking farmers harvested the ears of grain.
  - 春雨滋润了禾苗(春雨滋潤了禾苗)，预示着丰收(預示著豐收)。
    - Chūnyǔ zīrùn le hé miáo, yùshì zhe fēngshōu.
    - The spring rain nourished the grain seedlings, foreshadowing a bountiful harvest.
