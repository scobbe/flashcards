## orange peel
### chéngpí
---
- **definition:** orange peel
- **pinyin:** chéngpí
- **components:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange
  - 皮
    - pí
    - peel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 橙(橙) (chéng, "orange") + 皮(皮) (pí, "peel") =
    - the peel or rind of an orange
  - **interpretation:** An 橙(橙) (chéng, "orange") is a fruit known for its citrus flavor, and its 皮(皮) (pí, "peel") refers to the outer covering. Thus, 橙皮 refers to the specific outer layer of an orange.
- **examples:**
  - 他将橙皮晒干后(他將橙皮曬乾後)，用来泡茶(用來泡茶)。
    - Tā jiāng chéngpí shài gān hòu, yòng lái pào chá.
    - He dried the orange peel and then used it to brew tea.
  - 我喜欢橙皮的清香(我喜歡橙皮的清香)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chéngpí de qīngxiāng.
    - I like the fresh aroma of orange peel.
  - 古人常用橙皮(古人常用橙皮)，入药(入藥)。
    - Gǔrén cháng yòng chéngpí, rù yào.
    - Ancient people often used orange peel in medicine.
