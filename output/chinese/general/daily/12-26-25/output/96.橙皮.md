- **components:**
  - 橙

    - pí

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 橙(橙) (chéng, "orange") + 皮(皮) (pí, "skin; peel") combines to refer directly to the literal outer layer or "peel" of the "orange" fruit.
