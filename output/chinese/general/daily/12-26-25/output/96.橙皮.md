### orange peel
---
- **pinyin:** orange peel
- **components:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange; orange tree
  - 皮
    - pí
    - skin; hide; peel; surface
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 橙(橙) (chéng, 'orange') + 皮(皮) (pí, 'skin') — orange skin
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as 橙皮 refers specifically to the outer layer of an orange, which can be used in cooking and flavoring. The term directly describes the skin of the orange fruit.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃橙皮(我喜歡吃橙皮)，它可以增加菜肴的香味(它可以增加菜餚的香味)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī chéngpí, tā kěyǐ zēngjiā càiyáo de xiāngwèi.
    - I like to eat orange peel, it can enhance the flavor of dishes.
  - 橙皮(橙皮)常用于制作甜点(常用於製作甜點)。
    - Chéngpí cháng yòng yú zhìzuò tiándiǎn.
    - Orange peel is often used in making desserts.
### 橙皮(橙皮) → 橙(橙)
- **definition:** orange; orange tree
- **pinyin:** chéng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree; wood
  - 登
    - dēng
    - to ascend; to climb
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 木 (mù, 'tree; wood') indicating a type of plant, and the phonetic component 登 (dēng), contributing its sound.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as oranges grow on trees, linking the fruit directly to its natural source.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢橙色(我喜歡橙色)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chéngsè.
    - I like the color orange.
  - 这个橙子很甜(這個橙子很甜)。
    - Zhège chéngzi hěn tián.
    - This orange is very sweet.
  - 她穿着橙色的衣服(她穿著橙色的衣服)。
    - Tā chuānzhuó chéngsè de yīfú.
    - She is wearing an orange outfit.

- **definition:** tree; wood
- **pinyin:** mù

  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
