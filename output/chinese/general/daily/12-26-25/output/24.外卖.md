## takeout
### wàimài
---
- **definition:** takeout
- **pinyin:** wàimài
- **components:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside
  - 卖(賣)
    - mài
    - sell
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 外(外) (wài, "outside") + 卖(賣) (mài, "sell") =
    - food or products sold for consumption elsewhere; takeout
  - **interpretation:** The term 外卖(外賣) (wàimài) describes a transaction where goods, often food, are sold to be consumed outside the place of purchase, thus encapsulating the idea of takeout.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天订了外卖(我今天訂了外賣)。
    - Wǒ jīntiān dìng le wàimài.
    - I ordered takeout today.
  - 晚上我没时间做饭(晚上我沒時間做飯)，所以叫了外卖(所以叫了外賣)。
    - Wǎnshàng wǒ méi shíjiān zuò fàn, suǒyǐ jiào le wàimài.
    - I had no time to cook in the evening, so I ordered takeout.
  - 当我忙的时候(當我忙的時候)，我就订外卖(我就訂外賣)。
    - Dāng wǒ máng de shíhòu, wǒ jiù dìng wàimài.
    - When I am busy, I order takeout.
### 外卖(外賣) → 外(外)
- **definition:** outside
- **pinyin:** wài
- **components:**
  - 卜

  - 月

  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

- **examples:**

  - 今天天气很好(今天天氣很好)，我们在外散步(我們在外散步)。

    - Lǚrén shēnchǔ yuǎnfāng, xīn què cháng xiàng wài xúnqiú.

### 外卖(外賣) → 卖(賣)
- **definition:** sell
- **pinyin:** mài
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - cowry
  - 売
    - mài
    - sell
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") + phonetic: 売(売) + 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") =
    - selling valuable shells ->
    - sell
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry"), which is associated with money or trade, and 売(売) as a phonetic component, along with 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") to suggest a transaction or market involving goods like shells, symbolizing commerce.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified character 卖(賣), the 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") component was removed, and 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") was replaced with smaller elements for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 她卖书(她賣書)。
    - Tā mài shū.
    - She sells books.
  - 他卖水果(他賣水果)，赚了一些钱(賺了一些錢)。
    - Tā mài shuǐguǒ, zhuànle yīxiē qián.
    - He sells fruits and earned some money.
  - 商贩卖纪念品(商販賣紀念品)。
    - Shāngfàn mài jìniànpǐn.
    - The vendor sells souvenirs.
### 外卖(外賣) → 卖(賣) → 貝(貝)

- **pinyin:** bèi

  - **interpretation:** 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") depicts the distinctive shape of a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China due to its unique appearance and rarity.
- **examples:**

    - Tā shōucáng yī méi bèi, yòng zuò gǔdài màoyì de xìnwù.
    - He collects a cowry shell, using it as a token in ancient trade.
  - 清朝时贝(清朝時貝)，常被镶嵌于玉器(常被鑲嵌於玉器)。
    - Qīngcháo shí bèi, cháng bèi xiāngqiàn yú yùqì.
    - During the Qing dynasty, cowries were often inlaid in jade pieces.
### 外卖(外賣) → 卖(賣) → 売(賣)
- **definition:** sell
- **pinyin:** mài
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Simplified from
  - **description:** 賣(賣) (mài, "sell") → simplified to 売 by removing 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") component
  - **interpretation:** The character 売 simplifies the traditional form 賣 by removing the 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") component, which historically denoted value or trade, retaining an abstract sense of selling.
  - **simplification:** The component 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") was omitted to create the simplified character 売, reducing complexity while maintaining the phonetic core.
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天卖了一辆车(我昨天売了一輛車)。
    - Wǒ zuótiān mài le yī liàng chē.
    - I sold a car yesterday.
  - 商店降价后(商店降價後)，他卖出所有商品(他売出所有商品)。
    - Shāngdiàn jiàngjià hòu, tā mài chū suǒyǒu shāngpǐn.
    - After the store lowered its prices, he sold all the goods.
  - 他把旧书卖给了朋友(他把舊書売給了朋友)。
    - Tā bǎ jiùshū mài gěi le péngyǒu.
    - He sold his old books to a friend.
