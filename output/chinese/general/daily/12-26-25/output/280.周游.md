## 周游(周遊)
---
## 
### travel around
---
- **pinyin:** travel around
- **components:**
  - 周(週)
    - zhōu
    - week; circle
  - 游(遊)
    - yóu
    - swim; travel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 周(週) (zhōu, 'week; circle') + 游(遊) (yóu, 'swim; travel') — circle travel
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to the act of traveling around or making a circle. It combines the ideas of 'circle' and 'travel,' emphasizing the movement across various places.
- **examples:**
  - 我们计划周游世界(我們計劃周遊世界)！
    - Wǒmen jìhuà zhōuyóu shìjiè!
    - We plan to travel around the world!
  - 他周游了很多国家(他周遊了很多國家)。
    - Tā zhōuyóu le hěn duō guójiā.
    - He traveled around many countries.
  - 周游的经历很有趣(周遊的經歷很有趣)。
    - Zhōuyóu de jīnglì hěn yǒuqù.
    - The experience of traveling around is very interesting.
### 周游(周遊) → 周(周)
- **definition:** week; circle
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a jade artifact with carved patterns, originally representing the act of carving.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the concept of intricacy and circularity associated with both the patterns on a jade object and the passage of time represented by weeks.
- **examples:**
  - 我们每周开会(我們每週開會)。
    - Wǒmen měi zhōu kāihuì.
    - We hold meetings every week.
  - 这个月有四个周(這個月有四個週)。
    - Zhège yuè yǒu sì gè zhōu.
    - This month has four weeks.
### 周游(周遊) → 游(遊)
- **definition:** swim; travel
- **pinyin:** yóu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 斿
    - yóu
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 水 ( 'water' ) + 斿 ( phonetic with reference to travelling ) — evokes the idea of moving through water.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of water and a phonetic element related to traveling intuitively conveys the idea of swimming or roaming in water.
  - **simplification:** The character 游 was simplified to 游 by reducing one stroke without altering the meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 他们在湖里游玩(他們在湖裡游玩)。
    - Tāmen zài hú lǐ yóuwán.
    - They are playing in the lake.
### 周游(周遊) → 游(遊) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the movement of water, evoking a sense of flow and fluidity. It is a direct representation of water as an element essential for life.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命的来源(水是生命的來源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng de láiyuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水(我喜歡喝水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
  - 海水很咸(海水很鹹)。
    - Hǎishuǐ hěn xián.
    - Sea water is very salty.
