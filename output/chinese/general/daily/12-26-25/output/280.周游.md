## travel around
### zhōuyóu
---
- **definition:** travel around
- **pinyin:** zhōuyóu
- **components:**
  - 周
    - zhōu
    - circle; complete; cycle
  - 游(遊)
    - yóu
    - travel; wander; roam
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 周(周) (zhōu, "circle; complete") + 游(遊) (yóu, "travel; wander") =
    - to travel around; to roam
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 周(周) (zhōu, "circle; complete") and 游(遊) (yóu, "travel; wander") conveys the idea of traveling in a circular or comprehensive manner, suggesting extensive travel or wandering across different places.
- **examples:**
  - 他周游世界(他周遊世界)。
    - Tā zhōuyóu shìjiè.
    - He traveled around the world.
  - 艺术家周游名城(藝術家周遊名城)，寻找灵感源泉(尋找靈感源泉)。
    - Yìshùjiā zhōuyóu míngchéng, xúnzhǎo línggǎn yuánquán.
    - The artist traveled around famous cities, seeking sources of inspiration.
  - 学者周游古国(學者周遊古國)，采撷典故(採擷典故)。
    - Xuézhě zhōuyóu gǔguó, cǎixié diǎngù.
    - The scholar traveled around ancient kingdoms, collecting classic allusions.

### 周游(周遊) → 游(遊)
- **definition:** travel; wander; roam
- **pinyin:** yóu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 斿
    - yóu
    - travel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") + Phonetic: 斿(斿) (yóu, originally having meaning related to travel) =
    - movement through or on water ->
    - swim/travel/wander
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the water radical with a component originally meaning travel, highlighting both the aspect of swimming in water and wandering or traveling. This reflects the versatility in its meanings.
  - **simplification:** Traditional 遊(遊) (yóu, "wander") uses 辵(辵) (chuò, "walk") for traveling, simplified by replacing it with 水(水) (shuǐ, "water"), focusing on swimming, which in Chinese culture is closely related to wandering or traveling freely.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 我们周末去郊外游(我們週末去郊外遊)，感受大自然(感受大自然)。
    - Wǒmen zhōumò qù jiāowài yóu, gǎnshòu dàzìrán.
    - We will travel to the countryside on the weekend and enjoy nature.
  - 他梦想环游世界(他夢想環遊世界)。
    - Tā mèngxiǎng huányóu shìjiè.
    - He dreams of traveling the world.
