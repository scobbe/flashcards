### zhōuyóu
---
- **definition:** travel around
- **pinyin:** zhōuyóu
- **components:**
  - 周
    - zhōu
    - around; week; circumference
  - 游(遊)
    - yóu
    - travel; swim; roam; wander
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 周(周) (zhōu, "around") and 游(遊) (yóu, "travel") together describe the action of traveling around.
  - **interpretation:** Combining 周(周) (zhōu, "around") with 游(遊) (yóu, "travel") conveys the idea of traveling to different places, often for leisure or exploration.
- **examples:**
  - 他梦想着周游世界(他夢想著周遊世界)。
    - Tā mèngxiǎngzhe zhōuyóu shìjiè.
    - He dreams of traveling around the world.
  - 暑假来了(暑假來了)，他们决定周游祖国(他們決定周遊祖國)。
    - Shǔjià lái le, tāmen juédìng zhōuyóu zǔguó.
    - With summer vacation arriving, they decided to travel around their homeland.
  - 他利用长假(他利用長假)，周游山水(周遊山水)。
    - Tā lìyòng chángjià, zhōuyóu shānshuǐ.
    - He uses his long vacation to travel around scenic landscapes.
### 周游(周遊) → 周(周)
- **definition:** around; week; circumference
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Oracle bone script: depicts a jade artifact with carvings ->
    - borrowed as country name ->
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") added as mark ->
    - top part became similar to 用(用) (yòng, "to use")
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 周(周) (zhōu, "around; week; circumference") depicted a patterned jade artifact, reflecting its artistic value. The addition of 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") and changing top portion resemble 用(用) (yòng, "to use") highlights its transition from a pictogram to a more abstract word for 'circulate' or 'week.'
  - **simplification:** None, traditional and simplified forms are identical.
- **examples:**
  - 我每周去市场(我每週去市場)。
    - Wǒ měi zhōu qù shìchǎng.
    - I go to the market every week.
  - 这条河周长五百米(這條河周長五百米)。
    - Zhè tiáo hé zhōu cháng wǔ bǎi mǐ.
    - The river's circumference is 500 meters.
  - 村子周围有绿树(村子周圍有綠樹)，环境优美(環境優美)。
    - Cūnzi zhōuwéi yǒu lǜ shù, huánjìng yōuměi.
    - There are green trees around the village, and the environment is beautiful.
### 周游(周遊) → 游(遊)
- **definition:** travel; swim; roam; wander
- **pinyin:** yóu
- **components:**
  - 辵
    - chuò
    - walk
  - 斿
    - yóu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 辵(辵) (chuò, "walk") + phonetic 斿(斿) (yóu) ->
    - movement or traveling
  - **interpretation:** The character 游(遊) (yóu) combines the semantic element 辵(辵) (chuò, "walk") with the phonetic element 斿(斿) (yóu) which has associations with movement, hinting at the idea of traveling or wandering.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character 游 replaces 辵(辵) (chuò, "walk") with 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") indicating the semantic idea of swimming in water, while retaining 斿(斿) for phonetic guidance, aligning with the similar-sounding 游.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢游泳(我喜歡游泳)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yóuyǒng.
    - I like swimming.
  - 他在湖中游(他在湖中游)，感受水的魅力(感受水的魅力)。
    - Tā zài hú zhōng yóu, gǎnshòu shuǐ de mèilì.
    - He swims in the lake and feels the charm of water.
  - 他漫游古城(他漫遊古城)，仿佛穿越时空(仿佛穿越時空)。
    - Tā mànyóu gǔchéng, fǎngfú chuānyuè shíkōng.
