### 阴道(陰道) → 阴(陰)
- **definition:** dark; negative; female
- **pinyin:** yīn
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - mound
  - 侌
    - yīn
    - dark; shadowy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 阜, representing a mound, with the phonetic component 侌, which contributes to the pronunciation and meaning.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of darkness emerging from a mound, suggesting a hidden or shaded area.
  - **simplification:** 侌 was replaced with 月 for simplification, making the character easier to write while maintaining a similar phonetic quality.
- **examples:**
  - 天色越来越阴(天色越來越陰)，要下雨了(要下雨了)！

  - 阴天(陰天)总让人感觉沉闷(總讓人感覺沉悶)。
    - yīn tiān zǒng ràng rén gǎnjué chénmèn.
    - A cloudy day always makes people feel dull.
  - 这部电影有些阴暗(這部電影有些陰暗)，但我喜欢(但我喜歡)。
    - zhè bù diànyǐng yǒuxiē yīn'àn, dàn wǒ xǐhuān.
