## bully
### qīfu
---
- **definition:** bully
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **components:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - deceive
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - bear; suffer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 欺(欺) (qī, "deceive") + 负(負) (fù, "bear; suffer") is interpreted as causing someone to suffer through deceptive or harmful actions, which aligns with the concept of bullying.
  - **interpretation:** 欺负(欺負) (qīfu, "bully") combines the ideas of deceit and causing someone to bear suffering, representing the unfair and harmful behavior typical of bullying.
- **examples:**
  - 他欺负同学(他欺負同學)。
    - Tā qīfù tóngxué.
    - He bullies his classmates.
  - 小明欺负同学(小明欺負同學)，结果被老师批评(結果被老師批評)。
    - Xiǎomíng qīfù tóngxué, jiéguǒ bèi lǎoshī pīpíng.
    - Xiaoming bullies his classmates, and as a result, he was criticized by the teacher.

### 欺负(欺負) → 欺(欺) → 其(其)
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket pictogram) ->
    - 丌 (stand) added under basket as phonetic and to enhance meaning ->
    - borrowed for pronoun 'his' ->
    - 箕(箕) (jī, 'basket') derived with additional 竹(竹) (zhú, 'bamboo')

- **examples:**
