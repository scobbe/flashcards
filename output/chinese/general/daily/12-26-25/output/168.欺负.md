## bully
### qīfu
---
- **traditional:** 欺負
- **definition:** bully
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **components:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - deceive
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - bear; suffer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 欺 (qī, "deceive") + 负(負) (fù, "bear; suffer") is interpreted as causing someone to suffer through deceptive or harmful actions, which aligns with the concept of bullying.
  - **interpretation:** 欺负(負) (qīfu, "bully") combines the ideas of deceit and causing someone to bear suffering, representing the unfair and harmful behavior typical of bullying.
- **examples:**
  - 他欺负(負)同学(學)。
    - Tā qīfù tóngxué.
    - He bullies his classmates.
  - 小明欺负(負)同学(學)，结(結)果被老师(師)批评(評)。
    - Xiǎomíng qīfù tóngxué, jiéguǒ bèi lǎoshī pīpíng.
    - Xiaoming bullies his classmates, and as a result, he was criticized by the teacher.
### 欺( )负(負) → 欺
- **definition:** deceive
- **pinyin:** qī
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 气(氣) (qì, "air") + phonetic: 其 (qí, "his") =
    - giving false atmosphere ->
    - deceive
  - **interpretation:** 欺 (qī, "deceive") combines 气(氣) (qì, "air"), suggesting something intangible, with 其 (qí, "his"), which provides the phonetic element. Together, these imply creating a false impression or atmosphere, aligning with the meaning of deception.
- **examples:**
  - 不要欺别(別)人。
    - Bùyào qī bié rén.
    - Do not deceive others.
  - 他以花言巧语(語)欺骗(騙)了村民。
    - Tā yǐ huāyán qiǎoyǔ qī piàn le cūnmín.
    - He deceived the villagers with flowery words.
  - 商家欺骗(騙)消费(費)者，赢(贏)得了一时(時)的利益。
    - Shāngjiā qī piàn xiāofèi zhě, yíng dé le yìshí de lìyì.
    - The merchant deceived the consumers and gained a temporary profit.
### 欺( )负(負) → 欺 → 气(氣)
- **traditional:** 氣
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 乞
    - qǐ
    - beg
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Clouds flowing through the sky ->
    - altered to avoid confusion with 三 (sān, "three") ->
    - 乞 (qǐ, "beg") as a variant for phonetic borrowing
  - **interpretation:** The character 气(qì, "air") started as a pictogram representing clouds in the sky, which evolved to differentiate from the character for "three." Over time, 乞 was used as a variant for phonetic reasons, specifically for the meaning "to beg." The meanings 'air, flow' were later borrowed from 气.
- **examples:**
  - 房间里(間裡)的气(氣)很清新。
    - Fángjiān lǐ de qì hěn qīngxīn。
    - The air in the room is very fresh.
  - 打开(開)窗户(戶)，让(讓)新鲜(鮮)的气进来(氣進來)。
    - Dǎkāi chuānghù, ràng xīnxiān de qì jìnlái。
    - Open the window to let fresh air in.
  - 他深吸一口气(氣)，感觉(覺)心情舒畅(暢)。
    - Tā shēn xī yīkǒu qì, gǎnjué xīnqíng shūchàng。
    - He took a deep breath and felt his mood lift.

- **examples:**

### 欺( )负(負) → 欺 → 其
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket pictogram) ->
    - 丌 (stand) added under basket as phonetic and to enhance meaning ->
    - borrowed for pronoun 'his' ->
    - 箕 (jī, 'basket') derived with additional 竹 (zhú, 'bamboo')
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a basket, symbolized by 𠀠, emphasizing its use. The addition of 丌 beneath the basket strengthened the meaning of a structural object or container, eventually leading to its phonetic borrowing to represent the pronoun 'his'. To reclaim the original meaning of a 'basket', 箕 (jī, 'basket') was formed with an added bamboo radical.
- **examples:**
  - 将军(將軍)因其英勇闻(聞)名。
    - Jiāngjūn yīn qí yīngyǒng wénmíng。
    - The general is famous for his valor.
  - 同袍尊其忠诚(誠)，士卒佩服其英明。
    - Tóngpáo zūn qí zhōngchéng, shìzú pèifú qí yīngmíng。
    - His comrades revere his loyalty, and the soldiers admire his wisdom.
### 欺( )负(負) → 欺 → 其 → 箕
- **definition:** basket
- **pinyin:** jī
- **components:**
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + 其 (qí, "his") =
    - combines bamboo element with phonetic component 其 to convey 'basket' ->
    - specialization of 其
  - **interpretation:** The character 箕 (jī, "basket") was created to specify the meaning of 'basket' by adding the bamboo radical 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") to the character 其 (qí, "his"), which provides the phonetic cue. This reflects its use as a container often made from bamboo.
- **examples:**
  - 老农(農)用箕收割稻谷(穀)。
    - Lǎo nóng yòng jī shōugē dào gǔ。
    - The old farmer uses a basket to harvest rice.
  - 在村口，小贩将粮(販將糧)食倒进(進)箕中。
    - Zài cūnkǒu, xiǎofàn jiāng liángshí dào jìn jī zhōng。
    - At the village entrance, the vendor pours grain into the basket.

### 欺( )负(負) → 负(負)
- **traditional:** 負
- **definition:** bear; suffer
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - cowry
  - 孚
    - fú
    - trust
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 貝 (bèi, "cowry") + phonetic: 𠂆 (simplified to 孚 (fú, "trust") =
    - to carry valuables ->
    - bear, suffer
  - **interpretation:** The character 負 (fù, "bear; suffer") combines 貝 (bèi, "cowry"), indicating something of value, with the phonetic component 𠂆, suggesting the act of carrying or bearing. Together, they convey the idea of carrying a burden or responsibility, aligning with the meanings of bearing or suffering.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified form 负(負), the 貝 (bèi, "cowry") component was replaced with a simpler shape that resembles a trapezoid, reducing the number of strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 他毅然负(負)起照顾(顧)家庭的责(責)任。
    - Tā yìrán fù qǐ zhàogù jiātíng de zérèn.
    - He resolutely took on the responsibility of caring for his family.
  - 困难来临时(難來臨時)，他从(從)不负众(負眾)望。
    - Kùnnan láilín shí, tā cóng bù fù zhòngwàng.
    - When difficulties arise, he never disappoints everyone's expectations.
### 欺( )负(負) → 负(負) → 貝
- **definition:** cowry
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cowrie shell ->
    - used as currency in ancient China ->
    - stylized as 貝
  - **interpretation:** The character 貝(bèi, 'shell/money') visually represents a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China. This explains why it is associated with money or valuables.
  - **simplification:** 贝 is a cursive script simplification of 貝, transforming the character into a more streamlined form while retaining its essence.
- **examples:**
  - 他把贝(貝)当作护(護)身符，放在口袋里(裡)。
    - Tā bǎ bèi dāng zuò hùshēn fú, fàng zài kǒudài lǐ.
    - He uses shells as a talisman, placing them in his pocket.
  - 古代人用贝(貝)作为货币(為貨幣)。
    - Gǔdài rén yòng bèi zuòwéi huòbì.
    - In ancient times, people used shells as currency.
