## bully
### qīfu
---
- **definition:** bully
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **components:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - deceive
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - bear; carry on the back
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 欺(欺) (qī, "deceive") + 负(負) (fù, "bear; carry on the back")
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests the act of deceiving or harassing someone, which metaphorically bears or imposes hardship upon them, thus constituting bullying.
- **examples:**
  - 不要欺负弱小(不要欺負弱小)。
    - Bùyào qīfu ruòxiǎo.
    - Do not bully the weak.
  - 他总是欺负同学(他總是欺負同學)，使他们感到恐惧(使他們感到恐懼)。
    - Tā zǒng shì qīfu tóngxué, shǐ tāmen gǎndào kǒngjù.
    - He always bullies his classmates, making them feel terrified.

### 欺负(欺負) → 负(負) → 貝(貝)
- **definition:** cowry
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cowrie shell ->
    - used as currency in ancient China
  - **interpretation:** 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") depicts the distinctive shape of a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China due to its unique appearance and rarity.
- **examples:**
  - 他收藏一枚贝(他收藏一枚貝)，用作古代贸易的信物(用作古代貿易的信物)。
    - Tā shōucáng yī méi bèi, yòng zuò gǔdài màoyì de xìnwù.
    - He collects a cowry shell, using it as a token in ancient trade.
  - 清朝时贝(清朝時貝)，常被镶嵌于玉器(常被鑲嵌於玉器)。
    - Qīngcháo shí bèi, cháng bèi xiāngqiàn yú yùqì.
    - During the Qing dynasty, cowries were often inlaid in jade pieces.
