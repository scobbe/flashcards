## bath towel
### yùjīn
---
- **definition:** bath towel
- **pinyin:** yùjīn
- **components:**
  - 浴
    - yù
    - bath
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - towel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 浴(浴) (yù, "bath") + 巾(巾) (jīn, "towel") refers to a towel specifically used for drying off after bathing, hence the term "bath towel."
  - **interpretation:** A 巾(巾) (jīn, "towel") used in connection with 浴(浴) (yù, "bath") is meant for drying the body after a bath.
- **examples:**
  - 我刚洗完澡(我剛洗完澡)，需要用浴巾(需要用浴巾)。
    - Wǒ gāng xǐ wán zǎo, xūyào yòng yùjīn.
    - I just finished bathing, and I need to use a bath towel.
  - 酒店提供浴巾(酒店提供浴巾)。
    - Jiǔdiàn tígōng yùjīn.
    - The hotel provides a bath towel.

- **definition:** bath
- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 谷
    - gǔ
    - valley
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 谷(谷) (gǔ, "valley") =
    - bathe in water ->
    - bath
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") and 谷(谷) (gǔ, "valley") is intuitive as it suggests immersing in water, similar to how water might flow through a valley, which symbolizes the action of bathing.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢泡浴(她喜歡泡浴)。
    - Tā xǐhuān pào yù.
    - She enjoys taking a bath.
  - 他去浴室(他去浴室)，洗去疲惫(洗去疲憊)。
    - Tā qù yùshì, xǐ qù píbèi.

    - Xǐ yù hòu, tā gǎnjué shénqīngqìshuǎng.
