### bath towel
---
- **pinyin:** bath towel
- **components:**
  - 浴
    - yù
    - bathe; wash; bath; bask
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - towel; cloth; napkin; scarf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 浴(浴) (yù, "bathe") + 巾(巾) (jīn, "towel") — bathe towel
  - **interpretation:** A 浴巾(浴巾) (yùjīn) is essential for drying off after bathing. The combination of 浴(浴) (yù, "bathe") and 巾(巾) (jīn, "towel") clearly conveys its purpose.
- **examples:**
  - 我准备好浴巾(我準備好浴巾)，要去洗澡(要去洗澡)。
    - wǒ zhǔnbèi hǎo yùjīn, yào qù xǐzǎo.
    - I have prepared the bath towel and am going to take a shower.
  - 请递给我浴巾(請遞給我浴巾)。
    - qǐng dì gěi wǒ yùjīn.
    - Please pass me the bath towel.
