## instruct; teach
### jiàodǎo
---
- **traditional:** 教導
- **definition:** instruct; teach
- **pinyin:** jiàodǎo
- **components:**
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach; instruct; educate; guidance
  - 导(導)
    - dǎo
    - guide; lead; direct; conduct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 教 (jiào, "teach") and 导(導) (dǎo, "guide") together form the compound 教导(導) (jiàodǎo, "instruct; teach"). The word conveys the notion of not only imparting knowledge but also guiding and mentoring someone.
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense as it encompasses both teaching and guiding, suggesting a comprehensive approach to education that includes mentorship and leadership.
- **examples:**
  - 老师(師)教导学(導學)生。
    - Lǎoshī jiàodǎo xuéshēng.
    - The teacher instructs the students.
  - 父母教导(導)孩子，要做正直的人。
    - Fùmǔ jiàodǎo háizi, yào zuò zhèngzhí de rén.
    - Parents instruct their children to be honest people.
