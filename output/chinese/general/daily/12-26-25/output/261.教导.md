## instruct; teach
### jiàodǎo
---
- **definition:** instruct; teach
- **pinyin:** jiàodǎo
- **components:**
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach
  - 导(導)
    - dǎo
    - guide
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 教(教) (jiào, "teach") + 导(導) (dǎo, "guide") =
    - instruct; teach
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 教(教) (jiào, "teach"), indicating imparting knowledge or skills, with 导(導) (dǎo, "guide"), suggesting leading or directing someone. Together, they form the concept of instructing someone through guidance.
- **examples:**
  - 老师教导学生认真学习(老師教導學生認真學習)。
    - Lǎoshī jiàodǎo xuéshēng rènzhēn xuéxí.
    - The teacher instructs the students to study diligently.
  - 他常教导家人(他常教導家人)，如何尊重长辈(如何尊重長輩)。
    - Tā cháng jiàodǎo jiārén, rúhé zūnzhòng zhǎngbèi.
    - He often teaches his family how to respect their elders.
  - 父母教导孩子独立思考(父母教導孩子獨立思考)。
    - Fùmǔ jiàodǎo háizi dúlì sīkǎo.
    - Parents instruct their children to think independently.

### 教导(教導) → 导(導)
- **definition:** guide
- **pinyin:** dǎo
- **components:**
  - 道
    - dào
    - path
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 道(道) (dào, "path") + Semantic: 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") =
    - lead someone along a path ->
    - guide
  - **interpretation:** The character 导(導) (dǎo, "guide") combines the idea of a path represented by 道(道) (dào, "path") and the action of using one's hand, 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand"), to suggest leading or guiding someone along a path.
  - **simplification:** 导(導) was reduced by removing the 'head' component from 道(道) (dào, "path"), leaving just the key part for its phonetic sound, while retaining 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") for its semantic aspect.
- **examples:**
  - 他导我走向成功(他導我走向成功)。
    - Tā dǎo wǒ zǒuxiàng chénggōng.
    - He guides me toward success.
  - 思想导行为(思想導行為)，信念导前程(信念導前程)。
    - Sīxiǎng dǎo xíngwéi, xìnniàn dǎo qiánchéng.
    - Thought guides actions, and beliefs guide the future.
