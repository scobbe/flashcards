## 
### spray
---
- **pinyin:** spray
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air, gas
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 气 (qì, 'air, gas') with the phonetic component 喷 (pēn, 'to spray').
  - **interpretation:** This character visually suggests the action of air or gas being expelled in a spray form, making the meaning intuitive.
  - **simplification:** 噴 is simplified to 喷 by reducing strokes, specifically dropping the '言' (yán, 'speech') component which wasn't essential for the core meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我看到喷出的水(我看到噴出的水)。
    - Wǒ kàn dào pēn chū de shuǐ.
    - I saw the water spraying out.
  - 他用喷雾器清洁窗户(他用噴霧器清潔窗戶)。
    - Tā yòng pēnwùqì qīngjié chuānghù.
    - He used a spray cleaner to clean the windows.
### 喷(噴) → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air, gas
- **pinyin:** qì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts clouds flowing through the sky.
  - **interpretation:** The character represents the ethereal nature of air, akin to clouds that move freely in the sky. This visualization evokes a sense of the intangible and fluid qualities of air.
  - **simplification:** The simplification replaced the traditional character's more complex strokes with fewer, more streamlined lines for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 天上有气(天上有氣)，风吹得很大(風吹得很大)！
    - Tiānshàng yǒu qì, fēng chuī dé hěn dà!
    - There is air in the sky, and the wind is blowing strongly!
  - 我喜欢呼吸新鲜气(我喜歡呼吸新鮮氣)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hūxī xīnxiān qì.
    - I enjoy breathing fresh air.
  - 他的气(他的氣)很重！
    - Tā de qì hěn zhòng!
    - His aura is very strong!
