- **pinyin:** pēn
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu

  - 賁
    - bēn
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + 賁(賁) (bēn, phonetic) =
    - mouth action with sound ->
    - spray
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") to indicate the action involving the mouth, with 賁(賁) (bēn) for its phonetic element, to convey the idea of emitting something forcibly from the mouth, like spraying.
  - **simplification:** 喷(噴): 賁(賁) (bēn, phonetic) was simplified to 贲(贲) (bēn), keeping the phonetic element while reducing stroke count.
