## Urumqi (capital of Xinjiang)
### Wūlǔmùqí Shì
---
- **traditional:** 烏魯木齊市
- **definition:** Urumqi (capital of Xinjiang)
- **pinyin:** Wūlǔmùqí Shì
- **components:**
  - 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊)
    - Wūlǔmùqí
    - Urumqi
  - 市
    - shì
    - city
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊) (Wūlǔmùqí, "Urumqi") + 市 (shì, "city") =
    - Urumqi city
  - **interpretation:** 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊) (Wūlǔmùqí, "Urumqi") is the name of the capital city of the Xinjiang Uygur Autonomous Region in China. The term 市 (shì, "city") denotes it as an urban area. Urumqi is historically significant as a major hub on the northern route of the ancient Silk Road.
- **examples:**
  - 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊)市魅力无(無)限。
    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì mèilì wúxiàn.
    - Urumqi has endless charm.
  - 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊)市位于(於)新疆中部，经济发(經濟發)展迅速。
    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì wèiyú Xīnjiāng zhōngbù, jīngjì fāzhǎn xùnsù.
    - Urumqi is located in the central part of Xinjiang, and its economy is developing rapidly.
  - 乌鲁(烏魯)木齐(齊)市交通便利，文化活动丰(動豐)富。
    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì jiāotōng biànlì, wénhuà huódòng fēngfù.
    - Urumqi has convenient transportation, and cultural activities are abundant.
