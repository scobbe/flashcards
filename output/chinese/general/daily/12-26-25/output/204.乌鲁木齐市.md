## Urumqi (capital of Xinjiang)
### Wūlǔmùqí Shì
---
- **definition:** Urumqi (capital of Xinjiang)
- **pinyin:** Wūlǔmùqí Shì
- **components:**
  - 乌鲁木齐(烏魯木齊)
    - Wūlǔmùqí
    - Urumqi; transliteration of Uyghur Ürümchi
  - 市
    - shì
    - city; municipality; market; urban area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word (phonetic transliteration + suffix)
  - **description:** 乌鲁木齐 (phonetic of Ürümchi) + 市 (city) = “Urumqi City.”
  - **interpretation:** Phonetic rendering of the Uyghur/Oirat name Ürümchi, with characters chosen for sound rather than meaning. The suffix 市 marks it as a city-level administrative unit and the capital of Xinjiang.
  - **simplification:** 烏→乌, 魯→鲁, 齊→齐 were simplified per PRC standards; transliteration preserved; 木、市 unchanged.
- **examples:**

    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì shì Xīnjiāng de shǒufǔ.
    - Urumqi is the capital of Xinjiang.
  - 我们从北京飞往乌鲁木齐市(我們從北京飛往烏魯木齊市)。
    - Wǒmen cóng Běijīng fēiwǎng Wūlǔmùqí Shì.
    - We flew from Beijing to Urumqi.
  - 乌鲁木齐市的名字源自当地的民族语言(烏魯木齊市的名字源自當地的民族語言)。
    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì de míngzi yuán zì dāngdì de mínzú yǔyán.
    - The name Urumqi comes from local ethnic languages.

### 乌鲁木齐市(烏魯木齊市) → 市(市) → 之(之) → 止(止)
- **definition:** stop; foot
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a footprint pointing up (originally “foot”), later stylized as 龰; oracle forms show three toes and a sole.
