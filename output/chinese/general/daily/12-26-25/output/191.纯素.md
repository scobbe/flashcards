## vegan
### chúnsù
---
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** chúnsù
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure
  - 素
    - sù
    - simple; plain; vegetarian
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 纯(純) (chún, "pure") and 素(素) (sù, "simple; plain; vegetarian") come together to describe a diet or lifestyle free of animal products. The use of 素(素) (sù, "simple; plain; vegetarian") has historical roots as a term for vegetarianism, and 纯(純) (chún, "pure") emphasizes the complete exclusion of animal-derived ingredients.
  - **interpretation:** The term 纯素(純素) (chúnsù, "vegan") reflects an emphasis on a wholly plant-based lifestyle, avoiding any animal products. By combining the ideas of purity and simplicity or plainness, it effectively conveys a focus on ethical and dietary clarity.
- **examples:**
  - 我只吃纯素食物(我只吃純素食物)。
    - Wǒ zhǐ chī chúnsù shíwù.
    - I only eat vegan food.
  - 她开了一家纯素餐厅(她開了一家純素餐廳)，提供各种美味菜肴(提供各種美味菜餚)。
    - Tā kāile yī jiā chúnsù cāntīng, tígōng gèzhǒng měiwèi càiyáo.
    - She opened a vegan restaurant, offering a variety of delicious dishes.
  - 选择纯素生活(選擇純素生活)，关爱动物(關愛動物)。
    - Xuǎnzé chúnsù shēnghuó, guān'ài dòngwù.
    - Choose a vegan lifestyle, care for animals.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純)
- **definition:** pure
- **pinyin:** chún
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 屯
    - tún
    - station
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") + phonetic: 屯(屯) (tún, "station") =
    - silk quality ->
    - pure
  - **interpretation:** 纯(純) (chún, "pure") uses silk, symbolizing fine quality, combined with 屯(屯) (tún, "station") to express the idea of pure and unadulterated silk.
  - **simplification:** The character 纯(純) was simplified by removing the middle component from 屯(屯) while retaining the recognizable phonetic and semantic elements.
- **examples:**
  - 这水真纯(這水真純)。
    - Zhè shuǐ zhēn chún.
    - This water is very pure.
  - 她的心灵纯洁(她的心靈純潔)，她的笑容纯真(她的笑容純真)。
    - Tā de xīnlíng chúnjié, tā de xiàoróng chúnzhēn.
    - Her heart is pure, and her smile is genuine.
  - 这首音乐纯粹动人(這首音樂純粹動人)。
    - Zhè shǒu yīnyuè chúncuì dòngrén.
    - This piece of music is purely moving.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純) → 糸(糸)
- **definition:** silk
- **pinyin:** mì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a coil of silk thread
  - **interpretation:** The character 糸(mì, 'silk thread') visually represents a bundle or coil of silk threads, aligning with its meaning of thread or textile.
- **examples:**
  - 古人用糸制作衣物(古人用糸製作衣物)。
    - Gǔrén yòng mì zhìzuò yīwù.
    - Ancient people used silk thread to make clothes.
  - 他用糸织成精致的布料(他用糸織成精緻的布料)，装饰了贵族的衣裳(裝飾了貴族的衣裳)。
    - Tā yòng mì zhīchéng jīngzhì de bùliào, zhuāngshì le guìzú de yīshang.
    - He wove exquisite fabric with silk thread, decorating the robes of nobles.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純) → 屯(屯)
- **definition:** station
- **pinyin:** tún
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a seedling pushing up from the ground
  - **interpretation:** The character 屯(屯) (zhūn, "sprout") visually represents a sprout or seedling emerging from the earth. The top part resembles a plant breaking through the soil, which is a common interpretation for the concept of sprouting.
- **examples:**
  - 种子遇春风激发能量(種子遇春風激發能量)，开始屯发新绿(開始屯發新綠)。
    - zhǒngzǐ yù chūnfēng jīfā néngliàng, kāishǐ zhūn fā xīn lǜ.
    - The seeds, energized by the spring breeze, begin to sprout fresh green.
  - 小苗吸收雨露后(小苗吸收雨露後)，纷纷屯出嫩绿叶子(紛紛屯出嫩綠葉子)。
    - xiǎo miáo xīshōu yǔlù hòu, fēnfēn zhūn chū nèn lǜ yèzi.
    - After absorbing the rain and dew, the young sprouts begin to bud tender green leaves.

### 纯素(純素) → 素(素) → 粟(粟)
- **definition:** millet
- **pinyin:** sù
- **components:**
  - 角
    - jiǎo
    - horn
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 西
    - xī
    - west
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Oracle bone script shows seeds → 角(角) (jiǎo, "horn") for phonetic + 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") for semantic → 角 evolved to 𠧪 → further changed to 西(西) (xī, "west")
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting seeds, 粟(sù, "millet") combined phonetic and semantic elements. 米(米) (mǐ, "rice") indicates the grain aspect, while 角(角) (jiǎo) initially added sound. Over time, the original depiction evolved through several script forms to match pronunciation changes.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天丰收时(秋天豐收時)，田野中的粟闪着金光(田野中的粟閃著金光)。
    - Qiūtiān fēngshōu shí, tiányě zhōng de sù shǎnzhe jīnguāng.
    - During the autumn harvest, the millet in the fields glimmers with golden light.
  - 农夫把粟从田里收集(農夫把粟從田裡收集)，准备储存过冬(準備儲存過冬)。
    - Nóngfū bǎ sù cóng tián lǐ shōují, zhǔnbèi chúcún guòdōng.
    - The farmer collects the millet from the fields, preparing to store it for the winter.
  - 古代诗人常以粟象征丰收(古代詩人常以粟象徵豐收)，歌颂农事的喜悦(歌頌農事的喜悅)。
    - Gǔdài shīrén cháng yǐ sù xiàngzhēng fēngshōu, gēsòng nóngshì de xǐyuè.
    - Ancient poets often used millet to symbolize a bountiful harvest, praising the joy of farming.

### 纯素(純素) → 素(素) → 粟(粟) → 西(西)
- **definition:** west
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a bag or basket ->
    - borrowed for phonetic value to mean 'west'
  - **interpretation:** The character 西(xī, 'west') originally depicted a bag or basket and was phonetically borrowed to represent 'west'. Although traditionally linked to the image of a bird in a nest symbolizing the setting sun, this interpretation is disputed. Instead, it reflects a simple phonetic borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他家住在西边(他家住在西邊)，附近有一座大山(附近有一座大山)。

    - His home is on the west side, and there is a big mountain nearby.
  - 风筝向西飞去(風箏向西飛去)。
    - Fēngzhēng xiàng xī fēi qù.
    - The kite flies toward the west.
