## vegan
### chúnsù
---
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** chúnsù
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure; clean; unmixed; solely
  - 素
    - sù
    - element; plain; white; vegetable-based food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 纯(純) (chún, "pure") + 素(素) (sù, "element; plain; vegetable-based food") together emphasize the idea of purely vegetable-based or plant-originating, hence 'vegan.'
  - **interpretation:** The word combines the idea of 'pure' with 'plain' or 'vegetable-based,' underscoring the concept of a diet consisting solely of plant-based elements, which aligns perfectly with the meaning of 'vegan.'
  - **simplification:** 纯→純
- **examples:**
  - 我享受纯素饮食(我享受純素飲食)。
    - Wǒ xiǎngshòu chúnsù yǐnshí.
    - I enjoy a vegan diet.
  - 她选择了纯素生活(她選擇了純素生活)，坚持健康理念(堅持健康理念)。
    - Tā xuǎnzé le chúnsù shēnghuó, jiānchí jiànkāng lǐniàn.
    - She has chosen a vegan lifestyle and adheres to healthy principles.
  - 这家餐厅提供纯素餐点(這家餐廳提供純素餐點)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng chúnsù cāndiǎn.
    - This restaurant offers vegan dishes.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純)
- **definition:** pure; clean; unmixed; solely
- **pinyin:** chún
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk
  - 屯
    - tún
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") + phonetic: 屯(屯) (tún) =
    - pure silk ->
    - pure
  - **interpretation:** 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") gives the semantic idea of purity or cleanness, relating to the texture or quality of silk, while 屯(屯) (tún) serves as a phonetic component indicating the character's pronunciation.
  - **simplification:** 純(純) was simplified to 纯(純), with 屯(屯) (tún) being retained, while 糸(糸) (mì, "silk") was simplified to 纟(纟), a three-stroke cursive form that retains semantic meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 牛奶纯(牛奶純)。
    - Niúnǎi chún.
    - The milk is pure.
  - 这药是纯(這藥是純)，没有添加剂(沒有添加劑)。
    - Zhè yào shì chún, méiyǒu tiānjiājì.
    - This medicine is pure and contains no additives.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純) → 糸(糸)
- **definition:** silk
- **pinyin:** mì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a skein of silk
  - **interpretation:** The character 糸(糸) (sī, "silk") visually represents twisted threads of silk, capturing the appearance and texture of a silk skein. This depiction makes intuitive sense for representing the concept of silk due to the familiar form of such threads in historical textile production.
- **examples:**
  - 工匠用糸织布(工匠用糸織布)，制作精美服饰(製作精美服飾)。
    - Gōngjiàng yòng sī zhī bù, zhìzuò jīngměi fúshì.
    - The artisan weaves cloth with silk and creates exquisite garments.
  - 博物馆展出古代糸织品(博物館展出古代糸織品)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū gǔdài sī zhī pǐn.
    - The museum exhibits ancient silk-woven artifacts.
  - 在传统纺织技艺中(在傳統紡織技藝中)，糸扮演了重要的角色(糸扮演了重要的角色)。
    - Zài chuántǒng fǎngzhī jìyì zhōng, sī bànyǎn le zhòngyào de juésè.
    - In traditional textile craftsmanship, silk plays an important role.
### 纯素(純素) → 纯(純) → 屯(屯)
- **definition:** phonetic
- **pinyin:** tún
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts sprout above ground ->
    - assembly
  - **interpretation:** The character 屯(屯) (tún, "assembled") visually represents the sprout emerging from the ground, symbolizing gathering or collecting, which conveys the sense of assembly.
- **examples:**
  - 军队屯兵于城外(軍隊屯兵於城外)。
    - Jūnduì tún bīng yú chéngwài.
    - The army assembled their troops outside the city.
  - 夜幕降临时(夜幕降臨時)，士兵开始屯集(士兵開始屯集)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín shí, shìbīng kāishǐ tún jí.
    - When night fell, the soldiers began to assemble.
### 纯素(純素) → 素(素)
- **definition:** element; plain; white; vegetable-based food
- **pinyin:** sù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 廾(廾) (gǒng, "two hands") + 糸(糸) (mì, "silk thread") =
    - hands braiding thread ->
    - plain/simplistic ->
    - modern form resembles 生 without diagonal stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 素(sù, "plain") represents the action of hands braiding a silk thread, signifying simplicity and purity. It visually encapsulates the idea of creating something basic yet essential by hand.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢穿素色衣服(他喜歡穿素色衣服)。
    - Tā xǐhuān chuān sù sè yīfú.
    - He likes wearing plain-colored clothes.
  - 他不吃荤菜，只吃素食(他不吃葷菜，只吃素食)。
    - Tā bù chī hūncài, zhǐ chī sùshí.
    - He doesn't eat meat; he only eats vegetarian food.
  - 她坚持素食生活(她堅持素食生活)。
    - Tā jiānchí sùshí shēnghuó.
    - She adheres to a vegetarian lifestyle.
