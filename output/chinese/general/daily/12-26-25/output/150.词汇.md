### vocabulary
---
- **pinyin:** vocabulary
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; vocabulary; term; speech
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - collection; converge; gather; assemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 词(詞) (cí, "word") + 汇(彙) (huì, "collection") — word collection
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as it refers to a collection of words and terms used in a language, emphasizing the importance of words in communication.
- **examples:**
  - 我正在学习新词汇(我正在學習新詞彙)。
    - Wǒ zhèngzài xuéxí xīn cíhuì.
    - I am learning new vocabulary.
  - 教室里有很多有用的词汇(教室裡有很多有用的詞彙)。
    - Jiàoshì lǐ yǒu hěn duō yǒuyòng de cíhuì.
    - There are many useful vocabulary terms in the classroom.
  - 扩充你的词汇(擴充你的詞彙)对学习很重要。
    - Kuòchōng nǐ de cíhuì duì xuéxí hěn zhòngyào.
    - Expanding your vocabulary is important for learning.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞)

- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司

    - to manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic element 言 (yán, 'speech') with the phonetic element 司 (sī).
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively relates to words as it incorporates the meaning of speech, indicating its connection to language and expression.
- **examples:**
  - 这句话有很多意义(這句話有很多意義)。
    - Zhè jù huà yǒu hěn duō yìyì.
    - This sentence has many meanings.
  - 我学习新词(我學習新詞)。
    - Wǒ xuéxí xīn cí.
    - I learn new words.
  - 词典里有很多词(詞典裏有很多詞)。
    - Cídiǎn lǐ yǒu hěn duō cí.
    - There are many words in the dictionary.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 言(言)

- **examples:**

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯)
- **definition:** collection; converge; gather; assemble
- **pinyin:** huì
- **components:**
  - 㣇
    - huì
    - gathering
  - 胃
    - wèi
    - stomach
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Contains the semantic component 㣇 (representing gathering) and the phonetic component 胃 (providing the pronunciation).
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys the idea of gathering together or collecting, with the 㣇 suggesting a confluence or assembly.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 汇 by removing the component 隹, making the character easier to write.
- **examples:**
  - 我们去汇款(我們去匯款)。
    - Wǒmen qù huìkuǎn.
    - We are going to transfer money.
  - 信息会汇聚在一起(信息會匯聚在一起)。
    - Xìnxī huì huìjù zài yīqǐ.
    - The information will converge together.
  - 这本书汇集了很多知识(這本書匯集了很多知識)。
    - Zhè běn shū huìjíle hěnduō zhīshì.
    - This book collects a lot of knowledge.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯) → 㣇(㣇)
- **definition:** gathering
- **pinyin:** huì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a pig-like creature with fur, showing the head of a pig at the top and its body features at the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a pig-like creature suggests themes of gathering, as pigs are often associated with feasting and communal meals.
- **examples:**
  - 在社区组织聚会(在社區組織聚會)。
    - Zài shèqū zǔzhī jùhuì.
    - We organize gatherings in the community.
  - 每年春节我们家都会聚会(每年春節我們家都會聚會)。
    - Měi nián chūnjié wǒmen jiā dū huì jùhuì.
    - Every year during Spring Festival, our family holds a gathering.

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯) → 胃(胃) → 韋(韋)
- **definition:** to enclose; soft leather
- **pinyin:** wéi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character is historically associated with leather or hide, indicating a form of protection, which connects to the idea of surrounding or enclosing.
  - **interpretation:** The protective quality of leather evokes the notion of surrounding or enclosing, similar to how an animal hide might envelop and protect what is inside.
- **examples:**
  - 韋編三絕(韋編三絕)，書法藝術無人能敵(書法藝術無人能敵)。
    - wéi biān sān jué, shūfǎ yìshù wú rén néng dí.
    - The leather binding is exceptional, and no one can rival his calligraphy.
  - 韋昭(韋昭)是三國時期的名將(三國時期的名將)。
    - wéi zhāo shì sān guó shíqī de míngjiàng.
    - Wei Zhao was a famous general of the Three Kingdoms period.
