## vocabulary
### cíhuì
---
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; term
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - collection; to collect
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") + 汇(彙) (huì, "collection; to collect") =
    - a collection of words ->
    - vocabulary
  - **interpretation:** 词汇(詞彙) (cíhuì, "vocabulary") refers to a collection or list of words, particularly those known or used by a person or within a particular language. It implies an organized collection of terms, essential for communication and language learning.
  - **simplification:** 词汇(詞彙)
- **examples:**
  - 他每天学习词汇(他每天學習詞彙)，以扩充语言能力(以擴充語言能力)。
    - Tā měitiān xuéxí cíhuì, yǐ kuòchōng yǔyán nénglì.
    - He studies vocabulary every day to expand his language ability.
  - 词汇是语言的宝库(詞彙是語言的寶庫)。
    - Cíhuì shì yǔyán de bǎokù.
    - Vocabulary is the treasure house of language.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞)
- **definition:** word; term
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 讠(言)
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司
    - sī
    - manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 言(言) (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 司(司) (sī, "manage") =
    - word or term relating to speech ->
    - simplified by replacing 言(言) (yán, "speech") with 讠(讠) (yán, "speech") ->
    - final form 词(詞)
  - **interpretation:** The character 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") combines the speech radical 言(言) (yán, "speech") with 司(司) (sī, "manage") to indicate a managed or structured form of speech, i.e., a word or term. The simplified form retains this concept, making writing faster with 讠(讠) (yán, "speech") being a cursive form of the speech radical.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by changing the full radical 言(言) (yán, "speech") to its cursive form 讠(讠) (yán, "speech"), facilitating quicker writing while retaining the original meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这个词意义丰富(這個詞意義豐富)。
    - Zhè ge cí yìyì fēngfù.
    - This word has a rich meaning.
  - 诗歌中的每个词都富有韵律(詩歌中的每個詞都富有韻律)，展现了作者的才华(展現了作者的才華)。
    - Shīgē zhōng de měi ge cí dōu fùyǒu yùnlǜ, zhǎnxiànle zuòzhě de cáihuá.
    - Every word in the poem is full of rhythm, revealing the author's talent.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 讠(訁)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a curved line with a dot, symbolizing speech or words
  - **interpretation:** 讠(訁) (yán, "speech") is the simplified radical for words or speech. As a pictogram, it represents the essential concept of communication.
- **examples:**
  - 汉字中的讠构件(漢字中的言構件)，常出现在有关话语的字中(常出現在有關話語的字中)。
    - Hànzì zhōng de yán gòujiàn, cháng chūxiàn zài yǒuguān huàyǔ de zì zhōng.
    - The 讠 component in Chinese characters often appears in characters related to speech.
  - 古人重视讠之力量(古人重視言之力量)，认为言传身教胜过万言(認為言傳身教勝過萬言)。
    - Gǔrén zhòngshì yán zhī lìliàng, rènwéi yán chuán shēnjiào shèngguò wànyán.
    - Ancient people valued the power of speech, believing that teaching by example was superior to a thousand words.
  - 含讠字形的汉字(含言字形的漢字)，通常与言语表达密切相关(通常與言語表達密切相關)。
    - Hán yán zìxíng de hànzì, tōngcháng yǔ yányǔ biǎodá mìqiè xiāngguān.
    - Chinese characters with the 讠 radical are usually closely associated with speech expression.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 司(司)
- **definition:** manage
- **pinyin:** sī
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 一(一) (yī, "one") + 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - overseeing one's speech or words ->
    - manage
  - **interpretation:** The character 司(司) (sī, "manage") combines 一(一) (yī, "one") and 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") suggesting the idea of control or direction over speech, symbolizing management or supervision.
- **examples:**
  - 他司图书馆(他司圖書館)，井井有条(井井有條)。
    - Tā sī lǐ túshūguǎn, jǐng jǐng yǒu tiáo.
    - He manages the library, keeping it in perfect order.
  - 他亲自司掌全局(他親自司掌全局)。
    - Tā qīnzì sī zhǎng quán jú.
    - He personally takes charge of the overall situation.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 司(司) → 一(一)
- **definition:** one
- **pinyin:** yī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** A horizontal stroke representing the concept of 'one'.
  - **interpretation:** The character 一(yī, "one") is a simple horizontal line signifying the singular and unified concept of 'one'. It directly represents being singular or the number one.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个苹果(我有一個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī gè píngguǒ.
    - I have one apple.
  - 他只看到一只鸟(他只看到一隻鳥)，飞进了花园(飛進了花園)。
    - Tā zhǐ kàn dào yī zhī niǎo, fēi jìn le huā yuán.
    - He only saw one bird, which flew into the garden.
  - 一条直路通向远方(一條直路通向遠方)。
    - Yī tiáo zhí lù tōng xiàng yuǎn fāng.
    - One straight road leads to the distance.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 司(司) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu) represents a pictorial depiction of an open mouth, indicating its meaning as 'mouth.' Its pictographic origin makes it intuitively connected to the concept it represents—related to speaking or openings. This character is also a common radical, forming part of many complex characters with meanings related to mouths or openings.
- **examples:**
  - 我家有三口人(我家有三口人)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén.
    - My family has three members.
  - 地处河口的平台很美丽(地處河口的平台很美麗)。
    - Dì chǔ hé kǒu de píngtái hěn měilì.
    - The platform located at the river's mouth is beautiful.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯)
- **definition:** collection; to collect
- **pinyin:** huì
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 匚
    - fāng
    - box
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 匚(匚) (fāng, "box") =
    - water flowing together ->
    - to collect, to converge
  - **interpretation:** The character 汇(匯) combines the semantic element of water, suggesting flow or gathering, with a phonetic component suggesting containment, which together convey the idea of collecting or converging, much like water pooling together.
  - **simplification:** 汇(匯): The simplified form uses 匚(匚) (fāng, "box") as a direct visual and phonetic component, replacing 龠(龠) (yuè, "flute") for a more concise representation.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天在图书馆收集文献(他每天在圖書館收集文獻)，汇成一部史诗(彙成一部史詩)。
    - Tā měitiān zài túshūguǎn shōují wénxiàn huì chéng yī bù shǐshī.
    - He collects documents in the library every day and turns them into an epic.
  - 邮局每天汇集邮件(郵局每天彙集郵件)，统一分送到各地(統一分送到各地)。
    - Yóujú měitiān huìjí yóujiàn, tǒngyī fēnsòng dào gèdì.
    - The post office gathers mail every day and uniformly distributes it to various regions.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯) → 氵(氵)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水(水) (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源(水是生命之源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉字中偏旁氵(漢字中偏旁氵)，代表水(代表水)。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明(河水清澈透明)，鱼儿欢快戏水(魚兒歡快戲水)。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(匯) → 匚(匚)
- **definition:** box
- **pinyin:** fāng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a box
  - **interpretation:** The character 匚(fāng, "box") is a pictogram representing the shape of an open box, viewed from above. It captures the simple geometric form of a container, emphasizing its open top and defined edges.
- **examples:**
  - 他在匚里找到了一枚古钱(他在匚裡找到了一枚古錢)。
    - Tā zài fāng lǐ zhǎodàole yī méi gǔ qián.
    - He found an old coin in the box.
  - 古老匚上刻满了神秘符号(古老匚上刻滿了神秘符號)。
    - Gǔlǎo fāng shàng kè mǎnle shénmì fúhào.
    - The ancient box is engraved with mysterious symbols.
