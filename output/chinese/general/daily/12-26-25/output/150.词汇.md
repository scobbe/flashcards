## vocabulary
### cíhuì
---
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; phrase
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - collection; to gather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 词(詞) (cí, "word") + 汇(彙) (huì, "collection") =
    - vocabulary is a collection of words.
  - **interpretation:** The word refers to a collection or compilation of words used in a language or by a particular person, which is essential for communication and understanding.
  - **simplification:** 词 is the simplified form of 詞, and 汇 is the simplified form of 彙.
- **examples:**
  - 掌握词汇(掌握詞彙)有助于日常交流(有助於日常交流)。
    - zhǎngwò cíhuì yǒuzhù yú rìcháng jiāoliú.
    - Mastering vocabulary helps with daily communication.
  - 记忆词汇(記憶詞彙)是学习语言的基础(是學習語言的基礎)。
    - jìyì cíhuì shì xuéxí yǔyán de jīchǔ.
    - Memorizing vocabulary is the foundation of learning a language.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞)
- **definition:** word; phrase
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司
    - sī
    - phonetic component
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 言(言) (yán, "speech") + 司(司) (sī, phonetic) =
    - related to speech ->
    - word
  - **interpretation:** The character uses 言(言) (yán, "speech") to indicate its connection with spoken language. 司(司) (sī) is used phonetically to approximate the pronunciation of the character, which aligns with its meaning related to words and phrases.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 詞(詞) by replacing 言(言) (yán, "speech") with 讠(讠) (yán), a cursive form of the speech radical.
- **examples:**
  - 这个词很美(這個詞很美)。
    - Zhège cí hěn měi.
    - This word is beautiful.
  - 她在诗中反复重复同一个词(她在詩中反復重複同一個詞)，以加强情感(以加強情感)。
    - Tā zài shī zhōng fǎn fù chóng fù tóng yīgè cí, yǐ jiā qiáng qíng gǎn.
    - She repeatedly used the same word in the poem to intensify the emotion.
  - 古文中的每个词背后都有故事(古文中的每個詞背後都有故事)。
    - Gǔwén zhōng de měi gè cí bèihòu dōu yǒu gùshì.
    - Every word in ancient literature has a story behind it.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech
- **pinyin:** yán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 舌(舌) (shé, "tongue") + horizontal stroke =
    - movement of tongue ->
    - speech
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") originates from the depiction of a tongue 舌(舌) with an added stroke to represent its movement, signifying the action of speaking. This visual representation highlights the tongue's role in speech, which is central to communication.
- **examples:**
  - 言简意赅可以表达深刻思想(言簡意賅可以表達深刻思想)。
    - yán jiǎn yì gāi kěyǐ biǎo dá shēn kè sī xiǎng。
    - Concise speech can express profound thoughts.
  - 他的言论总能激发思考(他的言論總能激發思考)。
    - tā de yán lùn zǒng néng jī fā sī kǎo。
    - His speech always inspires reflection.
  - 老师常说(老師常說)，言出必行(言出必行)。
    - lǎo shī cháng shuō, yán chū bì xíng。
    - The teacher often says that one should act on one's words.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 司(司)
- **definition:** phonetic component
- **pinyin:** sī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic component 䇂(䇂) (sī) =
    - indicates sound and meaning related to governing or overseeing ->
    - "to manage"
  - **interpretation:** 司(司) (sī, "to manage") combines 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth"), indicating a verbal aspect, with 䇂(䇂) (sī), suggesting the action of overseeing or managing. The mouth suggests issuing commands or making reports as part of management duties.
  - **simplification:** None, traditional and simplified forms are the same.
- **examples:**
  - 在造字学中(在造字學中)，‘司’常作为声旁使用(‘司’常作為聲旁使用)。
    - Zài zào zì xué zhōng, 'sī' cháng zuòwéi shēngpáng shǐyòng.
    - In the study of Chinese character formation, '司' is often used as the phonetic component.
  - ‘司’是表示发音的符号(‘司’是表示發音的符號)。
    - 'sī' shì biǎoshì fāyīn de fúhào.
    - '司' is a symbol that represents pronunciation.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙)
- **definition:** collection; to gather
- **pinyin:** huì
- **components:**
  - 㣇
    - huì
    - to gather
  - 胃
    - wèi
    - stomach
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 㣇(㣇) (huì, "to gather") as semantic + 胃(胃) (wèi, "stomach") as phonetic =
    - collection
  - **interpretation:** The character 彙(彙) (huì, "collection") combines the semantic component 㣇(㣇) (huì, "to gather") with the phonetic component 胃(胃) (wèi, "stomach") to suggest the action of gathering or collecting items, similar to collecting food in a stomach.
  - **simplification:** 彙(彙) was simplified to 汇(彙) by using simplified components, with the water radical 氵(氵) (shuǐ, "water") added for phonetic distinction.
- **examples:**
  - 博物馆收藏着各国名画(博物館收藏著各國名畫)，汇聚成一部艺术史(彙聚成一部藝術史)。
    - Bówùguǎn shōucáng zhe gèguó mínghuà, huìjù chéng yībù yìshùshǐ.
    - The museum collects famous paintings from various countries, gathering them into a history of art.
  - 他汇编了古文典籍(他彙編了古文典籍)。
    - Tā huìbiān le gǔwén diǎnjí.
    - He compiled ancient texts.

    - depicts a pig-like creature
  - **interpretation:** The character 㣇 visually represents a pig-like creature, incorporating 豕(豕) (shǐ, "pig-like creature") and 彑(彑) (jì, "pig head") to depict an animal with distinctive features such as fur and hooves.
  - **simplification:** None, simplified and traditional forms are the same.
- **examples:**

  - 学生们㣇在图书馆前(學生們㣇在圖書館前)，等待老师到来(等待老師到來)。

  - 月
