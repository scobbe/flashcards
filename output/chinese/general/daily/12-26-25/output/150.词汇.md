## vocabulary
### cíhuì
---
- **traditional:** 詞彙
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; term
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - collection; to collect
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") + 汇(彙) (huì, "collection; to collect") =
    - a collection of words ->
    - vocabulary
  - **interpretation:** 词(詞)汇(彙) (cíhuì, "vocabulary") refers to a collection or list of words, particularly those known or used by a person or within a particular language. It implies an organized collection of terms, essential for communication and language learning.
  - **simplification:** 词(詞)汇(彙)
- **examples:**
  - 他( )每( )天( )学(學)习(習)词(詞)汇(彙)，以( )扩(擴)充( )语(語)言( )能( )力( )。
    - Tā měitiān xuéxí cíhuì, yǐ kuòchōng yǔyán nénglì.
    - He studies vocabulary every day to expand his language ability.
  - 词(詞)汇(彙)是( )语(語)言( )的( )宝(寶)库(庫)。
    - Cíhuì shì yǔyán de bǎokù.
    - Vocabulary is the treasure house of language.
### 词(詞)汇(彙) → 词(詞)
- **traditional:** 詞
- **definition:** word; term
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 讠(言)
    - yán
    - speech
  - 司
    - sī
    - manage
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 司 (sī, "manage") =
    - word or term relating to speech ->
    - simplified by replacing 言 (yán, "speech") with 讠 (yán, "speech") ->
    - final form 词(詞)
  - **interpretation:** The character 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") combines the speech radical 言 (yán, "speech") with 司 (sī, "manage") to indicate a managed or structured form of speech, i.e., a word or term. The simplified form retains this concept, making writing faster with 讠 (yán, "speech") being a cursive form of the speech radical.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by changing the full radical 言 (yán, "speech") to its cursive form 讠 (yán, "speech"), facilitating quicker writing while retaining the original meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)词(詞)意( )义(義)丰(豐)富( )。
