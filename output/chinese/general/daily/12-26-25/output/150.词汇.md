## vocabulary
### cíhuì
---
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; term; expression; lyrics
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - collection; to gather; category; compilation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 词(word) + 汇(collection) = collection of words (vocabulary/lexicon).
  - **interpretation:** Means the stock or collection of words used in a language, field, or by a person. Common in linguistics, education, and lexicography.
  - **simplification:** 詞 → 词 simplifies the 言 radical to 讠 to reduce strokes; 彙 → 汇 merges with 匯 into a single simplified form, leveraging the idea of ‘gathering/converging’ and streamlining high‑frequency usage.
- **examples:**
  - 学习新词汇有助于提高阅读理解(學習新詞彙有助於提高閱讀理解)。
    - Xuéxí xīn cíhuì yǒu zhù yú tígāo yuèdú lǐjiě.
    - Learning new vocabulary helps improve reading comprehension.
  - 他的英语词汇量很大(他的英語詞彙量很大)。
    - Tā de Yīngyǔ cíhuìliàng hěn dà.
    - His English vocabulary size is large.
  - 这本词典按主题分类词汇(這本詞典按主題分類詞彙)。
    - Zhè běn cídiǎn àn zhǔtí fēnlèi cíhuì.
    - This dictionary organizes vocabulary by topic.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞)
- **definition:** word; term; expression; lyrics
- **pinyin:** cí
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech; to say
  - 司
    - sī
    - to manage; to administer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 言 (“speech”) + phonetic 司; simplified from 詞 (言 → 讠).
  - **interpretation:** Combines the speech element 言/讠 with the phonetic 司, signaling a meaning related to spoken language while borrowing its sound from 司. The form points to utterances or expressions, aligning with senses like “word” and “lyrics.”
  - **simplification:** Component reduction in the PRC script reform: the semantic component 言 was contracted to 讠 on the left side; the phonetic 司 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 这个词很难理解(這個詞很難理解)。
    - Zhège cí hěn nán lǐjiě.
    - This word is hard to understand.
  - 学习新词能丰富表达(學習新詞能豐富表達)。
    - Xuéxí xīn cí néng fēngfù biǎodá.
    - Learning new words can enrich expression.
  - 这首歌的歌词很感人(這首歌的歌詞很感人)。
    - Zhè shǒu gē de gēcí hěn gǎnrén.
    - The song’s lyrics are very moving.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 言(言)
- **definition:** speech; to say
- **pinyin:** yán
- **components:**
  - 舌
    - shé
    - tongue
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Ideogram: formed by adding a horizontal stroke to ancient 舌 to show tongue movement (speech). Shuowen erroneously treats it as phono-semantic: phonetic 䇂 (qiān) + semantic 口 (“mouth”).
  - **interpretation:** Built on 舌 (tongue), an added stroke marks motion, abstracting the act of speaking into a simple sign for “speech/words.” Later standardization added a dot on top and simplified the tongue shape, moving from a pictorial tongue to a conventional symbol for utterance. The Shuowen’s phono-semantic reading is a historical misinterpretation rather than the true origin.
- **examples:**
  - 请注意你的言语(請注意你的言語)。
    - Qǐng zhùyì nǐ de yányǔ.
    - Please mind your language.
  - 他发言很简短(他發言很簡短)。
    - Tā fāyán hěn jiǎnduǎn.
    - His remarks were brief.
  - 言行一致(言行一致)，方能取信于人(方能取信於人)。
    - Yán xíng yízhì, fāng néng qǔxìn yú rén.
    - Only by matching words with deeds can one gain others’ trust.

### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 言(言) → 舌(舌) → 口(口)

- **pinyin:** kǒu

  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**

  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 词(詞) → 司(司)

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙)
- **definition:** collection; to gather; category; compilation
- **pinyin:** huì
- **components:**
  - 㣇
    - huì
    - to gather; assemble
  - 胃
    - wèi
    - stomach (phonetic component here)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 㣇 + abbreviated phonetic 胃
  - **interpretation:** 㣇 conveys the idea of gathering/bringing things together, matching the meaning of ‘collection/category’. 胃 (wèi) supplies the sound value huì in Old Chinese, guiding the pronunciation. In simplified Chinese, this meaning is written with 汇, which was standardized by merging with the simplified form of 匯/滙.
  - **simplification:** Adopted the existing simplified form 汇 (from 匯/滙 by removing 隹), merging 彙 and 匯 into the single simplified graph 汇 for ease and uniformity.
- **examples:**
  - 这本词典按主题分类词汇(這本詞典按主題分類詞匯)，方便查找(方便查找)。
    - Zhè běn cídiǎn àn zhǔtí fēnlèi cíhuì, fāngbiàn cházhǎo.
    - This dictionary categorizes vocabulary by topic for easy lookup.
  - 编辑正在汇编资料(編輯正在匯編資料)，准备出版专题合集(準備出版專題閤集)。
    - Biānjí zhèngzài huìbiān zīliào, zhǔnbèi chūbǎn zhuāntí héjí.
    - The editors are compiling materials to publish a themed collection.
  - 我们把各地的案例汇集起来(我們把各地的案例匯集起來)，做成一份报告(做成一份報告)。
    - Wǒmen bǎ gèdì de ànlì huìjí qǐlái, zuòchéng yí fèn bàogào.
    - We gathered cases from various regions and made them into a report.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙) → 㣇

- **pinyin:** huì
- **components:**
  - 彑

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** a pig-like creature with fur, depicted with the head of a pig (彑) on top and fur and hooves on the bottom
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents an animal resembling a pig, with an emphasis on its head and fur. This character makes use of shapes that symbolically convey its features, indicative of its usage to denote gathering, akin to livestock or animals being assembled.
- **examples:**
  - 在古代文献中(在古代文獻中)，㣇字用于描述动物聚集的场景(㣇字用於描述動物聚集的場景)。

    - In ancient texts, the character 㣇 was used to describe scenes of animals gathering.
  - 有人认为㣇代表了一种已灭绝或神话中的动物(有人認為㣇代表了一種已滅絕或神話中的動物)。
    - Yǒurén rènwéi huì dàibiǎo le yī zhǒng yǐ mièjué huò shénhuà zhōng de dòngwù.

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙) → 胃(胃)
- **definition:** stomach (phonetic component here)
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **components:**
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - flesh; meat
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
  - 米

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram (ideogrammic compound)
  - **description:** semantic: 肉 (“flesh”) + pictograph of stomach/food (later simplified to 田, referencing rice 米).

- **examples:**

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙) → 胃(胃) → 肉(肉)
- **definition:** flesh; meat
- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: ribs/section of meat; stylized as 月 when beside a phonetic, and as 𱼀 or 夕 inside some characters.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the cross-section of flesh with ribs or a slice of meat. In compounds it often appears as a component written like 月, or reduced to 𱼀/夕, which can be mistaken for the moon component but actually signals ‘meat/flesh.’
- **examples:**

    - I don't eat pork.
  - 这道菜肉很多(這道菜肉很多)，很鲜嫩(很鮮嫩)。

    - This dish has a lot of meat; it's very tender.
  - 受伤后(受傷後)，他手上的肉裂开了(他手上的肉裂開了)。
    - Shòushāng hòu, tā shǒushang de ròu liè kāi le.
    - After being injured, the flesh on his hand split open.
### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙) → 胃(胃) → 田(田)
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: pictographic representation of a field divided into four sections.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a top-down view of a plot divided by paths or furrows, which naturally came to denote farmland. The square-with-cross motif also lent itself in some ancient contexts to a mask-like image, influencing related graphs.
- **examples:**

    - Chūntiān nóngmín zài tián lǐ chāyāng.

### 词汇(詞彙) → 汇(彙) → 胃(胃) → 米(米)
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: dehusked rice kernels; horizontal stroke as a bamboo sieve; cf. 粟 (with husk); unrelated to 木/禾/来.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts dehusked rice grains being sifted on a bamboo sieve, contrasting with 粟 which shows husked kernels. Despite visual resemblance, its form is not derived from 木, 禾, or 来.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两斤米(我買了兩斤米)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le liǎng jīn mǐ.
    - I bought two jin of rice.
  - 这家店的东北米很香(這家店的東北米很香)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de Dōngběi mǐ hěn xiāng.
    - The Northeastern rice at this shop is very fragrant.
  - 请把米洗一洗(請把米洗一洗)，再煮饭(再煮飯)。
    - Qǐng bǎ mǐ xǐ yì xǐ, zài zhǔ fàn.
    - Please rinse the rice, then cook it.
