## auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
### ruì
---
- **definition:** auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
- **pinyin:** ruì
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 耑(端)
    - zhuān
    - accurate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") + phonetic 耑(端) (zhuān, "accurate") =
    - jade symbolizing luck ->
    - auspicious
  - **interpretation:** The character 瑞(瑞) (ruì, "auspicious") combines 玉(玉) (yù, "jade"), signifying something precious and valuable in Chinese culture, with 耑(端) (zhuān, "accurate") as the phonetic component, suggesting auspiciousness and accuracy or correctness.
- **examples:**
  - 他的名字叫瑞(他的名字叫瑞)。
    - Tā de míngzì jiào ruì.
    - His name is Rui.
  - 天降瑞雪(天降瑞雪)，民心欢喜(民心歡喜)。
    - Tiān jiàng ruìxuě, mínxīn huānxǐ.
    - When the sky sends down auspicious snow, the people's hearts rejoice.
  - 那枚瑞玉象征吉祥(那枚瑞玉象徵吉祥)。
    - Nà méi ruìyù xiàngzhēng jíxiáng.
    - That jade token symbolizes good fortune.
### 瑞(瑞) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Picture of a cong (ritual jade totem) ->
    - three pieces of jade strung together ->
    - current form
  - **interpretation:** The character 玉(yù, "jade") visually resembles a picture of a ritual jade object, emphasizing its cultural and material significance.
- **examples:**
  - 这块玉非常光滑(這塊玉非常光滑)。
    - Zhè kuài yù fēicháng guānghuá.
    - This piece of jade is very smooth.
  - 博物馆展出了精美玉器(博物館展出了精美玉器)，吸引了众多观众(吸引了眾多觀眾)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū le jīngměi yùqì, xīyǐn le zhòngduō guānzhòng.
    - The museum exhibited exquisite jade artifacts, attracting numerous visitors.
### 瑞(瑞) → 耑(耑)
- **definition:** accurate
- **pinyin:** zhuān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the initial leaves and branches of a plant on top, and its roots on the bottom ->
    - signifying ends or extremity
  - **interpretation:** The character 耑(zhuān, "accurate") symbolizes the extremities or endpoints of something, like the roots and tips of a plant, representing precision or specificity at these defined boundaries.
- **examples:**
  - 他的判断耑准确(他的判断端準確)。
    - Tā de pànjué zhuān zhǔnquè.
    - His judgment is accurate.
  - 科学家测定温度(科學家測定溫度)，仪器显示耑数据(儀器顯示端數據)。
    - Kēxuéjiā cèdìng wēndù, yíqì xiǎnshì zhuān shùjù.
    - The scientist measured the temperature, and the instrument displayed accurate data.
