## 
### auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
---
- **pinyin:** auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character 瑞 signifies good fortune or auspiciousness, linked to a positive prediction, often symbolized by jade tokens in ancient culture.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of good luck and favorable omens, making it a popular choice in festivals and celebrations.
- **examples:**
  - 祝你好运(祝你好运)！
    - zhù nǐ hǎo yùn！
    - Wishing you good luck!
  - 在新年期间，瑞兆常被提及(在新年期間，瑞兆常被提及)。
    - zài xīn nián qī jiān, ruì zhào cháng bèi tí jí。
    - During the New Year, auspicious signs are often mentioned.
  - 瑞代表着希望和祝福(瑞代表著希望和祝福)。
    - ruì dài biǎo zhe xī wàng hé zhù fú。
    - Auspiciousness represents hope and blessings.
