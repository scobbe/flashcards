## auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
### ruì
---
- **definition:** auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
- **pinyin:** ruì
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 睿
    - ruì
    - bright
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") + phonetic: 瑞(睿) (ruì, "bright") =
    - jade as a symbol of brightness and good fortune ->
    - auspicious
  - **interpretation:** The character 瑞(rui) combines 玉(玉) (yù, "jade"), symbolizing value and purity, with the original sense of brightness or clarity from the phonetic component. This association captures the idea of jade as a token of good fortune.
- **examples:**
  - 瑞雪兆丰年(瑞雪兆豐年)。
    - Ruì xuě zhào fēng nián.
    - Auspicious snowfall signifies a bountiful year.
  - 李瑞出席晚宴(李瑞出席晚宴)。
    - Lǐ ruì chūxí wǎnyàn.
    - Mr. Li Rui attended the dinner party.
  - 玉器中的瑞玉十分珍贵(玉器中的瑞玉十分珍貴)。
    - Yùqì zhōng de ruì yù shífēn zhēnguì.
    - The jade token among the jade artifacts is extremely precious.
### 瑞(瑞) → 玉(玉)
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cong (琮, a ritual jade totem)
  - **interpretation:** 玉(玉) (yù, "jade") visually represents the concept of jade, either as a cong or three pieces of jade strung together. It is distinct from 王(王) (wáng, "king") thanks to the added dot, ensuring clarity in their usage and meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他收藏了一块古玉(他收藏了一塊古玉)。
    - Tā shōucáng le yī kuài gǔ yù.
    - He collected a piece of antique jade.
  - 晴空万里时(晴空萬里時)，玉石在阳光下闪烁(玉石在陽光下閃爍)。
    - Qíngkōng wànlǐ shí, yù shí zài yángguāng xià shǎnshuò.
    - Under a clear sky, the jade sparkled under the sunlight.
  - 在宫廷中(在宮廷中)，玉制的饰物象征着高贵(玉製的飾物象徵著高貴)。
    - Zài gōngtíng zhōng, yù zhì de shìwù xiàngzhēng zhe gāoguì.
    - In the palace, jade ornaments symbolize nobility.
### 瑞(瑞) → 睿(叡)
- **definition:** bright
- **pinyin:** ruì
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 爰
    - yuán
    - because
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, 'eye') + 爰(爰) (yuán, 'therefore; because') =
    - sharp and discerning eyes ->
    - bright
  - **interpretation:** The character 睿(叡) (ruì, 'bright') combines 目(目) (mù, 'eye') to represent vision, with 爰(爰) (yuán, 'therefore') serving as a phonetic indicator. This implies heightened perception or insight, thus denoting 'bright' in terms of intelligence or wisdom.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by removing 爰(爰) (yuán) and leaving a simplified form with just 目(目) (mù) + 一(一) (yī, 'one') as a representation of clarity or singular focus.
- **examples:**
  - 他有着睿的智慧(他有著睿的智慧)。
    - Tā yǒuzhe ruì de zhìhuì.
    - He possesses bright wisdom.
  - 面对复杂问题(面對複雜問題)，他总能做出睿断的选择(他總能做出睿斷的選擇)。
    - Miànduì fùzá wèntí, tā zǒng néng zuòchū ruì duàn de xuǎnzé.
    - In the face of complex issues, he always makes wise decisions.

### 瑞(瑞) → 睿(叡) → 爰(爰) → 爫(爫)
- **definition:** hand, claw
- **pinyin:** zhǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hand grabbing from above or the side in earlier forms ->
    - Represents a claw or hand
  - **interpretation:** The character 爫(爪) (zhuǎ, 'hand, claw') historically represented a human hand depicted from a side view, grabbing something, although it can extend its meaning to indicate a claw. This visual depiction closely associates with functions performed by a hand or a claw, making the graphical representation intuitive as both human and animal hands or claws engage in similar grasping actions.
- **examples:**
