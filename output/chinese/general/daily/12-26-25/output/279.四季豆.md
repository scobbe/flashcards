## green bean; string bean
### sìjìdòu
---
- **definition:** green bean; string bean
- **pinyin:** sìjìdòu
- **components:**
  - 四
    - sì
    - four
  - 季
    - jì
    - seasons
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 四(四) (sì, "four"), 季(季) (jì, "seasons"), 豆(豆) (dòu, "bean")
  - **interpretation:** 四季豆 (sìjìdòu, "green bean") refers to a type of bean, emphasizing that it is available throughout the 'four seasons,' indicating its common availability and use in various dishes year-round.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃四季豆(我喜歡吃四季豆)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī sìjìdòu.
    - I like to eat green beans.
  - 四季豆营养丰富(四季豆營養豐富)，适合健康饮食(適合健康飲食)。
    - Sìjìdòu yíngyǎng fēngfù, shìhé jiànkāng yǐnshí.
    - Green beans are nutritious and suitable for a healthy diet.
  - 妈妈炒四季豆(媽媽炒四季豆)，加点蒜末(加點蒜末)。
    - Māmā chǎo sìjìdòu, jiā diǎn suàn mò.
    - Mom fries green beans and adds some minced garlic.
### 四季豆(四季豆) → 四(四)
- **definition:** four
- **pinyin:** sì
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 八
    - bā
    - divide
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亖(亖) (sǐ, "four before Western Zhou") was replaced by 四(四) due to confusion with 二(二) (èr, "two") and 三(三) (sān, "three") in Western Zhou; 四 started as 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + 八(八) (bā, "divide") to indicate dispersal of breath ->
    - standard form in Qin; 口 with 八 suggests "divided mouth" originally related to dispersal ->
    - borrowing for 'four'
  - **interpretation:** The character 四 evolved as a solution to potential confusion in writing with 亖. The combination of 口 and 八 originally meant dispersing breath, which later was borrowed to represent 'four', making it an example where a character's meaning shifted over time.
- **examples:**
  - 我有四个苹果(我有四個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ yǒu sì gè píngguǒ.
    - I have four apples.
  - 她有四只小猫(她有四隻小貓)，它们非常可爱(它們非常可愛)。
    - Tā yǒu sì zhī xiǎo māo, tāmen fēicháng kě'ài.
    - She has four little cats, and they are very cute.
  - 桌子上放着四本书(桌子上放著四本書)，它们是新书(它們是新書)。
    - Zhuōzi shàng fàngzhe sì běn shū, tāmen shì xīn shū.
    - There are four books on the table, and they are new.

### 四季豆(四季豆) → 四(四) → 八(八)
- **definition:** divide
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts two bent lines representing division ->
    - borrowed to mean 'eight' due to homonymy
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting the action of dividing, 八(bā, 'eight') was borrowed to represent the number eight because of a phonetic similarity, rendering its original meaning obsolete. The original meaning is preserved in characters like 分(fēn, 'divide') and 別(bié, 'separate').
- **examples:**
  - 他深吸一口八(他深吸一口八)。
    - Tā shēn xī yī kǒu bā.
    - He took a deep breath.
  - 她的八充满温柔(她的八充滿溫柔)，散发着诗意(散發著詩意)。
    - Tā de bā chōngmǎn wēnróu, sànfā zhe shīyì.
    - Her speech is full of tenderness and exudes a poetic charm.
  - 他轻轻讲出一段八(他輕輕講出一段八)，令听者陶醉(令聽者陶醉)。
    - Tā qīngqīng jiǎng chū yī duàn bā, lìng tīngzhě táozuì.
    - He softly delivered a speech that enchanted his listeners.
### 四季豆(四季豆) → 季(季)
- **definition:** seasons
- **pinyin:** jì
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") + phonetic: 子(子) (zǐ) =
    - grain growing in certain time ->
    - season
  - **interpretation:** The character 季(jì, "seasons") combines the semantic element 禾(hé, "grain"), indicating agriculture and hence time divisions, with the phonetic component 子(zǐ), which hints at phonetic approximation. Together, they imply a period or season important to agriculture.
- **examples:**
  - 春季来临时(春季來臨時)，花儿开始盛开(花兒開始盛開)。

  - 每个季节都有不同的风景(每個季節都有不同的風景)。
    - Měi gè jìjié dōu yǒu bùtóng de fēngjǐng.
    - Every season has its own scenery.
  - 夏季炎热(夏季炎熱)，人们纷纷寻找清凉(人們紛紛尋找清涼)。
    - Xiàjì yánrè, rénmen fēnfēn xúnzhǎo qīngliáng.
