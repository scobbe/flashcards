### green bean; string bean
---
- **pinyin:** green bean; string bean
- **components:**
  - 四季
    - sìjì
    - four seasons; seasonality
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean; legume
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 四(四) (sì, 'four') + 季(季) (jì, 'season') + 豆(豆) (dòu, 'bean') — four season bean
  - **interpretation:** The term represents a type of bean that can be grown throughout the seasons. This connection makes sense as these beans are versatile in terms of cultivation time.
- **examples:**
  - 四季豆是我最喜欢的蔬菜之一。(四季豆是我最喜歡的蔬菜之一。)
    - Sìjìdòu shì wǒ zuì xǐhuān de shūcài zhī yī。
    - Green beans are one of my favorite vegetables.
  - 我今天买了些四季豆。(我今天買了些四季豆。)
    - Wǒ jīntiān mǎile xiē sìjìdòu。
    - I bought some green beans today.

  - **description:** 四(四) (sì, "four") + 季(季) (jì, "season") — four seasons
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense as it directly represents the concept of the four distinct periods in a year, reflecting the natural cycle and changes in climate.
- **examples:**

    - Sìjì rú chūn, huā kāi huā luò.
