## green bean; string bean
### sìjìdòu
---
- **definition:** green bean; string bean
- **pinyin:** sìjìdòu
- **components:**
  - 四
    - sì
    - four
  - 季
    - jì
    - season
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 四 (sì, "four"), 季 (jì, "season"), 豆 (dòu, "bean") combine to describe a vegetable available throughout the year.
  - **interpretation:** The term 四季豆 (sìjìdòu) refers to green beans or string beans, emphasizing their availability in all four seasons. This suggests that they can be grown and consumed year-round.
- **examples:**
  - 我午饭(飯)吃了四季豆。
    - Wǒ wǔfàn chīle sìjìdòu.
    - I ate string beans for lunch.
  - 妈妈(媽媽)炒四季豆，加了蒜末。
    - Māmā chǎo sìjìdòu, jiāle suàn mò.
    - Mom stir-fried the string beans with minced garlic.
  - 我们种(們種)了四季豆，享受健康生活。
    - Wǒmen zhòngle sìjìdòu, xiǎngshòu jiànkāng shēnghuó.
    - We grew string beans, enjoying a healthy life.
### 四季豆 → 四
- **definition:** four

- **components:**
