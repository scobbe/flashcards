### hē
---
- **definition:** drink
- **pinyin:** hē
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 曷
    - hé
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") + 曷(曷) (hé, phonetic)
  - **interpretation:** The character 喝(hē, "drink") combines 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth"), representing the semantic element associated with drinking, with 曷(曷) (hé) as the phonetic component to suggest pronunciation. The combination intuitively suggests using the mouth for drinking.
- **examples:**
  - 我喝咖啡(我喝咖啡)。
    - wǒ hē kāfēi
    - I drink coffee.
  - 天气热的时候(天氣熱的時候)，喝一杯冰水(喝一杯冰水)。
    - tiānqì rè de shíhòu, hē yī bēi bīng shuǐ
    - When it's hot, drink a glass of ice water.
  - 运动后(運動後)，我们喝水(我們喝水)。
    - yùndòng hòu, wǒmen hē shuǐ
    - After exercise, we drink water.
