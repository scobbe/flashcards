### Fójiàotú
---
- **definition:** Buddhist
- **pinyin:** Fójiàotú
- **components:**
  - 佛
    - fó
    - Buddha
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teaching; religion
  - 徒
    - tú
    - follower; disciple
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 佛(佛) (fó, "Buddha"), 教(教) (jiào, "teaching" or "religion"), 徒(徒) (tú, "follower" or "disciple")
  - **interpretation:** This word combines 佛(佛) (fó, "Buddha"), 教(教) (jiào, "teaching" or "religion"), and 徒(徒) (tú, "follower" or "disciple"). Together, these characters describe someone who follows the teachings of Buddha, effectively referring to a Buddhist.
- **examples:**
  - 那个佛教徒每天冥想(那個佛教徒每天冥想)。
    - Nàgè fójiàotú měitiān míngxiǎng.
    - That Buddhist meditates every day.
  - 一些佛教徒信仰因果法则(一些佛教徒信仰因果法則)，追求内心平静(追求內心平靜)。
    - Yīxiē fójiàotú xìnyǎng yīnguǒ fǎzé, zhuīqiú nèixīn píngjìng.
    - Some Buddhists believe in the law of cause and effect, seeking inner peace.
  - 年长的佛教徒在庙会上布施(年長的佛教徒在廟會上布施)，启发信众心灵(啟發信眾心靈)。
    - Niánzhǎng de fójiàotú zài miàohuì shàng bùshī, qǐfā xìnzhòng xīnlíng.
    - The elderly Buddhist gives alms at the temple fair, inspiring the faithful.
