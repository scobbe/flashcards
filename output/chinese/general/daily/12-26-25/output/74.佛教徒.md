    - fójiào
    - Buddhism; religious study; doctrine
  - 徒
    - tú
    - follower; apprentice; disciple
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 佛教(佛教) (fójiào, "Buddhism") + 徒(徒) (tú, "follower") — Buddhism follower
  - **interpretation:** 佛教徒 refers to a follower of Buddhism. This combination makes sense as it directly describes a person who follows the teachings of Buddhism.
- **examples:**

    - I am a Buddhist.
  - 佛教徒常常参加禅修(佛教徒常常參加禪修)。
    - Fójiàotú chángcháng cānjiā chánxiū.
    - Buddhists often participate in meditation retreats.
