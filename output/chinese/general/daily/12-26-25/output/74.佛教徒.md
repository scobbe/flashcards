### Fójiàotú
---
- **definition:** Buddhist
- **pinyin:** Fójiàotú
- **components:**
  - 佛
    - Fó
    - Buddha
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach; religion
  - 徒
    - tú
    - follower; disciple
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 佛(佛) (Fó, "Buddha") + 教(教) (jiào, "teach; religion") + 徒(徒) (tú, "follower; disciple")
  - **interpretation:** The word 佛教徒 refers to someone who follows the teachings of 佛(佛) (Fó, "Buddha") within the framework of 教(教) (jiào, "religion"), emphasizing their role as a 徒(徒) (tú, "follower") of Buddhism. - **examples:**
