## 人民(人民)
---
## 
### the people
---
- **pinyin:** the people
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person; people; human; man
  - 民
    - mín
    - folk; people; subjects; citizens
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人(人) (rén, 'person') + 民(民) (mín, 'folk') — person folk
- **examples:**
  - 人民的力量很强大(人民的力量很強大)。
    - rénmín de lìliàng hěn qiángdà。
    - The power of the people is very strong.
  - 我们应该为人民服务(我們應該為人民服務)。
    - wǒmen yīnggāi wèi rénmín fúwù。
    - We should serve the people.
### 人民(人民) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person; people; human; man
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a person standing, emphasizing human form. This direct depiction makes it easy to associate the character with the meaning of a 'person' or 'human'.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个好人(他是一個好人)。
    - tā shì yī gè hǎo rén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 人们都知道这件事(人們都知道這件事)。
    - rén men dōu zhī dào zhè jiàn shì.
    - People all know about this matter.
### 人民(人民) → 民(民)
- **definition:** folk; people; subjects; citizens
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of 'the people'; claimed to depict an eye pierced by a dagger.
  - **interpretation:** The imagery evokes vulnerability, reflecting the plight and struggles of people.
- **examples:**
  - 这个地区的民众很团结(這個地區的民眾很團結)。
    - Zhège dìqū de mínzhòng hěn tuánjié.
    - The people in this area are very united.
  - 政府听取了民意(政府聽取了民意)。
    - Zhèngfǔ tīngqǔle mínyì.
    - The government listened to the people's opinions.
  - 许多民众表达了不满(許多民眾表達了不滿)。
    - Xǔduō mínzhòng biǎodále bùmǎn.
    - Many citizens expressed their dissatisfaction.
