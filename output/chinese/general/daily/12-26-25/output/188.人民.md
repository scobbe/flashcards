## the people
### rénmín
---
- **definition:** the people
- **pinyin:** rénmín
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person; people; human
  - 民
    - mín
    - people; citizens; nationality
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 人(人) (rén, "person") + 民(民) (mín, "people") =
    - "the people"
  - **interpretation:** This term combines 人(人) (rén, "person") and 民(民) (mín, "people") to suggest a collective of individuals, representing the general public or the masses.
- **examples:**
  - 人民享有和平生活(人民享有和平生活)。
    - Rénmín xiǎngyǒu hépíng shēnghuó.
    - The people enjoy a peaceful life.
  - 政府为人民服务(政府為人民服務)，努力促进社会发展(努力促進社會發展)。
    - Zhèngfǔ wèi rénmín fúwù, nǔlì cùjìn shèhuì fāzhǎn.
    - The government serves the people and strives to promote social development.
### 人民(人民) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person; people; human
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts side view of a standing man
  - **interpretation:** The character 人(人) (rén, "person") visually represents a human figure standing upright, highlighting key anatomical features like an arm and a leg. This pictogram captures the essence of a person in a simple, efficient form.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个好人(他是一個好人)。
    - Tā shì yīgè hǎorén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 很多人喜欢运动(很多人喜歡運動)，也喜欢音乐(也喜歡音樂)。
    - Hěnduō rén xǐhuān yùndòng, yě xǐhuān yīnyuè.
    - Many people like sports, and they also like music.
  - 人多力量大(人多力量大)。
    - Rénduō lìliàng dà.
    - There is strength in numbers.
### 人民(人民) → 民(民)
- **definition:** people; citizens; nationality
- **pinyin:** mín
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted an eye pierced by a dagger (claimed) ->
    - later borrowed for 民 (mín, 'the people')
  - **interpretation:** The character 民(mín) primarily serves as a loan character, having lost its original pictorial depiction possibly related to an eye and a dagger. Its current meaning as 'the people' is a result of its phonetic borrowing and widespread use.
- **examples:**
  - 民众拥有参与决策的权利(民眾擁有參與決策的權利)。
    - Mín zhòng yǒngyǒu cānyù juécè de quánlì.
    - The people have the right to participate in decision-making.
  - 国家依靠民意趋向稳定(國家依靠民意趨向穩定)，推动社会进步(推動社會進步)。
    - Guójiā yīkào mínyì qūxiàng wěndìng, tuīdòng shèhuì jìnbù.
    - The country relies on the people's opinions for stability, promoting social progress.
  - 选民积极行使权力(選民積極行使權力)，维护国家安全(維護國家安全)。
    - Xuǎn mín jījí xíngshǐ quánlì, wéihù guójiā ānquán.
    - Voters actively exercise their rights, safeguarding national security.
