## true; real
### zhēn
---
- **definition:** true; real
- **pinyin:** zhēn
- **components:**
  - 匕
    - bǐ
    - spoon
  - 直
    - zhí
    - straight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匕(匕) (bǐ, "spoon") + phonetic: 直(直) (zhí, "straight") =
    - spoon combined with straight line ->
    - true meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 真(真) (zhēn, "true") combines the component 匕(匕) (bǐ, "spoon") with the phonetic component 直(直) (zhí, "straight") to convey the idea of something being 'straightforward' or 'true.'
- **examples:**
  - 他的笑容真迷人(他的笑容真迷人)。
    - Tā de xiàoróng zhēn mírén.
    - His smile is truly enchanting.
  - 这部电影情节扣人心弦(這部電影情節扣人心弦)，真让人印象深刻(真讓人印象深刻)。
    - Zhè bù diànyǐng qíngjié kòu rén xīnxián, zhēn ràng rén yìnxiàng shēnkè.
    - The plot of this movie is captivating, truly leaving a deep impression.
  - 这件首饰看起来真精致(這件首飾看起來真精緻)。
    - Zhè jiàn shǒushì kàn qǐlái zhēn jīngzhì.
    - This piece of jewelry appears truly exquisite.
### 真(真) → 匕(匕)
- **definition:** spoon
- **pinyin:** bǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a spoon
  - **interpretation:** The character 匕(bǐ, "spoon") directly represents the shape of a spoon, illustrating its use as an eating utensil. While its form suggests an object for carrying or scooping, it is unrelated to similar shapes in other characters, emphasizing its unique depiction.
- **examples:**
  - 他用匕搅拌汤羹(他用匕攪拌湯羹)。
    - Tā yòng bǐ jiǎobàn tāng gēng.
    - He stirs the soup with the spoon.
  - 这把匕精致美观(這把匕精緻美觀)，实用耐用(實用耐用)。
    - Zhè bǎ bǐ jīngzhì měiguān, shíyòng nàiyòng.
    - This spoon is exquisitely elegant and practical.
  - 博物馆展出一把古代的匕(博物館展出一把古代的匕)，引人深思(引人深思)。
    - Bówùguǎn zhǎnchū yī bǎ gǔdài de bǐ, yǐn rén shēnsī.
    - The museum displays an ancient spoon that provokes contemplation.
### 真(真) → 直(直)
- **definition:** straight
- **pinyin:** zhí
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 丨
    - gǔn
    - vertical stroke
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, "eye") + 丨(丨) ("vertical stroke") ->
    - vertical stroke above the eye; looking straight ahead ->
    - black dot inside became horizontal stroke forming 十 ->
    - line on side stretches and appears under 目 ->
    - final form 直
  - **interpretation:** The character combines an eye with a vertical stroke, illustrating the idea of looking directly or straight ahead. Over time, the addition of a horizontal stroke reinforced the notion of straightforwardness.
- **examples:**
  - 他直视我的眼睛(他直視我的眼睛)，表达真诚(表達真誠)。
    - tā zhíshì wǒ de yǎnjīng, biǎodá zhēnchéng.
    - He looked straight into my eyes, expressing sincerity.
  - 他的笔迹很直(他的筆跡很直)。
    - tā de bǐjì hěn zhí.
    - His handwriting is very straight.
### 真(真) → 直(直) → 目(目)
- **definition:** eye
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(目) (mù, "eye") represents a human eye but is now commonly depicted rotated by 90 degrees. It serves as the basis for several other characters related to vision or perception.
- **examples:**
  - 医生检查我的目(醫生檢查我的目)，确认没有异常(確認沒有異常)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchá wǒ de mù, quèrèn méiyǒu yìcháng.
    - The doctor examined my eye and confirmed there was nothing abnormal.
  - 他左目受伤(他左目受傷)，依旧炯炯有神(依舊炯炯有神)。
    - Tā zuǒ mù shòushāng, yījiù jiǒngjiǒng yǒu shén.
    - His left eye was injured, yet it still shone with a determined spark.
  - 那只猫的目似乎闪烁智慧(那隻貓的目似乎閃爍智慧)。
    - Nà zhī māo de mù sìhū shǎnshuò zhìhuì.
    - The cat's eye seemed to shimmer with wisdom.
### 真(真) → 直(直) → 丨(丨)
- **definition:** vertical stroke
- **pinyin:** gǔn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a vertical wooden stick
  - **interpretation:** Initially representing a vertical stick, 丨(gǔn) served as a simple pictographic element symbolizing straightforwardness or authority through its verticality.
- **examples:**
  - 书法中的丨承载着传统美感(書法中的丨承載著傳統美感)。
    - Shūfǎ zhōng de gǔn chéngzài zhe chuántǒng měigǎn.
    - The vertical stroke in calligraphy carries a sense of traditional beauty.
  - 作文时注意笔画顺序(作文時注意筆畫順序)，尤其是丨必须刚劲有力(尤其是丨必須剛勁有力)。
    - Zuòwén shí zhùyì bǐhuà shùnxù, yóuqí shì gǔn bìxū gāngjìng yǒulì.
    - When writing, pay attention to the order of strokes, especially the vertical stroke must be strong and forceful.
