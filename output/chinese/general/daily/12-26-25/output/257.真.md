### true; real
---
- **pinyin:** true; real
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character is composed of the radical 亻 (person) and the component 真 (truth), emphasizing the idea of a real or true person.
  - **interpretation:** The character conveys authenticity by combining 'person' and 'truth', suggesting a genuine or real individual.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个真正的朋友。(他是一個真正的朋友。)
    - tā shì yī gè zhēn zhèng de péng yǒu.
    - He is a true friend.
  - 我想要真相。(我想要真相。)
    - wǒ xiǎng yào zhēn xiàng.
    - I want the truth.
  - 这件事情很真。(這件事情很真。)
    - zhè jiàn shì qíng hěn zhēn.
    - This matter is very real.
### 真(真) → 亻(亻)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** A stylization of 人, which represents a person or human figure.
  - **interpretation:** This character represents a simplified form of a person, emphasizing humanity and individual identity.
- **examples:**
  - 我是一个人(我是一个人)。
    - Wǒ shì yī gè rén.
    - I am a person.
  - 人的权利很重要(人的權利很重要)。
    - Rén de quánlì hěn zhòngyào.
    - The rights of a person are very important.
