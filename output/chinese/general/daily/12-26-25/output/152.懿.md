### virtuous
---
- **pinyin:** virtuous
- **components:**
  - 美
    - měi
    - beautiful
  - 意
    - yì
    - meaning
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the meaning of ‘beauty or virtue’ from the semantic component 美 (美, měi, 'beautiful') and phonetic component 意 (意, yì, 'meaning')
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of beauty intertwined with meaning, suggesting a virtuous quality.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位德才兼备的优秀年轻人(她是一位德才兼備的優秀年輕人)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi dé cái jiān bèi de yōuxiù niánqīngrén.
    - She is an excellent young person with both virtue and talent.
  - 古代帝王都提倡懿德(古代帝王都提倡懿德)。
    - Gǔdài dìwáng dōu tíchàng yì dé.
    - Ancient emperors promoted virtuous conduct.
### 懿(懿) → 美(美)
- **definition:** beautiful
- **pinyin:** měi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** The character represents a combination of components that symbolize beauty and fine qualities.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively conveys the sense of beauty, as it captures the essence of pleasing and fine qualities.
- **examples:**
  - 她很美(她很美)。
    - Tā hěn měi.
    - She is very beautiful.
  - 这个风景美(這個風景美)。
    - Zhège fēngjǐng měi.
    - This scenery is beautiful.
### 懿(懿) → 意(意)
- **definition:** meaning
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 立
    - lì
    - to stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is a compound of the semantic component 心(心) (xīn, 'heart') indicating thought or emotion and the phonetic component 立(立) (lì, 'to stand'). This suggests that meaning arises from the heart or feeling.
  - **interpretation:** This makes intuitive sense as meaning is often associated with one's feelings and thoughts.
- **examples:**
  - 我不明白这个词的意思(我不明白這個詞的意思)。
    - Wǒ bù míngbái zhège cí de yìsi.
    - I don't understand the meaning of this word.
  - 你的意见很重要(你的意見很重要)。
    - Nǐ de yìjiàn hěn zhòngyào.
    - Your opinion is very important.
### 懿(懿) → 意(意) → 心(心)
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a highly stylized heart.
  - **interpretation:** The shape of the character evokes the physical form of a heart, symbolizing emotions and feelings.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱我的心。(我愛我的心。)
    - Wǒ ài wǒ de xīn.
    - I love my heart.
  - 他的心情很好。(他的心情很好。)
    - Tā de xīnqíng hěn hǎo.
    - His mood is very good.
### 懿(懿) → 意(意) → 立(立)
- **definition:** to stand
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a figure standing upright, representing the concept of standing or being erect.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually captures the act of standing upright, embodying stability and presence.
- **examples:**
  - 他正在立于高峰(他正在立於高峰)，看风景(看風景)。
    - Tā zhèngzài lì yú gāofēng, kàn fēngjǐng.
    - He is standing on the summit, enjoying the scenery.
  - 立刻回来(立刻回來)，我有事情(我有事情)要告诉你(要告訴你)。
    - Lìkè huílái, wǒ yǒu shìqíng yào gàosù nǐ.
    - Come back immediately, I have something to tell you.
