## virtuous
### yì
---
- **definition:** virtuous
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 異
    - yì
    - different
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 心(心) (xīn, "heart") + phonetic: 異(異) (yì, "different") =
    - impressive heart ->
    - virtuous
  - **interpretation:** The character 懿(yì, "virtuous") combines 心(心) (xīn, "heart") to signify emotions or intentions and 異(異) (yì, "different") which provides the sound, suggesting a heart that stands out impressively in virtue.
- **examples:**
  - 他以懿德感化人心(他以懿德感化人心)。
    - Tā yǐ yìdé gǎnhuà rénxīn。
    - He influences people's hearts with his virtuous character.
  - 她追求学问(她追求學問)，怀抱懿志(懷抱懿志)。
    - Tā zhuīqiú xuéwèn，huáibào yìzhì。
    - She pursues knowledge and cherishes her virtuous aspirations.
  - 古人以懿德树立榜样(古人以懿德樹立榜樣)，因此后人皆仰之(因此後人皆仰之)。
    - Gǔrén yǐ yìdé shùlì bǎngyàng，yīncǐ hòurén jiē yǎng zhī。
    - Ancient people established a role model with virtuous character, and thus later generations looked up to them.
### 懿(懿) → 心(心)
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the shape of a heart organ with three lobes
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, 'heart') visually resembles a heart organ with its three internal sections, emphasizing its role as a core or central part of the human body.
- **examples:**
  - 我的心充满了爱(我的心充滿了愛)。
    - Wǒ de xīn chōngmǎnle ài.
    - My heart is filled with love.
  - 他把心放在艺术上(他把心放在藝術上)，追求完美(追求完美)。
    - Tā bǎ xīn fàng zài yìshù shàng, zhuīqiú wánměi.
    - He devotes his heart to art and pursues perfection.
### 懿(懿) → 異(異)
- **definition:** different
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
  - 共
    - gòng
    - together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 田(田) (tián, "field") + 共(共) (gòng, "together") =
    - two different fields ploughed together ->
    - different
  - **interpretation:** The character 異(yì) represents the idea of combining two distinct fields, highlighting the concept of being different or divergent.
- **examples:**
  - 每个人的兴趣各异(每個人的興趣各異)。
    - měi gèrén de xìngqù gè yì.
    - Everyone's interests are different.
  - 她的看法与别人异(她的看法與別人異)，观点独特且新颖(觀點獨特且新穎)。
    - tā de kànfǎ yǔ biérén yì, guāndiǎn dútè qiě xīnyǐng.
    - Her views are different from others', and her perspectives are unique and innovative.
### 懿(懿) → 異(異) → 田(田)
- **definition:** field
- **pinyin:** tián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a field
  - **interpretation:** The character 田(tián, 'field') is a pictogram that represents the layout of a field, showing a square with lines inside to indicate plots of land.
- **examples:**
  - 那块田肥沃(那塊田肥沃)。
    - Nà kuài tián féiwò.
    - That field is fertile.
  - 我们在田边休息(我們在田邊休息)，享受大自然的美景(享受大自然的美景)。
    - Wǒmen zài tián biān xiūxí, xiǎngshòu dà zìrán de měijǐng.
    - We rest by the field and enjoy the beauty of nature.
### 懿(懿) → 異(異) → 共(共)
- **definition:** together
- **pinyin:** gòng
- **components:**
  - 龷
    - gàn
    - dry
  - 廾
    - gǒng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

    - together

- **examples:**

  - 朋友相聚时(朋友相聚時)，共叙往昔(共敘往昔)。
    - Péngyǒu xiāngjù shí, gòng xù wǎngxī.
    - When friends gather, they reminisce about the past together.
### 懿(懿) → 異(異) → 共(共) → 龷(龷)
- **definition:** dry
- **pinyin:** gàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ⿻巛(巛) (chuān, "streams") + 一(一) (yī, "one/line") =
    - depiction of floodwaters ->
    - later became associated with dryness
  - **interpretation:** The character 龷 originally depicted floodwaters with the components 巛(巛) (chuān, "streams") and 一(一) (yī, "line"), suggesting a convergence of streams. Over time, it evolved to represent the concept of dryness, despite its original association with water, possibly as a contrast to the idea of flood.
- **examples:**
  - 天气十分龷(天氣十分龷)。
    - Tiānqì shífēn gàn.
    - The weather is extremely dry.
  - 夏日炎炎(夏日炎炎)，河床龷(河床龷)。
    - Xiàrì yányán, hé chuáng gàn.
    - In the scorching summer, the riverbed is dry.
  - 旧书页老旧(舊書頁老舊)，岁月使其龷(歲月使其龷)。
    - Jiù shūyè lǎojiù, suìyuè shǐ qí gàn.
    - Old pages are worn, as time makes them dry.
### 懿(懿) → 異(異) → 共(共) → 廾(廾)
- **definition:** hands joined
- **pinyin:** gǒng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𠂇 ("left hand") + 又 ("right hand") =
    - both hands ->
    - two hands
  - **interpretation:** The character 廾(gǒng) ingeniously combines representations of a left and a right hand to depict the concept of 'two hands', emphasizing cooperative or joined action.
- **examples:**
  - 他用廾托起书卷(他用廾托起書卷)，献上敬意(獻上敬意)。
    - Tā yòng gǒng tuōqǐ shūjuàn, xiànshàng jìngyì.
    - He lifts the scroll with both hands, offering his respect.
  - 孩子们用廾接住飞来的纸飞机(孩子們用廾接住飛來的紙飛機)，笑声充满校园(笑聲充滿校園)。
    - Háizimen yòng gǒng jiēzhù fēilái de zhǐ fēijī, xiàoshēng chōngmǎn xiàoyuán.
    - The children caught the flying paper airplane with both hands, and laughter filled the schoolyard.
