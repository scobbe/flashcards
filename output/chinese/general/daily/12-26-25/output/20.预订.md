## reserve; book
### yùdìng
---
- **traditional:** 預訂
- **definition:** reserve; book
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance
  - 订(訂)
    - dìng
    - order; agree; conclude
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 预(預) (yù, "in advance") + 订(訂) (dìng, "order") =
    - to order in advance, hence to reserve or book
  - **interpretation:** The word 预(預)订(訂) (yùdìng, "reserve; book") combines the idea of taking action in advance with the act of ordering. This concept is applied when setting aside services or items before they are needed, such as booking a hotel room or reserving a table at a restaurant.
  - **simplification:** 预订 is the simplified form of 預訂.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )预(預)订(訂)了( )酒( )店( )。
    - Wǒ yùdìngle jiǔdiàn.

    - Tā yùdìngle cānwèi, dǎsuàn yāoqǐng péngyǒu yīqǐ yòngcān.
    - He reserved a table, planning to invite friends to dine together.
  - 我( )们(們)预(預)订(訂)了( )演( )唱( )会(會)门(門)票( )，期待美好的夜晚。

    - We booked concert tickets, looking forward to a wonderful evening.
