## 医生(醫生)
---
## 
### doctor
---
- **pinyin:** doctor
- **components:**
  - 医(醫)
    - yī
    - medicine; doctor; medical; to cure
  - 生
    - shēng
    - born; to be born; to generate; life
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") + 生(shēng, "born; to generate") — medicine born
  - **interpretation:** The term combines ‘medicine’ with the concept of ‘born’ or ‘to generate.’ This makes sense as a doctor is someone who practices medicine and helps to bring about health and life.
- **examples:**
  - 我去找医生(我去找醫生)，看看我的健康状况。
    - Wǒ qù zhǎo yīshēng, kàn kàn wǒ de jiànkāng zhuàngkuàng.
    - I am going to see a doctor to check my health condition.
  - 这位医生(這位醫生)非常有经验。
    - Zhè wèi yīshēng fēicháng yǒu jīngyàn.
    - This doctor is very experienced.
### 医生(醫生) → 医(醫)
- **definition:** medicine; doctor; medical; to cure
- **pinyin:** yī
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xǐ
    - to hide; to store
  - 矢
    - shǐ
    - arrow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Combines 匸 (hides/stores) and 矢 (arrow) to suggest the idea of using arrows (as in needles) hidden away to provide healing or cure.
  - **interpretation:** The concept intuitively connects the hidden tools of healing, such as needles, with the act of providing medical care.
  - **simplification:** The character simplified to 医 by reducing the number of strokes and substituting the more complex components with a simpler version, simplifying the form of 匸 while retaining meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 医生(醫生)会治疗病人(會治療病人)。
    - Yīshēng huì zhìliáo bìngrén.
    - Doctors can treat patients.
  - 我想成为医生(我想成為醫生)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng chéngwéi yīshēng.
    - I want to become a doctor.
  - 她对医学(她對醫學)很感兴趣(很感興趣)。
    - Tā duì yīxué hěn gǎn xìngqù.
    - She is very interested in medicine.
### 医生(醫生) → 医(醫) → 匸(匸)
- **definition:** to hide; to store
- **pinyin:** xǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a container or something hidden away.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a square or closed box, symbolizing the act of hiding or storing something away.
- **examples:**
  - 这个箱子用来藏东西(這個箱子用來藏東西)。
    - Zhège xiāngzi yòng lái cáng dōngxi.
    - This box is used to hide things.
  - 他把旧书藏在柜子里(他把舊書藏在櫃子裡)。
    - Tā bǎ jiù shū cáng zài guìzi lǐ.
    - He hid the old books in the cabinet.
### 医生(醫生) → 医(醫) → 矢(矢)
- **definition:** arrow
- **pinyin:** shǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arrow with a pointed tip, representing the concept of an arrow.
  - **interpretation:** The shape clearly communicates the idea of an arrow, a tool used for shooting.
- **examples:**
  - 他射箭很准(他射箭很準)，使用的矢很利(使用的矢很利)。
    - tā shèjiàn hěn zhǔn, shǐyòng de shǐ hěn lì.
    - He shoots arrows accurately, using sharp arrows.
  - 这支矢是古代的武器(這支矢是古代的武器)。
    - zhè zhī shǐ shì gǔdài de wǔqì.
    - This arrow is an ancient weapon.
  - 战争中士兵使用矢(戰爭中士兵使用矢)。
    - zhànzhēng zhōng shìbīng shǐyòng shǐ.
    - Soldiers used arrows in war.
### 医生(醫生) → 生(生)
- **definition:** born; to be born; to generate; life
- **pinyin:** shēng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of life or birth, depicting a sprout emerging from the ground.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively symbolizes the emergence of new life, as it represents a plant coming to life from the earth.
- **examples:**
  - 生命(生命)，是宝贵的。
    - shēngmìng, shì bǎoguì de.
    - Life is precious.
  - 他的生日(他的生日)是四月一日。
    - tā de shēngrì shì sì yuè yī rì.
    - His birthday is April 1st.
