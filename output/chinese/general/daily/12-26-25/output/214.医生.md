## doctor
### yīshēng
---
- **definition:** doctor
- **pinyin:** yīshēng
- **components:**
  - 医(醫)
    - yī
    - medicine
  - 生
    - shēng
    - student; life; to be born
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") + 生(生) (shēng, "student; life; to be born") combines to describe someone who practices medicine or heals others.
  - **interpretation:** A doctor is someone who studies and practices medicine, focusing on healing and maintaining health, thus 'a student/life of medicine'.
- **examples:**
  - 医生治病(醫生治病)，救死扶伤(救死扶傷)。

    - Tā mèngxiǎng chéngwéi yī míng yīshēng.
    - He dreams of becoming a doctor.
