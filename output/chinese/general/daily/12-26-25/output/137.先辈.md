## 先辈(先輩)
---
## 
### forebears
---
- **pinyin:** forebears
- **components:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - first; prior; before; early
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation; lineage; class; group
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 先(先) (xiān, "first; prior") + 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation; lineage") — first generation
  - **interpretation:** This term refers to those who came before us in a family or lineage. It emphasizes the respect and acknowledgment of one's ancestors and their contributions.
- **examples:**
  - 我常常想起我的先辈(我常常想起我的先輩)。
    - wǒ chángcháng xiǎngqǐ wǒ de xiānbèi.
    - I often think of my forebears.
  - 我们应该尊重先辈(我們應該尊重先輩)。
    - wǒmen yīnggāi zūnzhòng xiānbèi.
    - We should respect our forebears.
### 先辈(先輩) → 先(先)
- **definition:** first; prior; before; early
- **pinyin:** xiān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character consists of the semantic component '先' indicating a sense of 'before' and the phonetic component that suggests its pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively represents the concept of 'before', often used in contexts indicating priority or precedence. The phonetic aspect lends it sound, while the meaning aligns with priority.
- **examples:**
  - 他是第一(他是第一)。
    - tā shì dì yī.
    - He is the first.
  - 你先来(你先來)。
    - nǐ xiān lái.
    - You come first.
  - 先吃饭(先吃飯)，再讨论(再討論)。
    - xiān chī fàn, zài tǎo lùn.
    - Eat first, then discuss.
### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩)
- **definition:** generation; lineage; class; group
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **components:**
  - 辶
    - zǒu
    - walking
  - 輩
    - bèi
    - group, generation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the semantic component 辶 (walking) and the phonetic component 輩 (to take a car), which indicates a group or generation of people traveling together.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of a group or generation is intuitively linked to people traveling together or 'walking' as a collective, conveying a sense of lineage or class.
  - **simplification:** 輩 is simplified to 辈 by replacing the component 車 with 车.
- **examples:**
  - 我们是同一个辈分(我們是同一個輩分)，有很多共同点(有很多共同點)！
    - Wǒmen shì tóng yīgè bèifèn, yǒu hěnduō gòngtóngdiǎn！
    - We are of the same generation and have many common points!
  - 这一辈的年轻人(這一輩的年輕人)都很有才华(都很有才華)。
    - Zhè yī bèi de niánqīngrén dōu hěn yǒu cáihuá！
    - Young people of this generation are very talented.
  - 家族的辈分(家族的輩分)十分重要(十分重要)。
    - Jiāzú de bèifèn shífēn zhòngyào！
    - The lineage of the family is very important.

### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩) → 輩(輩)
- **definition:** group, generation
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **components:**
  - 車
    - chē
    - vehicle
  - 非
    - fēi
    - not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 車 (chē, 'vehicle') with the phonetic component 非 (fēi), creating a character that evokes a sense of a group or lineage, like vehicles in a convoy.
  - **interpretation:** The idea of a convoy of vehicles suggests a collective or group, aligning with the meaning of generation or cohort.
  - **simplification:** 輩 was simplified to 辈 by replacing 車 with 车, which is a standard simplification for easier writing.
- **examples:**
  - 这一辈(這一輩)的人很友好。
    - zhè yī bèi de rén hěn yǒuhǎo.
    - This generation of people is very friendly.
  - 老人们对年轻辈(對年輕輩)的期望很高。
    - lǎorén men duì niánqīng bèi de qīwàng hěn gāo.
    - The elders have high expectations for the younger generation.

  - 我们坐车去旅行(我們坐車去旅行)。

### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩) → 輩(輩) → 非(非)
- **definition:** not
- **pinyin:** fēi
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** 非(非) (fēi, "not") signifies negation or opposition, visually representing the concept of 'not being' or 'the opposite of being.'
  - **interpretation:** This character suggests negation or the idea of not belonging to a certain category, which is straightforwardly intuitive.
- **examples:**
