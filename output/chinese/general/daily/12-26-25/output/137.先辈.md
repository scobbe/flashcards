## forebears
### xiānbèi
---
- **traditional:** 先輩
- **definition:** forebears
- **pinyin:** xiānbèi
- **components:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - first; previous
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation; lifetime
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 先 (xiān, "first; previous") + 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation; lifetime")
  - **interpretation:** The term 先( )輩(辈) (xiānbèi, "forebears") combines 先 (xiān, "first; previous") to indicate those who have come before or earlier, with 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation; lifetime") referring to people from an earlier generation. Together, it denotes ancestors or forebears, emphasizing respect for those who preceded us.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )们(們)缅(緬)怀(懷)先( )辈(輩)。
    - Wǒmen miǎnhuái xiānbèi.
    - We remember our forebears.
  - 我( )们(們)应(應)当(當)追( )随(隨)先( )辈(輩)，传(傳)承( )优(優)秀( )传(傳)统(統)。
    - Wǒmen yīngdāng zhuīsuí xiānbèi, chuánchéng yōuxiù chuántǒng.
    - We should follow our forebears and pass on the excellent traditions.
  - 古( )代( )文( )献(獻)记(記)载(載)了( )先( )辈(輩)的( )智( )慧( )，激( )励(勵)后(後)人( )。
    - Gǔdài wénxiàn jìzǎile xiānbèi de zhìhuì, jīlì hòurén.
    - Ancient texts recorded the wisdom of our forebears, inspiring later generations.
