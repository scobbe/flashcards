## forebears
### xiānbèi
---
- **definition:** forebears
- **pinyin:** xiānbèi
- **components:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - first; previous
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation; lifetime
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 先(先) (xiān, "first; previous") + 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation; lifetime")
  - **interpretation:** The term 先輩(先辈) (xiānbèi, "forebears") combines 先(先) (xiān, "first; previous") to indicate those who have come before or earlier, with 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation; lifetime") referring to people from an earlier generation. Together, it denotes ancestors or forebears, emphasizing respect for those who preceded us.
- **examples:**
  - 我们缅怀先辈(我們緬懷先輩)。
    - Wǒmen miǎnhuái xiānbèi.
    - We remember our forebears.
  - 我们应当追随先辈(我們應當追隨先輩)，传承优秀传统(傳承優秀傳統)。
    - Wǒmen yīngdāng zhuīsuí xiānbèi, chuánchéng yōuxiù chuántǒng.
    - We should follow our forebears and pass on the excellent traditions.
  - 古代文献记载了先辈的智慧(古代文獻記載了先輩的智慧)，激励后人(激勵後人)。
    - Gǔdài wénxiàn jìzǎile xiānbèi de zhìhuì, jīlì hòurén.
    - Ancient texts recorded the wisdom of our forebears, inspiring later generations.
### 先辈(先輩) → 先(先)
- **definition:** first; previous
- **pinyin:** xiān
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") + 儿(儿) (ér, "person") =
    - person with feet moving forward ->
    - first; previous
  - **interpretation:** The character 先(xiān) combines the image of a foot 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") and a person 儿(儿) (ér, "person") to suggest the idea of leading or going ahead, translating to 'first' or 'previous'. It visually represents someone with feet moving forward, emphasizing the concept of precedence or priority.
- **examples:**
  - 先喝汤(先喝湯)，再吃饭(再吃飯)。
    - Xiān hē tāng, zài chī fàn.
    - First, drink the soup, then eat the meal.
  - 你先问问老师(你先問問老師)，再做决定(再做決定)。
    - Nǐ xiān wènwèn lǎoshī, zài zuò juédìng.
    - Ask the teacher first, then make a decision.

### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩)
- **definition:** generation; lifetime
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **components:**
  - 车(車)
    - chē

    - not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 車(車) (chē, "car") + 非(非) (fēi) =
    - vehicle metaphorical meaning plus sound ->
    - generation, lifetime
  - **interpretation:** The character 辈(輩) combines 車(車) (chē, "car") which represents progression over time or distance, with 非(非) (fēi) providing the phonetic component, to convey the idea of a generation or lifetime as a progression through time.
  - **simplification:** 车(車) (chē, "car") simplified to 车(车), retaining the original sound and meaning without affecting the interpretation.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我们这一辈的骄傲(他是我們這一輩的驕傲)。

    - Lǎorén gàosù wǒ, lìshǐ shì yóu bùtóng bèi zhùjiù de.
