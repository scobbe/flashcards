## forebears
### xiānbèi
---
- **definition:** forebears
- **pinyin:** xiānbèi
- **components:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - before; first
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 先(先) (xiān, "before; first") + 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation") =
    - people of a previous generation ->
    - forebears
  - **interpretation:** The word 先辈(先輩) (xiānbèi, "forebears") refers to people from an earlier generation, emphasizing their precedence in time. The character 先(先) (xiān, "before; first") suggests priority or precedence, while 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation") indicates familial or generational lineage.
  - **simplification:** 辈 is the simplified form of 輩.
- **examples:**
  - 我们应当学习先辈(我們應當學習先輩)，传承其精神(傳承其精神)。
    - Wǒmen yīngdāng xuéxí xiānbèi, chuánchéng qí jīngshén.
    - We should learn from our forebears and pass on their spirit.
  - 先辈留下的足迹(先輩留下的足跡)，激励着我们(激勵著我們)。
    - Xiānbèi liú xià de zújì, jīlì zhe wǒmen.
    - The traces left by our forebears inspire us.
### 先辈(先輩) → 先(先)
- **definition:** before; first
- **pinyin:** xiān
- **components:**
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
  - 儿
    - ér
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") + 儿(儿) (ér, "person") =
    - to go forward ->
    - before/first
  - **interpretation:** The character 先(先) (xiān, "before; first") combines 止(止) (zhǐ, "foot") and 儿(儿) (ér, "person"), suggesting someone who moves ahead, thus representing the concept of doing something before others or being first.
- **examples:**
  - 我先做完作业(我先做完作業)。
    - Wǒ xiān zuò wán zuòyè.
    - I finish my homework first.
  - 我们先讨论问题(我們先討論問題)，再制定计划(再制定計劃)。
    - Wǒmen xiān tǎolùn wèntí, zài zhìdìng jìhuà.
    - We first discuss the problem, then formulate a plan.
### 先辈(先輩) → 先(先) → 止(止)
- **definition:** foot
- **pinyin:** zhǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a footprint pointing up originally meaning 'foot'
  - **interpretation:** 止(止) (zhǐ, 'foot') is a pictogram depicting a footprint pointing upwards, emphasizing its connection to the idea of 'foot'. Its visual form with three toes aligns with similar characters representing human appendages.
- **examples:**
  - 他走路时不慎扭伤了止(他走路時不慎扭傷了止)。
    - Tā zǒulù shí bù shèn niǔshāng le zhǐ.
    - He accidentally sprained his foot while walking.
  - 冬日寒风刺骨(冬日寒風刺骨)，使他的止感到冰冷(使他的止感到冰冷)。
    - Dōngrì hánfēng cìgǔ, shǐ tā de zhǐ gǎndào bīnglěng.
    - The piercing winter wind made his foot feel icy.
  - 长途跋涉后(長途跋涉後)，他的止疲惫不堪(他的止疲憊不堪)。
    - Chángtú báshè hòu, tā de zhǐ píbèi bùkān.
    - After the long journey, his foot was utterly exhausted.
### 先辈(先輩) → 先(先) → 儿(兒)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** ér
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a child with legs
  - **interpretation:** The character 儿(兒) (ér, "person") visually represents a person, specifically focusing on the legs of a child, making it a simple and intuitive depiction of a human figure.
  - **simplification:** Simplification involved removing the top element from 兒(兒) to create 儿, focusing on the lower body for simplicity.
- **examples:**
  - 那个儿十分机智(那個兒十分機智)。
    - Nàgè ér shífēn jīzhì.
    - That person is very clever.
  - 他是个勤劳的儿(他是個勤勞的兒)，总能带来欢乐(總能帶來歡樂)。
    - Tā shìgè qínláo de ér, zǒng néng dàilái huānlè.
    - He is a hardworking person, always able to bring joy.
  - 古人说儿可成大器(古人說兒可成大器)，今日亦应秉持此理(今日亦應秉持此理)。
    - Gǔrén shuō ér kě chéng dàqì, jīnrì yìng yīng bǐngchí cǐ lǐ.
    - The ancients said that a person can achieve great things, and this principle should be upheld today as well.
### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩)
- **definition:** generation
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **components:**
  - 車
    - chē
    - cart
  - 非
    - fēi
    - not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 車(車) (chē, "cart") + phonetic 非(非) (fēi, "not") =
    - conveyance of non-essentials by cart ->
    - generation
  - **interpretation:** The character incorporates the concept of transport or continuity from 車(車) (chē, "cart") with the sound element 非(非) (fēi, "not"), creating a metaphorical link to the continuity of generational succession.
  - **simplification:** 辈(輩): The component 車(車) (chē, "cart") is simplified to 车(车) for ease of writing in simplified Chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我这一辈的人(他是我這一輩的人)。
    - Tā shì wǒ zhè yī bèi de rén.
    - He is someone of my generation.
  - 不同辈的人(不同輩的人)，有不同的理想(有不同的理想)。
    - Bùtóng bèi de rén, yǒu bùtóng de lǐxiǎng.
    - People of different generations have different ideals.
  - 老一辈有老一辈的智慧(老一輩有老一輩的智慧)。
    - Lǎo yī bèi yǒu lǎo yī bèi de zhìhuì.
    - The older generation possesses its own wisdom.
### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩) → 車(車)
- **definition:** cart
- **pinyin:** chē
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a carriage seen from above: large wheels on both sides, sun shade on top ->
    - strokes become wheels, 田 (tián, "field") marks loading area ->
    - modern vertical depiction of carriage
  - **interpretation:** The character 車(chē, "vehicle") visually represents a carriage as it would be seen from above, highlighting key features like wheels and a covered loading area.
- **examples:**
  - 车是一种方便的交通工具(車是一種方便的交通工具)。
    - Chē shì yī zhǒng fāngbiàn de jiāotōng gōngjù.
    - The vehicle is a convenient means of transportation.
  - 他开车(他開車)，去接朋友(去接朋友)。
    - Tā kāichē, qù jiē péngyǒu.
    - He drives to pick up his friend.
  - 老车虽然年久失修(老車雖然年久失修)，仍然可靠(仍然可靠)。
    - Lǎo chē suīrán niánjiǔ shī xiū, réngrán kěkào.
    - Although the old car is long in disrepair, it remains reliable.
### 先辈(先輩) → 辈(輩) → 非(非)
- **definition:** not
- **pinyin:** fēi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts two bent wings opposing each other
  - **interpretation:** The character 非(非) (fēi, "not") is a pictogram that illustrates two wings in opposition, symbolizing the concept of negation or opposing forces.
- **examples:**
  - 这绝对非假的(這絕對非假的)。
    - Zhè juéduì fēi jiǎ de.
    - This is definitely not fake.
  - 非你莫属(非你莫屬)。
    - Fēi nǐ mò shǔ.
    - It can only be you.
  - 他并非为了钱(他並非為了錢)，而是为了梦想(而是為了夢想)。
    - Tā bìng fēi wèi le qián, ér shì wèi le mèngxiǎng.
    - He is not doing it for money, but rather for his dreams.
