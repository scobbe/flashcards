## try; attempt
### chángshì
---
- **definition:** try; attempt
- **pinyin:** chángshì
- **components:**
  - 尝(嘗)
    - cháng
    - taste; try
  - 试(試)
    - shì
    - test; try
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 尝(嘗) (cháng, "taste; try") and 试(試) (shì, "test; try") come together to form the word meaning "try" or "attempt."
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 尝(嘗) (cháng, "taste; try") and 试(試) (shì, "test; try") means to "try" or "attempt," indicating the action of testing or experimenting with something.
  - **simplification:** 尝试 is the simplified form of 嘗試.
- **examples:**
  - 我尝试新的方法(我嘗試新的方法)。
    - Wǒ chángshì xīn de fāngfǎ.
    - I try a new method.
  - 他总是尝试解决问题(他總是嘗試解決問題)，寻找创新方法(尋找創新方法)。
    - Tā zǒng shì chángshì jiějué wèntí, xúnzhǎo chuàngxīn fāngfǎ.
    - He always tries to solve problems, seeking innovative ways.
