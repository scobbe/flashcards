### try; attempt
---
- **pinyin:** try; attempt
- **components:**
  - 尝(嘗)
    - cháng
    - taste; try; sample; experience
  - 试(試)
    - shì
    - test; try; experiment; examine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 尝(嘗) (cháng, 'taste') + 试(試) (shì, 'to test') — taste test
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 尝(嘗) and 试(試) makes sense as it expresses the act of testing or trying something through personal experience, especially in the context of tasting new things.
- **examples:**
  - 我想尝试新菜(我想嘗試新菜)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng chángshì xīn cài.
    - I want to try new dishes.
  - 他总是尝试不同的方法(他總是嘗試不同的方法)。
    - Tā zǒng shì chángshì bùtóng de fāngfǎ.
    - He always tries different methods.
### 尝试(嘗試) → 尝(嘗)
- **definition:** taste; try; sample; experience
- **pinyin:** cháng
- **components:**
  - 尚
    - shàng
    - to be above
  - 旨
    - zhǐ
    - tasty
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Phonetic 尚 (shàng, 'to be above') + semantic 旨 (zhǐ, 'tasty').
  - **interpretation:** This character combines the phonetic element indicating a high standard and the semantic element relating to taste, suggesting an elevated or refined experience of flavor.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing the component 嘗 with 尝, which maintains the phonetic aspect while modernizing the character's appearance.
- **examples:**
  - 我想尝尝这个菜(我想嘗嘗這個菜)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng chángcháng zhège cài.
    - I want to taste this dish.
  - 他喜欢尝试新的食物(他喜歡嘗試新的食物)。
    - Tā xǐhuān chángshì xīn de shíwù.
    - He enjoys trying new foods.

- **pinyin:** shì
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 式
    - shì
    - style, method
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Contains the semantic component 言 (yán, 'speech') which relates to communication or expression, and the phonetic component 式 (shì, 'style, method') that gives it its sound.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively combines the idea of communicating through an exam or method, reflecting the notion of testing or trying by expressing something verbally.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing 言 (yán, 'speech') with its simplified form 讠 (yán, 'speech'), while keeping the phonetic component intact.
- **examples:**

    - Zhège shíyàn hái zài jìnxíng zhōng.
    - This experiment is still underway.
