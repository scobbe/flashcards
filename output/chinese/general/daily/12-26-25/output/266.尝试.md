## try; attempt
### chángshì
---
- **traditional:** 嘗試
- **definition:** try; attempt
- **pinyin:** chángshì
- **components:**
  - 尝(嘗)
    - cháng
    - taste; try
  - 试(試)
    - shì
    - test; experiment
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 尝(嘗) (cháng, "taste; try") and 试(試) (shì, "test; experiment") together form the concept of making an attempt or trial.
  - **interpretation:** The word 尝试(嘗試) (chángshì, "try; attempt") combines the idea of 'trying' something akin to 'tasting' it, with the action of a 'test' or 'experiment', signifying an effort to do something in order to see the result.
  - **simplification:** 尝试 is the simplified form, 嘗試 is the traditional form.
- **examples:**
  - 他尝试(嘗試)新方法，解决难题(決難題)。
    - tā chángshì xīn fāngfǎ, jiějué nán tí.
    - He tries a new method, solving a difficult problem.
  - 她尝试(嘗試)解决问题(決問題)，然后(後)成功了。
    - tā chángshì jiějué wèntí, ránhòu chénggōng le.
    - She tries to solve the problem, and then she succeeds.
### 尝(嘗)试(試) → 尝(嘗)
- **traditional:** 嘗
- **definition:** taste; try
- **pinyin:** cháng
- **components:**
  - 尚
    - shàng
    - of high esteem
  - 旨
    - zhǐ
    - tasty
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 尚 (shàng) + Semantic: 旨 (zhǐ, "tasty") =
    - relating to flavor ->
    - taste; try
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 尚 (shàng) for its similar sound with 旨 (zhǐ, "tasty") to convey the idea of experiencing or trying different flavors.
  - **simplification:** 尝(嘗) replaces the complex 龍 (lóng, "dragon") with a simpler phonetic 尚 (shàng), while retaining the essential meaning with 旨 (zhǐ, "tasty") component.
- **examples:**
  - 我想尝这个(嘗這個)菜。
    - Wǒ xiǎng cháng zhège cài.
    - I want to taste this dish.
  - 他尝(嘗)了果汁，觉(覺)得味道不错(錯)。
    - Tā cháng le guǒ zhī, juéde wèidào búcuò.
    - He tasted the juice and thought the flavor was good.
### 尝(嘗)试(試) → 尝(嘗) → 尚
- **definition:** of high esteem
- **pinyin:** shàng
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 冋
    - jiōng
    - closed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 小 (xiǎo, "small") + 冋 (jiōng, "closed") =
    - closed top ->
    - high ->
    - esteem
  - **interpretation:** The character 尚 (shàng, "esteem") combines 小 (xiǎo, "small") with 冋 (jiōng, "closed") to form a pictorial top. This signifies something being elevated or raised, symbolizing esteem or admiration. The closed top connotes positioning at a height or high standing.
- **examples:**
  - 他非常尚重传统(傳統)。
    - Tā fēicháng shàng zhòng chuántǒng.
    - He holds tradition in high esteem.
  - 公司尚人才，视员(視員)工为(為)家人。
    - Gōngsī shàng réncái, shì yuángōng wéi jiārén.
    - The company esteems talent and regards its employees as family.

### 尝(嘗)试(試) → 尝(嘗) → 尚 → 冋 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开(張開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, dàshēng hǎnjiào.
    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.

### 尝(嘗)试(試) → 试(試)
- **traditional:** 試
- **definition:** test; experiment
- **pinyin:** shì
- **components:**
  - 言
    - yán
    - speech
  - 讠(言)
    - yán
    - speech
  - 式
    - shì
    - form
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + Phonetic: 式 (shì, "form") =
    - related to speaking or expressing via structured form ->
    - test or experiment
  - **interpretation:** 試 (shì, "test") combines 言 (yán, "speech") for the semantic meaning of communication and 式 (shì, "form") as a phonetic guide, symbolizing the act of trying out or testing through structured verbal expressions or methods.
  - **simplification:** 試 was simplified to 试(試) by replacing 言 (yán, "speech") with 讠 (yán, "speech") for a simpler form with the same phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 让(讓)我们试(們試)一试(試)。
    - Ràng wǒmen shì yī shì.
    - Let's give it a try.
  - 他试着(試著)解决问题(決問題)，以求进(進)步。
    - Tā shìzhe jiějué wèntí, yǐ qiú jìnbù.
    - He is trying to solve the problem in order to make progress.
  - 科学(學)家试图发现(試圖發現)新理论(論)。
    - Kēxuéjiā shìtú fāxiàn xīn lǐlùn.
    - Scientists try to discover a new theory.

- **etymology:**

  - **description:**

    - modern form adds another stroke on top
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") visually emphasizes the movement of the tongue, illustrating how speech is produced through tongue actions. The additional stroke and evolution to a more abstract form highlight the facet of communication.
- **examples:**

    - Tā de yán lìng rén xìn fú.

    - Tā chángshé wújì, jīngcháng xièlòu mìmì.
