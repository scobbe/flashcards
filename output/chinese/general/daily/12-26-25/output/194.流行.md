## 流行(流行)
---
## 
### popular; prevalent
---
- **pinyin:** popular; prevalent
- **components:**
  - 流
    - liú
    - flow; pour; stream; circulate
  - 行
    - háng
    - go; walk; conduct; trade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 流(流) (liú, 'flow') + 行(行) (háng, 'go; walk') — flow go
  - **interpretation:** This combination suggests the notion of something that flows or moves through society, indicating its popularity or prevalence. It conveys the idea that popular trends spread easily and widely.
- **examples:**
  - 这种音乐很流行(這種音樂很流行)。
    - zhè zhǒng yīn yuè hěn liú xíng。
    - This kind of music is very popular.
  - 流行时尚总是变化(流行時尚總是變化)。
    - liú xíng shí shàng zǒng shì biàn huà。
    - Fashion trends are always changing.

- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 水

  - 㐬

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Semantic 水 (water) + phonetic 㐬.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the idea of water with a phonetic element, hinting at the fluidity and movement associated with flowing water.
- **examples:**

    - Hé liú hěn cháng, yǒu hěn duō yú.

### 流行(流行) → 流(流) → 水(水)
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents the movement of water, evoking a sense of flow and fluidity. It is a direct representation of water as an element essential for life.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命的来源(水是生命的來源)。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng de láiyuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 我喜欢喝水(我喜歡喝水)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē shuǐ.
    - I like to drink water.
  - 海水很咸(海水很鹹)。
    - Hǎishuǐ hěn xián.
    - Sea water is very salty.

### 流行(流行) → 行(行)
- **definition:** go; walk; conduct; trade
- **pinyin:** háng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person walking.
  - **interpretation:** The character shows a figure in motion, emphasizing the action of walking or traveling.
- **examples:**
  - 我行走在公园里(我行走在公園裡)。
    - wǒ xíngzǒu zài gōngyuán lǐ.

  - 她行得很快(她行得很快)。
    - tā xíng dé hěn kuài.
    - She walks very fast.
  - 他每天都要行走(他每天都要行走)。
