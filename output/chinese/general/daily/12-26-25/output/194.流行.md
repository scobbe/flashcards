## popular; prevalent
### liúxíng
---
- **definition:** popular; prevalent
- **pinyin:** liúxíng
- **components:**
  - 流
    - liú
    - flow; circulate; drift
  - 行
    - xíng
    - move; travel; perform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 流(流) (liú, "flow") + 行(行) (xíng, "move")
  - **interpretation:** The word 流行(liúxíng) ("popular; prevalent") combines the idea of something that flows smoothly and moves along, which metaphorically relates to trends or things that spread quickly and widely in society, becoming popular or widespread.
- **examples:**
  - 这首歌曲非常流行(這首歌曲非常流行)。
    - Zhè shǒu gēqǔ fēicháng liúxíng.
    - This song is very popular.
  - 这种设计流行于时尚界(這種設計流行於時尚界)，引发了众多讨论(引發了眾多討論)。
    - Zhè zhǒng shèjì liúxíng yú shíshàng jiè, yǐnfā le zhòngduō tǎolùn.
    - This design is popular in the fashion industry, sparking much discussion.
  - 在网络时代(在網絡時代)，流行文化迅速传播(流行文化迅速傳播)。
    - Zài wǎngluò shídài, liúxíng wénhuà xùnsù chuánbō.
    - In the internet age, popular culture spreads rapidly.
