---
- **definition:** matcha
- **pinyin:** mǒchá
- **components:**
  - 抹
    - mǒ
    - apply; rub; grind
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 抹 (mǒ, "apply; rub; grind") 茶 (chá, "tea")
  - **interpretation:** 抹茶 (mǒchá, "matcha") refers to a type of finely ground powdered green tea. The term combines 抹 (mǒ, "apply; rub; grind"), highlighting the process of grinding the tea leaves, with 茶 (chá, "tea"), indicating the primary ingredient. This describes the unique method of preparing matcha, distinct from other forms of tea, which involves grinding tea leaves into a powder.
