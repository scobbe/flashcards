## matcha
### mǒchá
---
- **definition:** matcha
- **pinyin:** mǒchá
- **components:**
  - 抹
    - mǒ
    - apply; wipe
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 抹(抹) (mǒ, "apply; wipe") + 茶(茶) (chá, "tea") =
    - powdered tea ->
    - matcha
  - **interpretation:** The word 抹茶 (mǒchá) combines the idea of finely ground or 'applied' (抹) tea (茶), which refers to the powdered form of green tea known as matcha.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝抹茶(我喜歡喝抹茶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē mǒchá.
    - I like drinking matcha.
  - 春天来了(春天來了)，我们一起品尝抹茶(我們一起品嚐抹茶)。
    - Chūntiān láile, wǒmen yīqǐ pǐncháng mǒchá.
    - Spring has come, let's taste matcha together.

    - 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic 余(余) (yú) =
