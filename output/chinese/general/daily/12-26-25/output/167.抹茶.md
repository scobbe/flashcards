### matcha
---
- **pinyin:** matcha
- **components:**
  - 抹
    - mǒ
    - to smear; to spread
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 抹(抹) (mǒ, "to smear; to spread") + 茶(茶) (chá, "tea") — smear tea
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as 抹(抹) (mǒ, "to smear; to spread") suggests the preparation method of the tea by mixing it to create a smooth texture, and 茶(茶) (chá, "tea") specifies that it pertains to tea. Matcha is finely ground tea leaves used in traditional tea ceremonies.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝抹茶(我喜歡喝抹茶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē mǒchá.
    - I enjoy drinking matcha.
  - 她用抹茶做了蛋糕(她用抹茶做了蛋糕)。
    - Tā yòng mǒchá zuòle dànkāo.
    - She made a cake with matcha.
