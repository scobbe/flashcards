## imagination; imagine
### xiǎngxiàng
---
- **traditional:** 想像
- **definition:** imagination; imagine
- **pinyin:** xiǎngxiàng
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; ponder; consider; want
  - 象(像)
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; elephant; phenomenon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想 (xiǎng, "think") combined with 象(像) (xiàng, "image") to express the concept of forming mental images or envisioning something in the mind.
  - **interpretation:** The word 想象(像) (xiǎngxiàng, "imagination; imagine") brings together the concepts of thinking and forming images, conveying the idea of creating or conceiving mental pictures.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢(歡)想象(像)未来(來)，描绘梦(繪夢)想蓝图(藍圖)。
    - Tā xǐhuān xiǎngxiàng wèilái, miáohuì mèngxiǎng lántú.
    - He enjoys imagining the future and sketching a blueprint of his dreams.
  - 孩子们(們)沉浸在故事里(裡)，尽(盡)情想象(像)奇妙世界。
    - Háizimen chénjìn zài gùshì lǐ, jìnqíng xiǎngxiàng qímiào shìjiè.
    - Children are immersed in stories and unabashedly imagine a magical world.
  - 夜深人静时(靜時)，他常在心中想象(像)童年时(時)光。
    - Yè shēn rénjìng shí, tā cháng zài xīnzhōng xiǎngxiàng tóngnián shíguāng.
    - When the night is deep and quiet, he often imagines the times of his childhood in his heart.
### 想( )象(像) → 想
- **definition:** think; ponder; consider; want
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **components:**
  - 相
    - xiāng
    - appearance
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart, mind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 相 (xiāng, "appearance") + Semantic: 心 (xīn, "heart, mind") =
    - visualizing in the mind ->
    - to think, to consider
  - **interpretation:** 想 (xiǎng, "think") combines the phonetic component 相 (xiāng, "appearance") with the semantic component 心 (xīn, "heart, mind") to convey the idea of visualizing or pondering something with the heart or mind. This effectively captures the essence of internal contemplation or consideration.
- **examples:**
  - 我想去图书馆(圖書館)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù túshūguǎn.
    - I want to go to the library.
  - 妈妈(媽媽)想做饭(飯)，我们(們)想吃中餐。
    - Māmā xiǎng zuò fàn, wǒmen xiǎng chī zhōngcān.
    - Mom wants to cook, and we want to eat Chinese food.
  - 他想一想，就决(決)定了。
    - Tā xiǎng yīxiǎng, jiù juédìng le.
    - He gave it some thought, then made a decision.
### 想( )象(像) → 想 → 相
- **definition:** appearance
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 木 (mù, "tree") + Phonetic: 目 (mù) =
    - eye on tree ->
    - to look at each other ->
    - mutual
  - **interpretation:** The character 相(xiāng, "mutual") combines 目 (mù, "eye") suggesting seeing or observing, with 木 (mù, "tree"), reflecting the idea of looking out over a distance or surveying. This is extended to mean looking at each other, thus becoming associated with mutual or reciprocal actions.
- **examples:**
  - 他们(們)相互扶持。
    - Tāmen xiānghù fúchí.
    - They support each other.
  - 朋友之间(間)要相互理解，共同进(進)步。
    - Péngyǒu zhījiān yào xiānghù lǐjiě, gòngtóng jìnbù.
    - Friends should understand each other and make progress together.
  - 夫妻之间应(間應)相互尊重。
    - Fūqī zhījiān yīng xiānghù zūnzhòng.
    - Husbands and wives should mutually respect each other.
### 想( )象(像) → 想 → 相 → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把椅子的主要材料是木。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
### 想( )象(像) → 想 → 相 → 目
- **definition:** eye
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a human eye, now represented and rotated 90 degrees ->
    - eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(mù, 'eye') is a pictogram representing a human eye. Its original orientation has changed over time, resembling an eye seen from a sideways view.
- **examples:**
  - 他的目光炯炯有神。
    - Tā de mùguāng jiǒngjiǒng yǒu shén.
    - His gaze is bright and full of spirit.
  - 她目不转(轉)睛，注视(視)前方。
    - Tā mù bù zhuǎn jīng, zhùshì qiánfāng.
    - She stared intently, gazing ahead.
  - 雄鹰(鷹)展翅，双(雙)目炯炯看天际(際)。
    - Xióng yīng zhǎn chì, shuāng mù jiǒngjiǒng kàn tiānjì.
    - The eagle spreads its wings, its eyes piercingly gaze at the horizon.
### 想( )象(像) → 想 → 心
- **definition:** heart, mind
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized representation of a heart
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, 'heart') visually represents the shape of a heart, emphasizing its central and essential role in both a physical and metaphorical sense.
- **examples:**
  - 他的心温(溫)暖无(無)比。
    - Tā de xīn wēnnuǎn wúbǐ.
    - His heart is incredibly warm.
  - 她把心放在艺术(藝術)上，追求完美。
    - Tā bǎ xīn fàng zài yìshù shàng, zhuīqiú wánměi.
    - She dedicates her heart to art, pursuing perfection.
  - 音乐(樂)直击(擊)心灵(靈)。
    - Yīnyuè zhí jī xīnlíng.
    - Music strikes straight at the heart (soul).
### 想( )象(像) → 象
- **definition:** image; likeness; elephant; phenomenon
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an elephant: ⺈ (trunk) + 𫩏 (head) + 𧰨 (body) ->
    - elephant
  - **interpretation:** The character 象 (xiàng, 'elephant') is a pictogram that visually represents an elephant with distinct features: the trunk, head, and body. This straightforward depiction was used to convey the concept of an elephant.
- **examples:**
  - 大象步伐缓(緩)慢。
    - dà xiàng bùfá huǎnmàn
    - Elephants move slowly.
  - 小象在河边戏(邊戲)水，妈妈紧紧护卫(媽媽緊緊護衛)。
    - xiǎo xiàng zài hé biān xì shuǐ, māma jǐn jǐn hùwèi
    - The baby elephant splashes in the river, while its mother watches closely.
  - 象牙被视为(視為)珍贵(貴)，常被用于(於)雕刻。
    - xiàng yá bèi shì wéi zhēn guì, cháng bèi yòng yú diāo kè
    - Ivory is considered precious and is often used for carving.
