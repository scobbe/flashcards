## imagination; imagine
### xiǎngxiàng
---
- **definition:** imagination; imagine
- **pinyin:** xiǎngxiàng
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; consider; remember; imagine
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - resemble; image; likeness; statue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想(想) (xiǎng, "think"; "consider") + 象(像) (xiàng, "resemble"; "image")
  - **interpretation:** The word 想像(xiǎngxiàng) combines 想(想) (xiǎng, "think") to imply the process of thinking or considering, with 象(像) (xiàng, "image") suggesting forming images or resemblances in the mind. Together, they convey the concept of imagining or creating mental images.
  - **simplification:** 象(像) is simplified to 像 in modern usage.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢想象未来(他喜歡想像未來)。
    - Tā xǐhuān xiǎngxiàng wèilái.
    - He likes to imagine the future.
  - 我们常常想象遥远的国度(我們常常想像遙遠的國度)，梦想着改变现实(夢想著改變現實)。
    - Wǒmen chángcháng xiǎngxiàng yáoyuǎn de guódù, mèngxiǎngzhe gǎibiàn xiànshí.
    - We often imagine distant lands and dream of changing reality.
  - 艺术家凭借丰富的想象创作奇迹(藝術家憑藉豐富的想像創作奇蹟)。
    - Yìshùjiā píngjiè fēngfù de xiǎngxiàng chuàngzuò qíjī.
    - Artists create miracles using their rich imagination.
### 想象(想像) → 想(想)
- **definition:** think; consider; remember; imagine
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **components:**
  - 相
    - xiāng
    - to each other
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart, mind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic 相(xiāng, 'to each other') + Semantic 心(xīn, 'heart, mind') =
    - consideration in the mind ->
    - think
  - **interpretation:** The character 想(xiǎng, 'think') combines the phonetic element 相(xiāng, 'to each other'), which associates with interaction or reflection, with the semantic element 心(xīn, 'heart, mind'), representing the mental process or consideration, to convey the meaning of thinking or contemplating.
- **examples:**
  - 我在想答案(我在想答案)。
    - Wǒ zài xiǎng dá'àn.
    - I am thinking of the answer.
  - 他坐在窗前(他坐在窗前)，想未来的出路(想未來的出路)。
    - Tā zuò zài chuāngqián, xiǎng wèi lái de chūlù.
    - He sits by the window, contemplating his future path.
  - 她常常一个人(她常常一個人)，独自想旧日时光(獨自想舊日時光)。
    - Tā chángcháng yīgè rén, dúzì xiǎng jiù rì shíguāng.
    - She often spends time alone, reminiscing about old times.
### 想象(想像) → 想(想) → 相(相)
- **definition:** to each other
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木(木) (mù, 'tree') + 目(目) (mù, 'eye') =
    - an eye looking at a tree ->
    - original meaning 'to observe' ->

- **examples:**
  - 他们之间相互理解(他們之間相互理解)。
    - Tāmen zhījiān xiānghù lǐjiě.

  - 朋友相扶持(朋友相扶持)，共度难关(共度難關)。
    - Péngyǒu xiāng fúchí, gòng dù nàn guān.
    - Friends support each other to overcome difficulties together.

### 想象(想像) → 像(像)
- **definition:** resemble; image; likeness; statue
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻(人)
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - elephant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻(人) (rén, "person") + 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant") =
    - person + likeness ->
    - likeness/statue
  - **interpretation:** The character 像 combines the semantic component 亻(亻) (rén, "person") with 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant"), which serves as the phonetic component. This implies a representation or likeness of a person or object, akin to creating a statue or an image.
- **examples:**
  - 他长得像他的父亲(他長得像他的父親)，步伐也像榜样(步伐也像榜樣)。
    - Tā zhǎng de xiàng tā de fùqīn, bùfá yě xiàng bǎngyàng.
    - He resembles his father, and his gait is also exemplary.
  - 这尊雕像看起来像一位古代英雄(這尊雕像看起來像一位古代英雄)。
    - Zhè zūn diāoxiàng kàn qǐlái xiàng yī wèi gǔdài yīngxióng.
    - This statue looks like an ancient hero.
### 想象(想像) → 像(像) → 亻(亻)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person ->
    - stylized as 人(人) (rén, "person") ->
    - simplified to a side component form 亻
  - **interpretation:** The character 亻 is a simplified form of 人(人) (rén, "person") used as a side component to denote human-related meanings. Its simplification helps integrate into compound characters where the full form of 人(人) might be cumbersome.
- **examples:**
  - 这个字的左边是亻(這個字的左邊是亻)，表示人(表示人)。
    - Zhège zì de zuǒbiān shì rén, biǎoshì rén.
    - The left side of this character is 亻, which represents a person.
  - 每个人都有自己的故事(每個人都有自己的故事)。
    - Měi gèrén dōu yǒu zìjǐ de gùshì.
    - Every person has their own story.
  - 老人的经验是宝贵的(老人的經驗是寶貴的)，请尊重他人(請尊重他人)。
    - Lǎorén de jīngyàn shì bǎoguì de, qǐng zūnzhòng tārén.
    - The elderly's experience is valuable; please respect others.
