## imagination; imagine
### xiǎngxiàng
---
- **definition:** imagination; imagine
- **pinyin:** xiǎngxiàng
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; ponder; consider; want
  - 象(像)
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; elephant; phenomenon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想(想) (xiǎng, "think") combined with 象(像) (xiàng, "image") to express the concept of forming mental images or envisioning something in the mind.
  - **interpretation:** The word 想象(想像) (xiǎngxiàng, "imagination; imagine") brings together the concepts of thinking and forming images, conveying the idea of creating or conceiving mental pictures.
- **examples:**
  - 他喜欢想象未来(他喜歡想像未來)，描绘梦想蓝图(描繪夢想藍圖)。
    - Tā xǐhuān xiǎngxiàng wèilái, miáohuì mèngxiǎng lántú.
    - He enjoys imagining the future and sketching a blueprint of his dreams.
  - 孩子们沉浸在故事里(孩子們沉浸在故事裡)，尽情想象奇妙世界(盡情想像奇妙世界)。
    - Háizimen chénjìn zài gùshì lǐ, jìnqíng xiǎngxiàng qímiào shìjiè.
    - Children are immersed in stories and unabashedly imagine a magical world.
  - 夜深人静时(夜深人靜時)，他常在心中想象童年时光(他常在心中想像童年時光)。
    - Yè shēn rénjìng shí, tā cháng zài xīnzhōng xiǎngxiàng tóngnián shíguāng.
    - When the night is deep and quiet, he often imagines the times of his childhood in his heart.
### 想象(想像) → 想(想)
- **definition:** think; ponder; consider; want
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **components:**
  - 相
    - xiāng
    - appearance
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart, mind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 相(相) (xiāng, "appearance") + Semantic: 心(心) (xīn, "heart, mind") =
    - visualizing in the mind ->
    - to think, to consider
  - **interpretation:** 想(想) (xiǎng, "think") combines the phonetic component 相(相) (xiāng, "appearance") with the semantic component 心(心) (xīn, "heart, mind") to convey the idea of visualizing or pondering something with the heart or mind. This effectively captures the essence of internal contemplation or consideration.
- **examples:**
  - 我想去图书馆(我想去圖書館)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù túshūguǎn.
    - I want to go to the library.
  - 妈妈想做饭(媽媽想做飯)，我们想吃中餐(我們想吃中餐)。
    - Māmā xiǎng zuò fàn, wǒmen xiǎng chī zhōngcān.
    - Mom wants to cook, and we want to eat Chinese food.
  - 他想一想(他想一想)，就决定了(就決定了)。
    - Tā xiǎng yīxiǎng, jiù juédìng le.
    - He gave it some thought, then made a decision.
### 想象(想像) → 想(想) → 相(相)
- **definition:** appearance
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 木(木) (mù, "tree") + Phonetic: 目(目) (mù) =
    - eye on tree ->
    - to look at each other ->
    - mutual
  - **interpretation:** The character 相(xiāng, "mutual") combines 目(目) (mù, "eye") suggesting seeing or observing, with 木(木) (mù, "tree"), reflecting the idea of looking out over a distance or surveying. This is extended to mean looking at each other, thus becoming associated with mutual or reciprocal actions.
- **examples:**
  - 他们相互扶持(他們相互扶持)。
    - Tāmen xiānghù fúchí.
    - They support each other.
  - 朋友之间要相互理解(朋友之間要相互理解)，共同进步(共同進步)。
    - Péngyǒu zhījiān yào xiānghù lǐjiě, gòngtóng jìnbù.
    - Friends should understand each other and make progress together.
  - 夫妻之间应相互尊重(夫妻之間應相互尊重)。
    - Fūqī zhījiān yīng xiānghù zūnzhòng.
    - Husbands and wives should mutually respect each other.

### 想象(想像) → 象(象)
- **definition:** image; likeness; elephant; phenomenon
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an elephant: ⺈ (trunk) + 𫩏 (head) + 𧰨 (body) ->
    - elephant
  - **interpretation:** The character 象(象) (xiàng, 'elephant') is a pictogram that visually represents an elephant with distinct features: the trunk, head, and body. This straightforward depiction was used to convey the concept of an elephant.
- **examples:**
  - 大象步伐缓慢(大象步伐緩慢)。
    - dà xiàng bùfá huǎnmàn
    - Elephants move slowly.
  - 小象在河边戏水(小象在河邊戲水)，妈妈紧紧护卫(媽媽緊緊護衛)。
    - xiǎo xiàng zài hé biān xì shuǐ, māma jǐn jǐn hùwèi
    - The baby elephant splashes in the river, while its mother watches closely.
  - 象牙被视为珍贵(象牙被視為珍貴)，常被用于雕刻(常被用於雕刻)。
    - xiàng yá bèi shì wéi zhēn guì, cháng bèi yòng yú diāo kè
    - Ivory is considered precious and is often used for carving.
