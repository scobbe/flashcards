## 
### imagination; imagine
---
- **pinyin:** imagination; imagine
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; wish; suppose
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - image; shape
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 想(想) (xiǎng, "think; wish; suppose") + 象(象) (xiàng, "elephant; image; shape") — think image
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 想(想) (xiǎng, "think") implies a process of cognitive activity, while 象(象) (xiàng, "image") suggests the output of that thought process as a mental image. Together, they convey the concept of forming images in one's mind through thought.
- **examples:**
  - 我可以想象未来的生活(我可以想像未來的生活)。
    - Wǒ kěyǐ xiǎngxiàng wèilái de shēnghuó.
    - I can imagine the future life.
  - 他的想象力很丰富(他的想像力很豐富)。
    - Tā de xiǎngxiànglì hěn fēngfù.
    - His imagination is very rich.
### 想象(想象) → 想(想)
- **definition:** think; wish; suppose
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **components:**
  - 相
    - xiāng
    - to see
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart/mind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines 相(xiāng, 'to see') with 心(xīn, 'heart/mind') — representing the act of thinking or desiring.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea that thinking or wanting involves the heart or mind's engagement with what is perceived.
- **examples:**
  - 我在想你(我在想你)。
    - Wǒ zài xiǎng nǐ.
    - I am thinking of you.
  - 他想去旅行(他想去旅行)。
    - Tā xiǎng qù lǚxíng.
    - He wants to travel.
### 想象(想象) → 想(想) → 相(相)
- **definition:** to see
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Comprises the components 目(目) (mù, 'eye') which relates to seeing, and 相(相) (xiāng, 'mutual'); visually representing the concept of seeing each other.
  - **interpretation:** The element 目 (mù, 'eye') indicates the action of seeing, while 相 (xiāng, 'mutual') reinforces the idea of mutual observation or interaction.
- **examples:**
  - 我们相互帮助(我們相互幫助)，这是友谊的体现(這是友誼的體現)。
    - wǒmen xiānghù bāngzhù, zhè shì yǒuyì de tǐxiàn.
    - We help each other, this is a manifestation of friendship.
  - 他俩在公园里相遇(他倆在公園裡相遇)，互相微笑(互相微笑)。
    - tā liǎ zài gōngyuán lǐ xiāngyù, hùxiāng wēixiào.
    - The two met in the park, smiling at each other.
### 想象(想象) → 想(想) → 相(相) → 目(目)
- **definition:** eye
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human eye, now rotated 90 degrees.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively represents the eye, symbolizing vision and perception.
- **examples:**
  - 我看到了眼睛(我看到了眼睛)。
    - Wǒ kàn dào le yǎnjīng.
    - I saw the eye.
  - 他的眼睛很明亮(他的眼睛很明亮)。
    - Tā de yǎnjīng hěn míngliàng.
    - His eyes are very bright.
### 想象(想象) → 想(想) → 心(心)
- **definition:** heart/mind
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a highly stylized heart.
  - **interpretation:** The shape of the character evokes the physical form of a heart, symbolizing emotions and feelings.
- **examples:**
  - 我爱我的心。(我愛我的心。)
    - Wǒ ài wǒ de xīn.
    - I love my heart.
  - 他的心情很好。(他的心情很好。)
    - Tā de xīnqíng hěn hǎo.
    - His mood is very good.
### 想象(想象) → 象(象)
- **definition:** image; shape
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an elephant with parts representing the trunk, head, and body.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents an elephant, with distinct features like the trunk and body clearly illustrated.
- **examples:**
  - 大象在森林里(大象在森林裡)。
    - dà xiàng zài sēn lín lǐ.
    - The elephant is in the forest.
  - 我喜欢看大象(我喜歡看大象)。
    - wǒ xǐ huān kàn dà xiàng.
    - I like to see elephants.
