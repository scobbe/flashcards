## gentle; harmonious; and
### hé
---
- **definition:** gentle; harmonious; and
- **pinyin:** hé
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") + phonetic 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") =
    - grain and mouth suggest harmony ->
    - harmonious, gentle
  - **interpretation:** 禾(禾) (hé, "grain") symbolizes agriculture and sustenance, while 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") represents speech. Together, they convey the idea of harmony and balance, as in a well-nourished society.
- **examples:**
  - 他的性格和蔼(他的性格和藹)，深受大家喜爱(深受大家喜愛)。
    - Tā de xìnggé hé ǎi, shēn shòu dàjiā xǐ'ài.
    - His character is gentle, and he is deeply loved by everyone.
  - 这杯茶温和(這杯茶溫和)。
    - Zhè bēi chá wēnhé.
    - This cup of tea is mild.
### 和(和) → 禾(禾)
- **definition:** grain
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a plant stalk
  - **interpretation:** 禾(禾) (hé, 'grain') is a pictogram that represents a plant stalk, illustrating its association with crops or grains. Its form is designed to visually resemble the vertical growth of a grain plant.
- **examples:**
  - 田里的禾苗生长得旺盛(田裡的禾苗生長得旺盛)。
    - Tián lǐ de hé miáo shēngzhǎng dé wàngshèng.
    - The grain seedlings in the field are growing vigorously.
  - 秋天来临时(秋天來臨時)，金色的禾穗低垂(金色的禾穗低垂)。
    - Qiūtiān láilín shí, jīnsè de hé suì dīchuí.
    - When autumn arrives, the golden grain ears droop.
  - 庄稼包括小米(莊稼包括小米)，和其他禾类(和其他禾類)。
    - Zhuāngjià bāokuò xiǎomǐ, hé qítā hé lèi.
    - Crops include millet and other types of grains.
### 和(和) → 口(口)
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") visually represents an open mouth, making it straightforward to connect to its meaning related to speaking and openings.
- **examples:**
  - 请张开你的口(請張開你的口)。
    - Qǐng zhāngkāi nǐ de kǒu.
    - Please open your mouth.
  - 他家有三口人(他家有三口人)，个个活泼可爱(個個活潑可愛)。
    - Tā jiā yǒu sān kǒu rén, gègè huópō kě'ài.
    - His family consists of three people, and each one is lively and adorable.
  - 医生检查了他的口(醫生檢查了他的口)，确认健康无虞(確認健康無虞)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchále tā de kǒu, quèrèn jiànkāng wú yú.
    - The doctor examined his mouth and confirmed there were no health concerns.
