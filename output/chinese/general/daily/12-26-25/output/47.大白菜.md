## Chinese cabbage
### dàbáicài
---
- **definition:** Chinese cabbage
- **pinyin:** dàbáicài
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
  - 白
    - bái
    - white
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 大(大) (dà, "big") + 白(白) (bái, "white") + 菜(菜) (cài, "vegetable") =
    - a type of large white vegetable, referring to Chinese cabbage.
  - **interpretation:** The term 大白菜 (dàbáicài) refers to a large variety of cabbage that is white in color, which is the characteristic appearance of Chinese cabbage.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了大白菜(我買了大白菜)。
    - Wǒ mǎile dàbáicài.
    - I bought Chinese cabbage.
  - 大白菜营养丰富(大白菜營養豐富)，适合家庭烹饪(適合家庭烹飪)。
    - Dàbáicài yíngyǎng fēngfù, shìhé jiātíng pēngrèn.
    - Chinese cabbage is nutritious and suitable for home cooking.
  - 在冬天里，我总是买大白菜(在冬天裡，我總是買大白菜)，用来做热汤(用來做熱湯)。
    - Zài dōngtiān lǐ, wǒ zǒng shì mǎi dàbáicài, yòng lái zuò rè tāng.
    - In winter, I always buy Chinese cabbage to make hot soup.
### 大白菜(大白菜) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 人(人) (rén, "person") ->
    - borrowed for 大(大) (dà, "big")
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(大) (dà, "big") originally depicted a person standing, which later evolved to express the meaning 'big.' The resemblance to a person was used phonetically to convey largeness, not necessarily to portray outstretched arms as sometimes explained.
- **examples:**
  - 这只狗很大(這隻狗很大)。
    - Zhè zhī gǒu hěn dà.
    - This dog is big.
  - 我们的新家很大(我們的新家很大)，设备也很齐全(設備也很齊全)。
    - Wǒmen de xīn jiā hěn dà, shèbèi yě hěn qí quán.
    - Our new home is big, and the equipment is also complete.
  - 城堡大门敞开(城堡大門敞開)，迎接远方的客人(迎接遠方的客人)。
    - Chéngbǎo dàmén chǎngkāi, yíngjiē yuǎnfāng de kèrén.
    - The castle's big gate is wide open, welcoming guests from afar.
### 大白菜(大白菜) → 白(白)
- **definition:** white
- **pinyin:** bái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts the sun or an object with radiance ->
    - white
  - **interpretation:** The character 白(bái, "white") originally depicted the radiance of the sun or a round object, conveying the concept of brightness associated with the color white.
- **examples:**

    - Nà zhī māo de máo zhēn bái.

### 大白菜(大白菜) → 菜(菜)
- **definition:** vegetable; dish
- **pinyin:** cài
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 采
    - cǎi
    - to gather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") + 采(采) (cǎi, "to gather") =
    - grass-related and gathering ->
    - vegetable
  - **interpretation:** The character 菜(cài, "vegetable") combines 艹(艹) (cǎo, "grass") as a semantic component to indicate plants or greens, with 采(采) (cǎi, "to gather") as a phonetic component suggesting the activity of collecting plants, emphasizing its association with edible greens.
- **examples:**
  - 妈妈做菜(媽媽做菜)。
    - Māmā zuò cài.
    - Mom cooks dishes.
  - 这盘菜非常美味(這盤菜非常美味)，色香味俱全(色香味俱全)。
    - Zhè pán cài fēicháng měiwèi, sè xiāng wèi jù quán.
    - This dish is very delicious, with a perfect appearance, aroma, and taste.
  - 菜不仅指蔬菜(菜不僅指蔬菜)，也可以代表一道菜(也可以代表一道菜)。
    - Cài bù jǐn zhǐ shūcài, yě kěyǐ dàibiǎo yī dào cài.
    - The word 'cài' not only refers to vegetables, but can also mean a dish.
