### Chinese cabbage
---
- **pinyin:** Chinese cabbage
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large; great; vast
  - 白菜
    - báicài
    - cabbage; white vegetable; Chinese cabbage; cole
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大(大) (dà, "big") + 白菜(白菜) (báicài, "cabbage") — big cabbage
  - **interpretation:** This term describes a type of cabbage that is larger in size, hence 'big cabbage.' 大(大) (dà, "big") highlights the size aspect of 白菜(白菜) (báicài, "cabbage").
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃大白菜。(我喜歡吃大白菜。)
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī dà báicài.
    - I like to eat Chinese cabbage.
  - 市场上有很多新鲜的大白菜。(市場上有很多新鮮的大白菜。)
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu hěn duō xīnxiān de dà báicài.
    - There are many fresh Chinese cabbages in the market.
### 大白菜(大白菜) → 大(大)
- **definition:** big; large; great; vast
- **pinyin:** dà
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person facing forward, originally associated with the character for 'man'.
  - **interpretation:** The character embodies the concept of size and presence, represented by a figure standing upright, suggesting greatness or largeness.
- **examples:**

    - Zhège fángzi hěn dà, shìhé yījiā rén jūzhù.

    - Zhège wèntí tài dà le, xūyào rènzhēn jiějué.
    - This problem is too big and needs to be solved seriously.

    - Tā shì yī gè dà huàjiā, xiǎngyù shìjiè.

### 大白菜(大白菜) → 白菜(白菜)

- **components:**
  - 白
    - bái
    - white; clear; pure; blank
  - 菜
    - cài

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 白(白) (bái, "white") + 菜(菜) (cài, "vegetable") — white vegetable

  - 她买了很多白菜(她買了很多白菜)。
    - Tā mǎile hěn duō báicài.
    - She bought a lot of cabbage.
