## 去(去)
---
## 
### go
---
- **pinyin:** go
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents movement away from a location.
  - **interpretation:** The character intuitively depicts the action of going away from a place, signifying departure or movement.
- **examples:**
  - 我去商店(我去商店)。
    - Wǒ qù shāngdiàn.
    - I go to the store.
  - 我们快去吧(我們快去吧)！
    - Wǒmen kuài qù ba!
    - Let's go quickly!
