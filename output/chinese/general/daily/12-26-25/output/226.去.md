## go
### qù
---
- **definition:** go
- **pinyin:** qù
- **components:**
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
  - 厶
    - sī
    - private
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 土(土) (tǔ, "earth") + 厶(厶) (sī, "private") as phonetic =
    - leaving ground ->
    - go
  - **interpretation:** The character 去(qù, "go") combines 土(tǔ, "earth") indicating a relation to location or ground, with 厶(sī, "private") as the phonetic component, to convey the sense of moving away from one's current location or 'going.'
- **examples:**
  - 我去市场(我去市場)。
    - Wǒ qù shìchǎng.
    - I go to the market.
  - 我们去山上(我們去山上)，散步放松(散步放鬆)。
    - Wǒmen qù shānshàng, sànbù fàngsōng.
    - We go to the mountains, strolling to relax.
### 去(去) → 土(土)
- **definition:** earth
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel ->
    - stylized as a vertical stroke with a horizontal stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 土(tǔ, 'earth') visually represents a mound or pile of earth, capturing the essence of soil or clay. The simplification to strokes reflects the transition to a more abstract and recognizable symbol.
- **examples:**
  - 农夫耕种土(農夫耕種土)。
    - Nóngfū gēngzhòng tǔ.
    - The farmer plows the earth.
  - 雨后湿润的土(雨後濕潤的土)，孕育着生命(孕育著生命)。
    - Yǔ hòu shīrùn de tǔ, yùnyù zhe shēngmìng.
    - The moist soil after the rain nurtures life.
  - 儿童在土上玩耍(兒童在土上玩耍)。
    - Értóng zài tǔ shàng wánshuǎ.
    - Children play on the ground.
### 去(去) → 厶(厶)
- **definition:** private
- **pinyin:** sī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a private enclosure or cocoon
  - **interpretation:** The character 厶(厶) (sī, "private") originates as a depiction of an enclosure, suggesting something confined or private. This visual representation aligns with the concept of being 'private'.
- **examples:**
  - 厶代表个人隐私(厶代表個人隱私)。
    - Sī dàibiǎo gèrén yǐnsī.
    - ‘Sī’ represents personal privacy.
  - 他的厶事只属于他自己(他的厶事只屬於他自己)。
    - Tā de sī shì zhǐ shǔyú tā zìjǐ.
    - His private affairs belong solely to him.
  - 在公共场合(在公共場合)，他总是保持厶心(他總是保持厶心)。
    - Zài gōnggòng chǎnghé, tā zǒng shì bǎochí sī xīn.
    - In public settings, he always maintains a private demeanor.
