### wallet
---
- **pinyin:** wallet
- **components:**
  - 钱(錢)
    - qián
    - money; cash; wealth; gold
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bag; package; wrap; contain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 钱(錢) (qián, 'money') + 包(包) (bāo, 'bag') — money bag
  - **interpretation:** This term literally translates to 'money bag', which accurately describes a container used to hold money and other personal items. The combination makes sense as wallets are designed to carry both cash and cards.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一个新的钱包(我需要一個新的錢包)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yīgè xīn de qiánbāo.
    - I need a new wallet.
  - 我的钱包(我的錢包)丢了！
    - Wǒ de qiánbāo diūle!
    - My wallet is lost!
