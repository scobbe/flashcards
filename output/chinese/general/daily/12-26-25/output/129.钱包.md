## wallet
### qiánbāo
---
- **definition:** wallet
- **pinyin:** qiánbāo
- **components:**
  - 钱(錢)
    - qián
    - money
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bag; wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 钱(錢) (qián, "money") + 包(包) (bāo, "bag"; "wrap") =
    - a bag for money ->
    - wallet
  - **interpretation:** A 钱包(錢包) (qiánbāo, "wallet") is a small bag or case used to hold money, making the combination logical as it carries the function of storing and securing one's money.
- **examples:**
  - 我把钱包忘在家里(我把錢包忘在家裡)。
    - Wǒ bǎ qiánbāo wàng zài jiālǐ.
    - I forgot my wallet at home.
  - 走进商店时(走進商店時)，他从口袋里拿出钱包(他從口袋裡拿出錢包)。
    - Zǒu jìn shāngdiàn shí, tā cóng kǒudài lǐ ná chū qiánbāo.
    - When he entered the store, he took his wallet from his pocket.
  - 看到钱包时(看到錢包時)，他急忙回家(他急忙回家)。
    - Kàn dào qiánbāo shí, tā jímáng huí jiā.
    - When he saw the wallet, he hurried home.
### 钱包(錢包) → 钱(錢)
- **definition:** money
- **pinyin:** qián
- **components:**
  - 金
    - jīn
    - metal
  - 戔
    - jiān
    - double edge
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 金(金) (jīn, "metal; gold; money") + 戔(戔) (jiān, "double edge") =
    - metal shaped by opposing edges ->
    - coin ->
    - money
  - **interpretation:** 錢(錢) (qián, "money") combines 金(金) (jīn, "metal") to represent the material used in ancient coins and 戔(戔) (jiān), which provides the phonetic cue. The idea of shaping metal links both components logically through metalwork, symbolizing currency.
  - **simplification:** 錢(錢)→钱(錢): 釒(釒) (jīn, "metal") was simplified to 钅(钅) (jīn, "metal"), and 戔(戔) (jiān, "double edge") was simplified to 戋(戋) (jiān, "double edge") for ease of writing, similar to the simplification in 残(残).
- **examples:**
  - 他存了很多钱(他存了很多錢)。
    - Tā cún le hěn duō qián.
    - He saved a lot of money.
  - 她花钱(她花錢)，买了新衣服(買了新衣服)。
    - Tā huā qián, mǎi le xīn yīfú.
    - She spent money to buy new clothes.
  - 没有钱(沒有錢)，他无法买车(他無法買車)。
    - Méiyǒu qián, tā wúfǎ mǎi chē.
    - Without money, he cannot buy a car.
