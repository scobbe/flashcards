---

- **pinyin:** qiánbāo
- **components:**
  - 钱(錢)
    - qián
    - money
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bag
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word 钱包(錢包) (qiánbāo) is a compound word which combines 钱(錢) (qián, "money") and 包(包) (bāo, "bag"). The combination literally means a 'money bag', which refers to a wallet.
  - **interpretation:** Combining 钱(錢) (qián, "money") and 包(包) (bāo, "bag"), 钱包(錢包) signifies a container or pouch for carrying money, fitting the modern concept of a wallet.
  - **simplification:** Traditional '錢' simplified to '钱'.
- **examples:**
  - 我丢了钱包(我丟了錢包)。
    - Wǒ diū le qiánbāo.
    - I lost my wallet.
  - 今天出门时(今天出門時)，我发现钱包不见了(我發現錢包不見了)。
    - Jīntiān chūmén shí, wǒ fāxiàn qiánbāo bùjiàn le.
    - Today when leaving home, I discovered that my wallet was missing.
