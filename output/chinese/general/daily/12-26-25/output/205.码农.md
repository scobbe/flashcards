## programmer (slang)
### mǎnóng
---
- **definition:** programmer (slang)
- **pinyin:** mǎnóng
- **components:**
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number; yard
  - 农(農)
    - nóng
    - farmer; agriculture; to farm
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 码(碼) (mǎ, "code; number") + 农(農) (nóng, "farmer") =
    - a humorous slang term for a programmer, suggesting someone who works hard writing lines of code like a farmer tending a field.
  - **interpretation:** The term 码农(碼農) (mǎnóng, "programmer") humorously implies that programmers tirelessly produce lines of code as farmers cultivate crops. This reflects the repetitive and labor-intensive nature of programming work.
  - **simplification:** 码: 碼, 农: 農
- **examples:**
  - 他是码农(他是碼農)。
    - Tā shì mǎnóng.
    - He is a programmer (slang).
  - 码农加班晚(碼農加班晚)，生活也不轻松(生活也不輕鬆)。
    - Mǎnóng jiābān wǎn, shēnghuó yě bù qīngsōng.
    - The programmer works overtime until late, and life is not easy.
### 码农(碼農) → 码(碼)
- **definition:** code; number; yard
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 石(石) (shí, 'stone') + Phonetic: 馬(馬) (mǎ, 'horse') =
    - used for counting stones ->
    - number, code
  - **interpretation:** The character 码(碼) combines 石(石) (shí, 'stone') symbolizing things that can be counted, with 馬(馬) (mǎ, 'horse'), chosen as a phonetic match. Together, they formed a character initially used for counting objects like stones, which later evolved to mean 'number' or 'code'.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character uses the exact same components as the traditional one.
- **examples:**
  - 请输入密码(請輸入密碼)。
    - Qǐng shūrù mìmǎ.
    - Please enter the password.

    - The system automatically generates a verification code.
  - 扫描二维码(掃描二維碼)，即可获取信息(即可獲取信息)。
    - Sǎomiáo èrwéi mǎ, jí kě huòqǔ xìnxī.
    - Scan the QR code to get the information.
### 码农(碼農) → 码(碼) → 石(石)
- **definition:** stone
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stone beneath a cliff, where the upper part originally looked like a cliff (厂) but was distorted into 丆 over time.
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') visually represents a stone in a natural setting, highlighting the element of a cliff or mountain. Its visual design aims to reflect the appearance of rocks lodged or embedded in a mountainous or cliff-like setting.
- **examples:**
  - 他在河边发现一块石(他在河邊發現一塊石)。
    - Tā zài hé biān fāxiàn yī kuài shí.

  - 孩子们喜欢堆石(孩子們喜歡堆石)，玩得开心(玩得開心)。

### 码农(碼農) → 农(農)
- **definition:** farmer; agriculture; to farm
- **pinyin:** nóng
- **components:**
  - 辰
    - chén
    - fifth earthly branch
  - 曲
    - qū
    - bent
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 辰(辰) (chén, 'fifth earthly branch') + phonetic: 曲(曲) (qū, 'bent') =
    - farm work at the time of 辰 ->
    - agriculture, farming
  - **interpretation:** The character 农(農) (nóng, 'farmer; agriculture') conveys the concept of farming activities aligned with the cycles of time, represented by 辰(辰) (chén, 'fifth earthly branch') which signifies a specific time, and 曲(曲) (qū, 'bent') indicating the physical nature of farm work.
  - **simplification:** 农(農) was simplified by removing the 田(田) (tián, 'field') radical and using a simplified form of the original components.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位农民(他是一位農民)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi nóngmín.
    - He is a farmer.
  - 他专注于农业研究(他專注於農業研究)，推广农耕技术(推廣農耕技術)。
    - Tā zhuānzhù yú nóngyè yánjiū, tuīguǎng nónggēng jìshù.
    - He focuses on agricultural research and promotes farming techniques.
  - 古时候有农夫耕作(古時候有農夫耕作)，辛勤劳作(辛勤勞作)，换来丰收(換來豐收)。
    - Gǔ shíhòu yǒu nóngfū gēngzuò, xīnqín láozuò, huàn lái fēngshōu.
    - In ancient times, farmers toiled in the fields, working hard and reaped a bountiful harvest.
