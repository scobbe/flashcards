### programmer (slang)
---
- **pinyin:** programmer (slang)
- **components:**
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number; measure; regulation
  - 农(農)
    - nóng
    - farmer; agriculture; farming; cultivate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 码(碼) (mǎ, 'code') + 农(農) (nóng, 'farmer') — code farmer
  - **interpretation:** The term describes someone who 'farms' or writes code, much like a farmer cultivates crops. It humorously conveys the idea of programming as a labor-intensive task.
- **examples:**
  - 我刚成为一名码农(我剛成為一名碼農)，感觉压力很大(感覺壓力很大)。
    - Wǒ gāng chéngwéi yī míng mǎnóng, gǎnjué yālì hěn dà.
    - I just became a programmer, and I feel a lot of pressure.
  - 作为一名码农(作為一名碼農)，我每天都在写代码(我每天都在寫代碼)。
    - Zuòwéi yī míng mǎnóng, wǒ měitiān dōu zài xiě dàimǎ.
    - As a programmer, I write code every day.
### 码农(碼農) → 码(碼)
- **definition:** code; number; measure; regulation
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **components:**
  - 犬(犭)
    - quǎn
    - dog
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 犭 (dog) suggesting 'to bark' and the phonetic component 马 (horse) which is pronounced 'mǎ'.
  - **interpretation:** The combination evokes an image of a dog barking, which may be associated with signaling or communication, thereby relating to the concept of a 'code' or 'symbol'. The horse phonetic implies speed or movement, further emphasizing communication.
  - **simplification:** It simplifies from the traditional form 碼 by using fewer strokes; the dog radical is modified for ease in modern writing.
- **examples:**
  - 这个代码(這個代碼)很复杂(很複雜)。
    - zhège dàimǎ hěn fùzá.
    - This code is very complex.
  - 请帮我解码(請幫我解碼)。
    - qǐng bāng wǒ jiěmǎ.
    - Please help me decode.
### 码农(碼農) → 码(碼) → 犬(犬)
- **definition:** dog
- **pinyin:** quǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** The side view of a dog.
  - **interpretation:** This character intuitively represents a dog as it shows its profile, capturing its distinctive features.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢狗(我喜歡狗)，他们很忠诚(他們很忠誠)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān gǒu, tāmen hěn zhōngchéng.
    - I like dogs, they are very loyal.
  - 家里有一只狗(家裡有一隻狗)，每天陪我散步(每天陪我散步)。
    - Jiālǐ yǒu yī zhī gǒu, měi tiān péi wǒ sànbù.
    - There is a dog at home, it walks with me every day.
### 码农(碼農) → 码(碼) → 马(馬)
- **definition:** horse
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a horse with its head facing left, showing a flowing mane.
  - **interpretation:** The horse's iconic shape and flowing mane are easily recognizable, making this character intuitive as a representation of a horse.
  - **simplification:** 马(馬) is simplified from 馬 following cursive script forms. The flowing mane is less detailed, and the legs become simplified dots.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢马(我喜歡馬)，尤其是赛马(賽馬)！
    - Wǒ xǐhuān mǎ, yóuqí shì sàimǎ！
    - I like horses, especially horse racing!
  - 马儿在草地上吃草(馬兒在草地上吃草)。
    - Mǎ er zài cǎodì shàng chī cǎo。
    - The horse is grazing in the grass.
### 码农(碼農) → 农(農)
- **definition:** farmer; agriculture; farming; cultivate
- **pinyin:** nóng
- **components:**
  - 農
    - nóng
    - agriculture
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic component 農 (nóng, indicating agriculture) and the phonetic component 𠳉 (which contributes to the pronunciation).
  - **interpretation:** The character illustrates the idea of cultivating land, with the farming aspect highlighted by the agriculture-related component.
- **examples:**

  - 我学习农业(我學習農業)。
    - Wǒ xuéxí nóngyè.
    - I study agriculture.
