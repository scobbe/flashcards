## 醉翁之意不在酒
---
## the real intention is not in the wine
### zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ
---
- **definition:** the real intention is not in the wine
- **pinyin:** zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ
- **components:**
  - 醉翁
    - zuìwēng
    - drunkard; elderly drinker; the Drunken Old Man (Ouyang Xiu)
  - 之意
    - zhī yì
    - intention; purpose; meaning; motive
  - 不在
    - bú zài
    - not lie in; not be at; not located in
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - wine; alcohol; liquor; drink
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** idiom (classical allusion)
  - **description:** 醉翁 + 之意 + 不在 + 酒 = the drunk old man's intention is not in the wine.
  - **interpretation:** From Ouyang Xiu’s Zuiweng Pavilion Essay, saying the drinker’s heart lies in the scenery and company rather than the wine. Now used to imply someone’s stated focus is a pretext and their true aim lies elsewhere.
- **examples:**
  - 他总请客吃饭(他總請客吃飯)，醉翁之意不在酒(醉翁之意不在酒)，其实是想谈合作(其實是想談合作)。
    - Tā zǒng qǐngkè chīfàn, zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ, qíshí shì xiǎng tán hézuò.
    - He often treats others to meals; the real intention isn’t the food or drink—he actually wants to discuss cooperation.
  - 商家搞低价促销(商家搞低價促銷)，醉翁之意不在酒(醉翁之意不在酒)，目的是抢占市场份额(目的是搶佔市場份額)。
    - Shāngjiā gǎo dījià cùxiāo, zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ, mùdì shì qiǎngzhàn shìchǎng fèn'é.
    - The merchant runs low-price promotions; the true aim isn’t the discount but to seize market share.
  - 他说来旅游(他說來旅遊)，醉翁之意不在酒(醉翁之意不在酒)，是想见老朋友(是想見老朋友)。
    - Tā shuō lái lǚyóu, zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ, shì xiǎng jiàn lǎo péngyou.
    - He said he came to travel; in fact, his real intention was to meet old friends.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 醉翁
- **definition:** drunkard; elderly drinker; the Drunken Old Man (Ouyang Xiu)
- **pinyin:** zuìwēng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 醉(drunk/intoxicated) + 翁(old man/elder) = drunken old man; reveler; literary persona (esp. Ouyang Xiu).
  - **interpretation:** Combines intoxication with a respectful term for an elder, evoking a genial, wine-loving gentleman. In classical usage, especially with Ouyang Xiu, it signals a persona who enjoys wine while seeking delight beyond it.
- **examples:**
  - 欧阳修自号醉翁(歐陽修自號醉翁)。
    - Ōuyáng Xiū zìhào Zuìwēng.
    - Ouyang Xiu styled himself Zuiweng.
  - 游人慕名来到醉翁亭，感受“醉翁之意不在酒”的意境(遊人慕名來到醉翁亭，感受「醉翁之意不在酒」的意境)。
    - Yóurén mùmíng láidào Zuìwēngtíng, gǎnshòu “Zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ” de yìjìng.
    - Visitors come to Zuiweng Pavilion to feel the mood of 'the drunken old man’s intent is not in the wine'.
  - 他喝得微醺，自称醉翁，自嘲又自乐(他喝得微醺，自稱醉翁，自嘲又自樂)。
    - Tā hē de wēixūn, zìchēng zuìwēng, zìcháo yòu zìlè.
    - Slightly tipsy, he called himself a drunken old man, half-mocking and half-pleased.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 之意
- **definition:** intention; purpose; meaning; motive
- **pinyin:** zhī yì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 之 (possessive/linker) + 意 (meaning; intention) = ‘the meaning/intention of (something)’
  - **interpretation:** Classical-style noun phrase where 之 marks possession or linkage, yielding ‘the meaning/intention of X’. Often follows a noun or clause in formal/literary Chinese to express attribution.
- **examples:**
  - 我无嘲讽之意(我無嘲諷之意)。
    - Wǒ wú cháofěng zhī yì.
    - I mean no sarcasm.
  - 此言并无冒犯之意(此言並無冒犯之意)。
    - Cǐ yán bìng wú màofàn zhī yì.
    - These words carry no intent to offend.
  - 他的沉默似有拒绝之意(他的沉默似有拒絕之意)。
    - Tā de chénmò sì yǒu jùjué zhī yì.
    - His silence seems to suggest a refusal.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 不在
- **definition:** not lie in; not be at; not located in
- **pinyin:** bú zài
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** negation + verb
  - **description:** 不 (not) + 在 (be at/in; be present) = 'not be in/at; not present; not lie in'.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the negative marker with the locative/existential verb 在 to indicate absence from a place or that a cause/quality does not reside in something. Common in patterns like 不在 + 地点 and 不在于 + 所在/方面.
- **examples:**
  - 他现在不在办公室(他現在不在辦公室)。
    - Tā xiànzài bù zài bàngōngshì.
    - He is not in the office right now.
  - 书不在桌上，可能在包里(書不在桌上，可能在包裡)。
    - Shū bù zài zhuō shàng, kěnéng zài bāo lǐ.
    - The book isn’t on the desk; it might be in the bag.
  - 成功不在于运气，而在于努力(成功不在於運氣，而在於努力)。
    - Chénggōng bù zàiyú yùnqì, ér zàiyú nǔlì.
    - Success does not lie in luck but in effort.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 酒
- **definition:** wine; alcohol; liquor; drink
- **pinyin:** jiǔ
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - 10th Earthly Branch; wine vessel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 水 + phonetic: 酉 (OC *luʔ); 酉 was the original pictogram for this word; 水 was added later to distinguish.
  - **interpretation:** Water indicates a liquid substance, while 酉 provides the sound and depicts a wine vessel, tying the meaning to fermented drinks. The later addition of the water component clarified that the word refers specifically to a liquid alcohol.
- **examples:**
  - 我不常喝酒(我不常喝酒)。
    - Wǒ bù cháng hē jiǔ.
    - I don't often drink alcohol.
  - 他带了一瓶米酒去聚会(他帶了一瓶米酒去聚會)。
    - Tā dài le yì píng mǐjiǔ qù jùhuì.
    - He brought a bottle of rice wine to the party.
  - 酒后不开车(酒後不開車)。
    - Jiǔ hòu bù kāi chē.
    - Do not drive after drinking.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 酒 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram of water flowing; sometimes written as the variant 氺; next to a character it is compressed and stylized into 氵 (unrelated to 冫).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts flowing water with a main stream and side droplets. In compound characters it condenses to the left-side form 氵 to signal a water-related meaning, distinct from 冫 which indicates ice or cold.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝一杯水(我想喝一杯水)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng hē yì bēi shuǐ.
    - I want to drink a glass of water.
  - 城市的自来水很干净(城市的自來水很乾淨)。
    - Chéngshì de zìláishuǐ hěn gānjìng.
    - The tap water in the city is very clean.
  - 旱季来了(旱季來了)，我们要节约用水(我們要節約用水)。
    - Hànjì lái le, wǒmen yào jiéyuē yòngshuǐ.
    - The dry season has arrived; we should conserve water.
### 醉翁之意不在酒 → 酒 → 酉
- **definition:** 10th Earthly Branch; wine vessel
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram (象形)
  - **description:** A wine vessel with a pointed bottom; original character for 酒.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a pointed-bottom wine vessel used in antiquity, which had to be set in a stand or into the ground. As the original graph for 酒 ‘alcohol’, it became associated with fermented beverages and later designated the tenth Earthly Branch.
- **examples:**
  - 现在是酉时(現在是酉時)。
    - Xiànzài shì yǒu shí.
    - It is the You hour (around 5–7 p.m.).
  - 地支的第十位是酉(地支的第十位是酉)。
    - Dìzhī de dì shí wèi shì yǒu.
    - The tenth Earthly Branch is You.
  - 卯酉之间代表东西方向(卯酉之間代表東西方向)。
    - Mǎo-yǒu zhījiān dàibiǎo dōngxī fāngxiàng.
    - The span from Mao to You represents the east–west direction.
