## wife of a younger brother
### lǐ
---
- **definition:** wife of a younger brother
- **pinyin:** lǐ
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - li (a unit of distance), neighborhood
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") + 里(里) (lǐ, phonetic) =
    - indicates a woman + pronunciation based on 里 ->
    - wife of a younger brother
  - **interpretation:** The character 娌 combines 女(女) (nǚ, "woman") to emphasize the female relation and 里(里) (lǐ) as a phonetic component, straightforwardly denoting a female family relationship, specifically the wife of one's younger brother.
- **examples:**
  - 我弟之娌十分聪慧(我弟之娌十分聰慧)。
    - Wǒ dì zhī lǐ shífēn cōnghuì.
    - My younger brother's wife is very intelligent.
  - 家中来客时(家中來客時)，弟娌总是恭敬待客(弟娌總是恭敬待客)。
    - Jiā zhōng lái kè shí, dì lǐ zǒng shì gōngjìng dàikè.
    - When guests come home, the wife of a younger brother always treats them respectfully.
### 娌(娌) → 女(女)
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman with breasts kneeling or standing ->
    - modern form reversed facing right
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") originally depicted a woman with pronounced breasts, illustrating the concept of 'woman.' Over time, the pictogram evolved to face right in its modern form, losing details like the left breast to simplification.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有魅力的女(她是一位有魅力的女)。
    - Tā shì yī wèi yǒu mèi lì de nǚ.
    - She is an attractive woman.
  - 那位女战士英勇无畏(那位女戰士英勇無畏)，带领大家前行(帶領大家前行)。
    - Nà wèi nǚ zhàn shì yīng yǒng wú wèi, dài lǐng dà jiā qián xíng.
    - That female warrior is brave and fearless, leading everyone forward.
### 娌(娌) → 里(里)
- **definition:** li (a unit of distance), neighborhood
- **pinyin:** lǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a field surrounded by boundaries, indicating a village or community.
  - **interpretation:** The character 里(里) (lǐ, "li; neighborhood") represents a walled area, resembling a unit of measurement for land or a small settlement.
- **examples:**
  - 学校离家有两里(學校離家有兩里)。
    - Xuéxiào lí jiā yǒu liǎng lǐ.
    - The school is two li away from home.
  - 村里有老人和小孩(村裡有老人和小孩)。
    - Cūn lǐ yǒu lǎorén hé xiǎohái.
    - There are elderly people and children in the village.
  - 家与学校相隔一里(家與學校相隔一里)，邻里互助共创和谐(鄰里互助共創和諧)。
    - Jiā yǔ xuéxiào xiānggé yī lǐ, lín lǐ hùzhù gòng chuàng héxié.
    - Home and school are one li apart, and neighbors work together to create harmony.
