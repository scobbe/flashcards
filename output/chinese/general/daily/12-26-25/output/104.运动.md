### exercise; sports
---
- **pinyin:** exercise; sports
- **components:**
  - 运动(運動)
    - yùndòng
    - exercise; sports
  - 运(運)
    - yùn
    - carry; transport; move
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - move; act; change
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 运(運) (yùn, "carry; transport; move") + 动(動) (dòng, "move; act; change") — moving activities
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 运(運) (yùn) and 动(動) (dòng) reflects the idea of engaging in activities that involve movement. This makes sense as it encompasses both the physical exertion of sports and general exercise.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都去运动。(我每天都去運動。)
    - Wǒ měi tiān dū qù yùndòng.
    - I go exercise every day.
  - 运动是保持健康的好方法。(運動是保持健康的好方法。)
    - Yùndòng shì bǎochí jiànkāng de hǎo fāngfǎ.
    - Exercise is a good way to maintain health.
### 运动(運動) → 运(運)
- **definition:** carry; transport; move
- **pinyin:** yùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** 运 combines the semantic element 运 (yùn) meaning 'to transport' with the phonetic element 运 (yùn), indicating its pronunciation.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes the idea of movement and transportation, aligning with its meaning of carrying or moving something from one place to another.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要运货(我需要運貨)。
    - Wǒ xūyào yùn huò.
    - I need to transport goods.
  - 他们运送食物(他們運送食物)。
    - Tāmen yùnsòng shíwù.
    - They transport food.
  - 这辆车很好用来运人(這輛車很好用來運人)。
    - Zhè liàng chē hěn hǎo yòng lái yùn rén.
    - This vehicle is great for carrying people.
### 运动(運動) → 动(動)
- **definition:** move; act; change
- **pinyin:** dòng
- **components:**
  - 力
    - lì
    - force
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Consists of the semantic element 力 (lì, 'force, strength') and the phonetic element 动 (dòng, 'move').
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 'force' and 'move' clearly conveys the idea of motion or action, emphasizing the power involved in moving or changing.
  - **simplification:** The character 动 (動) simplifies by replacing the traditional component '動' with '动' while retaining the phonetic sound.
- **examples:**
  - 这个机器很容易动(這個機器很容易動)。
    - Zhège jīqì hěn róngyì dòng.
    - This machine is easy to move.
  - 请不要动(請不要動)。
    - Qǐng bùyào dòng.
    - Please do not move.
### 运动(運動) → 动(動) → 力(力)
- **definition:** force
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arm bending out from the side of the body or a wooden plough.
  - **interpretation:** The representation of an arm suggests physical strength and effort, aligning with the character's meaning of strength.
- **examples:**
  - 我有力量(我有力量)。
    - wǒ yǒu lìliàng.
    - I have strength.
  - 通过努力(通過努力)，我变得更强壮(變得更強壯)。
    - tōngguò nǔlì, wǒ biàn dé gèng qiángzhuàng.
    - Through effort, I have become stronger.
