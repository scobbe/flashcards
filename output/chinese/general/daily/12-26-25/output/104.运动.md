## exercise; sports
### yùndòng
---
- **definition:** exercise; sports
- **pinyin:** yùndòng
- **components:**
  - 运(運)
    - yùn
    - to move; luck; fortune; to transport
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - movement; to move; action; to stir
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 运(運) (yùn, "to move") and 动(動) (dòng, "movement") combine to describe physical activity involving movement.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of moving (运(運)) and the concept of movement/activity (动(動)) naturally refers to physical activities, such as exercise or sports.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢运动(我喜歡運動)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yùndòng.
    - I like exercise.
  - 每天适量运动(每天適量運動)，可以保持健康(可以保持健康)。
    - Měitiān shìliàng yùndòng, kěyǐ bǎochí jiànkāng.
    - Moderate daily exercise can maintain health.
  - 早上起床后运动(早上起床後運動)，使一天充满活力(使一天充滿活力)。
    - Zǎoshang qǐchuáng hòu yùndòng, shǐ yītiān chōngmǎn huólì.
    - Exercising after getting up in the morning fills the day with energy.

- **definition:** to move; luck; fortune; to transport
- **pinyin:** yùn
- **components:**
  - 辶(辵)
    - chuò

    - yún

- **etymology:**

  - **description:**
    - 辵(辶) (chuò, "walk") + phonetic: 云(云) (yún) =
    - associated with movement ->
    - movement, transport
  - **interpretation:** The character 运(運) (yùn, "to move") combines the semantic 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk") with the phonetic 云(云) (yún), suggesting the idea of movement associated with walking.
  - **simplification:** 运(運): 辶(辶) (chuò, "walk") + 云(云) (yún) reduced from 雲(雲), keeping the phonetic component intact but simplifying the writing.
- **examples:**

### 运动(運動) → 运(運) → 云(雲)
- **definition:** cloud
- **pinyin:** yún
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound (會意 / 会意) and phono-semantic compound (形聲 / 形声)
  - **description:**
    - 云(云) (yún, "cloud") ->
    - rain radical 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") added for distinction ->
    - 雲(雲) (yún, "cloud")
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a cloud 云(云) (yún, "cloud") but when the character was borrowed to mean "to speak," the rain radical 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") was added to clarify the meaning of "cloud."
  - **simplification:** 雲(雲) reverted to its original form 云(云) (yún, "cloud") while separating the meaning of "cloud" from the borrowed meaning "to speak" which became its own character.
- **examples:**
  - 蓝天中一朵云缓缓移动(藍天中一朵雲緩緩移動)。
    - Lán tiān zhōng yī duǒ yún huǎn huǎn yí dòng.
    - A cloud moves slowly in the blue sky.
  - 冬日黄昏时(冬日黃昏時)，云低得似乎要落下(雲低得似乎要落下)。
    - Dōng rì huáng hūn shí, yún dī dé sì hū yào luò xià.
    - At winter dusk, the clouds seem about to fall.
