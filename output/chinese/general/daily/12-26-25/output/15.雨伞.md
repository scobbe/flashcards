## umbrella
### yǔsǎn
---
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella; parasol; shelter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") + 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") =
    - a device used for protection against rain
  - **interpretation:** The word 雨伞(雨傘) combines 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain"), representing the weather element, with 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella"), an object designed specifically to shield one from rain. This combination indicates a tool for keeping dry during rainy weather.
  - **simplification:** 伞 is the simplified form of 傘.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天带了雨伞(我今天帶了雨傘)。
    - wǒ jīntiān dài le yǔsǎn。
    - I brought an umbrella today.
  - 外面下雨(外面下雨)，别忘了拿雨伞(別忘了拿雨傘)。
    - wàimiàn xiàyǔ, bié wàngle ná yǔsǎn。
    - It is raining outside, don't forget to grab your umbrella.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 雨(雨)
- **definition:** rain
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it ->
    - horizontal stroke added later ->
    - rain
  - **interpretation:** 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") visually represents rain falling from a cloud, capturing its essential form.
- **examples:**
  - 今天雨下得很大(今天雨下得很大)。
    - Jīntiān yǔ xià de hěn dà.
    - It rained hard today.
  - 他忘记带伞(他忘記帶傘)，雨却毫不留情(雨卻毫不留情)。
    - Tā wàngjì dài sǎn, yǔ què háobù liúqíng.
    - He forgot to bring an umbrella, and the rain showed no mercy.
  - 细雨如丝(細雨如絲)，轻拂着花瓣(輕拂著花瓣)。
    - Xì yǔ rú sī, qīng fú zhe huā bàn.
    - The fine rain falls like silk, gently brushing the petals.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘)
- **definition:** umbrella; parasol; shelter
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **components:**
  - 𠈌
    - é
    - container
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𠈌 (container) + 人(人) (rén, "person") =
    - a person under a container shape ->
    - umbrella
  - **interpretation:** The character 傘 depicts a person beneath a structure resembling a cover or umbrella. This visually represents protection or covering, aligning with the meaning of 'umbrella' or 'shelter.'
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 傘 was simplified to 伞 by replacing 𠈌 with 丷, reducing the number of strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 她用伞遮阳(她用傘遮陽)。
    - Tā yòng sǎn zhē yáng.
    - She uses an umbrella to shield from the sun.
  - 下雨时(下雨時)，我常带伞(我常帶傘)。
    - Xià yǔ shí, wǒ cháng dài sǎn.
    - When it rains, I always carry an umbrella.
  - 暴风雨来临时(暴風雨來臨時)，那把伞成了我的庇护(那把傘成了我的庇護)。
    - Bào fēng yǔ lái lín shí, nà bǎ sǎn chéng le wǒ de bì hù.
    - When the storm comes, that umbrella becomes my shelter.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘) → 𠈌(𠈌)
- **definition:** container
- **pinyin:** é
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人(人) (rén, "person") quadruplicated to depict a container or enclosure formed by multiple people ->
    - 𠈌
  - **interpretation:** The character 𠈌 is formed by repeating the symbol for 'person' four times, symbolizing a container or enclosed area surrounded by people.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘) → 𠈌(𠈌) → 人(人)
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg ->
    - visual representation of a person
  - **interpretation:** The character 人(人) (rén, 'person') effectively uses simple lines to depict the essential form of a human, making it immediately recognizable and intuitive as meaning 'person.'
- **examples:**
  - 他是个好人(他是個好人)。
    - Tā shì gè hǎo rén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 每个人都有责任(每個人都有責任)，为社会作出贡献(為社會作出貢獻)。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu zérèn, wèi shèhuì zuòchū gòngxiàn.
    - Every person has a responsibility to contribute to society.
  - 人在旅途中(人在旅途中)，心灵获得了成长(心靈獲得了成長)。
    - Rén zài lǚtú zhōng, xīnlíng huòdé le chéngzhǎng.
    - People gain growth along their journey.
