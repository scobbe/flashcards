## umbrella
### yǔsǎn
---
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") + 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") =
    - a tool for rain protection
  - **interpretation:** An umbrella 雨伞(雨傘) (yǔsǎn, "umbrella") is an essential tool designed to provide protection from rain 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain").
- **examples:**
  - 我有一把漂亮的雨伞(我有一把漂亮的雨傘)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī bǎ piàoliang de yǔsǎn.
    - I have a beautiful umbrella.
  - 天气转冷时(天氣轉冷時)，雨伞成了常见的物品(雨傘成了常見的物品)。
    - Tiānqì zhuǎn lěng shí, yǔsǎn chéng le chángjiàn de wùpǐn.
    - When the weather turns cold, umbrellas become common items.
  - 下雨了(下雨了)，记得带上雨伞(記得帶上雨傘)。
    - Xiàyǔ le, jìde dài shàng yǔsǎn.
    - It's raining, remember to bring an umbrella.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 雨(雨)
- **definition:** rain
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it ->
    - final form with a horizontal stroke at the top
  - **interpretation:** The character 雨(yǔ, 'rain') visually represents rain droplets falling from a cloud, effectively conveying the concept of 'rain.' The horizontal stroke above possibly suggests the sky or clouds.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的雨很美(今天的雨很美)。
    - Jīntiān de yǔ hěn měi.
    - Today's rain is beautiful.
  - 初秋时分(初秋時分)，雨带着诗意降临(雨帶著詩意降臨)。
    - Chūqiū shífēn, yǔ dàizhe shīyì jiànglín.
    - In early autumn, the rain arrives with a poetic charm.
  - 雨过天晴(雨過天晴)，彩虹挂在天空(彩虹掛在天空)。
    - Yǔ guò tiānqíng, cǎihóng guà zài tiānkōng.
    - After the rain, the sky clears and a rainbow hangs in the sky.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘)
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified form
  - **description:**
    - 傘(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") ->
    - 𠈌(𠈌) (rán, "long-haired icon") used,丷(八) (bā, "divide") added ->
    - 伞(傘)
  - **interpretation:** The character 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") is a simplified version of 傘(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella"), which is a pictogram. In this simplification, the component 𠈌 was simplified to 丷, maintaining a similar visual representation of an umbrella.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by replacing the complex pictogram 𠈌(𠈌) with 丷(八) (bā, "divide") to streamline the shape while retaining the concept of a divided or spread structure resembling an umbrella.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢这把伞(我喜歡這把傘)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zhè bǎ sǎn.
    - I like this umbrella.
  - 下雨时(下雨時)，别忘记带伞(別忘記帶傘)。
    - Xià yǔ shí, bié wàngjì dài sǎn.
    - When it rains, don't forget to bring an umbrella.
  - 暴风雨来临(暴風雨來臨)，人们争先恐后拿伞(人們爭先恐後拿傘)。
    - Bào fēng yǔ láilín, rénmen zhēngxiān kǒnghòu ná sǎn.
    - When a storm comes, people rush to grab an umbrella.
