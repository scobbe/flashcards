## umbrella
### yǔsǎn
---
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella; parasol; shelter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") + 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") =
    - a device used for protection against rain
  - **interpretation:** The word 雨伞(雨傘) combines 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain"), representing the weather element, with 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella"), an object designed specifically to shield one from rain. This combination indicates a tool for keeping dry during rainy weather.
  - **simplification:** 伞 is the simplified form of 傘.
- **examples:**
  - 我今天带了雨伞(我今天帶了雨傘)。
    - wǒ jīntiān dài le yǔsǎn。
    - I brought an umbrella today.
  - 外面下雨(外面下雨)，别忘了拿雨伞(別忘了拿雨傘)。
    - wàimiàn xiàyǔ, bié wàngle ná yǔsǎn。
    - It is raining outside, don't forget to grab your umbrella.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 雨(雨)
- **definition:** rain
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it ->
    - horizontal stroke added later ->
    - rain
  - **interpretation:** 雨(雨) (yǔ, "rain") visually represents rain falling from a cloud, capturing its essential form.
- **examples:**
  - 今天雨下得很大(今天雨下得很大)。
    - Jīntiān yǔ xià de hěn dà.
    - It rained hard today.
  - 他忘记带伞(他忘記帶傘)，雨却毫不留情(雨卻毫不留情)。
    - Tā wàngjì dài sǎn, yǔ què háobù liúqíng.
    - He forgot to bring an umbrella, and the rain showed no mercy.
  - 细雨如丝(細雨如絲)，轻拂着花瓣(輕拂著花瓣)。
    - Xì yǔ rú sī, qīng fú zhe huā bàn.
    - The fine rain falls like silk, gently brushing the petals.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘)
- **definition:** umbrella; parasol; shelter
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **components:**
  - 丷
    - bā
    - eight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified from
  - **description:**
    - 𠈌(𠈌) (sǎn, “umbrella”) originally depicted four people under a canopy ->
    - simplified by replacing 𠈌 with 丷(丷, “eight”) ->
    - 伞(伞) (sǎn, “umbrella”)
  - **interpretation:** The character 伞(伞) (sǎn, “umbrella”) simplifies 傘(傘) by changing the complex canopy depiction to 丷(丷), maintaining the concept of separation under an umbrella.
  - **simplification:** 𠈌(𠈌) was simplified to 丷(丷), removing the detailed depiction of people for a simpler, more abstract representation.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用伞在雨中漫步(我喜歡用傘在雨中漫步)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng sǎn zài yǔzhōng mànbù.
    - I like to stroll in the rain with an umbrella.
  - 暴风雨来临时(暴風雨來臨時)，伞可以保护我们(傘可以保護我們)。
    - Bàofēngyǔ láilín shí, sǎn kěyǐ bǎohù wǒmen.
    - When a storm comes, an umbrella can protect us.
  - 夏日阳光下(夏日陽光下)，一把伞带来清凉(一把傘帶來清涼)。
    - Xiàrì yángguāng xià, yī bǎ sǎn dàilái qīngliáng.
    - Under the summer sunshine, an umbrella brings coolness.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘) → 丷(丷)
- **definition:** eight
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Represents division or separation
  - **interpretation:** The character 丷(bā, "eight") appears to depict a simple representation of a division, which relates to its usage in certain compound characters rather than directly representing the number eight.
- **examples:**
  - 我数了一下(我數了一下)，有丷个星辰(有丷個星辰)。
    - Wǒ shǔle yīxià, yǒu bā gè xīngchén.
    - I counted and found eight stars.
  - 丷是一个吉利的数字(丷是一個吉利的數字)。
    - Bā shì yī gè jílì de shùzì.
    - Eight is considered a lucky number.
  - 在庆典上(在慶典上)，号码丷显得特别醒目(號碼丷顯得特別醒目)。
    - Zài qìngdiǎn shàng, hàomǎ bā xiǎnde tèbié xǐngmù.
    - At the celebration, the number eight appeared especially striking.
