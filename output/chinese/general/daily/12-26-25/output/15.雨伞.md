---
## 
### umbrella
---
- **pinyin:** umbrella
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain; water; to rain; rainy
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella; parasol; screen; shade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 雨(雨) (yǔ, 'rain') + 伞(傘) (sǎn, 'umbrella') — rain umbrella
  - **interpretation:** The combination makes sense as an umbrella is specifically used to shield against rain. The word literally translates to 'rain umbrella', highlighting its primary function.
- **examples:**
  - 下雨了(下雨了)，我带上雨伞(我帶上雨傘)。
    - Xià yǔ le, wǒ dài shàng yǔ sǎn.
    - It started to rain, so I took my umbrella.
  - 外面很冷(外面很冷)，请记得带雨伞(請記得帶雨傘)。
    - Wàimiàn hěn lěng, qǐng jì dé dài yǔ sǎn.
    - It's very cold outside, please remember to take the umbrella.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 雨(雨)
- **definition:** rain; water; to rain; rainy
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it.
  - **interpretation:** The image of a cloud with rain falling conveys the concept of rain directly, making it intuitive to associate with water.
- **examples:**
  - 今天有雨，外面湿滑。(今天有雨，外面濕滑。)
    - jīntiān yǒu yǔ, wàimiàn shīhuá.
    - It is raining today, and the outside is slippery.
  - 明天预报会下雨。(明天預報會下雨。)
    - míngtiān yùbào huì xià yǔ.
    - Tomorrow's forecast says it will rain.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘)
- **definition:** umbrella; parasol; screen; shade
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **components:**
  - 亘
    - gèn
    - extend; span
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Simplified from 傘 (𠈌 → 丷), where 傘 depicts an umbrella with a covering.
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts an umbrella extending over a 木 (mù, 'tree'), signifying shelter from rain or sun.
  - **simplification:** The character was simplified by removing complex strokes and retaining a simpler form while preserving the meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 今天下雨，记得带伞(今天下雨，記得帶傘)！
    - jīntiān xià yǔ, jìde dài sǎn!
    - It's raining today, remember to take an umbrella!
  - 这个伞很大，能遮住很多地方(這個傘很大，能遮住很多地方)。
    - zhège sǎn hěn dà, néng zhē zhù hěn duō dìfāng.
    - This umbrella is very big and can cover a lot of areas.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘) → 亘(亘)
- **definition:** extend; span
- **pinyin:** gèn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** Represents the concept of extending across or spanning an area.
  - **interpretation:** The character suggests a continuous line or connection, evoking the idea of something stretching across a distance.
- **examples:**
  - 这条河亘古流淌(這條河亘古流淌)，见证了历史的变化(見證了歷史的變化)。
    - Zhè tiáo hé gèn gǔ liú tǎng, jiàn zhèng le lì shǐ de biàn huà.
    - This river has flowed since ancient times, witnessing the changes in history.
  - 他的观点亘古不变(他的觀點亘古不變)。
    - Tā de guān diǎn gèn gǔ bù biàn.
    - His viewpoint is unchanging through the ages.
### 雨伞(雨傘) → 伞(傘) → 木(木)
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a tree with its distinguishing features, conveying the idea of wood directly through its shape.
- **examples:**
  - 这棵树是木的代表。(這棵樹是木的代表。)
    - Zhè kē shù shì mù de dàibiǎo.
    - This tree is a representation of wood.
  - 木材是建筑的重要材料。(木材是建築的重要材料。)
    - Mùcái shì jiànzhú de zhòngyào cáiliào.
    - Wood is an important material for construction.
