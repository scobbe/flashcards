## same
### tóng
---
- **definition:** same
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a pipe ->
    - original form of 筒(筒) (tǒng, "pipe") ->
    - 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") added as decoration
  - **interpretation:** The character 同(同) (tóng, "together; same") originally depicted a pipe, with 口(口) (kǒu, "mouth") added later for decorative reasons, illustrating the idea of things coming together like a passageway.
- **examples:**
  - 我们同游公园(我們同遊公園)。
    - Wǒmen tóng yóu gōngyuán.
    - We tour the park together.
  - 他们穿着同样的衣服(他們穿著同樣的衣服)，看起来十分相似(看起來十分相似)。
    - Tāmen chuānzhe tóngyàng de yīfú, kàn qǐlái shífēn xiāngsì.
    - They wear the same clothes, and they look very similar.
