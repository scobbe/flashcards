## 开封(開封)
---
## 
### Kaifeng
---
- **pinyin:** Kaifeng
- **components:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open; start; drive; operate
  - 封
    - fēng
    - seal; close; confine; envelope
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 开(開) (kāi, "open") + 封 (fēng, "seal; close") — open seal
  - **interpretation:** The term 开封 (開封) suggests the notion of opening a sealed area or being a historical capital, which aligns with its historical significance as a political center. It embodies a sense of access and governance.
- **examples:**
  - 开封是一个美丽的城市。(開封是一個美麗的城市。)
    - Kāifēng shì yī gè měilì de chéngshì.
    - Kaifeng is a beautiful city.
  - 我们计划去开封旅游。(我們計畫去開封旅遊。)
    - Wǒmen jìhuà qù Kāifēng lǚyóu.
    - We plan to travel to Kaifeng.
### 开封(開封) → 开(開)
- **definition:** open; start; drive; operate
- **pinyin:** kāi
- **components:**
  - 門
    - mén
    - door
  - 一
    - yī
    - latch
  - 廾
    - gǒng
    - pair of hands
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** The character depicts a pair of hands (廾) opening a latched door (門) using a latch (一), symbolizing the action of opening.
  - **interpretation:** This representation makes intuitive sense as it visually conveys the action of two hands actively engaging with a door to open it.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by removing the structural component 門, resulting in 开, which retains the core meaning of opening.
- **examples:**
  - 我想开门。(我想開門。)
    - Wǒ xiǎng kāi mén.
    - I want to open the door.
  - 请打开窗户。(請打開窗戶。)
    - Qǐng dǎkāi chuānghù.
    - Please open the window.

### 开封(開封) → 封(封)
- **definition:** seal; close; confine; envelope
- **pinyin:** fēng
- **components:**
  - 豕
    - shǐ
    - pig
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Composed of the sound element 封 (fēng) and the meaning element 豕 (shǐ), which suggests sealing or confining something inside.
  - **interpretation:** The character portrays the act of enclosing or sealing, evoking a sense of confinement represented by the pig component. It intuitively suggests closing off something within.
- **examples:**
  - 信封(信封)，我用它寄信(我用它寄信)。
    - xìnfēng, wǒ yòng tā jì xìn.
    - I use an envelope to mail a letter.
  - 封锁(封鎖)，交通非常繁忙(交通非常繁忙)。
    - fēngsuǒ, jiāotōng fēicháng fánmáng.
    - The blockade makes traffic very busy.
