## Kaifeng
### Kāifēng
---
- **definition:** Kaifeng
- **pinyin:** Kāifēng
- **components:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open; start; turn on
  - 封
    - fēng
    - seal; envelope; confer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 開(開) (kāi, "open") + 封(封) (fēng, "seal")
  - **interpretation:** The name 開封(開封) (Kāifēng) represents the idea of 'opening a seal,' symbolizing the city as an important gateway and a hub of activity throughout its history in China.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算游览开封(開封)，体验古都风情(體驗古都風情)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn yóulǎn Kāifēng, tǐyàn gǔdū fēngqíng.
    - I plan to tour Kaifeng and experience the charm of the ancient capital.
  - 开封是历史名城(開封是歷史名城)。
    - Kāifēng shì lìshǐ míngchéng.
    - Kaifeng is a historic city.
### 开封(開封) → 开(開)
- **definition:** open; start; turn on
- **pinyin:** kāi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** 門(門) (mén, "door") + 一(一) (yī, "latch") + 廾(廾) (gǒng, "hands") → pair of hands opening latched door → opening
  - **interpretation:** The traditional form 開(開) (kāi, "open") symbolizes opening a door with hands, where 門(門) (mén, "door") represents the door itself, while 一(一) (yī, "latch") and 廾(廾) (gǒng, "hands") act together to indicate removal of a barrier.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 開 by eliminating 門(門) (mén, "door"), retaining only the 幵(幵) (components resembling two 干) which focuses on the concept of trunk parts, visually simplifying it without changing the meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 请开门(請開門)。
    - Qǐng kāi mén.
    - Please open the door.
  - 他开了灯(他開了燈)。
    - Tā kāile dēng.
    - He turned on the light.
  - 天微亮时(天微亮時)，商店开门营业(商店開門營業)。
    - Tiān wēiliàng shí, shāngdiàn kāimén yíngyè.
    - At dawn, the store opened for business.
### 开封(開封) → 封(封)
- **definition:** seal; envelope; confer
- **pinyin:** fēng
- **components:**
  - 丰
    - fēng
    - seedling
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - dirt
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 丰(丰) (fēng, "seedling") + 土(土) (tǔ, "dirt") + 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") ->
    - mark boundaries by planting trees
  - **interpretation:** 封(封) (fēng, "seal") originally depicted a seedling, symbolizing planting to mark territory. Adding 土(土) (tǔ, "dirt") and 寸(寸) (cùn, "hand") emphasizes marking boundaries by physically altering the landscape.
- **examples:**
  - 皇帝封他为大将军(皇帝封他為大將軍)。
    - Huángdì fēng tā wéi dà jiāngjūn。
    - The emperor conferred on him the title of general.
  - 她仔细封上信件(她仔細封上信件)，交给邮差(交給郵差)。
    - Tā zǐxì fēng shàng xìnjiàn, jiāo gěi yóuchāi。
    - She carefully sealed the letter and handed it to the mailman.
### 开封(開封) → 封(封) → 丰(豐)
- **definition:** seedling
- **pinyin:** fēng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a lush plant
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a plant with flourishing growth, representing abundance or richness, aligning with its meaning as 'bountiful' or 'lush.'
- **examples:**
  - 农夫种下的丰正悄然发芽(農夫種下的豐正悄然發芽)。
    - Nóngfū zhòng xià de fēng zhèng qiǎorán fāyá.
    - The seedling planted by the farmer is quietly sprouting.
  - 微风过境时(微風過境時)，幼小的丰开始展露新绿(幼小的豐開始展露新綠)。
    - Wēifēng guòjìng shí, yòuxiǎo de fēng kāishǐ zhǎnlù xīn lǜ.
    - When the gentle breeze passes by, the tiny seedling begins to show its fresh green.
### 开封(開封) → 封(封) → 土(土)
- **definition:** dirt
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a lump of clay either on the ground or on a potter’s wheel ->
    - stylized as a vertical stroke with a horizontal stroke
  - **interpretation:** The character 土(tǔ, 'earth') visually represents a mound or pile of earth, capturing the essence of soil or clay. The simplification to strokes reflects the transition to a more abstract and recognizable symbol.
- **examples:**
  - 农夫耕种土(農夫耕種土)。
    - Nóngfū gēngzhòng tǔ.
    - The farmer plows the earth.
  - 雨后湿润的土(雨後濕潤的土)，孕育着生命(孕育著生命)。
    - Yǔ hòu shīrùn de tǔ, yùnyù zhe shēngmìng.
    - The moist soil after the rain nurtures life.
  - 儿童在土上玩耍(兒童在土上玩耍)。
    - Értóng zài tǔ shàng wánshuǎ.
    - Children play on the ground.
### 开封(開封) → 封(封) → 寸(寸)
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogram (指事)
  - **description:**
    - Line on hand indicating pulse point on forearm ->
    - original form of 肘 (zhǒu, 'elbow') ->
    - unit of measurement derived from this distance
  - **interpretation:** 寸(cùn, 'inch') represents a specific point on the forearm used for pulse measurement, historically linked to its measurement definition. The form depicts a line that combines with the hand to signify this location, showing a logical evolution from anatomy to distance measurement.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布的宽度只有五寸(這塊布的寬度只有五寸)。
    - Zhè kuài bù de kuāndù zhǐyǒu wǔ cùn.
    - The width of this cloth is only five inches.
  - 木匠在制作家具时(木匠在製作家具時)，要精确到一寸(要精確到一寸)。
    - Mùjiàng zài zhìzuò jiājù shí, yào jīngquè dào yī cùn.
    - Carpenters must be precise to the inch when making furniture.
