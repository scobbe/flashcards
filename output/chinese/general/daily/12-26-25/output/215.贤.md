## virtuous; worthy
### xián
---
- **definition:** virtuous; worthy
- **pinyin:** xián
- **components:**
  - 臤
    - qiàn
    - strong
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - money
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 臤(臤) (qiàn, "strong") + semantic: 貝(貝) (bèi, "money") =
    - strong as money ->
    - worthy
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 臤(臤) (qiàn, "strong"), providing the pronunciation, with 貝(貝) (bèi, "money"), suggesting worth or value. This implies someone who is as valuable or strong as money, hence "virtuous" or "worthy."
  - **simplification:** 贤(賢): The 貝(貝) (bèi, "money") component was simplified by removing the inner details.
- **examples:**
  - 他是个贤德的人(他是個賢德的人)。
    - Tā shì gè xiándé de rén.
    - He is a virtuous person.
  - 学识渊博且贤良(學識淵博且賢良)，品行端正(品行端正)。
    - Xuéshí yuānbó qiě xiánliáng, pǐnxíng duānzhèng.
    - He is erudite and virtuous, with upright conduct.
  - 她总是以贤慧著称(她總是以賢慧著稱)。
    - Tā zǒngshì yǐ xiánhuì zhùchēng.
    - She is always known for her virtuous and kind nature.
### 贤(賢) → 臤(臤)
- **definition:** strong
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **components:**
  - 艮
    - gèn
    - stopping
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艮(艮) (gèn, "stopping") + 夫(夫) (fū, "man") =
    - a man who can stop or resist ->
    - strong
  - **interpretation:** The character 臤(qiàn, "strong") combines 艮(艮) (gèn, "stopping") as the semantic component, suggesting strength and resistance, with 夫(夫) (fū, "man") serving as the phonetic component, pointing to the idea of a strong person.
- **examples:**
  - 他的体魄臤(臤)。
    - Tā de tǐpò qiàn.
    - His physique is strong.
  - 这位老将军臤(臤)，千锤百炼不改旧志(千錘百煉不改舊志)。
    - Zhè wèi lǎo jiāngjūn qiàn, qiān chuí bǎi liàn bù gǎi jiù zhì.
    - This old general is strong, remaining steadfast despite countless trials.
  - 关羽武艺臤(臤)，忠义扬名天下(忠義揚名天下)。
    - Guān Yǔ wǔyì qiàn, zhōngyì yángmíng tiānxià.
    - Guan Yu's martial skills are strong, and his loyalty made his name renowned throughout the land.
### 贤(賢) → 臤(臤) → 艮(艮)
- **definition:** stopping
- **pinyin:** gèn
- **components:**
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 目(目) (mù, "eye") + 人(人) (rén, "person") =
    - a person looking back ->
    - disobedience/limit
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 目(目) (mù, "eye") and 人(人) (rén, "person") to depict someone looking back, symbolizing hesitation or the idea of a limit, suggesting the act of reconsideration or disobedience.
- **examples:**
  - 古文字中(古文字中)，艮作为表音符号较为常见(艮作為表音符號較為常見)。
    - Gǔ wénzì zhōng, gèn zuòwéi biǎoyīn fúhào jiào wéi chángjiàn.
    - In ancient scripts, 'gèn' is commonly used as a phonetic component.
  - 他欣赏艮的独特音调(他欣賞艮的獨特音調)。
    - Tā xīnshǎng gèn de dútè yīndiào.
    - He appreciates the unique tone of 'gèn'.
  - 诗人用艮作为音韵的象征(詩人用艮作為音韻的象徵)，表达内心的顿悟(表達內心的頓悟)。
    - Shīrén yòng gèn zuòwéi yīnyùn de xiàngzhēng, biǎodá nèixīn de dùnwù.
    - The poet uses 'gèn' as a symbol of phonetics to express inner enlightenment.
### 贤(賢) → 臤(臤) → 艮(艮) → 目(目)

- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(目) (mù, "eye") represents a human eye but is now commonly depicted rotated by 90 degrees. It serves as the basis for several other characters related to vision or perception.
- **examples:**
  - 医生检查我的目(醫生檢查我的目)，确认没有异常(確認沒有異常)。

    - Nà zhī māo de mù sìhū shǎnshuò zhìhuì.

### 贤(賢) → 臤(臤) → 夫(夫)
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts 大(大) (dà, "big") with an added stroke on top to indicate a head ->
    - powerful person ->
    - man/husband
  - **interpretation:** The character 夫(夫) (fū, "man") resembles the character 大(大) (dà, "big") with an additional stroke on top to represent the head. It suggests a powerful or authoritative figure, naturally extended to mean "man" or "husband."
- **examples:**
  - 他是个好夫(他是個好夫)。
    - Tā shì gè hǎo fū.
    - He is a good husband.
  - 妻子尊敬着贤夫(妻子尊敬著賢夫)，家庭生活充满和谐(家庭生活充滿和諧)。
    - Qīzi zūnjìng zhe xián fū, jiātíng shēnghuó chōngmǎn héxié.
    - The wife respects her virtuous husband, and the family life is full of harmony.
### 贤(賢) → 貝(貝)
- **definition:** money
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cowrie shell ->
    - used as currency in ancient China
  - **interpretation:** 貝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") depicts the distinctive shape of a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China due to its unique appearance and rarity.
- **examples:**
  - 他收藏一枚贝(他收藏一枚貝)，用作古代贸易的信物(用作古代貿易的信物)。
    - Tā shōucáng yī méi bèi, yòng zuò gǔdài màoyì de xìnwù.
    - He collects a cowry shell, using it as a token in ancient trade.
  - 清朝时贝(清朝時貝)，常被镶嵌于玉器(常被鑲嵌於玉器)。
    - Qīngcháo shí bèi, cháng bèi xiāngqiàn yú yùqì.
    - During the Qing dynasty, cowries were often inlaid in jade pieces.
