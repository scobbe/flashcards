### virtuous; worthy
---
- **pinyin:** virtuous; worthy
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - wealth
  - 賢
    - xián
    - virtuous
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** Combines the semantic component 貝 (bèi, 'wealth') with the phonetic component 賢 (xián, 'virtuous') to convey the idea of a person who is virtuous and worthy in relation to wealth.
  - **interpretation:** The concept of virtue (賢) is linked to wealth (貝), suggesting that a virtuous person should also be someone who properly handles wealth and resources.
  - **simplification:** The character has a simplified form that reduces the complexity of the components while maintaining the phonetic and semantic meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个非常贤良的人(他是一個非常賢良的人)。
    - tā shì yī gè fēi cháng xián liáng de rén.
    - He is a very virtuous person.
  - 贤者常被尊敬(賢者常被尊敬)。
    - xián zhě cháng bèi zūn jìng.
    - The virtuous are often respected.
  - 我们应该学习贤良的品德(我們應該學習賢良的品德)。
    - wǒ men yīng gāi xué xí xián liáng de pǐn dé.
    - We should learn virtuous qualities.
### 贤(賢) → 貝(貝)
- **definition:** wealth
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cowrie shell which was used as currency in ancient China.
  - **interpretation:** The character visually represents a cowrie shell, linking it directly to its historical use as money. This makes it intuitive as currency symbolizing wealth and trade.
- **examples:**
  - 我用貝(我用贝)买东西。
    - Wǒ yòng bèi mǎi dōngxi.
    - I use cowries to buy things.
  - 古代人用貝(古代人用贝)交易。
    - Gǔdài rén yòng bèi jiāoyì.
    - Ancient people used cowries for trade.
### 贤(賢) → 賢(賢)
- **definition:** virtuous
- **pinyin:** xián
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - precious, wealth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is formed by combining the semantic element 貝 (bèi, 'precious, wealth') with the phonetic element 賢 (xián, 'virtuous').
  - **interpretation:** The combination suggests that a virtuous person holds precious values, which enhances their reputation and integrity. The inclusion of wealth implies the value of virtuous behavior.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个非常贤良的人。
    - tā shì yīgè fēicháng xiánliáng de rén。
    - He is a very virtuous person.
  - 贤者通常会被尊重。
    - xiánzhě tōngcháng huì bèi zūnzhòng。
    - The virtuous are often respected.
