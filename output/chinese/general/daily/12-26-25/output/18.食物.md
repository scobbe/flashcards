## 食物(食物)
---
## 
### food
---
- **pinyin:** food
- **components:**
  - 食
    - shí
    - eat; food; meal; to consume
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing; object; matter; substance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 食(食) (shí, "eat") + 物(物) (wù, "thing") — eat thing
  - **interpretation:** This combination specifically refers to things that are consumed for nourishment. 食(食) (shí, "eat") indicates the action of consuming, while 物(物) (wù, "thing") refers to the items being consumed, thus creating a clear definition of food.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢所有的食物(我喜歡所有的食物)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān suǒyǒu de shíwù.
    - I like all kinds of food.
  - 这道菜是我最喜欢的食物(這道菜是我最喜歡的食物)。
    - Zhè dào cài shì wǒ zuì xǐhuān de shíwù.
    - This dish is my favorite food.
### 食物(食物) → 食(食)
- **definition:** eat; food; meal; to consume
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a mouth (亼) over a bowl of rice on a stand.
  - **interpretation:** The imagery of a mouth above a bowl of rice represents the action of eating, directly linking the symbol to its meaning. This visual connection intuitively conveys the concept of food consumption.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢食物(我喜歡食物)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān shíwù.
    - I like food.
  - 她每天吃三餐(她每天吃三餐)。
    - Tā měitiān chī sān cān.
    - She eats three meals a day.
  - 我们去吃晚饭(我們去吃晚飯)。
    - Wǒmen qù chī wǎnfàn.
    - We are going to have dinner.
### 食物(食物) → 物(物)
- **definition:** thing; object; matter; substance
- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 兀
    - wù
    - to stand or to exist
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** The character is composed of the phonetic component 兀 (wù) and the semantic component 牛 (niú, 'cow'). The combination suggests a connection to physical forms or substances.
  - **interpretation:** The cow may symbolize a tangible item, which aligns with the meaning of a material object or substance.
- **examples:**
  - 这件物品很有价值(這件物品很有價值)。
    - zhè jiàn wùpǐn hěn yǒu jiàzhí。
    - This item is very valuable.
  - 我在检查这些物品(我在檢查這些物品)。
    - wǒ zài jiǎnchá zhèxiē wùpǐn。
    - I am checking these items.
### 食物(食物) → 物(物) → 兀(兀)
- **definition:** to stand or to exist
- **pinyin:** wù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a standing figure or shape that suggests stability and existence.
  - **interpretation:** The character evokes a sense of something that stands firmly in place, representing existence.
- **examples:**
  - 兀自留心(兀自留心)，以免误事(以免誤事)。
    - wù zì liú xīn, yǐ miǎn wù shì.
    - Stand guard to avoid mistakes.
  - 兀鹫(兀鷲)在山顶(在山頂)飞翔(飛翔)。
    - wù jiù zài shān dǐng fēixiáng.
    - The vulture soars at the mountain peak.
### 食物(食物) → 物(物) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** cow
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the head of a cow, representing a domesticated animal used for labor and milk.
  - **interpretation:** The shape straightforwardly resembles the head of a cow, conveying the idea of the animal.
- **examples:**
  - 我家养了一头牛。(我家養了一頭牛。)
    - Wǒ jiā yǎngle yī tóu niú.
    - My family raises a cow.
  - 牛是重要的农作物。(牛是重要的農作物。)
    - Niú shì zhòngyào de nóngzuòwù.
    - Cows are important agricultural animals.
