## food
### shíwù
---
- **definition:** food
- **pinyin:** shíwù
- **components:**
  - 食
    - shí
    - eat; meal; food
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing; object; matter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 食(食) (shí, "eat") + 物(物) (wù, "thing") =
    - things that can be eaten ->
    - food
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense as it directly refers to 'things' that are meant to be 'eaten', hence 'food'.
- **examples:**
  - 他准备了美味的食物(他準備了美味的食物)。
    - Tā zhǔnbèi le měiwèi de shíwù.
    - He prepared delicious food.
  - 丰盛的食物吸引了客人(豐盛的食物吸引了客人)。
    - Fēngshèng de shíwù xīyǐn le kèrén.
    - The abundant food attracted the guests.
  - 市场上有各种食物(市場上有各種食物)。
    - Shìchǎng shàng yǒu gèzhǒng shíwù.
    - There is a variety of food in the market.
### 食物(食物) → 食(食)
- **definition:** eat; meal; food
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 亼(亼) (jí, 'gather') above 艮(艮) (gèn, 'stopping') ->
    - mouth over bowl of rice ->
    - eating
  - **interpretation:** 食(食) (shí, 'eat') originally depicted a mouth over a bowl of rice, conveying the act of eating. This evolved into the current form, retaining the visual representation of consuming food.
- **examples:**
  - 我食快餐(我食快餐)。
    - Wǒ shí kuàicān.
    - I eat fast food.
  - 他食晚餐(他食晚餐)，然后出门(然後出門)。
    - Tā shí wǎncān, ránhòu chūmén.
    - He eats dinner, then goes out.
  - 古人云：食色性也(古人云：食色性也)。
    - Gǔrén yún: shí sè xìng yě.
    - The ancients said, 'Food and sex are human nature.'
### 食物(食物) → 物(物)
- **definition:** thing; object; matter
- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
  - 勿
    - wù
    - do not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") + 勿(勿) (wù, "do not") =
    - cow + phonetic 勿 ->
    - object, thing
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 牛(牛) (niú, "cow"), symbolizing an object of value or importance, with 勿(勿) (wù, "do not"), which provides the phonetic component, to denote a general sense of an object or thing.
- **examples:**
  - 这个物令人惊讶(這個物令人驚訝)。
    - Zhè ge wù lìng rén jīng yà.
    - This object is surprising.
  - 这个物很古老(這個物很古老)，充满传说(充滿傳說)。
    - Zhè ge wù hěn gǔ lǎo, chōng mǎn chuán shuō.
    - This object is ancient and full of legends.
### 食物(食物) → 物(物) → 牛(牛)
- **definition:** cow
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bull's head with two horns and two ears
  - **interpretation:** The character 牛(牛) (niú, "cow") visually represents the distinct features of a bull, with the two upward curving horns and the two ears forming its shape. This direct pictographic representation makes it easily recognizable and intuitive as a depiction of a cow.
- **examples:**
  - 农夫养牛(農夫養牛)。
    - Nóngfū yǎng niú.
    - The farmer raises cows.
  - 田野开阔(田野開闊)，牛自由漫步(牛自由漫步)。
    - Tiányě kāikuò, niú zìyóu mànbù.
    - In the vast fields, the cows stroll freely.
### 食物(食物) → 物(物) → 勿(勿)
- **definition:** do not
- **pinyin:** wù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an ancient depiction of fingers pinching a cloth indicating 'not to handle'
  - **interpretation:** 勿(勿) (wù, "do not") originates as a pictogram symbolizing a taboo against handling. The character resembles fingers pinching cloth, metaphorically representing prohibition.
- **examples:**
  - 考试前勿紧张(考試前勿緊張)。
    - kǎoshì qián wù jǐnzhāng
    - Do not be nervous before the exam.
  - 勿忘初衷(勿忘初衷)，保持清醒(保持清醒)。
    - wù wàng chūzhōng, bǎochí qīngxǐng
    - Do not forget your original intention, remain clear-headed.
  - 勿触碰禁区(勿觸碰禁區)。
    - wù chùpèng jìnqū
    - Do not touch the forbidden area.
