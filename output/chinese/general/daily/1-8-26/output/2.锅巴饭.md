### guō bā fàn
---
- **definition:** rice dish made with crispy scorched rice from the bottom of the pot
- **pinyin:** guō bā fàn
- **components:**

  - 巴
    - bā

  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鍋(鍋) (guō, "pot") + 巴(巴) (bā, "cling") + 飯(飯) (fàn, "cooked rice")
