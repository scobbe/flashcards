### Wǔ Bā Tóngchéng
---
- **definition:** Chinese online classifieds platform
- **pinyin:** Wǔ Bā Tóngchéng
- **components:**
  - 同
    - tóng
    - same; together
  - 城
    - chéng
    - city; town
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 同 (tóng, "same; together") signifies unity or being alike, while 城 (chéng, "city") refers to a town or urban area. Combined, 同城 (tóngchéng) intuitively represents 'within the same city', suggesting locality or community.
  - **interpretation:** 同 (tóng, "same; together") evokes the idea of uniformity or being in unison. 城 (chéng, "city") conveys the physical space of an urban area. Thus, the combination refers to the concept of being in the same urban space, highlighting a shared locality or community context.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )在( )同( )城( )网(網)上找到了一个好工作。
    - Wǒ zài Tóngchéng wǎng shàng zhǎodào le yī gè hǎo gōngzuò.

    - Tóngchéng píngtái tígōng le xǔduō zūfáng xìnxī.

  - **description:**
