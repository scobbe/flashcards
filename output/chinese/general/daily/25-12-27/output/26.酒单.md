## drinks menu
### jiǔ dān
---
- **traditional:** 酒單
- **definition:** drinks menu
- **pinyin:** jiǔ dān
- **components:**
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol; liquor; spirits; wine
  - 单(單)
    - dān
    - list; bill; form; single
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") refers to alcoholic beverages in general. 單 (dān, "list") signifies a list or menu of items.
  - **interpretation:** 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") names the main content of interest, while 單 (dān, "list") provides the format, resulting in a 'drinks menu' — a list specifically for alcoholic beverages.
- **examples:**

### 酒( )单(單) → 单(單)
- **traditional:** 單
- **definition:** list; bill; form; single
- **pinyin:** dān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Y-shaped wooden stick with stones ->
    - hunting or war weapon ->
    - resemblance to 中 ->
    - stylized to 田
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting a Y-shaped weapon with stones suggesting a hunting tool, the character evolved visually, resembling a pole or stick (中), later taking a form similar to 田. This innovative stylization reveals its original purpose as a practical tool for warfare or hunting, thereby informing its association with tasks and lists in the modern sense.
  - **simplification:** 单(單): The character was simplified by removing the complex elements within 田, reducing it to a more direct form that still suggests the idea of a singular, straightforward entity akin to a list.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)给(給)我( )一( )张(張)清( )单(單)。
