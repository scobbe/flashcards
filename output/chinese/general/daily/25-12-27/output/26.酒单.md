## drinks menu
### jiǔ dān
---
- **traditional:** 酒單
- **definition:** drinks menu
- **pinyin:** jiǔ dān
- **components:**
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol; liquor; spirits; wine
  - 单(單)
    - dān
    - list; bill; form; single
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") refers to alcoholic beverages in general. 單 (dān, "list") signifies a list or menu of items.
  - **interpretation:** 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") names the main content of interest, while 單 (dān, "list") provides the format, resulting in a 'drinks menu' — a list specifically for alcoholic beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 我想看看酒单(單)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng kàn kàn jiǔ dān.
    - I would like to see the drinks menu.
  - 酒单(單)上有很多选(選)择(擇)。
    - Jiǔ dān shàng yǒu hěn duō xuǎnzé.
    - There are many options on the drinks menu.
  - 我们(們)决(決)定从(從)酒单(單)上点(點)红(紅)酒。
    - Wǒmen juédìng cóng jiǔ dān shàng diǎn hóngjiǔ.
    - We decided to order red wine from the drinks menu.
### 酒单(單) → 酒
- **definition:** alcohol; liquor; spirits; wine
- **pinyin:** jiǔ
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - complete cycle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") =
    - a liquid associated with 酉 (fermentation/vessels) ->
    - alcohol
  - **interpretation:** The character 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") uses 氵 (shuǐ, "water") to indicate a liquid, and 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") to suggest fermentation or storage vessels, representing alcoholic beverages.
- **examples:**
  - 他喝了太多酒。
    - Tā hēle tài duō jiǔ.
    - He drank too much alcohol.
  - 夜深人静(靜)时(時)，他独(獨)自品酒。
    - Yè shēn rén jìng shí, tā dúzì pǐn jiǔ.
    - In the quiet of the night, he savored his drink alone.
  - 那杯香醇的酒，令他回味无(無)穷(窮)。
    - Nà bēi xiāngchún de jiǔ, lìng tā huíwèi wúqióng.

### 酒单(單) → 酒 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。

  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.

### 酒单(單) → 酒 → 酉
- **definition:** complete cycle
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **components:**
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a wine vessel with a pointed bottom ->
    - original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol")
  - **interpretation:** 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") originally depicted a wine vessel, indicating the integral role of alcohol in traditional cycles, such as moon phases or ceremonial events. It is the original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol"), highlighting its use in fermentation processes.
- **examples:**

    - Every time I look back, it seems to witness a complete cycle coming to an end.

### 酒单(單) → 单(單)
- **traditional:** 單
- **definition:** list; bill; form; single
- **pinyin:** dān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Y-shaped wooden stick with stones ->
    - hunting or war weapon ->
    - resemblance to 中 ->
    - stylized to 田
  - **interpretation:** Initially depicting a Y-shaped weapon with stones suggesting a hunting tool, the character evolved visually, resembling a pole or stick (中), later taking a form similar to 田. This innovative stylization reveals its original purpose as a practical tool for warfare or hunting, thereby informing its association with tasks and lists in the modern sense.
  - **simplification:** 单(單): The character was simplified by removing the complex elements within 田, reducing it to a more direct form that still suggests the idea of a singular, straightforward entity akin to a list.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)给(給)我一张(張)清单(單)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī zhāng qīngdān.
    - Please give me a list.
  - 他列出了需要购(購)买(買)的物品清单(單)。
    - Tā liè chūle xūyào gòumǎi de wùpǐn qīngdān.
    - He made a list of items to purchase.
  - 她准(準)备(備)了一份客人的名单(單)。
    - Tā zhǔnbèi le yī fèn kèrén de míngdān.
    - She prepared a list of guests.
