## make do with a meal
### còu còu huǒ
---
- **traditional:** 湊湊火
- **definition:** make do with a meal
- **pinyin:** còu còu huǒ
- **components:**
  - 凑(湊)
    - còu
    - gather; put together; improvise; approach
  - 火
    - huǒ
    - fire; flame; cooking; urgent
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 凑(湊) (còu, "gather; put together") suggests the idea of bringing elements together, often with the sense of improvisation or making do. 火 (huǒ, "fire") here metaphorically represents cooking or preparing food, as fire is essential for cooking. Combining 凑(湊) and 火, the phrase illustrates the concept of hurriedly gathering and preparing a meal.
  - **interpretation:** In the expression 湊湊火 (còu còu huǒ), the sense of 'make do with a meal' is captured by the notion of 凑(湊) (còu, "gather")—implying hastily collecting resources or ingredients. 火 (huǒ, "fire") is symbolic of cooking or the act of preparing food. The phrase thus conveys the idea of quickly putting together a meal using whatever is available, akin to making do with the resources at hand.
### 凑(湊)凑(湊)火 → 凑(湊)
- **traditional:** 湊
- **definition:** gather; put together; improvise; approach
- **pinyin:** còu
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 奏
    - zòu
    - perform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 奏 (zòu) =
    - actions associated with water flowing together ->
    - gather together
  - **interpretation:** The character 湊 (còu, "gather together") uses 氵 (shuǐ, "water") to suggest actions akin to water pooling or coming together, with 奏 (zòu) providing the sound. This indicates the coming together of elements or people.
  - **simplification:** 湊 was simplified to 凑(湊) by removing the water radical 氵 (shuǐ, "water"), keeping the phonetic 奏 (zòu), simplifying the structure.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)凑(湊)些钱(錢)，买(買)生日礼(禮)物。

    - Tā còu shàng lǐnjū de qián, jiějué le jíshì.
    - He gathered money from his neighbors to deal with an urgent matter.
  - 没(沒)有足够(夠)的材料，她只好凑(湊)合做饭(飯)。
    - Méiyǒu zúgòu de cáiliào, tā zhǐhǎo còu hé zuò fàn.

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
