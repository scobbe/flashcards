### qiū kuí

- **definition:** okra
- **pinyin:** qiū kuí
- **components:**
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn
  - 葵
    - kuí
    - mallow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 秋 (qiū, "autumn") 葵 (kuí, "mallow")
  - **interpretation:** The character 秋 (qiū, "autumn") reflects the harvest season, associated with crops reaching maturity, and identifies the time for harvesting many vegetables. 葵 (kuí, "mallow") represents okra as it belongs to the mallow family. Thus, 秋葵 (qiū kuí) describes okra as a plant that matures and is harvested around autumn, belonging to the mallow family.
- **examples:**
  - 秋葵是绿(綠)色的蔬菜。
    - Qiūkuí shì lǜsè de shūcài.
    - Okra is a green vegetable.
  - 我最喜欢(歡)炒秋葵。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuān chǎo qiūkuí.
    - I like fried okra the most.
  - 中餐时(時)，我们(們)吃了秋葵汤(湯)。
    - Zhōngcān shí, wǒmen chīle qiūkuí tāng.
    - During lunch, we had okra soup.
