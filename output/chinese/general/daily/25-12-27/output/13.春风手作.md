## Chun Feng handmade
### chūn fēng shǒu zuò
---
- **traditional:** 春風手作
- **definition:** Chun Feng handmade
- **pinyin:** chūn fēng shǒu zuò
- **components:**
  - 春風
    - chūnfēng
    - spring wind
  - 手作
    - shǒuzuò
    - handmade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word 春风手作 (春風手作) is a compound word combining the elements 春风(風) (chūnfēng, "spring wind") and 手作 (shǒuzuò, "handmade").
  - **interpretation:** 春风(風) (chūnfēng, "spring wind") symbolizes the beginning or renewal as spring represents growth and new life. The gentle and refreshing qualities of 春风(風) (chūnfēng, "spring wind") metaphorically convey a sense of creativity and rejuvenation found in handmade crafts. 手作 (shǒuzuò, "handmade") directly indicates something crafted by hand, emphasizing personal touch and uniqueness. Together, the phrase implies crafting items with the refreshing, renewing energy of spring, suggesting creativity and artistry.
- **examples:**
  - 春风(風)手作的产(產)品很受欢(歡)迎。
    - Chūn fēng shǒu zuò de chǎnpǐn hěn shòu huānyíng.
    - Chun Feng handmade products are very popular.
  - 我喜欢(歡)春风(風)手作的设(設)计(計)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chūn fēng shǒu zuò de shèjì.
    - I like the design of Chun Feng handmade.
  - 春风(風)手作每件作品都很独(獨)特。
    - Chūn fēng shǒu zuò měi jiàn zuòpǐn dōu hěn dútè.
    - Every piece from Chun Feng handmade is unique.

  - **interpretation:** The character 昨 (zuó, "yesterday") combines the semantic component 日 (rì, "sun"), representing the day, with the phonetic component 乍 (zhà), which provides the sound. The combination intuitively indicates the concept of 'yesterday,' which is the day that has passed.
- **examples:**
  - 昨天我和朋友去看电(電)影。
    - Zuótiān wǒ hé péngyǒu qù kàn diànyǐng.
    - Yesterday, my friend and I went to watch a movie.
  - 他昨天做了什么(麼)？
    - Tā zuótiān zuòle shénme?
    - What did he do yesterday?
  - 昨天下了雨，所以路上很湿(濕)。
    - Zuótiān xiàle yǔ, suǒyǐ lùshàng hěn shī.
    - It rained yesterday, so the roads are very wet.
