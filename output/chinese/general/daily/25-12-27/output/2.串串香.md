## skewered hot pot
### chuàn chuàn xiāng
---
- **definition:** skewered hot pot
- **pinyin:** chuàn chuàn xiāng
- **components:**
  - 串串
    - chuànchuàn
    - a series of skewers
  - 香
    - xiāng
    - fragrant; aromatic; savory
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** In this word, 串 (chuàn, "skewer") is the main concept of skewering food; repeated to 串串 (chuànchuàn, "a series of skewers"), and 香 (xiāng, "fragrant") refers to the aroma from cooking or seasoning, describing the aromatic quality of the dish.
  - **interpretation:** 串串 (chuànchuàn, "a series of skewers") signifies multiple skewered items, a defining characteristic of this dining style where small portions are cooked on skewers. 香 (xiāng, "fragrant") emphasizes the appealing scent released as the ingredients cook in the hot pot broth, drawing people in with its aroma. This describes a type of hot pot where individual skewers of ingredients are cooked in a communal pot, appealing both visually and aromatically.
- **examples:**

    - Wǒmen zuótiān wǎnshàng qù chī le chuàn chuàn xiāng.
    - We went to eat skewered hot pot last night.
  - 小( )李( )特( )别(別)喜( )欢(歡)成( )都( )的( )串( )串( )香( )。
    - Xiǎo Lǐ tèbié xǐhuān Chéngdū de chuàn chuàn xiāng.
    - Xiao Li especially likes Chengdu's skewered hot pot.
  - 串( )串( )香( )的( )汤(湯)底( )有( )很( )多( )种(種)口( )味( )，可( )以( )根( )据(據)自( )己( )的( )喜( )好( )选(選)择(擇)。
