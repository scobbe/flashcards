- **definition:** Cungen Gai Old Style Hot Pot
- **pinyin:** cún gēn gài · bù gǎi lǎo huǒ guō
- **components:**
  - 存根

    - lǎo

  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 存根 (cún gēn, "retain the base"); 蓋 (gài, "cover"); 不改 (bù gǎi, "do not change"); 老 (lǎo, "old"); 火鍋 (huǒ guō, "hot pot")
