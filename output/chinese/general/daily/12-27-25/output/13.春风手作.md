### chūn fēng shǒu zuò
---
- **traditional:** 春風手作
- **definition:** Chun Feng handmade
- **pinyin:** chūn fēng shǒu zuò

- **etymology:**

    - I purchased Chun Feng Handmade's handicrafts and experienced the brand's warmth.
