## 庆(慶)
---
- **pinyin:** qìng
- **definition:** celebrate
- **etymology:** From 慶 (elimination of bottom right component to 大) in the 1956 Chinese Character Simplification Scheme by the People's Republic of China.
- **examples:**
  - 举国同庆这一历史性的时刻(舉國同慶這一歷史性的時刻)。
    - jǔ guó tóng qìng zhè yī lì shǐ xìng de shí kè
    - The whole country celebrates this historic moment.
  - 公司开业(公司開業)，设宴以庆(設宴以慶)。
    - gōngsī kāiyè, shè yàn yǐ qìng
    - The company opened and held a banquet to celebrate.
