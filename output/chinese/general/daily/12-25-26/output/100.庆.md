## 庆(慶)
---
- **pinyin:** qìng
- **definition:** celebrate
- **etymology:** 庆(慶) (qìng, "celebrate") is a phono-semantic formation: 心 (xīn, "heart; feelings") supplies the emotion sense, while 殸 (qìng, "stone chime"; the phonetic seen in 磬 (qìng, "stone chime") and 罄 (qìng, "empty/exhaust")) links it to ritual music and joyous rites. The simplified form streamlines the shape under 广 (guǎng, "building/house") with 大 (dà, "big"), visually hinting at a grand celebration in a hall while preserving the original meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 朋友们齐聚一堂(朋友們齊聚一堂)，同庆她的喜讯(同慶她的喜訊)。
    - Péngyǒumen qíjù yì táng, tóngqìng tā de xǐxùn.
    - Friends gathered together to celebrate her good news.
