## 释迦(釋迦)
---
- **pinyin:** shìjiā
- **definition:** sugar apple
- **characters:**
  - 释(釋)
    - shì
    - explain, elucidate; release, set free; dispel, resolve; Buddhism
  - 迦
    - jiā
    - used in transliterations, esp. Sanskrit and Buddhist terms
- **etymology:** Combines 释(釋) (shì, "Buddhism; Shakya") with 迦 (jiā, "used in transliterations") to render the transliteration “Śākya,” i.e., 释(釋)迦牟尼 (Shakyamuni), and by visual metaphor names the fruit whose knobby segments resemble the Buddha’s coiled hair.
- **examples:**
  - 台湾台东的释迦很有名(臺灣臺東的釋迦很有名)。
    - Táiwān Táidōng de shìjiā hěn yǒumíng.
    - Sugar apples from Taitung, Taiwan are famous.
  - 成熟的释迦果肉很甜(成熟的釋迦果肉很甜)，口感很绵密(口感很綿密)。
    - Chéngshú de shìjiā guǒròu hěn tián, kǒugǎn hěn miánmì.
    - When ripe, the flesh of a sugar apple is very sweet with a creamy texture.
