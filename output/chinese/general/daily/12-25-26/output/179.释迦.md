## 释迦(釋迦)
---
- **pinyin:** shìjiā
- **definition:** sugar apple
- **characters:**
  - 释(釋)
    - shì
    - explain
  - 迦
    - jiā
    - transliteration syllable
- **etymology:** Combines 释(釋) (shì, "explain") with 迦 (jiā, "transliteration syllable") to express sugar apple.
- **examples:**
  - 台东的释迦很有名(台東的釋迦很有名)。
    - táidōng de shìjiā hěn yǒumíng
    - The sugar apples from Taitung are very famous.
  - 成熟的释迦很甜(成熟的釋迦很甜)，果肉像奶油一样(果肉像奶油一樣)。
    - chéngshú de shìjiā hěn tián, guǒròu xiàng nǎiyóu yīyàng
    - Ripe sugar apples are very sweet, and the flesh is as creamy as butter.
