## 带走(帶走)
---
- **pinyin:** dàizǒu
- **definition:** take away
- **characters:**
  - 带(帶)
    - dài
    - bring
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - go
- **etymology:** Combines 带(帶) (dài, "bring") with 走 (zǒu, "go") to express take away.
- **examples:**
  - 警察把他带走了(警察把他帶走了)。
    - jǐngchá bǎ tā dàizǒu le
    - The police took him away.
