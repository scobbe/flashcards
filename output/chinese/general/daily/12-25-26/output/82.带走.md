## 带走(帶走)
---
- **pinyin:** dàizǒu
- **definition:** take away
- **characters:**
  - 带(帶)
    - dài
    - belt, band, ribbon; zone, region; to bring, take along, carry; to have, be equipped with
  - 走
    - zǒu
    - to walk; to go, leave, depart; to travel, move; to run away, flee
- **etymology:** Combines 带(帶) (dài, "to bring/carry") with 走 (zǒu, "to go/leave") to convey "carry as you go," i.e., removing something by taking it along when departing.
- **examples:**
  - 这份炒饭可以带走吗(這份炒飯可以帶走嗎)？
    - Zhè fèn chǎofàn kěyǐ dàizǒu ma?
    - Can I get this fried rice to go?
  - 警察把他带走了(警察把他帶走了)。
    - Jǐngchá bǎ tā dàizǒu le.
    - The police took him away.
