## 商场(商場)
---
- **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
- **definition:** shopping mall
- **characters:**
  - 商
    - shāng
    - commerce
  - 场(場)
    - chǎng
    - field
- **examples:**
  - 我们在新开的商场里逛了一下午(我們在新開的商場裡逛了一下午)。
    - wǒmen zài xīn kāi de shāngchǎng lǐ guàng le yí xiàwǔ
    - We strolled around the newly opened shopping mall all afternoon.
  - 这家商场周末打折(這家商場週末打折)，人特别多(人特別多)。
    - zhè jiā shāngchǎng zhōumò dǎzhé, rén tèbié duō
    - This shopping mall offers discounts on weekends, and it's really crowded.
