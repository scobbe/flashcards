## 商场(商場)
---
- **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
- **definition:** shopping mall
- **characters:**
  - 商
    - shāng
    - commerce, business; to discuss, consult; merchant, trader; quotient
  - 场(場)
    - chǎng, cháng
    - field, open space, venue; yard, grounds, threshing floor; measure word for events, performances, natural phenomena; scene, occasion
- **etymology:** 商 (shāng, "commerce") with 场(場) (chǎng, "field/venue") forms the idea of a "field for commerce," originally an open market ground and by extension a modern shopping mall.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算周末去新开的商场逛逛(我打算週末去新開的商場逛逛)，顺便买几件衣服(順便買幾件衣服)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn zhōumò qù xīn kāi de shāngchǎng guàngguang, shùnbiàn mǎi jǐ jiàn yīfu.
    - I’m planning to check out the newly opened mall this weekend and pick up a few clothes.
