## 话(話)
---
- **pinyin:** huà
- **definition:** speech; word; talk
- **examples:**
  - 领导的开场话很简短(領導的開場話很簡短)。
    - lǐngdǎo de kāichǎng huà hěn jiǎnduǎn
    - The leader's opening speech was brief.
  - 别说脏话！(別說髒話！)。
    - bié shuō zānghuà
    - Don't say dirty words!
  - 我们改天找个时间好好说话(我們改天找個時間好好說話)。
    - wǒmen gǎitiān zhǎo gè shíjiān hǎohāo shuōhuà
    - Let's find time another day to have a proper talk.
