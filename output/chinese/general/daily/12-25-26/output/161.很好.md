## 很好
---
- **pinyin:** hěn hǎo
- **definition:** very good
- **characters:**
  - 很
    - hěn
    - very, quite
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine, well, nice; OK, all right; easy to, convenient; very, quite, so
- **etymology:** Degree adverb 很 (hěn, "very") modifies the adjective 好 (hǎo, "good"), yielding "very good." In modern Mandarin, 很 often also serves as a neutral predicative marker before adjectives, so it can read as mildly emphatic or simply as "is good" depending on context.
- **examples:**
  - 你的发音很好(你的發音很好)。
    - Nǐ de fāyīn hěn hǎo.
    - Your pronunciation is very good.
  - 这家餐厅的服务很好(這家餐廳的服務很好)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng de fúwù hěn hǎo.
    - The service at this restaurant is very good.
