## 四季豆
---
- **pinyin:** sìjìdòu
- **definition:** green bean; string bean
- **characters:**
  - 四: sì, four
  - 季: jì, season
  - 豆: dòu, bean
- **examples:**
  - 这道清炒四季豆很脆嫩(這道清炒四季豆很脆嫩) (zhè dào qīng chǎo sìjìdòu hěn cuìnèn, "These stir-fried green beans are very crisp and tender")
  - 我买了一些四季豆准备做沙拉(我買了一些四季豆準備做沙拉) (wǒ mǎi le yìxiē sìjìdòu zhǔnbèi zuò shālā, "I bought some string beans to make a salad")
