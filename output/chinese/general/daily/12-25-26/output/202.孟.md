## 孟
---
- **pinyin:** Mèng
- **definition:** surname Meng
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 子 (zǐ, "child") supplies the semantic idea of kinship/age order, while 皿 (mǐn, "dish") serves as the phonetic. Originally “eldest (child)” and later extended to a surname; visually, a child sits above the dish-shaped phonetic.
- **examples:**
  - 我姓孟(我姓孟)。
    - Wǒ xìng Mèng.
    - My surname is Meng.
  - 孟先生明天会到(孟先生明天會到)。
    - Mèng xiānshēng míngtiān huì dào.
    - Mr. Meng will arrive tomorrow.
