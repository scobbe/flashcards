## 卫生(衛生)
---
- **pinyin:** wèishēng
- **definition:** hygiene
- **characters:**
  - 卫(衛)
    - wèi
    - protect, defend; guard, bodyguard; surname Wèi; ancient state of Wei
  - 生
    - shēng
    - be born; give birth to; raw, uncooked; student
- **etymology:** Combines 卫(衛) (wèi, "protect") with 生 (shēng, "life") to mean "protecting life," which extends to the practices that preserve health—hygiene.
- **examples:**
  - 医生强调良好的个人卫生可以预防疾病(醫生強調良好的個人衛生可以預防疾病)，尤其是在流感季节(尤其是在流感季節)。
    - Yīshēng qiángdiào liánghǎo de gèrén wèishēng kěyǐ yùfáng jíbìng, yóuqí shì zài liúgǎn jìjié.
    - The doctor emphasized that good personal hygiene can prevent disease, especially during flu season.
