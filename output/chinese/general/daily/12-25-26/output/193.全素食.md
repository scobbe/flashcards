## 全素食
---
- **pinyin:** quánsùshí
- **definition:** vegan diet
- **characters:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - whole, entire; all; complete; completely
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain, simple, unadorned; vegetarian; white; usually, normally
  - 食
    - shí, sì
    - food; to eat; meal; to feed
- **etymology:** 全 (quán, "complete") intensifies 素 (sù, "vegetarian/plain") before 食 (shí, "food; to eat") to mean eating that is entirely vegetarian—i.e., vegan.
- **examples:**
  - 我在医生的建议下改成全素食(我在醫生的建議下改成全素食)，现在感觉更有精神(現在感覺更有精神)。
    - Wǒ zài yīshēng de jiànyì xià gǎi chéng quánsùshí, xiànzài gǎnjué gèng yǒu jīngshén.
    - On my doctor’s advice, I switched to a vegan diet, and now I feel more energetic.
