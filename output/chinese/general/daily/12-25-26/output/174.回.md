## 回
---
- **pinyin:** huí
- **definition:** return; turn back
- **etymology:** Pictograph showing an inner square enclosed by an outer boundary, evoking something going back into a space—hence the sense of "return; turn back." Traditionally analyzed as 口 (kǒu, "mouth") inside an enclosure, but 口 here is purely graphic rather than semantic or phonetic.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天下班就回家(我每天下班就回家)。
    - Wǒ měitiān xiàbān jiù huí jiā.
    - I go back home after work every day.
  - 我们走错路了(我們走錯路了)，回头吧(回頭吧)。
    - Wǒmen zǒu cuò lù le, huí tóu ba.
    - We went the wrong way; let's turn back.
