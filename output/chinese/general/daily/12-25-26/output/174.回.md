## 回
---
- **pinyin:** huí
- **definition:** return; turn back
- **etymology:** A character meaning return; turn back.
- **examples:**
  - 下班后我就回家(下班後我就回家)。
    - xiàbān hòu wǒ jiù huí jiā
    - I return home after work.
  - 前面封路了，我们只好回转(我們只好回轉)。
    - qiánmiàn fēng lù le, wǒmen zhǐhǎo huízhuǎn
    - The road ahead is closed, so we have to turn back.
