## 龙华(龍華)
---
- **pinyin:** Lónghuá
- **definition:** Longhua
- **characters:**
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon; imperial, of the emperor; surname Long
  - 华(華)
    - huá, huà
    - splendid, magnificent; China; Chinese; surname Hua
- **etymology:** Combines 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon")—an auspicious, imperial symbol—with 华(華) (huá, "splendid") to form an elegant, favorable proper name for places and institutions. It also echoes the Buddhist “dragon-flower” motif, lending a sacred, cultured resonance.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在龙华见面吧(我們在龍華見面吧)，那里有很多咖啡馆(那裡有很多咖啡館)。
    - Wǒmen zài Lónghuá jiànmiàn ba, nàlǐ yǒu hěn duō kāfēiguǎn.
    - Let’s meet in Longhua; there are plenty of cafés there.
  - 他搬到深圳龙华区工作(他搬到深圳龍華區工作)，现在通勤很方便(現在通勤很方便)。
    - Tā bān dào Shēnzhèn Lónghuá Qū gōngzuò, xiànzài tōngqín hěn fāngbiàn.
    - He moved to Shenzhen’s Longhua District for work, and commuting is very convenient now.
