## 龙华(龍華)
---
- **pinyin:** Lónghuá
- **definition:** Longhua
- **characters:**
  - 龙(龍): lóng, dragon
  - 华(華): huá, Chinese
- **examples:**
  - 我住在深圳市龙华区(我住在深圳市龍華區) (wǒ zhù zài shēnzhèn shì lónghuá qū, "I live in Longhua District of Shenzhen")
  - 上海的龙华寺很有名(上海的龍華寺很有名) (shànghǎi de lónghuá sì hěn yǒumíng, "Shanghai's Longhua Temple is very famous")
