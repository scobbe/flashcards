## 龙华(龍華)
---
- **pinyin:** Lónghuá
- **definition:** Longhua
- **characters:**
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - Chinese
- **etymology:** Auspicious might of 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon") paired with the flourishing "flower/splendor" sense of 华(華) (huá, "splendor; flower") evokes thriving, noble prosperity; in Buddhist tradition 龍華 names the 'Dragon-Flower' assemblies of Maitreya under the 龍華樹, so place and temple names like Longhua invoke that auspice and promise.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在深圳市龙华区(我住在深圳市龍華區)。
    - wǒ zhù zài shēnzhèn shì lónghuá qū
    - I live in Longhua District of Shenzhen.
  - 上海的龙华寺很有名(上海的龍華寺很有名)。
    - shànghǎi de lónghuá sì hěn yǒumíng
    - Shanghai's Longhua Temple is very famous.
