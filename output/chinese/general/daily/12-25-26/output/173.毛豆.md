## 毛豆
---
- **pinyin:** máodòu
- **definition:** edamame
- **characters:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair, fur, down, wool; 0.1 yuan, jiao; surname Mao
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean, legume; surname Dou; ancient ceremonial food vessel
- **etymology:** 毛 (máo, "hair/fur") describes the fine fuzz on immature soybean pods, while 豆 (dòu, "bean") names the legume—together meaning a 'hairy bean' referring to the fuzzy young soybeans eaten as edamame.
- **examples:**
  - 在居酒屋里(在居酒屋裡)，大家一边喝啤酒一边吃毛豆(大家一邊喝啤酒一邊吃毛豆)。
    - Zài jūjiǔwū lǐ, dàjiā yìbiān hē píjiǔ yìbiān chī máodòu.
    - At the izakaya, everyone drinks beer while snacking on edamame.
