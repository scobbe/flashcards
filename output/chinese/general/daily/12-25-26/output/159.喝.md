## 喝
---
- **pinyin:** hē
- **definition:** drink
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") gives the semantic cue of a mouth action, while 曷 (hé, "why") serves as the phonetic, approximating the sound hē. The left 口 pictographically depicts an open mouth, fitting the idea of ingesting liquid.
- **examples:**
  - 我早上喜欢喝咖啡(我早上喜歡喝咖啡)。
    - Wǒ zǎoshang xǐhuan hē kāfēi.
    - I like to drink coffee in the morning.
