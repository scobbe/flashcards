## 梳子
---
- **pinyin:** shūzi
- **definition:** comb
- **characters:**
  - 梳
    - shū
    - to comb (hair); to sort out, straighten out; comb
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child, son; seed, kernel; noun suffix, diminutive; master, philosopher
- **etymology:** 梳 (shū, "to comb") combines with 子 (zǐ, "noun suffix; diminutive") to nominalize the action, naming the small tool used for combing.
- **examples:**
  - 她拿起梳子(她拿起梳子)，轻轻地梳头(輕輕地梳頭)。
    - Tā ná qǐ shūzi, qīngqīng de shū tóu.
    - She picked up the comb and gently combed her hair.
