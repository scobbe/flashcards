## 梳子
---
- **pinyin:** shūzi
- **definition:** comb
- **characters:**
  - 梳
    - shū
    - to comb
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:** 梳 (shū, "to comb") already evokes a wooden implement via its 木 component and the sense of disentangling, while 子 (zǐ, "child") functions as a long-standing diminutive/instrumental noun suffix in the vernacular for small handheld tools; together they mean the concrete “combing-thing,” i.e., the comb, traditionally made of wood.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要一把梳子(我需要一把梳子)。
    - wǒ xūyào yì bǎ shūzi
    - I need a comb.
  - 她用梳子梳头(她用梳子梳頭)。
    - tā yòng shūzi shū tóu
    - She uses a comb to comb her hair.
