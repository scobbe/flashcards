## 汽水
---
- **pinyin:** qìshuǐ
- **definition:** sparkling water
- **characters:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - steam
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一瓶常温的汽水(請給我一瓶常溫的汽水)，不加柠檬(不加檸檬)。
    - qǐng gěi wǒ yì píng chángwēn de qìshuǐ, bù jiā níngméng
    - Please give me a bottle of sparkling water at room temperature, without lemon.
