## 汽水
---
- **pinyin:** qìshuǐ
- **definition:** sparkling water
- **characters:**
  - 汽
    - qì
    - steam, vapor; gas; automotive (in compounds)
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water; river, stream; liquid
- **etymology:** Combines 汽 (qì, "steam; vapor; gas"), signaling gaseous/effervescent quality, with 水 (shuǐ, "water"), yielding "gas-water"—water infused with bubbles, i.e., sparkling/carbonated water.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝纯的汽水(我喜歡喝純的汽水)，没有糖(沒有糖)，也没有味道(也沒有味道)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan hē chún de qìshuǐ, méiyǒu táng, yě méiyǒu wèidào.
    - I like to drink plain sparkling water; it has no sugar and no flavor.
