## 民主
---
- **pinyin:** mínzhǔ
- **definition:** democracy
- **characters:**
  - 民
    - mín
    - people, populace; civilian; nationality, ethnic group; private, non-governmental
  - 主
    - zhǔ
    - master, owner, host; main, primary; to advocate, to propose; God, the Lord
- **etymology:** 民 (mín, "people") with 主 (zhǔ, "master; to be in charge") conveys that the people are the masters, i.e., rule by the people.
- **examples:**
  - 在这个国家(在這個國家)，人民享有民主选举的权利(人民享有民主選舉的權利)。
    - Zài zhège guójiā, rénmín xiǎngyǒu mínzhǔ xuǎnjǔ de quánlì.
    - In this country, the people enjoy the right to democratic elections.
