## 大白菜
---
- **pinyin:** dàbáicài
- **definition:** Chinese cabbage
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great; eldest; adult, grown-up
  - 白
    - bái
    - white; pure, plain; in vain, for nothing; free of charge
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable, greens; dish; cuisine; unskilled, noob (slang)
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big"), 白 (bái, "white"), and 菜 (cài, "vegetable") combine as a straightforward descriptor meaning "big white vegetable," aptly naming the large, pale napa cabbage commonly eaten in Chinese cuisine.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一棵大白菜(我買了一棵大白菜)，准备做酸菜(準備做酸菜)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī kē dàbáicài, zhǔnbèi zuò suāncài.
    - I bought a head of Chinese cabbage and plan to make pickled cabbage.
