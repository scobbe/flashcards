## 精制品(精製品)
---
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **definition:** refined products
- **characters:**
  - 精: jīng, refined
  - 制(製): zhì, make
  - 品: pǐn, product
- **examples:**
  - 这家企业专门生产高附加值的精制品(這家企業專門生產高附加值的精製品) (zhè jiā qǐyè zhuānmén shēngchǎn gāo fùjiāzhí de jīngzhìpǐn, "This company specializes in producing high value-added refined products")
  - 市场对石油精制品的需求在冬季会增加(市場對石油精製品的需求在冬季會增加) (shìchǎng duì shíyóu jīngzhìpǐn de xūqiú zài dōngjì huì zēngjiā, "Demand for petroleum refined products increases in winter")
