## 精制品(精製品)
---
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **definition:** refined products
- **characters:**
  - 精
    - jīng
    - essence; refined, pure, fine; skilled, proficient; spirit, vitality
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make, manufacture; system, institution; control, restrain; formulate, draw up
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods, product; quality, grade; taste, savor; character, moral quality
- **etymology:** 精 (jīng, "refined, pure") modifies 制(製) (zhì, "make, manufacture") to denote fine processing, while 品 (pǐn, "goods, product") names the resulting items—together, "refined products".
- **examples:**
  - 炼油厂生产的精制品包括汽油和柴油(煉油廠生產的精製品包括汽油和柴油)。
    - Liànyóuchǎng shēngchǎn de jīngzhìpǐn bāokuò qìyóu hé cháiyóu.
    - The refinery's refined products include gasoline and diesel.
