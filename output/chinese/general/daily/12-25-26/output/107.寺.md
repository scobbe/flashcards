## 寺
---
- **pinyin:** sì
- **definition:** temple
- **etymology:** Semantic compound: top 土 (tǔ, "earth/place") and bottom 寸 (cùn, "inch; hand/authority") depict administering affairs at an official site/altar, yielding the sense "government office." In Han times, bureaus were called 寺 (sì, "office"), and the label was extended to Buddhist monasteries, hence "temple."
- **examples:**
  - 这座古老的寺坐落在山脚下(這座古老的寺坐落在山腳下)。
    - Zhè zuò gǔlǎo de sì zuòluò zài shānjiǎo xià.
    - This ancient temple sits at the foot of the mountain.
  - 我们在寺里听到了钟声(我們在寺裡聽到了鐘聲)。
    - Wǒmen zài sì lǐ tīng dào le zhōngshēng.
    - We heard the bell inside the temple.
