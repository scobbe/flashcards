## 寺
---
- **pinyin:** sì
- **definition:** temple
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: phonetic 𡳿 + semantic 又 (yòu, "hand") – to grasp, to hold.
- **examples:**
  - 这座寺很古老(這座寺很古老)。
    - zhè zuò sì hěn gǔlǎo
    - This temple is very old.
  - 他在寺里出家了(他在寺裡出家了)。
    - tā zài sì lǐ chūjiā le
    - He became a monk at the temple.
