## 热敏(熱敏)
---
- **pinyin:** rèmǐn
- **definition:** thermo-sensitive
- **characters:**
  - 热(熱)
    - rè
    - hot; heat; fever; enthusiastic, fervent
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - quick, agile; clever; keen, sharp; sensitive
- **etymology:** 热(熱) (rè, "heat") modifies 敏 (mǐn, "sensitive"), forming a stimulus–response compound meaning "sensitive to heat."
- **examples:**
  - 这种热敏纸遇到热就会变色(這種熱敏紙遇到熱就會變色)。
    - Zhè zhǒng rèmǐn zhǐ yù dào rè jiù huì biànsè.
    - This kind of thermal paper changes color when exposed to heat.
