## 斯蒂芬
---
- **pinyin:** Sīdìfēn
- **definition:** Stephen
- **characters:**
  - 斯
    - sī
    - this
  - 蒂
    - dì
    - fruit stalk
  - 芬
    - fēn
    - fragrance
- **etymology:** Combines 斯 (sī, "this"), 蒂 (dì, "fruit stalk"), and 芬 (fēn, "fragrance") to convey stephen.
- **examples:**
  - 斯蒂芬今天工作很忙(斯蒂芬今天工作很忙)。
    - Sīdìfēn jīntiān gōngzuò hěn máng
    - Stephen is very busy at work today.
