## 小皇帝
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎohuángdì
- **definition:** spoiled child
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, unimportant; less than
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - emperor; imperial, royal; majestic, august
  - 帝
    - dì
    - emperor, sovereign; god, deity; supreme ruler
- **etymology:** 小 (xiǎo, "small/young") modifies 皇 (huáng, "imperial") and 帝 (dì, "emperor") to yield a "little emperor," metaphorically a child indulged as if ruling the household.
- **examples:**
  - 在家里他像个小皇帝(在家裡他像個小皇帝)，所有人都得让着他(所有人都得讓著他)。
    - Zài jiālǐ tā xiàng gè xiǎo huángdì, suǒyǒu rén dōu děi ràngzhe tā.
    - At home he’s like a little emperor; everyone has to give in to him.
