## 小皇帝
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎohuángdì
- **definition:** spoiled child
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - imperial
  - 帝
    - dì
    - emperor
- **etymology:** Combines 小 (xiǎo, "small"), 皇 (huáng, "imperial"), and 帝 (dì, "emperor") to convey spoiled child.
- **examples:**
  - 爷爷奶奶把他当小皇帝(爺爺奶奶把他當小皇帝)，什么要求都满足(什麼要求都滿足)。
    - yéye nǎinai bǎ tā dàng xiǎo huángdì, shénme yāoqiú dōu mǎnzú
    - His grandparents treat him like a spoiled child and satisfy his every demand.
