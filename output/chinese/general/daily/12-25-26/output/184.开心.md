## 开心(開心)
---
- **pinyin:** kāixīn
- **definition:** happy
- **characters:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open; start, begin; turn on, switch on; drive, operate (a vehicle, machine)
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; mind; intention, thought; center, core
- **etymology:** Combines 开(開) (kāi, "open") with 心 (xīn, "heart") to evoke an "opened heart," a metaphor for feelings unblocked and a cheerful state—thus happiness.
- **examples:**
  - 见到你(見到你)，我很开心(我很開心)。
    - Jiàn dào nǐ, wǒ hěn kāixīn.
    - I'm happy to see you.
