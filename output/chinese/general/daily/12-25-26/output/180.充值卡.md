## 充值卡
---
- **pinyin:** chōngzhíkǎ
- **definition:** recharge card
- **characters:**
  - 充
    - chōng
    - to fill, replenish; to charge, recharge; to act as, serve as; to pass for, pretend to be
  - 值
    - zhí
    - value; worth, to be worth; to be on duty; to happen to, coincide with
  - 卡
    - kǎ/qiǎ
    - card; to get stuck, jam, choke; checkpoint, barrier; calorie
- **etymology:** 充 (chōng, "to fill/recharge") combines with 值 (zhí, "value") to mean 'add value,' and 卡 (kǎ, "card") names the medium—together, a card used to top up stored value, metaphorically 'filling' the card with credit.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一张手机充值卡(我買了一張手機充值卡)，给妈妈的号码充了话费(給媽媽的號碼充了話費)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yí zhāng shǒujī chōngzhíkǎ, gěi māma de hàomǎ chōng le huàfèi.
    - I bought a mobile phone recharge card and topped up my mom’s number.
  - 这张游戏充值卡(這張遊戲充值卡)只能在大陆服务器使用(只能在大陸伺服器使用)。
    - Zhè zhāng yóuxì chōngzhíkǎ zhǐ néng zài Dàlù fúwùqì shǐyòng.
    - This game recharge card can only be used on Mainland servers.
