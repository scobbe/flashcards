## 江山
---
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **definition:** landscape; country
- **characters:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river; Yangtze River; surname Jiang
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain, hill; pile, heap; surname Shan
- **etymology:** 江 (jiāng, "river") with 山 (shān, "mountain") pairs the two fundamental features of terrain to denote the natural landscape, which by metonymy extends to the country's territory and realm under rule.
- **examples:**
  - 远望群山连绵(遠望群山連綿)，江山如画(江山如畫)。
    - Yuǎn wàng qúnshān liánmián, jiāngshān rú huà.
    - Looking from afar, the mountains roll on and on—the landscape looks like a painting.
  - 改朝换代之后(改朝換代之後)，江山易主(江山易主)。
    - Gǎi cháo huàn dài zhīhòu, jiāngshān yì zhǔ.
    - After a change of dynasty, the country passes into new hands.
