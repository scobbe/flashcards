## 江山
---
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **definition:** landscape; country
- **characters:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
- **etymology:** Combines 江 (jiāng, "river") with 山 (shān, "mountain") to express landscape; country.
- **examples:**
  - 这里的江山如画(這裡的江山如畫)，游客流连忘返(遊客流連忘返)。
    - zhè lǐ de jiāngshān rú huà, yóukè liúlián wàngfǎn
    - The landscape here is picturesque, and visitors are reluctant to leave.
  - 他为守护江山社稷而奉献一生(他為守護江山社稷而奉獻一生)。
    - tā wèi shǒuhù jiāngshān shèjì ér fèngxiàn yìshēng
    - He devoted his life to safeguarding the country.
