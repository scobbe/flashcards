## 中南海
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngnánhǎi
- **definition:** Zhongnanhai
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng, zhòng
    - middle, center; among, within; China; hit the mark, be hit
  - 南
    - nán
    - south, southern; surname Nan
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea, ocean; maritime, nautical; vast number, multitude; surname Hai
- **etymology:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") and 南 (nán, "south") identify the two lakes, while 海 (hǎi, "sea") is the historical term for these Beijing lakes. Together they name the area around the Middle and South Lakes, which by metonymy denotes the leadership compound.
- **examples:**
  - 中南海位于北京西城区(中南海位於北京西城區)，是中国中央政府和中共中央的重要办公地(是中國中央政府和中共中央的重要辦公地)。
    - Zhōngnánhǎi wèiyú Běijīng Xīchéng Qū, shì Zhōngguó zhōngyāng zhèngfǔ hé Zhōnggòng Zhōngyāng de zhòngyào bàngōngdì.
    - Zhongnanhai is located in Beijing’s Xicheng District; it is an important office area for the Chinese central government and the Communist Party’s Central Committee.
  - 很多重大政策都在中南海制定(很多重大政策都在中南海制定)。
    - Hěn duō zhòngdà zhèngcè dōu zài Zhōngnánhǎi zhìdìng.
    - Many major policies are formulated in Zhongnanhai.
