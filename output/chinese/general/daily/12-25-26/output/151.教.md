## 教
---
- **pinyin:** jiāo
- **definition:** teach
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: phonetic 爻 + semantic 子 (zǐ, "child") + semantic 攵 (pū, "teaching cane; stick that represents authority") – teaching a child.
- **examples:**
  - 老师教学生中文(老師教學生中文)。
    - lǎoshī jiāo xuéshēng zhōngwén
    - The teacher teaches the students Chinese.
