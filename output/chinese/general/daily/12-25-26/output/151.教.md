## 教
---
- **pinyin:** jiāo
- **definition:** teach
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left side 孝 (xiào, "filial piety") supplies the sound and evokes transmitting filial norms, while right-side 攴 (pū, "tap/rap") depicts a hand with a stick indicating an instructive action. The imagery suggests imparting proper conduct through guidance/correction, hence "teach."
- **examples:**
  - 他在大学教历史(他在大學教歷史)。
    - Tā zài dàxué jiāo lìshǐ.
    - He teaches history at the university.
