## 全
---
- **pinyin:** quán
- **definition:** complete; whole
- **etymology:** A character meaning complete; whole.
- **examples:**
  - 信息不全(資訊不全)，无法做决定(無法做決定)。
    - xìnxī bù quán, wúfǎ zuò juédìng
    - The information is incomplete, so it's impossible to make a decision.
  - 全世界都在关注这件事(全世界都在關注這件事)。
    - quán shìjiè dōu zài guānzhù zhè jiàn shì
    - The whole world is paying attention to this matter.
