## 全
---
- **pinyin:** quán
- **definition:** complete; whole
- **etymology:** In early forms, 人 (rén, "person") stands above 王 (wáng, "jade/king"), picturing a person preserving an intact piece of jade, hence "complete/whole." A semantic compound: 人 suggests guarding/keeping, while 王 evokes a prized object (玉 (yù, "jade")), tying the visual to the idea of intactness.
- **examples:**
  - 请把表格填全(請把表格填全)。
    - Qǐng bǎ biǎogé tián quán.
    - Please fill out the form completely.
  - 今天全家都来了(今天全家都來了)。
    - Jīntiān quán jiā dōu lái le.
    - The whole family came today.
