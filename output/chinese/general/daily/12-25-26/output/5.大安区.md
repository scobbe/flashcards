## 大安区
---
- **pinyin:** Dà’ān Qū
- **definition:** Da’an District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; major, main; eldest, senior
  - 安
    - ān
    - peaceful, calm; safe, secure; install, fix; settle, pacify
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - area, region, district, zone; distinguish, differentiate
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big") and 安 (ān, "peaceful") combine into an auspicious sense of great peace, modifying 区(區) (qū, "district") to denote the administrative area literally as "Great Peace District."
- **examples:**
  - 我住在台北市大安区(我住在臺北市大安區)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Táiběi Shì Dà’ān Qū.
    - I live in Da’an District, Taipei.
  - 大安森林公园在大安区(大安森林公園在大安區)。
    - Dà’ān Sēnlín Gōngyuán zài Dà’ān Qū.
    - Daan Forest Park is in Da’an District.
