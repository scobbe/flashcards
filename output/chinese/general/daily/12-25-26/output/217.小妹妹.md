## 小妹妹
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèimei
- **definition:** little sister; young girl
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, lesser; petty, trivial
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister; young woman, girl
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister; young woman, girl
- **etymology:** 小 (xiǎo, "little") modifies the reduplicated 妹 (mèi, "younger sister"), with reduplication adding affection/diminutiveness, yielding "little sister" and by extension "young girl".
- **examples:**
  - 今天下午我带我的小妹妹去公园玩(今天下午我帶我的小妹妹去公園玩)。
    - Jīntiān xiàwǔ wǒ dài wǒ de xiǎo mèimei qù gōngyuán wán.
    - This afternoon I took my little sister to the park to play.
  - 那位小妹妹很勇敢(那位小妹妹很勇敢)，主动帮我捡起掉在地上的书(主動幫我撿起掉在地上的書)。
    - Nà wèi xiǎo mèimei hěn yǒnggǎn, zhǔdòng bāng wǒ jiǎnqǐ diào zài dì shàng de shū.
    - That young girl was brave and took the initiative to pick up the book I dropped.
