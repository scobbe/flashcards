## 小妹妹
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèimei
- **definition:** little sister; young girl
- **characters:**
  - 小: xiǎo, small
  - 妹: mèi, younger sister
  - 妹: mèi, younger sister
- **examples:**
  - 我带小妹妹去公园玩(我帶小妹妹去公園玩) (wǒ dài xiǎo mèimei qù gōngyuán wán, "I took my little sister to the park to play")
  - 那个小妹妹看起来很害羞(那個小妹妹看起來很害羞) (nà gè xiǎo mèimei kàn qǐlái hěn hàixiū, "That young girl looks shy")
