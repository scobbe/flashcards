## 电荷(電荷)
---
- **pinyin:** diànhè
- **definition:** electric charge
- **characters:**
  - 电(電)
    - diàn
    - electricity
  - 荷
    - hè
    - charge
- **etymology:** Combines 电(電) (diàn, "electricity") with 荷 (hè, "charge") to express electric charge.
- **examples:**
  - 电子带负电荷(電子帶負電荷)。
    - diànzǐ dài fù diànhè
    - An electron carries a negative electric charge.
  - 在封闭系统中总电荷守恒(在封閉系統中總電荷守恆)。
    - zài fēngbì xìtǒng zhōng zǒng diànhè shǒuhéng
    - In a closed system, total electric charge is conserved.
