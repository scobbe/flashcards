## 武昌起义(武昌起義)
---
- **pinyin:** Wǔchāng Qǐyì
- **definition:** Wuchang Uprising
- **characters:**
  - 武
    - wǔ
    - martial, military; force, violence; weapons, arms
  - 昌
    - chāng
    - prosperous, flourishing; to prosper, to flourish
  - 起
    - qǐ
    - to rise, get up; to start, begin; to raise, lift; from (starting at)
  - 义(義)
    - yì
    - justice, righteousness; meaning, sense; duty, obligation; adoptive, adopted
- **etymology:** 武 (wǔ, "martial") and 昌 (chāng, "prosperous") name the locale Wuchang, while 起 (qǐ, "rise") with 义(義) (yì, "righteousness") denotes a righteous uprising, yielding the righteous uprising at Wuchang.
- **examples:**
  - 武昌起义爆发于1911年10月10日(武昌起義爆發於1911年10月10日)，被视为辛亥革命的开端(被視為辛亥革命的開端)。
    - Wǔchāng Qǐyì bàofā yú yī jiǔ yī yī nián shí yuè shí rì, bèi shìwéi Xīnhài Gémìng de kāiduān.
    - The Wuchang Uprising broke out on October 10, 1911, and is regarded as the beginning of the Xinhai Revolution.
  - 在历史课上(在歷史課上)，老师详细讲解了武昌起义的背景和影响(老師詳細講解了武昌起義的背景和影響)。
    - Zài lìshǐ kè shàng, lǎoshī xiángxì jiǎngjiě le Wǔchāng Qǐyì de bèijǐng hé yǐngxiǎng.
    - In history class, the teacher explained in detail the background and impact of the Wuchang Uprising.
