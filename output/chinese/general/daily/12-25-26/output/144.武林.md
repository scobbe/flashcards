## 武林
---
- **pinyin:** wǔlín
- **definition:** martial arts circles
- **characters:**
  - 武
    - wǔ
    - martial
  - 林
    - lín
    - forest
- **etymology:** Combines 武 (wǔ, "martial") with 林 (lín, "forest") to express martial arts circles.
- **examples:**
  - 他在武林中名声很大(他在武林中名聲很大)。
    - tā zài wǔlín zhōng míngshēng hěn dà
    - He is very famous in the martial arts circles.
