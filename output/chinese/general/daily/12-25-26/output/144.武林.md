## 武林
---
- **pinyin:** wǔlín
- **definition:** martial arts circles
- **characters:**
  - 武
    - wǔ
    - martial, military; force, violence; surname Wǔ
  - 林
    - lín
    - forest, woods; a multitude, great number; surname Lín
- **etymology:** Combines 武 (wǔ, "martial") with 林 (lín, "forest; multitude") to portray a 'forest' of martial practitioners, a metaphor for the community or circles engaged in martial arts.
- **examples:**
  - 在武林中(在武林中)，名门正派与邪派之间的斗争从未停止(名門正派與邪派之間的鬥爭從未停止)。
    - Zài wǔlín zhōng, míngmén zhèngpài yǔ xiépài zhījiān de dòuzhēng cóng wèi tíngzhǐ.
    - In the martial arts world, the struggle between the orthodox sects and the demonic sects has never ceased.
