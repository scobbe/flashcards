## 人民币(人民幣)
---
- **pinyin:** rénmínbì
- **definition:** Renminbi (RMB)
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person; people, mankind; someone
  - 民
    - mín
    - people, citizens; civilian; folk; nationality, ethnic group
  - 币(幣)
    - bì
    - currency; money; coin; token
- **etymology:** 人 (rén, "person") with 民 (mín, "people/citizens") forms 人民, "the people," and adding 币(幣) (bì, "currency") yields a literal "people’s currency," i.e., money issued in the name of the populace.
- **examples:**
  - 人民币是中国的法定货币(人民幣是中國的法定貨幣)。
    - Rénmínbì shì Zhōngguó de fǎdìng huòbì.
    - The renminbi is China’s official currency.
  - 这家店只收人民币(這家店只收人民幣)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn zhǐ shōu rénmínbì.
    - This shop only accepts RMB.
