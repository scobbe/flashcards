## 师傅(師傅)
---
- **pinyin:** shīfu
- **definition:** master; skilled worker
- **characters:**
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - teacher
  - 傅
    - fù
    - tutor
- **etymology:** 师(師) (shī, "teacher/leader") signals authoritative, specialized expertise (from an original sense of an army "division" → "master/teacher"), while 傅 (fù, "tutor; attendant") evokes close, personal instruction like a court tutor. Together they denote the master in a guild apprenticeship who trains hands-on, hence its modern use as a respectful address for skilled workers.
- **examples:**
  - 李师傅教我们太极拳(李師傅教我們太極拳)。
    - Lǐ shīfu jiāo wǒmen tàijíquán
    - Master Li teaches us tai chi.
  - 维修师傅很快就把洗衣机修好了(維修師傅很快就把洗衣機修好了)。
    - Wéixiū shīfu hěn kuài jiù bǎ xǐyījī xiū hǎo le
    - The repair worker quickly fixed the washing machine.
