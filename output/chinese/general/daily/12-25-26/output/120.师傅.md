## 师傅(師傅)
---
- **pinyin:** shīfu
- **definition:** master; skilled worker
- **characters:**
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - teacher, master; division (military unit); expert, specialist (as suffix)
  - 傅
    - fù
    - tutor, instructor; to apply, to smear (powder, ointment); surname Fu
- **etymology:** 师(師) (shī, "teacher, master") combines with 傅 (fù, "tutor, instructor"), both denoting instructors, forming a respectful address for someone who imparts or practices a craft, hence "master; skilled worker". Compared with 师(師) (shī, "teacher, master") + 父 (fù, "father") used for lineage or religious masters, using 傅 (fù, "tutor") emphasizes professional, practical instruction.
- **examples:**
  - 师傅(師傅)，这一招该怎么练(這一招該怎麼練)？
    - Shīfu, zhè yì zhāo gāi zěnme liàn?
    - Master, how should I practice this move?
  - 这位木工师傅手艺很好(這位木工師傅手藝很好)，可以把旧柜子修得跟新的一样(可以把舊櫃子修得跟新的一樣)。
    - Zhè wèi mùgōng shīfu shǒuyì hěn hǎo, kěyǐ bǎ jiù guìzi xiū de gēn xīn de yíyàng.
    - This carpenter is very skilled; he can fix the old cabinet so it looks brand new.
