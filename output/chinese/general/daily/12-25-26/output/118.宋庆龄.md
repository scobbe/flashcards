## 宋庆龄(宋慶齡)
---
- **pinyin:** Sòng Qìnglíng
- **definition:** Song Qingling
- **characters:**
  - 宋
    - sòng
    - surname Song; Song dynasty; state of Song (ancient)
  - 庆(慶)
    - qìng
    - celebrate; celebration; congratulate; surname
  - 龄(齡)
    - líng
    - age; years of service, seniority; length of use
- **etymology:** Combines the family name 宋 (sòng, "surname Song") with the given name 庆(慶) (qìng, "celebrate") and 龄(齡) (líng, "age"), an auspicious pairing that conveys celebrating years and a wish for longevity.
- **examples:**
  - 宋庆龄是中国著名的政治家(宋慶齡是中國著名的政治家)，也是社会活动家(也是社會活動家)。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng shì Zhōngguó zhùmíng de zhèngzhìjiā, yě shì shèhuì huódòngjiā.
    - Song Qingling is a renowned Chinese politician and social activist.
  - 她与孙中山结婚(她與孫中山結婚)，并长期致力于儿童福利事业(並長期致力於兒童福利事業)。
    - Tā yǔ Sūn Zhōngshān jiéhūn, bìng chángqī zhìlì yú értóng fúlì shìyè.
    - She married Sun Yat-sen and devoted herself for many years to children's welfare.
