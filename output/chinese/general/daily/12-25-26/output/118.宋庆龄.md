## 宋庆龄(宋慶齡)
---
- **pinyin:** Sòng Qìnglíng
- **definition:** Song Qingling
- **characters:**
  - 宋: sòng, Song dynasty
  - 庆(慶): qìng, celebrate
  - 龄(齡): líng, age
- **examples:**
  - 宋庆龄是中国著名的政治家和社会活动家(宋慶齡是中國著名的政治家和社會活動家) (Sòng Qìnglíng shì Zhōngguó zhùmíng de zhèngzhìjiā hé shèhuì huódòngjiā, "Song Qingling was a famous Chinese politician and social activist")
