## 朴(樸)
---
- **pinyin:** pǔ
- **definition:** plain; simple
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic: 木 (mù, "wood") provides the semantic of unworked wood, with 卜 (bǔ, "divination") as the phonetic in simplified 朴(樸) (pǔ, "plain; simple"); the traditional form of 朴(樸) instead uses 菐 (pú, "phonetic"). Evokes the image of "uncarved wood," hence the sense "plain, simple."
- **examples:**
  - 她的穿着很朴素(她的穿著很樸素)。
    - Tā de chuānzhuó hěn pǔsù.
    - Her clothing is very plain.
  - 他们过着简朴的生活(他們過著簡樸的生活)。
    - Tāmen guòzhe jiǎnpǔ de shēnghuó.
    - They lead a simple life.
