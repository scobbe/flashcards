## 想起来(想起來)
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎngqǐlái
- **definition:** remember; recall
- **characters:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - to think; to want, would like; to miss, long for; to imagine
  - 起
    - qǐ
    - to rise, get up; to start, begin; to raise, lift; to happen, occur
  - 来(來)
    - lái
    - to come, arrive; future, coming, next; to do, take on
- **etymology:** 想 (xiǎng, "to think") with 起 (qǐ, "to rise; begin") and 来(來) (lái, "to come") depicts a thought rising and coming to mind, hence recalling/remembering.
- **examples:**
  - 我一下子想起来了他的名字(我一下子想起來了他的名字)。
    - Wǒ yīxiàzi xiǎng qǐ lái le tā de míngzi.
    - I suddenly remembered his name.
  - 听到这首歌(聽到這首歌)，我就想起来了小时候在老家的夏天(我就想起來了小時候在老家的夏天)。
    - Tīng dào zhè shǒu gē, wǒ jiù xiǎng qǐ lái le xiǎo shíhou zài lǎo jiā de xià tiān.
    - Hearing this song, I immediately recall childhood summers back home.
