## rabbit
### tù
---
- **definition:** rabbit
- **pinyin:** tù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a rabbit.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of a rabbit with prominent ears and hind legs. The modern form has been stylized into regular strokes, but the silhouette still evokes the animal’s posture.
- **examples:**
  - 我家养了一只兔子(我家養了一隻兔子)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǎng le yì zhī tùzi.
    - My family has a pet rabbit.
  - 它最喜欢吃胡萝卜(它最喜歡吃胡蘿蔔)。
    - Tā zuì xǐhuan chī húluóbo.
    - It likes eating carrots the most.
  - 秋夜里(秋夜裡)，我们看见玉兔在月亮上奔跑(我們看見玉兔在月亮上奔跑)。
    - Qiū yè lǐ, wǒmen kànjiàn Yùtù zài yuèliàng shàng bēnpǎo.
    - On an autumn night, we saw the Jade Rabbit running on the moon.
