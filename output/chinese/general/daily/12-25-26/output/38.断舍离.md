## 断舍离
---
- **pinyin:** duànshělí
- **definition:** decluttering; minimalism
- **characters:**
  - 断(斷)
    - duàn
    - break, snap; cut off, sever; stop, cease, interrupt; decide, judge, determine
  - 舍(捨)
    - shě, shè
    - give up, abandon, discard, part with; house, dwelling; lodging, quarters, dormitory
  - 离(離)
    - lí
    - leave, depart; be away from, at a distance from; separate, part; without, free from
- **etymology:** 断(斷) (duàn, "cut off/decide") stops the inflow of nonessential things, 舍(捨) (shě, "give up/discard") removes what is already possessed, and 离(離) (lí, "separate/be free from") detaches one from attachment, together denoting decluttering and minimalist living.
- **examples:**
  - 为了搬家(為了搬家)，我开始断舍离(我開始斷捨離)，把不需要的东西统统捐掉(把不需要的東西統統捐掉)。
    - Wèile bānjiā, wǒ kāishǐ duànshělí, bǎ bù xūyào de dōngxi tǒngtǒng juāndiào.
    - To prepare for moving, I started decluttering and donated everything I don’t need.
  - 他把断舍离当作一种生活方式(他把斷捨離當作一種生活方式)，只保留让自己快乐的必需品(只保留讓自己快樂的必需品)。
    - Tā bǎ duànshělí dàngzuò yì zhǒng shēnghuó fāngshì, zhǐ bǎoliú ràng zìjǐ kuàilè de bìxūpǐn.
    - He embraces minimalism as a lifestyle, keeping only the essentials that make him happy.
