## 春节(春節)
---
- **pinyin:** Chūn Jié
- **definition:** Spring Festival
- **characters:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring
  - 节(節)
    - jié
    - festival
- **etymology:** Combines 春 (chūn, "spring") with 节(節) (jié, "festival") to express spring festival.
- **examples:**
  - 我们全家在春节团聚(我們全家在春節團聚)。
    - wǒmen quánjiā zài chūn jié tuánjù
    - Our whole family reunites during the Spring Festival.
