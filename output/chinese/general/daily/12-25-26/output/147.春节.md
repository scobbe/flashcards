## 春节(春節)
---
- **pinyin:** Chūn Jié
- **definition:** Spring Festival
- **characters:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring, springtime; youth, vitality, prime of life; erotic love, sexual desire, lust
  - 节(節)
    - jié
    - festival, holiday; section, segment, part; to save, economize; joint, node, knot
- **etymology:** 春 (chūn, "spring") combines with 节(節) (jié, "festival; node")—originally a calendrical "joint"—to denote the festival marking the year's turning point into spring (Lunar New Year).
- **examples:**
  - 今年春节(今年春節)，我们全家回老家团圆(我們全家回老家團圓)。
    - Jīnnián Chūn Jié, wǒmen quánjiā huí lǎojiā tuányuán.
    - This Spring Festival, our whole family is going back to our hometown for a reunion.
