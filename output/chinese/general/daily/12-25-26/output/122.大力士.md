## 大力士
---
- **pinyin:** dàlìshì
- **definition:** strongman
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great, grand; main, major; eldest, older
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength, power; force; effort; to strive, to exert
  - 士
    - shì
    - scholar, gentleman; soldier, warrior; man, person; professional, specialist
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big") + 力 (lì, "strength") describe 士 (shì, "man/person"), yielding a person of great strength, with 士 functioning as a suffix for one defined by a trait or skill.
- **examples:**
  - 他是著名的大力士(他是著名的大力士)，曾在世界大力士比赛中夺冠(曾在世界大力士比賽中奪冠)。
    - Tā shì zhùmíng de dàlìshì, céng zài shìjiè dàlìshì bǐsài zhōng duóguàn.
    - He is a famous strongman who once won a world strongman competition.
