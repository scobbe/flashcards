## 大力士
---
- **pinyin:** dàlìshì
- **definition:** strongman
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength
  - 士
    - shì
    - scholar
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big") amplifies 力 (lì, "strength") into 'great strength,' while 士 (shì, "man of rank; specialist") is the classical person-suffix—originally a warrior/gentleman—marking one defined by that trait. Hence 大力士 is literally a 'great-strength man,' culturally aligned with the heroic/guardian archetype seen in 力士 of Buddhist iconography (e.g., 金剛力士).
- **examples:**
  - 那个大力士能举起一辆汽车(那個大力士能舉起一輛汽車)。
    - nà gè dàlìshì néng jǔ qǐ yì liàng qìchē
    - That strongman can lift a car.
