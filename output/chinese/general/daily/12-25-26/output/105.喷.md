## 喷(噴)
---
- **pinyin:** pēn
- **definition:** spray
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 賁 Etymology: STEDT compares it to Proto-Tibeto-Burman *m/s-prat ~ *pran ("to sprinke; to spray") , whence Burmese ဖျန်း (hpyan: , " to spray, to sprinkle").
- **examples:**
  - 他往叶子上喷农药(他往葉子上噴農藥)。
    - tā wǎng yèzi shàng pēn nóngyào
    - He sprays pesticide on the leaves.
  - 鲸鱼从头顶喷出一道水花(鯨魚從頭頂噴出一道水花)。
    - jīngyú cóng tóudǐng pēn chū yí dào shuǐhuā
    - The whale sprays a jet of water from the top of its head.
