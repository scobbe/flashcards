## 喷(噴)
---
- **pinyin:** pēn
- **definition:** spray
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") gives the idea of emitting, while 贲(賁) (bēn, "ornate; vigorous") serves as the phonetic, hinting at the sound pēn/pèn. Visually evokes something forcefully expelled from the mouth, hence "spray/spurt."
- **examples:**
  - 他用喷壶把水喷在花上(他用噴壺把水噴在花上)。
    - Tā yòng pēnhú bǎ shuǐ pēn zài huā shàng.
    - He used a watering can to spray water onto the flowers.
