## 纯素(純素)
---
- **pinyin:** chúnsù
- **definition:** vegan
- **characters:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure, unadulterated, unalloyed; simple, plain; sheer, purely; innocent, naive
  - 素
    - sù
    - vegetarian; plain, simple, unadorned; normally, all along; element, basic factor
- **etymology:** 纯(純) (chún, "pure") intensifies 素 (sù, "vegetarian; plain") to mean purely vegetarian, excluding all animal-derived products.
- **examples:**
  - 她坚持纯素饮食(她堅持純素飲食)，连蜂蜜也不吃(連蜂蜜也不吃)。
    - Tā jiānchí chúnsù yǐnshí, lián fēngmì yě bù chī.
    - She sticks to a vegan diet and doesn’t even eat honey.
  - 这家餐厅提供纯素菜单(這家餐廳提供純素菜單)，所有菜品都不含动物性成分(所有菜品都不含動物性成分)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng chúnsù càidān, suǒyǒu càipǐn dōu bù hán dòngwùxìng chéngfèn.
    - This restaurant offers a vegan menu, and none of the dishes contain animal products.
