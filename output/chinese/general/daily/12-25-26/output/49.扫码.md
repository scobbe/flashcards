## 扫码
---
- **pinyin:** sǎomǎ
- **definition:** scan a code
- **characters:**
  - 扫(掃)
    - sǎo, sào
    - to sweep, to clean; to scan; to sweep away, to wipe out
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number, size; yard (unit of length); to stack, to pile up
- **etymology:** 扫(掃) (sǎo, "to sweep; to scan") conveys the sweeping/reading action, while 码(碼) (mǎ, "code") names the encoded symbol; together it literally means to sweep over a code, i.e., scan it.
- **examples:**
  - 请你扫码付款(請你掃碼付款)。
    - Qǐng nǐ sǎomǎ fùkuǎn.
    - Please scan the code to pay.
