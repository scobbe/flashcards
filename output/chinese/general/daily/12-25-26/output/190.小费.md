## 小费(小費)
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎofèi
- **definition:** tip; gratuity
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, petty
  - 费(費)
    - fèi
    - fee, expense, cost; to spend, expend; to waste, squander; taxing, laborious, time-consuming
- **etymology:** Literally “small expense”: 小 (xiǎo, "small") qualifies 费(費) (fèi, "fee, expense") to denote a modest, discretionary payment offered in thanks for service.
- **examples:**
  - 在美国(在美國)，给服务员小费是很常见的(給服務員小費是很常見的)。
    - Zài Měiguó, gěi fúwùyuán xiǎofèi shì hěn chángjiàn de.
    - In the U.S., leaving a tip for servers is very common.
  - 请问(請問)，账单里包含小费吗(帳單裡包含小費嗎)？
    - Qǐngwèn, zhàngdān lǐ bāohán xiǎofèi ma?
    - Excuse me, is the tip included in the bill?
