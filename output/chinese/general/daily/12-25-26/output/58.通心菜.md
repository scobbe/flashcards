## 通心菜
---
- **pinyin:** tōngxīncài
- **definition:** water spinach
- **characters:**
  - 通
    - tōng
    - go through, pass through; open, unobstructed; communicate, connect; expert, proficient
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart; mind; intention, will; center, core
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable, greens; dish, course; cuisine; unskilled, lousy
- **etymology:** 通 (tōng, "go through/open") + 心 (xīn, "center") conveys "through the center"—a hollow stem—while 菜 (cài, "vegetable") marks it as an edible green.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢吃清炒通心菜(我最喜歡吃清炒通心菜)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan chī qīngchǎo tōngxīncài.
    - I love eating stir-fried water spinach.
  - 市场上的通心菜很新鲜(市場上的通心菜很新鮮)，我买了两把(我買了兩把)。
    - Shìchǎng shàng de tōngxīncài hěn xīnxiān, wǒ mǎi le liǎng bǎ.
    - The water spinach at the market is very fresh, so I bought two bunches.
