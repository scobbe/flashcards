## 通心菜
---
- **pinyin:** tōngxīncài
- **definition:** water spinach
- **characters:**
  - 通
    - tōng
    - through
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable
- **etymology:** Combines 通 (tōng, "through"), 心 (xīn, "heart"), and 菜 (cài, "vegetable") to convey water spinach.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢用蒜蓉清炒通心菜(我最喜歡用蒜蓉清炒通心菜)。
    - wǒ zuì xǐhuān yòng suànróng qīngchǎo tōngxīncài
    - I love to stir-fry water spinach with garlic.
