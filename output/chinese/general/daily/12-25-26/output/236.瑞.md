## 瑞
---
- **pinyin:** ruì
- **definition:** auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
- **examples:**
  - 瑞雪兆丰年(瑞雪兆豐年) (ruìxuě zhào fēngnián, "Auspicious snow is a sign of a good harvest")
  - 使者手持玉制的瑞作为凭信(使者手持玉製的瑞作為憑信) (shǐzhě shǒuchí yùzhì de ruì zuòwéi píngxìn, "The envoy held a jade token as a credential")
  - 他姓瑞，名志强(他姓瑞，名志強) (tā xìng ruì, míng zhìqiáng, "His surname is Rui, and his given name is Zhiqiang")
