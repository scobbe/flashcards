## 捏
---
- **pinyin:** niē
- **definition:** pinch
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left is 手 (shǒu, "hand") indicating a hand action; right is 臬 (niè, "target; rule") supplying the sound (niè > niē). 手 is pictographic of a hand, fitting the idea of fingers pressing together to "pinch," while 臬 originally depicted a target on a stand and contributes phonetics rather than meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 她轻轻地捏了捏我的脸颊(她輕輕地捏了捏我的臉頰)，我笑了出来(我笑了出來)。
    - Tā qīngqīng de niē le niē wǒ de liǎnjiá, wǒ xiào le chūlái.
    - She gently pinched my cheek, and I burst out laughing.
