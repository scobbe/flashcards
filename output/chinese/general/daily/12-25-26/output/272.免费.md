## 免费(免費)
---
## free of charge
### miǎnfèi
---
- **definition:** free of charge
- **pinyin:** miǎnfèi
- **components:**
  - 免
    - miǎn
    - to exempt; to excuse; to avoid; to spare
  - 费(費)
    - fèi
    - expense; fee; to spend; to waste
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 免 (exempt; avoid) + 费 (fee; expense) = free of charge
  - **interpretation:** Indicates that a service, product, or event doesn't require payment. Common in notices and offers, modifying nouns (免费门票) or verbs (免费提供).
  - **simplification:** 費 was simplified to 费 to reduce stroke count by streamlining the complex 貝-based structure; 免 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 这家博物馆免费开放(這家博物館免費開放)。
    - Zhè jiā bówùguǎn miǎnfèi kāifàng.
    - This museum is open free of charge.
  - 购买一送一的饮料，第二杯免费(購買一送一的飲料，第二杯免費)。
    - Gòumǎi yī sòng yī de yǐnliào, dì èr bēi miǎnfèi.
    - For buy-one-get-one drinks, the second cup is free.
  - 作为新用户，你可以免费试用三天(作為新用戶，你可以免費試用三天)。
    - Zuòwéi xīn yònghù, nǐ kěyǐ miǎnfèi shìyòng sān tiān.
    - As a new user, you can try it free for three days.
### 免费(免費) → 免
- **definition:** to exempt; to excuse; to avoid; to spare
- **pinyin:** miǎn
- **components:**
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - child; son
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a man wearing a ceremonial hat; later borrowed for abstract meanings; original sense preserved in 冕.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a person wearing a ceremonial crown, denoting that concrete object. Over time it was loaned to write the verbal idea ‘to avoid/exempt,’ while 冕 kept the crown meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 免税政策帮助小企业(免稅政策幫助小企業)。
    - Miǎnshuì zhèngcè bāngzhù xiǎo qǐyè.
    - The tax-exemption policy helps small businesses.
  - 老师说这次作业就免交了(老師說這次作業就免交了)。
    - Lǎoshī shuō zhè cì zuòyè jiù miǎn jiāo le.
    - The teacher said this assignment can be waived.
  - 为了安全，尽量免去不必要的出行(為了安全，盡量免去不必要的出行)。
    - Wèile ānquán, jǐnliàng miǎnqù bú bìyào de chūxíng.
    - For safety, try to avoid unnecessary outings.
### 免费(免費) → 免 → 儿(兒)
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** ér
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 臼
    - jiù
    - mortar
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a standing person; radical form of 人; also simplified from 兒 by eliminating 臼
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person standing on two legs, which is why it doubles as the person radical form. In traditional 兒, an added 臼 element evoked a baby’s soft skull, but simplification removed that part, leaving only the person/legs shape.
  - **simplification:** Stroke reduction by dropping 臼 from 兒 and reusing the existing person-radical form 儿.
- **examples:**
  - 我儿子在学校(我兒子在學校)。
    - Wǒ érzi zài xuéxiào.
    - My son is at school.
  - 她是我的女儿(她是我的女兒)。
    - Tā shì wǒ de nǚ'ér.
    - She is my daughter.
  - 这些电影很适合儿童观看(這些電影很適合兒童觀看)。
    - Zhèxiē diànyǐng hěn shìhé értóng guānkàn.
    - These movies are very suitable for children to watch.
### 免费(免費) → 免 → 儿(兒) → 人
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg; left-side form 亻.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person in profile, with one stroke suggesting the torso/arm and the other the leg, hence the slightly splayed shape. In compounds it often appears as the left-hand form 亻 to mark human-related meaning; not to be confused with 彳.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个很好的人(他是一個很好的人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè hěn hǎo de rén.
    - He is a very good person.
  - 我认识很多人(我認識很多人)。
    - Wǒ rènshi hěn duō rén.
    - I know many people.
  - 这里有三个人(這裡有三個人)。
    - Zhèlǐ yǒu sān gè rén.
    - There are three people here.
### 免费(免費) → 免 → 儿(兒) → 臼
- **definition:** mortar
- **pinyin:** jiù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a mortar. See the original version of 舂, in which a pestle is visible on top.
  - **interpretation:** A direct drawing of a mortar, showing the bowl used for pounding. Connection to 舂 (to pound) is historical, where a pestle appears above. Similar shapes seen in 學、兒、䳔、鼠、叟 are coincidental and unrelated.
- **examples:**
  - 他用杵在臼里舂米(他用杵在臼裡舂米)。
    - Tā yòng chǔ zài jiù lǐ chōng mǐ.
    - He uses a pestle to pound rice in the mortar.
  - 这个石臼很重(這個石臼很重)。
    - Zhège shíjiù hěn zhòng.
    - This stone mortar is very heavy.
  - 实验室里常用研钵和研杵(實驗室裡常用研鉢和研杵)。
    - Shíyànshì lǐ cháng yòng yánbō hé yánchǔ.
    - In laboratories, a mortar and pestle are commonly used.
### 免费(免費) → 费(費)
- **definition:** expense; fee; to spend; to waste
- **pinyin:** fèi
- **components:**
  - 弗
    - fú
    - not
  - 贝(貝)
    - bèi
    - cowry; money
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 貝 (‘cowry; money’) + phonetic: 弗
  - **interpretation:** 貝 points to the semantic field of money and valuables, fitting meanings like fees and expenses. 弗 supplies the sound, and over time the graph came to cover spending and the idea of being costly.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 费 by streamlining strokes, effectively retaining the phonetic 弗 and using the simplified 貝 component (贝) to reduce complexity.
- **examples:**
  - 这次报销的费用太高(這次報銷的費用太高)。
    - Zhè cì bàoxiāo de fèiyòng tài gāo.
    - The reimbursable expenses this time are too high.
  - 学校每学期都要交学费(學校每學期都要交學費)。
    - Xuéxiào měi xuéqī dōu yào jiāo xuéfèi.
    - The school requires tuition to be paid every semester.
  - 这台空调很费电，少开一会儿吧(這臺空調很費電，少開一會兒吧)。
    - Zhè tái kòngtiáo hěn fèidiàn, shǎo kāi yíhuìr ba.
    - This air conditioner uses a lot of electricity; let’s run it a bit less.
### 免费(免費) → 费(費) → 弗
- **definition:** not
- **pinyin:** fú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): rope wrapped around two pieces of wood to keep them straight; later borrowed phonetically for “no”. The derivative 拂 stands for the original word.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two sticks bound by a cord to keep them straight. Its common negative meaning arose from a phonetic loan, unrelated to the original image. The concrete sense later shifted to the character 拂.
- **examples:**
  - 他对命令弗从(他對命令弗從)。
    - tā duì mìnglìng fú cóng.
    - He did not comply with the order.
  - 吾弗知其故(吾弗知其故)。
    - wú fú zhī qí gù.
    - I do not know the reason.
  - 医者仁心(醫者仁心)，弗可欺(弗可欺)。
    - yī zhě rén xīn, fú kě qī.
    - Physicians are benevolent; they must not be deceived.
### 免费(免費) → 费(費) → 贝(貝)
- **definition:** cowry; money
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram – cowrie used as currency in ancient China; simplified form of 貝 in cursive script.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cowry shell, an object that functioned as early money, which motivates the senses “cowry; money.” In the simplified set, its strokes were streamlined by adopting a cursive-style form of 貝.
  - **simplification:** Adopted the cursive-script variant of 貝, reducing strokes and complexity.
- **examples:**
  - 古代用贝(貝)当作货币(貨幣)。
    - Gǔdài yòng bèi dāngzuò huòbì.
    - In ancient times, cowries were used as currency.
  - 海滩(海灘)上有很多贝(貝)。
    - Hǎitān shang yǒu hěn duō bèi.
    - There are many shells on the beach.
  - 这串项链(這串項鍊)是用贝(貝)做的。
    - Zhè chuàn xiàngliàn shì yòng bèi zuò de.
    - This necklace is made of shells.
