## 钱包(錢包)
---
- **pinyin:** qiánbāo
- **definition:** wallet
- **characters:**
  - 钱(錢)
    - qián
    - money, cash; coin; surname Qian; mace, 1/10 of a tael
  - 包
    - bāo
    - bag, package; to wrap; to include, encompass; to charter, hire
- **etymology:** 钱(錢) (qián, "money") specifies the contents, while 包 (bāo, "bag") names the container, together meaning a bag for money (a wallet).
- **examples:**
  - 我把钱包落在出租车上了(我把錢包落在出租車上了)，你能帮我联系司机吗(你能幫我聯繫司機嗎)？
    - Wǒ bǎ qiánbāo là zài chūzūchē shàng le, nǐ néng bāng wǒ liánxì sījī ma?
    - I left my wallet in the taxi—can you help me contact the driver?
