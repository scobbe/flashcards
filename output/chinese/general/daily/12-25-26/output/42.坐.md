## 坐
---
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **etymology:** Compound ideograph: 从(從) (cóng, "follow") depicts two persons together, set over 土 (tǔ, "earth") to show people on the ground, i.e., sitting. Purely semantic (no phonetic), with meaning later extended to "ride" via sitting in or on a vehicle.
- **examples:**
  - 她坐在窗边看书(她坐在窗邊看書)。
    - Tā zuò zài chuāngbiān kàn shū.
    - She sits by the window reading.
  - 我每天坐地铁上班(我每天坐地鐵上班)。
    - Wǒ měitiān zuò dìtiě shàngbān.
    - I take the subway to work every day.
