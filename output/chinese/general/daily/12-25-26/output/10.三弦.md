## 三弦
---
- **pinyin:** sānxián
- **definition:** three-stringed Chinese lute used in Pingtan
- **characters:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three
  - 弦
    - xián
    - string
- **etymology:** Combines 三 (sān, "three") with 弦 (xián, "string") to express three-stringed chinese lute used in pingtan.
- **examples:**
  - 在苏州的评弹表演中(在蘇州的評彈表演中)，三弦是不可或缺的伴奏乐器(三弦是不可或缺的伴奏樂器)。
    - zài sūzhōu de píngtán biǎoyǎn zhōng, sānxián shì bùkě huòquē de bànzòu yuèqì
    - In Suzhou Pingtan performances, the sanxian is an indispensable accompaniment instrument.
