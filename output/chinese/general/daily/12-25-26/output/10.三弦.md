## 三弦
---
- **pinyin:** sānxián
- **definition:** three-stringed Chinese lute used in Pingtan
- **characters:**
  - 三
    - sān
    - three; third
  - 弦
    - xián
    - string (of a musical instrument); bowstring; chord (geometry); crescent moon
- **etymology:** 三 (sān, "three") quantifies 弦 (xián, "string"), literally "three strings," naming a lute defined by its three-string setup used in Pingtan.
- **examples:**
  - 他擅长弹三弦(他擅長彈三弦)，在评弹表演中经常担任伴奏(在評彈表演中經常擔任伴奏)。
    - Tā shàncháng tán sānxián, zài píngtán biǎoyǎn zhōng jīngcháng dānrèn bànzòu.
    - He excels at playing the sanxian and often provides accompaniment in Pingtan performances.
