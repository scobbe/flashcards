## 教育
---
- **pinyin:** jiàoyù
- **definition:** education
- **characters:**
  - 教
    - jiāo
    - teach
  - 育
    - yù
    - educate
- **etymology:** Combines 教 (jiāo, "teach") with 育 (yù, "educate") to express education.
- **examples:**
  - 教育是改变命运的钥匙(教育是改變命運的鑰匙)。
    - jiàoyù shì gǎibiàn mìngyùn de yàoshi
    - Education is the key to changing one's destiny.
