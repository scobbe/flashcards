## 现(現)
---
- **pinyin:** xiàn
- **definition:** appear; now
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 玉 (yù, "jade") provides the semantic idea of brightness/manifestation, while 见(見) (jiàn, "see") contributes the notion of visibility and acts as the phonetic (historically read xiàn). In simplified form the jade component appears as 王 (wáng, "king") beside 见(見), visually suggesting something coming into view.
- **examples:**
  - 他突然出现在门口(他突然出現在門口)。
    - Tā tūrán chūxiàn zài ménkǒu.
    - He suddenly appeared at the doorway.
  - 我现在没空(我現在沒空)。
    - Wǒ xiànzài méi kòng.
    - I don’t have time right now.
