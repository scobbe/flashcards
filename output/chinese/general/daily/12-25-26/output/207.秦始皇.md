## 秦始皇
---
- **pinyin:** Qín Shǐhuáng
- **definition:** Qin Shi Huang (first emperor of Qin)
- **characters:**
  - 秦
    - qín
    - Qin (ancient state); Qin dynasty; surname Qin; abbreviation for Shaanxi
  - 始
    - shǐ
    - to begin, start; beginning, initial, first; only then, not until
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - emperor, sovereign; imperial, royal, august
- **etymology:** 秦 (qín, "Qin") names the state/dynasty, while 始 (shǐ, "first; beginning") modifies 皇 (huáng, "emperor") to mean the inaugural sovereign—"the first emperor of Qin."
- **examples:**
  - 秦始皇统一了六国(秦始皇統一了六國)，建立了中央集权的秦朝(建立了中央集權的秦朝)。
    - Qín Shǐhuáng tǒngyī le liù guó, jiànlì le zhōngyāng jíquán de Qíncháo.
    - Qin Shi Huang unified the six states and established the centralized Qin dynasty.
  - 西安的兵马俑是为秦始皇的陵墓而建造的(西安的兵馬俑是為秦始皇的陵墓而建造的)。
    - Xī'ān de Bīngmǎyǒng shì wèi Qín Shǐhuáng de língmù ér jiànzào de.
    - The Terracotta Army in Xi'an was built for Qin Shi Huang's mausoleum.
