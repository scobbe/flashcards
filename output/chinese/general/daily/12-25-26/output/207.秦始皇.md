## 秦始皇
---
- **pinyin:** Qín Shǐhuáng
- **definition:** Qin Shi Huang (first emperor of Qin)
- **characters:**
  - 秦
    - qín
    - Qin dynasty
  - 始
    - shǐ
    - begin
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - emperor
- **etymology:** Combines 秦 (qín, "Qin dynasty"), 始 (shǐ, "begin"), and 皇 (huáng, "emperor") to convey qin shi huang (first emperor of qin).
- **examples:**
  - 秦始皇统一了六国(秦始皇統一了六國)，建立了秦朝(建立了秦朝)。
    - Qín Shǐhuáng tǒngyī le liù guó, jiànlì le Qíncháo
    - Qin Shi Huang unified the six states and founded the Qin dynasty.
