## 粥
---
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **definition:** congee
- **etymology:** Pictographic-ideogram: 粥 (zhōu, "congee") shows grain 米 (mǐ, "rice") enclosed by two curved 弓 (gōng, "bow") shapes, evoking a pot with handles and rice inside being cooked. 米 supplies the food/porridge meaning, while the flanking 弓 are pictorial (not phonetic), reinforcing the vessel-and-boiling imagery.
- **examples:**
  - 天冷了(天冷了)，我煮了一锅粥(我煮了一鍋粥)。
    - Tiān lěng le, wǒ zhǔ le yì guō zhōu.
    - It’s cold, so I cooked a pot of congee.
