## 粥
---
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **definition:** congee
- **etymology:** A character meaning congee.
- **examples:**
  - 早上喝一碗热腾腾的粥最舒服(早上喝一碗熱騰騰的粥最舒服)。
    - zǎoshang hē yì wǎn rè téngténg de zhōu zuì shūfu
    - A hot bowl of congee in the morning is the most comforting.
  - 生病的时候(生病的時候)，他只喝白粥(他只喝白粥)。
    - shēngbìng de shíhou, tā zhǐ hē bái zhōu
    - When he's sick, he only drinks plain congee.
