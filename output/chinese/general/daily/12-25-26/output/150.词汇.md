## 词汇(詞彙)
---
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **characters:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word, term; lyrics; lyric poetry (cí); wording, diction
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - to collect, to compile; collection, compendium; category, classification
- **etymology:** 词(詞) (cí, "word, term") names individual lexical items, while 汇(彙) (huì, "to collect; compendium") conveys gathering/compiling, together forming the idea of a collected set of words—i.e., vocabulary.
- **examples:**
  - 老师让我们记住这些词汇(老師讓我們記住這些詞彙)，用它们写一段话(用它們寫一段話)。
    - Lǎoshī ràng wǒmen jìzhù zhèxiē cíhuì, yòng tāmen xiě yí duàn huà.
    - The teacher asked us to memorize these vocabulary words and use them to write a short paragraph.
