## 姚滨(姚濱)
---
- **pinyin:** Yáo Bīn
- **definition:** Yao Bin (name)
- **characters:**
  - 姚
    - yáo
    - surname Yao
  - 滨(濱)
    - bīn
    - shore
- **etymology:** Combines 姚 (yáo, "surname Yao") with 滨(濱) (bīn, "shore") to express yao bin (name).
- **examples:**
  - 姚滨是中国著名的花样滑冰教练(姚濱是中國著名的花樣滑冰教練)。
    - yáo bīn shì zhōngguó zhùmíng de huāyàng huábīng jiàoliàn
    - Yao Bin is a famous Chinese figure skating coach.
