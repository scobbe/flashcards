## 欺负(欺負)
---
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **definition:** bully
- **characters:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - deceive
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - bear
- **etymology:** Combines 欺 (qī, "deceive") with 负(負) (fù, "bear") to express bully.
- **examples:**
  - 他总是欺负年纪小的同学(他總是欺負年紀小的同學)。
    - tā zǒngshì qīfu niánjì xiǎo de tóngxué
    - He always bullies the younger classmates.
  - 不要再欺负她了(不要再欺負她了)。
    - bú yào zài qīfu tā le
    - Don't bully her anymore.
