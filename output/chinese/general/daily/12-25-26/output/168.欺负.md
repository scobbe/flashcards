## 欺负(欺負)
---
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **definition:** bully
- **characters:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - to deceive, cheat; to bully
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - to bear, shoulder, carry on the back; to be defeated, lose; negative (math); to betray, be unfaithful to
- **etymology:** 欺 (qī, "deceive; bully") plus 负(負) (fù, "bear; be defeated; betray") conveys deceiving/oppressing someone and making them bear the loss or defeat—hence to bully.
- **examples:**
  - 别欺负他(別欺負他)，他只是个孩子(他只是個孩子)。
    - Bié qīfu tā, tā zhǐ shì gè háizi.
    - Don't bully him; he's just a kid.
