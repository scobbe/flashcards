## 琢
---
- **pinyin:** zhuó
- **definition:** carve; polish (jade)
- **examples:**
  - 师傅正琢一块上好和田玉(師傅正琢一塊上好和田玉) (shīfu zhèng zhuó yí kuài shànghǎo Hétián yù, "The craftsman is carving a fine piece of Hetian jade")
  - 玉不琢，不成器 (yù bù zhuó, bù chéng qì, "If jade is not polished, it cannot become a useful implement")
