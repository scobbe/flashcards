## 帮助(幫助)
---
- **pinyin:** bāngzhù
- **definition:** help
- **characters:**
  - 帮(幫)
    - bāng
    - help, assist; gang, clique, group
  - 助
    - zhù
    - help, assist, aid; to promote, foster; assistant, auxiliary
- **etymology:** Combines 帮(幫) (bāng, "assist, lend a hand") with 助 (zhù, "aid, support") as a synonymous binome that intensifies the notion of providing assistance. 帮 emphasizes the act of helping while 助 underscores the assistance itself, together forming the standard term for “help.”
- **examples:**
  - 请帮助我完成这份报告(請幫助我完成這份報告)。
    - Qǐng bāngzhù wǒ wánchéng zhè fèn bàogào.
    - Please help me finish this report.
  - 谢谢你的帮助(謝謝你的幫助)。
    - Xièxie nǐ de bāngzhù.
    - Thank you for your help.
