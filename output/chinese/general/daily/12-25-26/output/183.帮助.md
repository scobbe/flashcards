## help
### bāngzhù
---
- **definition:** help
- **pinyin:** bāngzhù
- **components:**
  - 帮(幫)
    - bāng
    - help; assist; aid; group/faction
  - 助
    - zhù
    - help; assist; aid; promote
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 帮(assist) + 助(help) = help/assist.
  - **interpretation:** Combines two near-synonyms to emphasize giving assistance or support; used as both a verb and a noun. Slightly more formal than simply using 帮/幫 alone.
  - **simplification:** 幫 was simplified to 帮 by reducing strokes and merging components to a simpler form; 助 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我可以帮助你(我可以幫助你)。
    - Wǒ kěyǐ bāngzhù nǐ.
    - I can help you.
  - 谢谢你的帮助(謝謝你的幫助)。
    - Xièxie nǐ de bāngzhù.
    - Thank you for your help.
  - 如果你需要帮助(如果你需要幫助)，就告诉我(就告訴我)。
    - Rúguǒ nǐ xūyào bāngzhù, jiù gàosu wǒ.
    - If you need help, just tell me.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫)
- **definition:** help; assist; aid; group/faction
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 邦
    - bāng
    - nation; state
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth; towel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** simplified form (of a phono-semantic compound)
  - **description:** Simplified from 幚 (帛 → 巾), a variant of 幫; 幫 is phono-semantic: semantic 帛 “silk” + phonetic 封 (variant phonetic 邦).
  - **interpretation:** Original formation pointed to textiles (帛) with a phonetic element 封 to convey the sound, referring first to the upper part of a shoe (鞋幫). The later variant swapped the phonetic to 邦 and the simplified form replaced the more complex 帛 with 巾, preserving the idea of cloth while streamlining strokes.
  - **simplification:** Replaced 帛 with simpler 巾 and standardized the variant using 邦 as phonetic; officially adopted in the 1956 Character Simplification Scheme.
- **examples:**
  - 你能帮我一下吗(你能幫我一下嗎)。
    - Nǐ néng bāng wǒ yíxià ma.
    - Can you help me for a moment?
  - 谢谢你的帮助(謝謝你的幫助)。
    - Xièxie nǐ de bāngzhù.
    - Thank you for your help.
  - 他加入了一个帮派(他加入了一個幫派)。
    - Tā jiārù le yí gè bāngpài.
    - He joined a gang/faction.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦
- **definition:** nation; state
- **pinyin:** bāng
- **components:**
  - 丰(豐)
    - fēng
    - abundant; plentiful
  - 邑
    - yì
    - city; town; settlement
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 丰 + semantic: 阝(邑)
  - **interpretation:** Combines the city/territory component 邑 (written as 阝 on the right) with the sound component 丰 to denote a political domain or state. The settlement element points to lands and cities, while 丰 provides a similar ancient sound that developed into bāng.
- **examples:**
  - 我们与友邦签署了协议(我們與友邦簽署了協議)。
    - Wǒmen yǔ yǒubāng qiānshǔle xiéyì.
    - We signed an agreement with a friendly nation.
  - 古代的诸侯邦国互相征战(古代的諸侯邦國互相征戰)。
    - Gǔdài de zhūhóu bāngguó hùxiāng zhēngzhàn.
    - In ancient times, the feudal states fought each other.
  - 这个联盟逐步走向联邦体制(這個聯盟逐步走向聯邦體制)。
    - Zhège liánméng zhúbù zǒuxiàng liánbāng tǐzhì.
    - This union is gradually moving toward a federal system.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦 → 丰(豐)
- **definition:** abundant; plentiful
- **pinyin:** fēng
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean; vessel
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a luxuriant plant growing on the ground (土); the bottom stroke is missing in the modern form.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a lush plant sprouting from the soil, naturally extending to the idea of abundance and fullness. Over time the base stroke for “ground” was lost, leaving a streamlined shape that still suggests upright, thriving growth.
  - **simplification:** Mainland reform replaced 豐 with the older pictographic form 丰, dropping the 豆 ‘vessel’ component and reverting to a simpler ancient glyph to reduce strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 今年风调雨顺(今年風調雨順)，粮食丰收(糧食豐收)。
    - Jīnnián fēngtiáo yǔshùn, liángshi fēngshōu.
    - This year the weather was favorable, and the grain harvest was abundant.
  - 这个地区物产丰富(這個地區物產豐富)。
    - Zhège dìqū wùchǎn fēngfù.
    - This region is rich in products and resources.
  - 五谷丰登(五穀豐登)，家家喜庆(家家喜慶)。
    - Wǔgǔ fēngdēng, jiājiā xǐqìng.
    - With all grains abundant, every household celebrates.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦 → 丰(豐) → 豆
- **definition:** bean; vessel
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **components:**
  - 二
    - èr
    - two
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth; opening
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: some kind of container; later borrowed phonetically for ‘bean’; displaced Old Chinese 菽 (shū); unrelated to the bottom of 豊.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a lidded vessel or ceremonial container. Its sound was later borrowed to write the word for beans/legumes, supplanting the older term 菽, while the pictorial container form remained.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃黄豆和黑豆(我喜歡吃黃豆和黑豆)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huángdòu hé hēidòu.
    - I like to eat soybeans and black beans.
  - 这道甜品是用红豆煮成的(這道甜品是用紅豆煮成的)。
    - Zhè dào tiánpǐn shì yòng hóngdòu zhǔ chéng de.
    - This dessert is made with red beans.
  - 豆类富含蛋白质，对素食者很重要(豆類富含蛋白質，對素食者很重要)。
    - Dòulèi fùhán dànbáizhì, duì sùshízhě hěn zhòngyào.
    - Legumes are rich in protein and are important for vegetarians.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦 → 丰(豐) → 豆 → 二
- **definition:** two
- **pinyin:** èr
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: two parallel strokes; duplication of 一.
  - **interpretation:** Two horizontal lines directly depict the quantity ‘two’ as simple tally marks. It parallels 一 and 三 as incremental strokes, with historical 亖 for four, emphasizing straightforward counting by repetition.
- **examples:**
  - 今天是二月二日(今天是二月二日)。
    - Jīntiān shì èr yuè èr rì.
    - Today is February 2nd.
  - 我住在二楼(我住在二樓)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài èr lóu.
    - I live on the second floor.
  - 二加二等于四(二加二等於四)。
    - Èr jiā èr děngyú sì.
    - Two plus two equals four.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦 → 丰(豐) → 豆 → 口
- **definition:** mouth; opening
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 邦 → 邑
- **definition:** city; town; settlement
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 卪
    - jié
    - kneeling person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** Ideogrammic compound: 囗 ‘enclosure; city wall’ + 卪 ‘kneeling person’; bottom part unrelated to 巴 and top most likely unrelated to original 丁.
  - **interpretation:** Combines a walled enclosure with a person to depict a community where people live or gather within protective walls. By showing someone inside fortifications, it evokes the idea of a town or settlement organized around its walls and civic center.
- **examples:**
  - 古代诸侯在其邑中修筑城墙(古代諸侯在其邑中修築城牆)。
    - Gǔdài zhūhóu zài qí yì zhōng xiūzhù chéngqiáng.
    - In ancient times, feudal lords built city walls within their settlements.
  - 此邑临河(此邑臨河)，贸易兴盛(貿易興盛)。
    - Cǐ yì lín hé, màoyì xīngshèng.
    - This town lies by a river, and trade flourishes.
  - 史书记载此邑曾为郡治(史書記載此邑曾為郡治)。
    - Shǐshū jìzǎi cǐ yì céng wéi jùnzhì.
    - Historical records note this settlement once served as a commandery seat.

### 帮助(幫助) → 帮(幫) → 巾
- **definition:** cloth; towel
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – hanging piece of cloth.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cloth draped and hanging, with the long stroke as the edge and the shorter strokes suggesting the folds. The image conveys a towel or piece of fabric suspended from a line or rod.
- **examples:**
  - 我用湿毛巾擦脸(我用濕毛巾擦臉)。
    - Wǒ yòng shī máojīn cā liǎn.
    - I use a wet towel to wipe my face.
  - 她戴着红色的头巾(她戴著紅色的頭巾)。
    - Tā dài zhe hóngsè de tóujīn.
    - She is wearing a red headscarf.
  - 冬天出门要围上围巾(冬天出門要圍上圍巾)。
    - Dōngtiān chūmén yào wéi shàng wéijīn.
    - In winter, put on a scarf before going out.
### 帮助(幫助) → 助
- **definition:** help; assist; aid; promote
- **pinyin:** zhù
- **components:**
  - 且
    - qiě
    - moreover; for the time being
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength; force; power
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 且 (OC *sʰjaːʔ, *ʔsa) + semantic: 力 ("strong arm")
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of strength (力) with a phonetic cue from 且, evoking the notion of lending one’s strength to assist. The phonetic component reflects an older pronunciation; modern Mandarin zhù developed from those earlier readings.
- **examples:**
  - 请你帮助我完成作业(請你幫助我完成作業)。
    - Qǐng nǐ bāngzhù wǒ wánchéng zuòyè.
    - Please help me finish my homework.
  - 他担任项目助理，协助团队沟通(他擔任項目助理，協助團隊溝通)。
    - Tā dānrèn xiàngmù zhùlǐ, xiézhù tuánduì gōutōng.
    - He serves as a project assistant, helping the team communicate.
  - 这笔资金将有助于推动科研(這筆資金將有助於推動科研)。
    - Zhè bǐ zījīn jiāng yǒuzhù yú tuīdòng kēyán.
    - This funding will help promote scientific research.
### 帮助(幫助) → 助 → 且
- **definition:** moreover; for the time being
- **pinyin:** qiě
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): in oracle bones and bronze inscriptions commonly used as 祖; usage as 俎 attested only in Warring States bamboo; see also the original form of 查.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a simple drawing whose identification varied across periods. Early forms were used for 祖, while the link to 俎 appears later; over time the graph stabilized as 且 and came to serve a grammatical role meaning “and; moreover.”
- **examples:**
  - 此书内容丰富，且语言简练(此書內容豐富，且語言簡練)。
    - Cǐ shū nèiróng fēngfù, qiě yǔyán jiǎnliàn.
    - The book is rich in content, and its language is concise.
  - 天气寒冷，且风很大(天氣寒冷，且風很大)。
    - Tiānqì hánlěng, qiě fēng hěn dà.
    - The weather is cold, and the wind is strong.
  - 他聪明，且勤奋(他聰明，且勤奮)。
    - Tā cōngmíng, qiě qínfèn.
    - He is intelligent and diligent.
### 帮助(幫助) → 助 → 力
- **definition:** strength; force; power
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): interpreted as a bent arm extending from the body, or as a wooden plough; original form 𠠲.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an arm applying force, a concrete image that came to represent strength and effort. Some traditions also saw it as a plough, linking power with labor in the fields.
- **examples:**
  - 他力气很大(他力氣很大)。
    - Tā lìqi hěn dà.
    - He is very strong.
  - 我们要尽力完成任务(我們要盡力完成任務)。
    - Wǒmen yào jìn lì wánchéng rènwù.
    - We must do our best to finish the task.
  - 科技给人类带来巨大力量(科技給人類帶來巨大力量)。
    - Kējì gěi rénlèi dàilái jùdà lìliàng.
    - Technology brings great power to humanity.
