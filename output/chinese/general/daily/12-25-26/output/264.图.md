## 图(圖)
---
- **pinyin:** tú
- **definition:** picture; map; plan
- **etymology:** From 圖 (啚 → 冬).
- **examples:**
  - 这张图很清楚(這張圖很清楚)。
    - zhè zhāng tú hěn qīngchu
    - This picture is very clear.
  - 出发前别忘了看地图(出發前別忘了看地圖)。
    - chū fā qián bié wàng le kàn dì tú
    - Before setting off, don't forget to look at the map.
  - 建筑师正在修改平面图(建築師正在修改平面圖)。
    - jiàn zhù shī zhèng zài xiū gǎi píng miàn tú
    - The architect is revising the floor plan.
