## 粗
---
- **pinyin:** cū
- **definition:** coarse; rough
- **etymology:** A character meaning coarse; rough.
- **examples:**
  - 这种盐很粗(這種鹽很粗)，得再磨细一点(得再磨細一點)。
    - zhè zhǒng yán hěn cū, děi zài mó xì yìdiǎn
    - This kind of salt is very coarse; it needs to be ground finer.
  - 他做事很粗，常常忽略细节(常常忽略細節)。
    - tā zuòshì hěn cū, chángcháng hūlüè xìjié
    - He does things roughly and often overlooks details.
