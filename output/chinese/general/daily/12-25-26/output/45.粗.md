## coarse; rough
### cū
---
- **definition:** coarse; rough
- **pinyin:** cū
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
  - 且
    - qiě
    - moreover; and
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 米 (“rice”) + phonetic: 且
  - **interpretation:** Rice suggests grains or texture, pointing to something unrefined or granular, while 且 supplies the historical sound that developed into cū. Together they evoke a grainy, rough quality in materials or appearance.
- **examples:**
  - 这块布很粗糙(這塊布很粗糙)。
    - Zhè kuài bù hěn cūcāo.
    - This piece of cloth is very coarse.
  - 他把盐磨得太粗(他把鹽磨得太粗)。
    - Tā bǎ yán mó de tài cū.
    - He ground the salt too coarse.
  - 这种砂纸太粗(這種砂紙太粗)。
    - Zhè zhǒng shāzhǐ tài cū.
    - This sandpaper is too coarse.
### 粗 → 米
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: dehusked rice kernels; horizontal stroke as a bamboo sieve; cf. 粟 (with husk); unrelated to 木/禾/来.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts dehusked rice grains being sifted on a bamboo sieve, contrasting with 粟 which shows husked kernels. Despite visual resemblance, its form is not derived from 木, 禾, or 来.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两斤米(我買了兩斤米)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le liǎng jīn mǐ.
    - I bought two jin of rice.
  - 这家店的东北米很香(這家店的東北米很香)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de Dōngběi mǐ hěn xiāng.
    - The Northeastern rice at this shop is very fragrant.
  - 请把米洗一洗(請把米洗一洗)，再煮饭(再煮飯)。
    - Qǐng bǎ mǐ xǐ yì xǐ, zài zhǔ fàn.
    - Please rinse the rice, then cook it.
### 粗 → 且
- **definition:** moreover; and
- **pinyin:** qiě
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): in oracle bones and bronze inscriptions commonly used as 祖; usage as 俎 attested only in Warring States bamboo; see also the original form of 查.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a simple drawing whose identification varied across periods. Early forms were used for 祖, while the link to 俎 appears later; over time the graph stabilized as 且 and came to serve a grammatical role meaning “and; moreover.”
- **examples:**
  - 此书内容丰富，且语言简练(此書內容豐富，且語言簡練)。
    - Cǐ shū nèiróng fēngfù, qiě yǔyán jiǎnliàn.
    - The book is rich in content, and its language is concise.
  - 天气寒冷，且风很大(天氣寒冷，且風很大)。
    - Tiānqì hánlěng, qiě fēng hěn dà.
    - The weather is cold, and the wind is strong.
  - 他聪明，且勤奋(他聰明，且勤奮)。
    - Tā cōngmíng, qiě qínfèn.
    - He is intelligent and diligent.
