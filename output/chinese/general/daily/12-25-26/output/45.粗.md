## 粗
---
- **pinyin:** cū
- **definition:** coarse; rough
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left 米 (mǐ, "rice") gives the semantic field of grain/texture, while right 且 (qiě, "and; moreover") serves as the phonetic, approximating cū. The sense "coarse; rough" evokes unpolished, grainy rice, visually reflected by the rice component paired with a sound-bearing element.
- **examples:**
  - 这袋盐的颗粒很粗(這袋鹽的顆粒很粗)。
    - Zhè dài yán de kēlì hěn cū.
    - The grains in this bag of salt are coarse.
  - 这张桌子的做工很粗(這張桌子的做工很粗)，边角处理得不细(邊角處理得不細)。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi de zuògōng hěn cū, biānjiǎo chǔlǐ de bù xì.
    - The workmanship of this table is rough, and the edges aren’t finished finely.
