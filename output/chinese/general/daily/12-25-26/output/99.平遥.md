## Pingyao
### Píngyáo
---
- **definition:** Pingyao
- **pinyin:** Píngyáo
- **components:**
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat;level;calm;peace
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo
    - distant;far;remote;long-distance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound toponym
  - **description:** 平 (level; peaceful) + 遥/遙 (distant; far) = place name.
  - **interpretation:** Place name for a county centered on the famed Ancient City of Pingyao in Shanxi, China. Characters convey a poetic/auspicious feel rather than a literal description.
  - **simplification:** 遙 → 遥: replaced the complex phonetic component with 肴 under the 辶 ‘walk’ radical to reduce strokes while preserving the yáo sound; 平 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我们准备去山西的平遥旅游。
    - Wǒmen zhǔnbèi qù Shānxī de Píngyáo lǚyóu.
    - We plan to travel to Pingyao in Shanxi.
  - 平遥古城保存完好，是世界文化遗产(平遙古城保存完好，是世界文化遺產)。
    - Píngyáo gǔchéng bǎocún wánhǎo, shì shìjiè wénhuà yíchǎn.
    - The Ancient City of Pingyao is well preserved and is a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site.
  - 从太原坐火车到平遥很方便(從太原坐火車到平遙很方便)。
    - Cóng Tàiyuán zuò huǒchē dào Píngyáo hěn fāngbiàn.
    - It’s very convenient to take a train from Taiyuan to Pingyao.

### 平遥(平遙) → 遥(遙)
- **definition:** distant;far;remote;long-distance
- **pinyin:** yáo
- **components:**
  - 肴
    - yáo
    - dish; prepared food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 辶 ‘walk, movement’ + phonetic 肴 (yáo).
  - **interpretation:** The movement radical hints at travel or paths, suggesting distance or going afar. 肴 supplies the sound yáo; together they convey the idea of going a long way, hence “distant/remote.”
  - **simplification:** Traditional 遙’s complex right-side component was replaced with the simpler, clearly phonetic 肴 to reduce strokes and better reflect the pronunciation; the 辶 component was retained.
- **examples:**
  - 我们进行一次遥控测试(我們進行一次遙控測試)。
    - Wǒmen jìnxíng yī cì yáokòng cèshì.
    - We conducted a remote-control test.
  - 他住在遥远的山村(他住在遙遠的山村)。
    - Tā zhù zài yáoyuǎn de shāncūn.
    - He lives in a remote mountain village.
  - 遥感技术让我们观察地球(遙感技術讓我們觀察地球)。
    - Yáogǎn jìshù ràng wǒmen guānchá dìqiú.
    - Remote sensing technology lets us observe the Earth.
