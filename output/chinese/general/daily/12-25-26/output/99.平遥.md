## 平遥(平遙)
---
- **pinyin:** Píngyáo
- **definition:** Pingyao
- **characters:**
  - 平
    - píng
    - flat, level; peaceful, calm; equal, fair; ordinary, common
  - 遥(遙)
    - yáo
    - distant, far; from afar; long-range
- **etymology:** 平 (píng, "flat/peaceful") points to level, tranquil terrain, while 遥(遙) (yáo, "distant") evokes far reach. Together they picture a flat plain extending into the distance, fitting as a toponym.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算去平遥旅游(我打算去平遙旅遊)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn qù Píngyáo lǚyóu.
    - I'm planning to travel to Pingyao.
  - 平遥古城保存得非常完整(平遙古城保存得非常完整)，是世界文化遗产(是世界文化遺產)。
    - Píngyáo gǔchéng bǎocún de fēicháng wánzhěng, shì shìjiè wénhuà yíchǎn.
    - The ancient city of Pingyao is very well preserved and is a UNESCO World Cultural Heritage site.
