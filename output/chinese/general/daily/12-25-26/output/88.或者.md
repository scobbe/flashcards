## 或者
---
- **pinyin:** huòzhě
- **definition:** or; perhaps
- **characters:**
  - 或
    - huò
    - or
  - 者
    - zhě
    - one who
- **examples:**
  - 我们明天去爬山(我們明天去爬山)，或者在家休息(或者在家休息)。
    - wǒmen míngtiān qù páshān, huòzhě zài jiā xiūxi
    - We can go hiking tomorrow, or stay home and rest.
  - 他没来(他沒來)，或者是路上堵车了(或者是路上堵車了)。
    - tā méi lái, huòzhě shì lù shàng dǔchē le
    - He didn't come; perhaps there was a traffic jam on the way.
