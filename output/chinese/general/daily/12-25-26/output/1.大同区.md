## 大同区
---
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great; major, main; eldest
  - 同
    - tóng
    - same, identical; together, in common; with; to agree
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area, region, zone
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "great") with 同 (tóng, "together") forms 大同, the classical idea of "great unity" used as a place name; 区(區) (qū, "district") marks it as an administrative district of Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算去台北的大同区逛迪化街(我打算去臺北的大同區逛迪化街)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn qù Táiběi de Dàtóng Qū guàng Díhuà Jiē.
    - I’m planning to go to Datong District in Taipei to stroll along Dihua Street.
