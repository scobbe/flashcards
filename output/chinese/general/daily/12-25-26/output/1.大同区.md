## 大同区
---
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great, major; eldest, senior; very, extremely
  - 同
    - tóng
    - same, identical; together, with; to agree
  - 区(區)
    - qū, ōu
    - area, region, district; to distinguish, to classify; to divide, to partition; surname Ou
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** Brief: 大 (dà, "great") + 同 (tóng, "together") + 区(區) (qū, "district") = "Datong District"
  - **interpretation:** 大 (dà, "great") and 同 (tóng, "together") form the proper name Datong, and 区(區) (qū, "district") marks it as an administrative district in Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在台北的大同区(我住在臺北的大同區)，那里有很多传统市场(那裡有很多傳統市場)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Táiběi de Dàtóng Qū, nàlǐ yǒu hěn duō chuántǒng shìchǎng.
    - I live in Datong District in Taipei; there are many traditional markets there.
### 大
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区
- **pinyin:** dà
- **definition:** big, large; great, major; eldest, senior; very, extremely
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictograph: a person with arms and legs spread to signify largeness.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a human figure spreading limbs wide to indicate something big. The image generalized to mean 'large' and by extension 'great,' 'major,' or 'eldest.'
### 同
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **definition:** same, identical; together, with; to agree
- **components:**
  - 冂
    - jiōng
    - enclosure; border
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 冂 (jiōng, "enclosure") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** An enclosure with a mouth inside suggests mouths gathered within the same boundary, expressing being together or unified. From this idea of sharing one space/voice come senses like “same,” “together/with,” and by extension “to agree.”
### 冂
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区 → 同
- **pinyin:** jiōng
- **definition:** enclosure; border
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictograph of an open border/enclosure.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the outline of a boundary or frame with the bottom open, suggesting an outer edge or surrounding limit. Over time it came to mean a border, enclosure, or outskirts.
### 口
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区 → 同
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram of an open mouth/opening.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of a square opening representing a mouth. It naturally came to mean mouth and, by extension, any opening or matters of speech.
### 区(區)
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区
- **pinyin:** qū, ōu
- **definition:** area, region, district; to distinguish, to classify; to divide, to partition; surname Ou
- **components:**
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - items; many mouths
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** semantic: enclosure + 品 (pǐn, 'many mouths; items').
  - **interpretation:** Shows several 'mouths' grouped within a boundary, suggesting subdivided places or classifications. By extension it refers to an area or district, and also the act of distinguishing or partitioning. The simplified form preserves the idea of an enclosed division.
### 品
- **breadcrumbs:** 大同区 → 区
- **pinyin:** pǐn
- **definition:** items; many mouths
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Three mouths together signifies many mouths, indicating plurality. By extension it refers to multiple items or goods and, through the idea of many people evaluating, also ties to quality or grade.
