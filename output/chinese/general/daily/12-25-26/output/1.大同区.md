## 大同区
---
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
  - 同
    - tóng
    - same
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big") and 同 (tóng, "together") were selected in 1946 to mark the unification of the two historic quarters 大稻埕 and 大龍峒—both starting with 大—into one area, i.e., a "great togetherness." The name simultaneously invokes the Confucian ideal 大同 (Great Unity) favored in ROC-era toponyms, while 区(區) (qū, "district") signals its administrative status.
- **examples:**
  - 大同区是台北市的一个行政区(大同區是臺北市的一個行政區)。
    - dàtóng qū shì táiběi shì de yí gè xíngzhèng qū
    - Datong District is an administrative district of Taipei City.
