## 江湖
---
- **pinyin:** jiānghú
- **definition:** martial world
- **characters:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river, large river; Yangtze River; surname Jiang
  - 湖
    - hú
    - lake
- **etymology:** 江 (jiāng, "river") and 湖 (hú, "lake") form a merism for the broad, unregulated world beyond officialdom, metaphorically the roaming sphere of itinerants—later specialized in wuxia as the "martial world."
- **examples:**
  - 他年轻时行走江湖(他年輕時行走江湖)，凭一身武艺闯出了名气(憑一身武藝闖出了名氣)。
    - Tā niánqīng shí xíngzǒu jiānghú, píng yī shēn wǔyì chuǎng chūle míngqì.
    - When he was young, he roamed the martial world and, relying on his skills, made a name for himself.
  - 在武林中行走江湖(在武林中行走江湖)，最重要的是守住江湖规矩(最重要的是守住江湖規矩)。
    - Zài wǔlín zhōng xíngzǒu jiānghú, zuì zhòngyào de shì shǒu zhù jiānghú guīju.
    - In the martial world, the most important thing is to uphold the codes of the jianghu.
