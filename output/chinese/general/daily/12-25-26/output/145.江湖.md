## 江湖
---
- **pinyin:** jiānghú
- **definition:** martial world
- **characters:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 湖
    - hú
    - lake
- **etymology:** Combines 江 (jiāng, "river") with 湖 (hú, "lake") to express martial world.
- **examples:**
  - 初入江湖的他还不懂规矩(初入江湖的他還不懂規矩)。
    - chū rù jiānghú de tā hái bù dǒng guījǔ
    - As a newcomer to the martial world, he still doesn't understand the rules.
