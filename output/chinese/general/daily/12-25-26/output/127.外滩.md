## 外滩(外灘)
---
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **definition:** the Bund
- **characters:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach
- **etymology:** 外 (wài, "outside") marks the Huangpu riverbank outside Shanghai’s old walled city, while 滩(灘) (tān, "shoal/riverfront") names the shallow alluvial foreshore suited to landings and embankments; this labeled the outlying riverfront that developed into the foreign concessions’ embanked waterfront, 'the Bund'.
- **examples:**
  - 上海的外滩很适合傍晚散步(上海的外灘很適合傍晚散步)。
    - shànghǎi de wàitān hěn shìhé bàngwǎn sànbù
    - The Bund in Shanghai is perfect for an evening stroll.
