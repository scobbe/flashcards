## 外滩(外灘)
---
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **definition:** the Bund
- **characters:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside, external, outer; foreign; besides, in addition; maternal, on the mother's side
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach; sandbank, sandbar, shoal; mudflat, tidal flat; rapids
- **etymology:** 外 (wài, "outside/outer") plus 滩(灘) (tān, "beach/sandbank") literally yields "outer shoal," a sandbank on the outside beyond the city or river bend; by extension it names the waterfront embankment known as the Bund.
- **examples:**
  - 傍晚我们去外滩散步(傍晚我們去外灘散步)，夜景特别漂亮(夜景特別漂亮)。
    - Bàngwǎn wǒmen qù wàitān sànbù, yèjǐng tèbié piàoliang.
    - We took a walk along the Bund at dusk; the night view was stunning.
  - 外滩是来上海必去的景点(外灘是來上海必去的景點)。
    - Wàitān shì lái Shànghǎi bì qù de jǐngdiǎn.
    - The Bund is a must-see attraction when you come to Shanghai.
