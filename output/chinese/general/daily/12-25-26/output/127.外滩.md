## 外滩(外灘)
---
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **definition:** the Bund
- **characters:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach
- **etymology:** Combines 外 (wài, "outside") with 滩(灘) (tān, "beach") to express the bund.
- **examples:**
  - 上海的外滩很适合傍晚散步(上海的外灘很適合傍晚散步)。
    - shànghǎi de wàitān hěn shìhé bàngwǎn sànbù
    - The Bund in Shanghai is perfect for an evening stroll.
