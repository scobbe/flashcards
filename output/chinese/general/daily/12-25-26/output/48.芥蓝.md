## 芥蓝
---
- **pinyin:** gàilán
- **definition:** Chinese broccoli
- **characters:**
  - 芥
    - jiè
    - mustard
  - 蓝(藍)
    - lán
    - blue
- **etymology:** Combines 芥 (jiè, "mustard") with 蓝(藍) (lán, "blue") to express chinese broccoli.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢清炒芥蓝(我最喜歡清炒芥蘭)，爽脆又清香(爽脆又清香)。
    - wǒ zuì xǐhuān qīng chǎo gàilán, shuǎngcuì yòu qīngxiāng
    - I love stir-fried Chinese broccoli; it's crisp and fragrant.
  - 这家餐厅的蒜蓉芥蓝很地道(這家餐廳的蒜蓉芥蘭很地道)。
    - zhè jiā cāntīng de suànróng gàilán hěn dìdào
    - The garlic Chinese broccoli at this restaurant is very authentic.
