## 芥蓝
---
- **pinyin:** gàilán
- **definition:** Chinese broccoli
- **characters:**
  - 芥
    - jiè
    - mustard; mustard seed; trifling, tiny; speck, mote
  - 蓝(藍)
    - lán
    - blue; indigo; indigo plant; blue dye
- **etymology:** 芥 (jiè, "mustard") marks the mustard/Brassica family. 蓝(藍) (lán, "blue/indigo") evokes the plant’s bluish-green leaves, together naming a blue-green mustard-family vegetable (Chinese broccoli).
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢清炒芥蓝(我最喜歡清炒芥藍)，口感又脆又嫩(口感又脆又嫩)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuān qīngchǎo gàilán, kǒugǎn yòu cuì yòu nèn.
    - I love stir-fried Chinese broccoli; it’s crisp and tender.
  - 这道芥蓝牛肉是粤菜的经典(這道芥藍牛肉是粵菜的經典)。
    - Zhè dào gàilán niúròu shì Yuècài de jīngdiǎn.
    - This Chinese broccoli and beef dish is a Cantonese classic.
