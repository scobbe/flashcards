## 甜菜
---
- **pinyin:** tiáncài
- **definition:** beet
- **characters:**
  - 甜
    - tián
    - sweet
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable
- **etymology:** Combines 甜 (tián, "sweet") with 菜 (cài, "vegetable") to express beet.
- **examples:**
  - 甜菜可以用来制糖(甜菜可以用来制糖)。
    - tiáncài kěyǐ yònglái zhìtáng
    - Beets can be used to make sugar.
