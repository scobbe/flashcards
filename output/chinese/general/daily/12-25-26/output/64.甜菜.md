## 甜菜
---
- **pinyin:** tiáncài
- **definition:** beet
- **characters:**
  - 甜
    - tián
    - sweet (taste), sugary; pleasant, delightful; sound (sleep), restful
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable, greens; dish, course; cuisine, style of cooking; (slang) poor, weak (at something), noob
- **etymology:** 甜 (tián, "sweet") modifies 菜 (cài, "vegetable") to mean a "sweet vegetable," aptly naming the beet for its sugar-rich root (especially the sugar beet).
- **examples:**
  - 我买了几颗甜菜(我買了幾顆甜菜)，准备拿来做沙拉(準備拿來做沙拉)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le jǐ kē tiáncài, zhǔnbèi ná lái zuò shālā.
    - I bought a few beets to make a salad.
