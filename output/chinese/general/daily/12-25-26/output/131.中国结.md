## 中国结(中國結)
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó jié
- **definition:** Chinese knot
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng, zhòng
    - middle, center; in, among, within; China, Chinese; to hit the mark, be hit
  - 国(國)
    - guó
    - country, nation, state; homeland, motherland; national, of the state
  - 结(結)
    - jié, jiē
    - to tie, knot, bind; knot, tie; to conclude, settle, close; to form, bear fruit
- **etymology:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") and 国(國) (guó, "country") combine as 中国 (China), and this attributive modifies 结(結) (jié, "knot") to denote the traditional Chinese decorative knot.
- **examples:**
  - 奶奶亲手编了一个红色的中国结(奶奶親手編了一個紅色的中國結)，挂在门口保佑平安(掛在門口保佑平安)。
    - Nǎinai qīnshǒu biān le yí gè hóngsè de Zhōngguó jié, guà zài ménkǒu bǎoyòu píng'ān.
    - Grandma hand-wove a red Chinese knot and hung it at the door for good luck.
