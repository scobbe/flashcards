## 娌
---
- **pinyin:** lǐ
- **definition:** wife of a younger brother
- **examples:**
  - 我小娌今天请我们去她家吃饭(我小娌今天請我們去她家吃飯) (wǒ xiǎo lǐ jīntiān qǐng wǒmen qù tā jiā chīfàn, "My younger brother's wife invited us to her place for a meal today")
  - 她刚过门，成了我们家的小娌(她剛過門，成了我們家的小娌) (tā gāng guòmén, chéng le wǒmen jiā de xiǎo lǐ, "She just married into the family and became our younger brother's wife")
