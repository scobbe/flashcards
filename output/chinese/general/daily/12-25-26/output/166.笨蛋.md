## 笨蛋
---
- **pinyin:** bèndàn
- **definition:** idiot
- **characters:**
  - 笨
    - bèn
    - stupid, foolish; clumsy, awkward
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg; bastard, jerk; person, guy (pejorative suffix); testicle(s)
- **etymology:** Combines 笨 (bèn, "stupid; clumsy") with 蛋 (dàn, "egg; pejorative 'guy'") to mean a contemptible or foolish person—literally a "stupid guy/egg," following a pattern where 蛋 marks an insulting label for a person.
- **examples:**
  - 别再做这种事了(別再做這種事了)，你这个笨蛋(你這個笨蛋)！
    - Bié zài zuò zhè zhǒng shì le, nǐ zhège bèndàn!
    - Stop doing that, you idiot!
  - 他不是笨蛋(他不是笨蛋)，他只是没理解问题(他只是沒理解問題)。
    - Tā bù shì bèndàn, tā zhǐshì méi lǐjiě wèntí.
    - He’s not an idiot; he just didn’t understand the problem.
