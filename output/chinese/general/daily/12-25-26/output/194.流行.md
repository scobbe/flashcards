## 流行
---
- **pinyin:** liúxíng
- **definition:** popular; prevalent
- **characters:**
  - 流
    - liú
    - to flow; to spread, circulate; to drift, wander; class, grade
  - 行
    - xíng,háng
    - to go, walk; to do, carry out; OK, all right, permitted; trade, profession, industry
- **etymology:** Combines 流 (liú, "to flow; to spread") with 行 (xíng, "to go; to be practiced") to depict ideas or trends that flow through society and are actively carried out, hence "popular; prevalent".
- **examples:**
  - 这首歌现在很流行(這首歌現在很流行)。
    - Zhè shǒu gē xiànzài hěn liúxíng.
    - This song is really popular right now.
  - 最近感冒很流行(最近感冒很流行)，大家要注意卫生(大家要注意衛生)。
    - Zuìjìn gǎnmào hěn liúxíng, dàjiā yào zhùyì wèishēng.
    - Colds are prevalent lately; everyone should pay attention to hygiene.
