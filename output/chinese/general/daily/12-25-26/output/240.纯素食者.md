## 纯素食者(純素食者)
---
- **pinyin:** chúnsùshízhě
- **definition:** vegan
- **characters:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain
  - 食
    - shí
    - food
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person
- **etymology:** Combines 纯(純) (chún, "pure"), 素 (sù, "plain"), 食 (shí, "food"), and 者 (zhě, "person") to express vegan.
- **examples:**
  - 作为纯素食者(作為純素食者)，我不吃任何动物产品(我不吃任何動物產品)。
    - zuòwéi chúnsùshízhě, wǒ bù chī rènhé dòngwù chǎnpǐn
    - As a vegan, I don't eat any animal products.
