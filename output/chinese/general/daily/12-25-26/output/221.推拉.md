## 推拉
---
- **pinyin:** tuīlā
- **definition:** push and pull
- **characters:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - push
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull
- **examples:**
  - 门上贴着“推拉”的标志(門上貼著「推拉」的標誌)，请按提示操作(請按提示操作)。
    - mén shàng tiēzhe "tuīlā" de biāozhì
    - qǐng àn tíshì cāozuò, "A sign reading push and pull is posted on the door; please follow the instructions.
  - 这种窗户采用推拉设计(這種窗戶採用推拉設計)，通风很好(通風很好)。
    - zhè zhǒng chuānghu cǎiyòng tuīlā shèjì, tōngfēng hěn hǎo
    - This type of window uses a push-pull design and has good ventilation.
