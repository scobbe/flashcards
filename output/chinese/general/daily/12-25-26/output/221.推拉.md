## 推拉
---
- **pinyin:** tuīlā
- **definition:** push and pull
- **characters:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - push; infer, deduce; promote, spread; recommend
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull, drag; play (a bowed instrument); solicit, attract; defecate, crap
- **etymology:** Pairs 推 (tuī, "push") with 拉 (lā, "pull") as opposing, complementary actions, forming a binomial that denotes the two directions of applied force or motion.
- **examples:**
  - 这扇门坏了(這扇門壞了)，推拉都不顺畅(推拉都不順暢)。
    - Zhè shàn mén huài le, tuīlā dōu bù shùnchàng.
    - This door is broken; pushing or pulling it isn’t smooth.
  - 请按照门上的指示推拉(請按照門上的指示推拉)。
    - Qǐng ànzhào mén shàng de zhǐshì tuīlā.
    - Please push and pull as indicated on the door.
