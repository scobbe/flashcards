## 冒
---
- **pinyin:** mào
- **definition:** emit; give off
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: phonetic 冃 + semantic 目 (mù, "eyes").
- **examples:**
  - 工厂的烟囱冒出浓烟(工廠的煙囪冒出濃煙)。
    - gōngchǎng de yāncōng mào chū nóngyān
    - The factory chimney emitted thick smoke.
  - 下水道口一直在冒臭气(下水道口一直在冒臭氣)。
    - xiàshuǐdào kǒu yìzhí zài mào chòuqì
    - The sewer opening keeps giving off a stench.
