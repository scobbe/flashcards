## coconut milk
### yēnǎi
---
- **definition:** coconut milk
- **pinyin:** yēnǎi
- **components:**
  - 椰
    - yē
    - coconut; coconut palm; palm tree; coconut-related
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; to breastfeed; (paternal) grandmother
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 椰(coconut) + 奶(milk) = coconut milk (liquid from coconut flesh).
  - **interpretation:** Refers to the rich white liquid expressed from grated coconut flesh, common in desserts and curries. Distinct from 椰子水, the clear liquid inside a fresh coconut.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝椰奶(我喜歡喝椰奶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē yēnǎi.
    - I like to drink coconut milk.
  - 这道咖喱用椰奶增添香味(這道咖哩用椰奶增添香味)。
    - Zhè dào gālí yòng yēnǎi zēngtiān xiāngwèi.
    - This curry uses coconut milk to add aroma and flavor.
  - 别把椰奶和椰子水混淆(別把椰奶和椰子水混淆)。
    - Bié bǎ yēnǎi hé yēzi shuǐ hùnxiáo.
    - Don't confuse coconut milk with coconut water.
### 椰奶 → 椰
- **definition:** coconut; coconut palm; palm tree; coconut-related
- **pinyin:** yē
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood; tree
  - 耶
    - yé
    - interrogative particle; used in transliterations
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 木 ("tree") + phonetic: 耶 (OC *laː)
  - **interpretation:** 木 signals a wood/tree-related meaning, fitting a palm or coconut tree. 耶 contributes the sound; through historical shifts from its Old Chinese reading, it guides the modern pronunciation yē rather than adding meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两个椰子(我買了兩個椰子)。
    - Wǒ mǎile liǎng gè yēzi.
    - I bought two coconuts.
  - 海边的椰树在风中摇摆(海邊的椰樹在風中搖擺)。
    - Hǎibiān de yēshù zài fēng zhōng yáobǎi.
    - The palm trees by the seaside sway in the wind.
  - 这款饮料用新鲜椰奶制成(這款飲料用新鮮椰奶製成)。
    - Zhè kuǎn yǐnliào yòng xīnxiān yēnǎi zhìchéng.
    - This drink is made with fresh coconut milk.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 木
- **definition:** wood; tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram (象形)
  - **description:** a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom (more visible in earlier forms).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tree with spreading branches above and roots below, as seen clearly in early scripts. The vertical stroke is the trunk, while the short horizontal strokes became stylized limbs and roots in the regular form.
- **examples:**
  - 这张桌子是木头做的(這張桌子是木頭做的)。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi shì mùtou zuò de.
    - This table is made of wood.
  - 山上长满了树木(山上長滿了樹木)。
    - Shān shang zhǎng mǎn le shùmù.
    - The mountainside is covered with trees.
  - 木材被运到工厂加工(木材被運到工廠加工)。
    - Mùcái bèi yùn dào gōngchǎng jiāgōng.
    - The timber was transported to the factory for processing.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 耶
- **definition:** interrogative particle; used in transliterations
- **pinyin:** yé
- **components:**
  - 耳
    - ěr
    - ear
  - 也
    - yě
    - also; too
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** corruption
  - **description:** Corruption of 邪 (OC *ljaː, *laː).
  - **interpretation:** A scribal corruption of 邪 that gained currency as an independent graph. It carried over a similar reading and came to serve as a sentence-final particle and as a phonetic element in transcriptions of foreign names.
- **examples:**
  - 此为何耶(此為何耶)。
    - Cǐ wèihé yé.
    - Why is this?
  - 他信奉耶稣(他信奉耶穌)。
    - Tā xìnfèng Yēsū.
    - He believes in Jesus.
  - 他们去了耶路撒冷(他們去了耶路撒冷)。
    - Tāmen qùle Yēlùsālěng.
    - They went to Jerusalem.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 耶 → 耳
- **definition:** ear
- **pinyin:** ěr
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: an ear
  - **interpretation:** Sketches the side view of a human ear: the vertical stroke outlines the rim while shorter strokes suggest inner folds. Early forms were more curved and naturalistic; later writing regularized the lines but the image remains recognizable.
- **examples:**
  - 他耳朵疼(他耳朵疼)。
    - Tā ěrduo téng.
    - His ear hurts.
  - 戴上耳机(戴上耳機)，听音乐(聽音樂)。
    - Dài shàng ěrjī, tīng yīnyuè.
    - Put on headphones and listen to music.
  - 医生检查他的左耳(醫生檢查他的左耳)。
    - Yīshēng jiǎnchá tā de zuǒ ěr.
    - The doctor examined his left ear.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 耶 → 也
- **definition:** also; too
- **pinyin:** yě
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a child (子) with an open mouth (口) crying; original form of 嗁 ‘to cry’. Arms were removed in bronze inscriptions and the components fused in the modern shape.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a crying child, combining the ideas of ‘child’ and ‘mouth’ to show wailing. Over time, the drawing simplified and the form was repurposed as a sentence-final particle and adverb meaning ‘also/too,’ while the crying sense survived in 嗁.
- **examples:**
  - 我也是学生(我也是學生)。
    - Wǒ yě shì xuéshēng.
    - I am also a student.
  - 他不去(他不去)，我也不去(我也不去)。
    - Tā bù qù, wǒ yě bù qù.
    - He isn’t going; I’m not going either.
  - 不仅她会唱歌(不僅她會唱歌)，他也会跳舞(他也會跳舞)。
    - Bùjǐn tā huì chànggē, tā yě huì tiàowǔ.
    - Not only can she sing, he can also dance.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 耶 → 也 → 子
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: image of a baby with a large head and spread arms; legs wrapped in a blanket.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a swaddled baby with a prominent head and outstretched arms. Over time the figure was stylized into the modern form while still evoking an infant.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我唯一的儿子(他是我唯一的兒子)。
    - Tā shì wǒ wéiyī de érzi.
    - He is my only son.
  - 她抱起孩子(她抱起孩子)。
    - Tā bào qǐ háizi.
    - She picked up the child.
  - 这个孩子三岁了(這個孩子三歲了)。
    - Zhège háizi sān suì le.
    - This child is three years old.
### 椰奶 → 椰 → 耶 → 也 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 椰奶 → 奶
- **definition:** milk; breast; to breastfeed; (paternal) grandmother
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman; female
  - 乃
    - nǎi
    - thus; then
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 女 + phonetic: 乃
  - **interpretation:** Combines the 'woman/female' semantic element with the sound value of 乃, pointing to meanings linked to mothers, breasts, and by extension milk or a nanny. The pronunciation follows 乃 while the 女 component anchors the maternal/feminine semantic field.
- **examples:**
  - 我早上常喝牛奶(我早上常喝牛奶)。
    - Wǒ zǎoshang cháng hē niúnǎi.
    - I often drink milk in the morning.
  - 孩子在吃奶，哭声渐小(孩子在吃奶，哭聲漸小)。
    - Háizi zài chī nǎi, kūshēng jiàn xiǎo.
    - The baby is nursing, and the crying is gradually fading.
  - 周末我们去看奶奶(週末我們去看奶奶)。
    - Zhōumò wǒmen qù kàn nǎinai.
    - We’re going to visit Grandma this weekend.
### 椰奶 → 奶 → 女
- **definition:** woman; female
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a woman with breasts kneeling or standing; modern form reversed from oracle forms, with the enclosed area as the remnant of the right breast and the left breast lost.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a female figure with a curved torso and a remaining breast, historically shown kneeling and later flipped to face right in standard script. Graphically related to 母 and 毋, which retain more breast/nipple detail, while the similar-looking 卩 is unrelated.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一个勇敢的女人(她是一個勇敢的女人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè yǒnggǎn de nǚrén.
    - She is a brave woman.
  - 他们有一个可爱的女儿(他們有一個可愛的女兒)，正在上小学(正在上小學)。
    - Tāmen yǒu yí gè kě'ài de nǚ'ér, zhèngzài shàng xiǎoxué.
    - They have a lovely daughter who is in primary school.
  - 这家公司的女员工很多(這家公司的女員工很多)，工作表现很出色(工作表現很出色)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngsī de nǚ yuángōng hěn duō, gōngzuò biǎoxiàn hěn chūsè.
    - This company has many female employees, and their work performance is outstanding.
### 椰奶 → 奶 → 乃
- **definition:** thus; then
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** unknown
  - **description:** Original form 𠄎; origin uncertain; various theories; unrelated to 廴.
  - **interpretation:** Earliest forms show a simple curved graph written as 𠄎, but scholars disagree on what it was intended to depict. Despite visual similarity, it is not derived from 廴, and no consensus formation analysis has been established.
- **examples:**
  - 他思索再三，乃作决定(他思索再三，乃作決定)。
    - Tā sīsuǒ zàisān, nǎi zuò juédìng.
    - After much deliberation, he then made a decision.
  - 事已至此，乃下令撤退(事已至此，乃下令撤退)。
    - Shì yǐ zhì cǐ, nǎi xiàlìng chètuì.
    - Now that matters had come to this point, he then gave the order to retreat.
  - 君臣同心，乃能安邦(君臣同心，乃能安邦)。
    - Jūn chén tóngxīn, nǎi néng ān bāng.
    - Only when ruler and ministers are of one mind can the state be stabilized.
