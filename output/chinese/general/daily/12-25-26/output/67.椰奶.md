## 椰奶
---
- **pinyin:** yēnǎi
- **definition:** coconut milk
- **characters:**
  - 椰
    - yē
    - coconut; coconut palm, coconut tree
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; to breast-feed, nurse; grandma, granny
- **etymology:** 椰 (yē, "coconut") modifies 奶 (nǎi, "milk"), extending 奶 from dairy/breast milk to a milky liquid derived from coconut—hence coconut milk.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢在咖啡里加椰奶(我喜歡在咖啡裡加椰奶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan zài kāfēi lǐ jiā yēnǎi.
    - I like adding coconut milk to my coffee.
  - 天气很热(天氣很熱)，来杯冰椰奶吧(來杯冰椰奶吧)。
    - Tiānqì hěn rè, lái bēi bīng yēnǎi ba.
    - It’s really hot—let’s have a cup of iced coconut milk.
