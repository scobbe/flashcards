## 花生
---
- **pinyin:** huāshēng
- **definition:** peanut
- **characters:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower, blossom; to spend (money, time); multicolored, gaudy, fancy
  - 生
    - shēng
    - to be born, give birth; raw, uncooked, unripe; life, living; grow, produce
- **etymology:** Combines 花 (huā, "flower") and 生 (shēng, "to be born; grow") to mean "born from the flower," alluding to the peanut's pods developing from the fertilized flower underground.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃花生(我喜歡吃花生)，但是我朋友对花生过敏(但是我朋友對花生過敏)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī huāshēng, dànshì wǒ péngyǒu duì huāshēng guòmǐn.
    - I like eating peanuts, but my friend is allergic to peanuts.
