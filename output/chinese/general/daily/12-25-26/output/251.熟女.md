## 熟女
---
- **pinyin:** shúnǚ
- **definition:** stylish mature woman
- **characters:**
  - 熟
    - shú
    - ripe
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:** Combines 熟 (shú, "ripe") with 女 (nǚ, "woman") to express stylish mature woman.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位有型的熟女(她是一位有型的熟女)。
    - tā shì yí wèi yǒuxíng de shúnǚ
    - She is a stylish mature woman.
