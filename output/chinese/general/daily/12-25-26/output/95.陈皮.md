## 陈皮(陳皮)
---
- **pinyin:** chénpí
- **definition:** dried tangerine peel
- **characters:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - to arrange, display, lay out; to state, set forth; old, stale; surname Chen
  - 皮
    - pí
    - skin, hide, leather; peel, rind; naughty, mischievous; superficial, skin-deep
- **etymology:** 陈(陳) (chén, "old, aged") marks that 皮 (pí, "peel, rind") has been matured/dried; together they literally mean "aged peel," referring to the dried, aged tangerine rind.
- **examples:**
  - 广东人喜欢在汤里加陈皮(廣東人喜歡在湯裡加陳皮)，去腻又提香(去膩又提香)。
    - Guǎngdōng rén xǐhuan zài tāng lǐ jiā chénpí, qù nì yòu tíxiāng.
    - People in Guangdong like to add dried tangerine peel to soups; it cuts the greasiness and boosts the aroma.
