## 陈皮(陳皮)
---
- **pinyin:** chénpí
- **definition:** dried tangerine peel
- **characters:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - surname Chen
  - 皮
    - pí
    - skin
- **examples:**
  - 我煲汤时会放一点陈皮增香(我煲湯時會放一點陳皮增香)。
    - wǒ bāo tāng shí huì fàng yìdiǎn chénpí zēngxiāng
    - When I make soup, I add a little dried tangerine peel to enhance the aroma.
