## 行动电源(行動電源)
---
- **pinyin:** xíngdòngdiànyuán
- **definition:** power bank
- **characters:**
  - 行
    - xíng, háng
    - to go, walk; to do, carry out; OK, all right; profession, trade
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - to move; to act, take action; to touch, stir; to change, alter
  - 电(電)
    - diàn
    - electricity; electric, electrical; lightning; telegram, telegraph
  - 源
    - yuán
    - source, origin; headwaters, fountainhead; cause; supply
- **etymology:** 行 (xíng, "to go") with 动(動) (dòng, "to move") conveys mobility, while 电(電) (diàn, "electricity") with 源 (yuán, "source") means a power source—together, a mobile source of electricity (a power bank).
- **examples:**
  - 我带着一个行动电源(我帶著一個行動電源)，以防手机没电(以防手機沒電)。
    - Wǒ dài zhe yí gè xíngdòng diànyuán, yǐfáng shǒujī méi diàn.
    - I carry a power bank in case my phone dies.
  - 这个行动电源可以同时给两部设备充电(這個行動電源可以同時給兩部設備充電)。
    - Zhège xíngdòng diànyuán kěyǐ tóngshí gěi liǎng bù shèbèi chōngdiàn.
    - This power bank can charge two devices at the same time.
