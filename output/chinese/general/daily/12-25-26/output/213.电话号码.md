## 电话号码(電話號碼)
---
- **pinyin:** diànhuà hàomǎ
- **definition:** telephone number
- **characters:**
  - 电(電)
    - diàn
    - electricity; electric, electrical; lightning; telecommunications (in compounds)
  - 话(話)
    - huà
    - speech, talk; words; saying, remark; language, dialect
  - 号(號)
    - hào
    - number; date (of the month); size; mark, sign
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number, numeral; yard (unit of length); to stack, pile up
- **etymology:** 电(電) (diàn, "telecommunications") plus 话(話) (huà, "speech") form a 'telephone'; 号(號) (hào, "number") plus 码(碼) (mǎ, "code") give a numeric identifier, hence a 'telephone number'.
- **examples:**
  - 他的电话号码是多少(他的電話號碼是多少)？
    - Tā de diànhuà hàomǎ shì duōshǎo?
    - What's his phone number?
