## 拿
---
- **pinyin:** ná
- **definition:** take; hold
- **etymology:** Formed as a semantic compound: top 合 (hé, "combine") over bottom 手 (shǒu, "hand"), visually suggesting a hand bringing things together. This imagery naturally extends to the sense of grasping, seizing, and thus "take; hold."
- **examples:**
  - 出门记得拿伞(出門記得拿傘)。
    - Chūmén jìde ná sǎn.
    - Remember to take an umbrella when you go out.
  - 他拿着孩子的手(他拿著孩子的手)，不让他走丢(不讓他走丟)。
    - Tā ná zhe háizi de shǒu, bú ràng tā zǒu diū.
    - He held the child's hand so he wouldn't get lost.
