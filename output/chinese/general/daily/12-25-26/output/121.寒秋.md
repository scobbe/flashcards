## 寒秋
---
- **pinyin:** hánqiū
- **definition:** late autumn
- **characters:**
  - 寒
    - hán
    - cold, chilly; cold weather; poor, needy; humble, of low status
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn, fall; harvest season
- **etymology:** 寒 (hán, "cold") + 秋 (qiū, "autumn") literally means "cold autumn," referring to the colder phase of the season, i.e., late autumn. The austerity and bleakness associated with 寒 (hán, "cold") reinforce the end-of-harvest mood.
- **examples:**
  - 寒秋时(寒秋時)，枫叶尽染(楓葉盡染)，凉意入骨(涼意入骨)。
    - Hánqiū shí, fēngyè jìn rǎn, liángyì rù gǔ.
    - In late autumn, the maple leaves are fully tinged with color, and the chill cuts to the bone.
  - 寒秋已至(寒秋已至)，秋收已尽(秋收已盡)，候鸟南飞(候鳥南飛)。
    - Hánqiū yǐ zhì, qiūshōu yǐ jìn, hòuniǎo nán fēi.
    - Late autumn has arrived; the harvest is over, and migratory birds fly south.
