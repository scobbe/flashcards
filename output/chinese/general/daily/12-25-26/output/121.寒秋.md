## 寒秋
---
- **pinyin:** hánqiū
- **definition:** late autumn
- **characters:**
  - 寒
    - hán
    - cold
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn
- **etymology:** In the traditional agrarian calendar, 寒 (hán, "cold") signals the late‑autumn turn—e.g., the solar terms 寒露 and 霜降—so attaching it to 秋 (qiū, "autumn") pinpoints the season’s cold phase, i.e., late autumn. Literary usage also lets 寒 (hán, "cold") evoke barrenness and decline, sharpening the image of autumn’s withering end.
- **examples:**
  - 寒秋已至，雁阵南飞(雁陣南飛)。
    - hánqiū yǐ zhì, yànzhèn nán fēi
    - Late autumn has arrived; the wild geese fly south.
