## 不客气(不客氣)
---
- **pinyin:** bú kèqi
- **definition:** you’re welcome; don’t mention it
- **characters:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not, no
  - 客
    - kè
    - guest; customer, client; visitor; traveler
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air, gas; breath; anger; vital energy, life force
- **etymology:** 不 (bù, "not") negates 客 (kè, "guest") with 气(氣) (qì, "air; breath; vital energy"), making a "guest‑air" (polite, formal demeanor) that one declines. Thus it means "don’t put on guest-like airs," i.e., "don’t be so polite," hence "you’re welcome."
- **examples:**
  - 谢谢你帮我修电脑(謝謝你幫我修電腦)。不客气(不客氣)！
    - Xièxie nǐ bāng wǒ xiū diànnǎo. Bú kèqi!
    - Thank you for helping me fix my computer. You’re welcome!
  - 真的麻烦你了(真的麻煩你了)，不客气(不客氣)。
    - Zhēn de máfan nǐ le, bú kèqi.
    - I really put you to a lot of trouble—don’t mention it.
