## 不客气(不客氣)
---
- **pinyin:** bú kèqi
- **definition:** you’re welcome; don’t mention it
- **characters:**
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 客
    - kè
    - guest
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air
- **etymology:** Combines 不 (bù, "not"), 客 (kè, "guest"), and 气(氣) (qì, "air") to convey you’re welcome; don’t mention it.
- **examples:**
  - 不客气(不客氣)。
    - bú kèqi
    - You're welcome
  - 不客气(不客氣)，这是我应该做的(這是我應該做的)。
    - bú kèqi
    - Don't mention it; it's what I should do.
