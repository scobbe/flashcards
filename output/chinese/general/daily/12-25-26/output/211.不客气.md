## 不客气(不客氣)
---
- **pinyin:** bú kèqi
- **definition:** you’re welcome; don’t mention it
- **characters:**
  - 不: bù, not
  - 客: kè, guest
  - 气(氣): qì, air
- **examples:**
  - 不客气(不客氣) (bú kèqi, "You're welcome")
  - 不客气，这是我应该做的(不客氣，這是我應該做的) (bú kèqi, "Don't mention it; it's what I should do")
