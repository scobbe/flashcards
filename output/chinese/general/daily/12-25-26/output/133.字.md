## character
### zì
---
- **definition:** character
- **pinyin:** zì
- **components:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; son
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 宀 (roof) + phonetic 子 (OC *ʔslɯʔ, child).
  - **interpretation:** A child under a roof evokes a domestic or scholastic setting, hinting at learning and writing, while 子 contributes the sound. Together it signals the idea of written symbols learned indoors, leading to the meaning “character.”
- **examples:**
  - 这个字很难写(這個字很難寫)。
    - Zhège zì hěn nán xiě.
    - This character is hard to write.
  - 我不认识这个字(我不認識這個字)。
    - Wǒ bù rènshi zhège zì.
    - I don’t recognize this character.
  - 请把每个字写清楚(請把每個字寫清楚)。
    - Qǐng bǎ měi gè zì xiě qīngchu.
    - Please write each character clearly.
### 字 → 子
- **definition:** child; son
- **pinyin:** zǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: image of a baby with a large head and spread arms; legs wrapped in a blanket.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a swaddled baby with a prominent head and outstretched arms. Over time the figure was stylized into the modern form while still evoking an infant.
- **examples:**
  - 他是我唯一的儿子(他是我唯一的兒子)。
    - Tā shì wǒ wéiyī de érzi.
    - He is my only son.
  - 她抱起孩子(她抱起孩子)。
    - Tā bào qǐ háizi.
    - She picked up the child.
  - 这个孩子三岁了(這個孩子三歲了)。
    - Zhège háizi sān suì le.
    - This child is three years old.
