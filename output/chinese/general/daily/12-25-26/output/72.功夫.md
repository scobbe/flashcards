## 功夫
---
- **pinyin:** gōngfu
- **definition:** kung fu
- **characters:**
  - 功
    - gōng
    - achievement
  - 夫
    - fū
    - husband
- **examples:**
  - 她每天练功夫(她每天練功夫)。
    - tā měitiān liàn gōngfu
    - She practices kung fu every day.
  - 他从小跟师父学功夫(他從小跟師父學功夫)。
    - tā cóngxiǎo gēn shīfu xué gōngfu
    - He has studied kung fu with a master since childhood.
