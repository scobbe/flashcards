## 园(園)
---
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **definition:** garden
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 囗 (wéi, "enclosure") conveys a walled, enclosed space, while the inner phonetic 袁 (yuán, "Yuan (surname)")—simplified to 元 (yuán, "origin")—provides the sound. Visually it shows something contained within walls, fitting the idea of an enclosed plot used as a garden.
- **examples:**
  - 她每天在后院的花园里浇水(她每天在後院的花園裡澆水)，那里种满了玫瑰(那裡種滿了玫瑰)。
    - Tā měitiān zài hòuyuàn de huāyuán lǐ jiāoshuǐ, nàlǐ zhòng mǎn le méiguī.
    - She waters the backyard garden every day, and it's filled with roses.
