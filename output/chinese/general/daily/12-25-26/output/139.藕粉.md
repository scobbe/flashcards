## 藕粉
---
- **pinyin:** ǒufěn
- **definition:** lotus root starch
- **characters:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder
- **etymology:** Combines 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") with 粉 (fěn, "powder") to express lotus root starch.
- **examples:**
  - 我奶奶常用藕粉给我做早餐(我奶奶常用藕粉給我做早餐)。
    - wǒ nǎinai cháng yòng ǒu fěn gěi wǒ zuò zǎocān
    - My grandma often uses lotus root starch to make me breakfast.
