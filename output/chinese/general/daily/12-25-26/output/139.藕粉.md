## 藕粉
---
- **pinyin:** ǒufěn
- **definition:** lotus root starch
- **characters:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root, lotus rhizome
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder; flour; noodles, vermicelli; fan, supporter (slang)
- **etymology:** Combines 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") with 粉 (fěn, "powder") to denote the powdered starch made from lotus root, with the first naming the source and the second specifying its processed form.
- **examples:**
  - 早餐我喜欢冲一杯藕粉(早餐我喜歡沖一杯藕粉)，暖胃又有营养(暖胃又有營養)。
    - Zǎocān wǒ xǐhuān chōng yì bēi ǒufěn, nuǎn wèi yòu yǒu yíngyǎng.
    - For breakfast I like to make a cup of lotus root starch drink; it’s warming and nutritious.
