## 食物
---
- **pinyin:** shíwù
- **definition:** food
- **characters:**
  - 食
    - shí, sì
    - to eat; food; meal; to feed
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing, object; matter, substance; creature, animal; goods, commodities
- **etymology:** 食 (shí, "to eat; food") with 物 (wù, "thing; object") combines as "things for eating," i.e., edible items or provisions.
- **examples:**
  - 如果食物变质了(如果食物變質了)，就不要吃(就不要吃)。
    - Rúguǒ shíwù biànzhì le, jiù búyào chī.
    - If the food has gone bad, don't eat it.
