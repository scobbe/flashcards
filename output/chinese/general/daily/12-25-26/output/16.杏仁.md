## 杏仁
---
- **pinyin:** xìngrén
- **definition:** almond
- **characters:**
  - 杏: xìng, apricot
  - 仁: rén, benevolence
- **examples:**
  - 这家店的杏仁饼特别香(這家店的杏仁餅特別香) (zhè jiā diàn de xìngrén bǐng tèbié xiāng, "The almond cookies at this shop are especially fragrant")
  - 他对杏仁过敏，所以不能吃(他對杏仁過敏，所以不能吃) (tā duì xìngrén guòmǐn, suǒyǐ bùnéng chī, "He's allergic to almonds, so he can't eat them")
