## 杏仁
---
- **pinyin:** xìngrén
- **definition:** almond
- **characters:**
  - 杏
    - xìng
    - apricot; apricot tree
  - 仁
    - rén
    - benevolence, humaneness; kernel, nut meat
- **etymology:** 杏 (xìng, "apricot") with 仁 (rén, "kernel, nut meat") literally means an "apricot kernel," which by extension denotes almonds due to the similar nut and flavor in culinary and medicinal contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 我对杏仁过敏(我對杏仁過敏)，所以不能吃含有杏仁的甜点(所以不能吃含有杏仁的甜點)。
    - Wǒ duì xìng rén guò mǐn, suǒ yǐ bù néng chī hán yǒu xìng rén de tián diǎn.
    - I’m allergic to almonds, so I can’t eat desserts that contain them.
