## 汤
---
- **pinyin:** tāng
- **definition:** soup
- **etymology:** From 湯 (昜 → 𠃓) Definitions: For pronunciation and definitions of 汤 – see 湯 ("hot or boiling water ; broth ; stock ; etc.
- **examples:**
  - 我想喝一碗热汤(我想喝一碗熱湯)。
    - wǒ xiǎng hē yì wǎn rè tāng
    - I want to drink a bowl of hot soup.
  - 这道鸡汤很鲜美(這道雞湯很鮮美)。
    - zhè dào jī tāng hěn xiānměi
    - This chicken soup is very tasty.
