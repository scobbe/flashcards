## invoice
### fāpiào
---
- **definition:** invoice
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - to issue; to send out; to emit; to develop
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; banknote; ballot; bill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 发(issue/emit) + 票(ticket/slip) = invoice.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the act of issuing with a slip/document to denote an officially issued bill or receipt. Commonly refers to tax-valid invoices used for reimbursement and accounting.
  - **simplification:** 發→发 to reduce strokes via a cursive-derived form; 票 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 请开一张发票(請開一張發票)。
    - Qǐng kāi yì zhāng fāpiào.
    - Please issue an invoice.
  - 我丢了昨天的发票(我丟了昨天的發票)。
    - Wǒ diū le zuótiān de fāpiào.
    - I lost yesterday’s invoice.
  - 报销需要正规发票(報銷需要正規發票)。
    - Bàoxiāo xūyào zhèngguī fāpiào.
    - Reimbursement requires an official invoice.

### 发票 → 票
- **definition:** ticket; banknote; ballot; bill
- **pinyin:** piào
- **components:**
  - 示
    - shì
    - altar; to show; sign
  - 西
    - xī
    - west
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound (會意/会意)
  - **description:** Ideogrammic: semantic 火 “fire” + semantic 𢍱 “to rise high” (two pairs of hands); simplified from 𤐫 with 火→示 and 𢍱→覀, later used for “token/label.”
  - **interpretation:** Combining fire with raised hands evoked sparks rising upward, a vivid image of lifting or flickering. That sense generalized to a “token/label,” which in later usage covers tickets, banknotes, and ballots as tangible proofs or markers.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两张电影票(我買了兩張電影票)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le liǎng zhāng diànyǐng piào.
    - I bought two movie tickets.
  - 请出示车票(請出示車票)。
    - Qǐng chūshì chēpiào.
    - Please show your ticket.
  - 他把选票投进票箱(他把選票投進票箱)。
    - Tā bǎ xuǎnpiào tóu jìn piàoxiāng.
    - He cast the ballot into the ballot box.
### 发票 → 票 → 示
- **definition:** altar; to show; sign
- **pinyin:** shì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): an altar; extra strokes added to avoid confusion with ancient 下 and 主; unrelated to 小 and 京; see also 帝.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted an altar used for offerings, matching meanings tied to ritual, signs, and showing. Later graphic tweaks distinguished it from similar ancient graphs while preserving the altar-based sense of revelation and display.
- **examples:**
  - 他向领导请示下一步计划(他向領導請示下一步計劃)。
    - Tā xiàng lǐngdǎo qǐngshì xià yī bù jìhuà.
    - He asked the leader for instructions on the next step.
  - 数据清楚地显示趋势(數據清楚地顯示趨勢)。
    - Shùjù qīngchǔ de xiǎnshì qūshì.
    - The data clearly shows the trend.
  - 群众在广场示威(群眾在廣場示威)。
    - Qúnzhòng zài guǎngchǎng shìwēi.
    - The crowd held a demonstration in the square.
### 发票 → 票 → 西
- **definition:** west
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram—bag or basket; later borrowed for phonetic value to mean “west”. Compare 東, 束, 鹵; cf. top component of 票.
  - **interpretation:** Originally sketched as a bag or basket, the graph was later repurposed for its sound to write the word meaning “west.” A traditional reading sees a bird settling into a nest, analogized with the setting sun in the west; this view is debated, though some scholars retain it.
- **examples:**
  - 太阳在西边落下(太陽在西邊落下)。
    - Tàiyáng zài xībiān luòxià.
    - The sun sets in the west.
  - 我住在城市的西部(我住在城市的西部)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài chéngshì de xībù.
    - I live in the western part of the city.
  - 她喜欢西餐(她喜歡西餐)。
    - Tā xǐhuan xīcān.
    - She likes Western food.
