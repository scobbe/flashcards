## 发票
---
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **definition:** invoice
- **characters:**
  - 发(發/髮)
    - fā, fà
    - send out, issue; emit, discharge; develop, expand; hair
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket, voucher; banknote, paper money; ballot, vote; IOU, promissory note
- **etymology:** 发(發) (fā, "issue/send out") with 票 (piào, "voucher/note") conveys an issued paper voucher or proof, hence an invoice.
- **examples:**
  - 我需要报销(我需要報銷)，请把发票给我(請把發票給我)。
    - Wǒ xūyào bàoxiāo, qǐng bǎ fāpiào gěi wǒ.
    - I need to get reimbursed, so please give me the invoice.
