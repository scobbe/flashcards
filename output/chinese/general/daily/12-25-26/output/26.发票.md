## 发票
---
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **definition:** invoice
- **characters:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - send
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket
- **etymology:** Combines 发(發) (fā, "send") with 票 (piào, "ticket") to express invoice.
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一张发票(請給我一張發票)。
    - qǐng gěi wǒ yì zhāng fāpiào
    - Please give me an invoice.
