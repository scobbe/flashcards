## 情变(情變)
---
- **pinyin:** qíngbiàn
- **definition:** breakup; change in relationship
- **characters:**
  - 情
    - qíng
    - emotion
  - 变(變)
    - biàn
    - change
- **examples:**
  - 他因为情变而消沉了很久(他因為情變而消沉了很久)。
    - tā yīnwèi qíngbiàn ér xiāochén le hěn jiǔ
    - He was depressed for a long time because of the breakup.
  - 两人的关系在那次误会后出现了情变(兩人的關係在那次誤會後出現了情變)。
    - liǎng rén de guānxi zài nà cì wùhuì hòu chūxiàn le qíngbiàn
    - Their relationship changed after that misunderstanding.
