## 好久不见(好久不見)
---
- **pinyin:** hǎojiǔ bújiàn
- **definition:** long time no see
- **characters:**
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good
  - 久
    - jiǔ
    - long time
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 见(見)
    - jiàn
    - see
- **etymology:** Combines 好 (hǎo, "good"), 久 (jiǔ, "long time"), 不 (bù, "not"), and 见(見) (jiàn, "see") to express long time no see.
- **examples:**
  - 好久不见啊！(好久不見啊！)。
    - hǎojiǔ bújiàn a
    - Long time no see!
