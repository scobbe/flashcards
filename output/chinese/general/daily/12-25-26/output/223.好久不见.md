## 好久不见(好久不見)
---
- **pinyin:** hǎojiǔ bújiàn
- **definition:** long time no see
- **characters:**
  - 好
    - hǎo
    - good, fine; well; to like, be fond of; easy to
  - 久
    - jiǔ
    - long (time); for a long time; long-standing
  - 不
    - bù
    - not; no; un-, non-
  - 见(見)
    - jiàn
    - to see, to perceive; to meet; to be seen; opinion, view
- **etymology:** 好 (hǎo, "good") intensifies 久 (jiǔ, "long time") to mean "a very long time," then 不 (bù, "not") + 见(見) (jiàn, "see") yields "not see," together expressing "haven't seen [each other] for a long time."
