## 草莓
---
- **pinyin:** cǎoméi
- **definition:** strawberry
- **characters:**
  - 草
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 莓
    - méi
    - berry
- **etymology:** Combines 草 (cǎo, "grass") with 莓 (méi, "berry") to express strawberry.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃草莓(我喜歡吃草莓)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān chī cǎoméi
    - I like to eat strawberries.
