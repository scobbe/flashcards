## 草莓
---
- **pinyin:** cǎoméi
- **definition:** strawberry
- **characters:**
  - 草
    - cǎo
    - grass, herb; straw, thatch; rough, cursory; draft, rough copy
  - 莓
    - méi
    - berry; strawberry (in compounds)
- **etymology:** 草 (cǎo, "grass/herb") marks an herbaceous, ground-level plant, while 莓 (méi, "berry") names the fruit; together, "grass-berry"—a berry that grows low among the grass, i.e., strawberry.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢草莓(我最喜歡草莓)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan cǎoméi.
    - Strawberries are my favorite.
  - 这些草莓很甜(這些草莓很甜)，不过有几颗有点酸(不過有幾顆有點酸)。
    - Zhèxiē cǎoméi hěn tián, búguò yǒu jǐ kē yǒudiǎn suān.
    - These strawberries are very sweet, but a few are a bit sour.
