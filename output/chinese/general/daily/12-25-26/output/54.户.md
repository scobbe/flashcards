## 户(戶)
---
- **pinyin:** hù
- **definition:** household; door
- **etymology:** Pictograph of a single-leaf door viewed from the side, showing the pivot post and door leaf; from this image came the senses ‘door’ and, by extension, ‘household’ (households counted by doors). Not a phono-semantic compound; distinct from 门(門) (mén, "gate").
- **examples:**
  - 村里共有一百户人家(村裡共有一百戶人家)。
    - Cūn lǐ gòng yǒu yībǎi hù rénjiā.
    - The village has a total of one hundred households.
  - 他轻轻把户关上(他輕輕把戶關上)，怕吵醒孩子(怕吵醒孩子)。
    - Tā qīngqīng bǎ hù guān shàng, pà chǎo xǐng háizi.
    - He gently closed the door, afraid of waking the child.
