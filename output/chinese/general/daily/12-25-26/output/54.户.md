## 户(戶)
---
- **pinyin:** hù
- **definition:** household; door
- **examples:**
  - 这个小区有八百多户居民(這個小區有八百多戶居民)。
    - zhège xiǎoqū yǒu bābǎi duō hù jūmín
    - This residential complex has over 800 households.
  - 夜深了，别忘了把门户关好(別忘了把門戶關好)。
    - yè shēn le, bié wàng le bǎ ménhù guān hǎo
    - It's late; don't forget to shut the doors properly.
