## 咏(詠)
---
- **pinyin:** yǒng
- **definition:** chant; eulogize
- **etymology:** A character(詠) meaning chant; eulogize.
- **examples:**
  - 他轻声咏着一首古诗(他輕聲詠著一首古詩)。
    - tā qīngshēng yǒng zhe yì shǒu gǔshī
    - He softly chanted an ancient poem.
  - 这部史诗咏民族的坚韧与荣耀(這部史詩詠民族的堅韌與榮耀)。
    - zhè bù shǐshī yǒng mínzú de jiānrèn yǔ róngyào
    - This epic eulogizes the nation's resilience and glory.
