## 鼓浪屿(鼓浪嶼)
---
- **pinyin:** Gǔlàngyǔ
- **definition:** Gulangyu Island
- **characters:**
  - 鼓
    - gǔ
    - drum; to beat, to strike; to arouse, to incite; to swell, bulge
  - 浪
    - làng
    - wave; unrestrained, dissolute; wasteful, prodigal; to loaf about, to mess around
  - 屿(嶼)
    - yǔ
    - islet, small island
- **etymology:** 鼓 (gǔ, "drum") with 浪 (làng, "wave") evokes waves beating like a drum, while 屿(嶼) (yǔ, "islet") identifies the landform—literally "drum-wave islet," i.e., Gulangyu.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢厦门的鼓浪屿(我最喜歡廈門的鼓浪嶼)，那里到处是历史建筑和音乐(那裡到處是歷史建築和音樂)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan Xiàmén de Gǔlàngyǔ, nàlǐ dàochù shì lìshǐ jiànzhù hé yīnyuè.
    - My favorite place in Xiamen is Gulangyu, where historic buildings and music are everywhere.
