## 开封(開封)
---
- **pinyin:** Kāifēng
- **definition:** Kaifeng
- **characters:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open
  - 封
    - fēng
    - seal
- **etymology:** Combines 开(開) (kāi, "open") with 封 (fēng, "seal") to express kaifeng.
- **examples:**
  - 开封是河南省的一座历史文化名城(開封是河南省的一座歷史文化名城)。
    - Kāifēng shì Hénán Shěng de yí zuò lìshǐ wénhuà míngchéng
    - Kaifeng is a famous historical and cultural city in Henan Province.
