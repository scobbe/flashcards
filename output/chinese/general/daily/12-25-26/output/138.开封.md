## 开封(開封)
---
- **pinyin:** Kāifēng
- **definition:** Kaifeng
- **characters:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open, turn on; start, begin; drive, operate (a vehicle, machine); hold (a meeting, event)
  - 封
    - fēng
    - seal, close up; envelope; measure word for letters; confer a title, enfeoff
- **etymology:** Combines 开(開) (kāi, "open") with 封 (fēng, "fief/enclosure"), literally "open the fief/enclosure," evoking the opening or development of a bounded domain that became the place name.
- **examples:**
  - 开封是河南省的一座古都(開封是河南省的一座古都)，拥有丰富的历史遗迹(擁有豐富的歷史遺跡)。
    - Kāifēng shì Hénán shěng de yī zuò gǔdū, yōngyǒu fēngfù de lìshǐ yíjì.
    - Kaifeng is an ancient capital in Henan Province with abundant historical sites.
