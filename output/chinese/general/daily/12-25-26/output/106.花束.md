## 花束
---
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **definition:** bouquet
- **characters:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle
- **etymology:** 束 (shù, "bundle") originally depicts a sheaf cinched by a band; placed after 花 (huā, "flower") as the head noun, it conceptualizes scattered blooms as a single tied unit for carrying or presenting—hence a bouquet. The form mirrors the measure pattern 一束花, effectively promoting the classifier 束 (shù, "bundle") to name the object.
- **examples:**
  - 她把花束放在窗台上(她把花束放在窗臺上)。
    - tā bǎ huāshù fàng zài chuāngtái shàng
    - She put the bouquet on the windowsill.
  - 婚礼结束后(婚禮結束後)，新娘把花束抛向天空(新娘把花束拋向天空)。
    - hūnlǐ jiéshù hòu, xīnniáng bǎ huāshù pāo xiàng tiānkōng
    - After the wedding, the bride tossed the bouquet into the air.
