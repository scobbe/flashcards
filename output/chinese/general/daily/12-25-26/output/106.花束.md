## 花束
---
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **definition:** bouquet
- **characters:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower, blossom; to spend, expend; multicolored, variegated; blurred, out of focus
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle, bunch; to tie up, bind, restrict; classifier for bunches, bundles, sheaves; beam, ray
- **etymology:** Literally a bundle of flowers: 花 (huā, "flower") plus 束 (shù, "bundle"), indicating flowers tied together into a bunch (a bouquet).
- **examples:**
  - 今天是她的生日(今天是她的生日)，我送给她一个玫瑰花束(我送給她一個玫瑰花束)。
    - Jīntiān shì tā de shēngrì, wǒ sòng gěi tā yí gè méiguī huāshù.
    - It’s her birthday today, and I gave her a rose bouquet.
