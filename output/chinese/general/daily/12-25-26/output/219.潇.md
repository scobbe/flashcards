## 潇(瀟)
---
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left 水 (shuǐ, "water") gives the water/river sense, while right 萧(蕭) (xiāo, "rustling; desolate; surname Xiao") serves as the phonetic, yielding xiāo and evoking a brisk, airy quality. Traditional 瀟 uses 蕭 as the phonetic; simplified 潇 replaces it with 萧 but preserves the "water + xiāo" structure, fitting meanings like "deep, clear, brisk (of water/wind)."
- **examples:**
  - 潭水潇澈(潭水瀟澈)，游鱼历历可数(游魚歷歷可數)。
    - Tánshuǐ xiāochè, yóuyú lìlì kě shǔ.
    - The pool water is limpid and deep; the fish are clearly visible and can be counted.
  - 清晨的海风潇爽(清晨的海風瀟爽)，吹得人精神一振(吹得人精神一振)。
    - Qīngchén de hǎifēng xiāoshuǎng, chuī de rén jīngshén yí zhèn.
    - The morning sea breeze is brisk and gives you an instant lift.
