## 潇(瀟)
---
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **examples:**
  - 潭水潇，月影可掬(潭水瀟，月影可掬) (tán shuǐ xiāo, yuè yǐng kě jū, "The pool water is deep and clear, with the moon’s reflection seemingly within reach")
  - 清晨的海风潇爽，令人精神一振(清晨的海風瀟爽，令人精神一振) (qīngchén de hǎifēng xiāo shuǎng, lìng rén jīngshén yí zhèn, "The morning sea breeze is brisk, instantly lifting one’s spirits")
