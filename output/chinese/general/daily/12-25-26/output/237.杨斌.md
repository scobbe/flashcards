## 杨斌(楊斌)
---
- **pinyin:** Yáng Bīn
- **definition:** Yang Bin (name)
- **characters:**
  - 杨(楊)
    - yáng
    - poplar
  - 斌
    - bīn
    - refined
- **etymology:** Combines 杨(楊) (yáng, "poplar") with 斌 (bīn, "refined") to express yang bin (name).
- **examples:**
  - 杨斌是我的同学(楊斌是我的同學)。
    - yáng bīn shì wǒ de tóngxué
    - Yang Bin is my classmate
