## 复兴(復興)
---
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **characters:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - again, once more; to return, go back; to copy, duplicate; to reply, answer
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng, xìng
    - to flourish, prosper; to rise, begin; to start, carry out; interest, mood
- **etymology:** Combines 复(復) (fù, "again; return") with 兴(興) (xīng, "rise; flourish") to mean "rise again," i.e., a return to prosperity. Metaphorically, it suggests something once declining reawakening and regaining its former vigor.
- **examples:**
  - 这座废弃多年的老火车站在社区的努力下逐步复兴了(這座廢棄多年的老火車站在社區的努力下逐步復興了)。
    - Zhè zuò fèiqì duō nián de lǎo huǒchēzhàn zài shèqū de nǔlì xià zhúbù fùxīng le.
    - The old train station, abandoned for years, has gradually been revived thanks to the community’s efforts.
  - 实现中华民族的伟大复兴是我们共同的愿景(實現中華民族的偉大復興是我們共同的願景)。
    - Shíxiàn Zhōnghuá mínzú de wěidà fùxīng shì wǒmen gòngtóng de yuànjǐng.
    - Achieving the great rejuvenation of the Chinese nation is our shared vision.
