## 复兴(復興)
---
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **characters:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - again
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - prosper
- **etymology:** 复(復) (fù, "again/return") evokes retracing one’s steps (its traditional form includes 彳 (chì, "step")), while 兴(興) (xīng, "raise/flourish") pictographically suggests lifting something up; together they express “to rise again,” i.e., a return to a former flourishing, a framing long used in Chinese history for political and cultural comebacks and restorations.
- **examples:**
  - 剧团努力复兴传统戏曲(劇團努力復興傳統戲曲)。
    - jùtuán nǔlì fùxīng chuántǒng xìqǔ
    - The troupe strives to revive traditional opera.
  - 我们为实现民族复兴而努力奋斗(我們為實現民族復興而努力奮鬥)。
    - wǒmen wèi shíxiàn mínzú fùxīng ér nǔlì fèndòu
    - We strive to achieve national rejuvenation.
