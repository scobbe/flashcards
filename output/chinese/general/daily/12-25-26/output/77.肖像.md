## 肖像
---
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **definition:** portrait
- **characters:**
  - 肖
    - xiào, xiāo
    - to resemble, be like; surname
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - to resemble, look like; seem, appear; like, such as; image, portrait, statue, icon
- **etymology:** Combines 肖 (xiào, "resemble") with 像 (xiàng, "image; likeness") to mean an image that resembles a person—hence a portrait.
- **examples:**
  - 画家正为她创作一幅油画肖像(畫家正為她創作一幅油畫肖像)。
    - Huàjiā zhèng wèi tā chuàngzuò yì fú yóuhuà xiàoxiàng.
    - The painter is creating an oil portrait of her.
  - 这张黑白照片是祖父年轻时的肖像(這張黑白照片是祖父年輕時的肖像)。
    - Zhè zhāng hēibái zhàopiàn shì zǔfù niánqīng shí de xiàoxiàng.
    - This black-and-white photo is a portrait of my grandfather when he was young.
