## 样板间(樣板間)
---
- **pinyin:** yàngbǎnjiān
- **definition:** showroom
- **characters:**
  - 样(樣): yàng, appearance
  - 板: bǎn, board
  - 间(間): jiān, room
- **examples:**
  - 我们在售楼处参观了两个样板间，了解了户型和装修风格(我們在售樓處參觀了兩個樣板間，了解了戶型和裝修風格) (wǒmen zài shòulóuchù cānguān le liǎng gè yàngbǎnjiān, "We visited two showrooms at the sales center to learn about the floor plans and interior design styles")
