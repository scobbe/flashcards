## 样板间(樣板間)
---
- **pinyin:** yàngbǎnjiān
- **definition:** showroom
- **characters:**
  - 样(樣)
    - yàng
    - appearance, form; type, kind; sample, specimen; style, manner
  - 板
    - bǎn
    - board, plank; slab, panel; stiff, rigid; beat, tempo
  - 间(間)
    - jiān, jiàn
    - between, among; room; interval, gap; to sow discord, alienate
- **etymology:** 样(樣) (yàng, "sample; style") plus 板 (bǎn, "board; template") yields the sense of a "model/sample," and adding 间(間) (jiān, "room") makes it a model room set up for display—a showroom.
- **examples:**
  - 在售楼处的样板间里(在售樓處的樣板間裡)，你可以体验未来新家的布局和装修(你可以體驗未來新家的佈局和裝修)。
    - zài shòulóuchù de yàngbǎnjiān lǐ, nǐ kěyǐ tǐyàn wèilái xīnjiā de bùjú hé zhuāngxiū.
    - In the showroom at the sales office, you can experience the layout and finishes of your future home.
