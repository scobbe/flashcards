## 送秋波
---
- **pinyin:** sòng qiūbō
- **definition:** cast amorous glances
- **characters:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - to send
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn
  - 波
    - bō
    - wave
- **etymology:** 秋 (qiū, "autumn") and 波 (bō, "wave") evoke the classical image of limpid, rippling autumn waters—a long-standing poetic metaphor for a woman's bright, shimmering eyes—so 秋波 became a refined euphemism for alluring eye-glints. 送 (sòng, "to send") adds the notion of projecting that "eye-wave" toward someone, i.e., transmitting feeling through a deliberate, flirtatious glance.
- **examples:**
  - 她对他频频送秋波(她對他頻頻送秋波)。
    - tā duì tā pínpín sòng qiūbō
    - She kept casting amorous glances at him.
