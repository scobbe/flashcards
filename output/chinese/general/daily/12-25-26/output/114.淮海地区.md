## 淮海地区(淮海地區)
---
- **pinyin:** Huáihǎi dìqū
- **definition:** Huaihai region
- **characters:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River; Huai region
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea, ocean; maritime; vast, immense; overseas, foreign
  - 地
    - dì; de
    - earth, ground, soil; place, location; structural particle forming adverbials
  - 区(區)
    - qū; ōu
    - district, area; region, zone; administrative division
- **etymology:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River") with 海 (hǎi, "sea") denotes the area spanning the Huai River and the coast, while 地 (dì, "place") and 区(區) (qū, "district/region") label it explicitly as a geographic region.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海地区以徐州为中心(淮海地區以徐州為中心)，辐射江苏、山东、安徽的部分城市(輻射江蘇、山東、安徽的部分城市)，是华东的重要交通枢纽(是華東的重要交通樞紐)。
    - Huáihǎi dìqū yǐ Xúzhōu wéi zhōngxīn, fúshè Jiāngsū, Shāndōng, Ānhuī de bùfèn chéngshì, shì Huádōng de zhòngyào jiāotōng shūniǔ.
    - Centered on Xuzhou, the Huaihai region extends into parts of Jiangsu, Shandong, and Anhui, and is an important transportation hub of East China.
