## 欣
---
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **definition:** happy; joyful
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 欠 (qiàn, "to yawn; breathe out") supplies the semantic idea of breath/emotion, while 斤 (jīn, "axe") serves as the phonetic, indicating the xīn reading by historical sound correspondence. Visually, 欠 depicts a person exhaling or yawning, so paired with a strong phonetic element it evokes a pleased sigh—hence "joyful".
- **examples:**
  - 得知这个好消息(得知這個好消息)，我们欣喜万分(我們欣喜萬分)。
    - Dézhī zhège hǎo xiāoxī, wǒmen xīnxǐ wànfēn.
    - Upon hearing the good news, we were overjoyed.
