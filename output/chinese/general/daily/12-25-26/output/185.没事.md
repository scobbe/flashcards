## 没事(沒事)
---
- **pinyin:** méishì
- **definition:** be free; no problem
- **characters:**
  - 没(沒)
    - méi, mò
    - not; not have, without; to sink, submerge; to confiscate, seize
  - 事
    - shì
    - matter, affair; thing; business; incident, accident
- **etymology:** 没(沒) (méi, "not; without") negates 事 (shì, "matter; affair"), literally "no matter/issue," hence both "no problem" and, by extension, "nothing to attend to" (being free).
- **examples:**
  - 我今天晚上没事(我今天晚上沒事)，要不要一起吃饭(要不要一起吃飯)？
    - Wǒ jīntiān wǎnshang méishì, yào bù yào yìqǐ chīfàn?
    - I’m free tonight—want to grab dinner together?
  - 没事(沒事)，我来处理(我來處理)。
    - Méishì, wǒ lái chǔlǐ.
    - No problem, I’ll handle it.
