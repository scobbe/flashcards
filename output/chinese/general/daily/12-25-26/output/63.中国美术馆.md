## 中国美术馆(中國美術館)
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó Měishùguǎn
- **definition:** National Art Museum of China
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle, center; among, within; China, Chinese
  - 国(國)
    - guó
    - country, nation; state; homeland
  - 美
    - měi
    - beautiful; beauty; good, fine; the United States, America
  - 术(術)
    - shù
    - art; technique, skill; method; surgery
  - 馆(館)
    - guǎn
    - hall; establishment; museum; restaurant
- **etymology:** 中 (zhōng, "middle/China") with 国(國) (guó, "country") denotes China. 美 (měi, "beautiful") plus 术(術) (shù, "art/technique") yields fine arts, and 馆(館) (guǎn, "hall/museum") marks the institution—together, China’s fine-arts museum (the national art museum).
- **examples:**
  - 到北京旅行时(到北京旅行時)，我打算去中国美术馆参观最新的展览(我打算去中國美術館參觀最新的展覽)。
    - Dào Běijīng lǚxíng shí, wǒ dǎsuàn qù Zhōngguó Měishùguǎn cānguān zuìxīn de zhǎnlǎn.
    - When traveling to Beijing, I plan to visit the National Art Museum of China to see the latest exhibition.
