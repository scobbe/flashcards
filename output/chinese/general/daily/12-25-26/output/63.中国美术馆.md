## 中国美术馆(中國美術館)
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó Měishùguǎn
- **definition:** National Art Museum of China
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
  - 国(國)
    - guó
    - country
  - 美
    - měi
    - beautiful
  - 术(術)
    - shù
    - art
  - 馆(館)
    - guǎn
    - hall
- **etymology:** Combines 中 (zhōng, "middle"), 国(國) (guó, "country"), 美 (měi, "beautiful"), 术(術) (shù, "art"), and 馆(館) (guǎn, "hall") to express national art museum of china.
- **examples:**
  - 我们今天去中国美术馆看展览(我們今天去中國美術館看展覽)。
    - wǒmen jīntiān qù Zhōngguó Měishùguǎn kàn zhǎnlǎn
    - We're going to the National Art Museum of China to see an exhibition today.
  - 中国美术馆位于北京东城区(中國美術館位於北京東城區)。
    - Zhōngguó Měishùguǎn wèiyú Běijīng Dōngchéng Qū
    - The National Art Museum of China is located in Beijing's Dongcheng District.
