## 发呆(發呆)
---
- **pinyin:** fādāi
- **definition:** be in a daze
- **characters:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - to send
  - 呆
    - dāi
    - stupid
- **etymology:** Combines 发(發) (fā, "to send") with 呆 (dāi, "stupid") to express be in a daze.
- **examples:**
  - 他上课时总是发呆(他上課時總是發呆)。
    - tā shàngkè shí zǒngshì fādāi
    - He is always in a daze during class.
