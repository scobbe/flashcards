## 发呆(發呆)
---
- **pinyin:** fādāi
- **definition:** be in a daze
- **characters:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā, fà
    - to send out, dispatch, issue; to emit, give off; to develop, expand, grow; hair
  - 呆
    - dāi
    - foolish, stupid; blank, expressionless; to be in a daze, space out; to stay, remain
- **etymology:** 发(發) (fā, "to emit; to develop") functions as an inchoative 'to enter/manifest a state,' combining with 呆 (dāi, "blank; dazed") to mean 'to lapse into a daze'—literally 'to send out daze'.
- **examples:**
  - 他在公交车上发呆(他在公交車上發呆)。
    - Tā zài gōngjiāo chē shàng fādāi.
    - He was zoning out on the bus.
