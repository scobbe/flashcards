## 淮海战役(淮海戰役)
---
- **pinyin:** Huáihǎi zhànyì
- **definition:** Huaihai Campaign
- **characters:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea
  - 战(戰)
    - zhàn
    - war
  - 役
    - yì
    - campaign
- **etymology:** Combines 淮 (huái, "Huai River"), 海 (hǎi, "sea"), 战(戰) (zhàn, "war"), and 役 (yì, "campaign") to express huaihai campaign.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海战役是解放战争三大战役之一(淮海戰役是解放戰爭三大戰役之一)。
    - huáihǎi zhànyì shì jiěfàng zhànzhēng sān dà zhànyì zhī yī
    - The Huaihai Campaign was one of the three major campaigns of the Chinese Civil War.
