## 淮海战役(淮海戰役)
---
- **pinyin:** Huáihǎi zhànyì
- **definition:** Huaihai Campaign
- **characters:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River; Huai River region
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea, ocean; maritime; vast, immense; great number
  - 战(戰)
    - zhàn
    - war; battle; to fight, wage war
  - 役
    - yì
    - corvée, forced labor; service, duty; military campaign
- **etymology:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River") and 海 (hǎi, "sea") combine as a toponym for the Huai River–coastal region that served as the theater. 战(戰) (zhàn, "battle") with 役 (yì, "campaign/service") denotes a military campaign, yielding the campaign conducted in the Huaihai area.
- **examples:**
  - 淮海战役是中国内战中的三大战役之一(淮海戰役是中國內戰中的三大戰役之一)，对战争的走向产生了深远影响(對戰爭的走向產生了深遠影響)。
    - Huáihǎi Zhànyì shì Zhōngguó nèizhàn zhōng de sān dà zhànyì zhī yī, duì zhànzhēng de zǒuxiàng chǎnshēng le shēnyuǎn yǐngxiǎng.
    - The Huaihai Campaign was one of the three major campaigns of the Chinese Civil War and had a profound impact on the course of the war.
  - 我爷爷参加了淮海战役(我爺爺參加了淮海戰役)，留下了许多回忆(留下了許多回憶)。
    - Wǒ yéye cānjiā le Huáihǎi Zhànyì, liúxià le xǔduō huíyì.
    - My grandfather took part in the Huaihai Campaign and left many memories.
