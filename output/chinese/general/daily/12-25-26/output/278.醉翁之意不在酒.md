## 醉翁之意不在酒
---
- **pinyin:** zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ
- **definition:** the real intention is not in the wine
- **characters:**
  - 醉
    - zuì
    - drunk
  - 翁
    - wēng
    - old man
  - 之
    - zhī
    - of
  - 意
    - yì
    - meaning
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
  - 在
    - zài
    - at
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol
- **examples:**
  - 他经常请客户喝酒(他經常請客戶喝酒)，醉翁之意不在酒，是为了谈合同(是為了談合同)。
    - tā jīngcháng qǐng kèhù hē jiǔ, zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ, shì wèile tán hétóng
    - He often treats clients to drinks; his real aim isn’t the alcohol—it’s to discuss the contract.
  - 他帮忙修电脑(他幫忙修電腦)，醉翁之意不在酒，实际上是想借机打听公司近况(實際上是想借機打聽公司近況)。
    - tā bāngmáng xiū diànnǎo, zuìwēng zhī yì bù zài jiǔ, shíjìshang shì xiǎng jièjī dǎtīng gōngsī jìnkuàng
    - He offered to fix the computer, but his real intention lay elsewhere—he wanted to use the chance to ask about the company's situation.
