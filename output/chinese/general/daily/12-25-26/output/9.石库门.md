## 石库门(石庫門)
---
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **characters:**
  - 石: shí, stone
  - 库(庫): kù, warehouse
  - 门(門): mén, door
- **examples:**
  - 上海的石库门建筑保留了中西合璧的特色(上海的石庫門建築保留了中西合璧的特色) (shànghǎi de shíkùmén jiànzhù bǎoliú le zhōng-xī hébì de tèsè, "Shikumen architecture in Shanghai preserves a blend of Chinese and Western features")
