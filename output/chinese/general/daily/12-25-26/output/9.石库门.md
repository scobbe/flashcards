## 石库门(石庫門)
---
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **characters:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone, rock; unit of measure; surname
  - 库(庫)
    - kù
    - warehouse, storehouse; repository, library (of data); armory, arsenal; treasury
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door, gate; family, school, sect; field of study, subject; classifier for courses, subjects, skills
- **etymology:** 石 (shí, "stone") with 库(庫) (kù, "warehouse, storehouse") and 门(門) (mén, "door, gate") literally forms "stone warehouse gate," referring to the iconic stone-framed gateway reminiscent of sturdy warehouse entrances; by extension it names the Shanghai residential style built around such gates.
- **examples:**
  - 上海的石库门建筑融合了中西风格(上海的石庫門建築融合了中西風格)，是城市的独特象征(是城市的獨特象徵)。
    - Shànghǎi de Shíkùmén jiànzhù rónghé le zhōng xī fēnggé, shì chéngshì de dútè xiàngzhēng.
    - Shikumen architecture in Shanghai blends Chinese and Western styles and is a unique symbol of the city.
  - 我们在新天地的石库门里弄漫步(我們在新天地的石庫門里弄漫步)，感受老上海的生活气息(感受老上海的生活氣息)。
    - Wǒmen zài Xīntiāndì de Shíkùmén lǐnòng mànbù, gǎnshòu Lǎo Shànghǎi de shēnghuó qìxī.
    - We strolled through the Shikumen lanes of Xintiandi, soaking in the everyday atmosphere of old Shanghai.
