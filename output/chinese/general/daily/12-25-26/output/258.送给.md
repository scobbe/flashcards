## 送给(送給)
---
- **pinyin:** sònggěi
- **definition:** give as a present; send to
- **characters:**
  - 送: sòng, send
  - 给(給): gěi, give
- **examples:**
  - 我想把这本书送给你(我想把這本書送給你) (wǒ xiǎng bǎ zhè běn shū sòng gěi nǐ, "I want to give you this book as a present")
  - 请把文件送给经理(請把文件送給經理) (qǐng bǎ wénjiàn sòng gěi jīnglǐ, "Please send the document to the manager")
