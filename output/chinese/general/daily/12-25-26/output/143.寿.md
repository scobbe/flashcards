## 寿(壽)
---
- **pinyin:** shòu
- **definition:** longevity
- **etymology:** From Middle Chinese 壽 / 寿 (dzyuw X , " longevity").
- **examples:**
  - 祝您福如东海(祝您福如東海)、寿比南山(壽比南山)。
    - zhù nín fú rú dōnghǎi shòu bǐ nánshān
    - Wishing you happiness as vast as the Eastern Sea and longevity as enduring as the Southern Mountain.
  - 他老人家高寿九十五岁(他老人家高壽九十五歲)，精神矍铄(精神矍鑠)。
    - tā lǎorénjiā gāoshòu jiǔshíwǔ suì jīngshén juéshuò
    - He is of a venerable age of ninety-five, still hale and hearty.
