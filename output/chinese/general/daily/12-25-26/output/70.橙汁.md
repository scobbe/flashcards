## 橙汁
---
- **pinyin:** chéngzhī
- **definition:** orange juice
- **characters:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange (color); orange (fruit); orange tree
  - 汁
    - zhī
    - juice; sap; gravy, sauce
- **etymology:** 橙 (chéng, "orange [fruit]") names the fruit, while 汁 (zhī, "juice; sap") denotes the extracted liquid, combining as juice from oranges.
- **examples:**
  - 请给我一杯橙汁(請給我一杯橙汁)，我不加冰(我不加冰)。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yì bēi chéngzhī, wǒ bù jiā bīng.
    - Please give me a glass of orange juice; no ice.
