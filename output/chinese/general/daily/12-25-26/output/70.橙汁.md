## 橙汁
---
- **pinyin:** chéngzhī
- **definition:** orange juice
- **characters:**
  - 橙: chéng, orange
  - 汁: zhī, juice
- **examples:**
  - 早上我喜欢喝一杯冰的橙汁(早上我喜歡喝一杯冰的橙汁) (zǎoshang wǒ xǐhuan hē yì bēi bīng de chéngzhī, "I like to drink a glass of cold orange juice in the morning")
  - 你要苹果汁还是橙汁(你要蘋果汁還是橙汁) (nǐ yào píngguǒzhī háishi chéngzhī, "Do you want apple juice or orange juice")
