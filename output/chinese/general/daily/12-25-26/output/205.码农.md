## 码农(碼農)
---
- **pinyin:** mǎnóng
- **definition:** programmer (slang)
- **characters:**
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number, numeral; size; yard (unit of length)
  - 农(農)
    - nóng
    - agriculture; farmer, peasant
- **etymology:** Combines 码(碼) (mǎ, "code") with 农(農) (nóng, "farmer") to depict a "code farmer," likening programmers to agricultural laborers who toil and "cultivate" code through repetitive, industrious work.
- **examples:**
  - 作为一名码农(作為一名碼農)，我每天都在写代码(我每天都在寫代碼)，加班也很常见(加班也很常見)。
    - Zuòwéi yī míng mǎnóng, wǒ měitiān dōu zài xiě dàimǎ, jiābān yě hěn chángjiàn.
    - As a code monkey, I write code every day, and working overtime is common.
  - 听说他们在大厂招码农(聽說他們在大廠招碼農)，待遇不错(待遇不錯)。
    - Tīngshuō tāmen zài dàchǎng zhāo mǎnóng, dàiyù búcuò.
    - I heard they’re hiring coders at a big tech firm; the compensation is good.
