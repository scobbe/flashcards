## 码农(碼農)
---
- **pinyin:** mǎnóng
- **definition:** programmer (slang)
- **characters:**
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code
  - 农(農)
    - nóng
    - agriculture
- **examples:**
  - 他自嘲自己是个天天加班的码农(他自嘲自己是個天天加班的碼農)。
    - tā zìcháo zìjǐ shì gè tiāntiān jiābān de mǎnóng
    - He self-deprecatingly calls himself a programmer who works overtime every day.
  - 在这家公司(在這家公司)，码农常被当成螺丝钉用(碼農常被當成螺絲釘用)。
    - zài zhè jiā gōngsī, mǎnóng cháng bèi dāng chéng luósīdīng yòng
    - At this company, programmers are often treated like replaceable cogs.
