## 中庸之道
---
- **pinyin:** zhōngyōng zhī dào
- **definition:** Doctrine of the Mean; middle way
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
  - 庸
    - yōng
    - mediocre
  - 之
    - zhī
    - of
  - 道
    - dào
    - way
- **etymology:** Combines 中 (zhōng, "middle"), 庸 (yōng, "mediocre"), 之 (zhī, "of"), and 道 (dào, "way") to express doctrine of the mean; middle way.
- **examples:**
  - 《中庸》所阐述的中庸之道是儒家思想的核心之一(《中庸》所闡述的中庸之道是儒家思想的核心之一)。
    - 《Zhōngyōng》 suǒ chǎnshù de zhōngyōng zhī dào shì rújiā sīxiǎng de héxīn zhī yī
    - The Doctrine of the Mean expounded in the Zhongyong is a core element of Confucian thought.
  - 在处理冲突时(在處理衝突時)，他总是坚持中庸之道(他總是堅持中庸之道)，既不偏袒也不极端(既不偏袒也不極端)。
    - zài chǔlǐ chōngtú shí, tā zǒng shì jiānchí zhōngyōng zhī dào, jì bù piāntǎn yě bù jíduān
    - When handling conflicts, he always adheres to the middle way, being neither biased nor extreme.
