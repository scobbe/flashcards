## 湛江市
---
- **pinyin:** Zhànjiāng Shì
- **definition:** Zhanjiang City
- **characters:**
  - 湛
    - zhàn
    - deep, clear
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 市
    - shì
    - city
- **etymology:** 湛 (zhàn, "deep, clear") evokes the area's unusually deep, limpid bay—a natural deep‑water harbor—while 江 (jiāng, "river") flags its estuarine setting where inland waters meet the sea; after the French‑leased era as 廣州灣, the renaming foregrounded this water identity. 市 (shì, "city") simply marks its administrative status.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算去湛江市旅游(我打算去湛江市旅遊)。
    - wǒ dǎsuàn qù zhànjiāng shì lǚyóu
    - I plan to travel to Zhanjiang City.
