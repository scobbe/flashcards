## 攀岩
---
- **pinyin:** pānyán
- **definition:** rock climbing
- **characters:**
  - 攀
    - pān
    - to climb, to scale; to cling to, to hang onto; to seek connections with, to curry favor with
  - 岩
    - yán
    - rock; cliff, crag; rocky
- **etymology:** Combines 攀 (pān, "to climb") with 岩 (yán, "rock; cliff") to denote climbing on rock faces—a verb–object structure literally meaning “to climb rocks,” evoking the upward grasping implied by 攀.
- **examples:**
  - 我每个周末都去攀岩(我每個週末都去攀岩)，这是我最喜欢的运动之一(這是我最喜歡的運動之一)。
    - Wǒ měi gè zhōumò dōu qù pānyán, zhè shì wǒ zuì xǐhuan de yùndòng zhī yī.
    - I go rock climbing every weekend; it's one of my favorite sports.
