## 攀岩
---
- **pinyin:** pānyán
- **definition:** rock climbing
- **characters:**
  - 攀: pān, climb
  - 岩: yán, rock
- **examples:**
  - 周末我们打算去室内攀岩(週末我們打算去室內攀岩) (zhōumò wǒmen dǎsuàn qù shìnèi pānyán, "We plan to go indoor rock climbing this weekend")
  - 攀岩需要良好的体能和安全意识(攀岩需要良好的體能和安全意識) (pānyán xūyào liánghǎo de tǐnéng hé ānquán yìshí, "Rock climbing requires good fitness and safety awareness")
