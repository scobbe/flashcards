## 词源(詞源)
---
- **pinyin:** cíyuán
- **definition:** etymology
- **characters:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word, term; lyrics (of a song); ci lyric poetry
  - 源
    - yuán
    - source, origin; headwaters, fountainhead; to originate, stem from
- **etymology:** Combines 词(詞) (cí, "word, term") with 源 (yuán, "source, origin; headwaters") to mean the "source of a word," i.e., its origin. 源 evokes a river’s headwaters, metaphorically framing etymology as tracing a word back to its fountainhead.
- **examples:**
  - 这本词典专门讲解汉字的词源(這本詞典專門講解漢字的詞源)，帮助读者理解字的演变(幫助讀者理解字的演變)。
    - Zhè běn cídiǎn zhuānmén jiǎngjiě Hànzì de cíyuán, bāngzhù dúzhě lǐjiě zì de yǎnbiàn.
    - This dictionary specifically explains the etymology of Chinese characters, helping readers understand the evolution of the characters.
  - 研究一个词的词源(研究一個詞的詞源)，可以揭示文化交流的历史(可以揭示文化交流的歷史)。
    - Yánjiū yí gè cí de cíyuán, kěyǐ jiēshì wénhuà jiāoliú de lìshǐ.
    - Studying the etymology of a word can reveal the history of cultural exchange.
