## 小妹
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèi
- **definition:** little sister; young woman
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, petty
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister; young woman, girl
- **etymology:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") modifies 妹 (mèi, "younger sister") to indicate a junior female relative, yielding little sister and by extension a young woman.
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个小妹(我有一個小妹)，她比我小三岁(她比我小三歲)。
    - Wǒ yǒu yí gè xiǎomèi, tā bǐ wǒ xiǎo sān suì.
    - I have a little sister; she is three years younger than me.
  - 那位柜台小妹很亲切(那位櫃檯小妹很親切)。
    - Nà wèi guìtái xiǎomèi hěn qīnqiè.
    - The young woman at the counter is very friendly.
