## 陈毅(陳毅)
---
- **pinyin:** Chén Yì
- **definition:** Chen Yi
- **characters:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - surname Chen
  - 毅
    - yì
    - resolute
- **etymology:** 陈(陳) (chén, "surname Chen") signals clan identity and harks back to the Zhou‑era State of Chen, while 毅 (yì, "resolute, steadfast") is a virtue-name expressing the hoped-for quality of firm resolve; together it reads as “a Chen marked by steadfastness,” exemplifying the Chinese pattern of hereditary surname + aspirational given name (apt for the famed marshal Chen Yi).
- **examples:**
  - 陈毅是中国著名的革命家和外交家(陳毅是中國著名的革命家和外交家)。
    - chén yì shì zhōngguó zhùmíng de gémìngjiā hé wàijiāojiā
    - Chen Yi was a famous Chinese revolutionary and diplomat.
