## 陈毅(陳毅)
---
- **pinyin:** Chén Yì
- **definition:** Chen Yi
- **characters:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - surname Chen
  - 毅
    - yì
    - resolute
- **etymology:** Combines 陈(陳) (chén, "surname Chen") with 毅 (yì, "resolute") to express chen yi.
- **examples:**
  - 陈毅是中国著名的革命家和外交家(陳毅是中國著名的革命家和外交家)。
    - chén yì shì zhōngguó zhùmíng de gémìngjiā hé wàijiāojiā
    - Chen Yi was a famous Chinese revolutionary and diplomat.
