## 陈毅(陳毅)
---
- **pinyin:** Chén Yì
- **definition:** Chen Yi
- **characters:**
  - 陈(陳)
    - chén
    - arrange, lay out, display; state, explain; old, stale; surname Chen
  - 毅
    - yì
    - resolute, steadfast; perseverance, fortitude
- **etymology:** 陈(陳) (chén, "surname Chen") marks the family lineage, while 毅 (yì, "resolute") is a virtue-laden given name, combining as the personal name "Chen [who is] resolute."
- **examples:**
  - 陈毅曾任中华人民共和国外交部长(陳毅曾任中華人民共和國外交部長)。
    - Chén Yì céng rèn Zhōnghuá Rénmín Gònghéguó wàijiāo bùzhǎng.
    - Chen Yi once served as the foreign minister of the People's Republic of China.
