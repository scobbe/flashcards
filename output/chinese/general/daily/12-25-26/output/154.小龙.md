## 小龙(小龍)
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎolóng
- **definition:** snake (zodiac)
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon
- **examples:**
  - 他属小龙(他屬小龍)，今年本命年(今年本命年)。
    - tā shǔ xiǎolóng, jīnnián běnmìngnián
    - He is a Snake by the Chinese zodiac, and this year is his zodiac year.
