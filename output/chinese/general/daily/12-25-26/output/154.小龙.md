## 小龙(小龍)
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎolóng
- **definition:** snake (zodiac)
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, secondary; petty, trivial
  - 龙(龍)
    - lóng
    - dragon; imperial; outstanding person
- **etymology:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") diminishes and endears, while 龙(龍) (lóng, "dragon") provides the auspicious, serpentine model; together they recast the snake zodiac as a "little dragon."
- **examples:**
  - 他属小龙(他屬小龍)，今年本命年(今年本命年)。
    - Tā shǔ xiǎolóng, jīnnián běnmìngnián.
    - He’s a Snake in the Chinese zodiac, and this year is his zodiac year.
