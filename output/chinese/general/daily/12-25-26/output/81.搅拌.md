## 搅拌(攪拌)
---
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **characters:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir, mix; agitate; disturb, bother; mess up, spoil
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix, blend, stir; toss (in sauce)
- **etymology:** Combines 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir; agitate")—emphasizing vigorous disturbance—with 拌 (bàn, "mix; toss") to specify combining ingredients, yielding the sense of actively stirring to mix.
- **examples:**
  - 用勺子把汤搅拌一下(用勺子把湯攪拌一下)，让味道均匀(讓味道均勻)。
    - Yòng sháozi bǎ tāng jiǎobàn yíxià, ràng wèidào jūnyún.
    - Stir the soup with a spoon for a moment so the flavors are evenly distributed.
  - 把面粉和鸡蛋搅拌成面糊(把麵粉和雞蛋攪拌成麵糊)。
    - Bǎ miànfěn hé jīdàn jiǎobàn chéng miànhú.
    - Mix the flour and eggs into a batter.
