## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - to stir up; to agitate; to disturb; to mix
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - to mix; to toss (food); to blend; to stir
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 搅(攪) + 拌 = agitate/stir + mix → stir and mix together.
  - **interpretation:** Combines two near-synonymous hand-actions, emphasizing continuous motion to make contents uniform. Often used for food, liquids, or powders.
  - **simplification:** 攪 was simplified to 搅 by replacing the phonetic 覺 with its simplified 觉 under the hand radical 扌, reducing strokes; 拌 remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 用勺子把汤搅拌均匀(用勺子把湯攪拌均勻)。
    - Yòng sháozi bǎ tāng jiǎobàn jūnyún.
    - Use a spoon to stir the soup evenly.
  - 把面粉和鸡蛋搅拌成糊(把麵粉和雞蛋攪拌成糊)。
    - Bǎ miànfěn hé jīdàn jiǎobàn chéng hú.
    - Mix the flour and eggs into a batter.
  - 先把沙拉酱和蔬菜搅拌一下(先把沙拉醬和蔬菜攪拌一下)。
    - Xiān bǎ shālājiàng hé shūcài jiǎobàn yíxià.
    - First toss the salad dressing with the vegetables.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪)
- **definition:** to stir up; to agitate; to disturb; to mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎo
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 觉(覺)
    - jué
    - feel; perceive; awaken
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 扌 (“hand”) + phonetic 覺 (OC *kruːɡs, *kruːɡ).
  - **interpretation:** A hand element signals a manual action, while 覺 contributes the sound. Together they suggest using the hand to move or disturb something, matching meanings like stir, agitate, or mix.
  - **simplification:** The traditional phonetic 覺 was replaced by its simplified form 觉 within the character, reducing strokes; overall 攪 → 搅.
- **examples:**
  - 请不要再搅我的工作了(請不要再攪我的工作了)。
    - Qǐng búyào zài jiǎo wǒ de gōngzuò le.
    - Please don’t disturb my work anymore.
  - 用勺子搅一搅汤，让味道更均匀(用勺子攪一攪湯，讓味道更均勻)。
    - Yòng sháozi jiǎo yì jiǎo tāng, ràng wèidào gèng jūnyún.
    - Stir the soup with a spoon to make the flavor more even.
  - 他总爱搅局，让大家很头疼(他總愛攪局，讓大家很頭疼)。
    - Tā zǒng ài jiǎo jú, ràng dàjiā hěn tóuténg.
    - He always likes to stir things up, giving everyone a headache.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪) → 手
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: hand and fingers; five digits, with top stroke as bent middle finger and horizontal strokes as fingers.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a human hand: the top stroke stands for a bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes stand for the remaining fingers. In contrast to related claw forms that show three digits, it consistently presents a five‑finger human hand.
- **examples:**
  - 我手上有伤口(我手上有傷口)。
    - Wǒ shǒu shàng yǒu shāngkǒu.
    - I have a wound on my hand.
  - 请举起右手(請舉起右手)。
    - Qǐng jǔ qǐ yòu shǒu.
    - Please raise your right hand.
  - 他动手做饭(他動手做飯)。
    - Tā dòngshǒu zuò fàn.
    - He started cooking by hand.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪) → 觉(覺)
- **definition:** feel; perceive; awaken
- **pinyin:** jué
- **components:**
  - 见(見)
    - jiàn
    - see
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 見 “see” + phonetic: 𦥯; simplified as 𦥯→𰃮 and 見→见.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the meaning of “seeing/perceiving” with a sound-bearing component, yielding the idea of perceiving or becoming aware. The phonetic 𦥯 guides the pronunciation, while 見 anchors the sense of perception.
  - **simplification:** Both components were graphically simplified to reduce strokes and standardize forms: phonetic 𦥯 to 𰃮 and semantic 見 to 见, aligning with Mainland script reform for ease of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 我觉得今天很冷(我覺得今天很冷)。
    - Wǒ juéde jīntiān hěn lěng.
    - I feel it’s very cold today.
  - 她终于觉醒，开始新的生活(她終於覺醒，開始新的生活)。
    - Tā zhōngyú juéxǐng, kāishǐ xīn de shēnghuó.
    - She finally awakened and began a new life.
  - 我察觉到空气里的变化(我察覺到空氣裡的變化)。
    - Wǒ chájué dào kōngqì lǐ de biànhuà.
    - I noticed a change in the air.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 搅(攪) → 觉(覺) → 见(見)
- **definition:** see
- **pinyin:** jiàn
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 拌
- **definition:** to mix; to toss (food); to blend; to stir
- **pinyin:** bàn
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 半
    - bàn
    - half
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 手 ‘hand’ + phonetic: 半 (bàn).
  - **interpretation:** The hand component signals a manual action, fitting actions like mixing or tossing. 半 provides the sound clue bàn and may also suggest the idea of dividing or turning ingredients over.
- **examples:**
  - 请把黄瓜拌一下(請把黃瓜拌一下)。
    - Qǐng bǎ huángguā bàn yíxià.
    - Please toss the cucumbers.
  - 她用芝麻油拌面(她用芝麻油拌麵)。
    - Tā yòng zhīma yóu bàn miàn.
    - She tosses the noodles with sesame oil.
  - 我喜欢拌凉菜(我喜歡拌涼菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan bàn liángcài.
    - I like to mix cold dishes.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 拌 → 半
- **definition:** half
- **pinyin:** bàn
- **components:**
  - 八
    - bā
    - eight
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow/ox
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Ideogrammic compound (會意/会意)
  - **description:** 八 ‘to divide’ + 牛 ‘cow’: to cut a cow into two halves.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of splitting (八) with a cow (牛) to picture an animal being divided into two equal parts, highlighting the notion of a half. The meaning generalized from a concrete butchering image to the abstract concept of “half.”
- **examples:**
  - 我们各付一半(我們各付一半)。
    - Wǒmen gè fù yī bàn.
    - We each pay half.
  - 把苹果切成两半(把蘋果切成兩半)。
    - Bǎ píngguǒ qiē chéng liǎng bàn.
    - Cut the apple into two halves.
  - 半小时后见(半小時後見)。
    - Bàn xiǎoshí hòu jiàn.
    - See you in half an hour.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 拌 → 半 → 八
- **definition:** eight
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: two bent lines indicating “to divide”; later borrowed to mean “eight” via homophony (original sense now in 分, 別).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two strokes diverging to suggest splitting or separation. Later became a rebus for the number eight because it shared the same sound, while the original sense lived on in 分 and 別. Not the “two hands” component found in other characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我有八本书(我有八本書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu bā běn shū.
    - I have eight books.
  - 她八岁了(她八歲了)。
    - Tā bā suì le.
    - She is eight years old.
  - 我买了八个苹果(我買了八個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le bā gè píngguǒ.
    - I bought eight apples.
### 搅拌(攪拌) → 拌 → 半 → 牛
- **definition:** cow/ox
- **pinyin:** niú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: bull’s head with two horns and two ears; perhaps unrelated to 告; unrelated to 先.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bull’s head with prominent horns and ears, a direct visual rendering of the animal. Stylization over time simplified the lines, but the bovine shape remains evident.
- **examples:**
  - 这头牛很壮(這頭牛很壯)。
    - Zhè tóu niú hěn zhuàng.
    - This ox is strong.
  - 牛奶很新鲜(牛奶很新鮮)。
    - Niúnǎi hěn xīnxiān.
    - The milk is very fresh.
  - 牧民在草原上赶牛(牧民在草原上趕牛)。
    - Mùmín zài cǎoyuán shàng gǎn niú.
    - The herders drive cattle on the grassland.
