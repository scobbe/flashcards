## 搅拌(攪拌)
---
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **characters:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix
- **etymology:** Combines 搅(攪) (jiǎo, "stir") with 拌 (bàn, "mix") to express stir; mix.
- **examples:**
  - 用勺子把锅里的汤慢慢搅拌(用勺子把鍋裡的湯慢慢攪拌)，以免糊底(以免糊底)。
    - yòng sháozi bǎ guō lǐ de tāng mànmàn jiǎobàn, yǐmiǎn hú dǐ
    - Stir the soup in the pot slowly with a spoon to prevent it from burning on the bottom.
  - 把面粉和鸡蛋搅拌在一起(把麵粉和雞蛋攪拌在一起)，直到均匀(直到均勻)。
    - bǎ miànfěn hé jīdàn jiǎobàn zài yīqǐ, zhídào jūnyún
    - Mix the flour and eggs together until evenly combined.
