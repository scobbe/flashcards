## 晶晶
---
- **pinyin:** jīngjīng
- **definition:** sparkling; gleaming
- **characters:**
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal
- **examples:**
  - 草叶上的露珠晶晶地闪着光(草葉上的露珠晶晶地閃著光)，在朝阳下跳动(在朝陽下跳動)。
    - cǎoyè shàng de lùzhū jīngjīng de shǎn zhe guāng, zài cháoyáng xià tiàodòng
    - The dewdrops on the blades of grass were sparkling, dancing in the morning sun.
  - 他把锅擦得晶晶发亮(他把鍋擦得晶晶發亮)。
    - tā bǎ guō cā de jīngjīng fāliàng
    - He polished the pot until it was gleaming.
