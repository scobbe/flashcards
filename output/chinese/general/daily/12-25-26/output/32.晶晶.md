## 晶晶
---
- **pinyin:** jīngjīng
- **definition:** sparkling; gleaming
- **characters:**
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal; bright, shining; clear, transparent
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - crystal; bright, shining; clear, transparent
- **etymology:** Reduplication of 晶 (jīng, "crystal; bright, shining") intensifies the brightness, turning a single sheen into multiple glints. The repeated form evokes flickering points of light, yielding the sense of sparkling and gleaming.
- **examples:**
  - 湖面在阳光下晶晶发亮(湖面在陽光下晶晶發亮)。
    - Húmiàn zài yángguāng xià jīngjīng fāliàng.
    - The surface of the lake gleamed in the sunlight.
  - 他把玻璃擦得晶晶发亮(他把玻璃擦得晶晶發亮)。
    - Tā bǎ bōli cā de jīngjīng fāliàng.
    - He polished the glass until it was sparkling.
