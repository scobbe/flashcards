## 胸大无脑(胸大無腦)
---
- **pinyin:** xiōng dà wú nǎo
- **definition:** bimbo (lit. big chest no brains)
- **characters:**
  - 胸
    - xiōng
    - chest
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
  - 无(無)
    - wú
    - without
  - 脑(腦)
    - nǎo
    - brain
- **etymology:** Combines 胸 (xiōng, "chest"), 大 (dà, "big"), 无(無) (wú, "without"), and 脑(腦) (nǎo, "brain") to express bimbo (lit. big chest no brains).
- **examples:**
  - 有人嘲笑她胸大无脑(有人嘲笑她胸大無腦)，但她用实力打脸了他们(但她用實力打臉了他們)。
    - yǒu rén cháo xiào tā xiōng dà wú nǎo, dàn tā yòng shí lì dǎ liǎn le tā men
    - Some people mock her as a 'bimbo,' but she proved them wrong with her ability.
  - 别把所有女主播都当成胸大无脑(別把所有女主播都當成胸大無腦)，她们也有专业能力(她們也有專業能力)。
    - bié bǎ suǒ yǒu nǚ zhǔ bō dōu dāng chéng xiōng dà wú nǎo, tā men yě yǒu zhuān yè néng lì
    - Don't assume all female streamers are bimbos; they have professional skills too.
