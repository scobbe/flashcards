## 预定
---
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **definition:** schedule; book
- **characters:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance
  - 定
    - dìng
    - decide
- **etymology:** Combines 预(預) (yù, "in advance") with 定 (dìng, "decide") to express schedule; book.
- **examples:**
  - 我们预定下周一召开会议(我們預定下週一召開會議)。
    - wǒmen yùdìng xià zhōuyī zhàokāi huìyì
    - We have scheduled a meeting for next Monday.
  - 我想预定一张两人桌(我想預訂一張兩人桌)。
    - wǒ xiǎng yùdìng yì zhāng liǎng rén zhuō
    - I'd like to book a table for two.
