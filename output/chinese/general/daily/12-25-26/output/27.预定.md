## 预定
---
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **definition:** schedule; book
- **characters:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance, beforehand; to prepare, make ready; to anticipate, expect; pre-, preliminary
  - 定
    - dìng
    - to decide, determine; to set, fix, appoint; fixed, settled, definite, certain
- **etymology:** Combines 预(預) (yù, "in advance; to anticipate") with 定 (dìng, "to decide; to fix"), literally "to set in advance," yielding the sense of making arrangements beforehand—hence "to schedule/book."
- **examples:**
  - 比赛预定于周五晚上七点举行(比賽預定於週五晚上七點舉行)。
    - Bǐsài yùdìng yú zhōu wǔ wǎnshang qī diǎn jǔxíng.
    - The match is scheduled to be held at 7 p.m. on Friday.
  - 我想预定两张明天晚上的演唱会门票(我想預定兩張明天晚上的演唱會門票)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yùdìng liǎng zhāng míngtiān wǎnshang de yǎnchànghuì ménpiào.
    - I’d like to book two tickets for tomorrow night’s concert.
