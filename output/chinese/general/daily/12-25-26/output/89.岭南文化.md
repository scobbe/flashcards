## 岭南文化(嶺南文化)
---
- **pinyin:** Lǐngnán wénhuà
- **definition:** Lingnan culture
- **characters:**
  - 岭(嶺)
    - lǐng
    - mountain ridge; mountain range
  - 南
    - nán
    - south; southern
  - 文
    - wén
    - writing, script; literature; culture, civilization; cultured, refined
  - 化
    - huà
    - to transform, change; to dissolve, melt; -ization, -ify (suffix); chemistry (abbreviation)
- **etymology:** 岭(嶺) (lǐng, "mountain ridge") with 南 (nán, "south") designates the region south of the mountain ranges (Lingnan); adding 文 (wén, "culture") and 化 (huà, "transformation; -ization") denotes the cultural formation of that region.
- **examples:**
  - 岭南文化融合了中原与南海的元素(嶺南文化融合了中原與南海的元素)，形成了独特的审美与生活方式(形成了獨特的審美與生活方式)。
    - Lǐngnán wénhuà rónghé le Zhōngyuán yǔ Nánhǎi de yuánsù, xíngchéng le dútè de shěnměi yǔ shēnghuó fāngshì.
    - Lingnan culture blends elements from the Central Plains and the South China Sea, forming a distinctive aesthetic and way of life.
  - 许多粤菜与建筑风格都体现了岭南文化的开放与包容(許多粵菜與建築風格都體現了嶺南文化的開放與包容)。
    - Xǔduō Yuècài yǔ jiànzhù fēnggé dōu tǐxiàn le Lǐngnán wénhuà de kāifàng yǔ bāoróng.
    - Many Cantonese dishes and architectural styles embody the openness and inclusiveness of Lingnan culture.
