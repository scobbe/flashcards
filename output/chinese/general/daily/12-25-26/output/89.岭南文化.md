## 岭南文化(嶺南文化)
---
- **pinyin:** Lǐngnán wénhuà
- **definition:** Lingnan culture
- **characters:**
  - 岭(嶺)
    - lǐng
    - mountain ridge
  - 南
    - nán
    - south
  - 文
    - wén
    - writing
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform
- **examples:**
  - 岭南文化体现在粤语(嶺南文化體現在粵語)、园林和饮食上(園林和飲食上)。
    - lǐngnán wénhuà tǐxiàn zài yuèyǔ, yuánlín hé yǐnshí shàng
    - Lingnan culture is reflected in Cantonese, gardens, and cuisine.
