## 长城(長城)
---
- **pinyin:** Chángchéng
- **definition:** Great Wall
- **characters:**
  - 长(長)
    - cháng, zhǎng
    - long; length; to grow, increase; chief, head
  - 城
    - chéng
    - city; city wall, fortifications
- **etymology:** 长(長) (cháng, "long") modifies 城 (chéng, "city; city wall, fortifications") to mean a "long city wall," i.e., an extended defensive wall; by extension it names the vast fortifications spanning northern China.
- **examples:**
  - 我们计划明年春天去北京参观长城(我們計劃明年春天去北京參觀長城)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà míngnián chūntiān qù Běijīng cānguān Chángchéng.
    - We’re planning to visit the Great Wall in Beijing next spring.
