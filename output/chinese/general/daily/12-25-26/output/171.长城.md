## 长城(長城)
---
- **pinyin:** Chángchéng
- **definition:** Great Wall
- **characters:**
  - 长(長)
    - cháng
    - long
  - 城
    - chéng
    - city
- **etymology:** Combines 长(長) (cháng, "long") with 城 (chéng, "city") to express great wall.
- **examples:**
  - 我一直想去参观长城(我一直想去參觀長城)。
    - wǒ yīzhí xiǎng qù cānguān chángchéng
    - I've always wanted to visit the Great Wall.
  - 长城是世界上最著名的古代建筑之一(長城是世界上最著名的古代建築之一)。
    - chángchéng shì shìjiè shàng zuì zhùmíng de gǔdài jiànzhù zhī yī
    - The Great Wall is one of the most famous ancient structures in the world.
