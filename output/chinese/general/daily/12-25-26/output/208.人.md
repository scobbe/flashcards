## 人
---
- **pinyin:** rén
- **definition:** person; people
- **etymology:** 人 (rén, "person") depicts a standing human figure, with the two slanting strokes representing the body and legs as seen in ancient forms. As a simple pictograph (not phono-semantic), it functions widely as a semantic element meaning "human," its shape directly matching its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 门口站着一个人(門口站著一個人)。
    - Ménkǒu zhànzhe yí gè rén.
    - There is a person standing at the door.
  - 很多人喜欢喝茶(很多人喜歡喝茶)。
    - Hěn duō rén xǐhuan hē chá.
    - Many people like to drink tea.
