## 凤梨酥
---
- **pinyin:** fènglísū
- **definition:** pineapple cake
- **characters:**
  - 凤(鳳)
    - fèng
    - phoenix; surname Feng
  - 梨
    - lí
    - pear
  - 酥
    - sū
    - crisp, flaky; clarified butter, ghee; tingly, numb
- **etymology:** 凤(鳳) (fèng, "phoenix") with 梨 (lí, "pear") forms a binome for "pineapple" (poetically a "phoenix pear"), and 酥 (sū, "crisp, flaky") marks a flaky pastry, yielding a pineapple cake/pastry.
- **examples:**
  - 凤梨酥是台湾的代表性伴手礼(鳳梨酥是臺灣的代表性伴手禮)。
    - Fènglísū shì Táiwān de dàibiǎoxìng bànshǒulǐ.
    - Pineapple cakes are a signature Taiwanese souvenir.
