## 凤梨酥
---
- **pinyin:** fènglísū
- **definition:** pineapple cake
- **characters:**
  - 凤(鳳): fèng, phoenix
  - 梨: lí, pear
  - 酥: sū, crisp
- **examples:**
  - 台湾的凤梨酥很有名(台灣的鳳梨酥很有名) (táiwān de fènglísū hěn yǒumíng, "Taiwan's pineapple cakes are famous")
  - 你想吃一块凤梨酥吗(你想吃一塊鳳梨酥嗎) (nǐ xiǎng chī yí kuài fènglísū ma, "Do you want a piece of pineapple cake")
