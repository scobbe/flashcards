## 苏(蘇)
---
- **pinyin:** Sū
- **definition:** surname Su; also shorthand for Jiangsu
- **examples:**
  - 苏先生今天不在办公室(蘇先生今天不在辦公室) (Sū xiānsheng jīntiān bú zài bàngōngshì, "Mr. Su is not in the office today")
  - 苏浙沪的经济很发达(蘇浙滬的經濟很發達) (Sū Zhè Hù de jīngjì hěn fādá, "The economies of Jiangsu, Zhejiang, and Shanghai are very developed")
