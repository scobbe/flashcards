## 吃饱了撑的(吃飽了撐的)
---
- **pinyin:** chī bǎo le chēng de
- **definition:** have nothing better to do (lit. stuffed from eating)
- **characters:**
  - 吃
    - chī
    - eat
  - 饱(飽)
    - bǎo
    - full
  - 了
    - le; aspect particle
  - 撑(撐)
    - chēng
    - support
  - 的
    - de; structural particle
- **etymology:** Combines 吃 (chī, "eat"), 饱(飽) (bǎo, "full"), and 撑(撐) (chēng, "support") to convey have nothing better to do (lit. stuffed from eating).
- **examples:**
  - 你没事在这里挑刺(你沒事在這裡挑刺)，真是吃饱了撑的(真是吃飽了撐的)。
    - nǐ méi shì zài zhèlǐ tiāo cì, zhēn shì chī bǎo le chēng de
    - You're nitpicking here for no reason; you really have nothing better to do.
