## 沉鱼落雁(沉魚落雁)
---
- **pinyin:** chén yú luò yàn
- **definition:** beauty that sinks fish and makes birds fall
- **characters:**
  - 沉: chén, to sink
  - 鱼(魚): yú, fish
  - 落: luò, to fall
  - 雁: yàn, wild goose
- **examples:**
  - 她的容貌可谓沉鱼落雁，让人惊艳(她的容貌可謂沉魚落雁，讓人驚豔) (tā de róngmào kěwèi chén yú luò yàn, ràng rén jīngyàn, "Her beauty could make fish sink and birds fall")
  - 传说中的西施被称为沉鱼落雁的佳人(傳說中的西施被稱為沉魚落雁的佳人) (chuánshuō zhōng de Xī Shī bèi chēngwéi chén yú luò yàn de jiārén, "Xi Shi is said to be a beauty who could make fish sink and birds fall")
