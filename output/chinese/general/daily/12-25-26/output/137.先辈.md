## 先辈(先輩)
---
- **pinyin:** xiānbèi
- **definition:** forebears
- **characters:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - first
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation
- **etymology:** Combines 先 (xiān, "first") with 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation") to express forebears.
- **examples:**
  - 我们怀念为国家奋斗的先辈(我們懷念為國家奮鬥的先輩)。
    - wǒmen huáiniàn wèi guójiā fèndòu de xiānbèi
    - We remember the forebears who fought for the country.
