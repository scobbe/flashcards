## 先辈(先輩)
---
- **pinyin:** xiānbèi
- **definition:** forebears
- **characters:**
  - 先
    - xiān
    - first; earlier, prior; in advance; the late (deceased)
  - 辈(輩)
    - bèi
    - generation; lifetime; peers of the same generation; class, type of people
- **etymology:** Combines 先 (xiān, "earlier") with 辈(輩) (bèi, "generation") to denote the earlier generation—those who came before. The temporal primacy of 先 frames the lineage unit 辈(輩) as predecessors.
- **examples:**
  - 我们要铭记先辈的牺牲(我們要銘記先輩的犧牲)，继续他们未竟的事业(繼續他們未竟的事業)。
    - Wǒmen yào míngjì xiānbèi de xīshēng, jìxù tāmen wèijìng de shìyè.
    - We must remember our forebears’ sacrifices and carry on their unfinished cause.
