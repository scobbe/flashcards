## 外卖
---
- **pinyin:** wàimài
- **definition:** takeout
- **characters:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside, outer; foreign; in addition, besides; maternal (side of family)
  - 卖(賣)
    - mài
    - to sell; to betray; to show off, flaunt; to work hard, put in effort
- **etymology:** 外 (wài, "outside") with 卖(賣) (mài, "to sell") forms "sell outward," i.e., food sold for consumption outside the premises (and by extension delivery), contrasting with dine-in service.
- **examples:**
  - 我们懒得做饭(我們懶得做飯)，今晚点外卖吧(今晚點外賣吧)。
    - Wǒmen lǎn de zuò fàn, jīnwǎn diǎn wàimài ba.
    - We’re too lazy to cook; let’s order takeout tonight.
