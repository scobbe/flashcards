## 宠物
---
- **pinyin:** chǒngwù
- **definition:** pet
- **characters:**
  - 宠(寵)
    - chǒng
    - to favor, show favor; to pamper, spoil, dote on; favor, grace
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing, object; matter, substance; creature, living being; goods, products
- **etymology:** Combines 宠(寵) (chǒng, "to favor, pamper") with 物 (wù, "creature") to denote a creature that is specially favored—i.e., a pampered animal kept by people.
- **examples:**
  - 他把流浪狗带回家(他把流浪狗帶回家)，当成宠物来照顾(當成寵物來照顧)。
    - Tā bǎ liúlàng gǒu dài huí jiā, dàngchéng chǒngwù lái zhàogù.
    - He took the stray dog home and cared for it as a pet.
