## 宠物
---
- **pinyin:** chǒngwù
- **definition:** pet
- **characters:**
  - 宠(寵)
    - chǒng
    - pamper
  - 物
    - wù
    - thing
- **etymology:** Anchored in courtly language, 宠(寵) (chǒng, "pamper/favor") denotes indulgent favor bestowed from above, while 物 (wù, "creature") is a neutral “being”; together they label an animal defined not by species or function but by its social role as the recipient of doting—literally a “pampered creature,” echoing pets’ historical roots as luxury companions rather than livestock or labor.
- **examples:**
  - 我养了一只猫作为宠物(我養了一隻貓作為寵物)。
    - wǒ yǎng le yì zhī māo zuòwéi chǒngwù
    - I keep a cat as a pet.
  - 这家咖啡馆允许带宠物吗？(這家咖啡館允許帶寵物嗎？)。
    - zhè jiā kāfēiguǎn yǔnxǔ dài chǒngwù ma
    - Does this café allow pets?
