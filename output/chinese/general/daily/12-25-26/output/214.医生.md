## 医生(醫生)
---
- **pinyin:** yīshēng
- **definition:** doctor
- **characters:**
  - 医(醫)
    - yī
    - medicine
  - 生
    - shēng
    - to be born
- **etymology:** Combines 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") with 生 (shēng, "to be born") to express doctor.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一名医生(她是一名醫生)。
    - tā shì yì míng yīshēng
    - She is a doctor.
