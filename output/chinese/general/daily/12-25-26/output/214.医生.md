## 医生(醫生)
---
- **pinyin:** yīshēng
- **definition:** doctor
- **characters:**
  - 医(醫)
    - yī
    - medicine, medical; doctor, physician; to treat, cure
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; to be born; raw, uncooked; student
- **etymology:** 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") combines with 生 (shēng, "life; student"), where 生 functions as a suffix meaning "scholar/person," yielding "medicine person"—originally a medical student and by extension a physician who cares for life.
- **examples:**
  - 这个医生很专业(這個醫生很專業)，耐心地解释了我的病情(耐心地解釋了我的病情)。
    - Zhège yīshēng hěn zhuānyè, nàixīn de jiěshì le wǒ de bìngqíng.
    - This doctor is very professional and patiently explained my condition.
