## 江山如画(江山如畫)
---
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān rúhuà
- **definition:** picturesque scenery
- **characters:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river; the Yangtze River
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain, hill
  - 如
    - rú
    - like, as; such as, for example; if; in accordance with, according to
  - 画(畫)
    - huà
    - to draw, paint; painting, picture; to mark, delineate; stroke (of a character)
- **etymology:** 江 (jiāng, "river") and 山 (shān, "mountain") evoke the natural landscape, while 如 (rú, "like/as") sets a simile to 画(畫) (huà, "painting"), depicting rivers and mountains as if a picture—hence a sense of picturesque scenery.
- **examples:**
  - 登上长城(登上長城)，远眺群山(遠眺群山)，真是江山如画(真是江山如畫)。
    - Dēng shàng Chángchéng, yuǎn tiào qúnshān, zhēn shì jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - Climbing the Great Wall and gazing at the distant mountains, the scenery is truly picturesque.
  - 江南的春景(江南的春景)，江山如画(江山如畫)，让人流连忘返(讓人流連忘返)。
    - Jiāngnán de chūnjǐng, jiāngshān rúhuà, ràng rén liúlián wàngfǎn.
    - The spring scenery in Jiangnan is picturesque and makes one reluctant to leave.
