## 柯
---
- **pinyin:** kē
- **definition:** branch; axe handle
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound with 木 (mù, "wood") providing the semantic field of wooden objects and 可 (kě, "can") serving as the phonetic, giving the sound kē. Originally denoted the wooden handle of an axe, later extended by association to a tree branch.
- **examples:**
  - 他折下一段柯(他折下一段柯)，当柴烧(當柴燒)。
    - Tā zhé xià yí duàn kē, dāng chái shāo.
    - He snapped off a branch to burn as firewood.
  - 斧柯断了(斧柯斷了)，得赶紧修(得趕緊修)。
    - Fǔ kē duàn le, děi gǎn jǐn xiū.
    - The axe handle broke; we need to fix it quickly.
