## 柯
---
- **pinyin:** kē
- **definition:** branch; axe handle
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 木 (mù, "tree") + phonetic 可 (kě, "can").
- **examples:**
  - 老树的枝柯上停着一只鸟(老樹的枝柯上停著一隻鳥)。
    - lǎo shù de zhī kē shàng tíng zhe yì zhī niǎo
    - A bird is perching on the branches of the old tree.
  - 木匠把斧柯换成了新的(木匠把斧柯換成了新的)。
    - mùjiàng bǎ fǔ kē huàn chéng le xīn de
    - The carpenter replaced the axe handle with a new one.
