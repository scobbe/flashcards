## 刻薄
---
- **pinyin:** kèbó
- **definition:** unkind; harsh; mean
- **characters:**
  - 刻
    - kè
    - carve
  - 薄
    - báo
    - thin
- **etymology:** Combines 刻 (kè, "carve") with 薄 (báo, "thin") to express unkind; harsh; mean.
- **examples:**
  - 她对新来的同事很刻薄(她對新來的同事很刻薄)，从不主动帮助(從不主動幫助)。
    - tā duì xīn lái de tóngshì hěn kèbó, cóng bù zhǔdòng bāngzhù
    - She is unkind to the new colleague and never offers help.
  - 他的评论太刻薄(他的評論太刻薄)，完全没有建设性(完全沒有建設性)。
    - tā de pínglùn tài kèbó, wánquán méiyǒu jiànshèxìng
    - His comments are too harsh and completely unconstructive.
  - 她那种刻薄的性格让周围的人都躲着她(她那種刻薄的性格讓周圍的人都躲著她)。
    - tā nà zhǒng kèbó de xìnggé ràng zhōuwéi de rén dōu duǒzhe tā
    - Her mean personality makes the people around her avoid her.
