## 中山区
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng, zhòng
    - middle, center; in, during, amidst; to hit the mark, be affected (by)
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain, hill
  - 区(區)
    - qū, ōu
    - district, administrative division; area, region
- **etymology:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") plus 山 (shān, "mountain") form the proper name "Zhongshan" (commonly honoring Sun Yat-sen), and 区(區) (qū, "district") marks it as an administrative district.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在台北市的中山区(我住在臺北市的中山區)，那里有很多咖啡馆和日式餐厅(那裡有很多咖啡館和日式餐廳)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Táiběi Shì de Zhōngshān Qū, nàlǐ yǒu hěn duō kāfēiguǎn hé Rìshì cāntīng.
    - I live in Taipei’s Zhongshan District; there are lots of cafes and Japanese restaurants there.
  - 如果你来台北旅游(如果你來臺北旅遊)，别错过中山区的林森北路夜生活(別錯過中山區的林森北路夜生活)。
    - Rúguǒ nǐ lái Táiběi lǚyóu, bié cuòguò Zhōngshān Qū de Línsēn Běilù yèshēnghuó.
    - If you visit Taipei, don’t miss the nightlife on Linsen North Road in Zhongshan District.
