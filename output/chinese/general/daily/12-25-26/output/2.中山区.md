## 中山区
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 中: zhōng, middle
  - 山: shān, mountain
  - 区(區): qū, district
- **examples:**
  - 我打算去台北的中山区逛夜市(我打算去臺北的中山區逛夜市) (wǒ dǎsuàn qù táiběi de zhōngshān qū guàng yèshì, "I'm planning to go to Zhongshan District in Taipei to browse the night market")
  - 他在台北市中山区上班(他在臺北市中山區上班) (tā zài táiběi shì zhōngshān qū shàngbān, "He works in Zhongshan District, Taipei")
