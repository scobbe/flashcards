## 日本
---
- **pinyin:** Rìběn
- **definition:** Japan
- **characters:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun; day; date; Japan, Japanese
  - 本
    - běn
    - root, origin; this, current; book, volume; classifier for books
- **etymology:** 日 (rì, "sun") with 本 (běn, "origin") literally means "origin of the sun," referencing Japan as the land where the sun rises to the east of China.
- **examples:**
  - 我明年想去日本旅游(我明年想去日本旅遊)。
    - Wǒ míngnián xiǎng qù Rìběn lǚyóu.
    - I want to travel to Japan next year.
  - 日本的樱花很有名(日本的櫻花很有名)。
    - Rìběn de yīnghuā hěn yǒumíng.
    - Cherry blossoms in Japan are famous.
