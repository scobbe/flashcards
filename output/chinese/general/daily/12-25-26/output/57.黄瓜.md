## 黄瓜(黃瓜)
---
- **pinyin:** huánggua
- **definition:** cucumber
- **characters:**
  - 黄(黃)
    - huáng
    - yellow; surname Huang; pornographic, obscene; to fall through, to be canceled
  - 瓜
    - guā
    - melon, gourd; silly person (colloquial); gossip (slang)
- **etymology:** 黄(黃) (huáng, "yellow") marks the color seen in the cucumber’s flower or ripe fruit, while 瓜 (guā, "melon/gourd") denotes cucurbits; together “yellow gourd” specifies the cucumber within the gourd family.
- **examples:**
  - 黄瓜很新鲜(黃瓜很新鮮)，我买了两根(我買了兩根)。
    - Huángguā hěn xīnxiān, wǒ mǎile liǎng gēn.
    - The cucumbers are very fresh; I bought two.
