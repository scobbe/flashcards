## 华侨(華僑)
---
- **pinyin:** huáqiáo
- **definition:** overseas Chinese
- **characters:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - China, Chinese; splendid, magnificent; flowery, ornate
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - expatriate, overseas resident; to reside abroad, to sojourn; overseas (attributive)
- **etymology:** Combines 华(華) (huá, "Chinese") marking ethnic/cultural identity with 侨(僑) (qiáo, "to reside abroad; expatriate") indicating overseas residence, yielding "Chinese living outside the homeland." The pairing preserves the traditional nuance of a temporary "sojourn" abroad.
- **examples:**
  - 她是第三代华侨(她是第三代華僑)，在巴西长大(在巴西長大)，但中文说得很好(但中文說得很好)。
    - Tā shì dì sān dài huáqiáo, zài Bāxī zhǎng dà, dàn Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
    - She is a third-generation overseas Chinese; she grew up in Brazil but speaks Chinese very well.
