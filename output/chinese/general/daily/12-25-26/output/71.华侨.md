## 华侨(華僑)
---
- **pinyin:** huáqiáo
- **definition:** overseas Chinese
- **characters:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - Chinese
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - resident abroad
- **etymology:** Combines 华(華) (huá, "Chinese") with 侨(僑) (qiáo, "resident abroad") to express overseas chinese.
- **examples:**
  - 很多华侨回国投资(很多華僑回國投資)。
    - hěn duō huáqiáo huíguó tóuzī
    - Many overseas Chinese return to invest in their home country.
