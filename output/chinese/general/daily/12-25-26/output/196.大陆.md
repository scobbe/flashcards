## 大陆(大陸)
---
- **pinyin:** dàlù
- **definition:** mainland
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great; major, main; eldest
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land, dry land; overland, by land; financial numeral for six
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big; main") combines with 陆(陸) (lù, "land; dry land") to mean "main land," i.e., the principal landmass as opposed to surrounding islands or seas.
- **examples:**
  - 他在大陆工作(他在大陸工作)，我在香港(我在香港)。
    - Tā zài Dàlù gōngzuò, wǒ zài Xiānggǎng.
    - He works on the mainland; I’m in Hong Kong.
  - 从台湾去大陆很方便(從台灣去大陸很方便)。
    - Cóng Táiwān qù Dàlù hěn fāngbiàn.
    - It’s very convenient to go to the mainland from Taiwan.
