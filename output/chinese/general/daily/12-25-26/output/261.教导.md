## 教导(教導)
---
- **pinyin:** jiàodǎo
- **definition:** instruct; teach
- **characters:**
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach
  - 导(導)
    - dǎo
    - guide
- **etymology:** Combines 教 (jiào, "teach") with 导(導) (dǎo, "guide") to express instruct; teach.
- **examples:**
  - 校长亲自教导我如何领导团队(校長親自教導我如何領導團隊)。
    - xiàozhǎng qīnzì jiàodǎo wǒ rúhé lǐngdǎo tuánduì
    - The principal personally instructed me on how to lead a team.
  - 这位老师以耐心教导学生(這位老師以耐心教導學生)。
    - zhè wèi lǎoshī yǐ nàixīn jiàodǎo xuéshēng
    - This teacher teaches students with patience.
