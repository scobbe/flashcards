## 阴道炎(陰道炎)
---
- **pinyin:** yīndàoyán
- **definition:** vaginitis
- **characters:**
  - 阴(陰): yīn, yin
  - 道: dào, way
  - 炎: yán, inflammation
- **examples:**
  - 医生说她患有阴道炎，需要按时用药(醫生說她患有陰道炎，需要按時用藥) (yīshēng shuō tā huàn yǒu yīndàoyán xūyào ànshí yòngyào, "The doctor said she has vaginitis and needs to take medication on time")
