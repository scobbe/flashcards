## 松树(松樹)
---
- **pinyin:** sōngshù
- **definition:** pine tree
- **characters:**
  - 松
    - sōng
    - pine
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
- **etymology:** Combines 松 (sōng, "pine") with 树(樹) (shù, "tree") to express pine tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这片山坡上长满了松树(這片山坡上長滿了松樹)。
    - zhè piàn shānpō shàng zhǎng mǎn le sōngshù
    - This hillside is covered with pine trees.
