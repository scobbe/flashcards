## 松树(松樹)
---
- **pinyin:** sōngshù
- **definition:** pine tree
- **characters:**
  - 松
    - sōng
    - pine, pine tree; loose, slack; to loosen, to relax
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; to plant; to set up, to establish
- **etymology:** 松 (sōng, "pine") names the species, and 树(樹) (shù, "tree") marks the category. Together they mean "pine tree," following the common "species + tree" pattern.
- **examples:**
  - 这片山坡上长满了松树(這片山坡上長滿了松樹)。
    - Zhè piàn shānpō shàng zhǎng mǎn le sōngshù.
    - This hillside is covered with pine trees.
