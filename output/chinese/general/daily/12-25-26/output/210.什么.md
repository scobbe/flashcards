## 什么(什麼)
---
- **pinyin:** shénme
- **definition:** what
- **characters:**
  - 什
    - shén, shí
    - assorted, various, mixed; household goods, utensils, odds and ends; ten, a group of ten; what (in 什么/甚麼)
  - 么(麼)
    - me, yāo, mó, ma
    - interrogative or emphatic suffix in 什么, 怎么, 多么, 这么, 那么; tiny, petty; one (variant of 幺, esp. in serial numbers)
- **etymology:** 什 (shén, "assorted; what") supplies the interrogative base—historically a phonetic substitute for 甚 (shén, "what/how")—while 么(麼) (me, "interrogative suffix") is a bound particle that finalizes it into the neutral question word "what".
- **examples:**
  - 这是什么(這是什麼)？
    - Zhè shì shénme?
    - What is this?
  - 你想吃什么(你想吃什麼)？
    - Nǐ xiǎng chī shénme?
    - What do you want to eat?
