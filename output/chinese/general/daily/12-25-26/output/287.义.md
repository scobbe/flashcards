## 义(義)
---
- **pinyin:** Yì
- **definition:** surname Yi; righteousness
- **etymology:** A character(義) meaning surname yi; righteousness.
- **examples:**
  - 义先生今天不在办公室(義先生今天不在辦公室)。
    - yì xiānsheng jīntiān bù zài bàngōngshì
    - Mr. Yi is not in the office today.
  - 他为了义而放弃私利(他為了義而放棄私利)。
    - tā wèile yì ér fàngqì sīlì
    - He gave up personal gain for righteousness.
