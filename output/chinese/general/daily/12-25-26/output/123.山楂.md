## 山楂
---
- **pinyin:** shānzhā
- **definition:** hawthorn
- **characters:**
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain, hill; mountainous area, countryside; mound, heap
  - 楂
    - zhā
    - Chinese hawthorn, hawthorn tree; hawthorn fruit
- **etymology:** 山 (shān, "mountain") serves as a modifier indicating wild or mountainous origin, paired with 楂 (zhā, "hawthorn tree/fruit") to denote the hawthorn that grows in the hills, i.e., Chinese hawthorn.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一些新鲜的山楂(我買了一些新鮮的山楂)，准备做山楂酱(準備做山楂醬)。
    - Wǒ mǎile yìxiē xīnxiān de shānzhā, zhǔnbèi zuò shānzhājiàng.
    - I bought some fresh hawthorn and plan to make hawthorn jam.
  - 山楂茶很解腻(山楂茶很解膩)，适合搭配油腻的菜(適合搭配油膩的菜)。
    - Shānzhā chá hěn jiěnì, shìhé dāpèi yóunì de cài.
    - Hawthorn tea cuts through greasiness and pairs well with rich dishes.
