## 同
---
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **definition:** same
- **etymology:** Pictographic-ideographic form with 口 (kǒu, "mouth/opening") set within an enclosing frame capped by 一 (yī, "one"), visualizing an opening gathered under one enclosure—"together, identical." Meaning is semantic rather than phono-semantic; the image of unifying within a single boundary gives the sense of "same."
- **examples:**
  - 我们住在同一个城市(我們住在同一個城市)。
    - Wǒmen zhù zài tóng yí gè chéngshì.
    - We live in the same city.
