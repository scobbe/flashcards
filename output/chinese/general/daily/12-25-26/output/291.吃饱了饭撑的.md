## 吃饱了饭撑的(吃飽了飯撐的)
---
- **pinyin:** chī bǎo le fàn chēng de
- **definition:** have nothing better to do
- **characters:**
  - 吃
    - chī
    - eat
  - 饱(飽)
    - bǎo
    - full
  - 了
    - le; aspect particle
  - 饭(飯)
    - fàn
    - meal
  - 撑(撐)
    - chēng
    - support
  - 的
    - de; possessive particle
- **etymology:** Combines 吃 (chī, "eat"), 饱(飽) (bǎo, "full"), 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal"), and 撑(撐) (chēng, "support") to express have nothing better to do.
- **examples:**
  - 你这是吃饱了饭撑的吧(你這是吃飽了飯撐的吧)，非要多管闲事(非要多管閒事)。
    - nǐ zhè shì chī bǎo le fàn chēng de ba, fēi yào duō guǎn xiánshì
    - Are you just doing this because you have nothing better to do, insisting on meddling?
  - 他一天到晚在网上喷人(他一天到晚在網上噴人)，真是吃饱了饭撑的(真是吃飽了飯撐的)。
    - tā yì tiān dào wǎn zài wǎngshàng pēn rén, zhēn shì chī bǎo le fàn chēng de
    - He spends all day flaming people online—he really has nothing better to do.
