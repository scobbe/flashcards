## 榕树(榕樹)
---
- **pinyin:** róngshù
- **definition:** banyan tree
- **characters:**
  - 榕: róng, banyan
  - 树(樹): shù, tree
- **examples:**
  - 公园里有一棵古老的榕树，夏天给人们带来阴凉(公園裡有一棵古老的榕樹，夏天給人們帶來陰涼) (gōngyuán lǐ yǒu yì kē gǔlǎo de róngshù, xiàtiān gěi rénmen dàilái yīnliáng, "There is an ancient banyan tree in the park that provides shade in summer")
