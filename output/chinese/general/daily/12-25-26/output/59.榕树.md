## 榕树(榕樹)
---
## banyan tree
### róngshù
---
- **definition:** banyan tree
- **pinyin:** róngshù
- **components:**
  - 榕
    - róng
    - banyan; Ficus microcarpa; evergreen fig; used in place names (e.g., Fuzhou)
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; to plant; to establish; to cultivate (virtue/reputation)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 榕 (banyan) + 树 (tree) = banyan tree; 榕 is phonosemantic (木 + 容).
  - **interpretation:** Combines the specific species name 榕 with the generic 树 to mark it as a type of tree. Mirrors a common Chinese pattern for plant names (X + 树).
  - **simplification:** 樹 → 树 by replacing the complex phonetic 尌 with a 对-based form to reduce strokes; 榕 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 村口有一棵古老的榕树(村口有一棵古老的榕樹)。
    - Cúnkǒu yǒu yī kē gǔlǎo de róngshù.
    - At the village entrance stands an ancient banyan tree.
  - 夏天时(夏天時)，榕树的树荫很浓(榕樹的樹蔭很濃)。
    - Xiàtiān shí, róngshù de shùyīn hěn nóng.
    - In summer, the banyan's shade is very dense.
  - 榕树的气根垂到地面(榕樹的氣根垂到地面)，像一道道帘子(像一道道簾子)。
    - Róngshù de qìgēn chuí dào dìmiàn, xiàng yī dào dào liánzi.
    - The banyan’s aerial roots hang to the ground like rows of curtains.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕
- **definition:** banyan; Ficus microcarpa; evergreen fig; used in place names (e.g., Fuzhou)
- **pinyin:** róng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood; tree
  - 容
    - róng
    - contain; allow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 木 + phonetic 容 (OC *loŋ)
  - **interpretation:** Wood/tree 木 signals that the word names a kind of tree. 容 supplies the sound róng, matching the banyan’s reading and linking it to place names like Fuzhou’s alias 榕城.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里有一棵大榕树(公園裡有一棵大榕樹)。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu yì kē dà róngshù.
    - There is a big banyan tree in the park.
  - 福州别称“榕城”(福州別稱「榕城」)。
    - Fúzhōu biéchēng “Róngchéng”.
    - Fuzhou’s nickname is “Rongcheng” (City of Banyans).
  - 榕树的气根垂到地面(榕樹的氣根垂到地面)。
    - Róngshù de qìgēn chuí dào dìmiàn.
    - The banyan’s aerial roots hang down to the ground.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕 → 木
- **definition:** wood; tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram (象形)
  - **description:** a tree: branches on top, roots on the bottom (more visible in earlier forms).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tree with spreading branches above and roots below, as seen clearly in early scripts. The vertical stroke is the trunk, while the short horizontal strokes became stylized limbs and roots in the regular form.
- **examples:**
  - 这张桌子是木头做的(這張桌子是木頭做的)。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi shì mùtou zuò de.
    - This table is made of wood.
  - 山上长满了树木(山上長滿了樹木)。
    - Shān shang zhǎng mǎn le shùmù.
    - The mountainside is covered with trees.
  - 木材被运到工厂加工(木材被運到工廠加工)。
    - Mùcái bèi yùn dào gōngchǎng jiāgōng.
    - The timber was transported to the factory for processing.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕 → 容
- **definition:** contain; allow
- **pinyin:** róng
- **components:**
  - 公
    - gōng
    - public; fair
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 宀 + phonetic: 公 (OC *kloːŋ); OC *loŋ; ‘to hold; to contain’.
  - **interpretation:** A roof (宀) evokes enclosure or shelter, pointing to the sense of containing or holding within. 公 supplies the -loŋ sound value, and the meaning broadened to ‘allow’ and to ‘appearance’ in later usage and compounds.
- **examples:**
  - 我们不容忽视(我們不容忽視)。
    - Wǒmen bù róng húshì.
    - We cannot allow it to be ignored.
  - 这个箱子可容三十本书(這個箱子可容三十本書)。
    - Zhège xiāngzi kě róng sānshí běn shū.
    - This box can contain thirty books.
  - 容器装满了水(容器裝滿了水)。
    - Róngqì zhuāng mǎn le shuǐ.
    - The container is filled with water.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕 → 容 → 公
- **definition:** public; fair
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **components:**
  - 八
    - bā
    - eight; to divide/share (as component)
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth; opening
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 八 ‘divide/opposite’ + 厶 ‘private’ → ‘public’; alt.: 八(分 ‘to divide’) + 口 ‘object’; alt. theory: pictogram (original form of 瓮).
  - **interpretation:** Combining the idea of dividing/opposing with “private” yields the notion of what is not private—shared, public, hence by extension fair and impartial. Another reading emphasizes equally dividing an object so resources become communal. Some scholars instead view it as a jar pictograph later borrowed for ‘public’ and related senses.
- **examples:**
  - 这个决定不公(這個決定不公)。
    - Zhège juédìng bù gōng.
    - This decision is unfair.
  - 公园向公众免费开放(公園向公眾免費開放)。
    - Gōngyuán xiàng gōngzhòng miǎnfèi kāifàng.
    - The park is open to the public for free.
  - 他为人很公道(他為人很公道)。
    - Tā wéirén hěn gōngdào.
    - He conducts himself in a very fair manner.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕 → 容 → 公 → 八
- **definition:** eight; to divide/share (as component)
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: two bent lines indicating “to divide”; later borrowed to mean “eight” via homophony (original sense now in 分, 別).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two strokes diverging to suggest splitting or separation. Later became a rebus for the number eight because it shared the same sound, while the original sense lived on in 分 and 別. Not the “two hands” component found in other characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我有八本书(我有八本書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu bā běn shū.
    - I have eight books.
  - 她八岁了(她八歲了)。
    - Tā bā suì le.
    - She is eight years old.
  - 我买了八个苹果(我買了八個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le bā gè píngguǒ.
    - I bought eight apples.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 榕 → 容 → 公 → 口
- **definition:** mouth; opening
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 树(樹)
- **definition:** tree; to plant; to establish; to cultivate (virtue/reputation)
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood; tree
  - 又
    - yòu
    - again; right hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 木 'tree' + phonetic: 尌 ('to plant a tree'); simplified: 壴 → 又
  - **interpretation:** 木 provides the meaning domain of wood/trees, while 尌 contributes the sound and is itself related to planting. In the simplified form, the complex 壴 inside the phonetic component was replaced by 又 to reduce strokes while retaining recognizability.
  - **simplification:** Component 壴 in the phonetic 尌 was replaced by 又 to lower stroke count while keeping 木 on the left for meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在公园里种树(我們在公園裡種樹)。
    - Wǒmen zài gōngyuán lǐ zhòng shù.
    - We plant trees in the park.
  - 学校门口的老树被保护起来了(學校門口的老樹被保護起來了)。
    - Xuéxiào ménkǒu de lǎo shù bèi bǎohù qǐlái le.
    - The old tree at the school gate has been protected.
  - 公司要树立良好的形象(公司要樹立良好的形象)。
    - Gōngsī yào shùlì liánghǎo de xíngxiàng.
    - The company needs to establish a good image.
### 榕树(榕樹) → 树(樹) → 又
- **definition:** again; right hand
- **pinyin:** yòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram – a right hand; original form of 右; unrelated to 𠬤 (> 译). Often written as 彐 in many modern characters (e.g., 事, 聿, 雪, 急, 爭, 尹, 建).
  - **interpretation:** Early graphs depict a right hand, which later gave rise to 右 ‘right (side)’. The adverbial sense ‘again’ developed later by semantic extension. In many compounds a similar shape is written as 彐, but that is a graphic convention rather than a direct historical component here.
- **examples:**
  - 他又来了(他又來了)。
    - Tā yòu lái le.
    - He came again.
  - 请向右转(請向右轉)。
    - Qǐng xiàng yòu zhuǎn.
    - Please turn right.
  - 她又买了一本书(她又買了一本書)。
    - Tā yòu mǎi le yī běn shū.
    - She bought another book.
