## 榕树(榕樹)
---
- **pinyin:** róngshù
- **definition:** banyan tree
- **characters:**
  - 榕
    - róng
    - banyan tree, Chinese banyan
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; to plant; to set up, to establish
- **etymology:** 榕 (róng, "banyan") names the species, while 树(樹) (shù, "tree") gives the generic category, forming a straightforward modifier-head compound meaning a banyan tree.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里有一棵百年榕树(公園裡有一棵百年榕樹)，枝叶如伞般铺开(枝葉如傘般鋪開)。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu yī kē bǎi nián róngshù, zhī yè rú sǎn bān pū kāi.
    - There is a century-old banyan tree in the park, its branches and leaves spread like an umbrella.
  - 村口的榕树下(村口的榕樹下)，老人们每天下棋聊天(老人們每天下棋聊天)。
    - Cūnkǒu de róngshù xià, lǎorénmen měitiān xiàqí liáotiān.
    - Under the banyan tree at the village entrance, the elderly play chess and chat every day.
