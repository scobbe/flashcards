## 榕树(榕樹)
---
- **pinyin:** róngshù
- **definition:** banyan tree
- **characters:**
  - 榕
    - róng
    - banyan
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
- **etymology:** Combines 榕 (róng, "banyan") with 树(樹) (shù, "tree") to express banyan tree.
- **examples:**
  - 公园里有一棵古老的榕树(公園裡有一棵古老的榕樹)，夏天给人们带来阴凉(夏天給人們帶來陰涼)。
    - gōngyuán lǐ yǒu yì kē gǔlǎo de róngshù, xiàtiān gěi rénmen dàilái yīnliáng
    - There is an ancient banyan tree in the park that provides shade in summer.
