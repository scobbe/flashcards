## 拉制
---
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **characters:**
  - 拉: lā, to pull
  - 制(製): zhì, to make
- **examples:**
  - 这种合金适合拉制细丝(這種合金適合拉製細絲) (zhè zhǒng héjīn shìhé lāzhì xìsī, "This alloy is suitable for drawing into fine wires")
  - 通过冷拉制工艺，钢管的尺寸公差更小(透過冷拉製工藝，鋼管的尺寸公差更小) (tōngguò lěng lāzhì gōngyì gāngguǎn de chǐcùn gōngchā gèng xiǎo, "By using cold drawing, the steel tube's dimensional tolerance is smaller")
