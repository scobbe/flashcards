## 拉制
---
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **characters:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - to pull
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - to make
- **etymology:** Combines 拉 (lā, "to pull") with 制(製) (zhì, "to make") to express metal drawing process.
- **examples:**
  - 这种合金适合拉制细丝(這種合金適合拉製細絲)。
    - zhè zhǒng héjīn shìhé lāzhì xìsī
    - This alloy is suitable for drawing into fine wires.
  - 通过冷拉制工艺(透過冷拉製工藝)，钢管的尺寸公差更小(鋼管的尺寸公差更小)。
    - tōngguò lěng lāzhì gōngyì gāngguǎn de chǐcùn gōngchā gèng xiǎo
    - By using cold drawing, the steel tube's dimensional tolerance is smaller.
