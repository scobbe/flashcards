## 拉制
---
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **characters:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - to pull, drag; to play a bowed string instrument; to chat, chit-chat; to defecate, have diarrhea
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - to make, manufacture; to control, restrain; system, institution; rule, regulation
- **etymology:** 拉 (lā, "to pull") names the pulling/drawing action, while 制(製) (zhì, "to make/shape") denotes manufacturing/processing, combining to mean shaping metal by pulling it through a die (metal drawing).
- **examples:**
  - 通过拉制(通過拉製)，我们把粗铜棒加工成细丝(我們把粗銅棒加工成細絲)。
    - tōngguò lāzhì, wǒmen bǎ cū tóng bàng jiāgōng chéng xì sī.
    - Through metal drawing, we turn thick copper rods into fine wire.
  - 工程师正在调整拉制模具的孔径(工程師正在調整拉製模具的孔徑)，以优化钢管的表面质量(以優化鋼管的表面質量)。
    - gōngchéngshī zhèngzài tiáozhěng lāzhì mójù de kǒngjìng, yǐ yōuhuà gāngguǎn de biǎomiàn zhìliàng.
    - The engineer is adjusting the aperture of the drawing die to optimize the surface quality of the steel tubes.
