## 贤(賢)
---
- **pinyin:** xián
- **definition:** virtuous; worthy
- **etymology:** From 賢 (臤 → 𰀡 and 貝 → 贝) Definitions: For pronunciation and definitions of 贤 – see 賢 ("virtuous ; good ; clever ; wise ; worthy ; able ; capable ; etc.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位贤母(她是一位賢母)，关心家人也乐于助人(關心家人也樂於助人)。
    - tā shì yī wèi xián mǔ, guānxīn jiārén yě lèyú zhùrén
    - She is a virtuous mother who cares for her family and is eager to help others.
  - 他是公司里值得重用的贤才(他是公司裡值得重用的賢才)。
    - tā shì gōngsī lǐ zhí de zhòngyòng de xiáncái
    - He is a worthy talent in the company, deserving to be entrusted with important tasks.
