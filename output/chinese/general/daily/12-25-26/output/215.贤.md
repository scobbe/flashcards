## 贤(賢)
---
- **pinyin:** xián
- **definition:** virtuous; worthy
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowrie; valuables") gives the sense of "value/worth," while 臤 (qián, "respectful; firm") supplies the sound, yielding "worthy/virtuous." The upper 臤 itself comprises 臣 (chén, "minister") + 又 (yòu, "hand"), evoking a capable minister at work and reinforcing the idea of meritorious worth.
- **examples:**
  - 她贤惠持家(她賢惠持家)，大家都敬重她(大家都敬重她)。
    - Tā xiánhuì chíjiā, dàjiā dōu jìngzhòng tā.
    - She is virtuous and manages the household well; everyone respects her.
  - 国家选贤任能(國家選賢任能)，才能长治久安(才能長治久安)。
    - Guójiā xuǎn xián rèn néng, cái néng chángzhì jiǔ'ān.
    - Only by selecting the worthy and appointing the capable can a country enjoy long-term stability.
