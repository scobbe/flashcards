## 牵牛花(牽牛花)
---
- **pinyin:** qiānniúhuā
- **definition:** morning glory
- **characters:**
  - 牵(牽): qiān, to pull
  - 牛: niú, cow
  - 花: huā, flower
- **examples:**
  - 清晨，牵牛花沿着围墙攀爬，开出一串串蓝紫色的花(清晨，牽牛花沿著圍牆攀爬，開出一串串藍紫色的花) (qīngchén, qiānniúhuā yán zhe wéiqiáng pān pá, kāi chū yī chuànchuàn lán zǐsè de huā, "At dawn, morning glories climb along the wall, blooming in clusters of blue-violet flowers")
