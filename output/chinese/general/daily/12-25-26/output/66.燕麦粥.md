## 燕麦粥(燕麥粥)
---
- **pinyin:** yànmàizhōu
- **definition:** oatmeal porridge
- **characters:**
  - 燕
    - yàn
    - swallow
  - 麦(麥)
    - mài
    - wheat
  - 粥
    - zhōu
    - porridge
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早餐都喝一碗燕麦粥(我每天早餐都喝一碗燕麥粥)，既健康又有饱腹感(既健康又有飽腹感)。
    - wǒ měitiān zǎocān dōu hē yì wǎn yànmàizhōu, jì jiànkāng yòu yǒu bǎofùgǎn
    - I have a bowl of oatmeal porridge for breakfast every day; it's healthy and filling.
