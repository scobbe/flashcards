## 燕麦粥(燕麥粥)
---
- **pinyin:** yànmàizhōu
- **definition:** oatmeal porridge
- **characters:**
  - 燕
    - yàn, yān
    - swallow (bird); banquet, feast; Yan (ancient state); surname
  - 麦(麥)
    - mài
    - wheat; cereal grains
  - 粥
    - zhōu
    - congee, porridge, gruel
- **etymology:** 燕 (yàn, "Yan (ancient state)") modifies 麦(麥) (mài, "wheat/grain") to denote the "Yan-region grain" (oats), and 粥 (zhōu, "porridge") specifies it prepared as a gruel.
- **examples:**
  - 早上喝一碗热乎乎的燕麦粥(早上喝一碗熱乎乎的燕麥粥)，暖胃又健康(暖胃又健康)。
    - Zǎoshang hē yì wǎn rèhūhū de yànmàizhōu, nuǎn wèi yòu jiànkāng.
    - Drinking a bowl of steaming-hot oatmeal porridge in the morning warms your stomach and is healthy.
