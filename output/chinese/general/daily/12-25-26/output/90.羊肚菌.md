## 羊肚菌
---
- **pinyin:** yángdùjùn
- **definition:** morel mushroom
- **characters:**
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep; goat
  - 肚
    - dù
    - belly, abdomen; stomach (organ); tripe
  - 菌
    - jūn, jùn
    - germ, microbe; fungus; mushroom
- **etymology:** 羊 (yáng, "sheep") and 肚 (dù, "belly; tripe") liken the cap's pitted, honeycomb surface to sheep tripe, while 菌 (jūn, "fungus; mushroom") classifies it as a mushroom.
- **examples:**
  - 春天的山里能采到很多羊肚菌(春天的山裡能採到很多羊肚菌)，它们炒蛋特别香(它們炒蛋特別香)。
    - Chūntiān de shān lǐ néng cǎi dào hěn duō yángdùjùn, tāmen chǎo dàn tèbié xiāng.
    - In the mountains in spring, you can find lots of morel mushrooms; they’re especially fragrant stir-fried with eggs.
