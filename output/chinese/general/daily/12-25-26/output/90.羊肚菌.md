## 羊肚菌
---
- **pinyin:** yángdùjùn
- **definition:** morel mushroom
- **characters:**
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
  - 肚
    - dù
    - belly
  - 菌
    - jūn
    - fungus
- **etymology:** Combines 羊 (yáng, "sheep"), 肚 (dù, "belly"), and 菌 (jūn, "fungus") to convey morel mushroom.
- **examples:**
  - 我用羊肚菌做菜(我用羊肚菌做菜)。
    - wǒ yòng yángdùjùn zuò cài
    - I cook with morel mushrooms.
