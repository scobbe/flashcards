## 练习(練習)
---
- **pinyin:** liànxí
- **definition:** practice
- **characters:**
  - 练(練)
    - liàn
    - practice, drill; train; skilled, well-practiced; refined, polished, concise
  - 习(習)
    - xí
    - study, learn; practice, review; habit, custom; be accustomed to, be used to
- **etymology:** Combines 练(練) (liàn, "train, refine") with 习(習) (xí, "study, repeated practice, habit") to express skill refined through habitual repetition—refinement (练) achieved by repeated learning (习).
- **examples:**
  - 我每天练习说中文(我每天練習說中文)，因为我想更流利(因為我想更流利)。
    - Wǒ měitiān liànxí shuō Zhōngwén, yīnwèi wǒ xiǎng gèng liúlì.
    - I practice speaking Chinese every day because I want to be more fluent.
  - 老师留了一些练习(老師留了一些練習)，让我们回家做(讓我們回家做)。
    - Lǎoshī liú le yìxiē liànxí, ràng wǒmen huíjiā zuò.
    - The teacher assigned some exercises for us to do at home.
