## 练习(練習)
---
- **pinyin:** liànxí
- **definition:** practice
- **characters:**
  - 练(練)
    - liàn
    - to practice
  - 习(習)
    - xí
    - to study
- **examples:**
  - 他每天练习弹钢琴两个小时(他每天練習彈鋼琴兩個小時)。
    - tā měitiān liànxí tán gāngqín liǎng gè xiǎoshí
    - He practices playing the piano for two hours every day.
