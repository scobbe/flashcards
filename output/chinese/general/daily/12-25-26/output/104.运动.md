## 运动(運動)
---
- **pinyin:** yùndòng
- **definition:** exercise; sports
- **characters:**
  - 运(運)
    - yùn
    - transport
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - move
- **etymology:** 运(運) (yùn, "to convey; to guide; to set in motion") adds the idea of directed, managed movement, while 动(動) (dòng, "move") is mere motion—together, motion that is mobilized and purposeful. This yields both the sense of organized "movement" (campaigns) in Chinese and—via Meiji-era Japanese undō later borrowed back—the meaning of deliberate bodily motion, i.e., exercise/sports.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天晚上去公园运动半个小时(我每天晚上去公園運動半個小時)。
    - wǒ měitiān wǎnshang qù gōngyuán yùndòng bàn gè xiǎoshí
    - I go to the park to exercise for half an hour every evening.
  - 他对运动很感兴趣(他對運動很感興趣)，特别是团队运动(特別是團隊運動)。
    - tā duì yùndòng hěn gǎn xìngqù, tèbié shì tuánduì yùndòng
    - He is very interested in sports, especially team sports.
