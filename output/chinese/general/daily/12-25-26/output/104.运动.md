## 运动(運動)
---
- **pinyin:** yùndòng
- **definition:** exercise; sports
- **characters:**
  - 运(運)
    - yùn
    - to transport, ship, carry; to use, apply; luck, fortune; to run, operate
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - to move, stir; to act, take action; to use, employ; to change, shift
- **etymology:** 运(運) (yùn, "to set in motion; operate; transport") joins 动(動) (dòng, "move; act") to mean "to put into motion," hence bodily movement and, by extension, exercise and sports. The pairing emphasizes initiating and carrying out movement, echoing the idea of mobilizing or circulating the body.
- **examples:**
  - 医生建议我每天运动至少三十分钟(醫生建議我每天運動至少三十分鐘)，有益于健康(有益於健康)。
    - Yīshēng jiànyì wǒ měitiān yùndòng zhìshǎo sānshí fēnzhōng, yǒuyì yú jiànkāng.
    - The doctor recommends I exercise at least thirty minutes every day; it’s good for my health.
  - 他对各种运动都很了解(他對各種運動都很了解)，比如足球和网球(比如足球和網球)。
    - Tā duì gè zhǒng yùndòng dōu hěn liǎojiě, bǐrú zúqiú hé wǎngqiú.
    - He knows a lot about all kinds of sports, such as soccer and tennis.
