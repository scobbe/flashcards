## 橙皮
---
- **pinyin:** chéngpí
- **definition:** orange peel
- **characters:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange (color); orange (fruit); orange tree
  - 皮
    - pí
    - skin, hide; leather; peel, rind; naughty, cheeky
- **etymology:** 橙 (chéng, "orange (fruit)") modifies 皮 (pí, "skin/peel") to denote the fruit’s outer covering—literally "orange skin/peel."
- **examples:**
  - 我把橙皮晒干(我把橙皮曬乾)，用来泡茶(用來泡茶)。
    - Wǒ bǎ chéngpí shài gān, yòng lái pào chá.
    - I dry the orange peel and use it to brew tea.
  - 橙皮很苦(橙皮很苦)，最好不要吃(最好不要吃)。
    - Chéngpí hěn kǔ, zuìhǎo bú yào chī.
    - Orange peel is quite bitter; it’s best not to eat it.
