## 橙皮
---
- **pinyin:** chéngpí
- **definition:** orange peel
- **characters:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange
  - 皮
    - pí
    - skin
- **etymology:** Combines 橙 (chéng, "orange") with 皮 (pí, "skin") to express orange peel.
- **examples:**
  - 橙皮可以用来做果酱(橙皮可以用來做果醬)。
    - chéngpí kěyǐ yòng lái zuò guǒjiàng
    - Orange peel can be used to make marmalade.
