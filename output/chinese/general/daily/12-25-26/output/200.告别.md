## 告别(告別)
---
- **pinyin:** gàobié
- **definition:** say goodbye; farewell
- **characters:**
  - 告
    - gào
    - to tell
  - 别(別)
    - bié
    - do not
- **examples:**
  - 我们在车站告别(我們在車站告別)。
    - wǒmen zài chēzhàn gàobié
    - We said goodbye at the station.
  - 他的告别演说感人至深(他的告別演說感人至深)。
    - tā de gàobié yǎnshuō gǎnrén zhì shēn
    - His farewell speech was deeply moving.
