## 告别(告別)
---
- **pinyin:** gàobié
- **definition:** say goodbye; farewell
- **characters:**
  - 告
    - gào
    - tell, inform; report; announce; accuse, sue
  - 别(別)
    - bié
    - do not; other, else; leave, part, separate; pin, fasten
- **etymology:** 告 (gào, "inform/announce") combines with 别(別) (bié, "separate; part") to mean announcing a separation, i.e., saying farewell.
- **examples:**
  - 我在机场跟她拥抱告别(我在機場跟她擁抱告別)，然后看着她过安检(然後看著她過安檢)。
    - Wǒ zài jīchǎng gēn tā yǒngbào gàobié, ránhòu kànzhe tā guò ānjiǎn.
    - At the airport, I hugged her to say goodbye, then watched her go through security.
  - 他写下这封信(他寫下這封信)，作为对故乡的告别(作為對故鄉的告別)。
    - Tā xiěxià zhè fēng xìn, zuòwéi duì gùxiāng de gàobié.
    - He wrote this letter as a farewell to his hometown.
