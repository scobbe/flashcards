## 枕头(枕頭)
---
- **pinyin:** zhěntou
- **definition:** pillow
- **characters:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - pillow
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head
- **etymology:** Combines 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") with 头(頭) (tóu, "head") to express pillow.
- **examples:**
  - 这个枕头很舒服(這個枕頭很舒服)。
    - zhège zhěntou hěn shūfu
    - This pillow is very comfortable.
