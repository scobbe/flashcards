## 一切
---
- **pinyin:** yīqiè
- **definition:** everything
- **characters:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
  - 切
    - qiē
    - to cut
- **etymology:** Combines 一 (yī, "one") with 切 (qiē, "to cut") to express everything.
- **examples:**
  - 一切都会好起来(一切都會好起來)。
    - yīqiè dōu huì hǎo qǐlái
    - Everything will get better
