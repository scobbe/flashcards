## 一切
---
- **pinyin:** yīqiè
- **definition:** everything
- **characters:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one, single; a, an; whole, entire; once, as soon as
  - 切
    - qiē, qiè
    - to cut, slice; close, intimate; appropriate, pertinent; urgent, pressing
- **etymology:** 一 (yī, "one; entire") and 切 (qiè, "all; every") combine to express the totality of things—literally "one-all," a Buddhist-influenced collocation meaning the whole of everything.
- **examples:**
  - 一切都会好起来的(一切都會好起來的)。
    - Yīqiè dōu huì hǎo qǐlái de.
    - Everything will get better.
