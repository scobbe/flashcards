## 损(損)
---
- **pinyin:** sǔn
- **definition:** decrease; damage
- **etymology:** A character(損) meaning decrease; damage.
- **examples:**
  - 岁月不损他的热情(歲月不損他的熱情)。
    - suìyuè bù sǔn tā de rèqíng
    - Time does not diminish his passion.
  - 这件事损了他的名誉(這件事損了他的名譽)。
    - zhè jiàn shì sǔn le tā de míngyù
    - This incident damaged his reputation.
