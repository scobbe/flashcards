## engineer
### gōngchéngshī
---
- **definition:** engineer
- **pinyin:** gōngchéngshī
- **components:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work; labor; craft; worker
  - 程
    - chéng
    - journey; course; procedure; measure
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - teacher; master; expert; (military) division
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 工程 + 师 = engineer/specialist in engineering
  - **interpretation:** Combines the field 工程 with the professional suffix 师 to denote a trained specialist. Literally an “engineering master/professional.”
  - **simplification:** 師 → 师 by character simplification; reduced strokes for a high‑frequency professional suffix.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一名软件工程师(他是一名軟體工程師)。
    - Tā shì yī míng ruǎnjiàn gōngchéngshī.
    - He is a software engineer.
  - 她通过考试成为注册工程师(她通過考試成為註冊工程師)。
    - Tā tōngguò kǎoshì chéngwéi zhùcè gōngchéngshī.
    - She passed the exam to become a licensed engineer.
  - 工程师负责设计和维护系统(工程師負責設計和維護系統)。
    - Gōngchéngshī fùzé shèjì hé wéihù xìtǒng.
    - Engineers are responsible for designing and maintaining systems.
### 工程师(工程師) → 工
- **definition:** work; labor; craft; worker
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a bladed tool to plow and mark a border; alternatively a carpenter’s square (cf. original meaning of 巨).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tool used to prepare the ground and set boundaries, naturally extending to the idea of labor and work. Some traditions instead see a carpenter’s square, connecting it with making and constructing, and with early senses linked to 巨.
- **examples:**
  - 我在工厂工作(我在工廠工作)。
    - Wǒ zài gōngchǎng gōngzuò.
    - I work in a factory.
  - 这些工人正在劳动(這些工人正在勞動)。
    - Zhèxiē gōngrén zhèngzài láodòng.
    - These workers are laboring now.
  - 他的工钱很高(他的工錢很高)。
    - Tā de gōngqián hěn gāo.
    - His wages are high.
### 工程师(工程師) → 程
- **definition:** journey; course; procedure; measure
- **pinyin:** chéng
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain; cereal
  - 呈
    - chéng
    - to present; to submit; to appear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 禾 (“grain”) + phonetic 呈 (OC *rleŋ, *l'eŋs) — measurements.
  - **interpretation:** Combines the semantic cue 禾 to suggest agriculture and measuring grain or fields with the phonetic 呈 to signal the pronunciation chéng. The sense broadened from measuring agricultural quantities to standardized procedures, courses, and schedules.
- **examples:**
  - 我把明天的行程安排好了(我把明天的行程安排好了)。
    - Wǒ bǎ míngtiān de xíngchéng ānpái hǎo le.
    - I’ve arranged tomorrow’s itinerary.
  - 新软件的安装程序很简单(新軟體的安裝程序很簡單)。
    - Xīn ruǎnjiàn de ānzhuāng chéngxù hěn jiǎndān.
    - The installation procedure of the new software is very simple.
  - 项目进程按预定的步骤进行(項目進程按預定的步驟進行)。
    - Xiàngmù jìnchéng àn yùdìng de bùzhòu jìnxíng.
    - The project’s course proceeds according to the planned steps.
### 工程师(工程師) → 程 → 禾
- **definition:** grain; cereal
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram — picture of a plant stalk; clearer in older forms; similar to 來 but unrelated to 釆; later gave 株 and 硃.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a stalk of grain with a head and leaves, representing cereal crops in general. Early forms make the plant shape more obvious; despite resembling 來, it has a separate origin and later served as a basis for forms in characters like 株 and 硃.
- **examples:**
  - 田里长满了禾苗(田裡長滿了禾苗)。
    - Tián lǐ zhǎng mǎn le hé miáo.
    - The field is full of grain seedlings.
  - 秋天田野里的禾熟了(秋天田野裡的禾熟了)。
    - Qiūtiān tiányě lǐ de hé shú le.
    - In autumn, the grain crops in the fields have ripened.
  - 禾本科作物需要充足的阳光(禾本科作物需要充足的陽光)。
    - Hé běnkē zuòwù xūyào chōngzú de yángguāng.
    - Crops of the grass family need plenty of sunlight.
### 工程师(工程師) → 程 → 呈
- **definition:** to present; to submit; to appear
- **pinyin:** chéng
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 口 + phonetic 𡈼 (not 壬 or 王)
  - **interpretation:** Mouth 口 provides the semantic field of speaking, presenting, or submitting, while 𡈼 supplies the sound value leading to chéng. The lower element is a distinct phonetic, not 壬 or 王, emphasizing its role in pronunciation rather than meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 请把报告呈交给主任(請把報告呈交給主任)。
    - Qǐng bǎ bàogào chéngjiāo gěi zhǔrèn.
    - Please present the report to the director.
  - 文件已呈报上级审批(文件已呈報上級審批)。
    - Wénjiàn yǐ chéngbào shàngjí shěnpī.
    - The document has been submitted for higher-level approval.
  - 天空呈现出淡粉色(天空呈現出淡粉色)。
    - Tiānkōng chéngxiàn chū dàn fěnsè.
    - The sky appears a light pink.
### 工程师(工程師) → 程 → 呈 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 工程师(工程師) → 师(師)
- **definition:** teacher; master; expert; (military) division
- **pinyin:** shī
- **components:**
  - 帀
    - zā
    - to go around; to surround
  - 刀
    - dāo
    - knife
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound (会意)
  - **description:** 𠂤 (“mound, hill”) + 帀 (“to surround”); simplified by replacing 𠂤 with 刂.
  - **interpretation:** A hill combined with the idea of encircling evokes troops camped around a rise, yielding the sense of an army and, by extension, a master/teacher who leads or trains. The simplified form keeps the encampment idea while swapping the hill for the knife component, leaving the core meaning intact.
  - **simplification:** 𠂤 was replaced with 刂 to reduce strokes, paralleling the simplification 帅 ← 帥; a graphic streamlining without phonetic or semantic reanalysis.
- **examples:**
  - 她是我的中文老师(她是我的中文老師)。
    - Tā shì wǒ de Zhōngwén lǎoshī.
    - She is my Chinese teacher.
  - 我向师傅请教(我向師傅請教)。
    - Wǒ xiàng shīfu qǐngjiào.
    - I seek advice from the master.
  - 第三师明天换防(第三師明天換防)。
    - Dì-sān shī míngtiān huànfáng.
    - The 3rd division will rotate duty tomorrow.
### 工程师(工程師) → 师(師) → 帀
- **definition:** to go around; to surround
- **pinyin:** zā
- **components:**
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - towel; cloth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** uncertain
  - **description:** Uncertain; Shuowen: inverted 之 (originally 㞢); Zhang Shichao: “roots” 柢; oracle bones: 之 represents a footprint.
  - **interpretation:** Scholars propose several origins: an inversion of 之, an image of spreading roots, or a footprint mark. Each points to motion or extension around something, which aligns with the sense of circling or surrounding.
- **examples:**
  - 敌军帀城而围之(敵軍帀城而圍之)。
    - Díjūn zā chéng ér wéi zhī.
    - The enemy army went around the city and laid siege to it.
  - 商人帀市叫卖(商人帀市叫賣)。
    - Shāngrén zā shì jiàomài.
    - Merchants went around the market hawking their goods.
  - 我们沿湖帀行一圈(我們沿湖帀行一圈)。
    - Wǒmen yán hú zā xíng yī quān.
    - We went around the lake for one lap.
### 工程师(工程師) → 师(師) → 帀 → 巾
- **definition:** towel; cloth
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – hanging piece of cloth.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cloth draped and hanging, with the long stroke as the edge and the shorter strokes suggesting the folds. The image conveys a towel or piece of fabric suspended from a line or rod.
- **examples:**
  - 我用湿毛巾擦脸(我用濕毛巾擦臉)。
    - Wǒ yòng shī máojīn cā liǎn.
    - I use a wet towel to wipe my face.
  - 她戴着红色的头巾(她戴著紅色的頭巾)。
    - Tā dài zhe hóngsè de tóujīn.
    - She is wearing a red headscarf.
  - 冬天出门要围上围巾(冬天出門要圍上圍巾)。
    - Dōngtiān chūmén yào wéi shàng wéijīn.
    - In winter, put on a scarf before going out.
### 工程师(工程師) → 师(師) → 刀
- **definition:** knife
- **pinyin:** dāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram — a knife; original form 𠚣, seen in a variant of 利 (𥝢) and atop 黎.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a knife with a distinct blade and handle. The graph was streamlined into the current two-stroke form and also serves as a component in characters like 利 and 黎.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿着一把刀(他拿著一把刀)。
    - Tā názhe yì bǎ dāo.
    - He is holding a knife.
  - 厨师用刀切蔬菜(廚師用刀切蔬菜)。
    - Chúshī yòng dāo qiē shūcài.
    - The chef uses a knife to cut vegetables.
  - 这把刀很锋利(這把刀很鋒利)。
    - Zhè bǎ dāo hěn fēnglì.
    - This knife is very sharp.
