## 工程师(工程師)
---
- **pinyin:** gōngchéngshī
- **definition:** engineer
- **characters:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
  - 程
    - chéng
    - journey
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - teacher
- **etymology:** Combines 工 (gōng, "work"), 程 (chéng, "journey"), and 师(師) (shī, "teacher") to convey engineer.
- **examples:**
  - 他是工程师(他是工程師)。
    - tā shì gōngchéngshī
    - He is an engineer.
