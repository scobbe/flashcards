## 工程师(工程師)
---
- **pinyin:** gōngchéngshī
- **definition:** engineer
- **characters:**
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work, labor; worker, laborer; craft, skill; industry
  - 程
    - chéng
    - journey, itinerary; distance; process, procedure; degree, extent
  - 师(師)
    - shī
    - teacher; master, expert; division (military unit); specialist
- **etymology:** 工 (gōng, "work/craft") with 程 (chéng, "process/course") forms 工程, meaning "engineering/project"—the crafted, measured process of making things. Adding 师(師) (shī, "master/expert") marks the specialist who practices it, i.e., an engineer.
- **examples:**
  - 他是机械工程师(他是機械工程師)，在工厂设计生产线(在工廠設計生產線)。
    - Tā shì jīxiè gōngchéngshī, zài gōngchǎng shèjì shēngchǎnxiàn.
    - He’s a mechanical engineer who designs production lines at a factory.
  - 作为土木工程师(作為土木工程師)，她负责监督桥梁的施工(她負責監督橋梁的施工)。
    - Zuòwéi tǔmù gōngchéngshī, tā fùzé jiāndū qiáoliáng de shīgōng.
    - As a civil engineer, she is responsible for overseeing the construction of the bridge.
