## 乌鲁木齐市(烏魯木齊市)
---
- **pinyin:** Wūlǔmùqí Shì
- **definition:** Urumqi (capital of Xinjiang)
- **characters:**
  - 乌(烏)
    - wū
    - crow, raven; black, dark; to blacken
  - 鲁(魯)
    - lǔ
    - crude, rude, boorish; dull, stupid; State of Lu, Shandong (abbrev.); surname Lu
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood, timber; tree; wooden, numb
  - 齐(齊)
    - qí
    - even, uniform, neat; together, in unison; to make even, align; Qi (ancient state)
  - 市
    - shì
    - market; city; to trade, buy and sell
- **etymology:** A phonetic transcription of the Uyghur/Mongolic toponym 'Ürümqi': 乌(烏) (wū, "black") + 鲁(魯) (lǔ, "Lu") + 木 (mù, "wood") + 齐(齊) (qí, "even") approximate the sounds u-lu-mu-qi, while 市 (shì, "city") marks it as a city; the literal meanings are incidental.
- **examples:**
  - 乌鲁木齐市是新疆的首府(烏魯木齊市是新疆的首府)。
    - Wūlǔmùqí Shì shì Xīnjiāng de shǒufǔ.
    - Urumqi is the capital of Xinjiang.
