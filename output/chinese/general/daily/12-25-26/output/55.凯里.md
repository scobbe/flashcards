## 凯里(凱里)
---
- **pinyin:** Kǎilǐ
- **definition:** Kaili City
- **characters:**
  - 凯(凱)
    - kǎi
    - triumphant, victorious; victory, triumph
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - inside, within; neighborhood, village; li, Chinese mile
- **etymology:** 凯(凱) (kǎi, "triumphant") combines with 里 (lǐ, "neighborhood/ward") to suggest an auspicious 'victorious settlement.' As a toponym, the characters also align with the local pronunciation "Kǎilǐ" while conferring favorable meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 凯里是贵州东南部的一座城市(凱里是貴州東南部的一座城市)，以苗侗文化闻名(以苗侗文化聞名)。
    - Kǎilǐ shì Guìzhōu dōngnánbù de yī zuò chéngshì, yǐ Miáo Dòng wénhuà wénmíng.
    - Kaili is a city in southeastern Guizhou, known for its Miao and Dong culture.
  - 我们打算从贵阳坐高铁到凯里(我們打算從貴陽坐高鐵到凱里)。
    - Wǒmen dǎsuàn cóng Guìyáng zuò gāotiě dào Kǎilǐ.
    - We plan to take a high-speed train from Guiyang to Kaili.
