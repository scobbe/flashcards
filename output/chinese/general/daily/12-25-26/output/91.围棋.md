## 围棋(圍棋)
---
- **pinyin:** wéiqí
- **definition:** Go game
- **characters:**
  - 围(圍)
    - wéi
    - surround, encircle; enclose; besiege; wrap
  - 棋
    - qí
    - chess; board game; chess piece, game piece
- **etymology:** 围(圍) (wéi, "surround; encircle") with 棋 (qí, "board game") names the board game defined by surrounding territory and capturing stones—literally an "encirclement game".
- **examples:**
  - 他从小就喜欢下围棋(他從小就喜歡下圍棋)。
    - Tā cóngxiǎo jiù xǐhuan xià wéiqí.
    - He’s loved playing Go since he was little.
  - 这家俱乐部每周末都举办围棋比赛(這家俱樂部每週末都舉辦圍棋比賽)。
    - Zhè jiā jùlèbù měi zhōumò dōu jǔbàn wéiqí bǐsài.
    - This club hosts Go tournaments every weekend.
