## 炒蛋
---
- **pinyin:** chǎodàn
- **definition:** scrambled eggs
- **characters:**
  - 炒
    - chǎo
    - stir-fry
  - 蛋
    - dàn
    - egg
- **examples:**
  - 这家餐厅的炒蛋很滑嫩(這家餐廳的炒蛋很滑嫩)。
    - zhè jiā cāntīng de chǎodàn hěn huá nèn
    - The scrambled eggs at this restaurant are very soft and silky.
