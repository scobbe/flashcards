## 控
---
- **pinyin:** kòng
- **definition:** control; accuse
- **examples:**
  - 他在关键时刻成功控场(他在關鍵時刻成功控場)，稳定了大家的情绪(穩定了大家的情緒)。
    - tā zài guānjiàn shíkè chénggōng kòngchǎng, wěndìng le dàjiā de qíngxù
    - At the critical moment he successfully controlled the situation and calmed everyone down.
  - 检方控他受贿数十万元(檢方控他受賄數十萬元)。
    - jiǎn fāng kòng tā shòuhuì shù shí wàn yuán
    - Prosecutors accused him of taking bribes totaling tens of thousands of yuan.
