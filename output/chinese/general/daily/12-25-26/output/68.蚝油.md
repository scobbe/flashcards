## 蚝油(蠔油)
---
- **pinyin:** háoyóu
- **definition:** oyster sauce
- **characters:**
  - 蚝(蠔)
    - háo
    - oyster
  - 油
    - yóu
    - oil; grease, fat; petroleum
- **etymology:** 蚝(蠔) (háo, "oyster") + 油 (yóu, "oil") literally yields "oyster oil," indicating a liquid derived from oysters. In culinary usage, 油 broadens from "oil" to any rich, glossy sauce-like liquid, thus referring to oyster sauce.
- **examples:**
  - 这道菜要放一点蚝油(這道菜要放一點蠔油)，味道才会更鲜(味道才會更鮮)。
    - Zhè dào cài yào fàng yìdiǎn háoyóu, wèidào cái huì gèng xiān.
    - Add a bit of oyster sauce to this dish and it will taste fresher.
  - 我喜欢用蚝油炒青菜(我喜歡用蠔油炒青菜)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng háoyóu chǎo qīngcài.
    - I like to stir-fry greens with oyster sauce.
