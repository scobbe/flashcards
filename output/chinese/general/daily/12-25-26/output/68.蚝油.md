## 蚝油(蠔油)
---
- **pinyin:** háoyóu
- **definition:** oyster sauce
- **characters:**
  - 蚝(蠔)
    - háo
    - oyster
  - 油
    - yóu
    - oil
- **etymology:** Combines 蚝(蠔) (háo, "oyster") with 油 (yóu, "oil") to express oyster sauce.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用蚝油炒青菜(我喜歡用蠔油炒青菜)。
    - wǒ xǐhuan yòng háoyóu chǎo qīngcài
    - I like to stir-fry greens with oyster sauce.
