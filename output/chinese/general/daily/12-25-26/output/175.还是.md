## 还是(還是)
---
- **pinyin:** háishi
- **definition:** still; yet
- **characters:**
  - 还(還)
    - hái, huán
    - still, yet; also, too; rather, fairly; return, give back, repay
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be; yes; correct, right; indeed, really
- **etymology:** 还(還) (hái, "still; yet") adds the idea of continuation (originally "to return/again") to 是 (shì, "to be; indeed"), yielding the sense "still is/indeed is"—i.e., a state persists despite expectation.
- **examples:**
  - 他还是没来(他還是沒來)。
    - Tā háishi méi lái.
    - He still hasn’t come.
