## 还是(還是)
---
- **pinyin:** háishi
- **definition:** still; yet
- **characters:**
  - 还(還)
    - hái
    - still
  - 是
    - shì
    - to be
- **etymology:** Combines 还(還) (hái, "still") with 是 (shì, "to be") to express still; yet.
- **examples:**
  - 他还是不明白这个道理(他還是不明白這個道理)。
    - tā háishi bù míngbai zhège dàolǐ
    - He still doesn't understand this principle.
  - 她很累，还是继续工作(還是繼續工作)。
    - tā hěn lèi, háishi jìxù gōngzuò
    - She was very tired, yet she kept working.
