## 喇叭花
---
- **pinyin:** lǎbahuā
- **definition:** morning glory
- **characters:**
  - 喇
    - lǎ
    - horn, trumpet (as in 喇叭); lama (Tibetan Buddhist monk)
  - 叭
    - bā
    - pop, bang (onomatopoeia)
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower, blossom; to spend (money, time); multicolored, variegated; blurred, out of focus
- **etymology:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn, trumpet") with 叭 (bā, "onomatopoeic blast") together denote a trumpet, and 花 (huā, "flower") labels the blossom whose flared, bell-like shape recalls a trumpet—hence a "trumpet flower" for the morning glory.
- **examples:**
  - 篱笆上开满了紫色的喇叭花(籬笆上開滿了紫色的喇叭花)。
    - Líba shàng kāi mǎn le zǐsè de lǎbahuā.
    - Purple morning glories are blooming all over the fence.
