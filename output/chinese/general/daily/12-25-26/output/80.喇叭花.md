## 喇叭花
---
- **pinyin:** lǎbahuā
- **definition:** morning glory
- **characters:**
  - 喇
    - lǎ
    - trumpet
  - 叭
    - bā
    - trumpet
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
- **etymology:** Combines 喇 (lǎ, "trumpet"), 叭 (bā, "trumpet"), and 花 (huā, "flower") to convey morning glory.
- **examples:**
  - 篱笆上爬满了喇叭花(籬笆上爬滿了喇叭花)。
    - lí ba shàng pá mǎn le lǎ ba huā
    - The fence is covered with morning glories.
  - 喇叭花在清晨绽放(喇叭花在清晨綻放)。
    - lǎ ba huā zài qīng chén zhàn fàng
    - Morning glories bloom in the early morning.
