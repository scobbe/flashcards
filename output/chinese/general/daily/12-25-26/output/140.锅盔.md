## 锅盔(鍋盔)
---
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **characters:**
  - 锅(鍋)
    - guō
    - pot; pan; wok; boiler
  - 盔
    - kuī
    - helmet
- **etymology:** 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot/wok") points to the cooking vessel or method (baked on a pot). 盔 (kuī, "helmet") evokes the bread’s round, domed, helmet-like shape—hence a pot-baked, helmet-shaped flatbread.
- **examples:**
  - 早上在街边摊(早上在街邊攤)，我买了一个热腾腾的锅盔(我買了一個熱騰騰的鍋盔)，外酥里软(外酥裡軟)，特别香(特別香)。
    - Zǎoshang zài jiēbiān tān, wǒ mǎi le yí gè rèténgténg de guōkuī, wài sū lǐ ruǎn, tèbié xiāng.
    - At a morning street stall, I bought a piping-hot guokui flatbread; it was crispy outside, soft inside, and smelled amazing.
  - 这家店的锅盔可以夹牛肉和泡菜(這家店的鍋盔可以夾牛肉和泡菜)，做成一份管饱的午餐(做成一份管飽的午餐)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de guōkuī kěyǐ jiā niúròu hé pàocài, zuò chéng yí fèn guǎn bǎo de wǔcān.
    - At this shop, the guokui flatbread can be stuffed with beef and pickled vegetables, making a filling lunch.
