## 锅盔(鍋盔)
---
- **pinyin:** guōkuī
- **definition:** round baked flatbread
- **characters:**
  - 锅(鍋): guō, pot
  - 盔: kuī, helmet
- **examples:**
  - 这家店的锅盔外酥里软，排队的人很多(這家店的鍋盔外酥裡軟，排隊的人很多) (zhè jiā diàn de guōkuī wài sū lǐ ruǎn, páiduì de rén hěn duō, "The shop's guokui flatbread is crispy outside and soft inside, and many people line up for it")
