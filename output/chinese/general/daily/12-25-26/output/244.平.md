## 平
---
- **pinyin:** Píng
- **definition:** surname Ping
- **etymology:** A character meaning surname ping.
- **examples:**
  - 平先生已经到了(平先生已經到了)。
    - píng xiānsheng yǐjīng dào le
    - Mr. Ping has already arrived
  - 她姓平，大家都叫她平老师(大家都叫她平老師)。
    - tā xìng píng, dàjiā dōu jiào tā píng lǎoshī
    - Her surname is Ping; everyone calls her Teacher Ping.
