## 的
---
- **pinyin:** de
- **definition:** possessive/attributive particle
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left 白 (bái, "white") evokes a white mark/spot, while right 勺 (sháo, "spoon") supplies the sound; originally referred to a marked target or bull’s-eye. In modern vernacular it grammaticalized into a possessive/attributive particle, largely supplanting 之 (zhī, "of").
- **examples:**
  - 这是我妹妹的红色的书(這是我妹妹的紅色的書)，不是他的(不是他的)。
    - Zhè shì wǒ mèimei de hóngsè de shū, bú shì tā de.
    - This is my younger sister’s red book, not his.
