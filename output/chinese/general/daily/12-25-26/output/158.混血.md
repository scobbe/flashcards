## 混血
---
- **pinyin:** hùnxuè
- **definition:** mixed blood
- **characters:**
  - 混
    - hùn, hún
    - to mix, blend; to confuse, muddle; to muddle along, get by; turbid, muddy
  - 血
    - xuè, xiě
    - blood; bloodline, lineage; bloodshed, gore; vitality, vigor
- **etymology:** 混 (hùn, "mix") signals blending, and 血 (xuè, "blood") stands for bloodline, so together denote blended bloodlines—i.e., mixed ancestry.
- **examples:**
  - 她是个混血儿(她是個混血兒)，长相很有特色(長相很有特色)。
    - Tā shì gè hùn xuè ér, zhǎngxiàng hěn yǒu tèsè.
    - She’s mixed-race, and her looks are very distinctive.
  - 他自称是日美混血(他自稱是日美混血)，能说两种语言(能說兩種語言)。
    - Tā zìchēng shì Rì Měi hùn xuè, néng shuō liǎng zhǒng yǔyán.
    - He says he’s Japanese-American mixed and can speak two languages.
