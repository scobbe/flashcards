## 阿
---
- **pinyin:** ā
- **definition:** prefix/particle used in names or address
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 阜 (fù, "mound, hill") supplies the semantic idea of a place/edge, while 可 (kě, "can") serves as the phonetic cue. The original sense was "by/near a hill; beside," which in vernacular usage extended to the familiar address prefix ā-, hence 阿 (ā, "prefix/particle used in names or address").
- **examples:**
  - 阿明(阿明)，过来一下(過來一下)！
    - Ā Míng, guòlái yíxià!
    - A-Ming, come here for a moment!
  - 大家都叫他阿伟(大家都叫他阿偉)。
    - Dàjiā dōu jiào tā Ā Wěi.
    - Everyone calls him A-Wei.
