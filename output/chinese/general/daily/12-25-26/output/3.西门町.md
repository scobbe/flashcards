## 西门町
---
- **pinyin:** Xīmén Dīng
- **definition:** Ximending, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 西
    - xī
    - west; the West, Occident; surname Xi
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door, gate; family, school, sect; classifier for academic subjects, skills; valve
  - 町
    - dīng
    - ward, town block, neighborhood; street, lane
- **etymology:** 西 (xī, "west") with 门(門) (mén, "gate") locates the area by the city's former west gate, while 町 (dīng, "ward/town block")—a Japanese-era ward term—marks it as an urban district, i.e., the West Gate district of Taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 西门町是台北著名的购物区(西門町是臺北著名的購物區)。
    - Xīmén Dīng shì Táiběi zhùmíng de gòuwù qū.
    - Ximending is a famous shopping district in Taipei.
