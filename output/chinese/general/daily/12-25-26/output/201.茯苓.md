## 茯苓
---
- **pinyin:** fúlíng
- **definition:** poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
- **characters:**
  - 茯: fú, poria
  - 苓: líng, fungus
- **examples:**
  - 中药里的茯苓有利水渗湿、健脾安神的作用(中藥裡的茯苓有利水滲濕、健脾安神的作用) (zhōngyào lǐ de fúlíng yǒu lìshuǐ shènshī, jiànpí ānshén de zuòyòng, "In Chinese medicine, poria has the functions of promoting urination and draining dampness, strengthening the spleen, and calming the mind")
  - 我用茯苓、红枣和莲子煮了一锅养生汤(我用茯苓、紅棗和蓮子煮了一鍋養生湯) (wǒ yòng fúlíng, hóngzǎo hé liánzi zhǔ le yì guō yǎngshēng tāng, "I cooked a pot of health soup with tuckahoe, red dates, and lotus seeds")
