## 茯苓
---
- **pinyin:** fúlíng
- **definition:** poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
- **characters:**
  - 茯
    - fú
    - poria, Poria cocos, tuckahoe
  - 苓
    - líng
    - poria, Poria cocos, tuckahoe; fungus, mushroom; medicinal herb
- **etymology:** Pairs 茯 (fú, "poria") with 苓 (líng, "poria; fungus/mushroom; medicinal herb"), both marked as herbal terms, to designate the poria fungus; the second element emphasizes its mushroom/herbal nature and the disyllabic pairing fixes the established pharmacological name.
- **examples:**
  - 这味汤药里放了茯苓(這味湯藥裡放了茯苓)，有助于利水消肿(有助於利水消腫)。
    - Zhè wèi tāngyào lǐ fàng le fúlíng, yǒu zhù yú lìshuǐ xiāozhǒng.
    - This medicinal soup includes poria (tuckahoe), which helps promote urination and reduce swelling.
  - 她每天喝含茯苓的代茶饮(她每天喝含茯苓的代茶飲)，想调理脾胃(想調理脾胃)。
    - Tā měitiān hē hán fúlíng de dàicháyǐn, xiǎng tiáolǐ píwèi.
    - She drinks a poria-infused herbal tea every day to regulate her spleen and stomach.
