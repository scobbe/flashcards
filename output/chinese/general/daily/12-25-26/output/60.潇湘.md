## 潇湘(瀟湘)
---
- **pinyin:** Xiāoxiāng
- **definition:** poetic name for Xiang River region
- **characters:**
  - 潇(瀟)
    - xiāo
    - elegant
  - 湘
    - xiāng
    - Xiang River
- **examples:**
  - “潇湘”是对湘江流域的雅称(「瀟湘」是對湘江流域的雅稱)。
    - Xiāoxiāng shì duì Xiāngjiāng liúyù de yǎchēng
    - Xiaoxiang is a poetic name for the Xiang River region.
