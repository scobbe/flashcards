## 潇湘(瀟湘)
---
- **pinyin:** Xiāoxiāng
- **definition:** poetic name for Xiang River region
- **characters:**
  - 潇(瀟)
    - xiāo
    - elegant, free and easy; soughing, pattering (of wind or rain); deep and clear (of water); Xiao River (tributary of the Xiang)
  - 湘
    - xiāng
    - Xiang River (in Hunan); Hunan (abbreviation); relating to Hunan, Hunanese
- **etymology:** Combines 潇(瀟) (xiāo, "Xiao River; deep/clear, soughing elegance") with 湘 (xiāng, "Xiang River; Hunan") to denote the Xiao–Xiang river region; the shared water and elegant connotations elevate the toponym into a refined, poetic epithet.
- **examples:**
  - 诗人远眺潇湘(詩人遠眺瀟湘)，感叹水云相接的苍茫(感嘆水雲相接的蒼茫)。
    - Shīrén yuǎn tiào Xiāoxiāng, gǎntàn shuǐ yún xiāng jiē de cāngmáng.
    - The poet gazed toward Xiao‑Xiang—the poetic name for the Xiang River region—and marveled at the vast meeting of water and clouds.
  - 我一直想去潇湘(我一直想去瀟湘)，看看传说中的“潇湘八景”(看看傳說中的「瀟湘八景」)。
    - Wǒ yīzhí xiǎng qù Xiāoxiāng, kànkan chuánshuō zhōng de Xiāoxiāng Bājǐng.
    - I’ve always wanted to visit Xiao‑Xiang—the poetic name for the Xiang River region—to see the legendary Eight Views of Xiao‑Xiang.
