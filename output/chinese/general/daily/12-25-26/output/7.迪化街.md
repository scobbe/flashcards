## 迪化街
---
- **pinyin:** Díhuà Jiē
- **definition:** Dihua Street, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 迪
    - dí
    - enlighten
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform
  - 街
    - jiē
    - street
- **examples:**
  - 迪化街位于台北大同区(迪化街位於臺北大同區)，以南北货和布行闻名(以南北貨和布行聞名)。
    - Díhuà Jiē wèiyú Táiběi Dàtóng Qū, yǐ nánběi huò hé bùháng wénmíng
    - Dihua Street is located in Taipei's Datong District and is famous for traditional dried goods and fabric shops.
