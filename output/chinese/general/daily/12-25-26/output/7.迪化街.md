## 迪化街
---
- **pinyin:** Díhuà Jiē
- **definition:** Dihua Street, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 迪
    - dí
    - guide, lead; enlighten, inspire; advance, progress; follow, comply
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform, change; convert; dissolve, melt; -ization suffix
  - 街
    - jiē
    - street, road
- **etymology:** 迪 (dí, "guide; enlighten") with 化 (huà, "transform") form a classical binome for "enlighten-and-transform" (also used as a toponym), and 街 (jiē, "street") marks it as a street named Dihua.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算明天去台北的迪化街逛年货大街(我打算明天去臺北的迪化街逛年貨大街)，买些干货和茶叶(買些乾貨和茶葉)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn míngtiān qù Táiběi de Díhuà Jiē guàng niánhuò dàjiē, mǎi xiē gānhuò hé cháyè.
    - I’m planning to go to Dihua Street in Taipei tomorrow to browse the Lunar New Year market and buy some dried goods and tea.
  - 迪化街的老建筑很有味道(迪化街的老建築很有味道)，我每次来台北都会去那儿走走(我每次來臺北都會去那兒走走)。
    - Díhuà Jiē de lǎo jiànzhù hěn yǒu wèidào, wǒ měi cì lái Táiběi dōu huì qù nàr zǒu zǒu.
    - The old buildings on Dihua Street have great character; whenever I come to Taipei, I always go there for a stroll.
