## 豆豉
---
- **pinyin:** dòuchǐ
- **definition:** fermented black beans
- **characters:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 豉
    - chǐ
    - fermented black beans
- **etymology:** Combines 豆 (dòu, "bean") with 豉 (chǐ, "fermented black beans") to express fermented black beans.
- **examples:**
  - 用豆豉蒸排骨最能带出咸香(用豆豉蒸排骨最能帶出鹹香)。
    - yòng dòuchǐ zhēng páigǔ zuì néng dài chū xiánxiāng
    - Steaming ribs with fermented black beans brings out the savory aroma best.
