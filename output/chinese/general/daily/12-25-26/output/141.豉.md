## 豉
---
- **pinyin:** chǐ
- **definition:** fermented soybeans
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left 豆 (dòu, "bean") pictographs a legume/bean vessel and supplies the meaning; right 旨 (zhǐ, "delicious") provides the sound (≈ chǐ) and also hints at the savory taste from fermentation. The visual pairing of “bean” + “delicious” succinctly encodes “fermented soybeans.”
- **examples:**
  - 豉是用黄豆发酵制成的调味料(豉是用黃豆發酵製成的調味料)。
    - Chǐ shì yòng huángdòu fājiào zhìchéng de tiáowèiliào.
    - Chǐ is fermented soybeans used as a seasoning.
  - 我们用豉蒸排骨(我們用豉蒸排骨)，味道很香(味道很香)。
    - Wǒmen yòng chǐ zhēng páigǔ, wèidào hěn xiāng.
    - We steam the ribs with fermented soybeans; it smells wonderful.
