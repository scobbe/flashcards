## 颜(顏)
---
- **pinyin:** Yán
- **definition:** surname Yan
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 彦(彥) (yàn, "accomplished; talented") provides the sound, while 页(頁) (yè, "head/face"), a stylized head, supplies the meaning of "face/appearance". Originally meant "face; complexion," later also used as the surname Yan.
