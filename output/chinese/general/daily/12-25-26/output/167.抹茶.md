## 抹茶
---
- **pinyin:** mǒchá
- **definition:** matcha
- **characters:**
  - 抹
    - mǒ
    - wipe
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea
- **examples:**
  - 抹茶很好喝(抹茶很好喝)。
    - mǒchá hěn hǎohē
    - Matcha tastes great
  - 我昨天买了一杯抹茶拿铁(我昨天買了一杯抹茶拿鐵)。
    - wǒ zuótiān mǎi le yì bēi mǒchá nátiě
    - I bought a cup of matcha latte yesterday.
