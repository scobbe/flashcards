## 抹茶
---
- **pinyin:** mǒchá
- **definition:** matcha
- **characters:**
  - 抹
    - mǒ, mò, mā
    - wipe, wipe off; smear, spread, apply; erase, obliterate; cross out, strike out
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea; tea plant; tea leaves; tea-colored, brownish
- **etymology:** 抹 (mǒ, "to rub/smear") signals grinding leaves to powder and whisking it into suspension, while 茶 (chá, "tea") names the beverage—together denoting powdered, whisked tea.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢抹茶(我喜歡抹茶)，但她更爱咖啡(但她更愛咖啡)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan mǒchá, dàn tā gèng ài kāfēi.
    - I like matcha, but she prefers coffee.
  - 这家店的抹茶拿铁很香浓(這家店的抹茶拿鐵很香濃)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de mǒchá nátiě hěn xiāngnóng.
    - The matcha latte at this shop is rich and aromatic.
