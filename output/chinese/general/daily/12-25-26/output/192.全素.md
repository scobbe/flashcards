## 全素
---
- **pinyin:** quánsù
- **definition:** vegan
- **characters:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - whole, entire; all; complete, perfect; to preserve, to keep intact
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain, simple; vegetarian; white, uncolored; element, basic
- **etymology:** 全 (quán, "whole/entire") quantifies 素 (sù, "vegetarian/plain") to mean "entirely vegetarian," extending the idea of being fully plant-based to the modern sense of vegan (no animal products).
- **examples:**
  - 我吃全素(我吃全素)，不喝牛奶也不吃鸡蛋(不喝牛奶也不吃雞蛋)。
    - Wǒ chī quánsù, bù hē niúnǎi yě bù chī jīdàn.
    - I’m vegan—I don’t drink milk or eat eggs.
  - 这家餐厅全素(這家餐廳全素)，所有菜都不含任何动物性成分(所有菜都不含任何動物性成分)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng quánsù, suǒyǒu cài dōu bù hán rènhé dòngwùxìng chéngfèn.
    - This restaurant is fully vegan; none of the dishes contain any animal ingredients.
