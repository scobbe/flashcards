## umbrella
### yǔsǎn
---
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain; rainfall; to rain; rainy
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella; parasol; parachute (abbr.); umbrella-shaped
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 雨 (rain) + 伞 (umbrella/parasol) = umbrella for rain.
  - **interpretation:** Names a canopy used to keep off rain. By specifying 雨, distinguishes it from a sunshade (阳伞).
  - **simplification:** 傘 was simplified to 伞 by reducing strokes to an umbrella-shaped outline; 雨 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我带雨伞(我帶雨傘)。
    - wǒ dài yǔsǎn.
    - I’m bringing an umbrella.
  - 今天下雨(今天下雨)，记得带雨伞(記得帶雨傘)。
    - jīntiān xià yǔ, jìde dài yǔsǎn.
    - It’s raining today; remember to bring an umbrella.
  - 这把雨伞很轻(這把雨傘很輕)。
    - zhè bǎ yǔsǎn hěn qīng.
    - This umbrella is very light.
### 雨伞 → 雨
- **definition:** rain; rainfall; to rain; rainy
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a cloud with drops of rain; the top horizontal stroke was added later.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a rain cloud shedding droplets, making the meaning immediately transparent. In its regular-script form, the four dots suggest raindrops, while the added top stroke stabilizes and balances the overall shape.
- **examples:**
  - 今天下雨了(今天下雨了。)
    - Jīntiān xià yǔ le.
    - It’s raining today.
  - 雨停了(雨停了)，地上还是湿的(地上還是濕的。)
    - Yǔ tíng le, dìshang háishi shī de.
    - The rain has stopped, but the ground is still wet.
  - 明天有大雨(明天有大雨)，记得带伞(記得帶傘。)
    - Míngtiān yǒu dàyǔ, jìde dài sǎn.
    - There will be heavy rain tomorrow; remember to bring an umbrella.
### 雨伞 → 伞(傘)
- **definition:** umbrella; parasol; parachute (abbr.); umbrella-shaped
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 八
    - bā
    - eight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: pictographic representation of an umbrella; simplified from 傘 (𠈌 → 丷).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an umbrella canopy with a central handle, originally with figures under it. The simplified form streamlines the under-canopy element into two short strokes while retaining the overall umbrella silhouette.
  - **simplification:** Stroke reduction by replacing the under-canopy component 𠈌 with the simpler 丷, preserving the canopy/handle outline.
- **examples:**
  - 我带伞(我帶傘)。
    - Wǒ dài sǎn.
    - I’m bringing an umbrella.
  - 下雨了(下雨了)，别忘了打伞(別忘了打傘)。
    - Xià yǔ le, bié wàng le dǎ sǎn.
    - It’s raining; don’t forget to use an umbrella.
  - 伞兵从天空降落(傘兵從天空降落)。
    - Sǎnbīng cóng tiānkōng jiàngluò.
    - Paratroopers descended from the sky.
### 雨伞 → 伞(傘) → 人
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg; left-side form 亻.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a person in profile, with one stroke suggesting the torso/arm and the other the leg, hence the slightly splayed shape. In compounds it often appears as the left-hand form 亻 to mark human-related meaning; not to be confused with 彳.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个很好的人(他是一個很好的人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè hěn hǎo de rén.
    - He is a very good person.
  - 我认识很多人(我認識很多人)。
    - Wǒ rènshi hěn duō rén.
    - I know many people.
  - 这里有三个人(這裡有三個人)。
    - Zhèlǐ yǒu sān gè rén.
    - There are three people here.
### 雨伞 → 伞(傘) → 八
- **definition:** eight
- **pinyin:** bā
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: two bent lines indicating “to divide”; later borrowed to mean “eight” via homophony (original sense now in 分, 別).
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two strokes diverging to suggest splitting or separation. Later became a rebus for the number eight because it shared the same sound, while the original sense lived on in 分 and 別. Not the “two hands” component found in other characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我有八本书(我有八本書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu bā běn shū.
    - I have eight books.
  - 她八岁了(她八歲了)。
    - Tā bā suì le.
    - She is eight years old.
  - 我买了八个苹果(我買了八個蘋果)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le bā gè píngguǒ.
    - I bought eight apples.
