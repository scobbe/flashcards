## 雨伞
---
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **definition:** umbrella
- **characters:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain; to rain
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella, parasol; parachute
- **etymology:** Determinative compound: 雨 (yǔ, "rain") modifies 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") to specify an umbrella for rain.
- **examples:**
  - 下雨了(下雨了)，别忘了带雨伞(別忘了帶雨傘)。
    - Xià yǔ le, bié wàng le dài yǔsǎn.
    - It’s raining—don’t forget to bring an umbrella.
  - 我的雨伞被风吹坏了(我的雨傘被風吹壞了)。
    - Wǒ de yǔsǎn bèi fēng chuī huài le.
    - My umbrella was damaged by the wind.
