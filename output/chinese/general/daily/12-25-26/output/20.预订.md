## 预订
---
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **definition:** reserve; book
- **characters:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance, beforehand; to prepare; to anticipate, expect; preliminary, prior
  - 订(訂)
    - dìng
    - to order, book, subscribe, reserve; to conclude, draw up (an agreement); to set, fix, determine; to revise, correct (text)
- **etymology:** Combines 预(預) (yù, "in advance, beforehand") with 订(訂) (dìng, "to set/fix; to order"), conveying the idea of fixing or placing an order ahead of time—hence "to reserve; book."
- **examples:**
  - 我想预订今晚六点的两人餐桌(我想預訂今晚六點的兩人餐桌)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yùdìng jīn wǎn liù diǎn de liǎng rén cānzhuō.
    - I'd like to reserve a table for two at 6 p.m. tonight.
  - 我们已经在网上预订了机票和酒店(我們已經在網上預訂了機票和酒店)。
    - Wǒmen yǐjīng zài wǎngshàng yùdìng le jīpiào hé jiǔdiàn.
    - We’ve already booked the flights and the hotel online.
