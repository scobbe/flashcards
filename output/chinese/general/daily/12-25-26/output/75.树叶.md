## 树叶(樹葉)
---
- **pinyin:** shùyè
- **definition:** leaves
- **characters:**
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree; to plant; to set up, establish
  - 叶(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf, foliage; blade, vane (of a propeller or fan)
- **etymology:** 树(樹) (shù, "tree") modifies 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf"), forming a straightforward modifier-head compound meaning the leaves of a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天的树叶变成了金黄色(秋天的樹葉變成了金黃色)。
    - Qiūtiān de shùyè biànchéng le jīn huángsè.
    - In autumn, the leaves turn golden.
  - 风一吹(風一吹)，树叶就落下来(樹葉就落下來)。
    - Fēng yī chuī, shùyè jiù luò xiàlái.
    - When the wind blows, the leaves fall down.
