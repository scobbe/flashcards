## 树叶(樹葉)
---
- **pinyin:** shùyè
- **definition:** leaves
- **characters:**
  - 树(樹)
    - shù
    - tree
  - 叶(葉)
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:** Combines 树(樹) (shù, "tree") with 叶(葉) (yè, "leaf") to express leaves.
- **examples:**
  - 秋天，树叶变黄了(樹葉變黃了)。
    - qiūtiān, shùyè biàn huáng le
    - In autumn, the leaves turn yellow.
