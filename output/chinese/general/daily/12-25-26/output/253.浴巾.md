## 浴巾
---
- **pinyin:** yùjīn
- **definition:** bath towel
- **characters:**
  - 浴
    - yù
    - bathe
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - towel
- **examples:**
  - 我洗完澡用柔软的浴巾擦干身体(我洗完澡用柔軟的浴巾擦乾身體)。
    - wǒ xǐ wán zǎo yòng róuruǎn de yùjīn cā gān shēntǐ
    - After showering, I dry my body with a soft bath towel.
