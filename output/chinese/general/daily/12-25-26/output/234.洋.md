## 洋
---
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **definition:** ocean; foreign
- **etymology:** A character meaning ocean; foreign.
- **examples:**
  - 我们在海洋上航行了三天(我們在海洋上航行了三天)。
    - wǒmen zài hǎiyáng shàng hángxíng le sān tiān
    - We sailed on the ocean for three days.
  - 她喜欢买洋货(她喜歡買洋貨)。
    - tā xǐhuan mǎi yánghuò
    - She likes to buy foreign goods.
