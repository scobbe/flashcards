## 信义区
---
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - believe, trust; letter, mail; message, information; honest, faithful
  - 义(義)
    - yì
    - righteousness, justice; meaning, sense; duty, obligation; adoptive
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district; area, region; distinguish, separate
- **etymology:** 信 (xìn, "trust, faith") with 义(義) (yì, "righteousness") evokes Confucian virtues, and adding 区(區) (qū, "district") marks it as an administrative area—hence a toponym meaning a district embodying trust and righteousness.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢在信义区逛街(我最喜歡在信義區逛街)，台北101就在附近(臺北101就在附近)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuan zài Xìnyì Qū guàngjiē, Táiběi yī líng yī jiù zài fùjìn.
    - I love shopping in Xinyi District; Taipei 101 is right nearby.
