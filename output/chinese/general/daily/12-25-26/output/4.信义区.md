## 信义区
---
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 信: xìn, letter
  - 义(義): yì, meaning
  - 区(區): qū, district
- **examples:**
  - 信义区是台北的商业中心之一(信義區是臺北的商業中心之一) (Xìnyì Qū shì Táiběi de shāngyè zhōngxīn zhī yī, "Xinyi District is one of Taipei's commercial centers")
