## 糖葫芦(糖葫蘆)
---
- **pinyin:** tánghúlu
- **definition:** candied hawthorn
- **characters:**
  - 糖
    - táng
    - sugar; candy, sweets
  - 葫
    - hú
    - gourd, calabash
  - 芦(蘆)
    - lú
    - reed
- **etymology:** 糖 (táng, "sugar") marks the sugary coating, while 葫 (hú, "gourd") + 芦(蘆) (lú, "reed") combine as a disyllable meaning gourd, metaphorically likening the bulging string of hawthorn fruits on a skewer to a gourd shape.
- **examples:**
  - 北京的冬天很冷(北京的冬天很冷)，一串糖葫芦特别解馋(一串糖葫蘆特別解饞)。
    - Běijīng de dōngtiān hěn lěng, yí chuàn tánghúlu tèbié jiěchán.
    - Winters in Beijing are very cold; a skewer of candied hawthorn really hits the spot.
