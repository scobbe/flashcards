## 糖葫芦(糖葫蘆)
---
- **pinyin:** tánghúlu
- **definition:** candied hawthorn
- **characters:**
  - 糖
    - táng
    - sugar
  - 葫
    - hú
    - gourd
  - 芦(蘆)
    - lú
    - reed
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一串糖葫芦(我買了一串糖葫蘆)，边走边吃(邊走邊吃)。
    - wǒ mǎi le yí chuàn tánghúlu, biān zǒu biān chī
    - I bought a skewer of candied hawthorn and ate it as I walked.
