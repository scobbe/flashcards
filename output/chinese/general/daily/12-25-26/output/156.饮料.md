## 饮料(飲料)
---
- **pinyin:** yǐnliào
- **definition:** beverage
- **characters:**
  - 饮(飲)
    - yǐn
    - drink
  - 料
    - liào
    - material
- **etymology:** Combines 饮(飲) (yǐn, "drink") with 料 (liào, "material") to express beverage.
- **examples:**
  - 你想喝什么饮料？(你想喝什麼飲料？)。
    - nǐ xiǎng hē shénme yǐnliào
    - What beverage would you like to drink?
