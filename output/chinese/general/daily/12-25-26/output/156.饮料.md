## 饮料(飲料)
---
- **pinyin:** yǐnliào
- **definition:** beverage
- **characters:**
  - 饮(飲)
    - yǐn
    - to drink; beverage, drink; to take (medicine)
  - 料
    - liào
    - material, stuff; ingredient; feed, fodder; to expect, anticipate
- **etymology:** Combines 饮(飲) (yǐn, "to drink") with 料 (liào, "material; ingredient") to mean 'stuff for drinking,' i.e., a prepared liquid for consumption; 料's sense of 'feed/ingredients' underscores it as consumable material.
- **examples:**
  - 冰箱里有很多饮料(冰箱裡有很多飲料)，你随便拿(你隨便拿)。
    - Bīngxiāng lǐ yǒu hěn duō yǐnliào, nǐ suíbiàn ná.
    - There are plenty of beverages in the fridge; help yourself.
