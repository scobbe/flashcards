## 屌丝(屌絲)
---
- **pinyin:** diǎosī
- **definition:** loser (internet slang)
- **characters:**
  - 屌
    - diǎo
    - penis
  - 丝(絲)
    - sī
    - silk
- **etymology:** Combines 屌 (diǎo, "penis") with 丝(絲) (sī, "silk") to express loser (internet slang).
- **examples:**
  - 他总觉得自己是个屌丝(他總覺得自己是個屌絲)，但还是努力改变现状(但還是努力改變現狀)。
    - tā zǒng juéde zìjǐ shì gè diǎosī, dàn háishi nǔlì gǎibiàn xiànzhuàng
    - He always feels like a loser, but still works hard to change his situation.
