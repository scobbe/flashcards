## 定
---
- **pinyin:** dìng
- **definition:** decide; fix
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: the roof radical (宀) conveys shelter/settling under a house, while 正 (zhèng, "upright; correct") supplies the sound and the sense of setting things straight. Visually, a roof over 正 suggests making affairs stable and orderly, hence "decide; fix."
- **examples:**
  - 晚饭吃什么(晚飯吃什麼)，你来定(你來定)。
    - Wǎnfàn chī shénme, nǐ lái dìng.
    - You decide what to eat for dinner.
  - 商家把价格定得太高了(商家把價格定得太高了)。
    - Shāngjiā bǎ jiàgé dìng de tài gāo le.
    - The seller set the price too high.
