## 定
---
- **pinyin:** dìng
- **definition:** decide; fix
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 宀 (mián, "roof") + phonetic 正 Etymology: Possibly related to Burmese တည် (tany , " to lie in repose; to build, form, establish").
- **examples:**
  - 这件事就这么定了！(這件事就這麼定了！)。
    - zhè jiàn shì jiù zhème dìng le
    - Then it's settled!
  - 会议时间定在周五下午两点。(會議時間定在週五下午兩點。)。
    - huì yì shí jiān dìng zài zhōu wǔ xià wǔ liǎng diǎn
    - The meeting time is fixed for Friday at 2 p.m.
