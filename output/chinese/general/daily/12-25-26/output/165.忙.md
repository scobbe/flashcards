## 忙
---
- **pinyin:** máng
- **definition:** busy
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left side 心 (xīn, "heart") supplies the semantic field of feelings/mental activity, while right side 亡 (wáng, "to lose; perish") serves as the phonetic. The graphic suggests a heart preoccupied, matching the sense of "busy".
- **examples:**
  - 我最近很忙(我最近很忙)，没空出去玩(沒空出去玩)。
    - Wǒ zuìjìn hěn máng, méi kòng chūqù wán.
    - I've been very busy lately and don't have time to hang out.
