## 一下儿(一下兒)
---
- **pinyin:** yíxiàr
- **definition:** a bit; for a moment
- **characters:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one; a, an; single; once
  - 下
    - xià
    - down; below, under; to descend, go down, get off; next, following
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - child, son; noun suffix, erhua, retroflex 'r'
- **etymology:** 一 (yī, "one/once") plus 下 (xià, "down; descend") conveys a single, slight downward action—metaphorically, doing something briefly or just a bit; 儿(兒) (ér, "erhua suffix") adds Beijing-style rhotacization and a colloquial tone without changing the core meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 让我想一下儿(讓我想一下兒)。
    - Ràng wǒ xiǎng yíxiàr.
    - Let me think a bit.
  - 你能等我一下儿吗(你能等我一下兒嗎)？
    - Nǐ néng děng wǒ yíxiàr ma?
    - Could you wait for me for a moment?
