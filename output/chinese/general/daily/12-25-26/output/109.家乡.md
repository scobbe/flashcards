## 家乡(家鄉)
---
- **pinyin:** jiāxiāng
- **definition:** hometown
- **characters:**
  - 家
    - jiā
    - home; family, household; specialist, expert, -ist; measure word for families, businesses, shops
  - 乡(鄉)
    - xiāng
    - countryside; hometown, native place; village; township (administrative division)
- **etymology:** 家 (jiā, "home") anchors familial belonging, while 乡(鄉) (xiāng, "native place; countryside") denotes the place of origin; together they mean the family's native place—one's hometown.
- **examples:**
  - 每逢春节(每逢春節)，我都会回家乡探亲(我都會回家鄉探親)。
    - Měi féng Chūnjié, wǒ dōu huì huí jiāxiāng tànqīn.
    - Every Spring Festival, I go back to my hometown to visit relatives.
