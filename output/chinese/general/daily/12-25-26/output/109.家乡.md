## 家乡(家鄉)
---
- **pinyin:** jiāxiāng
- **definition:** hometown
- **characters:**
  - 家
    - jiā
    - home
  - 乡(鄉)
    - xiāng
    - countryside
- **etymology:** 家 (jiā, "home") evokes the ancestral household—originally a pig under a roof—while 乡(鄉) (xiāng, "countryside") names the native township/community; together they point to the place where one’s lineage is rooted, the family’s village. In an agrarian, clan-based society, identity was anchored in one’s native 乡(鄉), so 家乡 naturally denotes the native place defined by family origin rather than mere residence.
- **examples:**
  - 我很想念我的家乡(我很想念我的家鄉)。
    - wǒ hěn xiǎngniàn wǒ de jiāxiāng
    - I really miss my hometown.
