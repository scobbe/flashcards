## 叫
---
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **definition:** call; shout
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") supplies the speech/vocalization meaning, while 丩 (jiū, "to entangle") serves as the phonetic, yielding the jiào reading. The left-placed 口 iconically signals a mouth producing sound, fitting the sense of calling/shouting.
- **examples:**
  - 我叫李华(我叫李華)。
    - Wǒ jiào Lǐ Huá.
    - My name is Li Hua.
  - 他对着山谷大声叫(他對著山谷大聲叫)，回声传了回来(回聲傳了回來)。
    - Tā duìzhe shāngǔ dàshēng jiào, huíshēng chuán le huílái.
    - He shouted loudly into the valley, and the echo came back.
