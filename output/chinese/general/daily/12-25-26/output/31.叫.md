## 叫
---
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **definition:** call; shout
- **etymology:** Contains 口 (kǒu, "mouth") indicating calling or shouting.
- **examples:**
  - 我叫他过来帮忙(我叫他過來幫忙)。
    - wǒ jiào tā guòlai bāngmáng
    - I called him over to help.
  - 她突然大声叫了一下(她突然大聲叫了一下)。
    - tā tūrán dàshēng jiào le yíxià
    - She suddenly shouted
