## call; shout
### jiào
---
- **definition:** call; shout
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 丩
    - jiū
    - to twist; to join; entangle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 口 (mouth) + phonetic: 丩; original meaning: "to shout" (OC *kiːws).
  - **interpretation:** Mouth signals a vocal action, while 丩 contributes the sound, anchoring the reading. The graph first denoted shouting and later broadened to calling out or summoning someone.
- **examples:**
  - 小孩大声叫(小孩大聲叫)。
    - Xiǎohái dàshēng jiào.
    - The child shouted loudly.
  - 她叫我的名字(她叫我的名字)。
    - Tā jiào wǒ de míngzi.
    - She called my name.
  - 我叫他快来(我叫他快來)。
    - Wǒ jiào tā kuài lái.
    - I called him to come quickly.
### 叫 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – resembles an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** A simple square outlines the opening of a mouth, with the empty center representing the oral cavity. It later became a common semantic component indicating speech, sounds, or openings, and in early compounds sometimes marked abstract meanings via association or borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 他张开口(他張開口)，说了几句(說了幾句)。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, shuō le jǐ jù.
    - He opened his mouth and said a few sentences.
  - 一家三口住在这个小城(一家三口住在這個小城)。
    - Yì jiā sān kǒu zhù zài zhège xiǎo chéng.
    - A family of three lives in this small town.
  - 她咬了一口苹果(她咬了一口蘋果)。
    - Tā yǎo le yì kǒu píngguǒ.
    - She took a bite of the apple.
### 叫 → 丩
- **definition:** to twist; to join; entangle
- **pinyin:** jiū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: two entangled ropes; original form of 糾
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two ropes twisting together, directly expressing the idea of entangling or joining. Later script forms standardized this meaning in 糾 (now simplified as 纠), while 丩 preserves an archaic pictorial shape reminiscent of a twist.
- **examples:**
  - 两条绳子丩成一股(兩條繩子丩成一股)。
    - Liǎng tiáo shéngzi jiū chéng yī gǔ.
    - Two ropes twist into a single strand.
  - 藤蔓丩住树干(藤蔓丩住樹幹)。
    - Téngmàn jiū zhù shùgàn.
    - Vines entangle the tree trunk.
  - 把两端丩在一起(把兩端丩在一起)。
    - Bǎ liǎng duān jiū zài yīqǐ.
    - Twist the two ends together.
