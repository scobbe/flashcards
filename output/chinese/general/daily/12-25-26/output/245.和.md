## 和
---
- **pinyin:** hé
- **definition:** gentle; harmonious; and
- **examples:**
  - 他脾气很和(他脾氣很和) (tā píqi hěn hé, "He has a gentle temper")
  - 他们相处得很和(他們相處得很和) (tāmen xiāngchǔ de hěn hé, "They get along harmoniously")
  - 我和他都喜欢音乐(我和他都喜歡音樂) (wǒ hé tā dōu xǐhuān yīnyuè, "He and I both like music")
