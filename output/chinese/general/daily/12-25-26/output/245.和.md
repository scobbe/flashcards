## 和
---
- **pinyin:** hé
- **definition:** gentle; harmonious; and
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: phonetic 禾 (hé, "grain") + semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") – harmony.
- **examples:**
  - 他脾气很和(他脾氣很和)。
    - tā píqi hěn hé
    - He has a gentle temper.
  - 他们相处得很和(他們相處得很和)。
    - tāmen xiāngchǔ de hěn hé
    - They get along harmoniously.
  - 我和他都喜欢音乐(我和他都喜歡音樂)。
    - wǒ hé tā dōu xǐhuān yīnyuè
    - He and I both like music.
