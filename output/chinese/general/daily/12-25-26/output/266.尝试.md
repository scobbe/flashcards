## 尝试(嘗試)
---
- **pinyin:** chángshì
- **definition:** try; attempt
- **characters:**
  - 尝(嘗)
    - cháng
    - taste
  - 试(試)
    - shì
    - try
- **examples:**
  - 我想尝试学习一门新语言(我想嘗試學習一門新語言)。
    - wǒ xiǎng chángshì xuéxí yì mén xīn yǔyán
    - I want to try learning a new language.
  - 他多次尝试打破纪录(他多次嘗試打破紀錄)。
    - tā duō cì chángshì dǎpò jìlù
    - He made multiple attempts to break the record.
