## 开瓶器(開瓶器)
---
- **pinyin:** kāipíngqì
- **definition:** bottle opener
- **characters:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - open
  - 瓶
    - píng
    - bottle
  - 器
    - qì
    - utensil
- **etymology:** Combines 开(開) (kāi, "open"), 瓶 (píng, "bottle"), and 器 (qì, "utensil") to convey bottle opener.
- **examples:**
  - 请把开瓶器递给我(請把開瓶器遞給我)。
    - qǐng bǎ kāipíngqì dì gěi wǒ
    - Please pass me the bottle opener.
