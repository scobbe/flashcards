## 开瓶器(開瓶器)
---
- **pinyin:** kāipíngqì
- **definition:** bottle opener
- **characters:**
  - 开(開)
    - kāi
    - to open; to start, begin; to turn on, switch on; to operate, drive (a vehicle)
  - 瓶
    - píng
    - bottle; vase; measure word for bottled liquids
  - 器
    - qì
    - utensil, tool; device, apparatus; vessel, ware; capacity, ability
- **etymology:** 开(開) (kāi, "open") modifies 瓶 (píng, "bottle"), with 器 (qì, "tool") as an implement suffix—literally a bottle-opening device.
- **examples:**
  - 你有开瓶器吗(你有開瓶器嗎)？
    - Nǐ yǒu kāipíngqì ma?
    - Do you have a bottle opener?
