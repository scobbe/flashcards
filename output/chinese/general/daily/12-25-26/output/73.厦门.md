## 厦门(廈門)
---
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **characters:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - mansion, large building, edifice
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door, gate; family, school, sect; field, discipline; goal, goalpost
- **etymology:** 厦(廈) (xià, "mansion") evokes a grand edifice and 门(門) (mén, "gate") denotes an entrance/pass, together suggesting an imposing gateway—fitting for a seaport city. 厦(廈) also functions phonophorically for the local Minnan reading (Ē), while 门(門) carries the core "gate/port" sense.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算明年去厦门旅游(我打算明年去廈門旅遊)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn míngnián qù Xiàmén lǚyóu.
    - I’m planning to travel to Xiamen next year.
