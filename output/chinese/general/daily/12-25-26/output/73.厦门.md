## Xiamen
### Xiàmén
---
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - mansion; tall building; large house; used in place names
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door; gate; entrance; classifier for branches of study
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word (toponym)
  - **description:** 厦(廈 ‘mansion; large building’) + 门(門 ‘gate’) = ‘gate of a grand building,’ extended to mean a strategic sea-gate and adopted as the city name.
  - **interpretation:** Literally ‘gate of a mansion,’ evoking a grand portal to the sea and to Fujian’s trade routes. Commonly understood as a maritime gateway guarding important approaches.
  - **simplification:** Both characters were simplified for stroke economy: 廈 → 厦 (radical reduced; inner parts streamlined) and 門 → 门 (door-frame reduced), easing handwriting and printing.
- **examples:**
  - 厦门位于福建省东南部，濒临台湾海峡(廈門位於福建省東南部，瀕臨臺灣海峽)。
    - Xiàmén wèiyú Fújiàn shěng dōngnán bù, bīnlín Táiwān Hǎixiá.
    - Xiamen is in southeastern Fujian, facing the Taiwan Strait.
  - 我们明天坐高铁去厦门旅游(我們明天坐高鐵去廈門旅遊)。
    - Wǒmen míngtiān zuò gāotiě qù Xiàmén lǚyóu.
    - We will take the high-speed rail to travel to Xiamen tomorrow.
  - 名称“厦门”字面为“大厦之门”，比喻海上门户(名稱「廈門」字面為「大廈之門」，比喻海上門戶)。
    - Míngchēng “Xiàmén” zìmiàn wéi “dàshà zhī mén”, bǐyù hǎishàng ménhù.
    - The name literally means “gate of a grand building,” a metaphor for a maritime gateway.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈)
- **definition:** mansion; tall building; large house; used in place names
- **pinyin:** xià
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - broad; wide
  - 夏
    - xià
    - summer; Xia (dynasty); great (literary)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 广 + phonetic 夏 (phonetic also conveys 'great').
  - **interpretation:** 广 depicts a building/shelter, signaling the idea of a structure. 夏 supplies the sound xià, and since it can also mean “great,” it reinforces the sense of a large, imposing building such as a mansion.
  - **simplification:** In Simplified Chinese, the shelter component 广 was regularized to 厂 and the structure compressed, reducing strokes while retaining the phonetic 夏.
- **examples:**
  - 城市里到处是高楼大厦(城市裡到處是高樓大廈)。
    - Chéngshì lǐ dàochù shì gāolóu dàshà.
    - There are tall buildings everywhere in the city.
  - 这座大厦很气派(這座大廈很氣派)。
    - Zhè zuò dàshà hěn qìpài.
    - This building looks impressive.
  - 我们计划去厦门旅游(我們計畫去廈門旅遊)。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà qù Xiàmén lǚyóu.
    - We plan to travel to Xiamen.
### 厦门(廈門) → 厦(廈) → 广(廣)
- **definition:** broad; wide
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a house with a roof, a building with three walls (cf. 宀 with four); unrelated to 床, 疒, or ⼚.
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a simple shelter or building, evoking the idea of coverage or expanse under a roof. Later it served as the semantic ‘building/space’ element in 廣, whose phonetic is 黃, while the simplified form keeps the pictographic outline and associates with the sense ‘broad, wide’.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by reducing 廣 to its semantic component 广 and dropping the complex phonetic 黃, retaining the roof-and-wall pictographic form.
- **examples:**
  - 这里的草原很广阔(這裡的草原很廣闊)。
    - Zhèlǐ de cǎoyuán hěn guǎngkuò.
    - The grassland here is vast.
  - 这家公司的业务很广泛(這家公司的業務很廣泛)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngsī de yèwù hěn guǎngfàn.
    - This company’s business is wide-ranging.
  - 我们在广场散步(我們在廣場散步)。
    - Wǒmen zài guǎngchǎng sànbù.
    - We stroll in the square.

### 厦门(廈門) → 门(門)
- **definition:** door; gate; entrance; classifier for branches of study
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: a gate; compare 戶 “(one) door”, half of the character.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a pair of swinging doors within a frame, representing a gate or entrance. Traditional 門 shows two door leaves; simplified 门 keeps the frame and a central stroke, preserving the concept of an opening.
  - **simplification:** Reduced stroke count by collapsing the inner leaves to a single vertical line and straightening the frame; standardized as the common radical form in simplified characters.
- **examples:**
  - 请把门关上(請把門關上)。
    - Qǐng bǎ mén guān shàng.
    - Please close the door.
  - 他选了三门课程(他選了三門課程)。
    - Tā xuǎn le sān mén kèchéng.
    - He chose three courses.
  - 我在门口等你(我在門口等你)。
    - Wǒ zài ménkǒu děng nǐ.
    - I'll wait for you at the entrance.
