## 软件(軟件)
---
- **pinyin:** ruǎnjiàn
- **definition:** software
- **characters:**
  - 软(軟)
    - ruǎn
    - soft, pliable; flexible; weak; gentle
  - 件
    - jiàn
    - item, piece; document; matter, case; classifier for events, things, clothing
- **etymology:** 软(軟) (ruǎn, "soft; flexible") highlights the intangible, malleable nature of code, while 件 (jiàn, "item; component") denotes a unit/component—together "soft components," contrasted with 硬 (yìng, "hard") 件 (jiàn, "component") for hardware.
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天更新了手机上的软件(我昨天更新了手機上的軟件)，现在运行得更快了(現在運行得更快了)。
    - Wǒ zuótiān gēngxīn le shǒujī shàng de ruǎnjiàn, xiànzài yùnxíng de gèng kuài le.
    - I updated the software on my phone yesterday, and now it runs faster.
