## 济南(濟南)
---
- **pinyin:** Jǐnán
- **definition:** Jinan (capital of Shandong)
- **characters:**
  - 济(濟)
    - jì, jǐ
    - to help, aid, relieve; to be of use, effective; to cross (a river), ford
  - 南
    - nán
    - south; southern; the south (region)
- **etymology:** Combines 济(濟) (jì, "Ji River") with 南 (nán, "south") to mean "south of the Ji River," a toponymic formation using the river as the defining landmark.
- **examples:**
  - 济南是山东省的省会(濟南是山東省的省會)，它以泉水闻名(它以泉水聞名)。
    - Jǐnán shì Shāndōng shěng de shěnghuì, tā yǐ quánshuǐ wénmíng.
    - Jinan is the capital of Shandong Province, and it’s famous for its springs.
