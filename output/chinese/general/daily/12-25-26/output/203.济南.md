## 济南(濟南)
---
- **pinyin:** Jǐnán
- **definition:** Jinan (capital of Shandong)
- **characters:**
  - 济(濟): jǐ, aid
  - 南: nán, south
- **examples:**
  - 山东省的省会是济南(山東省的省會是濟南) (shāndōng shěng de shěnghuì shì jǐnán, "The capital of Shandong Province is Jinan")
  - 我在济南长大(我在濟南長大) (wǒ zài jǐnán zhǎngdà, "I grew up in Jinan")
