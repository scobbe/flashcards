## 官二代
---
- **pinyin:** guān’èrdài
- **definition:** children of officials
- **characters:**
  - 官
    - guān
    - official, bureaucrat; government, public; office, bureau; organ (of the body)
  - 二
    - èr
    - two; second; stupid, foolish (slang)
  - 代
    - dài
    - generation; dynasty, era; to replace, to substitute; for, on behalf of
- **etymology:** 官 (guān, "official") combines with 二 (èr, "two") and 代 (dài, "generation") to denote the "second generation of officials," i.e., children of government officials who benefit from inherited status or connections.
- **examples:**
  - 他是官二代(他是官二代)，所以资源很多(所以資源很多)。
    - Tā shì guān’èrdài, suǒyǐ zīyuán hěn duō.
    - He’s a child of an official, so he has lots of resources.
  - 人们常说官二代有更多人脉(人們常說官二代有更多人脈)，但能力同样重要(但能力同樣重要)。
    - Rénmen cháng shuō guān’èrdài yǒu gèng duō rénmài, dàn nénglì tóngyàng zhòngyào.
    - People often say children of officials have more connections, but ability matters just as much.
