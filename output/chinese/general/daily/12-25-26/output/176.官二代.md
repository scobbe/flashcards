## 官二代
---
- **pinyin:** guān’èrdài
- **definition:** children of officials
- **characters:**
  - 官: guān, official
  - 二: èr, two
  - 代: dài, generation
- **examples:**
  - 很多人认为官二代享有更多的社会资源(很多人認為官二代享有更多的社會資源) (hěn duō rén rènwéi guān èr dài xiǎngyǒu gèng duō de shèhuì zīyuán, "Many people believe that children of officials enjoy more social resources")
  - 他是个官二代，但自己也很努力(他是個官二代，但自己也很努力) (tā shì gè guān èr dài, dàn zìjǐ yě hěn nǔlì, "He is a child of an official, but he also works hard")
