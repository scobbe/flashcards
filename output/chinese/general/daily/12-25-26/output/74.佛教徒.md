## 佛教徒
---
- **pinyin:** Fójiàotú
- **definition:** Buddhist
- **characters:**
  - 佛
    - fó
    - Buddha; Buddhist image, statue
  - 教
    - jiào
    - religion, faith; teaching, doctrine; education; to teach, instruct
  - 徒
    - tú
    - disciple, follower; apprentice; on foot; merely, only
- **etymology:** 佛 (fó, "Buddha") + 教 (jiào, "religion; teaching") name the Buddhist doctrine, and 徒 (tú, "disciple; follower") specifies an adherent—literally, a "follower of the Buddhist teaching."
- **examples:**
  - 她是一名虔诚的佛教徒(她是一名虔誠的佛教徒)，经常到寺庙做义工(經常到寺廟做義工)。
    - Tā shì yí míng qiánchéng de fójiàotú, jīngcháng dào sìmiào zuò yìgōng.
    - She is a devout Buddhist and often volunteers at the temple.
