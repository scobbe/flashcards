## 臭豆腐
---
- **pinyin:** chòudòufu
- **definition:** stinky tofu
- **characters:**
  - 臭
    - chòu
    - stinky, smelly; to stink, to reek; damned (pejorative intensifier), lousy, rotten
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean, legume
  - 腐
    - fǔ
    - rotten, decayed; to rot, to decompose; corrupt, decadent; tofu, bean curd
- **etymology:** 臭 (chòu, "stinky") modifies 豆 (dòu, "bean") and 腐 (fǔ, "rotten; tofu") to denote stinky bean curd; 腐 evokes the fermentation/decay that produces tofu, while 臭 highlights the pungent aroma from that process.
- **examples:**
  - 夜市的臭豆腐特别香(夜市的臭豆腐特別香)，外酥里嫩(外酥裡嫩)，让我一口接一口(讓我一口接一口)。
    - Yèshì de chòu dòufu tèbié xiāng, wài sū lǐ nèn, ràng wǒ yì kǒu jiē yì kǒu.
    - The stinky tofu at the night market smells amazing—crispy outside, tender inside—and I can’t stop eating it.
