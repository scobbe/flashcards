## 琪
---
- **pinyin:** Qí
- **definition:** fine jade; surname Qi
- **etymology:** A character meaning fine jade; surname qi.
- **examples:**
  - 这件佩饰用一块难得的琪雕成(這件佩飾用一塊難得的琪雕成)。
    - zhè jiàn pèishì yòng yí kuài nándé de qí diāo chéng
    - This ornament was carved from a rare piece of fine jade.
  - 新来的经理姓琪(新來的經理姓琪)。
    - xīn lái de jīnglǐ xìng qí
    - The new manager's surname is Qi.
