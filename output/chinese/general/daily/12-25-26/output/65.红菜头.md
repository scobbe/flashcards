## 红菜头(紅菜頭)
---
- **pinyin:** hóngcàitóu
- **definition:** beetroot
- **characters:**
  - 红(紅)
    - hóng
    - red; popular, in vogue; revolutionary, communist
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable, greens; dish, course; cuisine; unskilled, bad
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head; beginning, start; end, tip; measure word for livestock, garlic
- **etymology:** 红(紅) (hóng, "red") marks the vivid color, 菜 (cài, "vegetable") identifies it as a vegetable, and 头(頭) (tóu, "head") evokes the bulbous root—literally a "red vegetable head" for beetroot.
- **examples:**
  - 红菜头(紅菜頭)，也叫甜菜根(也叫甜菜根)，富含纤维和铁(富含纖維和鐵)。
    - Hóngcàitóu, yě jiào tiáncàigēn, fùhán xiānwéi hé tiě.
    - Beetroot, also called 甜菜根, is rich in fiber and iron.
  - 我把红菜头切丁后(我把紅菜頭切丁後)，再和橙子、核桃一起拌成沙拉(再和橙子、核桃一起拌成沙拉)。
    - Wǒ bǎ hóngcàitóu qiē dīng hòu, zài hé chéngzi、hétáo yìqǐ bàn chéng shālā.
    - After dicing the beetroot, I mix it with oranges and walnuts to make a salad.
