## 红菜头(紅菜頭)
---
- **pinyin:** hóngcàitóu
- **definition:** beetroot
- **characters:**
  - 红(紅)
    - hóng
    - red
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head
- **etymology:** Combines 红(紅) (hóng, "red"), 菜 (cài, "vegetable"), and 头(頭) (tóu, "head") to convey beetroot.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃红菜头沙拉(我喜歡吃紅菜頭沙拉)。
    - wǒ xǐhuān chī hóngcàitóu shālā
    - I like eating beetroot salad.
