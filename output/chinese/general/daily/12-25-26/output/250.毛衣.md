## 毛衣
---
- **pinyin:** máoyī
- **definition:** sweater
- **characters:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes
- **etymology:** Combines 毛 (máo, "hair") with 衣 (yī, "clothes") to express sweater.
- **examples:**
  - 这件毛衣很暖和(這件毛衣很暖和)。
    - zhè jiàn máoyī hěn nuǎnhuo
    - This sweater is very warm.
