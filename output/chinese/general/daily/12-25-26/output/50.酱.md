## 酱
---
- **pinyin:** jiàng
- **definition:** sauce
- **etymology:** From 醬 (將 → ⿰丬夕) Definitions: For pronunciation and definitions of 酱 – see 醬 ("sauce or paste made of fermented beans , flour , etc.
- **examples:**
  - 这道菜少了点酱(這道菜少了點醬)，味道不够(味道不夠)。
    - zhè dào cài shǎo le diǎn jiàng, wèidào bù gòu
    - This dish is missing some sauce; the flavor isn't strong enough.
