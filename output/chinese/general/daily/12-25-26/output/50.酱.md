## 酱
---
- **pinyin:** jiàng
- **definition:** sauce
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: 酱(醬) (jiàng, "sauce") combines semantic 酉 (yǒu, "wine vessel; alcohol") indicating fermented liquids with phonetic 将(將) (jiàng, "general") suggesting the sound. The jar-like pictograph 酉 evokes fermentation in jars, matching sauces made from fermented grains/beans.
- **examples:**
  - 把酱倒在碗里(把醬倒在碗裡)，再配上烤鸡(再配上烤雞)。
    - Bǎ jiàng dào zài wǎn lǐ, zài pèi shàng kǎo jī.
    - Pour the sauce into the bowl, then serve it with roast chicken.
