## 星期
---
- **pinyin:** xīngqī
- **definition:** week; weekday
- **characters:**
  - 星
    - xīng
    - star; tiny amount, trace
  - 期
    - qī
    - period, term; issue (of a periodical); deadline, due date; to expect, to hope
- **etymology:** Combines 星 (xīng, "star") with 期 (qī, "period") to denote a time period marked by celestial bodies, hence a seven-day cycle; by extension, it serves as the prefix for weekdays.
- **examples:**
  - 我们每个星期都开会(我們每個星期都開會)。
    - Wǒmen měi gè xīngqī dōu kāihuì.
    - We have a meeting every week.
  - 今天是星期二(今天是星期二)。
    - Jīntiān shì xīngqī èr.
    - Today is Tuesday.
