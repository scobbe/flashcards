## 鲜奶
---
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **characters:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān, xiǎn
    - fresh; delicious, tasty; bright, vivid; few, rare
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast, udder; to breastfeed, nurse; wet nurse, nanny
- **etymology:** 鲜(鮮) (xiān, "fresh") modifies 奶 (nǎi, "milk") to denote milk that is recently produced and unprocessed; the food-oriented sense of 鲜 highlights perishability and appealing taste.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用鲜奶泡咖啡(我喜歡用鮮奶泡咖啡)，因为味道更香(因為味道更香)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān yòng xiānnǎi pào kāfēi, yīnwèi wèidào gèng xiāng.
    - I like using fresh milk in my coffee because it tastes richer.
