## 鲜奶
---
## fresh milk
### xiānnǎi
---
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **components:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh; bright (color); delicious; few/rare
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; nanny; grandmother (in compounds)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鲜(fresh) + 奶(milk) = fresh milk
  - **interpretation:** Emphasizes milk that is newly obtained or minimally processed, retaining a fresh taste. Often contrasts with UHT/long-life milk or milk powder.
  - **simplification:** 鮮 was simplified to 鲜 by reducing the fish radical 魚 to 鱼 while keeping 羊; 奶 is unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢喝鲜奶(我喜歡喝鮮奶)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān hē xiānnǎi.
    - I like to drink fresh milk.
  - 这家店每天清晨送来鲜奶(這家店每天清晨送來鮮奶)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn měitiān qīngchén sòng lái xiānnǎi.
    - This shop delivers fresh milk every morning.
  - 请把鲜奶放进冰箱(請把鮮奶放進冰箱)。
    - Qǐng bǎ xiānnǎi fàng jìn bīngxiāng.
    - Please put the fresh milk in the refrigerator.
### 鲜奶 → 鲜(鮮)
- **definition:** fresh; bright (color); delicious; few/rare
- **pinyin:** xiān
- **components:**
  - 鱼(魚)
    - yú
    - fish
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 魚 ‘fish’ + abbreviated phonetic: 羴; alternatively ideogrammic: 魚 + 羊 ‘delicious’.
  - **interpretation:** Fish supplies the semantic field of food/seafood, while pronunciation follows 羴 (three-sheep), visually reduced to 羊 in the form. Pairing fish and sheep also evokes tasty, fresh fare, which extended to vivid colors; under a different reading it developed the sense ‘rare’.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by replacing 魚 with its simplified form 鱼; the rest of the structure remained unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的海鲜很新鲜，味道鲜美(今天的海鮮很新鮮，味道鮮美)。
    - Jīntiān de hǎixiān hěn xīnxiān, wèidào xiānměi.
    - Today's seafood is very fresh, and the taste is delicious.
  - 这条裙子的颜色很鲜艳(這條裙子的顏色很鮮豔)。
    - Zhè tiáo qúnzi de yánsè hěn xiānyàn.
    - The color of this dress is very bright.
  - 这种错误在正式文件里很鲜见(這種錯誤在正式文件裡很鮮見)。
    - Zhè zhǒng cuòwù zài zhèngshì wénjiàn lǐ hěn xiǎnjiàn.
    - Such errors are rare in formal documents.
### 鲜奶 → 鲜(鮮) → 鱼(魚)
- **definition:** fish
- **pinyin:** yú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – a fish.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of a fish with head, body, fins, and tail, stylized into a standard written form over time. The simplified form streamlines the lower strokes and internal details while keeping the recognizable fish outline.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 魚 by reducing the bottom 灬 to a single stroke 一 and compressing interior strokes to lower the stroke count and standardize the component used in many fish-related characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃鱼(我喜歡吃魚)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan chī yú.
    - I like to eat fish.
  - 他在家里养鱼(他在家裡養魚)。
    - Tā zài jiālǐ yǎng yú.
    - He raises fish at home.
  - 鱼在河里游来游去(魚在河裡遊來遊去)。
    - Yú zài hé lǐ yóu lái yóu qù.
    - Fish swim back and forth in the river.
### 鲜奶 → 鲜(鮮) → 羊
- **definition:** sheep
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形) – picture of a ram’s head.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a ram’s head with prominent horns above and facial/beard lines below. The image directly conveys the meaning of “sheep,” and its recognizable silhouette later served as a component in related characters.
- **examples:**
  - 我家养了三只羊(我家養了三隻羊)。
    - Wǒ jiā yǎng le sān zhī yáng.
    - My family raises three sheep.
  - 那只羊很温顺(那隻羊很溫順)。
    - Nà zhī yáng hěn wénshùn.
    - That sheep is very gentle.
  - 山上有很多野羊(山上有很多野羊)。
    - Shān shàng yǒu hěn duō yěyáng.
    - There are many wild sheep in the mountains.
### 鲜奶 → 奶
- **definition:** milk; breast; nanny; grandmother (in compounds)
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman; female
  - 乃
    - nǎi
    - thus; then
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 女 + phonetic: 乃
  - **interpretation:** Combines the 'woman/female' semantic element with the sound value of 乃, pointing to meanings linked to mothers, breasts, and by extension milk or a nanny. The pronunciation follows 乃 while the 女 component anchors the maternal/feminine semantic field.
- **examples:**
  - 我早上常喝牛奶(我早上常喝牛奶)。
    - Wǒ zǎoshang cháng hē niúnǎi.
    - I often drink milk in the morning.
  - 孩子在吃奶，哭声渐小(孩子在吃奶，哭聲漸小)。
    - Háizi zài chī nǎi, kūshēng jiàn xiǎo.
    - The baby is nursing, and the crying is gradually fading.
  - 周末我们去看奶奶(週末我們去看奶奶)。
    - Zhōumò wǒmen qù kàn nǎinai.
    - We’re going to visit Grandma this weekend.
### 鲜奶 → 奶 → 女
- **definition:** woman; female
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram: a woman with breasts kneeling or standing; modern form reversed from oracle forms, with the enclosed area as the remnant of the right breast and the left breast lost.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a female figure with a curved torso and a remaining breast, historically shown kneeling and later flipped to face right in standard script. Graphically related to 母 and 毋, which retain more breast/nipple detail, while the similar-looking 卩 is unrelated.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一个勇敢的女人(她是一個勇敢的女人)。
    - Tā shì yí gè yǒnggǎn de nǚrén.
    - She is a brave woman.
  - 他们有一个可爱的女儿(他們有一個可愛的女兒)，正在上小学(正在上小學)。
    - Tāmen yǒu yí gè kě'ài de nǚ'ér, zhèngzài shàng xiǎoxué.
    - They have a lovely daughter who is in primary school.
  - 这家公司的女员工很多(這家公司的女員工很多)，工作表现很出色(工作表現很出色)。
    - Zhè jiā gōngsī de nǚ yuángōng hěn duō, gōngzuò biǎoxiàn hěn chūsè.
    - This company has many female employees, and their work performance is outstanding.
### 鲜奶 → 奶 → 乃
- **definition:** thus; then
- **pinyin:** nǎi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** unknown
  - **description:** Original form 𠄎; origin uncertain; various theories; unrelated to 廴.
  - **interpretation:** Earliest forms show a simple curved graph written as 𠄎, but scholars disagree on what it was intended to depict. Despite visual similarity, it is not derived from 廴, and no consensus formation analysis has been established.
- **examples:**
  - 他思索再三，乃作决定(他思索再三，乃作決定)。
    - Tā sīsuǒ zàisān, nǎi zuò juédìng.
    - After much deliberation, he then made a decision.
  - 事已至此，乃下令撤退(事已至此，乃下令撤退)。
    - Shì yǐ zhì cǐ, nǎi xiàlìng chètuì.
    - Now that matters had come to this point, he then gave the order to retreat.
  - 君臣同心，乃能安邦(君臣同心，乃能安邦)。
    - Jūn chén tóngxīn, nǎi néng ān bāng.
    - Only when ruler and ministers are of one mind can the state be stabilized.
