## 鲜奶
---
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **characters:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk
- **etymology:** 鲜(鮮) (xiān, "fresh") encodes the culinary ideal of newly obtained perishables—魚 (yú, "fish") and 羊 (yáng, "sheep")—so when modifying 奶 (nǎi, "milk", originally "breast"), it pinpoints milk in the just-drawn, unpreserved state, i.e., "fresh milk".
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早上都喝一杯鲜奶(我每天早上都喝一杯鮮奶)。
    - wǒ měitiān zǎoshang dōu hē yì bēi xiānnǎi
    - I drink a glass of fresh milk every morning.
