## 豆浆
---
- **pinyin:** dòujiāng
- **definition:** soy milk
- **characters:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 浆(漿)
    - jiāng
    - thick liquid
- **etymology:** Combines 豆 (dòu, "bean") with 浆(漿) (jiāng, "thick liquid") to express soy milk.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早上都喝一杯热豆浆(我每天早上都喝一杯熱豆漿)。
    - wǒ měitiān zǎoshang dōu hē yì bēi rè dòujiāng
    - I drink a cup of hot soy milk every morning.
