## 豆浆
---
- **pinyin:** dòujiāng
- **definition:** soy milk
- **characters:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean, legume; ancient food vessel
  - 浆(漿)
    - jiāng
    - thick liquid, paste; starch, size; juice, sap, syrup; to starch, to size
- **etymology:** Combines 豆 (dòu, "bean") with 浆(漿) (jiāng, "thick liquid, paste") to denote a 'bean slurry'—the blended liquid from soybeans—hence 'soy milk'.
- **examples:**
  - 早上我喜欢喝热豆浆(早上我喜歡喝熱豆漿)，搭配油条(搭配油條)。
    - Zǎoshang wǒ xǐhuan hē rè dòujiāng, dāpèi yóutiáo.
    - In the morning I like to drink hot soy milk, paired with fried dough sticks.
