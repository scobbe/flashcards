## 中正区
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 中: zhōng, middle
  - 正: zhèng, correct
  - 区(區): qū, district
- **examples:**
  - 总统府位于台北市中正区(總統府位於臺北市中正區) (zǒngtǒngfǔ wèiyú táiběi shì zhōngzhèng qū, "The Presidential Office Building is located in Taipei's Zhongzheng District")
