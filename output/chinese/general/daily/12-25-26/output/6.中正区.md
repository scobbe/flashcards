## 中正区
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
  - 正
    - zhèng
    - correct
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - district
- **etymology:** Combines 中 (zhōng, "middle"), 正 (zhèng, "correct"), and 区(區) (qū, "district") to convey zhongzheng district, taipei.
- **examples:**
  - 总统府位于台北市中正区(總統府位於臺北市中正區)。
    - zǒngtǒngfǔ wèiyú táiběi shì zhōngzhèng qū
    - The Presidential Office Building is located in Taipei's Zhongzheng District.
