## 中正区
---
- **pinyin:** Zhōngzhèng Qū
- **definition:** Zhongzheng District, Taipei
- **characters:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle, center; among, within; during, in the course of; China
  - 正
    - zhèng
    - upright, proper; correct, right; exactly, precisely; just now, currently
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - area, region, district, zone; administrative district; to distinguish, differentiate
- **etymology:** 中 (zhōng, "center") and 正 (zhèng, "upright") form the proper name "Zhongzheng," evoking central uprightness, while 区(區) (qū, "district") marks it as an administrative district.
- **examples:**
  - 我住在台北的中正区(我住在臺北的中正區)，离中正纪念堂很近(離中正紀念堂很近)。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Táiběi de Zhōngzhèng Qū, lí Zhōngzhèng Jìniàntáng hěn jìn.
    - I live in Taipei’s Zhongzheng District, very close to the Chiang Kai-shek Memorial Hall.
