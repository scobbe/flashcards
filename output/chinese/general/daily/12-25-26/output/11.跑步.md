## 跑步
---
- **pinyin:** pǎobù
- **definition:** run; jog
- **characters:**
  - 跑
    - pǎo
    - to run; to flee, run away; to leak (gas, liquid); to make the rounds, run errands
  - 步
    - bù
    - step, pace; to walk; step (in a process), stage
- **etymology:** 跑 (pǎo, "to run") combines with 步 (bù, "step") as a verb–object compound meaning "to run [in steps]," conceptualizing running as making rapid steps. 步 frames/quantifies the motion, turning 跑 into the activity of running or jogging.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早上跑步五公里(我每天早上跑步五公里)，为马拉松训练(為馬拉松訓練)。
    - Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang pǎobù wǔ gōnglǐ, wèi mǎlāsōng xùnliàn.
    - I run five kilometers every morning to train for a marathon.
  - 晚饭后(晚飯後)，我会在公园里轻松地跑步二十分钟(我會在公園裡輕鬆地跑步二十分鐘)。
    - Wǎnfàn hòu, wǒ huì zài gōngyuán lǐ qīngsōng de pǎobù èrshí fēnzhōng.
    - After dinner, I jog in the park for twenty minutes.
