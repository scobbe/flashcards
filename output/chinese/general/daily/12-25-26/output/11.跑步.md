## 跑步
---
- **pinyin:** pǎobù
- **definition:** run; jog
- **characters:**
  - 跑
    - pǎo
    - run
  - 步
    - bù
    - step
- **etymology:** 跑 (pǎo, "run/flee quickly") governs 步 (bù, "step; paced measure") in a classical verb–object pattern where 步 names the unit of locomotion, yielding “to take steps in a running mode.” Because 步 historically denotes measured paces (e.g., in marching or as a unit of length), the pairing highlights rapid, successive paces rather than generic movement.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早上在公园里跑步(我每天早上在公園裡跑步)。
    - wǒ měitiān zǎoshang zài gōngyuán lǐ pǎobù
    - I run in the park every morning.
  - 周末我喜欢和朋友一起轻松地跑步(周末我喜歡和朋友一起輕鬆地跑步)。
    - zhōumò wǒ xǐhuan hé péngyou yìqǐ qīngsōng de pǎobù
    - On weekends I like to go jogging with friends.
