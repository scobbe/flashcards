## 痧子
---
- **pinyin:** shāzi
- **definition:** measles
- **characters:**
  - 痧: shā, cholera
  - 子: zǐ, child
- **examples:**
  - 他小时候得过痧子(他小時候得過痧子) (tā xiǎo shíhou dé guò shāzi, "He had measles when he was a child")
  - 医生诊断他患了痧子，需要隔离(醫生診斷他患了痧子，需要隔離) (yīshēng zhěnduàn tā huàn le shāzi, xūyào gélí, "The doctor diagnosed him with measles, and he needs to be isolated")
