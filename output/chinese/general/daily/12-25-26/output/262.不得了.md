## 不得了
---
- **pinyin:** bùdéliǎo
- **definition:** terrible; extremely; amazing
- **characters:**
  - 不: bù, not
  - 得: dé, get
  - 了: le, aspect particle
- **examples:**
  - 这次地震造成的破坏不得了(這次地震造成的破壞不得了) (zhè cì dìzhèn zàochéng de pòhuài bùdéliǎo, "The destruction caused by this earthquake is terrible")
  - 他紧张得不得了(他緊張得不得了) (tā jǐnzhāng de bùdéliǎo, "He is extremely nervous")
  - 她的歌声真不得了(她的歌聲真不得了) (tā de gēshēng zhēn bùdéliǎo, "Her singing is amazing")
