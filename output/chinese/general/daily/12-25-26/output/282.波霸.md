## 波霸
---
- **pinyin:** bōbà
- **definition:** big boobs (slang)
- **characters:**
  - 波
    - bō
    - wave
  - 霸
    - bà
    - tyrant
- **etymology:** Combines 波 (bō, "wave") with 霸 (bà, "tyrant") to express big boobs (slang).
- **examples:**
  - 他老是盯着她的波霸看(他老是盯著她的波霸看)，太没礼貌了(太沒禮貌了)。
    - tā lǎo shì dīng zhe tā de bōbà kàn, tài méi lǐmào le
    - He keeps staring at her big boobs; it's really rude.
