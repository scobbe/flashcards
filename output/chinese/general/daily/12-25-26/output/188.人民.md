## 人民
---
- **pinyin:** rénmín
- **definition:** the people
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person, human; people; others
  - 民
    - mín
    - people, citizenry; the public; civilian; non-governmental, private
- **etymology:** Combines 人 (rén, "person") with 民 (mín, "citizenry; the public") to denote the collective body of persons constituting a polity—the people.
- **examples:**
  - 政府应该倾听人民的声音(政府應該傾聽人民的聲音)。
    - Zhèngfǔ yīnggāi qīngtīng rénmín de shēngyīn.
    - The government should listen to the voice of the people.
  - 人民有权监督政府(人民有權監督政府)。
    - Rénmín yǒuquán jiāndū zhèngfǔ.
    - The people have the right to oversee the government.
