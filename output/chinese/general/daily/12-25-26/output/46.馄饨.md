## 馄饨
---
- **pinyin:** húntun
- **definition:** wonton
- **characters:**
  - 馄(餛)
    - hún
    - wonton
  - 饨(飩)
    - tún
    - wonton
- **etymology:** Reduplicative binome where 馄(餛) (hún, "wonton") and 饨(飩) (tún, "wonton") are near-synonymous food morphemes; their paired, rhyming syllables form the conventional name of the dumpling.
- **examples:**
  - 这家小店的馄饨特别好吃(這家小店的餛飩特別好吃)，我每周都来一碗(我每週都來一碗)。
    - Zhè jiā xiǎodiàn de húntun tèbié hǎochī, wǒ měi zhōu dōu lái yì wǎn.
    - The wontons at this little shop are especially delicious; I come for a bowl every week.
