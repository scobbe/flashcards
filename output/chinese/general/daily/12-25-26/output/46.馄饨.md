## 馄饨
---
- **pinyin:** húntun
- **definition:** wonton
- **characters:**
  - 馄(餛)
    - hún
    - wonton
  - 饨(飩)
    - tún
    - wonton
- **etymology:** Combines 馄(餛) (hún, "wonton") with 饨(飩) (tún, "wonton") to express wonton.
- **examples:**
  - 我想点一碗馄饨(我想點一碗餛飩)。
    - wǒ xiǎng diǎn yì wǎn húntun
    - I'd like to order a bowl of wontons.
