## 巴金
---
- **pinyin:** Bā Jīn
- **definition:** Ba Jin
- **characters:**
  - 巴
    - bā
    - to long for, to yearn for; to stick to, to cling to; Ba, ancient state; noun suffix (colloquial)
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold; metal; money; surname Jin
- **etymology:** 巴 (bā, "Ba") gives the phonetic Ba (also a surname/ancient state), and 金 (jīn, "gold") gives Jin (also a surname), combining into an auspicious-sounding pen name; it's often said to honor Bakunin and Kropotkin.
- **examples:**
  - 巴金是中国现代著名作家之一(巴金是中國現代著名作家之一)，他的《家》《春》《秋》影响深远(他的《家》《春》《秋》影響深遠)。
    - Bā Jīn shì Zhōngguó xiàndài zhùmíng zuòjiā zhī yī, tā de «Jiā» «Chūn» «Qiū» yǐngxiǎng shēnyuǎn.
    - Ba Jin is one of the most renowned modern Chinese writers; his novels Family, Spring, and Autumn have had a profound influence.
