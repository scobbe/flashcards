## 巴金
---
- **pinyin:** Bā Jīn
- **definition:** Ba Jin
- **characters:**
  - 巴
    - bā
    - long for
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold
- **etymology:** A pen name saluting the anarchist thinkers Bakunin and Kropotkin: 巴 from 巴枯寧 and 金 from 克魯泡特金, reflecting a Republican-era habit of clipping transliterated surnames to signal intellectual allegiance. By happy coincidence, the literal senses of 巴 (bā, "to long for") and 金 (jīn, "gold") also suggest yearning for something precious—fitting his youthful idealism.
- **examples:**
  - 巴金是二十世纪著名的中国作家。(巴金是二十世紀著名的中國作家。)。
    - Bā Jīn shì èrshí shìjì zhùmíng de Zhōngguó zuòjiā
    - Ba Jin was a renowned Chinese writer of the twentieth century.
