## 可不
---
- **pinyin:** kěbù
- **definition:** exactly; right
- **characters:**
  - 可: kě, can; may
  - 不: bù, not
- **examples:**
  - 可不，这天气真冷(可不，這天氣真冷) (kě bù, zhè tiānqì zhēn lěng, "Exactly, the weather is really cold")
  - 可不，这件事没那么简单(可不，這件事沒那麼簡單) (kě bù, zhè jiàn shì méi nàme jiǎndān, "Right, this matter isn't that simple")
