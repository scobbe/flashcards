## 可不
---
- **pinyin:** kěbù
- **definition:** exactly; right
- **characters:**
  - 可
    - kě
    - can
    - may
  - 不
    - bù
    - not
- **etymology:** Combines 可 (kě, "can") with 不 (bù, "not") to express exactly; right.
- **examples:**
  - 可不，这天气真冷(這天氣真冷)。
    - kě bù, zhè tiānqì zhēn lěng
    - Exactly, the weather is really cold.
  - 可不，这件事没那么简单(這件事沒那麼簡單)。
    - kě bù, zhè jiàn shì méi nàme jiǎndān
    - Right, this matter isn't that simple.
