## 洲
---
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left side is the water element from 水 (shuǐ, "water") indicating relation to water; right side 州 (zhōu, "prefecture; islet") supplies the sound zhōu. 州 originally depicted small islets/sandbanks, so combined with water it meant a river islet and later extended to a large landmass amid waters (continent).
- **examples:**
  - 亚洲是世界七大洲之一(亞洲是世界七大洲之一)。
    - Yàzhōu shì shìjiè qī dà zhōu zhī yī.
    - Asia is one of the seven continents of the world.
  - 江心有一座小洲(江心有一座小洲)，春天会长出芦苇(春天會長出蘆葦)。
    - Jiāngxīn yǒu yí zuò xiǎo zhōu, chūntiān huì zhǎng chū lúwěi.
    - There is a small river island in the middle of the river, and reeds sprout there in spring.
