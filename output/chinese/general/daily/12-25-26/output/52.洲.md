## 洲
---
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **etymology:** A character meaning continent; river island.
- **examples:**
  - 世界上有七大洲(世界上有七大洲)。
    - shìjiè shàng yǒu qī dàzhōu
    - There are seven continents in the world.
  - 江中有一片小洲，上面长满了芦苇(上面長滿了蘆葦)。
    - jiāng zhōng yǒu yí piàn xiǎo zhōu, shàngmiàn chángmǎn le lúwěi
    - There is a small river island in the middle of the river, covered with reeds.
