## 凉茶(涼茶)
---
- **pinyin:** liángchá
- **definition:** herbal tea
- **characters:**
  - 凉(涼)
    - liáng
    - cool, cold; to cool, let cool; discouraged, disheartened
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea, tea plant, tea leaves; brown, tawny
- **etymology:** 凉(涼) (liáng, "cool") indicates the drink's heat-dispelling, cooling function in traditional medicine, while 茶 (chá, "tea") frames it as a tea-like beverage, together denoting herbal decoctions consumed like tea to cool the body.
- **examples:**
  - 天气炎热的时候(天氣炎熱的時候)，广东人喜欢喝一杯凉茶降火(廣東人喜歡喝一杯涼茶降火)。
    - Tiānqì yánrè de shíhou, Guǎngdōng rén xǐhuān hē yì bēi liángchá jiànghuǒ.
    - When the weather is hot, people in Guangdong like to drink a cup of herbal tea to cool down.
