## 凉茶(涼茶)
---
- **pinyin:** liángchá
- **definition:** herbal tea
- **characters:**
  - 凉(涼)
    - liáng
    - cool
  - 茶
    - chá
    - tea
- **examples:**
  - 我喉咙有点痛(我喉嚨有點痛)，奶奶让我喝一碗凉茶(奶奶讓我喝一碗涼茶)。
    - wǒ hóulóng yǒudiǎn tòng, nǎinai ràng wǒ hē yì wǎn liángchá
    - My throat hurts a little, and Grandma told me to drink a bowl of herbal tea.
