## 阳具
---
- **pinyin:** yángjù
- **definition:** male genitals
- **characters:**
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun
  - 具
    - jù
    - tool
- **examples:**
  - 医生在体检时会检查男性的阳具是否有异常(醫生在體檢時會檢查男性的陽具是否有異常)。
    - yīshēng zài tǐjiǎn shí huì jiǎnchá nánxìng de yángjù shìfǒu yǒu yìcháng
    - During a physical exam, the doctor will check whether the male genitals have any abnormalities.
