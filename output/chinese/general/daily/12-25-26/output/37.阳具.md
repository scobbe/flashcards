## 阳具
---
- **pinyin:** yángjù
- **definition:** male genitals
- **characters:**
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - yang (male, active principle); sun, sunlight; sunlit side of a hill or river; positive (test result, electric charge)
  - 具
    - jù
    - tool, utensil, implement; to have, possess; measure word for a corpse, body, coffin; to state in writing, to list
- **etymology:** 阳(陽) (yáng, "yang; male") marks the male/active principle, and 具 (jù, "tool; implement") denotes an implement, combining as a metaphorical "male tool" for the male genitals.
- **examples:**
  - 医生在检查患者的阳具(醫生在檢查患者的陽具)，以确定是否有感染(以確定是否有感染)。
    - Yīshēng zài jiǎnchá huànzhě de yángjù, yǐ quèdìng shìfǒu yǒu gǎnrǎn.
    - The doctor examined the patient’s male genitals to determine whether there was an infection.
  - 在解剖学课上(在解剖學課上)，老师解释了阳具的结构和功能(老師解釋了陽具的結構和功能)。
    - Zài jiěpōuxué kè shàng, lǎoshī jiěshì le yángjù de jiégòu hé gōngnéng.
    - In anatomy class, the teacher explained the structure and function of the male genitals.
