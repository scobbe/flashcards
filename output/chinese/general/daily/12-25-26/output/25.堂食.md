## 堂食
---
- **pinyin:** tángshí
- **definition:** eat in
- **characters:**
  - 堂
    - táng
    - hall
  - 食
    - shí
    - food
- **etymology:** Combines 堂 (táng, "hall") with 食 (shí, "food") to express eat in.
- **examples:**
  - 你想堂食还是打包？(你想堂食還是打包？)。
    - nǐ xiǎng tángshí háishì dǎbāo?
    - Do you want to eat in or take it to go?
