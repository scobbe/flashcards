## 堂食
---
- **pinyin:** tángshí
- **definition:** eat in
- **characters:**
  - 堂
    - táng
    - hall, main room; measure word for a class session, lecture; paternal cousin (same generation on the father's side)
  - 食
    - shí
    - to eat; food; meal
- **etymology:** Combines 堂 (táng, "hall, main room") with 食 (shí, "eat; food") to express "eating in the hall," i.e., dining on the premises rather than taking food away.
- **examples:**
  - 这家餐厅现在开放堂食(這家餐廳現在開放堂食)，外卖也有优惠(外賣也有優惠)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng xiànzài kāifàng tángshí, wàimài yě yǒu yōuhuì.
    - This restaurant is now open for dine-in, and there are discounts for takeout too.
  - 我们想堂食(我們想堂食)，不打包(不打包)。
    - Wǒmen xiǎng tángshí, bù dǎbāo.
    - We’d like to eat in, not get it to go.
