## 高潮
---
- **pinyin:** gāocháo
- **definition:** high tide; climax
- **characters:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high, tall; loud, high-pitched; high-level, advanced; expensive, costly
  - 潮
    - cháo
    - tide; damp, moist, humid; moisture, dampness; trendy, fashionable
- **etymology:** 高 (gāo, "high") + 潮 (cháo, "tide") literally denotes a high tide; by metaphor, the peak or crest of an experience, hence "climax".
- **examples:**
  - 潮水将在今天下午两点达到高潮(潮水將在今天下午兩點達到高潮)，请远离礁石(請遠離礁石)。
    - Cháoshuǐ jiāng zài jīntiān xiàwǔ liǎng diǎn dádào gāocháo, qǐng yuǎnlí jiāoshí.
    - The tide will reach high tide at 2 p.m. today; please stay away from the rocks.
  - 剧情在最后十分钟达到高潮(劇情在最後十分鐘達到高潮)，观众都屏住了呼吸(觀眾都屏住了呼吸)。
    - Jùqíng zài zuìhòu shí fēnzhōng dádào gāocháo, guānzhòng dōu bǐngzhù le hūxī.
    - The plot reached its climax in the final ten minutes, and the audience held their breath.
