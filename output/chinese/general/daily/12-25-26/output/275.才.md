## 才
---
- **pinyin:** cái
- **definition:** talent; only then
- **etymology:** Pictogram — a sharp peg.
- **examples:**
  - 她很有才(她很有才)。
    - tā hěn yǒu cái
    - She is very talented.
  - 我等了两个小时(我等了兩個小時)，他才来(他才來)。
    - wǒ děng le liǎng gè xiǎoshí, tā cái lái
    - I waited for two hours; only then did he come.
