## 啤酒
---
- **pinyin:** píjiǔ
- **definition:** beer
- **characters:**
  - 啤
    - pí
    - beer
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcoholic drink, liquor, wine
- **etymology:** 啤 (pí, "beer") as a phonetic borrowing from English "beer" specifies the drink, and 酒 (jiǔ, "alcoholic drink, liquor, wine") classifies it as alcohol—literally “beer-alcohol.”
- **examples:**
  - 我想点一瓶冰的啤酒(我想點一瓶冰的啤酒)，可以吗(可以嗎)？
    - Wǒ xiǎng diǎn yì píng bīng de píjiǔ, kěyǐ ma?
    - I'd like to order a bottle of cold beer—is that okay?
  - 夏天最适合在阳台上喝啤酒(夏天最適合在陽台上喝啤酒)，吹着晚风(吹著晚風)。
    - Xiàtiān zuì shìhé zài yángtái shàng hē píjiǔ, chuī zhe wǎnfēng.
    - In summer, it's perfect to drink beer on the balcony, enjoying the evening breeze.
