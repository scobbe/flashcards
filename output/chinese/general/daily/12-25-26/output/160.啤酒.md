## 啤酒
---
- **pinyin:** píjiǔ
- **definition:** beer
- **characters:**
  - 啤
    - pí
    - beer
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol
- **etymology:** Combines 啤 (pí, "beer") with 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol") to express beer.
- **examples:**
  - 下班后我们去喝啤酒吧(下班後我們去喝啤酒吧)。
    - xiàbān hòu wǒmen qù hē píjiǔ ba
    - Let's go have some beer after work.
