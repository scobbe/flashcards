## 无法(無法)
---
- **pinyin:** wúfǎ
- **definition:** unable
- **characters:**
  - 无(無)
    - wú
    - without, lacking; not; none, nothing; -less, un-
  - 法
    - fǎ
    - law, statute; method, way; rule, regulation; France
- **etymology:** 无(無) (wú, "without/not") negates 法 (fǎ, "method; way"), yielding the sense of being without a method or way to proceed, hence unable.
- **examples:**
  - 因为暴风雪(因為暴風雪)，航班无法起飞(航班無法起飛)。
    - Yīnwèi bàofēngxuě, hángbān wúfǎ qǐfēi.
    - Because of the blizzard, the flight is unable to take off.
