## 超
---
- **pinyin:** chāo
- **definition:** exceed; surpass
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: semantic 走 (zǒu, "run") + phonetic 召 (zhào, "summon").
- **examples:**
  - 本月开支超了预算(本月開支超了預算)。
    - běn yuè kāi zhī chāo le yù suàn
    - This month's expenses exceeded the budget.
  - 他在比赛中超了对手(他在比賽中超了對手)。
    - tā zài bǐ sài zhōng chāo le duì shǒu
    - He surpassed/overtook his opponent in the competition.
