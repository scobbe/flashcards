## 超
---
- **pinyin:** chāo
- **definition:** exceed; surpass
- **etymology:** Formed as a phono-semantic compound: 走 (zǒu, "to walk/run") supplies the sense of movement, while 召 (zhào, "to summon") serves as the phonetic, indicating a zh-/ch- with -ao sound. Visual idea is “moving on foot past/beyond,” yielding the sense “exceed; surpass.”
- **examples:**
  - 本月支出超出预算了(本月支出超出預算了)。
    - Běn yuè zhīchū chāochū yùsuàn le.
    - This month’s expenses exceeded the budget.
  - 她在最后一圈超了第一名(她在最後一圈超了第一名)。
    - Tā zài zuìhòu yì quān chāo le dì yī míng.
    - She overtook the leader on the final lap.
