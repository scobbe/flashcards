## 菠萝包
---
- **pinyin:** bōluóbāo
- **definition:** pineapple bun
- **characters:**
  - 菠: bō, spinach
  - 萝(蘿): luó, radish
  - 包: bāo, bag
- **examples:**
  - 我想买两个菠萝包和一杯奶茶(我想買兩個菠蘿包和一杯奶茶) (wǒ xiǎng mǎi liǎng gè bō luó bāo hé yì bēi nǎi chá, "I want to buy two pineapple buns and a cup of milk tea")
