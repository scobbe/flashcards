## 菠萝包
---
- **pinyin:** bōluóbāo
- **definition:** pineapple bun
- **characters:**
  - 菠
    - bō
    - spinach
  - 萝(蘿)
    - luó
    - vine, creeper
  - 包
    - bāo
    - to wrap, to bundle; bag, package; to include, to cover; to hire, to charter, to contract
- **etymology:** 菠 (bō, "spinach") and 萝(蘿) (luó, "vine, creeper") combine as the modern plant name for "pineapple" (both use the plant radical and serve largely phonetically), and 包 (bāo, "to wrap; bun") marks it as a bun; the name evokes the cross-hatched crust resembling pineapple skin rather than containing pineapple.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢早上买热腾腾的菠萝包(我最喜歡早上買熱騰騰的菠蘿包)，配一杯奶茶(配一杯奶茶)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuān zǎoshang mǎi rè téngténg de bōluóbāo, pèi yì bēi nǎichá.
    - I love buying a piping-hot pineapple bun in the morning, paired with a cup of milk tea.
