## 人民日报(人民日報)
---
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 民
    - mín
    - people
  - 日
    - rì
    - day
  - 报(報)
    - bào
    - report
- **etymology:** Combines 人 (rén, "person"), 民 (mín, "people"), 日 (rì, "day"), and 报(報) (bào, "report") to express people’s daily.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都读《人民日报》(我每天都讀《人民日報》)。
    - wǒ měitiān dōu dú Rénmín Rìbào
    - I read the People's Daily every day.
  - 这篇消息最先刊登在《人民日报》上(這篇消息最先刊登在《人民日報》上)。
    - zhè piān xiāoxi zuìxiān kāndēng zài Rénmín Rìbào shàng
    - This piece of news was first published in the People's Daily.
