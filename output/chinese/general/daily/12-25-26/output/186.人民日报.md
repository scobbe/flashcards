## 人民日报(人民日報)
---
- **pinyin:** Rénmín Rìbào
- **definition:** People’s Daily
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person, human being; people
  - 民
    - mín
    - the people, populace; nationality, ethnic group
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun; day; date; Japan (abbr.)
  - 报(報)
    - bào
    - to report, inform; newspaper; to repay, requite; to retaliate, avenge
- **etymology:** 人 (rén, "person") with 民 (mín, "the people") denotes the populace, and 日 (rì, "day") plus 报(報) (bào, "report/newspaper") indicates a daily report; together, the daily newspaper of the people.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天都会阅读《人民日报》(我每天都會閱讀《人民日報》)，了解中国的时事与政策(了解中國的時事與政策)。
    - Wǒ měitiān dōu huì yuèdú 《Rénmín Rìbào》， liǎojiě Zhōngguó de shíshì yǔ zhèngcè.
    - I read People’s Daily every day to learn about China’s current affairs and policies.
