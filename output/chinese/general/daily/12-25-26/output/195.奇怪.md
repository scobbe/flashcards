## 奇怪
---
- **pinyin:** qíguài
- **definition:** strange
- **characters:**
  - 奇
    - qí, jī
    - strange, unusual; rare; wonderful, remarkable; odd (number)
  - 怪
    - guài
    - strange, odd; to blame; quite, rather; monster, freak
- **etymology:** Combines 奇 (qí, "odd/unusual") with 怪 (guài, "strange/monstrous"), a synonymous binome that intensifies the sense of strangeness; 怪 also evokes "monster" connotations, lending an uncanny flavor.
- **examples:**
  - 这个味道很奇怪(這個味道很奇怪)。
    - Zhège wèidào hěn qíguài.
    - This smell is strange.
  - 奇怪的是(奇怪的是)，门自己开了(門自己開了)。
    - Qíguài de shì, mén zìjǐ kāi le.
    - What’s strange is, the door opened by itself.
