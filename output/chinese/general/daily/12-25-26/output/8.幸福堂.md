## 幸福堂
---
- **pinyin:** Xìngfú Táng
- **definition:** Taiwanese beverage chain Xing Fu Tang
- **characters:**
  - 幸
    - xìng
    - good fortune, luck; fortunate, lucky; fortunately, luckily
  - 福
    - fú
    - blessing; good fortune, luck; happiness, well-being; prosperity
  - 堂
    - táng
    - hall, main room; establishment, public hall (as a suffix); measure word for classes, lessons; paternal (in kinship terms), of the same family branch
- **etymology:** 幸 (xìng, "good fortune") combines with 福 (fú, "blessing; happiness") to intensify auspiciousness, while 堂 (táng, "hall/establishment") marks it as a venue—literally a hall of fortune and blessing. As a brand name, it evokes a place that bestows happiness and prosperity, fitting for a welcoming beverage shop.
- **examples:**
  - 我最喜欢幸福堂的黑糖珍珠鲜奶(我最喜歡幸福堂的黑糖珍珠鮮奶)。
    - Wǒ zuì xǐhuān Xìngfú Táng de hēitáng zhēnzhū xiānnǎi.
    - I like Xing Fu Tang’s brown sugar boba fresh milk the most.
  - 附近新开了一家幸福堂(附近新開了一家幸福堂)，周末要不要去排队买杯饮料(週末要不要去排隊買杯飲料)？
    - Fùjìn xīn kāi le yì jiā Xìngfú Táng, zhōumò yào bù yào qù páiduì mǎi bēi yǐnliào?
    - A new Xing Fu Tang just opened nearby—do you want to line up this weekend for a drink?
