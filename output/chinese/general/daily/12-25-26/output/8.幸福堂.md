## 幸福堂
---
- **pinyin:** Xìngfú Táng
- **definition:** Taiwanese beverage chain Xing Fu Tang
- **characters:**
  - 幸: xìng, good fortune
  - 福: fú, blessing
  - 堂: táng, hall
- **examples:**
  - 我在台北旅行时每天都去幸福堂买黑糖珍珠鲜奶(我在臺北旅行時每天都去幸福堂買黑糖珍珠鮮奶) (wǒ zài táiběi lǚxíng shí měitiān dōu qù xìngfú táng mǎi hēitáng zhēnzhū xiānnǎi, "When I was traveling in Taipei, I went to Xing Fu Tang every day to buy brown sugar boba fresh milk")
