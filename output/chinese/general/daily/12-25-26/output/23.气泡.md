## 气泡
---
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **definition:** air bubble
- **characters:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air, gas; breath, vapor; vital energy, qi; to anger, infuriate
  - 泡
    - pào, pāo
    - bubble, foam; to soak, steep; blister; to hit on, pick up (slang)
- **etymology:** 气(氣) (qì, "air/gas") modifies 泡 (pào, "bubble/foam"), specifying a bubble filled with air rather than liquid or other substances. The combination literally denotes a pocket of gas trapped in liquid.
- **examples:**
  - 这杯汽水里有很多小气泡(這杯汽水裡有很多小氣泡)。
    - Zhè bēi qìshuǐ lǐ yǒu hěn duō xiǎo qìpào.
    - There are lots of tiny air bubbles in this soda.
