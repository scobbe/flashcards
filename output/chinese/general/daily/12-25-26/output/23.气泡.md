## air bubble
### qìpào
---
- **definition:** air bubble
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas; breath; vital energy
  - 泡
    - pào
    - bubble; foam; soak; steep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 气(air; gas) + 泡(bubble; foam) = air bubble.
  - **interpretation:** Names a bubble of gas within a liquid or solid. Often seen in boiling water or carbonated drinks.
  - **simplification:** 氣 → 气 reduces strokes by streamlining the ‘steam/vapor’ components; 泡 unchanged.
- **examples:**
  - 水里升起许多气泡(水裡升起許多氣泡)。
    - Shuǐ lǐ shēngqǐ xǔduō qìpào.
    - Many air bubbles rose in the water.
  - 碳酸饮料里的气泡让人打嗝(碳酸飲料裡的氣泡讓人打嗝)。
    - Tànsuān yǐnliào lǐ de qìpào ràng rén dǎgé.
    - The bubbles in a carbonated drink make people burp.
  - 潜水员看到岩石缝隙冒出气泡(潛水員看到岩石縫隙冒出氣泡)。
    - Qiánshuǐyuán kàn dào yánshí fèngxì màochū qìpào.
    - The diver saw air bubbles coming out of the rock crevices.
### 气泡 → 气(氣)
- **definition:** air; gas; breath; vital energy
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: clouds flowing through the sky; traditional 氣 is phono-semantic: semantic 米 (“rice”) + phonetic 气.
  - **interpretation:** Originally a drawing of vapor drifting across the sky, capturing the idea of unseen flow and movement. The traditional form adds 米 as a semantic cue for ‘rice/gift of food’ with 气 providing the sound; its common senses of ‘air, breath’ were later borrowed from the older pictographic form.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by reverting to the ancient pictographic form 气 and dropping the 米 component from 氣, emphasizing the ‘air/steam’ sense while reducing strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的空气很新鲜(今天的空氣很新鮮)。
    - Jīntiān de kōngqì hěn xīnxiān.
    - The air is very fresh today.
  - 别生气(別生氣)，慢慢呼吸一口气(慢慢呼吸一口氣)。
    - Bié shēngqì, mànman hūxī yì kǒu qì.
    - Don’t get angry; slowly take a breath.
  - 他在练气功(他在練氣功)，感受体内的气(感受體內的氣)。
    - Tā zài liàn qìgōng, gǎnshòu tǐnèi de qì.
    - He is practicing qigong, feeling the qi inside his body.
### 气泡 → 气(氣) → 米
- **definition:** rice
- **pinyin:** mǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: dehusked rice kernels; horizontal stroke as a bamboo sieve; cf. 粟 (with husk); unrelated to 木/禾/来.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts dehusked rice grains being sifted on a bamboo sieve, contrasting with 粟 which shows husked kernels. Despite visual resemblance, its form is not derived from 木, 禾, or 来.
- **examples:**
  - 我买了两斤米(我買了兩斤米)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le liǎng jīn mǐ.
    - I bought two jin of rice.
  - 这家店的东北米很香(這家店的東北米很香)。
    - Zhè jiā diàn de Dōngběi mǐ hěn xiāng.
    - The Northeastern rice at this shop is very fragrant.
  - 请把米洗一洗(請把米洗一洗)，再煮饭(再煮飯)。
    - Qǐng bǎ mǐ xǐ yì xǐ, zài zhǔ fàn.
    - Please rinse the rice, then cook it.
### 气泡 → 泡
- **definition:** bubble; foam; soak; steep
- **pinyin:** pào
- **components:**
  - 包
    - bāo
    - wrap; package; bag
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 氵 (‘water’) + phonetic: 包 (OC *pruː)
  - **interpretation:** Water on the left signals a meaning related to liquids. 包 provides the sound and hints at the idea of something enclosed, fitting the notion of bubbles or foam forming in water.
- **examples:**
  - 把茶叶泡五分钟(把茶葉泡五分鐘)。
    - Bǎ cháyè pào wǔ fēnzhōng.
    - Steep the tea leaves for five minutes.
  - 他在温泉里泡了一会儿(他在溫泉裡泡了一會兒)。
    - Tā zài wēnquán lǐ pào le yīhuìr.
    - He soaked in the hot spring for a while.
  - 孩子在吹泡泡(孩子在吹泡泡)。
    - Háizi zài chuī pàopao.
    - The child is blowing bubbles.
### 气泡 → 泡 → 包
- **definition:** wrap; package; bag
- **pinyin:** bāo
- **components:**
  - 巳
    - sì
    - 6th Earthly Branch; zodiac sign Snake
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram (象形): a fetus (巳 sì) in the womb (勹), or a swaddled baby.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a swaddled infant or a curled fetus enclosed by a wrapper, conveying enclosure and bundling. The image naturally extends to actions and objects for wrapping, hence meanings like “wrap,” “package,” and “bag.”
- **examples:**
  - 我买了一个背包(我買了一個背包)。
    - Wǒ mǎi le yī gè bèibāo.
    - I bought a backpack.
  - 请把礼物包起来(請把禮物包起來)。
    - Qǐng bǎ lǐwù bāo qǐlái.
    - Please wrap the gift.
  - 快递已经把包裹送到了(快遞已經把包裹送到了)。
    - Kuàidì yǐjīng bǎ bāoguǒ sòng dào le.
    - The courier has already delivered the package.
### 气泡 → 泡 → 包 → 巳
- **definition:** 6th Earthly Branch; zodiac sign Snake
- **pinyin:** sì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram (象形): coiled snake; an alternate theory analyzes it as a foetus; graph similar to 已.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a coiled serpent, which aligns with its use as the sixth Earthly Branch associated with the Snake. Its visual similarity to 已/己 has led to historical confusion and variant explanations of the graph.
- **examples:**
  - 现在是巳时(現在是巳時)。
    - Xiànzài shì sì shí.
    - It is the Si hour (9–11 a.m.).
  - 他属蛇，对应的地支是巳(他屬蛇，對應的地支是巳)。
    - Tā shǔ shé, duìyìng de dìzhī shì sì.
    - He is of the Snake sign; the corresponding Earthly Branch is Si.
  - 农历常用十二地支记时，其中之一是巳(農曆常用十二地支記時，其中之一是巳)。
    - Nónglì cháng yòng shí’èr dìzhī jì shí, qízhōng zhī yī shì sì.
    - The lunar calendar often uses the twelve Earthly Branches to mark hours; one of them is Si.
