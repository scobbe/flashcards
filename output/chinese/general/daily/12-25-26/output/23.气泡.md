## 气泡
---
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **definition:** air bubble
- **characters:**
  - 气(氣): qì, air
  - 泡: pào, bubble
- **examples:**
  - 水里冒出了很多气泡(水裡冒出了很多氣泡) (shuǐ lǐ mào chū le hěn duō qìpào, "Many air bubbles rose up in the water")
  - 打开汽水后，杯子里迅速产生了无数气泡(打開汽水後，杯子裡迅速產生了無數氣泡) (dǎkāi qìshuǐ hòu, bēizi lǐ xùnsù chǎnshēng le wúshù qìpào, "After opening the soda, countless air bubbles quickly formed in the glass")
