## 毛泽东思想(毛澤東思想)
---
- **pinyin:** Máo Zédōng Sīxiǎng
- **definition:** Mao Zedong Thought
- **characters:**
  - 毛
    - máo
    - hair
  - 泽(澤)
    - zé
    - marsh
  - 东(東)
    - dōng
    - east
  - 思
    - sī
    - think
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think
- **examples:**
  - 他在大学里研究毛泽东思想(他在大學裡研究毛澤東思想)。
    - tā zài dàxué lǐ yánjiū máo zédōng sīxiǎng
    - He studies Mao Zedong Thought at university.
