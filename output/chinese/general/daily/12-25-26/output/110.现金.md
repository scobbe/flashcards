## 现金(現金)
---
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **definition:** cash
- **characters:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - present, current; now, at present; to appear, become visible; to show, reveal, manifest
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold, golden; metal; money, funds; surname Jin
- **etymology:** Combines 现(現) (xiàn, "present; to appear") with 金 (jīn, "gold; money") to denote money that is immediately present/visible—i.e., ready money in physical form.
- **examples:**
  - 这家小店只收现金(這家小店只收現金)，不收信用卡(不收信用卡)。
    - Zhè jiā xiǎodiàn zhǐ shōu xiànjīn, bù shōu xìnyòngkǎ.
    - This small shop only accepts cash and doesn't take credit cards.
