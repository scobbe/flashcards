## 现金(現金)
---
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **definition:** cash
- **characters:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - present
  - 金
    - jīn
    - gold
- **etymology:** 现(現) (xiàn, "to appear; on hand") signals immediacy and physical presence, while 金 (jīn, "gold/money") is the traditional metonym for currency; together they denote money that literally manifests in hand—originally metal specie paid on the spot (contrasted with drafts or credit), later extended to include paper notes.
- **examples:**
  - 我可以用现金付款吗？(我可以用現金付款嗎？)。
    - wǒ kěyǐ yòng xiànjīn fùkuǎn ma?
    - Can I pay in cash?
