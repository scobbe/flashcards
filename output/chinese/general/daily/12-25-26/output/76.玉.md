## 玉
---
- **pinyin:** yù
- **definition:** jade
- **etymology:** Pictograph of pieces of jade strung on a cord—three horizontal pieces crossed by a vertical string, with an extra dot marking a jewel. The dot also distinguishes it from 王 (wáng, "king"); as a pure pictograph, it carries meaning directly without a phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 这块玉的雕工精细(這塊玉的雕工精細)，价值不菲(價值不菲)。
    - Zhè kuài yù de diāogōng jīngxì, jiàzhí bù fěi.
    - This piece of jade has exquisite craftsmanship and is worth a lot.
  - 她戴着一只玉手镯(她戴著一隻玉手鐲)。
    - Tā dài zhe yí zhī yù shǒuzhuó.
    - She is wearing a jade bracelet.
