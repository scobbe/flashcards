## 玉
---
- **pinyin:** yù
- **definition:** jade
- **etymology:** Pictogram: a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem.
- **examples:**
  - 这块玉很值钱(這塊玉很值錢)。
    - zhè kuài yù hěn zhíqián
    - This piece of jade is very valuable.
  - 她戴着一个玉镯(她戴著一個玉鐲)。
    - tā dàizhe yí gè yù zhuó
    - She is wearing a jade bracelet.
