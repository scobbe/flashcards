## 高丽菜(高麗菜)
---
- **pinyin:** gāolícài
- **definition:** cabbage
- **characters:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - tall, high; loud; high-ranking, noble; advanced
  - 丽(麗)
    - lì
    - beautiful, pretty, elegant
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable, greens; dish, course; cuisine
- **etymology:** 高 (gāo, "high") and 丽(麗) (lì, "beautiful") together serve as the historical Chinese name for Goryeo (Korea), and adding 菜 (cài, "vegetable") yields 'Korean vegetable,' i.e., cabbage, reflecting its perceived origin/import route.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢吃高丽菜(我喜歡吃高麗菜)，尤其是清炒的(尤其是清炒的)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī gāolícài, yóuqí shì qīngchǎo de.
    - I like eating cabbage, especially when it’s stir-fried.
