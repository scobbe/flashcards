## 逸
---
- **pinyin:** yì
- **definition:** ease; leisure; escape
- **etymology:** Phono-semantic compound: left uses 辵 (chuò, "to walk") to mark motion/escape, while right is 兔 (tù, "rabbit") as the phonetic, also evoking a swiftly fleeing animal. From the idea of 'fleeing' it extended to 'ease/leisure' as being free from constraint.
- **examples:**
  - 退休后(退休後)，他过着安逸的生活(他過著安逸的生活)。
    - Tuìxiū hòu, tā guòzhe ānyì de shēnghuó.
    - After retiring, he lived a life of ease.
  - 嫌疑人逃逸了(嫌疑人逃逸了)，但很快被抓获(但很快被抓獲)。
    - Xiányírén táoyì le, dàn hěn kuài bèi zhuāhuò.
    - The suspect escaped but was soon captured.
