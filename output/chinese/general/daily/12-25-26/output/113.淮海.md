## 淮海
---
- **pinyin:** Huái-Hǎi
- **definition:** Huai-Hai region
- **characters:**
  - 淮
    - huái
    - Huai River; the Huai River region, Jianghuai area
  - 海
    - hǎi
    - sea, ocean; vast, immense; overseas, abroad
- **etymology:** 淮 (huái, "Huai River") names the river/basin, and 海 (hǎi, "sea") denotes the coastal area; together they form a coordinate toponym for the region spanning from the Huai River to the sea (the Huai–Hai region).
- **examples:**
  - 淮海地区位于中国东部(淮海地區位於中國東部)，以丰富的河网和肥沃的平原闻名(以豐富的河網和肥沃的平原聞名)。
    - Huáihǎi dìqū wèiyú Zhōngguó dōngbù, yǐ fēngfù de héwǎng hé féiwò de píngyuán wénmíng.
    - The Huai-Hai region is located in eastern China and is known for its rich river network and fertile plains.
