## 过敏
---
- **pinyin:** guòmǐn
- **definition:** be allergic
- **characters:**
  - 过(過)
    - guò
    - to pass, go past, cross; to spend (time), celebrate (a festival); to exceed, be too; experiential aspect particle indicating past experience
  - 敏
    - mǐn
    - quick, nimble; clever, quick-witted; sensitive
- **etymology:** 过(過) (guò, "to exceed; too") intensifies 敏 (mǐn, "sensitive; quick") to mean "excessively sensitive," i.e., reacting beyond normal limits. This metaphor of surpassing normal sensitivity extends to the body's immune response, yielding the sense of an allergy.
- **examples:**
  - 我对花粉过敏(我對花粉過敏)，所以春天总是打喷嚏(所以春天總是打噴嚏)。
    - Wǒ duì huāfěn guòmǐn, suǒyǐ chūntiān zǒng shì dǎ pēntì.
    - I'm allergic to pollen, so I always sneeze in the spring.
