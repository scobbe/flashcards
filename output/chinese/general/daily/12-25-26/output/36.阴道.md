## 阴道
---
- **pinyin:** yīndào
- **definition:** vagina
- **characters:**
  - 阴(陰): yīn, yin
  - 道: dào, way
- **examples:**
  - 医生建议用正确的方法清洁阴道以避免感染(醫生建議用正確的方法清潔陰道以避免感染) (yīshēng jiànyì yòng zhèngquè de fāngfǎ qīngjié yīndào yǐ bìmiǎn gǎnrǎn, "The doctor recommends cleaning the vagina properly to prevent infection")
