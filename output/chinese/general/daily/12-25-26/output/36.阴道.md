## 阴道
---
- **pinyin:** yīndào
- **definition:** vagina
- **characters:**
  - 阴(陰)
    - yīn
    - cloudy, overcast; shade, shady side; secret, covert; Yin, negative, feminine principle
  - 道
    - dào
    - road, path; way, method, means; principle, doctrine, Dao, Tao; to say, speak
- **etymology:** Combines 阴(陰) (yīn, "yin; feminine; hidden") indicating the female/inner aspect with 道 (dào, "path; channel") to express the body's feminine passage, i.e., the vaginal canal.
- **examples:**
  - 医生解释说(醫生解釋說)，阴道感染需要及时治疗(陰道感染需要及時治療)。
    - Yīshēng jiěshì shuō, yīndào gǎnrǎn xūyào jíshí zhìliáo.
    - The doctor explained that a vaginal infection needs prompt treatment.
  - 在产检时(在產檢時)，医生会检查阴道的健康情况(醫生會檢查陰道的健康情況)。
    - Zài chǎnjiǎn shí, yīshēng huì jiǎnchá yīndào de jiànkāng qíngkuàng.
    - During a prenatal checkup, the doctor will examine the health of the vagina.
