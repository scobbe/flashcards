## 荷花
---
- **pinyin:** héhuā
- **definition:** lotus flower
- **characters:**
  - 荷
    - hé
    - lotus
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
- **etymology:** Combines 荷 (hé, "lotus") with 花 (huā, "flower") to express lotus flower.
- **examples:**
  - 湖面上开满了荷花(湖面上開滿了荷花)。
    - hú miàn shàng kāi mǎn le hé huā
    - The lake surface is covered with blooming lotus flowers.
