## 荷花
---
- **pinyin:** héhuā
- **definition:** lotus flower
- **characters:**
  - 荷
    - hé
    - lotus; load, burden; to shoulder, to carry
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower, blossom; to spend, to expend; pattern, design; multicolored, variegated
- **etymology:** Combines 荷 (hé, "lotus") with 花 (huā, "flower") to explicitly denote the blossom—literally “lotus flower.”
- **examples:**
  - 夏天(夏天)，池塘里的荷花开得正盛(池塘裡的荷花開得正盛)。
    - Xiàtiān, chítáng lǐ de héhuā kāi de zhèng shèng.
    - In summer, the lotus flowers in the pond are in full bloom.
