## 繁体(繁體)
---
- **pinyin:** fántǐ
- **definition:** traditional Chinese characters
- **characters:**
  - 繁
    - fán
    - numerous, many; complicated, complex; prosperous, flourishing
  - 体(體)
    - tǐ
    - body; form, shape; style, type; system, framework
- **etymology:** Combines 繁 (fán, "numerous/complex")—evoking many strokes—with 体(體) (tǐ, "style/form") to denote the "complex-form" style of characters, i.e., traditional full forms contrasted with simplified.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢用繁体写字(我喜歡用繁體寫字)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuan yòng fántǐ xiězì.
    - I like to write in traditional characters.
  - 这个网站可以在简体和繁体之间切换(這個網站可以在簡體和繁體之間切換)。
    - Zhège wǎngzhàn kěyǐ zài jiǎntǐ hé fántǐ zhījiān qiēhuàn.
    - This website can switch between simplified and traditional characters.
