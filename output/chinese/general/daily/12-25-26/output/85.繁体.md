## 繁体(繁體)
---
- **pinyin:** fántǐ
- **definition:** traditional Chinese characters
- **characters:**
  - 繁
    - fán
    - numerous
  - 体(體)
    - tǐ
    - body
- **etymology:** Combines 繁 (fán, "numerous") with 体(體) (tǐ, "body") to express traditional chinese characters.
- **examples:**
  - 这篇文章是用繁体写的(這篇文章是用繁體寫的)。
    - zhè piān wénzhāng shì yòng fántǐ xiě de
    - This article is written in traditional Chinese characters.
