## 蒙特利尔(蒙特利爾)
---
- **pinyin:** Méngtèlì'ěr
- **definition:** Montreal
- **characters:**
  - 蒙
    - méng
    - to cover, to conceal; to receive, to be granted; to cheat, to deceive; Mongolia, Mongol
  - 特
    - tè
    - special, particular; especially; exceptional, unusual
  - 利
    - lì
    - benefit, advantage, profit; sharp; to benefit, to profit
  - 尔(爾)
    - ěr
    - you, thou; thus, so; that, such; only, merely
- **etymology:** A phonetic transliteration of "Montreal," with 蒙 (méng, "cover; Mongol") + 特 (tè, "special") + 利 (lì, "benefit") + 尔(爾) (ěr, "you; thus") approximating meng-te-li-er; meanings are incidental rather than compositional.
- **examples:**
  - 我去年去过蒙特利尔(我去年去過蒙特利爾)，那里的法语很常用(那裡的法語很常用)。
    - Wǒ qùnián qùguò Méngtèlì'ěr, nàlǐ de fǎyǔ hěn chángyòng.
    - I visited Montreal last year, and French is widely used there.
