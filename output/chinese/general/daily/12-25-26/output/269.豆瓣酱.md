## 豆瓣酱(豆瓣醬)
---
- **pinyin:** dòubànjiàng
- **definition:** doubanjiang; chili bean paste
- **characters:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 瓣
    - bàn
    - segment
  - 酱(醬)
    - jiàng
    - sauce
- **examples:**
  - 这道回锅肉里一定要放一勺豆瓣酱(這道回鍋肉裡一定要放一勺豆瓣醬)，味道才地道(味道才地道)。
    - zhè dào huíguōròu lǐ yí dìng yào fàng yì sháo dòubànjiàng, wèidào cái dìdao
    - You must add a spoonful of doubanjiang to this twice-cooked pork for it to taste authentic.
  - 做麻婆豆腐时(做麻婆豆腐時)，先把豆瓣酱炒出红油(先把豆瓣醬炒出紅油)。
    - zuò mápó dòufu shí, xiān bǎ dòubànjiàng chǎo chū hóngyóu
    - When making Mapo tofu, first fry the chili bean paste until it releases red oil.
