## 猴子
---
- **pinyin:** hóuzi
- **definition:** monkey
- **characters:**
  - 猴
    - hóu
    - monkey
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - son, child; noun suffix, diminutive; seed; master, philosopher
- **etymology:** 猴 (hóu, "monkey") with 子 (zǐ, "son, child; noun suffix, diminutive; seed; master, philosopher") forms a colloquial animal name where 子 acts as a diminutive/nominalizing suffix—literally "monkey-child," the standard term for a monkey.
- **examples:**
  - 动物园里有很多猴子(動物園裡有很多猴子)，我们去看它们吧(我們去看它們吧)。
    - Dòngwùyuán lǐ yǒu hěn duō hóuzi, wǒmen qù kàn tāmen ba.
    - There are many monkeys at the zoo; let's go see them.
