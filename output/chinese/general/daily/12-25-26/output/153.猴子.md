## 猴子
---
- **pinyin:** hóuzi
- **definition:** monkey
- **characters:**
  - 猴
    - hóu
    - monkey
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:** Combines 猴 (hóu, "monkey") with 子 (zǐ, "child") to express monkey.
- **examples:**
  - 那只猴子在树上跳来跳去(那隻猴子在樹上跳來跳去)。
    - nà zhī hóuzi zài shù shàng tiào lái tiào qù
    - That monkey is jumping around in the tree.
