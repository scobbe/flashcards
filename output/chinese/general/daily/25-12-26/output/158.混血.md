## mixed blood
### hùnxuè
---
- **definition:** mixed blood
- **pinyin:** hùnxuè
- **components:**
  - 混
    - hùn
    - mix; confuse; blend
  - 血
    - xuè
    - blood; lineage; vital fluid
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 混 (hùn, "mix") and 血 (xuè, "blood") combine to express the idea of mixed ancestry or heritage, reflecting the literal combination of bloodlines from different ethnic or racial backgrounds.
  - **interpretation:** 混 (hùn, 'mix') signifies the blending or combining of elements, while 血 (xuè, 'blood') refers to lineage or ancestry. Together, these components vividly illustrate a mixing of different ethnic or racial bloodlines, hence the concept of 'mixed blood'. The transition from a literal blend of blood to a metaphor for lineages originates from seeing blood as the carrier of genetic heritage and identity, establishing the term's meaning in the context of diverse ancestry.
- **examples:**
  - 她是美国和日本的混血。
    - Tā shì Měiguó hé Rìběn de hùnxuè.
    - She is of American and Japanese mixed blood.
  - 这(這)个(個)城市有很多混血儿(兒)。
    - Zhège chéngshì yǒu hěn duō hùnxuè ér.
    - This city has many mixed-blood children.
  - 他的父亲(親)是中国(國)人，而母亲(親)是法国(國)混血。
    - Tā de fùqīn shì Zhōngguó rén, ér mǔqīn shì Fǎguó hùnxuè.
    - His father is Chinese, and his mother is of French mixed blood.
### 混血 → 混
- **definition:** mix; confuse; blend
- **pinyin:** hùn
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 昆
    - kūn
    - descendants
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 昆 (kūn) ->
    - mix; confuse
  - **interpretation:** The semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") suggests fluidity and combination, essential in the concept of mixing or blending substances like liquids. The phonetic component 昆 (kūn) contributes the sound without affecting the meaning. Together, they represent the idea of substances being mixed or confused, akin to how waters merge and blend indistinctly.
  - **simplification:** The character 混 was simplified by retaining the 水 (shuǐ, "water") component while simplifying and simplifying other parts for ease of writing, yet its core meaning was preserved.
- **examples:**
  - 他总(總)是把事情混在一起。
    - Tā zǒng shì bǎ shìqing hùn zài yīqǐ.
    - He always mixes things together.
  - 不要把糖和盐(鹽)混淆。
    - Bùyào bǎ táng hé yán hùnxiáo.
    - Don't confuse sugar with salt.
  - 他试(試)图(圖)在群体(體)中混得很好。
    - Tā shìtú zài qúntǐ zhōng hùn de hěn hǎo.
    - He tries to blend in well with the group.

### 混血 → 混 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 混血 → 混 → 昆
- **definition:** descendants
- **pinyin:** kūn
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 比
    - bǐ
    - compare
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日 (rì, 'sun') + phonetic: 比 (bǐ, 'compare') =
    - combined light of two suns ->
    - descendants
  - **interpretation:** The character 昆 (kūn, 'descendants') uses the sun 日 (rì, 'sun') to symbolize extension or brightness, while 比 (bǐ, 'compare'), as a phonetic component, suggests reproduction or a continuation of a lineage. The combined imagery of two lights metaphorically communicates to the continuation of the family line through descendants.
- **examples:**
  - 他把祖先的遗(遺)志传(傳)给(給)了他的昆。
    - Tā bǎ zǔxiān de yízhì chuán gěi le tā de kūn。
    - He passed his ancestors' will to his descendants.
  - 家族兴(興)旺靠传(傳)承美德，昆代绵(綿)延见(見)证(證)未来(來)。
    - Jiāzú xīngwàng kào chuánchéng měidé, kūn dài miányán jiànzhèng wèilái。
    - Family prosperity relies on passing on virtues, and the descendants sustain the legacy for the future.
  - 长(長)辈(輩)辛勤耕耘历(歷)史，昆继(繼)承文化薪火相传(傳)。
    - Zhǎngbèi xīnqín gēngyún lìshǐ, kūn jìchéng wénhuà xīnhuǒ xiāngchuán。
    - The elders diligently cultivate history, while the descendants inherit and pass on the cultural legacy.

### 混血 → 混 → 昆 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒(灑)满(滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.

### 混血 → 血
- **definition:** blood; lineage; vital fluid
- **pinyin:** xuè
- **components:**
  - 一
    - yī
    - one; drop
  - 皿
    - mǐn
    - container
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 一 ('drop of blood') + 皿 ('container') ->
    - drop of blood inside a chalice for sacrificial use
  - **interpretation:** The character 血(xuè, 'blood') combines 一(yī, 'drop') and 皿(mǐn, 'container') to depict a drop of blood in a container. This reflects ancient practices of collecting blood for sacrificial purposes. The concept of lineage and bloodline extends from the association with ancestral rituals.
- **examples:**
  - 他流了一些血。
    - Tā liúle yīxiē xuè.
    - He bled a little.
  - 他的血液里(裡)流淌着(著)勇敢的血统(統)。
    - Tā de xuèyè lǐ liútǎngzhe yǒnggǎn de xuètǒng.
    - Brave lineage flows in his blood.
  - 血是一种(種)非常宝(寶)贵(貴)的生命资(資)源。
    - Xuè shì yī zhǒng fēicháng bǎoguì de shēngmìng zīyuán.
    - Blood is a very valuable life resource.
