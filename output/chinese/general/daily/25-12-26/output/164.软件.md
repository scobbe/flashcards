## software
### ruǎnjiàn
---
- **traditional:** 軟件
- **definition:** software
- **pinyin:** ruǎnjiàn
- **components:**
  - 软(軟)
    - ruǎn
    - soft
  - 件
    - jiàn
    - item; component; piece; part
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 软(軟) (ruǎn, "soft") + 件 (jiàn, "item") =
    - software
  - **interpretation:** The term 软(軟)件 (ruǎnjiàn) is used to contrast the intangible nature of software with the tangible "hardware" of a computer. Here, 软(軟) (ruǎn) refers to the softness or flexibility of the software, in contrast to the solid, physical components of a computer, while 件 (jiàn) denotes an "item" or "component," indicating that it is a part of a larger system.
  - **simplification:** 软(軟) + 件
- **examples:**
  - 我安装(裝)了软(軟)件。
    - Wǒ ānzhuāng le ruǎnjiàn.
    - I installed the software.
  - 这(這)个(個)软(軟)件很实(實)用，帮(幫)助我提高效率。
    - Zhège ruǎnjiàn hěn shíyòng, bāngzhù wǒ tígāo xiàolǜ.
    - This software is very practical and helps me improve efficiency.
  - 使用开(開)源软(軟)件，促进(進)了技术(術)创(創)新。
    - Shǐyòng kāiyuán ruǎnjiàn, cùjìn le jìshù chuàngxīn.
    - Using open source software fosters technological innovation.
### 软(軟)件 → 软(軟)
- **traditional:** 軟
- **definition:** soft
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 車
    - chē
    - carriage
  - 耎
    - nuǎn
    - soft; weak
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 車 (carriage) semantic + 耎 (phonetic, sounds like "njon") ->
    - variant with 欠 replaces 耎 ->
    - soft
  - **interpretation:** This character's association with softness may come from the use of wood in carriage construction that is soft enough to be shaped, or the exhaustion associated with operating a heavy carriage. The phonetic component 耎 was replaced with 欠, which depicts exhaustion (a man breathing heavily), fitting the idea of softness as lacking hardness or rigidity.
  - **simplification:** 軟 was simplified to 软(軟) by replacing 車 (chē, "carriage") with 车, a simpler form representing the same meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)张(張)床很软(軟)。
    - Zhè zhāng chuáng hěn ruǎn.
    - This bed is very soft.
  - 她的声(聲)音很柔软(軟)。
    - Tā de shēngyīn hěn róuruǎn.
    - Her voice is very soft.
  - 这(這)种(種)面(麵)包很软(軟)，非常好吃。
    - Zhè zhǒng miànbāo hěn ruǎn, fēicháng hǎochī.
    - This type of bread is very soft and delicious.
### 软(軟)件 → 软(軟) → 車
- **definition:** carriage
- **pinyin:** chē
- **components:**
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a carriage viewed from above; wheels on both sides + sun shade on top ->
    - depicted vertically with wheels as strokes and loading area marked by 田 (tián, "field") ->
    - 車
  - **interpretation:** The character 車(chē, "vehicle") originally depicts a carriage from an overhead perspective. The transformation into the vertical form involved simplifying the depiction of wheels and emphasizing the loading area, marked by a field symbol, creating a streamlined pictographic representation.
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 車 was simplified to 车 by reducing the number of strokes, maintaining the overall shape of the vehicle but removing intricate details.
- **examples:**
  - 我坐车(車)去上班。
    - Wǒ zuò chē qù shàngbān.
    - I take a car to go to work.
  - 这(這)辆(輛)车(車)很快。
    - Zhè liàng chē hěn kuài.
    - This car is very fast.
  - 我想买(買)一辆(輛)新车(車)。
    - Wǒ xiǎng mǎi yī liàng xīn chē.
    - I want to buy a new car.

### 软(軟)件 → 软(軟) → 耎 → 需
- **definition:** need
- **pinyin:** xū
- **components:**
  - 天
    - tiān
    - person
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 而
    - ér
    - then
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 天 (tiān, "person") + 雨 (yǔ, "rain") =
    - person getting wet in the rain ->
    - 天 corrupted to 而 (ér) ->
    - final form is 需
  - **interpretation:** The character 需 originally depicted a person getting wet in the rain, showing a need or requirement to stay dry. Over time, 天 (tiān, "person") was replaced by a corrupted form 而 (ér) during the Han dynasty, leading to the modern character.
- **examples:**

    - Xuéshēng xū nǔlì xuéxí.
    - Students must study diligently.

    - Gōngchéngshī xū jiějué wèntí, tuánduì xū xiétóng hézuò.
    - Engineers need to solve problems, and the team needs to cooperate.

### 软(軟)件 → 件
- **definition:** item; component; piece; part
- **pinyin:** jiàn
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 牛
    - niú
    - cow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人 (rén, "person") + 牛 (niú, "cow") =
    - items or parts related to or involving work with animals ->
    - item; component; piece; part
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 人 (rén, "person") and 牛 (niú, "cow") suggests activities or tasks a person might perform with cattle, illustrating the idea of a task or work unit, and thus extending to mean an 'item' or 'component' as a distinct piece of work or object.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)是一件漂亮的衬(襯)衫。

    - He buys a new piece of clothing every week.

    - Nà shì yīgè zhòngyào de xiàngmù de yī bùfèn.
