### 软(軟)件( ) → 软(軟)

- **definition:** soft
- **pinyin:** ruǎn
- **components:**
  - 車
    - chē

  - 耎
    - nuǎn
    - soft; weak
  - 欠
    - qiàn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 車 (carriage) semantic + 耎 (phonetic, sounds like "njon") ->
    - variant with 欠 replaces 耎 ->

  - **simplification:** 軟 was simplified to 软(軟) by replacing 車 (chē, "carriage") with 车, a simpler form representing the same meaning.
- **examples:**
