### Sòng Qìnglíng
---
- **traditional:** 宋慶齡
- **definition:** Song Qingling
- **pinyin:** Sòng Qìnglíng
- **components:**
  - 宋
    - Sòng
    - Song dynasty; a surname
  - 庆(慶)
    - qìng
    - celebrate
  - 龄(齡)
    - líng
    - age
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 宋 (sòng, "Song dynasty; a surname") + 慶 (qìng, "celebrate") + 齡 (líng, "age") together specifically refer to Song Qingling, an influential historical figure.
  - **interpretation:** 宋 (sòng, "Song dynasty; a surname") is a common Chinese surname. This combination refers specifically to Song Qingling, a prominent Chinese political figure and the wife of Sun Yat-sen, founder of the Republic of China. Her full name uses 慶 (qìng, "celebrate") and 齡 (líng, "age") as given name characters. This use of characters for personal names does not follow the meaning from the characters’ meanings such as "celebrate" or "age."
- **examples:**
  - 宋庆(慶)龄(齡)是中国(國)革命的领(領)袖之一。
    - Sòng Qìnglíng shì Zhōngguó gémìng de lǐngxiù zhī yī.
    - Song Qingling was one of the leaders of the Chinese revolution.
  - 在历(歷)史上，宋庆(慶)龄(齡)的贡(貢)献(獻)很大。
    - Zài lìshǐ shàng, Sòng Qìnglíng de gòngxiàn hěn dà.
    - In history, Song Qingling's contributions were significant.
  - 许(許)多人敬仰宋庆(慶)龄(齡)的精神和勇气(氣)。
    - Xǔduō rén jìngyǎng Sòng Qìnglíng de jīngshén hé yǒngqì.
    - Many people admire Song Qingling's spirit and courage.

### 宋庆(慶)龄(齡) → 庆(慶)

- **pinyin:** qìng
- **components:**
  - 廌
    - zhì

    - xīn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 廌 (antelope) + 心 (heart) =
    - auspiciousness ->
    - celebration
  - **interpretation:** The character 慶 (qìng, 'celebrate') originally combines 廌 (zhì, 'antelope') and 心 (xīn, 'heart'). The antelope, due to its beauty, symbolizes something worth celebrating or a blessing when obtained, while the heart represents emotions like goodness. This combination conveys the idea of celebration through the joy and positivity associated with possessing something precious.

- **examples:**

    - We have a holiday every National Day.
  - 他们(們)为(為)新年的到来(來)举(舉)办(辦)了盛大的庆(慶)典。
