## Luoyang
### Luòyáng
---
- **traditional:** 洛陽
- **definition:** Luoyang
- **pinyin:** Luòyáng
- **components:**
  - 洛
    - luò
    - river name; Luo River
  - 阳(陽)
    - yáng
    - sun; yang side; north bank of a river
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 洛 (luò, 'river name') refers to the Luo River, which flows through the region, and 陽 (yáng, 'sun; yang side') means 'north bank of a river' (the 'sunny side' in Northern Hemisphere geography), indicating the north of the Luo River.
  - **interpretation:** 洛 (luò, 'river name') specifically references the Luo River. The term 陽 (yáng, 'north bank of a river') traditionally denotes the sunny side, typically associated with the north side of a river bank. Thus, 洛陽 (Luòyáng) refers to the city located on the north bank of the Luo River.
- **examples:**
  - 洛阳(陽)是一个(個)历(歷)史悠久的城市。
    - Luòyáng shì yī gè lìshǐ yōujiǔ de chéngshì.
    - Luoyang is a city with a long history.
  - 每年春天，许(許)多人去洛阳(陽)赏(賞)花。
    - Měinián chūntiān, xǔduō rén qù Luòyáng shǎng huā.
    - Every spring, many people go to Luoyang to see the flowers.
  - 我们(們)计(計)划(劃)去洛阳(陽)旅行。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà qù Luòyáng lǚxíng.
    - We plan to travel to Luoyang.

    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.

### 洛阳(陽) → 阳(陽)
- **traditional:** 陽
- **definition:** sun; yang side; north bank of a river
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 昜
    - yáng
    - sunshine
  - 阜
    - fù
    - hill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 昜 (yáng, "sunshine") + Semantic: 阜 (fù, "hill") ->
    - Brightness or influence from a hill exposed to the sun, extended to mean "male" or "positive."
  - **interpretation:** Originally, 昜 (yáng, "sunshine") depicted sunlight. The addition of 阜 (fù, "hill") emphasizes exposure to sunlight on a hill, leading to the association with brightness or positivity.
  - **simplification:** 阳(陽) simplifies the traditional 陽 by replacing 昜 with 日 (rì, "sun"), reflecting the focus on sunlight in the character's meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他是家中的阳(陽)。
    - Tā shì jiāzhōng de yáng.
    - He is the male figure in the family.
  - 在这(這)场(場)运(運)动(動)会(會)上，他的阳(陽)性能量感染了队(隊)友。
    - Zài zhè chǎng yùndònghuì shàng, tā de yángnéng liàng gǎnrǎn le duìyǒu.
    - At this sports meet, his positive energy inspired his teammates.

  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - breath
  - 彡
    - xiǎn
    - rays of light
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日 (rì, "sun") + 丂 (kǎo, "breath") + 彡 (xiǎn, "rays of light") =

  - **interpretation:** The character 昜(yáng) portrays the concept of the sun's rays shining down, symbolized by 日 (rì, "sun") with 丂 representing a tree branch and 彡 depicting the sun's rays. This compound visually encapsulates the light given off by the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 日光照耀下时(時)，旗帜(幟)高昜飘(飄)扬(揚)。
    - Rìguāng zhàoyào xià shí, qízhì gāo yáng piāoyáng.

### 洛阳(陽) → 阳(陽) → 昜 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**

    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.

    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
