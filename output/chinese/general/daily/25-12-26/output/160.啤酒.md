### píjiǔ
---
- **definition:** beer
- **pinyin:** píjiǔ
- **components:**
  - 啤
    - pí
    - beer
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcoholic drink; wine; liquor
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 啤 (pí, "beer") + 酒 (jiǔ, "alcoholic drink") =
    - alcoholic drink made of malt and hops, paralleling to beer in the Western sense
  - **interpretation:** 啤 (pí, "beer") was borrowed from English or European languages as a phonetic loan for 'beer,' emphasizing the foreign introduction of this type of alcoholic beverage. 酒 (jiǔ, "alcoholic drink") is a general term for any fermented alcoholic beverage in Chinese, such as wine and spirits. Together, 啤酒 (píjiǔ, "beer") combines the foreign concept of beer with the traditional Chinese term for alcohol, linking them as a specific type of alcoholic beverage.
