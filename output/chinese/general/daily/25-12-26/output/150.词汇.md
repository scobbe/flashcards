## vocabulary
### cíhuì
---
- **traditional:** 詞彙
- **definition:** vocabulary
- **pinyin:** cíhuì
- **components:**
  - 词(詞)
    - cí
    - word; term; expression
  - 汇(彙)
    - huì
    - to collect; collection
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 詞 (cí, "word; term; expression") and 彙(匯) (huì, "to collect; collection").
  - **interpretation:** 詞 (cí, "word; term; expression") represents the basic linguistic unit, a word or a term. 彙(匯) (huì, "to collect; collection") denotes the gathering or grouping of items. Together, 詞彙 (cíhuì, "vocabulary") describes a collection or compilation of words and expressions, much like a word list or glossary that groups individual words into a comprehensive set.
  - **simplification:** 词汇
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)学(學)生( )的( )词(詞)汇(彙)量( )很( )大( )。
    - Zhège xuéshēng de cíhuì liàng hěn dà.
    - This student has a large vocabulary.
  - 老( )师(師)要( )求( )我( )们(們)每( )天( )学(學)习(習)新( )词(詞)汇(彙)。
    - Lǎoshī yāoqiú wǒmen měitiān xuéxí xīn cíhuì.
    - The teacher requires us to learn new vocabulary every day.
  - 提( )高( )词(詞)汇(彙)量( )可( )以( )帮(幫)助( )你( )更( )好( )地( )表( )达(達)自( )己( )。
    - Tígāo cíhuì liàng kěyǐ bāngzhù nǐ gèng hǎo de biǎodá zìjǐ.
    - Improving your vocabulary can help you express yourself better.

- **etymology:**

  - **description:**
    - semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 司 (sī, "manage") =
    - word or term relating to speech ->

  - **interpretation:** The character 词(詞) (cí, "word; term") combines the speech radical 言 (yán, "speech") with 司 (sī, "manage") to indicate a managed or structured form of speech, i.e., a word or term. The simplified form retains this concept, making writing faster with 讠 (yán, "speech") being a cursive form of the speech radical.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by changing the full radical 言 (yán, "speech") to its cursive form 讠 (yán, "speech"), facilitating quicker writing while retaining the original meaning.
- **examples:**
