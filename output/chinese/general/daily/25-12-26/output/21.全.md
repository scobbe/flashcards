### quán
---
- **definition:** complete; whole
- **pinyin:** quán
- **components:**
  - 入
    - rù
    - enter
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 入 (rù, "enter") + 玉 (yù, "jade") =
    - a whole piece of jade; pure jade ->
    - complete
  - **interpretation:** 全 (quán, "complete") combines 入 (rù, "enter") and 玉 (yù, "jade") to symbolize a complete or unblemished piece of jade, reflecting purity and wholeness.
- **examples:**
  - 他的家全都整洁(潔)有序。
    - Tā de jiā quán dōu zhěngjié yǒuxù.
    - His whole house is neat and orderly.
  - 他全班都参(參)加了比赛(賽)。
    - Tā quán bān dōu cānjiāle bǐsài.
    - His entire class participated in the contest.

### 全 → 入
- **definition:** enter
- **pinyin:** rù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** An arrowhead indicating 'to enter'
  - **interpretation:** The character 入(rù, 'enter') visually represents the concept of entering, with the form resembling an arrowhead suggesting movement through an opening.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)入座。
    - Qǐng rùzuò.
    - Please take a seat.
  - 学(學)生走入校园(園)，开(開)始新学(學)期。
    - Xuéshēng zǒu rù xiàoyuán, kāishǐ xīn xuéqī.
    - The students enter the campus and start a new semester.
  - 游(遊)客走入古城，探索历(歷)史。
    - Yóukè zǒu rù gǔchéng, tànsuǒ lìshǐ.
    - The tourists enter the ancient city and explore its history.

### 全 → 玉
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem ->
    - dot added to differentiate from 王 (wáng, "king") ->
    - final form 玉 (yù, "jade")
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a picture of a jade cong, emphasizing its role in rituals and its cultural significance. The image eventually included a distinguishing dot to ensure clarity from similar characters like 王, underscoring the value jade held as a material in ancient contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 她收到了一个漂亮的玉手镯(鐲)。
    - Tā shōu dào le yī gè piàoliang de yù shǒuzhuó.
    - She received a beautiful jade bracelet.
  - 皇帝的王冠上镶(鑲)嵌着(著)闪(閃)耀的玉石。
    - Huángdì de wángguān shàng xiāngqiànzhe shǎnyào de yùshí.
    - The emperor's crown was inlaid with shining jade stones.
  - 古代的玉器常常被视(視)为(為)珍贵(貴)的艺(藝)术(術)品。
    - Gǔdài de yùqì chángcháng bèi shìwéi zhēnguì de yìshùpǐn.
    - Ancient jade artifacts are often regarded as precious works of art.
