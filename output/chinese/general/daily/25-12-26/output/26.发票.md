### fāpiào
---
- **traditional:** 發票
- **definition:** invoice
- **pinyin:** fāpiào
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - issue; emit
  - 票
    - piào
    - ticket; note
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 發 (fā, "issue; emit") and 票 (piào, "ticket; note") combine to form 發票, referring to an "invoice".
  - **interpretation:** The word 發票 (fāpiào, "invoice") makes sense as it combines 發 (fā, "issue") with 票 (piào, "ticket") to signify a document that records and issues the details of a transaction, much like a ticket or note.
  - **simplification:** 发票
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)给(給)我开(開)一张(張)发(發)票。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ kāi yī zhāng fāpiào.
    - Please issue me an invoice.
  - 商店给(給)我提供发(發)票，以便报(報)销(銷)。
    - Shāngdiàn gěi wǒ tígōng fāpiào, yǐbiàn bàoxiāo.
    - The store provided me with an invoice for reimbursement.
  - 他认(認)真保存发(發)票，以备(備)日后(後)核对(對)。
    - Tā rènzhēn bǎocún fāpiào, yǐbèi rìhòu héduì.
    - He kept the invoice carefully for future verification.
### 发(發)票 → 发(發)
- **traditional:** 發
- **definition:** issue; emit
- **pinyin:** fā
- **components:**
  - 攴
    - pū
    - hand
  - 癹
    - bā
    - foot steps, radical
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Pictogram of bow firing arrow ->
    - hand 攴 added ->
    - hand evolved to 癹 (phonetic) ->
    - final form 發
  - **interpretation:** 發 initially depicted a bow firing an arrow, symbolizing the idea of emitting or releasing. The added hand component indicated an action, aligning with the sense of 'issuing or emitting'. With 癹 serving the phonetic purpose, it unified into the character 發, emphasizing the concept of sending forth or dispatching, as in 'emit' or 'issue'.
  - **simplification:** 发 is the simplified form of 發, reducing the complex 癹 component into a simpler structure while retaining pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 公司明天会(會)发(發)布新产(產)品。
    - Gōngsī míngtiān huì fābù xīn chǎnpǐn.
    - The company will issue a new product tomorrow.
  - 她的表演发(發)出耀眼的光芒。
    - Tā de biǎoyǎn fāchū yàoyǎn de guāngmáng.
    - Her performance emitted a dazzling light.
  - 请(請)尽(盡)快发(發)送邮(郵)件。
    - Qǐng jǐnkuài fāsòng yóujiàn.
    - Please send the email as soon as possible.
