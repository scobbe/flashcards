## programmer (slang)
### mǎnóng
---
- **traditional:** 碼農
- **definition:** programmer (slang)
- **pinyin:** mǎnóng
- **components:**
  - 码(碼)
    - mǎ
    - code; number; yard
  - 农(農)
    - nóng
    - farmer; agriculture; to farm
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 码(碼) (mǎ, "code; number") + 农(農) (nóng, "farmer") =
    - a humorous slang term for a programmer, suggesting someone who works hard writing lines of code like a farmer tending a field.
  - **interpretation:** The term 码(碼)农(農) (mǎnóng, "programmer") humorously implies that programmers tirelessly produce lines of code as farmers cultivate crops. This reflects the repetitive and labor-intensive nature of programming work.
  - **simplification:** 码: 碼, 农: 農
- **examples:**
  - 他是码(碼)农(農)。
    - Tā shì mǎnóng.
    - He is a programmer (slang).
  - 码(碼)农(農)加班晚，生活也不轻(輕)松(鬆)。
    - Mǎnóng jiābān wǎn, shēnghuó yě bù qīngsōng.
    - The programmer works overtime until late, and life is not easy.
### 码(碼)农(農) → 码(碼)
- **traditional:** 碼
- **definition:** code; number; yard
- **pinyin:** mǎ
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 石 (shí, 'stone') + Phonetic: 馬 (mǎ, 'horse') =
    - used for counting stones ->
    - number, code
  - **interpretation:** The character 码(碼) combines 石 (shí, 'stone') symbolizing things that can be counted, with 馬 (mǎ, 'horse'), chosen as a phonetic match. Together, they formed a character initially used for counting objects like stones, which later evolved to mean 'number' or 'code'.
  - **simplification:** The simplified character uses the exact same components as the traditional one.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)输(輸)入密码(碼)。
    - Qǐng shūrù mìmǎ.
    - Please enter the password.
  - 系统(統)自动(動)生成验(驗)证(證)码(碼)。
    - Xìtǒng zìdòng shēngchéng yànzhèngmǎ.
    - The system automatically generates a verification code.
  - 扫(掃)描二维(維)码(碼)，即可获(獲)取信息。
    - Sǎomiáo èrwéi mǎ, jí kě huòqǔ xìnxī.
    - Scan the QR code to get the information.

### 码(碼)农(農) → 农(農)
- **traditional:** 農
- **definition:** farmer; agriculture; to farm
- **pinyin:** nóng
- **components:**
  - 曲
    - qū
    - bent; curved
  - 辰
    - chén
    - field
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 曲 (qū, "bent; curved") + 辰 (chén, "field") ->
    - originally two hands holding a bundle of produce from the field ->
    - farmer bringing goods to market
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted two hands holding a bundle of produce from the field, representing the act of farming and bringing agricultural goods to market. This image conveys the hard work and process involved in agriculture.
  - **simplification:** 农(農): The traditional character 農 was simplified by reducing the complexity and strokes of its components, creating the simpler form 农 with a similar overall structure.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)民正在田里(裡)工作。
    - Nóngmín zhèngzài tián lǐ gōngzuò.
    - The farmers are working in the field.
  - 农(農)业(業)对(對)国(國)家非常重要。
    - Nóngyè duì guójiā fēicháng zhòngyào.
    - Agriculture is very important to the country.
  - 他决(決)定回家乡(鄉)从(從)事农(農)业(業)。
    - Tā juédìng huí jiāxiāng cóngshì nóngyè.
    - He decided to return to his hometown to pursue agriculture.
### 码(碼)农(農) → 农(農) → 曲
- **definition:** bent; curved
- **pinyin:** qū
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**

- **examples:**
