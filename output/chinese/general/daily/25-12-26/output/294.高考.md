## national college entrance exam
### gāokǎo
---
- **definition:** national college entrance exam
- **pinyin:** gāokǎo
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall; above average; loud
  - 考
    - kǎo
    - exam; test; to verify; to check
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 高 (gāo, "high") + 考 (kǎo, "exam") combines to form 高考 (gāokǎo, "national college entrance exam").
  - **interpretation:** 高考 (gāokǎo, "national college entrance exam") is a significant assessment in China that determines university entry for students. 高 (gāo, "high") indicates the level or importance of the exam, while 考 (kǎo, "exam") explicitly states its nature as a test.
- **examples:**
  - 高( )考( )决(決)定( )命( )运(運)。
    - Gāokǎo juédìng mìngyùn.
    - The national college entrance exam determines one's destiny.
  - 学(學)生( )努( )力( )复(複)习(習)，希( )望( )高( )考( )取( )得( )好( )成( )绩(績)。
    - Xuéshēng nǔlì fùxí, xīwàng gāokǎo qǔdé hǎo chéngjì.
    - Students study hard, hoping to achieve good results in the national college entrance exam.

### 高考 → 考
- **definition:** exam; test; to verify; to check
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **components:**
  - 耂
    - lǎo
    - old man with long hair, bent over
  - 丂
    - kǎo
    - a walking cane
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 耂 ('an old man with long hair, bent over') + 丂 ('a walking cane' originally drawn as 匕) ->
    - an old man leaning on a cane
  - **interpretation:** The character 考(kǎo, 'exam; test; to verify; to check') depicts an old man with long hair and bent over, combined with a cane. This image represents the wisdom and scrutiny often associated with elders, which conceptually extends to the idea of examining, verifying, or testing knowledge or truth.
- **examples:**

### 高考 → 考 → 耂
- **definition:** old man with long hair, bent over
- **pinyin:** lǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bent-over figure with long hair, representing an old man
  - **interpretation:** The pictogram shows a figure representing an old man with long hair, bending forward, symbolizing age and the frailty that often accompanies it.
### 高考 → 考 → 丂
- **definition:** a walking cane
- **pinyin:** kǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts exhaled air; breath or vapor
  - **interpretation:** 丂(kǎo, "exhaled air") is traditionally depicted as the shape of breath or vapor being exhaled, characterizing the concept of air being expelled from the mouth. This led to meanings associated with breath or something that has escaped into the air.
