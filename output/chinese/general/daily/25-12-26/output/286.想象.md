## imagination; imagine
### xiǎngxiàng
---
- **traditional:** 想像
- **definition:** imagination; imagine
- **pinyin:** xiǎngxiàng
- **components:**
  - 想
    - xiǎng
    - think; miss; want
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word 想像 (xiǎngxiàng) consists of two characters: 想 (xiǎng, "think; miss; want") and 像 (xiàng, "image; resemble"). 想 represents the act of thinking or forming concepts in the mind, while 像 refers to images or likenesses, which are the mental representations created in the process of imagination.
  - **interpretation:** To imagine is to 'think' (想) and create or invoke 'images' (像) in the mind. The core idea of 想, 'thinking', connects with the visual elements and similarity of 'image' (像); together, these form the act of creating mental pictures or scenarios by using one's cognitive faculties. Thus, 'imagination' signifies this mental creation and visualization, composed through these elements in our thinking process.
- **examples:**
  - 孩子们(們)的想象(像)力很丰(豐)富。
    - Háizimen de xiǎngxiànglì hěn fēngfù.
    - Children's imaginations are very rich.
  - 通过(過)阅(閱)读(讀)小说(說)，我们(們)可以进(進)入不同的想象(像)世界。
    - Tōngguò yuèdú xiǎoshuō, wǒmen kěyǐ jìnrù bùtóng de xiǎngxiàng shìjiè.
    - Through reading novels, we can enter different worlds of imagination.
  - 在艺(藝)术(術)创(創)作中，想象(像)力是非常重要的。
    - Zài yìshù chuàngzuò zhōng，xiǎngxiànglì shì fēicháng zhòngyào de.
    - In artistic creation, imagination is very important.
### 想象(像) → 想
- **definition:** think; miss; want
- **pinyin:** xiǎng
- **components:**
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
  - 相
    - xiāng
    - mutual; appearance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 心 (heart, mind) + phonetic 相 (sound) ->
    - act of thinking and visualizing in one's mind
  - **interpretation:** The character 想 involves the heart 心 (xīn, 'heart', which symbolizes emotions and thought processes) with the phonetic component 相 (xiāng, which lends the sound and adds the idea of perception or visualization). This combination suggests using the heart/mind to perceive or think deeply, thereby leading to meanings such as 'think', 'ponder', 'consider', or 'believe'.
- **examples:**
  - 我想去看电(電)影。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù kàn diànyǐng.
    - I want to go watch a movie.
  - 他在安静(靜)的地方想事情。
    - Tā zài ānjìng de dìfāng xiǎng shìqíng.
    - He is thinking about things in a quiet place.
  - 我们(們)得好好想一想这(這)个(個)问(問)题(題)。
    - Wǒmen děi hǎo hǎo xiǎng yī xiǎng zhège wèntí.
    - We need to think carefully about this problem.
### 想象(像) → 想 → 心
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a heart
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, "heart") originally depicted the shape of an actual heart, representing thoughts, emotions, and the core of a person. Over time, it has become highly stylized, but it continues to convey the notion of the heart as the center of emotions and consciousness.
- **examples:**
  - 她的心一直都在帮(幫)助别(別)人。
    - Tā de xīn yīzhí dōu zài bāngzhù biérén.
    - Her heart is always in helping others.
  - 在城市的中心，有一个(個)美丽(麗)的公园(園)。
    - Zài chéngshì de zhōngxīn, yǒu yí gè měilì de gōngyuán.
    - At the center of the city, there is a beautiful park.
  - 对(對)于(於)许(許)多人来(來)说(說)，家庭是生活的核心。
    - Duìyú xǔduō rén lái shuō, jiātíng shì shēnghuó de héxīn.
    - For many people, family is the core of life.

### 想象(像) → 想 → 相
- **definition:** mutual; appearance
- **pinyin:** xiāng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木 (mù, "tree") + 目 (mù, "eye") =
    - eye looking at a tree ->
    - original character for observing ->
    - borrowed for 'mutual'
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts an eye watching a tree, symbolizing the act of observation. This original sense of observation evolved into 'mutual' as the action involves careful consideration or attention between parties.
- **examples:**
  - 他们(們)互相支持。
    - Tāmen hùxiāng zhīchí.
    - They support each other.
  - 我们(們)需要相互理解。
    - Wǒmen xūyào xiānghù lǐjiě.
    - We need to understand each other.
  - 相互尊重是重要的。
    - Xiānghù zūnzhòng shì zhòngyào de.
    - Mutual respect is important.
### 想象(像) → 想 → 相 → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把椅子的主要材料是木。

  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.

### 想象(像) → 像
- **definition:** image; resemble
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - elephant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 亻 (rén, "person") + phonetic: 象 (xiàng, "elephant") =
    - person + likeness of an elephant
  - **interpretation:** In 像 (xiàng, "image"), the component 亻 (rén, "person") indicates that the character is related to human figures or likenesses. 象 (xiàng, "elephant") serves as a phonetic component, hinting at the word's pronunciation, as well as symbolizing something large or prominent. Together, they suggest an image or likeness that conveys significant form.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)尊雕像像活着(著)的英雄。
    - Zhè zūn diāoxiàng xiàng huózhe de yīngxióng.
    - This statue resembles a living hero.
  - 他的面庞(龐)像雕刻般精致(緻)，给(給)人留下深刻的印象。
    - Tā de miànpáng xiàng diāokè bān jīngzhì, gěi rén liúchū shēnkè de yìnxiàng.
    - His features are as exquisitely carved as a sculpture, leaving a deep impression on people.
  - 孩子的笑容像一幅生动(動)的画(畫)像。
    - Háizi de xiàoróng xiàng yī fú shēngdòng de huàxiàng.
    - The child's smile is like a vivid portrait.

### 想象(像) → 像 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Stylization of 人 (rén, "person") as a side radical to depict a person
  - **interpretation:** 亻 is a stylized form of 人 (rén, "person") used to indicate human-related meanings when combined with other components in characters as a radical. It visually represents a standing person, maintaining the core meaning of 'person' while facilitating the construction of complex characters.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个(個)热(熱)心的人。
    - Tā shì yī gè rèxīn de rén.
    - He is a kind-hearted person.
  - 这(這)座城市里(裡)住着(著)很多优(優)秀的人。
    - Zhè zuò chéngshì lǐ zhùzhe hěn duō yōuxiù de rén.
    - There are many outstanding people living in this city.
  - 每个(個)人都有梦(夢)想。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has dreams.

- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**

  - **interpretation:** The character 人 (rén, 'person') effectively uses simple lines to depict the essential form of a human, making it immediately recognizable and intuitive as meaning 'person.'

### 想象(像) → 像 → 象
- **definition:** elephant
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an elephant: ⺈ represents the trunk, 𫩏 represents the head, and 𧰨 represents the body
  - **interpretation:** The character 象(xiàng, "elephant") is a direct pictorial representation of an elephant, emphasizing its prominent features such as the trunk, head, and body to convey its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 大象是陆(陸)地上最大的动(動)物。
    - Dàxiàng shì lùdì shàng zuì dà de dòngwù.
    - The elephant is the largest animal on land.
  - 在非洲的草原上，你能看到许(許)多野生的大象。
    - Zài Fēizhōu de cǎoyuán shàng，nǐ néng kàn dào xǔduō yěshēng de dàxiàng.
    - On the African savannah, you can see many wild elephants.
  - 我第一次看到大象是在动(動)物园(園)。
    - Wǒ dì yī cì kàn dào dàxiàng shì zài dòngwùyuán.
    - The first time I saw an elephant was at the zoo.
