## spoiled child
### xiǎohuángdì
---
- **definition:** spoiled child
- **pinyin:** xiǎohuángdì
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little
  - 皇帝
    - huángdì
    - emperor
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 小 (xiǎo, "small; little") + 皇帝 (huángdì, "emperor") =
    - a metaphor for a child treated like an emperor ->
    - spoiled child
  - **interpretation:** The term 小皇帝 (xiǎohuángdì, "spoiled child") describes a child who is overly pampered and indulged by their parents, as an emperor would be by his court. This metaphor reflects the increasing trend of indulged children in modern society.
- **examples:**
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)总(總)会(會)满(滿)足他的所有要求，因为(為)他就像个(個)小皇帝。
    - Māmā zǒng huì mǎnzú tā de suǒyǒu yāoqiú, yīnwèi tā jiù xiàng ge xiǎohuángdì.
    - Mom always meets all his demands because he acts like a spoiled child.
  - 他的行为(為)总(總)让(讓)人哭笑不得，仿佛小皇帝在发(發)号(號)施令。
    - Tā de xíngwéi zǒng ràng rén kū xiào bù dé, fǎngfú xiǎohuángdì zài fāhào shīlìng.
    - His behavior always confounds people, as if a spoiled child were giving orders.

### 小皇帝 → 皇帝
- **definition:** emperor
- **pinyin:** huángdì
- **components:**
  - 皇
    - huáng
    - emperor; royal; majestic; august
  - 帝
    - dì
    - emperor; sovereign; God; supreme being
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 皇 (huáng, "emperor; royal") + 帝 (dì, "emperor; sovereign") =
    - a royal sovereign ->
    - emperor
  - **interpretation:** 皇 (huáng, "emperor; royal") originally depicts the sovereign power and high status of a ruler, often linked to celestial or divine authority in Chinese culture. 帝 (dì, "emperor; sovereign") refers to a supreme ruler, specifically reinforced by its historical connection to the divine rulers in mythology. Combining 皇 (huáng, "emperor; royal") with 帝 (dì, "emperor; sovereign") strengthens the emphasis on supreme authority, thus forming the term for the highest authoritative ruler, the emperor.
- **examples:**
  - 秦始皇是中国(國)历(歷)史上的第一位皇帝。
    - Qínshǐhuáng shì Zhōngguó lìshǐ shàng de dì yī wèi huángdì.
    - Qin Shi Huang was the first emperor in Chinese history.
  - 古代的皇帝统(統)治着(著)辽(遼)阔(闊)的帝国(國)。
    - Gǔdài de huángdì tǒngzhì zhe liáokuò de dìguó.
    - The emperors of ancient times ruled over vast empires.
  - 新皇帝登基，全国(國)一片欢(歡)腾(騰)。
    - Xīn huángdì dēngjī, quánguó yīpiàn huānténg.
    - With the new emperor's enthronement, the entire nation celebrated with joy.
