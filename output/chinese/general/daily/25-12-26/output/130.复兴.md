## revive; rejuvenate
### fùxīng
---
- **traditional:** 復興
- **definition:** revive; rejuvenate
- **pinyin:** fùxīng
- **components:**
  - 复(復)
    - fù
    - return; repeat
  - 兴(興)
    - xīng
    - rise; prosper
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 復 (fù, "return; repeat") and 興 (xīng, "rise; prosper") together convey the meaning of reviving or rejuvenating something back to its prosperous state.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 復 (fù, "return; repeat") and 興 (xīng, "rise; prosper") reflects the idea of bringing something back to a state of prosperity or importance, echoing historical efforts to restore cultural or national greatness.
- **examples:**
  - 实(實)现(現)国(國)家复(復)兴(興)需要改革，团(團)结(結)民心共创(創)未来(來)。
    - Shíxiàn guójiā fùxīng xūyào gǎigé, tuánjié mínxīn gòngchuàng wèilái.
    - Reviving the nation requires reform, uniting the people to create a bright future.
  - 文化复(復)兴(興)激发(發)创(創)新，吸引世界关(關)注。
    - Wénhuà fùxīng jīfā chuàngxīn, xīyǐn shìjiè guānzhù.
    - Cultural revival stimulates innovation and attracts global attention.
### 复(復)兴(興) → 复(復)
- **traditional:** 復
- **definition:** return; repeat
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - walk
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 彳 (chì, "walk") + phonetic 复 (fù, "repeat") ->
    - walk back and forth
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the element 彳 (chì, "walk"), suggesting movement or return, with the phonetic element 复 (fù, "repeat"), giving the sense of walking repeatedly in the same path, which embodies the idea of returning or doing something again.
  - **simplification:** The character 復 was simplified to 复 by retaining the phonetic component 复 and dropping the semantic 彳 (chì, "walk").
- **examples:**
  - 他希望能复(復)读(讀)一年。
    - Tā xīwàng néng fùdú yī nián.
    - He hopes to repeat a year in school.
  - 老师(師)要我们(們)复(複)习(習)上节(節)课(課)的内(內)容。
    - Lǎoshī yào wǒmen fùxí shàng jié kè de nèiróng.
    - The teacher wants us to review the content from the last class.
  - 每次复(複)活节(節)，全家人都会(會)团(團)聚。
    - Měi cì fùhuó jié, quánjiā rén dōu huì tuánjù.
    - Every Easter, the whole family will reunite.

### 复(復)兴(興) → 兴(興)
- **traditional:** 興
- **definition:** rise; prosper
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 舁
    - yú
    - both hands of two people
  - 同
    - tóng
    - together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 舁 (yú, 'both hands of two people') + 同 (tóng, 'together') =
    - two people lifting something together ->
    - rise, prosper
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 舁 (yú, 'both hands') and 同 (tóng, 'together') to depict a joint effort, symbolizing the action of rising or prospering through cooperation.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to remove complexity while retaining the original structure: 兴(興) uses fewer strokes for simplicity.
- **examples:**
  - 公司业(業)务(務)日渐(漸)兴(興)，市场(場)环(環)境逐步改善兴(興)。
    - Gōngsī yèwù rìjiàn xīng, shìchǎng huánjìng zhúbù gǎishàn xīng.
    - The company's business is steadily prospering, and the market environment is gradually improving.
  - 改革开(開)放使国(國)家兴(興)。
    - Gǎigé kāifàng shǐ guójiā xīng.
    - Reform and opening-up make the nation prosper.

### 复(復)兴(興) → 兴(興) → 同
- **definition:** together
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - "mouth"
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted as a pipe 筒 (tǒng, "pipe") ->
    - 口 added as decoration ->
    - interpreted as mouth 口 (kǒu, "mouth") giving orders ->
    - evolved to mean togetherness and unity
  - **interpretation:** The character 同 (tóng, "same; unity; together; common") originally depicted a pipe but later took on the symbolic meaning of unity, possibly influenced by the shared cooperation in activities like music or rigid group tasks. The addition of 口 (kǒu, "mouth") emphasizes communication and collaboration.
- **examples:**
