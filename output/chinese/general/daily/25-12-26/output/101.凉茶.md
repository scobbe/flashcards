  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic 余 (yú) ->
    - modified from archaic 荼 (bitter plant) for tea
  - **interpretation:** The character 茶 (chá, "tea") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass") as the semantic element, indicating a plant-related concept, with 余 (yú) serving as the phonetic component.
