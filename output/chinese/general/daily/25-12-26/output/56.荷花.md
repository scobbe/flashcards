## lotus flower
### héhuā
---
- **definition:** lotus flower
- **pinyin:** héhuā
- **components:**
  - 荷
    - hé
    - lotus; load; burden
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; bloom; spend (money); fancy pattern
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 荷 (hé, "lotus") combined with 花 (huā, "flower") to refer specifically to the lotus flower, which is culturally significant in Chinese tradition as a symbol of purity and enlightenment because it rises unstained from the mud.
  - **interpretation:** 荷 (hé, "lotus") refers to the aquatic plant known for its large, fragrant blossoms that emerge from muddy waters, symbolizing purity amidst adversity. 花 (huā, "flower") signifies the characteristic colorful and fragrant bloom produced by plants. Combining 荷 and 花 highlights the lotus as a type of flower that holds a special place in nature and culture for its beauty and symbolism.
