## fermented black beans
### dòuchǐ
---
- **definition:** fermented black beans
- **pinyin:** dòuchǐ
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 豉
    - chǐ
    - fermented beans; black soybeans; condiments
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 豆 (dòu, "bean") refers to the seed of leguminous plants, commonly used for consumption. 豉 (chǐ, "fermented beans") specifically refers to fermented black soybeans, a staple ingredient in Chinese cooking.
  - **interpretation:** 豆 (dòu, "bean") emphasizes the primary substance being beans. 豉 (chǐ, "fermented beans") speaks to the process and transformation into a preserved, salty form useful in cooking. Together, 豆豉 symbolizes a specific food product that results from the fermentation process where beans become an umami-rich seasoning. The concept of transformation through fermentation often leads to ingredients associated with deep flavors, hence its culinary use in adding depth to dishes.
- **examples:**
  - 豆豉是一种常见的调味料。
    - Dòuchǐ shì yī zhǒng chángjiàn de tiáowèiliào.
    - Fermented black beans are a common seasoning.
  - 我们(們)在菜里(裡)加了豆豉，味道更加丰(豐)富。
    - Wǒmen zài cài lǐ jiāle dòuchǐ, wèidào gèng jiā fēngfù.
    - We added fermented black beans to the dish, making the flavor richer.
  - 炒牛肉时(時)，豆豉可以提鲜(鮮)。
    - Chǎo niúròu shí, dòuchǐ kěyǐ tíxiān.
    - When stir-frying beef, fermented black beans can enhance the flavor.
### 豆豉 → 豆
- **definition:** bean
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a container ->
    - borrowed phonetically for the plant name
  - **interpretation:** The character 豆(dòu, 'drum') originally depicted a type of container. It was later borrowed for its phonetic value to represent the plant name 'bean', distinguishing it from a container. This transition indicates how the physical shape of the container, resembling a drum or storage pot, facilitated a phonetic borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)在课(課)堂上学(學)习(習)如何制(製)作豆。
    - Wǒmen zài kètáng shàng xuéxí rúhé zhìzuò dòu.
    - We are learning how to make drums in class.
  - 村庄(莊)里(裡)有一个(個)古老的豆，它的声(聲)音很有魅力。
    - Cūnzhuāng lǐ yǒu yí gè gǔlǎo de dòu, tā de shēngyīn hěn yǒu mèilì.
    - There is an ancient drum in the village, and its sound is captivating.
  - 音乐(樂)会(會)以豆声(聲)开(開)场(場)，让(讓)观(觀)众(眾)立刻沉(沈)浸在气(氣)氛中。
    - Yīnyuèhuì yǐ dòu shēng kāichǎng, ràng guānzhòng lìkè chénjìn zài qìfēn zhōng.
    - The concert opened with the sound of drums, immersing the audience in the atmosphere immediately.

### 豆豉 → 豉
- **definition:** fermented beans; black soybeans; condiments
- **pinyin:** chǐ
- **components:**
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - bean
  - 支
    - zhī
    - branch
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 豆 (dòu, "bean") + phonetic 支 (zhī) ->
    - fermented soybeans
  - **interpretation:** 豆 (dòu, "bean") provides the context of beans, which is crucial as the character relates to fermented soybeans, a type of food product. 支 (zhī) is used for its phonetic contribution, associating the sound with the product type.
- **examples:**
  - 我特别(別)喜欢(歡)吃豆豉。
    - Wǒ tèbié xǐhuān chī dòuchǐ.
    - I especially like eating fermented soybeans.
  - 豆豉是一种(種)常用于(於)调(調)味的食材。
    - Dòuchǐ shì yī zhǒng cháng yòng yú tiáowèi de shícái.
    - Fermented soybeans are an ingredient often used for seasoning.
  - 他做的菜总(總)是加一点(點)豆豉。
    - Tā zuò de cài zǒngshì jiā yīdiǎn dòuchǐ.
    - He always adds a little fermented soybeans to his dishes.

### 豆豉 → 豉 → 支
- **definition:** branch
- **pinyin:** zhī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand holding a drumstick
  - **interpretation:** The character 支 (zhī, 'hand holding drumstick') visually represents a hand grasping a stick-like object, suggesting support or physical action, which aligns with its meanings related to branching or supporting.
- **examples:**
  - 他支鼓槌，敲响(響)大鼓。
    - Tā zhī gǔchuí, qiāo xiǎng dà gǔ.
    - He holds the drumstick and strikes the big drum.
  - 鼓手支着(著)鼓槌，打出动(動)人节(節)奏。
    - Gǔshǒu zhī zhe gǔchuí, dǎ chū dòngrén jiézòu.
    - The drummer holds the drumstick and produces a captivating rhythm.
