## fresh milk
### xiānnǎi
---
- **traditional:** 鮮奶
- **definition:** fresh milk
- **pinyin:** xiānnǎi
- **components:**
  - 鲜(鮮)
    - xiān
    - fresh; bright; delicious; rare
  - 奶
    - nǎi
    - milk; breast; to breastfeed
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鮮 (xiān, "fresh") refers to something that is fresh and newly produced. 奶 (nǎi, "milk") denotes milk, the liquid produced by mammals. Together, 鮮奶(鮮) (xiānnǎi, "fresh milk") signifies milk that is fresh and hasn’t undergone prolonged storage or processing.
  - **interpretation:** 鮮 (xiān, "fresh") naturally applies to milk to indicate that it is newly produced and unspoiled, emphasizing quality by implying it is at its natural, unadulterated state. 奶 (nǎi, "milk") straightforwardly indicates the type of substance, thus 鮮奶 (xiānnǎi, "fresh milk") simply communicates milk that is fresh.
