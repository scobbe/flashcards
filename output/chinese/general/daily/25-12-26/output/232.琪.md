## fine jade; surname Qi
### Qí
---
- **definition:** fine jade; surname Qi
- **pinyin:** Qí
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 玉 (yù, "jade") + phonetic 其 (qí, "his") ->
    - fine jade
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 玉 (yù, "jade"), indicating the material or essence of preciousness, with the phonetic 其 (qí, "his"), to convey the sound. The specific meaning of 'fine jade' arises from the semantic component representing high-quality and valuable jade.
- **examples:**
  - 她的名字是琪。
    - Tā de míngzì shì Qí.
    - Her name is Qi.
  - 他送了一块(塊)美丽(麗)的琪玉。
    - Tā sòng le yī kuài měilì de qí yù.
    - He gave a beautiful jade stone.
  - 琪家住在山的另一边(邊)。
    - Qí jiā zhù zài shān de lìng yī biān.
    - The Qi family lives on the other side of the mountain.
### 琪 → 玉
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem ->
    - dot added to differentiate from 王 (wáng, "king") ->
    - final form 玉 (yù, "jade")
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a picture of a jade cong, emphasizing its role in rituals and its cultural significance. The image eventually included a distinguishing dot to ensure clarity from similar characters like 王, underscoring the value jade held as a material in ancient contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 她收到了一个漂亮的玉手镯(鐲)。
    - Tā shōu dào le yī gè piàoliang de yù shǒuzhuó.
    - She received a beautiful jade bracelet.
  - 皇帝的王冠上镶(鑲)嵌着(著)闪(閃)耀的玉石。
    - Huángdì de wángguān shàng xiāngqiànzhe shǎnyào de yùshí.
    - The emperor's crown was inlaid with shining jade stones.
  - 古代的玉器常常被视(視)为(為)珍贵(貴)的艺(藝)术(術)品。
    - Gǔdài de yùqì chángcháng bèi shìwéi zhēnguì de yìshùpǐn.
    - Ancient jade artifacts are often regarded as precious works of art.

### 琪 → 其
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 丌
    - qí
    - stand
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket) ->
    - 𠔝 with added 丌 (stand, phonetic) ->
    - borrowed as pronoun
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a basket (𠀠), which later included a stand (丌) beneath it to stabilize the image on bronze inscriptions, forming 𠔝. This combination was later borrowed for its sound to represent the pronoun 'his, her, its', and the basket meaning was reassigned to 箕 (jī, 'basket').
- **examples:**
  - 他把其思绪(緒)整理了一遍。
    - Tā bǎ qí sīxù zhěnglǐ le yī biàn.
    - He organized his thoughts once.
  - 其琴声(聲)如水般柔软(軟)。
    - Qí qín shēng rú shuǐ bān róuruǎn.
    - His playing is as soft as water.
  - 其计(計)划(劃)得到了同事们(們)的支持。
    - Qí jìhuà dédào le tóngshìmen de zhīchí.
    - His plan received the support of his colleagues.

### 琪 → 其 → 丌
- **definition:** stand
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a support or stand for a basket ->
    - later representation of a table
  - **interpretation:** The character 丌 (jī, "table") originally represents a stand or support and was used as a component under 其 (qí, "his") to depict a basket on a stand. This association serves as its link to the meaning of 'table', conceptualizing a flat-topped support structure. This character was an ancient form of 其 and primarily served as a physical depiction of a supporting stand.

### 琪 → 其 → 箕
- **definition:** basket
- **pinyin:** jī
- **components:**
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic 其 (qí, "his") ->
    - basket made of bamboo
  - **interpretation:** The character 箕(jī, "basket") combines 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") as the semantic component, indicating that the item is made of bamboo, with 其 (qí, "his") serving as the phonetic component to provide the sound. Originally, 其 (qí, "his") depicted a basket or container shape, and accordingly, 箕 specializes in the meaning related to a bamboo basket.
- **examples:**
  - 他用箕收集落下的叶(葉)子。
    - Tā yòng jī shōují luòxià de yèzi.
    - He uses a basket to collect fallen leaves.
  - 箕的大小非常适(適)合装(裝)米。
    - Jī de dàxiǎo fēicháng shìhé zhuāng mǐ.
    - The size of the basket is perfect for holding rice.
  - 那个(個)老人总(總)在集市上卖(賣)他编(編)的箕。
    - Nàge lǎorén zǒng zài jíshì shàng mài tā biān de jī.
    - That old man always sells the baskets he weaves at the market.

### 琪 → 其 → 箕 → 竹
- **definition:** bamboo
- **pinyin:** zhú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts bamboo stalks with leaves
  - **interpretation:** The character 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") visually represents two stalks of bamboo with leaves at the top, illustrating the appearance of bamboo plants traditionally found in nature.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)根竹坚(堅)韧(韌)且美丽(麗)。
    - Zhè gēn zhú jiān rèn qiě měi lì.
    - This bamboo is strong and beautiful.
  - 竹子在风(風)中摇(搖)曳，发(發)出沙沙的声(聲)音。
    - Zhúzi zài fēng zhōng yáo yè, fā chū shā shā de shēng yīn.
    - The bamboo sways in the wind, making a rustling sound.
