## Peng Liyuan
### Péng Lìyuán
---
- **traditional:** 彭麗媛
- **definition:** Peng Liyuan
- **pinyin:** Péng Lìyuán
- **components:**
  - 彭
    - péng
    - a surname
  - 丽(麗)
    - lì
    - beautiful; elegant
  - 媛
    - yuán
    - woman of virtue; beauty
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 彭 (péng, "a surname") + 丽(麗) (lì, "beautiful") + 媛 (yuán, "woman of virtue").
  - **interpretation:** The name 彭 (péng, "a surname") is a common Chinese surname. 丽(麗) (lì, "beautiful") conveys the sense of attractiveness, often associated with elegance and grace. 媛 (yuán, "woman of virtue") denotes a woman who embodies grace and moral integrity. Together, the name evokes an image of a noble and beautiful woman.
- **examples:**
  - 彭丽(麗)媛是一位著名的歌唱家。
    - Péng Lìyuán shì yī wèi zhùmíng de gēchàng jiā.
    - Peng Liyuan is a renowned singer.
  - 我们在音乐会上听到了彭丽(麗)媛的演唱。
    - Wǒmen zài yīnyuè huì shàng tīngdào le Péng Lìyuán de yǎnchàng.
    - We heard Peng Liyuan's performance at the concert.
  - 彭丽(麗)媛以她的音乐才华而闻名。
    - Péng Lìyuán yǐ tā de yīnyuè cáihuá ér wénmíng.
    - Peng Liyuan is famous for her musical talent.

- **components:**
  - 壴
    - zhù
    - drum
  - 彡

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 壴 (鼓, 'drum') + 彡 (visualization of the sound of drumming) =

  - **interpretation:** The character combines 壴 (zhù, 'drum') and 彡 (shān, 'visualization of the sound of drumming') to form the sound of drumming. This represents the reverberating sound produced by a drum, conveying a sense of volume and resonance which can also be metaphorically extended to imply magnification or resonance in other contexts.

### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 彭 → 壴
- **definition:** drum
- **pinyin:** zhù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a drum 壴, with a base and feathers 土 on top ->
    - stylized in the Kangxi radical 鼓
  - **interpretation:** The character 壴 (zhù, "drum") visually represents a drum with two ends, with elements contributing to its structure, such as a base and feathers. It was later incorporated into characters like 鼓 (gǔ, "drum") by adding elements that illustrate additional actions like hands holding a drumstick.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)面壴声(聲)震天动(動)地。

    - Zhànshì qiāo xiǎng zhù, shì jǐng dírén.
    - The soldier beats the drum to warn the enemy.
  - 庄(莊)严(嚴)仪(儀)式上，祭司击(擊)打壴，宣告传(傳)统(統)。
    - Zhuāngyán yíshì shàng, jìsī jī dǎ zhù, xuānguò chuántǒng.

### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 彭 → 彡

  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** 彡(shān, "rays") originally depicted strands of hair but also came to represent light rays, reflecting a change in symbolic meaning over time. ### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 丽(麗)
- **traditional:** 麗
- **definition:** beautiful; elegant
- **pinyin:** lì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a deer with antlers
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a deer with prominent antlers, symbolizing the elegance and grace associated with these animals. This image connects intuitively to the meaning of 'beautiful,' as the natural grace of a deer is often appreciated in aesthetics.
  - **simplification:** 丽(麗): The traditional character 麗 was simplified by reducing the number of strokes and components, leading to a form that resembles the upper part of 麗 and represents two people.
- **examples:**
  - 她有一头(頭)乌(烏)黑亮丽(麗)的长(長)发(髮)。
    - Tā yǒu yī tóu wū hēi liàng lì de cháng fà.

  - 这(這)幅画(畫)风(風)景如画(畫)，美丽(麗)异(異)常。
    - Zhè fú huà fēng jǐng rú huà, měi lì yì cháng.
    - This painting is picturesque and exceptionally beautiful.
  - 他的笑容非常美丽(麗)，让(讓)人感到温(溫)暖。
    - Tā de xiàoróng fēicháng měi lì, ràng rén gǎndào wēnnuǎn.

### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 媛
- **definition:** woman of virtue; beauty
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
  - 爰
    - yuán
    - therefore; thus
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 女 (nǚ, "woman") + phonetic 爰 (yuán) ->
    - elegant woman
  - **interpretation:** The character 媛 uses 女 (nǚ, "woman") as the semantic element, indicating femininity or womanhood, combined with 爰 (yuán) as the phonetic element to suggest the pronunciation. The inclusion of 女 highlights the feminine aspect, and together they convey the notion of an 'elegant woman' or 'beauty'.
- **examples:**
  - 她被称(稱)为(為)京城第一媛。
    - Tā bèi chēng wéi jīngchéng dì yī yuán.
    - She is known as the first beauty of the capital.
  - 古代诗(詩)人常赞(讚)美是一位出众(眾)的媛。
    - Gǔdài shīrén cháng zànměi shì yī wèi chūzhòng de yuán.
    - Ancient poets often praised her as an outstanding beauty.
  - 她的风(風)度如同玉一般，真是一位媛。
    - Tā de fēngdù rútóng yù yìbān, zhēn shì yī wèi yuán.
    - Her demeanor is like jade, truly an elegant woman.
### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 媛 → 女
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
- **examples:**

### 彭丽(麗)媛 → 媛 → 爰 → 爫
- **definition:** hand, claw
- **pinyin:** zhǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hand grabbing from above or the side in earlier forms ->
    - Represents a claw or hand
  - **interpretation:** The character 爫(爪) (zhuǎ, 'hand, claw') historically represented a human hand depicted from a side view, grabbing something, although it can extend its meaning to indicate a claw. This visual depiction closely associates with functions performed by a hand or a claw, making the graphical representation intuitive as both human and animal hands or claws engage in similar grasping actions.
- **examples:**
  - 野猫(貓)舒展爫，捕捉小动(動)物。
    - Yě māo shūzhǎn zhuǎ, bǔzhuō xiǎo dòngwù.
    - The wild cat extends its claws and catches small animals.
  - 雕(鵰)弯(彎)曲爫，紧(緊)握猎(獵)物。
    - Diāo wānqū zhuǎ, jǐn wò lièwù.
    - The eagle curves its claws and tightly grips its prey.
