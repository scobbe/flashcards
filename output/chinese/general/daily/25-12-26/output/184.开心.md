### 开(開)心( ) → 开(開)
- **traditional:** 開
- **definition:** open; start
- **pinyin:** kāi
- **components:**
  - 門
    - mén
    - door
  - 一
    - yī
    - one; latch
  - 廾
    - gǒng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 門 (mén, "door") + 一 (latch/pole) + 廾 (gǒng, "two hands") ->
    - hands opening a latched door ->
