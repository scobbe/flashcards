## plain; simple
### pǔ
---
- **traditional:** 樸
- **definition:** plain; simple
- **pinyin:** pǔ
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 卜
    - bǔ
    - divine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 木 (mù, 'tree') + phonetic 卜 (bǔ) ->
    - representing simplicity and plainness associated with untreated wood
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 木 (mù, 'tree') signifies untreated or natural wood, conveying the notion of something simple and unembellished. The phonetic component 卜 (bǔ) lends its sound to form the character. Together, these elements suggest the quality of something that remains plain or unworked, akin to raw wood.
  - **simplification:** As part of the Chinese Character Simplification Scheme, the character 樸 was simplified to 朴 by removing the additional components and retaining the simpler structure.

    - Tā de chuānzhuó fēnggé fēicháng pǔsù.
    - Her style of dressing is very plain.

    - Zhè jiān fángzi de shèjì hěn pǔshí.

### 朴(樸) → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把( )椅( )子( )的( )主( )要( )材( )料( )是( )木( )。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.

  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.

### 朴(樸) → 卜
- **definition:** divine
- **pinyin:** bǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a crack in an oracle bone
  - **interpretation:** The character 卜 (bǔ, 'divination') directly depicts a crack being used in oracle bones to divine future events. This literal representation naturally extends to the concept of divination.
