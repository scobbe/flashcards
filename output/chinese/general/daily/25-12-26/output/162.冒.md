## emit; give off
### mào
---
- **definition:** emit; give off
- **pinyin:** mào
- **components:**
  - 冃
    - mào
    - hat
  - 目
    - mù
    - eyes
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 冃 (original form, phonetically as 'hat') + 目 (semantic, 'eyes') ->
    - final form 冒
  - **interpretation:** The character 冒(mào, "emit; give off") combines 冃, which provides the phonetic element related to head coverings, with 目(mù, "eyes") as a semantic element. This pairing illustrates the action of something coming across the eyes or under a covering, conceptually linked with emitting or giving off, such as light or vapor emerging from obscured areas.
- **examples:**
  - 水壶(壺)开(開)始冒蒸汽。
    - Shuǐhú kāishǐ mào zhēngqì.
    - The kettle began to emit steam.
  - 这(這)个(個)火山常常冒烟(煙)。
    - Zhège huǒshān chángcháng mào yān.
    - This volcano frequently gives off smoke.
  - 煮饭(飯)时(時)锅(鍋)盖(蓋)冒出了蒸汽。
    - Zhǔ fàn shí guō gài mào chūle zhēngqì.
    - When cooking, the lid emitted steam.

### 冒 → 冃
- **definition:** hat
- **pinyin:** mào
- **components:**
  - 帽
    - mào
    - hat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hat ->
    - original form of 帽
  - **interpretation:** The character 冃 (mào, "crown") is a pictogram depicting a hat, which serves as the original form for 帽 (mào, "hat") where 冃 appears as the component ⺜. The simplicity of its design reflects its function, representing a crown or cap. Over time, as the meaning became more specific, 帽 incorporated additional components like 巾 (jīn, "cloth") to emphasize its connection to headwear.

### 冒 → 冃 → 帽
- **definition:** hat
- **pinyin:** mào
- **components:**
  - 冒
    - mào
    - to risk; to face
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth
  - 冃
    - mào
    - covering
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 冃 ('covering') ->
    - phonetic 冒 + semantic 巾 ('cloth') added ->
    - 帽
  - **interpretation:** The character 帽 originally depicted a covering with the element 冃. By adding 冒 (mào), the phonetic element, and 巾 (jīn, 'cloth'), the semantic component, it specified something that covers the head made of cloth, which logically leads to the meaning 'hat'.
- **examples:**
  - 他戴了一顶(頂)红(紅)色的帽子。
    - Tā dài le yī dǐng hóngsè de màozi.
    - He wore a red hat.
  - 这(這)顶(頂)帽子是我去年买(買)的。
    - Zhè dǐng màozi shì wǒ qùnián mǎi de.
    - This hat is the one I bought last year.
  - 她在商店看见(見)了一顶(頂)漂亮的帽子。
    - Tā zài shāngdiàn kànjiàn le yī dǐng piàoliang de màozi.
    - She saw a beautiful hat at the store.

### 冒 → 冃 → 帽 → 巾
- **definition:** cloth
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a piece of cloth with two ends hanging down
  - **interpretation:** The character 巾(jīn, "cloth") visually represents a cloth or towel, with its depiction emphasizing the idea of a piece of fabric and its functionality.
- **examples:**
  - 爸爸用干(乾)净(淨)的巾擦手。
    - Bàba yòng gānjìng de jīn cā shǒu.
    - Dad uses a clean cloth to wipe his hands.
  - 她拿着(著)一条(條)巾，轻(輕)轻(輕)擦拭眼角。
    - Tā názhe yì tiáo jīn, qīngqīng cā shì yǎnjiǎo.
    - She holds a cloth and gently wipes the corners of her eyes.
  - 老人用巾给(給)手上包扎伤(傷)口。
    - Lǎorén yòng jīn gěi shǒu shàng bāozā shāngkǒu.
    - The elderly person uses a cloth to bandage the wound on his hand.

### 冒 → 目
- **definition:** eyes
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a human eye, now represented and rotated 90 degrees ->
    - eye
  - **interpretation:** The character 目(mù, 'eye') is a pictogram representing a human eye. Its original orientation has changed over time, resembling an eye seen from a sideways view.
- **examples:**
  - 他的目光炯炯有神。
    - Tā de mùguāng jiǒngjiǒng yǒu shén.
    - His gaze is bright and full of spirit.
  - 她目不转(轉)睛，注视(視)前方。
    - Tā mù bù zhuǎn jīng, zhùshì qiánfāng.
    - She stared intently, gazing ahead.
  - 雄鹰(鷹)展翅，双(雙)目炯炯看天际(際)。
    - Xióng yīng zhǎn chì, shuāng mù jiǒngjiǒng kàn tiānjì.
    - The eagle spreads its wings, its eyes piercingly gaze at the horizon.
