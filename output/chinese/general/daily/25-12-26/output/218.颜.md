- **traditional:** 顏
- **definition:** surname Yan
- **pinyin:** Yán
- **components:**
  - 頁
    - yè
    - head
  - 彥
    - yàn
    - elegant; virtuous
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 頁 (yè, "head") + phonetic 彥 (yàn) ->
    - face/head
  - **interpretation:** 顏 (yán, "face; facial appearance") combines the semantic component 頁 (yè, "head") indicating head or face, with the phonetic component 彥 (yàn), which contributes its sound. This composition naturally leads to the meaning related to facial features or appearance, as the head is associated with one's face.
  - **simplification:** Simplified 顏 to 颜(顏) by simplifying 彥 to 彦 and 頁 to 页, reducing stroke count while maintaining the original components' roles.
