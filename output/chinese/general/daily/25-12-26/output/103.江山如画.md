## picturesque scenery
### jiāngshān rúhuà
---
- **traditional:** 江山如畫
- **definition:** picturesque scenery
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān rúhuà
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
  - 如
    - rú
    - like; as
  - 畫
    - huà
    - painting; drawing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江 (jiāng, "river") refers to a large waterway, often symbolizing nature's grandeur. 山 (shān, "mountain") signifies elevated landforms, representing stability and natural majesty. 如 (rú, "like/as") is a comparison marker, highlighting similarity. 畫 (huà, "painting") denotes an artistic depiction. Combined, this word evokes the image of a landscape so beautiful it resembles a painting, emphasizing picturesque or idyllic natural scenery.
  - **interpretation:** 江 (jiāng, "river") and 山 (shān, "mountain") together describe natural landscapes. 如 (rú, "like") establishes a simile, indicating a resemblance. 畫 (huà, "painting") suggests an artistic quality. The term implies a natural scene so strikingly beautiful it could be a crafted artwork. The idea links aesthetic appreciation of nature to the refined art of painting, underscoring both the beauty of the landscape and the creator's artistic sensibility.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)片土地江山如画(畫)。
    - Zhè piàn tǔdì jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - This land has picturesque scenery.
  - 漫步在公园(園)里(裡)，我感叹(嘆)江山如画(畫)。
    - Mànbù zài gōngyuán lǐ, wǒ gǎntàn jiāngshān rúhuà.
    - Walking in the park, I marveled at the picturesque scenery.
  - 游(遊)客们(們)被这(這)里(裡)的江山如画(畫)所吸引。
    - Yóukèmen bèi zhèlǐ de jiāngshān rúhuà suǒ xīyǐn.
    - The tourists were attracted by the picturesque scenery here.
