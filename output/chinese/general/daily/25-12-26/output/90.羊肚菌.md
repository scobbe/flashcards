## morel mushroom
### yángdùjùn
---
- **definition:** morel mushroom
- **pinyin:** yángdùjùn
- **components:**
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
  - 肚
    - dù
    - stomach; belly
  - 菌
    - jùn
    - fungus; bacterium; mushroom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 羊 (yáng, "sheep") + 肚 (dù, "stomach") + 菌 (jùn, "fungus; mushroom")
  - **interpretation:** The word 羊肚菌 (yángdùjùn) literally translates to 'sheep-stomach fungus'. This name is due to the appearance of the morel mushroom, which has a texture and pattern reminiscent of a sheep's stomach lining. This similarity in appearance is a typical way in Chinese to describe objects through familiar imagery.
- **examples:**
  - 今晚的汤(湯)里(裡)有羊肚菌。
    - Jīn wǎn de tāng lǐ yǒu yángdùjùn.
    - There are morel mushrooms in tonight's soup.
  - 农(農)家小院里(裡)发(發)现(現)了鲜(鮮)嫩的羊肚菌，山路边(邊)隐(隱)约(約)飘(飄)来(來)诱(誘)人的香气(氣)。
    - Nóngjiā xiǎoyuàn lǐ fāxiàn le xiānnèn de yángdùjùn, shānlù biān yǐnyuē piāolái yòurén de xiāngqì.
    - In a rural courtyard, tender morel mushrooms were discovered, and along the mountain path, an enticing aroma floated.
  - 在森林深处(處)，偶遇一群采(採)摘羊肚菌的农(農)人。
    - Zài sēnlín shēnchù, ǒuyù yī qún cǎizhāi yángdùjùn de nóngrén.
    - Deep in the forest, I accidentally met a group of farmers picking morel mushrooms.
### 羊肚菌 → 羊
- **definition:** sheep
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a ram's head
  - **interpretation:** The character 羊(yáng, 'sheep') visually represents the head of a sheep or ram, capturing the animal's distinctive features. It serves as a straightforward pictogram, portraying one of the common animals in ancient agrarian societies.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)场(場)里(裡)有羊。
    - Nóngchǎng lǐ yǒu yáng.
    - There are sheep on the farm.
  - 春天来(來)了，羊群在草地上吃草。
    - Chūntiān lái le, yángqún zài cǎodì shàng chī cǎo.
    - In spring, the flock of sheep grazes on the meadow.
  - 那只(隻)羊看起来(來)很温(溫)顺(順)。
    - Nà zhī yáng kàn qǐlái hěn wēnshùn.
    - That sheep looks very gentle.

### 羊肚菌 → 肚
- **definition:** stomach; belly
- **pinyin:** dù
- **components:**
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat; flesh
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic ⺼(肉) (ròu, "meat; flesh") + phonetic 土 (tǔ, "earth") ->
    - stomach; belly
  - **interpretation:** The character 肚(dù, "stomach; belly") combines the semantic component ⺼(肉) (ròu, "meat; flesh") indicating the physical body or flesh, with the phonetic component 土 (tǔ, "earth") which provides the sound. This formation emphasizes the body part 'stomach' or 'belly', drawing on the notion of the fleshy part of the body.
- **examples:**
  - 他的肚子很饿(餓)。
    - Tā de dùzi hěn è.
    - His stomach is very hungry.
  - 她感到肚子疼痛，于(於)是去看医(醫)生。
    - Tā gǎndào dùzi téngtòng, yúshì qù kàn yīshēng.
    - She felt stomach pains, so she went to see the doctor.
  - 小猫(貓)睡在我的肚子上很可爱(愛)。
    - Xiǎo māo shuì zài wǒ de dùzi shàng hěn kě'ài.
    - The kitten sleeping on my belly is very cute.

- **pinyin:** ròu
- **etymology:**

- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃肉。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī ròu.
    - I like eating meat.
  - 这(這)块(塊)肉很鲜(鮮)美，营(營)养(養)丰(豐)富。
    - Zhè kuài ròu hěn xiānměi, yíngyǎng fēngfù.

### 羊肚菌 → 菌
- **definition:** fungus; bacterium; mushroom
- **pinyin:** jùn
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 囷
    - jùn
    - storing grains
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + 囷 (jùn, "storing grains") =
    - plant-related context + phonetic element ->
    - fungus, bacterium, mushroom
  - **interpretation:** The character 简(菌) combines 艹 for its semantic association with plants and fungi, and 囷 as a phonetic component. This fusion suggests the growth or existence of fungi, fitting well with their natural habitat among vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)种(種)菌可以用于(於)发(發)酵。
    - Zhè zhǒng jùn kěyǐ yòng yú fājiào.
    - This fungus can be used for fermentation.
  - 实(實)验(驗)室培养(養)出新型菌，有望用于(於)抗生素生产(產)。
    - Shíyànshì péiyǎng chū xīnxíng jùn, yǒuwàng yòng yú kàngshēngsù shēngchǎn.
    - A new type of fungus was cultivated in the laboratory, and it is expected to be used in antibiotic production.
### 羊肚菌 → 菌 → 艹
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.

  - 春天到来(來)时(時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
