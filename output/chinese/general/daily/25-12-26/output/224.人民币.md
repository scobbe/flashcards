## Renminbi (RMB)
### rénmínbì
---
- **traditional:** 人民幣
- **definition:** Renminbi (RMB)
- **pinyin:** rénmínbì
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 民
    - mín
    - people
  - 币(幣)
    - bì
    - currency; coin; money
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人 (rén, "person") + 民 (mín, "people") + 幣 (bì, "currency")
  - **interpretation:** 人 (rén, "person") and 民 (mín, "people") combine to mean 'the people' as a collective group. 幣 (bì, "currency") refers to money or a medium of exchange. Together, 人民幣 (rénmínbì) indicates the currency issued by and for the people, embodying the concept of a national currency serving the citizens of China. This combination underscores the purpose and ownership of the currency as belonging to the populace.
- **examples:**
  - 他用人民币(幣)买(買)了一本书(書)。
    - Tā yòng rénmínbì mǎi le yī běn shū.
    - He used Renminbi to buy a book.
  - 人民币(幣)汇(匯)率最近变(變)化很大。
    - Rénmínbì huìlǜ zuìjìn biànhuà hěn dà.
    - The Renminbi exchange rate has changed significantly recently.
  - 她计(計)划(劃)去中国(國)，所以正在换(換)人民币(幣)。
    - Tā jìhuà qù Zhōngguó, suǒyǐ zhèngzài huàn rénmínbì.
    - She plans to go to China, so she is exchanging for Renminbi.

### 人民币(幣) → 币(幣)
- **traditional:** 幣
- **definition:** currency; coin; money
- **pinyin:** bì
- **components:**
  - 敝
    - bì
    - to damage
  - 巾
    - jīn
    - cloth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 敝 (bi) + Semantic: 巾 (cloth) =
    - silk given as a gift or sacrificed
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts silk or cloth, represented by the 巾 (jīn, "cloth") component, used as a currency or gift, with the phonetic element 敝 (bì, "to damage"), indicating the sound. This reflects the historical practice of presenting cloth or silk as a form of currency.
  - **simplification:** 幣 was simplified to 币(幣) by removing the 巾 (jīn, "cloth") radical and retaining only the phonetic component to denote currency in modern script.
- **examples:**
  - 人民币(幣)是中国(國)的法定货(貨)币(幣)。
    - Rénmínbì shì Zhōngguó de fǎdìng huòbì.
    - The Renminbi is the official currency of China.
  - 他收集了很多古代的硬币(幣)。
    - Tā shōujíle hěn duō gǔdài de yìngbì.
    - He collected many ancient coins.
  - 在市场(場)上，不同的货(貨)币(幣)有不同的价(價)值。
    - Zài shìchǎng shàng, bùtóng de huòbì yǒu bùtóng de jiàzhí.
    - In the market, different currencies have different values.
