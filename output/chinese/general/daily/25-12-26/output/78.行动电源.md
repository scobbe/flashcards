## power bank
### xíngdòngdiànyuán
---
- **traditional:** 行動電源
- **definition:** power bank
- **pinyin:** xíngdòngdiànyuán
- **components:**
  - 行動
    - xíngdòng
    - action; movement
  - 電源
    - diànyuán
    - power source
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 行 (xíng, "to move") refers to moving or traveling, and 動 (dòng, "action") indicates action or motion. Together, 行動 (xíngdòng, "action; movement") conveys the concept of mobility or movement. 電 (diàn, "electricity") represents electricity or electric power, and 源 (yuán, "source") denotes a source or origin. Thus, 電源 (diànyuán, "power source") signifies a source of electric power. In this compound, 行動 (xíngdòng, "movement") + 電源 (diànyuán, "power source") forms the idea of a "power source that is mobile or portable," which accurately describes a power bank.
  - **interpretation:** 行 (xíng, "to move") and 動 (dòng, "action") together denote the concept of mobility, emphasizing something that can be moved or used while moving. This connects to portable or mobile functionality. 電 (diàn, "electricity") and 源 (yuán, "source") create the concept of an electrical source. Combining these two segments, the term signifies a source of electricity that can be used on the go, perfectly capturing the concept of a power bank—a device providing portable electric power.
- **examples:**
  - 我忘记(記)带(帶)行动(動)电(電)源。
    - Wǒ wàngjì dài xíngdòngdiànyuán.
    - I forgot to bring the power bank.
  - 旅行的时(時)候，行动(動)电(電)源很重要。
    - Lǚxíng de shíhòu, xíngdòngdiànyuán hěn zhòngyào.
    - A power bank is very important when traveling.
  - 他的手机(機)没(沒)电(電)了，但幸好有行动(動)电(電)源。
    - Tā de shǒujī méi diàn le, dàn xìnghǎo yǒu xíngdòngdiànyuán.
    - His phone ran out of battery, but luckily there was a power bank.
