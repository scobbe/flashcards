## air bubble
### qìpào
---
- **traditional:** 氣泡
- **definition:** air bubble
- **pinyin:** qìpào
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas; breath
  - 泡
    - pào
    - bubble; blister
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 氣 (qì, "air; gas; breath") + 泡 (pào, "bubble; blister") =
    - a bubble filled with air, specifically an air bubble.
  - **interpretation:** 氣 (qì, "air; gas; breath") represents the air or gas that is enclosed within the bubble. 泡 (pào, "bubble; blister") specifically refers to a small, spherical thin-walled enclosure of a liquid that can trap gas inside it. Together, 氣 and 泡 denote a bubble primarily filled with air or gas, hence an "air bubble."
- **examples:**
  - 水中有很多气(氣)泡。
    - Shuǐ zhōng yǒu hěn duō qìpào.
    - There are many air bubbles in the water.
  - 气(氣)泡在阳(陽)光下闪(閃)闪(閃)发(發)光。
    - Qìpào zài yángguāng xià shǎn shǎn fāguāng.
    - The air bubbles glisten in the sunlight.
  - 他用吸管吹出气(氣)泡。
    - Tā yòng xīguǎn chuī chū qìpào.
    - He blew air bubbles with a straw.
### 气(氣)泡 → 气(氣)
- **traditional:** 氣
- **definition:** air; gas; breath
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氣 (phono-semantic: 气 for sound + 米 for meaning) ->
    - originally 'steam from cooked rice' ->
    - later 'air, flow'
  - **interpretation:** The character 氣 combines 气, representing the sound, with 米 (mǐ, 'rice'), suggesting the original meaning of steam emanating from cooked rice. Over time, the meaning shifted to encompass 'air' or 'flow', capturing the essence of movement or circulation.
  - **simplification:** 气(氣): 气 (qì, 'air') is a cursive shorthand of 氣, retaining the phonetic component but losing the semantic 米 ('rice').
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)清晨的空气(氣)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān qīngchén de kōngqì.
    - I like the morning air.
  - 呼吸新鲜(鮮)空气(氣)会(會)让(讓)人精神振奋(奮)。
    - Hūxī xīnxiān kōngqì huì ràng rén jīngshén zhènfèn.
    - Breathing fresh air invigorates the spirit.
  - 他对(對)我们(們)发(發)脾气(氣)，可能是因为(為)工作压(壓)力太大。
    - Tā duì wǒmen fā píqì, kěnéng shì yīnwèi gōngzuò yālì tài dà.

### 气(氣)泡 → 泡
- **definition:** bubble; blister
- **pinyin:** pào
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 包
    - bāo
    - wrap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 包 (bāo) ->
    - foam
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") relates to the character's meaning of foam or bubbles, which form in water. The phonetic component 包 (bāo) provides the sound. Together, they effectively convey the concept of bubbles or foam forming in water.
- **examples:**
  - 杯子里(裡)有很多泡。
    - Bēizi lǐ yǒu hěn duō pào.
    - There are many bubbles in the cup.
  - 这(這)个(個)肥皂能产(產)生丰(豐)富的泡沫。
    - Zhège féizào néng chǎnshēng fēngfù de pàomò.
    - This soap can produce rich foam.
  - 用热(熱)水泡脚(腳)可以缓(緩)解疲劳(勞)。
    - Yòng rèshuǐ pào jiǎo kěyǐ huǎnjiě píláo.
    - Soaking your feet in hot water can relieve fatigue.

### 气(氣)泡 → 泡 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
