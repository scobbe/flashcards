## continent; river island
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - administrative division
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 水 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") =
    - area surrounded by water ->
    - continent; river island
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 水 (shuǐ, "water") to signify areas associated with water bodies and 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") which provides the sound element. This construction effectively depicts landmasses surrounded by water like continents or river islands.
- **examples:**
  - 亚(亞)洲是一个(個)巨大的洲。
    - Yàzhōu shì yīgè jùdà de zhōu.
    - Asia is a vast continent.
  - 非洲气(氣)候炎热(熱)，拥(擁)有丰(豐)富物产(產)的洲。
    - Fēizhōu qìhòu yánrè, yǒngyǒu fēngfù wùchǎn de zhōu.
    - Africa has a hot climate, a continent with abundant resources.
  - 河中心的小洲为(為)水鸟(鳥)搭建栖(棲)息地。
    - Hé zhōngxīn de xiǎo zhōu wéi shuǐniǎo dājiàn qīxīdì.
    - The small river island at the center of the river provides a habitat for waterfowl.
### 洲 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水是一种(種)生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 夏天很热(熱)，喝水能解暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺，沿岸绿(綠)树(樹)成荫(蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.

### 洲 → 州
- **definition:** administrative division
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an islet in a river
  - **interpretation:** 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") was originally a pictogram representing a small piece of land or islet amidst water, illustrating the ancient practice of using distinct land areas as administrative divisions.
- **examples:**
  - 我计(計)划(劃)参(參)观(觀)几(幾)个(個)州。
    - Wǒ jìhuà cānguān jǐ gè zhōu.
    - I plan to visit several states.
  - 美国(國)的各州都有独(獨)特的风(風)景。
    - Měiguó de gè zhōu dōu yǒu dútè de fēngjǐng.
    - Every state in the United States has unique scenery.
  - 州政府负(負)责(責)管理地方事务(務)，政策由州长(長)制定。
    - Zhōu zhèngfǔ fùzé guǎnlǐ dìfāng shìwù, zhèngcè yóu zhōu zhǎng zhìdìng.
    - The state government is responsible for managing local affairs, and the policies are set by the governor.
