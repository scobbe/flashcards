## continent; river island
### zhōu
---
- **definition:** continent; river island
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - administrative division
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 水 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") =
    - area surrounded by water ->
    - continent; river island
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 水 (shuǐ, "water") to signify areas associated with water bodies and 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") which provides the sound element. This construction effectively depicts landmasses surrounded by water like continents or river islands.
- **examples:**

    - Fēizhōu qìhòu yánrè, yǒngyǒu fēngfù wùchǎn de zhōu.

### 洲 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水( )是( )一( )种(種)生( )命( )之( )源( )。

### 洲 → 州
- **definition:** administrative division
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an islet in a river
  - **interpretation:** 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") was originally a pictogram representing a small piece of land or islet amidst water, illustrating the ancient practice of using distinct land areas as administrative divisions.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )计(計)划(劃)参(參)观(觀)几(幾)个(個)州( )。
