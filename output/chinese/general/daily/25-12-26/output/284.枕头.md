## pillow
### zhěntou
---
- **traditional:** 枕頭
- **definition:** pillow
- **pinyin:** zhěntou
- **components:**
  - 枕
    - zhěn
    - pillow
  - 头(頭)
    - tóu
    - head
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") indicates the object used to support the head during sleep. 頭 (tóu, "head") specifies the part of the body the object supports, emphasizing that this pillow is indeed for resting the head.
  - **interpretation:** 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") inherently denotes an object used for support during rest, notably under the head. 頭 (tóu, "head") clarifies the focus of support, indicating the primary function of a pillow during sleep or rest. Together, they clearly refer to an item designed to support the head.
- **examples:**
  - 我找不到我的枕头(頭)。
    - Wǒ zhǎo bù dào wǒ de zhěntou.
    - I can't find my pillow.
  - 她买(買)了一个(個)新的枕头(頭)。
    - Tā mǎi le yí gè xīn de zhěntou.
    - She bought a new pillow.
  - 枕头(頭)很柔软(軟)，使我能睡得更好。
    - Zhěntou hěn róuruǎn, shǐ wǒ néng shuì de gèng hǎo.
    - The pillow is very soft, which helps me sleep better.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound

### 枕头(頭) → 头(頭) → 頁

  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 首 (head/face) + 卩 (kneeling person) ->
    - depicts a person's head and body
  - **interpretation:** Originally represented a head and its body in a kneeling posture, emphasizing “head”.
