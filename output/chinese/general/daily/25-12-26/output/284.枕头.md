- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") indicates the object used to support the head during sleep. 頭 (tóu, "head") specifies the part of the body the object supports, emphasizing that this pillow is indeed for resting the head.
  - **interpretation:** 枕 (zhěn, "pillow") inherently denotes an object used for support during rest, notably under the head. 頭 (tóu, "head") clarifies the focus of support, indicating the primary function of a pillow during sleep or rest. Together, they clearly refer to an item designed to support the head.
