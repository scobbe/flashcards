## portrait
### xiàoxiàng
---
- **definition:** portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiào
    - resemble
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; statue; resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 肖 (xiào, 'resemble') combines with 像 (xiàng, 'image') to form the word 肖像, which means 'portrait'.
  - **interpretation:** 肖 (xiào, 'resemble') conveys likeness or similarity, which gives the idea of something being similar in appearance. 像 (xiàng, 'image') suggests a visual representation of an object or person. Thus, 肖像 involves creating a visual representation ('image') that captures the likeness ('resemblance') of a person, leading directly to the meaning of 'portrait'.
- **examples:**
  - 壁炉(爐)上挂(掛)着(著)一幅精美的肖像。
    - Bìlú shàng guàzhe yī fú jīngměi de xiàoxiàng.
    - A beautiful portrait hangs above the fireplace.
  - 他为(為)艺(藝)术(術)展准(準)备(備)了几(幾)幅肖像。
    - Tā wèi yìshù zhǎn zhǔnbèile jǐ fú xiàoxiàng.
    - He prepared several portraits for the art exhibition.
  - 这(這)幅肖像生动(動)地展现(現)了她的个(個)性。
    - Zhè fú xiàoxiàng shēngdòng de zhǎnxiànle tā de gèxìng.
    - This portrait vividly showcases her personality.
### 肖像 → 肖
- **definition:** resemble
- **pinyin:** xiào
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 小(xiǎo, "small") + semantic 肉(ròu, "meat") ->
    - resemble
  - **interpretation:** 肖(xiào, "resemble") combines the phonetic element 小(xiǎo, "small") to suggest its pronunciation with the semantic element 肉(ròu, "meat") indicating a connection to physical appearance. The core meaning 'resemble' arises from the idea of something appearing similar to another, as meat can take on various appearances, enhanced by the phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)幅画(畫)很好地描绘(繪)了他的肖像。
    - Zhè fú huà hěn hǎo de miáohuì le tā de xiàoxiàng.
    - This painting excellently depicts his likeness.
  - 他长(長)得很像他的父亲(親)。
    - Tā zhǎng de hěn xiàng tā de fùqīn.
    - He greatly resembles his father.
  - 这(這)个(個)模型逼真地模仿了原物。
    - Zhège móxíng bīzhēn de mófǎng le yuánwù.
    - This model realistically imitates the original.

### 肖像 → 像
- **definition:** image; likeness; statue; resemble
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 象
    - xiàng
    - elephant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 亻 (rén, "person") + phonetic: 象 (xiàng, "elephant") =
    - person + likeness of an elephant
  - **interpretation:** In 像 (xiàng, "image"), the component 亻 (rén, "person") indicates that the character is related to human figures or likenesses. 象 (xiàng, "elephant") serves as a phonetic component, hinting at the word's pronunciation, as well as symbolizing something large or prominent. Together, they suggest an image or likeness that conveys significant form.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)尊雕像像活着(著)的英雄。
    - Zhè zūn diāoxiàng xiàng huózhe de yīngxióng.
    - This statue resembles a living hero.
  - 他的面庞(龐)像雕刻般精致(緻)，给(給)人留下深刻的印象。
    - Tā de miànpáng xiàng diāokè bān jīngzhì, gěi rén liúchū shēnkè de yìnxiàng.
    - His features are as exquisitely carved as a sculpture, leaving a deep impression on people.
  - 孩子的笑容像一幅生动(動)的画(畫)像。
    - Háizi de xiàoróng xiàng yī fú shēngdòng de huàxiàng.
    - The child's smile is like a vivid portrait.

### 肖像 → 像 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Stylization of 人 (rén, "person") as a side radical to depict a person
  - **interpretation:** 亻 is a stylized form of 人 (rén, "person") used to indicate human-related meanings when combined with other components in characters as a radical. It visually represents a standing person, maintaining the core meaning of 'person' while facilitating the construction of complex characters.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个(個)热(熱)心的人。
    - Tā shì yī gè rèxīn de rén.
    - He is a kind-hearted person.
  - 这(這)座城市里(裡)住着(著)很多优(優)秀的人。
    - Zhè zuò chéngshì lǐ zhùzhe hěn duō yōuxiù de rén.
    - There are many outstanding people living in this city.
  - 每个(個)人都有梦(夢)想。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has dreams.

### 肖像 → 像 → 象
- **definition:** elephant
- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an elephant: ⺈ represents the trunk, 𫩏 represents the head, and 𧰨 represents the body
  - **interpretation:** The character 象(xiàng, "elephant") is a direct pictorial representation of an elephant, emphasizing its prominent features such as the trunk, head, and body to convey its meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 大象是陆(陸)地上最大的动(動)物。

    - Wǒ dì yī cì kàn dào dàxiàng shì zài dòngwùyuán.
