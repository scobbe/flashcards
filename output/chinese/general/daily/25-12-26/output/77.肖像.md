### xiàoxiàng
---
- **definition:** portrait
- **pinyin:** xiàoxiàng
- **components:**
  - 肖
    - xiào
    - resemble
  - 像
    - xiàng
    - image; likeness; statue; resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 肖 (xiào, 'resemble') combines with 像 (xiàng, 'image') to form the word 肖像, which means 'portrait'.
  - **interpretation:** 肖 (xiào, 'resemble') conveys likeness or similarity, which gives the idea of something being similar in appearance. 像 (xiàng, 'image') suggests a visual representation of an object or person. Thus, 肖像 involves creating a visual representation ('image') that captures the likeness ('resemblance') of a person, leading directly to the meaning of 'portrait'.
- **examples:**

    - A beautiful portrait hangs above the fireplace.
  - 他( )为(為)艺(藝)术(術)展( )准(準)备(備)了( )几(幾)幅( )肖( )像( )。
    - Tā wèi yìshù zhǎn zhǔnbèile jǐ fú xiàoxiàng.
