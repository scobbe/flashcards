## exercise; sports
### yùndòng
---
- **traditional:** 運動
- **definition:** exercise; sports
- **pinyin:** yùndòng
- **components:**
  - 运(運)
    - yùn
    - to transport; fortune; motion; luck
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - to move; movement; action; motion
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 運 (yùn, "to transport; fortune") refers to movement or transportation, which extends to imply activity. 動 (dòng, "to move; movement") reinforces the theme of motion or activity.
  - **interpretation:** 運 (yùn, "to transport; fortune") originally meant the act of carrying or moving something from one place to another, thus denoting activity and motion. 動 (dòng, "to move; movement") signifies physical movement or change in position, adding to the idea of actively engaging in physical activities. Together, the term 運動 encompasses physical exercises and sports as activities involving coordinated movement and effort.
