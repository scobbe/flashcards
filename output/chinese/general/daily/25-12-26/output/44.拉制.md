## metal drawing process
### lāzhì
---
- **traditional:** 拉製
- **definition:** metal drawing process
- **pinyin:** lāzhì
- **components:**
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; draw out
  - 制(製)
    - zhì
    - make; manufacture; control; system
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 拉 (lā, "pull") + 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") comes together to refer to the action of pulling or drawing a material, specifically in a manufacturing context.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 拉 (lā, "pull") and 制(製) (zhì, "make or manufacture") reflects a process in manufacturing where metal is drawn out through pulling, a common technique in the fabrication of metal products.
- **examples:**
  - 现(現)代工厂(廠)运(運)用拉制(製)技术(術)，制(製)造精密金属(屬)线(線)材。
    - Xiàndài gōngchǎng yùnyòng lāzhì jìshù, zhìzào jīngmì jīnshǔ xiàncái。
    - Modern factories use the metal drawing process to manufacture precision metal wires.
  - 借助拉制(製)工艺(藝)，金属(屬)板材变(變)得更加坚(堅)韧(韌)。
    - Jièzhù lāzhì gōngyì, jīnshǔ bǎncái biàn de gèngjiā jiānrèn。
    - With the help of the metal drawing process, metal plates become even tougher.
### 拉制(製) → 拉
- **definition:** pull; drag; draw out
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 手 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 立 (lì) ->
    - pull
  - **interpretation:** The character 拉 (lā, "pull; drag; draw out") combines 手 (shǒu, "hand") as the semantic element, suggesting an action performed with the hand, with 立 (lì) as the phonetic component, providing the 'lā' sound. The intuitive image is a hand performing the action of pulling or dragging.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)帮(幫)我把椅子拉过(過)来(來)。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ bǎ yǐzi lā guòlái.
    - Please help me pull the chair over.
  - 他喜欢(歡)在地上拉着(著)玩具车(車)。
    - Tā xǐhuān zài dìshàng lā zhe wánjùchē.
    - He likes to drag toy cars on the floor.
  - 我们(們)要用力把船拉到岸边(邊)。
    - Wǒmen yào yònglì bǎ chuán lā dào ànbiān.
    - We need to pull the boat to the shore with force.

### 拉制(製) → 拉 → 手
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand with fingers; top stroke is a bent over middle finger, horizontal strokes represent fingers
  - **interpretation:** The character 手 (shǒu, "hand") is a direct pictorial representation of a human hand with all five fingers, which distinguishes it from similar characters representing claws or simplified fingers. This visually conveys its meaning as a hand clearly.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手写(寫)字。
    - Tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - He writes with his hand.
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)牵(牽)着(著)我的手走路。
    - Māmā qiān zhe wǒ de shǒu zǒu lù.
    - Mom holds my hand while walking.
  - 他的手很温(溫)暖，带(帶)给(給)我力量。
    - Tā de shǒu hěn wēn nuǎn, dài gěi wǒ lìliàng.
    - His hand is very warm, giving me strength.

### 拉制(製) → 制(製)
- **traditional:** 製
- **definition:** make; manufacture; control; system
- **pinyin:** zhì
- **components:**
  - 衣
    - yī
    - clothes
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound and phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Phonetic: 制 (zhì, 'to cut out; to make') + Semantic: 衣 (yī, 'clothes') =
    - making clothes ->
    - manufacture/control
  - **interpretation:** The character 制(製) combines the idea of 制 (zhì, 'to cut out; to make'), which indicates an action, with 衣 (yī, 'clothes'), representing the product of that action. This suggests an act of crafting or creating clothing, which extends metaphorically to making or controlling systems and processes.
- **examples:**
  - 工厂(廠)制(製)鞋。
    - Gōngchǎng zhì xié.
    - The factory manufactures shoes.
  - 他们(們)制(製)订(訂)计(計)划(劃)，确(確)保生产(產)顺(順)利。
    - Tāmen zhìdìng jìhuà, quèbǎo shēngchǎn shùnlì.
    - They formulate plans to ensure smooth production.
  - 研究人员(員)制(製)备(備)新材料，推动(動)科技进(進)步。
    - Yánjiū rényuán zhìbèi xīn cáiliào, tuīdòng kējì jìnbù.
    - Researchers prepare new materials, driving technological progress.
