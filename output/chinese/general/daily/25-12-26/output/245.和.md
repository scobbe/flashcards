## gentle; harmonious; and
### hé
---
- **definition:** gentle; harmonious; and
- **pinyin:** hé
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 禾 (hé, 'grain') + semantic 口 (kǒu, 'mouth') ->
    - harmony
  - **interpretation:** The character 和(hé, 'gentle; harmonious; and') combines the phonetic component 禾(hé, 'grain') with the semantic component 口(kǒu, 'mouth'). The idea of harmony arises from the combination of grain, representing sustenance and peace, with the mouth, indicating communication or speech. In historical contexts, 和 was related to musical harmony, as indicated by the original version 龢 representing harmony in music. Overall, harmony reflects a state of balance, whether in sound, communication, or social relations.

    - Wǒmen hé jiārén yīqǐ lǚxíng.

    - Tā xìnggé wēnhé, shēn shòu dàjiā xǐ'ài.

  - 书(書)和( )笔(筆)都( )是( )学(學)习(習)的( )工( )具( )。

### 和 → 禾
- **definition:** grain
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stalk of grain or cereal plant
  - **interpretation:** The character 禾 (hé, 'grain') visually represents a plant with roots and leaves, focusing on the appearance of staple crops like rice or millet. This clearly conveys its meaning as it resembles the actual shape of a plant grown for its grain.

### 和 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
