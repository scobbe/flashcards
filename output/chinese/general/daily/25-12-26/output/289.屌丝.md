### diǎosī

- **traditional:** 屌絲
- **definition:** loser (internet slang)
- **pinyin:** diǎosī
- **components:**
  - 屌
    - diǎo
    - male genitalia; slang for cocky
  - 丝(絲)
    - sī

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 屌 (diǎo, "male genitalia/slang for cocky/), 丝(絲) (sī, "silk/thread")
  - **interpretation:** 屌( )丝(絲) (diǎosī) is a slang term originating from Chinese internet culture, humorously referring to someone seen as a 'loser' or lacking in success by the traditional standards of wealth, prestige, or relationships. It juxtaposes the raw and blunt connotation of 屌 (diǎo, "male genitalia/slang for cocky") with the soft, delicate imagery of 丝(絲) (sī, "silk/thread"), creating an ironic label for individuals who might not meet conventional success criteria but embrace their circumstances with humor and defiance.
  - **simplification:** 丝→絲
- **examples:**

### 屌( )丝(絲) → 丝(絲)
- **traditional:** 絲
- **definition:** silk; thread
- **pinyin:** sī
- **components:**
  - 糸

  - **description:**
