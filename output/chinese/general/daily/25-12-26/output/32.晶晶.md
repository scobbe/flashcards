## sparkling; gleaming
### jīngjīng
---
- **definition:** sparkling; gleaming
- **pinyin:** jīngjīng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晶 (jīng, "sparkle") repeated for emphasis, indicating something that is sparkling or gleaming.
  - **interpretation:** The repetition of 晶 (jīng, "sparkle") emphasizes the brightness or gleaming quality of an object or light. In Chinese, repeating a character can intensify the meaning, suggesting something that is shining or reflecting light vibrantly.
- **examples:**
  - 夜空布(佈)满(滿)星辰，晶晶光芒点(點)缀(綴)其间(間)。
    - Yè kōng bù mǎn xīngchén, jīngjīng guāngmáng diǎn zhuì qí jiān.
    - The night sky was filled with stars, and sparkling light decorated it.
  - 湖水泛起晶晶波纹(紋)，给(給)寂静(靜)的夜添了生机(機)。
    - Hú shuǐ fàn qǐ jīngjīng bōwén, gěi jìjìng de yè tiān le shēngjī.
    - The lake water rippled with sparkling waves, adding life to the quiet night.
  - 那颗(顆)钻(鑽)石晶晶夺(奪)目。
    - Nà kē zuànshí jīngjīng duó mù.
    - That diamond dazzled with a sparkling brilliance.
### 晶晶 → 晶
- **definition:** sparkle; sparkle; crystal; bright
- **pinyin:** jīng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 日 (rì, "sun") x 3 =
    - multiple suns shining brightly ->
    - bright; sparkle; crystal
  - **interpretation:** The character 晶(jīng, "sparkle; crystal; bright") is formed by three 日 (rì, "sun") characters combined to visually represent multiple suns. This suggests an intense brilliance or sparkle, much like how multiple suns would create a radiant or crystal-like shine.
- **examples:**
  - 她的眼睛像晶石一样(樣)明亮。
    - Tā de yǎnjīng xiàng jīngshí yīyàng míngliàng.
    - Her eyes are as bright as crystals.
  - 窗户(戶)上结(結)了许(許)多冰晶。
    - Chuānghù shàng jié le xǔduō bīngjīng.
    - Many ice crystals have formed on the window.
  - 这(這)个(個)城市的夜景闪(閃)烁(爍)着(著)晶光。
    - Zhège chéngshì de yèjǐng shǎnshuò zhe jīngguāng.
    - The night view of the city sparkles brightly.

### 晶晶 → 晶 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒(灑)满(滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.
