## cast amorous glances
### sòng qiūbō
---
- **definition:** cast amorous glances
- **pinyin:** sòng qiūbō
- **components:**
  - 送
    - sòng
    - send; deliver; give as a gift; see off
  - 秋波
    - qiūbō
    - autumn wave; flirtatious glance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 送 (sòng, "send") + 秋波 (qiūbō, "autumn wave")
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 送秋波 (sòng qiūbō) metaphorically means to cast amorous glances. 秋波 (qiūbō, "autumn wave") refers to the soft and gentle look in one's eyes, evoking the idea of a calm autumn scene or a gentle wave, often used in poetry to describe a flirtatious or enchanting gaze. By combining it with 送 (sòng, "send"), it creates the meaning of sending or directing such gazes toward someone.
- **examples:**
  - 她在晚会(會)中送秋波，让(讓)他心动(動)。
    - Tā zài wǎnhuì zhōng sòng qiūbō, ràng tā xīndòng.
    - She cast amorous glances during the party, making his heart flutter.
  - 帅(帥)哥见(見)到她便送秋波，悄悄表达(達)心意。
    - Shuài gē jiàn dào tā biàn sòng qiūbō, qiāoqiāo biǎodá xīnyì.
    - The handsome guy, upon seeing her, cast amorous glances, subtly expressing his feelings.
  - 在音乐(樂)会(會)间(間)隙，他悄悄送秋波，引起了她的注意。
    - Zài yīnyuèhuì jiànxì, tā qiāoqiāo sòng qiūbō, yǐnqǐ le tā de zhùyì.
    - During the intermission of the concert, he quietly cast amorous glances, catching her attention.

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flames rising from a fire
  - **interpretation:** The character 火 (huǒ, "fire") visually represents flames flickering upwards, which intuitively conveys the essence of fire. This simple depiction is an effective way to capture the dynamic and essential nature of fire.
- **examples:**
  - 火在壁炉(爐)中熊熊燃烧(燒)。
    - Huǒ zài bìlú zhōng xióngxióng ránshāo.
    - The fire burns fiercely in the fireplace.
  - 野外露营(營)时(時)，火能驱(驅)走寒冷。
    - Yěwài lùyíng shí, huǒ néng qūzǒu hánlěng.
    - When camping outdoors, the fire can drive away the cold.
  - 遇到火灾(災)时(時)，迅速疏散最为(為)重要。
    - Yù dào huǒzāi shí, xùnsù shū sàn zuìwéi zhòngyào.
    - In case of a fire, evacuating quickly is of utmost importance.

### 送秋波 → 秋波 → 波
- **definition:** wave; ripple; undulation
- **pinyin:** bō
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 皮
    - pí
    - surface
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 皮 (pí, "surface") ->
    - wave
  - **interpretation:** The character 波(bō, "wave") combines 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") as the semantic component, indicating its association with bodies of water, and 皮 (pí, "surface") as the phonetic component. The image evokes the concept of water moving across the surface, characteristic of a wave.
- **examples:**
  - 海浪卷起了大波。
    - Hǎilàng juǎn qǐle dà bō.
    - The ocean waves rolled into big waves.
  - 湖面上泛起了细(細)小的波纹(紋)。
    - Húmiàn shàng fàn qǐle xì xiǎo de bōwén.
    - Small ripples appeared on the surface of the lake.
  - 心如止水，波澜(瀾)不惊(驚)。
    - Xīn rú zhǐshuǐ, bōlán bù jīng.
    - The heart is like still water, unruffled by waves.

### 送秋波 → 秋波 → 波 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 送秋波 → 秋波 → 波 → 皮
- **definition:** surface
- **pinyin:** pí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a surface or the skin of an animal
  - **interpretation:** The character 皮 (pí, 'surface') visually represents an animal hide or skin stretched out, emphasizing the idea of an outer covering or surface. It captures the concept of a boundary between the interior and exterior, as seen in animal skins used as material.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)张(張)桌子的皮很光滑。
    - Zhè zhāng zhuōzi de pí hěn guānghuá.
    - The surface of this table is very smooth.
  - 新鞋的皮非常柔软(軟)，适(適)合长(長)时(時)间(間)行走。
    - Xīn xié de pí fēicháng róuruǎn, shìhé cháng shíjiān xíngzǒu.
    - The surface of the new shoes is very soft and suitable for long walks.
