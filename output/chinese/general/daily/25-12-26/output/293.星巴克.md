- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 星 (xīng, "star") + 巴 (bā, "area; wish") + 克 (kè, "to overcome; gram")

The components 星 (xīng, "star"), 巴 (bā, "area; wish"), and 克 (kè, "to overcome; gram") are selected to approximate the sound of 'Starbucks' rather than to convey a specific meaningful translation.
