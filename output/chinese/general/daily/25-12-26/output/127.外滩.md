## the Bund
### wàitān
---
- **traditional:** 外灘
- **definition:** the Bund
- **pinyin:** wàitān
- **components:**
  - 外
    - wài
    - outside
  - 滩(灘)
    - tān
    - beach; sandbank; shoal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 外 (wài, "outside") + 滩(灘) (tān, "beach") =
    - an area outside the regular land, associated with a waterfront ->
    - the Bund (a famous waterfront area in Shanghai).
  - **interpretation:** 外 (wài, "outside") indicates something beyond the usual boundary, in this case beyond the city's typical inland areas. 滩(灘) (tān, "beach") refers to the land near water, often sandy and flat. Together, 外滩(灘) evokes the 'outside beach' or waterfront areas developed for commerce and trade, leading to its historical significance and current identity as a prominent urban district along the Huangpu River in Shanghai.
- **examples:**
  - 外滩(灘)是上海最著名的旅游(遊)景点(點)之一。
    - Wàitān shì Shànghǎi zuì zhùmíng de lǚyóu jǐngdiǎn zhī yī.
    - The Bund is one of the most famous tourist attractions in Shanghai.
  - 晚上，我们(們)可以在外滩(灘)散步。
    - Wǎnshàng, wǒmen kěyǐ zài wàitān sànbù.
    - At night, we can take a walk on the Bund.
  - 外滩(灘)的夜景非常迷人。
    - Wàitān de yèjǐng fēicháng mírén.
    - The night view of the Bund is very enchanting.
