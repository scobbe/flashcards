## Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
### Shíkùmén
---
- **traditional:** 石庫門
- **definition:** Shikumen, traditional Shanghainese architectural style
- **pinyin:** Shíkùmén
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
  - 库(庫)
    - kù
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - door; gate
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 石 (shí, "stone") + 库(庫) (kù, "warehouse; storehouse") + 门(門) (mén, "door; gate")
  - **interpretation:** The term 石库(庫)门(門) (shíkùmén) describes a traditional Shanghainese architectural style where stone is used prominently in the construction of door frames and arches. This style combines Western and Chinese architectural elements, often featuring stone portals with a courtyard behind, reminiscent of a 'stone warehouse gate'.
- **examples:**
  - 石库(庫)门(門)是上海的传(傳)统(統)建筑(築)。
    - Shíkùmén shì Shànghǎi de chuántǒng jiànzhú.
    - Shikumen is a traditional architectural style of Shanghai.
  - 游(遊)客在石库(庫)门(門)巷弄中徘徊，感受老上海的韵(韻)味。
    - Yóukè zài shíkùmén xiàngnòng zhōng páihuái, gǎnshòu lǎo Shànghǎi de yùnwèi.
    - Tourists wander in the Shikumen lanes, experiencing the charm of old Shanghai.
  - 老居民讲(講)述石库(庫)门(門)背后(後)的历(歷)史，给(給)人留下深刻印象。
    - Lǎo jūmín jiǎngshù shíkùmén bèihòu de lìshǐ, gěi rén liúxià shēnkè yìnxiàng.
    - Elderly residents recount the history behind Shikumen, leaving a lasting impression.
### 石库(庫)门(門) → 石

- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**

  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') originally depicted a stone under a cliff, representing the idea of a solid, natural object found at the base of rocky formations.
- **examples:**

### 石库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫)
- **traditional:** 庫
- **definition:** warehouse; storehouse
- **pinyin:** kù
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - shelter
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter") + 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") =
    - shelter for vehicles ->
    - warehouse; storehouse
  - **interpretation:** The character 库(庫) (kù) combines 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter"), depicting a spacious building, with 車(车) (chē, "vehicle"), indicating its use as a place to house vehicles or goods. This combination suggests a structure designed for storage purposes.
  - **simplification:** 庫→库(庫): 車(车) (chē, "vehicle") was replaced with a simpler form of the character, adopting the same phonetic properties.
- **examples:**
  - 货(貨)物被安全存储(儲)在库(庫)中。
    - Huòwù bèi ānquán cúnchǔ zài kù zhōng.
    - The goods are safely stored in the warehouse.
  - 公司收到新材料，立即将(將)其分类(類)存入库(庫)。
    - Gōngsī shōudào xīn cáiliào, lìjí jiāng qí fēnlèi cúnrù kù.
    - The company received new materials and immediately sorted them into the warehouse.
### 石库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣)
- **traditional:** 廣
- **definition:** shelter
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 廣
    - guǎng
    - broad
  - 黃
    - huáng
    - yellow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 广(廣) (guǎng, "broad") + phonetic 黃 (huáng, "yellow") ->
    - broad
  - **interpretation:** The character 廣 (guǎng, "broad") combines the semantic element 广(廣), depicting a broad area or structure, with the phonetic element 黃 (huáng), which lends its sound. This combination signifies the idea of something wide or expansive.
  - **simplification:** 廣 was simplified to 广 by removing the 黃 (huáng, "yellow") phonetic component, leaving only the semantic element.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)条(條)河非常广(廣)阔(闊)。
    - Zhè tiáo hé fēicháng guǎngkuò.
    - This river is very broad.
  - 他的知识(識)面很广(廣)，总(總)是能解决(決)问(問)题(題)。
    - Tā de zhīshì miàn hěn guǎng, zǒng shì néng jiějué wèntí.
    - His knowledge is broad, and he is always able to solve problems.
  - 在学(學)校里(裡)，他参(參)加了许(許)多广(廣)泛的活动(動)。
    - Zài xuéxiào lǐ, tā cānjiāle xǔduō guǎngfàn de huódòng.

- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 广

    - huáng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 广 (roof; shelter) + phonetic 黃 (huáng, sound) ->
    - shelter
  - **interpretation:** The character 廣 (guǎng, 'shelter') combines the semantic component 广, which suggests the idea of covering or enclosure like a roof, with the phonetic component 黃 (huáng). The enclosure aspect of 广 underlines the meaning of providing shelter or an expansive space.

- **examples:**

### 石库(庫)门(門) → 库(庫) → 广(廣) → 廣 → 黃
- **definition:** yellow
- **pinyin:** huáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a pictogram depicting a disabled person or man with a ring of jade on the chest ->
    - phonetically borrowed for 'yellow' ->
    - 黃
  - **interpretation:** The character 黃 (huáng, 'yellow') originated from a pictogram representing either a disabled person or a man with a jade ring, showing a connection to cultural rituals or symbols of status. It was later phonetically borrowed to represent the color 'yellow' without semantic connection to its original depiction. In modern Chinese, its primary use is to denote the color yellow, disconnecting entirely from its debated pictographic origins.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)朵花是黄(黃)色的。
    - Zhè duǒ huā shì huángsè de.
    - This flower is yellow.
  - 秋天来(來)临(臨)时(時)，树(樹)叶(葉)变(變)成金黄(黃)色。
    - Qiūtiān láilín shí, shùyè biàn chéng jīnhuáng sè.
    - When autumn arrives, the leaves turn golden yellow.
  - 他穿了一件黄(黃)色的外套。
    - Tā chuānle yī jiàn huángsè de wàitào.
    - He wore a yellow jacket.

- **definition:** vehicle
- **pinyin:** chē

### 石库(庫)门(門) → 门(門)
- **traditional:** 門
- **definition:** door; gate
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 門 (mén, "gate") depicts a gate ->
    - simplified to 门 (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 门(門) (mén, "gate") represents the image of a double-sided gate. It simplifies to 门 but retains the depiction of an entrance or gateway.
  - **simplification:** The character 门 is a simplified form of 門, where the complex structure is replaced by a more straightforward representation while preserving the overall image of a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 学(學)校门(門)前站着(著)一位老师(師)。
    - Xuéxiào mén qián zhànzhe yī wèi lǎoshī(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - A teacher stands at the school gate.
  - 请(請)你从(從)这(這)扇门(門)进(進)来(來)，把鞋子脱(脫)掉。
    - Qǐng nǐ cóng zhè shàn mén(請你從這扇門) jìnlái(進來)，bǎ xiézi(把鞋子) tuōdiào(脫掉)。
    - Please come in through this door and take off your shoes.
  - 夜深了，只有微弱的光从(從)门(門)缝(縫)里(裡)泄(洩)出。
    - Yè shēn le(夜深了)，zhǐ yǒu wēiruò de guāng cóng mén fèng lǐ xiè chū(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - It's late at night, and only a faint light seeps through the gap in the door.
