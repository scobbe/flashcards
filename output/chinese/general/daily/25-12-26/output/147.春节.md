## Spring Festival
### Chūn Jié
---
- **traditional:** 春節
- **definition:** Spring Festival
- **pinyin:** Chūn Jié
- **components:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring
  - 节(節)
    - jié
    - festival; node; joint; segment
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 春 (chūn, "spring") and 節 (jié, "festival") combine directly to mean "Spring Festival." 春 (chūn, "spring") refers to the season marking new beginnings and renewal, and 節 (jié, "festival") signifies a celebratory time or occasion. The Spring Festival is a major holiday in Chinese culture, celebrated with various customs and traditions.
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 春 (chūn, "spring") and 節 (jié, "festival") makes sense as the festival is celebrated at the beginning of the new lunar year during the spring season, marking renewal and new beginnings.
- **examples:**

    - Jiārén tuánjù guò Chūn Jié, gòngxiǎng měishí qìng jiājié.
    - Family gathers during the Spring Festival and enjoys delicious food to celebrate the holiday.

### 春( )节(節) → 节(節)
- **traditional:** 節
- **definition:** festival; node; joint; segment
- **pinyin:** jié
- **components:**
  - 竹
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 即
    - jí
    - to approach
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic 即 (jí)
  - **interpretation:** 節(Jié, "festival; node; joint; segment") combines 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") with 即 (jí) as a phonetic complement. Bamboo represents the semantic aspect due to its segmented structure, reminiscent of nodes or joints. The phonetic 即 complements this by providing the sound, without contributing a semantic layer, leading to the meanings related to segmentation, such as 'node', 'joint', or 'segment', and through metaphorical extension, 'festival' as a demarcated time.
  - **simplification:** 节(節) simplifies 節 by removing the 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") radical, focusing solely on the phonetic 即 (jí) with a visually simpler form.
- **examples:**
  - 春( )节(節)是( )中( )国(國)最( )重( )要( )的( )节(節)日( )。
    - Chūnjié shì Zhōngguó zuì zhòngyào de jiérì.

    - Zhè gēn zhúzi de měi gè jié dōu hěn míngxiǎn.
