## late autumn
### hánqiū
---
- **definition:** late autumn
- **pinyin:** hánqiū
- **components:**
  - 寒
    - hán
    - cold; chilly
  - 秋
    - qiū
    - autumn; fall
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 寒 (hán, "cold") refers to the chilliness often associated with the colder months. 秋 (qiū, "autumn") represents the autumn season, a time known for cooler weather as it transitions towards winter.
  - **interpretation:** The word 寒秋 (hánqiū, "late autumn") combines 寒 (hán, "cold") and 秋 (qiū, "autumn"), reflecting the late stage of autumn when the weather becomes noticeably colder, marking the transition into winter. This captures the tangible sensation of chilliness experienced during this time of year.
- **examples:**
  - 寒秋的夜晚很冷。
    - Hánqiū de yèwǎn hěn lěng.
    - The late autumn nights are very cold.
  - 在寒秋的田野上，我( )欣( )赏(賞)到( )了( )迷( )人( )的( )景( )色( )。
    - Zài hánqiū de tiányě shàng, wǒ xīnshǎng dào le mírén de jǐngsè.

  - 寒( )秋( )到( )来(來)时(時)，树(樹)叶(葉)变(變)成( )了( )金( )黄(黃)色( )。
