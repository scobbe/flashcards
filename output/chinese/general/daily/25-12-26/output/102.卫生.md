## hygiene
### wèishēng
---
- **traditional:** 衛生
- **definition:** hygiene
- **pinyin:** wèishēng
- **components:**
  - 卫(衛)
    - wèi
    - guard; protect; defend
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; health; live; raw
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") + 生 (shēng, "life") =
    - guarding life ->
    - hygiene
  - **interpretation:** Hygiene involves practices that guard or protect one's health and life by promoting cleanliness and preventing disease. In this context, 卫(衛) (wèi, "guard") implies protection, while 生 (shēng, "life") refers to health and life itself.
  - **simplification:** 卫生 is the simplified form of 衛生.
- **examples:**
  - 保持卫(衛)生很重要。
    - Bǎochí wèishēng hěn zhòngyào.
    - Maintaining hygiene is important.
  - 在餐厅(廳)用餐前，请(請)检(檢)查卫(衛)生。
    - Zài cāntīng yòngcān qián, qǐng jiǎnchá wèishēng.
    - Before dining in the restaurant, please check the hygiene.
  - 环(環)境卫(衛)生关(關)系(係)到健康。
    - Huánjìng wèishēng guānxì dào jiànkāng.
    - Environmental hygiene is related to health.
### 卫(衛)生 → 卫(衛)
- **traditional:** 衛
- **definition:** guard; protect; defend
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **components:**
  - 行
    - xíng
    - walk
  - 韋
    - wéi
    - tanned leather
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 行 (xíng, 'walk') + phonetic 韋 (wéi, 'tanned leather') ->
    - guard; protect; defend
  - **interpretation:** The character 衛 (wèi, 'guard; protect; defend') combines the semantic element 行 (xíng, 'walk'), which suggests movement or patrolling, with the phonetic component 韋 (wéi, 'tanned leather'), which provides the pronunciation. The imagery of a person moving while wearing protective leather gear conveys the sense of guarding or defending an area.
  - **simplification:** The traditional form 衛 was simplified to 卫 by reducing it to a minimal structure that phonetically resembles the compound it was simplified from. The complex parts were replaced by a form influenced by certain katakana elements (as in Japanese writing), a process unrelated to preserving meaning but retaining phonetic recognition.
- **examples:**
  - 保安在大楼(樓)门(門)口守卫(衛)。
    - Bǎo'ān zài dàlóu ménkǒu shǒuwèi.
    - The security guard is stationed at the entrance of the building.
  - 小王负(負)责(責)卫(衛)士的工作。
    - Xiǎo Wáng fùzé wèishì de gōngzuò.
    - Xiao Wang is responsible for the work of the guard.
  - 我们(們)要卫(衛)国(國)，保家。
    - Wǒmen yào wèi guó, bǎo jiā.
    - We need to defend the country and protect the home.
