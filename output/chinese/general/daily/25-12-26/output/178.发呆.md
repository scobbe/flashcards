## be in a daze
### fādāi
---
- **traditional:** 發呆
- **definition:** be in a daze
- **pinyin:** fādāi
- **components:**
  - 发(發)
    - fā
    - send out; emit; develop
  - 呆
    - dāi
    - dull; foolish
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 发(發) (fā, "send out; emit; develop") + 呆 (dāi, "dull; foolish") =
    - to be in a daze
  - **interpretation:** 發(fā, "send out; emit") suggests an outward movement or releasing state, such as going into another mental state. 呆(dāi, "dull; foolish") indicates a state of being mentally unresponsive or stupefied. Together, 發呆(fādāi) refers to being in a mental state where one 'emits' or transitions into dullness or unresponsiveness, hence being 'in a daze'.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )坐( )在( )椅( )子( )上( )发(發)呆( )。

  - 这(這)把( )椅( )子( )的( )主( )要( )材( )料( )是( )木( )。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.
