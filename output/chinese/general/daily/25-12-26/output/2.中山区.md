## Zhongshan District, Taipei
### Zhōngshān Qū
---
- **traditional:** 中山區
- **definition:** Zhongshan District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Zhōngshān Qū
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle; center; within
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain; hill
  - 區
    - qū
    - district; area; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") 山 (shān, "mountain") 區 (qū, "district")
  - **interpretation:** 中 (zhōng, "middle") suggests centrality or being at the center, often used in names to signify the importance or central position of a place. 山 (shān, "mountain") refers to a mountain or a hilly area, commonly appearing in geographical names to indicate natural topography or to honor Sun Yat-sen (孫中山), a revered figure in Chinese history known as the "Father of the Nation." Therefore, 中山 (Zhōngshān, "central mountain") often signifies a place named in honor of Sun Yat-sen. Finally, 區 (qū, "district") denotes an administrative division or area. In the context of 中山區 (Zhōngshān Qū), it indicates a district named Zhongshan.
- **examples:**
