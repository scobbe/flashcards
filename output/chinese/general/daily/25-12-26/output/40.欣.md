## happy; joyful
### xīn
---
- **definition:** happy; joyful
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **components:**
  - 斤
    - jīn
    - axe
  - 欠
    - qiàn
    - yawn; lack
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 斤 (jīn, "axe") + semantic 欠 (qiàn, "yawn; lack") ->
    - happiness
  - **interpretation:** The character 欣 (xīn, "happy; joyful") combines 斤 (jīn, "axe") as the phonetic component providing the sound, with 欠 (qiàn, "yawn; lack") as the semantic component. The image of an open mouth 欠 combined with the sound from 斤 evokes the act of expressing joy or delight, as laughter often involves an open mouth. Therefore, the character represents a state of happiness or joy.
- **examples:**
  - 我欣然同意。
    - Wǒ xīnrán tóngyì.
    - I happily agreed.
  - 孩子们(們)在公园(園)里(裡)欣欣向荣(榮)。
    - Háizimen zài gōngyuán lǐ xīnxīn xiàngróng.
    - The children are joyfully thriving in the park.
  - 春天让(讓)大自然欣欣然。
    - Chūntiān ràng dàzìrán xīnxīnrán.
    - Spring makes nature joyful.
### 欣 → 斤
- **definition:** axe
- **pinyin:** jīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an axe
  - **interpretation:** The character 斤 (jīn, 'axe') is a pictogram that visually represents an axe. Its shape resembles the tool's blade and handle.
- **examples:**
  - 他拿起一把斤，熟练(練)地劈开(開)木头(頭)。
    - Tā ná qǐ yī bǎ jīn, shú liàn de pī kāi mù tóu.
    - He picked up an axe and skillfully split the firewood.
  - 古人制(製)造精巧的斤，以制(製)作雕刻工具。
    - Gǔ rén zhì zào jīng qiǎo de jīn, yǐ zhì zuò diāo kè gōng jù.
    - In ancient times, people crafted exquisite axes for carving tools.

### 欣 → 欠
- **definition:** yawn; lack
- **pinyin:** qiàn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a person in a bent posture with an open mouth, suggesting the act of yawning or slackening
  - **interpretation:** The character 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") visually represents a person with their mouth open, capturing the physical gesture associated with yawning. It symbolizes a relaxed or tired state.
- **examples:**
  - 他打哈欠。
    - tā dǎ hāqiàn
    - He yawns.
  - 上课(課)时(時)他总(總)是打哈欠，让(讓)老师(師)分心。
    - shàngkè shí tā zǒng shì dǎ hāqiàn, ràng lǎoshī fēnxīn
    - He keeps yawning during class, distracting the teacher.
  - 会(會)议(議)中，他不自觉(覺)地打哈欠。
    - huìyì zhōng, tā bù zìjué dì dǎ hāqiàn
    - During the meeting, he involuntarily yawns.
