## bouquet
### huāshù
---
- **definition:** bouquet
- **pinyin:** huāshù
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 束
    - shù
    - bundle; bunch; to bind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花 (huā, "flower") + 束 (shù, "bundle") =
    - bouquet
  - **interpretation:** A 花 (huā, "flower") 束 (shù, "bundle") is a collection of flowers arranged together as a bouquet. The term directly embodies the idea of a 'bundle of flowers', capturing the visual and physical arrangement typically associated with bouquets.
- **examples:**
  - 我给(給)母亲(親)买(買)了一束花束。
    - Wǒ gěi mǔqīn mǎile yī shù huāshù.
    - I bought a bouquet for my mother.
  - 他在纪(紀)念日晚宴上送她花束，表达(達)了深情。
    - Tā zài jìniànrì wǎnyàn shàng sòng tā huāshù, biǎodále shēnqíng.
    - He gave her a bouquet during the anniversary dinner, expressing his deep affection.
  - 花店为(為)婚礼(禮)准(準)备(備)了一束花束。
    - Huādiàn wèi hūnlǐ zhǔnbèile yī shù huāshù.
    - The florist prepared a bouquet for the wedding.
### 花束 → 花
- **definition:** flower
- **pinyin:** huā
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic 化 (huà, "transform") =
    - plant + phonetic indicator ->
    - flower
  - **interpretation:** 花 (huā, "flower") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") indicating its association with vegetation, and 化 (huà, "transform") for its phonetic value. This illustrates both the natural aspect and the phonetic pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 花香四溢。
    - huā xiāng sì yì。
    - The fragrance of flowers fills the air.
  - 阳(陽)光明媚，花儿(兒)争(爭)艳(艷)。
    - yáng guāng míng mèi，huā ér zhēng yàn。
    - The bright sunshine shines as the flowers compete in beauty.
  - 她用花装(裝)点(點)房间(間)。
    - tā yòng huā zhuāng diǎn fáng jiān。
    - She decorates the room with flowers.

### 花束 → 花 → 艹
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来(來)时(時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 花束 → 花 → 化
- **definition:** transform
- **pinyin:** huà
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 𠤎
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person") upside down =
    - two people, one upright, one reversed ->
    - transformation or change
  - **interpretation:** The character 化 (huà, "transform") visually represents change or transformation, signified by two 人 (rén, "person") characters in opposite orientations, suggesting reversal or alteration.
- **examples:**
  - 科学(學)进(進)步，推动(動)社会(會)化转(轉)型。
    - Kēxué jìnbù, tuīdòng shèhuì huà zhuǎnxíng.
    - Scientific progress transforms social structures.
  - 时(時)间(間)能让(讓)伤(傷)痛化作记(記)忆(憶)。
    - Shíjiān néng ràng shāngtòng huà zuò jìyì.
    - Time transforms pain into memories.
  - 困境中他不断(斷)努力，生命终(終)将(將)化为(為)辉(輝)煌。
    - Kùnjìng zhōng tā bùduàn nǔlì, shēngmìng zhōng jiāng huà wéi huīhuáng.

### 花束 → 花 → 化 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Stylization of 人 (rén, "person") as a side radical to depict a person
  - **interpretation:** 亻 is a stylized form of 人 (rén, "person") used to indicate human-related meanings when combined with other components in characters as a radical. It visually represents a standing person, maintaining the core meaning of 'person' while facilitating the construction of complex characters.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个(個)热(熱)心的人。
    - Tā shì yī gè rèxīn de rén.
    - He is a kind-hearted person.
  - 这(這)座城市里(裡)住着(著)很多优(優)秀的人。
    - Zhè zuò chéngshì lǐ zhùzhe hěn duō yōuxiù de rén.
    - There are many outstanding people living in this city.
  - 每个(個)人都有梦(夢)想。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has dreams.

### 花束 → 束
- **definition:** bundle; bunch; to bind
- **pinyin:** shù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Original: bag tied at both ends ->
    - Depicts a bundle ->
    - Immutable symbol for 'bundle'
  - **interpretation:** 束 (shù, "bundle") visually represents a bag tied at both ends. The act of tying bags into bundles parallels the general understanding of gathering and binding objects together, thus denoting the concept of 'bundle' or 'to bundle together'. The use of such a vivid image makes the meaning inherently clear.
- **examples:**
  - 他买(買)了一束花給她。
    - Tā mǎi le yī shù huā gěi tā.
    - He bought a bouquet of flowers for her.
  - 请(請)帮(幫)我把这(這)些书(書)捆成一束。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ bǎ zhèxiē shū kǔn chéng yí shù.
    - Please help me bundle these books together.
  - 阳(陽)光透过(過)云(雲)层(層)，形成了一束光线(線)。
    - Yángguāng tòuguò yúncéng, xíngchéng le yī shù guāngxiàn.
    - Sunlight penetrated the clouds, forming a beam of light.
