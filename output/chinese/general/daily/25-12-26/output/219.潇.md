## deep and clear (literary); brisk
### xiāo
---
- **traditional:** 瀟
- **definition:** deep and clear (literary); brisk
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 蕭
    - xiāo
    - desolate; whispering of wind
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 水 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 蕭 (xiāo) ->
    - deep and clear water
  - **interpretation:** The character 瀟 (xiāo, "deep and clear") combines the semantic 水 (shuǐ, "water"), indicating the element of water, with the phonetic 蕭 (xiāo), suggesting the sound. The combination suggests a sense of water that is deep and clear, as well as a brisk or lively character, as water in motion can appear both clear and dynamic.
  - **simplification:** 瀟 was simplified to 潇(瀟) by removing the 艹 (cǎo, "grass") component from 蕭, simplifying the overall form but retaining its essential sound.
- **examples:**
  - 她( )的( )名( )字( )叫( )潇(瀟)雪( )。
    - Tā de míngzì jiào Xiāo Xuě.
    - Her name is Xiao Xue.
  - 这(這)个(個)湖( )水( )清( )澈( )潇(瀟)洒(灑)。
    - Zhège húshuǐ qīngchè xiāosǎ.
    - The lake is clear and brisk.
  - 微( )风(風)习(習)习(習)，天( )气(氣)潇(瀟)爽( )。
    - Wēifēng xíxí, tiānqì xiāoshuǎng.
