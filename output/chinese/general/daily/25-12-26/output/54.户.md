## household; door
### hù
---
- **traditional:** 戶
- **definition:** household; door
- **pinyin:** hù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts one-half of 門 (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 戶 (hù, "household; door") resembles the side view of a gate, representing its function as an entrance or access point similar to a door or household entry.
- **examples:**
  - 每( )户(戶)人( )家( )都( )有( )自( )己( )的( )故( )事( )。
    - Měi hù rénjiā dōu yǒu zìjǐ de gùshì.
    - Every household has its own story.
  - 这(這)个(個)小( )区(區)住( )着(著)数(數)百( )户(戶)，居( )民( )和( )睦( )相( )处(處)。
    - Zhège xiǎoqū zhùzhe shù bǎi hù, jūmín hémù xiāngchǔ.
    - Hundreds of households live in this community, and the residents live in harmony.
