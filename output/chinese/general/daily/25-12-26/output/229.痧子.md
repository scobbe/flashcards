### shāzi
---
- **definition:** measles
- **pinyin:** shāzi
- **components:**
  - 痧
    - shā
    - rash; measles
  - 子
    - zi
    - child; small thing
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 痧 (shā, "rash") + 子 (zi, "child; small thing"), referring to measles
  - **interpretation:** The word 痧子 (shāzi) refers to measles by combining 痧 (shā, "rash"), which represents the characteristic rash associated with the disease, and 子 (zi, "child; small thing"), indicating something small or relating to children, as measles commonly affects young children.
- **examples:**
  - 他得了痧子。
    - Tā déle shāzi.
    - He contracted measles.
  - 当(當)孩( )子( )暴( )露( )在( )阳(陽)光( )下( )时(時)，痧( )子( )容( )易( )传(傳)播( )。

  - 医(醫)生( )建( )议(議)及( )时(時)治( )疗(療)痧( )子( )。
