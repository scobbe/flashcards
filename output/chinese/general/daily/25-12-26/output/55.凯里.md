## Kaili City
### Kǎilǐ
---
- **traditional:** 凱里
- **definition:** Kaili City
- **pinyin:** Kǎilǐ
- **components:**
  - 凱
    - kǎi
    - triumphant; victorious
  - 里
    - lǐ
    - neighborhood; village; within
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 凱 (kǎi, "triumphant") + 里 (lǐ, "neighborhood") form the name of Kaili City in Guizhou, China.
  - **interpretation:** The name 凱 (kǎi, "triumphant") gives a sense of victory or success, often associated with celebration or achievement. 里 (lǐ, "neighborhood") pertains to a local area or community. Together, 凱里 suggests a prosperous and thriving locality.
- **examples:**
  - 凯(凱)里市位于(於)中国(國)贵(貴)州省。
    - Kǎilǐ shì wèiyú Zhōngguó Guìzhōu shěng.
    - Kaili City is located in Guizhou Province, China.
  - 我们(們)打算去凯(凱)里旅游(遊)。
    - Wǒmen dǎsuàn qù Kǎilǐ lǚyóu.
    - We plan to travel to Kaili.
  - 凯(凱)里的苗族文化非常独(獨)特。
    - Kǎilǐ de Miáozú wénhuà fēicháng dútè.
    - The Miao culture in Kaili is very unique.
### 凯(凱)里 → 凱
- **definition:** triumphant; victorious
- **pinyin:** kǎi
- **components:**
  - 豈
    - kǎi
    - drum
  - 几
    - jī
    - small table
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 豈 (kǎi, "drum") + phonetic 几 (jī) ->
    - originally meant 'music of triumph; victory music'
  - **interpretation:** The character 凱(kǎi, "music of triumph") combines a semantic element 豈(kǎi, "drum"), suggesting a celebratory or victorious sound, with a phonetic element 几 (jī) to provide the sound component. The concept of music relating to triumph arises from the imagery of drums used in victory celebrations, lending the sense of 'triumphant music'.
  - **simplification:** 凯 is the simplified form of 凱, where 豈 (kǎi, "drum") is replaced by its simplified counterpart.
- **examples:**
  - 胜(勝)利凯(凱)旋后(後)，军(軍)队(隊)在广(廣)场(場)上演奏凯(凱)歌。
    - Shènglì kǎixuán hòu, jūnduì zài guǎngchǎng shàng yǎnzòu kǎigē.
    - After the victorious return, the army played the music of triumph in the plaza.
  - 他总(總)是为(為)他的成就感到骄(驕)傲，就像凯(凱)歌一样(樣)。
    - Tā zǒng shì wèi tā de chéngjiù gǎndào jiāo'ào, jiù xiàng kǎigē yīyàng.
    - He always feels proud of his achievements, just like the music of triumph.
  - 这(這)场(場)比赛(賽)的胜(勝)利是团(團)队(隊)合作的结(結)果，宛如凱旋之音。
