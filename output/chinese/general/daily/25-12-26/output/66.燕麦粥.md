## oatmeal porridge
### yànmàizhōu
---
- **traditional:** 燕麥粥
- **definition:** oatmeal porridge
- **pinyin:** yànmàizhōu
- **components:**
  - 燕麦(麥)
    - yànmài
    - oats
  - 粥
    - zhōu
    - porridge
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 燕 (yàn, "swallow" [the bird]) is a sound-loan here, borrowed only for its sound, not its meaning. 麥 (mài, "wheat") represents grains in general. 粥 (zhōu, "porridge") comes from the concept of grain boiled into a soft, consumable form."
  - **interpretation:** 燕 (yàn, "swallow" [the bird]) was borrowed for its phonetic value and reflects no semantic contribution to the meaning. 麥 (mài, "wheat") signifies cereal grains. Together, 燕麦(麥) (yànmài, "oats") designates a specific type of cereal grain, differentiated from wheat (麦). 粥 (zhōu, "porridge") refers to the preparation method of grains being cooked into a thick, soft dish suitable for eating. Thus, 燕麦(麥)粥 (yànmàizhōu, "oatmeal porridge") logically denotes a porridge made from oats, emphasizing the grain used and its transformation into a consumable form.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早餐都吃燕麦(麥)粥。
    - Wǒ měitiān zǎocān dōu chī yànmàizhōu.
    - I eat oatmeal porridge for breakfast every day.
  - 燕麦(麥)粥对(對)健康有益。
    - Yànmàizhōu duì jiànkāng yǒuyì.
    - Oatmeal porridge is beneficial to health.
  - 她在炉(爐)子上煮了一锅(鍋)燕麦(麥)粥。
    - Tā zài lúzi shàng zhǔ le yī guō yànmàizhōu.
    - She cooked a pot of oatmeal porridge on the stove.
