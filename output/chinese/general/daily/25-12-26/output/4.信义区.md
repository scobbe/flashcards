## Xinyi District, Taipei
### Xìnyì Qū
---
- **traditional:** 信義區
- **definition:** Xinyi District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Xìnyì Qū
- **components:**
  - 信
    - xìn
    - trust; letter
  - 義
    - yì
    - righteousness; justice
  - 區
    - qū
    - district; area
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 信 (xìn, "trust; letter") denotes the idea of trust or belief, an essential value in Confucianism. 義 (yì, "righteousness; justice") represents moral and ethical actions, another core Confucian concept. 區 (qū, "district; area") simply specifies a geographical area.
  - **interpretation:** 信 (xìn, "trust") implies a foundation of reliability, a value highly respected in society. 義 (yì, "righteousness") encompasses acting with integrity and justice, aligning with moral virtues. The combination 信義 (xìnyì) signifies a place named with these esteemed principles, reflecting the cultural importance placed on trust and righteousness in the area. The term is completed by 區 (qū, "district"), indicating it is a specific administrative or geographical zone.
- **examples:**

### 信( )义(義)区(區) → 義
- **definition:** righteousness; justice
- **pinyin:** yì
- **components:**
  - 我
    - wǒ
    - I
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - good; beautiful; auspicious
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 我 (wǒ, 'I') + semantic: 羊 (yáng, 'good; beautiful; auspicious') ->
    - righteousness
  - **interpretation:** The character 義(yì, 'righteousness') combines 我 (wǒ, 'I'), which provides the pronunciation, with 羊 (yáng, 'good; beautiful; auspicious'), suggesting moral goodness or virtue. This combination implies a personal embodiment of these positive qualities, aligning with the meaning of righteousness.
  - **simplification:** The simplified form 义 retains the basic structure but reduces the complexity by removing 我 (wǒ, 'I'), leaving a stylized version of 羊 (yáng, 'good; beautiful; auspicious').
- **examples:**
  - 他以義待人，不辜負朋友。

    - To promote righteousness, he fought valiantly.
  - 忠義無雙。
    - Zhōng yì wú shuāng.
    - His loyalty and righteousness are unparalleled.
