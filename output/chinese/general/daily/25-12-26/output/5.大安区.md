## Da’an District, Taipei
### Dà’ān Qū
---
- **traditional:** 大安區
- **definition:** Da’an District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dà’ān Qū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; great
  - 安
    - ān
    - peace; safe
  - 區
    - qū
    - area; district
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big; great") 安 (ān, "peace; safe") 區 (qū, "area; district")
  - **interpretation:** 大安 (dà'ān, "great peace") combines 'great' and 'peace', reflecting an ideal or desirable state of tranquility and well-being, often used as a name for places aiming to convey these qualities. 區 (qū, "area; district") denotes a division or administrative region, hence 大安區 (Dà'ān Qū) specifically refers to the Da’an District in Taipei, suggesting it as an area of large tranquility or prominence.
- **examples:**
  - 大安区(區)的公园(園)很美丽(麗)。
    - Dà’ān Qū de gōngyuán hěn měilì.
    - The parks in Da’an District are beautiful.
  - 我住在大安区(區)，每天都在附近跑步。
    - Wǒ zhù zài Dà’ān Qū, měitiān dōu zài fùjìn pǎobù.
    - I live in Da’an District and run nearby every day.
  - 大安区(區)有许(許)多不错(錯)的餐厅(廳)。
    - Dà’ān Qū yǒu xǔduō bùcuò de cāntīng.
    - Da’an District has many great restaurants.

- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫 (fū, 'man')
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(dà) depicts a person facing forward, representing 'big' by suggesting the idea of someone standing prominently. ### 大安区(區) → 安 → 宀
- **definition:** house
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->

  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. ### 大安区(區) → 區
- **definition:** area; district
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - concealed
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (xì, "concealed") + 品 (pǐn, "goods") =
    - many people or goods concealed in a space ->
    - area
  - **interpretation:** The character 區 (qū, "area") illustrates the concept of an enclosed space with multiple items or individuals represented by 品 (pǐn, "goods") within 匸 (xì, "concealed"). This evokes the sense of a defined area or district where such items are contained.
  - **simplification:** The traditional component 品 (pǐn, "goods") was replaced by 乂 (yì, "to govern") in the simplified form, 区 (区), keeping the enclosure concept.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)一区(區)的房价(價)很高。
    - Zhè yī qū de fángjià hěn gāo.
    - The house prices in this area are very high.
  - 他们(們)住在城市的南区(區)。
    - Tāmen zhù zài chéngshì de nán qū.
    - They live in the southern district of the city.
  - 这(這)个(個)区(區)以其丰(豐)富的文化遗(遺)产(產)而闻(聞)名。
    - Zhège qū yǐ qí fēngfù de wénhuà yíchǎn ér wénmíng.
    - This area is famous for its rich cultural heritage.

### 大安区(區) → 區 → 匸
- **definition:** concealed
- **pinyin:** xì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠃊 (symbolic graphic) ->
    - depicts an enclosure hiding something or someone ->
    - evolved to represent a concealed space or item
  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Its graphical form suggests something contained or hidden, which aligns with its meaning as a 'box' or an enclosure. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.
