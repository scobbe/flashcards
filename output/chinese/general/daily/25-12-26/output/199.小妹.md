## little sister; young woman
### xiǎomèi
---
- **definition:** little sister; young woman
- **pinyin:** xiǎomèi
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little
  - 妹
    - mèi
    - younger sister; girl
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") + 妹 (mèi, "younger sister").
  - **interpretation:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") denotes something younger or lesser in a family hierarchy, directly linking to 妹 (mèi, "younger sister"), who is smaller in age. The term is used both literally for a younger sister and metaphorically for a young woman, extending from familial contexts to imply youth, endearment, or lesser seniority in social situations.
- **examples:**
  - 我的小妹很聪(聰)明。
    - Wǒ de xiǎomèi hěn cōngmíng.
    - My little sister is very smart.
  - 小妹喜欢(歡)弹(彈)钢(鋼)琴。
    - Xiǎomèi xǐhuān tán gāngqín.
    - My little sister likes to play the piano.
  - 我和小妹一起去公园(園)。
    - Wǒ hé xiǎomèi yīqǐ qù gōngyuán.
    - My little sister and I went to the park together.

- **definition:** small; little
- **pinyin:** xiǎo
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts three small dots arranged symmetrically to represent something small
  - **interpretation:** The character 小(xiǎo, 'small') uses three small strokes to symbolize size or quantity, visually capturing the essence of 'smallness'.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)小猫(貓)很可爱(愛)。

    - Zhè zuò xiǎo qiáo héngkuà héliú, liánjiē liǎng àn.

  - 请(請)给(給)我一小杯水。
    - Qǐng gěi wǒ yī xiǎo bēi shuǐ.

### 小妹 → 妹
- **definition:** younger sister; girl
- **pinyin:** mèi
- **components:**
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - female; woman
  - 未
    - wèi
    - not yet
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 女 (female, woman) + phonetic 未 ->
    - younger sister
  - **interpretation:** The character 妹 (mèi, "younger sister") uses 女 (nǚ, "female, woman") for its semantic part, indicating the gender, while 未 (wèi) provides the pronunciation. This combination suggests a female family member distinct from an older sister, leading to the meaning "younger sister."
- **examples:**
  - 我有一个(個)妹妹。
    - Wǒ yǒu yī gè mèimei.
    - I have a younger sister.
  - 妹妹喜欢(歡)画(畫)画(畫)，她常常在家里(裡)画(畫)风(風)景。
    - Mèimei xǐhuān huàhuà, tā chángcháng zài jiālǐ huà fēngjǐng.
    - My younger sister likes painting, she often paints landscapes at home.
  - 我送给(給)妹妹一个(個)新书(書)包，她很开(開)心。
    - Wǒ sòng gěi mèimei yī gè xīn shūbāo, tā hěn kāixīn.
    - I gave my younger sister a new backpack, she was very happy.

### 小妹 → 妹 → 女
- **definition:** female; woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
- **examples:**
  - 那位才华(華)横(橫)溢的女，赢(贏)得了全场(場)注目。
    - Nà wèi cáihuá héngyì de nǚ, yíng dé le quánchǎng zhùmù.
    - That talented woman won everyone's attention.
  - 古时(時)候‘女’常被用来(來)象征(徵)温(溫)柔，而如今它代表独(獨)立自信。
    - Gǔshíhòu ‘nǚ’ cháng bèi yòng lái xiàngzhēng wēnróu, ér rújīn tā dàibiǎo dúlì zìxìn.
    - In ancient times, the character '女' was often used to symbolize gentleness, whereas today it represents independence and confidence.

### 小妹 → 妹 → 未
- **definition:** not yet
- **pinyin:** wèi
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a tree with lush branches on top ->
    - 未
  - **interpretation:** The character 未(mèi, "not yet") depicts a tree 木 (mù, "tree") with lush branches on top, representing incompletion or future potential. This image conveys the concept of something not yet ripe or complete, leading to the meaning 'not yet'.
- **examples:**
  - 我还(還)未听(聽)说(說)过(過)这(這)个(個)消息。

### 小妹 → 妹 → 未 → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**

  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots

- **examples:**
