## schedule; book
### yùdìng
---
- **traditional:** 預定
- **definition:** schedule; book
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance; beforehand; before; prepare
  - 定
    - dìng
    - decide; settle; fix; stable
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 預 (yù, "in advance") + 定 (dìng, "decide; settle") =
    - to arrange or schedule something in advance
  - **interpretation:** The word 預定 (yùdìng, "schedule; book") is composed of 預 (yù, "in advance") and 定 (dìng, "decide; settle"). Together, these morphemes convey the action of arranging or deciding something before it happens, which can apply to making reservations, scheduling events, or booking in general.
  - **simplification:** Simplified characters match the traditional ones provided.
- **examples:**
  - 我预(預)定了一张(張)电(電)影票。
    - Wǒ yùdìngle yī zhāng diànyǐng piào.
    - I booked a movie ticket.
  - 他预(預)定了会(會)议(議)室，打算安排讨(討)论(論)。
    - Tā yùdìngle huìyì shì, dǎsuàn ānpái tǎolùn.
    - He booked the conference room and plans to organize a discussion.
