- **pinyin:** Mèng
- **components:**
  - 子

  - 皿

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 子 (child; son) + phonetic 皿 (sound) ->
    - surname Meng
  - **interpretation:** The character 孟 (Mèng) combines 子 (zǐ, 'child; son') as the semantic component representing a generation or lineage, with 皿 (mǐn, as sound provider) as the phonetic element. It traditionally indicates the eldest in the family, such as 'firstborn' or 'eldest son', later used as a surname.
