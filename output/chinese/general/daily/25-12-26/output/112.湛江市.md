### Zhànjiāng Shì
---
- **definition:** Zhanjiang City
- **pinyin:** Zhànjiāng Shì
- **components:**
  - 湛
    - zhàn
    - deep; profound
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 市
    - shì
    - city
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 湛 (zhàn, "deep; profound") and 江 (jiāng, "river") combined with 市 (shì, "city") to refer to Zhanjiang City. 湛 can mean "deep" to describe the profound or deep qualities of the water, 江 represents "river," and 市 denotes "city." Together, they form the name of a city known for its water geography.
  - **interpretation:** 湛 (zhàn, "deep") reflects the quality of being profound or intense, particularly relating to water, such as a deep river. 江 (jiāng, "river") brings in the concept of a large body of flowing water, which is central to the city's layout and environment. 市 (shì, "city") specifies the urban or administrative aspect, completing the reference to a specific city locality. Thus, Zhanjiang City is named for its prominent riverine geography.
- **examples:**
  - 湛江市是一个(個)美丽(麗)的地方。
    - Zhànjiāng Shì shì yī gè měilì de dìfāng.
    - Zhanjiang City is a beautiful place.
  - 我打算去湛江市旅游(遊)。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn qù Zhànjiāng Shì lǚyóu.
    - I plan to travel to Zhanjiang City.
  - 湛江市以海鲜(鮮)闻(聞)名。
    - Zhànjiāng Shì yǐ hǎixiān wénmíng.
    - Zhanjiang City is famous for its seafood.
