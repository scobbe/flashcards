- **components:**

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word 平遙 (Píngyáo) refers to a county in Shanxi, China, known for its well-preserved ancient city.
  - **interpretation:** The character 平 (píng, "flat; peaceful") suggests a flat or level surface, often associated with tranquility or stability. The character 遙 (yáo, "distant; far away") represents the concept of distance or being far-reaching. In the context of 平遙 (Píngyáo), these characters together imply a place that is both vast and peaceful, reflecting the historical city's extensive and serene ancient architecture.
