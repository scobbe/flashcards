  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 榕 (róng, "banyan") refers specifically to the banyan tree, known for its sprawling branches and large canopy. 樹 (shù, "tree") is a general term for tree, representing the plant form.
  - **interpretation:** 榕 (róng, "banyan") directly indicates the banyan tree, a species known for its unique root system and broad coverage. 樹 (shù, "tree") places it in the broader category of trees as a natural organism; together, they clearly specify the type of tree being referred to as a banyan.
