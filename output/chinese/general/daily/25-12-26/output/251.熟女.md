## stylish mature woman
### shúnǚ
---
- **definition:** stylish mature woman
- **pinyin:** shúnǚ
- **components:**
  - 熟
    - shú
    - ripe; mature; familiar
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 熟 (shú, "ripe; mature; familiar") and 女 (nǚ, "woman") combine to describe a woman who is not only mature in age but also possesses the poise and style that can come from life experience.
  - **interpretation:** 熟 (shú, "ripe; mature; familiar") provides the sense of having reached a stage of full development or maturity, like ripe fruit. This metaphorically extends to describing someone who has gained wisdom and sophistication with age. 女 (nǚ, "woman") specifically targets this maturity to women, together forming the concept of a 'stylish mature woman' who exudes both confidence and elegance. The word captures the idea that life experience can lead to a more polished and attractive demeanor.
- **examples:**
  - 她是一位典雅的熟女。
    - Tā shì yī wèi diǎnyǎ de shúnǚ.
    - She is an elegant stylish mature woman.
  - 这(這)家( )时(時)装(裝)店( )的( )顾(顧)客( )多( )是( )熟( )女( )。

### 熟女 → 熟 → 孰
- **definition:** cooked; ripe
- **pinyin:** shú
- **components:**
  - 享
    - xiǎng
    - enjoy
  - 丸
    - wán
    - pill
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 𦎫 + semantic 丮 ->
    - modern script 享 + 丸 ->
    - 孰
  - **interpretation:** The character 孰 originally incorporates 𦎫, which provides the sound, and 丮, which signifies grasping, representing the concept of acquiring knowledge as something 'cooked' or 'ripe' through experience. Over time, in modern script, these components have transformed into 享, maintaining similar sounds, and 丸, completing the current form.
