## overseas Chinese
### huáqiáo
---
- **traditional:** 華僑
- **definition:** overseas Chinese
- **pinyin:** huáqiáo
- **components:**
  - 华(華)
    - huá
    - China; magnificent
  - 侨(僑)
    - qiáo
    - to live abroad
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 华(華) (huá, "China; magnificent") + 侨(僑) (qiáo, "to live abroad")
  - **interpretation:** 华(華)侨(僑) (huáqiáo) means 'overseas Chinese.' The word combines 华(華) (huá), representing 'China' and symbolizing magnificence, and 侨(僑) (qiáo), which means 'to live abroad.' This combination reflects Chinese people who establish themselves outside of China while maintaining cultural ties to their homeland.
- **examples:**
  - 许(許)多华(華)侨(僑)在国(國)外投资(資)。
    - xǔduō huáqiáo zài guówài tóuzī.
    - Many overseas Chinese invest abroad.
  - 他出生于(於)中国(國)，后(後)来(來)成为(為)成功的华(華)侨(僑)商人。
    - tā chūshēng yú zhōngguó, hòulái chéngwéi chénggōng de huáqiáo shāngrén.
    - He was born in China and later became a successful overseas Chinese businessman.
  - 不少华(華)侨(僑)热(熱)衷于(於)保护(護)中国(國)文化。
    - bùshǎo huáqiáo rèzhōng yú bǎohù zhōngguó wénhuà.
    - Many overseas Chinese are keen on protecting Chinese culture.
### 华(華)侨(僑) → 华(華)
- **traditional:** 華
- **definition:** China; magnificent
- **pinyin:** huá
- **components:**
  - 化
    - huà
    - to transform
  - 十
    - shí
    - stalk and leaves
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 化 (huà, "to transform") + semantic 十 (shí, "stalk and leaves") ->
    - 華 (huá, "magnificent")
  - **interpretation:** 華 (huá, "magnificent") combines 化 (huà, "to transform") providing the pronunciation, with 十 (shí, "stalk and leaves") symbolizing growth and abundance, resulting in the sense of magnificence and richness. This imagery naturally bridges to the idea of 'magnificent', depicting flourishing plants as a symbol of splendor, which extends to refer to China as a culturally rich and illustrious place.
  - **simplification:** 华(華): The top component of 華 was simplified by using 化 (huà, "to transform") in the simplified version 华, retaining phonetic resemblance while removing the detailed component 十.
- **examples:**
  - 中国(國)是一个(個)充满(滿)魅力的国(國)家。
    - Zhōngguó shì yīge chōngmǎn mèilì de guójiā.
    - China is a country full of charm.
  - 他的家在华(華)北平原。
    - Tā de jiā zài Huáběi píngyuán.
    - His home is on the North China Plain.
  - 这(這)座建筑(築)非常华(華)丽(麗)。
    - Zhè zuò jiànzhù fēicháng huálì.
    - This building is very magnificent.
### 华(華)侨(僑) → 华(華) → 化
- **definition:** to transform
- **pinyin:** huà
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 𠤎
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person") upside down =
    - two people, one upright, one reversed ->
    - transformation or change
  - **interpretation:** The character 化 (huà, "transform") visually represents change or transformation, signified by two 人 (rén, "person") characters in opposite orientations, suggesting reversal or alteration.
- **examples:**
  - 科学(學)进(進)步，推动(動)社会(會)化转(轉)型。
    - Kēxué jìnbù, tuīdòng shèhuì huà zhuǎnxíng.
    - Scientific progress transforms social structures.
  - 时(時)间(間)能让(讓)伤(傷)痛化作记(記)忆(憶)。
    - Shíjiān néng ràng shāngtòng huà zuò jìyì.
    - Time transforms pain into memories.
  - 困境中他不断(斷)努力，生命终(終)将(將)化为(為)辉(輝)煌。
    - Kùnjìng zhōng tā bùduàn nǔlì, shēngmìng zhōng jiāng huà wéi huīhuáng.
    - Amid difficulties he perseveres, and his life will eventually transform into brilliance.

### 华(華)侨(僑) → 华(華) → 化 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Stylization of 人 (rén, "person") as a side radical to depict a person
  - **interpretation:** 亻 is a stylized form of 人 (rén, "person") used to indicate human-related meanings when combined with other components in characters as a radical. It visually represents a standing person, maintaining the core meaning of 'person' while facilitating the construction of complex characters.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个(個)热(熱)心的人。
    - Tā shì yī gè rèxīn de rén.
    - He is a kind-hearted person.
  - 这(這)座城市里(裡)住着(著)很多优(優)秀的人。
    - Zhè zuò chéngshì lǐ zhùzhe hěn duō yōuxiù de rén.
    - There are many outstanding people living in this city.
  - 每个(個)人都有梦(夢)想。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has dreams.

- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**

  - **description:**

### 华(華)侨(僑) → 华(華) → 十
- **definition:** stalk and leaves
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Original vertical stroke ->
    - later horizontal stroke added ->
    - forms a cross ->
    - represents 'ten'
  - **interpretation:** The character 十(shí, 'ten') represents the number ten with a cross shape that could be seen as tally marks. It evolved from a simple vertical stroke to a cross by adding a horizontal stroke, symbolizing a complete set of ten.
- **examples:**
  - 我有十本书(書)。
    - Wǒ yǒu shí běn shū.
    - I have ten books.
  - 花园(園)里(裡)有十棵树(樹)，景色十分优(優)美。
    - Huāyuán lǐ yǒu shí kē shù, jǐngsè shífēn yōuměi.
    - There are ten trees in the garden, and the scenery is very beautiful.
  - 十年磨一剑(劍)。
    - Shí nián mó yī jiàn.
    - It takes ten years of practice to hone a sword.

### 华(華)侨(僑) → 侨(僑)
- **traditional:** 僑
- **definition:** to live abroad
- **pinyin:** qiáo
- **components:**
  - 亻
    - rén
    - person
  - 喬
    - qiáo
    - tall
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 亻 (rén, "person") + phonetic 喬 (qiáo, "tall") ->
    - to live abroad
  - **interpretation:** The character 僑 (qiáo), meaning 'to live abroad', combines the semantic element 亻 (rén, 'person') with the phonetic 喬 (qiáo, 'tall'). This suggests the notion of a 'person' living 'high or distant', metaphorically extending to residing in a different land, or 'abroad'.
  - **simplification:** The simplified form 侨 replaces the semantic 喬 (qiáo, "tall") with the simpler phonetic component 乔 (qiáo).
- **examples:**
  - 许(許)多华(華)侨(僑)在国(國)外努力工作。
    - Xǔduō huáqiáo zài guówài nǔlì gōngzuò.
    - Many overseas Chinese work hard abroad.
  - 他经(經)常思念异(異)国(國)他乡(鄉)的亲(親)戚，尤其是在节(節)假日。
    - Tā jīngcháng sīniàn yìguó tāxiāng de qīnqī, yóuqí shì zài jiéjiàrì.
    - He often misses his relatives in foreign countries, especially during holidays.
  - 海外侨(僑)胞为(為)家乡(鄉)的发(發)展做出了贡(貢)献(獻)。
    - Hǎiwài qiáobāo wèi jiāxiāng de fāzhǎn zuòchūle gòngxiàn.
    - Overseas Chinese contribute to the development of their hometowns.

### 华(華)侨(僑) → 侨(僑) → 喬
- **definition:** tall
- **pinyin:** qiáo
- **components:**
  - 高
    - gāo
    - high; tall
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** 高 (meaning 'high, tall') with a differentiating marker (variously a stroke, 屮, 止, 力, 九, or 夭) added
  - **interpretation:** The character 喬 (qiáo) is a differentiated form of 高 (gāo, 'high, tall'), created by marking it with additional strokes or components to distinguish it from the original character. This was done to differentiate its use for meanings specifically related to 'tall' or 'high', leading to its specific sense.
- **examples:**
  - 喬木常常长(長)得很高。
    - Qiáomù chángcháng zhǎng de hěn gāo.
    - Tall trees often grow very high.
  - 他住在一座喬楼(樓)中。
    - Tā zhù zài yī zuò qiáo lóu zhōng.
    - He lives in a tall building.
  - 那座喬山看起来(來)壮(壯)观(觀)。
    - Nà zuò qiáo shān kànqǐlái zhuàngguān.
    - That tall mountain looks magnificent.

### 华(華)侨(僑) → 侨(僑) → 喬 → 高
- **definition:** high; tall
- **pinyin:** gāo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tall building
  - **interpretation:** The character 高(gāo, "high; tall") visually represents a tall structure or building, symbolizing height or elevation. Unlike 京(jīng, "capital"), it is straightforwardly a representation of height.

    - Nà kē shù zhǎng de gāo, bǐ qítā shù gāo hěn duō.
    - That tree grows tall, much taller than the other trees.
  - 飞(飛)机(機)在高空飞(飛)行。
    - Fēijī zài gāokōng fēixíng.
    - The airplane flies in high altitude.
