  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 西 (xī, "west"), 門 (mén, "gate"), 町 (dīng, "pavilion; village; ward") is a term used in Taiwan to designate wards or urban districts.

  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a bag or basket, which was later borrowed for its phonetic value to represent the direction 'west'.
