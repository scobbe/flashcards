## cash
### xiànjīn
---
- **traditional:** 現金
- **definition:** cash
- **pinyin:** xiànjīn
- **components:**
  - 现(現)
    - xiàn
    - present; current
  - 金
    - jīn
    - metal; money; gold
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 現 (xiàn, "present; current") indicates immediacy, while 金 (jīn, "metal; money; gold") refers to wealth or money. Together, 現金 represents the concept of "cash" as current, tangible money or wealth in hand.
  - **interpretation:** 現 (xiàn, "present; current") suggests immediacy and existence in the now, while 金 (jīn, "metal; money; gold") traditionally represents wealth, especially in its physical form. The combination 現金 describes wealth that is immediately accessible and available to use, naturally leading to the concept of "cash" as opposed to other forms of wealth which might be less liquid, like bank accounts or investments.
- **examples:**
  - 请带现(VIP請帶現金)金来(來)付款。
    - Qǐng dài xiànjīn lái fùkuǎn.
    - Please bring cash to make the payment.
  - 他只有现(他只有現金)金，没(沒)有( )信( )用( )卡( )。
    - Tā zhǐ yǒu xiànjīn, méiyǒu xìnyòngkǎ.
    - He only has cash, no credit card.
  - 商店提供现(金商店提供現金)金折扣。
    - Shāngdiàn tígōng xiànjīn zhékòu.
    - The store offers cash discounts.

### 现(現)金( ) → 金 → 呂

  - **description:**
