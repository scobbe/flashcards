### wúfǎ
---
- **traditional:** 無法
- **definition:** unable
- **pinyin:** wúfǎ
- **components:**
  - 无(無)
    - wú
    - without; none; lack
  - 法
    - fǎ
    - method; law; way
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** These two characters together express the concept of being unable or without means.
  - **interpretation:** 無(wú, "without; none; lack") indicates nonexistence or the absence of something, often setting the stage for an impossibility. 法(fǎ, "method; law; way") refers to a manner, method, or means by which something can be done. Together, 無法(wúfǎ, "unable") suggests a lack of means or method, thus expressing the idea of being unable to do something.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )们(們)无(無)法( )抵( )达(達)那( )里(裡)。
    - Wǒmen wúfǎ dǐdá nàlǐ.
    - We are unable to reach there.
  - 他( )因( )受( )伤(傷)而( )无(無)法( )比( )赛(賽)。
    - Tā yīn shòushāng ér wúfǎ bǐsài.
    - He is unable to compete due to injury.
  - 这(這)个(個)问(問)题(題)太( )复(複)杂(雜)以( )至( )于(於)我( )们(們)无(無)法( )解( )决(決)。
