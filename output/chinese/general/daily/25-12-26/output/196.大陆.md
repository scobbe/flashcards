### dàlù
---
- **traditional:** 大陸
- **definition:** mainland
- **pinyin:** dàlù
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large; great; vast
  - 陆(陸)
    - lù
    - land; continent; six; shore
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big") + 陆(陸) (lù, "land") combine to imply a large mass of land, thus conveying the sense of 'mainland'.
  - **interpretation:** 大 (dà, "big") implies something large or extensive. 陆(陸) (lù, "land") specifically denotes ground or earth. Together, these characters create the image of a vast expanse of land, which naturally leads to the concept of the 'mainland' as opposed to islands or smaller landforms.
- **examples:**
  - 中( )国(國)大( )陆(陸)的人口很多。
    - Zhōngguó dàlù de rénkǒu hěn duō.
    - The population of the Chinese mainland is very large.
  - 我( )想( )去( )大( )陆(陸)旅( )行( )。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù dàlù lǚxíng.
    - I want to travel to the mainland.
  - 大( )陆(陸)的( )文( )化( )多( )样(樣)，非常有趣。
    - Dàlù de wénhuà duōyàng, fēicháng yǒuqù.
    - The culture of the mainland is diverse and very interesting.

### 大( )陆(陸) → 大 → 夫
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū

  - 大

    - big
- **etymology:**

### 大( )陆(陸) → 陆(陸)
- **traditional:** 陸
- **definition:** land; continent; six; shore
- **pinyin:** lù
- **components:**
  - 阝(阜)
    - fù
    - mound
  - 坴
    - lù
    - continent, land
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound') + Phonetic: 坴 (lù), used for phonetic value ->
    - land, elevated ground
  - **interpretation:** The character 陸(陆) (lù, 'land') combines the semantic component 阝(阜) (fù, 'mound'), indicating something related to earth or elevation, with 坴 (lù) for its phonetic representation. This effectively communicates the idea of elevated or significant land.
  - **simplification:** 陸 was simplified to 陆(陸) by retaining the 阝(阜) component for semantic value and using a simpler version of 坴 for phonetic association.
- **examples:**
  - 那艘船终将驶向陆(陸)。
    - Nà sōu chuán zhōng jiāng shǐ xiàng lù.
    - That boat will eventually sail towards land.
  - 他( )们(們)跨( )过(過)波( )涛(濤)，抵达陆(陸)。
    - Tāmen kuà guò bōtāo, dìdá lù.
    - They crossed the waves and reached the land.
