## Xiamen
### Xiàmén
---
- **traditional:** 廈門
- **definition:** Xiamen
- **pinyin:** Xiàmén
- **components:**
  - 厦(廈)
    - xià
    - mansion
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate; door; entrance
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 厦(廈) (xià, "mansion") and 门(門) (mén, "gate") together form a name for the city of Xiamen.
  - **interpretation:** 厦(廈)门(門) (Xiàmén, "Xiamen") originates from the area's historical significance as a gateway to the sea. "厦" indicates the grand structures or mansions often found in the city, while "门" signifies its strategic importance as a gate or entrance point.
- **examples:**
  - 我从(從)北京坐飞(飛)机(機)去厦(廈)门(門)。
    - Wǒ cóng Běijīng zuò fēijī qù Xiàmén.
    - I take a plane from Beijing to Xiamen.
  - 厦(廈)门(門)有美丽(麗)的海岸线(線)，吸引了许(許)多游(遊)客。
    - Xiàmén yǒu měilì de hǎi'ànxiàn, xīyǐnle xǔduō yóukè.
    - Xiamen has a beautiful coastline, attracting many tourists.

  - **simplification:** The character 门 is a simplified form of 門, where the complex structure is replaced by a more straightforward representation while preserving the overall image of a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 学(學)校门(門)前站着(著)一位老师(師)。
    - Xuéxiào mén qián zhànzhe yī wèi lǎoshī(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - A teacher stands at the school gate.
  - 请(請)你从(從)这(這)扇门(門)进(進)来(來)，把鞋子脱(脫)掉。
    - Qǐng nǐ cóng zhè shàn mén(請你從這扇門) jìnlái(進來)，bǎ xiézi(把鞋子) tuōdiào(脫掉)。
    - Please come in through this door and take off your shoes.
  - 夜深了，只有微弱的光从(從)门(門)缝(縫)里(裡)泄(洩)出。
    - Yè shēn le(夜深了)，zhǐ yǒu wēiruò de guāng cóng mén fèng lǐ xiè chū(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - It's late at night, and only a faint light seeps through the gap in the door.
