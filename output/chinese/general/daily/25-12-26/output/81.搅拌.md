## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **traditional:** 攪拌
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir; disturb
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix; blend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 攪 (jiǎo, "stir") is associated with using hands or tools to mix or disturb substances. 拌 (bàn, "mix") involves combining substances together, often using a tool to achieve an even blend.
  - **interpretation:** 攪 (jiǎo, "stir") involves the action of agitating a substance to disturb it, suggesting motion within. In combination, 拌 (bàn, "mix") indicates the purpose of the agitation, aiming for a uniform distribution of different elements. Thus, 攪拌 (jiǎobàn) conveys actively stirring to achieve thorough mixing, integrating the movements of disturbing and combining.
- **examples:**
  - 他需要搅(攪)拌这(這)汤(湯)。
    - Tā xūyào jiǎobàn zhè tāng.
    - He needs to stir this soup.
  - 请(請)用勺子慢慢搅(攪)拌。
    - Qǐng yòng sháozi mànmàn jiǎobàn.
    - Please stir slowly with a spoon.
  - 在加入鸡(雞)蛋后(後)，继(繼)续(續)搅(攪)拌混合物。
    - Zài jiārù jīdàn hòu, jìxù jiǎobàn hùnhéwù.
    - After adding the eggs, continue to stir the mixture.
### 搅(攪)拌 → 搅(攪)
- **traditional:** 攪
- **definition:** stir; disturb
- **pinyin:** jiǎo
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 考
    - kǎo
    - examine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌(手) (shǒu, 'hand') + phonetic 考 (kǎo) ->
    - action of the hand ->
    - stir
  - **interpretation:** The character 攪 (jiǎo, 'stir') uses 手 (shǒu, 'hand') as the semantic component, indicating an action performed by hand. 考 (kǎo) provides the phonetic element, lending its sound to the character. The combination suggests using the hand to agitate or mix, leading to the meaning 'stir'.
  - **simplification:** 搅 is the simplified form of 攪, where the 扌(手) (shǒu, 'hand') radical is retained while 考 (kǎo) is modified to simplify its structure.
- **examples:**
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)在锅(鍋)里(裡)搅(攪)汤(湯)。
    - Māma zài guō lǐ jiǎo tāng.
    - Mom is stirring the soup in the pot.
  - 他用勺子轻(輕)轻(輕)地搅(攪)着(著)，以防汤(湯)溢出。
    - Tā yòng sháozi qīngqīng de jiǎo zhe, yǐ fáng tāng yì chū.
    - He gently stirs with a spoon to prevent the soup from overflowing.
  - 烹饪(飪)时(時)，常常需要耐心地搅(攪)拌。
    - Pēngrèn shí, chángcháng xūyào nàixīn de jiǎobàn.
    - When cooking, you often need to stir patiently.
### 搅(攪)拌 → 搅(攪) → 手
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand with fingers; top stroke is a bent over middle finger, horizontal strokes represent fingers
  - **interpretation:** The character 手 (shǒu, "hand") is a direct pictorial representation of a human hand with all five fingers, which distinguishes it from similar characters representing claws or simplified fingers. This visually conveys its meaning as a hand clearly.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手写(寫)字。
    - Tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - He writes with his hand.
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)牵(牽)着(著)我的手走路。

### 搅(攪)拌 → 拌
- **definition:** mix; blend
- **pinyin:** bàn
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 半
    - bàn
    - half, middle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic: 半 (bàn) =
    - action performed with hands ->
    - mix
  - **interpretation:** The character 拌 (bàn, "mix") uses the hand radical 扌 (shǒu, "hand") to indicate an action typically performed with the hands, while 半 (bàn) provides the phonetic element to suggest the sound of the word. Together, they illustrate the action of mixing or stirring, which is commonly done with hands.
- **examples:**
  - 我将(將)蔬菜和调(調)料拌在一起，做成美味沙拉。
    - Wǒ jiāng shūcài hé tiáoliào bàn zài yìqǐ, zuò chéng měiwèi shālà.
    - I mix the vegetables and seasonings together to make a delicious salad.
  - 她拌面(麵)条(條)。
    - Tā bàn miàntiáo.
    - She mixes the noodles.
  - 厨(廚)师(師)拌汤(湯)时(時)，加入了秘密佐料。
    - Chúshī bàn tāng shí, jiārùle mìmì zuǒliào.

### 搅(攪)拌 → 拌 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞(飛)来(來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.

  - 她用扌(手)制(製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺(藝)术(術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细(細)节(節)。
