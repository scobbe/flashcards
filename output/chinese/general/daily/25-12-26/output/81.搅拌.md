## stir; mix
### jiǎobàn
---
- **traditional:** 攪拌
- **definition:** stir; mix
- **pinyin:** jiǎobàn
- **components:**
  - 搅(攪)
    - jiǎo
    - stir; disturb
  - 拌
    - bàn
    - mix; blend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 攪 (jiǎo, "stir") is associated with using hands or tools to mix or disturb substances. 拌 (bàn, "mix") involves combining substances together, often using a tool to achieve an even blend.
  - **interpretation:** 攪 (jiǎo, "stir") involves the action of agitating a substance to disturb it, suggesting motion within. In combination, 拌 (bàn, "mix") indicates the purpose of the agitation, aiming for a uniform distribution of different elements. Thus, 攪拌 (jiǎobàn) conveys actively stirring to achieve thorough mixing, integrating the movements of disturbing and combining.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )需( )要( )搅(攪)拌( )这(這)汤(湯)。
    - Tā xūyào jiǎobàn zhè tāng.
    - He needs to stir this soup.
  - 请(請)用( )勺( )子( )慢( )慢( )搅(攪)拌( )。
    - Qǐng yòng sháozi mànmàn jiǎobàn.
    - Please stir slowly with a spoon.
  - 在( )加( )入( )鸡(雞)蛋( )后(後)，继(繼)续(續)搅(攪)拌( )混( )合( )物( )。
