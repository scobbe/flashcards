## shopping mall
### shāngchǎng
---
- **traditional:** 商場
- **definition:** shopping mall
- **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
- **components:**
  - 商
    - shāng
    - commerce; business; trade; negotiate
  - 场(場)
    - chǎng
    - field; site; market; level
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 商 (shāng, "commerce") refers to business activities. 場 (chǎng, "field; place") denotes a specific location or space. Together, 商場 (shāngchǎng, "shopping mall") signifies a place designated for commercial activities, where various shops and businesses are situated in a central location.
  - **interpretation:** 商 (shāng, "commerce") indicates business dealings, giving the sense that the location is centered around trade and consumer activities. 場 (chǎng, "field; place") implies a designated area, making 商場 (shāngchǎng) a large, permanent site where commerce is concentrated, essentially capturing the concept of a shopping mall. The image of many different businesses coming together in the same field or site is the essence of this word.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)家商场(場)非常大。
    - Zhè jiā shāngchǎng fēicháng dà.
    - This shopping mall is very large.
  - 我们(們)在商场(場)买(買)到了很多东(東)西。
    - Wǒmen zài shāngchǎng mǎidào le hěn duō dōngxi.
    - We bought many things at the shopping mall.
  - 商场(場)里(裡)有许(許)多餐厅(廳)。
    - Shāngchǎng li yǒu xǔduō cāntīng.
    - There are many restaurants in the shopping mall.

The original combination of speaking slowly and considered estimation links to deliberation necessary in trading activities.

### 商场(場) → 商 → 㕯 → 言 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.

    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.

### 商场(場) → 商 → 章
- **definition:** chapter; seal
- **pinyin:** zhāng
- **components:**
  - 辛
    - xīn
    - chisel
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 辛 (xīn, "chisel") + 日 (rì, "sun") =
    - chisel piercing a piece of jade ->
    - originally meant "to treat jade" ->
    - later evolved to "chapter"
  - **interpretation:** The character 章 initially depicted a chisel (辛) piercing a piece of jade, represented by the 日 symbol. This depiction symbolized the process of treating jade. Over time, it likely evolved to be used for the concept of "chapter," as jade carvings and inscriptions often represented chapters or sections in a work.
- **examples:**
  - 第一章让(讓)人印象深刻。
    - Dì yī zhāng ràng rén yìnxiàng shēnkè.
    - The first chapter left a deep impression.
  - 他们(們)讨(討)论(論)了第二章的内(內)容。
    - Tāmen tǎolùn le dì èr zhāng de nèiróng.
    - They discussed the content of the second chapter.
  - 第一章启(啟)发(發)了读(讀)者，第二章展开(開)了故事。
    - Dì yī zhāng qǐfā le dúzhě, dì èr zhāng zhǎnkāi le gùshì.
    - The first chapter inspired the readers, and the second chapter unfolded the story.

- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a tool—chisel or awl—used to mark slaves and criminals ->
    - 辛
  - **interpretation:** The character 辛 (xīn) originally depicted a tool such as a chisel, used for marking or branding. The association with a sharp tool helps explain its extended meaning of 'spice,' as spices can have a pungent, sharp quality reminiscent of a tool's point or effect.

### 商场(場) → 商 → 章 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**

    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
