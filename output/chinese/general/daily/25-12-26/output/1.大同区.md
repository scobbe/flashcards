## Datong District, Taipei
### Dàtóng Qū
---
- **traditional:** 大同區
- **definition:** Datong District, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Dàtóng Qū
- **components:**
  - 大同
    - dàtóng
    - Great Unity; place name
  - 区(區)
    - qū
    - area; district
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big; great") conveys size or importance, 同 (tóng, "same; together") suggests unity or harmony, and 區 (qū, "area; district") designates a geographical division.
  - **interpretation:** 大同 (dàtóng, "Great Unity") originates from a Confucian ideal in the Book of Rites (禮記/礼记), describing a utopian society characterized by harmony and fairness; it refers here to a place name, conveying a sense of significant unity or community. 區 (qū, "area; district") simply denotes the administrative division.
- **examples:**
  - 大同区(區)有许(許)多历(歷)史建筑(築)。
    - Dàtóng Qū yǒu xǔduō lìshǐ jiànzhù.
    - Datong District has many historical buildings.
  - 我想去大同区(區)的夜市。
    - Wǒ xiǎng qù Dàtóng Qū de yèshì.
    - I want to visit the night market in Datong District.
  - 大同区(區)是台北的重要区(區)域。
    - Dàtóng Qū shì Táiběi de zhòngyào qūyù.
    - Datong District is an important area in Taipei.
### 大同区(區) → 大同
- **definition:** Great Unity; place name
- **pinyin:** dàtóng
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; great
  - 同
    - tóng
    - same; together
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 大 (dà, "big; great") + 同 (tóng, "same; together") =
    - Great Unity
  - **interpretation:** 大 (dà, "big; great") signifies something vast or significant. 同 (tóng, "same; together") indicates a sense of unity or coming together. In the Confucian context, 大同 (dàtóng, "Great Unity") refers to an ideal world where harmony and shared interests prevail, a vision detailed in the Book of Rites (禮記/礼记), aiming for a society where everyone works towards common welfare. The concept involves worldly unity and equality, projecting a utopian social order. Additionally, 大同 (dàtóng, "Datong") is a place name, reflecting either the historical influence or aspiration for such a harmony in certain regions.
- **examples:**
  - 大同是一个(個)历(歷)史悠久的城市。
    - Dàtóng shì yí gè lìshǐ yōujiǔ de chéngshì.
    - Datong is a city with a long history.
  - 理想的社会(會)应(應)该(該)实(實)现(現)大同。
    - Lǐxiǎng de shèhuì yīnggāi shíxiàn dàtóng.
    - An ideal society should achieve Great Unity.
  - 他计(計)划(劃)今年去大同旅游(遊)。
    - Tā jìhuà jīnnián qù Dàtóng lǚyóu.
    - He plans to travel to Datong this year.
### 大同区(區) → 大同 → 大
- **definition:** big; great
- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫 (fū, 'man')
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(dà) depicts a person facing forward, representing 'big' by suggesting the idea of someone standing prominently. Although often thought of as a person with outstretched arms, early scripts do not support this interpretation. It was originally the form of 夫 (fū, 'man') and was later phonetically borrowed for the meaning 'big.'
- **examples:**

    - The vast ocean waves surge, showcasing the power of nature.
  - 大人物登场(場)，留下传(傳)奇。
    - Dà rénwù dēngchǎng, liúxià chuánqí.
    - Great figures make an appearance, leaving behind legends.

### 大同区(區) → 大同 → 大 → 夫
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a variant form of 大 (dà, "big") representing an adult man with a hairpin ->
    - became associated with 夫 (fū, "man").
  - **interpretation:** 夫 (fū, "man") was visually similar to 大 (dà, "big") and was originally interchangeable. The addition of the horizontal stroke was interpreted as a hairpin, symbolizing adulthood for men.
- **examples:**

### 大同区(區) → 大同 → 同
- **definition:** same; together
- **pinyin:** tóng
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - "mouth"
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** Pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally depicted as a pipe 筒 (tǒng, "pipe") ->
    - 口 added as decoration ->
    - interpreted as mouth 口 (kǒu, "mouth") giving orders ->
    - evolved to mean togetherness and unity
  - **interpretation:** The character 同 (tóng, "same; unity; together; common") originally depicted a pipe but later took on the symbolic meaning of unity, possibly influenced by the shared cooperation in activities like music or rigid group tasks. The addition of 口 (kǒu, "mouth") emphasizes communication and collaboration.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)同心协(協)力。
    - Wǒmen tóngxīn xiélì.
    - We work together with one heart and mind.
  - 他们(們)穿着(著)同款衣服，看起来(來)十分整齐(齊)。
    - Tāmen chuānzhe tóng kuǎn yīfú, kàn qǐlái shífēn zhěngqí.

### 大同区(區) → 大同 → 同 → 口
- **definition:** "mouth"
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**
  - 他张(張)开(開)口，大声(聲)喊叫。
    - Tā zhāngkāi kǒu, dàshēng hǎnjiào.
    - He opened his mouth and shouted loudly.
  - 这(這)口饭(飯)入口即化。
    - Zhè kǒu fàn rùkǒu jíhuà.
    - This mouthful of food melts in your mouth.
  - 小女孩咬了一口苹(蘋)果，露出甜美的笑容。
    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
    - The little girl took a bite of the apple and smiled sweetly.

### 大同区(區) → 区(區)
- **traditional:** 區
- **definition:** area; district
- **pinyin:** qū
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - enclosure
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - goods; quality; many people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (a container or enclosure) + 品 (many people) =
    - people inside an area ->
    - district; region; area
  - **interpretation:** The character represents many people (品) within a boundary or enclosure (匸), intuitively suggesting a designated area or district where people congregate and interact.
  - **simplification:** 區 (qū, "district; region; area") was simplified by replacing 品 (pǐn, "goods") with 乂 (yì, "to govern"), reducing the complexity while retaining the structure of 匸 (xì, "enclosure").
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)区(區)域的空气(氣)非常清新。
    - Zhège qūyù de kōngqì fēicháng qīngxīn.
    - The air in this area is very fresh.
  - 我们(們)住在城市的商业(業)区(區)。
    - Wǒmen zhù zài chéngshì de shāngyèqū.
    - We live in the commercial district of the city.
  - 该(該)地区(區)以其风(風)景优(優)美而闻(聞)名。
    - Gāi dìqū yǐ qí fēngjǐng yōuměi ér wénmíng.
    - The region is known for its beautiful scenery.
### 大同区(區) → 区(區) → 匸
- **definition:** enclosure
- **pinyin:** xì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠃊 (symbolic graphic) ->
    - depicts an enclosure hiding something or someone ->
    - evolved to represent a concealed space or item
  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Its graphical form suggests something contained or hidden, which aligns with its meaning as a 'box' or an enclosure. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.
- **examples:**
  - 他把匸搬到了房间(間)角落。
    - Tā bǎ xì bān dào le fángjiān jiǎoluò.
    - He moved the box to the corner of the room.
  - 我们(們)在仓(倉)库(庫)找到了匸，里(裡)面装(裝)有秘密文件。
    - Wǒmen zài cāngkù zhǎodào le xì, lǐmiàn zhuāng yǒu mìmì wénjiàn.
    - We found the box in the warehouse, and it contained secret documents.
  - 工厂(廠)工人把旧(舊)匸清理了，然后(後)再利用。
    - Gōngchǎng gōngrén bǎ jiù xì qīnglǐ le, ránhòu zài lìyòng.
    - Factory workers cleaned the old box and then reused it.

### 大同区(區) → 区(區) → 品
- **definition:** goods; quality; many people
- **pinyin:** pǐn
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - Three 口 (kǒu, 'mouth') arranged together =
    - multiple mouths speaking or consuming ->
    - representing many or various items
  - **interpretation:** The use of three mouths suggests plurality or variety. It's similar in structure to 晶 (jīng, 'sparkle'), which uses three 日 (rì, 'sun') to suggest brightness or vibrancy with multiple light sources.
- **examples:**
  - 比赛(賽)吸引了数(數)以千计(計)的品，个(個)个(個)英姿飒(颯)爽。

    - The competition attracted thousands of people, and everyone looked heroic.
  - 节(節)日庆(慶)典上品众(眾)多，场(場)面热(熱)烈非凡。
    - Jiérì qìngdiǎn shàng pǐn zhòngduō, chǎngmiàn rèliè fēifán.
    - The festive celebration saw a large crowd, and the scene was extraordinarily lively.
  - 广(廣)场(場)上聚集了众(眾)多品，大家热(熱)情互动(動)。
    - Guǎngchǎng shàng jùjíle zhòngduō pǐn, dàjiā rèqíng hùdòng.
