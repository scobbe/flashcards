## vegan
### chúnsùshízhě
---
- **traditional:** 純素食者
- **definition:** vegan
- **pinyin:** chúnsùshízhě
- **components:**
  - 纯(純)
    - chún
    - pure; undiluted; clean
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain; vegetarian; simple
  - 食
    - shí
    - food; eat; meal
  - 者
    - zhě
    - person; one who...; -er
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 純 (chún, "pure") + 素 (sù, "plain; vegetarian") + 食 (shí, "food") + 者 (zhě, "person") indicate a person who eats pure and plain food, i.e., one who follows a vegan diet.
  - **interpretation:** 純 (chún, "pure") implies the exclusion of animal products, aligning with a purely plant-based diet. 素 (sù, "plain; vegetarian") refers to foods derived from plants without animal involvement. 食 (shí, "food") reinforces the dietary aspect, while 者 (zhě, "person") indicates an individual who practices this lifestyle. The combination distinctly describes someone who abstains from all animal products, a vegan.
- **examples:**
  - 我的朋友是一个(個)严(嚴)格的纯(純)素食者。
    - Wǒ de péngyǒu shì yīgè yángé de chúnsùshízhě.
    - My friend is a strict vegan.
  - 作为(為)纯(純)素食者，她总(總)是寻(尋)找新的食谱(譜)。
    - Zuòwéi chúnsùshízhě, tā zǒng shì xúnzhǎo xīn de shípǔ.
    - As a vegan, she is always looking for new recipes.
  - 他开(開)了一家纯(純)素食者餐厅(廳)。
    - Tā kāi le yī jiā chúnsùshízhě cāntīng.
    - He opened a vegan restaurant.
### 纯(純)素食者 → 纯(純)
- **traditional:** 純
- **definition:** pure; undiluted; clean
- **pinyin:** chún
- **components:**
  - 糸
    - mì
    - silk; thread
  - 屯
    - tún
    - barracks; station troops
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 糸 (mì, "silk; thread") + phonetic 屯 (tún)
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic element of 糸 (mì, "silk; thread"), representing something fine and uniform like a singular, untainted thread, with the phonetic 屯 (tún) to provide the pronunciation. Together, these components convey the sense of something being 'pure' or 'without impurities,' emphasizing the idea of uniformity and exclusivity in purity.
  - **simplification:** Simplified by reducing 糸 (mì, "silk; thread") to 纟, which is a simpler form used in several characters to maintain the semantic implication of 'silk; thread.'
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)水看起来(來)很纯(純)。
    - Zhège shuǐ kàn qǐlái hěn chún.
    - This water looks very pure.
  - 她有一颗(顆)纯(純)真的心。
    - Tā yǒu yī kē chúnzhēn de xīn.
    - She has a pure heart.
  - 为(為)了保持纯(純)净(淨)，不能往茶里(裡)加糖。
    - Wèile bǎochí chúnjìng，bùnéng wǎng chá lǐ jiā táng.
    - To keep it pure, you shouldn't add sugar to the tea.

### 纯(純)素食者 → 者
