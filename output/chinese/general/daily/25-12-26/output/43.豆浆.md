  - 寸

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 爿 + semantic ⺼ + semantic 寸 ->
    - offering meat as tribute
  - **interpretation:** The character 將 (jiāng, "lead") combines the phonetic component 爿 ("bed; small table") with semantic elements ⺼ ("meat") and 寸 ("hand"). Over time, this notion of directing or leading became associated with the act of guiding or commanding.
