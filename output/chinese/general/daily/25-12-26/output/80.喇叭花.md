## morning glory
### lǎbahuā
---
- **definition:** morning glory
- **pinyin:** lǎbahuā
- **components:**
  - 喇叭
    - lǎbā
    - horn; trumpet
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower; blossom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn") + 叭 (bā, "trumpet sound") + 花 (huā, "flower")
  - **interpretation:** The term 喇叭花 (lǎbahuā, "morning glory") describes the morning glory flower, which blooms in a shape reminiscent of a trumpet or horn (喇叭). Thus, the name captures both the flower's appearance and its vibrant, funnel-shaped blooms.
- **examples:**
  - 公园(園)里(裡)有盛开(開)的喇叭花。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ yǒu shèngkāi de lǎbahuā.
    - There are blooming morning glories in the park.
  - 早晨看到喇叭花，心情就变(變)好了。
    - Zǎochén kàn dào lǎbahuā, xīnqíng jiù biànhǎole.
    - Seeing morning glories in the early morning lifts the spirits.
### 喇叭花 → 喇叭
- **definition:** horn; trumpet
- **pinyin:** lǎbā
- **components:**
  - 喇
    - lǎ
    - horn sound; throat sound
  - 叭
    - bā
    - trumpet sound; a loud noise
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn sound") originally represents the sound of blowing an instrument. 叭 (bā, "trumpet sound") denotes the sound made by a horn or trumpet. Together, 喇叭(lǎbā, "horn; trumpet") represents a musical instrument that produces a loud, brassy sound.
  - **interpretation:** 喇 (lǎ, "horn sound") signifies the auditory experience associated with blowing through a horn-shaped instrument. 叭 (bā, "trumpet sound"), a sound loan, further emphasizes the distinct trumpet-like sound. The combination produces the concept of a 'horn' or 'trumpet,' directly capturing the loud, piercing sound associated with these musical instruments.
- **examples:**
  - 他说(說)话(話)像个(個)喇叭。
    - Tā shuōhuà xiàng gè lǎbā.
    - He talks like a horn.
  - 汽车(車)的喇叭响(響)了，司机(機)开(開)始紧(緊)张(張)。
    - Qìchē de lǎbā xiǎngle, sījī kāishǐ jǐnzhāng.
    - The car's horn sounded, and the driver began to feel nervous.
  - 小狗被喇叭的声(聲)音吓(嚇)到了。
    - Xiǎo gǒu bèi lǎbā de shēngyīn xià dào le.
    - The puppy was frightened by the sound of the horn.
