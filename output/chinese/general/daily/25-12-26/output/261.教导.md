## instruct; teach
### jiàodǎo
---
- **traditional:** 教導
- **definition:** instruct; teach
- **pinyin:** jiàodǎo
- **components:**
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach; educate; religion
  - 导(導)
    - dǎo
    - guide; lead; conduct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 教 (jiào, "teach") implies the traditional method of imparting knowledge, involving both explanation and example. 導 (dǎo, "guide") conveys the action of leading someone in a direction or showing them the way.
  - **interpretation:** 教 (jiào, "teach") involves explaining or showing someone how to do something, often invoking traditional methods or systems of thought. 導 (dǎo, "guide") adds the nuance of leading beyond just giving information, suggesting a process of steering someone in learning. Together, they form a comprehensive view of education as both imparting knowledge and guiding through learning journeys.
