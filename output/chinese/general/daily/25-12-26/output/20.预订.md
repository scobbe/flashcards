## reserve; book
### yùdìng
---
- **traditional:** 預訂
- **definition:** reserve; book
- **pinyin:** yùdìng
- **components:**
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - beforehand
  - 订(訂)
    - dìng
    - fix; set
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 預 (yù, "beforehand") indicating anticipation or planning in advance, and 訂 (dìng, "fix; set") meaning to establish or confirm. This combination suggests the action of confirming something in advance.
  - **interpretation:** The sense of 'reserve' or 'book' in 預訂 derives from the concept of making arrangements (訂) beforehand (預). The act of setting or fixing something (such as a schedule, seat, or service) ahead of time leads to the concept of reserving or booking.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )想( )预(預)订(訂)一( )张(張)餐( )桌( )。
    - Wǒ xiǎng yùdìng yī zhāng cānzhuō.
    - I would like to reserve a table.
  - 请(請)提( )前( )预(預)订(訂)机(機)票( )。
    - Qǐng tíqián yùdìng jīpiào.
    - Please book the plane tickets in advance.
  - 她( )已( )经(經)预(預)订(訂)了( )这(這)家( )酒( )店( )，我( )们(們)可( )以( )直( )接( )入( )住( )。
    - Tā yǐjīng yùdìng le zhè jiā jiǔdiàn, wǒmen kěyǐ zhíjiē rùzhù.

### 预(預)订(訂) → 预(預)

- **pinyin:** yù
- **components:**

- **etymology:**

  - **interpretation:** 頁 (yè, "page") suggests information or a plan, while 予 (yǔ) provides the sound, leading to the idea of preparation or arranging in advance. The concept of preparing or planning naturally precedes an event, reflecting the meaning 'in advance' or 'prepare'.

### 预(預)订(訂) → 预(預) → 頁

- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 首
    - shǒu

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

The concept of 'head' is also reflected in its modern usage as a radical in characters related to parts of the head or face.
  - **simplification:** 页 is a cursive shorthand of the traditional 頁, simplifying the structure while maintaining the core shape of a head.
- **examples:**
