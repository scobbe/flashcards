### huāshēng
---
- **definition:** peanut
- **pinyin:** huāshēng
- **components:**
  - 花
    - huā
    - flower
  - 生
    - shēng
    - birth; grow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 花 (huā, "flower") + 生 (shēng, "birth; grow") =
    - peanut
  - **interpretation:** The term 花生 (huāshēng, "peanut") uses the idea of a 'flower' (花) growing or being born (生). This reflects the peanut's growth process, as it flowers above ground and the pods develop underground, which might resemble a 'flower giving birth,' connecting it metaphorically to the earth.
