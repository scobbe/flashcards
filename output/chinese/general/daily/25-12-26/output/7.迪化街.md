## Dihua Street, Taipei
### Díhuà Jiē
---
- **definition:** Dihua Street, Taipei
- **pinyin:** Díhuà Jiē
- **components:**
  - 迪
    - dí
    - enlighten; illuminate
  - 化
    - huà
    - transform; change
  - 街
    - jiē
    - street
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 迪(dí, "enlighten; illuminate") 化(huà, "transform; change") 街(jiē, "street")
  - **interpretation:** 迪 (dí, "enlighten; illuminate") here suggests guidance or forward movement. 化 (huà, "transform; change") can imply transformation or cultural development. This reflects the street's historical significance as a center of commerce and cultural exchange in Taipei. 街 (jiē, "street") signifies the physical location. The name evokes an image of a place where people are guided and cultures transform, emphasizing its role as a vibrant commercial hub.
- **examples:**
  - 迪化街是一个(個)充满(滿)文化的地方。
    - Díhuà Jiē shì yí gè chōngmǎn wénhuà de dìfāng.
    - Dihua Street is a place full of culture.
  - 我喜欢(歡)在迪化街购(購)买(買)传(傳)统(統)草药(藥)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài Díhuà Jiē gòumǎi chuántǒng cǎoyào.
    - I like to buy traditional herbs at Dihua Street.
  - 每年春节(節)时(時)，迪化街都非常热(熱)闹(鬧)。
    - Měinián chūnjié shí, Díhuà Jiē dōu fēicháng rènào.
    - During the Spring Festival every year, Dihua Street is always very lively.
