## bully
### qīfu
---
- **traditional:** 欺負
- **definition:** bully
- **pinyin:** qīfu
- **components:**
  - 欺
    - qī
    - deceive
  - 负(負)
    - fù
    - bear; suffer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 欺 (qī, "deceive") + 负(負) (fù, "bear; suffer") is interpreted as causing someone to suffer through deceptive or harmful actions, which aligns with the concept of bullying.
  - **interpretation:** 欺负(負) (qīfu, "bully") combines the ideas of deceit and causing someone to bear suffering, representing the unfair and harmful behavior typical of bullying.
- **examples:**
  - 他欺负(負)同学(學)。
    - Tā qīfù tóngxué.
    - He bullies his classmates.
  - 小明欺负(負)同学(學)，结(結)果被老师(師)批评(評)。
    - Xiǎomíng qīfù tóngxué, jiéguǒ bèi lǎoshī pīpíng.
    - Xiaoming bullies his classmates, and as a result, he was criticized by the teacher.
### 欺负(負) → 欺
- **definition:** deceive
- **pinyin:** qī
- **components:**
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 气(氣) (qì, "air") + phonetic: 其 (qí, "his") =
    - giving false atmosphere ->
    - deceive
  - **interpretation:** 欺 (qī, "deceive") combines 气(氣) (qì, "air"), suggesting something intangible, with 其 (qí, "his"), which provides the phonetic element. Together, these imply creating a false impression or atmosphere, aligning with the meaning of deception.
- **examples:**
  - 不要欺别(別)人。
    - Bùyào qī bié rén.
    - Do not deceive others.
  - 他以花言巧语(語)欺骗(騙)了村民。
    - Tā yǐ huāyán qiǎoyǔ qī piàn le cūnmín.
    - He deceived the villagers with flowery words.
  - 商家欺骗(騙)消费(費)者，赢(贏)得了一时(時)的利益。
    - Shāngjiā qī piàn xiāofèi zhě, yíng dé le yìshí de lìyì.
    - The merchant deceived the consumers and gained a temporary profit.

### 欺负(負) → 欺 → 气(氣)
- **traditional:** 氣
- **definition:** air
- **pinyin:** qì
- **components:**
  - 米
    - mǐ
    - rice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氣 (phono-semantic: 气 for sound + 米 for meaning) ->
    - originally 'steam from cooked rice' ->
    - later 'air, flow'
  - **interpretation:** The character 氣 combines 气, representing the sound, with 米 (mǐ, 'rice'), suggesting the original meaning of steam emanating from cooked rice. Over time, the meaning shifted to encompass 'air' or 'flow', capturing the essence of movement or circulation.
  - **simplification:** 气(氣): 气 (qì, 'air') is a cursive shorthand of 氣, retaining the phonetic component but losing the semantic 米 ('rice').
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)清晨的空气(氣)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān qīngchén de kōngqì.

    - Hūxī xīnxiān kōngqì huì ràng rén jīngshén zhènfèn.
    - Breathing fresh air invigorates the spirit.
  - 他对(對)我们(們)发(發)脾气(氣)，可能是因为(為)工作压(壓)力太大。
    - Tā duì wǒmen fā píqì, kěnéng shì yīnwèi gōngzuò yālì tài dà.

### 欺负(負) → 欺 → 其
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 丌
    - qí
    - stand
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket) ->
    - 𠔝 with added 丌 (stand, phonetic) ->

  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a basket (𠀠), which later included a stand (丌) beneath it to stabilize the image on bronze inscriptions, forming 𠔝. - **examples:**

    - His playing is as soft as water.
  - 其计(計)划(劃)得到了同事们(們)的支持。
    - Qí jìhuà dédào le tóngshìmen de zhīchí.
    - His plan received the support of his colleagues.

### 欺负(負) → 负(負)
- **traditional:** 負
- **definition:** bear; suffer
- **pinyin:** fù
- **components:**
  - 貝
    - bèi
    - cowry
  - 否
    - fǒu
    - no; not
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 貝 (bèi, "cowry") as 'value' + phonetic 否 (fǒu) ->
    - bear burdens ->
    - bear; suffer
  - **interpretation:** In 負 (fù, "bear; suffer"), the 貝 (bèi, "cowry") represents carrying value or burdens, while 否 (fǒu) supplies the sound. The idea of carrying a burden connects to 'bear' and the related weight of responsibility links to 'suffer'.
  - **simplification:** The traditional 負 was simplified by removing the 貝 (bèi, "cowry") radical, retaining only the main strokes to reduce its complexity, reflecting 否 (fǒu).
- **examples:**
  - 他负(負)担(擔)了家庭的所有开(開)支。
    - Tā fùdānle jiātíng de suǒyǒu kāizhī.
    - He bore all the household expenses.
  - 这(這)个(個)项(項)目失败(敗)后(後)，他感到非常失望。
    - Zhège xiàngmù shībài hòu，tā gǎndào fēicháng shīwàng.
    - After the project failed, he felt very disappointed.
  - 她不堪负(負)荷，决(決)定放弃(棄)这(這)份工作。
    - Tā bùkān fùhè，juédìng fàngqì zhè fèn gōngzuò.
    - She couldn't bear the load and decided to quit the job.
### 欺负(負) → 负(負) → 貝
- **definition:** cowry
- **pinyin:** bèi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cowrie shell ->
    - used as currency in ancient China ->
    - stylized as 貝
  - **interpretation:** The character 貝(bèi, 'shell/money') visually represents a cowrie shell, which was used as a form of currency in ancient China. This explains why it is associated with money or valuables.
  - **simplification:** 贝 is a cursive script simplification of 貝, transforming the character into a more streamlined form while retaining its essence.
- **examples:**
  - 他把贝(貝)当作护(護)身符，放在口袋里(裡)。
    - Tā bǎ bèi dāng zuò hùshēn fú, fàng zài kǒudài lǐ.
    - He uses shells as a talisman, placing them in his pocket.
  - 古代人用贝(貝)作为(為)货(貨)币(幣)。
    - Gǔdài rén yòng bèi zuòwéi huòbì.
    - In ancient times, people used shells as currency.
