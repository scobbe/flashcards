## auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
### ruì
---
- **definition:** auspicious; jade token; surname Rui
- **pinyin:** ruì
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 睿
    - ruì
    - bright
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 玉 (yù, "jade") + phonetic: 瑞(睿) (ruì, "bright") =
    - jade as a symbol of brightness and good fortune ->
    - auspicious
  - **interpretation:** The character 瑞(rui) combines 玉 (yù, "jade"), symbolizing value and purity, with the original sense of brightness or clarity from the phonetic component. This association captures the idea of jade as a token of good fortune.
- **examples:**
  - 瑞雪兆丰(豐)年。
    - Ruì xuě zhào fēng nián.
    - Auspicious snowfall signifies a bountiful year.
  - 李瑞出席晚宴。
    - Lǐ ruì chūxí wǎnyàn.
    - Mr. Li Rui attended the dinner party.
  - 玉器中的瑞玉十分珍贵(貴)。
    - Yùqì zhōng de ruì yù shífēn zhēnguì.
    - The jade token among the jade artifacts is extremely precious.

### 瑞 → 玉
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem ->
    - dot added to differentiate from 王 (wáng, "king") ->
    - final form 玉 (yù, "jade")
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a picture of a jade cong, emphasizing its role in rituals and its cultural significance. The image eventually included a distinguishing dot to ensure clarity from similar characters like 王, underscoring the value jade held as a material in ancient contexts.
- **examples:**
  - 她收到了一个漂亮的玉手镯(鐲)。
    - Tā shōu dào le yī gè piàoliang de yù shǒuzhuó.
    - She received a beautiful jade bracelet.
  - 皇帝的王冠上镶(鑲)嵌着(著)闪(閃)耀的玉石。
    - Huángdì de wángguān shàng xiāngqiànzhe shǎnyào de yùshí.
    - The emperor's crown was inlaid with shining jade stones.
  - 古代的玉器常常被视(視)为(為)珍贵(貴)的艺(藝)术(術)品。
    - Gǔdài de yùqì chángcháng bèi shìwéi zhēnguì de yìshùpǐn.
    - Ancient jade artifacts are often regarded as precious works of art.

  - **description:**

    - deep insight
  - **interpretation:** In 睿, the character combines the visual of a gully 㕡 and the 目 (mù, "eye") to convey the sense of deep insight, where the eye peers into a metaphorical depth, symbolizing profound understanding or wisdom.

    - Jīnglǐ duì xīn fāng'àn zuò chūle ruìzhì de píngjià.
