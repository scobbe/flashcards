- **definition:** drink
- **pinyin:** hē
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 曷
    - hé
    - question; why
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 曷 ->
    - expression involving the mouth ->
    - to drink
  - **interpretation:** The semantic element 口 (kǒu, "mouth") indicates an action related to the mouth, such as drinking, while the phonetic component 曷 provides the sound. This combination naturally leads to the meaning of 'drink', as drinking involves using the mouth.
- **examples:**
  - 我每天早上都喝一杯咖啡。
    - Wǒ měitiān zǎoshang dōu hē yī bēi kāfēi.

It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
