## showroom
### yàngbǎnjiān
---
- **traditional:** 樣板間
- **definition:** showroom
- **pinyin:** yàngbǎnjiān
- **components:**
  - 样(樣)
    - yàng
    - appearance; pattern
  - 板
    - bǎn
    - board; template
  - 间(間)
    - jiān
    - room; between
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 樣 (yàng, "appearance; pattern") + 板 (bǎn, "board; template") + 間 (jiān, "room; between") combine to describe a space designed to show a template or example layout, hence 'showroom'. The idea is that the room (間) represents a pattern (樣板) that can be replicated or followed.
  - **interpretation:** 樣 (yàng, "appearance; pattern") suggests an outward appearance or model form. 板 (bǎn, "board; template") implies a standard or mold that can be followed. 間 (jiān, "room; between") refers to a physical space. Together, this forms the concept of a room set up to display a model for others to replicate, embodying the notion of a showroom.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)参(參)观(觀)了样(樣)板间(間)。
    - Wǒmen cānguānle yàngbǎnjiān.
    - We visited the showroom.
  - 装(裝)修公司提供了几(幾)个(個)样(樣)板间(間)，供客户(戶)参(參)考。
    - Zhuāngxiū gōngsī tígōngle jǐ gè yàngbǎnjiān, gōng kè hù cānkǎo.
    - The renovation company provided several showrooms for clients to reference.
  - 新的样(樣)板间(間)展示了最新的家居设(設)计(計)。
    - Xīn de yàngbǎnjiān zhǎnshìle zuìxīn de jiājū shèjì.
    - The new showroom displayed the latest home designs.
### 样(樣)板间(間) → 样(樣)
- **traditional:** 樣
- **definition:** appearance; pattern
- **pinyin:** yàng
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - wood
  - 羕
    - yàng
    - a place in ancient Shu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 木 (mù, 'wood') + phonetic 羕 (yàng) ->
    - meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 樣 (yàng, 'appearance; shape; manner') combines the semantic component 木 (mù, 'wood') to suggest a tangible or physical form, with the phonetic 羕 to provide the sound. The concept of 'appearance; shape; manner' relates to understanding form or style as if referring to forms made of or carved in wood.
  - **simplification:** 样(樣): 羊 (yáng, 'sheep') replaced 羕 (yàng) for a simpler phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 他的样(樣)子看起来(來)很疲惫(憊)。
    - Tā de yàngzi kàn qǐlái hěn pí bèi.
    - His appearance looks very tired.
  - 这(這)件衣服的款式和样(樣)式很流行。
    - Zhè jiàn yīfú de kuǎnshì hé yàngshì hěn liúxíng.
    - The style and appearance of this clothing are very popular.
  - 你应(應)该(該)学(學)学(學)他的工作样(樣)。
    - Nǐ yīnggāi xué xué tā de gōngzuò yàng.
    - You should learn his manner of working.

- **pinyin:** yǒng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

### 样(樣)板间(間) → 板
- **definition:** board; template
- **pinyin:** bǎn
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 反
    - fǎn
    - oppose
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 木 (mù, "tree") + phonetic 反 (fǎn) ->
    - board; plank
  - **interpretation:** The character 板 (bǎn, "board; plank") is a phono-semantic compound. The semantic 木 (mù, "tree") indicates the material of wood, which is the substance of a board. The phonetic 反 (fǎn) provides the pronunciation. This character directly refers to objects made from wood that are flat, like boards or planks.
- **examples:**
  - 他用力把钉(釘)子钉(釘)进(進)木板。

    - He forcefully nailed the nail into the wooden board.
  - 制(製)作这(這)个(個)模型需要许(許)多木板。
    - Zhìzuò zhège móxíng xūyào xǔduō mùbǎn.
    - Many wooden planks are needed to build this model.
  - 老师(師)在黑板上写(寫)下了答案。
    - Lǎoshī zài hēibǎn shàng xiěxià le dá'àn.
    - The teacher wrote the answers on the blackboard.

### 样(樣)板间(間) → 间(間)
- **traditional:** 間
- **definition:** room; between
- **pinyin:** jiān
- **components:**
  - 门(門)
    - mén
    - gate
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 門 (mén, "gate") + 日 (rì, "sun") =
    - light peeking through a gate ->
    - space/interval
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 門 (mén, "gate") as the semantic component, suggesting separation or division, with 日 (rì, "sun") as the phonetic component, invoking the image of sunlight streaming through a gap. This evokes the idea of a 'space' or 'interval' as it is visible between two gates or within an enclosure.
  - **simplification:** The simplification reduced the strokes in 日 (rì, "sun") for easier writing, but retained the overall structure.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)房间(間)很宽(寬)敞。
    - Zhège fángjiān hěn kuānchǎng.
    - This room is very spacious.
  - 两(兩)间(間)公寓，中间(間)隔着(著)花园(園)。
    - Liǎng jiān gōngyù, zhōngjiān gézhe huāyuán.
    - There are two apartments, with a garden in between.
  - 窗户(戶)和门(門)之间(間)，有个(個)狭(狹)小的空间(間)。
    - Chuānghù hé mén zhījiān, yǒu gè xiáxiǎo de kōngjiān.
    - Between the window and the door, there is a narrow space.
### 样(樣)板间(間) → 间(間) → 门(門)
- **traditional:** 門
- **definition:** gate
- **pinyin:** mén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 門 (mén, "gate") depicts a gate ->
    - simplified to 门 (mén, "gate")
  - **interpretation:** The character 门(門) (mén, "gate") represents the image of a double-sided gate. It simplifies to 门 but retains the depiction of an entrance or gateway.
  - **simplification:** The character 门 is a simplified form of 門, where the complex structure is replaced by a more straightforward representation while preserving the overall image of a gate.
- **examples:**
  - 学(學)校门(門)前站着(著)一位老师(師)。
    - Xuéxiào mén qián zhànzhe yī wèi lǎoshī(學校門前站著一位老師)。
    - A teacher stands at the school gate.
  - 请(請)你从(從)这(這)扇门(門)进(進)来(來)，把鞋子脱(脫)掉。
    - Qǐng nǐ cóng zhè shàn mén(請你從這扇門) jìnlái(進來)，bǎ xiézi(把鞋子) tuōdiào(脫掉)。
    - Please come in through this door and take off your shoes.
  - 夜深了，只有微弱的光从(從)门(門)缝(縫)里(裡)泄(洩)出。
    - Yè shēn le(夜深了)，zhǐ yǒu wēiruò de guāng cóng mén fèng lǐ xiè chū(只有微弱的光從門縫裡洩出)。
    - It's late at night, and only a faint light seeps through the gap in the door.
### 样(樣)板间(間) → 间(間) → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒(灑)满(滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.
