## umbrella
### yǔsǎn
---
- **traditional:** 雨傘
- **definition:** umbrella
- **pinyin:** yǔsǎn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 伞(傘)
    - sǎn
    - umbrella; parasol
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 雨 (yǔ, "rain") refers to precipitation that falls from clouds in the form of droplets, typically covering a wide area. 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") originally depicts a wooden frame covered with cloth for protection from rain. Because you place a wooden frame (冖) over an object to shield it from above, it's used alone for sun or rain shade. Combining these two, 雨傘 means an object specifically designed to shield from rain.
  - **interpretation:** 雨 (yǔ, "rain") signifies what umbrellas provide protection from. 伞(傘) (sǎn, "umbrella") indicates the tool used for such protection. The combination identifies a rain-specific shield, illustrating a straightforward link: a 雨傘 is an umbrella primarily for rain coverage.
- **examples:**
  - 今天下雨了，你( )带(帶)雨( )伞(傘)了( )吗(嗎)？
    - Jīntiān xià yǔ le, nǐ dài yǔsǎn le ma?
    - It's raining today, did you bring an umbrella?
  - 他( )旅( )行( )时(時)总(總)是( )随(隨)身( )携(攜)带(帶)雨( )伞(傘)。
    - Tā lǚxíng shí zǒngshì suíshēn xiédài yǔsǎn.
    - He always carries an umbrella with him when traveling.
  - 我( )把( )雨( )伞(傘)放( )在( )了( )门(門)口( )。
    - Wǒ bǎ yǔsǎn fàng zài le ménkǒu.

### 雨( )伞(傘) → 伞(傘)
- **traditional:** 傘
- **definition:** umbrella; parasol
- **pinyin:** sǎn
- **components:**
  - 𠈌
    - é
    - container
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𠈌 (container) + 人 (rén, "person") =
    - a person under a container shape ->
    - umbrella
  - **interpretation:** The character 傘 depicts a person beneath a structure resembling a cover or umbrella. This visually represents protection or covering, aligning with the meaning of 'umbrella' or 'shelter.'
  - **simplification:** The traditional character 傘 was simplified to 伞 by replacing 𠈌 with 丷, reducing the number of strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 她( )用( )伞(傘)遮( )阳(陽)。
