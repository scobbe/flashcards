### Fójiàotú
---
- **definition:** Buddhist
- **pinyin:** Fójiàotú
- **components:**
  - 佛
    - fó
    - Buddha
  - 教
    - jiào
    - teach; religion
  - 徒
    - tú
    - disciple; follower
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 佛 (fó, "Buddha"), 教 (jiào, "teach; religion"), 徒 (tú, "disciple; follower")
  - **interpretation:** 佛 (fó, "Buddha") refers to the enlightened being central to this religion. 教 (jiào, "teach; religion") denotes a systematic teaching or organized belief system. Thus, 佛教 (fójiào, "Buddhism") means the religious teachings of Buddha. 徒 (tú, "disciple; follower") refers to someone who adheres to or practices these teachings. Together, 佛教徒 (fójiàotú, "Buddhist") describes a person who follows the teachings of Buddhism or is a disciple of Buddha.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一位佛教徒。
    - Tā shì yī wèi Fójiàotú.
    - He is a Buddhist.
  - 很多佛教徒会(會)去( )寺( )庙(廟)祷(禱)告( )。
