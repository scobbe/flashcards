## breakup; change in relationship
### qíngbiàn
---
- **traditional:** 情變
- **definition:** breakup; change in relationship
- **pinyin:** qíngbiàn
- **components:**
  - 情
    - qíng
    - emotion; feeling; relationship
  - 变(變)
    - biàn
    - change; alter
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 情 (qíng, "emotion; feeling; relationship") represents emotions or the state of being related to someone. 變 (biàn, "change; alter") indicates a change or transformation. Together, 情變 (qíngbiàn) refers to a change or upheaval in a relationship, most commonly a breakup or a significant shift in emotional relations.
  - **interpretation:** The term combines the sense of personal emotions and feelings (情) with the concept of change or transformation (變). In this context, a change in feelings implies a shift or breakdown in a relationship, such as a breakup. The emotional component is forefronted, signaling significant emotional alteration.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )们(們)的( )感( )情( )经(經)历(歷)了( )情( )变(變)。
    - Tāmen de gǎnqíng jīnglìle qíngbiàn.
    - Their relationship experienced a breakup.
  - 情( )变(變)后(後)，他感到很失落。
    - Qíngbiàn hòu, tā gǎndào hěn shīluò.
    - After the breakup, he felt very lost.
  - 他( )们(們)因( )为(為)误(誤)会(會)而( )产(產)生( )了( )情( )变(變)，最( )后(後)分( )手( )了( )。
    - Tāmen yīnwèi wùhuì ér chǎnshēngle qíngbiàn, zuìhòu fēnshǒule.
