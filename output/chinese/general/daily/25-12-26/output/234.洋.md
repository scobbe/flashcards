## ocean; foreign
### yáng
---
- **definition:** ocean; foreign
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **components:**
  - 水
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 羊
    - yáng
    - sheep
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 羊 (yáng) ->
    - meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 洋(yáng, "ocean; foreign") combines 氵(shuǐ, "water") as the semantic component, indicating a connection to water or large bodies of water such as the ocean. The phonetic component 羊(yáng) lends its sound to the character. The expansive and vast nature of the ocean extends to the concept of 'foreign', mirroring the idea of distant or overseas places.
- **examples:**
  - 阳(陽)光洒(灑)在海洋上。
    - Yángguāng sǎ zài hǎiyáng shàng.
    - Sunlight spills over the ocean.
  - 他喜欢(歡)洋食，所以经(經)常去外国(國)餐厅(廳)。
    - Tā xǐhuān yángshí, suǒyǐ jīngcháng qù wàiguó cāntīng.
    - He likes foreign food, so he often goes to international restaurants.
  - 洋溢着(著)欢(歡)笑的气(氣)氛充满(滿)了整个(個)房间(間)。
    - Yángyìzhe huānxiào de qìfēn chōngmǎnle zhěnggè fángjiān.
    - An atmosphere filled with laughter pervaded the entire room.

### 洋 → 水
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts flowing water
  - **interpretation:** 水 (shuǐ, 'water') is a pictogram that directly represents the appearance of flowing water, making it intuitive and easy to recognize. Its simplicity and direct visual reference to its meaning have allowed it to remain unchanged over millennia.
- **examples:**
  - 水是一种(種)生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì yī zhǒng shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 夏天很热(熱)，喝水能解暑。
    - Xiàtiān hěn rè, hē shuǐ néng jiě shǔ.
    - It is very hot in summer, drinking water can relieve the heat.
  - 河水潺潺，沿岸绿(綠)树(樹)成荫(蔭)。
    - Hé shuǐ chánchán, yán'àn lǜshù chéng yīn.
    - The river flows babbling, and green trees along the bank provide shade.

### 洋 → 羊
- **definition:** sheep
- **pinyin:** yáng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a ram's head
  - **interpretation:** The character 羊(yáng, 'sheep') visually represents the head of a sheep or ram, capturing the animal's distinctive features. It serves as a straightforward pictogram, portraying one of the common animals in ancient agrarian societies.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)场(場)里(裡)有羊。
    - Nóngchǎng lǐ yǒu yáng.
    - There are sheep on the farm.
  - 春天来(來)了，羊群在草地上吃草。
    - Chūntiān lái le, yángqún zài cǎodì shàng chī cǎo.
    - In spring, the flock of sheep grazes on the meadow.
  - 那只(隻)羊看起来(來)很温(溫)顺(順)。
    - Nà zhī yáng kàn qǐlái hěn wēnshùn.
    - That sheep looks very gentle.
