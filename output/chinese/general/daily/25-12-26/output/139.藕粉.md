## lotus root starch
### ǒufěn
---
- **definition:** lotus root starch
- **pinyin:** ǒufěn
- **components:**
  - 藕
    - ǒu
    - lotus root
  - 粉
    - fěn
    - powder; starch; flour
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") and 粉 (fěn, "powder; starch") combine to form 藕粉, which refers to the starch derived from the lotus root.
  - **interpretation:** The character 藕 (ǒu, "lotus root") directly indicates the source of the starch, which is the lotus root itself. 粉 (fěn, "powder; starch") conveys the texture and form of the product, which is a finely ground, powdery substance made from processing the lotus root. Together, these characters unmistakably designate a type of starch derived from the lotus root, which is used as a food ingredient for its thickening properties, often in desserts and soups.
- **examples:**
  - 藕粉是一种(種)中式甜品。
    - Ǒufěn shì yī zhǒng zhōngshì tiánpǐn.
    - Lotus root starch is a type of Chinese dessert.
  - 小时(時)候，妈(媽)妈(媽)常煮藕粉羹。
    - Xiǎoshíhòu, māma cháng zhǔ ǒufěn gēng.
    - When I was young, my mom often cooked lotus root starch soup.
  - 藕粉不仅(僅)美味，而且营(營)养(養)丰(豐)富。
    - Ǒufěn bùjǐn měiwèi, érqiě yíngyǎng fēngfù.
    - Lotus root starch is not only delicious, but also rich in nutrients.
