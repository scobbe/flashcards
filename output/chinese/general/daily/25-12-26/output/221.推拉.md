## push and pull
### tuīlā
---
- **definition:** push and pull
- **pinyin:** tuīlā
- **components:**
  - 推
    - tuī
    - push; shove
  - 拉
    - lā
    - pull; drag; draw
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 推 (tuī, "push") and 拉 (lā, "pull") are combined directly to express the reciprocal actions of physically exerting force on an object away from and towards a person or center, respectively.
  - **interpretation:** 推 (tuī, "push") involves exerting force to move something away, while 拉 (lā, "pull") involves drawing something closer by force. In combination, 推拉 describes the dual physical actions or motions often encountered in daily activities, such as opening doors or operating machinery that requires both pushing and pulling actions.
- **examples:**
  - 推拉门(門)很方便。
    - Tuīlā mén hěn fāngbiàn.
    - Sliding doors are very convenient.
  - 请(請)勿用力推拉这(這)扇门(門)。
    - Qǐng wù yòng lì tuīlā zhè shàn mén.
    - Please do not forcefully push and pull this door.
  - 这(這)个(個)游(遊)戏(戲)需要团(團)队(隊)的推拉合作。
    - Zhège yóuxì xūyào tuánduì de tuīlā hézuò.
    - This game requires push and pull cooperation from the team.
### 推拉 → 推
- **definition:** push; shove
- **pinyin:** tuī
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 隹
    - zhuī
    - short-tailed bird
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird") =
    - push with the hand ->
    - push
  - **interpretation:** The character 推(tuī) combines 扌 (shǒu, "hand"), indicating the action done by hand, with 隹 (zhuī, "short-tailed bird"), which provides the phonetic cue. This combination visually represents the concept of pushing something with one's hand.
- **examples:**
  - 他推门(門)而入。
    - Tā tuī mén ér rù.
    - He pushed the door open and entered.
  - 我推他一把，他就赶(趕)快走开(開)。
    - Wǒ tuī tā yī bǎ, tā jiù gǎnkuài zǒu kāi.
    - I gave him a push, and he quickly moved away.
  - 警察推开(開)窗户(戶)查看。
    - Jǐngchá tuī kāi chuānghù chá kàn.
    - The police pushed the window open to inspect.
### 推拉 → 推 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞(飛)来(來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌(手)制(製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺(藝)术(術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细(細)节(節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
    - The artist meticulously carved every detail of the hand.

### 推拉 → 拉
- **definition:** pull; drag; draw
- **pinyin:** lā
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 立
    - lì
    - stand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 手 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 立 (lì) ->
    - pull
  - **interpretation:** The character 拉 (lā, "pull; drag; draw out") combines 手 (shǒu, "hand") as the semantic element, suggesting an action performed with the hand, with 立 (lì) as the phonetic component, providing the 'lā' sound. The intuitive image is a hand performing the action of pulling or dragging.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)帮(幫)我把椅子拉过(過)来(來)。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ bǎ yǐzi lā guòlái.
    - Please help me pull the chair over.
  - 他喜欢(歡)在地上拉着(著)玩具车(車)。
    - Tā xǐhuān zài dìshàng lā zhe wánjùchē.
    - He likes to drag toy cars on the floor.
  - 我们(們)要用力把船拉到岸边(邊)。
    - Wǒmen yào yònglì bǎ chuán lā dào ànbiān.
