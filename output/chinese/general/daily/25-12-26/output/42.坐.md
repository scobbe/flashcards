## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 留 ('to stay') + 土 ('ground') =
    - to stay on the ground ->
    - sit
  - **interpretation:** The character 坐 (zuò, 'sit; ride') combines the sense of 留 (liú, 'to stay') with 土 (tǔ, 'ground'), expressing the concept of remaining upon the ground, which naturally leads to the action of sitting. The idea of staying grounded naturally extends to 'sitting,' and by metaphor, to 'riding,' as one is seated while riding.
