## sit; ride
### zuò
---
- **definition:** sit; ride
- **pinyin:** zuò
- **components:**
  - 留
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - ground
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 留 ('to stay') + 土 ('ground') =
    - to stay on the ground ->
    - sit
  - **interpretation:** The character 坐 (zuò, 'sit; ride') combines the sense of 留 (liú, 'to stay') with 土 (tǔ, 'ground'), expressing the concept of remaining upon the ground, which naturally leads to the action of sitting. The idea of staying grounded naturally extends to 'sitting,' and by metaphor, to 'riding,' as one is seated while riding.
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)在阳(陽)台上坐着(著)读(讀)书(書)。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài yángtái shàng zuòzhe dúshū.
    - I enjoy sitting on the balcony to read.
  - 我们(們)可以一起坐火车(車)去海边(邊)。
    - Wǒmen kěyǐ yīqǐ zuò huǒchē qù hǎibiān.
    - We can take the train together to the seaside.
  - 她喜欢(歡)坐在长(長)椅上欣赏(賞)公园(園)的风(風)景。
    - Tā xǐhuān zuò zài cháng yǐ shàng xīnshǎng gōngyuán de fēngjǐng.
    - She likes sitting on the bench, enjoying the park's scenery.

### 坐 → 留
- **definition:** to stay
- **pinyin:** liú
- **components:**
  - 畱
    - liú
    - to stay
  - 丣
    - liǔ
    - nocturnal hour
  - 田
    - tián
    - field
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 畱 (liú, "to stay") originates as phonetic 丣 + semantic 田 ("field") ->
    - final form 留
  - **interpretation:** In its original form 畱, the combination of 田 ("field") suggests a location or nature, while 丣 adds the phonetic "liú" component. The idea of staying in one place is connected to the field as a fixed location.
- **examples:**
  - 我明天不走，我要留在这(這)里(裡)。
    - Wǒ míngtiān bù zǒu, wǒ yào liú zài zhèlǐ.
    - I'm not leaving tomorrow, I want to stay here.
  - 请(請)你留下来(來)帮(幫)我。
    - Qǐng nǐ liú xià lái bāng wǒ.
    - Please stay and help me.
  - 他决(決)定留在家乡(鄉)发(發)展。
    - Tā juédìng liú zài jiāxiāng fāzhǎn.
    - He decided to stay in his hometown to develop.

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 丣 + semantic 田 ->
    - phonetic 卯 + semantic 田 ->

### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 丣 → 酉
- **definition:** wine vessel
- **pinyin:** yǒu
- **components:**
  - 酒
    - jiǔ
    - alcohol
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a wine vessel with a pointed bottom ->
    - original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol")
  - **interpretation:** 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") originally depicted a wine vessel, indicating the integral role of alcohol in traditional cycles, such as moon phases or ceremonial events. It is the original character for 酒 (jiǔ, "alcohol"), highlighting its use in fermentation processes.
- **examples:**
  - 每一次回望旧(舊)地，都仿(彷)佛(彿)见(見)证(證)了一个(個)酉的完结(結)。

    - Every time I look back, it seems to witness a complete cycle coming to an end.
  - 日落黄(黃)昏后(後)，大地经(經)历(歷)了一个(個)酉的循环(環)。
    - Rìluò huánghūn hòu, dàdì jīnglìle yīgè yǒu de xúnhuán.
    - After sunset, the land has undergone a complete cycle.
### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 丣 → 酉 → 酒
- **definition:** alcohol
- **pinyin:** jiǔ
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 酉
    - yǒu
    - complete cycle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 酉 (yǒu, "complete cycle") =
    - a liquid associated with 酉 (fermentation/vessels) ->

### 坐 → 留 → 畱 → 田

- **pinyin:** tián

### 坐 → 土
- **definition:** ground
- **pinyin:** tǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a mound of earth
  - **interpretation:** The character 土(tǔ, "earth") visually represents a mound or piece of land, with a horizontal line indicating the surface and a vertical line the height of the mound. It's a straightforward depiction of earth or soil.
- **examples:**
  - 农(農)民耕土，种(種)出丰(豐)收。
    - Nóngmín gēng tǔ, zhòng chū fēngshōu.
    - Farmers cultivate the earth and harvest abundantly.
  - 院子里(裡)的土，适(適)合种(種)花。
    - Yuànzi lǐ de tǔ, shìhé zhòng huā.
    - The soil in the yard is suitable for planting flowers.
