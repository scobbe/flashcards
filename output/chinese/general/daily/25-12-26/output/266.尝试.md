## try; attempt
### chángshì
---
- **traditional:** 嘗試
- **definition:** try; attempt
- **pinyin:** chángshì
- **components:**
  - 尝(嘗)
    - cháng
    - taste; try
  - 试(試)
    - shì
    - test; experiment
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 尝(嘗) (cháng, "taste; try") and 试(試) (shì, "test; experiment") together form the concept of making an attempt or trial.
  - **interpretation:** The word 尝(嘗)试(試) (chángshì, "try; attempt") combines the idea of 'trying' something akin to 'tasting' it, with the action of a 'test' or 'experiment', signifying an effort to do something in order to see the result.
  - **simplification:** 尝试 is the simplified form, 嘗試 is the traditional form.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )尝(嘗)试(試)新( )方( )法( )，解( )决(決)难(難)题(題)。
    - tā chángshì xīn fāngfǎ, jiějué nán tí.
    - He tries a new method, solving a difficult problem.
  - 她( )尝(嘗)试(試)解( )决(決)问(問)题(題)，然( )后(後)成( )功( )了( )。
    - tā chángshì jiějué wèntí, ránhòu chénggōng le.
    - She tries to solve the problem, and then she succeeds.
