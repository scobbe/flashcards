## return; turn back
### huí
---
- **definition:** return; turn back
- **pinyin:** huí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a spiral ->
    - rotation
  - **interpretation:** The character 回 (huí, "return; turn back") is a pictogram depicting a spiral or circular motion, symbolizing the concept of rotation or turning back. This visually represents the idea of returning or turning back, as one would follow a circular path to come back to a starting point.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )明( )天( )会(會)回( )家( )。
    - Wǒ míngtiān huì huí jiā.
    - I will return home tomorrow.
  - 她( )走( )到( )门(門)口( )又( )回( )来(來)了( )。
    - Tā zǒu dào ménkǒu yòu huílái le.
    - She went to the door and then returned.
  - 会(會)议(議)结(結)束( )后(後)，我( )们(們)一( )起( )回( )去( )。
    - Huìyì jiéshù hòu, wǒmen yīqǐ huíqù.
    - After the meeting ends, we will return together.
