## traditional Chinese characters
### fántǐ
---
- **traditional:** 繁體
- **definition:** traditional Chinese characters
- **pinyin:** fántǐ
- **components:**
  - 繁
    - fán
    - complex; numerous
  - 體
    - tǐ
    - body; style
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 繁 (fán, "complex; numerous") + 體 (tǐ, "body; style") form the compound word 繁體 (fántǐ), referring to the traditional and complex style of Chinese characters, as opposed to simplified characters.
  - **interpretation:** 繁 (fán, "complex; numerous") indicates the intricate nature of traditional characters, which have numerous strokes. 體 (tǐ, "body; style") refers to the form or style of these characters. The combination conveys the concept of the traditional orthography, emphasizing its complexity and detailed form compared to the streamlined simplified characters.
- **examples:**
