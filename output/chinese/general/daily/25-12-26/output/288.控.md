## control; accuse
### kòng
---
- **definition:** control; accuse
- **pinyin:** kòng
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 空
    - kōng
    - empty; sky
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 空 (kōng) ->
    - control; accuse
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 扌 (shǒu, "hand"), representing action or manipulation, with 空 (kōng), which provides the phonetic component, to form the concept of using one's hand to exert influence or control. This manipulation or control can extend to accusations, as accusing requires taking hold of a situation verbally or legally.
- **examples:**
  - 她试(試)图(圖)控制局面。
    - Tā shìtú kòngzhì júmiàn.
    - She tried to control the situation.
  - 他被指控犯了严(嚴)重的罪行。
    - Tā bèi zhǐkòng fànle yánzhòng de zuìxíng.
    - He was accused of committing a serious crime.
  - 人们(們)需要学(學)会(會)控制自己的情绪(緒)。
    - Rénmen xūyào xuéhuì kòngzhì zìjǐ de qíngxù.
    - People need to learn to control their emotions.
### 控 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞(飛)来(來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌(手)制(製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺(藝)术(術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细(細)节(節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
    - The artist meticulously carved every detail of the hand.
### 控 → 空
- **definition:** empty; sky
- **pinyin:** kōng
- **components:**
  - 穴
    - xué
    - cave; hole
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work; craft
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 穴 (穴) (xué, "cave, hole") + phonetic 工 (工) (gōng) =
    - hollow space ->
    - empty
  - **interpretation:** The character 空(kōng, "empty") combines 穴 (穴) (xué, "cave, hole") as the semantic component, indicating a hollow or vacant area, with 工 (工) (gōng) serving as the phonetic component. This phono-semantic compound suggests the concept of a space that is hollow and without content, hence "empty." The abstract idea of vacancy is derived from the literal representation of a void or cavity.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)房间(間)是空的。
    - Zhè gè fángjiān shì kōng de.
    - This room is empty.
  - 他感到内(內)心空虚(虛)。
    - Tā gǎndào nèixīn kōngxū.
    - He feels empty inside.
  - 早晨的街道很空，没(沒)有人。
    - Zǎochén de jiēdào hěn kōng, méiyǒu rén.
    - The streets are empty in the morning, with nobody around.

### 控 → 空 → 穴
- **definition:** cave; hole
- **pinyin:** xué
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cave or hole, resembling a small dwelling or opening
  - **interpretation:** The character 穴 (xué, "cave") visually represents a hole or cave with an open entry, suggesting a sheltered space similar to a primitive dwelling or burrow.
- **examples:**
  - 山里(裡)隐(隱)匿着(著)一个(個)深深的穴。
    - Shān lǐ yǐn nì zhe yí gè shēn shēn de xué.
    - A deep cave is hidden in the mountains.
  - 探险(險)家在悬(懸)崖下发(發)现(現)了一个(個)幽静(靜)的穴，据(據)说(說)里(裡)面住着(著)古老的精灵(靈)。
    - Tàn xiǎnjiā zài xuán yá xià fāxiàn le yí gè yōu jìng de xué, jù shuō lǐmiàn zhùzhe gǔlǎo de jīng líng.
    - The explorer discovered a quiet cave beneath the cliff, and it is said that ancient spirits dwell inside.
  - 河岸边(邊)的岩石上有一个(個)小穴。
    - Hé àn biān de yán shí shàng yǒu yí gè xiǎo xué.
    - There is a small cave on the rock by the riverbank.

### 控 → 空 → 工
- **definition:** work; craft
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bladed tool to plow the ground and to mark a border, or a carpenter's square
  - **interpretation:** 工 (gōng, "work") represents either a tool used in agriculture for marking or a carpenter's square, reflecting its connection to traditional manual labor and craftsmanship.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天下午去做工。
    - Tā měitiān xiàwǔ qù zuò gōng.

    - Gōng chǎng lǐ de jīqì bù tíng de yùnzhuǎn.

  - 这(這)位工人技艺(藝)精湛。
    - Zhè wèi gōngrén jìyì jīngzhàn.
