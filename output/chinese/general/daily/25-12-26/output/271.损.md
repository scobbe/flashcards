## decrease; damage
### sǔn
---
- **traditional:** 損
- **definition:** decrease; damage
- **pinyin:** sǔn
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 員
    - yuán
    - a member; employee
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌(扌, "hand") + phonetic 員(員, "a member; employee") ->
    - damage or decrease related to actions involving hands
  - **interpretation:** The character 損 (sǔn, "decrease; damage") combines 扌(扌, "hand"), representing action or manipulation, with 員(員, "a member; employee") to suggest the act of diminishing or causing harm, typically through manual actions or handling.
  - **simplification:** Simplified from 損 by replacing 員 (yuán, "a member") with its simplified form 员(員).

### 损(損) → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**

### 损(損) → 員
- **definition:** a member; employee
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 鼎
    - dǐng
    - ding
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 〇 (round) indicating the top edge of a 鼎 (dǐng, "ding") ->
    - round
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted the top view of a 鼎 (dǐng, "ding"), which is a round ancient cooking vessel, capturing its round shape to convey the concept of 'member' or 'person', as in a complete or rounded group.
  - **simplification:** To simplify 員, 貝 (bèi, "cowry") was reduced to 贝(貝), maintaining the concept while reducing stroke count.
- **examples:**

### 损(損) → 員 → 鼎
