## Chinese cabbage
### dàbáicài
---
- **definition:** Chinese cabbage
- **pinyin:** dàbáicài
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big; large; major
  - 白
    - bái
    - white; clear; bright
  - 菜
    - cài
    - vegetable; dish; greens
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 大 (dà, "big") + 白 (bái, "white") + 菜 (cài, "vegetable")
  - **interpretation:** 大白菜 (dàbáicài, "Chinese cabbage") refers to the large, white leafy vegetable commonly found in Chinese cuisine. The combination of 大 (dà, "big"), 白 (bái, "white"), and 菜 (cài, "vegetable") emphasizes the large size and pale color of this cabbage variety, distinguishing it from other vegetables.
- **examples:**
  - 我买(買)了大白菜。
    - Wǒ mǎile dàbáicài.
    - I bought Chinese cabbage.
  - 他从(從)市场(場)买(買)大白菜，准(準)备(備)做泡菜。
    - Tā cóng shìchǎng mǎi dàbáicài, zhǔnbèi zuò pàocài.
    - He bought Chinese cabbage from the market and is ready to make pickles.
  - 大白菜营(營)养(養)丰(豐)富，非常适(適)合做汤(湯)。
    - Dàbáicài yíngyǎng fēngfù, fēicháng shìhé zuò tāng.
    - Chinese cabbage is nutritious and is very suitable for making soup.
### 大白菜 → 大
- **definition:** big; large; major
- **pinyin:** dà
- **components:**
  - 夫
    - fū
    - man
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person facing forward ->
    - Borrowed phonetically for 'big'; original form of 夫 (fū, 'man')
  - **interpretation:** The character 大(dà) depicts a person facing forward, representing 'big' by suggesting the idea of someone standing prominently. Although often thought of as a person with outstretched arms, early scripts do not support this interpretation. It was originally the form of 夫 (fū, 'man') and was later phonetically borrowed for the meaning 'big.'
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)房子很大。
    - Zhè ge fángzi hěn dà.
    - This house is very big.
  - 大海波涛(濤)汹(洶)涌(湧)，展现(現)出大自然的力量。
    - Dàhǎi bōtāo xiōngyǒng, zhǎnxiàn chū dàzìrán de lìliàng.
    - The vast ocean waves surge, showcasing the power of nature.
  - 大人物登场(場)，留下传(傳)奇。
    - Dà rénwù dēngchǎng, liúxià chuánqí.
    - Great figures make an appearance, leaving behind legends.

### 大白菜 → 大 → 夫
- **definition:** man
- **pinyin:** fū
- **components:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a variant form of 大 (dà, "big") representing an adult man with a hairpin ->
    - became associated with 夫 (fū, "man").
  - **interpretation:** 夫 (fū, "man") was visually similar to 大 (dà, "big") and was originally interchangeable. The addition of the horizontal stroke was interpreted as a hairpin, symbolizing adulthood for men.
- **examples:**
  - 此夫心怀(懷)大志。
    - Cǐ fū xīn huái dà zhì.
    - This man has lofty aspirations.
  - 他勤劳(勞)努力，堪称(稱)良夫。
    - Tā qín láo nǔ lì, kān chēng liáng fū.
    - He works diligently and is regarded as an exemplary man.

### 大白菜 → 白
- **definition:** white; clear; bright
- **pinyin:** bái
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts an acorn ->
    - white from the color of acorn's inside
  - **interpretation:** The character 白(bái, "white") is thought to depict an acorn, as suggested by its use in words related to oak and acorns. The character's meaning of "white" is likely derived from the white color of an acorn's interior.
- **examples:**
  - 白色是我最喜欢(歡)的颜(顏)色。
    - Báisè shì wǒ zuì xǐhuān de yánsè.
    - White is my favorite color.
  - 他的头(頭)发(髮)已经(經)全白了。
    - Tā de tóufà yǐjīng quán bái le.
    - His hair has turned completely white.
  - 在阳(陽)光下，白沙滩(灘)显(顯)得格外美丽(麗)。
    - Zài yángguāng xià, bái shātān xiǎnde géwài měilì.

### 大白菜 → 菜
- **definition:** vegetable; dish; greens
- **pinyin:** cài
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 采
    - cǎi
    - pluck
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (meaning 'grass; plant') + phonetic 采 (sound) ->
    - meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character 菜 (cài, 'vegetable; dish; cuisine; greens') combines the semantic 艹, representing plant life or vegetation, with 采 (cǎi), which provides the phonetic element. The semantic 艹 indicates that the meanings are related to plants or vegetation, while the phonetic 采 contributes to the character's pronunciation.
- **examples:**
  - 每道菜都有独(獨)特的风(風)味。
    - Měi dào cài dōu yǒu dútè de fēngwèi.
    - Every dish has a unique flavor.
  - 我喜欢(歡)吃绿(綠)色的蔬菜，因为(為)它们(們)对(對)健康有益。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān chī lǜsè de shūcài, yīnwèi tāmen duì jiànkāng yǒuyì.
    - I like eating green vegetables because they are good for health.
  - 中餐有很多不同的菜色，从(從)辣到甜都有。
    - Zhōngcān yǒu hěn duō bùtóng de càisè, cóng là dào tián dōu yǒu.
    - Chinese cuisine has many different dishes, ranging from spicy to sweet.

### 大白菜 → 菜 → 艹
- **definition:** grass; plant
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来(來)时(時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 大白菜 → 菜 → 采(採)
- **traditional:** 採
- **definition:** pluck
- **pinyin:** cǎi
- **components:**
  - 扌(手)
    - shǒu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 扌(hand) + 采(to pick) =
    - action of picking with hand ->
    - pluck
  - **interpretation:** The character 採 represents the action of picking or plucking using one's hand. The semantic component 扌(shǒu, "hand") indicates the action being performed, while 采(cǎi, "to pick") serves the phonetic role. Over time, 采 was retained in simplified usage, whereas 採 is used traditionally.
  - **simplification:** 采 is a simplification of 採 by using the original form that omits the 手 hand radical for brevity while retaining the phonetic element.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢(歡)在花园(園)里(裡)采(採)花。
    - Tā xǐhuān zài huāyuán lǐ cǎi huā.
    - She likes to pluck flowers in the garden.
  - 我们(們)去山上采(採)草药(藥)。
    - Wǒmen qù shān shàng cǎi cǎoyào.
    - We go to the mountains to pluck herbs.
  - 他在果园(園)里(裡)采(採)苹(蘋)果。
    - Tā zài guǒyuán lǐ cǎi píngguǒ.
    - He plucks apples in the orchard.

### 大白菜 → 菜 → 采(採) → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞(飛)来(來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌(手)制(製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺(藝)术(術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细(細)节(節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
    - The artist meticulously carved every detail of the hand.
