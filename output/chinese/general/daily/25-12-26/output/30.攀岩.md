## rock climbing
### pānyán
---
- **definition:** rock climbing
- **pinyin:** pānyán
- **components:**
  - 攀
    - pān
    - climb
  - 岩
    - yán
    - rock; cliff
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 攀 (pān, "climb") + 岩 (yán, "rock") combine to convey the concept of climbing rocks, which directly describes the activity of rock climbing.
  - **interpretation:** 攀 (pān, "climb") denotes the action of climbing or ascending, often requiring effort and agility. 岩 (yán, "rock") specifically refers to large rocks or cliffs. Together, they vividly illustrate the activity of scaling rock faces, which captures the essence of rock climbing.
