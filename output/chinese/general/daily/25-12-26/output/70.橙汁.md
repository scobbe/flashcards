- **definition:** orange juice
- **pinyin:** chéngzhī
- **components:**
  - 橙
    - chéng
    - orange (fruit)
  - 汁
    - zhī
    - juice; liquid
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 橙 (chéng, "orange") + 汁 (zhī, "juice") forms the term 橙汁 (chéngzhī, "orange juice"), referring to the liquid extracted from oranges.
  - **interpretation:** 橙 (chéng, "orange") refers to the fruit known for its vibrant color and citrus flavor. When combined with 汁 (zhī, "juice"), which denotes the liquid content obtained by pressing or squeezing fruits, it specifies the juice derived from oranges. Thus, 橙汁 captures the notion of the liquid essence of the orange fruit, commonly used as a refreshing drink.
- **examples:**
