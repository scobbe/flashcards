## landscape; country
### jiāngshān
---
- **definition:** landscape; country
- **pinyin:** jiāngshān
- **components:**
  - 江
    - jiāng
    - river
  - 山
    - shān
    - mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 江 (jiāng, "river") and 山 (shān, "mountain") together create the meaning of the country's natural landscape or the country as a whole.
  - **interpretation:** 江山 (jiāngshān, "landscape; country") metaphorically represents the country or nation by highlighting its natural features of rivers and mountains, which have historically been pivotal in defining and defending territories in China.
- **examples:**
  - 江山如此多娇(嬌)。
    - Jiāngshān rúcǐ duō jiāo.
    - The landscape is so enchanting.
  - 他保护(護)江山，像守护(護)自己的命根子。
    - Tā bǎohù jiāngshān, xiàng shǒuhù zìjǐ de mìnggēnzǐ.
    - He protects his country as if guarding his own lifeline.
### 江山 → 江
- **definition:** river
- **pinyin:** jiāng
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 工
    - gōng
    - work
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 工 (gōng) ->
    - meaning
  - **interpretation:** The water radical 氵 (shuǐ, "water") indicates the association with water, specifically a river. The 工 (gōng) provides the pronunciation, connecting the sound with the natural context of water courses.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)条(條)江流经(經)许(許)多城镇(鎮)。
    - Zhè tiáo jiāng liújīng xǔduō chéngzhèn.
    - This river flows through many towns.
  - 江水捧起一片叶(葉)子，飘(飄)向远(遠)方。
    - Jiāngshuǐ pěng qǐ yī piàn yèzi, piāo xiàng yuǎnfāng.
    - The river water lifted a leaf, floating it into the distance.
  - 我喜欢(歡)在江边(邊)散步，欣赏(賞)美景。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài jiāng biān sànbù, xīnshǎng měijǐng.
    - I like to walk along the river, enjoying the beautiful scenery.

### 江山 → 江 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 江山 → 江 → 工
- **definition:** work
- **pinyin:** gōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bladed tool to plow the ground and to mark a border, or a carpenter's square
  - **interpretation:** 工 (gōng, "work") represents either a tool used in agriculture for marking or a carpenter's square, reflecting its connection to traditional manual labor and craftsmanship.
- **examples:**
  - 他每天下午去做工。
    - Tā měitiān xiàwǔ qù zuò gōng.
    - He goes to work every afternoon.
  - 工厂(廠)里(裡)的机(機)器不停地运(運)转(轉)。
    - Gōng chǎng lǐ de jīqì bù tíng de yùnzhuǎn.
    - The machines in the factory run nonstop.
  - 这(這)位工人技艺(藝)精湛。
    - Zhè wèi gōngrén jìyì jīngzhàn.
    - This worker is highly skilled.

### 江山 → 山
- **definition:** mountain
- **pinyin:** shān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts three mountain peaks
  - **interpretation:** The character 山(shān, "mountain") visually represents the concept of a mountain using three vertical lines that suggest peaks. This depiction is consistent with early forms found in Oracle Bone Script, emphasizing the notion of natural elevation associated with mountains.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)去爬山。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We go climb the mountain.
  - 那座大山让(讓)人惊(驚)叹(嘆)。
    - Nà zuò dà shān ràng rén jīngtàn.
    - That mountain astonishes people.
  - 远(遠)处(處)的山隐(隱)约(約)在云(雲)中，令人心旷(曠)神怡。
    - Yuǎnchù de shān yǐnyuē zài yún zhōng, lìng rén xīnkuàng shényí.
    - The distant mountain fades into the clouds, leaving one in a state of peace.
