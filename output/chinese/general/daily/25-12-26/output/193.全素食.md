## vegan diet
### quánsùshí
---
- **definition:** vegan diet
- **pinyin:** quánsùshí
- **components:**
  - 全
    - quán
    - entire
  - 素食
    - sùshí
    - vegetarian food
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 全 (quán, "entire"), 素 (sù, "vegetarian; plain"), 食 (shí, "food")
  - **interpretation:** The term 全素食 (quánsùshí) meaning "vegan diet" is composed of 全 (quán, "entire"), emphasizing the completeness or exclusiveness of the practice, and 素食 (sùshí, "vegetarian food"), which refers to foods free from meat, focusing on a diet not including any animal products at all.
- **examples:**
  - 我决(決)定尝(嘗)试(試)全素食。
    - Wǒ juédìng chángshì quánsùshí.
    - I decided to try a vegan diet.
  - 他因健康问(問)题(題)选(選)择(擇)全素食，并(並)改善了生活。
    - Tā yīn jiànkāng wèntí xuǎnzé quánsùshí, bìng gǎishàn le shēnghuó.
    - He chose a vegan diet due to health issues, and improved his life.
  - 全素食能够(夠)提供充足的营(營)养(養)。
    - Quánsùshí nénggòu tígōng chōngzú de yíngyǎng.
    - A vegan diet can provide adequate nutrition.

### 全素食 → 素食
- **definition:** vegetarian food
- **pinyin:** sùshí
- **components:**
  - 素
    - sù
    - plain; simple; element; vegetarian
  - 食
    - shí
    - food; eat; meal; cuisine
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word 素食 (sùshí, "vegetarian food") is based on the combination of 素 (sù, "plain; simple") and 食 (shí, "food").
  - **interpretation:** 素 (sù, "plain; simple") refers to something lacking embellishment or basics, which conveys the idea of foods that are natural and unprocessed. This sense of simplicity extends to plant-based ingredients, often seen as original and wholesome. 食 (shí, "food") naturally refers to what is eaten. Thus, the term 素食 refers to foods that are plain in the sense of being plant-based, adhering to a vegetarian diet, without the inclusion of meat or extravagance.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢(歡)吃素食。
    - Tā xǐhuān chī sùshí.
    - She likes to eat vegetarian food.
  - 这(這)家餐厅(廳)提供很多素食选(選)项(項)。
    - Zhè jiā cāntīng tígōng hěn duō sùshí xuǎnxiàng.
    - This restaurant offers many vegetarian options.
  - 他决(決)定在宴会(會)中只吃素食。
    - Tā juédìng zài yànhuì zhōng zhǐ chī sùshí.
    - He decided to eat only vegetarian food at the banquet.
