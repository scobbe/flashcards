## pinch
### niē
---
- **definition:** pinch
- **pinyin:** niē
- **components:**
  - 扌
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 涅
    - niè
    - blacken
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 扌 (hand) + phonetic 涅 (sound niē) ->
    - pinch
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 扌(shǒu, 'hand') suggests the action related to the hand. The phonetic component 涅 (niè, 'blacken') provides the pronunciation. Together, they form the concept of using the hand to 'pinch', a small, squeezing action.
- **examples:**
  - 她喜欢(歡)捏孩子的脸(臉)。
    - Tā xǐhuān niē háizi de liǎn.
    - She likes to pinch the child's cheeks.
  - 他捏了一块(塊)泥巴，开(開)始做雕塑。
    - Tā niē le yī kuài níbā, kāishǐ zuò diāosù.
    - He pinched a lump of clay and started sculpting.
  - 我忍住不捏她的手，因为(為)怕弄疼她。
    - Wǒ rěnzhù bù niē tā de shǒu, yīnwèi pà nòng téng tā.
    - I refrained from pinching her hand because I feared hurting her.
### 捏 → 扌
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stylized hand. The top stroke is the bent middle finger, and the horizontal strokes each represent two fingers.
  - **interpretation:** 扌 is a simplified form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), retaining the key elements that visually represent a human hand. It is commonly used as a component in other characters related to hand actions.
  - **simplification:** 扌 is derived as a cursive or shorthand form of 手 (shǒu, 'hand'), making it easier to incorporate into complex characters as a radical or component.
- **examples:**
  - 他伸出扌(手)接住了飞(飛)来(來)的球。
    - Tā shēnchū shǒu jiēzhù le fēilái de qiú.
    - He stretched out his hand to catch the flying ball.
  - 她用扌(手)制(製)作美丽(麗)的陶艺(藝)，展现(現)她的匠心。
    - Tā yòng shǒu zhìzuò měilì de táoyì, zhǎnxiàn tā de jiàngxīn.
    - She used her hand to create beautiful pottery, showcasing her craftsmanship.
  - 艺(藝)术(術)家细(細)心雕刻扌(手)的每一细(細)节(節)。
    - Yìshùjiā xìxīn diāokè shǒu de měiyī xìjié.
    - The artist meticulously carved every detail of the hand.
### 捏 → 涅
- **definition:** blacken
- **pinyin:** niè
- **components:**
  - 氵
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 黒(黑)
    - hēi
    - black
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + 黒(黑) (hēi, "black") =
    - black mud mixed with water ->
    - mud
  - **interpretation:** The character 涅 (niè, "mud") combines the radical for water 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") with 黒(黑) (hēi, "black"), suggesting a dark substance associated with wetness, hence signifying 'mud.'
- **examples:**
  - 雨后(後)小路边(邊)累积(積)了厚厚的涅。
    - Yǔhòu xiǎolù biān lěijīle hòuhòu de niè.
    - After the rain, thick mud accumulated by the roadside.
  - 溪水冲(沖)刷着(著)石滩(灘)上的涅，大地呈现(現)出湿(濕)润(潤)的色彩。
    - Xīshuǐ chōngshuā zhe shítān shàng de niè, dàdì chéngxiàn chū shīrùn de sècǎi.
    - The stream washed the muddy stone beach, and the land displayed a moist hue.
  - 河畔旁堆满(滿)了流动(動)的涅。
    - Hépàn páng duīmǎnle liúdòng de niè.
    - By the riverbank, flowing mud had piled up.

### 捏 → 涅 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 捏 → 涅 → 黒(黑)
- **traditional:** 黑
- **definition:** black
- **pinyin:** hēi
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a chimney with soot building up upwards ->
    - darkening to black
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a chimney with soot accumulating, creating a visual representation of something becoming dark or black due to soot deposits.
  - **simplification:** 黒 is simplified by replacing two small dots with a single horizontal stroke, reflecting a cursive shorthand approach.
- **examples:**
  - 她穿了一件黒(黑)色的夹(夾)克。
    - Tā chuānle yī jiàn hēisè de jiākè.
    - She wore a black jacket.
  - 夜空中满(滿)是黒(黑)色的云(雲)。
    - Yèkōng zhōng mǎn shì hēisè de yún.
    - The night sky is full of black clouds.
  - 他的头(頭)发(髮)是深黒(黑)色。
    - Tā de tóufà shì shēn hēisè.
    - His hair is a deep black.
