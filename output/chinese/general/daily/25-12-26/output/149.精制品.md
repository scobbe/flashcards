## refined products
### jīngzhìpǐn
---
- **traditional:** 精製品
- **definition:** refined products
- **pinyin:** jīngzhìpǐn
- **components:**
  - 精
    - jīng
    - refined; essence
  - 製
    - zhì
    - make; manufacture
  - 品
    - pǐn
    - product; goods
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 精 (jīng, "refined; essence") + 製 (zhì, "make; manufacture") + 品 (pǐn, "product; goods") refers to high-quality or carefully produced products. 精 (jīng, "essence") emphasizes the quality or selectiveness involved; 製 (zhì, "make") refers to the process of creation, highlighting manufacturing; 品 (pǐn, "product") signifies the outcome or goods.
  - **interpretation:** 精 (jīng, "refined; essence") suggests purity or selectiveness, leading to the idea of refinement in quality. 製 (zhì, "make; manufacture") refers to the act of creating or producing something, thus when associated with 精 (jīng, "refined"), it implies a process of creating something with care or thoroughness. 品 (pǐn, "product") is the result or output, giving the sense of carefully crafted or high-grade goods.
- **examples:**
