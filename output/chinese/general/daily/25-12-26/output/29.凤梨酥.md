## pineapple cake
### fènglísū
---
- **traditional:** 鳳梨酥
- **definition:** pineapple cake
- **pinyin:** fènglísū
- **components:**
  - 鳳梨
    - fènglí
    - pineapple
  - 酥
    - sū
    - crisp; flaky
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鳳 (fèng, "phoenix") + 梨 (lí, "pear") + 酥 (sū, "crisp")
  - **interpretation:** 鳳梨 (fènglí, "pineapple") refers to the fruit itself, using a metaphorical reference to a phoenix to denote exoticness and nobility. 酥 (sū, "crisp") describes the texture of the pastry, which is flaky and delicate, giving the overall meaning of a sweet, crisp pastry made with pineapple.
  - **simplification:** 鳳 to 凤
- **examples:**
  - 我昨天买(買)了凤(鳳)梨酥。
    - Wǒ zuótiān mǎile fènglísū.
    - I bought a pineapple cake yesterday.
  - 周(週)末时(時)，我跟朋友一起品尝(嚐)凤(鳳)梨酥。
    - Zhōumò shí, wǒ gēn péngyǒu yīqǐ pǐncháng fènglísū.
    - On the weekend, I tasted pineapple cake with a friend.
  - 这(這)种(種)凤(鳳)梨酥，外皮松(鬆)脆。
    - Zhè zhǒng fènglísū, wàipí sōngcuì.
    - This type of pineapple cake has a crispy crust.
### 凤(鳳)梨酥 → 鳳梨
- **definition:** pineapple
- **pinyin:** fènglí
- **components:**
  - 鳳
    - fèng
    - phoenix
  - 梨
    - lí
    - pear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 鳳 (鳳) (fèng, "phoenix"), 梨 (梨) (lí, "pear")
  - **interpretation:** The word 鳳梨 (fènglí) literally means "phoenix pear." The phoenix, 鳳 (鳳) (fèng, "phoenix"), is a mythical bird associated with nobility and auspiciousness in Chinese culture. The second character, 梨 (梨) (lí, "pear"), refers to the fruit. The combination suggests an exotic or distinguished type of fruit, with the idea that the pineapple is as remarkable as a phoenix among pears.
- **examples:**
  - 超市里(裡)有很多新鲜(鮮)的鳳梨。
    - Chāoshì lǐ yǒu hěn duō xīnxiān de fènglí.
    - There are many fresh pineapples in the supermarket.
  - 我喜欢(歡)在夏天吃鳳梨。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān zài xiàtiān chī fènglí.
    - I like to eat pineapples in the summer.
  - 鳳梨可以用来(來)做沙拉，也可以榨成汁。
    - Fènglí kěyǐ yòng lái zuò shālā, yě kěyǐ zhà chéng zhī.
    - Pineapple can be used to make salad, and it can also be juiced.
