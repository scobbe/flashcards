## poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
### fúlíng
---
- **definition:** poria; tuckahoe (medicinal fungus)
- **pinyin:** fúlíng
- **components:**
  - 茯
    - fú
    - tuckahoe; a kind of herb
  - 苓
    - líng
    - fungus; medicinal plant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 茯苓 (fúlíng, "poria; tuckahoe") is a compound referring to a type of medicinal fungus highly regarded in Chinese herbal medicine. It is noted for its adaptogenic properties used to balance bodily functions.
  - **interpretation:** 茯 (fú, "tuckahoe; a kind of herb") and 苓 (líng, "fungus; medicinal plant") together specify a medicinal fungus used in traditional Chinese medicine. 茯 (fú) suggests the plant's herbal use, while 苓 (líng) highlights its classification as a fungus or plant component in herbal prescriptions. The combination integrates these aspects to directly denote the specific medicinal mushroom, poria, and its role as both herb and fungus in therapy.
- **examples:**
  - 汤(湯)里(裡)加了一些茯苓。
    - Tāng lǐ jiāle yīxiē fúlíng.
    - Some poria was added to the soup.
  - 茯苓常用于(於)中药(藥)。
    - Fúlíng cháng yòng yú zhōngyào.
    - Poria is commonly used in traditional Chinese medicine.
  - 她买(買)了一包茯苓，准(準)备(備)做药(藥)膳。
    - Tā mǎile yī bāo fúlíng, zhǔnbèi zuò yàoshàn.
    - She bought a package of poria to prepare herbal cuisine.
### 茯苓 → 茯
- **definition:** tuckahoe; a kind of herb
- **pinyin:** fú
- **components:**
  - 艹
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 伏
    - fú
    - conceal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic 伏 (fú, "conceal") ->
    - poria root
  - **interpretation:** 茯(fú, "poria root") combines 艹 (cǎo, "grass"), indicating a plant-related meaning, with 伏 (fú, "conceal"), which provides the phonetic sound. The poria root is used in traditional medicine and is often found concealed underground, which might connect the semantic field with its natural occurrence.
- **examples:**
  - 茯苓是中药(藥)中常用的材料。
    - Fúlíng shì zhōngyào zhōng cháng yòng de cáiliào.
    - Poria root is a commonly used ingredient in traditional Chinese medicine.
  - 在市场(場)上，你可以买(買)到优(優)质(質)的茯苓。
    - Zài shìchǎng shàng, nǐ kěyǐ mǎi dào yōuzhì de fúlíng.
    - In the market, you can buy high-quality poria root.
  - 茯苓对(對)身体(體)有很多好处(處)，例如增强(強)免疫力。
    - Fúlíng duì shēntǐ yǒu hěn duō hǎochù, lìrú zēngqiáng miǎnyì lì.
    - Poria root has many health benefits, such as boosting the immune system.
### 茯苓 → 茯 → 艹
- **definition:** grass
- **pinyin:** cǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts blades of grass
  - **interpretation:** 艹 is a pictogram representing grass or plants, resembling blades of grass. It is commonly used as a radical in other characters related to grass or vegetation.
- **examples:**
  - 他写(寫)字时(時)用艹作草字的偏旁。
    - Tā xiězì shí yòng cǎo zuò cǎozì de piānpáng.
    - He uses '艹' as the radical for grass in his characters.
  - 春天到来(來)时(時)，艹生长(長)得很快。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, cǎo shēngzhǎng de hěn kuài.
    - When spring arrives, grass grows rapidly.
### 茯苓 → 茯 → 伏
- **definition:** conceal
- **pinyin:** fú
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 犬
    - quǎn
    - dog
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 人 (rén, "person") + phonetic: 犬 (quǎn, "dog") =
    - person bending over like a dog ->
    - to bend down
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 人 (rén, "person") depicting the idea of a person with 犬 (quǎn, "dog") for its phonetic value, suggesting the action of a person bending or crouching down, akin to a dog.
- **examples:**
  - 猫(貓)咪伏在窗台(臺)上。
    - Māomī fú zài chuāngtái shàng.
    - The cat is crouching on the windowsill.
  - 他伏在树(樹)下，休息了一会(會)儿(兒)。
    - Tā fú zài shù xià, xiūxí le yīhuǐ'er.
    - He bent down under the tree, and rested for a while.

### 茯苓 → 茯 → 伏 → 人

- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**

### 茯苓 → 苓 → 令 → 卩
- **definition:** seal
- **pinyin:** jié
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a kneeling person from sideview
  - **interpretation:** The character 卩 (jié, "kneeling person") is a simple pictogram showing the silhouette of a person kneeling. It is unrelated to other kneeling or similar figures in different contexts such as 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") where the mouth is open, or 女 (nǚ, "woman") where the figure represents a female. Its form is solely based on the posture of kneeling.
- **examples:**
  - 那位卩正虔诚(誠)祈祷(禱)。
    - Nà wèi jié zhèng qiánchéng qídǎo.
    - That kneeling person is praying devoutly.
  - 礼(禮)仪(儀)师(師)指出，卩代表谦(謙)卑与(與)敬意。
    - Lǐyí shī zhǐchū, jié dàibiǎo qiānbēi yǔ jìngyì.
    - The ritual master pointed out that the kneeling person represents humility and respect.
  - 在庄(莊)严(嚴)仪(儀)式中，卩象征(徵)着(著)内(內)心的虔敬。
    - Zài zhuāngyán yíshì zhōng, jié xiàngzhēngzhe nèixīn de qiánjìng.
    - In the solemn ceremony, the kneeling figure symbolizes inner devotion.
