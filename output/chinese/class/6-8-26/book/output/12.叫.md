## shout; cry
### jiào
---
- **definition:** shout; cry
- **pinyin:** jiào
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 丩
    - jiū
    - twist
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 丩 (jiū, "twist") =
    - sound produced by mouth ->
    - call; shout
  - **interpretation:** The character 叫 (jiào, "call; shout") combines 口, representing a mouth making sounds, with 丩, a phonetic element. This reflects the act of shouting or calling, which involves using the mouth.
- **examples:**
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)叫( )我( )吃( )饭(飯)。

    - He calls his friend to come quickly, so as not to miss the grand event.
  - 校( )长(長)叫( )学(學)生( )认(認)真( )听(聽)讲(講)。

### 叫 → 丩
- **definition:** twist
- **pinyin:** jiū
- **components:**
  - 糾
    - jiū
    - to correct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts two entangled ropes ->
    - to entangle; to intertwine ->
    - original form of 糾 (jiū, "to correct")
  - **interpretation:** The character 丩 (jiū, "twist") visually represents two ropes entwined with each other, symbolizing the concept of twisting or entanglement. This form later evolved into characters like 糾 (jiū, "to correct"), retaining the notion of intricacy or complexity.
