## in a low voice
### xiǎoshēng
---
- **traditional:** 小聲
- **definition:** in a low voice
- **pinyin:** xiǎoshēng
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; few
  - 声(聲)
    - shēng
    - sound; voice
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小 (xiǎo, "small; few") refers to something small or in a reduced form. 聲 (shēng, "sound; voice") refers to sound or voice. Together, 小聲 refers to speaking or making sounds in a low, soft manner.
  - **interpretation:** 小( )聲(声) (xiǎoshēng, "in a low voice") literally means 'small voice', suggesting speaking quietly or softly, which is often used to describe speaking in a whisper or to avoid being overheard.
- **examples:**
