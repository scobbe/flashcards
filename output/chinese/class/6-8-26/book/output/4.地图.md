## map; plat; atlas
### dìtú
---
- **traditional:** 地圖
- **definition:** map; plat; atlas
- **pinyin:** dìtú
- **components:**
  - 地
    - dì
    - earth; ground; land; place
  - 图(圖)
    - tú
    - diagram; picture; drawing; map
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** In the word 地( )图(圖) (dìtú, "map"), 地 (dì, "earth; ground") refers to the land or ground, and 圖 (tú, "diagram; picture") refers to a drawing or diagram. Together, these characters are used to describe a diagram or representation of a section of the earth's surface or ground, which is essentially what a map is.
  - **interpretation:** A 地( )圖(图) (dìtú, "map") is a representation or diagram of the earth's surface, which fits logically with its components: 地 (dì, "earth") and 圖 (tú, "diagram").
- **examples:**

    - kǒu

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 囧 (jiǒng, "window") + semantic: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") =
