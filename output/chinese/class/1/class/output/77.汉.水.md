## water
### sub-component
#### Han; Chinese
---
- **traditional:** 水
- **simplified:** 水
- **pronunciation:** shuǐ
- **definition:** water
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 水平 (shuǐ píng, "skill level")
  - 水果 (shuǐ guǒ, "fresh fruit")
  - 水龙(龍)头(頭) (shuǐ lóng tóu, "water faucet")
  - 水电(電) (shuǐ diàn, "hydroelectric power")
- **etymology (水):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 水 (shuǐ, "water").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts flowing water with a central stream and side droplets. Over time the strokes were standardized, but the character retained the meaning "water" and functions as the semantic sign for liquid-related terms.
  - **component characters:**
    - 水
