## woman
### sub-component
#### Na (female name); lithe or graceful
---
- **traditional:** 女
- **simplified:** 女
- **pronunciation:** nǚ
- **definition:** woman
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 女人 (nǚ rén, "woman")
  - 女的 (nǚ de, "female")
  - 女演员(員) (nǚ yǎn yuán, "female actor")
  - 女儿(兒) (nǚ ér, "daughter")
- **etymology (女):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a kneeling woman with breasts → rotated to the left in modern form; inner stroke preserves one breast.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a woman with breasts, originally shown kneeling or standing. The graph later rotated left and simplified, leaving an inner stroke as the remnant of one breast while the other disappeared, and the meaning remained ‘female/woman’.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
