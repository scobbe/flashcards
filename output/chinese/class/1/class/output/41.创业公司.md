## startup company
---
- **traditional:** 創業公司
- **simplified:** 创业公司
- **pronunciation:** chuàng yè gōng sī
- **definition:** startup company
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 创(創)业(業)公司融资(資) (chuàngyè gōngsī róngzī, "startup financing")
  - 创(創)业(業)公司估值 (chuàngyè gōngsī gūzhí, "startup valuation")
  - 创(創)业(業)公司并(並)购(購) (chuàngyè gōngsī bìnggòu, "startup M&A")
  - 创(創)业(業)公司文化 (chuàngyè gōngsī wénhuà, "startup culture")
- **etymology (創業公司):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 创(創) (chuàng, "create") + 业(業) (yè, "enterprise") + 公司 (gōngsī, "company") → 创业公司(創業公司) (chuàngyè gōngsī, "startup company").
  - **interpretation:** It combines 创业, meaning to create or start an enterprise, with 公司, meaning company, to denote a company in its founding/early stage. The semantics directly convey a business founded to develop and grow from inception, hence “startup company.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 创
    - 創
    - 业
    - 業
    - 公
    - 司
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%89%B5%E6%A5%AD%E5%85%AC%E5%8F%B8
  - **simplification rule (创业公司):** 創 was simplified to 创 by reducing strokes and altering the phonetic component; 業 was simplified to 业 by collapsing stacked components into a single simplified form; 公司 remained unchanged.
