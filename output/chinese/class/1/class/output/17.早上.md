## morning
---
- **traditional:** 早上
- **simplified:** 早上
- **pronunciation:** zǎo shang
- **definition:** morning
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 早上八点(點) (zǎoshang bā diǎn, "eight a.m.")
  - 早上好 (zǎoshang hǎo, "good morning")
  - 早上到现(現)在 (zǎoshang dào xiànzài, "from morning to now")
  - 早上锻(鍛)炼(煉) (zǎoshang duànliàn, "morning exercise")
- **etymology (早上):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 早 (zǎo, "early") + 上 (shàng, "upon") → 早上 (zǎoshang, "morning").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 早 "early" with 上 "upon" to denote the early part of the day, i.e., morning. This matches the literal sense "upon the time when it is early" noted in the entry.
  - **component characters:**
    - 早
    - 上
