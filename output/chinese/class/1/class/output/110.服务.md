## service
### sub-word
#### server or waiter
---
- **traditional:** 服務
- **simplified:** 服务
- **pronunciation:** fúwù
- **definition:** service
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 服务(務)员(員) (fúwùyuán, "service staff")
  - 服务(務)器 (fúwùqì, "computer server")
  - 服务(務)业(業) (fúwùyè, "service industry")
  - 服务(務)区(區) (fúwùqū, "service area")
- **etymology (服務):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 服 (fú, "serve") + 务(務) (wù, "affairs").
  - **interpretation:** 服 conveys the sense of serving or obeying, while 务 denotes affairs or duties. Combined, they mean attending to affairs for others, yielding the verb "to serve" and the noun "service" in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 服
    - 务
    - 務
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%9C%8D%E5%8B%99
  - **simplification rule (服务):** Simplified by component replacement and stroke reduction: 服務 → 服务 via 務 → 务.
