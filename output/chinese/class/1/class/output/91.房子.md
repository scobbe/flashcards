## house; building
---
- **traditional:** 房子
- **simplified:** 房子
- **pronunciation:** fáng zi
- **definition:** house; building
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 房子在旧(舊)金山非常贵(貴) (fángzi zài jiùjīnshān fēicháng guì, "houses in San Francisco expensive")
  - 房子出租 (fángzi chūzū, "house for rent")
  - 房子前面 (fángzi qiánmiàn, "in front of house")
  - 房子里(裡) (fángzi lǐ, "inside the house")
- **etymology (房子):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 房 (fáng, "house") + 子 (zi, "noun suffix").
  - **interpretation:** 房 means a dwelling or building, and 子 functions as a nominal suffix indicating a concrete thing. Together they form a colloquial noun meaning a house or building, which in modern use can also refer to an apartment or a room.
  - **component characters:**
    - 房
    - 子
