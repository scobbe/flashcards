## establishment; hall
### sub-word
#### restaurant
---
- **traditional:** 館
- **simplified:** 馆
- **pronunciation:** guǎn
- **definition:** establishment; hall
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 宾(賓)馆(館) (bīnguǎn, "guesthouse")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館) (fànguǎn, "restaurant")
  - 图(圖)书(書)馆(館) (túshūguǎn, "library")
  - 博物馆(館) (bówùguǎn, "museum")
- **etymology (館):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 饣(食) (shí, "food") + phonetic: 官 (guān, "official").
  - **interpretation:** 饣(食) provides the semantic domain of food and provisions, while 官 supplies the sound guǎn. The original sense was a place associated with food or lodging, which broadened to halls and public institutions in modern compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 食
    - 官
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A4%A8
  - **simplification rule (馆):** The semantic component was simplified by replacing 食/飠 with the reduced side-form 饣, retaining the phonetic 官 to reduce strokes.
