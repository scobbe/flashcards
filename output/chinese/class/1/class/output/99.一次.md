## one time; once
### sub-word
#### first time
---
- **traditional:** 一次
- **simplified:** 一次
- **pronunciation:** yí cì
- **definition:** one time; once
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一次性 (yī cì xìng, "single-use item")
  - 一次函数(數) (yī cì hán shù, "linear function")
  - 一次方程式 (yī cì fāng chéng shì, "linear equation")
  - 一次能源 (yī cì néng yuán, "primary energy")
- **etymology (一次):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 一 (yī, "one") + 次 (cì, "number of times").
  - **interpretation:** Formed from 一 meaning one and 次 meaning time/occurrence, literally “one occurrence,” hence “once.” In extended use, it modifies nouns to indicate first in order or degree (e.g., linear/first-order, single-use), since 次 denotes count or order.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 次
