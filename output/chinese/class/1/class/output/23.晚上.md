## evening or night
---
- **traditional:** 晚上
- **simplified:** 晚上
- **pronunciation:** wǎn shang
- **definition:** evening or night
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 晚上好 (wǎnshang hǎo, "good evening")
  - 晚上见(見) (wǎnshang jiàn, "see you tonight")
  - 晚上回家 (wǎnshang huí jiā, "go home at night")
  - 晚上十点(點) (wǎnshang shí diǎn, "ten o'clock")
- **etymology (晚上):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 晚 (wǎn, "evening") + 上 (shàng, "on").
  - **interpretation:** 晚 denotes the late part of the day; 上 functions as a temporal suffix meaning “on/at” a time. Together they indicate the period on the late part of the day, yielding the sense “evening; night”.
  - **component characters:**
    - 晚
    - 上
