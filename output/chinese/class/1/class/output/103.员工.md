## employee or staff member
---
- **traditional:** 員工
- **simplified:** 员工
- **pronunciation:** yuán gōng
- **definition:** employee or staff member
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 员(員)工手册(冊) (yuángōng shǒucè, "employee handbook")
  - 员(員)工保留 (yuángōng bǎoliú, "employee retention")
  - 员(員)工电(電)梯 (yuángōng diàntī, "staff elevator")
  - 员(員)工流动(動)率 (yuángōng liúdònglǜ, "employee turnover")
- **etymology (員工):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 员(員) (yuán, "member") + 工 (gōng, "work").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 员(員) (yuán, "member") with 工 (gōng, "work") to denote a person belonging to the world of work, hence an employee or staff member. The meaning is transparent and remains the standard modern sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - 员
    - 員
    - 工
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%93%A1%E5%B7%A5
  - **simplification rule (员工):** 員 was simplified to 员 by reducing and regularizing the 貝 component, removing interior strokes to lower complexity and standardize the form across compounds.
