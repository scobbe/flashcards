## school
### sub-word
#### school
---
- **traditional:** 校
- **simplified:** 校
- **pronunciation:** xiào
- **definition:** school
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 军(軍)校 (jūnxiào, "military school")
  - 校园(園) (xiàoyuán, "school campus")
  - 校友 (xiàoyǒu, "school alumni")
  - 校长(長) (xiàozhǎng, "school principal")
- **etymology (校):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 木 (mù, "tree") + phonetic: 交 (jiāo, "cross") → 校.
  - **interpretation:** 木 (mù, "tree") is the semantic determinative, originally pointing to wooden implements or structures, while 交 (jiāo, "cross") provides the sound. Some sources analyze the verb sense jiào "to examine/compare" as a derivation from 交 with a causative suffix, and the school-related xiào usage later became a bound noun in compounds while retaining the same phonetic.
  - **component characters:**
    - 木 (mù, "tree")
    - 交 (jiāo, "cross")
