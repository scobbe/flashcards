- **definition:** person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人们(們) (rénmen, "the people")
  - 人类(類) (rénlèi, "human race")
  - 人口 (rénkǒu, "population size")
  - 人权(權) (rénquán, "human rights")
- **etymology (人):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a standing person seen in profile with two legs apart.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human figure. The original drawing directly meant a person and later served as a general marker for human-related meanings.
