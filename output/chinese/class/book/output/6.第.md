## ordinal prefix
---
- **traditional:** 第
- **simplified:** 第
- **pronunciation:** dì
- **definition:** ordinal prefix
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 第一 (dì yī, "first")
  - 第一次 (dì yī cì, "first time")
  - 第一名 (dì yī míng, "first place")
  - 第三者 (dì sān zhě, "third party")
- **etymology (第):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic: 弟 (dì, "younger brother") → 第.
  - **interpretation:** 竹 is the semantic determinative, referring to bamboo slips used for writing, accounting, and classification, linking the character to recording and ordering. 弟 supplies the sound dì. The form is thus read dì and came to mark ordinals via the notion of classification; some sources note it may have originally been written 弟.
  - **component characters:**
    - 竹
    - 弟
    - 第
