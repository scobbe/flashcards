## step
### sub-component
#### be OK or all right; profession or trade
---
- **traditional:** 亍
- **simplified:** 亍
- **pronunciation:** chù
- **definition:** step
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 彳亍 (chì chù, "hesitate and pace")
  - 彳亍不前 (chì chù bù qián, "hesitate to advance")
  - 彳亍街头(頭) (chì chù jiē tóu, "loiter on the street")
  - 彳亍而行 (chì chù ér xíng, "walk slowly")
- **etymology (亍):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 足 (zú, "foot").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a foot, indicating the act of stepping. By extension it came to mean taking small steps or specifically a right-foot step, which aligns with its use in literary expressions like 彳亍 (chì chù, "pace hesitantly").
  - **component characters:**
    - 足
