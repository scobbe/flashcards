## hand
### sub-component
#### skin
---
- **traditional:** 又
- **simplified:** 又
- **pronunciation:** yòu
- **definition:** hand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 又下雨了 (yòu xià yǔ le, "raining again")
  - 又是艺(藝)术(術)家 (yòu shì yì shù jiā, "also an artist")
  - 又饿(餓)又渴 (yòu è yòu kě, "hungry and thirsty")
  - 又聪(聰)明又漂亮 (yòu cōngmíng yòu piàoliang, "smart and pretty")
- **etymology (又):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of right hand.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a right hand. The modern adverbial sense “again; also” does not come from the hand pictograph but from an adverbial derivation of 有 (yǒu, "to have; there is"), whose additive notion motivates the contemporary meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
