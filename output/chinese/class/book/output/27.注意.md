## to pay attention
---
- **traditional:** 注意
- **simplified:** 注意
- **pronunciation:** zhùyì
- **definition:** to pay attention
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 注意不到 (zhù yì bù dào, "fail to notice")
  - 注意到 (zhù yì dào, "take notice")
  - 注意力 (zhù yì lì, "attention level")
  - 注意事项(項) (zhù yì shì xiàng, "important notes")
- **etymology (注意):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 注 (zhù, "focus") + 意 (yì, "thought").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 注 meaning to focus or concentrate with 意 meaning thought or mind, literally “focus the mind.” This yields the sense of directing one’s attention, which in modern usage functions as the verb “to pay attention.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 注
    - 意
