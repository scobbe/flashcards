## mouth
### sub-component
#### as; like
---
- **traditional:** 口
- **simplified:** 口
- **pronunciation:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 入口 (rù kǒu, "entrance")
  - 出口 (chū kǒu, "exit")
  - 门(門)口 (mén kǒu, "doorway")
  - 口子 (kǒu zi, "hole")
- **etymology (口):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts an open human mouth. From this concrete image, the meaning broadened to denote openings and entrances and to serve as a classifier for mouthfuls and members, aligning with modern senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
