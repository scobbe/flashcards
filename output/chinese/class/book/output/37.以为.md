## to think; to assume
---
- **traditional:** 以為
- **simplified:** 以为
- **pronunciation:** yǐwéi
- **definition:** to think; to assume
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 我以为(為)你不会(會)走 (wǒ yǐ wéi nǐ bú huì zǒu, "I thought you wouldn't leave")
  - 以为(為)是 (yǐ wéi shì, "assume it is")
  - 以为(為)自己是对(對)的 (yǐ wéi zì jǐ shì duì de, "think oneself right")
  - 以为(為)没(沒)事 (yǐ wéi méi shì, "assume it's fine")
- **etymology (以為):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 以 (yǐ, "take; use") + 为(為) (wéi, "regard as; consider").
  - **interpretation:** Originally a construction meaning to take something as something. With the object often omitted, it generalized to mean to consider or believe, yielding the modern sense “to think; to assume.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 以
    - 为
    - 為
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E4%BB%A5%E7%82%BA
  - **simplification rule (以为):** The simplified form replaces 為 with 为 through stroke reduction and component merging, while 以 remains unchanged.
