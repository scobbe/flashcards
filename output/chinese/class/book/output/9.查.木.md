- **etymology (木):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a tree with branches above and roots below.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a tree, showing the crown above and roots below. The meaning extended from the plant to the material ‘wood’, and it functions as a semantic determinative for tree- and wood-related characters.
