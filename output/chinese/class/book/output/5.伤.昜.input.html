Ideogrammic compound (會意 / 会意): 日 (“sun”) + 示 + 彡. The rays of the sun over a sacrificial altar. Sometimes, the latter component is indicated as 丂 and explained as tree branches, however the original drawing is closer to the original version of the sacrifical altar. See also the bottom part of 卓.
