## bicycle
---
- **traditional:** 自行車
- **simplified:** 自行车
- **pronunciation:** zìxíngchē
- **definition:** bicycle
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 骑(騎)自行车(車) (qí zìxíngchē, "ride a bicycle")
  - 自行车(車)头(頭)盔 (zìxíngchē tóukuī, "bicycle helmet")
  - 自行车(車)停放架 (zìxíngchē tíngfàngjià, "bicycle stand")
  - 自行车(車)道 (zìxíngchē dào, "bicycle lane")
- **etymology (自行車):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 自 (zì, "self") + 行 (xíng, "go") + 车(車) (chē, "vehicle").
  - **interpretation:** 自 "self" and 行 "to go" modify 车 "vehicle" to mean a vehicle moved by oneself. In modern usage this denotes a human-powered vehicle propelled by pedaling, hence a bicycle.
  - **component characters:**
    - 自
    - 行
    - 车
    - 車
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%87%AA%E8%A1%8C%E8%BB%8A
  - **simplification rule (自行车):** Simplified by replacing 車 with 车, a reduced-stroke component standardized in the character simplification scheme.
