## medicine; medical drugs
### sub-word
#### medical expenses
---
- **traditional:** 醫藥
- **simplified:** 医药
- **pronunciation:** yīyào
- **definition:** medicine; medical drugs
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 医(醫)药(藥)箱 (yī yào xiāng, "medicine kit")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)费(費) (yī yào fèi, "medical expenses")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)行业(業) (yī yào háng yè, "pharmaceutical industry")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)代表 (yī yào dài biǎo, "pharmaceutical sales rep")
- **etymology (醫藥):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 医(醫) (yī, "medical") + 药(藥) (yào, "drug").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 医 "medical/heal" and 药 "drug/medicine" to denote the field of medicine and pharmaceuticals. The coordinated meaning covers both medical treatment and the drugs used, which matches modern uses for the industry, products, and costs.
  - **component characters:**
    - 医
    - 醫
    - 药
    - 藥
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%86%AB%E8%97%A5
  - **simplification rule (医药):** Both characters were simplified by component replacement and stroke reduction: 醫 → 医 and 藥 → 药.
