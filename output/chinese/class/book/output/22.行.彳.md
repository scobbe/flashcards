## step
### sub-component
#### be OK or all right; profession or trade
---
- **traditional:** 彳
- **simplified:** 彳
- **pronunciation:** chì
- **definition:** step
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 彳亍 (chì chù, "pace slowly")
  - 单(單)单(單)彳 (dān dān chì, "only walking slowly")
  - 彳部 (chì bù, "radical section")
  - 彳亍徘徊 (chì chù pái huái, "pace back and forth")
- **etymology (彳):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 行 (xíng, "intersection") → simplified left-side form 彳 (chì, "step").
  - **interpretation:** 行 depicts a street intersection seen from above; its left-side form was abstracted and standardized as 彳 to denote stepping and movement. This component naturally marks words about walking and pace today; it is also related to the top element of 辶(辵) (chuò, "walk").
  - **component characters:**
    - 行
    - 彳
