## hide
### sub-component
#### skin
---
- **traditional:** 革
- **simplified:** 革
- **pronunciation:** gé
- **definition:** hide
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 革职(職) (gé zhí, "dismiss from office")
  - 革命 (gé mìng, "political revolution")
  - 革新 (gé xīn, "innovation and reform")
  - 革除 (gé chú, "to abolish")
- **etymology (革):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of stretched animal hide → later variants briefly added 臼 (jiù, "mortar") elements around the hide.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a stretched animal hide. Later forms sometimes added 臼 (jiù, "mortar") around the hide, but the core sense remained leather and extended to removal and reform in use. Some sources relate it to 殼 (ké, "shell"), though the origin is still pictographic.
  - **component characters:**
    - 臼
