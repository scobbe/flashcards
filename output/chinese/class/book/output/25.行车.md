## to drive; traffic
### sub-word
#### bicycle
---
- **traditional:** 行車
- **simplified:** 行车
- **pronunciation:** xíngchē
- **definition:** to drive; traffic
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 行车(車)执(執)照 (xíngchē zhízhào, "driver's license")
  - 行车(車)记(記)录(錄)仪(儀) (xíngchē jìlùyí, "dash camera")
  - 行车(車)记(記)录(錄)器 (xíngchē jìlùqì, "drive recorder")
  - 行车(車)距离(離) (xíngchē jùlí, "driving distance")
- **etymology (行車):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 行 (xíng, "to go") + 车(車) (chē, "vehicle").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 行 "to go/move" with 车 "vehicle" to denote movement by vehicle, hence driving. By extension it refers to vehicular traffic and matters related to operating vehicles.
  - **component characters:**
    - 行
    - 车
    - 車
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%A1%8C%E8%BB%8A
  - **simplification rule (行车):** Component replacement: 車 was simplified to 车 to reduce strokes.
