#### medicine or drug; to medicate
---
- **traditional:** 樂
- **simplified:** 乐
- **pronunciation:** lè / yuè
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 音乐(樂) (yīnyuè, "music")
  - 乐(樂)队(隊) (yuèduì, "band")
  - 乐(樂)器 (yuèqì, "musical instrument")
  - 乐(樂)谱(譜) (yuèpǔ, "sheet music")
- **etymology (樂):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 纟(糸) (sī, "silk") + 木 (mù, "wood") → ideogrammic: 纟(糸) (sī, "silk") + 白 (bái, "white") + 木 (mù, "wood").
  - **interpretation:** It combines strings on wood, depicting a stringed instrument, which yields the sense of music and, by association, joy. In later bronze forms, 白 was inserted between the strings, possibly depicting a thumb in playing or serving as a phonetic element.
  - **component characters:**
    - 糸
    - 木
    - 白
