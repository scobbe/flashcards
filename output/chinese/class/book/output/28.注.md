## to focus; to note
### sub-word
#### to pay attention
---
- **traditional:** 注
- **simplified:** 注
- **pronunciation:** zhù
- **definition:** to focus; to note
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 专(專)注 (zhuān zhù, "be focused")
  - 注视(視) (zhù shì, "watch closely")
  - 备(備)注 (bèi zhù, "add remarks")
  - 注意 (zhù yì, "pay attention")
- **etymology (注):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 水 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 主 (zhǔ, "master") → 注 (zhù, "pour").
  - **interpretation:** 水 is the semantic determinative indicating relation to liquids and flow, while 主 provides the sound zhù. The original sense was to pour or conduct water, which extends to actions like injecting and metaphorically to focusing attention. The annotation sense in modern Simplified is included because 註 was merged into 注.
  - **component characters:**
    - 水
    - 主
    - 注
