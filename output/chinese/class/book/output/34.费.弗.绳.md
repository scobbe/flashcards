- **contemporary usage:**
  - 绳(繩)子 (shéngzi, "rope or cord")
  - 绳(繩)索 (shéngsuǒ, "rope and cable")
  - 绳(繩)结(結) (shéngjié, "rope knot")

- **etymology (繩):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic 纟(糸) (mì, "silk") + phonetic 黾(黽) (mǐn, "frog").
  - **interpretation:** 纟(糸) is the silk/thread determinative, indicating a cord or rope made of fibers. 黾(黽) supplies the sound, matching the historical pronunciation that led to shéng. The sense "to restrain" extends from using rope to tie and control.
  - **component characters:**
    - 糸
    - 黾
    - 黽
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%B9%A9
  - **simplification rule (绳):** Simplified 绳 replaces the thread radical 糸 with 纟 and the phonetic 黽 with its simplified form 黾, reducing strokes through component replacement.
