## rising sun
### sub-component
#### to hurt; to wound
---
- **traditional:** 昜
- **simplified:** 昜
- **pronunciation:** yáng
- **definition:** rising sun
- **contemporary usage:**
  - None, not in contemporary use.
- **etymology (昜):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 日 (rì, "sun") + 示 (shì, "altar") + 彡 (shān, "rays").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the sun with radiating light over a sacrificial altar, conveying brightness and the rising sun. This visual composition underlies the sense of light/sunny aspect and aligns with later use as an alternative form related to 阳. Some sources instead analyze the lower part as 丂 representing branches, but the altar explanation matches earlier forms.
  - **component characters:**
    - 日
    - 示
    - 彡
