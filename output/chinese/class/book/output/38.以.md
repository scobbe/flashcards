## to use; by means of
### sub-word
#### to think; to assume
---
- **traditional:** 以
- **simplified:** 以
- **pronunciation:** yǐ
- **definition:** to use; by means of
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 以前 (yǐ qián, "in the past")
  - 以来(來) (yǐ lái, "since then")
  - 以及 (yǐ jí, "as well as")
  - 以为(為) (yǐ wéi, "to think")
- **etymology (以):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 人 (rén, "person") → carrying a load.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person carrying something. The modern sense ‘to use; by means of’ extends from the concrete act of taking up and employing an object to accomplish something.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
