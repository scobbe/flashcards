Etymology 1

[edit]

From Proto-Sino-Tibetan *bliŋ (“string; thread; cord”); compare Japhug tɯmbri (“rope”), Burmese အမျှင် (a.hmyang, “string”), Tangut 𘘫 (*bji², “rope”) (STEDT; Schuessler, 2007; Jacques, 2014; Zhang, Jacques and Lai, 2019).

Definitions

繩

  1. string; rope (Classifier: 條／条 m c)
  2. (literary) to restrict; to restrain
  3. a surname
