Adverb

[edit]

自行

  1. by oneself; on one's own
    由某人自行決定／由某人自行决定  ―  yóu mǒurén zìxíng juédìng  ―  to be at somebody's discretion
    請您自行斟酌。／请您自行斟酌。  ―  Qǐng nín zìxíng zhēnzhuó.  ―  Please think about this carefully by yourself.
  2. of one's own accord; voluntarily
