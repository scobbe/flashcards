## medical expenses
---
- **traditional:** 醫藥費
- **simplified:** 医药费
- **pronunciation:** yīyàofèi
- **definition:** medical expenses
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 医(醫)药(藥)费(費)缴(繳)纳(納) (yī yào fèi jiǎo nà, "pay medical fees")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)费(費)报(報)销(銷) (yī yào fèi bào xiāo, "medical expense reimbursement")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)费(費)发(發)票 (yī yào fèi fā piào, "medical expense invoice")
  - 医(醫)药(藥)费(費)清单(單) (yī yào fèi qīng dān, "itemized medical bill")
- **etymology (醫藥費):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 医(醫) (yī, "medical") + 药(藥) (yào, "medicine") + 费(費) (fèi, "expense").
  - **interpretation:** The parts combine transparently: 医药 modifies 费 to mean expenses for medical treatment and drugs. The compound straightforwardly denotes money spent on healthcare, which matches modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 医
    - 醫
    - 药
    - 藥
    - 费
    - 費
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%86%AB%E8%97%A5%E8%B2%BB
  - **simplification rule (医药费):** Traditional components 醫, 藥, 費 were simplified to 医, 药, 费 through component replacement and stroke reduction for easier writing.
