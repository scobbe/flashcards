## skin
### sub-component
#### by; passive marker
---
- **traditional:** 皮
- **simplified:** 皮
- **pronunciation:** pí
- **definition:** skin
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 皮鞋 (pí xié, "leather shoes")
  - 皮筋 (pí jīn, "rubber band")
  - 皮米 (pí mǐ, "picometre")
  - 调(調)皮 (tiáo pí, "naughty")
- **etymology (皮):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: 又 (yòu, "hand") stripping 革 (gé, "hide") → pelt shifts to the top-left and becomes abstracted.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a hand stripping an animal pelt. As the graph simplified, the pelt element was abstracted and repositioned, while the meaning settled on “skin/hide,” later extending to leather, wrappers, and related surfaces.
  - **component characters:**
    - 又 (yòu, "hand")
    - 革 (gé, "hide")
