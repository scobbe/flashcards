## person
### sub-component
#### to hurt; to wound
---
- **traditional:** 人
- **simplified:** 人
- **pronunciation:** rén
- **definition:** person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人手一册(冊) (rén shǒu yī cè, "everyone has a copy")
  - 人所共知 (rén suǒ gòng zhī, "common knowledge")
  - 人云亦云 (rén yún yì yún, "follow the crowd")
  - 人祸(禍) (rén huò, "human disaster")
- **etymology (人):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 人 (rén, "person").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a standing man in side view, with one stroke as the torso/leg and the other as an arm. The form was later stylized but the meaning remained ‘person’, extending to ‘people’ collectively and human-related senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
