## medicine expenses
### sub-word
#### medical expenses
---
- **traditional:** 藥費
- **simplified:** 药费
- **pronunciation:** yàofèi
- **definition:** medicine expenses
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 药(藥)费(費)报(報)销(銷) (yàofèi bàoxiāo, "medication reimbursement")
  - 药(藥)费(費)自理 (yàofèi zìlǐ, "self-pay medication")
  - 药(藥)费(費)发(發)票 (yàofèi fāpiào, "medication invoice")
  - 药(藥)费(費)补(補)助 (yàofèi bǔzhù, "medication subsidy")
- **etymology (藥費):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 药(藥) (yào, "medicine") + 费(費) (fèi, "expense").
  - **interpretation:** The compound combines 药, meaning medicine, with 费, meaning expense, to denote money spent on medications. The semantics are transparent and match modern usage for drug-related costs.
  - **component characters:**
    - 药
    - 藥
    - 费
    - 費
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%97%A5%E8%B2%BB
  - **simplification rule (药费):** 药费 is the simplified form of 藥費, with 藥 reduced to 药 by component replacement under 艹 and 費 simplified to 费 through stroke reduction and component regularization.
