## store
### sub-component
#### medical; doctor
---
- **traditional:** 匸
- **simplified:** 匸
- **pronunciation:** xì
- **definition:** store
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 匸度 (xì dù, "be here")
  - 匸边(邊) (xì biān, "at the side")
  - 匸屋企 (xì wū qǐ, "at home")
  - 匸公司 (xì gōng sī, "at the office")
- **etymology (匸):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: depiction of a hiding enclosure → 'to conceal'.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a cover enclosing something or someone, indicating hiding or storage. This concrete image gave rise to the meaning 'to cover; to conceal', now largely obsolete as a standalone character but retained as a radical associated with concealment.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
