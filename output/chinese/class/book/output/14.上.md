## up; on; to go up
### sub-word
#### immediately; right away
---
- **traditional:** 上
- **simplified:** 上
- **pronunciation:** shàng
- **definition:** up; on; to go up
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 上一年 (shàng yī nián, "last year")
  - 上集 (shàng jí, "last episode")
  - 上世纪 (shàng shìjì, "last century")
  - 在理论上 (zài lǐlùn shàng, "in theory")
- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: a line placed above another to indicate position → 𠄞 (shàng, "up") → 上 (shàng, "up").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a short stroke placed above a baseline to indicate the position ‘up/above’. The early form 𠄞 (shàng, "up") was later regularized to 上 (shàng, "up"), and the concrete spatial notion naturally extends to senses like ‘on’ and ‘upper/previous’ in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 𠄞
    - 上
