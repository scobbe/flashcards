## to finish; to run out
---
- **traditional:** 完
- **simplified:** 完
- **pronunciation:** wán
- **definition:** to finish; to run out
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 看完电(電)影 (kàn wán diànyǐng, "finish watching the movie")
  - 完成任务(務) (wánchéng rènwù, "complete the task")
  - 用完了 (yòng wán le, "used up")
  - 完美无(無)缺 (wánměi wúquē, "perfect and flawless")
- **etymology (完):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 宀 (mián, "roof") + phonetic: 元 (yuán, "origin").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 宀 as the semantic determinative indicating something under a roof, suggesting covered or intact, with 元 as the phonetic giving the reading close to wán. The roof semantic aligns with the idea of being whole or complete, which extends to modern senses like finishing a task or using something up.
  - **component characters:**
    - 宀
    - 元
