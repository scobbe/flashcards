## wood
### sub-component
#### to inspect
---
- **traditional:** 木
- **simplified:** 木
- **pronunciation:** mù
- **definition:** wood
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 木头(頭) (mù tou, "wood or timber")
  - 木屋 (mù wū, "wooden house")
  - 木讷(訥) (mù nè, "inarticulate and wooden")
  - 树(樹)木 (shù mù, "trees and woods")
- **etymology (木):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 树(樹) (shù, "tree") with branches above and roots below.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a tree with branches above and roots below. The graphic was extended from the concrete tree to the material sense ‘wood’, and it now also serves as a semantic element in compounds related to trees, timber, and plant matters.
  - **component characters:**
    - 树
    - 樹
