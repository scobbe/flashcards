### sub-component
#### to ride
---
- **traditional:** 馬
- **simplified:** 马
- **pronunciation:** mǎ
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 骑(騎)马(馬) (qí mǎ, "ride a horse")
  - 马(馬)上 (mǎ shàng, "at once")
  - 马(馬)路 (mǎ lù, "road")
  - 马(馬)车(車) (mǎ chē, "carriage")
- **etymology (馬):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 马(馬) (mǎ, "horse").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a horse with its head facing left and a flowing mane. The original sense is the animal itself; by extension it yields uses like a colloquial prefix meaning "big", and it has also converged with the insect prefix 蚂(螞) (mǎ, "insect prefix") in some formations. The simplified form 马 follows the cursive reduction of 馬.
  - **component characters:**
    - 马
    - 馬
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A6%AC
  - **simplification rule (马):** Reduced from 馬 by adopting a cursive-style outline that merges and omits interior strokes, yielding the streamlined form 马.
