## cloudy sky; overcast weather
### yīntiān
---
- **traditional:** 陰天
- **definition:** cloudy sky; overcast weather
- **pinyin:** yīntiān
- **components:**
  - 阴(陰)
    - yīn
    - cloudy; overcast
  - 天
    - tiān
    - sky
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 陰(阴) (yīn, "cloudy; overcast") + 天 (tiān, "sky") =
    - "cloudy sky; overcast weather"
  - **interpretation:** A 天 (tiān, "sky") that is 陰(阴) (yīn, "cloudy") refers to weather conditions where the sky is covered with clouds, resulting in overcast weather. The combination succinctly conveys the concept of a day with gloomy weather.
- **examples:**
  - 今天是阴(陰)天，我们(們)打算待在家里(裡)。
    - Jīntiān shì yīntiān, wǒmen dǎsuàn dāi zài jiālǐ.
    - Today is overcast, and we plan to stay at home.
  - 阴(陰)天使得景色显(顯)得柔和。
    - Yīntiān shǐdé jǐngsè xiǎnde róuhé.
    - The cloudy sky makes the scenery appear soft.
  - 虽(雖)然今天是阴(陰)天，但心情却(卻)很晴朗。
    - Suīrán jīntiān shì yīntiān, dàn xīnqíng què hěn qínglǎng.
    - Although today is overcast, the mood is still quite bright.
### 阴(陰)天( ) → 阴(陰)
- **traditional:** 陰
- **definition:** cloudy; overcast
- **pinyin:** yīn
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - mound
  - 侌
    - yīn
    - phonetic
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Semantic: 阜 (fù, "mound") + Phonetic: 侌 (yīn) =
    - concealed places or shade ->
    - moon and female associations
  - **interpretation:** The character 阴(陰) (yīn, "concealed") uses 阜 (fù, "mound") to suggest a connection to physical features like hills or mounds that create shade, while 侌 (yīn) contributes the sound. This relates to concepts of concealment, shadow, and indirectly the moon and femininity in traditional Chinese thought.
  - **simplification:** The character 阴 replaces the phonetic 侌 (yīn) with 月 (yuè, "moon"), making it visually more simple and retaining the association with moon-related meanings.
- **examples:**
  - 北方的阴(陰)雨绵绵(綿綿)，让(讓)人感到忧郁(憂鬱)。
    - Běifāng de yīnyǔ miánmián, ràng rén gǎndào yōuyù.
    - The continuous gloomy drizzle in the north makes people feel depressed.
  - 她展现(現)出阴(陰)柔之美，令人着(著)迷。
    - Tā zhǎnxiàn chū yīnróu zhī měi, lìng rén zháomí.
    - She displays a unique feminine grace that captivates people.
  - 夜幕降临时(臨時)，月色显(顯)得格外阴(陰)柔。
    - Yèmù jiànglín shí, yuèsè xiǎndé géwài yīnróu.
    - As night falls, the moonlight appears especially soft and feminine.
### 阴(陰)天( ) → 阴(陰) → 阜
- **definition:** mound
- **pinyin:** fù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a mound or hill, with a shape that suggests an elevated landform.
  - **interpretation:** The character 阜 (fù, "mound") originally depicted the silhouette of a small hill or mound, representing its natural shape and structure.
- **examples:**
  - 山边(邊)的小阜上，盛开着(開著)野花。
    - Shān biān de xiǎo fù shàng, shèng kāi zhe yě huā.
    - Wildflowers bloom atop the small mound by the mountainside.
  - 古道尽头(盡頭)有阜。
    - Gǔ dào jìn tóu yǒu fù.
    - There is a mound at the end of the ancient road.
### 阴(陰)天( ) → 阴(陰) → 侌
- **definition:** phonetic
- **pinyin:** yīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ancient form
  - **description:** 侌 (yīn, "phonetic") → ancient form of 陰 (yīn, "yin; shade")
  - **interpretation:** The character 侌 originally served as a phonetic component in the composition of 陰 (yīn, "yin; shade"), suggesting its antique usage and historical derivation.
### 阴(陰)天( ) → 天
- **definition:** sky
- **pinyin:** tiān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Horizontal line above 大(dà, "big") to depict sky ->
    - anthropomorphic man with large head symbolizing heaven
  - **interpretation:** The character 天(tiān) originally depicts the sky as a horizontal line above a person with outstretched arms, 大(dà, "big"), emphasizing the vastness or domain of heaven. Over time, the character evolved into an anthropomorphic representation of heaven, which later influenced its interpretation in classical texts.
- **examples:**
  - 夜幕降临(臨)，天边(邊)星光闪烁(閃爍)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín, tiānbiān xīnguāng shǎnshuò.
    - As night falls, starlight gleams on the horizon.
  - 他仰望高空，祈求天佑众(眾)生。
    - Tā yǎngwàng gāokōng, qíqiú tiānyòu zhòngshēng.
    - He gazes up to the sky and prays for heaven's protection over all living beings.
