## Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)
### Zhōngguó Mínháng
---
- **traditional:** 中國民航
- **definition:** Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó Mínháng
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
  - 國
    - guó
    - country
  - 民
    - mín
    - people; civil
  - 航
    - háng
    - navigation; aviation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 中 (zhōng, "middle") + 國 (guó, "country") =
    - 中國 (zhōngguó, "China"); 民 (mín, "people; civil") + 航 (háng, "navigation; aviation") =
    - 民航 (mínháng, "civil aviation")
  - **interpretation:** 中國 (zhōngguó, "China") refers to the country, while 民航 (mínháng, "civil aviation") denotes the sector focused on civilian air transportation. Combined, 中國民航 refers to the Civil Aviation Administration of China, the national body overseeing civil aviation in the country.
- **examples:**
  - 中国(國)民航监(監)管严(嚴)格。
    - Zhōngguó Mínháng jiānguǎn yángé.
    - The Civil Aviation Administration of China enforces strict oversight.
  - 中国(國)民航安全记录(記錄)良好，促进国际(進國際)合作。
    - Zhōngguó Mínháng ānquán jìlù liánghǎo, cùjìn guójì hézuò.
    - The CAAC has a good safety record, fostering international cooperation.
