## Guangzhou
### Guǎngzhōu
---
- **traditional:** 廣州
- **definition:** Guangzhou
- **pinyin:** Guǎngzhōu
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - broad
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - state; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 廣 (guǎng, "broad") refers to the vastness or expansiveness, while 州 (zhōu, "state; region") is a term used historically for an administrative division or region.
  - **interpretation:** 廣州 (Guǎngzhōu, "Guangzhou") is named for its historical significance as a broad and expansive region within China. It reflects its status as a major and influential administrative area.
- **examples:**
  - 我打算明年去广(廣)州，体验当(體驗當)地文化。
    - Wǒ dǎsuàn míngnián qù Guǎngzhōu, tǐyàn dāngdì wénhuà.
    - I plan to go to Guangzhou next year to experience the local culture.
  - 广(廣)州是中国(國)的南方大都市。
    - Guǎngzhōu shì Zhōngguó de nánfāng dà dūshì.
    - Guangzhou is a major metropolis in southern China.
  - 我昨天在广(廣)州参(參)加了一场(場)音乐会(樂會)，感受到了城市的魅力。
    - Wǒ zuótiān zài Guǎngzhōu cānjiāle yī chǎng yīnyuèhuì, gǎnshòu dào le chéngshì de mèilì.
    - I attended a concert in Guangzhou yesterday and experienced the charm of the city.
### 广(廣)州( ) → 广(廣)
- **traditional:** 廣
- **definition:** broad
- **pinyin:** guǎng
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with a roof, similar to 宀 (mián, "roof") ->
    - a building with three walls ->
    - shelter
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 广(廣) (guǎng, "shelter") visually represents a three-walled structure under a roof, conveying the concept of shelter or covering. It is closely related to the character 宀 (mián, "roof"), which includes four walls, indicating the structural nuance captured by ancient scribes.
  - **simplification:** 广(廣) was simplified by using the pictographic element alone, omitting the phonetic component 黃.
- **examples:**
  - 暴风骤(風驟)雨时(時)，山洞广(廣)可供人躲避。
    - Bào fēng zhòu yǔ shí, shān dòng guǎng kě gòng rén duǒ bì.
    - When the storm rages, the cave provides shelter.
  - 旅途中疲惫时(憊時)，老树(樹)下广给(廣給)予行人庇护(護)。
    - Lǚ tú zhōng pí bèi shí, lǎo shù xià guǎng jǐ yǔ xíng rén bì hù.
    - During a tiring journey, the shelter under the old tree gives travelers protection.
### 广(廣)州( ) → 广(廣) → 宀
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉(漢)字代表家的温(溫)暖。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.
    - Chinese characters that include the radical '宀' represent the warmth of home.
  - 屋顶(頂)遮风挡(風擋)雨，家园(園)散发温(發溫)馨。
    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
    - The roof shields from wind and rain, and the home radiates warmth.
### 广(廣)州( ) → 州
- **definition:** state; region
- **pinyin:** zhōu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an islet in a river
  - **interpretation:** 州 (zhōu, "administrative division") was originally a pictogram representing a small piece of land or islet amidst water, illustrating the ancient practice of using distinct land areas as administrative divisions.
- **examples:**
  - 我计划参观几个(計劃參觀幾個)州。
    - Wǒ jìhuà cānguān jǐ gè zhōu.
    - I plan to visit several states.
  - 美国(國)的各州都有独(獨)特的风(風)景。
    - Měiguó de gè zhōu dōu yǒu dútè de fēngjǐng.
    - Every state in the United States has unique scenery.
  - 州政府负责(負責)管理地方事务(務)，政策由州长(長)制定。
    - Zhōu zhèngfǔ fùzé guǎnlǐ dìfāng shìwù, zhèngcè yóu zhōu zhǎng zhìdìng.
    - The state government is responsible for managing local affairs, and the policies are set by the governor.
