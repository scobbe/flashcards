## dangerous
### wēixiǎn
---
- **traditional:** 危險
- **definition:** dangerous
- **pinyin:** wēixiǎn
- **components:**
  - 危
    - wēi
    - dangerous; high; steep; critical
  - 险(險)
    - xiǎn
    - danger; risk; hazard; peril
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 危 (wēi, "dangerous") + 險 (xiǎn, "danger") =
    - a state of danger or risk
  - **interpretation:** 危險 (wēixiǎn, "dangerous") describes a situation that involves a high degree of risk or the potential for harm. The combination of 危 (wēi, "dangerous") and 險 (xiǎn, "danger") emphasizes the seriousness and potential threat inherent in such circumstances.
- **examples:**
  - 这里(這裡)很危险(險)。
    - Zhèlǐ hěn wēixiǎn.
    - It is dangerous here.
  - 那条(條)路很危险(險)，许(許)多人避而远(遠)之。
    - Nà tiáo lù hěn wēixiǎn, xǔduō rén bì ér yuǎn zhī.
    - That road is very dangerous, so many people avoid it.
