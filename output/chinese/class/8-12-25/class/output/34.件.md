## item; piece
*sub-word of*
1. software
---
- **traditional:** 件
- **simplified:** 件
- **pronunciation:** jiàn
- **definition:** item; piece
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 软件(軟件)
    - ruǎn jiàn
    - software
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 文件
    - wénjiàn
    - document file
  - 邮件
    - yóujiàn
    - postal mail
  - 软件
    - ruǎnjiàn
    - software program
  - 证件
    - zhèngjiàn
    - identification document
- **etymology (件):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 人 (rén, "man") + 牛 (niú, "cow")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 人 (rén, "man") with 牛 (niú, "cow") to depict a person associated with a cow, highlighting a single unit. This generalized to the idea of a discrete item or piece, and in modern use extends to documents and a classifier for countable pieces.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
      - rén
      - person
    - 牛
      - niú
      - ox
