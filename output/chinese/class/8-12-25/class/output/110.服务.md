## service
*sub-word of*
1. server or waiter
---
- **traditional:** 服務
- **simplified:** 服务
- **pronunciation:** fúwù
- **definition:** service
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 服务员(服務員)
    - fú wù yuán
    - server or waiter
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 服务员
    - fúwùyuán
    - service staff
  - 服务器
    - fúwùqì
    - computer server
  - 服务业
    - fúwùyè
    - service industry
  - 服务区
    - fúwùqū
    - service area
- **etymology (服務):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 服 (fú, "serve") + 务(務) (wù, "affairs")
  - **interpretation:** 服 conveys the sense of serving or obeying, while 务 denotes affairs or duties. Combined, they mean attending to affairs for others, yielding the verb "to serve" and the noun "service" in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 服
      - fú
      - clothes; to serve or obey
    - 务(務)
      - wù
      - affairs or duties; to engage in
    - 务(務)
      - wù
      - affairs or duties; to engage in

  - **simplification rule (服务):** Simplified by component replacement and stroke reduction: 服務 → 服务 via 務 → 务.
