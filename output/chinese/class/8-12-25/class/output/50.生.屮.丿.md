## slash
*sub-component of*
1. life; person
2. bud
---
- **traditional:** 丿
- **simplified:** 丿
- **pronunciation:** piě / yì
- **definition:** slash
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; person
  - 屮
    - chè
    - bud
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 丿部首
    - piě bùshǒu
    - radical slash
  - 丿部
    - piě bù
    - radical 4
  - 丿笔画
    - piě bǐhuà
    - slash stroke
  - 丿这一笔(丿這一笔)
    - piě zhè yī bǐ
    - this slash stroke
- **etymology (丿):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a slanted brush stroke →.
    - used as the name of the left-falling stroke.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a slanted brush stroke. The graph was subsequently used as the radical/stroke name for the left-falling line in characters; some historical dictionaries also recorded a phonetic-loan sense ‘arrive’ (yì), now obsolete.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
