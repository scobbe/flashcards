- **etymology (目):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a human eye →.
    - rotated 90 degrees in modern script.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human eye, hence the original meaning ‘eye’. As the script standardized and rotated the form, the character continued to denote sight and by extension appears productively in compounds for viewing, categories, and targets.
