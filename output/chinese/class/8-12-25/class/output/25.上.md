- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:** ideogram

    - a short line placed above 一 (yī, "one") to indicate a position above
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a short stroke above a baseline, visually marking something as higher or above. This concrete spatial idea underlies modern uses like the postposition meaning "on/above" and semantic extensions such as "previous/first part" in ordered sequences.
