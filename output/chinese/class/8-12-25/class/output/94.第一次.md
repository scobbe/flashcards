## first time
---
- **traditional:** 第一次
- **simplified:** 第一次
- **pronunciation:** dì yī cì
- **definition:** first time
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 第一次见面(第一次見麵)
    - dì yī cì jiàn miàn
    - first meeting
  - 第一次约会
    - dì yī cì yuē huì
    - first date
  - 第一次尝试
    - dì yī cì cháng shì
    - first attempt
  - 第一次世界大战
    - dì yī cì shì jiè dà zhàn
    - First World War
- **etymology (第一次):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 第一 (dì yī, "first") + 次 (cì, "occurrence") →
    - 第一次 (dì yī cì, "first time")
  - **interpretation:** Formed by combining 第一 “first” with 次 “occurrence,” literally “first occurrence,” hence “first time.” In modern usage it denotes the earliest instance of an event and also serves as a euphemism for one’s first sexual experience.
  - **component characters:**
    - 第
      - dì
      - ordinal prefix; sequence
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
    - 次
      - cì
      - time (occurrence); next
