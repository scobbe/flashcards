## rice; meal; food
*sub-word of*
1. restaurant
---
- **traditional:** 飯
- **simplified:** 饭
- **pronunciation:** fàn
- **definition:** rice; meal; food
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 饭馆(飯館)
    - fàn guǎn
    - restaurant
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 吃饭(吃飯)
    - chī fàn
    - eat a meal
  - 早饭(早飯)
    - zǎo fàn
    - morning meal
  - 米饭(米飯)
    - mǐ fàn
    - cooked rice
  - 糯米饭(糯米飯)
    - nuò mǐ fàn
    - sticky rice
- **etymology (飯):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phono-semantic: semantic: 食 (shí, "food") + phonetic: 反 (fǎn, "reverse")
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic determinative 食 indicating food with the phonetic 反 that supplies the sound. This yields the sense of cooked grain or a meal, which extends in modern use to rice, meals, and food.
  - **component characters:**
    - 食
      - shí
      - eat
    - 反
      - fǎn
      - oppose
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A3%AF
  - **simplification rule (饭):** The simplified form replaces the left semantic component 食/飠 with the simplified food radical 饣 to reduce strokes.
