## six
*sub-component of*
1. to study; learning
---
- **traditional:** 六
- **simplified:** 六
- **pronunciation:** liù / lù
- **definition:** six
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 学(學)
    - xué
    - to study; learning
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 六月
    - liùyuè
    - month of June
  - 六十
    - liùshí
    - the number sixty
  - 六边形(六邊形)
    - liùbiānxíng
    - six-sided polygon
  - 六年级(六年級)
    - liùniánjí
    - sixth grade
- **etymology (六):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram of 庐(廬) (lú, "shed") →
    - semantic: 六 (liù, "six")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a shed or hut, and was later borrowed as a phonetic loan to write the numeral ‘six’. Over time the character’s value stabilized to mean the number. An alternative view treats it as an ancient counting symbol.
  - **component characters:**
    - 庐(廬)
      - lú
      - hut
    - 廬
      - lú
      - cottage
    - 六
      - liù / lù
      - six
