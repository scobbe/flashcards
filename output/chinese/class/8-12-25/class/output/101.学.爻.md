## trigram lines
*sub-component of*
1. to study; learning
---
- **traditional:** 爻
- **simplified:** 爻
- **pronunciation:** yáo
- **definition:** trigram lines
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 学(學)

- **contemporary usage:**

  - 阳爻(陽爻)

- **etymology (爻):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: 乂 (yì, "cross") + 乂 (yì, "cross") →
    - depiction of two stacked X marks indicating crossed lines for divination.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts two stacked X-shaped marks used to denote crossed lines in divination. This visual motif aligns with lines in the I Ching and thus came to name the lines of hexagrams. Alternatives noted include pairs of yarrow stalks or bamboo slips, or simply crossing marks.
  - **component characters:**
    - 乂
      - yì
      - to cut
