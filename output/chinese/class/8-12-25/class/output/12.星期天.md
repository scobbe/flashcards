## Sunday
---
- **traditional:** 星期天
- **simplified:** 星期天
- **pronunciation:** xīng qī tiān
- **definition:** Sunday
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 星期天还是星期六(星期天還是星期六)
    - xīngqītiān háishì xīngqīliù
    - Sunday or Saturday
  - 星期天早上
    - xīngqītiān zǎoshang
    - Sunday morning
  - 星期天晚上
    - xīngqītiān wǎnshang
    - Sunday night
  - 星期天开门吗(星期天開門嗎)
    - xīngqītiān kāimén ma
    - Open on Sunday?
- **etymology (星期天):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 星期 (xīngqī, "week") + 天 (tiān, "day") →
    - 星期天 (xīngqītiān, "Sunday")
  - **interpretation:** Formed by combining 星期 “week” with 天 “day,” literally ‘week day,’ used as the name for Sunday. 天 supplies the general ‘day’ sense; the term parallels 星期日, which uses 日 ‘sun/day,’ and remains a common modern synonym for Sunday.
  - **component characters:**
    - 星
      - xīng
      - star
    - 期
      - qī
      - period or term; to expect
    - 天
      - tiān
      - day; sky
