## down; to finish
*sub-word of*
1. get off work
---
- **traditional:** 下
- **simplified:** 下
- **pronunciation:** xià
- **definition:** down; to finish
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 下班
    - xià bān
    - get off work
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 天下
    - tiān xià
    - the world
  - 地下
    - dì xià
    - under the ground
  - 下层
    - xià céng
    - lower level
  - 以下
    - yǐ xià
    - as follows
- **etymology (下):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - ideogram: short stroke placed below 一 (yī, "one") indicating "below" →
    - standardized as 下 (xià, "down")
  - **interpretation:** It shows a short stroke beneath a horizontal line, marking the position "below". This spatial sense developed into modern meanings like "down/below" and extended to notions such as "lower, next" and verbs like "descend" or "put down".
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
    - 下
      - xià
      - down; to finish
