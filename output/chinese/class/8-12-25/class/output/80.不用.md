## need not or don't have to
---
- **traditional:** 不用
- **simplified:** 不用
- **pronunciation:** bú yòng
- **definition:** need not or don't have to
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 不用道歉
    - bù yòng dào qiàn
    - don't apologize
  - 不用考虑
    - bù yòng kǎo lǜ
    - no need to consider
  - 不用客气
    - bù yòng kè qì
    - you're welcome
  - 不用说(不用說)
    - bù yòng shuō
    - needless to say
- **etymology (不用):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 不 (bù, "not") + 用 (yòng, "use")
  - **interpretation:** Combines the negator 不 and the verb 用 “use,” yielding the sense “not to use; need not.” In modern Mandarin it functions adverbially before verbs to indicate that an action is unnecessary.
  - **component characters:**
    - 不
      - bù
      - not; no
    - 用
      - yòng
      - to use
