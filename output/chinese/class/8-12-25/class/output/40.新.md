## new
---
- **traditional:** 新
- **simplified:** 新
- **pronunciation:** xīn
- **definition:** new
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 新软件
    - xīn ruǎnjiàn
    - new software
  - 新产品
    - xīn chǎnpǐn
    - new product
  - 新风气(新風气)
    - xīn fēngqì
    - new trend
  - 新车(新車)
    - xīn chē
    - new car
- **etymology (新):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 亲(親) (qīn, "hazelnut tree") + semantic: 斤 (jīn, "axe") →
    - 新
  - **interpretation:** 亲(親) (qīn, "hazelnut tree") acts as the phonetic element, while 斤 (jīn, "axe") provides the semantic idea of cutting wood. The notion of chopping a tree suggests initiating something afresh, hence the modern sense "new." Some sources also view it as an ideogrammic combination related to firewood, with that older sense preserved in 薪 (xīn, "firewood").
  - **component characters:**
    - 亲(親)
      - qīn
      - hazelnut tree
    - 斤
      - jīn
      - axe
