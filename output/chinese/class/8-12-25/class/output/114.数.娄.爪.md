## hands
### sub-component
---
- **traditional:** 爪
- **simplified:** 爪
- **pronunciation:** zhuǎ / zhǎo
- **definition:** hands
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 爪子 (zhǎozi, "animal claw or paw")
  - 八爪鱼(魚) (bāzhuǎyú, "octopus")
  - 张(張)牙舞爪 (zhāngyáwǔzhǎo, "brandish claws")
  - 爪牙 (zhǎoyá, "henchmen")
- **etymology (爪):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 手 (shǒu, "hand") grabbing from above.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human hand grabbing from above, which later generalized to the claws or nails of animals. Although it can indicate a claw today, the original drawing was of a human hand, with the meaning extended by usage to non-human talons and paws.
  - **component characters:**
    - 手
