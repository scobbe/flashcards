## Na (female name); lithe or graceful
---
- **traditional:** 娜
- **simplified:** 娜
- **pronunciation:** nà; nuó
- **definition:** Na (female name); lithe or graceful
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 娜娜 (Nànà, "given name Nana")
  - 安娜 (Ānnà, "given name Anna")
  - 汉(漢)娜 (Hànnà, "given name Hannah")
  - 雅典娜 (Yǎdiǎnnà, "goddess Athena")
- **etymology (娜):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 女 (nǚ, "woman") + phonetic: 那 (nà, "that") → 娜.
  - **interpretation:** 女 serves as the semantic determinative indicating association with women or femininity, while 那 provides the phonetic value na. This suits the modern senses: an adjective for feminine grace and a common element in female given names.
  - **component characters:**
    - 女 (nǚ, "woman")
    - 那 (nà, "that")
