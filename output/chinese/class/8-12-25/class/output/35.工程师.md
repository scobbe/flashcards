## engineer
---
- **traditional:** 工程師
- **simplified:** 工程师
- **pronunciation:** gōng chéng shī
- **definition:** engineer
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 工程师团队(工程师团隊)
    - gōng chéng shī tuán duì
    - engineering team
  - 工程师资格证(工程师資格证)
    - gōng chéng shī zī gé zhèng
    - engineer qualification certificate
  - 工程师培训
    - gōng chéng shī péi xùn
    - engineer training
  - 工程师岗位
    - gōng chéng shī gǎng wèi
    - engineer position
- **etymology (工程師):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 工程 (gōngchéng, "engineering; project") + 师(師) (shī, "master; expert")
  - **interpretation:** 工程 names the field of engineering or projects, while 师 denotes a skilled specialist or professional. Together they mean an engineering specialist, hence “engineer”; by extension in modern computing, it also refers to software developers.
  - **component characters:**
    - 工
      - gōng
      - work; worker
    - 程
      - chéng
      - journey; procedure or program
    - 师(師)
      - shī
      - master; specialist
    - 师(師)
      - shī
      - master; specialist
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B7%A5%E7%A8%8B%E5%B8%AB
  - **simplification rule (工程师):** The simplified form replaces 師 with 师, while 工 and 程 remain unchanged; this is a component replacement to reduce strokes and regularize the graph.
