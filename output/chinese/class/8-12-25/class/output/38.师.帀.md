- **etymology (帀):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 一 (yī, "one") + 巾 (jīn, "cloth")
  - **interpretation:** Combining 一 and 巾 is interpreted as a graphic suggesting a cloth covering or going all around something, yielding the sense “encircle; go around.” The glyph origin is noted as uncertain; Shuowen alternatively describes it as an inverted 之, indicating differing ancient analyses.
