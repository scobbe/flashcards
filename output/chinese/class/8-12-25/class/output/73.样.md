## kind; appearance
### sub-word
#### same or identical
---
- **traditional:** 樣
- **simplified:** 样
- **pronunciation:** yàng
- **definition:** kind; appearance
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 这(這)样(樣) (zhè yàng, "like this")
  - 一样(樣) (yī yàng, "the same")
  - 样(樣)品 (yàng pǐn, "sample product")
  - 样(樣)式 (yàng shì, "design style")
- **etymology (樣):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 木 (mù, "wood") + phonetic: 羊 (yáng, "sheep").
  - **interpretation:** A phono-semantic compound: 木 (mù, "wood") supplies the semantic field of objects and forms, while 羊 (yáng, "sheep") provides the sound. In the simplified form, the phonetic was regularized to 羊 (yáng), keeping a similar reading; the meaning 'appearance; kind' aligns with the idea of a thing’s form or pattern indicated by 木.
  - **component characters:**
    - 木 (mù, "wood")
    - 羊 (yáng, "sheep")
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%A8%A3
  - **simplification rule (样):** Simplified by substituting the right-side phonetic 羕 in 樣 with the simpler 羊, reducing strokes while preserving a similar sound.
