## ordinal prefix; sequence
*sub-word of*
1. first time
---
- **traditional:** 第
- **simplified:** 第
- **pronunciation:** dì
- **definition:** ordinal prefix; sequence
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 第一次
    - dì yī cì
    - first time
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 第一
    - dì yī
    - first
  - 第二
    - dì èr
    - second
  - 第一次
    - dì yī cì
    - first time
  - 第一名
    - dì yī míng
    - first place
- **etymology (第):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic: 弟 (dì, "younger brother") →
    - 第
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic 竹, referring to bamboo slips used for writing, tallying, and classification, with the phonetic 弟 to indicate sound. The bamboo determinative aligns with ordering and ranking on slips, which underlies the modern sense of ordinal sequencing. Some sources note an alternative early writing as 弟, but the established form uses 竹 as the semantic determinative.
  - **component characters:**
    - 竹
      - zhú
      - bamboo
    - 弟
      - dì
      - younger brother
