## evening
### wǎnshang
---
- **definition:** evening
- **pinyin:** wǎnshang
- **components:**
  - 晚
    - wǎn
    - evening; late
  - 上
    - shang
    - above; on
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** This word consists of 晚 (wǎn, "evening; late") and 上 (shang, "above; on") to denote the time period in the evening.
  - **interpretation:** The combination 晚上 (wǎnshang, "evening") refers to the time of day after the sun sets, when the sky is darkening. It represents the period of day when evening activities typically occur.
- **examples:**
  - 晚( )上( )七( )点(點)我( )在( )家( )。
    - wǎnshang qī diǎn wǒ zài jiā.

    - wǎnshang jiē dēng liàng qǐ, chéngshì biàn dé wēnróu.
    - In the evening, the streetlights turn on, and the city becomes gentle.

### 晚上 → 晚 → 免 → 冕 → 冃
- **definition:** crown
- **pinyin:** mào
- **components:**
  - 帽
    - mào
    - hat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hat ->
    - original form of 帽
  - **interpretation:** The character 冃 (mào, "crown") is a pictogram depicting a hat, which serves as the original form for 帽 (mào, "hat") where 冃 appears as the component ⺜. Over time, as the meaning became more specific, 帽 incorporated additional components like 巾 (jīn, "cloth") to emphasize its connection to headwear.
