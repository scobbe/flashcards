## moon
*sub-component of*
1. period or term; to expect
---
- **traditional:** 月
- **simplified:** 月
- **pronunciation:** yuè
- **definition:** moon
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 期
    - qī
    - period or term; to expect
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 月亮
    - yuè liàng
    - the moon
  - 月份
    - yuè fèn
    - calendar month
  - 月薪
    - yuè xīn
    - monthly salary
  - 月饼
    - yuè bǐng
    - moon cake
- **etymology (月):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a crescent moon
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a crescent moon. The graph straightforwardly writes the lunar body in Chinese. Early texts sometimes alternated it with 夕 (xī, "evening"), but the two graphs are distinct in later usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
