## fontanelle
### sub-component
#### child; noun suffix
---
- **traditional:** 囟
- **simplified:** 囟
- **pronunciation:** xìn
- **definition:** fontanelle
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 囟脑(腦)门(門) (xìn nǎo mén, "fontanelle area")
  - 囟门(門) (xìn mén, "cranial fontanelle")
  - 前囟 (qián xìn, "anterior fontanelle")
  - 后(後)囟 (hòu xìn, "posterior fontanelle")
- **etymology (囟):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of the fontanelle on an infant's skull.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the soft spot on a baby's skull as seen from above. The pictograph was carried forward as the anatomical term, so its meaning remains literal in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
