## fontanelle
*sub-component of*
1. child; noun suffix
---
- **traditional:** 囟
- **simplified:** 囟
- **pronunciation:** xìn
- **definition:** fontanelle
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child; noun suffix
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 囟脑门(囟脑門)
    - xìn nǎo mén
    - fontanelle area
  - 囟门(囟門)
    - xìn mén
    - cranial fontanelle
  - 前囟
    - qián xìn
    - anterior fontanelle
  - 后囟
    - hòu xìn
    - posterior fontanelle
- **etymology (囟):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of the fontanelle on an infant's skull
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the soft spot on a baby's skull as seen from above. The pictograph was carried forward as the anatomical term, so its meaning remains literal in modern usage.

    - None, character is in atomic form.
