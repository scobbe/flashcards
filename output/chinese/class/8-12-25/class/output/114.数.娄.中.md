## middle
---
- **traditional:** 中
- **simplified:** 中
- **pronunciation:** zhōng / zhòng
- **definition:** middle
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 数(數)
    - shù; shǔ
    - number; count
  - 娄(婁)
    - lóu / lǘ / lǚ
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 中央
    - zhōngyāng
    - central authorities
  - 中国(中國)
    - zhōngguó
    - the country China
  - 中秋
    - zhōngqiū
    - Mid-Autumn Festival
  - 中毒
    - zhòngdú
    - be poisoned
- **etymology (中):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a flagpole with a drum in the middle to mark the center →.
    - semantic: "middle/center"
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a flagpole with a drum placed at the center of a field to gather people and sense wind direction, hence the meaning “middle/center”. From this core sense, the verb sense “to hit the center; to be affected” developed (zhòng). Alternative analyses note a vertical 丨 (gǔn, "vertical stroke") passing through 口 (kǒu, "mouth"), or an arrow striking a target’s center.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
