## city
*sub-component of*
1. Na (female name); lithe or graceful
2. that
---
- **traditional:** 邑
- **simplified:** 邑
- **pronunciation:** yì
- **definition:** city
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 娜
    - nà; nuó
    - Na (female name); lithe or graceful
  - 那
    - nà / nè / nèi
    - that
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 四邑
    - sì yì
    - Siyi region
  - 五邑
    - wǔ yì
    - Wuyi region
  - 邑人
    - yì rén
    - townspeople
  - 城邑
    - chéng yì
    - cities and towns
- **etymology (邑):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 囗 (wéi, "enclosure; city wall") + 卪 (jié, "kneeling person")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 囗 (wéi, "enclosure; city wall") with 卪 (jié, "kneeling person") to depict a person within walls, i.e., a settled, walled place. This composition naturally yields the meaning of an inhabited city or town and related administrative senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 囗
      - wéi
      - enclosure
    - 卪
      - jié
      - seal variant
