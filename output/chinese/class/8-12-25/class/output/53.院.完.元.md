- **etymology (元):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a person with an emphasized head, rendered as 二 (èr, "two") above 儿(兒) (ér, "legs")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person with an emphasized head. Many analyses parse it as 二 (èr, "two") over 儿(兒) (ér, "legs"), highlighting the head; from "head" the meanings extended to "first, primary" and then "origin". Some sources also analyze it as 一 (yī, "one") over 兀 (wù, "level").
