## industry or business; career
*sub-word of*
1. startup company
---
- **traditional:** 業
- **simplified:** 业
- **pronunciation:** yè
- **definition:** industry or business; career
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 创业公司(創業公司)
    - chuàng yè gōng sī
    - startup company
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 农业
    - nóngyè
    - agricultural sector
  - 商业
    - shāngyè
    - business and commerce
  - 行业
    - hángyè
    - industry sector
  - 事业
    - shìyè
    - career or cause
- **etymology (業):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a beam from which bells and chimes hang
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the beam used to hang bells and chimes, a concrete object associated with grand ritual displays; the sense broadened to “great undertakings” and then to “work, achievements, profession” in modern usage. Alternative views note a pictogram of a person lifting the beam, or an ideogrammic analysis combining 丵 (zhuó, "chisel") + 大 (dà, "person; big").
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%A5%AD
  - **simplification rule (业):** Modern simplification reduced the complex multi-part structure of 業 by compressing components and cutting strokes to the three-stroke 业 for ease and speed of writing.
