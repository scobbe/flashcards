## step
*sub-component of*
1. structural particle; must or have to
---
- **traditional:** 彳
- **simplified:** 彳
- **pronunciation:** chì
- **definition:** step
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 得
    - de; děi
    - structural particle; must or have to
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 彳亍
    - chì chù
    - pace to and fro
  - 彳亍而行
    - chì chù ér xíng
    - walk hesitantly
  - 彳亍独行
    - chì chù dú xíng
    - wander alone
  - 彳亍街头(彳亍街頭)
    - chì chù jiē tóu
    - loiter on the street
- **etymology (彳):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram of 行 (xíng, "crossroads") →
    - left-side component standardized as 彳 indicating "step"
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a street intersection from above, as in 行. The left portion was abstracted and standardized as 彳, carrying the sense of steps and movement. As a standalone character, it preserves the idea of slow, halting walking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 行
      - xíng
      - to go; to travel
    - 彳
      - chì
      - step
