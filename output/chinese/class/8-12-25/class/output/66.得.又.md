## hand
*sub-component of*
1. structural particle; must or have to
---
- **traditional:** 又
- **simplified:** 又
- **pronunciation:** yòu
- **definition:** hand
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 得
    - de; děi
    - structural particle; must or have to
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 又名
    - yòu míng
    - also known as
  - 又红又专(又紅又專)
    - yòu hóng yòu zhuān
    - red and expert
  - 又惊又喜(又驚又喜)
    - yòu jīng yòu xǐ
    - surprised and delighted
  - 少之又少
    - shǎo zhī yòu shǎo
    - extremely few
- **etymology (又):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of right hand
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a right hand. In later usage, the graph came to write the adverb meaning "again; also", explained as an adverbial derivation of 有 (yǒu, "to have; there is"), while the sense "right" continued separately in 右 (yòu, "right").
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
