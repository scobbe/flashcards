## server or waiter
---
- **traditional:** 服務員
- **simplified:** 服务员
- **pronunciation:** fú wù yuán
- **definition:** server or waiter
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 服务员专用通道(服务员專用通道)
    - fúwùyuán zhuānyòng tōngdào
    - staff-only passage
  - 服务员招聘
    - fúwùyuán zhāopìn
    - waiter recruitment
  - 服务员培训
    - fúwùyuán péixùn
    - staff training
  - 服务员态度
    - fúwùyuán tàidù
    - service attitude
- **etymology (服務員):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 服务(服務) (fúwù, "service") + 员(員) (yuán, "member") →
    - 服务员(服務員) (fúwùyuán, "attendant")
  - **interpretation:** 服务 provides the domain of service, while 员 denotes a person or member. Together they mean a person engaged in providing services, hence server/waiter/attendant in modern contexts.
  - **component characters:**
    - 服
      - fú
      - serve
    - 务(務)
      - wù
      - affairs
    - 员(員)
      - yuán
      - member
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%9C%8D%E5%8B%99%E5%93%A1
  - **simplification rule (服务员):** The traditional components 務 and 員 were simplified to 务 and 员 by reducing strokes and regularizing components, while 服 remained unchanged.
