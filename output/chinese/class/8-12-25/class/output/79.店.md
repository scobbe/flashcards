## shop or store
---
- **traditional:** 店
- **simplified:** 店
- **pronunciation:** diàn
- **definition:** shop or store
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 店铺
    - diàn pù
    - retail shop
  - 店员
    - diàn yuán
    - store clerk
  - 店长(店長)
    - diàn zhǎng
    - store manager
  - 店内
    - diàn nèi
    - inside the store
- **etymology (店):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phono-semantic: semantic: 广 (guǎng, "house by a cliff") + phonetic: 占 (zhàn, "occupy")
  - **interpretation:** 广 (guǎng, "house by a cliff") supplies the semantic field of a building or covered structure, which suits premises like shops. 占 (zhàn, "occupy") provides the sound, matching Old Chinese reconstructions leading through Middle Chinese temH to modern diàn. The graph thus denotes a building for occupation and commerce, yielding the modern sense "shop/store".
  - **component characters:**
    - 广
      - guǎng
      - cliff shelter
    - 占
      - zhàn
      - occupy
