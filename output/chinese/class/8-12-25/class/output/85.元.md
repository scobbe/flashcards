- **etymology (元):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a person with the head marked by 二 (èr, "two") lines over a body 儿(兒) (ér, "person") →
    - original meaning "head; beginning"
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human figure with the head emphasized by two lines, highlighting the head as the source or start. From this, the sense broadened to "first; primary" and later extended to the principal monetary unit in modern usage.
