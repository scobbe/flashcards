1. go to work

- **definition:** shift; class

- **etymology (班):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 珏 (jué, "pieces of jade") + 刀 (dāo, "knife")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 珏, representing pieces of jade, with 刀, a knife, depicting cutting jade into pieces. This concrete image led to the sense of dividing and distributing, which underlies modern uses like class/group and work shift.
  - **component characters:**
    - 珏
      - jué
      - two jades
    - 刀
      - dāo
      - knife
