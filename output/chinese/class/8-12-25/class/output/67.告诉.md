## tell; inform
---
- **traditional:** 告訴
- **simplified:** 告诉
- **pronunciation:** gào su
- **definition:** tell; inform
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 告诉他(告訴他)
    - gào sù tā
    - tell him
  - 告诉我(告訴我)
    - gào sù wǒ
    - tell me
  - 告诉大家(告訴大家)
    - gào sù dà jiā
    - tell everyone
  - 告诉老师(告訴老师)
    - gào sù lǎo shī
    - tell the teacher
- **etymology (告訴):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 告 (gào, "inform") + 诉(訴) (sù, "complain")
  - **interpretation:** Combining 告 "inform" with 诉 "complain/plead" yields the sense of conveying something to someone, hence "to tell/inform" in everyday use. In legal contexts, the 诉 component motivates the formal sense "to file a complaint" that persists in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 告
      - gào
      - to tell; to inform
    - 诉(訴)
      - sù
      - to sue; to appeal
    - 诉(訴)
      - sù
      - to sue; to appeal
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%91%8A%E8%A8%B4
  - **simplification rule (告诉):** The compound was simplified by replacing 訴 with 诉 via reducing the speech radical 言 to 讠 and streamlining strokes; 告 remained unchanged, regularizing variant forms.
