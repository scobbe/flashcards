## younger brother
*sub-component of*
1. ordinal prefix; sequence
---
- **traditional:** 弟
- **simplified:** 弟
- **pronunciation:** dì
- **definition:** younger brother
- **breadcrumbs:**

    - dì
    - ordinal prefix; sequence
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 弟弟
    - dì dì
    - younger brother
  - 堂弟

  - 内弟
    - nèi dì

  - 兄弟
    - xiōng dì
    - brothers
- **etymology (弟):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of the handle of 弋 (yì, "shoot") wrapped with a strap →
    - semantic: order/sequence →
    - semantic extension: younger brother
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a spear handle wrapped in a leather strap. The tied strap suggested arrangement or orderly sequence, which then extended to the kinship sense ‘younger brother’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 弋
      - yì
      - to shoot
