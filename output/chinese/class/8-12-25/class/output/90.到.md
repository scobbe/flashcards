## to; arrive
---
- **traditional:** 到
- **simplified:** 到
- **pronunciation:** dào
- **definition:** to; arrive
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 从九点到十八点
    - cóng jiǔ diǎn dào shí bā diǎn
    - from 9 to 6
  - 找到
    - zhǎo dào
    - to find
  - 做到
    - zuò dào
    - achieve it
  - 听到(聽到)
    - tīng dào
    - to hear
- **etymology (到):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 至 (zhì, "to arrive") + phonetic: 刀 (dāo, "knife")
  - **interpretation:** 至 supplies the core meaning of arriving or reaching, which matches the modern senses of motion and attainment. 刀 functions as the phonetic, giving the reading dào, while the semantic part anchors the character’s domain of meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 至
      - zhì
      - arrive
    - 刀
      - dāo
      - knife
