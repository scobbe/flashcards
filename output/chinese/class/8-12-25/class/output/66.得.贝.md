## shell; cowry

- **traditional:** 貝
- **simplified:** 贝
- **pronunciation:** bèi
- **definition:** shell; cowry
- **breadcrumbs:**

- **etymology (貝):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a cowry shell used as money
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cowry shell. Because cowries were used as currency in ancient China, the graph came to mean money/currency and now functions as a semantic radical for value and trade. The simplified form 贝 reflects a cursive-derived reduction of 貝.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%B2%9D
  - **simplification rule (贝):** Adopted the cursive-derived form of 貝 with stroke reduction and graphic regularization, producing the simpler 贝.
