## one
*sub-word of*
1. same or identical
---
- **traditional:** 一
- **simplified:** 一
- **pronunciation:** yī
- **definition:** one
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 一样(一樣)
    - yí yàng
    - same or identical
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一个(一個)
    - yí gè
    - a single
  - 一些
    - yì xiē
    - a few
  - 一起
    - yì qǐ
    - together with
  - 一定
    - yí dìng
    - for sure
- **etymology (一):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - ideogram: horizontal stroke →
    - 一 (yī, "one")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a single horizontal stroke used to tally one. The form directly denotes the numeral and has remained essentially unchanged, later serving as the radical for 'one' in compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
