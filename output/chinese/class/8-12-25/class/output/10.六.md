## six
*sub-word of*
1. Saturday
---
- **traditional:** 六
- **simplified:** 六
- **pronunciation:** liù
- **definition:** six
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 星期六
    - xīng qī liù
    - Saturday
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 六月
    - liù yuè
    - June
  - 六十
    - liù shí
    - sixty
  - 六边形(六邊形)
    - liù biān xíng
    - hexagon
  - 六年级(六年級)
    - liù nián jí
    - sixth grade
- **etymology (六):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram of 庐(廬) (lú, "hut")
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a hut or shed. Later, by phonetic loan from the similar-sounding 庐(廬) (lú, "hut"), it came to denote liù (six). Alternatively, some interpret it as an ancient counting symbol.
  - **component characters:**
    - 庐(廬)
      - lú
      - hut
    - 廬
      - lú
      - cottage
