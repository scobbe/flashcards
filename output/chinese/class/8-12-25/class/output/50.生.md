## life; person
*sub-word of*
1. doctor or physician
---
- **traditional:** 生
- **simplified:** 生
- **pronunciation:** shēng
- **definition:** life; person
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 医生(醫生)
    - yī shēng
    - doctor or physician
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 生活
    - shēnghuó
    - daily life
  - 生命
    - shēngmìng
    - human life
  - 生气
    - shēngqì
    - get angry
  - 生产
    - shēngchǎn
    - to produce
- **etymology (生):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 屮 (chè, "bud") + 一 (yī, "ground") →
    - 生 (shēng, "sprout")
  - **interpretation:** The graph combines 屮 ‘bud’ above 一 ‘ground’ to depict a sprout emerging from the soil, conveying growth and arising. This concrete image broadened to meanings like to be born, to grow, and by extension ‘life’ in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 屮
      - chè
      - bud
    - 一
      - yī
      - ground
