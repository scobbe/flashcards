- **etymology (星):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 晶 (jīng, "twinkling stars") + phonetic: 生 (shēng, "life") →
    - semantic: 日 (rì, "sun") + phonetic: 生 (shēng, "life")
  - **interpretation:** Originally the character combined the semantic component 晶, evoking the sparkle of stars, with the phonetic 生 to indicate the sound. Later the top component was simplified from 晶 to 日, while 生 remained the phonetic, keeping the sense within the celestial/brightness domain and yielding the modern meaning ‘star’.
