- **etymology (堡):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 保 (bǎo, "protect") + semantic: 土 (tǔ, "earth") → 堡 (bǎo, "fort").
  - **interpretation:** 保 provides the sound, pointing to a bǎo-like reading. 土 is the semantic determinative for earth/soil, suiting earthen defensive works. Together they denote an earthen fortification, underpinning the modern senses of fort and stronghold.
  - **component characters:**
    - 保
    - 土
    - 堡
