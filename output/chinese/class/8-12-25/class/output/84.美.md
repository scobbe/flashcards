## America; beautiful
### sub-word
#### U.S. dollar
---
- **traditional:** 美
- **simplified:** 美
- **pronunciation:** měi
- **definition:** America; beautiful
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 美国(國) (měi guó, "United States")
  - 中美关(關)系(係) (zhōng měi guān xì, "Sino-American relations")
  - 美食 (měi shí, "gourmet food")
  - 美容 (měi róng, "beauty treatment")
- **etymology (美):**
  - **type:** pictogram → ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 大 (dà, "person") wearing a headdress of 羊 (yáng, "ram") → ideogrammic: 羊 (yáng, "ram") + 大 (dà, "person").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person adorned with a horn or feather headdress, conveying adornment and attractiveness. In the later ideogrammic analysis, 羊 marks ornamentation while 大 indicates a person, yielding the sense of “beautiful” that extends to positive aesthetic judgment.
  - **component characters:**
    - 大
    - 羊
