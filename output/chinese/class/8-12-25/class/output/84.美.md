- **etymology (美):**
  - **type:** pictogram → ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of 大 (dà, "person") wearing a headdress of 羊 (yáng, "ram") →
    - ideogrammic: 羊 (yáng, "ram") + 大 (dà, "person")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person adorned with a horn or feather headdress, conveying adornment and attractiveness. In the later ideogrammic analysis, 羊 marks ornamentation while 大 indicates a person, yielding the sense of “beautiful” that extends to positive aesthetic judgment.
  - **component characters:**
    - 大
      - dà
      - big
    - 羊
      - yáng
      - sheep
