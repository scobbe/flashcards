- **etymology (旅):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 㫃 (qí, "flag") + 从(從) (cóng, "two people") →
    - 旅 (lǚ, "brigade")
  - **interpretation:** A flag together with two people depicts a group under a banner, yielding the sense of a military brigade. From the idea of organized bodies on the move, the word extended to people traveling and related notions in modern usage.
