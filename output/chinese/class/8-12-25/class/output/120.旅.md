## travel; journey
### sub-word
#### travel; trip
---
- **traditional:** 旅
- **simplified:** 旅
- **pronunciation:** lǚ
- **definition:** travel; journey
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 旅行 (lǚxíng, "to travel")
  - 旅游 (lǚyóu, "to go sightseeing")
  - 旅客 (lǚkè, "traveling passenger")
  - 旅馆(館) (lǚguǎn, "hotel or inn")
- **etymology (旅):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 㫃 (qí, "flag") + 从(從) (cóng, "two people") → 旅 (lǚ, "brigade").
  - **interpretation:** A flag together with two people depicts a group under a banner, yielding the sense of a military brigade. From the idea of organized bodies on the move, the word extended to people traveling and related notions in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 㫃
    - 从
    - 從
    - 旅
