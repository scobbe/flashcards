## period or term; to expect
*sub-word of*
1. Saturday
---
- **traditional:** 期
- **simplified:** 期
- **pronunciation:** qī
- **definition:** period or term; to expect
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 星期六
    - xīng qī liù
    - Saturday
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 期待
    - qī dài
    - look forward to
  - 限期
    - xiàn qī
    - deadline
  - 学期(學期)
    - xué qī
    - semester
  - 任期
    - rèn qī
    - term of office
- **etymology (期):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 其 (qí, "that") + semantic: 月 (yuè, "moon") →
    - 期
  - **interpretation:** The character uses 其 (qí, "that") as the phonetic and 月 (yuè, "moon") as the semantic, with the waxing and waning moon marking the passage of time to yield the sense "period/term." Some ancient forms used 日 (rì, "sun") instead of 月, but the timekeeping idea remained, leading to the modern meanings.
  - **component characters:**
    - 其
      - qí
      - that
    - 月
      - yuè
      - moon
