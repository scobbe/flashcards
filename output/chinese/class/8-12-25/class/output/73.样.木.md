- **etymology (木):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

  - **interpretation:** It depicts a tree. The concrete sense extended to materials from trees (wood, timber) and, by metaphor in compounds, to ‘wooden’ or ‘numb’, aligning with its role as the semantic determinative for wood-related terms.
