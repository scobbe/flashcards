- **etymology (小):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of three granules of sand →.
    - small
  - **interpretation:** Depicts three granules of sand, a concrete image of tiny particles, which generalized to the abstract sense ‘small’. Some sources instead treat it as an ideogram of three small dots, and Shuowen later reinterpreted elongated forms as an ideogrammic compound; these are secondary analyses.
