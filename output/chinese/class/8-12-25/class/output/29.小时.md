## hour
---
- **traditional:** 小時
- **simplified:** 小时
- **pronunciation:** xiǎo shí
- **definition:** hour
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 小时工(小時工)
    - xiǎo shí gōng
    - hourly worker
  - 小时制(小時制)
    - xiǎo shí zhì
    - hourly system
  - 小时候(小時候)
    - xiǎo shí hòu
    - in childhood
  - 小时数(小時数)
    - xiǎo shí shù
    - number of hours
- **etymology (小時):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 小 (xiǎo, "small") + 时(時) (shí, "time")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 小 “small” and 时 “time” to mean a small unit of time. This yields the modern sense “hour,” a standard short division of time.
  - **component characters:**
    - 小
      - xiǎo
      - small; little
    - 时(時)
      - shí
      - time; hour
    - 时(時)
      - shí
      - time; hour
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B0%8F%E6%99%82
  - **simplification rule (小时):** 時 was simplified to 时 by reducing strokes and collapsing the right-side phonetic; 小 remained unchanged.
