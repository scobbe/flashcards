## travel; trip
---
- **traditional:** 旅行
- **simplified:** 旅行
- **pronunciation:** lǚ xíng
- **definition:** travel; trip
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 旅行团
    - lǚxíngtuán
    - tour group
  - 旅行社
    - lǚxíngshè
    - travel agency
  - 旅行支票
    - lǚxíng zhīpiào
    - traveler's check
  - 旅行车(旅行車)
    - lǚxíngchē
    - station wagon
- **etymology (旅行):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 旅 (lǚ, "travel") + 行 (xíng, "go")
  - **interpretation:** 旅 contributes the idea of traveling, while 行 indicates going or moving. Together they form a transparent compound meaning to travel or a trip in general.
  - **component characters:**
    - 旅
      - lǚ
      - travel; journey
    - 行
      - xíng
      - to go; to travel
