## work; worker
*sub-word of*
1. engineer
---
- **traditional:** 工
- **simplified:** 工
- **pronunciation:** gōng
- **definition:** work; worker
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 工程师(工程師)
    - gōng chéng shī
    - engineer
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 工作
    - gōngzuò
    - work or job
  - 工地
    - gōngdì
    - construction site
  - 工农
    - gōngnóng
    - workers and peasants
  - 罢工
    - bàgōng
    - go on strike
- **etymology (工):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a bladed tool used to plow soil and mark borders; alternatively a carpenter's square
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a concrete tool associated with manual labor, which naturally extended to mean artisanship and thus ‘work’ and ‘worker’ in modern usage. An alternative sees it as a carpenter’s square, another tool of craft, leading to the same semantic domain.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
