## to see
---
- **traditional:** 見
- **simplified:** 见
- **pronunciation:** jiàn
- **definition:** to see
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 新
    - xīn
    - new
  - 亲(親)
    - qīn / qìng
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 见谅(見谅)
    - jiàn liàng
    - please forgive
  - 见光(見光)
    - jiàn guāng
    - exposed to light
  - 见解(見解)
    - jiàn jiě
    - opinion or view
  - 见得(見得)
    - jiàn dé
    - to seem
- **etymology (見):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 目 (mù, "eye") + 卩 (jié, "kneeling person")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 目 “eye” with 卩 “kneeling person” to depict a figure with a prominent eye, evoking the act of seeing. The meaning generalized to “to see,” and extended to senses like “to meet” and “opinion” in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 目
      - mù
      - eye
    - 卩
      - jié
      - seal radical
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%A6%8B
  - **simplification rule (见):** Adopted the cursive-script form of 見, collapsing its parts into the compact ⻅ shape to reduce strokes.
