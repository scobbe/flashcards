## school
---
- **traditional:** 學校
- **simplified:** 学校
- **pronunciation:** xué xiào
- **definition:** school
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 学(學)校老师(師) (xuéxiào lǎoshī, "school teacher")
  - 学(學)校生活 (xuéxiào shēnghuó, "school life")
  - 学(學)校教育 (xuéxiào jiàoyù, "school education")
  - 学(學)校门(門)口 (xuéxiào ménkǒu, "school entrance")
- **etymology (學校):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 学(學) (xué, "learn; study") + 校 (xiào, "school") → 学校.
  - **interpretation:** 学 conveys learning or study, while 校 in this collocation denotes the institution. Together they mean an institution for learning, hence ‘school’, which matches modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 学
    - 學
    - 校
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AD%B8%E6%A0%A1
  - **simplification rule (学校):** 学 was simplified from 學 by reducing and merging components; 校 remained unchanged.
