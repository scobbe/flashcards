## school
---
- **traditional:** 學校
- **simplified:** 学校
- **pronunciation:** xué xiào
- **definition:** school
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 学校老师(學校老师)
    - xuéxiào lǎoshī
    - school teacher
  - 学校生活(學校生活)
    - xuéxiào shēnghuó
    - school life
  - 学校教育(學校教育)
    - xuéxiào jiàoyù
    - school education
  - 学校门口(學校門口)
    - xuéxiào ménkǒu
    - school entrance
- **etymology (學校):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 学(學) (xué, "learn; study") + 校 (xiào, "school") →
    - 学校
  - **interpretation:** 学 conveys learning or study, while 校 in this collocation denotes the institution. Together they mean an institution for learning, hence ‘school’, which matches modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 学(學)
      - xué
      - to study; learning
    - 学(學)
      - xué
      - to study; learning
    - 校
      - xiào
      - school
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%AD%B8%E6%A0%A1
  - **simplification rule (学校):** 学 was simplified from 學 by reducing and merging components; 校 remained unchanged.
