## same or identical
---
- **traditional:** 一樣
- **simplified:** 一样
- **pronunciation:** yí yàng
- **definition:** same or identical
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一样的(一樣的)
    - yī yàng de
    - the same
  - 一样地(一樣地)
    - yī yàng de
    - in the same way
  - 一样吗(一樣嗎)
    - yī yàng ma
    - the same
  - 一样重要(一樣重要)
    - yī yàng zhòng yào
    - equally important
- **etymology (一樣):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 一 (yī, "one") + 样(樣) (yàng, "manner")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 一 “one” with 样 “manner/appearance” to indicate sharing a single manner, hence “the same” and the adverbial sense “equally.” The semantic idea persists in modern usage to mark identity of kind or equal degree.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
    - 样(樣)
      - yàng
      - kind; appearance
    - 样(樣)
      - yàng
      - kind; appearance
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E4%B8%80%E6%A8%A3
  - **simplification rule (一样):** The simplified form uses 样 for 樣 by replacing the complex right-side component with simpler 羊-like structure and reducing strokes, while 一 remains unchanged.
