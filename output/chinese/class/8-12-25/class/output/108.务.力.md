## strength
*sub-component of*
1. affairs or duties; to engage in
---
- **traditional:** 力
- **simplified:** 力
- **pronunciation:** lì
- **definition:** strength
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 务(務)
    - wù
    - affairs or duties; to engage in
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 力量
    - lì liàng
    - strength
  - 力气
    - lì qì
    - physical strength
  - 力争
    - lì zhēng
    - strive for
  - 攻击力
    - gōng jī lì
    - firepower
- **etymology (力):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a bent arm exerting strength
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a bent arm exerting strength. By association with muscular effort it came to mean strength, force, and by extension efficacy and capability in modern compounds. Some sources alternatively interpret the figure as a wooden plough.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
