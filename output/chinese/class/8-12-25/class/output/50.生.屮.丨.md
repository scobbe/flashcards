- **etymology (丨):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a vertical wooden stick
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a vertical wooden stick. This concrete image naturally gave the sense of a straight vertical stroke in writing and by extension a rod; the internet slang use is a homophonic substitution for gǔn and is unrelated to the original pictorial meaning.
