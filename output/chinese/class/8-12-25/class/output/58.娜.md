    - nǚ

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

    - used as a feminine name component/
  - **interpretation:** 娜(娜) (nà, "a name") combines 女(女) to indicate a female name or noun, and phonetic 那(那) to provide the pronunciation.
