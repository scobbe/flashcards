## down; to finish
*sub-word of*
1. afternoon
---
- **traditional:** 下
- **simplified:** 下
- **pronunciation:** xià
- **definition:** down; to finish
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 下午
    - xià wǔ
    - afternoon
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 下层
    - xià céng
    - lower level
  - 以下
    - yǐ xià
    - the following
  - 地下
    - dì xià
    - underground
  - 天下
    - tiān xià
    - the world
- **etymology (下):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - ideogram: 一 (yī, "one") above + short stroke below indicating position →
    - below
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a horizontal line with a shorter stroke placed beneath, indicating something positioned below another. The concrete sense ‘below’ broadened to sequence/time (‘next’) and to verbs meaning to bring an activity to an end (‘to finish’), aligning with moving down or to a subsequent stage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
