## to connect; to introduce

1. introduce; introduction
---
- **traditional:** 紹
- **simplified:** 绍
- **pronunciation:** shào
- **definition:** to connect; to introduce
- **breadcrumbs:**

- **etymology (紹):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phono-semantic: semantic: 纟(糹) (sī, "silk") + phonetic: 召 (zhào, "summon")
  - **interpretation:** 纟(糹) signals the semantic field of threads and continuity, matching senses like continuing or linking introductions. 召 supplies the sound shào and also evokes summoning/calling, compatible with introducing someone. Together they convey connection/continuation via the silk radical’s “thread” metaphor.
  - **component characters:**
    - 糸
      - mì
      - silk radical
    - 糹
      - mì
      - silk radical
    - 召
      - zhào
      - summon
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%B4%B9
  - **simplification rule (绍):** The left silk component was simplified from 糹 to 纟, a standard radical simplification reducing strokes while retaining the phonetic 召.
