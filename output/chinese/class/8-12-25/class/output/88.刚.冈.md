## ridge; mound
*sub-component of*
1. just; firm
---
- **traditional:** 岡
- **simplified:** 冈
- **pronunciation:** gāng / gǎng
- **definition:** ridge; mound
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 刚(剛)
    - gāng
    - just; firm
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 冈比亚
    - gāng bǐ yà
    - Gambia
  - 冈陵
    - gāng líng
    - ridges and hills
  - 梵蒂冈
    - fàn dì gāng
    - Vatican City
  - 云冈石窟(雲冈石窟)
    - yún gāng shí kū
    - Yungang Grottoes
- **etymology (岡):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic: 网(網) (wǎng, "net") + semantic: 山 (shān, "mountain")
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 山 as the semantic determinative for mountains with 网 as the phonetic element indicating the sound near gāng. The mountain determinative anchors the meaning to terrain features, yielding the sense of a mountain ridge or crest.
  - **component characters:**
    - 网(網)
      - wǎng
      - net
    - 網
      - wǎng
      - net
    - 山
      - shān
      - mountain
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B2%A1
  - **simplification rule (冈):** 岡 was simplified to 冈 by replacing the complex net-and-mountain structure with a reduced form using 冂 and 㐅, reducing strokes through component replacement.
