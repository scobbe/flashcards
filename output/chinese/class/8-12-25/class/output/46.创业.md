## to start a business
*sub-word of*
1. startup company
---
- **traditional:** 創業
- **simplified:** 创业
- **pronunciation:** chuàngyè
- **definition:** to start a business
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 创业公司(創業公司)
    - chuàng yè gōng sī
    - startup company
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 创业精神
    - chuàng yè jīng shén
    - enterprising spirit
  - 创业者
    - chuàng yè zhě
    - entrepreneur
  - 创业投资(创业投資)
    - chuàng yè tóu zī
    - venture capital
  - 创业板(创业闆)
    - chuàng yè bǎn
    - growth enterprise board
- **etymology (創業):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 创(創) (chuàng, "begin") + 业(業) (yè, "business")
  - **interpretation:** 创 means to initiate or begin, and 业 means enterprise or business. Combined, they denote initiating an enterprise, which in modern usage is starting a business and engaging in entrepreneurship.
  - **component characters:**
    - 创(創)
      - chuàng; chuāng
      - to create or found; wound or cut
    - 创(創)
      - chuàng; chuāng
      - to create or found; wound or cut
    - 业(業)
      - yè
      - industry or business; career
    - 业(業)
      - yè
      - industry or business; career
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%89%B5%E6%A5%AD
  - **simplification rule (创业):** Component replacement and stroke reduction: 創 was simplified to 创 and 業 to 业.
