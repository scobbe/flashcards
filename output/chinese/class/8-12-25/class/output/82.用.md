## to use
*sub-word of*
1. need not or don't have to
---
- **traditional:** 用
- **simplified:** 用
- **pronunciation:** yòng
- **definition:** to use
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 不用
    - bú yòng
    - need not or don't have to
- **contemporary usage:**

    - end user

  - 用途
    - yòngtú

- **etymology (用):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of 桶 (tǒng, "bucket")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a water bucket. As a common implement for carrying and doing tasks, the graph generalized to the verbal sense of employing or using something.
  - **component characters:**
    - 桶
      - tǒng
      - barrel
