## spicy

- **definition:** spicy

- **etymology (辛):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a chisel or awl used to mark slaves and criminals
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a marking tool (chisel/awl) used on slaves and criminals. The sharp, piercing tool motivated the sense of pungent or spicy taste and, by extension, hardship and suffering. Some scholars also connect it with a root meaning “bitter,” reinforcing the taste association.
