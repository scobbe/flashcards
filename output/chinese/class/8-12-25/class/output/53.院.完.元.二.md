- **etymology (二):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - ideogram: 一 (yī, "one") + 一 (yī, "one") →
    - 二 (èr, "two")
  - **interpretation:** The character doubles 一 ("one"), forming two parallel strokes to indicate the quantity two. As an ideogram, its form transparently maps to the modern meaning of the numeral two.
