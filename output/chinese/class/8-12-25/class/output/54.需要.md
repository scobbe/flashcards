## need or require
---
- **traditional:** 需要
- **simplified:** 需要
- **pronunciation:** xū yào
- **definition:** need or require
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 需要帮助
    - xūyào bāngzhù
    - need help
  - 需要数分钟(需要数分鐘)
    - xūyào shù fēnzhōng
    - require several minutes
  - 需要口译员
    - xūyào kǒuyìyuán
    - need an interpreter
  - 需要品
    - xūyào pǐn
    - required items
- **etymology (需要):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 需 (xū, "need") + 要 (yào, "vital; essential") →
    - 需要 (xūyào, "need; require")
  - **interpretation:** Formed by combining 需 ‘to need’ and 要 ‘to want; essential’, yielding the sense ‘to need; requirement’. Both morphemes contribute meaning rather than sound, so the compound transparently denotes necessity in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 需
      - xū
      - to need
    - 要
      - yào
      - to want; essential
