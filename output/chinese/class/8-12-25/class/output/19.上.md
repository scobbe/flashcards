## up; to go; previous
*sub-word of*
1. morning
---
- **traditional:** 上
- **simplified:** 上
- **pronunciation:** shàng
- **definition:** up; to go; previous
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 早上
    - zǎo shang
    - morning
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 上世纪(上世紀)
    - shàng shìjì
    - last century
  - 上集
    - shàng jí
    - previous episode
  - 上班
    - shàng bān
    - go to work
  - 上学(上學)
    - shàng xué
    - go to school
- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - ideogram: short stroke above 一 (yī, "one") indicating the upper position →
    - 上 (shàng, "up")
  - **interpretation:** The character shows a short stroke placed above a horizontal line to depict the idea of something being above. This spatial relation extended to locative uses like "on/above" and further to temporal or ordinal senses such as "previous" and "first" in sequences.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
      - yī
      - one
    - 上
      - shàng
      - up; to go; previous
