### fànguǎn
---
- **definition:** restaurant
- **pinyin:** fànguǎn
- **components:**
  - 饭(飯)
    - fàn
    - meal; cooked rice
  - 馆(館)
    - guǎn

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal; cooked rice") + 馆(館) (guǎn, "building") =
    - a place where meals are served ->
    - restaurant

    - Little Li dreams of one day owning his own restaurant.
