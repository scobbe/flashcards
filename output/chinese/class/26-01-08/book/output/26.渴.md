## thirsty
### kě
---
- **definition:** thirsty
- **pinyin:** kě
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 曷
    - hé
    - why, what
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 曷 (hé) ->
    - meaning: dry, exhausted (of water)
  - **interpretation:** The character 渴(kě, "thirsty") combines the semantic component 氵(shuǐ, "water"), essential for life and quenching thirst, with the phonetic component 曷(hé), which indicates the sound. The concept of being dry or exhausted due to a lack of water leads to the meaning of 'thirst'.
- **examples:**
  - 他很渴，所以喝了一大瓶水。
    - Tā hěn kě, suǒyǐ hēle yī dà píng shuǐ.
    - He was very thirsty, so he drank a big bottle of water.
  - 运(運)动(動)后(後)，我( )总(總)是( )感( )到( )渴( )。
    - Yùndòng hòu, wǒ zǒng shì gǎndào kě.
    - After exercising, I always feel thirsty.
  - 她渴了，需( )要( )喝( )点(點)水( )。
    - Tā kě le, xūyào hē diǎn shuǐ.
    - She is thirsty and needs to drink some water.

### 渴 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
