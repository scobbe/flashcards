## to help; to give assistance
### bāngmáng
---
- **traditional:** 幫忙
- **definition:** to help; to give assistance
- **pinyin:** bāngmáng
- **components:**
  - 幫
    - bāng
    - help; assist
  - 忙
    - máng
    - busy; hurry; be busy with
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 幫 (bāng, "help") + 忙 (máng, "busy") =
    - to help; to give assistance
  - **interpretation:** The word 幫忙 (bāngmáng, "to help; to give assistance") combines 幫 (bāng, "help") and 忙 (máng, "busy"). The term suggests the act of assisting someone who is busy, thus providing them with relief or support.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)你帮(幫)忙。
    - Qǐng nǐ bāngmáng.
    - Please help.
  - 我正在忙碌，无(無)法帮(幫)忙。
    - Wǒ zhèngzài mánglù, wúfǎ bāngmáng.
    - I am busy and cannot help.
  - 小明常常帮(幫)忙，他很受大家喜欢(歡)。
    - Xiǎomíng chángcháng bāngmáng, tā hěn shòu dàjiā xǐhuān.
    - Xiaoming often helps and is liked by everyone.
