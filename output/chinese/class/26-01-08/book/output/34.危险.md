## dangerous
### wēixiǎn
---
- **traditional:** 危險
- **definition:** dangerous
- **pinyin:** wēixiǎn
- **components:**
  - 危
    - wēi
    - dangerous; perilous; critical
  - 险(險)
    - xiǎn
    - perilous; risk; danger; hazard
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 危 (wēi, "dangerous") + 險 (xiǎn, "perilous; risk") =
    - the state of being prone to danger ->
    - dangerous
  - **interpretation:** 危 (wēi, "dangerous") conveys the idea of something unstable or at risk of tipping over, as seen at a cliff's edge. This visual of instability directly leads to the sense of danger. 險 (xiǎn, "perilous; risk") suggests a path or situation fraught with risk and potential for injury, such as a treacherous mountain trail. Combined, these characters evoke a sense of exposure to significant harm or risk, hence forming the concept of something being "dangerous."
- **examples:**
  - 骑(騎)车(車)没(沒)有戴头(頭)盔很危险(險)。
    - Qí chē méiyǒu dài tóukuī hěn wēixiǎn.
    - Riding a bike without a helmet is very dangerous.
  - 这(這)个(個)地区(區)晚上走路很危险(險)。
    - Zhège dìqū wǎnshàng zǒulù hěn wēixiǎn.
    - It's dangerous to walk in this area at night.
  - 他总(總)是做一些危险(險)的事情。
    - Tā zǒng shì zuò yīxiē wēixiǎn de shìqíng.
    - He always does some dangerous things.

### 危险(險) → 危 → 懸
- **definition:** hanging; suspended
- **pinyin:** xuán
- **components:**
  - 縣
    - xuán
    - county
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 縣 (xuán, "county") + semantic 心 (xīn, "heart") ->
    - meaning derived from phonetic component
  - **interpretation:** The character 懸(xuán, "hang") combines the phonetic element 縣 (xuán, "county") with the semantic element 心 (xīn, "heart"). Originally, 縣 conveyed the full meaning, but 心 was added to disambiguate its meaning towards 'hang' or 'suspend' as the character evolved. Thus, 懸 signifies a sense of something hanging or being suspended metaphorically close to one's 'heart'.
  - **simplification:** Simplified as 悬: the 心 (心) (xīn, "heart") was removed for simplicity, while retaining the phonetic portion.
- **examples:**
