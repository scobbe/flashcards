## Guangzhou
### Guǎngzhōu
---
- **traditional:** 廣州
- **definition:** Guangzhou
- **pinyin:** Guǎngzhōu
- **components:**
  - 广(廣)
    - guǎng
    - broad; vast; wide
  - 州
    - zhōu
    - prefecture; administrative division; state; region
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 廣 (guǎng, "broad") refers to a vast area; 州 (zhōu, "prefecture") indicates a region or administrative division.
  - **interpretation:** 廣 (guǎng, "broad") highlights the geographic expansiveness or prominent status of the region. 州 (zhōu, "prefecture") reflects its historical and administrative significance as an important regional center. Together, 廣 (guǎng, "broad") + 州 (zhōu, "prefecture") denotes a large and significant administrative area, which is true for Guangzhou as a major city in southern China.
