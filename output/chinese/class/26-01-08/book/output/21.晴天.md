## sunny day; clear sky
### qíngtiān
---
- **definition:** sunny day; clear sky
- **pinyin:** qíngtiān
- **components:**
  - 晴
    - qíng
    - clear; fine (weather)
  - 天
    - tiān
    - sky; day
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晴 (qíng, "clear; fine (weather)") combines with 天 (tiān, "sky; day") to describe a day when the sky is clear and bright, free from clouds, resulting in a 'sunny day' or 'clear sky'.
  - **interpretation:** 晴 (qíng, "clear; fine (weather)") represents clarity and brightness in the weather, indicating no clouds or rain, which naturally results in 天 (tiān, "sky; day") presenting clear visibility. Therefore, 晴天 (qíngtiān) describes a day with a clear sky, usually under sunny conditions.
- **examples:**
  - 今天是晴天。
    - Jīntiān shì qíngtiān.
    - Today is a sunny day.
  - 我们(們)在晴天去公园(園)散步。
    - Wǒmen zài qíngtiān qù gōngyuán sànbù.
    - We go for a walk in the park on sunny days.
  - 晴天时(時)，我喜欢(歡)骑(騎)自行车(車)。
    - Qíngtiān shí, wǒ xǐhuān qí zìxíngchē.
    - I like to ride a bicycle on sunny days.
### 晴天 → 晴
- **definition:** clear; fine (weather)
- **pinyin:** qíng
- **components:**
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 青
    - qīng
    - green; blue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 日 (rì, "sun") + phonetic 青 (qīng) ->
    - clear weather
  - **interpretation:** The sun 日 (rì, "sun") represents the element of daytime or light, and 青 (qīng) provides the phonetic sound. Together, they form 晴, indicating clear or fine weather, as sunlight naturally suggests clarity and brightness in the sky.
- **examples:**
  - 今天的天气(氣)很晴。
    - Jīntiān de tiānqì hěn qíng.
    - Today's weather is very clear.
  - 早上的天空是晴的，真美丽(麗)。
    - Zǎoshang de tiānkōng shì qíng de, zhēn měilì.
    - The morning sky is clear, truly beautiful.
  - 雨后(後)总(總)是会(會)放晴。
    - Yǔ hòu zǒng shì huì fàng qíng.
    - It always clears up after the rain.
### 晴天 → 晴 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒(灑)满(滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.

### 晴天 → 晴 → 青
- **definition:** green; blue
- **pinyin:** qīng
- **components:**
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life
  - 井
    - jǐng
    - well
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 生 (shēng, "life") + phonetic 井 (jǐng, "well") ->
    - final form
  - **interpretation:** The character 青(qīng, "green") originally combines the phonetic components 生(shēng, "life") and 井(jǐng, "well") for their sound values. The ancient interpretation associated 青(qīng) with the color of growing plants, possibly influenced by the idea of cinnabar for dyeing, reflecting a bluish-green shade common in nature. This phonetic pairing with 生 and 井 suggests a pronunciation that fits the word's meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 树(樹)叶(葉)是青色的。
    - Shùyè shì qīngsè de.
    - The leaves are green.
  - 青山环(環)绕(繞)着(著)村庄(莊)。
    - Qīngshān huánrào zhe cūnzhuāng.
    - Green hills surround the village.
  - 她有一双(雙)青色的眼睛。
    - Tā yǒu yī shuāng qīngsè de yǎnjīng.
    - She has a pair of green eyes.

### 晴天 → 天
- **definition:** sky; day
- **pinyin:** tiān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Horizontal line above 大(dà, "big") to depict sky ->
    - anthropomorphic man with large head symbolizing heaven
  - **interpretation:** The character 天(tiān) originally depicts the sky as a horizontal line above a person with outstretched arms, 大(dà, "big"), emphasizing the vastness or domain of heaven. Over time, the character evolved into an anthropomorphic representation of heaven, which later influenced its interpretation in classical texts.
- **examples:**
  - 夜幕降临(臨)，天边(邊)星光闪(閃)烁(爍)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín, tiānbiān xīnguāng shǎnshuò.
    - As night falls, starlight gleams on the horizon.
  - 他仰望高空，祈求天佑众(眾)生。
    - Tā yǎngwàng gāokōng, qíqiú tiānyòu zhòngshēng.
    - He gazes up to the sky and prays for heaven's protection over all living beings.
