## spring
### chūntiān
---
- **definition:** spring
- **pinyin:** chūntiān
- **components:**
  - 春
    - chūn
    - spring
  - 天
    - tiān
    - sky; day
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 春 (chūn, "spring") refers to the season following winter, characterized by new growth and renewal. 天 (tiān, "sky; day") can mean the sky or represent the broader context of a day, often symbolizing an overarching period of time.
  - **interpretation:** 春 (chūn, "spring") is the season when nature revitalizes after winter, with a burst of life and growth. 天 (tiān, "day") relates to the overall span or time frame during which these transformations occur. Together, 春天 (chūntiān, "spring") encapsulates the notion of the spring season as a period marked by rejuvenation and renewal, governed by the conditions and influences of both earth and sky.
- **examples:**
  - 春天到来(來)时(時)，鸟(鳥)儿(兒)开(開)始歌唱。
    - Chūntiān dàolái shí, niǎoér kāishǐ gēchàng.
    - When spring arrives, the birds begin to sing.
  - 春天是个(個)美丽(麗)的季节(節)。
    - Chūntiān shì gè měilì de jìjié.
    - Spring is a beautiful season.
  - 在春天，花儿(兒)绽(綻)放出新芽。
    - Zài chūntiān, huāér zhànfàng chū xīnyá.
    - In spring, flowers bloom with new buds.
### 春天 → 春
- **definition:** spring
- **pinyin:** chūn
- **components:**
  - ⾠(艸)
    - cǎo
    - grass
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 屯
    - zhūn
    - sprout
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - ⾠(艸) (cǎo, "grass") + 日 (rì, "sun") + 屯 (zhūn, "sprout") =
    - sun shining on grass with sprouts ->
    - spring
  - **interpretation:** The character 春 (chūn, "spring") involves elements representing natural growth and revival: grass, sun, and sprouts together illustrate the vibrant growth occurring in springtime.
- **examples:**
  - 春天来(來)了。
    - Chūntiān láile.
    - Spring has arrived.
  - 春天来(來)了，万(萬)物复(復)苏(蘇)。
    - Chūntiān láile, wànwù fùsū.
    - Spring has arrived, and all things are reviving.
  - 春光明媚，人们(們)心情愉快。
    - Chūnguāng míngmèi, rénmen xīnqíng yúkuài.
    - The spring light is bright, and people feel joyful.

  - **description:**

    - original form of removing weeds ->
    - possibly related to tools or a person holding onto a cliff
  - **interpretation:** The character ⾠ initially represented a tool used for agricultural purposes, possibly related to removing weeds, symbolizing labor and cultivation. Alternatively, it is seen as depicting a person holding onto a cliff, illustrating support and elevation.

### 春天 → 春 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.
    - At the break of dawn, the sun rises in the east.
  - 温(溫)暖的日光洒(灑)满(滿)大地。
    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.
    - At noon, the sun hangs high in the sky.

### 春天 → 春 → 屯
- **definition:** sprout
- **pinyin:** zhūn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a plant sprout (屮) with a middle emphasis line ->
    - emphasizes sprout
  - **interpretation:** The character 屯(tún, 'station') originally depicts a plant sprout with an added line to emphasize its growth. This imagery reflects the concept of development or establishment, leading to the sense of 'station' as a place where something is established or set up.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)附近有一个(個)储(儲)备(備)粮(糧)的屯。
    - Zhè fùjìn yǒu yīgè chǔbèi liáng de tún.
    - There is a grain storage station nearby.
  - 他们(們)准(準)备(備)在此地设(設)立一个(個)新的军(軍)事屯。
    - Tāmen zhǔnbèi zài cǐdì shèlì yīgè xīn de jūnshì tún.
    - They plan to establish a new military station at this location.
  - 村民们(們)在屯里(裡)储(儲)存了大量的粮(糧)食。
    - Cūnmín men zài tún lǐ chǔcún le dàliàng de liángshí.
    - The villagers have stored a large amount of grain in the station.

### 春天 → 天
- **definition:** sky; day
- **pinyin:** tiān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Horizontal line above 大(dà, "big") to depict sky ->
    - anthropomorphic man with large head symbolizing heaven
  - **interpretation:** The character 天(tiān) originally depicts the sky as a horizontal line above a person with outstretched arms, 大(dà, "big"), emphasizing the vastness or domain of heaven. Over time, the character evolved into an anthropomorphic representation of heaven, which later influenced its interpretation in classical texts.
- **examples:**
  - 夜幕降临(臨)，天边(邊)星光闪(閃)烁(爍)。
    - Yèmù jiànglín, tiānbiān xīnguāng shǎnshuò.
    - As night falls, starlight gleams on the horizon.
  - 他仰望高空，祈求天佑众(眾)生。
    - Tā yǎngwàng gāokōng, qíqiú tiānyòu zhòngshēng.
    - He gazes up to the sky and prays for heaven's protection over all living beings.
