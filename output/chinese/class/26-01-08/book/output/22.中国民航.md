## Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)
### Zhōngguó Mínháng
---
- **traditional:** 中國民航
- **definition:** Civil Aviation Administration of China (CAAC)
- **pinyin:** Zhōngguó Mínháng
- **components:**
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
  - 國
    - guó
    - country
  - 民
    - mín
    - people; citizen
  - 航
    - háng
    - navigation; sailing; flight
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 中 (zhōng, "middle") + 國 (guó, "country") =
    - 中國 (Zhōngguó, "China"); 民 (mín, "people") shares a semantic connection with "citizenship"; 航 (háng, "navigation") suggests the act of navigating or flying.
  - **interpretation:** 中國 (Zhōngguó, "China") represents the country; 民 (mín, "people") implies the national citizenry involved in aviation processes; 航 (háng, "navigation") focuses on flight and air travel. Together, 中國民航 (Zhōngguó Mínháng) denotes China's organized air operations involving citizens — thus representing the Civil Aviation Administration of China.
- **examples:**
  - 中国(國)民航是一个重要的机构。
    - Zhōngguó Mínháng shì yīgè zhòngyào de jīgòu.
    - The Civil Aviation Administration of China is an important organization.
  - 中国(國)民航的航班准点率很高。
    - Zhōngguó Mínháng de hángbān zhǔndiǎn lǜ hěn gāo.
    - The punctuality rate of flights by the Civil Aviation Administration of China is very high.
  - 很多人关(關)注中国(國)民航的最新政策。
    - Hěn duō rén guānzhù Zhōngguó Mínháng de zuìxīn zhèngcè.
    - Many people are paying attention to the latest policies of the Civil Aviation Administration of China.
