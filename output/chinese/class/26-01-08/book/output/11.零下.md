## zero; below zero
### líng (xià)
---
- **definition:** zero; below zero
- **pinyin:** líng (xià)
- **components:**
  - 零
    - líng
    - zero; nil; fragment; fractional
  - 下
    - xià
    - below; under; next; to descend
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 零 (líng, "zero") refers to the numerical value of zero, representing the absence of quantity. 下 (xià, "below") indicates a position that is below or underneath something.
  - **interpretation:** 零 (líng, "zero") sets a baseline or target level of nothingness. 下 (xià, "below") indicates a position or state that is beneath this baseline. Together, 零下 conveys the concept of measurements, typically temperature, that fall below zero, signifying negative values. The intuition is aiming below the absence of quantity, representing temperatures in the negative scale.
- **examples:**
  - 今天气(氣)温(溫)是零下五度。
    - Jīntiān qìwēn shì língxià wǔ dù.
    - Today's temperature is five degrees below zero.
  - 在零下的天气(氣)中，我们(們)需要穿厚外套。
    - Zài língxià de tiānqì zhōng, wǒmen xūyào chuān hòu wàitào.
    - In below zero weather, we need to wear thick coats.
  - 昨晚气(氣)温(溫)降到零下，让(讓)人觉(覺)得特别(別)冷。
    - Zuówǎn qìwēn jiàng dào língxià, ràng rén juédé tèbié lěng.
    - Last night the temperature dropped below zero, making it feel especially cold.
### 零下 → 零
- **definition:** zero; nil; fragment; fractional
- **pinyin:** líng
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 令
    - lìng
    - command
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 雨 (yǔ, "rain") + 令 (lìng, "command") =
    - rain with large droplets as depicted in oracle script ->
    - associated with the loss or remainder of rain droplets ->
    - zero
  - **interpretation:** The character 零(líng) combines the semantic element of 雨 (yǔ, "rain") with the phonetic component 令 (lìng, "command"). Originally, it depicted raindrops falling from the sky, denoting something remaining or leftover. This visual representation of raindrops reduced to nothing contributed to its current meaning of "zero."
- **examples:**
  - 零是一种(種)数(數)字。
    - líng shì yī zhǒng shùzì.
    - Zero is a number.
  - 他的分数(數)是零。
    - tā de fēnshù shì líng.
    - His score is zero.
  - 他的希望几(幾)乎为(為)零，前途黯淡无(無)光。
    - tā de xīwàng jīhū wéi líng, qiántú àndàn wúguāng.
    - His hopes are nearly zero, and his future is bleak.

### 零下 → 零 → 雨
- **definition:** rain
- **pinyin:** yǔ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a cloud with drops of rain falling from it ->
    - horizontal stroke added later ->
    - rain
  - **interpretation:** 雨 (yǔ, "rain") visually represents rain falling from a cloud, capturing its essential form.
- **examples:**
  - 今天雨下得很大。
    - Jīntiān yǔ xià de hěn dà.
    - It rained hard today.
  - 他忘记(記)带(帶)伞(傘)，雨却(卻)毫不留情。
    - Tā wàngjì dài sǎn, yǔ què háobù liúqíng.
    - He forgot to bring an umbrella, and the rain showed no mercy.
  - 细(細)雨如丝(絲)，轻(輕)拂着(著)花瓣。
    - Xì yǔ rú sī, qīng fú zhe huā bàn.
    - The fine rain falls like silk, gently brushing the petals.

### 零下 → 零 → 令
- **definition:** command
- **pinyin:** lìng
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 卩
    - jié
    - seal
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人 (rén, "person") + 卩 (jié, "seal") =
    - person with seal ->
    - issuing orders or commands
  - **interpretation:** The character 令 (lǐng, "order") combines 人 (rén, "person") and 卩 (jié, "seal") to depict someone with a seal or authority, signifying the power to issue orders or commands.
- **examples:**
  - 将(將)军(軍)令部队(隊)保持警戒，士兵严(嚴)格服从(從)。
    - jiāngjūn lǐng bùduì bǎochí jǐngjiè, shìbīng yángé fúcóng.
    - The general orders the troops to maintain vigilance, and the soldiers strictly obey.
  - 老师(師)令学(學)生认(認)真听(聽)讲(講)。
    - lǎoshī lǐng xuéshēng rènzhēn tīngjiǎng.
    - The teacher orders the students to listen attentively.
  - 国(國)王令大臣速速筹(籌)备(備)，迎接远(遠)方使节(節)。
    - guówáng lǐng dàchén sùsù chóubèi, yíngjiē yuǎnfāng shǐjié.

### 零下 → 零 → 令 → 卩
- **definition:** seal
- **pinyin:** jié
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a kneeling person from sideview
  - **interpretation:** The character 卩 (jié, "kneeling person") is a simple pictogram showing the silhouette of a person kneeling. It is unrelated to other kneeling or similar figures in different contexts such as 欠 (qiàn, "yawn") where the mouth is open, or 女 (nǚ, "woman") where the figure represents a female. Its form is solely based on the posture of kneeling.
- **examples:**
  - 那位卩正虔诚(誠)祈祷(禱)。
    - Nà wèi jié zhèng qiánchéng qídǎo.
    - That kneeling person is praying devoutly.
  - 礼(禮)仪(儀)师(師)指出，卩代表谦(謙)卑与(與)敬意。
    - Lǐyí shī zhǐchū, jié dàibiǎo qiānbēi yǔ jìngyì.
    - The ritual master pointed out that the kneeling person represents humility and respect.
  - 在庄(莊)严(嚴)仪(儀)式中，卩象征(徵)着(著)内(內)心的虔敬。
    - Zài zhuāngyán yíshì zhōng, jié xiàngzhēngzhe nèixīn de qiánjìng.
    - In the solemn ceremony, the kneeling figure symbolizes inner devotion.

### 零下 → 下
- **definition:** below; under; next; to descend
- **pinyin:** xià
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - a line below another ->
    - later evolved into 下
  - **interpretation:** The character 下(xià, 'down') is an ideogram showing a line positioned below another line, visually representing 'below' or 'downward'. This layout intuitively depicts the concept of being beneath or beneath another object.
- **examples:**
  - 水从(從)山上流下。
    - Shuǐ cóng shān shàng liú xià.
    - The water flows down from the mountain.
  - 他坐下来(來)看书(書)。
    - Tā zuò xià lái kàn shū.
    - He sits down to read a book.
  - 请(請)把箱子放在地下。
    - Qǐng bǎ xiāngzi fàng zài dìxià.
    - Please put the box down on the ground.
