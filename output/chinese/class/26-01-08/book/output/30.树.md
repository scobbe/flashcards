## tree
### shù
---
- **traditional:** 樹
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 尌
    - shù
    - to plant a tree
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - Originally 尌 (phonetic, 'to plant a tree') + 木 (mù, 'tree') added for clarity ->
    - tree
  - **interpretation:** 樹 combines the semantic 木 (mù, 'tree') with the phonetic 尌 to convey the meaning of 'tree'. The phonetic element 尌 originally depicted the act of planting a tree, and 木 emphasizes the botanical aspect.
  - **simplification:** 壴 (壴, 'drum or musical instrument') was replaced by 又 (又, 'again') to simplify stroke count, losing the original phonetic connection in favor of visual simplicity.
- **examples:**
  - 公园(園)里(裡)的树(樹)很高大。
    - Gōngyuán lǐ de shù hěn gāodà.
    - The trees in the park are very tall.
  - 秋天时(時)树(樹)叶(葉)变(變)黄(黃)。
    - Qiūtiān shí shùyè biàn huáng.
    - In autumn, the tree leaves turn yellow.
  - 那棵老树(樹)见(見)证(證)了许(許)多历(歷)史。
    - Nà kē lǎo shù jiànzhèngle xǔduō lìshǐ.
    - That old tree has witnessed a lot of history.
### 树(樹) → 木
- **definition:** tree
- **pinyin:** mù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a tree with branches and roots
  - **interpretation:** The character 木(mù) depicts a tree, showing the trunk in the center and two slanting lines representing branches or roots. It directly signifies the concept of wood or a tree.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)把椅子的主要材料是木。
    - Zhè bǎ yǐzi de zhǔyào cáiliào shì mù.
    - The main material of this chair is wood.
  - 工匠精心雕刻木，制成精美家具。
    - Gōngjiàng jīngxīn diāo kè mù, zhìchéng jīngměi jiājù.
    - The craftsman carefully carved the wood, crafting exquisite furniture.

### 树(樹) → 尌
- **definition:** to plant a tree
- **pinyin:** shù
- **components:**
  - 木
    - mù
    - tree
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - hand
  - 豆
    - dòu
    - drum
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 木 (mù, 'tree') + 寸 (cùn, 'hand') =
    - to plant a tree ->
    - 豆 (dòu, used phonetic) added later
  - **interpretation:** The character 尌 originally depicted planting a tree with a hand, represented by 木 (mù, 'tree') and 寸 (cùn, 'hand'). A phonetic component 豆 (dòu) was added later to distinguish its meaning, although in modern forms, 木 and 豆 have visually merged into 壴.

### 树(樹) → 尌 → 寸
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** cùn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - Line on the hand (ideographic) emphasizing the elbow ->
    - represents measurement unit between wrist and specific forearm position ->
    - meaning 'inch'
  - **interpretation:** 寸 (cùn, "inch") originated as a depiction of a line on the hand, indicating the area near the elbow. This specific placement was considered significant for pulse palpation, originally suggesting a forearm position, which then came to denote the measurement 'inch'.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)块(塊)布每一寸都是精心制(製)作。
    - Zhè kuài bù měi yī cùn dōu shì jīngxīn zhìzuò.
    - Every inch of this cloth is crafted with care.
  - 请(請)用卷尺量一寸，每一寸数(數)据(據)都准(準)确(確)。
    - Qǐng yòng juǎnchǐ liàng yī cùn, měi yī cùn shùjù dōu zhǔnquè.
    - Please use a tape measure to measure an inch, every inch's data is accurate.

### 树(樹) → 尌 → 豆
- **definition:** drum
- **pinyin:** dòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a container ->
    - borrowed phonetically for the plant name
  - **interpretation:** The character 豆(dòu, 'drum') originally depicted a type of container. It was later borrowed for its phonetic value to represent the plant name 'bean', distinguishing it from a container. This transition indicates how the physical shape of the container, resembling a drum or storage pot, facilitated a phonetic borrowing.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)在课(課)堂上学(學)习(習)如何制(製)作豆。
    - Wǒmen zài kètáng shàng xuéxí rúhé zhìzuò dòu.
    - We are learning how to make drums in class.
  - 村庄(莊)里(裡)有一个(個)古老的豆，它的声(聲)音很有魅力。
    - Cūnzhuāng lǐ yǒu yí gè gǔlǎo de dòu, tā de shēngyīn hěn yǒu mèilì.
    - There is an ancient drum in the village, and its sound is captivating.
  - 音乐(樂)会(會)以豆声(聲)开(開)场(場)，让(讓)观(觀)众(眾)立刻沉(沈)浸在气(氣)氛中。
    - Yīnyuèhuì yǐ dòu shēng kāichǎng, ràng guānzhòng lìkè chénjìn zài qìfēn zhōng.
    - The concert opened with the sound of drums, immersing the audience in the atmosphere immediately.
