## to snow
### xià xuě
---
- **definition:** to snow
- **pinyin:** xià xuě
- **components:**
  - 下
    - xià
    - down
  - 雪
    - xuě
    - snow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 下 (xià, "down") + 雪 (xuě, "snow")
  - **interpretation:** 下 (xià, "down") describes the direction in which snow falls, as it comes from the sky above. 雪 (xuě, "snow") refers to the frozen precipitation that falls during winter. Together, these parts describe the action of snow falling from the sky, hence 'to snow'.
- **examples:**
  - 冬天常常下雪。
    - Dōngtiān chángcháng xià xuě.
    - It often snows in winter.
  - 昨晚下雪了，早上地上有一层(層)雪。
    - Zuówǎn xià xuě le, zǎoshang dìshàng yǒu yī céng xuě.
    - It snowed last night, and there is a layer of snow on the ground this morning.
  - 如果晚上还(還)下雪，我们(們)明天就去滑雪。
    - Rúguǒ wǎnshàng hái xià xuě, wǒmen míngtiān jiù qù huáxuě.
    - If it continues to snow tonight, we'll go skiing tomorrow.

### 下雪 → 雪
- **definition:** snow
- **pinyin:** xuě
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 彗
    - huì
    - broom
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 雨 (yǔ, "rain") + phonetic 彗 (huì, "broom") ->
    - snow
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 雨 (yǔ, "rain") for the semantic idea of precipitation and 彗 (huì, "broom") for its phonetic value. The image of rain light and fluffy as a broom sweep implies the specific type of precipitation: snow.
- **examples:**
  - 冬天的时(時)候，我们(們)经(經)常去山上滑雪。
    - Dōngtiān de shíhòu, wǒmen jīngcháng qù shānshàng huáxuě.
    - In winter, we often go to the mountains to ski.
  - 昨天晚上下了很多雪。
    - Zuótiān wǎnshàng xiàle hěnduō xuě.
    - It snowed a lot last night.
  - 雪花在阳(陽)光下闪(閃)闪(閃)发(發)光。
    - Xuěhuā zài yángguāng xià shǎnshǎn fāguāng.
    - The snowflakes sparkle under the sunlight.
