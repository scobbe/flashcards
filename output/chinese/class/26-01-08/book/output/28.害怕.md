## to be afraid
### hàipà
---
- **definition:** to be afraid
- **pinyin:** hàipà
- **components:**
  - 害
    - hài
    - harm
  - 怕
    - pà
    - fear
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 害 (hài, "harm") + 怕 (pà, "fear") =
    - to experience fear from a potential harm, leading to the feeling of being afraid
  - **interpretation:** 害 (hài, "harm") suggests the presence of something that could cause trouble or injury, while 怕 (pà, "fear") represents the emotional response to such a threat. Together, they indicate the feeling of being afraid, as one experiences fear when there is a potential for harm or danger.
- **examples:**
  - 她害怕黑暗。
    - Tā hàipà hēi'àn.
    - She is afraid of the dark.
  - 他( )不( )再( )害( )怕( )说(說)话(話)。
