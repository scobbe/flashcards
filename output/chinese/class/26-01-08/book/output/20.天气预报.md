## weather forecast
### tiānqì yùbào
---
- **traditional:** 天氣預報
- **definition:** weather forecast
- **pinyin:** tiānqì yùbào
- **components:**
  - 天
    - tiān
    - sky; heaven
  - 气(氣)
    - qì
    - air; gas
  - 预(預)
    - yù
    - in advance; beforehand
  - 报(報)
    - bào
    - report; announce
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 天 (tiān, "sky; heaven") refers to the sky or the atmosphere. 氣 (qì, "air; gas") captures the concept of the air around us, often used in contexts related to the weather. 預 (yù, "in advance; beforehand") indicates something that is done ahead of time, pointing to the act of predicting or preparing. 報 (bào, "report; announce") involves conveying information, making it a natural fit for sharing predictions or announcements.
  - **interpretation:** The phrase combines 天 (tiān, "sky") and 氣 (qì, "air") to describe the 'state of the atmosphere,' essentially referring to 'weather.' 預 (yù, "in advance") and 報 (bào, "report") together signify 'reporting in advance,' which is the essence of forecasting as it informs people about future weather conditions. Thus, 天氣預報 (tiānqì yùbào) effectively means 'report on the condition of the sky and air expected in the future.'
- **examples:**
  - 今天的天气(氣)预(預)报(報)说(說)会(會)下雨。
    - Jīntiān de tiānqì yùbào shuō huì xiàyǔ.
    - Today's weather forecast says it will rain.
  - 我们(們)应(應)该(該)常常查看天气(氣)预(預)报(報)，以便做好计(計)划(劃)。
    - Wǒmen yīnggāi chángcháng chákàn tiānqì yùbào, yǐbiàn zuò hǎo jìhuà.
    - We should frequently check the weather forecast to make good plans.
  - 明天的天气(氣)预(預)报(報)很准(準)确(確)。
    - Míngtiān de tiānqì yùbào hěn zhǔnquè.
    - Tomorrow's weather forecast is very accurate.
### 天气(氣)预(預)报(報) → 天

- **pinyin:** tiān
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram

  - **interpretation:** The character 天(tiān) originally depicts the sky as a horizontal line above a person with outstretched arms, 大(dà, "big"), emphasizing the vastness or domain of heaven. Over time, the character evolved into an anthropomorphic representation of heaven, which later influenced its interpretation in classical texts.
- **examples:**

### 天气(氣)预(預)报(報) → 报(報)
- **traditional:** 報
- **definition:** report; announce
- **pinyin:** bào
- **components:**
  - 㚔
    - none
    - handcuffs
  - 𠬝
    - none
    - to subdue
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 㚔 (handcuffs) + 𠬝 (to subdue) =
    - to convict; to punish; to declare guilty
  - **interpretation:** The character 報 (bào, 'report; newspaper; recompense; respond') combines the imagery of handcuffs, symbolizing restraint, and an action of subduing, suggesting punishment or declaration of a verdict. This combination reflects the idea of delivering judgment or punishment, which extends metaphorically to mean 'reporting' or 'giving an account' of something. From delivering justice comes the idea of reciprocating actions, hence the sense of 'recompense' or 'respond'.
  - **simplification:** 报 is simplified from 報 by removing the 'handcuff' component 㚔, retaining the core representing the idea of reporting and responding without the punitive nuance.
- **examples:**
