## palm; slap
### bāzhang
---
- **definition:** palm; slap
- **pinyin:** bāzhang
- **components:**
  - 巴
    - bā
    - to hold onto; cling
  - 掌
    - zhǎng
    - palm; to slap
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 巴(bā, "to hold onto; cling") 掌(zhǎng, "palm; to slap")
  - **interpretation:** 巴(bā, "to hold onto; cling to") suggests grasping onto something, which links to the idea of a 'palm' where one holds or grasps items. 掌(zhǎng, "palm; to slap") directly describes the flat, broad shape of the hand's palm, fitting its role in slapping. Together, 巴掌(bāzhang) conveys the sense of a palm, a part of the hand capable of clinging and slapping.
- **examples:**
  - 他愤(憤)怒地拍了一下巴掌。
    - Tā fènnù de pāile yīxià bāzhang.
    - He angrily slapped his palm.
  - 孩子用力地击(擊)了一下自己的巴掌。
    - Háizi yònglì de jīle yīxià zìjǐ de bāzhang.
    - The child forcefully slapped his own palm.
  - 他的巴掌打得很响(響)。
    - Tā de bāzhang dǎ de hěn xiǎng.
    - His slap was very loud.

### 巴掌 → 掌
- **definition:** palm; to slap
- **pinyin:** zhǎng
- **components:**
  - 尚
    - shàng
    - esteem
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 尚 (shàng, "esteem") as phonetic + 手 (shǒu, "hand") as semantic =
    - related to the hand ->
    - palm, control
  - **interpretation:** The character 掌 combines the phonetic component 尚 (shàng, "esteem") with the semantic component 手 (shǒu, "hand") to indicate its meaning related to the hand, specifically the palm, which is often associated with handling or controlling something.
- **examples:**

- **pinyin:** xiàng
- **components:**

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 宀 (house) + 口 (mouth) =
    - echos in a place ->

  - **interpretation:** The character 向 (xiàng, 'towards') originally depicted echoes in a space, suggesting a sound emanating within a house. ### 巴掌 → 掌 → 尚 → 向 → 宀
- **definition:** house
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉(漢)字代表家的温(溫)暖。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.

    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
