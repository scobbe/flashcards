## rush
### chōng
---
- **traditional:** 沖
- **definition:** rush
- **pinyin:** chōng
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 中
    - zhōng
    - middle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 中 (zhōng) ->
    - rush of water
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 水 (shuǐ, "water") for its semantic element, conveying the idea of fluid movement, with 中 (zhōng) to provide the phonetic element, approximating the sound 'chōng'. Water naturally rushes or flows, leading to the idea of a rapid movement or 'rush'.
  - **simplification:** 沖 was simplified to 冲 by retaining 中 (zhōng, "middle") as it is, but reducing 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") to 冲's simpler top-stroke form.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )匆( )匆( )冲(沖)出( )门(門)。
    - Tā cōngcōng chōng chū mén.
    - He rushed out of the door in a hurry.
  - 清( )晨( )的( )海( )浪( )冲(沖)上( )沙( )滩(灘)。
    - Qīngchén de hǎilàng chōng shàng shātān.
    - The morning waves rushed onto the beach.
  - 孩( )子( )们(們)冲(沖)向( )操( )场(場)，欢(歡)呼( )雀( )跃(躍)。
    - Háizimen chōng xiàng cāochǎng, huānhū què yuè.

### 冲(沖) → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。

### 冲(沖) → 中
- **definition:** middle
- **pinyin:** zhōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arrow piercing through the center of a target
  - **interpretation:** The character 中 (zhōng, "middle") visually represents an arrow passing through the central point of a target, illustrating the concept of 'center' or 'middle' effectively. This visual imagery is straightforward and memorable, corresponding closely with its meaning of being at the center or middle of something.
- **examples:**
  - 他站在道路中，看( )着(著)远(遠)方( )。
    - tā zhàn zài dàolù zhōng, kànzhe yuǎnfāng.
    - He stands in the middle of the road, gazing into the distance.
  - 房( )间(間)正( )中( )，挂(掛)着(著)一( )幅( )名( )画(畫)。
    - fángjiān zhèng zhōng, guàzhe yī fú mínghuà.
    - A famous painting hangs in the exact center of the room.
