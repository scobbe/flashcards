## world
### shìjiè
---
- **definition:** world
- **pinyin:** shìjiè
- **components:**
  - 世
    - shì
    - generation; life; era
  - 界
    - jiè
    - boundary; sphere; realm
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 世 (shì, "generation; life; era") refers to the succession of human generations or life stages, encompassing time and overall human existence. 界 (jiè, "boundary; sphere") represents the limits or divisions between different entities or areas, leading to a conception of different realms or domains. Together, they form the idea of the "world" as a sphere consisting of all generations and realms, encompassing all of humankind and the physical domain around them.
  - **interpretation:** 世 (shì, "generation; life; era") emphasizes continuity and the passage of time across human generations, implying a collective human existence over eras. 界 (jiè, "boundary; sphere") is about demarcation, defining spaces or worlds in philosophical, physical, or existential terms. Thus, 世界 (shìjiè) embodies the concept of the world as the overarching domain that includes all human societies and the physical universe in which they exist.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )们(們)住( )在( )一( )个(個)美( )丽(麗)的( )世( )界( )。
    - Wǒmen zhù zài yī gè měilì de shìjiè.
    - We live in a beautiful world.
  - 世界上有很多不同的文化。
    - Shìjiè shàng yǒu hěn duō bùtóng de wénhuà.
    - There are many different cultures in the world.
  - 科( )学(學)家( )在( )探( )索( )新( )的( )世( )界( )。

  - **type:** jiajie

### 世界 → 世 → 枼
- **definition:** leaf
- **pinyin:** yè
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts leaves growing from a tree (original form of 葉 (yè, "leaf"))
  - **interpretation:** Depicts leaves on a branch, representing both the specific physical forms of 'leaf' as well as the larger conceptual category of things that are flat like leaves. This intuitive extension made it a fitting choice to also represent the concept of 'flat' due to the physical characteristic of flatness associated with leaves.

### 世界 → 世 → 葉
- **definition:** leaf
- **pinyin:** yè
- **components:**
  - 艸
    - cǎo
    - grass; plant
  - 枼
    - yè
    - leaf
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 艸 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic 枼 (yè, "leaf") ->
    - depiction of a leaf
  - **interpretation:** The character 葉(yè, "leaf") is formed by combining 艸 (cǎo, "grass; plant") as the semantic component, representing its association with plants, with 枼 (yè, "leaf") as the phonetic component conveying the pronunciation. This results in the character meaning 'leaf,' representing the part of a plant.
  - **simplification:** In the simplified version 叶(葉), the semantic 艸 (cǎo, "grass; plant") is removed while retaining the phonetic 十, maintaining the connection to the sound but with fewer strokes.
