## 停
---
- **pinyin:** tíng
- **definition:** to stop; to park
- **examples:**
  - 请在这儿停一下吧(請在這兒停一下吧) (qǐng zài zhèr tíng yíxià ba, "Please stop here for a moment")
  - 这条路边禁止停车(這條路邊禁止停車) (zhè tiáo lùbiān jìnzhǐ tíngchē, "Parking is prohibited along this road")
  - 停在路旁边(停在路旁邊) (tíng zài lù pángbiān, "stop by the roadside")
  - 停车(停車) (tíngchē, "to park")
  - 停课(停課) (tíngkè, "to suspend classes")
