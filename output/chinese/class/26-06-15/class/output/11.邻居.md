## neighbor
### línjū
---
- **traditional:** 鄰居
- **definition:** neighbor
- **pinyin:** línjū
- **components:**
  - 邻(鄰)
    - lín
    - neighbor
  - 居
    - jū
    - live; dwell; reside
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 邻(鄰) (lín, "neighbor") originally depicted ears of grain stacked close together, illustrating proximity. 居 (jū, "live") shows a person under a roof, representing habitation.
  - **interpretation:** 鄰 (lín, "neighbor") reflects people or homes situated close to one another, similar to how stacked grain ears are adjacent. 居 (jū, "live") indicates a place of residence. Together, 鄰居 (línjū, "neighbor") describes those who live nearby in relation to one's home.
