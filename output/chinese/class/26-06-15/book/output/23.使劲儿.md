## exert oneself (physically)
### shǐ jìnr
---
- **traditional:** 使勁兒
- **definition:** exert oneself (physically)
- **pinyin:** shǐ jìnr
- **components:**
  - 使
    - shǐ
    - cause; send; enable
  - 劲(勁)
    - jìn
    - strength; energy; enthusiasm
  - 儿(兒)
    - ér
    - child; diminutive; nominal suffix
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 使 (shǐ, "cause or send") is a verb meaning 'to cause' or 'send', often used to denote initiating an action. 勁 (jìn, "strength") refers to physical strength or energy. 兒 (ér, "child; diminutive or nominal suffix") is a common suffix in Chinese, often used in northern dialects to denote the diminutive or a casual tone.
  - **interpretation:** The phrase 使劲(勁)儿(兒) (shǐ jìnr) translates to 'exert oneself' by combining the notion of causing or using 使 (shǐ, 'cause or send') with 勁 (jìn, 'strength'), implying putting forth effort or using one's full strength. The added 兒 (ér) gives it a casual or colloquial tone. Commonly used in contexts requiring physical exertion.
- **examples:**
  - 他使劲(勁)儿(兒)推门(門)。
    - Tā shǐ jìnr tuī mén.
    - He exerted himself to push the door.
  - 请(請)使劲(勁)儿(兒)拉绳(繩)子。
    - Qǐng shǐ jìnr lā shéngzi.
    - Please pull the rope with all your strength.
  - 他使劲(勁)儿(兒)地跑，但还(還)是迟(遲)到了。
    - Tā shǐ jìnr de pǎo, dàn háishì chídào le.
    - He ran with all his might, but he was still late.

### 使劲(勁)儿(兒) → 劲(勁)
- **traditional:** 勁
- **definition:** strength; energy; enthusiasm
- **pinyin:** jìn
- **components:**
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength
  - 巠
    - jīng
    - stream
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 力 (lì, "strength") + phonetic 巠 (jīng, "stream")
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 力 (lì, "strength") directly conveys the meaning of physical power or strength. The phonetic component 巠 (jīng, "stream") contributes the pronunciation, connecting the imagery of flowing energy, which aligns with the sense of strength or vitality. This combination gives the character 勁(劲) (jìn, "strength; energy; enthusiasm"), carrying the sense of both physical and enthusiastic vigor.
  - **simplification:** 劲 is the simplified form of 勁, where the character 巠 (jīng, "stream") was removed, simplifying the stroke count while retaining 力 (lì, "strength") to maintain the core meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 他踢足球的时(時)候非常有劲(勁)。
    - Tā tī zúqiú de shíhòu fēicháng yǒu jìn.
    - He is very energetic when playing football.
  - 这(這)道菜有一种(種)特别(別)的劲(勁)。
    - Zhè dào cài yǒu yī zhǒng tèbié de jìn.
    - This dish has a special kick.
  - 加油！我们(們)还(還)有劲(勁)。
    - Jiāyóu! Wǒmen hái yǒu jìn.
    - Keep it up! We still have energy.
