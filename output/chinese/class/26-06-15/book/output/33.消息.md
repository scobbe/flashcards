## news; information
### xiāoxi
---
- **definition:** news; information
- **pinyin:** xiāoxi
- **components:**
  - 消
    - xiāo
    - to disappear; to eliminate
  - 息
    - xī
    - rest; interest; news
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 消 (xiāo, "to disappear; to eliminate") + 息 (xī, "rest; interest; news")
  - **interpretation:** The word 消息 (xiāoxi) combines 消 (xiāo, "to disappear; to eliminate") with 息 (xī, "rest; interest; news") to imply the concept of news or information as something that comes and goes, flows, or spreads, much like the elimination of silence with the arrival of sound or news.
- **examples:**
  - 我收到了一条(條)好消息。
    - Wǒ shōudào le yī tiáo hǎo xiāoxi.
    - I received a piece of good news.
  - 他打电(電)话(話)告诉(訴)我一个(個)重要的消息。
    - Tā dǎ diànhuà gàosù wǒ yī gè zhòngyào de xiāoxi.
    - He called to tell me an important piece of information.
  - 这(這)条(條)消息引起了广(廣)泛的关(關)注。
    - Zhè tiáo xiāoxi yǐnqǐ le guǎngfàn de guānzhù.
    - This news attracted widespread attention.
### 消息 → 消
- **definition:** to disappear; to eliminate
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 氵(水)
    - shuǐ
    - water
  - 肖
    - xiāo
    - to resemble
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 肖 (xiāo) ->
    - to extinguish something (with water) ->
    - disappear; eliminate
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the semantic component 氵(水) (shuǐ, "water"), symbolizing extinguishing or quenching, with the phonetic component 肖 (xiāo) to convey the sense of causing something to disappear or be eliminated, similar to how water can extinguish a fire.
- **examples:**
  - 冰块(塊)在阳(陽)光下很快消失。
    - Bīngkuài zài yángguāng xià hěn kuài xiāoshī.
    - The ice cubes disappear quickly under the sunlight.
  - 那些负(負)面情绪(緒)慢慢地消散了。
    - Nàxiē fùmiàn qíngxù mànmàn de xiāosàn le.
    - Those negative emotions gradually dissipated.
  - 药(藥)物可以帮(幫)助消除疼痛。
    - Yàowù kěyǐ bāngzhù xiāochú téngtòng.
    - The medication can help eliminate pain.

### 消息 → 消 → 氵
- **definition:** water
- **pinyin:** shuǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 氵is the radical form of 水 (shuǐ, "water") ->
    - depicts flowing water, compressed and stylized next to characters.
  - **interpretation:** The character 氵is a simplified variant used as a radical to represent water-related meanings in other characters. It retains the flowing water depiction of 水 (shuǐ, "water") but is stylized to fit alongside other components.
- **examples:**
  - 水是生命之源。
    - Shuǐ shì shēngmìng zhī yuán.
    - Water is the source of life.
  - 汉(漢)字中偏旁氵，代表水。
    - Hànzì zhōng piān páng shuǐ, dàibiǎo shuǐ.
    - In Chinese characters, the radical '氵' signifies water.
  - 河水清澈透明，鱼(魚)儿(兒)欢(歡)快戏(戲)水。
    - Hé shuǐ qīngchè tòumíng, yú er huānkuaì xì shuǐ.
    - The river water is clear and transparent, and the fish happily play in it.
### 消息 → 消 → 肖
- **definition:** to resemble
- **pinyin:** xiāo
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small
  - 肉
    - ròu
    - meat
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 小(xiǎo, "small") + semantic 肉(ròu, "meat") ->
    - resemble
  - **interpretation:** 肖(xiào, "resemble") combines the phonetic element 小(xiǎo, "small") to suggest its pronunciation with the semantic element 肉(ròu, "meat") indicating a connection to physical appearance. The core meaning 'resemble' arises from the idea of something appearing similar to another, as meat can take on various appearances, enhanced by the phonetic component.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)幅画(畫)很好地描绘(繪)了他的肖像。
    - Zhè fú huà hěn hǎo de miáohuì le tā de xiàoxiàng.
    - This painting excellently depicts his likeness.
  - 他长(長)得很像他的父亲(親)。
    - Tā zhǎng de hěn xiàng tā de fùqīn.
    - He greatly resembles his father.
  - 这(這)个(個)模型逼真地模仿了原物。
    - Zhège móxíng bīzhēn de mófǎng le yuánwù.
    - This model realistically imitates the original.

### 消息 → 息
- **definition:** rest; interest; news
- **pinyin:** xī
- **components:**
  - 自
    - zì
    - nose
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 自 (zì, "nose") + 心 (xīn, "heart") =
    - breathe through one's nose ->
    - rest ->
    - extension to 'interest' and 'news'
  - **interpretation:** The character 息 (xī, "rest") combines 自 (zì, "nose") and 心 (xīn, "heart") to depict the act of breathing, emphasizing relaxation and rejuvenation. This concept naturally extends to 'interest', as interest accumulates steadily like breath, and 'news', which circulates and spreads like shared breaths.
- **examples:**
  - 我累了，需要休息。
    - Wǒ lèi le, xūyào xiūxī.
    - I am tired and need to rest.
  - 他在银(銀)行的存款有很高的利息。
    - Tā zài yínháng de cúnkuǎn yǒu hěn gāo de lìxī.
    - His bank deposit earns high interest.
  - 有人传(傳)来(來)了一则(則)趣闻(聞)，我们(們)都笑了。
    - Yǒurén chuánlái le yī zé qùwén, wǒmen dōu xiào le.
    - Someone shared an interesting piece of news, and we all laughed.
### 消息 → 息 → 自
- **definition:** nose
- **pinyin:** zì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a nose ->
    - nose
  - **interpretation:** The character 自 (zì, "nose") is a pictogram representing the human nose, often used in ancient times to symbolize oneself, as pointing to the nose was a way of indicating oneself.
- **examples:**
  - 狗的自非常灵(靈)敏。
    - Gǒu de zì fēicháng língmǐn.
    - The dog's nose is very sensitive.
  - 他闻(聞)到花香，用自轻(輕)拂脸(臉)庞(龐)。
    - Tā wéndào huāxiāng, yòng zì qīngfú liǎnpáng.
    - He smelled the scent of flowers and softly brushed his nose against his cheek.

### 消息 → 息 → 心
- **definition:** heart
- **pinyin:** xīn
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a heart
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, "heart") originally depicted the shape of an actual heart, representing thoughts, emotions, and the core of a person. Over time, it has become highly stylized, but it continues to convey the notion of the heart as the center of emotions and consciousness.
- **examples:**
  - 她的心一直都在帮(幫)助别(別)人。
    - Tā de xīn yīzhí dōu zài bāngzhù biérén.
    - Her heart is always in helping others.
  - 在城市的中心，有一个(個)美丽(麗)的公园(園)。
    - Zài chéngshì de zhōngxīn, yǒu yí gè měilì de gōngyuán.
    - At the center of the city, there is a beautiful park.
  - 对(對)于(於)许(許)多人来(來)说(說)，家庭是生活的核心。
    - Duìyú xǔduō rén lái shuō, jiātíng shì shēnghuó de héxīn.
    - For many people, family is the core of life.
