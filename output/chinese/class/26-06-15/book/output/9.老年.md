## old age
### lǎonián
---
- **definition:** old age
- **pinyin:** lǎonián
- **components:**
  - 老
    - lǎo
    - old; venerable; experienced
  - 年
    - nián
    - year; age
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 老 (lǎo, "old") refers to age or elderliness, and 年 (nián, "year") refers to the concept of time or age in years. Together, 老年 specifically denotes the period of life known as old age.
  - **interpretation:** 老 (lǎo, "old") indicates a stage in life characterized by experience and wisdom accumulated over time. 年 (nián, "year") brings in the temporal aspect, emphasizing the cumulative passage of years. Thus, 老年 represents the accumulation of years in life, focusing on the advanced age stage, often associated with retirement and reflection.
- **examples:**
  - 在老年，保持健康是非常重要的。
    - Zài lǎonián, bǎochí jiànkāng shì fēicháng zhòngyào de.
    - In old age, maintaining health is very important.
  - 他的老年生活很幸福。
    - Tā de lǎonián shēnghuó hěn xìngfú.
    - His old age is very happy.
  - 人( )们(們)常( )常( )在( )老( )年( )时(時)回( )忆(憶)过(過)去( )。
    - Rénmen chángcháng zài lǎonián shí huíyì guòqù.
    - People often recall the past in old age.

### 老年 → 年
- **definition:** year; age
- **pinyin:** nián
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 禾(“wheat; grain”) + 人(“person”) as phonetic ->
    - originally “harvest” ->
    - later “year”
  - **interpretation:** The character 年 (nián, "year") originally depicted a harvest of grains, linking the concept of agricultural cycles with the passing of a year. Over time, the form evolved, retaining the semantic component 禾 (hé, "grain") and using 人 (rén, "person") for its phonetic sound in early stages, which later transformed into the current form through script evolution.
- **examples:**
  - 今年是兔年。
    - Jīnnián shì tùnián.
    - This year is the Year of the Rabbit.
  - 他( )今( )年( )二( )十( )岁(歲)。

    - He is twenty years old this year.
