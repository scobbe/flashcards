## take care of; look after
### zhàogù
---
- **traditional:** 照顧
- **definition:** take care of; look after
- **pinyin:** zhàogù
- **components:**
  - 照
    - zhào
    - shine; illuminate; reflect
  - 顾(顧)
    - gù
    - look after; attend to; care for
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 照 (zhào, "shine; illuminate; reflect") refers to the act of shining or projecting light, suggesting awareness and consideration. 顾(顧) (gù, "look after; attend to; care for") means to look in a certain direction or turn the head to regard something, implying attention and concern.
  - **interpretation:** 照 (zhào, "shine; illuminate") involves the projection of light, which can be linked to the notion of providing attention and insight, as if 'shining a light' on someone; this connects to the idea of looking after. 顾(顧) (gù, "look after; attend to") literally means turning the head to look, indicating focus and care. Together, 照( )顾(顧) (zhàogù, "take care of; look after") conveys the concept of paying careful attention to someone or something, as if continually illuminating and tending to it with care.
