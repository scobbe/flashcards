## old dream; past experience
### jiùmèng
---
- **traditional:** 舊夢
- **definition:** old dream; past experience
- **pinyin:** jiùmèng
- **components:**
  - 旧(舊)
    - jiù
    - old; past; former
  - 梦(夢)
    - mèng
    - dream; vision; illusion; aspiration
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 舊 (jiù, "old; past") represents something that belongs to a previous time or period. 夢 (mèng, "dream") refers to visions or experiences during sleep, and by extension, can refer to aspirations or past endeavors.
  - **interpretation:** 舊 (jiù, "old; past") conveys the sense of something that is no longer current, tying to the previous experiences or times. 夢 (mèng, "dream") connects to the experiences or aspirations that one may recall or reflect upon. Together, 舊夢 (jiùmèng) signifies a nostalgic recollection of past dreams or experiences, the old dreams that linger in memory, embodying both literal dreams and metaphorical longings or past aspirations.
- **examples:**

  - 他( )的( )小( )说(說)常( )常( )写(寫)到( )旧(舊)梦(夢)。
