### shuāng
---
- **traditional:** 雙
- **definition:** pair
- **pinyin:** shuāng
- **components:**
  - 雔
    - chóu
    - two birds
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 雔 (two birds) + 又 (hand) =
    - holding two birds in hand ->
    - pair
  - **interpretation:** The character depicts two birds (雔) held together by a hand (又), creating the image of a pair of birds, which conveys the meaning of 'pair' or 'double'. The concept of holding a pair of something is naturally associated with 'pair', reinforcing the meaning.
  - **simplification:** The traditional form 䨇 underwent simplification by eliminating the 雔 component, leading to the modern form 双 that retains the notion of duplicity through duplication of 又.
