## short (of stature)
### ǎi
---
- **definition:** short (of stature)
- **pinyin:** ǎi
- **components:**
  - 矢
    - shǐ
    - arrow
  - 委
    - wěi
    - to entrust
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 矢 (shǐ, "arrow") + phonetic 委 (wěi) ->
    - short (of stature)
  - **interpretation:** 矢 (shǐ, "arrow") suggests something being straight and upright, while 委 (wěi) gives the sound. The use of an arrow, typically straight and long, juxtaposed in a context meaning 'short,' emphasizes a comparative sense of lower stature.
- **examples:**
  - 他的身材很矮。
    - Tā de shēncái hěn ǎi.
    - He is very short in stature.

### 矮 → 委
- **definition:** to entrust
- **pinyin:** wěi
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 禾 (hé, "grain") + semantic 女 (nǚ, "woman") ->
    - entrust
  - **interpretation:** The character 委 combines 禾 (hé, "grain") as the phonetic component to suggest sound, with 女 (nǚ, "woman") as the semantic component. Originally, the oracle bone representation used 𠃊, depicting a figure kneeling, which aligns with the idea of yielding or resigning. Hence, the combination implies entrusting or assigning a task, as one might with a trusted companion or subordinate.
- **examples:**
  - 我( )把( )重( )要( )的( )任( )务(務)委( )托(託)给(給)他( )。

    - Jīnglǐ wěirèn le yī míng xīn zhùlǐ.

### 矮 → 委 → 女
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
