## short (of stature)
### ǎi
---
- **definition:** short (of stature)
- **pinyin:** ǎi
- **components:**
  - 矢
    - shǐ
    - arrow
  - 委
    - wěi
    - to entrust
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 矢 (shǐ, "arrow") + phonetic 委 (wěi) ->
    - short (of stature)
  - **interpretation:** 矢 (shǐ, "arrow") suggests something being straight and upright, while 委 (wěi) gives the sound. The use of an arrow, typically straight and long, juxtaposed in a context meaning 'short,' emphasizes a comparative sense of lower stature.
- **examples:**
  - 他的身材很矮。
    - Tā de shēncái hěn ǎi.
    - He is very short in stature.
  - 虽(雖)然他很矮，但他在篮(籃)球队(隊)里(裏)表现(現)出色。
    - Suīrán tā hěn ǎi, dàn tā zài lánqiú duì lǐ biǎoxiàn chūsè.
    - Although he is short, he performs excellently in the basketball team.
  - 那个(個)女孩比她的妹妹矮一些。
    - Nàgè nǚhái bǐ tā de mèimei ǎi yīxiē.
    - That girl is a bit shorter than her sister.
### 矮 → 矢
- **definition:** arrow
- **pinyin:** shǐ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an arrow
  - **interpretation:** The character 矢(shǐ, 'arrow') is a pictogram representing an arrow. Its resemblance to arrows was more evident in ancient script forms.
- **examples:**
  - 他拉开(開)弓，射出一支矢。
    - Tā lā kāi gōng, shè chū yī zhī shǐ.
    - He drew his bow and released an arrow.
  - 古代战(戰)士用矢，攻破敌(敵)阵(陣)。
    - Gǔdài zhànshì yòng shǐ, gōng pò dí zhèn.
    - Ancient warriors used arrows to breach enemy formations.

### 矮 → 委
- **definition:** to entrust
- **pinyin:** wěi
- **components:**
  - 禾
    - hé
    - grain
  - 女
    - nǚ
    - woman
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 禾 (hé, "grain") + semantic 女 (nǚ, "woman") ->
    - entrust
  - **interpretation:** The character 委 combines 禾 (hé, "grain") as the phonetic component to suggest sound, with 女 (nǚ, "woman") as the semantic component. Originally, the oracle bone representation used 𠃊, depicting a figure kneeling, which aligns with the idea of yielding or resigning. Hence, the combination implies entrusting or assigning a task, as one might with a trusted companion or subordinate.
- **examples:**
  - 我把重要的任务(務)委托(託)给(給)他。
    - Wǒ bǎ zhòngyào de rènwù wěituō gěi tā.
    - I entrusted the important task to him.
  - 经(經)理委任了一名新助理。
    - Jīnglǐ wěirèn le yī míng xīn zhùlǐ.
    - The manager appointed a new assistant.
  - 她被公司委以重任。
    - Tā bèi gōngsī wěi yǐ zhòngrèn.
    - She was entrusted with significant responsibilities by the company.

### 矮 → 委 → 禾
- **definition:** grain
- **pinyin:** hé
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a stalk of grain or cereal plant
  - **interpretation:** The character 禾 (hé, 'grain') visually represents a plant with roots and leaves, focusing on the appearance of staple crops like rice or millet. This clearly conveys its meaning as it resembles the actual shape of a plant grown for its grain.
- **examples:**
  - 收获(穫)的禾香弥(彌)漫在田野中。
    - Shōuhuò de hé xiāng mímàn zài tiányě zhōng.
    - The fragrance of grain filled the fields.
  - 农(農)民辛勤耕作，收割禾穗。
    - Nóngmín xīnqín gēngzuò, shōugē hé suì.
    - The hardworking farmers harvested the ears of grain.
  - 春雨滋润(潤)了禾苗，预(預)示着(著)丰(豐)收。
    - Chūnyǔ zīrùn le hé miáo, yùshì zhe fēngshōu.
    - The spring rain nourished the grain seedlings, foreshadowing a bountiful harvest.

### 矮 → 委 → 女
- **definition:** woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
- **examples:**
  - 那位才华(華)横(橫)溢的女，赢(贏)得了全场(場)注目。
    - Nà wèi cáihuá héngyì de nǚ, yíng dé le quánchǎng zhùmù.
    - That talented woman won everyone's attention.
  - 古时(時)候‘女’常被用来(來)象征(徵)温(溫)柔，而如今它代表独(獨)立自信。
    - Gǔshíhòu ‘nǚ’ cháng bèi yòng lái xiàngzhēng wēnróu, ér rújīn tā dàibiǎo dúlì zìxìn.
    - In ancient times, the character '女' was often used to symbolize gentleness, whereas today it represents independence and confidence.
