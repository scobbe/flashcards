## first love
### chūliàn
---
- **traditional:** 初戀
- **definition:** first love
- **pinyin:** chūliàn
- **components:**
  - 初
    - chū
    - beginning; first
  - 恋(戀)
    - liàn
    - love; long for
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 初 (chū, "beginning; first") + 恋(戀) (liàn, "love; long for")
  - **interpretation:** 初 (chū, "beginning; first") indicates the earliest or first instance of something, establishing the context of 'firstness'. 恋(戀) (liàn, "love; long for") expresses the emotion of deep affection or desire. Together, 初戀 (chūliàn, "first love") suggests the initial experience of love or romantic feelings in a person's life.
- **examples:**
  - 他的初恋(戀)是一位邻(鄰)居家的女孩。
    - Tā de chūliàn shì yī wèi línjū jiā de nǚhái.
    - His first love was a girl from the neighbor's house.
  - 初恋(戀)的回忆(憶)总(總)是最甜美。
    - Chūliàn de huíyì zǒng shì zuì tiánměi.
    - The memories of first love are always the sweetest.
  - 我在十五岁(歲)时(時)遇到我的初恋(戀)。
    - Wǒ zài shíwǔ suì shí yùdào wǒ de chūliàn.
    - I met my first love when I was fifteen.

### 初恋(戀) → 恋(戀)
- **traditional:** 戀
- **definition:** love; long for
- **pinyin:** liàn
- **components:**
  - 䜌
    - lì
    - various, miscellaneous
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 䜌 + semantic 心 ('heart') ->
    - ties of the heart ->
    - romance
  - **interpretation:** 戀(lì) represents 'romance' by combining 心(xīn, 'heart') as the semantic element indicating emotions, with 䜌 as the phonetic component suggesting the sound 'liàn'. The heart tied up or entangled captures the sense of romantic attachment and longing.
  - **simplification:** 戀 was simplified to 恋(戀) by removing the phonetic 䜌 and retaining only the semantic 心(xīn, 'heart').
- **examples:**
  - 他们(們)的恋(戀)情很浪漫。
    - Tāmen de liànqíng hěn làngmàn.
    - Their romance is very romantic.
  - 她在校园(園)里(裡)与(與)他相恋(戀)。
    - Tā zài xiàoyuán lǐ yǔ tā xiāngliàn.
    - She fell in love with him on campus.
  - 他一直希望找到一份真诚(誠)的爱(愛)情。
    - Tā yīzhí xīwàng zhǎodào yī fèn zhēnchéng de àiqíng.
    - He has always hoped to find a sincere love.

### 初恋(戀) → 恋(戀) → 䜌 → 言

- **components:**

    - shé
    - tongue
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:**
    - 舌 (mouth with a forked tongue) with added stroke at top to suggest tongue movement ->
    - speech
  - **interpretation:** The character 言(yán, "speech") depicts the addition of a horizontal stroke to the ancient form of 舌(shé, "tongue") to indicate the motion of the tongue as it moves to articulate speech. This directly connects the organ involved in speaking to the concept of speech itself.
- **examples:**

### 初恋(戀) → 恋(戀) → 心

  - **description:** Depicts a heart
  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, "heart") originally depicted the shape of an actual heart, representing thoughts, emotions, and the core of a person. - **examples:**
