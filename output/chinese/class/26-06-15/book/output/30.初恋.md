## first love
### chūliàn
---
- **traditional:** 初戀
- **definition:** first love
- **pinyin:** chūliàn
- **components:**
  - 初
    - chū
    - beginning; first
  - 恋(戀)
    - liàn
    - love; long for
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 初 (chū, "beginning; first") + 恋(戀) (liàn, "love; long for")
  - **interpretation:** 初 (chū, "beginning; first") indicates the earliest or first instance of something, establishing the context of 'firstness'. 恋(戀) (liàn, "love; long for") expresses the emotion of deep affection or desire. Together, 初戀 (chūliàn, "first love") suggests the initial experience of love or romantic feelings in a person's life.
- **examples:**
