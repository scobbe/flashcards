### xuéxiào
---
- **traditional:** 學校
- **definition:** school
- **pinyin:** xuéxiào
- **components:**
  - 学(學)
    - xué
    - to learn; to study
  - 校
    - xiào
    - school; comparison; military field officer
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 學 (xué, "to learn") + 校 (xiào, "school") =
    - place of learning ->
    - school
  - **interpretation:** 學 (xué, "to learn") depicts a child under a roof, symbolizing receiving knowledge or education. 校 (xiào, "school") originally referred to a place of examination or comparison, where standards are upheld. Together, they create the concept of a structured environment or institution dedicated to learning and education.

### 学(學)校( ) → 学(學)
- **traditional:** 學
- **definition:** to learn; to study
- **pinyin:** xué
- **components:**
  - 爻
    - yáo
    - double cross
  - 六
    - liù
    - six
  - 𦥑
    - kuk
    - hands around
  - 子
    - zǐ
    - child
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 爻 + phonetic 六 ->
    - 𦥑 added ->
    - semantic 子 added ->
    - simplified 六 ->
    - final form
  - **interpretation:** The character 學 (xué, "study") originally combined two phonetic components, 爻 (yáo, "double cross") and 六 (liù, "six"), to represent its sound. Over time, the character evolved with the addition of 𦥑 (components positioned like hands around 爻) and the semantic 子 (zǐ, "child") to specify a child learning or studying, under a cover (冖). In its modern form, 六 is simplified to resemble 冖, which visually merges with the hands around 爻, reflecting the learning process enclosed as children gather to study.
  - **simplification:** "学" is the simplified form of "學." The transformation occurred by merging the visual elements into a compact form, where 冖 serves as a common phonetic and visual element, while the complex structure of 學 was streamlined.
- **examples:**
  - 他( )每( )天( )都( )努( )力( )学(學)习(習)。
    - Tā měitiān dōu nǔlì xuéxí.
    - He studies diligently every day.
  - 我( )在( )北( )京( )大( )学(學)学(學)习(習)中( )文( )。
    - Wǒ zài Běijīng Dàxué xuéxí zhōngwén.
    - I am studying Chinese at Peking University.
  - 这(這)个(個)学(學)校( )有( )很( )多( )优(優)秀( )的( )老( )师(師)。
    - Zhège xuéxiào yǒu hěnduō yōuxiù de lǎoshī.
