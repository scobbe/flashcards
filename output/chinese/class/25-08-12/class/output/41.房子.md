- **pinyin:** fángzi
- **components:**

  - 子

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 房 (fáng, "house") refers to a building or structure for dwelling; 子 (zi, "child; small thing") often indicates a diminutive or noun suffix, implying a specific type or instance.
  - **interpretation:** 房 (fáng, "house") conveys the primary concept of a building or space for living. 子 (zi, "child; small thing") functions as a nominalizing suffix, turning the general idea into a concrete, specific term for 'a house' or 'home'. The use of 子 suggests intimacy or familiarity, as if referring to a 'little home' or a place of belonging.
