## to have fun; to play
### wán
---
- **definition:** to have fun; to play
- **pinyin:** wán
- **components:**
  - 玉
    - yù
    - jade
  - 元
    - yuán
    - origin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 玉 (yù, "jade") + 元 (yuán, "origin") =
    - jade toys (ancient) ->
    - playing, having fun
  - **interpretation:** The character 玩(wán) associates the idea of valuable jade 玉 with 元, reflecting the historical use of jade objects in play or as toys, symbolizing amusement or leisure.

### 玩 → 玉
- **definition:** jade
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a picture of a cong (琮), a sort of ritual jade totem ->
    - dot added to differentiate from 王 (wáng, "king") ->
    - final form 玉 (yù, "jade")
  - **interpretation:** The character represents a picture of a jade cong, emphasizing its role in rituals and its cultural significance. The image eventually included a distinguishing dot to ensure clarity from similar characters like 王, underscoring the value jade held as a material in ancient contexts.

### 玩 → 元
- **definition:** origin
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human head above a horizontal line, symbolizing the origin or the head of something
  - **interpretation:** 元 (yuán, 'origin') represents the head, which is often considered the starting point or leader, aligning with the concept of 'origin'. This setup also connects metaphorically to beginnings or first principles.
- **examples:**
