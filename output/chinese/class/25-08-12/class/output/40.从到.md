- **traditional:** 從到
- **definition:** from … to …
- **pinyin:** cóng … dào …
- **components:**
  - 從
    - cóng
    - follow; from
  - 到
    - dào
    - arrive; to
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 從 (cóng, "follow; from") combined with 到 (dào, "arrive; to") creates a phrase that indicates a span or movement from one point to another.
  - **interpretation:** 從 (cóng, "follow; from") indicates the starting point or origin of a movement or action. 到 (dào, "arrive; to") signifies the end or destination point. Together, this structure describes a range or transition, illustrating the path or timeline from one point (從) to another (到), effectively mapping out a continuous trajectory or sequence.
