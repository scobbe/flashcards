## first time
### dì yī cì
---
- **definition:** first time
- **pinyin:** dì yī cì
- **components:**
  - 第
    - dì
    - prefix for ordinal numbers
  - 一
    - yī
    - one
  - 次
    - cì
    - time; occurrence
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 第 (dì, "prefix for ordinal numbers") is a prefix used to indicate the order or rank in a sequence, such as 'first', 'second', etc. 一 (yī, "one") specifies the number 'one'. 次 (cì, "time; occurrence") refers to an instance or occurrence of an event. Combined, 第 (dì, "prefix for ordinal numbers") + 一 (yī, "one") + 次 (cì, "time; occurrence") literally translates to the 'first instance' or 'first occurrence', thus meaning 'first time'.
  - **interpretation:** 第 (dì, "prefix for ordinal numbers") provides the ordinal sense, indicating position or sequence in a list. Following it, 一 (yī, "one") specifies the position as 'first', establishing the sequence. 次 (cì, "time; occurrence") then gives the notion of an event or instance. This leads to the understanding of 第一次 as 'the first event in a sequence' or simply 'the first time'.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)是我第一次去北京。
    - Zhè shì wǒ dì yī cì qù Běijīng.
    - This is my first time going to Beijing.
  - 他第一次看到大海，感到非常兴(興)奋(奮)。
    - Tā dì yī cì kàn dào dàhǎi, gǎndào fēicháng xīngfèn.
    - He felt very excited when he saw the sea for the first time.
  - 我们(們)第一次见(見)面，是在一个(個)朋友的聚会(會)。
    - Wǒmen dì yī cì jiànmiàn, shì zài yī gè péngyǒu de jùhuì.
    - The first time we met was at a friend's party.
### 第一次 → 第
- **definition:** prefix for ordinal numbers
- **pinyin:** dì
- **components:**
  - 𥫗
    - zhú
    - bamboo
  - 弟
    - dì
    - younger brother
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 𥫗 (zhú, "bamboo") + 弟 (dì) =
    - bamboo strips used for writing ->
    - order/sequencing concept
  - **interpretation:** The character 第(dì) combines the semantic element 𥫗 (zhú, "bamboo") with the phonetic element 弟 (dì). Bamboo was historically used for writing documents, such as lists or sequences, which informs the idea of ordering denoted by 第(dì).
- **examples:**

  - 今天老师(師)讲(講)解第二十五课(課)，布(佈)置第十二个(個)作业(業)。
    - Jīntiān lǎoshī jiǎngjiě dì èr shí wǔ kè, bùzhì dì shí èr gè zuòyè.
    - Today the teacher explained the 25th lesson and assigned the 12th homework.
  - 比赛(賽)中，他拿到第一名。
    - Bǐsài zhōng, tā ná dào dì yī míng.
    - During the competition, he won first place.
### 第一次 → 第 → 𥫗
- **definition:** bamboo
- **pinyin:** zhú
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts two bamboo stalks with leaves ->
    - earlier forms resembled 个 + 个 ->
    - current form resembles ケ + ケ or 亇 + 亇
  - **interpretation:** The character 𥫗 visually represents two bamboo stalks with leaves, capturing the botanical appearance of bamboo. Earlier iterations of the character resembled 个 + 个, indicating the dual stalks, while the current form stylizes this into ケ + ケ or 亇 + 亇, emphasizing the leaf and stalk structure.
- **examples:**
  - 青翠的𥫗点(點)缀(綴)在山坡上。
    - Qīngcuì de zhú diǎn zhuì zài shānpō shàng.
    - The verdant bamboo decorates the hillside.

    - Xiǎo hé biān de gǔ tíng páng yǒu yī cóng zhú, fēng zhōng tā qīngqīng yáoyè.
    - By the small river next to the ancient pavilion, a clump of bamboo gently sways in the wind.

### 第一次 → 次
- **definition:** time; occurrence
- **pinyin:** cì
- **components:**
  - 咨
    - zī
    - to sigh
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a person exhaling ->
    - Possibly original form of 咨 (zī, "to sigh")
  - **interpretation:** 次 (cì, "time; instance") originally depicted a person exhaling, indicative of a sigh or breath, which aligns with its original use related to respiration. Over time, it became abstracted to mean 'time' or 'instance' likely due to repetitive or rhythmic nature of breathing.
- **examples:**
  - 我去过(過)北京很多次。
    - Wǒ qù guò Běijīng hěn duō cì.
    - I have been to Beijing many times.
  - 经(經)过(過)第一次失败(敗)后(後)，他总(總)结(結)经(經)验(驗)。
    - Jīngguò dì yī cì shībài hòu, tā zǒngjié jīngyàn.
    - After failing the first time, he summed up his experience.
  - 这(這)次活动(動)充满(滿)乐(樂)趣。
    - Zhè cì huódòng chōngmǎn lèqù.
    - This event is full of fun.

### 第一次 → 次 → 咨
- **definition:** to sigh
- **pinyin:** zī
- **components:**
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth
  - 次
    - cì
    - next; sequence
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + phonetic 次 (cì) ->
    - to sigh
  - **interpretation:** The component 口 (kǒu, "mouth") suggests the action of speaking or uttering a sound, while 次 (cì) provides the phonetic cue. The combination creates an image of expressing a sound or sigh through the mouth.
- **examples:**

### 第一次 → 次 → 咨 → 口
- **definition:** mouth
- **pinyin:** kǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** The character 口(kǒu, 'mouth') is a straightforward pictogram that visually represents an open mouth. It is a fundamental character in the Chinese language, often used both as an independent word and as a component in more complex characters, lending a semantic association to ideas relating to speech, openings, or enclosures.
- **examples:**

    - Xiǎo nǚhái yǎole yī kǒu píngguǒ, lùchū tiánměi de xiàoróng.
