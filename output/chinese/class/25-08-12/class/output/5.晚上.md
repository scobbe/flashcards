## evening
### wǎnshang
---
- **definition:** evening
- **pinyin:** wǎnshang
- **components:**
  - 晚
    - wǎn
    - evening; late
  - 上
    - shàng
    - up; above; on
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 晚 (wǎn, "evening; late") and 上 (shàng, "up; above; on") combine to depict the concept of evening.
  - **interpretation:** 晚 (wǎn, "evening; late") denotes the time of day after sunset, when activities typically slow down. 上 (shàng, "up; above; on") can give the sense of something being on or upon, here suggesting the time that follows the day being 'upon' us, in the sense of happening now. Together, 晚上 ('evening') describes the period of transition from day to night when it becomes late.
- **examples:**
  - 晚上，我( )们(們)常( )常( )去( )散( )步( )。
