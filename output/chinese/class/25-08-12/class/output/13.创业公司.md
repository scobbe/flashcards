## startup
### chuàngyè gōngsī
---
- **traditional:** 創業公司
- **definition:** startup
- **pinyin:** chuàngyè gōngsī
- **components:**
  - 创(創)
    - chuàng
    - create
  - 业(業)
    - yè
    - business; industry; work; enterprise
  - 公
    - gōng
    - public; common; official
  - 司
    - sī
    - company; manage; control; conduct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 創業 combines 創 (chuàng, 'create') and 業 (yè, 'business'), literally forming 'create business'. 公司 combines 公 (gōng, 'public; common') and 司 (sī, 'company; manage'), meaning 'corporation' or 'company'. Together, it refers to a 'startup', emphasizing the act of creating a new business.
  - **interpretation:** 創 (chuàng, 'create') suggests initiating something new, while 業 (yè, 'business') reflects enterprise or undertaking, directly leading to 'entrepreneurship'. The marriage of these concepts underscores the action of founding a business. 公 (gōng, 'public; common') with 司 (sī, 'company; manage') indicates a structured entity concerned with economic activities, thus forming a 'company'. The complete phrase represents a 'startup', a company at its inception stage focusing on innovative and new ventures.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)家创(創)业(業)公司专(專)注于(於)人工智能领(領)域。
    - Zhè jiā chuàngyè gōngsī zhuānzhù yú réngōng zhìnéng lǐngyù.
    - This startup focuses on the field of artificial intelligence.
  - 他决(決)定加入一个(個)创(創)业(業)公司，以积(積)累更多经(經)验(驗)。
    - Tā juédìng jiārù yīgè chuàngyè gōngsī, yǐ jīlěi gèng duō jīngyàn.
    - He decided to join a startup to gain more experience.
  - 许(許)多创(創)业(業)公司都在快速增长(長)。
    - Xǔduō chuàngyè gōngsī dōu zài kuàisù zēngzhǎng.
    - Many startups are experiencing rapid growth.
