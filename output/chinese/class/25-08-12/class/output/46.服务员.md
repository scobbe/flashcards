## waiter/waitress
### fúwùyuán
---
- **traditional:** 服務員
- **definition:** waiter/waitress
- **pinyin:** fúwùyuán
- **components:**
  - 服
    - fú
    - serve; clothes; obey
  - 務
    - wù
    - duties; affairs; business
  - 員
    - yuán
    - member; person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 服 (fú, "serve") + 務 (wù, "duties; affairs") + 員 (yuán, "member; person") =
    - a person (員) who serves (服) duties (務) ->
    - waiter/waitress
  - **interpretation:** 服 (fú, "serve") implies attending to others' needs. 務 (wù, "duties; affairs") emphasizes the responsibilities or tasks involved. 員 (yuán, "member; person") specifies someone engaged in a particular role or job. Together, 服務員 (fúwùyuán) represents a person tasked with serving others by performing specific duties, hence a waiter or waitress, focusing on the service aspect in hospitality.
- **examples:**
  - 服务(務)员(員)正在为(為)我们(們)上菜。
    - Fúwùyuán zhèngzài wèi wǒmen shàng cài.
    - The waiter is serving us dishes.
  - 我叫服务(務)员(員)过(過)来(來)，然后(後)点(點)了饮(飲)料。
    - Wǒ jiào fúwùyuán guòlái, ránhòu diǎnle yǐnliào.
    - I called the waiter over and then ordered drinks.
  - 服务(務)员(員)给(給)了我们(們)很好的建议(議)。
    - Fúwùyuán gěile wǒmen hěn hǎo de jiànyì.
    - The waiter gave us great advice.
### 服务(務)员(員) → 服
- **definition:** serve; clothes; obey
- **pinyin:** fú
- **components:**
  - 凡
    - fán
    - tray
  - 𠬝
    - [not standardized]
    - to subdue
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 凡 ('tray') + phonetic 𠬝 ('to subdue') ->
    - to serve; 凡 corrupted to 月
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a tray, 凡 (fán, 'tray'), as a symbol for offering or serving, combined with 𠬝 (a pictogram for 'to subdue') to convey the act of serving or obeying. Over time, the component 凡 (fán) became corrupted into a form resembling 月 (yuè, 'moon'), which is retained in the final character. The concept of clothing is related to serving in ancient contexts where clothing represented a person's role or duty.
- **examples:**

- **pinyin:** wù
- **components:**
  - 敄
    - wù
    - choose, select
  - 力
    - lì

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 敄 + semantic 力 ('strength') ->

  - **interpretation:** Phonetic 敄 lends the sound, while the semantic component 力 (lì, 'strength') suggests the exertion of effort needed to manage 'affairs' or 'duties.' Essentially, it embodies attending to affairs with effort or strength, linking to the sense of 'to attend to.'

### 服务(務)员(員) → 員

- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**

  - **description:**

- **examples:**
