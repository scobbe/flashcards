## Saturday
### xīngqīliù
---
- **definition:** Saturday
- **pinyin:** xīngqīliù
- **components:**
  - 星
    - xīng
    - star
  - 期
    - qī
    - period; time
  - 六
    - liù
    - six
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 星 (xīng, "star") + 期 (qī, "period; time") + 六 (liù, "six") combine to denote the sixth day of the week, Saturday. The naming convention for days of the week in Chinese is based on a numerical sequence starting from Sunday.
  - **interpretation:** 星期六(xīngqīliù, "Saturday") follows the sequential naming pattern used for days of the week in Chinese, with 六 (liù, "six") indicating it is the sixth day.
- **examples:**
  - 星期六，我不上班。
    - Xīngqīliù, wǒ bù shàngbān.
    - I don't work on Saturday.
  - 星期六，我和朋友一起去郊游(遊)。
    - Xīngqīliù, wǒ hé péngyǒu yīqǐ qù jiāoyóu.
    - On Saturday, I go for a picnic with my friends.
  - 星期六，我喜欢(歡)睡到自然醒。
    - Xīngqīliù, wǒ xǐhuān shuì dào zìrán xǐng.
    - On Saturday, I like to sleep in.
### 星期六 → 星
- **definition:** star
- **pinyin:** xīng
- **components:**
  - 晶
    - jīng
    - twinkling stars
  - 生
    - shēng
    - life; birth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 晶 (jīng, 'twinkling stars') + phonetic 生 (shēng) ->
    - meaning
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted twinkling stars with 晶 (jīng, 'twinkling stars') indicating the twinkling or shining quality. The phonetic component 生 (shēng) was added to support the pronunciation but ultimately did not carry a separate semantic load. This compounded form solidified the modern representation of 'star'.
- **examples:**
  - 夜空中有许(許)多闪(閃)烁(爍)的星星。
    - Yèkōng zhōng yǒu xǔduō shǎnshuò de xīngxing.
    - There are many twinkling stars in the night sky.
  - 她的眼睛像星星一样(樣)明亮。
    - Tā de yǎnjing xiàng xīngxing yīyàng míngliàng.
    - Her eyes are as bright as stars.
  - 孩子们(們)在数(數)星星。
    - Háizimen zài shǔ xīngxing.
    - The children are counting stars.

### 星期六 → 星 → 晶 → 日
- **definition:** sun
- **pinyin:** rì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts the sun; a dot or line added in the center to distinguish from similar characters e.g. 囗
  - **interpretation:** As a pictogram, 日(rì, 'sun') directly represents the sun. In ancient times, the added dot helped visually differentiate it from other characters. This is analogous to the astronomical symbol ☉ and Egyptian hieroglyph 𓇳, both depicting the sun.
- **examples:**
  - 晨曦来(來)临(臨)时(時)，日出东(東)方。
    - Chénxī láilín shí, rìchū dōngfāng.

    - Wēnnuǎn de rìguāng sǎ mǎn dàdì.
    - The warm sunlight spreads over the earth.
  - 中午时(時)分，日高挂(掛)天。
    - Zhōngwǔ shífēn, rì gāo guà tiān.

### 星期六 → 期
- **definition:** period; time
- **pinyin:** qī
- **components:**
  - 其
    - qí
    - his
  - 月
    - yuè
    - moon
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - phonetic 其 (qí) + semantic 月 (yuè, "moon") ->
    - changing moon marks passage of time ->
    - period of time
  - **interpretation:** The character 期(qī, "period") combines the phonetic 其 (qí, "his") with the semantic 月 (yuè, "moon") to signify the phases of the moon, which naturally represent cycles and periods of time. The moon's regular changes provide a clear, cyclical marker of time's passage.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)个(個)项(項)目需要完成的时(時)间(間)是三个(個)月。
    - Zhège xiàngmù xūyào wánchéng de shíjiān shì sān gè yuè.
    - The period required to complete this project is three months.
  - 在考试(試)期间(間)，请(請)保持安静(靜)。
    - Zài kǎoshì qījiān, qǐng bǎochí ānjìng.
    - During the exam period, please remain quiet.
  - 我们(們)在下一个(個)阶(階)段继(繼)续(續)努力。
    - Wǒmen zài xià yī gè jiēduàn jìxù nǔlì.

### 星期六 → 期 → 其
- **definition:** his
- **pinyin:** qí
- **components:**
  - 丌
    - qí
    - stand
  - 箕
    - jī
    - basket
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠀠 (basket) ->
    - 𠔝 with added 丌 (stand, phonetic) ->
    - borrowed as pronoun
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicted a basket (𠀠), which later included a stand (丌) beneath it to stabilize the image on bronze inscriptions, forming 𠔝. This combination was later borrowed for its sound to represent the pronoun 'his, her, its', and the basket meaning was reassigned to 箕 (jī, 'basket').
- **examples:**
  - 他把其思绪(緒)整理了一遍。
    - Tā bǎ qí sīxù zhěnglǐ le yī biàn.
    - He organized his thoughts once.
  - 其琴声(聲)如水般柔软(軟)。
    - Qí qín shēng rú shuǐ bān róuruǎn.
    - His playing is as soft as water.
  - 其计(計)划(劃)得到了同事们(們)的支持。
    - Qí jìhuà dédào le tóngshìmen de zhīchí.
    - His plan received the support of his colleagues.

  - 其

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic 其 (qí, "his") ->

  - **interpretation:** The character 箕(jī, "basket") combines 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") as the semantic component, indicating that the item is made of bamboo, with 其 (qí, "his") serving as the phonetic component to provide the sound. ### 星期六 → 六
- **definition:** six
- **pinyin:** liù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Ancient counting symbol ->
    - character for 'six'
  - **interpretation:** 六 (liù, 'six') is a pictogram that may originally have depicted a shed, but was primarily used as an ancient counting symbol. The form resembles primitive tally markings used for numerical purposes.
- **examples:**
  - 我有六个(個)苹(蘋)果。

    - Liù zhī māo zài huāyuán lǐ wánshuǎ.
