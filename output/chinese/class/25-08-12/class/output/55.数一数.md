- **traditional:** 數一數
- **definition:** count
- **pinyin:** shǔ yī shǔ
- **components:**

    - shǔ

    - yī
    - one; single

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 數 (shǔ, "count") + 一 (yī, "one/single") + 數 (shǔ, "count")
  - **interpretation:** 數 (shǔ, "count") expresses the core action of recounting or numbering. When paired with 一 (yī, "one/single") and repeated as 數 (shǔ), the phrase creates an emphasis on the act of counting, as if to say "count each one attentively." Here, 一 (yī, "one/single") highlights the precision in the act of counting, ensuring attention to each single unit.
