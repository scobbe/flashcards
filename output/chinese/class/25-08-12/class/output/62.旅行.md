## to travel
### lǚxíng
---
- **definition:** to travel
- **pinyin:** lǚxíng
- **components:**
  - 旅
    - lǚ
    - trip; journey; travel
  - 行
    - xíng
    - to go; to walk; behavior; conduct
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 旅 (lǚ, "trip") refers to a journey or expedition. 行 (xíng, "to go; to walk") involves the action of moving or proceeding. Together, 旅行 (lǚxíng) literally means "to go on a trip," corresponding directly to the meaning "to travel."
- **examples:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)旅行。
    - Wǒ xǐhuān lǚxíng.
    - I like to travel.
  - 我们(們)计(計)划(劃)下个(個)月去旅行。
    - Wǒmen jìhuà xià gè yuè qù lǚxíng.
    - We are planning to travel next month.
  - 他想去欧(歐)洲旅行，体(體)验(驗)不同的文化。
    - Tā xiǎng qù Ōuzhōu lǚxíng, tǐyàn bùtóng de wénhuà.
    - He wants to travel to Europe to experience different cultures.

### 旅行 → 旅 → 㫃
- **definition:** flag
- **pinyin:** bì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a flag on a pole with a decoration on top ->
    - stylized as 方(㫃)
  - **interpretation:** The character 㫃 originally depicted a flag on a pole, complete with a decorative element on top. Over time, it was stylized into its current form with the flag and pole disconnected and the image abstracted as 方(㫃). Although it shares visual similarities with characters like 朝 (cháo, "morning"), it is unrelated to them in meaning.
- **examples:**
  - 撤下军(軍)㫃。
    - Chèxià jūn bì.
    - Take down the military flag.
  - 他站在城楼(樓)上挥(揮)动(動)㫃。
    - Tā zhàn zài chénglóu shàng huīdòng bì.
    - He waved the flag on the city gate tower.
  - 大风(風)把祭典上的㫃吹得猎(獵)猎(獵)作响(響)。
    - Dàfēng bǎ jìdiǎn shàng de bì chuī dé liè liè zuò xiǎng.
    - The strong wind made the flag at the ceremony flutter noisily.

### 旅行 → 旅 → 从(從)
- **traditional:** 從
- **definition:** two people
- **pinyin:** cóng
- **components:**
  - 辵
    - chuò
    - walk; movement
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 辵 ('walk; movement') + phonetic 从 (sound) ->
    - follow
  - **interpretation:** 從 (cóng, 'to follow') combines the idea of 'walking' or 'movement' from 辵 (chuò, 'walk; movement') with the sound symbolized by 从 (cóng, 'follow'), capturing the concept of moving along a path under guidance or direction.
  - **simplification:** 从 is a variant form directly used as the simplified form, adopting the earlier, less complex writing as the standard.
- **examples:**
  - 你需要从(從)这(這)里(裡)开(開)始。
    - Nǐ xūyào cóng zhèlǐ kāishǐ.
    - You need to start from here.
  - 她从(從)小就喜欢(歡)画(畫)画(畫)。
    - Tā cóngxiǎo jiù xǐhuān huàhuà.
    - She has liked drawing since she was a child.
  - 所有东(東)西都从(從)他的袋子里(裡)掉出来(來)了。
    - Suǒyǒu dōngxī dōu cóng tā de dàizi lǐ diào chūláile.
    - Everything fell out of his bag.

### 旅行 → 旅 → 从(從) → 辵
- **definition:** walk; movement
- **pinyin:** chuò
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - walk
  - 止
    - zhǐ
    - foot
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 彳 (chì, "walk") + 止 (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop") ->
    - 𣥆 ->
    - stylized as 龰 ->
    - 辵
  - **interpretation:** The character 辵 combines 彳 (chì, "walk"), which represents movement, with 止 (zhǐ, "foot" or "to stop"), suggesting the concept of stepping or traveling forward. This character represents the idea of movement, particularly the action of walking or traveling, as originally depicted by the more conservative form 𣥆. Over time, the bottom component has been stylized as 龰, forming the current representation.
- **examples:**
  - 他辵在乡(鄉)间(間)的小路上。
    - Tā chuò zài xiāngjiān de xiǎolù shàng.
    - He walks on the rural path.
  - 晨光洒(灑)满(滿)大地，他辵出门(門)开(開)始旅程。
    - Chén guāng sǎ mǎn dàdì, tā chuò chūmén kāishǐ lǚchéng.

- **pinyin:** zhǐ

    - foot
  - 龰
    - yù
    - stylized feet
  - 手
    - shǒu

  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**

### 旅行 → 旅 → 从(從) → 辵 → 止 → 龰
- **definition:** stylized feet
- **pinyin:** yù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a stylized footprint pointing upward ->
    - originally meaning 'foot' ->
    - later forms like 趾 (zhǐ, 'toe') refer back to original meaning
  - **interpretation:** 龰 (yù, 'stylized feet') is a pictogram showing a footprint pointing up, originally indicating the concept of a foot. The character 趾 (zhǐ, 'toe') evolved from this and retains the reference to the original meaning. It visually resembles a sole with three toes, similar to representations of the human hand in characters like 爪 (zhuǎ, 'claw'), 寸 (cùn, 'inch'), and 又 (yòu, 'again').

### 旅行 → 行
- **definition:** to go; to walk; behavior; conduct
- **pinyin:** xíng
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - step
  - 亍
    - chù
    - step
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Originally a symmetric depiction of a street intersection; simplified asymmetrically ->
    - 彳 (chì, "step") + 亍 (chù, "step")
  - **interpretation:** The character 行(xíng) visually represented a street intersection, with two symmetrical parts symbolizing the act of going or walking along paths. Despite its current breakdown into separate elements, it was originally a unified pictogram rather than a compound.
- **examples:**
  - 我行往学(學)校。
    - Wǒ xíng wǎng xuéxiào.
    - I go to school.
  - 他行于(於)青山之间(間)，徒步旅行至古城。
    - Tā xíng yú qīngshān zhī jiān, túbù lǚxíng zhì gǔchéng.
    - He walks among the green mountains and hikes to the ancient city.
  - 他们(們)行在林间(間)小路上。
    - Tāmen xíng zài línjiān xiǎolù shàng.
    - They walk on the forest trail.
