## hospital
### yīyuàn
---
- **traditional:** 醫院
- **definition:** hospital
- **pinyin:** yīyuàn
- **components:**
  - 医(醫)
    - yī
    - medicine; doctor
  - 院
    - yuàn
    - courtyard; institution
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 医(醫) (yī, "medicine; doctor") + 院 (yuàn, "courtyard; institution") =
    - an institution for practicing medicine ->
    - hospital
  - **interpretation:** A 院 (yuàn, "institution") where 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") is practiced is a place where people go to receive medical treatment and healthcare.
- **examples:**
  - 我去医(醫)院，看医(醫)生。
    - Wǒ qù yīyuàn, kàn yīshēng.
    - I go to the hospital to see a doctor.
  - 医(醫)院在城市中心，设(設)备(備)先进(進)。
    - Yīyuàn zài chéngshì zhōngxīn, shèbèi xiānjìn.
    - The hospital is located in the city center with advanced equipment.
  - 医(醫)生在医(醫)院救治病人。
    - Yīshēng zài yīyuàn jiùzhì bìngrén.
    - Doctors treat patients in the hospital.
### 医(醫)院 → 医(醫)
- **traditional:** 醫
- **definition:** medicine; doctor
- **pinyin:** yī
- **components:**
  - 匸
    - xì
    - that which hides; that which stores
  - 矢
    - shǐ
    - arrow
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 匸 (that which hides) + 矢 (arrow) =
    - a hidden arrow ->
    - metaphor for treating (hidden dangers addressed by medicine)
  - **interpretation:** The character combines the concepts of 'hide' with 'arrow', suggesting the idea of dealing with hidden threats or illnesses, akin to specialized knowledge required for healing.
  - **simplification:** 醫 was simplified by using the right side 耂 (lǎo), an abbreviated structure representing the character.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一名优(優)秀的医(醫)生。
    - Tā shì yì míng yōuxiù de yīshēng.
    - He is an excellent doctor.
  - 我感冒了，需要去医(醫)院。
    - Wǒ gǎnmào le, xūyào qù yīyuàn.
    - I caught a cold and need to go to the hospital.
  - 这(這)家医(醫)院以先进(進)的医(醫)疗(療)技术(術)闻(聞)名。
    - Zhè jiā yīyuàn yǐ xiānjìn de yīliáo jìshù wénmíng.
    - This hospital is famous for its advanced medical technology.

### 医(醫)院 → 医(醫) → 匸
- **definition:** that which hides; that which stores
- **pinyin:** xì
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - 𠃊 (symbolic graphic) ->
    - depicts an enclosure hiding something or someone ->
    - evolved to represent a concealed space or item
  - **interpretation:** The character 匸(xì, 'box') is a pictogram representing a concealed or enclosed space. Its graphical form suggests something contained or hidden, which aligns with its meaning as a 'box' or an enclosure. Unlike other related pictograms that depict physical containers, 匸 emphasizes the aspect of concealment or secrecy.
- **examples:**
  - 他把匸搬到了房间(間)角落。
    - Tā bǎ xì bān dào le fángjiān jiǎoluò.
    - He moved the box to the corner of the room.
  - 我们(們)在仓(倉)库(庫)找到了匸，里(裡)面装(裝)有秘密文件。
    - Wǒmen zài cāngkù zhǎodào le xì, lǐmiàn zhuāng yǒu mìmì wénjiàn.
    - We found the box in the warehouse, and it contained secret documents.
  - 工厂(廠)工人把旧(舊)匸清理了，然后(後)再利用。
    - Gōngchǎng gōngrén bǎ jiù xì qīnglǐ le, ránhòu zài lìyòng.
    - Factory workers cleaned the old box and then reused it.

### 医(醫)院 → 院
- **definition:** courtyard; institution
- **pinyin:** yuàn
- **components:**
  - 阝
    - fù
    - hill; mound
  - 完
    - wán
    - complete
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 阝 (fù, "hill; mound") + phonetic 完 (wán, "complete") ->
    - courtyard
  - **interpretation:** The character 院(yuàn, "courtyard; institution") is formed by the semantic component 阝 (fù, "hill; mound"), suggesting an enclosed or defined space, combined with the phonetic component 完 (wán, "complete"), contributing the sound. The idea of a 'courtyard' as a defined, enclosed space aligns with the semantic implication of 阝. In extended use, the character refers to institutions, which are also defined spaces pertaining to specific functions.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)在医(醫)院对(對)面吃饭(飯)。
    - Wǒmen zài yīyuàn duìmiàn chīfàn.
    - We are eating opposite the hospital.
  - 学(學)校的院子里(裡)有很多花。
    - Xuéxiào de yuànzi lǐ yǒu hěn duō huā.
    - There are many flowers in the school's courtyard.
  - 这(這)家研究院专(專)注于(於)环(環)保技术(術)。
    - Zhè jiā yánjiūyuàn zhuānzhù yú huánbǎo jìshù.
    - This research institute focuses on environmental technology.

### 医(醫)院 → 院 → 阝
- **definition:** hill; mound
- **pinyin:** fù
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a small mound or hillock.
  - **interpretation:** The character 阝 (fù, 'mound') visually represents a small elevation of land, resembling a hillside or embankment.
- **examples:**
  - 小山顶(頂)上耸(聳)立着(著)阝(阜)。
    - Xiǎo shāndǐng shàng sǒnglì zhe fù.
    - A small mound stands atop the hill.
  - 山坡上长(長)满(滿)了绿(綠)色的草丛(叢)，山丘边(邊)坐落着(著)一座古老的阝(阜)。
    - Shānpō shàng cháng mǎn le lǜsè de cǎocóng, shānqiū biān zuòluò zhe yī zuò gǔlǎo de fù.
    - The hillside is covered with green grass, and an ancient mound sits by the hill.
  - 远(遠)处(處)沙丘边(邊)隐(隱)约(約)可见(見)一堆阝(阜)，仿(彷)佛(彿)历(歷)史的见(見)证(證)。
    - Yuǎnchù shāqiū biān yǐnyuē kějiàn yī duī fù, fǎngfú lìshǐ de jiànzhèng.
    - In the distance, a pile of mounds is barely visible by the sand dunes, as if witnessing history.
### 医(醫)院 → 院 → 完
- **definition:** complete
- **pinyin:** wán
- **components:**
  - 宀
    - mián
    - roof
  - 元
    - yuán
    - origin
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 宀 (roof) + phonetic 元 (sound) ->
    - finished; intact
  - **interpretation:** The character 完 (wán, "finished; intact") combines the semantic element 宀 (mián, "roof"), suggesting a protective or structural component, and the phonetic element 元 (yuán, "origin"), indicating the pronunciation. Together, they imply a structure that is completed and covered, hence the sense of something being "finished" or "intact."
- **examples:**

  - 晚餐做好了，可以开(開)饭(飯)了。
    - Wǎncān zuò hǎo le, kěyǐ kāifàn le.
    - Dinner is finished and ready to be served.
  - 我的工作还(還)没(沒)完，需要加班。
    - Wǒ de gōngzuò hái méi wán, xūyào jiābān.

### 医(醫)院 → 院 → 完 → 宀
- **definition:** roof
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.
- **examples:**
  - 含有宀的汉(漢)字代表家的温(溫)暖。
    - Hán yǒu mián de hànzì dàibiǎo jiā de wēnnuǎn.
    - Chinese characters that include the radical '宀' represent the warmth of home.
  - 屋顶(頂)遮风(風)挡(擋)雨，家园(園)散发(發)温(溫)馨。
    - Wūdǐng zhē fēng dǎng yǔ, jiāyuán sànfā wēnxīn.
    - The roof shields from wind and rain, and the home radiates warmth.

### 医(醫)院 → 院 → 完 → 元
- **definition:** origin
- **pinyin:** yuán
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a human head above a horizontal line, symbolizing the origin or the head of something
  - **interpretation:** 元 (yuán, 'origin') represents the head, which is often considered the starting point or leader, aligning with the concept of 'origin'. This setup also connects metaphorically to beginnings or first principles.
- **examples:**
  - 万(萬)物之元来(來)自于(於)混沌。
    - Wànwù zhī yuán láizì yú húndùn.
    - The origin of all things comes from chaos.
  - 道家的理念追寻(尋)心灵(靈)的元。
    - Dàojiā de lǐniàn zhuīxún xīnlíng de yuán.
    - Taoist philosophy seeks the origin of the soul.
