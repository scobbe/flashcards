## Xi’an city; capital of Shaanxi Province
### Xī’ān
---
- **definition:** Xi’an city; capital of Shaanxi Province
- **pinyin:** Xī’ān
- **components:**
  - 西
    - xī
    - west; western
  - 安
    - ān
    - peace; safe; calm; tranquil
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 西 (xī, "west") + 安 (ān, "peace") =
    - Xi'an, a historical city in western China known for being a peaceful and important ancient capital.
  - **interpretation:** 西 (xī, "west") indicates the geographic location in western China. 安 (ān, "peace") traditionally signifies tranquility and stability. Combining these, 西安 suggests a place known for its peacefulness in the west. The name reflects the city's historical status as a key capital in China's west, where it was a center of political stability and cultural development.

### 西安 → 西
- **definition:** west; western
- **pinyin:** xī
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a bag or basket ->
    - borrowed for phonetic value ->
    - meaning 'west'
  - **interpretation:** The character originally depicted a bag or basket, which was later borrowed for its phonetic value to represent the direction 'west'. This borrowing relied on the resemblance of the shape and was not directly semantically related to the concept of 'west'. Hence, the meaning 'west' is abstractly tied to its original pictographic form.

### 西安 → 安 → 宀
- **definition:** house
- **pinyin:** mián
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a house with four walls ->
    - roof
  - **interpretation:** 宀(mián, "roof") is a pictogram representing a house with four walls. It emphasizes an enclosed space, distinguishing it from similar structures like 广(guǎng, "broad") which depicts a building with three walls. This makes 宀 a logical choice for representing a roof in various characters.

### 西安 → 安 → 女
- **definition:** a kneeling woman
- **pinyin:** nǚ
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a woman kneeling or standing ->
    - enclosed area as remnant of right breast
  - **interpretation:** The character 女(nǚ, "woman") is a pictogram originally depicting a woman with highlighted bodily features, such as the breasts, symbolizing femininity. Its orientation changed from the Oracle Bone script to face right, simplifying over time while still retaining the essence of its original depiction.
