## personnel; staff
### rényuán
---
- **traditional:** 人員
- **definition:** personnel; staff
- **pinyin:** rényuán
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 员(員)
    - yuán
    - member; employee; personnel; staff
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人 (rén, "person") refers to individual people, while 員 (yuán, "member") denotes a member of a group or organization. Together, 人( )員(员) (rényuán, "personnel; staff") refers to the people who are members of a workforce or staff.
  - **interpretation:** 人 (rén, "person") represents individuals, and 員 (yuán, "member") implies belonging to a group or organization. Thus, 人( )員(员) (rényuán) signifies individuals collectively as members of an organization's staff. This combination highlights the idea of individuals functioning collectively within the framework of a workforce or team.

### 人( )员(員) → 员(員)

- **pinyin:** yuán
- **components:**
  - 鼎
    - dǐng

- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**

    - roundness ->

  - **interpretation:** 〇 indicates the top edge of a 鼎 (dǐng, "ding") representing roundness, which metaphorically connects to the idea of a group of members forming a unified whole.
  - **simplification:** 員: 貝 (bèi, "cowry") was simplified to 贝(貝) in the character 員 to form the simplified character 员, which visually reduces the strokes.
