## to exchange; to change
### huàn
---
- **traditional:** 換
- **definition:** to exchange; to change
- **pinyin:** huàn
- **components:**
  - 手
    - shǒu
    - hand
  - 奐
    - huàn
    - abundant
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 手 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 奐 (huàn)
  - **interpretation:** The character 换(換) uses the semantic part 手 (shǒu, "hand") to signify an action typically performed by the hand, such as exchanging or swapping. The phonetic 奐 (huàn) provides the pronunciation, and together they indicate the act of using hands to make an exchange.
  - **simplification:** The traditional form 換 switched from 手 (shǒu, "hand") to 扌, the simplified hand radical, and retained 奐 (huàn) for phonetic purpose.
- **examples:**
  - 请(請)帮(幫)我换(換)桌子。
    - Qǐng bāng wǒ huàn zhuōzi.
    - Please help me exchange the table.
  - 如果你不喜欢(歡)这(這)件衣服，可以去店里(裡)换(換)另一件。
    - Rúguǒ nǐ bù xǐhuān zhè jiàn yīfu, kěyǐ qù diàn lǐ huàn lìng yī jiàn.
    - If you don't like this piece of clothing, you can go to the store to exchange it for another.
  - 我每两(兩)个(個)月换(換)一次手机(機)壳(殼)。
    - Wǒ měi liǎng gè yuè huàn yīcì shǒujī ké.
    - I change my phone case every two months.
### 换(換) → 手
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** shǒu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a hand with fingers; top stroke is a bent over middle finger, horizontal strokes represent fingers
  - **interpretation:** The character 手 (shǒu, "hand") is a direct pictorial representation of a human hand with all five fingers, which distinguishes it from similar characters representing claws or simplified fingers. This visually conveys its meaning as a hand clearly.
- **examples:**
  - 他用手写(寫)字。
    - Tā yòng shǒu xiě zì.
    - He writes with his hand.
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)牵(牽)着(著)我的手走路。
    - Māmā qiān zhe wǒ de shǒu zǒu lù.
    - Mom holds my hand while walking.
  - 他的手很温(溫)暖，带(帶)给(給)我力量。
    - Tā de shǒu hěn wēn nuǎn, dài gěi wǒ lìliàng.
    - His hand is very warm, giving me strength.

### 换(換) → 奐
- **definition:** abundant
- **pinyin:** huàn
- **components:**
  - 夐
    - xiòng
    - eminent
  - 廾
    - gǒng
    - hands
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 夐 (phonetic, indicating 'eminent') + 廾 (hands, 'numerous') ->
    - 'brilliant', 'abundant'
  - **interpretation:** The character 奐 (huàn, 'eminent; abundant') combines the phonetic element 夐 with the semantic element 廾, representing hands, which implies abundance or copiousness. This idea is further supported by oracle bone scripts where the hands symbolized numerous objects. While it also represented the idea of 'brilliant' due to its ancient context of authority, it was used phonetically in the context of 換 (huàn, 'change'). The ideogrammatic explanation suggests hands opening a yurt to let in sunlight, further linking to 'brilliant'.
  - **simplification:** Simplified to 奂 by removing strokes for ease of writing.
### 换(換) → 奐 → 夐
- **definition:** eminent
- **pinyin:** xiòng
- **components:**
  - 穴
    - xué
    - cave; yurt
  - 目
    - mù
    - eye
  - 支
    - zhī
    - to support
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 穴 (xué, 'cave; yurt') with 目 (mù, 'eye') + a hand with a stick like 支 (zhī, 'to support') indicating authority ->
    - concept of 'eminent'
  - **interpretation:** The character 夐 combines the elements of a cave or yurt, an eye, and a hand holding a stick. These components collectively symbolize a figure of authority, representing the idea of 'eminent'. The presence of a hand with a stick suggests control or command, reinforcing the leader or prominent person's status.
### 换(換) → 奐 → 夐 → 穴
- **definition:** cave; yurt
- **pinyin:** xué
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a cave or hole, resembling a small dwelling or opening
  - **interpretation:** The character 穴 (xué, "cave") visually represents a hole or cave with an open entry, suggesting a sheltered space similar to a primitive dwelling or burrow.
- **examples:**
  - 山里(裡)隐(隱)匿着(著)一个(個)深深的穴。
    - Shān lǐ yǐn nì zhe yí gè shēn shēn de xué.
    - A deep cave is hidden in the mountains.
  - 探险(險)家在悬(懸)崖下发(發)现(現)了一个(個)幽静(靜)的穴，据(據)说(說)里(裡)面住着(著)古老的精灵(靈)。
    - Tàn xiǎnjiā zài xuán yá xià fāxiàn le yí gè yōu jìng de xué, jù shuō lǐmiàn zhùzhe gǔlǎo de jīng líng.
    - The explorer discovered a quiet cave beneath the cliff, and it is said that ancient spirits dwell inside.
  - 河岸边(邊)的岩石上有一个(個)小穴。
    - Hé àn biān de yán shí shàng yǒu yí gè xiǎo xué.
