## to form a line; to queue up
### páiduì
---
- **traditional:** 排隊
- **definition:** to form a line; to queue up
- **pinyin:** páiduì
- **components:**
  - 排
    - pái
    - arrange; line up
  - 队(隊)
    - duì
    - team; line; rank
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 排 (pái, "arrange; line up") + 隊 (duì, "team; line; rank")
  - **interpretation:** 排 (pái, "arrange; line up") denotes the action of arranging or aligning items or people. 隊 (duì, "team; line; rank") refers to a collection of people or objects organized into a sequence or formation. Combining them, 排( )队(隊) (páiduì) means organizing individuals into a line, emphasizing orderly arrangement—a queue or process of lining up people.
