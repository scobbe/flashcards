## a row of people; line
### duì
---
- **traditional:** 隊
- **definition:** a row of people; line
- **pinyin:** duì
- **components:**
  - 阜
    - fù
    - mound
  - 㒸
    - suì
    - examine, scrutinize
  - 人
    - rén
    - people
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 阜 (fù, "mound") + 㒸 (suì) =
    - semantic element for 'many' + phonetic providing sound
  - **interpretation:** The character originally incorporates the idea of 'many' from the 阜 (fù, "mound") meaning, suggesting a group or collection, while 㒸 (suì) serves as a phonetic guide. Together, they create the notion of gathering, as in a 'team' or 'row'.
  - **simplification:** In simplified Chinese, 队(隊) uses 阜 (fù, "mound") with 人 (rén, "people") to emphasize gathering of people, aligning it more with the modern meaning of 'team' or 'row'.

### 队(隊) → 㒸
- **definition:** examine, scrutinize
- **pinyin:** suì
- **components:**
  - 豕
    - shǐ
    - pig
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 丷 (divide) + phonetic 豕 (suì, pig) ->
    - examine, scrutinize
  - **interpretation:** The character 㒸 combines the semantic 丷, which suggests division or separation, with the phonetic 豕 (suì, 'pig') that only contributes the sound. The process of examination or scrutiny involves dividing or breaking down information, which explains the semantic component's involvement. The pig is used solely for its phonetic contribution and does not influence the meaning.
