## be excited
### jīdòng
---

- **definition:** be excited
- **pinyin:** jīdòng
- **components:**

  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 激 (jī, "stimulate") refers to inciting or provoking energy or emotion; 動 (dòng, "move") involves physical or emotional movement or change.
