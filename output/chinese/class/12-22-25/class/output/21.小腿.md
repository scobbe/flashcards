## 小腿
---
- **pinyin:** xiǎo tuǐ
- **definition:** lower leg; calf
- **characters:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small, little; young; minor, petty
  - 腿
    - tuǐ
    - leg; leg of a table, furniture leg; drumstick, leg meat, ham
- **etymology:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") marks the lesser segment within a natural pair, so 小腿 specifies the narrower, distal part of 腿 (tuǐ, "leg") below the knee, contrasted with 大 (dà, "big") + 腿 for the thigh. This size-based anatomical partition (cf. classical 胫 for “shin”) underlies colloquial formations like 小腿肚 for the calf muscle.
- **examples:**
  - 他的小腿被划伤了(他的小腿被劃傷了)。
    - tā de xiǎo tuǐ bèi huá shāng le
    - His lower leg was cut.
  - 跑步后(跑步後)，我的小腿很酸(我的小腿很酸)。
    - pǎo bù hòu, wǒ de xiǎo tuǐ hěn suān
    - After running, my calves are very sore.
