## 伤心(傷心)
---
- **pinyin:** shāng xīn
- **definition:** sad
- **characters:**
  - 伤(傷)
    - shāng
    - to injure, hurt; to damage, impair; wound, injury; to sadden, grieve
  - 心
    - xīn
    - heart (organ); heart, mind, feelings; intention, thought; core, center
- **etymology:** Rooted in the classical view that 心 (xīn, "heart; mind") is the seat of feeling and thought, 伤(傷) (shāng, "to injure; to grieve") frames sadness as a literal wound to that inner organ—a long-standing metaphor in literature and traditional medicine (“emotions injure the heart”)—so 伤心 conveys heartbreak as damage to one’s emotional core rather than a fleeting mood.
- **examples:**
  - 听到这个消息(聽到這個消息)，我很伤心(我很傷心)。
    - tīng dào zhège xiāo xī, wǒ hěn shāng xīn
    - I was very sad to hear the news.
  - 别伤心(別傷心)，我们会一起想办法(我們會一起想辦法)。
    - bié shāng xīn, wǒmen huì yìqǐ xiǎng bànfǎ
    - Don’t be sad; we’ll figure something out together.
