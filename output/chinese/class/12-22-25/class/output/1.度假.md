## 度假(度假)
---
- **pinyin:** dù jià
- **definition:** to go on vacation
- **characters:**
  - 度
    - dù; duó
    - degree; extent, measure; to spend, pass (time); to estimate, gauge
  - 假
    - jiǎ; jià
    - fake, false; to pretend, feign; to borrow; vacation, holiday, leave
- **etymology:** 度 (dù, "to spend, pass [time]") is the verb seen in 度日/度歲, and 假 (jià, "leave, vacation") developed from the bureaucratic notion of a granted temporary absence from duty; together they mean “to pass one’s leave,” i.e., to spend the period officially away from work—hence ‘to go on vacation.’.
- **examples:**
  - 我们打算明年夏天去海南度假(我們打算明年夏天去海南度假)。
    - wǒmen dǎsuàn míngnián xiàtiān qù hǎinán dùjià
    - We plan to go to Hainan for vacation.
