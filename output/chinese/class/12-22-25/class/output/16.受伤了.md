## 受伤了(受傷了)
---
- **pinyin:** shòu shāng le
- **definition:** got injured
- **characters:**
  - 受
    - shòu
    - receive
  - 伤(傷)
    - shāng
    - injure
  - 了
    - le; perfective particle
- **examples:**
  - 他在比赛中受伤了(他在比賽中受傷了)。
    - tā zài bǐsài zhōng shòu shāng le
    - He got injured during the match.
