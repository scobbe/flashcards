## 病得很重
---
- **pinyin:** bìng de hěn zhòng
- **definition:** seriously ill
- **characters:**
  - 病: bìng, illness
  - 得: dé, to get
  - 很: hěn, very
  - 重: zhòng, heavy
- **examples:**
  - 他病得很重，需要住院 (tā bìng de hěn zhòng, xūyào zhùyuàn, "He's seriously ill and needs to be hospitalized")
  - 医生说她病得很重，必须马上治疗(醫生說她病得很重，必須馬上治療) (yīshēng shuō tā bìng de hěn zhòng, bìxū mǎshàng zhìliáo, "The doctor said she is seriously ill and must be treated immediately")
