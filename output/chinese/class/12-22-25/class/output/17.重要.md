## 重要(重要)
---
- **pinyin:** zhòng yào
- **definition:** important
- **characters:**
  - 重
    - zhòng, chóng
    - heavy, weighty; important, significant; weight; again, repeatedly
  - 要
    - yào, yāo
    - to want, desire; to need, require; will, be going to; important, essential
- **etymology:** 重 (zhòng, "heavy; weighty") draws on the age-old metaphor of physical weight for seriousness and value, while 要 (yào, "essential; to require") denotes what is vital or a strategic key point (as in 要地). Together they picture a weighty essential at a crucial node, yielding the sense of importance.
- **examples:**
  - 这件事很重要(這件事很重要)。
    - zhè jiàn shì hěn zhòng yào
    - This matter is very important.
