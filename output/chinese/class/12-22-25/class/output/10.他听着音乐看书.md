## 他听着音乐看书(他聽著音樂看書)
---
- **pinyin:** tā tīng zhe yīn yuè kàn shū
- **definition:** He reads while listening to music
- **characters:**
  - 他: tā, he
  - 听(聽): tīng, to listen
  - 着(著): zhe, durative particle
  - 音: yīn, sound
  - 乐(樂): yuè, music
  - 看: kàn, to look
  - 书(書): shū, book
- **examples:**
  - 他听着音乐看书(他聽著音樂看書) (tā tīng zhe yīn yuè kàn shū, "He reads while listening to music")
