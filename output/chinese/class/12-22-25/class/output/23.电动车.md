## 电动车(電動車)
---
- **pinyin:** diàn dòng chē
- **definition:** electric vehicle
- **characters:**
  - 电(電): diàn, electricity
  - 动(動): dòng, move
  - 车(車): chē, vehicle
- **examples:**
  - 我打算换一辆电动车，省钱又环保(我打算換一輛電動車，省錢又環保) (wǒ dǎsuàn huàn yī liàng diàn dòng chē, shěng qián yòu huánbǎo, "I'm planning to switch to an electric vehicle; it saves money and is eco-friendly")
