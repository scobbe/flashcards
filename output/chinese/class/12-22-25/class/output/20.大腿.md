## 大腿(大腿)
---
- **pinyin:** dà tuǐ
- **definition:** thigh
- **characters:**
  - 大
    - dà
    - big, large; great, major, main; eldest, oldest; adult, grown-up
  - 腿
    - tuǐ
    - leg, thigh; leg of furniture, support
- **etymology:** 大 (dà, "big") marks the leg's thicker, weight-bearing upper section in the common 大/小 partition of a whole, while 腿 (tuǐ, "leg") names the limb, together denoting the leg's big part: the thigh. Classical 股 (gǔ, "thigh") exists, but vernacular favored this transparent contrast with 小 (xiǎo, "small") in 小腿 (xiǎo tuǐ, "calf"), making 大腿 the natural counterpart.
- **examples:**
  - 他的大腿受伤了(他的大腿受傷了)。
    - tā de dà tuǐ shòu shāng le
    - He injured his thigh.
  - 跑完马拉松后(跑完馬拉松後)，我的大腿很酸痛(我的大腿很酸痛)。
    - pǎo wán mǎlāsōng hòu wǒ de dà tuǐ hěn suān tòng
    - After finishing the marathon, my thighs are very sore.
