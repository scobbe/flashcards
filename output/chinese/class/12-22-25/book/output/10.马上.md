## 马上(馬上)
---
- **pinyin:** mǎshàng
- **definition:** right away; immediately
- **examples:**
  - 我马上给你打电话(我馬上給你打電話) (wǒ mǎshàng gěi nǐ dǎ diànhuà, "I'll call you right away")
  - 马上就来(馬上就來) (mǎshàng jiù lái, "will come right away")
  - 马上去(馬上去) (mǎshàng qù, "going right away")
  - 马上看完(馬上看完) (mǎshàng kàn wán, "finish right away")
  - 马上出发(馬上出發) (mǎshàng chūfā, "depart right away")
