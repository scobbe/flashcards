## 马上(馬上)
---
- **pinyin:** mǎshàng
- **definition:** right away; immediately
- **characters:**
  - 马(馬)
    - mǎ
    - horse; Ma (surname); horse piece in Chinese chess
  - 上
    - shàng
    - above, on, over; to go up, ascend; previous, earlier; to attend, go to
- **etymology:** Literally “on horseback,” evoking the speed and readiness of a mounted rider, so something done 马上 happens without delay; by extension from acting while still in the saddle to “immediately.”
- **examples:**
  - 我马上就给你回电话(我馬上就給你回電話)。
    - wǒ mǎshàng jiù gěi nǐ huí diànhuà
    - I'll call you back right away.
  - 听到警报(聽到警報)，我们马上撤离(我們馬上撤離)。
    - tīng dào jǐngbào wǒmen mǎshàng chèlí
    - Upon hearing the alarm, we evacuated immediately.
  - 马上就来(馬上就來)。
    - mǎshàng jiù lái
    - I'll be right there.
  - 马上去(馬上去)。
    - mǎshàng qù
    - I'll go right away.
  - 马上看完(馬上看完)。
    - mǎshàng kàn wán
    - I'll finish it right away.
  - 马上出发(馬上出發)。
    - mǎshàng chūfā
    - We'll set off immediately.
