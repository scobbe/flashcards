## 停
---
- **pinyin:** tíng
- **definition:** to stop; to park
- **etymology:** Composed of the 'person' radical (亻) plus 'pavilion' (亭), with 亭 providing the sound; the image of a person by a pavilion evokes stopping to rest, yielding the meaning 'to stop; to park.'
- **examples:**
  - 看到红灯(看到紅燈)，就停下(就停下)。
    - kàn dào hóng dēng jiù tíng xià
    - When you see a red light, stop.
  - 我把车停在车库里(我把車停在車庫裡)。
    - wǒ bǎ chē tíng zài chē kù lǐ
    - I parked the car in the garage.
  - 停在路旁边(停在路旁邊)。
    - tíng zài lù páng biān
    - Stop by the roadside.
  - 停车(停車)。
    - tíng chē
    - To park; parking.
  - 停课(停課)。
    - tíng kè
    - To suspend classes.
