## 撞
---
- **pinyin:** zhuàng
- **definition:** to knock; to collide
- **examples:**
  - 他不小心撞到门上(他不小心撞到門上)。
    - tā bù xiǎoxīn zhuàng dào mén shàng
    - He accidentally bumped into the door.
  - 两车在路口相撞了(兩車在路口相撞了)。
    - liǎng chē zài lùkǒu xiāngzhuàng le
    - Two cars collided at the intersection.
  - 被车撞(被車撞)。
    - bèi chē zhuàng
    - be hit by a car
  - 撞车(撞車)。
    - zhuàngchē
    - car crash; to crash cars
  - 撞人(撞人)。
    - zhuàng rén
    - to hit someone
