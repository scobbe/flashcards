## 送
---
- **pinyin:** sòng
- **definition:** to take someone somewhere; to see someone off
- **etymology:** Combines the walking radical 辶 with phonetic 关 ‘gate/pass’: the motion element signals going, and the gate imagery evokes accompanying someone to or beyond the gate—hence to send or see off.
- **examples:**
  - 我送你回家(我送你回家)。
    - wǒ sòng nǐ huí jiā
    - I'll take you home.
  - 我们去机场送他(我們去機場送他)。
    - wǒmen qù jīchǎng sòng tā
    - We'll go to the airport to see him off.
  - 他送我回家(他送我回家)。
    - tā sòng wǒ huí jiā
    - He took me home.
  - 他开着车送我去学校(他開著車送我去學校)。
    - tā kāi zhe chē sòng wǒ qù xuéxiào
    - He drove me to school.
  - 他送我们到医院(他送我們到醫院)。
    - tā sòng wǒmen dào yīyuàn
    - He took us to the hospital.
  - 他把我们送到门外边(他把我們送到門外邊)。
    - tā bǎ wǒmen sòng dào mén wàibiān
    - He saw us to the door.
  - 我把她送到火车站(我把她送到火車站)。
    - wǒ bǎ tā sòng dào huǒchēzhàn
    - I took her to the train station.
