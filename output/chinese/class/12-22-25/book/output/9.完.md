## 完
---
- **pinyin:** wán
- **definition:** to finish; to run out
- **examples:**
  - 我已经写完作业了(我已經寫完作業了) (wǒ yǐjīng xiě wán zuòyè le, "I have finished my homework")
  - 我手机流量用完了(我手機流量用完了) (wǒ shǒujī liúliàng yòng wán le, "My mobile data has run out")
  - 看完电影(看完電影) (kàn wán diànyǐng, "finish watching a movie")
  - 做完练习(做完練習) (zuò wán liànxí, "finish the exercises")
  - 用完钱(用完錢) (yòng wán qián, "run out of money")
  - 吃完饭(吃完飯) (chī wán fàn, "finish the meal")
