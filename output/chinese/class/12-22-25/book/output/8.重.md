## 重
---
- **pinyin:** zhòng
- **definition:** serious; heavy
- **examples:**
  - 病情很重需要住院(病情很重需要住院)。
    - bìngqíng hěn zhòng xūyào zhùyuàn
    - The condition is serious and requires hospitalization.
  - 这箱子很重小心搬(這箱子很重小心搬)。
    - zhè xiāngzi hěn zhòng xiǎoxīn bān
    - This box is heavy; carry carefully.
  - 伤重(傷重)。
    - shāng zhòng
    - seriously injured
  - 伤不重(傷不重)。
    - shāng bù zhòng
    - not seriously injured
  - 衣服很重(衣服很重)。
    - yīfu hěn zhòng
    - the clothes are heavy.
  - 包裹不重(包裹不重)。
    - bāoguǒ bù zhòng
    - the package is not heavy.
  - 工作不重(工作不重)。
    - gōngzuò bù zhòng
    - the work isn't heavy.
