## 伤(傷)
---
- **pinyin:** shāng
- **definition:** to hurt; to wound
- **examples:**
  - 他不小心伤了自己(他不小心傷了自己) (tā bù xiǎoxīn shāng le zìjǐ, "He accidentally hurt himself")
  - 他被箭伤得很重了(他被箭傷得很重了) (tā bèi jiàn shāng de hěn zhòng le, "He was badly wounded by an arrow")
  - 被撞伤(被撞傷) (bèi zhuàng shāng, "be injured in a crash")
  - 撞伤人(撞傷人) (zhuàng shāng rén, "to hit and injure someone")
