## 着(著)
---
- **pinyin:** zhe
- **definition:** indicating the continuous aspect
- **examples:**
  - 墙上还挂着一幅画(牆上還掛著一幅畫) (qiáng shàng hái guà zhe yī fú huà, "There's still a painting hanging on the wall")
  - 说着(說著) (shuō zhe, "saying; while speaking")
  - 笑着(笑著) (xiào zhe, "smiling; while laughing")
  - 等着(等著) (děng zhe, "waiting")
  - 开着车(開著車) (kāi zhe chē, "driving; with the car on")
  - 带着花儿(帶著花兒) (dài zhe huār, "carrying flowers; with flowers")
  - 穿着旗袍(穿著旗袍) (chuān zhe qípáo, "wearing a qipao")
