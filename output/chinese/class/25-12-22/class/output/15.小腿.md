## lower leg; calf
### xiǎo tuǐ
---
- **definition:** lower leg; calf
- **pinyin:** xiǎo tuǐ
- **components:**
  - 小
    - xiǎo
    - small; little; young; minor
  - 腿
    - tuǐ
    - leg; thigh
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 小 (xiǎo, "small") refers to something that is not large in size. 腿 (tuǐ, "leg") refers to the limb used for walking on which the body stands.
  - **interpretation:** The term 小腿 (xiǎo tuǐ) literally means "small leg," which is analogous to the lower part of the leg when compared to the entire limb. The focus on 'small' highlights the lower leg's position below the larger thigh, emphasizing its role as the narrowing part between the knee and the ankle.
