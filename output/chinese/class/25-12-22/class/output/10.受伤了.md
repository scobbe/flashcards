## got injured
### shòu shāng le
---
- **traditional:** 受傷了
- **definition:** got injured
- **pinyin:** shòu shāng le
- **components:**
  - 受
    - shòu
    - receive; endure; bear; accept
  - 伤(傷)
    - shāng
    - injury; wound; damage; hurt
  - 了
    - le
    - particle indicating change; completed action; new situation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 受 (shòu, "receive"), 伤(傷) (shāng, "injury"), 了 (le, "particle indicating change")
  - **interpretation:** This phrase combines 受 (shòu, "receive") and 伤(傷) (shāng, "injury"), indicating someone has received an injury. The particle 了 (le, "indicating change") adds a sense of completion or change, signifying that an injury has occurred.
- **examples:**
  - 他在比赛(賽)中受伤(傷)了。
    - Tā zài bǐsài zhōng shòu shāng le.
    - He got injured in the competition.
  - 他摔了个(個)跤，受伤(傷)了。
    - Tā shuāi le gè jiāo, shòu shāng le.
    - He fell and got injured.

### 受伤(傷)了 → 伤(傷)
- **traditional:** 傷
- **definition:** injury; wound; damage; hurt
- **pinyin:** shāng
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 𥏻
    - break, fall apart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 人 (rén, "person") + phonetic 𥏻
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 人 (rén, "person") as the semantic element indicates the human aspect or experience, and 𥏻 serves as the phonetic element lending the sound 'shāng'. Thus, the character relates to the human experience of injury or harm by using the human radical together with a phonetic indicator.
  - **simplification:** In the simplification to 伤, 昜 was replaced with 力 (lì, "strength") for simplicity and stroke reduction while maintaining the sound.
- **examples:**
  - 他由于(於)摔倒而受了轻(輕)伤(傷)。
    - Tā yóuyú shuāidǎo ér shòu le qīng shāng.
    - He suffered a minor injury from the fall.
  - 妈(媽)妈(媽)的话(話)深深伤(傷)到了她。
    - Māma de huà shēnshēn shāng dào le tā.
    - Her mother's words deeply hurt her.
  - 请(請)小心，以免伤(傷)到自己。
    - Qǐng xiǎoxīn, yǐmiǎn shāng dào zìjǐ.
    - Please be careful to avoid hurting yourself.

### 受伤(傷)了 → 了
- **definition:** particle indicating change; completed action; new situation
- **pinyin:** le
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a baby without arms (compare 子 with arms) ->
    - entangled legs ->
    - sound loan for 'finish'
  - **interpretation:** The original depiction is of a baby shape illustrating a state of vulnerability or incompleteness. Over time, the idea of 'entangled legs' metaphorically transitioned into 'something tied or completed.' 'Finish' likely arose from a sound loan rather than semantic combination, as 'entangled' implies coming to an end or conclusion in a motion.
- **examples:**
  - 事情已经(經)结(結)束了。
    - Shìqíng yǐjīng jiéshù le.
    - The matter has already ended.
  - 他终(終)于(於)明白了。
    - Tā zhōngyú míngbái le.
    - He finally understood.
  - 她在一小时(時)内(內)完成了这(這)项(項)任务(務)。
    - Tā zài yī xiǎoshí nèi wánchéng le zhè xiàng rènwù.
    - She completed this task within an hour.
