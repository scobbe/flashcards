### zhòng yào
---
- **definition:** important
- **pinyin:** zhòng yào
- **components:**
  - 重
    - zhòng
    - heavy; important; repeat
  - 要
    - yào
    - want; need; essential
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** The word consists of 重 (zhòng, "heavy; important") and 要 (yào, "want; need; essential").
  - **interpretation:** 重 (zhòng, "heavy") conveys importance through the metaphor of weight — something heavy demands attention or care, reflecting its significance. 要 (yào, "essential") indicates something crucial or necessary. Together, the combination implies something of significant importance or necessity, something of "weight" and "essence" that demands attention.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)件( )事( )对(對)我( )来(來)说(說)非( )常( )重( )要( )。
    - Zhè jiàn shì duì wǒ lái shuō fēicháng zhòng yào.
    - This matter is very important to me.
  - 了解文化背景是成功的重要因素。
    - Liǎojiě wénhuà bèijǐng shì chénggōng de zhòng yào yīnsù.
    - Understanding cultural background is an important factor for success.
  - 教( )育( )在( )现(現)代( )社( )会(會)中( )扮( )演( )着(著)重( )要( )角( )色( )。
    - Jiàoyù zài xiàndài shèhuì zhōng bànyǎn zhe zhòng yào juésè.

  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**

### 重要 → 重 → 東
- **definition:** bag
- **pinyin:** dōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bundle or bag tied at both ends
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicting a bundle or bag tied at both ends, it suggests convergence or collection, visually resembling a tied shape. This pictorial representation is historically connected to the direction 'east', as a bag captures and holds items, analogous to the rising sun being 'held' in the east. Despite alternative interpretations involving the sun and tree, the pictogram of a bundle remains the most direct visual link.
