## electric vehicle
### diàn dòng chē
---
- **traditional:** 電動車
- **definition:** electric vehicle
- **pinyin:** diàn dòng chē
- **components:**
  - 电(電)
    - diàn
    - electric
  - 动(動)
    - dòng
    - move
  - 车(車)
    - chē
    - vehicle
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:**
    - 电(電) (diàn, "electric") + 动(動) (dòng, "move") + 车(車) (chē, "vehicle") =
    - vehicle that moves using electricity
  - **interpretation:** This combination makes sense because 电(電) (diàn, "electric") refers to electricity, 动(動) (dòng, "move") indicates movement, and 车(車) (chē, "vehicle") denotes a form of transportation. Together, the word describes a vehicle that uses electricity to power its movement.
- **examples:**
  - 电(電)动(動)车(車)是一种(種)环(環)保的交通工具。
    - diàn dòng chē shì yī zhǒng huán bǎo de jiāo tōng gōng jù.
    - Electric vehicles are an environmentally friendly means of transportation.
  - 他昨天租了一辆(輛)电(電)动(動)车(車)，在市区(區)内(內)自由穿行。
    - tā zuótiān zū le yī liàng diàn dòng chē, zài shì qū nèi zì yóu chuān xíng.
    - He rented an electric vehicle yesterday and freely maneuvered through the city.
  - 很多人选(選)择(擇)电(電)动(動)车(車)作为(為)环(環)保出行方式，以减(減)少汽车(車)尾气(氣)排放。
    - hěn duō rén xuǎn zé diàn dòng chē zuò wéi huán bǎo chū xíng fāng shì, yǐ jiǎn shǎo qì chē wěi qì pái fàng.
    - Many people choose electric vehicles as an environmentally friendly travel mode in order to reduce car exhaust emissions.
### 电(電)动(動)车(車) → 电(電)
- **traditional:** 電
- **definition:** electric
- **pinyin:** diàn
- **components:**
  - 雨
    - yǔ
    - rain
  - 申
    - shēn
    - lightning
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 雨 ('rain') + 申 ('lightning') =
    - depiction of a thunderstorm ->
    - electric
  - **interpretation:** The character 電 combines 雨 (yǔ, 'rain') as the semantic element suggesting a natural event involving water, with 申 (shēn, 'lightning') indicating the associated sound and visual phenomenon of a thunderstorm, representing electric energy.
  - **simplification:** 电(電): The rain radical 雨 (yǔ, 'rain') was simplified to a single horizontal line and reduced with fewer strokes.
- **examples:**
  - 商店已经(經)没(沒)有电(電)。

    - Tā dǎkāi diàndēng, fángjiān shùnjiān míngliàng qǐlái.
    - She turned on the light, and the room instantly brightened up.
  - 这(這)些电(電)器都是最新型号(號)。
    - Zhèxiē diànqì dōu shì zuìxīn xínghào.

### 电(電)动(動)车(車) → 动(動)
- **traditional:** 動
- **definition:** move
- **pinyin:** dòng
- **components:**
  - 力
    - lì
    - strength
  - 重
    - zhòng
    - heavy
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 力 (lì, "strength") + phonetic 重 (zhòng) ->
    - movement aided by strength ->
    - move
  - **interpretation:** The character 動 suggests using strength 力 (lì, "strength") to move something heavy, as indicated by the phonetic component 重 (zhòng, "heavy"). This conveys the concept of movement or change.
  - **simplification:** The character 動 was simplified by replacing 重 (zhòng, "heavy") with 云 (yún, "cloud") to form 动, reducing the complexity of writing.
- **examples:**
  - 他动(動)了一下椅子。
    - Tā dòng le yīxià yǐzi.
    - He moved the chair slightly.
  - 我的心被她的话(話)感动(動)了。
    - Wǒ de xīn bèi tā de huà gǎn dòng le.
    - My heart was moved by her words.
  - 天气(氣)一变(變)动(動)，就会(會)下雨。
    - Tiānqì yī biàn dòng, jiù huì xià yǔ.
    - As soon as the weather changes, it will rain.

### 电(電)动(動)车(車) → 动(動) → 重
- **definition:** heavy
- **pinyin:** zhòng
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - man
  - 東
    - dōng
    - bag
  - 土
    - tǔ
    - earth
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - 人 (rén, "man") + 東 (dōng, "bag") =
    - a man carrying a bag ->
    - 土 (tǔ, "earth") added ->
    - symbol of load ->
    - final form
  - **interpretation:** Originally, it depicts a man carrying a bag, indicating the concept of weight. With 土 (tǔ, "earth") added to signify being grounded, it amplifies the idea of heaviness due to the man's connection to the ground or earth, suggesting substantial weight or burden. This visual representation naturally evolved into "heavy", as a person burdened by earth is weighed down.
- **examples:**
  - 这(這)箱苹(蘋)果很重。
    - Zhè xiāng píngguǒ hěn zhòng.
    - This box of apples is very heavy.
  - 他的行李太重，无(無)法一个(個)人搬动(動)。

    - His luggage is too heavy to carry alone.
  - 雨后(後)的空气(氣)显(顯)得特别(別)重。
    - Yǔ hòu de kōngqì xiǎnde tèbié zhòng.

### 电(電)动(動)车(車) → 动(動) → 重 → 東
- **definition:** bag
- **pinyin:** dōng
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a bundle or bag tied at both ends
  - **interpretation:** Originally depicting a bundle or bag tied at both ends, it suggests convergence or collection, visually resembling a tied shape. This pictorial representation is historically connected to the direction 'east', as a bag captures and holds items, analogous to the rising sun being 'held' in the east. Despite alternative interpretations involving the sun and tree, the pictogram of a bundle remains the most direct visual link.

- **examples:**
