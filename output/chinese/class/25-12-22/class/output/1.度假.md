## to go on vacation
### dù jià
---
- **definition:** to go on vacation
- **pinyin:** dù jià
- **components:**
  - 度
    - dù
    - spend; measure
  - 假
    - jià
    - vacation; leave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") + 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") - to spend one's time on leave or vacation.
  - **interpretation:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") refers to the allocation or passage of time. 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") is specifically the time taken off from regular duties. Together, 度假 (dù jià) translates to spending one's time on vacation, emphasizing the idea of taking a break from routine activities.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)打算去海边(邊)度假。
    - Wǒmen dǎsuàn qù hǎibiān dù jià.
    - We plan to go to the seaside for vacation.
  - 度假时(時)，我喜欢(歡)读(讀)书(書)。
    - Dù jià shí, wǒ xǐhuān dúshū.
    - When on vacation, I like to read books.
  - 他们(們)去了欧(歐)洲度假，游(遊)览(覽)了许(許)多地方。
    - Tāmen qùle Ōuzhōu dù jià, yóulǎnle xǔduō dìfāng.
    - They went on vacation to Europe and visited many places.
### 度假 → 度
- **definition:** spend; measure
- **pinyin:** dù
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - rock
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 石 (shí, "rock") + 又 (yòu, "hand") =
    - hand measuring rock ->
    - 石 phonetic component for pronunciation ->
    - 石 corrupted to 𲟁
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a hand measuring a rock underlies the concept of measuring or gauging, leading to its meanings related to degree and limit. Over time, the rock component served as a phonetic element, though it was corrupted into another form.
- **examples:**
  - 他度过(過)了愉快的一天。
    - Tā dùguò le yúkuài de yītiān.
    - He spent a pleasant day.
  - 水的沸点(點)是100度。
    - Shuǐ de fèidiǎn shì yībǎi dù.
    - The boiling point of water is 100 degrees.
  - 这(這)次考试(試)的难(難)度很高。
    - Zhè cì kǎoshì de nándù hěn gāo.
    - This exam is very challenging.

### 度假 → 度 → 石
- **definition:** rock
- **pinyin:** shí
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a stone beneath a cliff (厂) ->
    - distorted to 丆 ->
    - stone
  - **interpretation:** The character 石(shí, 'stone') originally depicted a stone under a cliff, representing the idea of a solid, natural object found at the base of rocky formations.
- **examples:**
  - 那个(個)花园(園)里(裡)有一块(塊)很大的石头(頭)。
    - Nàge huāyuán lǐ yǒu yīkuài hěn dà de shítou.
    - There is a big stone in the garden.
  - 石头(頭)变(變)得很滑，因为(為)下雨了。
    - Shítou biàn de hěn huá, yīnwèi xià yǔ le.
    - The stone became very slippery because it rained.
  - 他收集了很多不同种(種)类(類)的石头(頭)。
    - Tā shōujíle hěnduō bùtóng zhǒnglèi de shítou.
    - He collected many different kinds of stones.

### 度假 → 度 → 又
- **definition:** hand
- **pinyin:** yòu
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Depicts a right hand
  - **interpretation:** The character 又 (yòu, "hand") is a pictogram representing a right hand, showing the fingers and palm, which highlights its original meaning related to manual actions.
- **examples:**
  - 「又」这(這)个(個)字最早来(來)源于(於)手。
    - 「Yòu」 zhè gè zì zuì zǎo láiyuán yú shǒu。
    - The character '又' originally comes from the hand.
  - 很多汉(漢)字含有又部首，表示与(與)手有关(關)的动(動)作。
    - Hěn duō hànzì hányǒu Yòu bùshǒu, biǎoshì yǔ shǒu yǒuguān de dòngzuò。
    - Many Chinese characters include the '又' radical, indicating actions related to the hand.
  - 古人用又描绘(繪)手的形态(態)，体(體)现(現)了古汉(漢)字的独(獨)特魅力。
    - Gǔrén yòng Yòu miáohuì shǒu de xíngtài, tǐxiàn le gǔ hànzì de dútè mèilì。
    - Ancient people used '又' to depict the shape of the hand, showcasing the unique charm of ancient Chinese characters.

### 度假 → 假
- **definition:** vacation; leave
- **pinyin:** jià
- **components:**
  - 亻(人)
    - rén
    - person
  - 叚
    - jià
    - half-leaf door
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic 亻(人) (rén, "person") + phonetic 叚 (jià) ->
    - holiday; vacation
  - **interpretation:** The character 假 combines the semantic element 亻(人) (rén, "person") suggesting human activity or state, with the phonetic 叚 (jià) to form the meaning related to taking a break or time off from daily duties, represented by the concept of 'holiday' or 'vacation'.
- **examples:**
  - 我决(決)定在暑假去旅行。
    - Wǒ juédìng zài shǔjià qù lǚxíng.
    - I decided to travel during the summer vacation.
  - 假期时(時)，我们(們)去海滩(灘)度假。
    - Jiàqī shí, wǒmen qù hǎitān dùjià.
    - During the holiday, we went to the beach for vacation.
  - 她申请(請)了两(兩)周(週)的假期，以便更好地放松(鬆)。
    - Tā shēnqǐngle liǎng zhōu de jiàqī, yǐbiàn gèng hǎo de fàngsōng.
    - She applied for a two-week vacation to relax better.
- **historical forms:**

### 度假 → 假 → 亻
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Stylization of 人 (rén, "person") as a side radical to depict a person
  - **interpretation:** 亻 is a stylized form of 人 (rén, "person") used to indicate human-related meanings when combined with other components in characters as a radical. It visually represents a standing person, maintaining the core meaning of 'person' while facilitating the construction of complex characters.
- **examples:**
  - 他是一个(個)热(熱)心的人。
    - Tā shì yī gè rèxīn de rén.
    - He is a kind-hearted person.
  - 这(這)座城市里(裡)住着(著)很多优(優)秀的人。
    - Zhè zuò chéngshì lǐ zhùzhe hěn duō yōuxiù de rén.
    - There are many outstanding people living in this city.
  - 每个(個)人都有梦(夢)想。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu mèngxiǎng.
    - Everyone has dreams.
### 度假 → 假 → 亻 → 人
- **definition:** person
- **pinyin:** rén
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts side view of a standing man, highlighting an arm and a leg ->
    - visual representation of a person
  - **interpretation:** The character 人 (rén, 'person') effectively uses simple lines to depict the essential form of a human, making it immediately recognizable and intuitive as meaning 'person.'
- **examples:**
  - 他是个(個)好人。
    - Tā shì gè hǎo rén.
    - He is a good person.
  - 每个(個)人都有责(責)任，为(為)社会(會)作出贡(貢)献(獻)。
    - Měi gè rén dōu yǒu zérèn, wèi shèhuì zuòchū gòngxiàn.
    - Every person has a responsibility to contribute to society.
  - 人在旅途中，心灵(靈)获(獲)得了成长(長)。
    - Rén zài lǚtú zhōng, xīnlíng huòdé le chéngzhǎng.
    - People gain growth along their journey.

### 度假 → 假 → 叚
- **definition:** half-leaf door
- **pinyin:** jià
- **components:**
  - 爪
    - zhǎo
    - hand; claw
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
  - 石
    - shí
    - stone
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 爪 (hand) + 又 (hand) + 石 (stone) =
    - two hands interacting with a stone ->
    - concept of lending/borrowing
  - **interpretation:** 叚 visualizes two hands interacting with a stone, effectively showing the action of borrowing or lending. This imagery aligns with early practices where physical objects exchanged hands, indicating temporary possession.

### 度假 → 假 → 叚 → 爪
- **definition:** hand; claw
- **pinyin:** zhǎo
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - Depicts a hand grabbing from above, resembles a claw ->
    - stylized to represent claws
  - **interpretation:** The character 爪 (zhǎo, "claw") originally depicted a hand grabbing from above, stylized to resemble the form of claws.
- **examples:**
  - 猫(貓)的爪非常敏捷。
    - Māo de zhǎo fēicháng mǐnjié.
    - The cat's claws are very agile.
  - 狗的爪稳(穩)固，使它轻(輕)松(鬆)爬山。
    - Gǒu de zhǎo wěngù, shǐ tā qīngsōng páshān.
    - The dog's claws are sturdy, allowing it to climb mountains easily.
  - 鹰(鷹)的爪锋(鋒)利，能牢牢抓住猎(獵)物。
    - Yīng de zhǎo fēnglì, néng láoláo zhuā zhù lièwù.
    - The eagle's claws are sharp, enabling it to firmly grasp its prey.
