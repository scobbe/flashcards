## to go on vacation
### dù jià
---
- **definition:** to go on vacation
- **pinyin:** dù jià
- **components:**
  - 度
    - dù
    - spend; measure
  - 假
    - jià
    - vacation; leave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") + 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") - to spend one's time on leave or vacation.
  - **interpretation:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") refers to the allocation or passage of time. 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") is specifically the time taken off from regular duties. Together, 度假 (dù jià) translates to spending one's time on vacation, emphasizing the idea of taking a break from routine activities.
- **examples:**
  - 我们(們)打算去海边(邊)度假。
    - Wǒmen dǎsuàn qù hǎibiān dù jià.
    - We plan to go to the seaside for vacation.
  - 度假时(時)，我喜欢(歡)读(讀)书(書)。
    - Dù jià shí, wǒ xǐhuān dúshū.
    - When on vacation, I like to read books.
  - 他们(們)去了欧(歐)洲度假，游(遊)览(覽)了许(許)多地方。
    - Tāmen qùle Ōuzhōu dù jià, yóulǎnle xǔduō dìfāng.
    - They went on vacation to Europe and visited many places.
### 度假 → 度
- **definition:** spend; measure
- **pinyin:** dù
- **components:**
  - 石
    - shí
    - rock
  - 又
    - yòu
    - hand
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - 石 (shí, "rock") + 又 (yòu, "hand") =
    - hand measuring rock ->
    - 石 phonetic component for pronunciation ->
    - 石 corrupted to 𲟁
  - **interpretation:** The combination of a hand measuring a rock underlies the concept of measuring or gauging, leading to its meanings related to degree and limit. Over time, the rock component served as a phonetic element, though it was corrupted into another form.
- **examples:**
  - 他度过(過)了愉快的一天。
    - Tā dùguò le yúkuài de yītiān.
    - He spent a pleasant day.
  - 水的沸点(點)是100度。
    - Shuǐ de fèidiǎn shì yībǎi dù.
    - The boiling point of water is 100 degrees.
  - 这(這)次考试(試)的难(難)度很高。
    - Zhè cì kǎoshì de nándù hěn gāo.
    - This exam is very challenging.

## got injured
### shòu shāng le
---
- **traditional:** 受傷了
- **definition:** got injured
- **pinyin:** shòu shāng le
- **components:**
  - 受
    - shòu
    - receive; endure; bear; accept
  - 伤(傷)
    - shāng
    - injury; wound; damage; hurt
  - 了
    - le
    - particle indicating change; completed action; new situation
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 受 (shòu, "receive"), 伤(傷) (shāng, "injury"), 了 (le, "particle indicating change")
  - **interpretation:** This phrase combines 受 (shòu, "receive") and 伤(傷) (shāng, "injury"), indicating someone has received an injury. The particle 了 (le, "indicating change") adds a sense of completion or change, signifying that an injury has occurred.
- **examples:**
  - 他在比赛(賽)中受伤(傷)了。
    - Tā zài bǐsài zhōng shòu shāng le.
    - He got injured in the competition.
  - 他摔了个(個)跤，受伤(傷)了。
    - Tā shuāi le gè jiāo, shòu shāng le.
    - He fell and got injured.

### 受伤(傷)了 → 伤(傷)
- **traditional:** 傷
- **definition:** injury; wound; damage; hurt
- **pinyin:** shāng
- **components:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person
  - 𥏻
    - break, fall apart
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic 人 (rén, "person") + phonetic 𥏻
  - **interpretation:** The combination of 人 (rén, "person") as the semantic element indicates the human aspect or experience, and 𥏻 serves as the phonetic element lending the sound 'shāng'. Thus, the character relates to the human experience of injury or harm by using the human radical together with a phonetic indicator.
  - **simplification:** In the simplification to 伤, 昜 was replaced with 力 (lì, "strength") for simplicity and stroke reduction while maintaining the sound.
- **examples:**

Over time, it has become highly stylized, but it continues to convey the notion of the heart as the center of emotions and consciousness.
- **examples:**
  - 她的心一直都在帮(幫)助别(別)人。
    - Tā de xīn yīzhí dōu zài bāngzhù biérén.
    - Her heart is always in helping others.
  - 在城市的中心，有一个(個)美丽(麗)的公园(園)。
    - Zài chéngshì de zhōngxīn, yǒu yí gè měilì de gōngyuán.
    - At the center of the city, there is a beautiful park.
  - 对(對)于(於)许(許)多人来(來)说(說)，家庭是生活的核心。
    - Duìyú xǔduō rén lái shuō, jiātíng shì shēnghuó de héxīn.
    - For many people, family is the core of life.
