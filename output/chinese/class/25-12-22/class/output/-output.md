### dù jià
---
- **definition:** to go on vacation
- **pinyin:** dù jià
- **components:**
  - 度
    - dù
    - spend; measure
  - 假
    - jià
    - vacation; leave
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") + 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") - to spend one's time on leave or vacation.
  - **interpretation:** 度 (dù, "spend; measure") refers to the allocation or passage of time. 假 (jià, "vacation; leave") is specifically the time taken off from regular duties. Together, 度假 (dù jià) translates to spending one's time on vacation, emphasizing the idea of taking a break from routine activities.

  - **interpretation:** The character 心(xīn, "heart") originally depicted the shape of an actual heart, representing thoughts, emotions, and the core of a person. Over time, it has become highly stylized, but it continues to convey the notion of the heart as the center of emotions and consciousness.
