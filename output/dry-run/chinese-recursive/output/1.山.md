## 山
---
- **pinyin:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: stylized mountain with three peaks.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a mountain range, with a tall central peak flanked by two lower ones. Over time the drawing was simplified but still looks like three rising peaks, clearly suggesting rugged mountains.
- **examples:**
  - 那座山很高(那座山很高)。
    - Nà zuò shān hěn gāo.
    - That mountain is very tall.
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We are going to climb a mountain.
