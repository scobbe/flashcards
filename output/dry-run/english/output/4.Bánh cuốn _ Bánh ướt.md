## Bánh cuốn / Bánh ướt
---
- **definition:**
  - vietnamese rice-noodle dishes made from thin, steamed rice batter sheets
  - bánh cuốn: the sheet is rolled around savory fillings; bánh ướt: the sheet is left unrolled and served with toppings
- **origin:**
  - from vietnamese: bánh “cake/rice batter item”; cuốn “rolled/wrapped”; ướt “wet”
  - made by steaming rice batter on cloth over boiling water; technique parallels exist (e.g., cantonese cheung fun), but the names are distinctly vietnamese
  - entered english via vietnamese restaurants after the 1970s; diacritics are often dropped as “banh cuon” and “banh uot”
- **pronunciation:**
  - bahn KWOON / bahn UHT
