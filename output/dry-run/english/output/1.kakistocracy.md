## kakistocracy
---
- **definition:**
  - government run by the worst, least qualified, or most unscrupulous people
  - a state or situation where such people hold power; a scathing label for misrule
- **origin:**
  - from Greek kakistos “worst” (superlative of kakos “bad”) + -kratia “rule, power”
  - formed on the model of words like aristocracy and plutocracy; recorded in English by the 19th century
  - often used in political rhetoric to condemn corruption and incompetence
- **pronunciation:**
  - kak-iss-TAH-kruh-see
