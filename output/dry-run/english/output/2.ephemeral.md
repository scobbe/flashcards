## ephemeral
---
- **definition:**
  - lasting a very short time; fleeting or short-lived
  - intended to be temporary or quickly disappearing, like online posts or trends
- **origin:**
  - from Greek "ephēmeros" meaning "lasting a day" (epi "upon" + hēmera "day")
  - entered English via Latin "ephemerus/ephemeralis" and French, first used in medicine for fevers that lasted a day
  - later broadened to mean anything short-lived, from mayflies to pop culture fads
- **pronunciation:**
  - ih-FEM-uh-rul
