## 银(銀)
---
- **pinyin:** yín
- **definition:** silver
- **etymology:** From 銀 (金 → 钅) Definitions: For pronunciation and definitions of 银 – see 銀 ("silver ; silvern ; made of silver ; etc.
- **examples:**
  - 这枚戒指是纯银做的(這枚戒指是純銀做的)。
    - zhè méi jièzhǐ shì chún yín zuò de
    - This ring is made of pure silver.
