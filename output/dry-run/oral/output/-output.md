## 山
---
- **pinyin:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **examples:**
  - 这座山很高(這座山很高) (zhè zuò shān hěn gāo, "This mountain is very tall")
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山) (wǒmen qù pá shān, "We're going to hike in the mountains")

## 银(銀)

- **definition:** silver
- **examples:**
  - 这枚戒指是纯银的(這枚戒指是純銀的) (zhè méi jièzhi shì chún yín de, "This ring is made of pure silver")

## 人口
---
- **pinyin:** rénkǒu
- **definition:** population
- **characters:**
  - 人: rén, person
  - 口: kǒu, mouth
- **examples:**
  - 这个国家的人口正在快速增长。(這個國家的人口正在快速增長。) (Zhège guójiā de rénkǒu zhèngzài kuàisù zēngzhǎng, "The country's population is growing rapidly.")
  - 中国人口很多(中國人口很多) (Zhōngguó rénkǒu hěn duō, "China has a large population")
%%%


## 说话(說話)
---
- **pinyin:** shuōhuà
- **definition:** to speak
- **characters:**
  - 说(說): shuō, say
  - 话(話): huà, speech
- **examples:**
  - 别在图书馆说话(別在圖書館說話) (bié zài túshūguǎn shuōhuà, "Don't speak in the library")
  - 请小声说话(請小聲說話) (qǐng xiǎoshēng shuōhuà, "Please speak softly")
%%%


## 得
---
- **pinyin:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
- **examples:**
  - 他跑得很快 (tā pǎo de hěn kuài, "He runs very fast")
  - 我得到了一条好消息(我得到了一條好消息) (wǒ dé dào le yì tiáo hǎo xiāo xī, "I received some good news")
  - 我得早点出发(我得早點出發) (wǒ děi zǎodiǎn chūfā, "I have to set off earlier")
