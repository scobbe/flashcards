## 得
---
- **pinyin:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
- **examples:**
  - 他跑得很快 (tā pǎo de hěn kuài, "He runs very fast")
  - 她终于得到了一份工作(她終於得到了一份工作) (tā zhōngyú dédào le yí fèn gōngzuò, "She finally got a job")
  - 你今天得交作业(你今天得交作業) (nǐ jīntiān děi jiāo zuòyè, "You have to turn in your homework today")
