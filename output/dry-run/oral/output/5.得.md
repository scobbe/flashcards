## 得
---
- **pinyin:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
- **examples:**
  - 他跑得很快 (tā pǎo de hěn kuài, "He runs very fast")
  - 我得到了一条好消息(我得到了一條好消息) (wǒ dé dào le yì tiáo hǎo xiāo xī, "I received some good news")
  - 我得早点出发(我得早點出發) (wǒ děi zǎodiǎn chūfā, "I have to set off earlier")
