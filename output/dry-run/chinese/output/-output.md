## 山
---
- **pinyin:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram of a mountain with three peaks.
  - **interpretation:** Early forms draw jagged peaks rising from the ground, directly depicting a mountainous shape. The tall central peak with two flanking peaks conveys the idea of crags and summits, so the meaning is literal.
- **examples:**
  - 这座山很高(這座山很高)。
    - Zhè zuò shān hěn gāo.
    - This mountain is very tall.
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We’re going to climb a mountain.

## 银(銀)
---
- **pinyin:** yín
- **definition:** silver
- **components:**
  - 金
    - jīn
    - metal
  - 艮
    - gèn
    - to stop; tough
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 金 (jīn, "metal") + phonetic: 艮 (gèn, "tough").
  - **interpretation:** 金 (jīn, "metal") flags it as a type of metal. 艮 (gèn, "tough") supplies the sound, approximating yín in 银(銀) (yín, "silver"), and also evokes hardness/firmness, fitting a metal.
- **examples:**
  - 这把勺子是银做的(這把勺子是銀做的)，很轻(很輕)。
    - Zhè bǎ sháozi shì yín zuò de, hěn qīng.
    - This spoon is made of silver; it's very light.

## 人口
---
- **pinyin:** rénkǒu
- **definition:** population
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person, human; people
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth; opening, orifice; mouthful; classifier for people in a household
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人 (rén, "person") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth; classifier for people in a household") = population
  - **interpretation:** Pairs 人 (rén, "person") with 口 (kǒu, "mouth")—a metonym and measure word for people—to express the count of persons, i.e., population.
- **examples:**
  - 这个城市的人口增长很快(這個城市的人口增長很快)。
    - Zhège chéngshì de rénkǒu zēngzhǎng hěn kuài.
    - The city's population is growing quickly.
  - 中国人口很多(中國人口很多)。
    - Zhōngguó rénkǒu hěn duō.
    - China has a large population.
%%%


## 说话(說話)
---
- **pinyin:** shuōhuà
- **definition:** to speak
- **characters:**
  - 说(說)
    - shuō, shuì
    - to speak, to say, to tell; to explain; to persuade; theory, doctrine, saying
  - 话(話)
    - huà
    - words, speech, talk; remark, statement; dialect, language
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 说(說) (shuō, "to speak; to say") + 话(話) (huà, "words; speech") = "to speak"
  - **interpretation:** The action of speaking plus 'words/speech' yields the literal sense 'speak words,' i.e., to talk.
- **examples:**
  - 在图书馆里不要大声说话(在圖書館裡不要大聲說話)。
    - Zài túshūguǎn lǐ bú yào dàshēng shuōhuà
    - Don't speak loudly in the library.
  - 请小声说话(請小聲說話)。
    - Qǐng xiǎoshēng shuōhuà
    - Please speak quietly.

## 得
---
- **pinyin:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** structural particle; to get; must
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - step; movement
  - 㝵
    - dé
    - phonetic element (archaic)
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - inch; hand (action)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 彳 (chì, "step; movement") + phonetic: 㝵 (dé, "phonetic").
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of movement/steps with a phonetic element that itself contains a 'hand' idea (寸), evoking proceeding to take or secure something in hand—hence 'to obtain' or 'to get.' Over time it also developed a grammatical use marking result/complement after verbs, and in modal use (děi) it expresses necessity ('must/need to').
- **examples:**
  - 他中文说得很好(他中文說得很好)。
    - Tā Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
    - He speaks Chinese very well.
  - 他得了一个奖(他得了一個獎)。
    - Tā dé le yí gè jiǎng.
    - He got an award.
  - 我得早点儿走(我得早點兒走)。
    - Wǒ děi zǎodiǎnr zǒu.
    - I have to leave early.
