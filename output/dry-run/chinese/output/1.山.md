## 山
---
- **pinyin:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** Pictogram of a mountain with three peaks.
  - **interpretation:** Early forms draw jagged peaks rising from the ground, directly depicting a mountainous shape. The tall central peak with two flanking peaks conveys the idea of crags and summits, so the meaning is literal.
- **examples:**
  - 这座山很高(這座山很高)。
    - Zhè zuò shān hěn gāo.
    - This mountain is very tall.
  - 我们去爬山(我們去爬山)。
    - Wǒmen qù pá shān.
    - We’re going to climb a mountain.
