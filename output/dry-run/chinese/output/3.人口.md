## 人口
---
- **pinyin:** rénkǒu
- **definition:** population
- **characters:**
  - 人
    - rén
    - person, human; people
  - 口
    - kǒu
    - mouth; opening, orifice; mouthful; classifier for people in a household
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** compound word
  - **description:** 人 (rén, "person") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth; classifier for people in a household") = population
  - **interpretation:** Pairs 人 (rén, "person") with 口 (kǒu, "mouth")—a metonym and measure word for people—to express the count of persons, i.e., population.
- **examples:**
  - 这个城市的人口增长很快(這個城市的人口增長很快)。
    - Zhège chéngshì de rénkǒu zēngzhǎng hěn kuài.
    - The city's population is growing quickly.
  - 中国人口很多(中國人口很多)。
    - Zhōngguó rénkǒu hěn duō.
    - China has a large population.
