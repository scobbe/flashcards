## 得
---
- **pinyin:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** structural particle; to get; must
- **components:**
  - 彳
    - chì
    - step; movement
  - 㝵
    - dé
    - phonetic element (archaic)
  - 日
    - rì
    - sun
  - 寸
    - cùn
    - inch; hand (action)
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 彳 (chì, "step; movement") + phonetic: 㝵 (dé, "phonetic").
  - **interpretation:** Combines the idea of movement/steps with a phonetic element that itself contains a 'hand' idea (寸), evoking proceeding to take or secure something in hand—hence 'to obtain' or 'to get.' Over time it also developed a grammatical use marking result/complement after verbs, and in modal use (děi) it expresses necessity ('must/need to').
- **examples:**
  - 他中文说得很好(他中文說得很好)。
    - Tā Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo.
    - He speaks Chinese very well.
  - 他得了一个奖(他得了一個獎)。
    - Tā dé le yí gè jiǎng.
    - He got an award.
  - 我得早点儿走(我得早點兒走)。
    - Wǒ děi zǎodiǎnr zǒu.
    - I have to leave early.
