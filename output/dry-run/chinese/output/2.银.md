## 银(銀)
---
- **pinyin:** yín
- **definition:** silver
- **components:**
  - 金
    - jīn
    - metal
  - 艮
    - gèn
    - to stop; tough
- **etymology:**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 金 (jīn, "metal") + phonetic: 艮 (gèn, "tough").
  - **interpretation:** 金 (jīn, "metal") flags it as a type of metal. 艮 (gèn, "tough") supplies the sound, approximating yín in 银(銀) (yín, "silver"), and also evokes hardness/firmness, fitting a metal.
- **examples:**
  - 这把勺子是银做的(這把勺子是銀做的)，很轻(很輕)。
    - Zhè bǎ sháozi shì yín zuò de, hěn qīng.
    - This spoon is made of silver; it's very light.
