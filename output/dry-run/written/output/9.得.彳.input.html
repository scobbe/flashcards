Glyph origin

[edit]
Historical forms of the character 彳
Western Zhou Shuowen Jiezi (compiled in Han) Liushutong (compiled in Ming)
Bronze inscriptions Small seal script Transcribed ancient scripts

Simplified from 行 – the left side of the character, which represents a street intersection as seen from above. See also the top component of 辵.

Definitions

[edit]

彳

  1. to walk slowly
  2. to step with the left foot
  3. to suddenly stop when walking

Compounds

[edit]
  • 單單彳 / 单单彳
  • 彳亍 (chìchù)
  • 彳部

See also

[edit]
  • 雙人旁／双人旁 (shuāngrénpáng)
