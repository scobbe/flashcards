## step
*sub-component of*
1. (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
---
- **traditional:** 彳
- **simplified:** 彳
- **pronunciation:** chì
- **definition:** step
- **breadcrumbs:**
  - 得
    - de/dé/děi
    - (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 彳亍
    - chì chù
    - pace hesitantly
  - 彳亍而行
    - chì chù ér xíng
    - walk hesitantly
  - 彳亍其间(彳亍其間)
    - chì chù qí jiān
    - pace among them
  - 彳亍在街头(彳亍在街頭)
    - chì chù zài jiē tóu
    - pace on the street
- **etymology (彳):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of a street intersection as seen from above →.
    - left side of 行 (xíng, "crossroads")
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a street intersection, reflecting movement along crossing paths. As an extraction of the left side of 行, it came to serve as a semantic indicator for walking and steps, matching its use as a radical in motion-related characters.
  - **component characters:**
    - 行
      - xíng
      - go
