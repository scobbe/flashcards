## 
### sub-component
#### (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
---
- **traditional:** 貝
- **simplified:** 贝
- **pronunciation:** bèi
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 贝(貝)壳(殼) (bèi ké, "sea shell")
  - 贝(貝)类(類) (bèi lèi, "shellfish category")
  - 贝(貝)母 (bèi mǔ, "fritillaria bulb")
  - 贻(貽)贝(貝) (yí bèi, "marine mussel")
- **etymology (貝):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a cowry shell, which served as money in ancient China. Because cowries were used as currency, the character came to denote shells and, by extension, money and valuables, and it now serves as a semantic element in many money-related characters.
  - **component characters:**
    - 贝
    - 貝
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%B2%9D
  - **simplification rule (贝):** Simplified from 貝 by adopting its cursive handwritten form, removing interior details and streamlining the outline to reduce strokes.
