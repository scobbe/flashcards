- **simplified:** 贝
- **pronunciation:** bèi
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 贝(貝)聿铭(銘) (bèi yù míng, "Ieoh Ming Pei")
  - 贝(貝)壳(殼) (bèi ké, "sea shell")

- **etymology (貝):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of cowry shell used as money → simplified from 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") via cursive script.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a cowry shell, reflecting its use as currency in ancient China. The simplified form comes from a cursive abbreviation of 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry"), preserving the original 'shell/money' sense while reducing strokes.
  - **component characters:**
    - 贝
    - 貝
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%B2%9D
  - **simplification rule (贝):** Reduced from 貝 to the streamlined 贝 by adopting the cursive form and removing inner strokes for fewer, simpler lines.
