## person; people; human
### sub-word
#### population
---
- **traditional:** 人
- **simplified:** 人
- **pronunciation:** rén
- **definition:** person; people; human
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人类(類) (rén lèi, "the human species")
  - 人手一册(冊) (rén shǒu yī cè, "everyone has a copy")
  - 人所共知 (rén suǒ gòng zhī, "common knowledge")
  - 人云(雲)亦云(雲) (rén yún yì yún, "follow the crowd")
- **etymology (人):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a standing man.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a side view of a person with an arm and a leg emphasized, directly conveying the meaning ‘human/person’. The strokes were later streamlined as the script standardized, while the core meaning ‘person; people’ remained and underlies its broad modern use.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
