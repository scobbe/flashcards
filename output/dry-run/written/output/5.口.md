## mouth; opening; entrance
### sub-word
#### population
---
- **traditional:** 口
- **simplified:** 口
- **pronunciation:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth; opening; entrance
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 入口 (rù kǒu, "entrance")
  - 出口 (chū kǒu, "exit")
  - 门(門)口 (mén kǒu, "doorway")
  - 人口 (rén kǒu, "population")
- **etymology (口):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of an open mouth.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts an open mouth. From this concrete image, the meaning naturally extended to general openings and entrances, and by association it serves as a classifier for mouthfuls and items with openings.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
