1. population

- **traditional:** 口
- **simplified:** 口
- **pronunciation:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth; opening; classifier for family members, wells, etc.
- **breadcrumbs:**

  - 伤口

- **etymology (口):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of 口 (kǒu, "mouth")
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an open human mouth. The concrete sense ‘mouth’ extends to ‘opening’ and motivates its use as a classifier for mouthfuls and for counting people in a household.

    - 口
      - kǒu
