## mouth; opening; classifier for family members, wells, etc.
### sub-word
#### population
---
- **traditional:** 口
- **simplified:** 口
- **pronunciation:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth; opening; classifier for family members, wells, etc.
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 门(門)口 (mén kǒu, "doorway")
  - 出口 (chū kǒu, "exit")
  - 人口 (rén kǒu, "population")
  - 伤(傷)口 (shāng kǒu, "wound")
- **etymology (口):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an open human mouth. The concrete sense ‘mouth’ extends to ‘opening’ and motivates its use as a classifier for mouthfuls and for counting people in a household.
  - **component characters:**
    - 口
