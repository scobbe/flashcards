## to speak
---
- **traditional:** 說話
- **simplified:** 说话
- **pronunciation:** shuōhuà
- **definition:** to speak
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 说(說)话(話)小声(聲)点(點) (shuō huà xiǎo shēng diǎn, "speak more quietly")
  - 说(說)话(話)算数(數) (shuō huà suàn shù, "keep one's word")
  - 说(說)话(話)技巧 (shuō huà jì qiǎo, "speaking skills")
  - 说(說)话(話)方式 (shuō huà fāng shì, "manner of speaking")
- **etymology (說話):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 说(說) (shuō, "speak") + 话(話) (huà, "speech").
  - **interpretation:** A verb–object compound combining the verb for speaking with the noun for speech, yielding the sense “to speak; to talk”. The semantics directly match modern usage: performing the action 说 on the object 话 produces speaking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 说
    - 說
    - 话
    - 話
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%AA%AA%E8%A9%B1
  - **simplification rule (说话):** Simplified by reducing 言 to 讠 and regularizing components: 說 → 说 by replacing the right part with 兑, and 話 → 话 as 讠 + 舌.
