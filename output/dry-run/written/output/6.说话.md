## to speak
---
- **traditional:** 說話
- **simplified:** 说话
- **pronunciation:** shuōhuà
- **definition:** to speak
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 说(說)话(話)小声(聲)点(點) (shuō huà xiǎo shēng diǎn, "speak softly")
  - 说(說)话(話)算数(數) (shuō huà suàn shù, "keep one's word")
  - 说(說)话(話)方式 (shuō huà fāng shì, "speaking style")
  - 说(說)话(話)声(聲) (shuō huà shēng, "speaking voice")
- **etymology (說話):**
  - **type:** verb-object compound
  - **description:** verb-object: 说(說) (shuō, "speak") + 话(話) (huà, "words").
  - **interpretation:** Literally “speak words”. The first morpheme denotes the action of speaking and the second the object ‘speech/words’, yielding the verb–object meaning ‘to speak; to talk’ in Modern Chinese.
  - **component characters:**
    - 说
    - 說
    - 话
    - 話
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%AA%AA%E8%A9%B1
  - **simplification rule (说话):** Both characters simplify the speech radical 言 to 讠; 说 also regularizes 兌 to 兑. These component replacements reduce strokes and standardize forms.
