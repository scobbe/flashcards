## to speak; to say
### sub-word
#### to speak
---
- **traditional:** 說
- **simplified:** 说
- **pronunciation:** shuō
- **definition:** to speak; to say
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 说(說)话(話) (shuō huà, "to speak")
  - 说(說)明 (shuō míng, "to explain")
  - 说(說)不定 (shuō bù dìng, "maybe")
  - 说(說)到 (shuō dào, "to mention")
- **etymology (說):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 兑(兌) (duì, "exchange").
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 言 signals the domain of speech and language, matching modern senses like “to say,” “to explain,” and related nouns such as “theory.” The phonetic component 兑(兌) supplies the sound. Some scholars further connect its pronunciations to a Proto-Sino-Tibetan root for “loosen/release” or alternatively “to speak,” but the graphic formation clearly anchors it in the speech domain.
  - **component characters:**
    - 言 (yán, "speech")
    - 兑(兌) (duì, "exchange")
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%AA%AA
  - **simplification rule (说):** Simplified by replacing the speech radical 訁 with its abbreviated form 讠 and by simplifying the phonetic 兌 to 兑, reducing strokes while preserving structure.
