## mountain
---
- **traditional:** 山
- **simplified:** 山
- **pronunciation:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 爬山 (pá shān, "go hiking")
  - 登山 (dēng shān, "mountain climbing")
  - 冰山 (bīng shān, "an iceberg")
  - 山区(區) (shān qū, "mountainous area")
- **etymology (山):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of three mountain peaks.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts three adjacent mountain peaks. Early forms used triangular shapes; later the form was regularized to three strokes, but it continued to mean natural mountains and hills.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B1%B1

## silver
---
- **traditional:** 銀
- **simplified:** 银
- **pronunciation:** yín
- **definition:** silver
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 白银(銀) (bái yín, "silver")
  - 银(銀)仔 (yín zǎi, "coins")
  - 两(兩)蚊银(銀) (liǎng wén yín, "two-dollar coin")
  - 几(幾)千银(銀) (jǐ qiān yín, "a few thousand bucks")
- **etymology (銀):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 金 (jīn, "metal") + phonetic: 艮 (gèn, "stopping").
  - **interpretation:** 金 (jīn, "metal") is the semantic determinative, marking the character as related to metal. 艮 (gèn, "stopping") provides the sound, approximating the historical reading that led to yín; the metal determinative fits the core meaning "silver" and its extension to money.
  - **component characters:**
    - 金
    - 艮
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%8A%80
  - **simplification rule (银):** Simplified by replacing the left-side 金 with the simplified metal radical 钅 while retaining the phonetic 艮.

## to speak
---
- **traditional:** 說話
- **simplified:** 说话
- **pronunciation:** shuōhuà
- **definition:** to speak
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 说(說)话(話)小声(聲)点(點) (shuō huà xiǎo shēng diǎn, "speak more quietly")
  - 说(說)话(話)算数(數) (shuō huà suàn shù, "keep one's word")
  - 说(說)话(話)技巧 (shuō huà jì qiǎo, "speaking skills")
  - 说(說)话(話)方式 (shuō huà fāng shì, "manner of speaking")
- **etymology (說話):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 说(說) (shuō, "speak") + 话(話) (huà, "speech").
  - **interpretation:** A verb–object compound combining the verb for speaking with the noun for speech, yielding the sense “to speak; to talk”. The semantics directly match modern usage: performing the action 说 on the object 话 produces speaking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 说
    - 說
    - 话
    - 話
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%AA%AA%E8%A9%B1
  - **simplification rule (说话):** Simplified by reducing 言 to 讠 and regularizing components: 說 → 说 by replacing the right part with 兑, and 話 → 话 as 讠 + 舌.

## to speak; to say; to explain; to persuade
### sub-word
#### to speak
---
- **traditional:** 說
- **simplified:** 说
- **pronunciation:** shuō
- **definition:** to speak; to say; to explain; to persuade
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 说(說)话(話) (shuōhuà, "to speak")
  - 说(說)明 (shuōmíng, "to explain")
  - 说(說)起 (shuōqǐ, "to mention")
  - 说(說)相声(聲) (shuō xiàngsheng, "perform crosstalk")
- **etymology (說):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 言 (yán, "speech") + phonetic: 兑(兌) (duì, "exchange") → "to speak" meaning.
  - **interpretation:** Formed as a phono-semantic compound: 言 (yán, "speech") is the semantic determinative indicating speech, while 兑(兌) (duì, "exchange") supplies the sound. This matches modern senses related to speaking and explaining. Some sources relate the word family to a root meaning "to loosen," while others compare a Proto‑Sino‑Tibetan root "to speak," the latter fitting the semantics better.
  - **component characters:**
    - 言
    - 兑
    - 兌
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%AA%AA
  - **simplification rule (说):** Component reduction and regularization: the speech radical 訁 was simplified to 讠, and the standardized form follows the variant 説 with 兑 as the phonetic, yielding 说.

#### to speak

- **traditional:** 話

- **contemporary usage:**

  - 话(話)别(別) (huà bié, "say goodbye")
  - 话(話)家常 (huà jiā cháng, "chit-chat")
  - 第一话(話) (dì yī huà, "episode one")
- **etymology (話):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic compound: semantic: 言 (yán, "word") + phonetic: 𠯑 (guō, "phonetic sign").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 言 as a semantic determinative for speech with 𠯑 as a phonetic element indicating the pronunciation. Although the right side resembles 舌, the source notes it is historically 𠯑 and not related to 舌, aligning the meaning with speaking and words.
  - **component characters:**
    - 言
    - 𠯑
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%A9%B1
  - **simplification rule (话):** The traditional left component 言 was simplified to the abbreviated radical 讠, with overall stroke reduction and graphic regularization to yield 话.
