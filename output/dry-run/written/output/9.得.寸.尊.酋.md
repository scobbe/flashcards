## wine
### sub-component
#### esteem
---
- **traditional:** 酋
- **simplified:** 酋
- **pronunciation:** qiú
- **definition:** wine
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 酋长(長) (qiúzhǎng, "tribal chief")
  - 敌(敵)酋 (díqiú, "enemy chieftain")
  - 酋长(長)制度 (qiúzhǎng zhìdù, "chieftain system")
  - 酋长(長)岩 (qiúzhǎng yán, "Chieftain Rock")
- **etymology (酋):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 八 (bā, "eight") above 酉 (yǒu, "wine vessel").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts exposed liquid above a wine vessel, pointing to liquor and its handling. From this, it came to denote the person in charge of liquor preparation, and later was extended to a title for a tribal leader, now chiefly seen in compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 八
    - 酉
