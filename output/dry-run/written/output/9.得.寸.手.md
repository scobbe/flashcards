## hand
### sub-component
#### inch
---
- **traditional:** 手
- **simplified:** 手
- **pronunciation:** shǒu
- **definition:** hand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 手机(機) (shǒu jī, "mobile phone")
  - 手册(冊) (shǒu cè, "handbook")
  - 手指 (shǒu zhǐ, "finger")
  - 手腕 (shǒu wàn, "wrist")
- **etymology (手):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 手 (shǒu, "hand").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a human hand with a bent middle finger at the top and horizontal strokes representing pairs of fingers. The pictorial meaning “hand” remained primary, later extending to roles (expert, -er) and items handled by hand in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 手
