## hand
### sub-component
#### (1) structural particle (verb complements); (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
---
- **traditional:** 又
- **simplified:** 又
- **pronunciation:** yòu
- **definition:** hand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 又下雨了 (yòu xiàyǔ le, "raining again")
  - 聪(聰)明又漂亮 (cōngmíng yòu piàoliang, "smart and pretty")
  - 四小时(時)又十分钟(鐘) (sì xiǎoshí yòu shí fēnzhōng, "four hours and ten minutes")
  - 一次又一次 (yī cì yòu yī cì, "again and again")
- **etymology (又):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 右 (yòu, "right") 手 (shǒu, "hand").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a right hand. The character’s modern adverbial meanings are explained as an adverbial derivation of 有 (yǒu, "to have; there is"), extending from possession/existence to senses of addition and repetition.
  - **component characters:**
    - 右
    - 手
