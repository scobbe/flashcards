## population
---
- **traditional:** 人口
- **simplified:** 人口
- **pronunciation:** rénkǒu
- **definition:** population
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人口很多 (rén kǒu hěn duō, "large population")
  - 人口密度 (rén kǒu mì dù, "population density")
  - 人口普查 (rén kǒu pǔ chá, "population census")
  - 人口结(結)构(構) (rén kǒu jié gòu, "population structure")
- **etymology (人口):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 人 (rén, "person") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 人 "person" and 口 "mouth", literally referring to mouths to feed as a way of counting people. The sense broadened from counting individuals in a household or group to the aggregate population of a place in modern demographics.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
    - 口
