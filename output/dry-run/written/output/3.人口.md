## population
---
- **traditional:** 人口
- **simplified:** 人口
- **pronunciation:** rénkǒu
- **definition:** population
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人口很多
    - rénkǒu hěn duō
    - large population
  - 人口增长(人口增長)
    - rénkǒu zēngzhǎng
    - population growth
  - 人口密度
    - rénkǒu mìdù
    - population density
  - 人口普查
    - rénkǒu pǔchá
    - population census
- **etymology (人口):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:**
    - semantic: 人 (rén, "person") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth") →
    - 人口 (rénkǒu, "population")
  - **interpretation:** Combines 人 'person' with 口 'mouth', historically used as a counter for people, to denote the number of people. The semantic pairing straightforwardly yields the modern sense 'population' as a count of persons.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
      - rén
      - person; people; human
    - 口
      - kǒu
      - mouth; opening; classifier for family members, wells, etc.
