Verb

[edit]

說⫽話 (verb-object)

  1. to speak; to talk
    來不及說話／来不及说话  ―  láibùjí shuōhuà  ―  to not have a chance to speak
    他是南方人，所以一張口說話就帶有南方口音。 [MSC, trad.]
    他是南方人，所以一张口说话就带有南方口音。 [MSC, simp.]
    Tā shì nánfāngrén, suǒyǐ yī zhāngkǒu shuōhuà jiù dàiyǒu nánfāng kǒuyīn. [Pinyin]
    He is from the South, so when he opens his mouth to speak, you can hear his Southern accent.
    這個人說話太霸氣了！ [MSC, trad.]
    这个人说话太霸气了！ [MSC, simp.]
    Zhè ge rén shuōhuà tài bàqì le! [Pinyin]
    The way this person speaks is too aggressive!
  2. (Guanzhong Mandarin) to chat
  3. to scold; to berate
  4. (Teochew) to pray before a deity

Noun

[edit]

說話

  1. (archaic or dialectal) words; what one says
    唔好再同我講呢啲說話。 [Cantonese, trad.]
    唔好再同我讲呢啲说话。 [Cantonese, simp.]
    m4 hou2 zoi3 tung4 ngo5 gong2 ni1 di1 syut3 waa6. [Jyutping]
    Don't say those words to me ever again.
  2. (archaic, Tang and Song dynasties) a genre of traditional storytelling
  3. (Cantonese) saying (Classifier: 句 c)
    自古有句說話，「[…]」 [Cantonese, trad.]
    自古有句说话，「[…]」 [Cantonese, simp.]
    zi6 gu2 jau5 geoi3 syut3 waa6, “[…]” [Jyutping]
    there is a saying that " […] "
  4. (Linshao Wu, Nantong Wu and Quzhou Wu) language; dialect
    崇明說話／崇明说话 [Northern Wu]  ―  Chongmingese
