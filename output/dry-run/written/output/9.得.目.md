## eye
### sub-component
#### (1) structural particle (verb complements); (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
---
- **traditional:** 目
- **simplified:** 目
- **pronunciation:** mù
- **definition:** eye
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 目录(錄) (mù lù, "table of contents")
  - 目标(標) (mù biāo, "target or goal")
  - 目的 (mù dì, "purpose or goal")
  - 目击(擊)者 (mù jī zhě, "an eyewitness")
- **etymology (目):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of human eye.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human eye. The original sense ‘eye’ broadened to related actions like seeing and to organizational senses such as ‘item/entry’ and ‘target/goal’ through semantic extension in administrative and evaluative contexts.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
