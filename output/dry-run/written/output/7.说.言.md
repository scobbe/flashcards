## speech
### sub-component
#### to speak; to say
---
- **traditional:** 言
- **simplified:** 言
- **pronunciation:** yán
- **definition:** speech
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 言语(語) (yányǔ, "spoken words")
  - 言论(論) (yánlùn, "public discourse")
  - 言谈(談) (yántán, "way of speaking")
  - 言辞(辭) (yáncí, "choice of words")
- **etymology (言):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: 舌 (shé, "tongue") in 口 (kǒu, "mouth") with an added top stroke marking tongue movement → depiction of speaking → speech.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a tongue in a mouth with an extra stroke indicating motion, i.e., speaking. This visual indication naturally extended to mean speech and words. Some traditional sources misanalyze it as phono-semantic, but the core formation is indicative of tongue movement in the mouth.
  - **component characters:**
    - 舌
    - 口
