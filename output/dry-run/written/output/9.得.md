## (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
---
- **traditional:** 得
- **simplified:** 得
- **pronunciation:** de/dé/děi
- **definition:** (1) structural particle for verb complements; (2) to get, obtain; (3) must, have to
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 得到
    - dé dào
    - to obtain
  - 得体(得體)
    - dé tǐ
    - socially appropriate
  - 得意
    - dé yì
    - pleased with oneself
  - 得奖
    - dé jiǎng
    - win a prize
- **etymology (得):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**
    - ideogrammic: 贝(貝) (bèi, "cowry") + 又 (yòu, "hand") →
    - semantic: 彳 (chì, "step") added to indicate picking up on the road
  - **interpretation:** A hand taking a cowry shell depicts acquiring valuables, which yields the sense ‘to obtain’. The added 彳 serves as a semantic indicator of movement or path, reinforcing the action of picking something up. Later graphic changes did not alter the core meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 贝(貝)
      - bèi
      - cowry
    - 又
      - yòu
      - hand
    - 彳
      - chì
      - step
