## mountain
---
- **traditional:** 山
- **simplified:** 山
- **pronunciation:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 我们(們)去爬山 (wǒ men qù pá shān, "we go hiking")
  - 登山 (dēng shān, "mountain climbing")
  - 冰山 (bīng shān, "iceberg")
  - 山区(區) (shān qū, "mountainous area")
- **etymology (山):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of three mountain peaks.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts three mountain peaks; early oracle-bone forms used triangles that were later stylized into vertical strokes. The straightforward pictorial origin directly yields the modern meaning “mountain.”
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
