## mountain
---
- **traditional:** 山
- **simplified:** 山
- **pronunciation:** shān
- **definition:** mountain
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 爬山
    - pá shān
    - go hiking
  - 登山
    - dēng shān
    - mountain climbing
  - 冰山
    - bīng shān
    - an iceberg
  - 山区
    - shān qū
    - mountainous area
- **etymology (山):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:**
    - pictogram: depiction of three mountain peaks
  - **interpretation:** It depicts three adjacent mountain peaks. Early forms used triangular shapes; later the form was regularized to three strokes, but it continued to mean natural mountains and hills.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form.
