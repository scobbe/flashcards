## stopping
### sub-component
#### silver
---
- **traditional:** 艮
- **simplified:** 艮
- **pronunciation:** gèn
- **definition:** stopping
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 儒艮 (rú gèn, "dugong")
  - 艮山 (gèn shān, "Gen mountain")
  - 艮岳(嶽) (gèn yuè, "Genyue imperial garden")
  - 艮苦冰凉(涼) (gèn kǔ bīng liáng, "very bitter and icy-cold")
- **etymology (艮):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 目 (mù, "eye") + 人 (rén, "person") → a person looking back → disobedience or limit.
  - **interpretation:** Combining 目 "eye" and 人 "person" depicts a person looking back. This visual conveys the idea of stopping or being held back, which developed into senses like disobedience and limit.
  - **component characters:**
    - 目
    - 人
