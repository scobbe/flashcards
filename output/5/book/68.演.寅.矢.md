## arrow
### sub-component
#### third earthly branch
---
- **traditional:** 矢
- **simplified:** 矢
- **pronunciation:** shǐ
- **definition:** arrow
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 有的放矢 (yǒu de fàng shǐ, "aim at the target")
  - 众(眾)矢之的 (zhòng shǐ zhī dì, "target of public criticism")
  - 矢量 (shǐ liàng, "vector")
  - 矢口否认(認) (shǐ kǒu fǒu rèn, "flatly deny")
- **etymology (矢):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of an arrow.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts an arrow, reflecting the original concrete object. The core meaning remained “arrow,” and by extension it underlies modern idioms and terms drawing on archery imagery for ideas like accuracy and targeting.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
