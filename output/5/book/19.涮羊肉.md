## thin slices of mutton boiled in water
---
- **traditional:** 涮羊肉
- **simplified:** 涮羊肉
- **pronunciation:** shuànyángròu
- **definition:** thin slices of mutton boiled in water
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 涮羊肉火锅(鍋) (shuàn yáng ròu huǒ guō, "mutton hot pot")
  - 涮羊肉店 (shuàn yáng ròu diàn, "mutton hot pot restaurant")
  - 涮羊肉片 (shuàn yáng ròu piàn, "thin mutton slices")
  - 涮羊肉套餐 (shuàn yáng ròu tào cān, "mutton hot pot set meal")
