## music
### sub-word
#### concert hall
---
- **traditional:** 音樂
- **simplified:** 音乐
- **pronunciation:** yīnyuè
- **definition:** music
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 音乐(樂)会(會) (yīnyuèhuì, "music concert")
  - 音乐(樂)家 (yīnyuèjiā, "musical artist")
  - 音乐(樂)剧(劇) (yīnyuèjù, "musical theater")
  - 音乐(樂)视(視)频(頻) (yīnyuè shìpín, "music video")
- **etymology (音樂):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 音 (yīn, "sound") + 乐(樂) (yuè, "music").
  - **interpretation:** 音 (yīn, "sound") modifies 乐(樂) (yuè, "music"), yielding the idea of organized sound as music. 乐 supplies the core sense "music", while 音 specifies the domain of sounds, aligning with the modern meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 音
    - 乐
    - 樂
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82
  - **simplification rule (音乐):** The simplified form replaces 樂 with 乐, reducing strokes through component replacement, while 音 remains unchanged.
