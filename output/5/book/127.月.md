## moon
### sub-word
#### traditional music composition
---
- **traditional:** 月
- **simplified:** 月
- **pronunciation:** yuè
- **definition:** moon
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 月亮 (yuèliàng, "moon")
  - 月球 (yuèqiú, "the Moon")
  - 月饼(餅) (yuèbǐng, "mooncake")
  - 月薪 (yuèxīn, "monthly wage")
- **etymology (月):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a crescent moon.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a crescent moon. The character came to denote the moon itself and, by extension, a month based on the lunar cycle. Early usage notes that it was sometimes interchangeable with 夕.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
