## person
### sub-component
#### to catch; to succeed
---
- **traditional:** 者
- **simplified:** 者
- **pronunciation:** zhě
- **definition:** person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 记(記)者 (jì zhě, "news reporter")
  - 读(讀)者 (dú zhě, "book reader")
  - 作者 (zuò zhě, "book author")
  - 患者 (huàn zhě, "medical patient")
- **etymology (者):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane") with an open mouth below → phonetic loan to nominalizing suffix 'one who'.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a sugarcane stalk with stems and an open mouth below. It was later borrowed for its sound to serve as a nominalizing suffix meaning 'one who' or 'that which'. Some analyses note it may originally have written 煮 (zhǔ, "to boil"), and its grammatical use is also related to 之 (zhī, "nominalizer/possessive") and 的 (de, "nominalizer").
  - **component characters:**
    - 蔗
