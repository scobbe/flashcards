## with; by
### sub-word
#### so
---
- **traditional:** 以
- **simplified:** 以
- **pronunciation:** yǐ
- **definition:** with; by
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 以来(來) (yǐlái, "since")
  - 以上 (yǐshàng, "above")
  - 以为(為) (yǐwéi, "to think")
  - 以及 (yǐjí, "as well as")
- **etymology (以):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 人 (rén, "person") carrying something.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person carrying a load, reflecting the concrete action of taking or using. This physical sense broadened into prepositional uses indicating means, cause, or purpose, hence modern meanings like "by; with; in order to".
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
