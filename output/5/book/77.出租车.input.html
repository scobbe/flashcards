Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 出租车 – see 出租車 (“taxi; taxicab”).
(This term is the simplified form of 出租車).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Noun

[edit]

出租車

  1. (chiefly Mainland China) taxi; taxicab (Classifier: 輛／辆 m; 臺／台 m)
    出租車司機／出租车司机  ―  chūzūchē sījī  ―  taxi driver
    出租車專用停車位 [MSC, trad.]
    出租车专用停车位 [MSC, simp.]
    chūzūchē zhuānyòng tíngchēwèi [Pinyin]
    taxi parking only
