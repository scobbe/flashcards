## measure word for articles
---
- **traditional:** 篇
- **simplified:** 篇
- **pronunciation:** piān
- **definition:** measure word for articles
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一篇文章 (yì piān wénzhāng, "one article")
  - 篇章 (piānzhāng, "chapters and sections")
  - 篇幅 (piānfú, "length of text")
  - 篇首 (piānshǒu, "beginning of article")
- **etymology (篇):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic: 扁 (biǎn, "flat") → 篇 (piān, "piece of writing").
  - **interpretation:** 竹 provides the semantic domain of bamboo slips and writing materials, indicating a relation to texts. 扁 supplies the sound, and the compound thus came to denote a written piece and by extension serves as a classifier for articles.
  - **component characters:**
    - 竹
    - 扁
    - 篇
