- **contemporary usage:**

  - 南爿 (nán pán, "south side")
- **etymology (爿):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of bed → original form of 床(牀) (chuáng, "bed").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a bed, which originally wrote the word now written as 床(牀). Later the bed meaning was carried by 床(牀), while 爿 remained as a radical meaning “half tree trunk”, with dialectal senses like “side” and “slice” reflecting a chopped piece.
  - **component characters:**
