## to change
---
- **traditional:** 化
- **simplified:** 化
- **pronunciation:** huà
- **definition:** to change
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 化冰为(為)水 (huà bīng wéi shuǐ, "turn ice into water")
  - 化学(學) (huà xué, "chemistry")
  - 化妆(妝) (huà zhuāng, "makeup")
  - 变(變)化 (biàn huà, "change")
- **etymology (化):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person upside down") → reversal; change.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines an upright person with an inverted person to express reversal and transformation, yielding the sense of change. Some sources note the right element may also serve as a phonetic, but the primary design is semantic and aligns with the modern sense of transforming states.
  - **component characters:**
    - 亻
    - 𠤎
