Korean

[edit]

Etymology

[edit]

From Middle Chinese 郊 (MC kaew). Recorded as Middle Korean 교 (kywo) (Yale: kyo) in Hunmong Jahoe (訓蒙字會 / 훈몽자회), 1527.

Hanja

[edit]
Korean Wikisource has texts containing the hanja:
郊

郊 (eumhun 들 교 (deul gyo))

  1. hanja form? of 교 (“outskirts”)
