## crooked
### sub-component
#### pot; pan
---
- **traditional:** 咼
- **simplified:** 咼
- **pronunciation:** wāi / kuāi / guō / wō / wǒ / hé
- **definition:** crooked
- **contemporary usage:**
  - None, not in contemporary use.
- **etymology (咼):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 冎 (guǎ, "bone") + semantic: 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** A phono-semantic compound with 冎 supplying the sound and 口 marking the semantic field of the mouth. The sense ‘slanting’ likely reflects an extension from the idea of a wry or askew mouth, while other listed uses (surname, toponyms) preserve the phonetic value.
  - **component characters:**
    - 冎
    - 口
