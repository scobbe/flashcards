## to rent out
### sub-word
#### taxi
---
- **traditional:** 出租
- **simplified:** 出租
- **pronunciation:** chūzū
- **definition:** to rent out
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 出租房 (chū zū fáng, "rental apartment")
  - 出租汽车(車) (chū zū qì chē, "taxi cab")
  - 出租车(車) (chū zū chē, "taxi cab")
  - 出租人 (chū zū rén, "the lessor")
- **etymology (出租):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 出 (chū, "go out") + 租 (zū, "rent") → 出租 (chū zū, "rent out").
  - **interpretation:** 出 means to go out or let out, and 租 means rent. Together they express letting something out for rent, which extends to the noun shorthand for taxi via 出租汽车.
  - **component characters:**
    - 出
    - 租
