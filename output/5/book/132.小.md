## small
### sub-word
#### primary school
---
- **traditional:** 小
- **simplified:** 小
- **pronunciation:** xiǎo
- **definition:** small
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 小孩 (xiǎohái, "young child")
  - 小狗 (xiǎogǒu, "a puppy")
  - 小猫(貓) (xiǎomāo, "a kitten")
  - 小学(學) (xiǎoxué, "primary school")
- **etymology (小):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 沙 (shā, "sand") → small.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts three granules of sand, emphasizing minuteness; from this, the sense generalized to small. Some sources instead interpret it as an ideogram of three small dots, and Shuowen further reanalyzed it as an ideogrammic compound 八 (bā, "divide") + 丨 (gǔn, "small object").
  - **component characters:**
    - 沙
