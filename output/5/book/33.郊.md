## suburbs
### sub-word
#### suburb; outskirts
---
- **traditional:** 郊
- **simplified:** 郊
- **pronunciation:** jiāo
- **definition:** suburbs
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 郊区(區) (jiāo qū, "suburban area")
  - 郊外 (jiāo wài, "the outskirts")
  - 郊游(遊) (jiāo yóu, "suburban outing")
  - 郊野公园(園) (jiāo yě gōng yuán, "country park")
- **etymology (郊):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 交 (jiāo, "cross") + semantic: 阝 (yì, "city") → place outside the city.
  - **interpretation:** 交 serves as the phonetic, indicating the reading jiāo, while 阝 (yì, "city", i.e., 邑 (yì, "city")) supplies the semantic domain. Together they denote an area pertaining to but outside the city limits, which aligns with the modern sense "suburbs".
  - **component characters:**
    - 交
    - 阜
