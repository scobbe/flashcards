## primary school
---
- **traditional:** 小學
- **simplified:** 小学
- **pronunciation:** xiǎoxué
- **definition:** primary school
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 小学(學)教师(師) (xiǎoxué jiàoshī, "primary school teacher")
  - 小学(學)生 (xiǎoxué shēng, "primary school student")
  - 小学(學)教育 (xiǎoxué jiàoyù, "primary education")
  - 小学(學)课(課)本 (xiǎoxué kèběn, "primary school textbook")
- **etymology (小學):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 小 (xiǎo, "small") + 学(學) (xué, "learning").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 小 “small” and 学 “learning” to denote “elementary learning,” which extends to the modern institution of primary school. Historically, 小學 also named the field of traditional scholarship on characters and sounds; the contemporary school sense is a later institutional application.
  - **component characters:**
    - 小
    - 学
    - 學
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%B0%8F%E5%AD%B8
  - **simplification rule (小学):** The traditional 學 was simplified to 学 by replacing complex components and reducing strokes (notably removing the lower 子 element and regularizing the top), while 小 remained unchanged.
