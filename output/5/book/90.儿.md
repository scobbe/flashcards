## child
### sub-word
#### flower
---
- **traditional:** 兒
- **simplified:** 儿
- **pronunciation:** ér
- **definition:** child
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 儿(兒)童 (ér tóng, "children")
  - 婴(嬰)儿(兒) (yīng ér, "infant")
  - 味儿(兒) (wèir, "taste")
  - 儿(兒)孙(孫) (ér sūn, "children and grandchildren")
- **etymology (兒):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 人 (rén, "person").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a standing person (legs), expressing the human domain. In Simplified Chinese, it was selected as the simplified form of 儿(兒) (ér, "child") by eliminating the top component 臼 (jiù, "mortar"), leaving the person glyph, which still suits the meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%85%92
  - **simplification rule (儿):** Component elimination: 兒 dropped the top component 臼, retaining 儿 to reduce strokes.
