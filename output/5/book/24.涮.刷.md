## brush
### sub-component
#### to cook thin slices of meat in boiling water
---
- **traditional:** 刷
- **simplified:** 刷
- **pronunciation:** shuā / shuà
- **definition:** brush
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 刷牙 (shuā yá, "brush teeth")
  - 刷卡 (shuā kǎ, "swipe a card")
  - 刷手机(機) (shuā shǒu jī, "check phone")
  - 刷新 (shuā xīn, "refresh")
- **etymology (刷):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 刂 (dāo, "knife") + phonetic: 𡰯 (shuā, "phonetic") → shave flat with a knife → scrape; print.
  - **interpretation:** 刂 supplies the semantic domain of cutting/scraping, while 𡰯 indicates the pronunciation near shuā. The original sense of shaving something flat broadened to scraping/brush-like actions and later extended to printing and modern swipe/refresh usages.
  - **component characters:**
    - 刂
    - 𡰯
