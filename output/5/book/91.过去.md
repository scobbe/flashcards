## in or of the past
---
- **traditional:** 過去
- **simplified:** 过去
- **pronunciation:** guòqù
- **definition:** in or of the past
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 过(過)去的人 (guò qù de rén, "people in the past")
  - 过(過)去分词(詞) (guò qù fēn cí, "past participle")
  - 过(過)去式 (guò qù shì, "past tense")
  - 过(過)去时(時) (guò qù shí, "past tense")
- **etymology (過去):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 过(過) (guò, "to pass") + 去 (qù, "to go").
  - **interpretation:** This is a Buddhist calque of Sanskrit atīta “gone by”. It combines 过 “to pass” and 去 “to go” to mean “what has gone past”, yielding the noun “the past” and related verbal senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 过
    - 過
    - 去
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%81%8E%E5%8E%BB
  - **simplification rule (过去):** Simplified 过 was formed by component replacement and stroke reduction from 過, while 去 remained unchanged.
