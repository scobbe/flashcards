Ideogrammic compound (會意 / 会意) of two 人 (“person”), one upright (亻) and one upside down (𠤎) — reversal; change. Shuowen also considers 𠤎 to be a phonetic component.

Compare 并, 比, 卬, 尼 and 北.

Etymology 1

[edit]

A possible derived term is 瓦 (OC *ŋʷraːlʔ, *ŋʷraːls, “earthenware, tile”), by eliminating causative devoicing and adding an endoactive rising tone (上聲), thus literally “that which has transformed (through fire)”. Also related to 貨 (OC *hŋʷaːls, “property, goods”), 吪 (OC *ŋʷaːl, “move, act”), and perhaps 訛 (OC *ŋʷaːl, “false”) (Schuessler, 2007).

Definitions

[edit]

化

  1. to change; to transform
  2. to become; to turn into
  3. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to die
  4. to melt
    把雪化掉  ―  bǎ xuě huàdiào  ―  make snow melt
  5. to dissolve
  6. Suffix denoting "change into ...". Akin to English -ize and -ization.
    民主化  ―  mínzhǔhuà  ―  democratize
    物化  ―  wùhuà  ―  objectify
  7. (chemistry) Affix denoting combination.
    氧化鈣／氧化钙  ―  yǎnghuàgài  ―  calcium oxide
    氫氧化鈉／氢氧化钠  ―  qīngyǎnghuànà  ―  sodium hydroxide
  8. short for 教化 (jiàohuà, “enlightenment; cultivation”)
  9. (in compounds) short for 化學／化学 (huàxué, “chemistry”)
    日化  ―  rìhuà  ―  household chemicals
    化工  ―  huàgōng  ―  chemical industry
  10. to burn (clarification of this definition is needed)
    化寶／化宝  ―  huàbǎo  ―  to burn offerings
  11. (Cantonese) open-minded; enlightened: accept the change
    睇化 [Cantonese]  ―  tai2 faa3 [Jyutping]  ―  to see things in an open-minded manner
    教極都唔化／教极都唔化 [Cantonese]  ―  gaau3 gik6 dou1 m4 faa3 [Jyutping]  ―  He don’t accept to change his habit even after being taught many times

Definitions

化 (Hokkien)

  1. to ferment; to brew; to dissolve
    化醬清／化酱清  ―  òa chiùⁿ-chheng  ―  to ferment soybeans and extract soy sauce
    化灰  ―  òa-he  ―  to slake lime
  2. (Zhangzhou Hokkien) to soak
    化田  ―  òa-chhân  ―  to soak paddy field with water

Etymology

[edit]
Kanji in this term
化
か
Grade: 3
on'yomi

From Middle Chinese 化 (MC xwaeH).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • IPA(key): [ka̠]

Affix

[edit]

化(か) • (ka) ←くわ (kwa)?

  1. to change; to take the form of
  2. to influence

Suffix

[edit]

化(か) • (-ka) ←くわ (-kwa)?

  1. -ization, -ification; action of making something that way

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • (SK Standard/Seoul) IPA(key): [ɸwa̠(ː)]
  • Phonetic hangul: [화(ː)]
