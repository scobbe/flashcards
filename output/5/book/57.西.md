## west
---
- **traditional:** 西
- **simplified:** 西
- **pronunciation:** xī
- **definition:** west
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 西边(邊) (xī biān, "west side")
  - 西方 (xī fāng, "the West")
  - 西瓜 (xī guā, "watermelon")
  - 西医(醫) (xī yī, "Western medicine")
- **etymology (西):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a bird settling into its nest → semantic: analogy with the setting sun yields 'west'.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bird settling into its nest. By analogy with the sun setting toward that side of the sky, it came to denote the west in modern directional use. Some scholars instead see a bag or basket pictogram later borrowed for its sound to write the word for west.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
