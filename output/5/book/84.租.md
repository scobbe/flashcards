## to rent
### sub-word
#### to rent; to hire
---
- **traditional:** 租
- **simplified:** 租
- **pronunciation:** zū
- **definition:** to rent
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 租金 (zūjīn, "rent payment")
  - 租约(約) (zūyuē, "lease agreement")
  - 租赁(賃) (zūlìn, "leasing")
  - 租户(戶) (zūhù, "tenant")
- **etymology (租):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 禾 (hé, "grain") + phonetic: 且 (qiě, "moreover") → "rent; land tax".
  - **interpretation:** 禾 (hé, "grain") provides the semantic field of agriculture, reflecting rent or tax paid in grain. 且 (qiě, "moreover") supplies the sound, while the sense extended from "land tax/rent in grain" to the modern "to rent/lease".
  - **component characters:**
    - 禾 (hé, "grain")
    - 且 (qiě, "moreover")
