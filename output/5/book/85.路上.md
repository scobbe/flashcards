## on the road
---
- **traditional:** 路上
- **simplified:** 路上
- **pronunciation:** lùshang
- **definition:** on the road
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 路上小心 (lùshàng xiǎoxīn, "take care on the road")
  - 路上堵车(車) (lùshàng dǔchē, "traffic jam on the road")
  - 路上见(見) (lùshàng jiàn, "see you on the way")
  - 路上注意安全 (lùshàng zhùyì ānquán, "stay safe on the road")
- **etymology (路上):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 路 (lù, "road") + 上 (shàng, "on").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 路 (lù, "road") with 上 (shàng, "on") to indicate location on a road and, by extension, being en route. The modern sense is a transparent composition of the two meanings.
  - **component characters:**
    - 路
    - 上
