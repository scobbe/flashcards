## camera
---
- **traditional:** 照相機
- **simplified:** 照相机
- **pronunciation:** zhàoxiàngjī
- **definition:** camera
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 照相机(機)新款 (zhàoxiàngjī xīnkuǎn, "new camera model")
  - 照相机(機)镜(鏡)头(頭) (zhàoxiàngjī jìngtóu, "camera lens")
  - 照相机(機)包 (zhàoxiàngjī bāo, "camera bag")
  - 照相机(機)配件 (zhàoxiàngjī pèijiàn, "camera accessories")
- **etymology (照相機):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 照相 (zhàoxiàng, "to photograph") + 机(機) (jī, "machine").
  - **interpretation:** Here 照相 (zhàoxiàng, "to photograph") denotes the action, and 机(機) (jī, "machine") names the device, yielding a "machine for taking photographs", i.e., a camera. The semantic determinative is 机(機), aligning with the modern sense of a device, while 照相 specifies its function.
  - **component characters:**
    - 照
    - 相
    - 机
    - 機
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%85%A7%E7%9B%B8%E6%A9%9F
  - **simplification rule (照相机):** 機 was simplified to 机 by replacing the complex phonetic 幾 with the simpler 几 and reducing strokes, a standard component-replacement simplification.
