## sun
### sub-component
#### third earthly branch
---
- **traditional:** 日
- **simplified:** 日
- **pronunciation:** rì
- **definition:** sun
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 日班 (rì bān, "day shift")
  - 三月四日 (sān yuè sì rì, "March 4")
  - 中日词(詞)典 (zhōng rì cí diǎn, "Chinese-Japanese dictionary")
  - 日式咖喱 (rì shì gā lí, "Japanese-style curry")
- **etymology (日):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of the sun → addition of a central dot or line to distinguish it from similar graphs.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the disk of the sun; a central mark was added to avoid confusion with similar box-like graphs. The concrete image led to the core meanings sun and, by extension, day in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
