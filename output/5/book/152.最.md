## most
---
- **traditional:** 最
- **simplified:** 最
- **pronunciation:** zuì
- **definition:** most
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 这(這)个(個)最好 (zhè gè zuì hǎo, "this is the best")
  - 最后(後) (zuì hòu, "in the end")
  - 最近 (zuì jìn, "in recent times")
  - 最高法院 (zuì gāo fǎ yuàn, "supreme court")
- **etymology (最):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 宀 (mián, "roof") + phonetic: 取 (qǔ, "to gather") → addition of 二 (èr, "two") as distinguishing marks → 最 (zuì, "most").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 宀 as the semantic determinative with 取 as the phonetic, originally written as a phono-semantic form. Later, 二 was added to distinguish the superlative meaning, yielding the modern sense "most."
  - **component characters:**
    - 宀 (mián, "roof")
    - 取 (qǔ, "to gather")
    - 二 (èr, "two")
