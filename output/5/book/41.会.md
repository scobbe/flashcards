## meeting; event
### sub-word
#### concert
---
- **traditional:** 會
- **simplified:** 会
- **pronunciation:** huì
- **definition:** meeting; event
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 社会(會) (shèhuì, "human society")
  - 帮(幫)会(會) (bānghuì, "secret society")
  - 同乡(鄉)会(會) (tóngxiānghuì, "hometown association")
  - 会(會)议(議) (huìyì, "formal meeting")
- **etymology (會):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 合 (hé, "join") with a central vessel of 脍(膾) (kuài, "minced meat"), the top resembling 食 (shí, "food"), expressing bringing together and closure.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts mouths brought together with a vessel of 脍(膾) (kuài, "minced meat") between them, like 合 (hé, "join") with a food container. This concrete image yields the sense "bring together, meet", extending to "meeting/association" in modern usage. Some analyses also note the top resembles 食 (shí, "food"), reinforcing the vessel interpretation.
  - **component characters:**
    - 合
    - 脍
    - 膾
    - 食
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%9C%83
  - **simplification rule (会):** Adopted a long-used cursive shorthand of 會, replacing its complex interior with a simplified component and reducing strokes for easier writing.
