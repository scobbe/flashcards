## one (person)
### sub-word
#### or
---
- **traditional:** 者
- **simplified:** 者
- **pronunciation:** zhě
- **definition:** one (person)
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 记(記)者 (jì zhě, "journalist")
  - 读(讀)者 (dú zhě, "reader")
  - 作者 (zuò zhě, "author")
  - 学(學)者 (xué zhě, "scholar")
- **etymology (者):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane") or 煮 (zhǔ, "to boil").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a sugarcane stalk with surrounding leaves or droplets and an open mouth at the bottom. It originally wrote the word for 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane") or the verb 煮 (zhǔ, "to boil"), and was later borrowed for its sound as a nominalizing suffix meaning "one who/-er". Some traditional analyses take the bottom as 白 (bái, "white"), but this is secondary.
  - **component characters:**
    - 蔗 (zhè, "sugarcane")
    - 煮 (zhǔ, "to boil")
