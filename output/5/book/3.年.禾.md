## grain
### sub-component
#### year
---
- **traditional:** 禾
- **simplified:** 禾
- **pronunciation:** hé
- **definition:** grain
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 禾苗 (hé miáo, "grain seedlings")
  - 禾本科 (hé běn kē, "grass family")
  - 禾虫(蟲) (hé chóng, "rice pest")
  - 禾场(場) (hé chǎng, "threshing ground")
- **etymology (禾):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a plant stalk with the head of grain.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a cereal plant stalk with an ear, more recognizable in early forms. From this concrete drawing, the character came to mean grain or cereal and by extension the rice plant.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
