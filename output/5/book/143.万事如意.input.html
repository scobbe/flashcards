Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 万事如意 – see 萬事如意 (“may all your hopes be fulfilled; all the best”).
(This term is the simplified form of 萬事如意).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
all; everything to be as one wishes; to be satisfactory
trad. (萬事如意) 萬事 如意
simp. (万事如意) 万事 如意

Idiom

[edit]

萬事如意

  1. may all your hopes be fulfilled; all the best
    祝你身體健康，萬事如意。 [MSC, trad.]
    祝你身体健康，万事如意。 [MSC, simp.]
    Zhù nǐ shēntǐ jiànkāng, wànshì rúyì. [Pinyin]
    Wish you good health, and all the best.

Synonyms

[edit]
  • (chiefly Cantonese) 萬事勝意／万事胜意 (wànshìshèngyì)
