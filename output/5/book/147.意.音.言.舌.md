## tongue
### sub-component
#### speech
---
- **traditional:** 舌
- **simplified:** 舌
- **pronunciation:** shé
- **definition:** tongue
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 舌头(頭) (shétou, "tongue")
  - 舌尖 (shéjiān, "tongue tip")
  - 舌战(戰) (shézhàn, "verbal battle")
  - 舌苔 (shétāi, "tongue coating")
- **etymology (舌):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 口 (kǒu, "mouth") with a forked tongue emerging upward.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a forked tongue rising from a mouth (口), representing the physical tongue. The anatomical sense is primary, and by extension it appears in compounds about speech and articulation in modern Chinese.
  - **component characters:**
    - 口
