  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
flower; blossom; to spend
flower; blossom; to spend; fancy pattern
 
 son; child; diminutive suffix
trad. (花兒) 花 兒
simp. (花儿) 花 儿

Noun

[edit]

花兒

  1. Diminutive of 花 (huā, “flower”).
    • 1944, 金玉谷, “夜來香”, performed by 李香蘭:
      月下的花兒都入夢　只有那夜來香吐露著芬芳 [MSC, trad.]
      月下的花儿都入梦　只有那夜来香吐露着芬芳 [MSC, simp.]
      Yuè xià dì huā'ér dōu rù mèng, zhǐ yǒu nà yèláixiāng tǔlù zhe fēnfāng [Pinyin]
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
    • 2001, 韩红, “那片海”, performed by 韩红:
      你看那花儿都谢了　你看那海儿都哭了 [MSC, simp.]
      你看那花兒都謝了　你看那海兒都哭了 [MSC, trad.]
      Nǐ kàn nà huā'ér dōu xiè le, nǐ kàn nà hǎi'ér dōu kū le [Pinyin]
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
    • 2005, “寂寞沙洲冷”, performed by 周傳雄:
      有花兒伴著蝴蝶　孤雁可以雙飛 [MSC, trad.]
      有花儿伴着蝴蝶　孤雁可以双飞 [MSC, simp.]
      Yǒu huā'ér bàn zhe húdié, gūyàn kěyǐ shuāngfēi [Pinyin]
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
    • (Can we date this quote?), “風車 [fēngchē]”, 鄭櫻綸 [England Cheng] (lyrics), performed by 佘詩曼 [Charmaine Sheh]:
      求豐收　雨點降下　花兒別怕 [Literary Cantonese, trad.]
      求丰收　雨点降下　花儿别怕 [Literary Cantonese, simp.]
      kau4 fung1 sau1, jyu5 dim2 gong3 haa6, faa1 ji4 bit6 paa3 [Jyutping]
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
    1. (Mandarin, Jin) Erhua form of 花 (huā, “flower”).
      • 2006, “桃花朵朵開”, 阿牛, 友弟 (lyrics), performed by 阿牛:
        哎喲哎喲　你比花兒還美妙　叫我忘不了 [MSC, trad.]
        哎哟哎哟　你比花儿还美妙　叫我忘不了 [MSC, simp.]
        Āiyō āiyō, nǐ bǐ huār hái měimiào, jiào wǒ wàng bù liǎo [Pinyin]
        (please add an English translation of this quotation)
  2. (Mandarin) Erhua form of 花 (huā, “pattern”).
  3. (Mandarin) Erhua form of 花 (huā, “smallpox”).

Noun

[edit]

花兒

  1. a style of folk song popular in northwest China, including the provinces of Gansu, Qinghai, and Ningxia (Classifier: 首)
    Hypernym: 山歌 (shāngē)
