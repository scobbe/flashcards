## tune; melody
### sub-word
#### musical composition
---
- **traditional:** 曲
- **simplified:** 曲
- **pronunciation:** qǔ
- **definition:** tune; melody
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 曲调(調) (qǔdiào, "melody")
  - 曲谱(譜) (qǔpǔ, "music score")
  - 乐(樂)曲 (yuèqǔ, "musical piece")
  - 主题(題)曲 (zhǔtíqǔ, "theme song")
- **etymology (曲):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: depiction of a bent object.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a bent object, expressing the original sense “bent/curved.” The musical sense “tune; melody” is a later use of the same graph and is not tied to the original formation. In Simplified Chinese, the graph is also used as the simplified form of 麴.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
