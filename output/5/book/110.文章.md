## essay; article
---
- **traditional:** 文章
- **simplified:** 文章
- **pronunciation:** wénzhāng
- **definition:** essay; article
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 文章很好 (wén zhāng hěn hǎo, "the article is great")
  - 文章标(標)题(題) (wén zhāng biāo tí, "article title")
  - 文章内(內)容 (wén zhāng nèi róng, "article content")
  - 文章评(評)论(論) (wén zhāng píng lùn, "article comments")
- **etymology (文章):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 文 (wén, "writing") + 章 (zhāng, "section").
  - **interpretation:** 文 means writing and 章 means section or marked segment; together they denote a piece of writing with structured sections, hence a composition. Classical citations also show 文章 used for decorative patterns, but in modern usage it primarily refers to essays and articles.
  - **component characters:**
    - 文
    - 章
