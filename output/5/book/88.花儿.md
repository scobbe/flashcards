## flower
---
- **traditional:** 花兒
- **simplified:** 花儿
- **pronunciation:** huār
- **definition:** flower
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 花儿(兒)我喜欢(歡) (huār wǒ xǐhuan, "I like flowers")
  - 花儿(兒)都入梦(夢) (huār dōu rù mèng, "flowers fall asleep")
  - 花儿(兒)都谢(謝)了 (huār dōu xiè le, "flowers have withered")
  - 花儿(兒)伴着(著)蝴蝶 (huār bàn zhe húdié, "flowers with butterflies")
- **etymology (花兒):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 花 (huā, "flower") + 儿(兒) (ér, "diminutive suffix").
  - **interpretation:** 儿 is the erhua diminutive suffix added to 花, yielding a colloquial or affectionate form meaning “flower.” The suffix marks a northern colloquial style without changing the core sense; in poetry and songs it may be pronounced separately as huā ér.
  - **component characters:**
    - 花
    - 儿
    - 兒
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%8A%B1%E5%85%92
  - **simplification rule (花儿):** 兒 was simplified to 儿 by component replacement and stroke reduction; 花 remained unchanged.
