- **definition:** cave
- **contemporary usage:**

- **etymology (凵):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a cave.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the mouth of a cave or an open receptacle. This concrete image underlies its role as a radical for openings/containers and explains the later verbal sense “to open one’s mouth”; it also served as an ancient form of 坎 (kǎn, "pit").
