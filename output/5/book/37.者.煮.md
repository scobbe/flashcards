## to boil
### sub-component
#### one (person)
---
- **traditional:** 煮
- **simplified:** 煮
- **pronunciation:** zhǔ
- **definition:** to boil
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 煮面(麵) (zhǔ miàn, "cook noodles")
  - 煮饭(飯) (zhǔ fàn, "cook rice")
  - 煮粥 (zhǔ zhōu, "cook porridge")
  - 煮沸 (zhǔ fèi, "bring to a boil")
- **etymology (煮):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 者 (zhě, "person") + semantic: 灬 (huǒ, "fire").
  - **interpretation:** 灬 is the semantic determinative indicating fire and heating, fitting the domain of cooking. 者 serves as the phonetic component, giving the approximate sound that developed into zhǔ, while the fire element anchors the meaning ‘to cook/boil’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 者
    - 灬
