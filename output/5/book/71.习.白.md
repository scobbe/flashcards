## white
### sub-component
#### to study; to practice
---
- **traditional:** 白
- **simplified:** 白
- **pronunciation:** bái / bó
- **definition:** white
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 白色 (báisè, "white color")
  - 白茶 (báichá, "white tea")
  - 白纸(紙) (báizhǐ, "blank sheet of paper")
  - 白话(話) (báihuà, "spoken language")
- **etymology (白):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 拇 (mǔ, "thumb").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a thumb, reflecting an early pictorial origin. Some scholars alternatively interpret it as an acorn, from which the sense “white” would derive via the pale interior flesh; this view contrasts with the thumb interpretation where the modern color meaning is a later conventional assignment.
  - **component characters:**
    - 拇
