## fire; heat
---
- **traditional:** 火
- **simplified:** 火
- **pronunciation:** huǒ
- **definition:** fire; heat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 火很大 (huǒ hěn dà, "the fire is big")
  - 火锅(鍋) (huǒ guō, "hot pot")
  - 火山 (huǒ shān, "volcano")
  - 火箭 (huǒ jiàn, "rocket")
- **etymology (火):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 火 (huǒ, "fire").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the flames of a fire. The character originally represented literal fire, and its meaning naturally extended to heat and related concepts such as brightness, anger, and intense popularity in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 火
