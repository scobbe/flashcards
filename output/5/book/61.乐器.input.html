(This term is the simplified form of 樂器).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Noun

[edit]

樂器

  1. (countable) musical instrument (Classifier: 件 m c)
    樂器的一種／乐器的一种  ―  yuèqì de yīzhǒng  ―  a type of musical instrument
