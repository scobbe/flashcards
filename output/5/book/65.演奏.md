## to perform (instrumental)
---
- **traditional:** 演奏
- **simplified:** 演奏
- **pronunciation:** yǎnzòu
- **definition:** to perform (instrumental)
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 演奏小提琴 (yǎn zòu xiǎo tí qín, "play the violin")
  - 演奏家 (yǎn zòu jiā, "instrumental performer")
  - 演奏会(會) (yǎn zòu huì, "instrumental concert")
  - 演奏完毕(畢) (yǎn zòu wán bì, "end of performance")
- **etymology (演奏):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 演 (yǎn, "perform") + 奏 (zòu, "play music").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 演 “to perform” with 奏 “to play music” to denote performing music, especially instrumentally. Both morphemes contribute directly to the modern sense of giving an instrumental performance.
  - **component characters:**
    - 演
    - 奏
