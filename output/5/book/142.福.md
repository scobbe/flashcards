## blessing
### sub-word
#### happy
---
- **traditional:** 福
- **simplified:** 福
- **pronunciation:** fú
- **definition:** blessing
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 祈福 (qífú, "pray for blessings")
  - 祝福 (zhùfú, "to bless")
  - 福利 (fúlì, "welfare benefits")
  - 福气(氣) (fúqì, "good fortune")
- **etymology (福):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 畐 (fú, "full") → semantic: 示 (shì, "altar").
  - **interpretation:** 示 is the semantic determinative indicating the domain of rites and divine favor, while 畐 supplies the sound fú and the notion of fullness or abundance. Together they yield the sense of blessedness and prosperity associated with ritual blessings. Over time this combined idea settled into the modern meaning of blessing and good fortune.
  - **component characters:**
    - 畐
    - 示
