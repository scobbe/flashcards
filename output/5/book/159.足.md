## foot
---
- **traditional:** 足
- **simplified:** 足
- **pronunciation:** zú
- **definition:** foot
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 足球 (zúqiú, "soccer")
  - 足部 (zúbù, "foot region")
  - 足踝 (zúhuái, "foot and ankle")
  - 足跟 (zúgēn, "the heel")
- **etymology (足):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a leg.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human leg or foot. The concrete image extended to the body part ‘foot’, to legs of objects, and to actions involving the foot such as stepping, matching its modern senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
