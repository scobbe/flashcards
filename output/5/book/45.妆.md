## makeup
### sub-word
#### to put on makeup
---
- **traditional:** 妝
- **simplified:** 妆
- **pronunciation:** zhuāng
- **definition:** makeup
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 化妆(妝) (huà zhuāng, "apply makeup")
  - 化妆(妝)品 (huà zhuāng pǐn, "cosmetics")
  - 卸妆(妝) (xiè zhuāng, "remove makeup")
  - 浓(濃)妆(妝) (nóng zhuāng, "heavy makeup")
- **etymology (妝):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 爿 (qiáng, "split wood") + semantic: 女 (nǚ, "female") → 妆.
  - **interpretation:** 女 functions as the semantic determinative, situating the character in the realm of women and personal adornment, which matches the modern sense of makeup. 爿 provides the phonetic cue, approximating the historical pronunciation that developed into zhuāng.
  - **component characters:**
    - 爿
    - 女
    - 妆
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%A6%9D
  - **simplification rule (妆):** Simplified by replacing the component 爿 with its left-side simplified form 丬, reducing strokes and regularizing the component shape.
