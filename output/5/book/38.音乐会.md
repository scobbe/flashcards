## concert
---
- **traditional:** 音樂會
- **simplified:** 音乐会
- **pronunciation:** yīnyuèhuì
- **definition:** concert
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 音乐(樂)会(會)听(聽)众(眾) (yīn yuè huì tīng zhòng, "concert audience")
  - 慈善音乐(樂)会(會) (cí shàn yīn yuè huì, "charity concert")
  - 音乐(樂)会(會)门(門)票 (yīn yuè huì mén piào, "concert ticket")
  - 音乐(樂)会(會)节(節)目单(單) (yīn yuè huì jié mù dān, "concert program")
- **etymology (音樂會):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 音乐(音樂) (yīnyuè, "music") + 会(會) (huì, "gathering").
  - **interpretation:** 音乐 denotes the art of music, and 会 means a meeting or gathering; together they denote a gathering for music, i.e., a concert. By extension, when 会 is understood as association, the term can also refer to a music society.
  - **component characters:**
    - 音
    - 乐
    - 樂
    - 会
    - 會
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82%E6%9C%83
  - **simplification rule (音乐会):** The traditional components 樂 and 會 were simplified to 乐 and 会 to reduce strokes and standardize forms, while 音 was unchanged.
