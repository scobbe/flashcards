## sun
### sub-component
#### to study; to practice
---
- **traditional:** 日
- **simplified:** 日
- **pronunciation:** rì
- **definition:** sun
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 日班 (rìbān, "day shift")
  - 三月四日 (sān yuè sì rì, "March 4")
  - 中日词(詞)典 (zhōngrì cídiǎn, "Chinese-Japanese dictionary")
  - 日式咖喱 (rìshì gālí, "Japanese curry")
- **etymology (日):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 日 (rì, "sun").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts the sun's disk with a central dot or short line inside to ensure it is recognized as the sun. From the original meaning "sun", it extended to "day" and by extension to dates and daily matters in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 日
