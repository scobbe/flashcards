## to photograph; shine
### sub-word
#### to take a picture
---
- **traditional:** 照
- **simplified:** 照
- **pronunciation:** zhào
- **definition:** to photograph; shine
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 照片 (zhàopiàn, "a photo")
  - 照相 (zhàoxiàng, "to take photos")
  - 照顾(顧) (zhàogù, "to take care of")
  - 照明 (zhàomíng, "artificial lighting")
- **etymology (照):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 灬 (huǒ, "fire") + phonetic: 昭 (zhāo, "bright").
  - **interpretation:** 灬 serves as the semantic determinative for light/heat, while 昭 provides the pronunciation and contributes the idea of brightness. This composition yields the sense ‘to shine/illuminate’, later extended to photography and photos via shining light on a subject. Some sources also view it as an exoactive derivative of 昭 ‘bright’, aligning with its core meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 灬
    - 昭
