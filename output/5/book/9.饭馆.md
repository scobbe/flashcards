## restaurant
---
- **traditional:** 飯館
- **simplified:** 饭馆
- **pronunciation:** fànguǎn
- **definition:** restaurant
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)吃饭(飯) (fànguǎn chīfàn, "eat at a restaurant")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)儿(兒) (fànguǎnr, "small restaurant")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)菜单(單) (fànguǎn càidān, "restaurant menu")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)服务(務)员(員) (fànguǎn fúwùyuán, "restaurant server")
- **etymology (飯館):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal") + 馆(館) (guǎn, "establishment").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal") with 馆(館) (guǎn, "establishment") to denote an establishment serving meals. In modern usage it straightforwardly means a restaurant or eatery.
  - **component characters:**
    - 饭
    - 飯
    - 馆
    - 館
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A3%AF%E9%A4%A8
  - **simplification rule (饭馆):** Both characters simplify the 食 component to the radical 饣, with further stroke reduction: 飯 → 饭 and 館 → 馆 by keeping the phonetic parts and replacing the food radical.
