## fire; heat
### sub-word
#### train
---
- **traditional:** 火
- **simplified:** 火
- **pronunciation:** huǒ
- **definition:** fire; heat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 火锅(鍋) (huǒ guō, "hot pot")
  - 火车(車) (huǒ chē, "train")
  - 火柴 (huǒ chái, "matchstick")
  - 火山 (huǒ shān, "volcano")
- **etymology (火):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 火 (huǒ, "fire").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a fire with flames rising. The concrete picture directly denoted fire and naturally extended to related senses such as burning, heat, brightness, anger, and by metaphor, popularity in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 火
