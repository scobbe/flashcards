## tune; melody
---
- **traditional:** 曲
- **simplified:** 曲
- **pronunciation:** qǔ
- **definition:** tune; melody
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 曲子 (qǔzi, "musical tune")
  - 曲调(調) (qǔdiào, "musical melody")
  - 乐(樂)曲 (yuèqǔ, "musical piece")
  - 主题(題)曲 (zhǔtíqǔ, "theme song")
- **etymology (曲):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: depiction of a bent object.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bent or curved object, giving the original sense ‘bent/curved’. This concrete shape motivated uses about things not straight; the same graph also writes qǔ ‘tune’, where a melody is conceived as winding, linking the shape to the musical sense. It is also used as the simplified form of 麴 ‘yeast’ via the variant 麯.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
