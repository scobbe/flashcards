## meat
### sub-component
#### foot
---
- **traditional:** 肉
- **simplified:** 肉
- **pronunciation:** ròu / rù
- **definition:** meat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 猪(豬)肉 (zhūròu, "pork")
  - 牛肉 (niúròu, "beef")
  - 肉体(體) (ròutǐ, "physical body")
  - 肉松(鬆) (ròusōng, "pork floss")
- **etymology (肉):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of ribs of an animal’s torso.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts ribs of an animal’s torso, hence the original sense ‘meat/flesh’. As script styles standardized, the graph’s form regularized but the meaning remained concrete ‘flesh’, which underlies modern senses like edible meat and the human body.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
