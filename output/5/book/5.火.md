## fire; heat
### sub-word
#### hotpot
---
- **traditional:** 火
- **simplified:** 火
- **pronunciation:** huǒ
- **definition:** fire; heat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 火灾(災) (huǒzāi, "fire disaster")
  - 火锅(鍋) (huǒguō, "Chinese hot pot")
  - 火箭 (huǒjiàn, "space rocket")
  - 火车(車) (huǒchē, "railway train")
- **etymology (火):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 火 (huǒ, "fire").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts flames rising from a fire. Because it directly depicts burning flames, the graph came to denote “fire” and extended to related ideas such as heat, burning, and by metaphor emotional “heat” like anger or popularity.
  - **component characters:**
    - 火
