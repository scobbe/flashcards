Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 着急 – see 著急 (“to worry; to feel anxious; to be worried; anxious; worried”).
(This term is the simplified and variant traditional form of 著急).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Verb

[edit]

著⫽急 (verb-object)

  1. to worry; to feel anxious; to be worried
    你著什麼急啊？ [MSC, trad.]
    你着什么急啊？ [MSC, simp.]
    Nǐ zháo shénme jí a? [Pinyin]
    What are you worried about?
    我覺得很著急，我該怎麼辦啊？ [MSC, trad.]
    我觉得很着急，我该怎么办啊？ [MSC, simp.]
    Wǒ juéde hěn zháojí, wǒ gāi zěnmebàn a? [Pinyin]
    I feel worried. What should I do?
