- **traditional:** 牀
- **simplified:** 床
- **pronunciation:** chuáng
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 车(車)床(牀) (chē chuáng, "lathe")
  - 牙床(牀) (yá chuáng, "gingiva")
  - 河床(牀) (hé chuáng, "riverbed")
  - 岩床(牀) (yán chuáng, "sill")
- **etymology (牀):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 广(廣) (guǎng, "house") + 木 (mù, "wood").
  - **interpretation:** 广 conveys a house/shelter context while 木 denotes wood, together indicating a wooden furnishing within a dwelling, hence a bed. Historically, 床 was an unorthodox variant of 牀 and later became standardized with this ideogrammic analysis.
  - **component characters:**
    - 广
    - 廣
    - 木
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%89%80
  - **simplification rule (床):** Standardization adopted the long-used variant 床 by replacing 爿 with 广 to reduce strokes and regularize the component structure.
