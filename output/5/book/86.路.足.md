## foot

#### road
---
- **traditional:** 足
- **simplified:** 足
- **pronunciation:** zú
- **definition:** foot
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 足球 (zúqiú, "soccer")
  - 足疗(療) (zúliáo, "foot massage")
  - 足跟 (zúgēn, "heel")
  - 足部 (zúbù, "foot region")
- **etymology (足):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 足 (zú, "foot").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a human leg or foot. The original pictographic sense yields meanings like the anatomical foot, the leg of an animal or object, and actions done with the feet. Separately, the sense “sufficient” is a distinct etymology likely from a Sino-Tibetan root, later written with the same graph.
