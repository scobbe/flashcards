## ethnic group
### sub-word
#### nation; nationality
---
- **traditional:** 族
- **simplified:** 族
- **pronunciation:** zú
- **definition:** ethnic group
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 民族 (mín zú, "ethnicity")
  - 汉(漢)族 (hàn zú, "Han Chinese")
  - 藏族 (zàng zú, "Tibetans")
  - 低头(頭)族 (dī tóu zú, "smartphone zombie")
- **etymology (族):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 㫃 (fāng, "flag") + 矢 (shǐ, "arrow").
  - **interpretation:** It combines a flag and arrows to depict mustering under a banner, indicating a body of people gathered together. This concrete image generalized to mean a group or lineage and in modern usage an ethnic group or a class of people.
  - **component characters:**
    - 㫃
    - 矢
