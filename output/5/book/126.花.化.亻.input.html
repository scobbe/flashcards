Glyph origin

[edit]

A stylization of 人. See also 千. Similar but unrelated to 彳.

Definitions

[edit]

亻

  1. Side form of the person (人 (rén)) radical.

Usage notes

[edit]

Only used as a component in a character. Referred to as 單人旁／单人旁 (dānrénpáng).
