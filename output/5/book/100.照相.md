## to take a picture
---
- **traditional:** 照相
- **simplified:** 照相
- **pronunciation:** zhàoxiàng
- **definition:** to take a picture
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 照相爱(愛)好者 (zhào xiàng ài hào zhě, "photography enthusiast")
  - 照相机(機) (zhào xiàng jī, "camera")
  - 照相馆(館) (zhào xiàng guǎn, "photo studio")
  - 照相纸(紙) (zhào xiàng zhǐ, "photographic paper")
- **etymology (照相):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 照 (zhào, "shine") + 相 (xiàng, "image").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 照, meaning to shine or illuminate, with 相, meaning image or appearance, to denote producing an image by light. By extension it came to mean taking a photograph with a camera, where light creates an image on film or a sensor.
  - **component characters:**
    - 照
    - 相
