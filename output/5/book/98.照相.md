## to take photos
### sub-word
#### camera
---
- **traditional:** 照相
- **simplified:** 照相
- **pronunciation:** zhàoxiàng
- **definition:** to take photos
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 照相机(機) (zhào xiàng jī, "camera")
  - 照相馆(館) (zhào xiàng guǎn, "photo studio")
  - 照相纸(紙) (zhào xiàng zhǐ, "photographic paper")
  - 照相店 (zhào xiàng diàn, "photo shop")
- **etymology (照相):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 照 (zhào, "photograph") + 相 (xiàng, "image").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 照 (zhào, "photograph") indicating the act of photographing with 相 (xiàng, "image") denoting the picture or appearance. Together they express producing an image by photographing, yielding the modern sense "to take photos."
  - **component characters:**
    - 照
    - 相
