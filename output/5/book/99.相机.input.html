Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 相机 – see 相機 (“camera; to watch for the opportunity to”).
(This term is the simplified form of 相機).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
