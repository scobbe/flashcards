## mouth
### sub-component
#### house
---
- **traditional:** 口
- **simplified:** 口
- **pronunciation:** kǒu
- **definition:** mouth
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 入口 (rùkǒu, "way in")
  - 出口 (chūkǒu, "way out")
  - 门(門)口 (ménkǒu, "at the doorway")
  - 口子 (kǒuzi, "small opening")
- **etymology (口):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts an open human mouth. The concrete image naturally extended to mean the mouth and, by analogy, openings and ports in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 口
