## music
### sub-word
#### concert
---
- **traditional:** 音樂
- **simplified:** 音乐
- **pronunciation:** yīnyuè
- **definition:** music
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 音乐(樂)会(會) (yīn yuè huì, "concert")
  - 音乐(樂)家 (yīn yuè jiā, "musician")
  - 音乐(樂)剧(劇) (yīn yuè jù, "stage musical")
  - 音乐(樂)厅(廳) (yīn yuè tīng, "concert hall")
- **etymology (音樂):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 音 (yīn, "sound") + 乐(樂) (yuè, "music").
  - **interpretation:** 音 (yīn, "sound") denotes the acoustic element, while 乐(樂) (yuè, "music; joy") contributes the artistic/pleasurable sense. Combined, they mean music as organized sound for enjoyment, matching the modern sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - 音
    - 乐
    - 樂
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%9F%B3%E6%A8%82
  - **simplification rule (音乐):** The simplified form replaces 樂 with 乐 through component replacement and stroke reduction, while 音 remains unchanged.
