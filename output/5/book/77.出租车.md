## taxi
---
- **traditional:** 出租車
- **simplified:** 出租车
- **pronunciation:** chūzūchē
- **definition:** taxi
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 出租车(車)服务(務) (chū zū chē fú wù, "taxi service")
  - 出租车(車)司机(機) (chū zū chē sī jī, "taxi driver")
  - 出租车(車)费(費) (chū zū chē fèi, "taxi fare")
  - 出租车(車)专(專)用停车(車)位 (chū zū chē zhuān yòng tíng chē wèi, "taxi parking only")
- **etymology (出租車):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 出 (chū, "go out") + 租 (zū, "rent out") + 车(車) (chē, "vehicle").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 出 "go out", 租 "rent out", and 车 "vehicle" to denote a vehicle available for hire. The parts transparently yield the modern sense of a car rented for transport, i.e., a taxi.
  - **component characters:**
    - 出
    - 租
    - 车
    - 車
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%87%BA%E7%A7%9F%E8%BB%8A
  - **simplification rule (出租车):** The traditional component 車 was simplified to 车 for stroke reduction; 出 and 租 remained unchanged.
