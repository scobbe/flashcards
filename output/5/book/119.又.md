## again
---
- **traditional:** 又
- **simplified:** 又
- **pronunciation:** yòu
- **definition:** again
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 他又来(來)了 (tā yòu lái le, "he came again")
  - 又下雨了 (yòu xià yǔ le, "it's raining again")
  - 聪(聰)明又漂亮 (cōngmíng yòu piàoliang, "smart and pretty")
  - 又惊(驚)又喜 (yòu jīng yòu xǐ, "both surprised and delighted")
- **etymology (又):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a right hand → semantic: adverbial derivation of 有 (yǒu, "to have; there is").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a right hand, reflecting its original concrete form. Later, the grammatical adverb yòu developed by derivation from 有 “to have; there is,” coming to signal addition or repetition, hence meanings like “again” and “also.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 有
