## suburb; outskirts
---
- **traditional:** 郊區
- **simplified:** 郊区
- **pronunciation:** jiāoqū
- **definition:** suburb; outskirts
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 郊区(區)生活 (jiāoqū shēnghuó, "suburban life")
  - 郊区(區)居民 (jiāoqū jūmín, "suburban residents")
  - 郊区(區)公园(園) (jiāoqū gōngyuán, "suburban park")
  - 郊区(區)交通 (jiāoqū jiāotōng, "suburban transport")
- **etymology (郊區):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 郊 (jiāo, "outskirts") + 区(區) (qū, "district").
  - **interpretation:** 郊 (jiāo, "outskirts") names the area outside a city, while 区(區) (qū, "district") denotes an administrative division. Together they mean an administrative district on a city's outskirts, matching the modern sense of suburbs or outskirts.
  - **component characters:**
    - 郊
    - 区
    - 區
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%83%8A%E5%8D%80
  - **simplification rule (郊区):** 區 was simplified to 区 through graphic abbreviation and stroke reduction, replacing the complex enclosure and interior components with the simpler 区.
