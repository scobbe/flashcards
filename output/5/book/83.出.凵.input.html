Radical 17 凵

凵 (Kangxi radical 17, 凵+0, 2 strokes, cangjie input 女中 (VL) or 難女中 (XVL), four-corner 22770, composition ⿰𠃊丨)

  1. Kangxi radical #17, ⼐.
  2. Shuowen Jiezi radical №23

Definitions

[edit]

凵

  1. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to open one's mouth
