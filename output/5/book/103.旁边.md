## side; beside
---
- **traditional:** 旁邊
- **simplified:** 旁边
- **pronunciation:** pángbiān
- **definition:** side; beside
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 旁边(邊)座位 (pángbiān zuòwèi, "adjacent seat")
  - 旁边(邊)儿(兒) (pángbiānr, "by the side")
  - 旁边(邊)的人 (pángbiān de rén, "the person next to")
  - 旁边(邊)的桌子 (pángbiān de zhuōzi, "the neighboring table")
- **etymology (旁邊):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 旁 (páng, "side") + 边(邊) (biān, "edge") → 旁边(旁邊) (pángbiān, "side").
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 旁 “to the side” and 边(邊) “edge,” yielding the sense of a side or the area next to something. In modern usage it denotes a location immediately adjacent to a reference point, hence meanings like beside or at the side.
  - **component characters:**
    - 旁
    - 边
    - 邊
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%97%81%E9%82%8A
  - **simplification rule (旁边):** 邊 was simplified to 边 by component replacement to reduce strokes; 旁 was unchanged, producing the modern simplified form 旁边.
