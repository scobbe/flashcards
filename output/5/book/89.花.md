## flower
### sub-word
#### flower
---
- **traditional:** 花
- **simplified:** 花
- **pronunciation:** huā
- **definition:** flower
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 开(開)花 (kāihuā, "to blossom")
  - 花纹(紋) (huāwén, "decorative pattern")
  - 烟(煙)花 (yānhuā, "firework display")
  - 花卉 (huāhuì, "flowers and plants")
- **etymology (花):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic: 化 (huà, "transform").
  - **interpretation:** The semantic component 艹 (cǎo, "grass") marks the plant domain, while the phonetic 化 (huà, "transform") supplies the hua sound. Together they yield the sense of a plant’s blossom, matching the modern meaning. Some scholars discuss external cognates, but the graphic structure is straightforwardly phono-semantic.
  - **component characters:**
    - 艸
    - 化
