## road
### sub-word
#### on the road
---
- **traditional:** 路
- **simplified:** 路
- **pronunciation:** lù
- **definition:** road
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 路口 (lù kǒu, "road intersection")
  - 路线(線) (lù xiàn, "travel route")
  - 路费(費) (lù fèi, "travel expenses")
  - 路过(過) (lù guò, "pass by")
- **etymology (路):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 足 (zú, "foot") + phonetic: 各 (gè, "each").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 足 (zú, "foot") as the semantic determinative for movement/paths with 各 (gè, "each") as the phonetic, matching the historical pronunciation and yielding the sense of a road or path associated with walking and travel. Alternative proposals relate it to 格 ("to come, to arrive") or to a Proto-Austroasiatic root meaning "road, way".
  - **component characters:**
    - 足 (zú, "foot")
    - 各 (gè, "each")
