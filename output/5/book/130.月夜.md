## moonlit night
### sub-word
#### traditional music composition
---
- **traditional:** 月夜
- **simplified:** 月夜
- **pronunciation:** yuèyè
- **definition:** moonlit night
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 月夜里(裡) (yuèyè lǐ, "in the moonlit night")
  - 月夜下 (yuèyè xià, "under the moonlight")
  - 月夜美景 (yuèyè měijǐng, "moonlit scenery")
  - 月夜散步 (yuèyè sànbù, "moonlit stroll")
- **etymology (月夜):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 月 (yuè, "moon") + 夜 (yè, "night").
  - **interpretation:** A transparent compound: 月 means the moon and 夜 means night, combining to denote a night illuminated by the moon. The modern sense remains the same, referring to a moonlit night.
  - **component characters:**
    - 月
    - 夜
