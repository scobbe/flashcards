## machine or device; opportunity or chance
### sub-word
#### camera
---
- **traditional:** 機
- **simplified:** 机
- **pronunciation:** jī
- **definition:** machine or device; opportunity or chance
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 机(機)场(場) (jīchǎng, "airport")
  - 机(機)票 (jīpiào, "plane ticket")
  - 无(無)人机(機) (wúrénjī, "drone")
  - 机(機)密 (jīmì, "top secret")
- **etymology (機):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 木 (mù, "wood") + phonetic: 几(幾) (jī, "small table").
  - **interpretation:** 木 serves as the semantic determinative, pointing to wooden tools and devices, while 几 provides the sound jī. The wood semantic aligns with early machinery and frames, and the inherited reading underlies modern senses like "machine/device" and the extended abstract sense of a pivotal "opportunity."
  - **component characters:**
    - 木
    - 几
    - 幾
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%A9%9F
  - **simplification rule (机):** Simplified by replacing the phonetic component 幾 with its simpler form 几, reducing strokes while retaining the semantic 木.
