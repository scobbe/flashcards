## sheep
### sub-word
#### thin slices of mutton boiled in water
---
- **traditional:** 羊
- **simplified:** 羊
- **pronunciation:** yáng
- **definition:** sheep
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 羊毛 (yángmáo, "wool")
  - 羊群 (yángqún, "flock of sheep")
  - 羊乳 (yángrǔ, "sheep milk")
  - 羊皮纸(紙) (yángpízhǐ, "parchment")
- **etymology (羊):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a ram's head.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a ram's head with prominent curved horns. As a direct pictograph, it came to denote the animal itself, and this basic sense persists in modern usage for sheep and related caprids.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
