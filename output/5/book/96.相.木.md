## tree
### sub-component
#### image; appearance
---
- **traditional:** 木
- **simplified:** 木
- **pronunciation:** mù
- **definition:** tree
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 树(樹)木 (shùmù, "forest trees")
  - 灌木 (guànmù, "woody shrub")
  - 木头(頭) (mùtou, "wooden log")
  - 木屋 (mùwū, "wooden house")
- **etymology (木):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 木 (mù, "tree").
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a tree with spreading branches on top and roots below. The original pictorial meaning extended naturally to the material sense "wood; timber," and it serves as the semantic basis for many wood-related compounds today.

    - 木
