## hotpot
---
- **traditional:** 火鍋
- **simplified:** 火锅
- **pronunciation:** huǒguō
- **definition:** hotpot
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 火锅(鍋)儿(兒) (huǒguōr, "hotpot (Beijing)")
  - 火锅(鍋)店 (huǒguō diàn, "hotpot restaurant")
  - 火锅(鍋)底料 (huǒguō dǐliào, "hotpot base")
  - 火锅(鍋)蘸料 (huǒguō zhànliào, "hotpot dipping sauce")
- **etymology (火鍋):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 火 (huǒ, "fire") + 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot").
  - **interpretation:** The compound combines 火 (huǒ, "fire") and 锅(鍋) (guō, "pot"), literally a pot heated by fire. It extends to the communal meal cooked in the pot, which is the modern culinary sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - 火
    - 锅
    - 鍋
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%81%AB%E9%8D%8B
  - **simplification rule (火锅):** Simplified by replacing 鍋's metal radical 金 with its simplified form 钅 to reduce strokes; 火 is unchanged.
