## to play (instrumental)
---
- **traditional:** 奏
- **simplified:** 奏
- **pronunciation:** zòu
- **definition:** to play (instrumental)
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 奏乐(樂) (zòu yuè, "play music")
  - 演奏 (yǎn zòu, "perform music")
  - 弹(彈)奏 (tán zòu, "play an instrument")
  - 吹奏 (chuī zòu, "play a wind instrument")
- **etymology (奏):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 廾 (gǒng, "two hands") + 夲 (běn, "root").
  - **interpretation:** Shows two hands holding an object, likely a musical instrument used in ritual, hence senses like formal presentation and performing music. An alternative view sees two hands 廾 (gǒng, "two hands") collecting resin 桼 (qī, "lacquer") from a cut tree drawn like 屮 (chè, "sprout").
  - **component characters:**
    - 廾
    - 夲
