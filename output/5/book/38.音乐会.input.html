Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 音乐会 – see 音樂會 (“concert; music society”).
(This term is the simplified form of 音樂會).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

to meet; to gather; group
to meet; to gather; group; to assemble; association; be able to; be likely to; to balance an account; accounting

Noun

[edit]

音樂會

  1. concert (Classifier: 場／场 m)
    慈善音樂會／慈善音乐会  ―  císhàn yīnyuèhuì  ―  charity concert
  2. music society (Classifier: 個／个 m; 家 m)
