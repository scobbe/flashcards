Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 旁边 – see 旁邊 (“side; beside; to the side”).
(This term is the simplified form of 旁邊).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Noun

[edit]

旁邊

  1. side; beside; to the side
    我在一輛旅行車旁邊找到了一個車位。 [MSC, trad.]
    我在一辆旅行车旁边找到了一个车位。 [MSC, simp.]
    Wǒ zài yī liàng lǚxíngchē pángbiān zhǎo dào le yī ge chēwèi. [Pinyin]
    I found a place to park beside a station wagon.
    她站在水池旁邊，正要往裡跳。 [MSC, trad.]
    她站在水池旁边，正要往里跳。 [MSC, simp.]
    Tā zhàn zài shuǐchí pángbiān, zhèngyào wǎng lǐ tiào. [Pinyin]
    She was standing by a pool, about to dive in.
    住在菜市場旁邊吵死了。 [MSC, trad.]
    住在菜市场旁边吵死了。 [MSC, simp.]
    Zhù zài càishìchǎng pángbiān chǎo sǐ le. [Pinyin]
    It's very noisy living next to the food market.
