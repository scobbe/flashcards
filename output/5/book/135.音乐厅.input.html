Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 音乐厅 – see 音樂廳 (“concert hall; funeral home”).
(This term is the simplified form of 音樂廳).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
music (reception) hall; office
trad. (音樂廳) 音樂 廳
simp. (音乐厅) 音乐 厅

Noun

[edit]

音樂廳

  1. concert hall
  2. (Pearl River Delta) funeral home
