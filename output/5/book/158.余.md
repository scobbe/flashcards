## surplus; remainder
### sub-word
#### surplus; unneeded
---
- **traditional:** 餘
- **simplified:** 余
- **pronunciation:** yú
- **definition:** surplus; remainder
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 余额(額) (yú é, "account balance")
  - 余(餘)数(數) (yú shù, "math remainder")
  - 余(餘)热(熱) (yú rè, "residual heat")
  - 多余(餘) (duō yú, "not necessary")
- **etymology (餘):**
  - **type:** pictogram d phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** pictogram: 舍 (she8, "house") d phono-semantic: semantic: 食 (shed, "food") + phonetic: 余 (yfa, "I; me").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a thatched house, the original form associated with 舍. Later, 余 served as the phonetic in 餘 with 食 as the semantic determinative, yielding the idea of leftover food and generalizing to the modern sense of surplus or remainder.
  - **component characters:**
    - 舍 (she8, "house")
    - 食 (shed, "food")
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A4%98
  - **simplification rule (余):** The traditional form 餘 was simplified by dropping the food radical 食 and reusing the existing phonetic 余 as the whole character for stroke reduction and regularization.
