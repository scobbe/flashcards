## wings
### sub-component
#### to study; to practice
---
- **traditional:** 羽
- **simplified:** 羽
- **pronunciation:** yǔ / hù
- **definition:** wings
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 羽毛球 (yǔmáoqiú, "badminton")
  - 羽绒(絨) (yǔróng, "down")
  - 羽毛 (yǔmáo, "feathers")
  - 羽量级(級) (yǔliàngjí, "featherweight")
- **etymology (羽):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of wings or feathers.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a bird’s feather or wings. The concrete depiction yielded the meaning “feather” and extended to “wing” in use, which aligns with its role in modern terms for plumage, wings, and related items.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
