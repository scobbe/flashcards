## 
### sub-component
#### foot
---
- **traditional:** 卻
- **simplified:** 却
- **pronunciation:** què
- **definition:** 
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 退却(卻) (tuìquè, "to retreat")
  - 忘却(卻) (wàngquè, "to forget")
  - 省却(卻) (shěngquè, "to save")
  - 却(卻)步 (quèbù, "step back")
- **etymology (卻):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 𧮫 (jué, "phonetic element") + semantic: 卩 (jié, "seal").
  - **interpretation:** 𧮫 provides the sound, matching an Old Chinese reading that developed into modern què. 卩 serves as the semantic determinative related to a person/gesture, aligning with senses of stepping back, withdrawing, or refusing, which later extended to adversative ‘but’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 𧮫
    - 卩
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%8D%BB
  - **simplification rule (却):** Component replacement: the left phonetic 𧮫/谷 was replaced by the simpler 去, reducing strokes while retaining 卩 on the right.
