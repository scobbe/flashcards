Noun

[edit]

新年

  1. New Year (in the Gregorian calendar or Chinese calendar)
  2. (Quanzhou, Jinjiang and Philippine Hokkien) next year

Etymology

[edit]

From 新 (shin, “new”) +‎ 年 (nen, “year”).

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • (Tokyo) しんねん [shíꜜǹnèǹ] (Atamadaka – [1])[1]
  • IPA(key): [ɕĩnːẽ̞ɴ]

Noun

[edit]

新(しん)年(ねん) • (shinnen) 

  1. a new year
  2. New Year
  3. New Year's Day

Synonyms

[edit]
  • (a new year): 新春(しんしゅん) (shinshun)
  • (New Year): 正月(しょうがつ) (shōgatsu)
  • (New Year's Day): 元旦(がんたん) (gantan)

Derived terms

[edit]
  • 謹(きん)賀(が)新(しん)年(ねん) (kinga shinnen)
  • 新(しん)年(ねん)おめでとうございます (shinnen omedetō gozaimasu)
  • 新(しん)年(ねん)明(あ)けましておめでとうございます (shinnen akemashite omedetō gozaimasu)
