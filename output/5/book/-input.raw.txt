1. 新年 xīnnián – new year, 祝你新年快乐！Zhù nǐ xīnnián kuàilè! Wish you a happy new year!
2. 火锅 huǒguō – hotpot, 因为天气冷，所以北京人常吃火锅。Yīnwèi tiānqì lěng, suǒyǐ Běijīng rén cháng chī huǒguō. Because the weather is cold, people in Beijing often eat hotpot.
3. 火 huǒ – fire; heat, 火很大。Huǒ hěn dà. The fire is big.
4. 锅 guō – pot; pan, 锅里有汤。Guō lǐ yǒu tāng. There is soup in the pot.
5. 饭馆 fànguǎn – restaurant, 我们去那家饭馆吃饭吧。Wǒmen qù nà jiā fànguǎn chīfàn ba. Let’s go eat at that restaurant.
6. 因为 yīnwèi – because, 因为他感冒了，所以他没有来上课。Yīnwèi tā gǎnmào le, suǒyǐ tā méiyǒu lái shàngkè. Because he caught a cold, he didn’t come to class.
7. 所以 suǒyǐ – so, 因为银行排队的人多，所以他想明天再去换钱。Yīnwèi yínháng páiduì de rén duō, suǒyǐ tā xiǎng míngtiān zài qù huàn qián. Because there were many people in line at the bank, he decided to exchange money tomorrow.
8. 爱 ài – to love, 我爱吃中国菜。Wǒ ài chī Zhōngguó cài. I love to eat Chinese food.
9. 涮羊肉 shuànyángròu – thin slices of mutton boiled in water, 北京人就爱吃火锅，主要是涮羊肉，天气热的时候也吃。Běijīng rén jiù ài chī huǒguō, zhǔyào shì shuànyángròu, tiānqì rè de shíhou yě chī. People in Beijing love hotpot, mainly boiled mutton slices, and they eat it even when the weather is hot.
10. 涮 shuàn – to cook thin slices of meat in boiling water, 涮羊肉最好吃。Shuànyángròu zuì hǎo chī. Boiled mutton slices are the best.
11. 羊 yáng – sheep, 这是山上的羊。Zhè shì shān shàng de yáng. This is a sheep on the mountain.
12. 肉 ròu – meat, 我不吃肉。Wǒ bù chī ròu. I don’t eat meat.
13. 热 rè – hot, 天气很热。Tiānqì hěn rè. The weather is hot.
14. 开车 kāichē – to drive a car, 他每天开车上班。Tā měitiān kāichē shàngbān. He drives to work every day.
15. 开 kāi – to drive; to operate, 开门！Kāi mén! Open the door!
16. 郊区 jiāoqū – suburb; outskirts, 我家在郊区。Wǒ jiā zài jiāoqū. My home is in the suburbs.
17. 或者 huòzhě – or, 我们去看电影或者听音乐会。Wǒmen qù kàn diànyǐng huòzhě tīng yīnyuèhuì. We can go watch a movie or attend a concert.
18. 音乐会 yīnyuèhuì – concert, 你喜欢听音乐会吗？Nǐ xǐhuān tīng yīnyuèhuì ma? Do you like going to concerts?
19. 化妆 huàzhuāng – to put on makeup, 她在化妆。Tā zài huàzhuāng. She is putting on makeup.
20. 化 huà – to change, 化冰为水。Huà bīng wéi shuǐ. Ice changes into water.
21. 妆 zhuāng – makeup, 化妆很漂亮。Huàzhuāng hěn piàoliang. The makeup looks pretty.
22. 正式 zhèngshì – formal, 他穿得很正式。Tā chuān de hěn zhèngshì. He is dressed formally.
23. 民乐 mínyuè – folk music, 我喜欢听中国民乐。Wǒ xǐhuān tīng Zhōngguó mínyuè. I like listening to Chinese folk music.
24. 西方 xīfāng – the West; western, 西方音乐很流行。Xīfāng yīnyuè hěn liúxíng. Western music is very popular.
25. 西 xī – west, 太阳从东边升起，在西边落下。Tàiyáng cóng dōngbiān shēngqǐ, zài xībiān luòxià. The sun rises in the east and sets in the west.
26. 民族 mínzú – nation; nationality, 中国是一个多民族的国家。Zhōngguó shì yí ge duō mínzú de guójiā. China is a multiethnic country.
27. 乐器 yuèqì – musical instrument, 他会很多乐器。Tā huì hěn duō yuèqì. He can play many instruments.
28. 器 qì – utensil, 乐器很贵。Yuèqì hěn guì. Musical instruments are expensive.
29. 演奏 yǎnzòu – to perform (instrumental), 她在台上演奏小提琴。Tā zài tái shàng yǎnzòu xiǎotíqín. She is performing the violin on stage.
30. 演 yǎn – to perform, 她演得很好。Tā yǎn de hěn hǎo. She acted very well.
31. 奏 zòu – to play (instrumental), 她奏得很动听。Tā zòu de hěn dòngtīng. She plays beautifully.
32. 习惯 xíguàn – to be accustomed to; habit, 我习惯早起。Wǒ xíguàn zǎo qǐ. I’m used to getting up early.
33. 乐曲 yuèqǔ – musical composition, 这是一首著名的乐曲。Zhè shì yì shǒu zhùmíng de yuèqǔ. This is a famous musical piece.
34. 曲 qǔ – tune; melody, 这曲子很好听。Zhè qǔzi hěn hǎotīng. This tune is beautiful.
35. 出租车 chūzūchē – taxi, 我们坐出租车去吧。Wǒmen zuò chūzūchē qù ba. Let’s take a taxi.
36. 出租 chūzū – to rent; to hire, 我出租房子。Wǒ chūzū fángzi. I rent out my house.
37. 路上 lùshang – on the road, 我在路上。Wǒ zài lùshang. I’m on the way.
38. 花儿 huār – flower, 我喜欢花儿。Wǒ xǐhuān huār. I like flowers.
39. 过去 guòqù – in or of the past, 过去的人都很勤劳。Guòqù de rén dōu hěn qínláo. People in the past were hardworking.
40. 照相机 zhàoxiàngjī – camera, 我买了一个新照相机。Wǒ mǎile yí ge xīn zhàoxiàngjī. I bought a new camera.
41. 照相 zhàoxiàng – to take a picture, 我喜欢照相。Wǒ xǐhuān zhàoxiàng. I like taking photos.
42. 旁边 pángbiān – side; beside, 他坐在我旁边。Tā zuò zài wǒ pángbiān. He sits beside me.
43. 出发 chūfā – to set out; to start off, 我们早上八点出发。Wǒmen zǎoshang bā diǎn chūfā. We set off at 8 a.m.
44. 篇 piān – measure word for articles, 我写了一篇文章。Wǒ xiě le yì piān wénzhāng. I wrote an article.
45. 文章 wénzhāng – essay; article, 这篇文章很好。Zhè piān wénzhāng hěn hǎo. This article is very good.
46. 恭喜 gōngxǐ – congratulations, 恭喜发财！Gōngxǐ fācái! Wishing you prosperity!
47. 火车 huǒchē – train, 火车到了。Huǒchē dàole. The train has arrived.
48. 又 yòu – again, 他又来了。Tā yòu lái le. He came again.
49. 着急 zháojí – anxious; worried, 别着急。Bié zháojí. Don’t worry.
50. 春江花月夜 Chūn Jiāng Huā Yuè Yè – traditional music composition, 我喜欢《春江花月夜》。Wǒ xǐhuān “Chūn Jiāng Huā Yuè Yè.” I like “Spring River Flower Moon Night.”
51. 小学 xiǎoxué – primary school, 他在小学教书。Tā zài xiǎoxué jiāoshū. He teaches in a primary school.
52. 辆 liàng – measure word for vehicles, 一辆车。Yí liàng chē. One car.
53. 音乐厅 yīnyuètīng – concert hall, 音乐厅很大。Yīnyuètīng hěn dà. The concert hall is large.
54. 幸福 xìngfú – happy, 他们的生活很幸福。Tāmen de shēnghuó hěn xìngfú. Their life is happy.
55. 万事如意 wàn shì rú yì – may all your wishes come true, 新年万事如意！Xīnnián wàn shì rú yì! May all your wishes come true in the new year!
56. 条 tiáo – measure word for long objects, 一条裤子。Yì tiáo kùzi. One pair of trousers.
57. 蛇 shé – snake, 我怕蛇。Wǒ pà shé. I’m afraid of snakes.
58. 最 zuì – most, 这个最好。Zhè ge zuì hǎo. This is the best.
59. 添 tiān – to add, 请添点儿菜。Qǐng tiān diǎnr cài. Please add some more dishes.
60. 脚 jiǎo – foot, 我的脚疼。Wǒ de jiǎo téng. My foot hurts.
61. 完 wán – to finish, 我写完了。Wǒ xiě wán le. I finished writing.
62. 多余 duōyú – surplus; unneeded, 这些是多余的。Zhèxiē shì duōyú de. These are unnecessary.
63. 足 zú – foot, 足球是用脚踢的。Zúqiú shì yòng jiǎo tī de. Soccer is played with the feet.
