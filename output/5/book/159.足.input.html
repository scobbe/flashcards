Definitions

[edit]

足

  1. (anatomy) foot
  2. (anatomy) leg (of an animal)
  3. leg (of an object)
  4. (in compounds) football; soccer
    女足  ―  nǚzú  ―  women's football
    足協／足协  ―  zúxié  ―  football association
  5. to walk
  6. to tread; to stamp; to step
  7. a surname, Zu
