## year
### sub-word
#### new year
---
- **traditional:** 年
- **simplified:** 年
- **pronunciation:** nián
- **definition:** year
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 今年 (jīnnián, "this year")
  - 明年 (míngnián, "next year")
  - 年度 (niándù, "annual period")
  - 年龄(齡) (niánlíng, "age in years")
- **etymology (年):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound → phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 禾 (hé, "grain") + 人 (rén, "person") → phono-semantic: semantic 禾 (hé, "grain") + phonetic 千 (qiān, "thousand").
  - **interpretation:** Originally it depicts a person carrying grain, i.e., a harvest, combining 禾 (hé, "grain") with 人 (rén, "person"). Later it regularized into a phono-semantic structure with 禾 as the semantic determinative and 千 (qiān, "thousand") as the phonetic to fix the reading. The notion of harvest naturally extended to the yearly harvest cycle, yielding the sense "year."
  - **component characters:**
    - 禾 (hé, "grain")
    - 人 (rén, "person")
    - 千 (qiān, "thousand")
