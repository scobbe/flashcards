Verb

[edit]

演奏

  1. to play a musical instrument; to perform music; to give an instrumental performance
    演奏完畢／演奏完毕  ―  yǎnzòu wánbì  ―  end of performance

Etymology

[edit]

From 演 ("perform," "put on") + 奏 ("play (music)").

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • (Tokyo) えんそー [èńsóó] (Heiban – [0])
  • IPA(key): [ẽ̞ɰ̃so̞ː]

Noun

[edit]

演(えん)奏(そう) • (ensō) 

  1. a performance (of music); a recital
    演奏(えんそう)時間(じかん)の短(みじか)い曲(きょく)
    ensō jikan no mijikai kyoku
    short song
    (literally, “song with a short playing time”)

Verb

[edit]

演(えん)奏(そう)する • (ensō suru) transitive suru (stem 演(えん)奏(そう)し (ensō shi), past 演(えん)奏(そう)した (ensō shita))

  1. to play (an instrument)
    ピアノを演奏(えんそう)する
    piano o ensō suru
    play the piano
  2. to perform (a piece of music)
    機械(きかい)によって自動的(じどうてき)に楽曲(がっきょく)を演奏(えんそう)すること
    kikai ni yotte jidōteki ni gakkyoku o ensō suru koto
    playing a musical composition automatically by means of a machine
