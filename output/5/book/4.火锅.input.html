Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 火锅 – see 火鍋 (“pot used for hot pot; hot pot; fondue”).
(This term is the simplified form of 火鍋).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
fire; angry; fierce
fire; angry; fierce; fiery; thriving
 
 pot; pan; boiler
trad. (火鍋) 火 鍋
simp. (火锅) 火 锅
Literally: “fire pot”.

Noun

[edit]

火鍋

  1. pot used for hot pot; (Chinese-style) hot pot; (Western-style) fondue
