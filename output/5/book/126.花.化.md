## transform
### sub-component
#### flower
---
- **traditional:** 化
- **simplified:** 化
- **pronunciation:** huà
- **definition:** transform
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 化学(學) (huàxué, "chemical science")
  - 化妆(妝) (huàzhuāng, "put on makeup")
  - 化工 (huàgōng, "chemical industry")
  - 化验(驗) (huàyàn, "laboratory test")
- **etymology (化):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 亻 (rén, "person") + 𠤎 (rén, "person").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts one person upright beside another inverted, signaling reversal or change, which extends to the idea of transforming state or form. Some sources (e.g., Shuowen) take 𠤎 as phonetic, but the mainstream view interprets both parts semantically to express change, aligning with the modern sense of transformation.
  - **component characters:**
    - 亻 (rén, "person")
    - 𠤎 (rén, "person")
