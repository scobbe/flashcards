## traditional music composition
---
- **traditional:** 春江花月夜
- **simplified:** 春江花月夜
- **pronunciation:** Chūn Jiāng Huā Yuè Yè
- **definition:** traditional music composition
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 春江花月夜名曲 (chūn jiāng huā yuè yè míng qǔ, "classic piece")
  - 春江花月夜古筝(箏)曲 (chūn jiāng huā yuè yè gǔ zhēng qǔ, "guzheng piece")
  - 春江花月夜琵琶曲 (chūn jiāng huā yuè yè pí pá qǔ, "pipa piece")
  - 春江花月夜音乐(樂)会(會) (chūn jiāng huā yuè yè yīn yuè huì, "concert performance")
- **etymology (春江花月夜):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 春 (chūn, "spring") + 江 (jiāng, "river") + 花 (huā, "flowers") + 月 (yuè, "moon") + 夜 (yè, "night").
  - **interpretation:** The five nouns combine to depict a tranquil scene of a spring river with flowers and a moonlit night. This evocative imagery became the title of a renowned traditional instrumental composition, matching the mood the music conveys.
  - **component characters:**
    - 春
    - 江
    - 花
    - 月
    - 夜
