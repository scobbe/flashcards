## folk music
---
- **traditional:** 民樂
- **simplified:** 民乐
- **pronunciation:** mínyuè
- **definition:** folk music
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 我喜欢(歡)听(聽)中国(國)民乐(樂)。 (wǒ xǐhuan tīng zhōngguó mínyuè, "I like listening to Chinese folk music")
  - 民乐(樂)团(團) (mínyuè tuán, "folk orchestra")
  - 民乐(樂)演出 (mínyuè yǎnchū, "folk music performance")
  - 民乐(樂)作品 (mínyuè zuòpǐn, "folk music piece")
- **etymology (民樂):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 民 (mín, "people") + 乐(樂) (yuè, "music") → 民乐 (mínyuè, "folk music").
  - **interpretation:** The word combines 民 “people” and 乐 “music” to mean music of the people. In modern Mainland usage it refers to traditional Chinese instrumental/orchestral music, with 乐 giving the domain and 民 indicating folk/traditional provenance.
  - **component characters:**
    - 民
    - 乐
    - 樂
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%B0%91%E6%A8%82
  - **simplification rule (民乐):** The traditional 樂 was simplified to 乐 through stroke reduction and component regularization, while 民 was unchanged, yielding 民乐.
