## meal; rice
### sub-word
#### restaurant
---
- **traditional:** 飯
- **simplified:** 饭
- **pronunciation:** fàn
- **definition:** meal; rice
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 吃饭(飯) (chī fàn, "eat a meal")
  - 米饭(飯) (mǐ fàn, "cooked rice")
  - 早饭(飯) (zǎo fàn, "breakfast meal")
  - 饭(飯)店 (fàn diàn, "restaurant or hotel")
- **etymology (飯):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 食 (shí, "food") + phonetic: 反 (fǎn, "reverse") → 饭(飯) (fàn, "meal").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 食 as the semantic determinative indicating the food domain with 反 as the phonetic element yielding the reading fàn. This formation matches the modern senses like meal and cooked rice because 食 signals edibles while 反 provides the sound.
  - **component characters:**
    - 食
    - 反
    - 饭
    - 飯
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A3%AF
  - **simplification rule (饭):** The simplified form replaces the semantic component 食 with the simplified food radical 饣, reducing strokes while retaining the phonetic 反.
