## congratulations
---
- **traditional:** 恭喜
- **simplified:** 恭喜
- **pronunciation:** gōngxǐ
- **definition:** congratulations
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 恭喜发(發)财(財) (gōng xǐ fā cái, "wish you prosperity")
  - 恭喜你 (gōng xǐ nǐ, "congratulations to you")
  - 恭喜恭喜 (gōng xǐ gōng xǐ, "congratulations")
  - 恭喜获(獲)奖(獎) (gōng xǐ huò jiǎng, "congrats on winning")
- **etymology (恭喜):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 恭 (gōng, "respectful") + 喜 (xǐ, "happiness") → 恭喜 (gōng xǐ, "congratulate").
  - **interpretation:** 恭 conveys respectful or deferential attitude, and 喜 denotes happiness or joy. Together they express offering respectful joy to someone, which yields the meaning "to congratulate" and the interjection "congratulations."
  - **component characters:**
    - 恭
    - 喜
