Phono-semantic compound (形聲 / 形声, OC *sroːns): semantic 氵 (“water”) + phonetic 刷 (OC *sroːd, *srod).

Definitions

[edit]

涮

  1. to rinse clean; to swill
    把衣服涮一涮  ―  bǎ yīfú shuàn yī shuàn  ―  rinse the clothes clean
  2. to scald thin slices of meat in boiling water (then immediately taken out)
    涮火鍋／涮火锅  ―  shuàn huǒguō  ―  to have hot pot

Compounds

[edit]
  • 涮羊肉
  • 涮腰
  • 涮鍋子 / 涮锅子 (shuàn guōzi)
