## meat
---
- **traditional:** 肉
- **simplified:** 肉
- **pronunciation:** ròu
- **definition:** meat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 我不吃肉 (wǒ bù chī ròu, "I don't eat meat")
  - 肉体(體) (ròu tǐ, "physical body")
  - 肉丝(絲) (ròu sī, "shredded meat")
  - 肉丸 (ròu wán, "meatball")
- **etymology (肉):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of ribs of an animal's torso → semantic: meat; flesh.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts ribs or a slice of animal flesh. The concrete depiction was generalized to the notion of meat and human or animal flesh, which matches its modern senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
