Etymology 1

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 𠤎 – see 化 (“to change; to transform; to become; to turn into; etc.”).
(This character is an ancient form of 化).

Etymology 2

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 𠤎 – see 匕 (“a kind of spoon in ancient China; dagger; etc.”).
(This character is a variant form of 匕).
