Definitions

[edit]

演

  1. to develop; to evolve
    演變／演变  ―  yǎnbiàn  ―  to develop
    演化  ―  yǎnhuà  ―  to evolve
  2. to deduce
    推演  ―  tuīyǎn  ―  deduce
    演算  ―  yǎnsuàn  ―  to perform mathematical calculations
  3. to perform; to act; to put on
    表演  ―  biǎoyǎn  ―  to perform
    演員／演员  ―  yǎnyuán  ―  performer
  4. to drill; to practice; to exercise
    演練／演练  ―  yǎnliàn  ―  to drill
    演習／演习  ―  yǎnxí  ―  to do field practice
  5. a surname, Yan
