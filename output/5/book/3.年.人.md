- **pronunciation:** rén
- **definition:** person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 人类(類) (rén lèi, "humans")
  - 人手一册(冊) (rén shǒu yī cè, "everyone has a copy")
  - 人所共知 (rén suǒ gòng zhī, "common knowledge")
  - 人云(雲)亦云(雲) (rén yún yì yún, "follow the crowd")
- **etymology (人):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a standing man in profile with one arm and one leg emphasized.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human figure seen from the side, highlighting an arm and a leg. The original meaning was simply a human being, which remains the core sense in modern usage.
