Definitions

[edit]

煮

  1. to boil; to cook
    煮麵／煮面  ―  zhǔ miàn  ―  to make (cook) noodles
  2. (neologism, humorous) Alternative form of 主 (Zhǔ, “Lord”) referring to the Flying Spaghetti Monster.
  3. a surname, Zhu

Synonyms

[edit]
  • (to cook): (Hokkien) 咴 (huī)

煮

(Jōyō kanji, shinjitai kanji, kyūjitai form 煮)

  1. to boil, to cook
