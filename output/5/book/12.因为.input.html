Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 因为 – see 因為 (“because; since; as; owing to; due to; as a result of; because of”).
(This term is the simplified form of 因為).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
cause; reason; because
because of; for; to
because of; for; to; act as; take...to be; to be; to do; to serve as; to become
 
trad. (因為/因爲) 因 為/爲
simp. (因为) 因 为
anagram 為因／为因

Conjunction

[edit]

因為

  1. because; since; as
    昨天的野餐取消了，因為從早上開始就一直在下雨。 [MSC, trad.]
    昨天的野餐取消了，因为从早上开始就一直在下雨。 [MSC, simp.]
    Zuótiān de yěcān qǔxiāo le, yīnwèi cóng zǎoshàng kāishǐ jiù yīzhí zài xiàyǔ. [Pinyin]
    Yesterday's picnic was cancelled because it had been raining since morning.
    • 不受教育的人，因為不識字，上人的當。受教育的人，因為識了字，上印刷品的當。 [MSC, trad.]
      不受教育的人，因为不识字，上人的当。受教育的人，因为识了字，上印刷品的当。 [MSC, simp.]
      From: 1947, Qian Zhongshu, Fortress Besieged
      Bù shòu jiàoyù de rén, yīnwèi bù shízì, shàng rén de dāng. Shòu jiàoyù de rén, yīnwèi shí le zì, shàng yìnshuāpǐn de dāng. [Pinyin]
      Because the uneducated are illiterate, they are fooled by people. Because the educated are literate, they are fooled by printed products.
