## anxious; urgent
### sub-word
#### anxious; worried
---
- **traditional:** 急
- **simplified:** 急
- **pronunciation:** jí
- **definition:** anxious; urgent
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 急诊(診) (jízhěn, "emergency treatment")
  - 急救 (jíjiù, "first aid")
  - 急忙 (jímáng, "in a hurry")
  - 急性子 (jíxìngzi, "impatient person")
- **etymology (急):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic 及 (jí, "reach") + semantic 心 (xīn, "heart") → 急 (jí, "urgent").
  - **interpretation:** The character combines phonetic 及 (jí) with the semantic determinative 心 (xīn, "heart"), associating the meaning with emotional agitation and pressing urgency. The phonetic 及 supplies the reading jí, while 心 situates the sense in feelings and inner states. It is not a variant of 亟 according to the cited note.
  - **component characters:**
    - 及
    - 心
    - 急
