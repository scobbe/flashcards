Phono-semantic compound (形聲 / 形声, OC *ɡ·raːɡs): semantic 足 + phonetic 各 (OC *klaːɡ).

Etymology

[edit]

  • Either related to 格 (OC *klaːɡ, *kraːɡ, “to come, to arrive (obsolete)”) (Baxter, 1992) or from Proto-Austroasiatic *kraʔ (“road, way”) (Schuessler, 2007), whence Proto-Vietic *kraːʔ (→ fossilized Vietnamese sá & Muong khá (“road, way”)), Chong [script needed] (khraa), Kuy [script needed] ((k)na), and Proto-Waic *kraʔ. Possibly cognate with 略 (OC *ɡ·raɡ).

    "-year-old, in multiples of ten"
    Orthographic borrowing from Japanese 路(じ) (ji). See Japanese 三十路(みそじ) (misoji).

      Definitions

      [edit]

      路

      1. road; path; street (Classifier: 條／条 m c mn)
        修路  ―  xiūlù  ―  to build a road
        • 這正如地上的路；其實地上本沒有路，走的人多了，也便成了路。 [MSC, trad.]
          这正如地上的路；其实地上本没有路，走的人多了，也便成了路。 [MSC, simp.]
          From: 1921, Lu Xun, 故鄉 (My Old Home)
          Zhè zhèngrú dìshàng de lù; qíshí dìshàng běn méiyǒu lù, zǒu de rén duō le, yě biàn chéng le lù. [Pinyin]
          It is just like roads across the earth. For actually the earth had no roads to begin with, but when many men pass one way, a road is made.
      2. pattern; arrangement; sequence
        思路  ―  sīlù  ―  train of thought
        紋路／纹路  ―  wénlù  ―  veined pattern
      3. way; method
        生路  ―  shēnglù  ―  means of livelihood; way of subsistence
        • 無路請纓，等終軍之弱冠。 [Classical Chinese, trad.]
          无路请缨，等终军之弱冠。 [Classical Chinese, simp.]
          From: 7th century, 王勃 (Wang Bo), 滕王閣序
          Wúlù qǐngyīng, děng Zhōng Jūn zhī ruòguàn. [Pinyin]
          (please add an English translation of this usage example)
      4. journey
        回家的路很遠。／回家的路很远。  ―  Huíjiā de lù hěn yuǎn.  ―  It's a long journey to return home.
        佢身體好虛，行幾步路都會暈。 [Cantonese, trad.]
        佢身体好虚，行几步路都会晕。 [Cantonese, simp.]
        keoi5 san1 tai2 hou2 heoi1, haang4 gei2 bou6 lou6 dou1 wui5 wan4. [Jyutping]
        His body is very weak, walking only a few steps already makes him dizzy.
        還有一公里的路要走。 [MSC, trad.]
        还有一公里的路要走。 [MSC, simp.]
        Háiyǒu yī gōnglǐ de lù yào zǒu. [Pinyin]
        (please add an English translation of this usage example)
        • 三十功名塵與土，八千里路雲和月。 [Classical Chinese, trad.]
          三十功名尘与土，八千里路云和月。 [Classical Chinese, simp.]
          From: Yue Fei (1103-1142), Man Jiang Hong (滿江紅)
          Sānshí gōngmíng chén yǔ tǔ, bāqiān lǐ lù yún hé yuè. [Pinyin]
          (please add an English translation of this usage example)
      5. route
        三路進軍／三路进军  ―  sān lù jìnjūn  ―  to advance along three routes
        12路公交車／12路公交车  ―  12 lù gōngjiāochē  ―  No. 12 bus
      6. kind; type
      7. region
        各路人馬／各路人马  ―  gè lù rénmǎ  ―  people from different places
      8. Classifier for lines or rows.
      9. -year-old, in multiples of ten; decade(s) of one's life; Used for describing the style of people in such age range.
        三十路大叔  ―  sānshílù dàshū  ―  a man in his thirties
      10. (historical) circuit (an administrative unit during the Song and Yuan dynasties)
      11. a surname

        Nara-ji
        road to Nara
      3. attaches to a time duration, indicating a trip taking that length of time
        四(よっ)日(か)路(じ)
        yokka-ji
        a four-day trip
