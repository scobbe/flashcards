## joy; happiness
### sub-word
#### congratulations
---
- **traditional:** 喜
- **simplified:** 喜
- **pronunciation:** xǐ
- **definition:** joy; happiness
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 喜欢(歡) (xǐhuān, "to like")
  - 喜剧(劇) (xǐjù, "comedy genre")
  - 喜讯(訊) (xǐxùn, "happy news")
  - 喜悦(悅) (xǐyuè, "joyful delight")
- **etymology (喜):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 壴 (zhù, "drum") + 口 (kǒu, "mouth").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 壴 (drum) and 口 (mouth) to depict drumming and singing, symbolizing celebration and joy. The semantic combination naturally extends to modern senses of happiness, festive events, and liking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 壴
    - 口
