## to perform
### sub-word
#### to perform (instrumental)
---
- **traditional:** 演
- **simplified:** 演
- **pronunciation:** yǎn
- **definition:** to perform
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 演出 (yǎnchū, "put on a show")
  - 演员(員) (yǎnyuán, "actor or performer")
  - 演唱 (yǎnchàng, "perform a song")
  - 演奏 (yǎnzòu, "perform music")
- **etymology (演):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 寅 (yín, "Yin branch") → 演.
  - **interpretation:** 氵 supplies the semantic field of water and flowing, pointing to the original sense ‘to flow out; extend’. 寅 provides the sound, while the meaning broadened from physical flow to development and progression, and further to carrying out or staging actions, hence ‘to perform’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 水
    - 寅
    - 演
