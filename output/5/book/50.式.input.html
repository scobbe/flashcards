Definitions

[edit]

式

  1. (literary, or in compounds) standard; established practice; norm
  2. (literary, or in compounds) type; style; pattern
  3. (literary, or in compounds) ritual; ceremony
  4. (literary, or in compounds) example
  5. (mathematics, chemistry) formula (Classifier: 條／条 c)
  6. (grammar) tense
  7. (grammar) mood; mode
    敘述式／叙述式  ―  xùshùshì  ―  indicative mood
  8. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to follow the example of
  9. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to set an example
  10. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) alternative form of 試 / 试 (shì, “to apply”)
  11. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) alternative form of 軾 / 轼 (shì, “to perform a rite”)

Kanji

式

(Third grade kyōiku kanji)

Readings

[edit]
  • Go-on: しき (shiki, Jōyō)
  • Kan-on: しょく (shoku)
  • Kun: のり (nori, 式)、のっとる (nottoru, 式る)
  • Nanori: つね (tsune)、のり (nori)、もち (mochi)

Pronunciation

[edit]
  • (Tokyo) しき [shìkíꜜ] (Odaka – [2])
  • IPA(key): [ɕikʲi]

Noun

[edit]

式(しき) • (shiki) 

  1. type, style
  2. (mathematics) a formula or expression
  3. ceremony, ritual, function
    式(しき)を挙(あ)げる
    shiki o ageru
    to hold a ceremony
  4. short for 結婚式 (kekkon-shiki, “wedding ceremony”)

Suffix

[edit]

式(しき) • (-shiki) 

  1. ceremony
  2. formula, expression
  3. style

  • 式(しき)神(がみ) (shikigami), 式(しき)神(じん) (shikijin)

  • 株(かぶ)式(しき) (kabushiki)
