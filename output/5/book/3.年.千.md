## thousand
### sub-component
#### year
---
- **traditional:** 千
- **simplified:** 千
- **pronunciation:** qiān
- **definition:** thousand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 千万(萬) (qiān wàn, "ten million")
  - 千米 (qiān mǐ, "kilometer")
  - 千年 (qiān nián, "a thousand years")
  - 千瓦 (qiān wǎ, "kilowatt")
- **etymology (千):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 一 (yī, "one") + phonetic: 人 (rén, "person") → phono-semantic compound.
  - **interpretation:** The character combines 一 as a semantic indicator of numbers with 人 as the phonetic, reflecting similar Old Chinese pronunciations. This semantic determinative suits its modern numeral sense. A traditional alternative view holds that an added stroke signifies extension in counting, hence the value reaching a large number like a thousand.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 人
