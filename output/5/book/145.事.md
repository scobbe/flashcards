## matter or affair
### sub-word
#### may all your wishes come true
---
- **traditional:** 事
- **simplified:** 事
- **pronunciation:** shì
- **definition:** matter or affair
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 事情 (shìqíng, "a matter")
  - 事件 (shìjiàn, "an incident")
  - 事实(實) (shìshí, "a fact")
  - 事业(業) (shìyè, "a career")
- **etymology (事):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 又 (yòu, "hand") + 中 (zhōng, "flag").
  - **interpretation:** It combines a hand with a flag, depicting holding a flag to carry out duties, which led to the sense of performing one's job and later to the general meaning of an affair or matter. Alternatively, some analyze it as an exopassive of 士 (shì, "to serve"), meaning "what has been assigned".
  - **component characters:**
    - 又
    - 中
