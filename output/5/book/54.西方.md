## the West; western
---
- **traditional:** 西方
- **simplified:** 西方
- **pronunciation:** xīfāng
- **definition:** the West; western
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 西方音乐(樂) (xīfāng yīnyuè, "Western music")
  - 西方中心主义(義) (xīfāng zhōngxīn zhǔyì, "Western centrism")
  - 西方人 (xīfāng rén, "Western person")
  - 西方化 (xīfāng huà, "to westernize")
- **etymology (西方):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 西 (xī, "west") + 方 (fāng, "direction").
  - **interpretation:** 西 means west and 方 means direction or region, so together they denote the western direction or region. In modern usage this extends to the Western world, and in Buddhist contexts it refers to the Western Paradise.
  - **component characters:**
    - 西
    - 方
