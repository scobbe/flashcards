## to rent; to hire
---
- **traditional:** 出租
- **simplified:** 出租
- **pronunciation:** chūzū
- **definition:** to rent; to hire
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 出租房子 (chū zū fáng zi, "rent out a house")
  - 出租房 (chū zū fáng, "rental apartment")
  - 出租汽车(車) (chū zū qì chē, "taxicab")
  - 出租车(車) (chū zū chē, "taxi")
- **etymology (出租):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 出 (chū, "to let out") + 租 (zū, "rent").
  - **interpretation:** Combining 出 meaning to let out with 租 meaning rent yields the sense “to rent out,” i.e., to let something for hire. By extension, 出租 is also used as a clipped noun from 出租汽车 for a taxi, preserving the idea of a vehicle offered for hire.
  - **component characters:**
    - 出
    - 租
