## style; type
### sub-word
#### formal
---
- **traditional:** 式
- **simplified:** 式
- **pronunciation:** shì
- **definition:** style; type
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 方式 (fāngshì, "method or way")
  - 正式 (zhèngshì, "official or formal")
  - 公式 (gōngshì, "mathematical formula")
  - 样(樣)式 (yàngshì, "design style")
- **etymology (式):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 工 (gōng, "work") + phonetic: 弋 (yì, "dart").
  - **interpretation:** 工 is the semantic determinative indicating craft, pattern, or method, while 弋 supplies the pronunciation. This composition yielded the sense of a set pattern or style, which extended to modern meanings like ritual forms and mathematical formulas.
  - **component characters:**
    - 工
    - 弋
