  - **description:** pictogram of 屮 (chè, "sprout") → duplication to depict grass blades.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts two sprouting shoots, representing clumps of grass. The form doubles 屮 (sprout) to convey vegetation and became the plant radical and ancient form corresponding to the modern character for grass.
