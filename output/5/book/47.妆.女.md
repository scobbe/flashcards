## female
### sub-component
#### makeup
---
- **traditional:** 女
- **simplified:** 女
- **pronunciation:** nǚ
- **definition:** female
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 女卫(衛)生间(間) (nǚ wèi shēng jiān, "women's restroom")
  - 女演员(員) (nǚ yǎn yuán, "actress")
  - 女士兵 (nǚ shì bīng, "female soldier")
  - 女人 (nǚ rén, "woman")
- **etymology (女):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a kneeling woman with breasts.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a kneeling woman with breasts. Over time the figure was rotated to face left and details were reduced, leaving a single enclosed stroke for the remaining breast. The character therefore directly came to mean “woman” and, by extension, “female.”
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
