## to photograph; shine
### sub-word
#### camera
---
- **traditional:** 照
- **simplified:** 照
- **pronunciation:** zhào
- **definition:** to photograph; shine
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 照片 (zhàopiàn, "a photograph")
  - 照相 (zhàoxiàng, "take photographs")
  - 照明 (zhàomíng, "indoor lighting")
  - 照射 (zhàoshè, "to irradiate")
- **etymology (照):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 灬 (huǒ, "fire") + phonetic: 昭 (zhāo, "bright").
  - **interpretation:** 灬 provides the semantic field of fire/light, while 昭 supplies the sound zhào and the sense of brightness. This combination yields the meaning ‘to shine/illuminate’, which extends naturally to modern uses such as lighting and photography. Some sources also analyze it as a derivative of 昭 ‘bright’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 灬
    - 昭
