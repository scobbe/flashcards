## mutton
### sub-word
#### thin slices of mutton boiled in water
---
- **traditional:** 羊肉
- **simplified:** 羊肉
- **pronunciation:** yángròu
- **definition:** mutton
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 羊肉十大名菜 (yáng ròu shí dà míng cài, "ten famous mutton dishes")
  - 羊肉串 (yáng ròu chuàn, "mutton skewers")
  - 羊肉汤(湯) (yáng ròu tāng, "mutton soup")
  - 羊肉泡馍(饃) (yáng ròu pào mó, "mutton bread soup")
- **etymology (羊肉):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 羊 (yáng, "sheep") + 肉 (ròu, "meat").
  - **interpretation:** 羊 (yáng, "sheep") and 肉 (ròu, "meat") combine literally to mean the meat of sheep or goats. In modern usage, 羊 includes both sheep and goats, so the term covers lamb, mutton, and goat meat.
  - **component characters:**
    - 羊
    - 肉
