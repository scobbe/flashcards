## meat
### sub-word
#### thin slices of mutton boiled in water
---
- **traditional:** 肉
- **simplified:** 肉
- **pronunciation:** ròu
- **definition:** meat
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 猪(豬)肉 (zhū ròu, "pork")
  - 牛肉 (niú ròu, "beef")
  - 肉体(體) (ròu tǐ, "physical body")
  - 肉松(鬆) (ròu sōng, "pork floss")
- **etymology (肉):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of the ribs of an animal’s torso or a slice of meat.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the ribs/torso of an animal or a cut of meat, directly representing flesh. This concrete image naturally yields the modern senses of meat as food and flesh of the body.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
