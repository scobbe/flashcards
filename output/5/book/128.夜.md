## night or nighttime
### sub-word
#### traditional music composition
---
- **traditional:** 夜
- **simplified:** 夜
- **pronunciation:** yè
- **definition:** night or nighttime
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 夜市 (yèshì, "night market")
  - 夜宵 (yèxiāo, "late-night snack")
  - 夜勤 (yèqín, "night duty")
  - 夜场(場) (yèchǎng, "nightlife venue")
- **etymology (夜):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 亦 (yì, "also") + semantic: 夕 (xī, "moon") → 夜.
  - **interpretation:** The character uses 夕 (xī, "moon") as the semantic determinative pointing to nighttime, while 亦 (yì, "also") supplies the sound, yielding yè. Some analyses note that 夕 may also contribute phonetic value, but its semantic link to night explains the modern meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - 亦
    - 夕
    - 夜
