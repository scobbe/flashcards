## to add
---
- **traditional:** 添
- **simplified:** 添
- **pronunciation:** tiān
- **definition:** to add
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 请(請)添点(點)儿(兒)菜 (qǐng tiān diǎnr cài, "please add some dishes")
  - 添麻烦(煩) (tiān má fan, "cause trouble")
  - 添油加醋 (tiān yóu jiā cù, "exaggerate the story")
  - 添丁 (tiān dīng, "have a baby")
- **etymology (添):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic 水 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic 忝 (tiǎn, "honored").
  - **interpretation:** The left component 水 provides the domain of liquids and adding by pouring. 忝 serves as the phonetic, giving the syllable and guiding the pronunciation. The meaning generalized from adding water to the broader sense of adding or increasing.
  - **component characters:**
    - 水
    - 忝
