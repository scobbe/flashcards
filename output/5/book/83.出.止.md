## foot
### sub-component
#### to go out; to emit
---
- **traditional:** 止
- **simplified:** 止
- **pronunciation:** zhǐ
- **definition:** foot
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 止血 (zhǐ xuè, "stop bleeding")
  - 禁止 (jìn zhǐ, "to prohibit")
  - 终(終)止 (zhōng zhǐ, "to terminate")
  - 适(適)可而止 (shì kě ér zhǐ, "stop at the right time")
- **etymology (止):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a footprint with three toes and a sole pointing upward → extended to the sense ‘stop’ via a halted step.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a footprint with three toes and a sole pointing upward. It originally meant ‘foot’, and the sense ‘stop/cease’ arose by association with a foot coming to a halt. The derivative 趾 (zhǐ, "toe") preserves the original bodily sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
