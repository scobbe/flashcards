## to rent
### sub-word
#### taxi
---
- **traditional:** 租
- **simplified:** 租
- **pronunciation:** zū
- **definition:** to rent
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 租赁(賃) (zūlìn, "to lease")
  - 租约(約) (zūyuē, "lease agreement")
  - 租金 (zūjīn, "rental fee")
  - 租户(戶) (zūhù, "home renter")
- **etymology (租):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 禾 (hé, "grain") + phonetic: 且 (qiě, "and").
  - **interpretation:** 禾 is the semantic determinative pointing to grain and agriculture, while 且 supplies the sound. The character originally referred to rent or tax paid in grain, which later generalized to modern uses meaning rent and to lease.
  - **component characters:**
    - 禾
    - 且
