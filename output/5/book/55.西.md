## west
### sub-word
#### the West; western
---
- **traditional:** 西
- **simplified:** 西
- **pronunciation:** xī
- **definition:** west
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 西方 (xīfāng, "the West")
  - 西瓜 (xīguā, "watermelon")
  - 西医(醫) (xīyī, "Western medicine")
  - 西装(裝) (xīzhuāng, "suit")
- **etymology (西):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a bag or basket → borrowed phonetically for "west".
  - **interpretation:** It originally depicts a bag or basket. The graph was later borrowed for its sound to write the word meaning “west,” which is the modern sense. A traditional alternative sees a bird settling into a nest as an analogy for the setting sun and thus “west,” a view disputed by some but defended by Sagart.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
