## sugarcane
### sub-component
#### one (person)
---
- **traditional:** 蔗
- **simplified:** 蔗
- **pronunciation:** zhè
- **definition:** sugarcane
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 蔗糖 (zhètáng, "cane sugar")
  - 蔗渣 (zhèzhā, "cane bagasse")
  - 蔗汁 (zhèzhī, "sugarcane juice")
  - 蔗农(農) (zhènóng, "sugarcane farmer")
- **etymology (蔗):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic 艹(艸) (cǎo, "grass") + phonetic 庶 (shù, "numerous").
  - **interpretation:** 艹 marks the character as related to plants. 庶 provides the sound value approximating zhè while contributing no core meaning. The plant determinative aligns with the modern meaning ‘sugarcane,’ which is a plant.
  - **component characters:**
    - 艸
    - 庶
