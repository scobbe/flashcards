## eye
### sub-component
#### image; appearance
---
- **traditional:** 目
- **simplified:** 目
- **pronunciation:** mù
- **definition:** eye
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 引人注目 (yǐn rén zhù mù, "draw attention")
  - 目录 (mù lù, "catalog")
  - 目标 (mù biāo, "target")
  - 题目 (tí mù, "title")
- **etymology (目):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a human eye → 目 (mù, "eye").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human eye, later standardized in orientation. From the concrete sense of the organ of sight, it extended to senses related to seeing and, by analogy, to labeled items and goals in compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 目
