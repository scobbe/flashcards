## respectful
### sub-word
#### congratulations
---
- **traditional:** 恭
- **simplified:** 恭
- **pronunciation:** gōng
- **definition:** respectful
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 恭喜 (gōng xǐ, "offer congratulations")
  - 恭敬 (gōng jìng, "respectful and deferential")
  - 恭维(維) (gōng wéi, "to flatter")
  - 恭贺(賀) (gōng hè, "extend congratulations")
- **etymology (恭):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: phonetic: 共 (gòng, "together") + semantic: 心 (xīn, "heart").
  - **interpretation:** Formed with 心 as the semantic determinative indicating feelings and attitudes of the heart, and 共 as the phonetic giving the gōng reading. The sense "respectful; reverent" aligns with the heart determinative as a matter of inner attitude and propriety.
  - **component characters:**
    - 共
    - 心
