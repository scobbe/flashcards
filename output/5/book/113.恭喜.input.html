Verb

[edit]

恭喜

  1. to congratulate
    表示恭喜  ―  biǎoshì gōngxǐ  ―  to express congratulations

Synonyms

[edit]
edit

Interjection

[edit]

恭喜

  1. congratulations!
    恭喜手機尾號是XXXX的朋友獲得一等獎。 [MSC, trad.]
    恭喜手机尾号是XXXX的朋友获得一等奖。 [MSC, simp.]
    Gōngxǐ shǒujī wěihào shì XXXX de péngyou huòdé yīděngjiǎng. [Pinyin]
    Congratulations to the participant whose phone number ends in XXXX for winning first prize.
