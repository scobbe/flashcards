## say
### sub-component
#### sound
---
- **traditional:** 曰
- **simplified:** 曰
- **pronunciation:** yuē
- **definition:** say
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 子曰 (zǐ yuē, "Confucius said")
  - 或曰 (huò yuē, "someone said")
  - 子曰诗(詩)云(雲) (zǐ yuē shī yún, "quoting classics")
  - 书(書)曰 (shū yuē, "the book says")
- **etymology (曰):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") + 一 (yī, "breath").
  - **interpretation:** It combines 口 (kǒu, "mouth") with 一 (yī, "breath") to depict breath or words issuing from a mouth. This directly denotes speaking, explaining the meaning "to say" in classical texts and its continued use in quotation formulas today.
  - **component characters:**
    - 口
    - 一
