## to cook thin slices of meat in boiling water
---
- **traditional:** 涮
- **simplified:** 涮
- **pronunciation:** shuàn
- **definition:** to cook thin slices of meat in boiling water
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 涮羊肉 (shuàn yángròu, "instant-boiled mutton")
  - 涮火锅(鍋) (shuàn huǒguō, "have hot pot")
  - 涮锅(鍋)子 (shuàn guōzi, "have hot pot")
  - 涮腰 (shuàn yāo, "swish kidneys")
- **etymology (涮):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 氵 (shuǐ, "water") + phonetic: 刷 (shuā, "brush").
  - **interpretation:** 氵 is the semantic determinative indicating an action involving water or liquid. 刷 provides the sound and suggests a swift swishing motion, matching rinsing and the quick dip-and-swish cooking in hot pot.
  - **component characters:**
    - 氵 (shuǐ, "water")
    - 刷 (shuā, "brush")
