## camera
### sub-word
#### camera
---
- **traditional:** 相機
- **simplified:** 相机
- **pronunciation:** xiàngjī
- **definition:** camera
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 相机(機)镜(鏡)头(頭) (xiàngjī jìngtóu, "camera lens")
  - 相机(機)应(應)用 (xiàngjī yìngyòng, "camera app")
  - 相机(機)设(設)置 (xiàngjī shèzhì, "camera settings")
  - 相机(機)包 (xiàngjī bāo, "camera bag")
- **etymology (相機):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 相 (xiàng, "image") + 机(機) (jī, "machine").
  - **interpretation:** 相 (xiàng, "image") combines with 机(機) (jī, "machine") to yield the literal sense "image machine". This straightforward composition explains the modern meaning "camera" as a machine for capturing images.
  - **component characters:**
    - 相
    - 机
    - 機
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%9B%B8%E6%A9%9F
  - **simplification rule (相机):** 相機 was simplified to 相机 by reducing 機 to 机 through component replacement and stroke reduction (simplifying 幾 to 几).
