## as or like
### sub-word
#### may all your wishes come true
---
- **traditional:** 如
- **simplified:** 如
- **pronunciation:** rú
- **definition:** as or like
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 如果 (rúguǒ, "if")
  - 如此 (rúcǐ, "like this")
  - 比如 (bǐrú, "for example")
  - 不如 (bùrú, "not as good as")
- **etymology (如):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 女 (nǚ, "woman") + semantic: 口 (kǒu, "mouth") → phono-semantic compound.
  - **interpretation:** Formed with 女 (nǚ, "woman") as the phonetic and 口 (kǒu, "mouth") as the semantic indicator for speech or commands. The original sense of complying or being in accordance led to modern meanings like "as; like" and conditional "if" uses. Some sources also read 女 ideographically as indicating obedience, reinforcing the idea of compliance.
  - **component characters:**
    - 女
    - 口
