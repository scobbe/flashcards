## or
### sub-word
#### or
---
- **traditional:** 或
- **simplified:** 或
- **pronunciation:** huò
- **definition:** or
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 或者 (huòzhě, "or")
  - 或是 (huòshì, "or")
  - 或许(許) (huòxǔ, "perhaps")
  - 间(間)或 (jiànhuò, "occasionally")
- **etymology (或):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 戈 (gē, "dagger-axe") + 囗 (wéi, "city wall") + 一 (yī, "one") → 或 (yù, "territory").
  - **interpretation:** Combining 戈 (gē, "dagger-axe") with 囗 (wéi, "city wall") and a mark 一 (yī, "one") depicts a defended bounded area, i.e., a territory. The graph was later borrowed for abstract grammatical uses in Classical Chinese, which developed into modern disjunctive "or" and indefinite "some" senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 戈
    - 囗
    - 一
    - 或
