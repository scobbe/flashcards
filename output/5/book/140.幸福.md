## happy
---
- **traditional:** 幸福
- **simplified:** 幸福
- **pronunciation:** xìngfú
- **definition:** happy
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 幸福的生活 (xìngfú de shēnghuó, "happy life")
  - 幸福的家庭 (xìngfú de jiātíng, "happy family")
  - 幸福感 (xìngfúgǎn, "sense of happiness")
  - 幸福度 (xìngfúdù, "happiness level")
- **etymology (幸福):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 幸 (xìng, "fortunate") + 福 (fú, "blessing").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 幸 (xìng, "fortunate") and 福 (fú, "blessing") to express a blessed, satisfactory state of life. In modern usage it denotes durable contentment with one's circumstances and relationships reflecting both auspicious and well-being senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 幸
    - 福
