## flower
### sub-word
#### traditional music composition
---
- **traditional:** 花
- **simplified:** 花
- **pronunciation:** huā
- **definition:** flower
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 花纹(紋) (huāwén, "decorative pattern")
  - 花卉 (huāhuì, "flowers and plants")
  - 花丛(叢) (huācóng, "flower thicket")
  - 花冠 (huāguān, "flower corolla")
- **etymology (花):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") + phonetic: 化 (huà, "transform") → 花 (huā, "flower").
  - **interpretation:** Formed as a phono-semantic compound: 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant") provides the semantic domain of vegetation, while 化 (huà, "transform") supplies the sound. The plant determinative naturally aligns with the modern botanical meaning "flower".
  - **component characters:**
    - 艹 (cǎo, "grass; plant")
    - 化 (huà, "transform")
