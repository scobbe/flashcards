## side
### sub-word
#### side; beside
---
- **traditional:** 旁
- **simplified:** 旁
- **pronunciation:** páng
- **definition:** side
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 旁边(邊) (pángbiān, "at the side")
  - 旁观(觀) (pángguān, "watch as bystander")
  - 旁听(聽) (pángtīng, "audit a class")
  - 旁证(證) (pángzhèng, "corroborating evidence")
- **etymology (旁):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 辛 (xīn, "punishment") + phonetic: 方 (fāng, "direction") → 旁 (páng, "side").
  - **interpretation:** Most analyses take this as a phono-semantic formation: the upper element 辛 provides a semantic hint of punishment/branding, while 方 supplies the sound. By extension from an outlying or marked-off position, the sense shifted to “side/beside” in modern usage. Other proposals see earlier tops as 冖, 凡, or 用, reflecting uncertainty about the original semantic.
  - **component characters:**
    - 辛 (xīn, "punishment")
    - 方 (fāng, "direction")
