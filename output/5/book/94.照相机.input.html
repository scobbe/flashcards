Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 照相机 – see 照相機 (“camera”).
(This term is the simplified form of 照相機).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
to take a picture; to photograph; photo machine; opportunity; secret
trad. (照相機) 照相 機
simp. (照相机) 照相 机
