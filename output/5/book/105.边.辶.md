#### edge; side

- **contemporary usage:**

- **etymology (辵):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 彳 (chì, "walk") + 止 (zhǐ, "foot").
  - **interpretation:** It combines 彳 (chì, "walk") indicating movement with 止 (zhǐ, "foot") indicating the act of stepping, yielding the idea of going or traveling. The streamlined form 辶 is used as the semantic radical for characters related to movement, paths, and roads.
  - **component characters:**
    - 彳
    - 止
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E8%BE%B5
  - **simplification rule (辶):** Reduced-stroke combining form of 辵 used as a radical: dots and strokes were condensed into the three-stroke 辶 for ease and consistency in components.
