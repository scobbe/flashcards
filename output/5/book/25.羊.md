## sheep
---
- **traditional:** 羊
- **simplified:** 羊
- **pronunciation:** yáng
- **definition:** sheep
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 山上的羊 (shān shàng de yáng, "sheep on the mountain")
  - 羊肉 (yáng ròu, "mutton")
  - 羊毛 (yáng máo, "wool")
  - 羊群 (yáng qún, "flock of sheep")
- **etymology (羊):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a ram's head.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the head of a horned ram, indicating the animal directly. The drawing broadened to denote sheep and related caprids in general, which matches its modern use.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%BE%8A
