## to be accustomed to; habit
---
- **traditional:** 習慣
- **simplified:** 习惯
- **pronunciation:** xíguàn
- **definition:** to be accustomed to; habit
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 习(習)惯(慣)早起 (xí guàn zǎo qǐ, "used to getting up early")
  - 习(習)惯(慣)性 (xí guàn xìng, "habitual nature")
  - 习(習)惯(慣)用语(語) (xí guàn yòng yǔ, "idiomatic expression")
  - 习(習)惯(慣)成自然 (xí guàn chéng zì rán, "habit becomes second nature")
- **etymology (習慣):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 习(習) (xí, "practice") + 惯(慣) (guàn, "accustomed").
  - **interpretation:** 习 conveys repeated practice or learning, while 惯 denotes being accustomed. Together they express a state built from repeated practice, yielding the modern senses of habit and being used to.
  - **component characters:**
    - 习
    - 習
    - 惯
    - 慣
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E7%BF%92%E6%85%A3
  - **simplification rule (习惯):** Simplified by reducing 習 to 习 and by simplifying 慣 via the component change 貫 → 贯, following stroke reduction and component replacement conventions.
