## nation; nationality
---
- **traditional:** 民族
- **simplified:** 民族
- **pronunciation:** mínzú
- **definition:** nation; nationality
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 民族国(國)家 (mínzú guójiā, "nation-state")
  - 民族主义(義) (mínzú zhǔyì, "nationalism")
  - 民族区(區)域自治区(區) (mínzú qūyù zìzhìqū, "ethnic autonomous regions")
  - 民族运(運)动(動) (mínzú yùndòng, "national movement")
- **etymology (民族):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 民 (mín, "people") + 族 (zú, "clan").
  - **interpretation:** A semantic compound: 民 (mín, "people") plus 族 (zú, "clan; lineage") yields the idea of a people defined by lineage or ethnicity, hence "ethnic group; nation". Early attestations show a broader sense closer to 民众(民眾) (mínzhòng, "general populace"), which later specialized to ethnicity/nationality and extended to nation-state contexts.
  - **component characters:**
    - 民
    - 族
