## hand
### sub-component
---
- **traditional:** 手
- **simplified:** 手
- **pronunciation:** shǒu
- **definition:** hand
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 手机(機) (shǒujī, "mobile phone")
  - 手术(術) (shǒushù, "surgical operation")
  - 手表(錶) (shǒubiǎo, "wrist watch")
  - 手指 (shǒuzhǐ, "human finger")
- **etymology (手):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a human hand with five fingers.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human hand with five fingers; the top stroke represents a bent finger and the horizontal strokes represent pairs of fingers. Because it directly depicts a hand, it naturally came to mean “hand” and serves as the semantic base for hand-related terms and the hand radical.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
