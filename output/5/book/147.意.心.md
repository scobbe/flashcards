## heart
### sub-component
#### meaning or intention
---
- **traditional:** 心
- **simplified:** 心
- **pronunciation:** xīn
- **definition:** heart
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 心脏(臟) (xīnzàng, "heart organ")
  - 心理 (xīnlǐ, "mental state")
  - 心情 (xīnqíng, "emotional mood")
  - 心态(態) (xīntài, "state of mind")
- **etymology (心):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of the anatomical heart.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts the anatomical heart. Over time the graph was extended to represent the mind, feelings, and intentions, and by further extension the center or core in compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
