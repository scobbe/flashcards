## new year
---
- **traditional:** 新年
- **simplified:** 新年
- **pronunciation:** xīnnián
- **definition:** new year
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 新年快乐(樂) (xīn nián kuài lè, "happy New Year")
  - 新年好 (xīn nián hǎo, "New Year greeting")
  - 新年红(紅)包 (xīn nián hóng bāo, "New Year red envelope")
  - 新年计(計)划(劃) (xīn nián jì huà, "New Year plan")
- **etymology (新年):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 新 (xīn, "new") + 年 (nián, "year").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 新 (xīn, "new") with 年 (nián, "year") to mean the year that is new. In modern usage it denotes the New Year, whether the Gregorian 1 January or the Lunar New Year.
  - **component characters:**
    - 新
    - 年
