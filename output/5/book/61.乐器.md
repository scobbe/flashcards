## musical instrument
---
- **traditional:** 樂器
- **simplified:** 乐器
- **pronunciation:** yuèqì
- **definition:** musical instrument
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 乐(樂)器种(種)类(類) (yuèqì zhǒnglèi, "instrument types")
  - 乐(樂)器店 (yuèqì diàn, "instrument store")
  - 乐(樂)器教学(學) (yuèqì jiàoxué, "instrument instruction")
  - 乐(樂)器维(維)修 (yuèqì wéixiū, "instrument repair")
- **etymology (樂器):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 乐(樂) (yuè, "music") + 器 (qì, "instrument").
  - **interpretation:** 乐(樂) (yuè, "music") indicates the musical domain, and 器 (qì, "instrument") denotes a tool or device; together they compose the sense "musical instrument." The meaning is a straightforward compositional noun formed by combining the domain with the object category.
  - **component characters:**
    - 乐
    - 樂
    - 器
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%A8%82%E5%99%A8
  - **simplification rule (乐器):** The simplified form 乐器 replaces 樂 with 乐 through component reduction and stroke minimization, while 器 remains unchanged.
