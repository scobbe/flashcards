## roof
### sub-component
#### third earthly branch
---
- **traditional:** 宀
- **simplified:** 宀
- **pronunciation:** mián
- **definition:** roof
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 宀部 (mián bù, "roof radical")
  - 宀部首 (mián bùshǒu, "Kangxi roof radical")
  - 宀字 (mián zì, "roof-radical characters")
  - 宀字旁 (mián zìpáng, "roof component")
- **etymology (宀):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a house with four walls and a roof.
  - **interpretation:** Depicts a house with four walls and a roof. In later use it serves as a semantic radical for words related to buildings, rooms, and shelter; traditional scholarship contrasts it with 广 as four-walled versus three-walled structures.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
