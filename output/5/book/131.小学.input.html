Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 小学 – see 小學 (“primary school; elementary school; traditional Chinese philology, phonology and semantics”).
(This term is the simplified form of 小學).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Noun

[edit]

小(しょう)学(がく) • (shōgaku) 

  1. elementary school (grades 1-6)

[edit]

Noun

[edit]

小學

  1. primary school; elementary school
    我的孩子正在小學讀書。 [MSC, trad.]
    我的孩子正在小学读书。 [MSC, simp.]
    Wǒ de háizi zhèngzài xiǎoxué dúshū. [Pinyin]
    My child is currently in primary school.
  2. (historical) traditional Chinese philology, phonology and semantics
    Hyponyms: 文字學 / 文字学 (wénzìxué), 音韻學 / 音韵学 (yīnyùnxué), 訓詁學 / 训诂学 (xùngǔxué)
    • 以足下讀古人書，為文章，善小學，其為多能若是。 [Classical Chinese, trad.]
      以足下读古人书，为文章，善小学，其为多能若是。 [Classical Chinese, simp.]
      From: circa 806, Liu Zongyuan (柳宗元),The template Template:lw does not use the parameter(s): 
      collapsed=y
      Please see Module:checkparams for help with this warning.      《賀進士王參元失火書》, translated into English as Congratulations on a Fire by Herbert Allen Giles, 1922.
      Yǐ zúxià dú gǔrén shū, wéi wénzhāng, shàn xiǎoxué, qí wéi duōnéng ruòshì. [Pinyin]
      (Not translated literally) My friend, you are widely read in ancient lore. You are an accomplished scholar: a man, in fact, of many gifts.
