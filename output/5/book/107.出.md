## to go out; to emit
### sub-word
#### to set out; to start off
---
- **traditional:** 出
- **simplified:** 出
- **pronunciation:** chū
- **definition:** to go out; to emit
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 出来(來) (chūlái, "come out")
  - 出国(國) (chūguó, "go abroad")
  - 出现(現) (chūxiàn, "appear")
  - 出发(發) (chūfā, "set off")
- **etymology (出):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 止 (zhǐ, "foot") + 凵 (kǎn, "cave") → 出 (chū, "go out").
  - **interpretation:** Combines 止 (zhǐ, "foot") stepping out of 凵 (kǎn, "cave") to depict emergence, yielding the sense "to go out/exit" that aligns with modern uses. Later scholars note the graph’s form changed; Shuowen alternatively took it as 屮 (chè, "plant") sprouting, but this is considered a misinterpretation.
  - **component characters:**
    - 止 (zhǐ, "foot")
    - 凵 (kǎn, "cave")
