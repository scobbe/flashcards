Pictogram (象形): 士 + 士 – two ritual axes, perhaps made of jade, one on top of the other. Duplication of 士. Stylized into but unrelated to 土; according to folk etymologies, it's a reduplication of the character 土.

Definitions

[edit]

圭

  1. jade pointed at top
  2. (historical) jade tablet used by officials when addressing the emperor in court
    See also: 珪
  3. (historical) gui (type of ancient Chinese sundial)
  4. a surname
