Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 工作人员 – see 工作人員 (“staff; employee; personnel”).
(This term is the simplified form of 工作人員).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Chinese

[edit]
to work; to work
staff; personnel; employee
staff; personnel; employee; worker; officer; person
 
trad. (工作人員) 工作 人員
simp. (工作人员) 工作 人员

Noun

[edit]

工作人員

  1. staff; employee; personnel
    請找工作人員協助。 [MSC, trad.]
    请找工作人员协助。 [MSC, simp.]
    Qǐng zhǎo gōngzuò rényuán xiézhù. [Pinyin]
    Please see staff for assistance.

Synonyms

[edit]
edit

  • 僱員 / 雇员 (gùyuán)
  • 員工 / 员工 (yuángōng)
  • 職員 / 职员 (zhíyuán)
  • 職工 / 职工 (zhígōng)
