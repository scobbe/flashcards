Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 兵马俑 – see 兵馬俑 (“terracotta warriors and horses buried with the dead”).

Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

Noun

[edit]

兵馬俑

  1. terracotta warriors and horses buried with the dead
