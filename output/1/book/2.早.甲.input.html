From Proto-Sino-Tibetan *krap (“shell; shield”). Cognate with Tibetan ཁྲབ (khrab, “coat of mail; shield”). Perhaps related to 鞈 (OC *kuːb, *kruːb, “leather jerkin or cuirass”) (Schuessler, 2007).

    Definitions

    [edit]

    甲

    1. shell; carapace
    2. plate armor
    3. nail (plate at the end of fingers and toes)
    4. the first of the ten heavenly stems
    5. number one; first; A
      中國足球協會甲級聯賽 [MSC, trad.]
      中国足球协会甲级联赛 [MSC, simp.]
      zhōngguó zúqiú xiéhuì jiǎjí liánsài [Pinyin]
      Chinese Football Association League 1
    6. (organic chemistry) metho-
    7. to be first in; to be unparalleled among
      桂林山水甲天下  ―  Guìlín shānshuǐ jiǎ tiānxià  ―  The mountains and rivers of Guilin are the finest under heaven. (A proverb)
      • 匡廬奇秀，甲天下山，山北峰曰香爐峰，北寺曰遺愛寺，介峰寺間，其境勝絕，又甲廬山。 [Literary Chinese, trad.]
        匡庐奇秀，甲天下山，山北峰曰香炉峰，北寺曰遗爱寺，介峰寺间，其境胜绝，又甲庐山。 [Literary Chinese, simp.]
        From: 817, Bai Juyi,《廬山草堂記》, translated by Lin Yutang
        Kuānglú qíxiù, jiǎ tiānxià shān, shān běi fēng yuē Xiānglúfēng, běi sì yuē Yí'àisì, jiè fēng sì jiān, qí jìng shèngjué, yòu jiǎ Lúshān. [Pinyin]
        The beauties of Kuanglu Mountain top all mountains of the world. On its north stand the Xianglu Peak and the Wei-ai Temple. The view between the peak and the temple again tops that of all Lushan.
    8. pronoun for an unspecified or unknown person or thing
    9. a surname
