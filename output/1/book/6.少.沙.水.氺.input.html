Han character

[edit]

氺 (Kangxi radical 85, 水+1, 5 strokes, cangjie input 難水 (XE) or 火火 (FF) or 難難火火 (XXFF), composition ⿻亅⿱丷八 or ⿲冫亅⿱丿丶)

Usage notes

[edit]

Radical-like form; used in compound characters, usually at the bottom. Unrelated to the bottom part of 暴, in which it is a stylization of 米.
