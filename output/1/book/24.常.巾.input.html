Definitions

[edit]

巾

  1. a square item of cloth; towel; kerchief
  2. (historical) piece of clothing worn on the head in China

Korean

Etymology

[edit]

From Middle Chinese 巾 (MC kin). Recorded as Middle Korean 건 (ken) (Yale: ken) in Hunmong Jahoe (訓蒙字會 / 훈몽자회), 1527.

Hanja

[edit]

巾 (eumhun 수건 건 (sugeon geon))

  1. hanja form? of 건 (“towel”)
  2. hanja form? of 건 (“piece of cloth; fabric”)
  3. hanja form? of 건 (“headscarf”)
