皂 *zuːʔ

Early bronze inscriptions: Pictogram (象形) – acorn. Original form of 皂 (OC *zuːʔ) through an intermediate variant 皁 (once also written as 草 (cǎo)). Borrowed phonetically for "morning" sense.

Late bronze inscriptions: Phono-semantic compound (形聲 / 形声, OC *ʔsuːʔ): semantic 日 (“sun”) + phonetic 棗 (OC *ʔsuːʔ), with 棗 sometimes simplified as 朿.

Small seal script: Ideogrammic compound (會意 / 会意): 日 (“sun”) + 甲 (“first”) – when the sun first rises. However, Xu Shen's interpretation is not based on oracle bones and bronzes inscriptions.

In some ancient texts, 蚤 (OC *ʔsuːʔ) was often used instead.

Unrelated to the ancient version of 是: in this character, it doesn't represent an acorn but a spoon.

Etymology

[edit]

Possible cognate found in Proto-Hmong-Mien *ntsi̯ouˣ (“early”), whence White Hmong ntxov and Iu Mien nziou (Baxter and Sagart, 2014; Schuessler, 2007), and Proto-Tai *ɟaːwꟲ (“early morning”), whence Thai เช้า (cháao) and Lü ᦋᧁᧉ (tsaw²). Note also similarities to Burmese စော (cau:, “early”).

Definitions

[edit]

早

  1. early; in advance; soon
    我醒得很早。  ―  Wǒ xǐng dé hěn zǎo.  ―  I woke up early.
    你能不能早一點來？ [MSC, trad.]
    你能不能早一点来？ [MSC, simp.]
    Nǐ néng bù néng zǎo yīdiǎn lái? [Pinyin]
    Can you be here a little earlier?
    請問，我今天早走一會兒行嗎？ [MSC, trad.]
    请问，我今天早走一会儿行吗？ [MSC, simp.]
    Qǐngwèn, wǒ jīntiān zǎo zǒu yīhuìr xíng ma? [Pinyin]
    Please could I leave early today?
    Antonym: 遲 / 迟
  2. long ago; for a long time
    他早走了。  ―  Tā zǎo zǒu le.  ―  He left long ago.
    這個變壓器已經燒毀了，早該拆換一個新的了。 [MSC, trad.]
    这个变压器已经烧毁了，早该拆换一个新的了。 [MSC, simp.]
    Zhè ge biànyāqì yǐjīng shāo huǐ le, zǎo gāi chāi huàn yī ge xīn de le. [Pinyin]
    This transformer has burnt out. It should have been replaced a long time ago.
  3. (early) morning
    我今早沒有見到他。 [MSC, trad.]
    我今早没有见到他。 [MSC, simp.]
    Wǒ jīn zǎo méi yǒu jiàndào tā. [Pinyin]
    I didn't see him this morning.
  4. good morning
    老師早！／老师早！  ―  Lǎoshī zǎo!  ―  Good morning, teacher!
    Synonym: 早安 (zǎo'ān)
  5. before; in the past time
    現在這麼匆忙，你早幹嗎去了。 [MSC, trad.]
    现在这么匆忙，你早干吗去了。 [MSC, simp.]
    Xiànzài zhème cōngmáng, nǐ zǎo gànmá qù le. [Pinyin]
    So now you are in a hurry. What were you doing before?
  6. short for 早上 (zǎoshang); a.m.
    早八  ―  zǎo bā  ―  8:00 a.m.
  7. (Hainanese) breakfast
  8. a surname
