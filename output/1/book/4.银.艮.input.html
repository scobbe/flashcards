Definitions

[edit]

艮

  1. (dialectal) blunt; straightforward
    • 這人真艮。／这人真艮。  ―  Zhè rén zhēn gěn.  ―  That fellow is really blunt.
    • 他說的話太艮。 [MSC, trad.]
      他说的话太艮。 [MSC, simp.]
      Tā shuō de huà tài gěn. [Pinyin]
      He put it too sharply.
  2. (dialectal) tough, chewy
    • 艮蘿蔔不好吃。 [MSC, trad.]
      艮萝卜不好吃。 [MSC, simp.]
      Gěn luóbo bù hǎochī. [Pinyin]
      Tough radishes are not good for eating.

Noun

[edit]

艮(ごん) • (gon) 

  1. the seventh of the eight trigrams in the I Ching, symbolizing mountains. ☶
    Hypernym: 八卦
    Coordinate terms: 乾, 兌, 離, 震, 巽, 坎, 艮, 坤
  2. the fifty-second of the sixty-four hexagrams of the I Ching ䷳
    Hypernym: 六十四卦
