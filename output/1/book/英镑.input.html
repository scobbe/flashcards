Chinese

[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 英镑 – see 英鎊 (“pound sterling”).
(This term is the simplified form of 英鎊).
Notes:
  • Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia, and Singapore.
  • Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.

[edit]
United Kingdom pound (unit of currency)
trad. (英鎊) 英 鎊
simp. (英镑) 英 镑
alternative forms 英磅
