Etymology

[edit]
"bank"
Possibly coined in British Hong Kong; attested as early as 1854 in Chinese Serial.

Noun

[edit]

銀行

  1. bank (financial institution) (Classifier: 家 m; 所 m; 個／个 m; 間／间 c mn)
    銀行出納員／银行出纳员  ―  yínháng chūnàyuán  ―  bank teller
    銀行業務／银行业务  ―  yínháng yèwù  ―  banking business
    銀行集團／银行集团  ―  yínháng jítuán  ―  banking group
    中國郵政儲蓄銀行 [MSC, trad.]
    中国邮政储蓄银行 [MSC, simp.]
    Zhōngguó Yóuzhèng Chǔxù Yínháng [Pinyin]
    Postal Savings Bank of China
    銀行的貸款應按期償還。 [MSC, trad.]
    银行的贷款应按期偿还。 [MSC, simp.]
    Yínháng de dàikuǎn yīng ànqī chánghuán. [Pinyin]
    Bank loans should be paid back on time.
  2. (literary, archaic) jewellery dealer
