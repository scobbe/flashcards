Chinese

[edit]
row of logs or boards; a platoon; line up squadron; team; group
trad. (排隊) 排 隊
simp. (排队) 排 队

Verb

[edit]

排隊

  1. to line up; to queue up
    排隊買票／排队买票  ―  páiduì mǎipiào  ―  to queue to buy tickets
    請排隊上車。／请排队上车。  ―  Qǐng páiduì shàngchē.  ―  Please line up for boarding.
    請您排隊等候。／请您排队等候。  ―  Qǐng nín páiduì děnghòu.  ―  Please wait in line.
    請在此排隊。／请在此排队。  ―  Qǐng zài cǐ páiduì.  ―  Line forms here.
    我排緊隊㗎，唔好打櫼喎。 [Cantonese, trad.]
    我排紧队㗎，唔好打櫼㖞。 [Cantonese, simp.]
    ngo5 paai4 gan2 deoi6-2 gaa3, m4 hou2 daa2 zim1 wo3. [Jyutping]
    I'm in line. Don't cut in line.
