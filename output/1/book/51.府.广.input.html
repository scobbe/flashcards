Chinese

Glyph origin

[edit]
Historical forms of the character 广
Western Zhou Shuowen Jiezi (compiled in Han) Liushutong (compiled in Ming)
Bronze inscriptions Small seal script Transcribed ancient scripts

Pictogram (象形): A house with a roof, similar to 宀. According to Wang Yun (王筠), the former character depicts a building with three walls, while the latter depicts a house with four walls.

Unrelated to 床 and 疒, in which it represents a bed. Unrelated to ⼚, which represents a cliff.

Definitions

[edit]

廣

  1. broad; wide; extensive; vast
  2. many; numerous; multitudinous
  3. to expand; to enlarge
  4. common; widespread; universal; pervasive; general; ubiquitous
  5. short for 廣州／广州 (Guǎngzhōu, “Guangzhou”)
  6. short for 廣東／广东 (Guǎngdōng, “Guangdong”)
    • c. 1733, 藍鼎元, 論南洋事宜書; quoted in 魏源 [Wei Yuan], 賀長齡 [He Changling], editors, 皇朝經世文編, 1826:
      閩廣人稠地狹，田園不足於耕。望海謀生，十居五六。 [Classical Chinese, trad.]
      闽广人稠地狭，田园不足于耕。望海谋生，十居五六。 [Classical Chinese, simp.]
      Mǐn Guǎng rén chóu dì xiá, tiányuán bù zú yú gēng. Wàng hǎi móushēng, shí jū wǔ liù. [Pinyin]
      (please add an English translation of this quotation)
  7. (Sichuanese) overseas; foreign; western; imported
  8. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) width; breadth
  9. a surname, Guang
