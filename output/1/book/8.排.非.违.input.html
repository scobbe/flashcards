Definitions

[edit]

違

  1. to disobey, to violate
    違規／违规  ―  wéiguī  ―  to violate regulations
    • 孝子之養老也，樂其心不違其志。 [Traditional Chinese poetry, trad.]
      孝子之养老也，乐其心不违其志。 [Traditional Chinese poetry, simp.]
      From: The Book of Rites, c. 4th – 2nd century BCE
      Xiàozǐ zhī yǎnglǎo yě, lè qí xīn bù wéi qí zhì. [Pinyin]
      A filial son, in nourishing his aged, (seeks to) make their hearts glad, and not to go against their wishes.
  2. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to leave; to depart; to go away from
    • 何斯違斯，莫敢遑息？ [Pre-Classical Chinese, trad.]
      何斯违斯，莫敢遑息？ [Pre-Classical Chinese, simp.]
      From: The Classic of Poetry, c. 11th – 7th centuries BCE, translated based on James Legge's version
      Hé sī wéi sī, mò gǎn huáng xī? [Pinyin]
      How was it he went away from this, not daring to take a little rest?
  3. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to be separated by (a distance of); to be apart by
  4. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to dodge; to avoid

違

(Jōyō kanji)

  1. to differ, to vary
  2. to be incorrect, to make a mistake
  3. to run counter to, to go against, to defy
