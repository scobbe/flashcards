Phono-semantic compound (形聲 / 形声, OC *beds, *bed): phonetic 巾 (OC *krɯn) + semantic 攴; then, the left component evolved into 㡀, in which the points around the cloth represent dust and/or dirt.

Similar but unrelated to 黹.

Definitions

[edit]

敝

  1. (polite) my; our
    • 歡迎來到敝校／欢迎来到敝校  ―  huānyíng láidào bì xiào  ―  Welcome to our school.
  2. (literary) worn-out; shabby; tattered
  3. (literary) decayed; corrupted
  4. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) tired; fatigued
  5. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to fail; to be defeated
  6. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) to break; to destroy
  7. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) alternative form of 弊 (bì, “fault; defect; drawback”)
  8. (obsolete on its own in Standard Chinese) alternative form of 蔽 (bì, “to cover; to shield; to deceive”)
