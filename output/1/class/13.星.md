## star
### sub-word
#### Sunday
---
- **traditional:** 星
- **simplified:** 星
- **pronunciation:** xīng
- **definition:** star
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 星星 (xīng xing, "stars")
  - 恒(恆)星 (héng xīng, "fixed star")
  - 明星 (míng xīng, "celebrity")
  - 五角星 (wǔ jiǎo xīng, "five-pointed star")
- **etymology (星):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 晶 (jīng, "twinkling stars") → semantic: 日 (rì, "sun") + phonetic: 生 (shēng, "life").
  - **interpretation:** Originally the word was written with 晶 to evoke the sparkle of stars. Later 生 was added as a phonetic to fix the reading, and 晶 was reduced to 日, which now serves as the semantic determinative indicating light/heavenly bodies.
  - **component characters:**
    - 晶
    - 日
    - 生
