## modifier or possessive particle
---
- **traditional:**: 的
- **simplified:**: 的
- **pronunciation:**: de
- **definition:**: modifier or possessive particle
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 新的软(軟)件 (xīn de ruǎn jiàn, "new software")
  - 的话(話) (de huà, "if clause marker")
  - 的确(確) (dí què, "truly indeed")
  - 我的书(書) (wǒ de shū, "my book")
- **etymology (的):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 日 (rì, "sun") + phonetic: 勺 (sháo, "ladle") → semantic: 白 (bái, "white") + phonetic: 勺 (sháo, "ladle").
  - **interpretation:**: The character combines a semantic determinative of light/color (originally 日, later 白) with the phonetic 勺. This explains early senses like “bright” and the target mark. The possessive particle use is a later borrowing to write the high‑frequency grammatical marker de, supplanting 底; proposals link it to lenited 之, 者, or 底.
  - **component characters:**
    - 日
    - 勺
    - 白
