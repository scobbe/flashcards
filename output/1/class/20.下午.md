## afternoon
---
- **traditional:**: 下午
- **simplified:**: 下午
- **pronunciation:**: xià wǔ
- **definition:**: afternoon
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 下午三点(點) (xià wǔ sān diǎn, "3 p.m.")
  - 下午茶 (xià wǔ chá, "afternoon tea")
  - 下午班 (xià wǔ bān, "afternoon shift")
  - 下午好 (xià wǔ hǎo, "good afternoon")
- **etymology (下午):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 下 (xià, "down") + 午 (wǔ, "noon").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 下 meaning “after/below” with 午 meaning “noon” to denote the time after noon. The compound straightforwardly yields the modern sense “afternoon.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 下
    - 午
