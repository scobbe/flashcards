## first time
---
- **traditional:**: 第一次
- **simplified:**: 第一次
- **pronunciation:**: dì yī cì
- **definition:**: first time
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 第一次见(見)面 (dì yī cì jiàn miàn, "first meeting")
  - 第一次约(約)会(會) (dì yī cì yuē huì, "first date")
  - 第一次尝(嘗)试(試) (dì yī cì cháng shì, "first attempt")
  - 第一次世界大战(戰) (dì yī cì shì jiè dà zhàn, "First World War")
- **etymology (第一次):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 第一 (dì yī, "first") + 次 (cì, "occurrence") → 第一次 (dì yī cì, "first time").
  - **interpretation:**: Formed by combining 第一 “first” with 次 “occurrence,” literally “first occurrence,” hence “first time.” In modern usage it denotes the earliest instance of an event and also serves as a euphemism for one’s first sexual experience.
  - **component characters:**
    - 第
    - 一
    - 次
