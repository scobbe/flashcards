## scorch
### sub-component
#### Han; Chinese
---
- **traditional:** 暵
- **simplified:** 暵
- **pronunciation:** hàn
- **definition:** scorch
- **contemporary usage:**
  - None, not in contemporary use
- **etymology (暵):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 日 (rì, "sun") + phonetic: 堇 (jǐn, "burned man").
  - **interpretation:** 日 supplies the semantic idea of the sun, indicating heat and drying. 堇 provides the pronunciation and, being traditionally associated with a man burned alive, reinforces the sense of scorching and dryness.
  - **component characters:**
    - 日
    - 堇
