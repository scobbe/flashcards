## house; room
### sub-word
#### house; building
---
- **traditional:**: 房
- **simplified:**: 房
- **pronunciation:**: fáng
- **definition:**: house; room
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 房子 (fáng zi, "house")
  - 房屋 (fáng wū, "residential building")
  - 房间(間) (fáng jiān, "room")
  - 心房 (xīn fáng, "heart atrium")
- **etymology (房):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: phono-semantic: semantic: 户(戶) (hù, "door") + phonetic: 方 (fāng, "side").
  - **interpretation:**: The semantic component 户(戶) points to doors and dwellings, fitting the domain of houses and rooms. 方 supplies the pronunciation fáng and may also hint at a side room beside a central hall. This combination accounts for the modern senses of house and room.
  - **component characters:**
    - 户
    - 戶
    - 方
