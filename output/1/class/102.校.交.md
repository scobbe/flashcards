## cross
### sub-component
#### school
---
- **traditional:** 交
- **simplified:** 交
- **pronunciation:** jiāo
- **definition:** cross
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 交通 (jiāo tōng, "transportation")
  - 交易 (jiāo yì, "trade")
  - 交给(給) (jiāo gěi, "hand over to")
  - 交换(換) (jiāo huàn, "to exchange")
- **etymology (交):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of 人 (rén, "person") seated with crossed legs.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human with crossed legs, visually suggesting crossing or meeting. This concrete image generalized to senses of intersecting, mixing, mutual contact, and exchange, which motivates modern meanings like trading, handing over, and communication.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
