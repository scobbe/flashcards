## journey; procedure or program
### sub-word
#### engineer
---
- **traditional:** 程
- **simplified:** 程
- **pronunciation:** chéng
- **definition:** journey; procedure or program
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 工程 (gōngchéng, "engineering field")
  - 工程师(師) (gōngchéngshī, "professional engineer")
  - 回程 (huíchéng, "return trip")
  - 单(單)程 (dānchéng, "one-way trip")
- **etymology (程):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 禾 (hé, "grain") + phonetic: 呈 (chéng, "present") → "measurements".
  - **interpretation:** 禾 supplies the semantic idea of measuring grain and, by extension, standardized measures and ordered courses. 呈 provides the phonetic value chéng. From the sense of measurements and standards, the character extended in compounds to schedules, journeys, and modern procedural meanings.
  - **component characters:**
    - 禾 (hé, "grain")
    - 呈 (chéng, "present")
