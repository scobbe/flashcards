## member; personnel
### sub-word
#### employee or staff member
---
- **traditional:** 員
- **simplified:** 员
- **pronunciation:** yuán
- **definition:** member; personnel
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 员(員)工 (yuángōng, "company employees")
  - 公务(務)员(員) (gōngwùyuán, "civil servant")
  - 乘务(務)员(員) (chéngwùyuán, "service attendant")
  - 团(團)员(員) (tuányuán, "league member")
- **etymology (員):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 〇 (líng, "circle") + 鼎 (dǐng, "tripod") → round.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a circle marking the top edge of a ding tripod, conveying the idea of roundness. The character later served as the original form of 圆/圓 “round” and was subsequently used to write the morpheme meaning “member; personnel” in modern compounds.
  - **component characters:**
    - 〇
    - 鼎
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%93%A1
  - **simplification rule (员):** Simplified from 員 by replacing the bottom component 貝 with its simplified form 贝 under 口, reducing strokes and regularizing the shape.
