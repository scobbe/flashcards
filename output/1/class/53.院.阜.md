## mound
### sub-component
#### courtyard; institute
---
- **traditional:** 阜
- **simplified:** 阜
- **pronunciation:** fù
- **definition:** mound
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 阴(陰)阜 (yīn fù, "mons pubis")
  - 阜阳(陽) (fù yáng, "Fuyang (city)")
  - 曲阜 (qū fù, "Qufu (city)")
  - 物阜民丰(豐) (wù fù mín fēng, "abundant goods, people prosperous")
- **etymology (阜):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of steps on a hill.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a stepped slope or terrace on a hillside, hence the original sense ‘mound’. By extension, it came to mean ‘abundant’ in literary usage, evoking things piled up or richly supplied.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
