## to want; essential
### sub-word
#### need or require
---
- **traditional:** 要
- **simplified:** 要
- **pronunciation:** yào
- **definition:** to want; essential
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 要求 (yāoqiú, "to request")
  - 要挟(挾) (yāoxié, "to threaten")
  - 要约(約) (yāoyuē, "contract offer")
  - 要职(職) (yàozhí, "important post")
- **etymology (要):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: 大 (dà, "big") later depicted as 女 (nǚ, "woman") with hands indicating the waist, with 目 (mù, "eye") above → 角 (jiǎo, "horn") stylization forming the top.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person, later a woman, with hands pointing to the waist and an eye above; the upper element was simplified through a horn-like form. The original sense was “waist,” which was later borrowed for a near-homophonous morpheme meaning “to demand/want,” leading to modern senses like “want/need” and “important.”
  - **component characters:**
    - 大 (dà, "big")
    - 女 (nǚ, "woman")
    - 目 (mù, "eye")
    - 角 (jiǎo, "horn")
