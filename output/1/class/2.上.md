## up; to go; previous
### sub-word
#### go to work
---
- **traditional:**: 上
- **simplified:**: 上
- **pronunciation:**: shàng
- **definition:**: up; to go; previous
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 上一年 (shàng yī nián, "last year")
  - 上集 (shàng jí, "previous episode")
  - 上世纪(紀) (shàng shì jì, "last century")
  - 上古汉(漢)语(語) (shàng gǔ hàn yǔ, "Old Chinese")
- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:**: ideogram
  - **description:**: ideogram: a short stroke placed above 一 (yī, "one") to indicate the position "above" → regularized form with the upper stroke positioned over 一 (yī, "one").
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a short mark above a baseline (一), concretely showing something located above. This spatial relation naturally extends to modern meanings like on/above and, by metaphor, to earlier or higher rank such as previous or superior.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
