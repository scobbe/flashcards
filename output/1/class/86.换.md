## exchange money; change or replace
---
- **traditional:**: 換
- **simplified:**: 换
- **pronunciation:**: huàn
- **definition:**: exchange money; change or replace
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 换(換)大的 (huàn dà de, "switch to bigger one")
  - 换(換)衣服 (huàn yīfu, "change clothes")
  - 换(換)钱(錢) (huàn qián, "exchange money")
  - 换(換)车(車) (huàn chē, "change trains")
- **etymology (換):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: phono-semantic: semantic 手 (shǒu, "hand") + phonetic 奂(奐) (huàn, "brilliant").
  - **interpretation:**: 手 (shǒu, "hand") signals a manual action, aligning with exchanging or replacing items. 奂(奐) (huàn, "brilliant") supplies the sound; the simplified form retains this phonetic to preserve the reading.
  - **component characters:**
    - 手
    - 奂
    - 奐
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E6%8F%9B
  - **simplification rule (换):**: The simplified form 换 replaces the right-side phonetic 奐 with its simplified counterpart 奂 while keeping the hand radical, reducing strokes.
