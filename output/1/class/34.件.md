## item; piece
### sub-word
#### software
---
- **traditional:**: 件
- **simplified:**: 件
- **pronunciation:**: jiàn
- **definition:**: item; piece
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 文件 (wénjiàn, "document file")
  - 邮(郵)件 (yóujiàn, "postal mail")
  - 软(軟)件 (ruǎnjiàn, "software program")
  - 证(證)件 (zhèngjiàn, "identification document")
- **etymology (件):**
  - **type:**: ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**: ideogrammic: 人 (rén, "man") + 牛 (niú, "cow").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 人 (rén, "man") with 牛 (niú, "cow") to depict a person associated with a cow, highlighting a single unit. This generalized to the idea of a discrete item or piece, and in modern use extends to documents and a classifier for countable pieces.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人
    - 牛
