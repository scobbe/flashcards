## slash
### sub-component
#### bud
---
- **traditional:**: 丿
- **simplified:**: 丿
- **pronunciation:**: piě / yì
- **definition:**: slash
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 丿部首 (piě bùshǒu, "radical slash")
  - 丿部 (piě bù, "radical 4")
  - 丿笔(筆)画(畫) (piě bǐhuà, "slash stroke")
  - 丿这(這)一笔(筆) (piě zhè yī bǐ, "this slash stroke")
- **etymology (丿):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a slanted brush stroke → used as the name of the left-falling stroke.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a slanted brush stroke. The graph was subsequently used as the radical/stroke name for the left-falling line in characters; some historical dictionaries also recorded a phonetic-loan sense ‘arrive’ (yì), now obsolete.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E4%B8%BF
