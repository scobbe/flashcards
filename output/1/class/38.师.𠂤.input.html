Glyph origin

[edit]
Historical forms of the character 𠂤
Shang Western Zhou Shuowen Jiezi (compiled in Han)
Oracle bone script Bronze inscriptions Small seal script

Pictogram (象形) – hills. It could also represent buttocks, but it's less likely.

In 官 it represents multiple rooms under a roof. See also 師 (> 师) and 追.
