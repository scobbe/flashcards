## noon
---
- **traditional:**: 中午
- **simplified:**: 中午
- **pronunciation:**: zhōng wǔ
- **definition:**: noon
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 中午一点(點) (zhōngwǔ yī diǎn, "1 pm")
  - 中午饭(飯) (zhōngwǔ fàn, "lunch")
  - 中午休息 (zhōngwǔ xiūxi, "noon break")
  - 中午时(時)分 (zhōngwǔ shífēn, "around noon")
- **etymology (中午):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 中 (zhōng, "middle") + 午 (wǔ, "noon").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 中 (zhōng, "middle") and 午 (wǔ, "noon") to mean the middle of the noon period. 午 also names the earthly branch time of roughly 11 a.m.–1 p.m., so 中午 naturally denotes noon in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 中
    - 午
