## big
### sub-component
#### to want; essential
---
- **traditional:** 大
- **simplified:** 大
- **pronunciation:** dà
- **definition:** big
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 大雨 (dà yǔ, "heavy rain")
  - 大不列颠(顛) (dà bù liè diān, "Great Britain")
  - 很大 (hěn dà, "very big")
  - 太大 (tài dà, "too big")
- **etymology (大):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 人 (rén, "person").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a person facing forward. The graph was originally used for 夫 (fū, "man") and was later borrowed phonetically to write 大 (dà, "big"), which became the current meaning. Some later explain it as a person with outstretched arms, but early forms do not show stretching.
  - **component characters:**
    - 人 (rén, "person")
