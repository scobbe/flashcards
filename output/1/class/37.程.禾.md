## grain
### sub-component
#### journey; procedure or program
---
- **traditional:**: 禾
- **simplified:**: 禾
- **pronunciation:**: hé
- **definition:**: grain
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 禾苗 (hé miáo, "crop seedlings")
  - 禾仔 (hé zǎi, "rice plant")
  - 禾本科 (hé běn kē, "grass family")
  - 禾谷类(類) (hé gǔ lèi, "cereal grains")
- **etymology (禾):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a plant stalk.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a stalk of grain with leaves and an ear, representing growing cereal plants. This direct depiction yielded the meanings 'grain/cereal' and 'rice plant' in use. The graph later served as a semantic radical for crop-related characters.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
