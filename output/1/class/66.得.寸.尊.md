## venerate
### sub-component
#### inch
---
- **traditional:**: 尊
- **simplified:**: 尊
- **pronunciation:**: zūn
- **definition:**: venerate
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 尊敬 (zūn jìng, "to respect")
  - 尊重 (zūn zhòng, "show respect")
  - 尊严(嚴) (zūn yán, "dignity")
  - 尊崇 (zūn chóng, "to venerate")
- **etymology (尊):**
  - **type:**: ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**: ideogrammic: 酋 (qiú, "wine") + 寸 (cùn, "hand") → 尊.
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 酋 (qiú, "wine") and 寸 (cùn, "hand") to depict offering wine with the hand as a sign of respect. This ritual offering yields the meanings ‘to honor’ and ‘to venerate’ in modern usage. An alternative analysis reads it as 酋 (qiú, "wine") with 廾 (gǒng, "two hands"), likewise indicating a respectful two‑handed offering.
  - **component characters:**
    - 酋
    - 寸
    - 尊
