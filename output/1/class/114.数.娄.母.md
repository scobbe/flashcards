## mother
### sub-component
---
- **traditional:**: 母
- **simplified:**: 母
- **pronunciation:**: mǔ
- **definition:**: mother
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 母子 (mǔ zǐ, "mother and son")
  - 母牛 (mǔ niú, "female cow")
  - 母校 (mǔ xiào, "alma mater")
  - 父母 (fù mǔ, "father and mother")
- **etymology (母):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a woman with breasts → differentiated from 女 (nǚ, "woman") by added dots.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a woman’s torso with prominent breasts, specifying the idea of a mother. In early oracle inscriptions, both 女 (nǚ, "woman") and the mother graph were written alike; added dots distinguished the maternal graph from the general woman sign. These distinguishing dots persist and mark its maternal sense in modern use.
  - **component characters:**
    - 女
