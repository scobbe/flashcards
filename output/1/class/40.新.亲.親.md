- **etymology (親):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phonetic: 辛 (xīn, "bitter") + semantic: 见(見) (jiàn, "see").
  - **interpretation:** 见(見) functions as the semantic determinative, evoking seeing or face-to-face contact, which aligns with senses like being close, in person, or kissing. 辛 serves as the phonetic element, giving the syllable and tone; the word later generalized to kinship and intimacy.

    - 辛 (xīn, "bitter")
