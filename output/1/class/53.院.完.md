## complete
### sub-component
#### courtyard; institute
---
- **traditional:**: 完
- **simplified:**: 完
- **pronunciation:**: wán
- **definition:**: complete
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 完成 (wánchéng, "to accomplish")
  - 完工 (wángōng, "finish work")
  - 完美 (wánměi, "perfect and flawless")
  - 完全版 (wánquán bǎn, "complete edition")
- **etymology (完):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: phono-semantic: semantic: 宀 (mián, "roof") + phonetic: 元 (yuán, "origin") → 完 (wán, "complete").
  - **interpretation:**: The semantic component 宀 "roof" evokes shelter and intactness, fitting the idea of being whole or preserved. 元 serves as the phonetic element approximating the sound and also suggests an original/whole state, aligning with the modern sense of "complete."
  - **component characters:**
    - 宀 (mián, "roof")
    - 元 (yuán, "origin")
