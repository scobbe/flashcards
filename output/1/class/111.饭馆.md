## restaurant
---
- **traditional:**: 飯館
- **simplified:**: 饭馆
- **pronunciation:**: fàn guǎn
- **definition:**: restaurant
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)儿(兒) (fànguǎnr, "eatery")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)里(裡) (fànguǎn lǐ, "in the restaurant")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)老板 (fànguǎn lǎobǎn, "restaurant owner")
  - 饭(飯)馆(館)菜单 (fànguǎn càidān, "restaurant menu")
- **etymology (飯館):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 饭(飯) (fàn, "food; meal") + 馆(館) (guǎn, "house; establishment").
  - **interpretation:**: 饭 means food or a meal, and 馆 means a house or establishment. Together they literally mean a food establishment, which naturally came to denote a place that serves meals, i.e., a restaurant.
  - **component characters:**
    - 饭
    - 飯
    - 馆
    - 館
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%A3%AF%E9%A4%A8
  - **simplification rule (饭馆):**: Form simplified by component reduction: 飯 → 饭 and 館 → 馆, replacing the 食 radical with 饣 and streamlining the remaining parts to reduce strokes.
