## younger brother
### sub-component
#### ordinal prefix; sequence
---
- **traditional:**: 弟
- **simplified:**: 弟
- **pronunciation:**: dì
- **definition:**: younger brother
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 弟弟 (dì dì, "younger brother")
  - 堂弟 (táng dì, "paternal younger cousin")
  - 内(內)弟 (nèi dì, "wife's younger brother")
  - 兄弟 (xiōng dì, "brothers")
- **etymology (弟):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of the handle of 弋 (yì, "shoot") wrapped with a strap → semantic: order/sequence → semantic extension: younger brother.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a spear handle wrapped in a leather strap. The tied strap suggested arrangement or orderly sequence, which then extended to the kinship sense ‘younger brother’.
  - **component characters:**
    - 弋
