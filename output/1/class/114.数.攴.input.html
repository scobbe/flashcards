攴 *pʰoːɡ, *pʰroːɡ

Pictogram (象形): a hand (又) holding a tool of some sort (possibly a weapon or whip), meaning “to hit”. The tool later corrupted into the phonetic component 卜 (OC *poːɡ). Today, the character is mostly stylized except in some archaic variants.

Similar but unrelated to 支, 伊, and 父. The stylized version is unrelated to 囱 (OC *sʰroːŋ, *sʰloːŋ) (> 窗) and 文.

Definitions

[edit]

攴

  1. to hit lightly; to tap

Usage notes

[edit]

When used as a component in other characters, 攴 can be referred to as 敲字邊／敲字边 (qiāozìbiān).
