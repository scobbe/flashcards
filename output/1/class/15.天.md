## day; sky
### sub-word
#### Sunday
---
- **traditional:** 天
- **simplified:** 天
- **pronunciation:** tiān
- **definition:** day; sky
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 天空 (tiān kōng, "the sky")
  - 今天 (jīn tiān, "this day")
  - 天桥(橋) (tiān qiáo, "pedestrian overpass")
  - 天灾(災) (tiān zāi, "natural disaster")
- **etymology (天):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 一 (yī, "one") + 大 (dà, "man").
  - **interpretation:** A horizontal line above a person depicts the sky over human beings. The parts combine to mean what is overhead, hence 'sky', later extended to 'heaven', 'day', and weather-related senses in modern usage. Ancient anthropomorphic forms existed, and later head-top interpretations were proposed, but these are now considered mistaken.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 大
