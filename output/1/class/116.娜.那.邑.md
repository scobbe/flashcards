## city
### sub-component
#### that
---
- **traditional:**: 邑
- **simplified:**: 邑
- **pronunciation:**: yì
- **definition:**: city
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 四邑 (sì yì, "Siyi region")
  - 五邑 (wǔ yì, "Wuyi region")
  - 邑人 (yì rén, "townspeople")
  - 城邑 (chéng yì, "cities and towns")
- **etymology (邑):**
  - **type:**: ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**: ideogrammic: 囗 (wéi, "enclosure; city wall") + 卪 (jié, "kneeling person").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 囗 (wéi, "enclosure; city wall") with 卪 (jié, "kneeling person") to depict a person within walls, i.e., a settled, walled place. This composition naturally yields the meaning of an inhabited city or town and related administrative senses.
  - **component characters:**
    - 囗
    - 卪
