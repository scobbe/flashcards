## one
### sub-word
#### first time
---
- **traditional:**: 一
- **simplified:**: 一
- **pronunciation:**: yī
- **definition:**: one
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一起 (yì qǐ, "together with")
  - 一样(樣) (yí yàng, "the same")
  - 一直 (yì zhí, "all along")
  - 一边(邊) (yì biān, "on one side")
- **etymology (一):**
  - **type:**: ideogram
  - **description:**: ideogram: horizontal stroke → numeral value “one”.
  - **interpretation:**: The character depicts a single horizontal stroke to mark a single unit. This simple ideographic form directly denotes the number one and was standardized across scripts, preserving the original meaning into modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
