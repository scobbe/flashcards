## roof
### sub-component
#### cave
---
- **traditional:**: 宀
- **simplified:**: 宀
- **pronunciation:**: mián
- **definition:**: roof
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 宀部 (mián bù, "roof radical section")
  - 宀字头(頭) (mián zì tóu, "roof top component")
  - 宀部字 (mián bù zì, "characters under 宀")
  - 宀部首 (mián bù shǒu, "the 宀 radical")
- **etymology (宀):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a house with four walls and a roof → later standardized as a small top component indicating roof or shelter.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a house with four walls and a roof, i.e., a complete dwelling. Over time it was regularized and reduced in size to function as a top component that signals meanings related to houses, roofs, and shelter.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
