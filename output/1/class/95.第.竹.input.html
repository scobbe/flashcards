Pictogram (象形) – two bamboo stalks, with leaves. Earlier forms resembled 个 + 个, current form resembles rather ケ + ケ, or 亇 + 亇.

See also the original version of 散.

Etymology

[edit]

Compare Proto-Tai *b.twuːkᴰ (“bamboo strip for tying or weaving”), whence Thai ตอก (dtɔ̀ɔk, “bamboo strip”).

    Definitions

    [edit]

    竹

    1. (～亞科) bamboo (Classifier: 枝; 棵 m; 根 m; 轆／辘 c)
      竹子  ―  zhúzi  ―  bamboo
    2. (music) musical instrument made of bamboo
    3. a surname, Zhu
