## avoid
### sub-component
#### evening; late
---
- **traditional:**: 免
- **simplified:**: 免
- **pronunciation:**: miǎn
- **definition:**: avoid
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 免税(稅) (miǎnshuì, "duty free")
  - 免签(簽) (miǎnqiān, "visa free")
  - 免职(職) (miǎnzhí, "dismiss from office")
  - 免疫力 (miǎnyìlì, "immune strength")
- **etymology (免):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a man wearing a ceremonial hat → borrowed for the senses "avoid" and "exempt".
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a man wearing a ceremonial hat. The graph was later borrowed to write a different word meaning “avoid, exempt,” which accounts for the modern senses such as exemption and avoidance.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
