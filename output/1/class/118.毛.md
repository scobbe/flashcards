## hair or fur; 0.1 yuan
---
- **traditional:**: 毛
- **simplified:**: 毛
- **pronunciation:**: máo
- **definition:**: hair or fur; 0.1 yuan
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一毛钱(錢) (yī máo qián, "ten cents")
  - 毛利 (máo lì, "gross profit")
  - 毛毛雨 (máo máo yǔ, "drizzle")
  - 毛手毛脚(腳) (máo shǒu máo jiǎo, "be careless")
- **etymology (毛):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a tuft of hair or feather.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a small tuft of hair or a feather. The original concrete sense ‘hair/fur’ remains primary, and the character later appears in compounds extending to related notions such as ‘gross/rough’ and the colloquial monetary usage for a jiao.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
