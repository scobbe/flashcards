## live; stay
---
- **traditional:**: 住
- **simplified:**: 住
- **pronunciation:**: zhù
- **definition:**: live; stay
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 住在 (zhù zài, "live at")
  - 住所 (zhù suǒ, "residence")
  - 住址 (zhù zhǐ, "address")
  - 住宅区(區) (zhù zhái qū, "residential area")
- **etymology (住):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 亻 (rén, "person") + phonetic: 主 (zhǔ, "master") → 住 (zhù, "live; stay").
  - **interpretation:**: 亻 (rén, "person") supplies the semantic field of human activity and habitation. 主 (zhǔ, "master") serves as the phonetic, pointing to the reading zhù, while the person determinative aligns with meanings like living, residing, or staying. A proposed Proto-Sino-Tibetan root is mentioned in the source, but the phono-semantic analysis explains the character’s structure and sense.
  - **component characters:**
    - 亻
    - 主
    - 住
