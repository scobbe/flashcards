## line
### sub-component
#### bud
---
- **traditional:**: 丨
- **simplified:**: 丨
- **pronunciation:**: gǔn
- **definition:**: line
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 丨笔(筆)画(畫) (gǔn bǐhuà, "vertical stroke")
  - 丨部首 (gǔn bùshǒu, "line radical")
  - 丨的写(寫)法 (gǔn de xiěfǎ, "how to write 丨")
  - 丨在网(網)络(絡)用语(語) (gǔn zài wǎngluò yòngyǔ, "丨 in internet slang")
- **etymology (丨):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a vertical wooden stick.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a vertical wooden stick. This concrete image naturally gave the sense of a straight vertical stroke in writing and by extension a rod; the internet slang use is a homophonic substitution for gǔn and is unrelated to the original pictorial meaning.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
