## cave
### sub-component
#### wear; put on
---
- **traditional:**: 穴
- **simplified:**: 穴
- **pronunciation:**: xué / xuè
- **definition:**: cave
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 太阳(陽)穴 (tài yáng xué, "temple of head")
  - 洞穴 (dòng xué, "natural cave")
  - 墓穴 (mù xué, "grave pit")
  - 巢穴 (cháo xué, "lair or den")
- **etymology (穴):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a cave entrance → standardized as 宀 (mián, "roof") over 八 (bā, "eight").
  - **interpretation:**: Depicts the opening of a cave or a door flap. From this concrete image it came to mean holes, caves, and dens, and by extension the acupoints of the body seen as small openings.
  - **component characters:**
    - 宀 (mián, "roof")
    - 八 (bā, "eight")
