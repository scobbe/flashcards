## unit; yuan
### sub-word
#### U.S. dollar
---
- **traditional:** 元
- **simplified:** 元
- **pronunciation:** yuán
- **definition:** unit; yuan
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 欧(歐)元 (ōu yuán, "euro")
  - 美元 (měi yuán, "US dollar")
  - 日元 (rì yuán, "Japanese yen")
  - 港元 (gǎng yuán, "Hong Kong dollar")
- **etymology (元):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a person with the head marked by 二 (èr, "two") lines over a body 儿(兒) (ér, "person") → original meaning "head; beginning".
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a human figure with the head emphasized by two lines, highlighting the head as the source or start. From this, the sense broadened to "first; primary" and later extended to the principal monetary unit in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 二
    - 儿
    - 兒
