## tap
### sub-component
#### number; count
---
- **traditional:**: 攴
- **simplified:**: 攴
- **pronunciation:**: pū
- **definition:**: tap
- **contemporary usage:**
  - None, not in contemporary use
- **etymology (攴):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: 又 (yòu, "hand") holding a tool → phonetic: 卜 (bǔ, "divination").
  - **interpretation:**: Depicts a hand holding a striking tool, expressing the act of hitting lightly. Over time the tool element regularized to resemble 卜 and was reinterpreted as a phonetic, while 又 supplies the semantic cue for actions of striking/tapping.
  - **component characters:**
    - 又
    - 卜
