## work; worker
### sub-word
#### employee or staff member
---
- **traditional:**: 工
- **simplified:**: 工
- **pronunciation:**: gōng
- **definition:**: work; worker
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 工作 (gōngzuò, "to work")
  - 工地 (gōngdì, "construction site")
  - 工农(農) (gōngnóng, "workers and peasants")
  - 工程 (gōngchéng, "engineering project")
- **etymology (工):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a bladed tool used to plow the ground and mark borders → semantic: "work; artisan".
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a bladed tool for plowing and marking boundaries, which naturally associates the graph with labor and craftsmanship. The sense broadened to mean work in general and, by extension, worker and related industrial/technical domains. An alternative view takes it as a carpenter’s square and relates it to the original sense of 巨 (jù, "huge").
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
