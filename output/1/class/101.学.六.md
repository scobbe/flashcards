## six
### sub-component
#### to study; learning
---
- **traditional:** 六
- **simplified:** 六
- **pronunciation:** liù / lù
- **definition:** six
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 六月 (liùyuè, "month of June")
  - 六十 (liùshí, "the number sixty")
  - 六边(邊)形 (liùbiānxíng, "six-sided polygon")
  - 六年级(級) (liùniánjí, "sixth grade")
- **etymology (六):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram of 庐(廬) (lú, "shed") → semantic: 六 (liù, "six").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a shed or hut, and was later borrowed as a phonetic loan to write the numeral ‘six’. Over time the character’s value stabilized to mean the number. An alternative view treats it as an ancient counting symbol.
  - **component characters:**
    - 庐
    - 廬
    - 六
