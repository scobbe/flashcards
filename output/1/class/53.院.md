## courtyard; institute
### sub-word
#### hospital
---
- **traditional:**: 院
- **simplified:**: 院
- **pronunciation:**: yuàn
- **definition:**: courtyard; institute
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 院子 (yuàn zi, "residential courtyard")
  - 医(醫)院 (yī yuàn, "public hospital")
  - 法院 (fǎ yuàn, "law court")
  - 学(學)院 (xué yuàn, "academic institute")
- **etymology (院):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: phono-semantic: semantic: 阜 (fù, "mound") + phonetic: 完 (wán, "complete") → 院.
  - **interpretation:**: Formed as a phono-semantic compound with 阜 (fù, "mound") as the semantic determinative indicating a place/enclosure and 完 (wán, "complete") as the phonetic element. The place/enclosure determinative aligns with modern senses like courtyards and institutions (courts, hospitals), while the phonetic component supplies the reading yuàn.
  - **component characters:**
    - 阜 (fù, "mound")
    - 完 (wán, "complete")
