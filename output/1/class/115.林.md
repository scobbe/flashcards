## forest; Lin (surname)
---
- **traditional:**: 林
- **simplified:**: 林
- **pronunciation:**: lín
- **definition:**: forest; Lin (surname)
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 林业(業) (lín yè, "forestry sector")
  - 竹林 (zhú lín, "bamboo forest")
  - 防风(風)林 (fáng fēng lín, "windbreak belt")
  - 武林 (wǔ lín, "martial arts community")
- **etymology (林):**
  - **type:**: ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**: ideogrammic: 木 (mù, "tree") + 木 (mù, "tree") → 林 (lín, "forest").
  - **interpretation:**: Two instances of 木 are combined to depict multiple trees, yielding the idea of a grove or forest. The duplication conveys plurality, which naturally extends to the modern sense of “forest” and metaphorical groups such as communities or circles.
  - **component characters:**
    - 木
    - 林
