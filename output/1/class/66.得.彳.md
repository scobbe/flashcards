## step
### sub-component
#### structural particle; must or have to
---
- **traditional:**: 彳
- **simplified:**: 彳
- **pronunciation:**: chì
- **definition:**: step
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 彳亍 (chì chù, "pace to and fro")
  - 彳亍而行 (chì chù ér xíng, "walk hesitantly")
  - 彳亍独(獨)行 (chì chù dú xíng, "wander alone")
  - 彳亍街头(頭) (chì chù jiē tóu, "loiter on the street")
- **etymology (彳):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram of 行 (xíng, "crossroads") → left-side component standardized as 彳 indicating "step".
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a street intersection from above, as in 行. The left portion was abstracted and standardized as 彳, carrying the sense of steps and movement. As a standalone character, it preserves the idea of slow, halting walking.
  - **component characters:**
    - 行
    - 彳
