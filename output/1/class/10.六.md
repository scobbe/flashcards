## six
### sub-word
#### Saturday
---
- **traditional:**: 六
- **simplified:**: 六
- **pronunciation:**: liù
- **definition:**: six
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 六月 (liù yuè, "June")
  - 六十 (liù shí, "sixty")
  - 六边(邊)形 (liù biān xíng, "hexagon")
  - 六年级(級) (liù nián jí, "sixth grade")
- **etymology (六):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram of 庐(廬) (lú, "hut").
  - **interpretation:**: Depicts a hut or shed. Later, by phonetic loan from the similar-sounding 庐(廬) (lú, "hut"), it came to denote liù (six). Alternatively, some interpret it as an ancient counting symbol.
  - **component characters:**
    - 庐
    - 廬
