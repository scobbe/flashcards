## get off work
---
- **traditional:**: 下班
- **simplified:**: 下班
- **pronunciation:**: xià bān
- **definition:**: get off work
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 下班后(後) (xià bān hòu, "after work")
  - 下班时(時)间(間) (xià bān shí jiān, "quitting time")
  - 下班高峰期 (xià bān gāo fēng qī, "after-work rush hour")
  - 下班回家 (xià bān huí jiā, "go home after work")
- **etymology (下班):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 下 (xià, "down") + 班 (bān, "work shift").
  - **interpretation:**: 下 means to go down/finish, and 班 denotes a work shift. Together they literally mean leaving one’s shift, hence “to finish work” and, by extension, a business being closed after hours. In transport, 下 “next” plus 班 “scheduled service” gives the adjective meaning “next service”.
  - **component characters:**
    - 下
    - 班
