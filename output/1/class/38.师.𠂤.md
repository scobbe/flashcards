- **traditional:** 𠂤

- **definition:** mound, hill
- **contemporary usage:**
  - None, not in contemporary use
- **etymology (𠂤):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of hills.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts small hills or mounds, hence the original meaning 'mound' or 'hill'. Some sources have proposed it depicts buttocks, but this is less likely and not reflected in its primary sense. It later functioned as the archaic form of 堆 (duī, "heap"), preserving the topographic/accumulative sense.
