## sheep
### sub-component
#### kind; appearance
---
- **traditional:**: 羊
- **simplified:**: 羊
- **pronunciation:**: yáng
- **definition:**: sheep
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 羊角面(麵)包 (yáng jiǎo miàn bāo, "croissant pastry")
  - 羊肉串 (yáng ròu chuàn, "lamb skewers")
  - 羊毛 (yáng máo, "sheep wool")
  - 羊奶 (yáng nǎi, "goat milk")
- **etymology (羊):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a ram's head.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a ram's head with prominent horns, representing the animal. The meaning remained for sheep/goats and extends as the radical for words related to caprids in modern Chinese.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
