Glyph origin

[edit]
Historical forms of the character 丨
Shuowen Jiezi (compiled in Han) Liushutong (compiled in Ming)
Small seal script Transcribed ancient scripts

According to Shuowen Jiezi, it is a pictogram of a vertical wooden stick. It appears in the original version of 朕, which originally meant "to mend a boat", and in 尹, in which the stick symbolizes authority.

Definitions

[edit]

丨

  1. To connect vertically; to pass through from top to bottom.
  2. A stick; a rod.
  3. The vertical stroke (known as 竖, shù) in Chinese characters.
  4. (Internet slang) A homophone of 滚 (gǔn, “get lost; go away”).
