## introduce; introduction
---
- **traditional:**: 介紹
- **simplified:**: 介绍
- **pronunciation:**: jiè shào
- **definition:**: introduce; introduction
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 介绍(紹)一下 (jiè shào yí xià, "briefly introduce")
  - 介绍(紹)信 (jiè shào xìn, "letter of introduction")
  - 介绍(紹)人 (jiè shào rén, "introducing person")
  - 介绍(紹)所 (jiè shào suǒ, "matchmaking agency")
- **etymology (介紹):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 介 (jiè, "between") + 绍(紹) (shào, "connect").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 介 (jiè, "between") and 绍(紹) (shào, "connect") to express introducing by linking two parties through an intermediary. The earliest attestation in the Book of Rites describes messages being passed through attendants, aligning with this mediated sense. The modern meanings extend naturally to recommending or briefing by creating a connection.
  - **component characters:**
    - 介
    - 绍
    - 紹
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E4%BB%8B%E7%B4%B9
  - **simplification rule (介绍):**: The traditional 紹 was simplified to 绍 via the series-wide replacement of the silk radical 糸 with 纟 and stroke reduction; 介 was unchanged, yielding 介绍.
