## wait; etc.
---
- **traditional:** 等
- **simplified:** 等
- **pronunciation:** děng
- **definition:** wait; etc.
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 等一下 (děng yī xià, "wait a moment")
  - 等待 (děng dài, "to wait")
  - 等级(級) (děng jí, "rank")
  - 等于(於) (děng yú, "equal to")
- **etymology (等):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** phono-semantic: semantic: 竹 (zhú, "bamboo") + phonetic: 寺 (sì, "temple").
  - **interpretation:** 竹 marks bamboo slips once used for records such as grading and classification, aligning with senses like rank/class and the enumeration particle. 寺 supplies the approximate sound for the syllable in Old Chinese; some sources note later borrowing for a morpheme of similar meaning without phonetic relation to 寺.
  - **component characters:**
    - 竹
    - 寺
