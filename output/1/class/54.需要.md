## need or require
---
- **traditional:** 需要
- **simplified:** 需要
- **pronunciation:** xū yào
- **definition:** need or require
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 需要帮(幫)助 (xūyào bāngzhù, "need help")
  - 需要数(數)分钟(鐘) (xūyào shù fēnzhōng, "require several minutes")
  - 需要口译(譯)员(員) (xūyào kǒuyìyuán, "need an interpreter")
  - 需要品 (xūyào pǐn, "required items")
- **etymology (需要):**
  - **type:** semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 需 (xū, "need") + 要 (yào, "vital; essential") → 需要 (xūyào, "need; require").
  - **interpretation:** Formed by combining 需 ‘to need’ and 要 ‘to want; essential’, yielding the sense ‘to need; requirement’. Both morphemes contribute meaning rather than sound, so the compound transparently denotes necessity in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 需
    - 要
