## tree
### sub-component
#### school
---
- **traditional:** 木
- **simplified:** 木
- **pronunciation:** mù
- **definition:** tree
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 木头(頭) (mùtou, "log")
  - 木屋 (mùwū, "wooden house")
  - 木讷(訥) (mùnè, "plain and inarticulate")
  - 灌木 (guànmù, "shrub")
- **etymology (木):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a tree with spreading branches above and roots below.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a tree. The concrete sense extended to materials from trees (wood, timber) and, by metaphor in compounds, to ‘wooden’ or ‘numb’, aligning with its role as the semantic determinative for wood-related terms.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
