## to start a business
### sub-word
#### startup company
---
- **traditional:**: 創業
- **simplified:**: 创业
- **pronunciation:**: chuàngyè
- **definition:**: to start a business
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 创(創)业(業)精神 (chuàng yè jīng shén, "enterprising spirit")
  - 创(創)业(業)者 (chuàng yè zhě, "entrepreneur")
  - 创(創)业(業)投资(資) (chuàng yè tóu zī, "venture capital")
  - 创(創)业(業)板 (chuàng yè bǎn, "growth enterprise board")
- **etymology (創業):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 创(創) (chuàng, "begin") + 业(業) (yè, "business").
  - **interpretation:**: 创 means to initiate or begin, and 业 means enterprise or business. Combined, they denote initiating an enterprise, which in modern usage is starting a business and engaging in entrepreneurship.
  - **component characters:**
    - 创
    - 創
    - 业
    - 業
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%89%B5%E6%A5%AD
  - **simplification rule (创业):**: Component replacement and stroke reduction: 創 was simplified to 创 and 業 to 业.
