## week
### sub-word
#### Saturday
---
- **traditional:**: 星期
- **simplified:**: 星期
- **pronunciation:**: xīngqī
- **definition:**: week
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 星期一 (xīng qī yī, "Monday (weekday)")
  - 星期天 (xīng qī tiān, "Sunday (weekday)")
  - 星期几(幾) (xīng qī jǐ, "which weekday")
  - 星期五 (xīng qī wǔ, "Friday (weekday)")
- **etymology (星期):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 星 (xīng, "star") + 期 (qī, "period") → 星期 (xīngqī, "week").
  - **interpretation:**: Combines 星 “star” and 期 “period/time” to mean a period marked by the stars, i.e., a weekly cycle tied to celestial reckoning. In modern usage it simply denotes a week and, with numerals, specific weekdays.
  - **component characters:**
    - 星
    - 期
