## life; person
### sub-word
#### doctor or physician
---
- **traditional:**: 生
- **simplified:**: 生
- **pronunciation:**: shēng
- **definition:**: life; person
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 生活 (shēnghuó, "daily life")
  - 生命 (shēngmìng, "human life")
  - 生气(氣) (shēngqì, "get angry")
  - 生产(產) (shēngchǎn, "to produce")
- **etymology (生):**
  - **type:**: ideogrammic compound
  - **description:**: ideogrammic: 屮 (chè, "bud") + 一 (yī, "ground") → 生 (shēng, "sprout").
  - **interpretation:**: The graph combines 屮 ‘bud’ above 一 ‘ground’ to depict a sprout emerging from the soil, conveying growth and arising. This concrete image broadened to meanings like to be born, to grow, and by extension ‘life’ in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 屮 (chè, "bud")
    - 一 (yī, "ground")
