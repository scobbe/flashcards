## noon
### sub-word
#### noon
---
- **traditional:**: 午
- **simplified:**: 午
- **pronunciation:**: wǔ
- **definition:**: noon
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 中午 (zhōng wǔ, "at noon")
  - 上午 (shàng wǔ, "in the morning")
  - 下午 (xià wǔ, "in the afternoon")
  - 午饭(飯) (wǔ fàn, "midday meal")
- **etymology (午):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a pestle.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a pestle. The graph was later borrowed for the seventh earthly branch and, by association with the midday double-hour, developed the sense ‘noon’ used today.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
