## hospital
---
- **traditional:**: 醫院
- **simplified:**: 医院
- **pronunciation:**: yī yuàn
- **definition:**: hospital
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 医(醫)院看病 (yī yuàn kàn bìng, "see a doctor")
  - 医(醫)院感染 (yī yuàn gǎn rǎn, "hospital-acquired infection")
  - 医(醫)院门(門)诊(診) (yī yuàn mén zhěn, "outpatient clinic")
  - 医(醫)院管理 (yī yuàn guǎn lǐ, "hospital management")
- **etymology (醫院):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") + 院 (yuàn, "institution").
  - **interpretation:**: The word combines 医(醫) (yī, "medicine") with 院 (yuàn, "institution") to denote a medical institution. This composition straightforwardly yields the modern meaning "hospital" and the sense has remained stable.
  - **component characters:**
    - 医
    - 醫
    - 院
  - **reference:**: https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E9%86%AB%E9%99%A2
  - **simplification rule (医院):**: The simplified form replaces 醫 with the ancient variant 医 to reduce strokes, while 院 remains unchanged.
