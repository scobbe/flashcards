Compound - the radical of the sun 日 and 堇 as a phonetic component; however, the phonetic component is linked to fire and heat since it represents a man burned alive.

Definitions

[edit]

暵

  1. (literary) to dry by exposing to the sun
  2. (literary) to wither; to become dry
  3. (literary) dry; hot
