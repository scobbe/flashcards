## moxibustion
### sub-word
#### acupuncture and moxibustion
---
- **traditional:** 灸
- **simplified:** 灸
- **pronunciation:** jiǔ
- **definition:** moxibustion
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 灸法 (jiǔ fǎ, "moxibustion method")
  - 直接灸 (zhí jiē jiǔ, "direct moxibustion")
  - 间(間)接灸 (jiàn jiē jiǔ, "indirect moxibustion")
  - 针(針)灸 (zhēn jiǔ, "acupuncture and moxibustion")
- **etymology (灸):**
  - **type:** phono-semantic compound
  - **description:** semantic: 火 (huǒ, "fire") + phonetic: 久 (jiǔ, "long time") → 灸 (jiǔ, "moxibustion").
  - **interpretation:** 火 serves as the semantic determinative for burning and heat, while 久 provides the phonetic value jiǔ. The character thus denotes cauterizing with heat and today names moxibustion, a heat-based therapy.
  - **component characters:**
    - 火
    - 久
    - 灸
