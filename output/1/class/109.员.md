## member; personnel
### sub-word
#### server or waiter
---
- **traditional:** 員
- **simplified:** 员
- **pronunciation:** yuán
- **definition:** member; personnel
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 员(員)工 (yuángōng, "employee")
  - 公务(務)员(員) (gōngwùyuán, "civil servant")
  - 全员(員) (quányuán, "all members")
  - 伤(傷)员(員) (shāngyuán, "wounded person")
- **etymology (員):**
  - **type:** ideogrammic compound
  - **description:** ideogrammic: 〇 (líng, "zero") + 鼎 (dǐng, "cauldron") → 圆(圓) (yuán, "round").
  - **interpretation:** The graph combines a circle marking the rounded top rim of 鼎, yielding the idea of “round,” the original sense of 員. In modern Chinese, the “member/personnel” sense is a later semantic extension, while the original “round” sense survives in 圆.
  - **component characters:**
    - 〇
    - 鼎
    - 圆
    - 圓
  - **reference:** https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/%E5%93%A1
  - **simplification rule (员):** Component replacement and stroke reduction: 員 simplified by replacing 貝 with 贝 under the ⿱口 structure, regularizing and reducing strokes.
