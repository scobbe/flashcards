## few or little; less
---
- **traditional:**: 少
- **simplified:**: 少
- **pronunciation:**: shǎo
- **definition:**: few or little; less
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 多少人 (duō shǎo rén, "how many people")
  - 少数(數)民族 (shǎo shù mín zú, "ethnic minority")
  - 最少 (zuì shǎo, "at least")
  - 少之又少 (shǎo zhī yòu shǎo, "very few")
- **etymology (少):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of four dots (grains of sand) indicating a few → phonetic borrowing: 小 (xiǎo, "small").
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a handful of dots like grains of sand, conveying the idea of a small quantity. It was later borrowed for the sound of 小, which aligns with and reinforced the sense of ‘small/few’ in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 小
