## middle; China
### sub-word
#### traditional Chinese medicine; TCM doctor
---
- **traditional:**: 中
- **simplified:**: 中
- **pronunciation:**: zhōng
- **definition:**: middle; China
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 中央 (zhōng yāng, "central authorities")
  - 中国(國) (zhōng guó, "China")
  - 中心 (zhōng xīn, "center")
  - 中文 (zhōng wén, "Chinese language")
- **etymology (中):**
  - **type:**: pictogram
  - **description:**: pictogram: depiction of a flagpole with a drum placed at the center of a field.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a flagpole with a drum marking the center of a field, yielding the meaning ‘middle/center’. From this core sense, it extended to ‘hit the center; attain’, which underlies the zhòng reading. As alternatives, Shuowen explains it as 丨 (gǔn, "vertical stroke") passing through 口 (kǒu, "mouth"), and others see an arrow striking the center of a target.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
