## up; to go; previous
### sub-word
#### evening or night
---
- **traditional:**: 上
- **simplified:**: 上
- **pronunciation:**: shàng
- **definition:**: up; to go; previous
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 上一年 (shàng yī nián, "last year")
  - 上集 (shàng jí, "last episode")
  - 上世纪(紀) (shàng shì jì, "last century")
  - 上古汉(漢)语(語) (shàng gǔ hàn yǔ, "Old Chinese")
- **etymology (上):**
  - **type:**: ideogram
  - **description:**: ideogram: 一 (yī, "one") → a short line placed above 一 (yī, "one") to indicate a position above.
  - **interpretation:**: It depicts a short stroke above a baseline, visually marking something as higher or above. This concrete spatial idea underlies modern uses like the postposition meaning "on/above" and semantic extensions such as "previous/first part" in ordered sequences.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
