## stool
### sub-component
#### child; noun suffix
---
- **traditional:** 几
- **simplified:** 几
- **pronunciation:** jī / jǐ
- **definition:** stool
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 茶几(幾) (chá jī, "tea table")
  - 几(幾)案 (jī àn, "writing desk")
  - 窗明几(幾)净(淨) (chuāng míng jī jìng, "bright windows, clean table")
  - 梳妆(妝)几(幾) (shū zhuāng jī, "dressing table")
- **etymology (几):**
  - **type:** pictogram
  - **description:** pictogram: depiction of a small stool viewed from the side.
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a small stool seen from the side; the lower horizontal stroke originally marked the floor. The furniture sense persisted and remains in modern compounds for small tables and stools.
  - **component characters:**
    - None, character is in atomic form
