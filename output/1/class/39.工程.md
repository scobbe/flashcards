## engineering; project
### sub-word
#### engineer
---
- **traditional:**: 工程
- **simplified:**: 工程
- **pronunciation:**: gōngchéng
- **definition:**: engineering; project
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 工程学(學) (gōngchéngxué, "engineering science")
  - 工程师(師) (gōngchéngshī, "professional engineer")
  - 工程兵 (gōngchéngbīng, "military engineer")
  - 工程大 (gōngchéngdà, "engineering university")
- **etymology (工程):**
  - **type:**: semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 工 (gōng, "work") + 程 (chéng, "procedure").
  - **interpretation:**: The two morphemes combine literally as “work procedures/course,” referring to organized processes of work. This sense naturally extends to the discipline of engineering and, by extension, to specific projects.
  - **component characters:**
    - 工
    - 程
