## play; have fun
---
- **traditional:**: 玩
- **simplified:**: 玩
- **pronunciation:**: wán
- **definition:**: play; have fun
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 玩游(遊)戏(戲) (wán yóuxì, "play games")
  - 玩积(積)木 (wán jīmù, "play with blocks")
  - 玩耍 (wán shuǎ, "play around")
  - 去北京玩 (qù běijīng wán, "visit Beijing for fun")
- **etymology (玩):**
  - **type:**: phono-semantic compound
  - **description:**: semantic: 玉 (yù, "jade") + phonetic: 元 (yuán, "origin") → 玩.
  - **interpretation:**: 玉 (yù, "jade") provides the semantic idea of jade objects or ornaments, while 元 (yuán, "origin") serves as the phonetic indicator of the syllable. The word originally referred to jade playthings and later broadened to the general sense of playing and having fun.
  - **component characters:**
    - 玉
    - 元
    - 玩
