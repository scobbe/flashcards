## two
### sub-component
#### origin
---
- **traditional:** 二
- **simplified:** 二
- **pronunciation:** èr
- **definition:** two
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 二等品 (èr děng pǐn, "second-rate goods")
  - 二十岁(歲) (èr shí suì, "twenty years old")
  - 二十斤 (èr shí jīn, "twenty jin")
  - 二手车(車) (èr shǒu chē, "used car")
- **etymology (二):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: 一 (yī, "one") + 一 (yī, "one") → 二 (èr, "two").
  - **interpretation:** The character doubles 一 ("one"), forming two parallel strokes to indicate the quantity two. As an ideogram, its form transparently maps to the modern meaning of the numeral two.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
    - 二
