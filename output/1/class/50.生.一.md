## ground
### sub-component
#### life; person
---
- **traditional:** 一
- **simplified:** 一
- **pronunciation:** yī / yí / yì
- **definition:** ground
- **contemporary usage:**
  - 一起 (yì qǐ, "together")
  - 一样(樣) (yí yàng, "the same")
  - 一点(點)儿(兒) (yì diǎn r, "a little")
  - 一线(線) (yí xiàn, "front line")
- **etymology (一):**
  - **type:** ideogram
  - **description:** ideogram: horizontal stroke → number 一 (yī, "one").
  - **interpretation:** It depicts a single horizontal stroke, representing the concept of one. This simple mark naturally came to denote the numeral and basic unity, which is retained in modern usage.
  - **component characters:**
    - 一
